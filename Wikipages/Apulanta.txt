'''Apulanta''' is a Finnish [[rock band]], founded in 1991 when its members were in their mid-teens.

In September 2009, Apulanta members established an [[independent record label|independent label]], Päijät-Hämeen Sorto ja Riisto ("Päijänne Tavastian Oppression and Exploitation").<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.noise.fi/uutiset/?id=10961|title=Uusi levy-yhtiö – Päijät-Hämeen Sorto ja Riisto|first=Tero|last=Kallio|date=2009-09-30|publisher=noise.fi|language=Finnish|accessdate=2011-06-16}}</ref> Since its beginnings, the label has so far gotten one artist, pop singer [[Kaija Koo]] in the summer of 2010.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.mtv3.fi/viihde/uutiset/musiikki.shtml/1146365/apulannan-pojat-nappasivat-kaija-koon-levy-yhtioonsa|title=Apulannan pojat nappasivat Kaija Koon levy-yhtiöönsä|first=Ann-Mari|last=Leinonen|date=2010-06-20|publisher=[[Bonnier Group]]|language=Finnish|accessdate=2011-06-16}}</ref> In February 2011, they revealed that they had a new prospective artist in the label, though not disclosing their name.<ref name="apulanta-label-new-artist-thevoice">{{Cite web|url=http://www.voice.fi/index.php?mw=Apulanta+P%C3%A4ij%C3%A4t-H%C3%A4meen%20Sorto%20ja%20Riisto+&option=com_sbsarticle&tmpl=blog&cid=23595&cat=1|title=Uusi artisti kiikarissa Apulannan levy-yhtiöön|first=Eeva|last=Rajala|date=2011-02-09|publisher=[[ProSiebenSat.1 Media#European Free-to-air, Pay-TV- and Radio channels|The Voice]]|language=Finnish|accessdate=2011-06-16}}</ref>

== Members ==
=== Current members ===
* [[Toni Wirtanen]] – [[lead vocals]] and [[guitar]] (+ bass 1991–1992) (1991–)
* [[Simo Santapukki|Simo ”Sipe” Santapukki]] – [[Drum kit|drums]] and [[backing vocalist|backing vocals]] (1991–)
* [[Ville Mäkinen]] – [[bass guitar]] and [[contrabass]] (2014-)

=== Former members ===
* Pauli Hauta-aho – touring guitar (2016)
* [[Sami Lehtinen|Sami ”Parta-Sami” Lehtinen]] – [[bass guitar|bass]] and backing vocals (touring guitar 2001–2004) (2005–2014)
* [[Antti Lautala]] – lead vocals and guitar (+ bass 1991–1992) (1991–1994)
* [[Amanda Gaynor|Amanda ”Mandy” Gaynor]] – bass, [[trombone]] and vocals (1992–1993)
* [[Tuukka Temonen]] – bass (1993–2004)
* [[Jani Törmälä]] – [[concert|touring]] [[guitar]] (1997, 1998, 2000)
* [[Sami Yli-Pihlaja]] – touring guitar (1998–1999, 2000)
* [[Marzi Nyman]] – touring guitar (2000)
* [[Markus "Masi" Hukari]] – touring guitar (2001)
