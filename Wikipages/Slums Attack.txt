'''Slums Attack''' is a [[Poland|Polish]] [[gangsta rap|street rap]], [[Rap music|rap]] and [[hip-hop music|hip-hop]] group, founded in 1993 in [[Poznań]] by [[Peja (rapper)|Peja]] and Marcin "Iceman" Maćkowiak. Since 1998, the group consists of Peja and Dariusz "DJ Decks" Działek. The group became popular in Poland in 2000s.
