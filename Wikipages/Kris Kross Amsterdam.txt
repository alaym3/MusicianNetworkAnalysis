'''Kris Kross Amsterdam''' is a Dutch DJ and record producer trio formed by brothers Jordy and Sander Huisman and Yuki Kempees. Their music is a mix of various music styles including [[Contemporary R&B|R&B]], [[Hip hop music|hip hop]], [[funk]], [[Soul music|soul]], [[Pop music|pop]] and [[House music|house]].

The name is a nod to the famous rap duo [[Kris Kross]] from the early 1990s, known for their hit single "[[Jump (Kris Kross song)|Jump]]".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://musicallstarsmgmt.com/artist/kris-kross-amsterdam/|title=Kris Kross Amsterdam|publisher=MusicAllStars Management|access-date=28 August 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170326102143/http://musicallstarsmgmt.com/artist/kris-kross-amsterdam/|archive-date=26 March 2017|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.djguide.nl/djinfo.p?djid=9109|title=DJ Kris Kross Amsterdam (Sander Huisman, Jordy Huisman, Yuki Kempees)|publisher=thedjlist.com}}</ref>

==Career==

Kris Kross Amsterdam originated as two brothers in 2011 in Amsterdam. With their name paying homage to the legendary American hip hop duo [[Kris Kross]], they started hosting parties in Amsterdam where they began to play a mix of R&B, hip hop, twerk, trap and house. In 2014, MC Yuki Kempees joined them.

In June 2015 Kris Kross Amsterdam signed a deal with dance record label [[Spinnin’ Records]] and released their debut single "Until the Morning" with MC CHOCO.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/nl/album/until-the-morning-single/id1003771717|title='Until the Morning - Single' van Kris Kross Amsterdam & CHOCO op Apple Music|publisher=iTunes}}</ref>

In February 2016, KKA dropped multiple releases on the label, catching worldwide attention. Amongst those releases are the international hit single "[[Sex (Cheat Codes and Kris Kross Amsterdam song)|Sex]]" with American electronic music trio [[Cheat Codes (DJs)|Cheat Codes]], which samples the chorus from "[[Let's Talk About Sex]]".,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/sex-single/id1118482598|title=Sex - Single by Cheat Codes & Kris Kross Amsterdam on Apple Music|date=27 May 2016|publisher=iTunes}}</ref> [[Are You Sure? (Kris Kross Amsterdam song)|Are You Sure?]], a collaboration with British pop star [[Conor Maynard]] and American hip hop artist Ty Dolla $ign, and the most recent release with Conor and [[The Boy Next Door (DJ)|The Boy Next Door]], "[[Whenever (song)|Whenever]]". This track originated from [[Shakira]]'s "[[Whenever, Wherever]]". The song got over a million streams each day and charted on the ''Billboard'' Dance Chart.

Next to making singles, they do shows worldwide. They hosted several stages and highlighted sets at [[Mysteryland]] (NL), [[Tomorrowland (festival)|Tomorrowland]] (BE), [[Parookaville]] (GE), [[Ultra Music Festival]] (US).
