'''Eyyub Yaqubov''' ({{lang-az|Əyyub Yaqubov}}, born April 26, 1965, [[Baku]], [[Azerbaijan SSR]], [[USSR]]) is a popular [[Azerbaijani people|Azerbaijani]] singer.<ref>{{cite web|title=Эйюб Ягубов споет в дуэте с турецкой звездой|url=http://news.day.az/showbiz/413978.html|website=news.day.az|publisher=[[Day.az]]|accessdate=8 August 2014}}</ref> He is widely known for bringing Baku chanson genre to popularity in 1992.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Valiyev|first1=Suleyman|script-title=ru:Бакинский шансон умирает - певец Самед Самедов|url=http://www.trend.az/life/showbiz/972947.html|website=www.trend.az|accessdate=26 June 2014|language=Russian}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|script-title=ru:БОКА: «ЕСЛИ БУДЕТ ПРЕДЛОЖЕНИЕ ИЗ БАКУ, ТО Я ЕГО ПРИМУ»|url=http://vesti.az/news/38773#ad-image-0|website=vesti.az|accessdate=26 June 2014|language=Russian|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140810051129/http://vesti.az/news/38773#ad-image-0|archive-date=10 August 2014|url-status=dead}}</ref>
