'''Cadet''' was an American [[Christian alternative rock]] band from [[Eugene, Oregon]], and they formed in 2000 and disbanded in 2004, while their frontman was Ryan Smith. They released, [[Cadet (album)|''Cadet'']], with [[BEC Recordings]], in 2001. The same label released, [[The Observatory (album)|''The Observatory'']], in 2002.

==Background==
The [[Christian alternative rock]] band formed in [[Eugene, Oregon]],<ref name="AllMusic Biography">{{cite web |first=Mario |last=Mesquita Borges |title=Cadet : Biography  |url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/cadet-mn0000639210/biography |publisher=[[AllMusic]]|accessdate=July 19, 2015}}</ref> in 2000.<ref name="JFH Profile">{{cite web|title=Cadet  |url=http://www.jesusfreakhideout.com/artists/Cadet.asp |author=Jesus Freak Hideout |publisher=Jesus Freak Hideout|accessdate=July 19, 2015}}</ref> Their members were lead vocalist and lead guitarist, Ryan Smith, guitarist, keyboardist, and background vocalist, Matt Lenhart, bassist and background vocalist, Jason Kennedy, drummer and background vocalist, Chad Basom.<ref name="AllMusic Biography"/><ref name="JFH Profile"/> They disbanded as a group, in 2004.<ref name="JFH Profile"/>
