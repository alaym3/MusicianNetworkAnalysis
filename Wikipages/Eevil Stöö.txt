'''Tommi Liikka''', professionally known as '''Eevil Stöö''', is a Finnish rapper.<ref>{{cite web|title=Eevil Stöö Discography|url=http://www.discogs.com/artist/Evil+Stöö?anv=Eevil+St%C3%B6%C3%B6&filter_anv=1|publisher=discogs|accessdate=3 February 2013}}</ref> In addition to his solo work, he is also one half of the rap duo Siniset Punaiset Miehet.<ref>{{cite web|title=Siniset Punaiset Miehet Discography|url=http://www.discogs.com/artist/Siniset+Punaiset+Miehet|publisher=discogs|accessdate=3 February 2013}}</ref>

==Career==

Eevil Stöö's first album ''Pinkkibikinihinkkichiksi'' was released in 2002,<ref>{{cite web|title=Eevil Stöö* – Pinkkibikinihinkkichiksi|url=http://www.discogs.com/Eevil-Stöö-Pinkkibikinihinkkichiksi/release/311508|publisher=discogs|accessdate=3 February 2013}}</ref> but it was his 2011 album ''Stöö of Destruction'' that first brought him commercial success. Produced entirely by [[DJ Kridlokk]],<ref>{{cite web|title=Eevil Stöö: Stöö of Destruction vol 1.|url=http://www.hs.fi/arviot/Levy/Eevil+Stöö+Stöö+of+Destruction+vol+1/a1353042393632|publisher=Helsingin Sanomat|accessdate=3 February 2013}}</ref> the album peaked at number 24 on the [[The Official Finnish Charts|Official Finnish Album Chart]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Eevil Stö: Stöö Of Destruction|url=http://www.ifpi.fi/tilastot/virallinen-lista/artistit/eevil+stö/stöö+of+destruction+vol.+1|publisher=Musiikkituottajat ry|accessdate=3 February 2013}}</ref>

On 20 July 2012 Eevil Stöö and a fellow rapper [[Koksukoo]] released an album ''Fuck Vivaldi''. In the first week of its release, the album debuted at number one on the Official Finnish Album Chart.<ref>{{cite web|title=Musiikkituottajat – Tilastot – Suomen virallinen lista – Artistit|url=http://www.ifpi.fi/tilastot/virallinen-lista/artistit/eevil+stöö+x+koksu+koo/fuck+vivaldi|publisher=Musiikkituottajat ry|accessdate=3 February 2013}}</ref> It was later nominated for an [[Emma-gaala|Emma Award]] in the category of the best Hip Hop / Reggae / Urban Album of 2012.<ref>{{cite web|title=Emma Gaala – Ehdokaslista|url=http://www.emmagaala.fi/fi/ehdokaslista/12|publisher=Musiikkituottajat ry|accessdate=3 February 2013}}</ref>
