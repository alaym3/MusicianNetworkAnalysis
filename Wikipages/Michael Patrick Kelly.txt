'''Michael Patrick Kelly''' (Paddy Kelly) was born on December 5th 1977. He is an Irish-American<ref>[http://www.allmusic.com/artist/michael-patrick-kelly-mn0003383417/biography AllMusic biography]</ref> singer, musician and composer. Kelly was born in [[Dublin]]. He came to fame as the third-youngest member of the pop and folk band [[The Kelly Family]], who belong to the commercially most successful acts in Europe with over 20 million records sold since the mid-1990s. After years in the public eye, Kelly released his debut solo album ''[[In Exile (Michael Patrick Kelly album)|In Exile]]'' and shortly thereafter retired to a monastery in France.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/6577018/michael-patrick-kelly/biography|title=Michael Patrick Kelly – Biography – Billboard|website=Billboard}}</ref> After six years he returned to the music business in 2011. Kelly currently resides in Germany.
