'''Anuv Jain''' is an Indian singer, songwriter and composer. Having made his professional debut with the single ''Baarishein'' in 2018, he has since recorded seven tracks.

==Early life==
Anuv Jain was born on 11 March 1995 in [[Ludhiana
]]. He has a twin sister. Jain attended school in Ludhiana, before completing a Bachelor's of Business Administration from Narsee Monjee Institute of Management, Mumbai.<ref name="tie1">{{cite web |title=‘You can turn your sadness into one of your biggest assets’: Anuv Jain |url=https://indianexpress.com/article/lifestyle/life-positive/you-can-turn-your-sadness-into-one-of-your-biggest-assets-anuv-jain-7379768/ |website=The Indian Express |access-date=1 November 2021 |language=en |date=8 September 2021}}</ref>

Jain released his first song, "Meri Baaton Me Tu" on Youtube in 2012, when he was still in high school.<ref>{{cite web |title=Meri Baaton Mein Tu-Anuv Jain |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6W9S_p9nqgY |access-date=1 November 2021 |language=en|publisher=Aakriti |via=Youtube|date=14 May 2011}}</ref>

==Career==
Jain released his first single, entitled "Baarishein" in 2016 on his own [[YouTube]] channel. The track has since amassed over 25 million views on the platform.<ref name="yt1">{{cite web |title=Baarishein (Studio) Anuv Jain |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PJWemSzExXs |publisher=Anuv Jain |access-date=1 November 2021 |language=en |via=Youtube|date=9 July 2016}}</ref>
