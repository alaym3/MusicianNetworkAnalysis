'''Oona''' is a feminine [[given name]]. It is an [[Anglicisation]] of the [[Irish-language]] name ''[[Úna]]''. Apart from Ireland, it is also a popular name in Finland.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.behindthename.com/php/view.php?name=oona|title=Meaning, origin and history of the name Oona}}</ref> A variant spelling of ''Oona'' is ''Oonagh''.
