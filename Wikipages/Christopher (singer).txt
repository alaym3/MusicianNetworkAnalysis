'''Christopher Lund Nissen''' (born 31 January 1992) is a Danish singer from Copenhagen, signed to EMI Denmark. In November 2012, he won an award at the [[Danish Music Awards 2012]]. He was awarded ''New Thinker of the Year'' (Årets nytænker), given by Spotify as an innovation prize.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.mynewsdesk.com/dk/pressroom/emi-music-denmark/pressrelease/view/christopher-vinder-innovationspris-aarets-nytaenker-til-danish-music-awards-debutalbum-colours-genudgives-12-nov-med-nyt-materiale-811389 |title=Mynewsdesk.com |access-date=December 29, 2012 |archive-url=https://archive.today/20130129095343/http://www.mynewsdesk.com/dk/pressroom/emi-music-denmark/pressrelease/view/christopher-vinder-innovationspris-aarets-nytaenker-til-danish-music-awards-debutalbum-colours-genudgives-12-nov-med-nyt-materiale-811389 |archive-date=January 29, 2013 |url-status=dead }}</ref> Christopher released four albums, the debut ''[[Colours (Christopher album)|Colours]]'' (2012), followed by ''[[Told You So (Christopher album)|Told You So]]'' (2014), ''[[Closer (Christopher album)|Closer]]'' (2016) and ''Under the Surface'' (2019). Christopher's songs include "[[Against the Odds (song)|Against the Odds]], "[[Nothing in Common (Christopher song)|Nothing in Common]]", "[[Told You So (Christopher song)|Told You So]]", "CPH Girls" and "[[I Won't Let You Down (Christopher song)|I Won't Let You Down]]".

==Career==
Christopher started singing [[Cover version|covers]] of other singers' songs. His debut single was "[[Against the Odds (song)|Against the Odds]]", co-written by Kay & Ndustry, Kasper Larsen, Ole Brodersen, Curtis Richa and Johan Wetterberg, and produced by Kay & Ndustry and GL Music's Lasse Lindorff.<ref>[http://www.glmusic.dk/news/christopher-against-the-odds.aspx GL Music: Christopher – 'Against The Odds'] {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111001015348/http://www.glmusic.dk/news/christopher-against-the-odds.aspx |date=October 1, 2011 }}</ref> It was released in September 2011, reaching number 23 on the Danish Singles Chart.<ref>[https://danishcharts.dk/showinterpret.asp?interpret=Christopher+%5BDK%5D DanishCharts: Christopher page]</ref> The accompanying music video is directed by Nicolas Tobias Følsgaard and Jonas Lodahl Andersen. His follow-up single "[[Nothing in Common (Christopher song)|Nothing in Common]]" entered the Danish Singles Chart at number 5 in 2012. His debut album ''[[Colours (Christopher album)|Colours]]'' was released in 2013. "CPH Girls" was Christopher's 1st single number one in Denmark. Christopher's third studio album ''[[Closer (Christopher album)|Closer]]'' came out on 15 April 2016. It includes his earlier hit "Tulips" that came out in Denmark, Norway and China, "[[I Won't Let You Down (Christopher song)|I Won't Let You Down]]" featuring [[Bekuh Boom]] becoming his second number 1 in Denmark and the track "Limousine" with the artist [[Madcon]]. Christopher gets his inspiration for [[Michael Jackson]], [[Justin Timberlake]], [[Bruno Mars]] and [[John Mayer]].

==Personal life==
He was born in Frederiksberg, but later moved to Amager, Denmark with his parents and little brother Oliver.<ref>{{cite web|title=Christopher|url=http://www.seoghoer.dk/vipedia/christopher|website=Se og hør|publisher=Aller Media A / S|accessdate=27 December 2015|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151227142855/http://www.seoghoer.dk/vipedia/christopher|archivedate=December 27, 2015 |language=Danish}}</ref> In 2012  Christopher moved in with his girlfriend  the Danish singer [[Medina (singer)|Medina]]. The couple later split up. In 2014, he began a relationship with Danish model Cecilie Haugaard. The couple married in June 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.instagram.com/p/ByS-_-oAcIJ/|title=Christopher on Instagram: “It's hard to describe how magical this weekend was!! I can't wait to spend the rest of my life with this happy girl, I now get to call my…”|website=Instagram|language=en|access-date=2019-06-22}}</ref> They welcomed their first child, a daughter named Noelle, in 2021.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Lagerstorf|first=Nicklas Erik|title=Endelig: Christopher og Cecilie afslører navnet på datteren|url=https://www.seoghoer.dk/kendte/endelig-christopher-og-cecilie-afsloerer-navnet-paa-datteren|access-date=2021-06-05|website=SE og HØR|language=da}}</ref>
