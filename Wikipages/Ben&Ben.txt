'''Ben&Ben''', formerly known as '''The Benjamins''', is a [[folk pop]]/[[pop rock]] band in the [[Philippines]]. Formed in 2015, the band has since been known for their popular hits such as "[[Pagtingin]]" and "[[Lifetime (Ben&Ben song)|Lifetime]]", among others.<ref name="b&b">{{cite web | url=https://www.bandwagon.asia/articles/mapping-out-musical-journey-ben&ben-manila-band-folk-pop-philippines | title=Mapping out the musical journey of Ben&Ben | publisher=Bandwagon.asia | date=August 31, 2017 | access-date=July 16, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-11-06|title=Ben&Ben on their new pop-rock sound for 'Di Ka Sayang'|url=https://preen.ph/118129/benben-di-ka-sayang-new-era-pop-rock|access-date=2020-12-30|website=Preen.ph|language=en-US}}</ref> One of the most popular acts nationally, they are ranked as the number one most streamed Filipino artist on [[Spotify]] in 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|first=Franchesca Judine|last=Basbas|title=BTS, Ben&Ben, BLACKPINK, Taylor Swift, and more top Spotify Wrapped Philippines 2020|url=https://www.bandwagon.asia/articles/bts-ben-and-ben-blackpink-taylor-swift-top-spotify-wrapped-most-streamed-artists-songs-in-the-philippines-list-2020|date=1 December 2020|access-date=17 January 2021|website=Bandwagon Asia}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Admin|title=Spotify reveals list of most streamed artists, songs in the Philippines for 2019|url=https://www.thesummitexpress.com/2019/12/spotify-reveals-list-of-most-streamed-artists-philippines-2019.html|access-date=November 18, 2020|website=The Summit Express}}</ref> They have won several awards, including multiple [[Awit Award]]s, [[Myx Music Award|Myx Awards]], and [[Wish 107.5 Music Awards|Wish Awards]]. Their debut studio album ''[[Limasawa Street]]'' was certified 3× Platinum in the Philippines by [[Philippine Association of the Record Industry|PARI]] earning 45,000 copies.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Bonifacio|first=Mark|date=2021-07-08|title=Newly minted hitmaker makes his mark with 'Paraluman'|url=https://www.manilatimes.net/2021/07/08/entertainment-lifestyle/show-times/newly-minted-hitmaker-makes-his-mark-with-paraluman/1806083|access-date=2021-10-21|website=The Manila Times|language=en}}</ref>

==History==
===2015–2017: Band history, formation, and debut EP===

Prior to its formation as a band, the Guico twins, Paolo Benjamin and Miguel Benjamin Guico (then known as The Benjamins) were best known for writing the single "Tinatangi" (interpreted by [[Bayang Barrios]] and Cooky Chua featuring The Benjamins), which won 2nd runner up and Best Music Video at the [[Philippine Popular Music Festival|PhilPop Music Festival]] in 2016.<ref>{{cite news | last=Policarpio | first=Allan | url=http://entertainment.inquirer.net/198053/itchyworms-di-na-muli-bags-philpop-2016-grand-prize | title=Itchyworms' 'Di Na Muli' bags PhilPop 2016 grand prize | newspaper=[[Philippine Daily Inquirer]] | date=July 25, 2016 | access-date=December 14, 2017}}</ref> They formally changed their name in 2016 to Ben&Ben, a homage to their shared first name. The duo released their debut EP in late 2016, signing with major record label [[Warner Music Group|Warner Music Philippines]].<ref name="debut-ep">{{cite web|date=January 1, 2017|title=Ben&Ben Successfully Launched Their Self-Titled EP|url=http://indiemanila.com/benandbenlaunch/|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170617115237/http://indiemanila.com/benandbenlaunch/|archive-date=June 17, 2017|access-date=June 22, 2017|publisher=Indie Manila}}</ref>

Expanding as an ensemble, the group became a 9-piece band on May 10, 2017,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KwjLlt5cmpE|title=Happy 3rd Anniversary to us! Story Time Part 2 at "Fall" Video Shoot {{!}} Ben&Ben Vlogs|date=May 10, 2020|website=[[YouTube]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3calHFqoefY|title=Who Is Ben&Ben? {{!}} An Introduction|date=August 24, 2020|website=[[YouTube]]}}</ref> adding Poch Barreto on electric guitar, Jam Villanueva on drums, Agnes Reoma on bass, Patricia Lasaten on keyboards, Toni Muñoz and Andrew de Pano on percussions and back up vocals, and Keifer Cabugao on violin.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|last=Benavidez|first=Hannah|date=November 11, 2019|title=Ben and Ben members: Discover fun facts about the music geniuses|url=https://kami.com.ph/97983-ben-and-ben-members-age-real-names-songs-albums.html|access-date=October 10, 2020|website=Kami.com.ph – Philippines news.|language=en}}</ref>

===2018: Rise through singing soundtracks===
In 2018, the band rose to fame appearing in soundtracks for popular Philippine films, including new singles and collaborations, most notably "Susi" for the movie ''[[Goyo: Ang Batang Heneral]]'' <ref>{{Cite news|url=https://entertainment.inquirer.net/282459/story-behind-goyos-theme-song-susi|title=The story behind ‘Goyo’s’ theme song ‘Susi’|last=Policarpio|first=Allan|access-date=September 25, 2018|language=en}}</ref> and "Maybe the Night" for the movie ''[[Exes Baggage]]''.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://preen.inquirer.net/84114/story-behind-maybe-night-will-give-exes-baggage-feels|title=The story behind "Maybe The Night" will give you ‘Exes Baggage’ feels all over again|publisher=Inquirer|date=October 4, 2018|access-date=September 17, 2019}}</ref>
They composed a song, "Balik-Balikan"<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://open.spotify.com/album/1K8IhETY3w6DnPVWRwApK8 |title=Beautiful Girl by Ben&Ben |website=Spotify |access-date=September 18, 2019}}</ref> for the music program [[Coke Studio Philippines|Coke Studio Homecoming]] together with Sam Concepcion.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.bandwagon.asia/articles/coke-studio-homecoming-philippines-season-2-december-avenue-moira-ben-and-ben-iv-of-spades-quest-saab-magalona-juan-miguel-severo|title=Coke Studio Homecoming lineup revealed: IV of Spades, QUEST, Ben&Ben, Juan Miguel Severo, and more|date=September 3, 2018|work=Coke Studio Homecoming lineup revealed: IV of Spades, QUEST, Ben&amp;Ben, Juan Miguel Severo, and more {{!}} Editorial {{!}} Bandwagon – Live music, bands and concert guide for Singapore, Manila and Jakarta|access-date=September 25, 2018|language=en}}</ref>
In the same program, Ben&Ben also covered a classic [[Sam Concepcion]] and [[Tippy Dos Santos]] song, as well as the grand winning [[Philippine Popular Music Festival#2nd Philippine Popular Music Festival|Philpop]] single "Dati" for ''Coke Studio Homecoming Season 2''. Ben&Ben performed in the 2018 Toyota Music Fest which was held at Filinvest City Events Grounds in [[Alabang]], [[Muntinlupa]]. Included in the music festival's line up are bands like [[IV of Spades]], [[December Avenue (band)|December Avenue]], [[Parokya Ni Edgar]], [[Hale (band)|Hale]], [[Sponge Cola]], [[6cyclemind]], [[Callalily]], [[Silent Sanctuary]], [[Rivermaya]], [[Cueshé]], [[The Ransom Collective]], and [[Eraserheads]]' lead vocalist, [[Ely Buendia]].<ref>{{cite news|date=July 2, 2018|title=Ely Buendia, December Avenue, IV of Spades, Ben&Ben, and more to perform at Toyota Music Fest|publisher=Bandwagon Asia|url=https://www.bandwagon.asia/articles/ely-buendia-december-avenue-iv-of-spades-ben-ben-and-more-to-perform-at-toyota-music-fest|access-date=September 25, 2019}}</ref>

===2019: Critical success with ''Limasawa Street''===
====Debut album: ''Limasawa Street''====
{{main|Limasawa Street}}
Ben&Ben announced that they will be releasing their debut album in May 2019 after topping various streaming platforms and radio charts with hit singles “Kathang Isip,” “Ride Home” and “Maybe the Night”.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://cnnphilippines.com/videos/2019/5/7/Ben-Ben-to-release-new-debut-album--Limawasa-Street-.html|title=Ben&Ben to release new debut album 'Limasawa Street'|publisher=CNN Philippines|date=May 7, 2019|access-date=September 17, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://cnnphilippines.com/life/entertainment/Music/2019/5/14/benandben-album.html|title=Ben&Ben want to be your beacon of hope|publisher=CNN Philippines|date=May 14, 2019|access-date=September 17, 2019}}</ref>
“Limasawa Street” is derived from the [[Butuan]]on word "Masawa" meaning light and is also the name of a street. ''Limasawa Street'' would win the [[Awit Award|Awit Award for Favorite Album of the Year]].<ref>{{cite news|date=May 10, 2019|title=Ben&Ben’s debut album ‘Limasawa Street’ with 13 songs comes to life after 3 months|publisher=Conan Daily|url=https://conandaily.com/2019/05/10/benbens-debut-album-limasawa-street-with-13-songs-comes-to-life-after-3-months/|access-date=September 17, 2019}}</ref>

Ben&Ben would also attempt acting, starring in the 2019 [[Pista ng Pelikulang Pilipino]] music film, ''[[LSS (2019 film)|LSS (Last Song Syndrome)]]'' alongside [[Gabbi Garcia]] and [[Khalil Ramos]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.philstar.com/entertainment/2019/08/24/1945814/music-movies-theres-just-no-stopping-benben|title=From music to movies, there’s just no stopping Ben&Ben|publisher=The Philippine Star|date=August 24, 2019|access-date=September 17, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.spot.ph/entertainment/movies-music-tv/77434/ben-and-ben-lss-movie-a1174-20190329|title=Ben&Ben Is Starring in a New Movie This September|website=Spot PH|date=March 29, 2019|access-date=September 17, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.onemusic.ph/news/ben-ben-to-star-in-an-upcoming-movie-4407|title=Ben&Ben to star in an upcoming movie!|website=One Music PH|date=April 4, 2019|access-date=September 17, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.wheninmanila.com/gabbi-garcia-khalil-ramos-film-ben-and-ben/|title=Ben&Ben is the star of this new film featuring Gabbi Garcia and Khalil Ramos|website=When In Manila|date=April 5, 2019|access-date=September 17, 2019}}</ref> 10 original songs were featured in the film, namely "Ride Home," "Branches," "Leaves," "Kathang Isip," "Bibingka," "Fall," "Maybe The Night," "Mitsa," "[[Araw-Araw]]," "Sunrise," and "[[Pagtingin]]".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.spot.ph/entertainment/movies-music-tv/78337/lss-the-movie-teaser-a4373-20190711|title=The Teaser for Ben&Ben's LSS Is Finally Here|website=Spot PH|date=July 11, 2019|access-date=September 17, 2019}}</ref> For these songs, Ben&Ben received positive response, with "Araw-Araw" winning Best Original Song at [[Pista ng Pelikulang Pilipino|Pista ng Pelikulang Pilipino Awards]] and "Branches" winning [[Awit Award|Awit Award for Best Folk Recording]].

In August 2019, it was announced that Ben&Ben will be performing in the 2019 edition of the [[Clockenflap]], an annual music and arts festival held in [[Hong Kong]]. Including in the line up are artists like [[Halsey (singer)|Halsey]] and [[Lil Pump]], and bands like [[The Kooks]] and [[Mumford & Sons]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.abs-cbn.com/entertainment/08/08/19/benben-to-play-in-hk-music-fest-headlined-by-mumford-sons-halsey|title=Ben&Ben to play in HK music fest headlined by Mumford & Sons, Halsey|publisher=ABS-CBN News|date=August 8, 2019|access-date=September 25, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.timeout.com/hong-kong/news/clockenflap-2019-first-lineup-announced-080819|title=Clockenflap 2019 first lineup announced|publisher=TimeOut|date=August 8, 2019|access-date=September 25, 2019}}</ref> They covered a new classic cover of the [[Jose Mari Chan]] hit ''Beautiful Girl'' in 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Beautiful Girl by Ben&Ben|url=https://open.spotify.com/album/3UL37TgEAcc58GXvm8hdnr|access-date=September 18, 2019|website=Spotify}}</ref>  In November 2019, during the [[National Geographic]] Explorers Festival in [[Singapore]], Ben&Ben became the first Filipino band to be named as ''#PlanetorPlastic'' ambassadors for Asia.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.gmanetwork.com/news/showbiz/chikaminute/715625/ben-amp-ben-tapped-as-planetorplastic-ambassadors-for-asia/story/|title=Ben&Ben tapped as #PlanetorPlastic Ambassadors for Asia|publisher=GMA News|date=November 15, 2019|access-date=March 6, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://entertainment.inquirer.net/351809/benben-band-named-national-geographic-asia-ambassador-against-plastic-trash/|title=Ben&Ben band named National Geographic Asia ambassador against plastic trash|publisher=Inquirer|date=November 15, 2019|access-date=March 6, 2020}}</ref>
