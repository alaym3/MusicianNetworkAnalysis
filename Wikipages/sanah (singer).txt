'''Zuzanna Irena Jurczak''' (born 2 September 1997), known professionally as '''sanah''' (stylized in all [[lowercase]]), is a Polish singer-songwriter. She received nationwide popularity in 2020, with the release of her single "[[Szampan]]", which became a number-one hit in Poland. She later released her debut studio album ''[[Królowa dram]]'' (2020), which peaked at number-one in Poland.

==Career==
=== 2019–2020: Career beginnings and ''Królowa dram''===
Born in [[Warsaw]], Jurczak graduated from the [[Fryderyk Chopin University of Music]] in 2019, receiving her music degree with a specialization in violin.<ref>{{cite web |title = Sanah – tego o niej nie wiedzieliście. Zaskakujące fakty o autorce hitu "Szampan" |date = 2 June 2020|url =https://otopress.pl/2020/03/14/sanah-zuzanna-jurczacz-wzrost-wiek-rodzenstwo/|language=Polish}}</ref> After graduating, she began a music career and was signed to [[Magic Records]]. Her debut extended play ''Ja na imię niewidzialna mam'' was released in October 2019.<ref>{{cite web|title=sanah|url=https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCqTRe9EIv0apJgPqkng-Gtw|agency=YouTube|accessdate=20 February 2020|language=pl}}</ref> In January 2020, she received three nominations at the [[Fryderyk|2020 Fryderyk Awards]], the Polish equivalent of the [[Grammys]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Fryderyk 2020 – nominowani|url=https://fryderyki.pl/fryderyk-2020/|accessdate=20 February 2020|language=pl-PL}}</ref>

She released the single "[[Szampan]]" in January 2020, which went on to become a number one hit in Poland.<ref>{{cite web|title=Subtelna, zwiewna i świeża. Sanah – nowe oblicze polskiej sceny muzycznej (wywiad)|url=https://fashionpost.pl/subtelna-zwiewna-i-swieza-sanah-nowe-oblicze-polskiej-sceny-muzycznej-wywiad|author=Antonina Rafałowska|agency=Fashion Post|date=12 July 2019|accessdate=15 February 2020|language=en}}</ref> It was followed by the single "[[Melodia (Sanah song)|Melodia]]", which also reached number one. Her debut studio album ''[[Królowa dram]]'' was later released in May 2020, which became her first chart-topping album in her home country.<ref>{{cite web|title=Sanah wydała singiel i zapowiada płytę|url=https://kulturalnemedia.pl/muzyka/sanah-wydala-singiel-i-zapowiada-plyte/|author=Kaja Wiśniowska|agency=Kulturalne Media|date=3 January 2020|accessdate=20 February 2020|language=pl}}</ref>

=== 2021–present: ''Irenka'' ===
Sanah released her second studio album ''[[Irenka]]'' in May 2021. It became the [[List of number-one albums of 2021 (Poland)|number one album in Poland]].<ref name="OLiS" />
