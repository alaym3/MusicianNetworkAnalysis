'''William Perry Moore''' (born December 31, 1990)<ref>https://www.ascap.com/repertory#ace/search/workID/904341122</ref> known professionally as '''DJ Esco''', is an American DJ, record producer, and record executive.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/dj-esco-mn0003549339/biography |title=DJ Esco - Biography & History |last=Yeung |first=Neil |website=[[AllMusic]] |access-date=2018-10-19}}</ref>

==Career==
A resident DJ at [[Magic City (club)|Magic City]] Mondays in Atlanta, Moore has worked as a [[disc jockey|DJ]] and [[Artists and repertoire|A&R]] representative for rapper [[Future (rapper)|Future]] since meeting him in 2008. Future's mixtape ''[[56 Nights]]'' was named after Moore's time spent in prison for marijuana possession in the [[United Arab Emirates]].<ref name="HNHHP"/> In 2016, Moore released the mixtape [[Project E.T.]], which features rappers such as [[Drake (musician)|Drake]], [[Juicy J]], and [[Lil Uzi Vert]].<ref>{{Cite magazine |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/8275791/dj-esco-kolorblind-album-stream |title=Stream DJ Esco's Latest Album 'Kolorblind' Featuring Future, Nas & Young Thug |magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] |access-date=2018-10-19}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/22077-project-et-esco-terrestrial/ |title=Future / DJ Esco: Project E.T. Esco Terrestrial Album Review |last=Daramola |first=Israel |website=[[Pitchfork (website)|Pitchfork]] |language=en |access-date=2018-10-19}}</ref> On March 30, 2018, Moore released the album ''Kolorblind'', featuring artists such as [[Schoolboy Q]] and [[Rich the Kid]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.thefader.com/2018/03/23/dj-esco-new-album-kolorblind |title=DJ Esco announces new project Kolorblind |last=Darville |first=Jordan |date=23 March 2018 |website=[[The Fader]] |language=en |access-date=2018-10-19}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.xxlmag.com/rap-music/new-music/2018/03/dj-esco-kolor-blind/ |title=DJ Esco Delivers 'Kolorblind' Album Featuring Future and More - XXL |website=[[XXL Mag]] |language=en |access-date=2018-10-19}}</ref> The album includes previously released songs such as "Walk Thru" and "Code of Honor".<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.complex.com/music/2018/03/stream-dj-esco-kolorblind-project |title=Stream DJ Esco's 'Kolorblind' Project f/ Future, Nas, Young Thug, and More |website=[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]] |language=en |access-date=2018-10-19}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.stereogum.com/1988931/dj-esco-walk-thru-feat-future-nas/music/ |title=DJ Esco – "Walk Thru" (Feat. Future & Nas) |date=2018-03-28 |website=[[Stereogum]] |access-date=2018-10-19}}</ref> Moore has served as an executive producer on Future's ''[[Hndrxx]]'' album.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://hiphopdx.com/reviews/id.3127/title.review-dj-escos-kolorblind-reignites-futures-trap-revival# |title=Review: DJ Esco's "Kolorblind" Reignites Future's Trap Revival |website=[[HipHopDX]] |language=en-US |access-date=2018-10-19}}</ref>
