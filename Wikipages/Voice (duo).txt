'''Voice''' also known as '''Alexandros and Christina''' was a Cypriot musical duo made up of Christina Argyri and [[Alexandros Panayi]]. The duo represented Cyprus in [[Eurovision Song Contest 2000|2000 Eurovision Song Contest]] with the song "[[Nomiza]]" ([[Greek alphabet|Greek script]]: Νόμιζα, [[English language|English]] translation: "I Believed").
