'''Ulige Numre''' was a [[Denmark|Danish]] [[rock music|rock]] band made up of [[Carl Emil Møller Petersen]] (vocals and guitar), Nick Lee (bass), Teis Lindeskov Søgaard (drums) and Jacob Ulstrup (keyboards). They are signed to A:larm / [[Universal Music]] label.

==Career==
Ulige Numre ("odd numbers" in Danish), played their debut gig 26 November 2010 in "HUSET-KBH", placed in [[Magstræde]] in Copenhagen. Playing early songs such as "Sølvvognen"("silver chariot") and "Hænder"("hands"). Through 2011 they built up a small fanbase in Copenhagen, while they recorded their music.
The 26 September 2011 their debut single "København" ("Copenhagen") was released. Straight away it went to be a charting single. The self-produced music video was launched on their [[YouTube]] channel.<ref>{{cite web | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111010055538/http://politiken.dk/kultur/tvogradio/ECE1416699/ukendt-youtube-faenomen-genopliver-koebenhavner-sangen/ | url=http://politiken.dk/kultur/tvogradio/ECE1416699/ukendt-youtube-faenomen-genopliver-koebenhavner-sangen/ | archive-date=10 October 2011 | work=Politiken | title=Ukendt Youtube-fænomen genopliver københavner-sangen | date=8 October 2011 | first=Sandra | last=Brovall | language=da | access-date=31 December 2018}}</ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vSaUWDOexXU] The video, containing old footage about [[Copenhagen]] and clips from feature films, received wide media coverage and attention on social media sites.<ref>{{cite news | url = http://www.urban.dk/2011/09/byen-elsker-ulige-numre/ |title= Byen elsker Ulige numre |work= Urban |date= 30 September 2011 |access-date= 13 April 2012 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20111001045456/http://www.urban.dk/2011/09/byen-elsker-ulige-numre/ |archive-date= 1 October 2011 |language= da}}</ref> The single was promoted on [[DR P3]], and the band made appearances on TV shows including ''Det Nye Talkshow - med Anders Lund Madsen''. In 2012, "København" won "Best Song 2012" at Årets Steppeulv awards.<ref>{{cite news | url = http://politiken.dk/kultur/ECE1535584/hiphoppens-superhelte-sejrede-paa-ny/ | first = Anders | last = Hjort |title= Hiphoppens superhelte sejrede på ny |work= [[Politiken]] |date= 10 February 2012 |access-date= 13 April 2012 |language= da}}</ref>

On 31 October 2011, the band released its well-received self-titled EP ''Ulige numre''.<ref>{{cite news | url = http://www.information.dk/283778 | first = Ralf | last = Christensen |title= Hjemstavn i fugleperspektiv |work= [[Dagbladet Information]] |date= 2 November 2011 |access-date= 13 April 2012 |language=da}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | url = http://www.b.dk/musik/har-alt-kan-alt | first = Thomas Søie | last = Hansen |title= Har alt, kan alt |work= [[Berlingske]] |date= 30 October 2011 |access-date= 13 April 2012 |language=da}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | url = http://politiken.dk/kultur/musik/anmeldelser_rockpop/ECE1436317/koebenhavner-faenomen-stjaeler-og-slipper-afsted-med-det/ | first = Pernille | last = Jensen |title= Københavner-fænomen stjæler og slipper af sted med det |work= [[Politiken]] |date= 4 November 2011 |access-date= 13 April 2012 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20111106213830/http://politiken.dk/kultur/musik/anmeldelser_rockpop/ECE1436317/koebenhavner-faenomen-stjaeler-og-slipper-afsted-med-det |archive-date= 6 November 2011 |language=da}}</ref> It was released on Auditorium label and is produced by Nikolaj Nørlund.
The singles "København" and "Navn i sneen" ("name in the snow") received heavy airplay on Danish radio station P3.
In 2012 Ulige Numre had a growing fanbase and played concerts all over Denmark, mainly on Danish festivals, the biggest being Roskilde Festival. 
In June 2013, the single "Blå" (meaning "blue") was released and received a lot of airplay on Danish radio. The single reached number 38 on the Danish charts. 
The 12 August their debut album "Nu til Dags" ("now a days") was released, and went number 1 on the Danish charts.
The 19 September 2013 Ulige Numre began their "Nu til Dags"-tour around Denmark. The tour began in Aarhus and ended in Copenhagen 26 October.

== Split ==
The members of Ulige Numre announced that they were dissolving the band in a letter posted on their Facebook page on 15 March 2017.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/uligenumre/posts/1298200646882367:0|title=Ulige Numre - Ulige Numre added a new photo.|publisher=Facebook|language=en|access-date=2017-03-17}}</ref>
