'''Filippo Maria Fanti''', known as '''Irama''', (born 20 December 1995) is an Italian singer and songwriter.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.ilpost.it/2019/02/09/irama-chi-e/ |title=Chi è Irama e cosa ha fatto prima di Sanremo |date=2019-02-09 |website=ilpost.it |access-date=2019-12-22}}</ref>

He rose to fame in 2018, following his win on the 17th edition of the talent show ''[[Amici di Maria De Filippi]].''<ref>{{Cite web|title=Amici 17: a sorpresa entra nella scuola il ciclone Irama|url=https://www.allmusicitalia.it/news/amici-17-sorpresa-irama.html|date=2018-01-27|website=All Music Italia|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-04-30}}</ref> He participated at the [[Sanremo Music Festival 2016|Sanremo Music Festival]] in 2016 with the single "Cosa resterà"<ref>{{Cite web|title=IRAMA esce il 12 febbraio con il suo Ep omonimo. Sarà la rivelazione di SANREMO 2016?|url=https://www.allmusicitalia.it/news/irama-esce-il-12-febbraio-con-il-suo-ep-omonimo-sara-la-rivelazione-di-sanremo-2016.html|date=2016-01-26|website=All Music Italia|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-04-30}}</ref> in [[Sanremo Music Festival 2019|2019]], with the song "La ragazza con il cuore di latta",<ref>{{Cite web|title=√ Festival di Sanremo 2019, i big in gara e i titoli delle canzoni|url=https://www.rockol.it/news-699074/festival-di-sanremo-2019-big-in-gara-e-titoli-delle-canzoni|last=s.r.l|first=Rockol com|website=Rockol|language=it|access-date=2020-04-30}}</ref> and in [[Sanremo Music Festival 2021|2021]] with the song "[[La genesi del tuo colore]]".<ref>{{Cite web|title=Sanremo 2021: Irama will NOT be disqualified... rehearsal footage to be used instead|url=https://wiwibloggs.com/2021/03/03/sanremo-2021-irama-will-not-be-disqualified-rehearsal-footage-to-be-used-instead/262774/| access-date=2021-03-24|website=wiwibloggs}}</ref>

Since his debut, he has sold over 1.1 million copies in Italy, twice topping the [[Fimi|Italian Albums Chart]] and realizing several media successful singles, including the multi-platinum "Nera", "Arrogante" and "Mediterranea".

== Biography and career ==
Born in [[Carrara]] and growing up in [[Monza]], he declared to have been interested in the music of [[Francesco Guccini]] and [[Fabrizio De André]] since he was a child, and then he approached [[hip pop]]. His artistic name, chosen after having anagrammed the second name, Maria, means "rhythm" in [[Malay language|Malay]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Festival di Sanremo 2016, intervista a Irama in gara tra le Nuove Proposte con "Cosa resterà"|url=http://www.outune.net/livetune/interviste/sanremo-2016-irama-cosa-restera-nuove-proposte/125357|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160405005429/http://www.outune.net/livetune/interviste/sanremo-2016-irama-cosa-restera-nuove-proposte/125357|url-status=dead|archive-date=2016-04-05|date=2016-04-05|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref>

On November 27, 2015, with the song "Cosa resterà", written with Giulio Nenna, he was among the eight winners of the eighth edition of the [[Sanremo Music Festival|''Sanremo Giovani'']] song contest, which allowed him to be admitted to the [[Sanremo Music Festival 2015|Sanremo Music Festival]] in the ''New Proposte'' category.<ref>{{Cite web|title=SOGNANDO SANREMO: intervistiamo IRAMA|url=https://www.allmusicitalia.it/interviste/sognando-sanremo-intervistiamo-irama.html|date=2015-11-27|website=All Music Italia|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Sanremo 2016, nuove proposte: ecco chi sono i giovani in gara|url=https://www.deejay.it/liste/sanremo-2016-dal-basket-ai-flash-mob-8-curiosita-sui-giovani-in-gara/|date=2016-01-21|website=www.deejay.it|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref> The single reached the 100th position of the Italian Singles Charts and anticipated the singer's debut studio album, titled ''Irama'', released on February 12, 2016, under the [[Warner Music Italy]] label, debuting at the 51st position of the [[Fimi|FIMI]]'s Chart.<ref>{{Cite web|title=IRAMA esce il 12 febbraio con il suo Ep omonimo. Sarà la rivelazione di SANREMO 2016?|url=https://www.allmusicitalia.it/news/irama-esce-il-12-febbraio-con-il-suo-ep-omonimo-sara-la-rivelazione-di-sanremo-2016.html|date=2016-01-26|website=All Music Italia|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref><ref name="History - FIMI">{{Cite web|title=History - FIMI|url=https://www.fimi.it/top-of-the-music/history.kl|website=www.fimi.it|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref> On May 27, 2015, the second single "Tornerai da me" was released and lately certified gold by FIMI.<ref>{{Cite web|title=IRAMA: il nuovo singolo è "Tornerai da me" mentre su Facebook spunta una sorpresa realizzata con gli amici BENJI & FEDE (VIDEO)|url=https://www.allmusicitalia.it/news/irama-tornera-da-me.html|date=2016-05-10|website=All Music Italia|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref> The commercial launch of the single "Mi drogherò" marked the end of the artistic partnership between Irama and Warner Music Italy, due to the low promotion that the label reserved to the songwriter.<ref>{{Cite web|title="Mi drogherò" è il provocatorio titolo del nuovo singolo di IRAMA (TESTO)|url=https://www.allmusicitalia.it/news/mi-droghero-provocatorio-singolo-irama.html|date=2017-06-15|website=All Music Italia|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref>

At the end of 2016, in search of a record relaunch, Irama decided to participate in the seventeenth edition of the talent show ''[[Amici di Maria De Filippi]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Amici 17: a sorpresa entra nella scuola il ciclone Irama|url=https://www.allmusicitalia.it/news/amici-17-sorpresa-irama.html|date=2018-01-27|website=All Music Italia|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref> After the victory of the program, he signed again the contract with Warner Records,<ref>{{Cite web|title=AMICI 17 finale: Irama con il 63% dei voti vince la 17esima edizione del talent show più amato d'Italia|url=https://www.allmusicitalia.it/news/amici-17-vince.html|date=2018-06-11|website=All Music Italia|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref> releasing the single "Un giorno in più", debuting at position 24 of the FIMI ranking and obtaining the gold certification.<ref name="History - FIMI" /> The second single "Nera", also performed during the television show, was launched as a single on June 1, achieving the second position of the Top Singles Chart, solding over 150,000 copies.<ref>{{Cite web|title=IRAMA: dall'1 giugno in radio "Nera" e in esclusiva su Amazon la versione Deluxe con piuma e accesso prioritario agli instore. Ecco come acquistarla!|url=https://www.allmusicitalia.it/news/irama-nera-plume-nera.html|date=2018-05-29|website=All Music Italia|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref> In the same month he published the EP, produced by Giulio Nenna and Andrea Debernardi, entitled ''Plume'', which reached the top of the FIMI Album ranking and was certified double platinum for having sold over 100,000 copies.<ref>{{Cite web|title="Plume" è il titolo del nuovo disco di Irama in uscita l'1 giugno (COPERTINA IN ANTEPRIMA)|url=https://www.allmusicitalia.it/news/irama-plume.html|date=2018-05-23|website=All Music Italia|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref>

On October 19, 2018, just four months after the previous recording project, the second studio album by the singer-songwriter entitled ''Giovani,'' which debuted at the top of the FIMI Albums Chart and was lately certified Platinum.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Irama pronto a lanciare Giovani. Primo singolo Bella e rovinata|url=https://www.allmusicitalia.it/news/irama-bella-e-rovinata.html|date=2018-10-17|website=All Music Italia|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref> The album was supported by the commercial launch of the single "Bella e rovinata", which reached twenty-first place in the Top Singles.  In January 2019, his participation in the [[Sanremo Music Festival 2019|Sanremo Music Festival]] was announced with the song "La ragazza con il cuore di latta", which peaked at number three of FIMI's chart and sold over 100,000 copies.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Sanremo 2019 {{!}} Vincitori {{!}} Classifica finale {{!}} Premio giuria {{!}} Auditel|url=http://www.ilpost.it/2019/02/10/sanremo-2019-classifica-finale-premi/|date=2019-02-10|website=Il Post|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref>  On 3 June 2019 the single "Arrogante", written by [[Federica Abbate]], was released. It became the singer's third Top10 single on the Italian charts and also sold 100,000 copies.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Arrogante di Irama è la canzone che ti dice quanto possa essere sbagliata la prima impressione|url=https://www.cosmopolitan.com/it/lifestyle/musica/a27750751/arrogante-irama-testo-significato/|last=Adorno|first=Di Mary|date=2019-06-05|website=Cosmopolitan|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref>

On March 27, 2020 the new single "Milano" was  published, with the participation of [[Francesco Sarcina]], frontman of the group [[Le Vibrazioni]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Milano: il nuovo singolo di Irama con Francesco Sarcina|url=https://notiziemusica.it/irama-milano-il-video/news/|date=2020-03-27|website=Notizie Musica|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref> In May 2020 Irama was cast for the [[spin-off (media)|spin-off]] [[Amici di Maria De Filippi|''Amici Speciali'']] with other [[Amici di Maria De Filippi|''Amici'']]'s previous contestant, including [[Giordana Angi]], [[The Kolors]], [[Gaia Gozzi|Gaia]] and [[Alberto Urso]], and released the single "Mediterranea", which peaked at number one of Italian Singles Chart.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Amici Speciali, il promo con i concorrenti Vip: "Sembra tutto strano", attesa per il debutto|url=https://www.liberoquotidiano.it/news/spettacoli/televisione/22551717/amici_speciali_promo_concorrenti_vip_tutto_strano_attesa_debutto.html|website=www.liberoquotidiano.it|language=it|access-date=2020-05-21}}</ref> On May 19, 2020 he released "Mediterranea" as the lead single of his forth studio album ''Crepe.''<ref>{{Cite web|last=Redazione|first=Eleonora|date=2020-07-30|title=Tutto sul nuovo EP di Irama, Crepe: copertina e significato titolo|url=https://www.teamworld.it/musica/irama-ep-crepe/|access-date=2021-01-18|website=Team World|language=it-IT}}</ref> Both the single and the album debuted at number one of FIMI's charts.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Irama: fuori il video ufficiale del nuovo singolo "Crepe"|url=https://www.r101.it/news/musica-e-spettacolo/1271118/irama-fuori-il-video-ufficiale-del-nuovo-singolo-crepe.html|access-date=2021-01-18|website=www.r101.it}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=TG24|first=Sky|title=Irama racconta a Sky Tg24 il successo di 'Mediterranea'|url=https://tg24.sky.it/spettacolo/musica/2020/07/01/irama-racconta-a-sky-tg24-il-sucesso-di-mediterranea|access-date=2021-01-18|website=tg24.sky.it|language=it}}</ref>

Sanremo 2021 was a highly memorable experience for the singer/songwriter as he was not able to perform his entry "La genesi del tuo colore" live. A member of his team was tested positive for COVID-19 right before the show and he was forced to undergo a 10-day quarantine despite the negative test results. However, RAI allowed him to pursue in the competition using his rehearsal footage as an alternative.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Sanremo 2021: Irama will NOT be disqualified... rehearsal footage to be used instead|url=https://wiwibloggs.com/2021/03/03/sanremo-2021-irama-will-not-be-disqualified-rehearsal-footage-to-be-used-instead/262774/| access-date=2021-03-24|website=wiwibloggs}}</ref> Despite his absence during the live show, his rehearsal recordings still wowed the juries, viewers and fans catapulting him to fifth place in the final ranking. Three weeks later, "La genesi del tuo colore" was certified gold.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Irama, lo straordinario annuncio dopo Sanremo: "Solo amore" - FOTO| url= https://www.bloglive.it/2021/03/23/irama-annuncio-disco-oro-sanremo-foto-instagram/| access-date=2021-03-24|website=bloglive.it}}</ref>
