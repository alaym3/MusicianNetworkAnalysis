'''Wet Bed Gang''' is a Portuguese rap and [[hip hop tuga]] group from [[Vialonga, Portugal|Vialonga]]. The group was formed in 2014 by João "La Bella Mafia" Rossi and Pizzy. The group consists of four Portuguese singers and rappers with the stage names Gson,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://genius.com/artists/Gson|title=Gson|website=Genius|access-date=31 August 2018}}</ref> Zara G,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://genius.com/artists/Zara-g|title=Zara G|website=Genius|access-date=31 August 2018}}</ref> Kroa<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://genius.com/artists/Kroa|title=Kroa|website=Genius|access-date=31 August 2018}}</ref> and Zizzy Jr.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://genius.com/artists/Zizzy-jr|title=Zizzy Jr.|website=Genius|access-date=31 August 2018}}</ref> The group received national recognition after their songs gained millions of plays on YouTube and [[Spotify]].<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.europavox.com/bands/wet-bed-gang/|title=Wet Bed Gang – Europavox|website=europavox.com|access-date=31 August 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://genius.com/artists/Wet-bed-gang|title=Wet Bed Gang|website=Genius|access-date=31 August 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.comunidadeculturaearte.com/andre-c-santos-filma-os-wet-bed-gang-sente-se-um-fervilhar-de-vida-dentro-deles/|title=André C. Santos filma os Wet Bed Gang: ‘Sente-se um fervilhar de vida dentro deles’ – Comunidade Cultura e Arte|last=Mendes|first=Tiago|website=comunidadeculturaearte.com|language=pt-PT|access-date=31 August 2018}}</ref>

== Career ==
The group was co-founded by João "La Bella Mafia" Rossi and Pizzy in 2014. Following Rossi's death, the remaining members chose to continue with the development of the musical group. Their first [[extended play]], called "Filhos do Rossi", or "Sons of Rossi" in English, was a tribute to the group's co-founder.<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://open.spotify.com/artist/5jfz7uWPwf03hdEewW8AI8/about|title=Wet Bed Gang|website=Spotify|access-date=31 August 2019}}</ref>

In August 2019, they performed at the [[Meo Sudoeste]] music festival.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://observador.pt/2018/08/09/hardwell-cancela-atuacao-no-meo-sudoeste-devido-a-doenca-subita/|title=Hardwell cancela atuação no Meo Sudoeste devido a "doença súbita"|last=Observador|website=Observador|language=pt-PT|access-date=31 August 2018}}</ref>
