'''Eugenio in Via Di Gioia''' is an Italian [[indie folk]]/[[Indie pop|pop]] band formed in 2012.<ref name="intervista">{{Cite web|url=https://www.open.online/2019/11/27/interviste-emergenti-eugenio-in-via-di-gioia-la-strada-e-lo-spartito-torino/|title=Interviste emergenti: Eugenio in Via Di Gioia, la strada è lo spartito – Torino|author=Felice Florio|date=27 November 2019|language=it|access-date=17 February 2020}}</ref>

==History==
The band is named after its members – "Eugenio" after Eugenio Cesaro; "Via" after Emanuele Via; "Di Gioia" after Paolo Di Gioia – and can be roughly translated both as "Eugene in Joy Street" and "Eugene [is] about to [have] joy".<ref name="intervista"/><ref name="ondarock">{{Cite web|url=https://www.ondarock.it/recensioni/2014_eugenioinviadigioia_lorenzofederici.htm|title=Eugenio In Via Di Gioia - Lorenzo Federici|author=Alessandro Fiorito|access-date=17 February 2020}}</ref>

They debuted in 2013 with the [[extended play]] ''EP urrà''<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://testicanzoni.mtv.it/testi-Eugenio-In-Via-Di-Gioia_27472539/Ep-Urr%C3%A0-album_19006171|title=Album Ep Urrà, Eugenio In Via Di Gioia. Tracking list e i testi dell'album|language=it|access-date=17 February 2020}}</ref> and their first studio album ''Lorenzo Federici'' – named after the fourth member Lorenzo Federici – was released in 2014.<ref name="ondarock"/>

The band participated at the "Newcomers" section of the [[Sanremo Music Festival 2020]] with the song "Tsunami", produced by [[Dardust]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rockol.it/news-708258/sanremo-2020-ecco-65-selezionati-entrare-a-giovani|title=Eugenio in via di gioia a Sanremo Giovani 2020|access-date=17 February 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.earone.it/news/eugenio_in_via_di_gioia_tsunami_radio_date_24_01_2020_40001991/|title=Eugenio In Via Di Gioia - Tsunami (Radio Date: 24-01-2020)|author=Eleonora Forastiero|language=it|access-date=17 February 2020}}</ref> The song won the Critics' Award "[[Mia Martini]]".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.lastampa.it/torino/2020/02/07/news/festival-di-sanremo-gli-eugenio-in-via-di-gioia-vincono-il-premio-della-critica-1.38439458|title=Festival di Sanremo, gli Eugenio in Via Di Gioia vincono il premio della critica - La Stampa|website=[[La Stampa]]|date=7 February 2020|language=it|access-date=17 February 2020}}</ref>
