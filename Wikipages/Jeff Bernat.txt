'''Jeff Bernat''' (born December 11, 1989)<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://coridelent.com/artist/jeff-bernat/|title=Jeff Bernat Profile|work=Coridel Entertainment|access-date=April 13, 2018}}</ref> is a [[Filipino-American]] singer, songwriter and record producer.<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=http://www.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20141016000930|title=R&B artist Bernat talks Korean success|last=Jackson|first=Julie|date=October 16, 2014|work=The Korea Herald|access-date=April 13, 2018}}</ref> He debuted in 2012 with the album, ''The Gentleman Approach,''<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://koreajoongangdaily.joins.com/news/article/article.aspx?aid=3040545|title=Crush will join Jeff Bernat at Seoul concert|last=Hong|first=You-kyoung|date=November 9, 2017|website=Korea JoongAng Daily|access-date=April 13, 2018}}</ref> and developed a significant fan base in [[South Korea]] after the album track, "Call You Mine," was featured on the 2014 [[Korean drama]], ''[[You Are My Destiny (2014 TV series)|You Are My Destiny]]''.<ref name=":0" />

==Early life==
Bernat was born in [[Subic, Zambales|Subic]], [[Philippines]], but grew up in [[Reno, Nevada|Reno]], Nevada.<ref>{{cite web|first=Jae-hoon|last=Lee|title=제프 버넷, 내 노래 들으면 팬될걸요…e메일 인터뷰|url=http://www.newsis.com/ar_detail/view.html?ar_id=NISX20130427_0012039748&cID=10604&pID=10600|publisher=Newsis|accessdate=October 15, 2014|date=April 28, 2013|language=Korean}}</ref>
