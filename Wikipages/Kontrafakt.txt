'''Kontrafakt''' is a Slovak [[hip hop]] [[rap]] group originating from [[Piešťany]]. On album Bozk na rozlúčku, [[Nate Dogg]] appeared on one track. They frequently appear at [[Hip Hop Kemp]]. In 2014 their 4th studio album, Navždy was released, which contains a song produced by [[DJ Premier]] named O5 S5. In 2019, they released their 5th studio album called Real Newz, which had some new names on it such as Dalyb, Samey and such.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Kontrafakt zverejňuje kompletný tracklist k albumu Real Newz. Počuť na ňom budeme aj Dalyba, Sameya a Simu|url=https://refresher.sk/78103-Kontrafakt-zverejnuje-kompletny-tracklist-k-albumu-Real-Newz-Pocut-na-nom-budeme-aj-Dalyba-Sameya-a-Simu|last=REFRESHER|website=refresher.sk|language=sk|access-date=2020-05-01}}</ref> The album is considered new school.

In 2020, Kontrafakt<nowiki>'s music video ''We Go In''</nowiki> was nominated for Best Cinematography at the Berlin Music Video Awards. The cinematographer behind this music video is Radim Strelka.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Berlin Music Video Awards|date=7 May 2021|title=Nominees 2020|url=https://www.berlinmva.com/bmva-nominees-2020/|url-status=live|website=www.berlinmva.com}}</ref>
