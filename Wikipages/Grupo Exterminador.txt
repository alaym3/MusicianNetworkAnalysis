'''Grupo Exterminador''', (formerly known as '''Los Hermanos Corona''' between 1991 & 1992) is a [[Mexico|Mexican]] [[Norteño (music)|norteño]] band. Though known for performing various song styles such as [[Ranchera|rancheras]], [[Cumbia|cumbias]], and [[Sentimental ballad|ballads]], they are especially famous for their brash [[Narcocorrido|narcocorridos]].

==History==

Grupo Exterminador started in 1992. The band's debut album, ''Dos Plebes II'', was released by EGO Records in 1994. Their biggest chart hit was when their album ''Nuestras Romanticas'' reached no.9 on the [[Billboard charts]] in 2007,<ref>http://www.billboard.com/artist/303188/grupo-exterminador/chart</ref> and as of November 2016 their song "El Padre De Todos" has over 7.1 million views on [[YouTube]].<ref>https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=14pOff4sHBs</ref>
