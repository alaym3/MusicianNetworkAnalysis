'''Davut Altundal''', known professionally as '''AK Ausserkontrolle''' ({{lit}} "AK Outta Control"), is a German rapper of Kurdish descent from [[Berlin]].

== Biography ==
Altundal was born in Germany.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ll3Lllbmmrc|title=GERMANIA - AK Ausserkontrolle - YouTube|website=youtube.com|accessdate=10 November 2020}}</ref> His parents are from the [[Mardin Province]] in Turkey. They emigrated to [[Lebanon]] and later to Germany. He grew up in [[Wedding (Berlin)|Wedding]], a borough of Berlin. His friend Fux Ausserkontrolle founded the hip hop group ''Ausserkontrolle'' where he was member a long time.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.laut.de/AK-AusserKontrolle|title=AK AusserKontrolle|website=laut.de|accessdate=10 November 2020}}</ref>

In 2017, Altundal decided to leave. In the same year, he also signed at ''Erstguterjunge'', the music label of the rapper [[Bushido (rapper)|Bushido]] and his business partner Arafat Abou Chaker. After a dispute between Bushido and Abou Chaker, the label split up and AK Ausserkontrolle and almost all the other signings have left it in 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.spit-tv.de/behind-the-scenes/ak-ausserkontrolle-trennung-ersguterjunge-statement/|title=AK AusserKontrolle trennt sich von ersguterjunge & veröffentlicht ausführliches Statement|website=spit-tv.de|accessdate=10 November 2020}}</ref> After that, he founded his own label ''XY Records''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://hiphop.de/magazin/news/ak-ausserkontrolle-gruendet-eigenes-label-bushido-rapszene-gratulieren-313064|title=AK Ausserkontrolle gründet eigenes Label: Bushido und die Rapszene gratulieren|website=hiphop.de|accessdate=10 November 2020}}</ref>

Altundal has previously committed burglaries and robberies in stores.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.16bars.de/newsartikel/256915/ak-ausserkontrolle-haftstrafe-des-bruders-wegen-songtexten-verlaengert/|title=AK Ausserkontrolle: Haftstrafe des Bruders wegen Songtexten verlängert?|website=16bars.de|accessdate=10 November 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bz-berlin.de/tatort/das-sind-die-blitzeinbrecher-von-berlin|title=Das sind die Blitzeinbrecher von Berlin – B.Z. Berlin|website=bz-berlin.de|accessdate=10 November 2020}}</ref> Because of his previous convictions, he was requested to leave Germany to Turkey in April 2019,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bz-berlin.de/berlin/einem-der-bekanntesten-berliner-gangsta-rapper-droht-die-abschiebung|title=Einem der bekanntesten Berliner Gangsta-Rapper droht die Abschiebung|website=bz-berlin.de|accessdate=10 November 2020}}</ref> but he refused, and in May 2019 he got a residence permit valid for at least three years.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://hiphop.de/magazin/news/ak-ausserkontrolle-entgeht-abschiebung-319388|title=AK Ausserkontrolle entgeht Abschiebung|website=hiphop.de|accessdate=10 November 2020}}</ref>

AK Ausserkontrolle wears a [[bandana]] which covers his face to stay anonymous.
