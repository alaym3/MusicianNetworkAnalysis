==Personal life==
YoungstaCPT is a [[Coloureds|Coloured]] Muslim of [[Cape Malays|Cape Malay]] descent whose music is inspired by his Muslim roots and Cape Malay culture.<ref>{{Cite news|last=Valentine|first=Venecia|date=5 December 2019|title=Rapper wrapped in Musallah|work=[[Daily Voice (South African newspaper)|Daily Voice]]|url=https://www.dailyvoice.co.za/lifestyle-entertainment/rapper-wrapped-in-musallah-38680732|access-date=30 May 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Voice of the Cape|url=https://africasacountry.com/2019/04/voice-of-the-cape|last=Jappie|first=Zayaan|date=30 May 2020|website=Africa Is a Country|language=en-US|access-date=30 May 2020}}</ref>
