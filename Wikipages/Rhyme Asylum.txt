'''Rhyme Asylum''' was an English, London-based [[hip hop (music)|hip hop]] group formed in 2002 by MCs Possessed, Psiklone and Skirmish. Leatherface and Plazma (who did not return for the second album) joined in 2004.<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20090203043918/http://www.ukhh.com/features/interviews/rhyme_asylum/index.html] </ref> 

The group are known for their vivid imagery, complex wordplay and intricate rhyme schemes. Despite remaining unsigned, they have amassed a growing cult following.{{citation needed|date=May 2020}}

==Biography==
Their recognition originates from the Deal Real open mic sessions between 2004 and 2005.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.tourdates.co.uk/londontourdates/issue-049/2009/06/19/1650-under-the-influence-rhyme-asylum|title=Tourdates Magazine Interview|website=Tourdates.co.uk}}</ref>
Their debut album ''[[State of Lunacy]]'' was released in June 2008 and featured US rappers [[Copywrite (rapper)|Copywrite]] and [[Diabolic (rap artist)|Diabolic]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.discogs.com/Rhyme-Asylum-State-Of-Lunacy/release/1372407|title=Rhyme Asylum - State Of Lunacy|website=Discogs}}</ref> Both Plazma and Leatherface left the group shortly after the release of ''[[State of Lunacy]]''.

Their second album ''[[Solitary Confinement (Rhyme Asylum album)|Solitary Confinement]]'' was released in April 2010 featuring [[Crooked I]], [[Ill Bill]] and frequent collaborator Reain.<ref name="auto">{{Cite web|url=https://www.discogs.com/Rhyme-Asylum-Solitary-Confinement/release/2242302|title=Rhyme Asylum - Solitary Confinement|website=Discogs.com}}</ref> For the first time, Rhyme Asylum recruited outside producers.<ref name="auto"/> 

Later in 2010, the group released ''Solitary Confinement: The Overdose EP'' on 12" vinyl.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.discogs.com/Rhyme-Asylum-Solitary-Confinement-The-Overdose-EP/release/2312309|title=Rhyme Asylum - Solitary Confinement: The Overdose EP|website=Discogs.com}}</ref> The EP contains eight songs from ''[[Solitary Confinement (Rhyme Asylum album)|Solitary Confinement]]'' and was launched at a [[Dilated Peoples]] & Rhyme Asylum show in Hamburg, Germany.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rhymeasylum.com/releases.html|title=RELEASES - Rhyme Asylum|website=Rhymeasylum.com}}</ref>

Rhyme Asylum have performed across the UK and Europe.<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20071031032527/http://www.britishhiphop.co.uk/news/latest/speakers_corner_demise.html] </ref><ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20101110222315/http://www.hiphopug.com/1/post/2010/10/hhug-interview-with-rhyme-asylum.html] </ref>
