'''L'Impératrice''' ("The Empress") is a French pop and disco [[Band (rock and pop)|band]], founded in 2012 in Paris.

==Career==
L'Impératrice is a group of six musicians: Charles de Boisseguin, the group's founder (keyboards), Hagni Gwon (keyboards), David Gaugué (bass guitar), Achille Trocellier (electric guitar), Tom Daveau (drums), as well as Flore Benguigui, vocalist, who joined the band in 2015.

They released their first EP, self-titled, in 2012, followed by a second (''Sonate Pacifique'') in 2014, and then a third (''Odyssée'') in 2015. This last one was re-edited a year later under the name ''L'Empereur'', in a slower version of the original, inspired by a fan's mistake of playing the album at the wrong speed.<ref name="cliquetv">[http://www.clique.tv/limperatrice-erreur-vinyle/ « L’heureuse erreur du vinyle de L’Impératrice »], ''clique.tv''.</ref> An acoustic version using violin, cello and acoustic guitar was also released, in February 2017.

The band received the [[Deezer]] Adami Fans' award in 2016.<ref>[https://www.virginradio.fr/prix-deezer-adami-2016-her-l-imperatrice-et-bachar-mar-khalife-en-concert-le-6-juin-au-casino-de-paris-a524713.html « Prix Deezer Adami 2016 : Her, L'Impératrice et Bachar Mar-Khalifé en concert le 6 juin au Casino de Paris avec Virgin Radio »], ''[[Virgin Radio]]''.</ref>

In 2016, a single "Vanille Fraise" was released,<ref>{{cite news |title=Le clip du jour : Vanille Fraise de L'Impératrice |url=https://www.paulette-magazine.com/le-clip-du-jour-vanille-fraise-de-limperatrice/ |access-date=30 January 2021 |work=Paulette Magazine |date=1 February 2016 |language=French}}</ref> based on a sample by the soul singer [[Anita Ward]].<ref>{{cite web |title=L'Impératrice - Concert |url=https://www.institutfrancais.de/fr/hamburg/event/limperatrice-concert-14947 |website=Institut français d'Allemagne |access-date=30 January 2021 |language=French |date=26 February 2020}}</ref>

The band then joined the independent label microqlima, releasing an EP title ''Séquences'' in June 2017.<ref name="tsugi">[https://www.tsugi.fr/en-ecoute-sequences-le-nouvel-ep-de-limperatrice/ Lucas Martin, « En écoute : “Séquences”, le nouvel EP de L’Impératrice »], {{ill|Tsugi (magazine)|lt=Tsugi|fr|Tsugi}}, 16 June 2017.</ref> Remixes of its songs, one by the Australian group Parcels, were released that September.<ref>[http://www.lesinrocks.com/2017/09/news/limperatrice-remixe-par-les-parcels/ « L’Impératrice remixé par les Parcels »], ''[[Les Inrockuptibles]]'', 27 Septembre 2017.</ref>

On 17 October 2017, the band released "Erreur 404", the first single of their first full-length album, ''Matahari''.<ref>[https://www.huffingtonpost.fr/2017/10/17/limperatrice-devoile-le-premier-titre-de-son-album-avec-un-clip-hommage-aux-romans-photo-des-annees-70_a_23242212/ « L'Impératrice dévoile le premier titre de son album avec un clip hommage aux romans photo des années 70 »], ''[[Huffington Post]]'', 17 October 2017.</ref>  L'impératrice appeared on the French television show ''[[Quotidien]]'' on [[TMC (TV channel)|TMC]] on 9 January 2018.<ref>[https://www.lesinrocks.com/inrocks.tv/limperatrice-devoile-un-allechant-avant-gout-de-son-prochain-album-dans-quotidien/ « L’Impératrice dévoile un alléchant avant-goût de son prochain album dans Quotidien »], ''[[Les Inrockuptibles]]'', 10 January 2018.</ref>

The band performed at [[La Cigale]] on 12 October 2017, and then at the [[Casino de Paris]] on 3–4 April 2018.<ref name=lemonde>{{cite web|title=L’Impératrice, une " French touch " pas si fraîche|url=https://www.lemonde.fr/musiques/article/2018/04/11/l-imperatrice-une-french-touch-pas-si-fraiche_5283764_1654986.html|author=Bruno Lesprit|agency=''[[Le Monde]]''|date=11 April 2018|publication-date=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=EAu Casino de Paris, L'Impératrice aux multiples visages|url=http://www.lefigaro.fr/musique/2018/04/02/03006-20180402ARTFIG00008-au-casino-de-paris-l-imperatrice-aux-multiples-visages.php|author=Agathe Moreaux|website=[[Le Figaro]]|date=2 April 2018|publication-date=}}</ref> It completed a French tour between February and May 2018. An extension of this tour took place between October 2018 and January 2019.<ref>{{cite web|title=L’Impératrice ajoute de nouvelles dates à sa tournée française|url=https://live-arena.com/limperatrice-ajoute-de-nouvelles-dates-a-sa-tournee-francaise/|agency=Live Arena|date=18 April 2018|publication-date=}}</ref><ref name=lemonde />

On 21 June 2018, during the [[Fête de la Musique]], the band performed at the French [[National Assembly (France)|National Assembly]].<ref>{{cite web|title=L’Impératrice au Palais Bourbon pour la Fête de la musique|url=https://www.lemonde.fr/musiques/article/2018/06/21/l-imperatrice-au-palais-bourbon-pour-la-fete-de-la-musique_5319066_1654986.html|website=lemonde.fr|date=21 June 2018|access-date=22 June 2018|publication-date=}}</ref>
