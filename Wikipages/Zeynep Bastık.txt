'''Zeynep Bastık''' (born 8 July 1993) is a Turkish singer, songwriter, dancer, and actress.

== Life ==
Zeynep Bastık was born on 8 July 1993 in [[Çanakkale]]. Her family members are also musicians.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://onedio.com/haber/zeynep-bastik-kimdir-zeynep-bastik-kac-yasinda-ve-nereli-iste-tum-zeynep-batik-sarkilari-965880|title=Zeynep Bastık Kimdir? Zeynep Bastık Kaç Yaşında ve Nereli? İşte Tüm Zeynep Batık Şarkıları...|work=Onedio|first=Müge|last=Kakşi|date=15 February 2021|accessdate=4 April 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Zeynep Bastık kaç yaşında? Zeynep Bastık kimdir?|url=https://www.haberturk.com/zeynep-bastik-kac-yasinda-zeynep-bastik-kimdir-2973127|website=Habertürk|accessdate=5 April 2020|language=Turkish}}</ref><ref name=":Engür">{{cite web |last1=Engür |first1=Simay |title=Her yerde! Zeynep Bastık |url=https://www.elele.com.tr/unluler/roportajlar/her-yerde-zeynep-bastik |publisher=Elele |access-date=23 April 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.newstr.net/zeynep-bastik-kimdir/|title=Zeynep Bastık Kimdir? Kaç Yaşında? Sevgilisi Kim?|first=Mehmet Fatih|last=Altun|date=9 March 2021}}</ref> Her mother was a folk dance teacher. When she was three years old, her family moved to [[İzmir]] and she completed her secondary and high school education there. At the age of fifteen she joined the [[Fire of Anatolia]] as a dancer and remained part of the group for eight years. She later became interested in Latin dance and pole dance. She also worked as actress in musical theatres.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cnnturk.com/magazin/zeynep-bastik-kiymetinin-farkindayim|title=Zeynep Bastık: Kıymetinin farkındayım|website=CNN Türk}}</ref> In 2010, she started professional music with the band "Jackpot" in İzmir, performing mainly cover songs. Until 2012, she performed with this group on stage in different cities, including İzmir, [[Eskişehir]] and [[Bursa]].<ref name=":Engür" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.haberturk.com/zeynep-bastik-kac-yasinda-zeynep-bastik-kimdir-2973127|title=Zeynep Bastık kimdir?|website=www.haberturk.com}}</ref>

Her backing vocals were featured in [[Murat Dalkılıç]]'s song "Lüzumsuz Savaş", and she worked as Dalkılıç's backing vocalist for three years. With Dalkılıç's help, she released her first single "Fırça" in 2014. She performed the duet "Ben Kalp Sen", which was composed by Dalkılıç for the series ''Aşk Yeniden''. She later released the single "Şahaneyim", which was composed by Murat Dalkılıç and Caner Yemez, in 2017.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.ranini.tv/ozel/3958/1/murat-dalkilictan-ask-yeniden-e-ozel-sarki|title=Murat Dalkılıç'tan 'Aşk Yeniden' e özel şarkı!|website=ranini.tv}}</ref> She also had minor roles in various TV series such as ''Adı Mutluluk'', ''[[Yasak Elma]]'', ''Sil Baştan'', ''Umuda Kelepçe Vurulmaz'' and ''Yuvamdaki Düşman''.<ref>{{cite news |title=Zeynep Bastık kimdir? Bir dönem oyunculuk yapıyordu |url=https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/kelebek/magazin/zeynep-bastik-kimdir-bir-donem-oyunculuk-yapiyordu-41310727 |newspaper=Hürriyet |access-date=19 April 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.magazinmax.com/zeynep-bastik-hangi-dizilerde-rol-aldi|title=Zeynep Bastık hangi dizilerde rol aldı?|work=Magazin Max|date=17 October 2019|accessdate=11 May 2021}}</ref> She started her solo music career shortly afterwards. She became famous after releasing a cover version of [[Ezhel]]'s song "Felaket".<ref>{{cite web |title=Zeynep Bastık reklam anlaşmasından 2 milyon TL kazandı |url=https://www.milliyet.com.tr/cadde/zeynep-bastik-reklam-anlasmasindan-2-milyon-tl-kazandi-6109246 |publisher=Milliyet |access-date=19 April 2020}}</ref> Additionally, she covered a number of other songs, notably by [[Fikri Karayel]], Harun Kolçak and [[Ümit Sayın]], which she shared on her YouTube channel. These covers helped with increasing her fame.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Güzel |first1=Yağız |title=Zeynep Bastık gerçekten yeni neslin Sezen Aksu'su mu ? |url=https://www.milliyet.com.tr/zeynep-bastik-gercekten-yeni-neslin-sezen-aksu-su-mu--molatik-13340/ |publisher=Milliyet Molatik |access-date=19 April 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Şişman |first1=Can |title=Zeynep Bastık'ın popülerleşmesi neden tepki çekiyor? |url=https://www.milliyet.com.tr/zeynep-bastik-in-populerlesmesi-neden-tepki-cekiyor--molatik-12433/ |publisher=Milliyet Molatik |access-date=19 April 2020}}</ref>

In June 2019, Bastık released a single with Anıl Piyancı titled "Bırakman Doğru Mu?". She has also collaborated with composers [[Emir Can İğrek]] and [[Mustafa Sandal]], and voiced songs written and composed by [[Sezen Aksu]] and [[Oğuzhan Koç]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://karmaturkiye.com/2020/09/04/emir-can-igrek-ve-zeynep-bastiktan-duet-dargin/|title=Emir Can İğrek ve Zeynep Bastık'tan Düet: Dargın|first=Aygün|last=Akdağ|date=4 September 2020}}</ref> She wrote the lysics for her single "Çukur" composed together with Çağrı Telkıvıran.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://muzikonair.com/zeynep-bastik-cukur-video-klibiyle-yayinda/|title=Zeynep Bastık - Çukur Video Klibiyle Yayında -|date=21 January 2020}}</ref> Together with Serhat Şensesli, she wrote and composed the song "Kendi Yolumuzda" as a collaboration with [[Sunsilk|Elidor]] to mark the Women's Day. She is reported to have co-written and co-composed of some songs for her upcoming first album ''Zeynodisco''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.takvim.com.tr/saklambac/2020/12/19/zeynep-bastik-bos-yapmadi|title=Zeynep Bastık 'Boş yapma'dı|website=takvim.com.tr}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/yazarlar/tolga-akyildiz/disko-kralicesi-olacak-41784478|title=Disko kraliçesi olacak|first=Tolga|last=AKYILDIZ|website=www.hurriyet.com.tr}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.gunes.com/magazin/zeynep-bastik-bos-yapmayin-1102399|title=Zeynep Bastık ‘Boş Yapma'yın!|website=gunes.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.ntv.com.tr/sanat/zeynep-bastiktan-sezen-aksu-imzali-yeni-sarki,G_O8TtBjfEmxDCZkf0z5Gw|title=Zeynep Bastık'tan Sezen Aksu imzalı yeni şarkı|website=www.ntv.com.tr}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.muzikhabercisi.com/14986/muzik/oguzhan-koc-yazdi-zeynep-bastik-soyledi.html|title=Oğuzhan Koç Yazdı, Zeynep Bastık Söyledi!|first=Michael|last=Kuyucu|website=Müzik Habercisi}}</ref>

Bastık became an advertising face for [[Adidas]] in 2019 and [[Sunsilk|Elidor]] in 2020.<ref>{{cite web |title=Adidas’ın yeni marka elçisi Zeynep Bastık |url=https://mediacat.com/adidasin-yeni-marka-elcisi-zeynep-bastik/ |publisher=MediaCat |access-date=19 April 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Zeynep Bastık Elidor Marka Elçisi Oldu |url=https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/mahmure/zeynep-bastik-elidor-marka-elcisi-oldu-41404772 |newspaper=Hürriyet |access-date=19 April 2020}}</ref>

== Music style ==
Bastık mainly performed rock and later acoustic and electronic music. According to Tolga Akyıldız, performing the songs of artists such as [[Aylin Aslım]], [[İzel]], Ferda Anıl Yarkın, [[Kenan Doğulu]], [[Levent Yüksel]], Jabbar, [[Tarkan (singer)|Tarkan]], [[Mustafa Sandal]], [[Şebnem Ferah]], Harun Kolçak, [[Ajda Pekkan]], [[Nilüfer (singer)|Nilüfer]], Kerim Tekin, Şahsenem, [[Pinhani]] and [[Sezen Aksu]], and her duets with multiple artists have contributed to her popularity in the digital music market.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Akyıldız |first1=Tolga |title=Zeynep Bastık’ın sırrı ne? |url=https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/yazarlar/tolga-akyildiz/zeynep-bastikin-sirri-ne-41316762 |newspaper=Hürriyet |access-date=19 April 2020}}</ref> Bastık was able to come to the fore with these advantages.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Akyıldız |first1=Tolga |title=Tam bir sürpriz yeni yıl hediyesi |url=https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/yazarlar/tolga-akyildiz/tam-bir-surpriz-yeni-yil-hediyesi-41416978 |newspaper=Hürriyet |access-date=19 April 2020}}</ref> It has been suggested that [[Can Ozan]], who made his debut in the same time as Bastık, became more popular and known after his duet with her was released.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Erdoğan |first1=Sevil |title=Can Ozan Gece Uyutmayan Duyguların Şarkılarını Yapıyor |url=https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/mahmure/yazarlar/sevil-erdogan/can-ozan-gece-uyutmayan-duygularin-sarkilarini-yapiyor-41446469 |newspaper=Hürriyet |access-date=19 April 2020}}</ref>
