'''Ardian Bujupi''' ({{IPA-sq|aɾdian bujupi}};{{fix|text=add stress mark}} born 27 April 1991) is a German-Albanian singer and songwriter.

Born in [[Pristina]], [[Kosovo]], he moved to Germany at a young age and he has been living there ever since. He gained recognition in [[German speaking Europe|German-speaking Europe]] after finishing third in the [[Deutschland sucht den Superstar (season 8)|eighth series]] of ''[[Deutschland sucht den Superstar]]''. He is active in both German and Albanian music industries and is known for his songs "Andiamo" and "Na jena njo", among others.

== Biography ==

Bujupi was born on 27 April 1991 into an Albanian family in the city of [[Pristina]], then part of the [[Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia]], present Kosovo.<ref name="TeksteShqip">{{cite web |publisher=TeksteShqip |title=Ardian Bujupi Biografia |url=https://www.teksteshqip.com/ardian-bujupi |access-date=4 February 2020 |language=sq}}</ref><ref name="Top Channel">{{cite web |publisher=[[Top Channel]] |title=Ardian Bujupi #Bio |url=http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/artist/ardian-bujupi/ |access-date=19 February 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201025161832/http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/artist/ardian-bujupi/ |archive-date=25 October 2020 |language=sq}}</ref> At an early age, his family fled to [[Heidelberg]], Germany, as a refugee to escape the persecution of Albanians in connection with the disintegration of [[Yugoslavia]].<ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[RTL (German TV channel)|RTL]] |title=Ardian Bujupi aus Heidelberg bei DSDS 2011 |url=https://www.rtl.de/cms/biografie-von-dsds-kandidat-ardian-bujupi-635264.html |access-date=19 February 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191109225718/https://www.rtl.de/cms/biografie-von-dsds-kandidat-ardian-bujupi-635264.html |archive-date=9 November 2019 |language=de |date=8 December 2011}}</ref>

In 2011, Bujupi auditioned for the [[Deutschland sucht den Superstar (season 8)|eighth season]] of ''[[Deutschland sucht den Superstar]]'', singing "Und wenn ein Lied" by [[Söhne Mannheims]] in front of judges [[Dieter Bohlen]], [[Fernanda Brandão]], and [[Patrick Nuo]]. After progressing through the rounds, he reached the semi-final and finished in third place on 30 April 2011.<ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[RTL (German TV channel)|RTL]] |title=Das große DSDS-Halbfinale: Wer sang was? [[File:Ardian Bujupi1.jpg|thumb|upright|Bujupi performing in 2012]]
Just a few months after ''DSDS'', Bujupi released his first single "This Is My Time", written in English, which became a hit on the radios and social networks, counting more than a million views over a week.{{Citation needed|date=March 2021}} He later released "Rise to the Top" and "I'm Feeling Good", all which had the same success as his first single. He started touring immediately after releasing his first songs. He mostly toured in Germany and Switzerland, but later was invited to tour in Kosovo and Albania. Bujupi was invited to perform in ''[[X Factor Albania|The X Factor Albania]]'' to promote his single.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.teksteshqip.com/ardian-bujupi/lajme_2180.php |title=Performanca Ne X Factor Albania |access-date=22 August 2013 |archive-url=https://archive.today/20130822163331/http://www.teksteshqip.com/ardian-bujupi/lajme_2180.php |archive-date=22 August 2013 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

He later became popular in the Albanian-speaking territories and released "Want U Now" also in Kosovo and Albania. The single was well-received and was one of the most played summer songs.{{Citation needed|date=March 2021}} He toured around the Albanian coast in the summer.{{Specify |reason=What year? |date=July 2021}}<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.teksteshqip.com/ardian-bujupi/lajme_4632.php |title=Së Shpejti Turne Në Kosovë |access-date=22 August 2013 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130910110132/http://teksteshqip.com/ardian-bujupi/lajme_4632.php |archive-date=10 September 2013 |url-status=dead }}</ref>
