'''Shin Hyo-seob''' ({{Korean|hangul=신효섭}}; born May 3, 1992),<ref>{{cite web|title=Crush (신효섭)|website=Melon |url=http://www.melon.com/artist/timeline.htm?artistId=674710 |access-date=January 28, 2016}}</ref> professionally known as '''Crush''' ({{Korean|hangul=크러쉬|labels=no}}), is a South Korean [[contemporary R&B|R&B]] and [[hip hop music|hip hop]] singer-songwriter and producer. Crush often uses Jazz musical instruments in his style of music, implementing modern tunes. He debuted on April 1, 2014, with the single "Sometimes" and  released his first album ''[[Crush on You (Crush album)|Crush on You]]'' on June 5, 2014.

Thirteen of Crush's singles have peaked in the top ten on the [[Gaon Digital Chart]], with nine of Crush's singles also becoming top five hits including "Just", "Beautiful", "Don't Forget", "Bittersweet" and "Sleepness Night".

==Life and career==
===Early life and career beginnings===
Crush was born Shin Hyo-seob on May 3, 1992.<ref name="Sports Dong-a">{{cite web|last1=Lee|first1=Jeong-yeon|script-title=ko:[인터뷰] 가수 크러쉬 "영혼 불어넣은 곡들 팬들께 위로 됐으면…"|url=https://sports.donga.com/3/all/20191208/98710645/3|website=[[Sports Dong-a]]|publisher=Dong-a Ilbo Company|access-date=January 17, 2020|language=ko|date=December 9, 2019}}</ref> Raised in [[Seoul]], he began listening to [[Rhythm and blues|R&B]] and [[Hip hop music|hip hop]] music at a young age and aspired to become a singer. [[Dynamic Duo (South Korean duo)|Dynamic Duo]]'s debut album ''Taxi Driver'' left a "deep impression" on him, which inspired him to begin [[rapping]].<ref name="Starnews">{{cite web|last1=Yoon|first1=Seong-yeol|script-title=ko:크러쉬 "피처링 목소리 각인..내 정체성 보여줄 때"(인터뷰)|url=http://star.mt.co.kr/stview.php?no=2014060511563973491|website=Starnews|access-date=January 17, 2020|language=ko|date=June 5, 2014}}</ref> He began making music during his first year of middle school with a friend and sending [[demo (music)|demos]] to the group's record label [[Amoeba Culture]]. Nicknamed "Shi Syeob" ({{Korean|hangul=시셥|labels=no}}), he wrote the moniker "C-sub" in English as a tentative stage name. His friend misread it as "Crush", which he took an immediate liking to and decided to use.<ref name="10asia">{{cite web|last1=Kwon|first1=Seok-jeong|script-title=ko:크러쉬와 우린 오늘밤에 Yeah! (인터뷰)|url=http://tenasia.hankyung.com/archives/283172|website=10asia|publisher=Korea Entertainment Media|access-date=January 24, 2020|language=ko|date=July 15, 2014}}</ref> Crush attended [[Hoseo University]] and graduated from the Department of Practical Music.<ref name="Sports Dong-a"/>

While taking a break from school, Crush sent Zion.T his demos after meeting at a hip hop party. The two became acquainted and, along with [[Gray (singer)|Gray]], [[Loco (rapper)|Loco]], and [[Elo (singer)|Elo]], formed the music crew VV:D in 2012.<ref name="10asia"/>

Crush released the single "Red Dress" featuring TakeOne on December 7, 2012.<ref>{{cite web|title=Red Dress|website=Melon |url=http://www.melon.com/album/detail.htm?albumId=2169352 |access-date=January 28, 2016}}</ref> He contributed to his first song as a record producer and guest artist on [[Loco (rapper)|Loco]]'s single "No More".<ref name="10asia"/> In the following year, he released the singles "Crush on You" and "Where Do You Wanna Go" with Taewan and [[Gary (rapper)|Gary.]]<ref>{{Cite web|date=2013-07-27|title=Taewan (C-Luv), Gary, and Crush Team Up For "Where Do You Want To Go?" Release ⋆ The latest kpop news and music {{!}} Officially Kmusic|url=https://officiallykmusic.com/taewan-c-luv-gary-and-crush-team-up-for-where-do-you-want-to-go-release/|access-date=2021-09-12|website=The latest kpop news and music {{!}} Officially Kmusic|language=en-US}}</ref> In July 2013, Crush signed an exclusive contract with [[Amoeba Culture]].<ref name="Starnews"/>

=== 2014-2015: Solo debut and rising popularity ===
Crush officially debuted on April 1, 2014, with a single titled "Sometimes".<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VN4EusA9zmE &#91;MV&#93; Crush(크러쉬) _ Sometimes(가끔)]</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.fnnews.com/news/201404021335030973?t=y|title=Crush, April 2 debut song "Sometimes" public music album released|date=April 2, 2014|access-date=April 26, 2018}}</ref> His first album, ''[[Crush on You (Crush album)|Crush on You]]'', was released on June 5, 2014, and went on to win Best R&B & Soul Album at the 12th [[Korean Music Awards]]. In 2014, Crush also collaborated with [[Gaeko]] for the single "Hug Me".<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.joongboo.com/news/articleView.html?idxno=927030|title=Crush has released his first full album. Gaeko, Simon D.Jay Park participated.|author=중부일보 |work=중부일보 |access-date=April 26, 2018|language=ko}}</ref> In August, he sang the OST "Sleepless Night" for the [[Seoul Broadcasting System|SBS]] drama ''[[It's Okay, That's Love]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.melon.com/album/detail.htm?albumId=2272322 |script-title=ko:Melon, 괜찮아 사랑이야 OST Part 3}}</ref> In October, Crush released the track "Sofa".<ref>{{cite web |title=[Video] Crush Cries Alone in 'Sofa' Music Video |last=Hong |first=Grace Danbi |website=CJ E&M enewsWorld |url=http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/79528/video-crush-cries-alone-in-sofa-music-video |access-date=January 28, 2016 |date=October 30, 2014 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160203030845/http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/79528/video-crush-cries-alone-in-sofa-music-video |archive-date=February 3, 2016 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

In 2015, Crush collaborated with [[Zion.T]] for the single "Just". The song was released on January 30, 2015, and debuted at number one on the [[Gaon Singles Chart]].<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20150913180255/http://gaonchart.co.kr/main/section/chart/online.gaon?nationGbn=T&serviceGbn=ALL&targetTime=07&hitYear=2015&termGbn=week South Korea's Gaon Singles Chart (in Korean)] Originally from [[Gaon Music Chart]] (February 2015). Archived on September 13, 2015. Retrieved on October 17, 2015.</ref> Crush and Zion.T won Best Collaboration at the 17th [[Mnet Asian Music Awards]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://chicnews.mk.co.kr/article.php?aid=144912069082511009|title=Zion.T and Crush won Best Collaboration & Unit|website=chicnews.mk.co.kr|language=ko|access-date=April 27, 2018}}</ref> In July, Crush released the single "Oasis" which featured [[Block B]]'s [[Zico (rapper)|Zico]].<ref>{{cite web |title=[Video] Crush Releases 'Oasis' with Block B's Zico |last=Lee |first=Nancy |website=CJ E&M enewsWorld |url=http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/95238/video-crush-releases-oasis-with-block-bs-zico |access-date=January 28, 2016 |date=July 9, 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150711220008/http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/95238/video-crush-releases-oasis-with-block-bs-zico |archive-date=July 11, 2015 |url-status=dead }}</ref> In November, Crush held his first solo concert, ''Crush on You''.<ref>{{cite web |title=Crush's Concert Tickets Sell Out Immediately |last=Kim |first=Ji-yeon |website=CJ E&M enewsWorld |url=http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/98448/crushs-concert-tickets-sell-out-immediately |access-date=January 28, 2016 |date=October 2, 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160317233213/http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/98448/crushs-concert-tickets-sell-out-immediately |archive-date=March 17, 2016 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

=== 2016-2018: Breakthrough and widespread recognition ===
[[File:20170708 Liiv 리브콘서트 크러쉬 2.jpg|thumb|Crush performing in 2017]]

In January 2016, Crush released the single "Don't Forget" featuring [[Girls' Generation]]'s [[Kim Tae-yeon|Taeyeon]].<ref>{{cite web|title=SNSD's Taeyeon to Feature on Crush's New Song|last=Lee|first=So-dam|website=Newsen|url=http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/101976/snsds-taeyeon-to-feature-on-crushs-new-song|access-date=January 28, 2016|date=January 20, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160130083615/http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/101976/snsds-taeyeon-to-feature-on-crushs-new-song|archive-date=January 30, 2016|url-status=dead}}</ref> "Don't Forget" won first place on [[Show Champion]] on January 27, marking his first music show win.<ref>{{cite web|title=Crush Wins With "Don't Forget" Featuring Taeyeon on "Show Champion," Performances by GFRIEND, Dal Shabet, and More|website=Soompi|url=http://www.soompi.com/2016/01/27/crush-wins-with-dont-forget-featuring-taeyeon-on-show-champion-performances-by-gfriend-dal-shabet-and-more/|access-date=January 28, 2016|date=January 27, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://osen.mt.co.kr/article/G1110342268|title=Crush won first place on Show Champion|access-date=April 27, 2018|language=ko}}</ref> Crush also went on to win Best Vocal Performance - Male Solo at the 18th [[Mnet Asian Music Awards]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=2016 MAMA award winners|url=http://kpopherald.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=201612041517160601991_2|access-date=2021-09-10|website=kpopherald.koreaherald.com}}</ref>

In 2016 Crush became an active member of the hip-hop crew Fanxy Child, consisting of [[Zico (rapper)|Zico]] (rapper, producer), [[Dean (South Korean singer)|Dean]] (singer, producer), [[Penomeco]] (rapper), Millic (DJ, producer), Staytuned (producer).

In July 2018, Crush released his third [[extended play]] ''Wonderlost''.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Crush's new EP to be titled 'Wonderlost'|url=http://kpopherald.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=201807061242332143664_2}}</ref> As with his previous projects, Crush was heavily involved in the songwriting, composition, and production of his album, stating: “I’m able to do the full production… I can make my own arrangements, and oversee the entire process.”<ref>{{Cite web|title=Crush Talks His 'Really Personal, Jazzy' Upcoming EP 'wonderlost'|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/k-town/7580553/crush-wonderlust-ep-wonderlost-interview/|access-date=2021-09-12|website=Billboard|language=en}}</ref>

In December 2016, Crush also released the hit OST “Beautiful” for the [[TvN (South Korean TV channel)|tvN]] drama [[Guardian: The Lonely and Great God|''Guardian: The Lonely and Great God,'']] and later received a nomination for Best OST at the 19th [[Mnet Asian Music Awards]].<ref>{{Cite web|title='도깨비' 신드롬, OST로 이어진다..크러쉬 '뷰티풀' 차트 1위 석권|url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=404&aid=0000161131|access-date=2021-09-10|website=entertain.naver.com|language=ko}}</ref>

=== 2019: Second full-length album and continued popularity ===
On June 4, 2019, Crush stated on his Instagram that he would be leaving Amoeba Records to start his own agency.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.koreaboo.com/news/crush-leave-amoeba-culture-6-years/|title=Crush Announces That He Will Be Leaving Amoeba Culture After 6 Years|date=June 4, 2019|website=Koreaboo|language=en-US|access-date=June 7, 2019}}</ref> However, on July 17, 2019, he announced that he had decided to sign with [[Psy|PSY]]'s agency P Nation.<ref>{{Cite web|last=기자|first=황수연|title=크러쉬 "6년 몸 담은 아메바컬쳐 떠난다, 1인 기획사로 새 출발" [전문]|url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=311&aid=0000999615|access-date=2021-09-12|website=entertain.naver.com|language=ko}}</ref>

On December 5, 2019, Crush released his critically acclaimed second full-length album ''From Midnight To Sunrise'', with "Alone" and "With You" serving as the album's double title tracks.<ref>{{Cite web|title=[인터뷰③] 크러쉬 "싸이와 계약이유? 대화 잘 통해, 전폭적인 지원 감사"|url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=076&aid=0003505414|access-date=June 12, 2020|website=entertain.naver.com|language=ko}}</ref> Crush once again took control of songwriting and production for the album, and introduced [[jazz]] elements alongside R&B.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Cy|date=2019-12-09|title=Crush’s "From Midnight to Sunrise" is an Aural Masterpiece|url=https://hiphopkr.com/reviews/crush-midnight-sunrise/|access-date=2021-09-12|website=HiphopKR|language=en-US}}</ref> ''From Midnight To Sunrise'' won R&B Album of the Year at the [[Korean Hip-hop Awards]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=TinNhac.com|date=2020-03-05|title=Kết quả lễ trao giải Korean Hip Hop Awards 2020|url=https://tinnhac.com/ket-qua-le-trao-giai-korean-hip-hop-awards-2020-137287.html|access-date=2021-09-13|website=TinNhac.com|language=vi}}</ref>

In February 2020, Crush released the OST “Let Us Go” for the tvN drama [[Crash Landing on You|''Crash Landing On You'']].<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-05-31|title=Crash Landing On You OST part 10 - Let Us Go, MV & Lyrics|url=http://kpopndrama.com/crash-landing-on-you-ost-part-10-let-us-go-mv-lyrics/|access-date=2021-09-10|website=Kpopndrama|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-02-20|title=The Boyz, Zico, BTS, And "Crash Landing On You" OST Top Gaon Weekly Charts - KpopHit|url=https://kpophit.com/the-boyz-zico-bts-and-crash-landing-on-you-ost-top-gaon-weekly-charts-kpophit/|access-date=2021-09-13|website=KPOP HIT|language=en-US}}</ref>

In May 2020, it was announced that Crush had asked [[Joy (singer)|Joy]] of [[Red Velvet (group)|Red Velvet]] to feature on the title track of his new album.<ref>{{Cite web|date=May 21, 2020|title=Crush Releases New Song 'Mayday' Featuring Red Velvet's Joy|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/k-town/9387426/crush-mayday-featuring-joy-listen|access-date=May 22, 2020|website=Billboard}}</ref> The song "Mayday" topped multiple music charts upon its release.<ref>{{Cite web|title=크러쉬, 신곡 '자나깨나 (Feat. 조이 of Red Velvet)' 주요 음원 차트 1위+최상위권 섭렵|url=http://www.nextdaily.co.kr/news/article.html?id=20200521800015|last=www.nextdaily.co.kr|date=May 21, 2020|website=미래를 여는 종합미디어 넥스트데일리|access-date=May 22, 2020}}</ref>

On October 20, 2020, Crush released his fourth extended play ''With Her.''<ref>{{Cite web|title=크러쉬 측 "역대급 女 아티스트 대거 참여, 초호화 라인업"|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/now/article/609/0000339570|access-date=October 13, 2020|website=news.naver.com|language=ko}}</ref>

== Personal life ==
On November 12, 2020, Crush enlisted for his [[conscription in South Korea|mandatory military service]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=크러쉬, 직접 전한 입대 소식 "다시 만날 날 고대하며" [전문]|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/now/article/396/0000563240|access-date=October 19, 2020|website=news.naver.com|language=ko}}</ref>

On August 23, 2021, Crush was confirmed to be in a relationship with singer and actress [[Joy (singer)|Joy]], whom he had collaborated with during the previous year.<ref>{{cite web|title=조이♥크러쉬, 스타커플 탄생!&nbsp;...&nbsp;어쩐지 MV서도 달달했다 [공식입장](종합)|trans-title=Joy♥Crush, a star couple is born!&nbsp;...&nbsp;Somehow the MV was sweet too [Official position] (General)|url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=117&aid=0003529625|via=Naver|publisher=My Daily|language=ko|author=Lee Seung-rok|date=August 23, 2021|access-date=August 23, 2021}}</ref>
