"'''Pumpy'''" is a song by Da Beatfreakz featuring [[Cadet (rapper)|Cadet]], AJ & [[Deno (singer)|Deno]] and Swarmz. It was released on 26 October 2018 and peaked at number 70 on the [[UK Singles Chart]].
