'''In Her Own Words''' is an American [[Rock music|rock]] band from [[Los Angeles, California]].

==History==
In Her Own Words released their first EP in 2011 titled ''Brand New Me''.<ref name="bandcamp">{{cite web|url=https://inherownwords.bandcamp.com/album/brand-new-me|publisher=inherownwords.bandcamp.com|title=Brand New Me &#124; In Her Own Words|accessdate=20 October 2016}}</ref> In 2013, the band released their first full-length album on Ice Grill$ titled ''Everything I Used To Trust''.<ref name="newnoise">{{cite web|last1=Shrum|first1=Tony|title=Album Review: In Her Own Words – "Everything I Used To Trust"|url=http://newnoisemagazine.com/album-review-words-everything-trust/|publisher=New Noise Magazine|accessdate=20 October 2016}}</ref><ref name="mtscollective1">{{cite web|last1=Parra|first1=Mario|title=EXCLUSIVE: IN HER OWN WORDS - "EVERYTHING I USED TO TRUST" STREAM|url=http://www.mtscollective.com/2013/07/exclusive-in-her-own-words-everything-i.html|publisher=MTS Collective|accessdate=20 October 2016}}</ref> In 2016, In Her Own Words signed to [[InVogue Records]] and released their second full-length album titled ''Unfamiliar''.<ref name="altpress">{{cite web|last1=Dickman|first1=Maggie|title=Pop punkers In Her Own Words premiere new song "Silver Lights" off debut album|date=15 August 2016|url=http://www.altpress.com/features/entry/in_her_own_words_drop_new_song_silver_lights|publisher=[[Alternative Press (music magazine)|Alternative Press]]|accessdate=20 October 2016}}</ref><ref name="thepunksite">{{cite web|last1=Phink|first1=AJ|title=In Her Own Words Release New Single Off 'Unfamiliar' Album|date=20 September 2016|url=http://thepunksite.com/news/in-her-own-words-release-new-single-off-unfamiliar-album/|publisher=The Punk Site|accessdate=20 October 2016}}</ref><ref name="mtscollective">{{cite web|last1=Parra|first1=Mario|title=IN HER OWN WORDS RELEASE DEBUT LP, "UNFAMILIAR"|url=http://www.mtscollective.com/2016/10/in-her-own-words-release-debut-lp.html|publisher=MTS Collective|accessdate=20 October 2016}}</ref><ref name="vandalamagazine">{{cite web|title=IN HER OWN WORDS RELEASE NEW SINGLE OFF "UNFAMILIAR" LP, OUT TOMORROW|date=13 October 2016|url=https://vandalamagazine.com/2016/10/13/in-her-own-words-release-new-single-off-unfamiliar-lp-out-tomorrow/|publisher=Vandala Magazine|accessdate=20 October 2016}}</ref><ref name="soundfiction">{{cite web|last1=Jordan|first1=Michael|title=IN HER OWN WORDS STREAM NEW SONG, 'I WOULD SIT ALONE IN SILENCE'|date=20 September 2016|url=http://www.soundfiction.net/2016/09/20/words-stream-new-song-sit-alone-silence|publisher=Sound Fiction|accessdate=20 October 2016}}</ref>
