'''Kobe Vidal Crawford''' (born August 20, 1998),<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://pitchfork.com/news/alabama-rapper-nocap-arrested-for-allegedly-shooting-into-occupied-house/|title=Alabama Rapper NoCap Arrested for Allegedly Shooting Into Occupied House|first=Matthew|last=Strauss|website=Pitchfork.com|accessdate=April 11, 2020}}</ref> known professionally as '''NoCap''', is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter.<ref name="Break">{{cite news | last=Seabrook III| first=Robby| title =The Break Presents: NoCap| work =XXL| date =June 12, 2019 | url =https://www.xxlmag.com/news/2019/06/nocap-interview-the-break/| accessdate =September 7, 2019}}</ref> Since 2017, he has released one EP and five mixtapes. His highest-charting project, ''Steel Human'', was released in July 2020, peaking at number 31 on the [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]]. He is noted for his use of [[wordplay]].

==Career==
NoCap began his career in earnest with the release of his first EP, ''Joker'', in May 2017.<ref name="HNHH">{{cite news | last=A.| first=Aron| title =NoCap Taps Quando Rondo, OMB Peezy & More For "The Backend Child" Tape | work =HotNewHipHop| date =May 28, 2019 | url =https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/nocap-taps-quando-rondo-omb-peezy-and-more-for-the-backend-child-tape-new-mixtape.119190.html| accessdate =September 7, 2019}}</ref> In early 2018, he released the song "Legend". The music video for that song accumulated over 1 million views on YouTube and would appear on NoCap's debut mixtape, ''Neighborhood Hero'',<ref name="Break"/> in July 2018.<ref name="Fest">{{cite news | title =Rising Alabama Stars Rylo Rodriguez & NoCap Share New Single "How We Roll"| work =RapFest| date =December 3, 2018 | url =http://therapfest.com/rising-alabama-stars-rylo-rodriguez-nocap-share-new-single-roll/| accessdate =September 7, 2019}}</ref> He followed that with a collaborative mixtape, ''Rogerville'', with [[Rylo Rodriguez]]<ref name="HNHH"/> in September 2018.<ref name="Fest"/> In November of that year, he was featured on the [[Lil Baby]] track, "Dreams 2 Reality", off the mixtape, ''[[Street Gossip (mixtape)|Street Gossip]]''.<ref name="HNHHBaby">{{cite news | last=Jahmal| first=Karlton| title =Lil Baby Drops 'Street Gossip' Featuring Meek Mill, Gunna, Young Thug & More| work =HotNewHipHop| date =November 30, 2018 | url =https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/lil-baby-drops-street-gossip-featuring-meek-mill-gunna-young-thug-and-more-new-mixtape.118683.html| accessdate =September 7, 2019}}</ref>

In March 2019, NoCap released the single, "Punching Bag" produced by [[CashMoneyAP]].<ref name="HNHHBag">{{cite news | last=Ch| first=Devin| title =NoCap Reps 'Bama Strong On "Punching Bag" Produced By CashMoneyAp| work =HotNewHipHop| date =March 9, 2019 | url =https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/nocap-reps-bama-strong-on-punching-bag-produced-by-cashmoneyap-new-song.1982252.html| accessdate =September 7, 2019}}</ref> Later that month, he released another single, "Ghetto Angels".<ref name="PFGhetto">{{cite news | last=Pierre| first=Alphonse| title =The Ones: NoCap's "Ghetto Angels"| work =Pitchfork| date =March 25, 2019 | url =https://pitchfork.com/levels/nocap-ghetto-angels-new-song-listen/| accessdate =September 7, 2019}}</ref> The music video for that song attained over 40 million views on YouTube.<ref name="Break"/> In May, NoCap and [[Polo G]] were featured on the CashMoneyAP song, "No Patience".<ref name="Bill">{{cite news | last=Saponara| first=Michael| title =CashMoneyAP Mobs With Polo G & NoCap For 'No Patience' Video: Premiere| work =Billboard| date =May 6, 2019 | url =https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/8510119/cashmoneyap-no-patience-video-polo-g-nocap| accessdate =September 7, 2019}}</ref> Later that month, he released his second solo mixtape with ''The Backend Child''. The project featured guest appearances from Rylo Rodriguez ("Dead Faces"), [[OMB Peezy]] ("Demons"), and [[Quando Rondo]] ("New Ones").<ref name="HNHH"/> NoCap also released music videos for songs off the album including those for "Blind Nights",<ref name="Blind">{{cite news | title =NoCap – Blind Nights (Official Music Video)| publisher=[[YouTube]]| date =May 21, 2019 | url =https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZqkUAEbkx5A| accessdate =September 7, 2019}}</ref> "Set It Off",<ref name="JN">{{cite news | title =Watch NoCap's Set It Off music video| work =JustNje| date =May 30, 2019 | url =https://justnje.com/watch-nocaps-set-it-off-music-video/| accessdate =September 7, 2019}}</ref> and "No Jewelry".<ref name="Complex">{{cite news | last=Espinoza| first=Joshua| title =Premiere: NoCap Drops New Video for "No Jewelry"| work =Complex| date =June 12, 2019 | url =https://www.complex.com/music/2019/06/nocap-no-jewelry-video| accessdate =September 7, 2019}}</ref>

In July 2019, NoCap delivered a [[Freestyle rap|freestyle]] as a part of ''DJ Booth''{{'}}s ''Bless The Booth'' series on [[Tidal (service)|Tidal]].<ref name="DJB">{{cite news | last=Chesman| first=Donna-Claire| title =NoCap Spits an Exclusive Freestyle for DJBooth & TIDAL's "Bless The Booth" Series| work =DJ Booth| date =July 8, 2019 | url =https://djbooth.net/features/2019-07-08-nocap-bless-the-booth-tidal-freestyle| accessdate =September 7, 2019}}</ref> The following month, he released the single, "Exotic",<ref name="PFExotic">{{cite news | last=Pierre| first=Alphonse| title =The Ones: NoCap's "Exotic"| work =Pitchfork| date =August 5, 2019 | url =https://pitchfork.com/levels/nocap-exotic-new-song-listen/| accessdate =September 7, 2019}}</ref> and performed another freestyle on the ''HotNewHipHop'' Freestyle Session.<ref name="HNHHFree">{{cite news | last=C| first=Noah| title =NoCap Finds His Pocket in His HNHH Freestyle Session | work =HotNewHipHop| date =August 24, 2019 | url =https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/nocap-finds-his-pocket-in-his-hnhh-freestyle-session-new-song.1984270.html| accessdate =September 7, 2019}}</ref> He was also featured on the [[Mozzy]] song, "Sad Situations".<ref name="HypeMozzy">{{cite news | last=Montes| first=Patrick| title =Mozzy Recruits NoCap for Heartfelt New Single 'Sad Situations'| work =HypeBeast| date =August 28, 2019 | url =https://hypebeast.com/2019/8/mozzy-sad-situations-nocap-song-stream| accessdate =September 7, 2019}}</ref> In November 2019, NoCap released his fourth mixtape, ''The Hood Dictionary''. The album featured guest appearances from Quando Rondo, [[Lil Durk]], and [[Jagged Edge (American group)|Jagged Edge]]<ref name="HNHHDictionary">{{cite news | last=Zidel| first=Alex| title =NoCap's "The Hood Dictionary" Features Quando Rondo, Lil Durk & More| work=HotNewHipHop | date =20 November 2019 | url =https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/nocaps-the-hood-dictionary-features-quando-rondo-lil-durk-and-more-new-mixtape.119799.html| accessdate =2 December 2019}}</ref> and peaked at number 80 on the ''Billboard'' 200.<ref name="Bill200">{{cite news | title =Billboard 200| work=Billboard | date =30 November 2019 | url =https://www.billboard.com/charts/billboard-200| accessdate =3 December 2019}}</ref> On July 16, 2020, NoCap released his fifth mixtape, ''Steel Human''.<ref name="Steel">{{cite web|url=https://music.apple.com/us/album/steel-human/1523648309|title=Steel Human by NoCap on Apple Music|via=[[Apple Music]]|date=July 16, 2020|accessdate=July 16, 2020}}</ref>
