'''Belmont''' is an American [[easycore]] band from [[Chicago]], [[Illinois]].

==History==
After forming in high school in 2014, Belmont self-released their debut EP titled ''Vicissitude'' which was produced by Mat Kerekes of [[Citizen (band)|Citizen]] and his brother.<ref name="columbiachronicle">{{cite web|last1=EnYart|first1=Frank|title=Belmont hopes to carry on Chicago pop-punk tradition with new EP|url=http://www.columbiachronicle.com/arts_and_culture/article_8b7199ac-5e72-11e5-bf56-773e8a69c249.html|publisher=Columbia Chronicle|accessdate=7 October 2017}}</ref> In 2016, after solidifying their lineup, Belmont announced their signing to [[Mutant League Records]] and released the EP ''Between You & Me''.<ref name="newnoisemagazine">{{cite web|last1=Shrum|first1=Tony|title=Belmont Release ‘Between You & Me’ EP, Stream It Now|url=http://newnoisemagazine.com/belmont-stream-between-you-me-ep/|publisher=New Noise Magazine|accessdate=7 October 2017}}</ref><ref name="altpress">{{cite magazine|last1=Wellendorf|first1=Kiley|title=Mutant League Records signs Chicago pop punks Belmont|url=http://www.altpress.com/features/entry/mutant_league_records_signs_pop_punk_band_belmont|magazine=[[Alternative Press (magazine)|Alternative Press]]|accessdate=7 October 2017}}</ref> In 2017, Belmont released a music video for a new single ''Step Aside''.<ref name="altpress1">{{cite magazine|last1=Dickman|first1=Maggie|title=Pop-punkers Belmont have an epic house show in music video for new song “Step Aside”—watch|url=http://www.altpress.com/features/entry/belmont_step_aside_music_video|magazine=Alternative Press|accessdate=7 October 2017}}</ref> The band released 'Belmont' a self titled full-length album on August 17, 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/belmontband/|title=Belmont|website=www.facebook.com|language=en|access-date=2018-09-28}}</ref>
