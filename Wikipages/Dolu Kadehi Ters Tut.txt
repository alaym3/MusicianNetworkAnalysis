'''Dolu Kadehi Ters Tut''' ({{literally|Hold the Full Glass Upside Down}}) are a Turkish [[pop rock]] duo consisting of vocalist Uğurhan Özay and guitarist Mürsel Oğulcan Ava founded in 2014.<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.facebook.com/pg/DoluKadehiTersTut/about/?ref=page_internal | title = Dolu Kadehi Ters Tut - Hakkında | accessdate =  | date =  | work =  | publisher = Dolu Kadehi Ters Tut | url-status =live}}</ref> The first album ''Polonya’nın Başı Belada'' (2015) was a home-recorded album.<ref>{{cite web | last1 = Vural | first1 = Sinem | title = Yap ve yapma listesi | url = https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/yazarlar/sinem-vural/yap-ve-yapma-listesi-40630131 | publisher = Hürriyet | accessdate = 2 April 2020 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20171105012950/https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/yazarlar/sinem-vural/yap-ve-yapma-listesi-40630131 | archivedate = 5 November 2017}}</ref> Their first studio album ''Dünyanın En İyi Albümü'' was released in 2017. Their funk rock and alternative rock albums are inspired by the works of [[Red Hot Chili Peppers]] and [[Twenty One Pilots]]. Their self-produced 4 albums, 3 [[Extended play|EP]] and 12 singles have been released on digital platforms.

The group got its name from a verse in [[Omar Khayyam]]'s No. 27 [[Rubaʿi]]. Most of the duo's music videos, which are made in the [[mini-film]] format, have been directed by Mürsel Oğulcan Ava. The majority of their compositions were inspired by the works of [[Sabahattin Ali]] and Özdemir Asaf.<ref>{{cite web | last1 = Özcan | first1 = Sema | title = Alternatif Sesler: Dolu Kadehi Ters Tut | url = https://www.ntv.com.tr/sanat/alternatif-sesler-dolu-kadehi-ters-tut,O_F1mvc2QE-LQ8WpM41fIw | publisher = NTV | accessdate = 2 April 2020 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20190808141802/https://www.ntv.com.tr/sanat/alternatif-sesler-dolu-kadehi-ters-tut,O_F1mvc2QE-LQ8WpM41fIw | archivedate = 8 August 2019}}</ref>

== History ==
The group's original name was "Lavuks".<ref>{{cite web | last1 = Altun | first1 = Taha | title = Ciddiyetsiz biçimde daha ciddi: Dolu Kadehi Ters Tut | url = https://www.dergy.com/roportaj-ciddiyetsiz-bicimde-daha-ciddi-dolu-kadehi-ters-tut/ | publisher = der.gy | accessdate = 3 May 2020 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20200515182306/https://www.dergy.com/roportaj-ciddiyetsiz-bicimde-daha-ciddi-dolu-kadehi-ters-tut/ | archivedate = 15 May 2020}}</ref> After releasing their self-recorded acoustic album ''Polonya’nın Başı Belada'', they published their first music video "Aldattın Mı" on 4 February 2016, followed by a single, titled "#22", on 14 November 2016. Also in 2016, the due began performing acoustic songs as a band in various places. Their second single, "Unut Ama Sev", was released in early 2017. Their third single, "Anamız Babamız Yok Deriz", addressed issues such as [[homophobia]] and social pressures.<ref>{{cite web | title = Ya bizi kapının ardında sevişirken yakalarlarsa? | url = https://www.kaosgl.org/haber/ya-bizi-kapinin-ardinda-sevisirken-yakalarlarsa | publisher = Kaos GL | accessdate = 3 May 2020 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20200515182320/https://www.kaosgl.org/haber/ya-bizi-kapinin-ardinda-sevisirken-yakalarlarsa | archivedate = 15 May 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = https://geekyapar.com/metro/sikismisligimizin-sarkisi-nefretimizin-klibi-anamiz-babamiz-yok-deriz/ | title = SIKIŞMIŞLIĞIMIZIN Şarkısı, NEFRETİMİZİN Klibi: ANAMIZ BABAMIZ YOK DERİZ | accessdate = 3 May 2020 | date =  | work =  | publisher = Geekyapar! | last1 = Erdoğan | first1 = Yiğitcan | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20200515182327/https://geekyapar.com/metro/sikismisligimizin-sarkisi-nefretimizin-klibi-anamiz-babamiz-yok-deriz/ | archivedate = 15 May 2020}}</ref> It was followed by another single, titled "Evim". They attracted more attention by the song "Belki" and is music video, which featured [[Can Ozan]] and [[Deniz Tekin]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://caz.iksv.org/tr/yirmibesinci-istanbul-caz-festivali-2018/dolu-kadehi-ters-tut|title=DOLU KADEHİ TERS TUT|accessdate=2 April 2020|archivedate=1 July 2018|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180701145850/https://caz.iksv.org/tr/yirmibesinci-istanbul-caz-festivali-2018/dolu-kadehi-ters-tut|publisher=İKSV}}</ref> Their first studio album ''Dünyanın En İyi Albümü'', consisting of 11 songs, was released on 3 November 2017. The songs "Tehlikeli İkilem" and "Çöpçatan" from this album were turned into music videos.<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.theravemag.com/dolu-kadehi-ters-tutun-ilk-studyo-albumu-yayinlandi/ | title = Dolu Kadehi Ters Tut’un İlk Stüdyo Albümü Yayınlandı! | accessdate = 3 May 2020 | publisher = The Rave Mag | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20171107163330/http://www.theravemag.com/dolu-kadehi-ters-tutun-ilk-studyo-albumu-yayinlandi/ | archivedate = 7 November 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = https://medium.com/bomontiada/bir-baba-indie-dolu-kadehi-ters-tut-bir-dinletiden-fazlas%C4%B1-7f2f35468f46 | title = Bir Baba Indie — Dolu Kadehi Ters Tut: "bir dinletiden fazlası" | accessdate = 3 May 2020 | publisher = Bir Baba İndie | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20200515182335/https://medium.com/bomontiada/bir-baba-indie-dolu-kadehi-ters-tut-bir-dinletiden-fazlas%C4%B1-7f2f35468f46 | archivedate = 15 May 2020}}</ref>''

[[File:Dolu Kadehi Ters Tut.jpg|thumb|Mürsel Oğulcan Ava and Uğurhan Özay during a photo shoot]]
In 2018, the duo collaborated with [[Can Ozan]] on the song "Aklım Hep Firarda" which was released in February 2018.<ref>{{cite web | title = Dolu Kadehi Ters Tut ve Canozan’dan Yeni Şarkı: "Aklım Hep Firarda" | url = https://www.birbabaindie.com/dktt-ve-canozan-yeni-sarki-aklim-hep-firarda/ | publisher = Bir Baba İndie | accessdate = 3 May 2020 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20180308150316/https://www.birbabaindie.com/dktt-ve-canozan-yeni-sarki-aklim-hep-firarda/ | archivedate = 8 March 2018}}</ref> On 15 March 2018, the group had its first appearance on the [[mainstream media]] by appearing on ''Akustikhane'' program. In the same month, together with Dilan Balkay, they released the song "Siz Bana Aldırmayın". Following its release, Dilan Balkay started accompanying the group as trumpeter on their live stage performances. The due received the "Best Debut by a Group" award at the Mustafa Kemal Culture and Arts Awards, organized by [[Vefa High School]].<ref>{{cite web | title = Kemal Sunal ödülleri sahiplerini buldu | url = https://www.haberturk.com/kemal-sunal-odulleri-sahiplerini-buldu-1996450 | publisher = HaberTürk | accessdate = 3 May 2020 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20180603163110/https://www.haberturk.com/kemal-sunal-odulleri-sahiplerini-buldu-1996450 | archivedate = 3 June 2018}}</ref> In the same year, Yağız Nevzat İpek joined the group by playing drums during their live performances. For the cover of their next single, "Anlamı Yok", they used the ''[[Pale Blue Dot]]'' photograph taken by [[Voyager 1]].<ref>{{cite web | title = Dolu Kadehi Ters Tut gitarları bırakıyor, Auto-Tune’un tadını çıkarıyor! | url = https://www.birbabaindie.com/dolu-kadehi-ters-tut-gitarlari-birakiyor-auto-tuneun-tadini-cikariyor/ | publisher = Bir Baba İndie | accessdate = 3 May 2020 | url-status =live}}</ref> On 21 September 2018, their released their [[Extended play|EP]] ''Akustik Takılmacalar'', consisting of 7 songs.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.grandpera.com/performans/dolu-kadehi-ters-tut?date=26%20Ocak%20Cumartesi|title=Dolu Kadehi Ters Tut|accessdate=2 April 2020|archivedate=5 April 2020|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20200405172312/https://www.grandpera.com/performans/dolu-kadehi-ters-tut?date=26%20Ocak%20Cumartesi|publisher=Grand Pera}}</ref> Sedef Sebüktekin was the featured artist on the song "Sen ve Ben" from this EP. Soon Billur Battal joined the group as a backing vocalist for their live performances.

On 5 April 2019, their next studio album, ''Karanlık'', was released.<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/yazarlar/tolga-akyildiz/cesur-ve-guzel-41244069 | title = Cesur ve güzel | accessdate =  | date = 15 June 2019 | work = Tolga Akyıldız | publisher = Hürriyet | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20190808112722/https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/yazarlar/tolga-akyildiz/cesur-ve-guzel-41244069 | archivedate = 8 August 2019}}</ref> Sedef Sebüktekin was again a featured artist in this worked and voiced the song "Gitme" with the due.<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.birbabaindie.com/dolu-kadehi-ters-tuttan-album-oncesi-son-single/ | title = Dolu Kadehi Ters Tut’tan albüm öncesi son single! | accessdate = 3 May 2020 | publisher = Bir Baba İndie | url-status =live}}</ref> The album was described by the due as "Our first album where we could really do what we wanted to do". Mixing of the pieces was done by Barış Ergün, while mastering was done by [[Vlado Meller]], who had previously worked on [[Red Hot Chili Peppers]]'s albums ''[[Californication (album)|Californication]]'', ''[[By the Way]]'' and ''[[Stadium Arcadium]]''.<ref>{{cite web | title = Dolu Kadehi Ters Tut’un Bizi Gizemli Bir Yolculuğa Sürüklediği Albüm: Karanlık | url = https://www.wannart.com/dolu-kadehi-ters-tutun-bizi-gizemli-bir-yolculuga-surukledigi-album-karanlik/ | publisher = Wannart | accessdate = 3 May 2020 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20190606053653/https://www.wannart.com/dolu-kadehi-ters-tutun-bizi-gizemli-bir-yolculuga-surukledigi-album-karanlik/ | archivedate = 6 June 2019}}</ref> In the same year, Bahadır Kartal joined the group on base and electric guitar. The group received the Best Alternative Music Group of the Year award at the Face to Face event organized by [[Kocaeli University]] in the same year.<ref>{{cite web | last1 = Güzel | first1 = Duygu | title = KOÜ’de yıldızlar geçidi! Ünlü sanatçılar ödül aldı! - Kocaeli Haberleri | url = https://www.kocaelibarisgazetesi.com/guncel/koude-yildizlar-gecidi-unlu-sanatcilar-odul-aldi-2-h108905.html | publisher = Kocaeli Barış Newspaper | language = tr | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20200515182357/https://www.kocaelibarisgazetesi.com/guncel/koude-yildizlar-gecidi-unlu-sanatcilar-odul-aldi-2-h108905.html | archivedate = 15 May 2020}}</ref>

After releasing another single, "Kaçar Gider", on 28 February 2020, the band published their first cover song "Duvar" as a part of [[Ezginin Günlüğü]]'s ''40 Yıllık Şarkılar'' tribute album.<ref>{{cite web | title = 40 yıllık şarkılara yeni yorumlar | url = http://www.cumhuriyet.com.tr/haber/40-yillik-sarkilara-yeni-yorumlar-1733014 | publisher = Cumhuriyet | accessdate = 3 May 2020 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20200418061539/http://www.cumhuriyet.com.tr/haber/40-yillik-sarkilara-yeni-yorumlar-1733014 | archivedate = 18 April 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.dergy.com/dolu-kadehi-ters-tuttan-duvar-yayinlandi/ | title = Dolu Kadehi Ters Tut’tan "Duvar" yayınlandı | accessdate =  | date =  | work =  | publisher = Dergy | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20200515182407/https://www.dergy.com/dolu-kadehi-ters-tuttan-duvar-yayinlandi/ | archivedate = 15 May 2020}}</ref> Their tenth single, "Neyin Nesi", was released on 1 May 2020.<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.dergy.com/dolu-kadehi-ters-tut-evde-dansa-davet-ediyor/ | title = Dolu Kadehi Ters Tut evde dansa davet ediyor | accessdate =  | date =  | work =  | publisher = Dergy | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20200515182421/https://www.dergy.com/dolu-kadehi-ters-tut-evde-dansa-davet-ediyor/ | archivedate = 15 May 2020}}</ref>
