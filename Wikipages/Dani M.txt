[[File:DaniM13.jpg|thumb|Daniel Monserrat]]

'''Daniel Monserrat''', better known by his stage name '''Dani M''' (born in [[Gävle]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ng.se/artiklar/valkommen-till-roda-linjen|title=Välkommen till röda linjen|first1=Daniel Adams-Ray- Innan vi suddas|last1=ut|first2=Malcolm B.-Tid och|last2=rum|first3=Drake-Nothing Was The|last3=Same|website=NÖJESGUIDEN|accessdate=22 October 2021}}</ref> on 6 January 1990), is a [[rapper]], [[reggae]] and [[hip hop music|hip hop]] solo artist from [[Uppsala]], [[Sweden]]. Monserrat first gained recognition after being a featured singer in hit songs in 2012-2013 by [[Kartellen]], [[Syster Sol]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://sverigesradio.se/artikel/5633373|title=Syster Sol dissekerar sin stora hit "Kärleksrevolt"!|date=31 August 2013|accessdate=22 October 2021|via=sverigesradio.se}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.barometern.se/kultur-noje/superproffsigsyrra-nar-inte-riktigt-hjartat/|title=Superproffsigsyrra når inte riktigt hjärtat|first=Rasmus|last=Jeppsson|date=17 May 2015|website=Barometern|accessdate=22 October 2021}}</ref> and [[Labyrint (band)|Labyrint]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://pdf.direktpress.se/flashpublisher/magazine/8123/page/25|title=Mitt i, Direktpress|website=pdf.direktpress.se|accessdate=22 October 2021}}</ref> His own solo career thereafter gained popular acclaim alongside [[Syster Sol]], both singers in the reggae & hiphop scenes, and he quickly received top reviews among Swedish newspapers' music critics and music magazines.

He was born to a [[Finland|Finnish]] mother and a [[Venezuela]]n father and is the brother of Swedish rapper [[Moncho]].<ref>[http://sverigesradio.se/sida/artikel.aspx?programid=1319&artikel=5519490 Dani M kiersi jo Suomea ennen julkisuutta Ruotsissa!] Sveriges Radio {{in lang|fi}}</ref>

On several occasions, Montserrat has been criticised in Swedish media for making repeated [[antisemitism|antisemitic]] and [[conspiracy theory|conspiratory]] remarks.<ref>[http://www.dn.se/nyheter/sverige/svensk-rappare-bakom-antisemitiska-konspirationsteorier Svensk rappare bakom antisemitiska konspirationsteorier] ''[[Svenska Dagbladet]]''</ref><ref>[http://www.svd.se/kultur/kulturdebatt/gammalt-gift-i-livsfarlig-ny-kombination_4340595.svd Gammalt gift i livsfarlig kombination] ''Svenska Dagbladet''</ref> Montserrat himself has repeatedly denied this and responded to all media with a statement that he is not a racist or anti-semite,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.resume.se/kommunikation/tv/dani-m-s-svar-pa-kritiken-jag-ar-inte-antisemit/|title=Dani M:s svar på kritiken: ”Jag är inte antisemit”|website=www.resume.se|accessdate=22 October 2021}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.svt.se/kultur/medier/jag-ar-inte-antisemit|title=”Jag är inte antisemit”|first=S. V. T.|last=Nyheter|date=25 September 2015|website=SVT Nyheter|accessdate=22 October 2021}}</ref><ref>https://expo.se/2012/11/dani-m-jag-%C3%A4r-inte-rasist</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.dn.se/kultur-noje/dani-m-bryter-tystnaden-jag-ar-inte-antisemit/|title=Dani M bryter tystnaden: Jag är inte antisemit|date=25 September 2015|accessdate=22 October 2021|via=Dagens Nyheter}}</ref> and that he himself is an immigrant in Sweden who "loves people from all the world, and has no racism towards jews". He also wrote on his official Facebook page that he was sorry for having "spoken clumsy".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.kingsizemag.se/musik/dani-m-forsvarar-sig-mot-expo-artikel-om-antisemitism/|title=Dani M försvarar sig mot Expo-artikel om antisemitism|website=Kingsizemag.se &#124; Svensk rap hiphop musik dans kläder artister tv|accessdate=22 October 2021}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.expressen.se/noje/dani-m-svarar-pa-den-harda-kritiken-sluta/|title=Dani M svarar på den hårda kritiken: "Sluta"|website=Expressen|accessdate=22 October 2021}}</ref>
