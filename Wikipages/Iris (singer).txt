'''Laura van den Bruel''', known as '''Iris''' or '''Airis''' (born 19 January 1995), is a [[Belgian]] singer.<ref name="grenzecho1">{{cite web|url=http://www.grenzecho.net/ArtikelLoad.aspx?aid=4f71747b-71a5-4ecb-b4a4-a3777f9d1293 |title=Iris fährt für Belgien nach Baku &#124; GRENZECHO.net - Ostbelgien grenzenlos |publisher=Grenzecho.net |date=2011-11-19 |access-date=2012-01-25}}</ref>

==Eurovision==
On 18 November 2011, it was announced by [[Vlaamse Radio- en Televisieomroeporganisatie]] (VRT),<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.standaard.be/artikel/detail.aspx?artikelid=2B3II1ER |title=VRT stuurt 16-jarige Iris naar Azerbeidzjan |newspaper=De Standaard |date=2011-11-19 |access-date=2012-01-25}}</ref> that Iris would represent [[Belgium in the Eurovision Song Contest 2012|Belgium]] in the [[Eurovision Song Contest 2012]].<ref name="grenzecho1" /><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.eurovision.tv/page/news?id=40403&_t=iris_to_represent_belgium_in_baku|title=Iris to represent Belgium in Baku|last=Storvik-Green|first=Simon|publisher=Eurovision.tv|access-date=18 November 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.esctoday.com/news/read/17837|title=Belgium: Iris to represent Belgium in Baku|last=Jiandani|first=Sanjay|publisher=Esctoday.com|access-date=18 November 2011}}</ref> She performed the song "[[Would You? (Iris song)|Would You?]]", finished second last in the first semi-final, and did not make the Eurovision final. In 2014, she returned to the contest as a member of the Belgian jury.
