'''Shironamhin''' ({{lang-bn|শিরোনামহীন}} ''Śirōnāmahīn'' English: ''Untitled'') is a Bengali [[Rock music|rock]] band formed in [[Dhaka]] in 1996. Following as a rock group emerging from Dhaka's [[underground music]] in the late 1990s, they were distinguished for their extended [[psychedelic rock|psychedelic]] and [[progressive rock|progressive]] compositions, multi-instrumental experimentation, and philosophical lyrics and became one of the leading band of the Bengali progressive rock genre.

Shironamhin were founded by students [[Ziaur Rahman (musician)|Ziaur Rahman]] (guitar), Jewel (guitar) and Bulbul Hasan (vocals). Vocalist [[Tanzir Tuhin]] joined in 2002 as a fourth member of the band. Zia became the primary lyricist and thematic leader, devising the concepts behind the albums ''[[Jahaji]]'' (2004), ''[[Icche Ghuri]]'' (2006), ''[[Bondho Janala]]'' (2009) and ''[[Shironamhin Rabindranath]]'' (2010). The last Shironamhin studio album, ''[[Shironamhin Shironamhin]]'' (2013), was based on unreleased material from the long term musical journey of the group. Their song "[[Hashimukh]]" from their debut album became well recognised after its release. The band also composed several [[Shironamhin discography#Film scores|film scores]].

Following personal tensions, Tuhin left Shironamhin in 2017. Sheikh Ishtiaque later joined the band as a new vocalist. Later they produced six non-album singles "Jadukor" (2017), "Boheimian" (2018), "Barud Shomudro" (2018), "Ei Obelay" (2019), "Cafeteria Periye" (2020) and "Kashfuler Shohor Dekha" (2021).

They have presented [[orchestration]] with [[rock music|rock]] gesture in [[Music of Bengal|Bengali music]]. They have infused classical instruments such as [[sarod]], [[cello]], [[flute]], [[esraj]], [[violin]] and many more. In 2010, a documentary on Shironamhin was published. On 2008, the band became a member of the [[Bangladesh Musical Bands Association]] (BAMBA).

[[Ziaur Rahman (musician)|Ziaur Rahman]] was acquainted with Jewel while studying architecture at [[Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology]]. ====''Jahaji'' (2004)====
{{main|Jahaji}}

''Jahaji'' was based on urban lifestyle and struggling. The title track of the album, itself speaks so. Shironamhin, tried to portray the usual daily life and elements from middle class career seeking young sailor of a [[Bronx]]. The cultural interest, the gossip in a crowded cityscape, the life of mono busy traffic etc. were conveyed with metaphor and events by this album. Jahajee was the only album from the band Shironamhin that was refused and rejected by every known record label company of [[Dhaka]]. "[[Hashimukh|হাসিমুখ (Smiling)]]" (2004), from the album ''[[Jahajee|জাহাজী (Navigator)]]'' was a widely used song in the countries television advertisement.

===2005–2017: Transition and success===
====''Icche Ghuri'' (2006)====
{{main|Icche Ghuri}}
Ichchhe Ghuri was second album of Shironamhin released from the record label company [[G-Series (record label)|G-Series]] in 2006.<ref name="thedailystar.net">{{cite news |title=Shironamhin launches its third album Bondho Janala |url=http://www.thedailystar.net/news-detail-75863 |newspaper=The Daily Star |date=15 February 2009 |access-date=23 March 2018 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180324041634/http://www.thedailystar.net/news-detail-75863 |archive-date=24 March 2018 |url-status=live }}</ref> They tried to do some experiments, draw some maturity in compositions, suited compositions for Live performances and fix in a style for Shironamhin's own. Shironamhin is keen on lyrics from nature. They tried to convey urban crisis in this album in which they did not to draw any comment or did not even try to draw any suggestion. Shironamhin just wanted the listeners to get the message with their own understanding and draw the images or characters with own personal fantasy. They mostly worked on certain topic or events on short-time span. They loved to talk about urban life, draw the urban texture. ''Cafeteria'' was a story of one second judging from vivid angles. It is based on a moment someone entering into his campus cafeteria and feel two silent eyes among so many noise and fumes around from the cafe gossiping. Shironamhin tried to make the listeners recall their own cafeteria in their campus life.

====''Bondho Janala'' (2009)====
{{Main|Bondho Janala}}
''Bondho Janala'' was a bit challenging for Shironamhin as they were very much used to with [[sarod]] sound [[Musical composition|compositions]]. In the album they had to explore many more [[acoustic instruments]] like [[esraj]], silver [[flute]], trumpet as the album was enriched with vivid orchestration. Lyrically the band Shironamhin tried to work on their already established genre of urban life talks with some bigger aspects and along with historical events or movements. It was released by the record label company [[G-Series (record label)|G-Series]] in 2009.<ref name="thedailystar.net"/>

====''Shironamhin Rabindranath'' (2010)====
{{Main|Shironamhin Rabindranath}}
{{quote box|quote="We believe Rabindranath himself was a cultural institute for us."|source=—Shironamhin |width=20% |align=right |style=padding:8px;}}

In 2010, Diat Khan (guitar) joined the band instead of Tushar.<ref>{{harvnb|Shironamhin}}: Diat (guitar) joined in 2010. (Primary source)।</ref>{{r|কাক-দুই দশক}} The fourth album ''[[Shironamhin Rabindranath]]'' was a risky task for the band, which is basically a collection of [[Rabindra Sangeet]].{{r|Tareque}} The album was released in 2010 on the occasion of the 150th death anniversary of Bengali [[polymath]] [[Rabindranath Tagore]].{{r|Mahmud}} This was the first Rabindra Sangeet album of any band in Bangladesh.{{r|হেঁটে}} Shironamhin considers Tagore as an institution of [[Bengali culture]], as a [[Bengalis|Bengali]], anyone who is at least sensitive to culture should know Rabindranath. Tuhin learned Rabindra Sangeet while studying at [[Bulbul Lalitakala Academy]].{{r|Mahmud-Jamil}} In the album, they pay homage to Tagore. Shironamhin vocalist Tuhin said that Rabindra Sangeet has a combination of [[Vaishnavism|Vaishnava]] and [[Upanishads|Upanishad]] ideology as well as [[Indian classical music]], Bengali [[folk music]] and [[Western classical music|western music]] in terms of melody.{{r|রবীন্দ্রসঙ্গীত নিয়ে}} Zia thinks that many Shironamhin fans, especially teenagers, are not familiar with Rabindra Sangeet. As a result, it is an attempt to increase the interest of the young audience towards the Bengali poet Tagore.{{r|Mahmud}}

Nine time-honoured songs of Rabindranath have been added to the album.{{r|রবীন্দ্রনাথ}} With the help of Swarabitan published in 64 volumes, the main words and melody of Rabindra Swaralipi have been kept correct.{{r|রবীন্দ্রসঙ্গীত নিয়ে}} The album also features classical, [[baul]], folk, kirtan, sentimental and even western music.{{r|রবীন্দ্রসঙ্গীত নিয়ে}} They used about 39 local and foreign musical instruments in the composition of the album.{{r|রবীন্দ্রসঙ্গীত নিয়ে}} For this album they use Brazilian rainmaker, Egyptian darbuka, Irish tin whistle and Scottish Bodhran. On the cover of the album, members of the group around Rabindranath appear as disciples.{{r|Mahmud}}{{r|রবীন্দ্রনাথ}} Mainly it was a performance of classical music in the rock genre.{{r|Adnin-Influence}} The album was a commercial success after its release.{{r|Khan}}

====''Shironamhin Shironamhin'' (2013)====
{{Main|Shironamhin Shironamhin}}


After the Rabindranath project in 2010, the band started working on the songs of the new album.{{r|এবার সবার}} In 2011, Rajib was replaced by Russel Kabir (keyboard).{{r|কাক-দুই দশক}}<ref>{{harvnb|Shironamhin}}: Russel (keyboard) joined in 2011. (Primary source)</ref> In 2013, Shironamhin released their first self-titled<ref>{{harvnb|Shironamhin}}: After 17 years of a musical journey, Shironamhin could dare to release their self-titled album. (Primary source)</ref> and their fifth studio album ''[[Shironamhin Shironamhin]]'', after an 18-year musical tour. This album is a collection of 10 tracks selected from 25 tracks. The album was recorded at Incarrson Music of Banglamotor, Dhaka.{{r|আজ আসছে}} The song "Abar Hashimukh" from the album is a sequel of their previous song "[[Hashimukh]]".{{r|Adnin}} A music video of the song "Abar Hashimukh" directed by Tanim Rahman Anshu, starring 2007 [[Miss Bangladesh]] beauty pageant titleholder [[Jannatul Ferdoush Peya]].{{r|নামহীন}}{{r|Star Women}} The album strives to compile their confessions, demands, rage, hopes and anticipation to invite new light through the old windows of the untitled past. From the lyrical point of view, the album is more specific on topic, searching the positive sides of negative living. Musically, Shironamhin tried western classic orchestration based on [[violin]], [[cello]], contrabass section fused with rock guitar, bass and drums in public classical orchestration.

For the first time, the band released a collector's version of one of their albums. CDs have been added to the edition, and bound books have been added to the cloth covers. Where the lyric of the song, the relevant information including the background of the lyric has been compiled. The album recalled all the members and persons who worked for the band once and forever, expressed their gratitude to those who carried Shironamhin to their journey and dedicated their songs to the listeners whom they comfortable to call friends.{{r|অনেক}} Zia himself planned the cover design. On 14 April 2014, a collectors' edition of the album was released by [[Laser Vision]].{{r|এবার সবার}}

===From 2014 and recent years===
====Tuhin' departure and legal battles====
[[File:Tanzir Tuhin (2014).jpg|upright=.80|thumb|[[Tanzir Tuhin]] in 2014 perform as Shironamhin]]

In 2017, lead vocalist [[Tanzir Tuhin]] became embroiled in controversy,{{r|Ahmed}}{{r|reveals}} and left the band on 7 October.{{r|নতুন}} "I'm Tanzir Tuhin leaving Shironamhin for personal reasons, but not the song", Tuhin said on social media [[Facebook]].{{r|Masud}}{{r|এই তুহিনকে}} In 2017, Sheikh Ishtiaque joined as the new vocalist.{{r|Tareque}}{{r|নতুন}} At the moment Tuhin left the band, vocal went through the process of searching for the title through multiple headless auditions. The band selected Ishtiaque by recording their title "Jadukor" with two different vocals.{{r|Saiful}}

In 2017, Tuhin formed a new band named [[Avash]]. According to the 2019 Copyright Office, the founding member of the group, Zia, was the lyricist and composer of Shironamhin' songs.{{r|কাক-তুহিন}} In that case, there is a legal obligation to perform the songs without permission.{{r|সময়}}{{r|কাক-তুহিন}} In August of that year, Tuhin ignored the Copyright Office's ruling. Shironamhin filed a complaint in court in August. The court legally barred Tuhin and his band Avash from performing Shironamhin' songs.{{r|সময়}}{{r|কাক-তুহিন}} Avash later appealed against the verdict. On 21 October 2019, the [[High Court Division|High Court]] stayed the ban on 49 songs of the band for six months.{{r|সময়}}{{r|কাক-তুহিন}} As a result, Tuhin and his new band Avash were able to perform songs by 2020.{{r|সময়}}{{r|কাক-তুহিন}} Later, in the light of the same law, the band re-appealed to the High Court and on 20 January 2020, the band won the case on their behalf.{{r|কাক-তুহিন}}{{r|ট্রিবিউন-শিরোনামহীন}}

====Further performances and rereleases====
After Ishtiaque replaced Tuhin as the lead vocalist, the band five non-album singles.{{r|একক}} All the songs are written by Zia. The song "Jadukor" was released on 7 December 2017, which was composed by Diat Khan.{{r|নতুন}} This is the first song for the group sung by Ishtiaque.{{r|'জাদুকর' নিয়ে}} The music video of the song has been directed by [[Ashraf Shishir]].{{r|জাদুকর}} The song received mixed reactions after its release.{{r|নতুন কণ্ঠে}} The song "Bohemian" was released on 7 January 2016, and was composed by Shafin and the second title by Ishtiaque.{{r|বোহিমিয়ান}} Shishir has directed the music video as well.{{r|বোহেমিয়ান}} On 7 March of the same year, Shafin and Zia's joint tune "Barud Shomudro" was released.{{r|আহমেদ-বারুদ}} The song "Ei Abelaya" was released on 30 May 2019 to the tune of Shafin.{{r|Obelai}} The music video for the song was made by Mir Shariful Karim Shravan, who previously made another music video for the band's song "Bondho Janala".{{r|গানের}}

In 2020, the band celebrated their two eras. On 14 April 2020, Shironamhin released their latest song, "Cafeteria Periye".{{r|Hasan}} It is a sequel to the song "Cafeteria" from their previous album "[[Icche Ghuri]]" (2006). Zia composed his own lyrics and also played the [[cello]] in the song. Shafin also played [[sarod]].{{r|ক্যাফেটেরিয়া-১৪}} Like other Shironamhin songs, the music video was made by Shishir Ahmed.{{r|ভিডিও}} Shishir, the guitarist of the rock band [[Aurthohin]], also performed guitar as a guest artist in the music video.{{r|Cafeteria}} Their latest music video titled "Kashfuler Shohor Dekha" released on 7 May 2021, directed by Naimul Banin.{{r|DS-Ullah}} Composer and music director [[Bappa Mazumder]] and actress [[Quazi Nawshaba Ahmed]] are featured in special roles in the video.{{r|DS-Ullah}} "Kashfuler Shohor Dekha" is a sequel of "Shon Shon Jodio Kashbon" released on their album ''Shironamhin Shironamhin'' in 2013.

==Musicianship==
[[File:Diat Khan and Ziaur Rahman Zia.jpg|thumb|Diat Khan and Ziaur Rahman Zia perform at Joy Bangla Concert in 2018]]

Regarded as one of the pioneers of early progressive and psychedelic music groups in Bangladesh, Shironamhin began their musical career through the [[underground music]] in [[Dhaka]]. As a multi-instrumental group, the combination of different instruments in music has made them unique. In an interview with the ''[[BBC]]'' in 2011, Tuhin said that Shironamhin's songs have a sense of life and philosophy rather than love or separation.{{r|বিবিসি-তুহিন}} From the very beginning, they have been singing outside the traditional Bengali music genre.{{r|বিবিসি-তুহিন}}

===Film scores===
In 2015, Shironamhin composed and performed a title-song "Podmo Patar Jol" from the film ''[[Podmo Patar Jol]]'' directed by Tanmay Tansen. On 26 April 2019, the song "E Raat E" was released with lyrics and melody by Zia for the film ''[[Amra Ekta Cinema Banabo]]'' directed by [[Ashraf Shishir]].{{r|Ferdous}}

===Live performances===
Shironamhin is known for its campus-based concerts to live performances. In 2014, Shironamhin attended the [[Sri Lanka]]'s a most famous musical festival titled ''Galle Music Festival'' where they enthralled the audiences.{{r|দেশের বাইরে}} There were performances by audiences and bands from various countries, including Norway, Brazil, and India.{{r|হুসাইন}}{{r|Lanka}}{{r|Third season}} In November 2016, they performed at the   [[University of Malaya]].{{r|গান শোনাতে}}

==Awards and achievements==
Shironamhin is one of the most commercially successful and influential rock bands in Bangladesh. On 1 August 2008, Shironamhin obtained the membership of [[Bangladesh Music Bands Association]] (BAMBA).{{r|নূর}}

On 13 April 2014, the band became the first Bengali band to launch their official [[Android application]] titled "Shironamhin".
