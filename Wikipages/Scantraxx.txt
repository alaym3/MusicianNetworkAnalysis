'''Scantraxx''' is a [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[record label]] founded by [[Dov Elkabas]] in 2002, specializing in [[Hardstyle]] music.

== S.W.A.T ==
Between November 2007 and April 2008, the label organized a number of shows under the name of ''S.W.A.T'' (Scantraxx World Artist Tour) in [[Europe]] and [[Australia]]. Those events were repeated in 2009 and 2010, but only in Europe.<ref>{{Webarchiv|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110816080509/https://www.scantraxx.com/scantraxx/s.w.a.t.-tour.php|wayback=20110816080509|title=„Scantraxx S.W.A.T. 2009“|archiv-bot=2019-05-12 08:17:28 InternetArchiveBot}}</ref><ref>{{Webarchiv|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110224020202/http://scantraxx.com/scantraxx/s.w.a.t.-2010.php|wayback=20110224020202|title=„Scantraxx S.W.A.T. 2010“|archiv-bot=2019-05-12 08:17:28 InternetArchiveBot}}</ref>
