'''Alessandro Aleotti''' ({{IPA-it|alesˈsandro aleˈɔtti}}; born 5 August 1972), known as '''J-Ax''' ({{IPA-it|ˌdʒɛiˈaks|lang}}), is an Italian singer, formerly in the rap group [[Articolo 31]] with his DJ Vito Luca Perrini (DJ Jad).<ref name=allmusic>{{cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/j-ax-mn0002080046/biography|title=J-Ax – Biography & History|publisher=AllMusic|access-date=11 May 2018|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170912143312/http://www.allmusic.com/artist/j-ax-mn0002080046/biography|archive-date=12 September 2017}}</ref> He is the brother of [[Weedo]] (also known as Grido from boyband [[Gemelli DiVersi]]). On 6 May 2010, he created the band Due di Picche ('Two of Spades') with [[Neffa]].

== Biography ==
From 1990 to 2005, J-Ax (abbreviation of Joker Alex) rose to prominence as part of the popular Italian rap group [[Articolo 31]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/articolo-31-mn0000933462/biography|title=Articolo 31 – Biography & History|publisher=AllMusic|access-date=11 May 2018|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180511140617/https://www.allmusic.com/artist/articolo-31-mn0000933462/biography|archive-date=11 May 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|last1=Fabio Bernabei|title=Hip Hop Italia: Il rap italiano dalla breakdance alle rapstar|date=21 May 2014|publisher=Imprimatur editore, 2014|isbn=978-8868301705}}</ref> which he founded with Vito Luca Perrini, AKA "DJ Jad".<ref name=allmusic/> In 2014 he became a judge on the show ''[[The Voice of Italy]]''.

===Soloist===
In 2006 J-Ax released his first solo album, {{lang|it|Di sana pianta}}. This album, which was very pop oriented, spawned no fewer than six singles: "S.N.O.B.", "{{lang|it|italic=unset|Tua mamma}}", "{{lang|it|italic=unset|Ti amo o ti ammazzo}}", "{{lang|it|italic=unset|Acqua nella scuola}}", "{{lang|it|italic=unset|Piccoli per sempre}}" and "{{lang|it|italic=unset|Escono i pazzi}}". The single "{{lang|it|italic=unset|Ti amo o ti ammazzo}}" was also translated and released in Spanish as "{{lang|es|italic=unset|Te amo o te mato}}". This album continued themes used by Articolo 31, such as knowing love ("{{lang|it|italic=unset|Ti amo o ti ammazzo}}"), criticising society, ("S.N.O.B.", "{{lang|it|italic=unset|Tua mamma}}") and the protection of immigrants ("{{lang|it|italic=unset|Escono i pazzi}}", "{{lang|it|italic=unset|Fumo ancora}}").

In 2007 he teamed up with The Styles to release the single "{{lang|it|italic=unset|Più Stile}}", which won at the [[MTV Europe Music Awards]] for "Best Italian Act".

In the same year, he released material with some of "Spaghetti Funk", a crew which he created at the same time as [[Articolo 31]] in the 90s with Gemelli DiVersi, Space One, Raptuz TDK (a writer) and a lot of other singers that now aren't in the crew.

Later, on the album {{lang|it|Di sana pianta}} he released the song "S.N.O.B. reloaded" with [[Space One]], [[Gué Pequeno]], [[Jake La Furia]], [[Marracash]], Don Joe and Fabio B.
In 2007, he also participated in ''MTV Day'' with DJ Jad and reformed their old group for a day.

J-Ax did not release an album in 2007, although he had many projects on, which continued into 2008. He sang on "{{lang|it|italic=unset|Amici un cazzo}}" by Space One with [[Gemelli Diversi]], "{{lang|it|italic=unset|Come noi}}" by Pino Scotto and Fattore wow with Marracash and Gue Pequeno.
He also wrote three songs in that year: "{{lang|it|italic=unset|Uno di noi}}", a thank you to his fans; "{{lang|it|italic=unset|Limonare al multisala}}", for the soundtrack of the film {{lang|it|Ti stramo}}; and "{{lang|it|italic=unset|I vecchietti fanno oh}}", the first single of his next album.

In 2011, J-Ax appeared on the remix of [[Kasabian]]'s single "[[Man of Simple Pleasures]]".

===2009===
[[File:J-Ax 2009.jpg|thumb|200px|J-Ax in 2009]]
At the beginning of 2009 J-Ax launched a project: to release two CDs in the year. The first, ''Rap N' Roll'' was released at the end of January. As well as "{{lang|it|italic=unset|I vecchietti fanno oh}}", he released 3 singles: "{{lang|it|italic=unset|Aumentaci le dosi}}", "{{lang|it|italic=unset|3 paperelle}}" and "Rap N' Roll". Artists who worked with him on the album include Space One, DJ Zak, Gué Pequeno, Irene Viboras, Fabio B and Guido Style, with whom he had worked since "{{lang|it|italic=unset|Più Stile}}".

In the summer of 2009 he released his third CD as a solo artist, ''Deca dance''. J-Ax paid homage to house music with this album with the single "Deca Dance" as well as more traditional singles with "{{lang|it|italic=unset|Come un sasso}}" and "{{lang|it|italic=unset|Vendesi idolo}}". He also shows his sense of humour on "I love paranoia" and "{{lang|it|italic=unset|Come un sasso}}". [[Pino Daniele]], Marracash, [[Grido (rapper)|Grido]] and [[Jovanotti]] took part in the album as well as another partnership with The Styles.
