'''Portrayal of Guilt''' is an American [[hardcore punk]] band formed in [[Austin, Texas]] in 2017. It was founded by guitarist/vocalist Matt King, bassist Blake Given, and drummer James Beveridge.

== History ==
Portrayal of Guilt formed when Guitarist and Vocalist Matt King and drummer James Beveridge – members of Austin hardcore band Illustrations<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://clrvynt.com/portrayal-of-guilt-stream/|title=Portrayal of Guilt Resuscitate Screamo's Dangerous Potential|last=Hill|first=John|website=CLRVYNT|language=en|access-date=2019-02-18}}</ref> – joined forces with bassist Blake Given of Austin [[screamo]] outfit Lyed.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.brooklynvegan.com/anderson-paak-portrayal-of-guilt-leikeli47-city-girls-good-bad-queen-reviews/|title=Five Notable Releases of the Week (11/16)|website=BrooklynVegan|language=en-US|access-date=2019-02-18}}</ref> The trio released their self-titled EP in May 2017, and toured extensively across the United States and Europe with Centuries,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://idioteq.com/florida-hardcore-band-centuries-announce-european-tour-portrayal-guilt/|title=CENTURIES Announces European Tour Supporting New Album, The Lights Of This Earth Are Blinding!|last=Kamiński|first=Karol|date=2017-12-05|website=IDIOTEQ.com|language=en-US|access-date=2019-05-05}}</ref> [[Majority Rule (band)|Majority Rule]],<ref name=":2" /> [[Planning for Burial]], [[Street Sects]], and Slow Fire Pistol. 

In March 2018, they released a split EP with industrial hardcore band Street Sects, who they had previously toured with, to critical acclaim.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.revolvermag.com/music/hear-street-sects-brutal-new-industrial-hardcore-split-portrayal-guilt|title=Hear Street Sects' Brutal New Industrial-Hardcore Split With Portrayal of Guilt|date=2018-02-16|website=Revolver|language=en|access-date=2019-02-18}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://idioteq.com/portrayal-guilt-streaming-new-split-street-sects/|title=PORTRAYAL OF GUILT streaming new split with STREET SECTS|last=Kamiński|first=Karol|date=2018-02-16|website=IDIOTEQ.com|language=en-US|access-date=2019-02-18}}</ref> In this same month, [[Deathwish Inc.]] reissued their self-titled 7".

In July 2018, just before embarking on a full U.S. tour with French post-hardcore stalwarts [[Birds in Row]],<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://deathwishinc.com/blogs/news/birds-in-row-announce-u-s-tour-w-portrayal-of-guilt|title=Birds In Row Announce U.S. Tour w/ Portrayal of Guilt|last=Inc|first=Deathwish|website=Deathwish Inc|language=en|access-date=2019-02-18}}</ref> the band released a single-sided 5" single containing the song "Chamber of Misery (Pt. 1)" as the beginning of a campaign to support first their full-length, studio album.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://noisey.vice.com/en_us/article/ywkd8x/portrayal-of-guilt-chamber-of-misery-part-i-five-inch-ep|title=Hear a Dark, Brooding Song from Portrayal of Guilt's Five-Inch Picture Disc|last=Ozzi|first=Dan|date=2018-07-17|website=Noisey|language=en-US|access-date=2019-02-18}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://noisey.vice.com/en_us/article/ne5dm7/the-spirit-of-screamo-is-alive-and-well|title=The Spirit of Screamo Is Alive and Well|last=Ozzi|first=Dan|date=2018-08-01|website=Noisey|language=en-US|access-date=2019-02-18}}</ref> The album's name, ''Let Pain Be Your Guide'', was announced on September 6th of the same year, along with the premiere of the single "A Burden."<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.loudersound.com/news/intense-hardcore-mob-portrayal-of-guilt-stream-new-song-a-burden|title=Intense hardcore mob Portrayal Of Guilt stream new song A Burden|last=Hammer|first=Luke Morton 2018-09-06T15:59:03Z Metal|website=Metal Hammer Magazine|language=en|access-date=2019-02-18}}</ref> On October 1st, they premiered the second single from the album, "Your War," which featured vocals from Dylan Walker of [[Full of Hell (band)|Full of Hell]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.revolvermag.com/music/hear-full-hell-singer-rage-portrayal-guilts-furious-new-song-your-war|title=Hear Full of Hell Singer Rage on Portrayal of Guilt's Furious New Song "Your War"|date=2018-10-01|website=Revolver|language=en|access-date=2019-02-18}}</ref> On October 24th they released the last single from the record, "Among Friends."<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.decibelmagazine.com/2018/10/24/track-premiere-portrayal-of-guilt-among-friends/|title=Track Premiere: Portrayal of Guilt — "Among Friends"|date=2018-10-24|website=Decibel Magazine|language=en-US|access-date=2019-02-18}}</ref> On November 12th, the full album was made available to listen to via [[NPR]]. The album features artwork by Chris Taylor of [[Pg. 99]] and [[Pygmy Lush]], as well as additional vocals from Maha Shami of NØ MAN and Matt Michel of Majority Rule.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=https://www.npr.org/2018/11/12/664762934/first-listen-portrayal-of-guilt-let-pain-be-your-guide|title=Don't Just Scream — Portrayal Of Guilt Wants To 'Let Pain Be Your Guide'|website=NPR.org|language=en|access-date=2019-02-18}}</ref> On November 18th, ''Let Pain Be Your Guide'' was released in full, largely to critical acclaim.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://daily.bandcamp.com/2018/11/30/portrayal-of-guilt-let-pain-be-your-guide-review/|title=Album of the Day: Portrayal of Guilt, “Let Pain Be Your Guide”|last=Editorial|date=2018-11-30|website=Bandcamp Daily|language=en|access-date=2019-02-18}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.stereogum.com/2022573/portrayal-of-guilt-let-pain-be-your-guide/franchises/album-of-the-week/|title=Album Of The Week: Portrayal Of Guilt 'Let Pain Be Your Guide'|date=2018-11-13|website=Stereogum|access-date=2019-02-18}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.treblezine.com/reviews/album-of-the-week-portrayal-of-guilt-let-pain-be-your-guide/|title=Album of the Week: Portrayal of Guilt – Let Pain Be Your Guide|last=Terich|first=Jeff|language=en-US|access-date=2019-02-18}}</ref>

In February 2019, Portrayal of Guilt was featured in [[The Washington Post]], along with contemporaries [[Vein (band)|Vein]] and [[Infant Island]], as delivering "new life" to [[screamo]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/entertainment/music/as-the-world-shrivels-in-front-of-our-eyes-all-we-can-do-is-scream/2019/02/13/4bc180be-2be3-11e9-b011-d8500644dc98_story.html|title=As the world shrivels in front of our eyes, all we can do is scream|last=Chu|first=Hau|date=2019-02-15|work=The Washington Post}}</ref> 

In 2019, Portrayal of Guilt continued touring in support of their full length, opening for [[Pg. 99]], [[Majority Rule (band)|Majority Rule]], The HIRS Collective,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.treblezine.com/pageninetynine-tour-announced-majority-rule-portrayal-of-guilt/|title=Pageninetynine announce tour with Majority Rule, Portrayal of Guilt|last=Terich|first=Jeff|language=en-US|access-date=2019-02-18}}</ref> and Wrong.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://gileadmedia.net/blogs/news/portrayal-of-guilt-will-be-touring-with-wrong-in-november|title=portrayal of guilt will be touring with Wrong in November|website=Gilead Media|language=en|access-date=2019-02-18}}</ref> They also headlined a full U.S. tour with support from Fluoride, NØ MAN, and Stay Asleep.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.brooklynvegan.com/portrayal-of-guilt-announce-tour-with-no-man-fluoride-stay-asleep/|title=Portrayal of Guilt announce tour with NØ MAN, Fluoride, Stay Asleep|website=BrooklynVegan|language=en-US|access-date=2019-02-18}}</ref> In February 2019 it was announced that the band would be opening for [[Skeletonwitch]] on a number of dates across the American South.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.brooklynvegan.com/skeletonwitch-touring-with-soft-kill-wiegedood-portrayal-of-guilt-more/|title=Skeletonwitch touring with Soft Kill, Wiegedood, Portrayal of Guilt, more|website=BrooklynVegan|language=en-US|access-date=2019-02-18}}</ref>

On January 29, 2021, Portrayal of Guilt released their second album ''We Are Always Alone'' on Closed Casket Activities, a [[Troy, New York]]<ref>{{Cite web|title=Closed Casket Activities|url=https://www.deathwishdirect.com/closedcasketactivities|access-date=2021-03-29|website=Deathwish Direct Distribution|language=en-US}}</ref> based metal label.

== Musical style ==
Portrayal of Guilt's musical style has been described as [[screamo]] since the band's inception as the band openly draws from older bands in the genre like Pg.99 and [[City of Caterpillar]]<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|url=https://www.revolvermag.com/music/portrayal-guilt-meet-rising-texas-act-leading-screamos-new-age-aggression|title=Portrayal of Guilt: Meet Rising Texas Act Leading Screamo's New Age of Aggression|date=2019-02-07|website=Revolver|language=en|access-date=2019-02-18}}</ref> – even enlisting Matt Michel, the frontman of Majority Rule to record, mix, and produce their first record, ''Let Pain Be Your Guide.''<ref name=":0" /> They have been compared to the bands [[Converge (band)|Converge]] and [[Oathbreaker (band)|Oathbreaker]], and termed "blackened screamo."<ref name=":1" />
