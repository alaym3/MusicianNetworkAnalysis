== History ==
Querbeat was founded in 2001 as a school band of the [[Kardinal-Frings-Gymnasium]] in Bonn-Beuel. In the first years the band played on city events mainly in Bonn and the area around Bonn. In 2007 they started to play in the Cologne Carnival. Querbeat became known by playing on Carnival shows and on TV programs broadcast by the [[Westdeutscher Rundfunk|WDR]] radio and television.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.general-anzeiger-bonn.de/news/kultur-und-medien/bonn/Ehemalige-Mitglieder-einer-Bonner-Sch%C3%BCler-Big-Band-erleben-gro%C3%9Fen-Durchbruch-article3438219.html|title=Querbeat im Interview – Ehemalige Mitglieder einer Bonner Schüler-Big-Band erleben großen Durchbruch|last=Dietl|first=Heinz|date=1 January 2017|work=General-Anzeiger Bonn|access-date=18 February 2018|publisher=Bonner Zeitungsdruckerei und Verlagsanstalt H. Neusser GmbH|location=Bonn|language=de|trans-title=Interview with Querbeat – Former members of a Bonn school big band are experiencing big breakthrough}}</ref> In the season 2017/18 the band had 180 gigs.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.general-anzeiger-bonn.de/bonn/stadt-bonn/Eine-Nacht-unterwegs-mit-Querbeat-article3768011.html|title=Der Ovend is dud, lang lääve der Morgen – Eine Nacht unterwegs mit Querbeat|last=Bartsch|first=Simon|date=6 February 2018|work=General-Anzeiger Bonn|access-date=18 February 2018|publisher=Bonner Zeitungsdruckerei und Verlagsanstalt H. Neusser GmbH|last2=Dyck|first2=Andreas|location=Bonn|language=de|last3=Heinz|first3=Johanna}}</ref>

In November 2010, Querbeat published the first song ''Colonia Tropical'', followed by ''Allez Olé Alaaf'' (2011), ''Stonn op un danz'' (2012), ''Hück oder nie'' (2013) and ''Nie mehr Fastelovend'' (2014). The first album ''Cuba Colonia'' was released in 2014, which contains [[Cover version]]s of famous Carnival songs in [[Music of Latin America|latin american style]]. Querbeat received a Golden Jazz Award for the Album in 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Datenbank: BVMI|url=https://www.musikindustrie.de/markt-bestseller/gold-/platin-und-diamond-auszeichnung/datenbank|access-date=2020-09-24|website=www.musikindustrie.de}}</ref> Their first album with lyrics and music written exclusively by themselves was ''Fettes Q'' in 2016. Since 2017, this album is also available as a live DVD ''Fettes Q: Live im Palladium.''<ref>{{Citation|title=Fettes Q: Live im Palladium|date=8 December 2017|url=https://www.amazon.de/Fettes-Q-Live-im-Palladium/dp/B01FH9NW3K|publisher=Rhingtön (Universal Music)|access-date=17 February 2018}}</ref> In the same year the band started their first tour through Germany.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://de-de.facebook.com/pg/querbeatbrass/about/|title=Querbeat – About|website=facebook.com|language=en|access-date=18 February 2018}}</ref> On 12 October 2018, the second album ''Randale & Hurra'' was released.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.general-anzeiger-bonn.de/news/kultur-und-medien/bonn/Querbeat-hat-einfach-Bock-auf-alles-article3957021.html|title=Bonner Band im GA-Interview – Querbeat hat einfach "Bock auf alles"|date=8 October 2018|work=General-Anzeiger Bonn|access-date=21 October 2018|language=de}}</ref> It was the band's first Top 10 album in the German Music Charts.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Suche - Offizielle Deutsche Charts|url=https://www.offiziellecharts.de/suche?artist_search=Querbeat&do_search=do|access-date=2020-09-24|website=www.offiziellecharts.de}}</ref>

In 2018 the band performed for the first time at some major German Festivals. Also several TV-appearances and their biggest tour to that point increased their popularity all over Germany.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Germany|first=programm ARD de-ARD Play-Out-Center Potsdam, Potsdam|title=ARD-Morgenmagazin|url=https://programm.ard.de/TV/Programm/Sender/?sendung=28106948749186|access-date=2020-09-24|website=programm.ARD.de}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Germany|first=programm ARD de-ARD Play-Out-Center Potsdam, Potsdam|title=Pufpaffs Happy Hour|url=https://programm.ard.de/TV/3sat/pufpaffs-happy-hour/eid_280071808940540|access-date=2020-09-24|website=programm.ARD.de}}</ref>

2019 took Querbeat to even bigger festivals. They performed at [[Lollapalooza]] Berlin (Germany), [[Hurricane Festival|Hurricane]] Festival (Germany), [[Open Air St. Gallen|OpenAir St. Gallen]] (Switzerland) and [[Donauinselfest]] Vienna (Austria) among others.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Worring|first=Stefan|date=2020-02-13|title=Nach Festival-Sommer: Bleibt die Band Querbeat dem Kölner Karneval treu?|url=https://www.ksta.de/koeln/karneval-in-koeln/nach-festival-sommer-bleibt-die-band-querbeat-dem-koelner-karneval-treu--36233846|access-date=2020-09-24|website=Kölner Stadt-Anzeiger|language=de-DE}}</ref>
