'''I.O.I''' ({{ko-hhrm|아이오아이}}; also known as '''IOI'''<ref>{{cite web|last1=Kim|first1=Ji-young|title=Produce 101' girl group to be named 'IOI'|url=http://kpopherald.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=201604011905149913238_2|website=Kpop Herald|access-date=April 2, 2016|archive-date=April 24, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160424105227/http://kpopherald.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=201604011905149913238_2|url-status=live}}</ref> or '''Ideal of Idol'''<ref>{{cite web|last1=Lee|first1=Jeong-min|title=[오마이포토] 아이오아이, 드디어 세상 속으로!|url=http://news.nate.com/view/20160505n07750|website=Nate|publisher=Oh My News|access-date=May 6, 2016|language=ko|archive-date=July 21, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190721173413/https://news.nate.com/view/20160505n07750|url-status=live}}</ref>) was a South Korean [[girl group]] formed by [[CJ E&M]] through the 2016 reality show ''[[Produce 101]]'' on [[Mnet (TV channel)|Mnet]]. The group was composed of eleven members chosen from a pool of 101 trainees from various entertainment companies: [[Yoo Yeon-jung]], [[Lim Na-young]], [[Kang Mi-na]], [[Kim Do-yeon (singer)|Kim Do-yeon]], [[Jung Chae-yeon]], [[Zhou Jieqiong]], [[Kim So-hye]], [[Chungha|Kim Chung-ha]], [[Choi Yoo-jung (singer)|Choi Yoo-jung]], [[Kim Se-jeong]] and [[Jeon So-mi]]. They debuted on May 4, 2016 with the [[Extended play|EP]] ''[[Chrysalis (EP)|Chrysalis]]'' and actively promoted as a whole and as a sub-unit for less than a year.

The group's concert titled ''Time Slip – I.O.I'', which was held on January 20 to 22, 2017, marked their last group activity on stage. They officially disbanded at the end of January 2017 and returned to their respective agencies.

==History==

{{Main|Produce 101 (season 1)}}

===Debut with ''Chrysalis''===
{{main|Chrysalis (EP)}}
I.O.I officially debuted on May 4, 2016 through the release of their first album, ''[[Chrysalis (EP)|Chrysalis]]'', accompanied with a music video for the title track "[[Dream Girls (I.O.I song)|Dream Girls]]". ===Last activities, final concert and disbandment===
On November 5, 2016, YMC Entertainment confirmed that I.O.I would be disbanding on January 31, 2017. The group would still be actively promoting until the end of their contracts; including guest appearance on variety shows such as ''[[Immortal Songs: Singing the Legend]]'', ''Yang and Nam Show'' and ''[[Knowing Bros]]''.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Jeon|first1=A-ram|title=아이오아이 측 "7일 '불후' 녹화…고별 무대는 아냐"|url=http://www.xportsnews.com/jenter/?ac=article_view&entry_id=786790|website=Xports News|publisher=Xports Media|access-date=November 29, 2016|language=ko|date=2016-11-05|archive-date=November 29, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161129150400/http://www.xportsnews.com/jenter/?ac=article_view&entry_id=786790|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Kang|first1=Bo-ra|title=[POP스포] '불후의명곡' 아이오아이, 그녀들의 마지막 꽃길|url=http://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=112&aid=0002865785|website=Naver|publisher=Herald Pop|access-date=November 29, 2016|language=ko|archive-date=August 11, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170811011213/http://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=112&aid=0002865785|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Kim|first1=Ji-ah|title='양남자쇼' 양세형, 아이오아이 선택 받고 토크 배틀 승리(종합)|url=http://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=112&aid=0002867251|website=Naver|publisher=Herald Pop|access-date=November 29, 2016|language=ko|archive-date=August 11, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170811011537/http://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=112&aid=0002867251|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=아이오아이(IOI), '아는형님' 재출격 예고편 공개…"우리 안 보고싶었어?"|url=http://www.honam.co.kr/read.php3?aid=1480254185509220215|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190112095051/http://www.honam.co.kr/read.php3?aid=1480254185509220215|url-status=dead|archive-date=January 12, 2019|website=Honam|publisher=Moodeung Ilbo|access-date=November 29, 2016|language=ko}}</ref> From January 20 to 22, 2017, I.O.I held their final concert titled ''Time Slip – I.O.I''.
