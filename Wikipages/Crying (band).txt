'''Crying''' is an American [[Rock music|rock]] band from [[Purchase, New York]] formed in 2013. They consist of Elaiza Santos (vocals), Ryan Galloway (guitars, keyboards) and Kynwyn Sterling (drums).<ref name="Allmusic">{{Cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/crying-mn0003338377/biography|title=Crying {{!}} Biography & History|last=Monger|first=Timothy|date=|website=AllMusic|publisher=|access-date=2016-11-15}}</ref> They have released two EPs and released their debut album ''Beyond the Fleeting Gales'' in October 2016.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/beyond-the-fleeting-gales-mw0002977406|title=Beyond the Fleeting Gales - Crying Review|last=Monger|first=Timothy|date=|website=AllMusic|publisher=|access-date=2016-11-15}}</ref>

==History==
Crying was formed in May 2013 at [[State University of New York at Purchase]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://pancakesandwhiskey.com/2014/12/18/we-interview-crying-after-thier-set-at-the-music-hall/|title=We Interview Crying After Their Set At The Music Hall|last=Hanley|first=Shane|date=December 18, 2014|website=Pancakes And Whiskey|publisher=|access-date=2016-11-15}}</ref> The band was formed by Elaiza Santos (Vocals), Ryan Galloway (Guitars/Programming), and Nick Corbo (Drums). In 2013, the group released their debut EP ''Get Olde'' via [[Double Double Whammy]].<ref name="Allmusic"/> The band later released a remix EP of ''Get Olde'' with additional new tracks and b-sides titled ''Return to Olde World: Demos and B-Sides.'' 

In 2014 Crying released their second EP, ''Second Wind'' via [[Bandcamp]]. The band then signed to Run for Cover Records and reissued their first two EPs in a compilation, Get Olde/Second Wind.<ref name="Allmusic"/> In 2015, Corbo departed the band to focus on his other band, [[LVL UP]]. In March, the band released a standalone single, ''"Patriot"'', via the band's Bandcamp, in which a remixed version would later be featured on the band's debut LP. 

In August 2016, the band announced their debut LP, Beyond the Fleeting Gales, would be released by Run For Cover on October 14, 2016. On August 24, 2016, the band released the track, ''"Wool in the Wash"'', from the upcoming LP.<ref name="Allmusic Beyond">{{cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/album/beyond-the-fleeting-gales-mw0002977406|title=Crying - Beyond the Fleeting Gales|first=Timothy|last=Monger|website=[[Allmusic]]|accessdate= 24 July 2020}}</ref>

In March 2017, the band performed a live session on [[Audiotree]] and it was released via streaming services.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://open.spotify.com/album/52gdLb2HcfE7gBJE8CyPf0|title=Crying on Audiotree Live|accessdate=December 17, 2020|via=[[Spotify]]}}</ref>

In 2020, Ryan Galloway formed a band with [[Japanese Breakfast|Michelle Zauner]] called Bumper. The band released their debut EP ''pop songs 2020'' on 4 September 2020.<ref name="Bumper Pitchfork"/>
