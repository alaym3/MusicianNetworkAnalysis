'''Urban Rescue''' are an American [[Christian music]] [[electropop]] group from [[Los Angeles]], California, who play [[contemporary Christian music|Christian pop]], [[Christian electronic dance music|Christian EDM]], and [[contemporary worship music]]. They have released one notable [[extended play]], ''[[Wild Heart (EP)|Wild Heart]]'', in 2016, from [[Rend Collective|Rend Family Records]] and [[Capitol Christian Music Group]]. Their first [[studio album]], ''[[Wild Heart (Urban Rescue album)|Wild Heart]]'', was released on May 6, 2016. In late 2017, they released a special project, ''City Sessions - Live in Los Angeles'', that reached over 150,000 views on YouTube.{{citation needed lead|date=July 2018}}
