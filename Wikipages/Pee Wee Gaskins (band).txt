'''Pee Wee Gaskins''' (often abbreviated as '''PWG''' or '''Peskins''') is a rock band from Indonesia formed in 2007. The lineup consists of Dochi, Sansan, Ai, Omo, and Aldy. They have released six albums, ''Stories From Our High School Years'' (2008), ''The Sophomore'' (2009), ''Ad Astra Per Aspera'' (2010), ''A Youth Not Wasted'' (2016), ''Salute to 90's'' (2018), and ''Mixed Feeling'' (2019).

==Name==
Sansan and Dochi wanted a serial killer as a name for their band, after doing some research on the internet, they found [[Donald Henry Gaskins]], an American serial killer and chose his nickname, "Pee Wee," as their band's name.<ref name="tjg">{{cite news |url= http://www.thejakartaglobe.com/arts/the-cult-of-pee-wee-gaskins/338201 |title= The Cult of Pee Wee Gaskins |first= Marcel |last= Thee |date= October 28, 2009 |accessdate= July 6, 2011 |newspaper= The Jakarta Globe |url-status= dead |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20110829035332/http://www.thejakartaglobe.com/arts/the-cult-of-pee-wee-gaskins/338201 |archivedate= August 29, 2011 }}</ref>

==Career==
In April 2007, the band released first their EP, ''Stories from Our High School Years'' through Knurd Records and sold 2000 copies.<ref name = "tjg"/>

In early 2009, the band released its first full-length album, ''The Sophomore'' through Variant Records and sold more than 20,000 copies.<ref name= "rsi1"/>

In 2010, ''Ad Astra Per Aspera'' was released. This album features reduced synthesizers and notably different lyrics compared to their two previous albums.<ref>{{cite web |url= http://rollingstone.co.id/read/2011/02/08/191524/1564173/1107/pee-wee-gaskins |title= Pee Wee Gaskins |first= Soleh |last= Solihun |date= February 8, 2011 |accessdate= July 6, 2011 |publisher= RollingStone Indonesia |language= id |url-status= dead |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20110324223502/http://rollingstone.co.id/read/2011/02/08/191524/1564173/1107/pee-wee-gaskins |archivedate= March 24, 2011 }}</ref>

In 2016, Pee Wee Gaskins signed to [[List of Universal Music Group labels|Universal Music Indonesia]] and released third album, ''A Youth Not Wasted.'' They also served as an opening act for Australian band, [[5 Seconds of Summer]]'s [[Sounds Live Feels Live World Tour|Sound Live Feels Live World Tour]] at the [[Indonesia Convention Exhibition]] in [[Jakarta]], Indonesia.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Pee Wee Gaskins akan Jadi Pembuka Konser 5 Second Of Summer|url=https://wartakota.tribunnews.com/2016/02/11/pee-wee-gaskins-akan-jadi-pembuka-konser-5-second-of-summer|access-date=2020-07-10|website=Warta Kota|language=id-ID}}</ref>

In 2018, Pee Wee Gaskins released a mini album, it's called "Salute To 90's". It features 5 cover songs of Indonesia's [[1990s in music|90's greatest hits]].

In 2019, Pee Wee Gaskins released new full-length album, it's called "Mixed Feeling". The album cover was made by Dochi's son and the sleeve boxset cover was made by the son of senior music journalist [[Adib Hidayat]], named Jemima. This album was produced by Erix Soekamti personnel from the band called "Endank Soekamti".<ref>{{cite web |url= https://hai.grid.id/read/071955415/mixed-feelings-jadi-bukti-pee-wee-gaskins-masih-bocah-meski-udah-berusia-13-tahun?page=all |title= 'Mixed Feelings' Jadi Bukti Pee Wee Gaskins Masih 'Bocah' Meski Udah Berusia 13 Tahun |first= HAI |last= Internship |date= December 16, 2019 |accessdate= July 6, 2020 |publisher= Hai Online |language= id }}</ref>

==Fans==
Fans of the band have several names, including Party Dorks,<ref name = "tjg"/> Dorkzilla, and Tatiana. The band also had a short-lived anti-fan group called Anti-Pee Wee Gaskins, abbreviated as APWG, but APWG has not appeared since 2009 . <ref name= "rsi1"/><ref name = "tjg"/>
