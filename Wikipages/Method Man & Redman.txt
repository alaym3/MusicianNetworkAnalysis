'''Method Man & Redman''' (also referred to as '''Meth & Red''', '''Red & Mef''', '''John Blaze and Funk Doc''' or '''Funk Doctor Spock and Johnny Blaze''') are an [[United States|American]] [[hip hop music|hip hop]] duo, composed of [[East Coast hip hop|East Coast rappers]]  [[Method Man]] (of [[Wu-Tang Clan]]) and [[Redman (rapper)|Redman]] (of [[Def Squad]]). They are signed to [[Def Jam Recordings]] both as solo artists and as a duo. While they had been collaborating since 1994, it was not until 1999 that they released a full-length collaborative effort, ''[[Blackout! (Method Man & Redman album)|Blackout!]]''. The follow-up album, ''[[Blackout! 2]]'', was released in 2009.

Method Man often gives a shout out to Redman on tracks that do not feature Redman; this usually comes in the form of "What up, Doc!" referring to Redman's alias "Funk Doctor Spock". In 2001, they starred alongside each other in the [[stoner film]] ''[[How High]]''. In 2004, the duo also starred in a short-lived [[Fox Broadcasting Company|Fox]] sitcom, titled ''[[Method & Red]]'', however they later disowned the series due to lack of creative control.

== History ==
The duo have known each other for a long time before signing with Def Jam. While at Def Jam, they reunited in 1994 on tour and met again in 1995 in the recording studio of [[West Coast hip hop]] legend [[Tupac Shakur|Tupac "2Pac" Shakur]]. They recorded "Got My Mind Made Up" for Shakur's multi-platinum album ''[[All Eyez on Me]]'' (1996). After this they had friendly battles [[Freestyle rap|freestyling]] with each other on ''[[Yo! MTV Raps]]'', which ultimately led to their collaborative debut single "[[How High (song)|How High]]". In 1999, the two rappers appeared together on "Rap Phenomenon" from [[The Notorious B.I.G.]] posthumous album ''[[Born Again (The Notorious B.I.G. album)|Born Again]]''.
 
On May 7, 2009, in promotion for their third studio album, Method Man & Redman released a mixtape titled ''Lights Out'', which was hosted by [[DJ Green Lantern]].<ref name="lightsout">{{Cite web|url=http://www.livemixtapes.com/mixtapes/10341/method_man_redman_lights_out.html|title=Method Man & Redman - Lights Out Hosted by DJ Green Lantern, Free Mixtape Stream and Download!|website=LiveMixtapes.com|access-date=2020-04-13}}</ref> In 2013, Method Man and Redman previewed a new track called "Lookin’ Fly Too" featuring Ehikmostz.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.xxlmag.com/news/2013/01/redman-method-man-preview-new-track-lookin-fly-too-video/|title=Redman & Method Man Preview New Track "Lookin' Fly Too" [Video] - XXL|first=Eric|last=Diep|website=Xxlmag.com|access-date=2020-04-13}}</ref>
