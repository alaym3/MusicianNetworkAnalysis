'''Ingested''' are an English [[death metal]] band from [[Manchester]], formed in 2006. The band consists of vocalist Jason Evans, guitarists Sam Yates and Sean Hynes, and drummer Lyn Jeffs. The band has released five studio albums since formation and their latest album, titled ''Where Only Gods May Tread'', was released in August 2020.

== History ==
===Formation and early years (2006–2012)===
The band was formed in the British city of [[Manchester]] in 2006, the group consists of vocalist Jason Evans, guitarists Sean Hynes and Sam Yates, bassist Brad Fuller and drummer Lyn Jeffs, who are all original members. Jeffs and Hynes formerly played in British deathcore act [[Annotations of an Autopsy]].<ref>''[[Nuclear Blast]]'': [http://www.nuclearblast.de/en/label/music/band/news/details/74429.71089.annotations-of-an-autopsy-drummer-calls-it-quits.html ANNOTATIONS OF AN AUTOPSY - Drummer calls it quits]</ref>

After signing to [[Candlelight Records]] and [[Siege of Amida Records]], Ingested released their debut album called ''Surpassing the Boundaries of Human Suffering'' in June 2009.

In 2010, Ingested toured throughout [[Europe]] for the first time as part of the ''Bonecrusher Fest'' which was headlined by [[The Black Dahlia Murder (band)|The Black Dahlia Murder]] and supported by [[Carnifex (band)|Carnifex]], [[3 Inches of Blood]] and [[The Faceless]].<ref>''[[Blabbermouth.net]]'': [http://www.blabbermouth.net/news/the-black-dahlia-murder-3-inches-of-blood-necrophobic-to-team-up-for-bonecrusher-fest/ THE BLACK DAHLIA MURDER, 3 INCHES OF BLOOD, NECROPHOBIC To Team Up For BONECRUSHER FEST]</ref> Tours alongside acts like [[Annotations of an Autopsy]] and [[Martyr Defiled]] as well as festival appearances at [[Ghostfest]] and [[Extremefest]] followed.

In 2011, Ingested released their second record ''The Surreption'' via Siege of Amida.

===''The Architect of Extinction'' and ''The Level Above Human'' (2013–2018)===
An EP followed in 2013 called ''Revered by No One, Feared by All''. After signing to [[Century Media]], the band released their third full-length album ''The Architect of Extinction'' in 2015.

The first USA tour Ingested ever did was in the Autumn of 2015 on the Devastation on the Nation tour. As they were supporting other extreme metal bands like [[Origin (band)|Origin]], [[Krisiun]], [[Aeon (band)|Aeon]], Alterbeast and [[Soreption]]. In 2016 Ingested toured North America for the second time, being part of the annual [[The Summer Slaughter Tour|Summer Slaughter Tour]].<ref name="ULR" /><ref>Robert Pasbani: ''Metalinjection'': [http://www.metalinjection.net/tour-dates/summer-slaughter-2016-lineup-leaks SUMMER SLAUGHTER 2016 Lineup Leaks (CANNIBAL CORPSE, NILE, SUFFOCATION)]</ref>

In 2016, Ingested signed to [[Unique Leader Records]] which will release their fourth LP ''The Level Above Humen'' in April 2018.<ref name="ULR">{{cite web|author= |url=https://uniqueleader.com/signings/ingested-joins-unique-leader-records-band-added-summer-slaughter-2016-lineup/ |title=INGESTED Joins Unique Leader Records; Band Added To Summer Slaughter 2016 Lineup - Unique Leader Records |website=Uniqueleader.com |date=2016-05-23 |accessdate=2020-05-29}}</ref>

It was announced that Ingested will return to North America including their first appearance in [[Mexico]] in mid of 2018 followed by another European tour in support of their fourth record ''The Level Above Human''.<ref>Vince Neilstein: ''MetalSucks'': [http://www.metalsucks.net/2018/02/07/ingested-announce-u-s-and-canadian-headline-tour/ Ingested Announce U.S. and Canadian Headline Tour]</ref><ref>''Summa Inferno'': [http://summainferno.com/ingested-confirma-gira-por-mexico-en-2018/ INGESTED confirma gira por México en 2018] (Spanish)</ref> In support for The Level Above Human, the band did their first full USA headliner in the Spring of 2018 with Signs of the Swarm and Bodysnatcher. They also played on the 2018 Devastation on the Nation tour with bands like [[Aborted]], [[Psycroptic]], [[Disentomb]], Venom Prison, Vale of Pnath & Arkaik. Jason Evans, the current vocalist of Ingested could not tour due to personal reasons; his fill in was Jason Keyser of the technical death metal band Origin.{{fact|date=November 2020}}

Ingested returned to Europe in the September of 2018, opening up for Sludge Metal band [[Crowbar (American band)|Crowbar]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://lambgoat.com/news/29993/Crowbar-Ingested-to-tour-UK |title=Crowbar, Ingested to tour UK |website=Lambgoat.com |date=2018-05-07 |accessdate=2020-05-29}}</ref> Ingested also did their second headliner in the United States known as the "Evisceratour" in late 2018. [[Enterprise Earth]], [[I Declare War (band)|I Declare War]], Bodysnatcher, Aether and I AM joined up as support.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://metalinjection.net/tour-dates/ingested-announces-u-s-tour-with-i-declare-war-enterprise-earth-bodysnatcher |title=Ingested Announces U.S. Tour With I Declare War, Enterprise Earth & Bodysnatcher |website=Metalinjection.net |date=2018-08-21 |accessdate=2020-05-29}}</ref>

===''Where Only Gods May Tread'' (2019–present)===
In 2019, Ingested supported [[Cryptopsy]] on their Spring European tour. [[Incite (band)|Incite]], Demonical, Nightrage and Gloryhole Guillotine joined up on the lineup as well.<ref>{{cite web|last=Fuchs |first=Rodney |url=http://everythingisnoise.net/news/cryptopsy-to-tour-europe-with-support-of-ingested-gloryhole-guillotine-and-more/ |title=Cryptopsy to Tour Europe with Support of Ingested, Gloryhole Guillotine, and More |website=Everythingisnoise.net |date= |accessdate=2020-05-29}}</ref> In April and May 2019, Ingested supported The Black Dahlia Murder on their European and UK tour. Ingested opened for [[Despised Icon]] on their fall 2019 tour in the United States. [[Kublai Khan (band)|Kublai Khan]] and Shadow of Intent joined up as support.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://lambgoat.com/news/31611/Despised-Icon-Kublai-Khan-Ingested-tour-dates |title=Despised Icon, Kublai Khan, Ingested tour dates |website=Lambgoat.com |date=2019-06-04 |accessdate=2020-05-29}}</ref> Ingested toured Europe in November 2019 on their "Decade Of Human Suffering" Tour performing ''Surpassing The Boundaries of Human Suffering'' in its entirety. Within Destruction, Signs of the Swarm and Distant accompanied them on this tour.

The band's fifth studio album, ''Where Only Gods May Tread'', was released on 14 August 2020.<ref>[https://metalinjection.net/video/ingested-drops-new-song-new-album-features-crowbar-the-acacia-strain-vocalist INGESTED Drops New Song, New Album Features CROWBAR & THE ACACIA STRAIN Vocalist]. Metal Injection. MAY 29, 2020.</ref> ''[[Metal Hammer]]'' named it as the 44th best metal album of 2020.<ref name="MHtop5020">{{cite web |title=The 50 best metal albums of 2020 |url=https://www.loudersound.com/features/the-50-best-metal-albums-of-2020 |website=[[Metal Hammer]] |publisher=[[Future plc]] |access-date=March 12, 2021 |date=January 8, 2020}}</ref>
