'''Gunship''' is a British [[synthwave]] band formed in 2014 by [[Dan Haigh]] and [[Alex Westaway]] and were later joined by drummer Alex Gingell.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.recordoftheday.com/news-and-press/metropolis-music-publishing-dan-haigh-and-al-westaways-gunship|title=Metropolis Music Publishing announce the signing of Fightstar’s Dan Haigh and Al Westaway’s new synth wave project, GUNSHIP|website=www.recordoftheday.com|access-date=2016-09-15}}</ref>

== History ==
After Westaway and Haigh's other musical act, [[Fightstar]], went on hiatus in 2010, the pair decided to focus on other musical ventures, including forming Gunship, eventually joined by Gingell. Their debut release, self-titled ''Gunship'', was released in 2015 to positive reviews. Their follow up, ''Dark All Day'', was released 5 October 2018.

In 2019 Gunship appeared in the documentary film [[The Rise of the Synths]] which explored the origins and growth of the Synthwave genre. Westaway, Haigh and Gingell appeared alongside various other composers from the scene, including [[John Carpenter]] who also starred in and narrated the film.

In May of 2021, tattoo artist and model [[Kat Von D]] collaborated with Haigh on her debut album, ''Love Made Me Do It''.<ref name="billboard.com">{{cite news |last1=Chan |first1=Anna |title=Kat Von D Announces Debut Album |url=https://www.billboard.com/music/music-news/kat-von-d-announces-debut-album-love-made-me-do-it-exclusive-9571565/ |access-date=November 23, 2021  |date=May 14, 2021}}</ref>

== Equipment and gear ==
Gunship have a large collection of synthesizers including the [[Roland Juno-106|Juno 106]], [[Dave Smith Instruments|DSI]] Prophet 12, [[Dave Smith Instruments|DSI]] Prophet 6, MOOG [[Minimoog]], MOOG Mother 32, and various [[Oberheim Electronics|Oberheim]] units.<ref name="auto">{{Cite web|url=http://www.driveradio.be/index.php/inside-drive/item/842-gunship.html|title=GUNSHIP|last=Damme|first=Jean-Pierre Van|access-date=13 September 2016|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151020195826/http://driveradio.be/index.php/inside-drive/item/842-gunship.html|archivedate=20 October 2015}}</ref>
