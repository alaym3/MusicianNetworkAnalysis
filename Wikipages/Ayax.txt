'''Ayax S.A.''' is an automobile dealer and former automobile manufacturer based in [[Montevideo]], [[Uruguay]].<ref name="ayax">{{Webarchiv|url=http://ayaxonline.com/historia/ |wayback=20170905191700 |text=Historia |archiv-bot=2019-08-25 13:55:49 InternetArchiveBot }} auf der Website von Ayax S.A.</ref>

==History==
The company AYAX S.A., founded in 1945, which initially imported various laboratory products as well as textile and paper machines in addition to vehicles from the [[Opel]] and [[Chevrolet]] brands, was taken over 100% by its managing director Emilio J. Curcio in 1962.<ref name="ayax" /> [[General Motors]]' agency was abandoned in 1968 in favor of the [[Fiat]] and [[Toyota]] brands.<ref name="ayax" /> While the representation and assembly of Fiat vehicles was given up again, the production of Toyota vehicles was continued.

The assembly activity ended in the 1990s.<ref name="uni2012">Eduardo Fernández Fenocchi, Matías Piñeiro da Rosa, Gastón Sorrento Fenocchi:  {{Webarchiv|text=Aplicación del Cuadro de Mando Integral en Nordex S.A. |url=https://www.colibri.udelar.edu.uy/bitstream/123456789/640/1/M-CD4451.pdf |wayback=20161120150303 }}, Montevideo 2012.</ref> The subsidiary Lucca Design, founded in 2004, works as a supplier for [[Toyota Argentina]].<ref name="uni2012" /><ref>{{Webarchiv | url=https://www.colibri.udelar.edu.uy/bitstream/123456789/649/1/M-CD4521.pdf | wayback=20170906182823 | text=Cecilia Inés Garmendia Ronco/ María Paula Hernández Llanos, ''La incidencia del Mercosur sobre la competitividad en la producción de autopartes en el Uruguay'', Montevideo 2013.}}</ref>

==Models==
The [[Toyota Hilux|Hilux]] model was assembled from 1971, while the [[Toyota Corolla|Corolla]] and other Hilux versions (pick-up and double cabin) were added from 1985.<ref name="ayax" /><ref>{{cite web|title=Los modelos que dejaron su huella en la industria automotriz uruguaya|periodical=elpais.com.uy|publisher=|url=http://www.elpais.com.uy/economia/gente-negocios/industria-automotriz-uruguaya-historia.html|url-status=live|access-date=2017-09-05|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170906183532/http://www.elpais.com.uy/economia/gente-negocios/industria-automotriz-uruguaya-historia.html|archive-date=2017-09-06|last=|date=2016-02-15|year=|language=|pages=|quote=}}</ref>
