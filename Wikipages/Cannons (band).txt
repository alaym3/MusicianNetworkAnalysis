'''Cannons''' are an American [[indie pop]] band formed in 2013 in [[Los Angeles]]. The band consists of lead vocalist Michelle Joy, guitarist Ryan Clapham, and keyboardist and bassist Paul Davis.<ref name="Levy"/>

==History==
Ryan Clapham and Paul Davis, who have been friends since childhood, began composing music together in their teenage years.<ref name="Levy">{{cite web|url=https://www.thedigitalfix.com/music/feature/we-chat-with-the-band-cannons/|title='I hope as a band we continue experimenting, and always give our fan base something new and refreshing.' We chat with the band Cannons|website=The Digital Fix|date=October 15, 2020|access-date=January 26, 2021|last=Levy|first=Mara}}</ref><ref name="Dunn">{{cite web|url=https://wrrv.com/getting-to-know-cannons-is-a-harry-styles-collab-imminent/|title=Getting To Know Cannons, Is A Harry Styles Collab Imminent?|publisher=[[WRRV]]|date=October 15, 2020|access-date=January 26, 2021|last=Dunn|first=Simon}}</ref> In 2013, after posting an advertisement on the website [[Craigslist]] seeking a singer for their musical project, Clapham and Davis connected with Joy, who was thereafter recruited as Cannons' frontwoman.<ref name="Dunn"/><ref>{{cite web|url=http://blurredculture.com/cannons-will-get-you-in-the-mood-in-a-heartbeat/|title=Cannons Will Get You In the Mood 'In A Heartbeat'|website=Blurred Culture|date=July 13, 2018|access-date=January 26, 2021|last=Lee|first=Derrick}}</ref>

Cannons issued their first [[extended play|EP]] ''Up All Night'' in 2014. The band then released their debut album ''Night Drive'' in 2017, as well as a second EP, ''In a Heartbeat'', the following year. ''Shadows'', Cannons' second album, was released in 2019 by AntiFragile Music.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://la.thedelimagazine.com/41040/cannons-fire-for-you-sparks-disco-fever-release-debut-album-shadows|title=Cannons' 'Fire for You' sparks disco fever, release debut album Shadows|website=[[The Deli]]|date=July 17, 2019|access-date=May 12, 2021|last=Hay|first=Kayla}}</ref> "Fire for You", the first single from ''Shadows'', gained significant commercial traction after being featured in a 2020 episode of the [[comedy-drama]] TV series ''[[Never Have I Ever (TV series)|Never Have I Ever]]''.<ref name="Levy"/><ref name="Dunn"/><ref name="Rutherford">{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/9405296/cannon-fire-for-you-top-tv-songs-chart|title=Cannons' 'Fire for You' Rules Top TV Songs Chart Amid 'Never Have I Ever' Appearance|website=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|date=June 18, 2020|access-date=January 26, 2021|last=Rutherford|first=Kevin}}</ref> The band subsequently signed to [[Columbia Records]].<ref name="Levy"/> In early 2021, "Fire for You" reached number one on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Alternative Airplay]] chart.<ref name="USAlt"/>
