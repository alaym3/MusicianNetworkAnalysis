'''Herzog''' is an [[indie rock]] band from Cleveland, Ohio formed in 2010. Their music has been described as "subtlety-free rock,"<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cleveland.com/localbands/index.ssf/2011/09/herzog_25_bands_to_watch.html|title=Herzog: 25 Bands to Watch|work=cleveland.com}}</ref> and has been compared to [[Cloud Nothings]],<ref>{{cite web|url=http://pitchfork.com/forkcast/14094-paul-blart-and-the-death-of-art/|title="Paul Blart and the Death of Art" - Forkcast Archive - Pitchfork|work=Pitchfork}}</ref> whose bassist, TJ Duke, formerly played in Herzog.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.cmj.com/columns/scene-report/herzog-on-cleveland-oh/2/ |title=Scene Report: Herzog On Cleveland, OH |work=[[CMJ]] |date=11 April 2012 |accessdate=23 May 2014 |df= }}{{dead link|date=December 2017|bot=medic}}{{cbignore|bot=medic}}</ref> The band's frontman, Nick Tolar, graduated from [[Saint Ignatius High School (Cleveland)|Saint Ignatius High School]] in 2000.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.coolcleveland.com/blog/2011/05/herzog-is-happy-in-cle/|title=Herzog is happy in Cle|work=CoolCleveland}}</ref> They became well known when [[NPR]] chose the song "Silence" from their debut album [[Search (album)|Search]] as a song of the year in 2011.<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.clevelandmagazine.com/ME2/dirmod.asp?sid=E73ABD6180B44874871A91F6BA5C249C&nm=&type=Publishing&mod=Publications%3A%3AArticle&mid=1578600D80804596A222593669321019&tier=4&id=58534D3C37EF452BB327CBC9AECEEAAF | title=Fall Arts & Entertainment Preview: Age Range | work=[[Cleveland Magazine]] | date=September 2013 | accessdate=15 June 2014 | author=Delamotte, Nikki}}</ref> Their third album, ''[[Boys (Herzog album)|Boys]]'', was released on May 20, 2014. Dan Price, the band's drummer, has said that its music resembles [[Weezer]] and the other '90's bands he and his bandmates grew up listening to. He has also said that ''Boys'' is the best representation of his band's sound yet.<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.cleveland.com/music/index.ssf/2014/05/cleveland_band_herzog_returns.html | title=Cleveland band Herzog returns with new studio effort "Boys" | work=[[Cleveland.com]] | date=9 May 2014 | accessdate=24 May 2014 | author=Benson, John}}</ref>
