'''Derik John Baker''' (born January 31, 1990), known as '''Virginia To Vegas''',  is an American-born Canadian singer and songwriter. He is based in [[Toronto]], [[Ontario]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Virginia to Vegas in Studio|url=http://www.chfi.com/2014/08/19/91118/|website=CHFI|access-date=17 February 2015}}</ref> He is best known for his gold and platinum-certified singles, "[[We Are Stars]]", "[[Lights Out (Virginia to Vegas song)|Lights Out]]", "Selfish" and "Just Friends" released through Wax Records and [[Ultra Records]].

==Beginnings==
Although Derik John Baker was born in the United States, he was raised in Guelph and Toronto, Ontario, where he attended high school at Guelph Collegiate Vocational Institute (GCVI). After high school, Derik attended [[Sheridan College]] for studies in Travel and Tourism.

==Career==
===2013–2014: Career beginnings and ''Vol. I''===
Virginia To Vegas was signed to an independent full service 100% Canadian music company Wax Records in July 2013, after label executive, Jamie Appleby, discovered him on [[YouTube]]. The first single "[[We Are Stars]]" was released on January 7, 2014, and entered the Canadian Singles Top 100 chart at 94 on week 5, February 1, 2014, peaked at 14 and stayed for one week.<ref>{{cite web|title=Virginia to Vegas and Alyssa Reid - We Are Stars|url=http://acharts.us/song/83073|website=ACharts.us|access-date=17 February 2015}}</ref> On November 10, 2014, Virginia to Vegas released his debut EP ''Vol. I'', which includes his Platinum-certified debut single "We Are Stars".<ref>{{cite web|title=Virginia to Vegas|url=http://www.feldman-agency.com/artist-roster/virginia-vegas|website=The Feldman Agency|access-date=17 February 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Pham|first1=Vivian|title=Listen—Virginia To Vegas Releases New Single 'Colourful'|url=http://popdust.com/2014/06/13/listen-virginia-vegas-releases-new-single-colourful/|website=Pop Dust|access-date=17 February 2015}}</ref>

===2015–2016: ''Utopian''===
Virginia to Vegas released a new single, "[[Our Story (song)|Our Story]]", on May 4, 2015.<ref>{{cite web|title=Don't Fight the Music - Virginia to Vegas {{!}} Release Info|url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/release/dont-fight-the-music-mr0004248143|work=[[AllMusic]]|publisher=[[All Media Network]]|access-date=December 3, 2016}}</ref> On September 2, 2016, he released a new single, "[[Lights Out (Virginia to Vegas song)|Lights Out]]", which samples [[Rockwell (musician)|Rockwell]]'s 1984 single, "[[Somebody's Watching Me]]".<ref name="iheartradio">{{cite news|last1=Kennedy|first1=John R|title=Virginia to Vegas Channels Rockwell In 'Lights Out'|url=http://www.iheartradio.ca/news/virginia-to-vegas-channels-rockwell-in-lights-out-1.2166678|access-date=December 3, 2016|work=[[iHeartRadio]]|publisher=[[iHeartMedia]]|date=November 8, 2016}}</ref> That fall, he announced that his debut studio album would be released by the end of 2016.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Doole|first1=Kerry|title=Virginia To Vegas: Lights Out|url=http://www.fyimusicnews.ca/articles/2016/11/25/virginia-vegas-lights-out|access-date=December 3, 2016|publisher=FYI Music News|date=November 25, 2016}}</ref> ''[[Utopian (album)|Utopian]]'' was released on December 2, 2016 and features all four of his singles released through that date.<ref>{{cite web|title=Utopian by Virginia to Vegas|url=https://itunes.apple.com/ca/album/utopian/id1173672200|website=[[iTunes Store]] (Canada)|publisher=[[Apple Inc]]|access-date=December 3, 2016}}</ref> This was when he first got signed to a U.S record company of [[Ultra Records]].

===2017–2019: ''Hartland St.''===
On February 24, 2017, Virginia To Vegas released a new single called "Selfish".<ref>{{citation|url=https://music.apple.com/ca/album/selfish-single/1445139205|title=Selfish - Single by Virginia to Vegas on Apple Music|website=[[iTunes Store]] (Canada)|access-date=July 6, 2019}}</ref> On November 8, he released another single called "Emotions".<ref>{{citation|url=https://music.apple.com/ca/album/emotions-single/1445138446|title=Emotions - Single by Virginia to Vegas on Apple Music|website=[[iTunes Store]] (Canada)|access-date=July 6, 2019}}</ref> His next single "Yesterday" was released on October 12, 2018.<ref>{{citation|url=https://music.apple.com/ca/album/yesterday-single/1438524152|title=Yesterday - Single by Virginia to Vegas on Apple Music|website=[[iTunes Store]] (Canada)|access-date=July 6, 2019}}</ref> Finally, he released "Just Friends" on May 24, 2019.<ref>{{citation|url=https://music.apple.com/ca/album/just-friends-single/1463625219|title=Just Friends - Single by Virginia to Vegas on Apple Music|website=[[iTunes Store]]|access-date=July 6, 2019}}</ref> All these songs are featured on his second EP ''Hartland St.'', which was released on June 14, 2019.<ref name="hartland"/> Hartland Street is the name of the actual street he lives on in real life. This was when he moved to, was living in, and was leaving [[Los Angeles]] at a time.

===2020–present: ''A Constant State of Improvement'' and ''Don't Wake Me, I'm Dreaming''===
Only months after the release of ''Hartland St.'', Virginia To Vegas released the single "Betterman" on February 14, 2020, followed by an EP titled ''A Constant State of Improvement'' on February 28, 2020, which includes the song "Betterman". On October 30, 2020, he released another EP, ''Don't Wake Me, I'm Dreaming'',<ref name=dont/> which includes the lead single "Palm Springs (The Way You Made Me Feel)", released on September 18.<ref name=palm/>

==Awards==
Virginia to Vegas performed at the 2014 [[Canadian Radio Music Awards]] where he was nominated for the Heartseeker Award. His radio success with his first three singles "We Are Stars", "Don't Fight the Music", and "Our Story" contributed to him being nominated for Pop Artist or Group of the Year at the 2015 [[Sirius XM]] Indie Awards.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Delmar|first1=Sari|title=2015 INDIE AWARDS NOMINEES ANNOUNCED|url=http://indies.ca/2015-siriusxm-indie-awards-nominees-announced/|website=INDIES|publisher=[[Sirius XM]]|access-date=December 3, 2016}}</ref>

"We Are Stars", "Lights Out" and "Selfish" all earned Virginia to Vegas [[Socan]] awards for pop recordings of the year.

In 2015 Virginia to Vegas was awarded at the Canadian Radio Music Awards for the Factor Breakthrough Artist of the Year.

He received a [[Juno Award]] nomination for [[Juno Award for Breakthrough Artist of the Year|Breakthrough Artist of the Year]] at the [[Juno Awards of 2018]].<ref>[http://www.cbc.ca/music/junos/junos-news/arcade-fire-and-daniel-caesar-lead-2018-juno-award-nominations-1.4520940 "Arcade Fire and Daniel Caesar lead 2018 Juno Award nominations"]. [[CBC Music]], February 6, 2018.</ref>
