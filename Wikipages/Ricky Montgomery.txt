'''Ricky Montgomery''' (born April 3, 1993)<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|last=Montgomery|first=Ricky|title=ricky montgomery|url=http://rickymontgomery.com/|url-status=live|access-date=June 3, 2021}}</ref> is an American [[Indie pop]] musician and songwriter. In 2020, his songs went viral on [[TikTok]], leading to being signed with [[Warner Records]] later that year.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|title=Ricky Montgomery - Paradigm Talent Agency|url=https://www.paradigmagency.com/music/ricky-montgomery/|url-status=live|access-date=June 3, 2021|website=Paradigm Talent Agency}}</ref>

== Early life ==
He was born in [[Los Angeles]]. His mother was a teacher, and his father was a [[Gaffer (filmmaking)|gaffer]].<ref name=":0" /> Early on, his parents got a divorce,<ref name=":7">{{Cite news|last=Myszkowski|first=Max|date=December 3, 2019|title=The Honeysticks Sweeten Storke Plaza|work=[[Daily Nexus]]|department=Music|url=https://dailynexus.com/2019-12-03/the-honeysticks-sweeten-storke-plaza/|access-date=June 3, 2021}}</ref> and, in 2005, he and his mother moved to [[St. Louis]], Missouri, where he began to play in various bands at the age of 14.<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1" /> In 2014, he gained a following on [[Vine (service)|Vine]], a short-form video service, posting comical original songs.<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|last=Ginsberg|first=Gab|date=February 5, 2021|title=Ricky Montgomery Signs With Warner Records, Debuts 'Line Without a Hook' With Mxmtoon: Exclusive|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/9521289/ricky-montgomery-signs-warner-records-line-without-a-hook-mxmtoon/|url-status=live|access-date=June 3, 2021|website=[[Billboard (magazine)]]}}</ref> He found the site the day after the app had launched, from a [[CNN]] news article. He saw the site as a potential "springboard for a possible music career."<ref name=":7" /> After finding success with that, Montgomery released his first [[Extended play|EP]], ''Caught On The Moon''.<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":2" /> Soon after, he dropped out of college and returned to Los Angeles to pursue a musical career.<ref name=":1" />

He has a sister.<ref name=":7" />

== Career ==
In LA, he released his first studio album "Montgomery Ricky" on April 1st, 2016.<ref name=":1" /><ref>{{Cite web|last=Caulfield|first=AJ|date=April 2, 2016|title=Album Review: Ricky Montgomery – “Montgomery Ricky”|url=https://www.theyoungfolks.com/review/76086/album-review-ricky-montgomery-montgomery-ricky/|url-status=live|access-date=June 3, 2021|website=The Young Folks}}</ref> Montgomery founded indie band The Honeysticks with a childhood friend of his<ref name=":4">{{Cite magazine|last=Crowley|first=Erin|date=February 5, 2021|title=Ricky Montgomery & mxmtoon Team up for New Version of ‘Line Without a Hook’|url=https://www.melodicmag.com/2021/02/05/ricky-montgomery-mxmtoon-team-up-for-new-version-of-line-without-a-hook/|magazine=Melodic Magazine|access-date=June 3, 2021}}</ref> to experiment musically.{{When|date=June 2021}}<ref name=":1" /><ref name=":2" /><ref>{{Cite web|date=February 26, 2018|title=Ricky Montgomery|url=https://modernmusicmaker.com/interviews/rickymontgomery|url-status=live|access-date=June 3, 2021|website=Modern Music Maker}}</ref> The band name was derived from a snack Montgomery ate every Saturday morning when he was a kid, made of sticks of [[honey]].<ref name=":7" /> In 2018 he took the year off of making music and started [[social media marketing]] companies to earn more money,{{cn|date=October 2021}} and by 2020 was considering quitting making music entirely.<ref name=":1" /><ref name=":2" /> However, in mid-2020, during the [[COVID-19 pandemic]], his two [[Single (music)|singles]]—''Mr Loverman'' and ''Line Without A Hook''—went viral on [[TikTok]].<ref name=":1" /> With his explosion in popularity, he had been contacted by "virtually every single major label [[Artists and repertoire|A&R]] in a matter of months."<ref name=":2" /> And in December 2020,<ref name=":2" /> after a large bidding war, he signed with Warner Records.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Ricky Montgomery Signs With Warner Records, Debuts 'Line Without a Hook' With Mxmtoon: Exclusive|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/9521289/ricky-montgomery-signs-warner-records-line-without-a-hook-mxmtoon|access-date=2021-11-12|website=Billboard|language=en}}</ref>

{{Quote|text=I think because we had a really traumatizing year, these songs kind of found a moment, because they're all, in their own way, about traumas in my life.|author=Ricky Montgomery<ref name=":4" />|source=}}
Since then, he has remixed two songs in 2021 from his debut album with fellow artists [[Chloe Moriondo]] and [[mxmtoon]] respectively.<ref name=":2" /><ref name=":4" /> Montgomery came in contact with mxmtoon after finding her perform one of his songs on the streaming platform [[Twitch (service)|Twitch]], learning that she had been a fan of his Vine content before it shut down.<ref name=":2" /> He found Moriondo from a song [[Cover version|cover]] of ''Out Like A Light''.<ref name=":5">{{Cite magazine|last=March|first=Kim|date=March 5, 2021|title=Ricky Montgomery and chloe moriondo Team Up on a Wistful Remake of “Mr. Loverman”|url=https://floodmagazine.com/85454/listen-ricky-montgomery-chloe-moriondo-mr-loverman/|magazine=Flood Magazine|access-date=June 3, 2021}}</ref>

Montgomery is planning on working on his second album.<ref name=":2" />
