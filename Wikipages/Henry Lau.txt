'''Henry Lau''' ({{zh|t=劉憲華|s=刘宪华}}; {{lang-ko|헨리}}; born October 11, 1989),<ref>{{cite book|author=Mark Russell|title=K-Pop Now!: The Korean Music Revolution|page=39|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=etDZAwAAQBAJ|date=April 29, 2014|publisher=Tuttle Publishing|isbn=978-1-4629-1411-1|access-date=October 30, 2016|archive-date=May 27, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160527064350/https://books.google.com/books?id=etDZAwAAQBAJ|url-status=live}}</ref> often mononymously referred to as '''Henry''', is a <!-- Maintain Canadian nationality in lead as per [[WP:MOS]] guidelines-->[[Canadians|Canadian]] singer, songwriter, musician, record producer, and actor based in [[South Korea]] and [[China]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=manual|title=몬스터엔터테인먼트|url=https://monstergroup.co/henry/|access-date=June 19, 2021|website=monstergroup.co|language=ko-KR|archive-date=June 24, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210624202326/https://monstergroup.co/henry/|url-status=live}}</ref> He debuted in 2008 as a member of [[Super Junior-M]] and launched his solo career in 2013 with ''[[Trap (EP)|Trap]]''. His original soundtrack "It's You" released in 2017 became the most streamed Korean OST on [[Spotify]] for two consecutive years in 2018 and 2019.<ref name="itsyou">{{Cite web|title=Best Korean OSTs of 2019|url=https://open.spotify.com/playlist/37i9dQZF1DX6HNmsqZ1nyc|access-date=June 19, 2021|website=Spotify|language=en|archive-date=December 25, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201225130528/https://open.spotify.com/playlist/37i9dQZF1DX6HNmsqZ1nyc|url-status=live}}</ref> In 2018, Henry left [[SM Entertainment]] following the expiration of his contract, and subsequently joined Monster Entertainment Group.<ref>{{Cite web|date=April 30, 2018|title=[공식입장]헨리, SM 엔터테인먼트와 계약 만료 "재계약 않기로"|url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=421&aid=0003345560|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210624202346/https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=421&aid=0003345560|archive-date=June 24, 2021|access-date=June 19, 2021|website=Naver|publisher=News1|language=ko}}</ref><ref name="monster">{{cite web|date=November 7, 2018|title=헨리, 몬스터엔터테인먼트와 전속 계약…"아낌없는 지원 예정"[공식]|trans-title=Henry Signs Exclusive Contract with Monster Entertainment, Will be "Supported Generously"|url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=112&aid=0003094303|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190502012410/https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=112&aid=0003094303|archive-date=May 2, 2019|access-date=May 22, 2019|website=[[Naver]]|publisher=Herald Pop|language=ko}}</ref>

Henry made his Hollywood debut in 2019 with the film ''[[A Dog's Journey (film)|A Dog's Journey]]'' produced by [[Amblin Entertainment]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt8385474/|title=A Dog's Journey|date=May 3, 2019|via=IMDb|access-date=November 2, 2019|archive-date=October 7, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191007110752/https://www.imdb.com/title/tt8385474/|url-status=live}}</ref> In 2020, he starred in the action-fantasy movie ''[[Double World]]''.<ref>{{Citation|title=Double World (2020) - IMDb|date=July 21, 2020|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt10508838/|language=en|access-date=June 19, 2021|archive-date=July 3, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210703201844/https://www.imdb.com/title/tt10508838/|url-status=live}}</ref> The film was released on [[Netflix]] and the Chinese streaming service [[iQIYI]],<ref>{{Cite web|last=Davis|first=Rebecca|date=July 15, 2020|title=Chinese Movie 'Double World' to Skip Cinemas For Netflix, iQiyi Debut|url=https://variety.com/2020/film/news/netflix-iqiyi-double-world-1234707473/|access-date=June 19, 2021|website=Variety|archive-date=June 24, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210624201721/https://variety.com/2020/film/news/netflix-iqiyi-double-world-1234707473/|url-status=live}}</ref> becoming the first movie produced in Mainland China to have a simultaneous global release.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Netflix购入电影《征途》海外播放权，24日全球同步上线 - 富豪 - 福布斯中国 {{!}} Forbes China|url=http://www.forbeschina.com/billionaires/50173|access-date=June 19, 2021|website=www.forbeschina.com|archive-date=August 1, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200801153542/http://www.forbeschina.com/billionaires/50173|url-status=live}}</ref>

Henry is also known for his television work in the programs ''[[Real Man (TV series)|Real Man]]'' and ''[[I Live Alone (TV series)|I Live Alone]]'', for which he received the Best Newcomer Award (2014) and Excellence Male Award (2017) respectively at the [[MBC Entertainment Awards]].

He never expected to become a singer and did not know anything about K-pop before his friends suggested him to audition for SM.<ref name="Laterals" /> For his audition, Henry performed a [[Antonio Vivaldi|Vivaldi]] piece on the violin while incorporating popping during the piece's fast passages, as well as a cappella singing; he received an offer from the company the following week.<ref name="Laterals">{{cite web|last=Che|first=Kandice|date=May 20, 2019|title=Henry Lau|url=https://thelaterals.com/articles/henry-lau/|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190519105542/https://www.thelaterals.com/identities-film/henry-lau|archive-date=May 19, 2019|access-date=May 21, 2019|website=The Laterals}}</ref> At the time, he applied for colleges for classical music, but ultimately decided to go "down the K-pop road", as he explains, "I'll be dancing and singing, but that doesn't mean I won't be able to play the violin anymore or piano. I decided that I had to go down this road because that was the only way I could do pretty much everything."<ref name="MTV2019" /> He initially declined the offer because his father wanted him to go to university. After his mother was impressed during a visit to the company in South Korea, she convinced his father for him to accept the offer.<ref name="Laterals" />

Henry is able to speak four languages. == Musicianship ==
Henry is a multi-instrumentalist and plays the violin, piano, drums, and guitar.<ref>{{Cite web|date=February 11, 2018|title=WE MEET YOU 第十五期丨刘宪华：小提琴暖男曾有一个设计师梦|url=https://www.sohu.com/a/222177387_623452|url-status=live|website=Sohu|publisher=Billboard China|access-date=August 16, 2021|archive-date=August 16, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210816191011/https://www.sohu.com/a/222177387_623452}}</ref> He is known for his performance style which often involves the use of multiple instruments and [[Live looping|loop pedals]] during live performances.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Kim|first=Soo-young|date=December 30, 2020|title=[스타:라이트] 헨리, 음악 하는 모습이 주는 쾌감이란|url=https://www.hankyung.com/entertainment/article/202012270523H|url-status=live|access-date=September 6, 2021|website=Hankyung Ilbo|language=ko}}</ref> He has been nicknamed "one-man band" by Chinese netizens after a viral performance at the [[Zhejiang Television|Zhejiang TV]] Autumn Festival in 2019, during which he performed using live looping and played drum pads, a glass bottle, kick drum, marimba, piano, and electric violin while singing.<ref>{{Cite web|date=October 19, 2019|title=秋季盛典上刘宪华一人撑起一个乐队，还在钢琴上涂鸦，内容很有才|url=https://baijiahao.baidu.com/s?id=1647834106014215609&wfr=spider&for=pc|url-status=live|website=Baidu|publisher=添福娱乐|access-date=August 16, 2021|archive-date=August 16, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210816191011/https://baijiahao.baidu.com/s?id=1647834106014215609&wfr=spider&for=pc}}</ref>
