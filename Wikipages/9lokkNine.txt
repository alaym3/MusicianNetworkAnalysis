'''Jacquavius Dennard Smith'''<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.orangepublicrecords.com/sample.php?id=36419|title=JACQUAVIUS DENNARD SMITH {{!}} Is There A JACQUAVIUS DENNARD SMITH On Facebook? {{!}} Is There A JACQUAVIUS DENNARD SMITH On LinkedIn?|website=orangepublicrecords.com|access-date=October 11, 2019}}</ref> (born May 1, 2000), professionally known as '''9lokkNine''' (pronounced and formerly typeset '''GlokkNine'''), is an American rapper. He is best known for his songs "10 Percent", which has received over 30 million views on [[YouTube]], and "[[223's]]" (with [[YNW Melly]]), which peaked at number 34 on the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]].<ref>{{Cite magazine|url=https://www.billboard.com/charts/search|title=Chart Search|magazine=Billboard}}</ref>

== Career ==
9lokkNine released his first [[mixtape]], ''Kold Face Kold Case'' with nine tracks on January 2, 2018. He released his second mixtape, ''Bloodshells Revenge'' with 18 tracks on April 17, 2018. On May 8, 2018, 9lokkNine released a music video for his single "I Don't Need No Help".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.xxlmag.com/news/2018/05/glokknine-i-dont-need-no-help-video/|title=Glokknine Takes Over South Beach in "I Don't Need No Help" Video - XXL|last=India|first=Lindsey|website=XXL Mag|access-date=May 30, 2019}}</ref> On July 23, 2018, he released his 17-track mixtape, ''Loyalty Kill Love''. On August 3, 2018, he signed to [[Cash Money Records]] and [[Republic Records]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.xxlmag.com/news/2018/08/glokknine-signs-cash-money-records/|title=Glokknine Signs to Cash Money Records - XXL|last=II|first=C. Vernon Coleman|website=XXL Mag|access-date=May 20, 2019}}</ref> According to a press release, the contract was worth $2 million.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://hiphop-n-more.com/2018/08/florida-rapper-glokknine-signs-2-million-deal-with-cash-money-records/|title=Florida Rapper GloKKNine Signs $2 Million Deal with Cash Money Records|last=Navjosh|date=August 7, 2018|website=HipHop-N-More|access-date=June 2, 2019}}</ref> On December 19, 2018, he released the 11-track mixtape, ''Lil Glokk That Stole Khristmas''. In April 2019, 9lokkNine released his single "Party Pooper".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/glokknine-drops-off-new-track-party-pooper-new-song.1982633.html|title=GlokkNine Drops Off New Track "Party Pooper"|website=HotNewHipHop|access-date=May 14, 2019}}</ref> 9lokkNine was featured on a YNW Melly track named "223's", though he was the one to originally release it as a part of his December 2018 mixtape, ''Lil Glokk That Stole Khristmas''. The song became a sensation when it was popularized by the mobile app [[TikTok]] where numerous popular creators took to dancing to the song.

On October 25, 2019, 9lokkNine released his 16-track mixtape ''Mind of Destruction'', which served as his first official mixtape release on Cash Money and Republic.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://respect-mag.com/2019/10/9lokknine-releases-new-mixtape-mind-of-destruction-today/|title=9LOKKNINE Releases New Mixtape "Mind Of Destruction" Today|date=October 25, 2019|website=RESPECT.|access-date=January 5, 2020}}</ref> On February 5, 2020, he released his single "Moods".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/9lokknine-gets-real-with-it-on-moods-new-song.1986069.html|title=9lokknine Gets Real With It On "Moods"|website=HotNewHipHop|access-date=February 11, 2020}}</ref>

== Legal issues ==
In September 2015, 9lokkNine, 15-years-old at the time, was arrested in connection to a shooting that left a 17-year-old hospitalized. Smith was charged with aggravated battery with a firearm and unlawful discharge of a firearm and also had a violation of probation for trespassing. He was then escorted to the Orange Regional Juvenile Detention Center.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.wftv.com/news/local/teen-charged-shooting-injured-17-year-old/27076409|title=Teen charged in shooting that injured 17-year-old|last=EndPlay|date=September 2, 2015|website=WFTV|access-date=April 14, 2019}}</ref>

On October 3, 2018, 9lokkNine was arrested on multiple charges, including possession of a concealed weapon by a convicted delinquent, grand theft in the third degree, and possession of less than 20 grams of [[Cannabis (drug)|marijuana]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.miamiherald.com/news/state/florida/article219547360.html|title=Florida rapper got pulled over for drag racing. What was in his car got him arrested. |website=Miami herald |access-date=March 24, 2019}}</ref>

On May 31, 2019, 9lokkNine was arrested in Orlando, Florida, and charged with multiple counts including possession of marijuana and illegal firearm possession.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://hiphopdx.com/news/id.51634/title.cash-money-rapper-glokknine-arrested-on-multiple-felony-gun-drug-charges|title=Cash Money Rapper GlokkNine Arrested On Multiple Felony Gun & Drug Charges|last=https://hiphopdx.com|first=HipHopDX-|date=June 1, 2019|website=HipHopDX|access-date=June 2, 2019}}</ref>

On January 3, 2020, 9lokkNine was arrested in Miami, Florida, and charged with carrying a concealed weapon.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.xxlmag.com/news/2020/01/glokknine-arrested-gun-charges-prison-sentence/|title=9lokknine Arrested for Carrying Concealed Firearm, Potentially Faces Up to Five Years in Prison|publisher=[[XXL (magazine)|XXL]]|first=Trent|last=Fitzgerald|date=January 6, 2020|access-date=January 11, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.xxlmag.com/news/2020/01/glokknine-arrested-gun-charges-prison-sentence/|title=9lokknine Arrested for Carrying Concealed Firearm, Potentially Faces Up to Five Years in Prison|last=Fitzgerald|first=Trent|website=XXL Mag|access-date=February 5, 2020}}</ref>

On July 24, 2020, he was arrested in Orlando and charged with attempted second degree murder.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Current Inmate Database|url=https://apps.ocfl.net/bailbond/default.asp?BookNumber=20016297&ID=5330520864086|access-date=July 26, 2020|website=apps.ocfl.net}}</ref>

On October 8, 2020, he was arrested in Orlando on an outstanding warrant related to charges for possession of a firearm by a convicted felon and possession of a short-barreled rifle.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Rapper Targeted in Shooting Outside Orlando Mall|date=October 9, 2020|url=https://www.foxnews.com/us/rapper-targeted-in-shooting-outside-orlando-mall}}</ref>

In January 2021, he was charged with multiple counts of attempted second-degree murder.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://hiphopdx.com/news/id.60143/title.cash-money-rapper-9lokknine-reportedly-hit-with-5-counts-of-attempted-2nd-degree-murder|title = Cash Money Rapper 9lokkNine Reportedly Hit with 5 Counts of Attempted 2nd-Degree Murder|date = January 20, 2021}}</ref>

On June 21, 2021, he was arrested on charges including racketeering and conspiracy to commit racketeering.<ref>{{cite web|author = Ashish Singh|date = June 21, 2021|title = Why was 9lokknine arrested? Orlando rapper slapped with $750K bond|url = https://www.meaww.com/amp/rapper-9-lokknine-arrested-legal-issues-orland-nativve-rico-racketeering-charges-conspiracy|website = Meaww.com|access-date = July 1, 2021}}</ref>
