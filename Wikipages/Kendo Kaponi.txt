'''José Fernando Morales Rivera''' (born August 19, 1984), known as '''Kendo Kaponi''', is a Puerto Rican rapper.

In 2018, he was arrested in [[Coamo, Puerto Rico|Coamo]] on charges of armed burglary and assault in [[Florida]],<ref name="billboard.com">{{cite magazine|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/8300921/reggaeton-singer-kendo-kaponi-arrested|title=Reggaeton Singer Kendo Kaponi Arrested In Puerto Rico|magazine=Billboard}}</ref> where he subsequently served two years in prison.

==Personal life==
Kaponi's life was tragic in many ways. With no family to care for him, he had begun to live in foster homes from when he was seven years old. His parents were drug addicts. He discovered his father's identity at same father's funeral.<ref name="primerahora.com">{{cite news|url=https://www.primerahora.com/entretenimiento/musica/nota/kendokaponiuntalentoenelbajomundodelamusica-627195/|title=Kendo Kaponi: un talento en el bajo mundo de la música|date=March 20, 2012|newspaper=Primera Hora|language=es}}</ref> His mother was a heroin addict. Kaponi was raised by his grandmother until she died of cancer and was then put into foster care because no one in his family wanted to take up his care.<ref name="Primer Impacto Kaponi" /> In prison from the age of 13, he attempted suicide at the age of 16. When he was released from prison, he did live with his rehabilitated mother, but only for six months because she died of [[AIDS]].<ref name="primerahora.com"/> Out of prison, Kaponi participated in a program at {{lang|es|Vida Independiente}} ("independent life") until he was 21, when he studied to be a barber. He has two daughters.<ref name="primerahora.com"/> Later in life, he spent two years in a Florida prison for aggravated assault and Kaponi stated that protecting his family was the reason for assaulting the man.<ref name="Primer Impacto Kaponi" />

==Career==
Kaponi has released one mixtape, ''El Alpha & El Omega'' in 2018. He is also known for participating in productions with [[Don Omar]] such as ''[[Don Omar Presents: Meet the Orphans]]'', which include ''El Duro'', ''Viviendo Con el Enemigo'', and ''RX''.<ref name="elnuevodia.com">{{cite news|url=https://www.elnuevodia.com/noticias/seguridad/nota/lasautoridadesfederalesarrestanakendokaponiencoamo-2413053/|title=Las autoridades federales arrestan a Kendo Kaponi en Coamo|date=April 9, 2018|newspaper=El Nuevo Dia|language=es}}</ref> He was also involved in a diss battle against [[Baby Rasta]], [[Cosculluela]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://remezcla.com/lists/music/anuel-aa-cosculluela-tiraera-breakdown/|title=A Breakdown of Anuel AA and Cosculluela’s Explosive Beef|date=September 12, 2018}}</ref> and [[Arcángel]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.primerahora.com/entretenimiento/musica/nota/kendokaponiledeclaralaguerraadosdesuscolegasreguetoneros-986486/|title=Kendo Kaponi le declara la guerra a dos de sus colegas reguetoneros|date=January 30, 2014|newspaper=Primera Hora|language=es}}</ref>

In 2020 Kaponi collaborated with [[Daddy Yankee]] and [[Anuel AA]] on [[Don Don]].<ref>{{cite web | title='Despacito' con otro reconocimiento – Fundación Nacional para la Cultura Popular | website=Fundación Nacional para la Cultura Popular | url=https://prpop.org/2020/10/despacito-con-otro-reconocimiento/ | language=es | access-date=2021-05-14}}</ref>

==Legal issues==
Kaponi's first arrest came at the age of 13, on a drug charge, gun law violation, attempted murder, and liberty restriction. He was released when he was 19.<ref name="primerahora.com"/>

In 2014, Kaponi was arrested for domestic violence, and made a bail.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.elnuevodia.com/noticias/seguridad/nota/librebajofianzaelreguetonerokendokaponi-1851613/|title= Libre banjo fianzá, el reggaetonero Kendo Kaponi|newspaper=El Nuevo Día|language=es}}</ref> In 2016, he was detained because his car was not up-to-date with its transfer process, and in January 2017, Kaponi was detained for contempt on an alimony order.<ref name="elnuevodia.com"/>

Kaponi was arrested by federal agents in 2018 on charges of armed burglary and assault.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.telemundopr.com/noticias/destacados/Emiten-orden-de-arresto-contra-el-cantante-Kendo-Kaponi-477510373.html|title=Orden de arresto contra el cantante Kendo Kaponi|newspaper=www.telemundopr.com|language=es}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.elnuevodia.com/noticias/seguridad/nota/lapoliciabuscaakendokaponiporroboyagresionenflorida-2408276/|title=La Policía busca a Kendo Kaponi por robo y agresión en Florida|date=March 21, 2018|newspaper=El Nuevo Día|language=es}}</ref> He was arrested on [[PR-143]]<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.metro.pr/pr/entretenimiento/2018/04/09/arrestan-al-reggaetonero-kendo-kaponi.html|title=Arrestan al reggaetonero Kendo Kaponi|newspaper=Metro.pr|language=es}}</ref> in the barrio of [[Hayales]] in [[Coamo]], Puerto Rico,<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.elcomercio.com/tendencias/detencion-reguetonero-kendokaponi-puertorico-agresion.html|title=Detuvieron en Puerto Rico al reguetonero Kendo Kaponi, buscado por robo y agresión|newspaper=El Comercio|language=es}}</ref> and extradited to Florida.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.metro.pr/pr/entretenimiento/2018/04/09/kendo-kaponi-sera-extraditado-florida.html|title=Kendo Kaponi será extraditado a Florida|newspaper=Metro.pr|language=es}}</ref> He was charged with attacking barber, Jon Erik Baez-Molano, at the Custom Kuts establishment in [[Winter Haven, Florida]] on July 28, 2017,<ref>{{cite newspaper|url=https://www.theledger.com/news/20170816/local-rapper-kendo-kaponi-still-at-large-sought-in-attack-on-haven-barber|title=Local rapper Kendo Kaponi still at large, sought in attack on Haven barber|newspaper=The Ledger}}</ref> with a metal pipe and a piece of wood. He and another person took the barber's wallet.<ref name="univision.com">{{cite news|url=https://www.univision.com/entretenimiento/al-amanecer-la-policia-le-corto-el-vuelo-al-reggaetonero-kendo-kaponi-en-puerto-rico|title=Al Amanecer la Policia Le Corto el Vuelo al Reggaetonero Kendo Kaponi en Puerto Rico|newspaper=Univision|language=es}}</ref><ref name="billboard.com"/> After two years in prison, Kendo was released and interviewed by [[Primer Impacto]], where he said he had beat the man because the man had hurt his son and not to rob him.<ref name="Primer Impacto Kaponi">{{cite web |title=Exclusivo: Kendo Kaponi revela qué lo llevó a cometer la agresión por la que fue a la cárcel |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=21jXyTDXHeQ |website=YouTube |publisher=Primer Impacto |language=es}}</ref>
