'''Christel Martina Sundberg''' (born January 3, 1982<ref>Me Naiset -magazine 7.6.2012, pp. 8–9</ref>), known by her stage name '''Chisu''' ({{IPA-fi|ˈkisu}}), is a [[Finland|Finnish]] pop artist, songwriter, and producer. She wrote her first single, ''[[Mun koti ei oo täällä]]'', to the soundtrack of the movie ''[[Sooloilua]]''. The song was a hit in Finland in Spring 2008, peaking at number one on both the [[List of number-one singles of 2008 (Finland)|Singles Chart and the Downloads Chart]] and spending seven and nine weeks atop, respectively. She released her second album ''[[Vapaa ja yksin]]'' on September 23, 2009. Her third album, ''[[Kun valaistun]]'', was released on October 5, 2011, through Warner Music Finland. Another version of the album, entitled ''Kun valaistun 2.0'' was released on June 6, 2012. Her fourth album, ''Polaris'' was released on October 2, 2015.

Chisu, who was born in [[Helsinki]], has written songs for [[Antti Tuisku]], [[Tarja Turunen]], [[Jippu]], [[Kristiina Brask]] and [[Kristiina Wheeler]].{{Citation needed|date=February 2011}} During her career, Chisu has sold over 230,000&nbsp;[[List of music recording certifications|certified]] records, which places her among the top 80 best-selling artists in Finland.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.ifpi.fi/tilastot/artistit/chisu|title=Chisu|publisher=[[Musiikkituottajat &ndash; IFPI Finland]]|language=Finnish|accessdate=2012-07-25}}</ref>

In November 2012, Chisu was connected to a copyright controversy in Finland when a nine-year-old girl's home was raided by the police and had her laptop confiscated due to allegedly downloading Chisu songs.<ref>{{cite web|author=enigmax|title=Police Raid 9-Year-Old Pirate Bay Girl, Confiscate Winnie The Pooh Laptop|url=http://torrentfreak.com/police-raid-9-year-old-pirate-bay-girl-confiscate-winnie-the-pooh-laptop-121122/|publisher=TorrentFreak|accessdate=23 November 2012|date=2012-11-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://yle.fi/uutiset/10-year-old_girl_fined_for_downloading_music/6397224 |title= 10-year-old girl fined for downloading music|date= 2012-11-29|website= |publisher=Yle |accessdate=2012-12-01}}</ref> The controversy was named as [[List of scandals with "-gate" suffix|Chisugate]] by media.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.uusisuomi.fi/aiheet/chisugate|title=Chisugate|publisher=[[Uusi Suomi]]|language=Finnish|accessdate=2012-12-01}}</ref> It has been alleged that the files the little girl downloaded and later shared, were bogus files disguised as the Chisu album and therefore she did not share Chisu's music.
