'''Isaac Dunbar''' is an alternative pop singer-songwriter from [[Barnstable, Massachusetts]].<ref>{{Cite web |url= https://www.barnstablepatriot.com/article/20151204/news/151209804 |title= Barnstable students compete in 'Penny Wars' to help others |website=barnstablepatriot |last=Devaney |first=Rachael |date=4 December 2015 |accessdate=5 February 2020}}</ref><ref name="Rowley">{{Cite web |url= https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8548544/isaac-dunbar-interview |title= Rising Teen Pop Star Isaac Dunbar Unwraps 'Isaac's Insects' Video, Talks Major Label Debut: Exclusive |website=billboard |last=Rowley |first=Glenn |date=22 January 2020 |accessdate=4 February 2020}}</ref> He signed with [[RCA Records]] in 2019.

==Early life==
He is of [[Italy|Italian]] and [[Liberia]]n descent. Dunbar is openly gay. 
<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rcarecords.com/artist/isaac-dunbar/|title=Isaac Dunbar|accessdate=4 April 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Isaac Dunbar's 'Isaac's Insects': Album Inspirations Interview|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/9354669/isaac-dunbar-isaacs-insects-inspirations-interview|date=2020-04-09|website=Billboard|access-date=2020-05-02}}</ref><ref name=":0">{{Cite web|title=Interview: Isaac Dunbar Talks 'scorton's creek,' New Music & Quarantine|url=https://www.idolator.com/7908593/interview-isaac-dunbar-talks-scortons-creek-new-music-quarantine|date=2020-03-30|website=idolator|access-date=2020-05-02}}</ref>

Isaac first started making music when he was nine, creating [[EDM music]].<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|title=Foundations: Isaac Dunbar|url=https://www.clashmusic.com/features/foundations-isaac-dunbar|website=Clash Magazine|language=en|access-date=2020-05-02}}</ref> As a big [[Lady Gaga]] fan, he was further inspired to learn music production when he discovered the music of [[Madeon]] (one of the producers working on Gaga's ''[[Artpop]]''). He learned synths and music theory through tutorials on [[YouTube]] and by reproducing songs from ''Artpop''.<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1" />

==Career==
On 31 October 2017, the blog We Are Going Solo reposted his track "Pharmacy",<ref>{{Cite web|title=isaac – PHARMACY demo|url=https://www.wearegoingsolo.com/tracks/isaac-pharmacy-demo|last=Shears|first=Jake|date=31 October 2017|website=wearegoingsolo|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|accessdate=4 February 2020}}</ref> drawing wider attention to his work. In late 2018 his tracks "Freshman Year" (which debuted on [[The Fader]] on 2 October)<ref>{{Cite web |url= https://www.thefader.com/2018/10/02/isaac-dunbar-freshman-year-video |title= Relive the best and worst parts of high school by watching this video |website=fader |last=Wang |first=Steffanee |date=2 October 2018 |accessdate=4 February 2020}}</ref> and "Blonde" attracted further notice, with an additional boost when "Pharmacy" was featured on [[Zane Lowe]]'s [[Beats 1]] radio show.<ref>{{Cite web |url= https://sodwee.com/blog/2019/03/isaac-dunbar-knows-how-addicting-love-can-be-self-medicate-with-new-belter-pharmacy/ |title= Isaac Dunbar knows how addicting love can be. Self-medicate with new belter "Pharmacy". |website=sodwee |date=March 2019 |accessdate=5 February 2020}}</ref> On 12 February 2019, Wonderland debuted his single "Mime".<ref>{{Cite web |url= https://www.wonderlandmagazine.com/2019/02/12/premiere-isaac-dunbar-mime/ |title= PREMIERE: ISAAC DUNBAR – "MIME" |website=wonderlandmagazine |date=2 February 2019 |accessdate=4 February 2020}}</ref>

In July 2019 he released his debut EP ''Balloons Don't Float Here'',<ref>{{Cite web |url= https://hypebeast.com/2019/7/isaac-dunbar-balloons-dont-float-here-album-stream |title= Isaac Dunbar Drops Latest EP 'balloons don't float here' |website=hypebeast |last=Zhang |first=Charlie |date=14 July 2020 |accessdate=4 February 2020}}</ref> and Complex premiered his single "Ferrari."<ref>{{Cite web |url= https://www.complex.com/music/2019/07/isaac-dunbar-ferrari |title= Rising Star Isaac Dunbar Drops New Single "isaac's insects" |website=complex |last=Price |first=Joe |date=10 July 2020 |accessdate=4 February 2020}}</ref> In the fall of that year he toured with [[Girl in Red]],<ref>{{Cite web |url= https://www.withguitars.com/isaac-dunbar-returns-with-new-single-body-girl-in-red-support-dates/ |title= Isaac Dunbar returns with new single 'Body' + girl in red support dates |website=withguitars |last=Peters |first=Jo |date=10 October 2019 |accessdate=5 February 2020}}</ref> and in November he appeared on [[MTV]]'s PUSH Live concert series.<ref>{{Cite web |url= http://www.mtv.com/news/3141646/mtv-push-live-october-november-2019/ |title= MTV PUSH LIVE ANNOUNCES FALL 2019 CONCERT SERIES LINEUP |website=mtv |date=8 October 2019 |accessdate=4 February 2020}}</ref>

In October 2019, [[RCA Records]] signed him, scheduling his debut on the label, ''Isaac's Insects,'' for April 2020. The title track was released in January 2020,<ref>{{Cite web |url= https://www.idolator.com/7905835/rising-star-isaac-dunbar-drops-new-single-isaacs-insects |title= Rising Star Isaac Dunbar Drops New Single "isaac's insects" |website=idolator |last=Nied |first=Mike |date=23 January 2020 |accessdate=4 February 2020}}</ref> and that month he was named to [[Idolator (website)|Idolator]]'s list of artists to watch<ref>{{Cite web |url= https://www.idolator.com/7905275/on-the-verge-40-artists-to-watch-in-2020 |title= On The Verge: 40 Artists To Watch In 2020 |website=idolator |last=Wass |first=Mike |date=10 January 2020 |accessdate=31 January 2020}}</ref> and [[E!News]]' "Future Pop Stars You Need to Know Now."<ref>{{Cite web |url= https://www.eonline.com/photos/29977/15-future-pop-stars-you-need-to-know-now/988439 |title= 15 FUTURE POP STARS YOU NEED TO KNOW NOW |website=eonline |last=Lyons |first=Trey |accessdate=31 January 2020}}</ref>''Paper'' then premiered and ''Billboard'' featured the followup video for "Makeup Drawer," a track he'd written when he was 14<ref>{{Cite web |url= https://www.papermag.com/isaac-dunbar-makeup-drawer-premiere-2645196213.html |title= Welcome to Isaac Dunbar's 'Makeup Drawer' |last=Goldfine |first=Jael |website=paper |date=20 February 2020 |accessdate=24 February 2020}}</ref> that had major significance for Isaac as he "explained that he'd been waiting to release this song until he was ready to get honest about his experience with homophobia."<ref>{{Cite web |url= https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/pride/8551555/isaac-dunbar-makeup-drawer-video |title= Isaac Dunbar Delivers a High-Fashion Kiss-Off to Internalized Homophobia With 'Makeup Drawer' |last=Daw |first=Stephen |website=billboard |date=20 February 2020 |accessdate=24 February 2020}}</ref> ''Isaac's Insects'' was released on April 9, 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Isaac Dunbar Drops Excellent 'isaac's insects' EP: Stream|url=https://www.idolator.com/7909167/isaac-dunbar-drops-isaacs-insects-ep-stream-it-here|last=Nied|first=Mike|date=2020-04-09|website=idolator|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2020-05-02}}</ref>

His U.S. and European tour was set to begin on 14 April 2020 at Moroccan Lounge in [[Los Angeles]],<ref name="Rowley"/><ref>{{Cite web |url= https://the360mag.com/isaac-dunbar-isaacs-insects/ |title= Isaac Dunbar – isaac's insects |website=the360mag |accessdate=4 February 2020}}</ref> but it was postponed due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Isaac Dunbar on Instagram: "in these strange times i unfortunately have to announce my european tour this month will be rescheduled to march 2021. please hold onto…"|url=https://www.instagram.com/p/B_2ajrogqeE/|access-date=2020-06-28|website=Instagram|language=en}}</ref>
