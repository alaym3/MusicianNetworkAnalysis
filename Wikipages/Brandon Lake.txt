'''Brandon Lake''' is a lake in [[Douglas County, Minnesota|Douglas County]], in the [[U.S. state]] of [[Minnesota]].<ref>{{GNIS|640437}}</ref>

Brandon Lake was named for John Brandon, a farmer who settled there.<ref>{{cite book|last=Upham|first=Warren|title=Minnesota Geographic Names: Their Origin and Historic Significance|url=https://archive.org/details/minnesotageogra00uphagoog|year=1920|publisher=Minnesota Historical Society|page=[https://archive.org/details/minnesotageogra00uphagoog/page/n200 181]}}</ref>
