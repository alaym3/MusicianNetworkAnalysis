'''Quest''' is an  [[United States|American]] [[jazz]] band, which includes saxophonist [[Dave Liebman]], pianist [[Richie Beirach]], bassist [[Ron McClure]], and drummer [[Billy Hart]].<ref name="LarkinJazz">{{cite book|title=[[Encyclopedia of Popular Music|The Guinness Who's Who of Jazz]]|editor=[[Colin Larkin (writer)|Colin Larkin]]|publisher=[[Guinness Publishing]]|date=1992|edition=First|isbn=0-85112-580-8|page=328/9}}</ref> (The original band line-up included bassists [[George Mraz]] and drummer [[Al Foster]].<ref name="LarkinJazz"/> They were featured on the group's first album only; all subsequent recordings feature the Liebman/Beirach/McClure/Hart line-up.)<ref name="AMG">{{Cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/quest-mn0000859441/biography|title=Quest &#124; Biography & History|website=[[AllMusic]]|access-date=July 31, 2021}}</ref>
