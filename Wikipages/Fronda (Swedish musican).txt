'''Hans ''Sebastian'' Pasquale Fronda''' (born November 3, 1981, Hökåsen, [[Västerås]]) is a [[Sweden|Swedish]] [[Hip hop music|hip-hop]] artist. He released his first studio album in 2004, and toured Sweden in summer 2006. He then took a time-out from doing live performances to work on his next album, ''Fridlysta Frekvenser''. The first single from that album was released on April 11, 2007. He made his stage comeback on the televised radio-show ''Radiogalan'' on April 29, 2007, along with [[Markoolio]], [[Emilia Rydberg]], and [[Sunrise Avenue]].<ref name="expressen1">{{cite news|url=http://www.expressen.se/nyheter/1.614218/sebastian-fronda-ar-tillbaka-med-ny-singel|title=Sebastian Fronda är tillbaka med ny singel|last=Wikström |first=Håkan|date=March 28, 2007|work=[[Expressen]]|accessdate=2009-07-04}}</ref>

Jonas Stentäpp of ''[[Dala-Demokraten]]'' has described Fronda as "one of the most entertaining hip-hop artists" in Sweden for his "heavy beats, unusually interesting choruses, and good flow in the lyrics."<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.dalademokraten.se/ArticlePages/200605/27/20060527091855_DD/20060527091855_DD.dbp.asp|title=Underbart är kort med Fronda|last=Stentäpp|first=Joans|date=May 27, 2006|work=[[Dala-Demokraten]]|accessdate=2009-07-04|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110719092805/http://www.dalademokraten.se/ArticlePages/200605/27/20060527091855_DD/20060527091855_DD.dbp.asp|archivedate=July 19, 2011}}</ref> Fronda participated in the 2008 [[Melodifestivalen]] with the song "Ingen mår så bra som jag", saying that his participation in the contest was "a step in the right direction for me both as a human and as an artist. [Being in Melodifestivalen is] the biggest thing you can experience as an artist in Sweden."<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.sr.se/sida/arkiv.aspx?ProgramID=2521&Max=2008-03-19&Min=2007-01-30&PeriodStart=2008-02-25&Period=2&Artikel=1919220|title=Artisterna anländer till Blekinge. Fronda: "Jag ska äta bullar och springa"|date=February 27, 2008|publisher=[[Sveriges Radio]]|accessdate=2009-07-04}}</ref> The song reached eleventh place on the [[Swedish Singles Chart]] in March 2008.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://vlt.se/nyheter/1.366754|title=Perrelli toppar singellistan|date=March 13, 2008|work=[[Västmanland|Vestmanland]]s Läns Tidning|accessdate=2009-07-04|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090726024023/http://vlt.se/nyheter/1.366754|archivedate=July 26, 2009}}</ref> Fronda was originally going to compete with another song in the 2007 Melodifestivalen, but was disqualified when he refused to sing the song by himself.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.expressen.se/nyheter/1.885935/fronda-jag-ar-helt-forvirrad|title=Fronda: "Jag är helt förvirrad"|last=Wikström |first=Håkan|date=October 17, 2007|work=[[Expressen]]|accessdate=2009-07-04}}</ref> He also participated in the 2004 Swedish celebrity version of ''[[Fort Boyard (TV series)|Fort Boyard]]''.<ref name="folket1">{{cite news|url=http://folket.se/kulturnoje/1.97049|title=Fronda jobbar sig uppåt|last=Leitner|first=Thomas|date=October 14, 2005|work=Folket|accessdate=2009-07-04|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090726024311/http://folket.se/kulturnoje/1.97049|archivedate=July 26, 2009}}</ref>
