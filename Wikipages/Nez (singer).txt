'''Nezihe Kalkan''' (born April 15, 1979), better known as '''Nez''', is a [[Turkish people|Turkish]] singer and dancer known for her modern-oriental hybrid dancing style combined with techno-inspired pop music.<ref>{{Cite news|url = http://www.dhatv.tv/magazin/nez-sokak-ortasinda-kavga-etti-nez-kiminle-ulu-orta-kavga-etti-h2379.html|title = Nez Sokak Ortasında Kavga Etti! Nez Kiminle Ulu Orta Kavga Etti?|date = 29 November 2015|work = DHA TV|access-date = 28 January 2016|language = Turkish|url-status = dead|archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160207083820/http://www.dhatv.tv/magazin/nez-sokak-ortasinda-kavga-etti-nez-kiminle-ulu-orta-kavga-etti-h2379.html|archivedate = 7 February 2016}}</ref>

She spent a long time in the [[United Kingdom|UK]] for education and after returning to her home country in early 2000s, she started performing regularly in a renowned [[jazz]] club in [[Istanbul]] with a unique show. She was an actor in the [[Fox (Turkey)|Fox Turkey's]] sitcom ''Sıkı Dostlar'' ("good friends") with [[Haluk Bilginer]] and [[Özkan Uğur]].
