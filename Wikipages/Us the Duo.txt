'''Us the Duo''' is an American folk [[Pop music|pop]] duo consisting of husband-and-wife Michael and Carissa Alvarado. The duo met in [[Los Angeles]], [[California]], United States, in 2011 on the set of an [[AJ Rafael]] music video. They got married in 2012. In 2013, the duo started uploading six-second [[cover version]]s of popular songs to their [[Vine (service)|Vine]] account, where they amassed over 4.8 million followers as of August 2015. In March 2014, they were signed to [[Republic Records]]. They are reported as being the first musicians to have signed to a major record label due to their presence on Vine.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://time.com/3616217/vine-us-the-duo-2014-songs/|title=Listen to the Hit Songs of 2014 in Just 2.5 Minutes|author=Locker, Melissa|publisher=[[Time (magazine)|TIME]]|date=December 3, 2014|access-date=August 11, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/5944884/folk-pop-duo-us-becomes-vines-first-major-label-signing-exclusive|title=Folk-Pop Duo Us Becomes Vine's First Major Label Signing (Exclusive)|publisher=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|author=Hampp, Andrew|date=March 24, 2014|access-date=August 11, 2015}}</ref>

==Career==
In spring of 2016, Us the Duo joined [[Pentatonix]] on their [[Pentatonix World Tour 2016|2016 World Tour]] visiting various states in the United States, before heading abroad to different venues including London, Paris, Prague, Rome and Tokyo. In addition to their two full-length albums, Us the Duo regularly releases new music, such as the songs "(Stop) Just Love", and "Slow Down Time" as well as a variety of covers, including "2015 Top Hits in 3.5 Minutes". [[Oprah Winfrey]] booked the duo for a tour in November 2014 after she saw the film ''[[The Book of Life (2014 film)|The Book of Life]]'', which featured the duo's song "No Matter Where You Are".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/business/6274202/us-the-duo-oprah-vine-book-of-life|title=Vine Stars Us the Duo Earn Oprah’s Seal of Approval (Plus, a Label Deal and Movie Too)|publisher=Billboard|author=Gallo, Phil|date=October 7, 2014|access-date=August 11, 2015}}</ref> Besides touring with Pentatonix, the duo embarked on the Just Love Tour in support of their album ''Just Love'', which was released on July 22, 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ustheduo.com/tour |title=Tour Dates |publisher=Us The Duo |access-date=August 26, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/UsTheDuo/status/729794941359038464 |title=Us The Duo on Twitter: "new album JUST LOVE available 7.22.16 " |publisher=[[Twitter]] |date=May 9, 2016 |access-date=August 26, 2016}}</ref>

On June 5, 2018, they auditioned for ''[[America's Got Talent (season 13)|America's Got Talent]]'', where they advanced to the next rounds and got eliminated at the semifinals. Shortly after exiting the show, their daughter, Xyla, was born in October of that year.

==Members==
Carissa Alvarado (née Carissa Martin) was born and raised in Los Angeles, California. She is [[Filipino American]]. She started singing at the age of 5 with a church choir, and has a fashion blog called ''Wear I Am.'' <ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vpNxUCiomHA |title=The Last Word: Us The Duo |publisher=YouTube |date=July 9, 2014 |access-date=August 26, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://weariam.co/ |title=Wear I Am |website=Weariam.co |access-date=August 26, 2016}}</ref>
Michael Alvarado was born and raised in [[North Carolina]] and attended [[Appalachian State University]]. He is of [[Puerto Ricans|Puerto Rican]] descent. He started piano lessons at the age of 7, and started singing when he was in a [[garage band]] in high school.
