'''Loona''' (stylized as '''LOOΠΔ'''; {{lang-ko|이달의 소녀|Idarui Sonyeo}}; {{lang-ja|今月の少女|Kongetsu no Shōjo|Girl of the Month}}) is a South Korean [[girl group]] formed by [[Blockberry Creative]]. The group was introduced to the public through a pre-debut project which began in October 2016, where each of the 12 members were revealed in a periodic fashion by releasing a promotional single over the following 18 months.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://star.ohmynews.com/NWS_Web/OhmyStar/at_pg.aspx?CNTN_CD=A0002260168|title='이달의 소녀' 앞으로 기대되는 이유|last=Park|first=Ji-hye|publisher=Oh My Star|language=ko|date=November 13, 2016|access-date=December 20, 2016|archive-date=December 21, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161221162302/http://star.ohmynews.com/NWS_Web/OhmyStar/at_pg.aspx?CNTN_CD=A0002260168|url-status=live}}</ref> They debuted as a full ensemble with the extended play, ''[[+ +|[+ +]]]'' (2018), supported by the lead single "[[Favorite (Loona song)|Favorite]]" and the title track "[[Hi High]]".

==Name==
Loona's English name is derived from the Hangul letters ㅇㄷㅇㅅㄴ, each an [[Hangul#Letter placement within a block|initial consonant]] in the [[Syllable|syllabic]] blocks that make {{lang|ko|이달의 소녀}} (''Idarui Sonyeo''). When rearranged to ㄴㅇㅇㄷㅅ, it resembles LOONA in the Latin alphabet.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://studio-xxx.com/LOONA-The-Debut-Project|title=LOONA: The Debut Project|last=Lee|first=Ji-yoon|website=Studio XXX|language=en|access-date=August 27, 2018|archive-date=November 1, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191101190157/http://studio-xxx.com/LOONA-The-Debut-Project|url-status=live}}</ref>
