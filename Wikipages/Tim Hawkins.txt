'''Timothy Aaron Hawkins''' (born March 30, 1968) is an American [[Christians|Christian]] comedian, songwriter, and singer, best known for parodying popular songs such as [[Carrie Underwood]]'s "[[Jesus, Take the Wheel]]", [[Kansas (band)|Kansas]]'s "[[Dust in the Wind]]", and "[[The Candy Man]]", along with stand-up material based on topics such as marriage, [[homeschooling]], and parenting.<ref name=news>{{cite news |last=Krieg |first=Sheryl |title=Tim Hawkins finds the funny in everyday living |url=http://www.news-sentinel.com/apps/pbcs.dll/article?AID=/20121003/ENT/310039988/1009/LIVING |newspaper=[[The News-Sentinel]] |location=Fort Wayne, Indiana |access-date=September 11, 2020 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141103025007/http://www.news-sentinel.com/apps/pbcs.dll/article?AID=%2F20121003%2FENT%2F310039988%2F1009%2FLIVING |archive-date=November 3, 2014 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.montgomeryadvertiser.com/story/entertainment/2017/07/11/tims-take-share-some-laughs-hawkins-mpac/467623001/|title=Tim's take: Share some laughs with Hawkins at MPAC |last=Heupel |first=Shannon |date=July 11, 2017 |newspaper=[[Montgomery Advertiser]] |access-date=September 11, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite news| url=http://www.modbee.com/entertainment/article78395562.html| title=Tim Hawkins brings good, clean fun to Gallo Center| first=Marijke| last=Rowland| date=May 28, 2016| newspaper=[[The Modesto Bee]]| access-date=September 11, 2020}}</ref> Hawkins began to release his comedy on the Internet, with his videos gaining more than 200 million views on YouTube, [[GodTube]], and Facebook as of early 2013.<ref name=crosswalk>{{cite journal| last=Hatcher| first=Lori| title=Tim Hawkins – Homeschooling Is Something to Laugh About| url=http://www.crosswalk.com/family/homeschool/encouragement/tim-hawkins-homeschooling-is-something-to-laugh-about.html| journal=[[Crosswalk.com]]| access-date=November 3, 2014}}</ref>

==Biography==
Tim has one brother, Todd Hawkins, who is also his stage manager. He married his best friend Heather in 1993 and they have four children (three boys and a girl&nbsp;– Spencer, Olivia, Levi, and Jackson). They live in [[St. Charles, Missouri]].<ref name="crosswalk" /> He played baseball as an outfielder for the [[University of Missouri]].<ref>{{cite web| url=http://www.mutigers.com/sports/m-basebl/spec-rel/062105aah.html| title=Missouri Baseball History| access-date=July 24, 2015| website=[[Missouri Tigers]]}}{{dead link|date=September 2020}}</ref> He is a cousin of [[Freight Mabrey]].<ref>{{cite podcast |host=Tim Hawkins| title=Poddy Break 053 – The Crew – Plivates, Ultraman, and Cannibals |website=timhawkins.net/pages/poddy-break| date=March 2, 2017| url=https://poddybreak.libsyn.com/053-the-crew-plivates-ultraman-and-cannibals| access-date=June 29, 2017}}</ref>
