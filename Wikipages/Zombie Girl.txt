'''Zombie Girl''' is a Canadian [[electro-industrial]]/[[industrial rock]] project started in 2005. Initially composed of Renee Cooper and producer [[Sebastian Komor]], the group now consists exclusively of Cooper, who uses live musicians. The band's lyrics and themes center on [[black humor]] and [[B movie]] [[horror film]]s. Zombie Girl also uses [[rock and roll]]-style grooves with their mostly synthetic instruments.<ref>[http://www.electrolyrics.com/modules.php?name=ETFrame&op=ver&fid=33 Electro Lyrics interview with Zombie Girl]{{dead link|date=July 2016 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref> She has produced two EPs and two full-length albums on the [[Alfa Matrix]] record label. Her albums are sold in the [[United States]] under the [[Metropolis Records]] label.

==History==
Zombie Girl released their first album, ''Back From the Dead'', in 2006.<ref name="yeung">[http://www.allmusic.com/artist/zombie-girl-mn0000713628/biography " Zombie Girl"]. ''AllMusic'', Biography by Neil Z. Yeung </ref>

In 2008 Zombie Girl released a second album, ''[[Blood, Brains and Rock 'N' Roll|Blood, Brains & Rock 'n' Roll]]''.<ref>[http://graveconcernsezine.com/reviews/cd-reviews/ebm-electro-electronica/1892-zombie-girl-blood-brains-and-rock-n-roll.html "Zombie Girl- Blood, Brains and Rock 'n' Roll"]. ''Grave Concerns'', by Matthew Johnson </ref> It was delayed due to three separate printing companies refusing to print artwork that contained copious amounts of blood, as well as a brain on a plate.<ref>[http://vampirefreaks.com/content/comment.php?entry=95&t=Zombie+Girl ''Vampire Freaks'' interview with Zombie Girl] {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080312143620/http://vampirefreaks.com/content/comment.php?entry=95&t=Zombie+Girl |date=March 12, 2008 }}</ref><ref>[http://www.vueweekly.com/zombie_girl/ "ZOMBIE GIRL"]. ''Vue Weekly'', April 18, 2007.</ref>

After Cooper and Komor parted ways, Komor released an EP without her, entitled ''Halloween'', in 2009. Cooper continued as a solo act, backed up by various musicians;<ref name="yeung" /> she released an EP, ''Panic Attack'', and an album, ''Killer Queen'', in 2015.<ref>[http://www.brutalresonance.com/review/zombie-girl-killer-queen/ "Zombie Girl - Killer Queen"]. ''Brutal Resonance'', September 2015.</ref>
