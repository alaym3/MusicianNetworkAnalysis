== Biography ==
Paul Baloche was born on June 4, 1962, in [[Camden, New Jersey]] in a Catholic family.<ref>Jenelle Janci, [https://lancasteronline.com/features/entertainment/paul-baloche-at-landis-hall-saturday-says-new-season-of/article_00cb35c4-b906-11e7-b0a8-a354bfcfb617.html Paul Baloche, at Landis Hall Saturday, says new season of life influenced latest album], lancasteronline.com, USA, October 25, 2017</ref><ref name="rfe">Radio-rfe, [https://www.radio-rfe.com/musiques-medias/paul-baloche Biographie Paul Baloche], radio-rfe.com, France. Retrieved January 23, 2019</ref> He studied at the Grove School of Music in Los Angeles.<ref name="rfe"/>

== Career ==
In 1989, he became a worship pastor at the Community Christian Fellowship Church in Lindale, [[Smith County, Texas]], until 2015 when he moved to New York City.<ref>Masoncountypress, [http://www.masoncountypress.com/2017/03/07/christian-musician-paul-baloche-to-host-competition-concerts/ Christian musician Paul Baloche to host competition, concerts], masoncountypress.com, USA, March 7, 2017</ref><ref>Urbanchristiannews.com, [http://urbanchristiannews.com/2016/09/paul-baloche-release-new-album-mercy-october-7/ Paul Baloche to Release New Album, “Your Mercy,” October 7], urbanchristiannews.com, USA, September 21, 2016</ref> In 1992, he released his first album ''He is Faithful''.<ref>Timothy Yap, [http://www.hallels.com/articles/1244/20140314/paul-baloche-live-album-review.htm Paul Baloche “Live” Album Review], hallels.com, USA, March 14, 2014</ref>

Top-ranked songs include "[[Open the Eyes of My Heart]]", "Hosanna", "Your Name", "Today Is the Day", and "[[Above All (song)|Above All]]".<ref name="CCLI"/><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.praisecharts.com/ccli-top-100/|title=CCLI Top 100 Christian Worship Song Trends |publisher= PraiseCharts (Sonreign Media, Inc.) |access-date=July 15, 2014}}</ref> Baloche has received numerous [[Dove Awards]] over the years. His song "Above All" was nominated for Song of the Year in both the [[2002 Dove Award Nominees|2002]] and [[2003 Dove Award Nominees|2003]] Dove Awards, and in 2002, Michael W. Smith's version of the same song won Inspirational Recorded Song of the Year. Baloche was also nominated for Songwriter of the Year in 2002.<ref>{{citation |title=33rd Annual Dove Award Nominations Announced Today |date=January 24, 2002 |url=http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1G1-82081272.html |ref=PR Newswire}}{{dead link|date=February 2019|bot=medic}}{{cbignore|bot=medic}}</ref> In 2009, Baloche won a Dove Award for Inspiration Recorded Song of the Year as co-author of "A New Hallelujah" with [[Michael W. Smith]] and his wife Debbie Smith.<ref>[http://www.doveawards.com/doveawards/09winners.php 2009 Dove Award Winners] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090427074357/http://www.doveawards.com/doveawards/09winners.php |date=April 27, 2009 }} 2009.</ref>

Baloche has been a pioneer in providing visual instructional resources for church worship teams. In 1995, he released ''The Worship Guitar Series'', a set of three videos. Between 2002 and 2006, he released ''The Modern Worship Series'', a set of eight instructional DVDs focused on helping church worship teams increase their skill and confidence. In 2005, he released the book ''God Songs'', a practical resource for worship songwriters, currently in its 8th printing.

His catalog of twelve albums is focused on providing [[worship music]] for churches to use in their Sunday morning services. His albums are released by his publisher [[Integrity Music]]. In addition to songwriting, Baloche participates in several seminars for worship leaders and musicians throughout North America and Asia.<ref name=06dates>{{citation |title=New Paul Baloche CD And Tour Dates |year=2006 |url=http://www.verticalmusic.com/index.php?page=news&ID=72}}</ref>

The 2006 release ''[[A Greater Song]]'' is a live recording featuring songs by Baloche, some co-written with worship artists including [[Matt Redman]] ("A Greater Song") and former Integrity Music president [[Don Moen (singer)|Don Moen]] ("Thank You Lord").<ref name=06dates/>

The 2009 release ''Glorious'' featured the single "Just to Be With You", which Baloche explained "is simply about intimacy with God."<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.songfacts.com/detail.php?id=18064 |title=Just To Be With You Songfacts |publisher=Songfacts.com |access-date=January 10, 2013}}</ref>

On April 3, 2012 Baloche released album ''The Same Love'' featuring the single "The Same Love".<ref name=allmusic>{{cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/the-same-love-mw0002303434|title=The Same Love – Paul Baloche|last=Ham|first=Robert|date=March 13, 2013|publisher=AllMusic|access-date=July 25, 2013}}</ref>

On October 29, 2013, Baloche also released a live Christmas worship album featured old and new songs suitable for use during the Christmas season.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://theundergroundsite.com/exclusive-paul-baloche-talks-about-new-christmas-album-christmas-worship |title=Exclusive: Paul Baloche talks about new Christmas album, ‘Christmas Worship’ |publisher=The Underground |access-date=February 18, 2014}}</ref>

In November 2013, Baloche won a [[GMA Canada]] [[Covenant Award]] for International Album of the Year for ''Glorieux'', a French language worship album.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.gmacanada.ca/winners/|title=2013 – 35th Annual Covenant Awards|publisher=Gospel Music Association of Canada|access-date=July 15, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131111165903/http://www.gmacanada.ca/winners/|archive-date=November 11, 2013|url-status=dead}}</ref>

Some of Baloche's songs rank among the most-performed songs in [[Service of worship|services]] according to [[Christian Copyright Licensing International|CCLI]].<ref name=CCLI>{{cite web|url=https://us.songselect.com/search/ccli-top-songs|title=CCLI Top 100|date=July 15, 2014|publisher=Church Copyright Licensing International|access-date=July 15, 2014|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160413094803/https://us.songselect.com/search/ccli-top-songs|archive-date=April 13, 2016}}</ref>

Baloche has a son, David, with whom he has collaborated on the 2017 album ''Labyrinth''.
