'''Boyish''' is an [[indie rock]] duo that got its start in [[Boston]] before relocating to [[New York City]] in 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Steindorf|first=Jack|date=2019-10-14|title=Rising indie band Boyish releases hypnotizing new "July" visuals|url=https://earmilk.com/2019/10/14/rising-indie-band-boyish-releases-hypnotizing-new-july-visuals/|access-date=2020-06-28|website=EARMILK|language=en-US}}</ref> The group currently consists of lead vocalist India Shore, vocalist and guitar player Claire Altendahl, background vocalist Sylvie Chiang, drummer Aleksi Godard, and bass player Jules Chappert.<ref>{{Cite web|title=BOYISH|url=https://boyishmusic.com/band|access-date=2020-06-28|website=Boyish|language=en-US}}</ref>

The band was formed by Shore and Altendahl for an audition at [[Berklee College of Music]] under the moniker The Blue before changing their name to Boyish.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-02-04|title=QUEEROPEDIA: Boyish|url=https://highclouds.org/queeropedia-playlist-curated-by-boyish/|access-date=2020-06-28|website=HighClouds|language=en-US}}</ref> They have released two studio albums to date: ''Carnation'' and ''Garden Spider.'' The former was nominated for an [[Independent Music Awards (IMAs)|Independent Music Awards]],<ref>{{Cite web|title=The 17th Independent Music Awards Nominees & Winners|url=https://independentmusicawards.com/17th-independent-music-awards-nominees/|access-date=2020-06-28|website=Independent Music Awards|language=en-US}}</ref> featured in the [[The New Yorker|New Yorker]],<ref>{{Cite magazine|title=Boyish|url=https://www.newyorker.com/goings-on-about-town/night-life/boyish|access-date=2020-06-28|magazine=The New Yorker|language=en}}</ref> and now streamed over 1 million times. The single 'FUCK YOU HEATHER' from ''Garden Spider'' premiered on [[Atwood Magazine]] and was played over 500,000 times on Spotify.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-05-18|title=Premiere: Boyish's "FUCK YOU HEATHER" Is a Stunning, Raw, & Impassioned Upheaval|url=http://atwoodmagazine.com/fuhb-boyish-fuck-you-heather-song-premiere/|access-date=2020-06-28|website=Atwood Magazine|language=en-US}}</ref> In February of 2021, Boyish released "Superstar" the first track off their upcoming EP.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-02-09|title= Boyish announce new EP with "Superstar" video|url=:https://highclouds.org/boyish-superstar-video//|access-date=2020-06-28|website=HighClouds|language=en-US}}</ref> It received positive write-ups from [[Lyrical Lemonade]], [[Highclouds]], and Atwood Magazine. Additionally, the song was added to several editorial playlists such as Lorem, All New Indie, Indie Pop & Chill, and Fresh Finds.
