'''Dallas Witherspoon''' (born August 28, 2001), better known by his stage name '''Teejayx6''', is an American rapper. Emerging from the Detroit scene in the late 2010's, he made a name for himself for rapping about [[confidence trick|scamming]], [[identity theft]] and [[credit card fraud]] with the song "Dark Web" released in 2019.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://journals.uic.edu/ojs/index.php/spir/article/view/11353|title=FNCKING WITH WHITE FRAMES: TEEJAYX6 SCAM RAPS THE DIGITAL|author=Tynes, Robert; Pearl, Alex|year=2020|website=[[Association of Internet Researchers|AoIR Selected Papers of Internet Research]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://centralsauce.com/teejayx6-kasher-quon-scammer-raps|title=In Conversation: Are Scammer Raps Up Next?|date=July 13, 2019|website=CentralSauce Collective}}</ref> He is called the originator of this subgenre, calling it "scam rap."<ref>{{cite web|url=https://hotspotatl.com/3783462/teejayx6-explains-black-air-force-activity-claims-title-a-originator-of-scammer-rap/|title=TeeJayx6 Explains Black Air Force Activity & Claims Title As Originator Of Scammer Rap|author=Shepard, Lalaa|date=January 13, 2020|website=[[WHTA]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/lifestyle/style/the-years-best-rappers-sounded-ahead-of-their-time-literally/2019/12/13/bfed45de-1d0c-11ea-87f7-f2e91143c60d_story.html|title=The year’s best rappers sounded ahead of their time. Literally.|date=December 13, 2019|author=Richards, Chris|website=[[The Washington Post]]}}</ref> He is also known for collaborating with fellow Detroit rapper [[Kasher Quon]] with their "Dynamic Duo" series.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://pitchfork.com/thepitch/detroit-is-2019s-most-important-rap-scenehere-are-the-best-songs-from-the-city-this-year/|title=Detroit is 2019’s Most Important Rap Scene—Here Are the Best Songs From the City This Year|author=Pierre, Alphonse|date=August 22, 2019|website=[[Pitchfork (website)|Pitchfork]]}}</ref>

== Career ==
On June 13, 2019, he released his debut album ''Under Pressure''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/teejayx6-cant-stop-getting-paid-on-cashapp-new-song.1988958.html|title=Teejayx6 Can't Stop Getting Paid On "CashApp"|date=October 11, 2020|website=HotNewHipHop}}</ref> On August 13, 2019, he released the video for the single "Swipe Story" after he staged an arrest by the [[United States Marshals Service]] for a promotional stunt during a show in [[Los Angeles]], and later staging his “release”.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.elevatormag.com/teejayx6-drops-swipe-story-visuals|title=Teejayx6 drops “Swipe Story” visuals|website=Elevator|date=August 14, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://lyricallemonade.com/p/swipe-story-teejayx6|title=Swipe Story – [Teejayx6]|date=August 14, 2019|website=[[Lyrical Lemonade]]|author=Harvey, Brodie}}</ref>

In June 2020, he released "Black Lives Matter," hiring producer [[TM88]] and speaks about the [[Black Lives Matter]] movement and how he feels as a Black man in America.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://lyricallemonade.com/p/black-lives-matter-teejayx6|title=Black Lives Matter – [Teejayx6]|date=June 5, 2020|author=Adams, Danny|website=[[Lyrical Lemonade]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.xxlmag.com/hip-hop-songs-police-brutality/|title=Here Are the Poignant Hip-Hop Songs Addressing Police Brutality, Racial Inequality and Social Justice Right Now Read More: Poignant Hip-Hop Songs Addressing Police Brutality|author=Johnson, Zoe; Coleman II, C. Vernon|date=June 15, 2020}}</ref> The song pays tribute to [[George Floyd]] as well.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://pitchfork.com/news/teejayx6-and-tm88-release-black-lives-matter-protest-song/|title=Teejayx6 and TM88 Release “BLACK LIVES MATTER” Protest Song|date=June 3, 2020|author=Torres, Eric|website=[[Pitchfork (website)|Pitchfork]]}}</ref>

On December 13, 2019, he released ''Black Air Force Activity 1'', which included the single "Dark Web."<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.thefader.com/2019/12/13/teejayx6-black-air-force-activity-1|title=Teejayx6 is beyond savage on his Black Air Force Activity 1 project|date=December 13, 2019|author=Hahn, Bryan|website=[[The Fader]]}}</ref> In February 2020, he released the single "On Tour" a song written on an airplane while on tour.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://closedcap.com/2020/02/04/teejayx6-throws-down-on-tour/|title=TeejayX6 Throws Down on Tour|date=February 4, 2020|website=Closed Captioned}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://hiphopcanada.com/teejayx6-ontour-video/|title=Detroit ‘scam rap’ artist Teejayx6 drops new visuals for “On Tour”|date=February 20, 2020|website=HipHopCanada}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://hypebeast.com/2020/2/teejayx6-takes-it-back-to-his-city-for-new-on-tour-music-video|title=Teejayx6 Takes it Back to His City for New "On Tour" Music Video|date=February 29, 2020|author=Zhang, Charlie|website=Hypebeast}}</ref>

In May 2020, he was scammed for a feature by OBN Dev and was chased by Dev.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://jukeboxculture.com/teejayx6-scammed-for-feature-ran-out-of-hood-by-obn-dev/|title=Teejayx6 scammed for feature, ran out of hood by OBN Dev|date=May 18, 2020|website=Jukebox Culture}}</ref>

In August 2020, he collaborated with [[NLE Choppa]] with the single "Punchin."<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.complex.com/music/2020/08/teejayx6-nle-choppa-punchin-video|title=Premiere: Teejayx6 and NLE Choppa Bring the Energy With “Punchin” Video|author=Price, Joe|date=August 13, 2020|website=[[Complex Networks]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://lyricallemonade.com/p/punchin-teejayx6-ft-nle-choppa|title=Punchin – [Teejayx6] ft. [NLE Choppa]|author=Adams, Danny|date=August 13, 2020|website=[[Lyrical Lemonade]]}}</ref> That same month, he released ''Black Air Force Activity: The Reload''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://pitchfork.com/news/teejayx6-releases-new-mixtape-black-air-force-activity-the-reload-listen/|title=Teejayx6 Releases New Mixtape Black Air Force Activity: The Reload: Listen|author=Sodomsky, Sam|date=August 28, 2020|website=[[Pitchfork (website)|Pitchfork]]}}</ref> That same month, he released "Blackmail"In October, he released the album ''Spooky'', which included the song "Stimulus Package" with Kasher Quon, Bandgang Lonnie, [[Boldy James]] and [[The Alchemist (musician)|Alchemist]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://sports.yahoo.com/listen-teejayx6-mixtape-spooky-042544794.html?|title=Listen to Teejayx6’s New Mixtape Spooky|author=Bloom, Madison|date=October 30, 2020|website=[[Yahoo! Sports]]}}</ref>
