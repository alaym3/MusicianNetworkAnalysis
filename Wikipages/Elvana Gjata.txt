'''Elvana Gjata''' ({{IPA-sq|ɛlˈvana ɟaˈta|lang}}; born 3 February 1987), also known mononymously as '''Elvana''', is an Albanian singer, songwriter, actress, composer and entrepreneur.{{efn|name=fn1|<ref name="TeksteShqip">{{cite web |publisher=TeksteShqip |title=Elvana Gjata–Biografia |url=https://www.teksteshqip.com/elvana-gjata |access-date=9 February 2020 |language=sq}}</ref><ref name="Top Awards">{{cite web |publisher=[[Top Channel]] |title=Elvana Gjata |url=http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/artist/elvana-gjata/ |access-date=28 December 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201021202536/http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/artist/elvana-gjata/ |archive-date=21 October 2020 |language=sq |quote=Stili i muzikës së Elvanës përmban frymat R&B, Hip Hop dhe Pop.}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |publisher=MelodyTv |title=Elvana Gjata Biografia |url=https://melodytv.al/artists/elvana-gjata/ |access-date=7 February 2021 |language=sq}}</ref>}} Born and raised in [[Tirana]], she has been referred to as the "Diva of Albanian music" and is noted for her versatility in music, performance and presentation.{{efn|name=fn2|<ref>{{cite web |publisher=Gazeta Blic |title=A është Elvana Gjata diva e muzikës shqiptare? In 2003, Gjata unsuccessfully attempted to [[Albania in the Eurovision Song Contest 2004|represent Albania]] in the [[Eurovision Song Contest 2004]], after her participation in the 42nd edition of [[Festivali i Këngës]] with the song "Pranë Teje", which she dedicated to God.<ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[RTSH|Radio Televizioni Shqiptar]] (RTSH) |title=Elvana Gjata{{spaced ndash}}Me tana |url=https://festivali.rtsh.al/kenget-konkurruese/elvana-gjata-me-tana |access-date=28 December 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201201125500/https://festivali.rtsh.al/kenget-konkurruese/elvana-gjata-me-tana |archive-date=1 December 2020 |language=sq |quote=Një vit më pas, Elvana merr pjesë për herë të parë në Festivalin e Këngës me këngën e shkruar nga vetë ajo, 'Pranë teje'.}}</ref> In 2004, she released her first single "Të Kam Xhan" composed for her participation in [[Kënga Magjike]] by her future collaborator and manager [[Flori Mumajesi]]. == Other ventures ==

Throughout her career, Gjata has ventured into other businesses and industries, including film, fashion and design. In 2014, she has posed for the cover of the British ''Velvet'' and Albanian ''Anabel'' magazines, respectively.<ref>{{cite web |publisher=Anabel |title=Elvana Gjata në kopertinën e revistës ndërkombëtare |url=https://www.anabelmagazine.com/artikull/2175/elvana-gjata-ne-coverin-e-revistes-nderkombetare/ |access-date=11 February 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210211233724/https://www.anabelmagazine.com/artikull/2175/elvana-gjata-ne-coverin-e-revistes-nderkombetare/ |archive-date=11 February 2021 |language=sq |date=1 June 2014}}</ref><ref name="15 moments"/> In 2016, she first entered the fashion industry partnering with international Albanian fashion designer Valdrin Sahiti to launch the exclusive gown collection line "Lejla".<ref>{{cite web |publisher=Zëri |title=Elvana Gjata këngëtarja me vëmendjen më të madhe |url=https://zeri.info/showbiz/118596/elvana-gjata-kengetarja-me-vemendjen-me-te-madhe-video/ |access-date=11 February 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181128034321/https://zeri.info/showbiz/118596/elvana-gjata-kengetarja-me-vemendjen-me-te-madhe-video/ |archive-date=28 November 2018 |language=sq |date=25 November 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Klan Kosova|Privé]] |title=Ekskluzive: Elvana flet për magjinë 'Lejla' |url=https://prive.al/30509-2/ |access-date=11 February 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200924074645/https://prive.al/30509-2/ |archive-date=24 September 2020 |language=sq |date=4 October 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last=Hoxha |first=E. |publisher=Iconstyle |title=E patë klipin 'Lejla'?
