'''Taylor Cameron Upsahl''' (born November 28, 1998), known professionally as '''Upsahl''' (stylized as '''UPSAHL'''), is an American singer-songwriter and multi-instrumentalist.

== Early life ==
Born and raised in Phoenix, AZ, Upsahl started playing both the guitar and piano at the age of 5. By the time she was 14, Upsahl had written and released a self-titled EP (under the name Taylor Upsahl), which gained popularity throughout the Phoenix music scene. A graduate of the Arizona School for the Arts, a performing arts middle and high school, Upsahl continued to hone her craft, while being classically trained on piano, guitar and choir every day. A local darling for national touring acts in Phoenix, Upsahl played the McDowell Mountain Music Festival in 2017, opening for The Shins, Beck, and [[Flume (musician)|Flume]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Escudero|first=Nicki|title=16-Year-Old Taylor Upsahl's Songwriting Will Blow You Away|url=https://www.phoenixnewtimes.com/music/16-year-old-taylor-upsahls-songwriting-will-blow-you-away-7820135|access-date=2021-08-17|website=Phoenix New Times|language=en}}</ref>

== Career ==

Upsahl released a self-titled EP at age 14. In 2015, she wrote and self-produced her full-length debut album, "Viscerotonic." In 2017, her third album was released, "Unfamiliar Light."<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://joyofviolentmovement.com/tag/upsahl-unfamiliar-light/|title = Upsahl Unfamiliar Light}}</ref>

Upsahl signed a recording deal with Arista Records in the summer of 2018. She was the first artist signed to the newly re-launched record label.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Upsahl Signs to Arista Records, Makes Vibrant Debut With 'The Other Team'|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8482849/upsahl-signs-arista-records-the-other-team|access-date=2021-09-29|website=Billboard|language=en}}</ref>

Upsahl released her first single, "Can You Hear Me Now", in 2017.<ref name=": 1">{{Cite web|author=allmusic|title=UPSAHL Biography & History {{!}} AllMusic|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/upsahl-mn0003856669/biography|access-date=July 5, 2020|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.lofficielusa.com/music/rising-artist-upsahl-premiere-music-2019 | title = Rising Musician UPSAHL |access-date=July 5, 2020 | language = en}}</ref> On March 8, 2019, she released her debut [[Extended play|EP]] ''Hindsight 20/20'' along with her single "Drugs."{{citation needed|date=April 2021}}

In 2020, Upsahl released the single "12345SEX".<ref>{{cite web | url = https://1883magazine.com/upsahl-12345sex-exclusive-premiere/ | title = UPSAHL – 12345SEX – Exclusive Premiere | date = March 27, 2020 | access-date= July 5, 2020 | language = en}}</ref> This was followed by the singles "People I Don't Like" and "MoneyOnMyMind", both songs were included on her second EP, ''Young Life Crisis''. In 2021, she released the singles "Douchebag", "Melatonin", "Time of my Life", and "Lunatic". All songs are featured on her debut studio album, ''Lady Jesus,'' which was released October 8, 2021.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-09-27|title=UPSAHL Announces Tracklist for Upcoming Album ‘Lady Jesus’|url=https://www.nerdsandbeyond.com/2021/09/27/upsahl-announces-tracklist-for-upcoming-album-lady-jesus/|access-date=2021-09-29|website=Nerds & Beyond|language=en-US}}</ref>
