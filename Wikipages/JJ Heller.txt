'''Jennifer Judy '''"'''JJ'''"''' Heller''' (born October 19, 1980, ''[[Married and maiden names|née]]'' Kellner) is an American [[Christian music|Christian]] [[folk music|folk singer]]. After releasing multiple albums, she had two songs that charted. "Your Hands" from her 2008 album ''Painted Red'' and "What Love Really Means" from the album ''When I'm with You'' both peaked at number 13.

==Career==
Heller grew up in [[San Jose, California]] in a family with strong Christian values.<ref name=ChristianMusic.com>{{cite web|title=JJ Heller Interview|url=http://christianmusic.com/jj_heller/jj_heller_interview.htm|publisher=ChristianMusic.com|accessdate=February 22, 2012}}</ref> She didn't listen to music much as a child.<ref name=ChristianMusic.com /> She attended high school at [[Valley Christian High School (San Jose, California)|Valley Christian High School]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://valleychristian.imodules.com/s/211/social.aspx?sid=211&gid=1&pgid=338|title=Alumni in the News|work=Valley Christian Schools Alumni|accessdate=4 October 2017}}</ref> and then went on to attend college at San Jose Christian College (now [[William Jessup University]])<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.jessup.edu/media/events/event/live-in-concert-josh-wilson-jason-gray-jj-heller/|title=Live in Concert – Josh Wilson, Jason Gray, JJ Heller|work=William Jessup University|accessdate=4 October 2017}}</ref> and began writing songs in her second year.<ref name=ChristianMusic.com /> She joined a band which played at coffee shops in the area; the band included her future husband Dave Heller.<ref name=ChristianMusic.com /> He continues to collaborate with writing, recording and producing her works.<ref name=AllMusicBiography />

Heller released multiple albums before she had her first chart appearance in 2009. Her song "Your Hands", from her album ''Painted Red'' hit number 13 on the ''Billboard'' [[Christian Songs]] chart.<ref name=AllMusicSinglesBillboard>{{cite web|title=Billboard singles|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/jj-heller-p712217/charts-awards/billboard-singles|publisher=[[AllMusic]]|accessdate=February 22, 2012}}</ref>

Heller released her next album ''When I'm with You'' in 2010 and it charted, peaking at number 33 on ''Billboard''{{'}}s [[Christian Albums]].<ref>{{cite web|title=JJ Heller album charts|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/jj-heller-p712217/charts-awards|publisher=[[AllMusic]]|accessdate=February 22, 2012}}</ref> She released "What Love Really Means" as the first single from the album and it peaked at number 13 on the ''Billboard'' Singles chart.<ref name=AllMusicSinglesBillboard /> In 2011 she released her ''Deeper'' album, which is a collection of acoustic songs, which were put aside to allow her to release the more pop-oriented ''When I'm with You''.<ref name=JFHDeeperReview /> ''When I'm With You'' was covered by [[Jennifer Love Hewitt]]'s character "Riley Parks" on Season 2 Episode 6 of "[[The Client List (TV series)|The Client List]]" entitled "Unanswered Prayers". The shortened cover was released on [[iTunes]] on April 9, 2013.

Heller released ''Loved'' on March 12, 2013.{{Citation needed|date=January 2012}} Her song "Control" was featured on Season 7 Episode 19 of ''One Tree Hill''.{{Citation needed|date=March 2013}}

==Musical style==
She credits [[Damien Rice]] and [[Patty Griffin]] as influences to her style.<ref>{{cite web|last=Jones|first=Jennifer E. |title=JJ Heller: The Pen of A Ready Songwriter| url=http://www.cbn.com/cbnmusic/interviews/jej_JJHeller1107.aspx |publisher=[[Christian Broadcasting Network]]|accessdate=February 22, 2012}}</ref> [[AllMusic]] describes her sound as being comparable with [[Jewel (singer)|Jewel]], [[Alison Krauss]], [[Sara Groves]] and Griffin.<ref name=AllMusicBiography>{{cite web|title=Biography|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/jj-heller-p712217/biography|publisher=[[AllMusic]]|accessdate=February 22, 2012}}</ref>
