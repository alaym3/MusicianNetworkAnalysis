'''Trim Ademi''' ({{IPA-sq|tɾim adɛmi}}; born 29 February 1992), known professionally as '''Capital T''', is a Kosovo-Albanian rapper, singer, songwriter and philanthropist.<ref name="Biography">{{cite web |publisher=TeksteShqip |title=Capital T–Biografia |url=https://www.teksteshqip.com/capital-t |access-date=13 February 2020 |language=sq}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Top Channel]] |title=Capital T #Bio |url=http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/artist/capital-t/ |access-date=12 February 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201025163641/http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/artist/capital-t/ |archive-date=25 October 2020 |language=sq}}</ref> Born and raised in [[Pristina]], his uncle introduced him to music and rapping at an early age. He rose to recognition in the [[Albanian language|Albanian-speaking]] world, after the release of his first two [[studio album|album]]s ''Replay'' (2010) and ''KAPO'' (2012).<ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Telegrafi]] |title=Capital T, për herë të parë në 'Kënga Magjike' |url=https://telegrafi.com/capital-t-per-here-te-pare-ne-kenga-magjike/ |language=Albanian |date=23 September 2010}}</ref> Due to the success of his albums, he founded his own record label Authentic Entertainment. He has more than 400 million views on [[YouTube]] being among the most viewed artist in the Albanian music industry.<ref>{{cite web |title=Capital T–Channel Info |url=https://www.youtube.com/user/TrueKapo/about |website=youtube.com}}</ref> He is also among the most followed artists on [[Instagram]] in [[Albania]], Kosovo and other Albanian-speaking territories.<ref>{{cite web |title=Të famshmit shqiptarë me mbi 1 milionë ndjekës në Instagram |url=https://reporteri.net/show-biz/te-famshmit-shqiptare-me-mbi-1-milione-ndjekes-ne-instagram/ |website=reporteri.net |language=Albanian |date=21 August 2019}}</ref>

== Life and career ==

Trim Ademi was born on 29 February 1992 into an [[Albanians|Albanian]] family in the city of [[Pristina]], then part of the [[Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia]], present [[Kosovo]].<ref name="Biography"/> His father, [[Agim Ademi]], is a former professional football player and current president of the [[Football Federation of Kosovo]].<ref>{{cite web |publisher=Revista Who |title=Babai i Capital T është shumë i njohur në Kosovë, por profesioni i tij nuk ka aspak lidhje me muzikën |url=https://www.revistawho.com/lajme/babai-i-capital-t-eshte-shume-i-njohur-ne-kosove-por-profesioni-i-tij-nuk-ka-aspak-lidhje-me-muziken/ |access-date=3 January 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200103195818/https://www.revistawho.com/lajme/babai-i-capital-t-eshte-shume-i-njohur-ne-kosove-por-profesioni-i-tij-nuk-ka-aspak-lidhje-me-muziken/ |archive-date=3 January 2020 |language=sq |date=28 March 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[IN TV (Albania)|IN TV]] |title=WOW, se dinim! S'do ta besoni kush është babai i Capital T, qenka i famshëm si i biri! |url=https://www.intv.al/wow-se-dinim-s-do-ta-besoni-kush-eshte-babai-i-capital-t-qenka-i-famshem-si-i-biri-foto-90132 |access-date=3 January 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200103195821/https://www.intv.al/wow-se-dinim-s-do-ta-besoni-kush-eshte-babai-i-capital-t-qenka-i-famshem-si-i-biri-foto-90132 |archive-date=3 January 2020 |language=sq |date=28 March 2019}}</ref> Ademi attended the Asim Vokshi Elementary School and enrolled, upon his graduation, at the [[Sami Frashëri High School]] in Pristina.<ref>{{cite web |title="Të gjitha ëndrrat më filluan këtu", Capital T sjell imazhe nga vizita që i bëri shkollës fillore |url=https://lajmi.net/te-gjitha-endrrat-me-filluan-ketu-capital-t-sjell-imazhe-nga-vizita-qe-i-beri-shkolles-fillore/ |website=lajmi.net |accessdate=25 November 2019 |language=Albanian |date=3 September 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Top Music Awards]] |title=Capital TBio |url=http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/artist/capital-t/ |website=topawards.top-channel.tv |accessdate=25 November 2019 |language=Albanian |quote=Trim Ademi i njohur si Capital-T kreu shkollën e mesme pranë Gjimnazit “Sami Frashëri“ në Prishtinë.}}</ref>
  
Ademi commenced his early music career in 2008 and debuted with the single "Shopping" by 2po2 featuring Capital T. The same year, he released his second single "1 Mëngjes" in collaboration with Albanian singer Adelina Thaqi.<ref name="Biography"/> The following year, he released almost three singles including "Shum Nalt" in collaboration with 2po2 and Albanian singer [[Dafina Zeqiri]]. In 2010, he announced his debut [[studio album]] ''Replay'' and released the record in the same year. That year, he participated with Eni Koçi in the 11th edition of [[Kënga Magjike]] with the song "Diva".<ref>{{cite web |title=Capital T, për herë të parë në "Kënga Magjike" |url=https://telegrafi.com/capital-t-per-here-te-pare-ne-kenga-magjike/ |website=telegrafi.com |accessdate=3 February 2020 |language=Albanian |date=23 September 2010}}</ref> The singer released his debut [[studio album]] "Replay" in 2010, his second album "Kapo" in 2012 which was followed by his third album "Slumdog Millionaire" in 2015.<ref name="Biography"/> His fourth album "Winter Is Here" was released through Authentic Entertainment in 2017 and achieved commercial success in [[Switzerland]] whereas it debuted on the [[Swiss Hitparade|Swiss Album Charts]] at number sixty-nine. In June 2016, he won the award for the "Video of the Year" at the [[Top Music Awards 2016]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Këta janë të gjithë laureatët e "Top Awards" |url=https://telegrafi.com/live-gjithcka-rreth-ceremonise-se-ndarjes-se-cmimeve-ne-top-awards-video/ |website=telegrafi.com |accessdate=25 November 2019 |language=Albanian |date=24 June 2016}}</ref>
