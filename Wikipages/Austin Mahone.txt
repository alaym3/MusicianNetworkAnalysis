'''Austin Harris Mahone''' ({{IPAc-en|m|ə|'|h|oʊ|n}};<ref name="y100.iheart.com">{{cite web|url=http://y100.iheart.com/onair/mack-in-the-afternoon-22391/austin-mahone-tells-us-a-secret-15387694/|title=Austin Mahone tells us a secret, his middle name.....|publisher=y100.iheart.com|access-date=February 19, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170220013632/http://y100.iheart.com/onair/mack-in-the-afternoon-22391/austin-mahone-tells-us-a-secret-15387694/|archive-date=February 20, 2017|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.seventeen.com/celebrity/a34453/celebrity-name-pronunciations/|title=11 Celeb Names You're Totally Pronouncing Wrong|work=[[Seventeen (American magazine)|Seventeen]]|date=2017-07-31|access-date=2017-11-19}}</ref> born April 4, 1996) is an American singer and songwriter. Mahone gained popularity performing covers of pop songs on [[YouTube]]. He released three albums to date. Famous releases include "[[Say Somethin (Austin Mahone song)|Say Somethin]]", "[[Say You're Just a Friend]]" featuring [[Flo Rida]], "[[What About Love (Austin Mahone song)|What About Love]]", "[[Mmm Yeah]]" with [[Pitbull (rapper)|Pitbull]]. After being dropped by his label, he continues releasing music independently.

==Early life==
Austin Harris Mahone<ref name="y100.iheart.com"/> was born in [[San Antonio, Texas]], the son of Michele Lee (née Demyanovich) and Charles Edgar Mahone.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:V81R-YFF|title=Person Details for Austin Mahone, "Texas Birth Index, 1903-1997" — FamilySearch.org}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.porterloring.com/memsol.cgi?user_id=733012|title=In Memory of Mary Loretta Demyanovich – Porter Loring Funeral Home, San Antonio, TX|access-date=July 10, 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304042611/http://www.porterloring.com/memsol.cgi?user_id=733012|archive-date=March 4, 2016|url-status=dead|df=mdy-all}}</ref> His father died when he was a year and a half old, and he was raised as a [[Catholic Church|Catholic]]<ref>{{cite web|url= https://hollowverse.com/austin-mahone/ |title= Austin Mahone}}</ref> by his single mother.<ref>[http://www.details.com/celebrities-entertainment/music-and-books/201306/tween-idol-austin-mahone-pop-singer?kw=movies_music Could Austin Mahone Be Bigger Than Bieber?: Music + Books]. Details. Retrieved on December 5, 2013.</ref> Mahone is of [[English people|English]], [[French people|French]], [[Germans|German]], and [[Slovaks|Slovak]] descent.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.everyjoe.com/2013/08/23/entertainment/austin-mahone-race-ethnicity-nationality-religion-mexican/#1 |title=Why You Need to Learn the Name Austin Mahone |publisher=everyjoe.com |date=2013-08-23 |access-date=2017-02-19 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170220014536/http://www.everyjoe.com/2013/08/23/entertainment/austin-mahone-race-ethnicity-nationality-religion-mexican/#1 |archive-date=February 20, 2017 |url-status=dead }}</ref> He attended [[Lady Bird Johnson High School]] in San Antonio but left after his freshman year and was homeschooled at his grandmother's house.<ref>{{cite web| url=http://hollywoodlife.com/celeb/austin-mahone/ |title=Austin Mahone |publisher=Hollywood Life |date=1996-04-04 |access-date=2014-05-30}}</ref>

===2015–2016: ''This Is Not the Album'' and ''ForMe+You''===
During 2015, he continued to independently release new music for his fans. === 2017–2018: ''Dirty Work'' and ''Oxygen'' ===
"Lady" served as the lead single, a remix of the 2001 "[[Lady (Hear Me Tonight)]]" by [[Modjo]], featuring [[Pitbull (rapper)|Pitbull]]. The song was released as a preview on December 22, 2016, and released officially on April 19, 2017.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.axs.com/listen-austin-mahone-works-his-magic-on-modjo-s-lady-with-pitbull-112685|title=Listen: Austin Mahone works his magic on Modjo's 'Lady' with Pitbull|website=axs|publisher=www.axs.com/|last1=Villa|first1=Lucas|access-date=10 February 2017}}</ref>
On New Year's Eve, Mahone performed "Lady" on [[Pitbull's New Year's Revolution]]. He has since then performed at two [[Dolce & Gabbana]] fashion shows in Italy with his new work. He performed two shows while on the Pitbull After Dark Cruise. At the end of March 2017 he performed at POPSPRING Japan with his Hot 100 song "[[Dirty Work (Austin Mahone song)|Dirty Work]]" which peaked at #1. He has now begun a campaign working with [[Fossil Group|Fossil]].
Mahone will be kicking off his A Tour For Me+You on Wednesday, May 31, starting in Ft. Lauderdale, Florida, going through Thursday, June 30, ending in Los Angeles, California. On Friday, April 12, Mahone released a new song, "Creatures of the Night", with DJ and record producer [[Hardwell]].
