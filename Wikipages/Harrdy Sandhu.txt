==Early life and career ==
He played cricket for over a decade as a fast bowler, but suffered a severe elbow injury, forcing him to give up the game in 2007.<ref name="nie"/> He was also part of the [[2004 Under-19 Cricket World Cup]] Indian squad.{{Cn|date=November 2021}} He shifted his focus from sports to singing, took vocal training for eighteen months and performed his first album "This is Harrdy Sandhu" in 2011 composed by V. Grooves.<ref name="nie">{{cite news|title=From sports to singing, his spirit is undying|url=http://www.newindianexpress.com/thesundaystandard/From-sports-to-singing-his-spirit-is-undying/2016/05/22/article3444957.ece|access-date=10 January 2018|work=The New Indian Express|date=22 May 2016}}</ref> He released the video of song "Tequilla Shot" from album but the song and album didn't perform so well. He was disappointed and decided to leave singing if his next single track did not perform well. He met lyricist Jaani and Music Director B Praak to made the song named "Soch" which was released in 2013. The song become huge hit and it changed the life of Sandhu and the full team associated with the song. The song was one of biggest hits of that year and it is highly loved by audience till date. 

He made his debut as actor with movie "Yaaran Da Katchup" in 2014. The movie proved to be an average movie. But he kept going as singer with tracks such as "Joker","Backbone", "Horn Blow","Yaar Ni Milya" with [[Jaani (songwriter)|Jaani]] And [[B Praak]] in later years that proved to be a big hits.  

Later in 2017 he released track "Naah" featuring [[Nora Fatehi]], with lyrics by Jaani and Music by B Praak. The track proved to be a big hit across the nation. In 2018 after huge success of Naah he released "Kya Baat Ay" with the same team, which also proved to be a huge hit. With the songs Naah and Kya Baat Ay, Harrdy become a National level superstar. Both songs has crossed 500+ million views on YouTube individually. 

In later years he released good tracks such as "Superstar", "Dance Like", "Jee Karda", "Titliaan Warga". He also sang tracks in bollywood films such as "Chandigarh" from [[Good Newwz]] and "Naah Goriye" from [[Bala (2019 film)|Bala]].  

In 2021, he is all set to make his debut in Bollywood film as an actor with the film "[[83 (film)|83]]", playing the role of cricketer [[Madan Lal]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.filmfare.com/news/bollywood/saqib-saleem-and-harrdy-sandhu-join-the-cast-of-ranveer-singhs-83_-32576.html|title=Saqib Saleem and Harrdy Sandhu join the cast of Ranveer Singh's '83|last=Pathak|first=Vedanshi|date=18 February 2019|website=Filmfare|access-date=23 February 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.bollywoodhungama.com/news/bollywood/parineeti-chopra-harrdy-sandhu-quietly-begin-shooting-ribhu-dasguptas-action-thriller-turkey|title=Parineeti Chopra and Harrdy Sandhu quietly begin shooting for Ribhu Dasgupta's action thriller in Turkey |date=21 April 2021 |work=Bollywood Hungama |access-date=21 June 2021}}</ref>
