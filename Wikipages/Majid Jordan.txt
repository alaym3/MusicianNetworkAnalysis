'''Majid Jordan''' is a Canadian [[Contemporary R&B|R&B]] duo, consisting of singer Majid Al Maskati and producer Jordan Ullman from [[Toronto]], [[Ontario]]. They formed in 2011 and signed to [[OVO Sound]], the [[record label]] co-founded by rapper [[Drake (rapper)|Drake]], producer [[Noah "40" Shebib]] and Oliver El-Khatib, releasing their debut [[Extended play|EP]], ''[[A Place Like This (EP)|A Place Like This]]'', in 2014. 

==Beginnings==
Majid Jordan was formed in 2011 by Jordan Ullman (born September 8, 1993), originally from [[Toronto]], [[Ontario]], and Majid Al Maskati (born October 22, 1990), originally from [[Bahrain]], after they met each other at Majid's birthday party at a bar in Toronto.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://twitter.com/majidalmaskati/status/773793543190110208?ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw&ref_url=http%3A%2F%2Fmajidjordan.com%2Ftweets%2Frt-majidalmaskati-happy-birthday-jordanullman | title=Majid Al Maskati on Twitter: Happy birthday @jordanullman | publisher=Twitter | access-date=8 September 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=https://twitter.com/jordanullman/status/789729368289513472?ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw&ref_url=http%3A%2F%2Fmajidjordan.com%2Ftweets%2Frt-jordanullman-happy-birthday-to-my-brother-for-life-majidalmaskati-%25f0%259f%258d%25be%25f0%259f%258d%25be%25f0%259f%258d%25be | title=Jordan Ullman on Twitter: Happy birthday to my brother for life @majidalmaskati !!! | publisher=Twitter | access-date=22 October 2016}}</ref> Both were students at the [[University of Toronto]], and later that week they began working on music together in Jordan's [[dorm]], releasing their first official single, "Hold Tight", in just one day, which they posted anonymously on [[SoundCloud]]. Working between Ullman's dorm room and his parents' [[basement]], they launched their first joint [[Extended play|EP]] titled ''Afterhours'' on SoundCloud under the pseudonym Good People.<ref>{{cite web | url=http://majidjordan.com/about | title=Majid Jordan - About | publisher=Official website | access-date=11 March 2015 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160818200011/http://majidjordan.com/about | archive-date=18 August 2016 | url-status=dead }}</ref> [[40 (record producer)|Noah "40" Shebib]], producer for the Toronto rapper [[Drake (rapper)|Drake]], was impressed by their ''Afterhours'' EP, and he signed them to his [[OVO Sound]] [[record label]].<ref>Majid Jordan Talk About Meeting Drake + New Album". YouTube. N.p., 2016. Web. 5 Apr. 2016.</ref>

==Career==
===2013–2016: ''A Place Like This'' and ''Majid'' ''Jordan''===
In 2013, Majid Jordan co-produced and were credited to be featured on Drake's track "[[Hold On, We're Going Home]]", which was included from his platinum-selling album ''[[Nothing Was the Same]]''. "Hold On, We're Going Home" went on to become Drake's most successful single-charting in several countries, including [[United States]], [[Canada]], [[United Kingdom]], [[Australia]], and [[New Zealand]].

On July 7, 2014, Majid Jordan released their first official single as a duo through SoundCloud.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://soundcloud.com/majidjordan/majid-jordan-a-place-like-this | title=A Place Like This by Majid Jordan | publisher=SoundCloud | access-date=July 7, 2014}}</ref> On July 17, 2014, ten days later, they released their official debut EP, ''[[A Place Like This (EP)|A Place Like This]]'', which was digitally through OVO Sound, the record label co-founded by rapper Drake. They went on to release music videos for three of the songs from the EP, including the title track "[[A Place Like This (song)|A Place Like This]]",<ref>{{cite web | url=http://uk.complex.com/music/2014/07/majid-jordan-video-a-place-like-this | title=Watch Majid Jordan's Video for "A Place Like This" | publisher=Complex | access-date=July 7, 2014}}</ref> "Her"<ref>{{cite web | url=http://uk.complex.com/music/2014/12/majid-jordan-her | title=Watch Majid Jordan's "Her" Video | publisher=Complex | access-date=December 8, 2014}}</ref> and "Forever".<ref>{{cite web | url=http://uk.complex.com/music/2015/01/majid-jordan-forever-video | title=Watch Majid Jordan's "Forever" Video | publisher=Complex | access-date=January 19, 2015}}</ref>

On July 10, 2015, Drake premiered their first single, titled "My Love" for their upcoming debut studio album on [[Apple Inc.|Apple]]'s Beats 1 radio, during an interview with radio host [[Zane Lowe]]; their label boss is also featured on the track. After its premiere it was released straight to [[Apple Music]] and the [[iTunes Store]].<ref>{{cite web | url=https://complex.com/music/2015/07/majid-jordan-and-drake-drop-new-music-on-apple-music | title=Majid Jordan and Drake Release "My Love" On Apple Music | publisher=Complex | access-date=July 10, 2015}}</ref>

In February 2016, the duo released its self-titled debut by OVO sound, featuring a song with [[Drake (rapper)|Drake]], called "My Love".<ref name="Majid Jordan to kick off tour"/> On February 17, 2016, the duo also revealed their first-ever North American tour<ref name="Majid Jordan to kick off tour">{{cite web|url=http://www.rap-up.com/2016/02/17/majid-jordan-announces-tour-dates/|title=Majid Jordan kicking off North American tour|work=[[Rap-Up]]|access-date=February 17, 2016}}</ref> starting with a show in [[San Francisco]], with other appearances were scheduled for [[Miami]], [[Brooklyn]], [[Atlanta]], [[Toronto]], [[Chicago]], and [[Los Angeles]].<ref name="Majid Jordan to kick off tour"/>

=== 2017–2019: ''The Space Between'' ===
On April 21, 2017, Majid Jordan released "Phases" as the lead single from their second studio album. Its music video was released on May 25, 2017.

Majid Jordan released "One I Want" as the second single from their sophomore album on June 15, 2017. The song features a guest appearance from OVO labelmate [[PartyNextDoor]]. A music video was released on July 7, 2017.

On July 31, 2017, Majid Jordan announced that their sophomore album, ''[[The Space Between (Majid Jordan album)|The Space Between]]'', would be released in the fall.

On September 29, 2017, Majid Jordan released "My Imagination" featuring OVO labelmate [[Dvsn]] as the third single from ''The Space Between.'' It was released along with the album pre-order. The album was released on October 27, 2017.

On September 7, 2018, [[Zhu (musician)|Zhu]] released his second studio album, ''[[Ringos Desert]]'', which features a guest appearance from Majid Jordan on the song "Coming Home." That same day, Majid Jordan released two songs titled "Spirit" and "All Over You".

On July 24, 2019, Majid Jordan released "Caught Up," a song that features [[Khalid (singer)|Khalid]]. The music video premiered on the Majid Jordan YouTube page the same day.

On July 25, 2019, [[DJ Snake]] released his album, ''[[Carte Blanche (DJ Snake album)|Carte Blanche]]'', which features a guest appearance from Majid Jordan on the song "Recognize." A remix of the song which features the French DJ Mercer, was released on September 27, 2019.

Majid Jordan released a song titled "Superstar" on October 4, 2019.

=== 2020–present: ''Wildest Dreams'' ===
On April 5, 2021, after a year-long hiatus and break from social media, Majid Jordan released their lead single "Waves of Blue" from their third studio album.

On June 18, 2021, Majid Jordan released "Been Through That" as the second single from their third studio album. The single premiered on the [[Sirius XM]] OVO Sound 42 Radio channel. 

Majid Jordan released "Summer Rain" as the third single from their third studio album on September 17, 2021. 

On October 3, 2021, Majid Jordan announced that their third studio album, ''Wildest Dreams'', would be released on October 22, 2021. The album marks the tenth anniversary of the duo forming, and the release date is on Majid Al Maskati's birthday.  

Majid Jordan released their fourth single, "Forget About the Party", from their third studio album. It premiered on October 18, 2021, on the [[Apple Music 1]] [[Zane Lowe]] Show.
