'''Howard Victor Carpendale''' (born 14 January 1946) is a South African-German singer living and working in Germany.

== Biography ==
Carpendale is a [[Pop music|pop]] singer who sings most of his songs in German. His 1979 song ''Nachts, wenn alles schläft'' had been among the 50 biggest-selling singles in Germany for one year. He has released 37 regular albums (live albums and compilations not included), most of which charted in the German Album Top 10.

Carpendale has two sons, [[Wayne Carpendale]] and Cass Carpendale.<ref>[http://www.berliner-zeitung.de/archiv/fuenf-jahre-nach-seinem-abschiedskonzert-steht-howard-carpendale-wieder-auf-der-buehne--ein-gespraech-ueber-heimat--altern-und-unerfuellte-sehnsuechte-so-ziemlich-alles-erreicht,10810590,10552998.html Berliner Zeitung: Fünf Jahre nach seinem Abschiedskonzert steht Howard Carpendale wieder auf der Bühne (in German)]</ref>
