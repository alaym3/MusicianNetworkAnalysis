'''Mehdi Benjelloun''' (born 10 November 1999), known by his stage name '''Petit Biscuit''' ({{IPA-fr|pəti biskɥi}}), is a French DJ and music producer.

The 22-year-old French producer has been making music with his distinctive style, forged through combining acoustic elements, electronic production, and his vocal manipulations. The eclecticism of this combination is reflective of his background in the polar spheres of electronica and classical music.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Jason |first1=Bentley |title=Petit Biscuit Mix and Q+A |url=https://www.kcrw.com/music/shows/metropolis/petit-biscuit-mix |website=KCRW |accessdate=13 July 2019}}</ref>  He has toured the world,<ref>{{cite web |last1=Bentley |first1=Jason |title=Petit Biscuit Mix and Q+A |url=https://www.kcrw.com/music/shows/metropolis/petit-biscuit-mix |website=www.kcrw.com |publisher=KCRW |accessdate=13 July 2019}}</ref> including a performance at [[Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival|Coachella]] in 2018.<ref>{{cite web |title=Beyonce, Petit Biscuit, the Weekend ... le meilleur de Coachella (week-end 1) |url=https://www.virginradio.fr/beyonce-petit-biscuit-kygo-le-meilleur-de-coachella-week-end-1-a646950.html |website=www.virginradio.fr |publisher=Virgin Radio}}</ref>

==Early life==
Benjelloun was born to a Moroccan father and a French mother.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.moustique.be/17434/petit-biscuit-la-revelation|title=Petit Biscuit, la révélation|last=Alsteen|first=Nicolas|date=18 January 2017|newspaper=Moustique.be|access-date=2017-02-04|language=fr}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.virginradio.fr/petit-biscuit-le-benjamin-de-cette-edition-d-electroshock-envoute-strasbourg-a587521.html|title=Petit Biscuit : Le benjamin de cette édition d'Electroshock envoûte Strasbourg|last=Pineau|first=Marine|date=2017-01-27|newspaper=virginradio.fr|access-date=2017-02-04|language=fr-FR}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.ouest-france.fr/normandie/saint-lo-50000/le-concert-de-petit-biscuit-au-normandy-saint-lo-complet-4732925|title=Le concert de Petit Biscuit au Normandy à Saint-Lô complet !|newspaper=Ouest-France.fr|access-date=2017-02-04|language=fr-FR}}</ref>

Benjelloun learned to play instruments such as piano, cello, bass and guitar but preferred electro house sounds as he was able to produce "unlimited combinations of music".<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.virginradio.fr/petit-biscuit-8-choses-a-savoir-sur-lui-avant-electroshock-strasbourg-interview-a586719.html|title=Petit Biscuit : 8 choses à savoir sur lui avant Electroshock Strasbourg (interview)|last=N|first=Nadia|date=2017-01-24|newspaper=virginradio.fr|language=fr-FR|access-date=2017-02-04}}</ref> He wrote his first songs in secret in his room. His early musical interests and inspirations included [[electronica]] and [[classical music]]. Benjelloun was a regular high school student who balanced school work by day and music by night. The name "Petit Biscuit" is said to come from his love of French pastries his grandmother would make. 

In July 2017, he graduated from high school, with a [[Baccalauréat|Baccalauréat Scientifique (high school diploma with a specialty in science)]] ''mention très bien (with honors)''. From September 2017, he started studying at [[Paris-Dauphine University]] and left the university to focus more on music in March 2018.<ref>{{cite web|language=fr|title=Petit Biscuit, un étudiant à Paris qui côtoie Taylor Swift au sommet des charts|date=2017-11-15|url=https://www.lesechos.fr/tech-medias/medias/030876975705-petit-biscuit-un-etudiant-a-dauphine-qui-cotoie-taylor-swift-au-sommet-des-charts-2130450.php#xtor=CS1-25|publisher=lesechos.fr}}</ref>

==Career==
He released his early works on [[SoundCloud]] until he got noticed by distributor Electro Posé, under whom he published "Sunset Lover" on 15 June 2015 as the lead single from his eponymous EP.<ref>[https://itunes.apple.com/fr/album/sunset-lover-single/id1084457295 Sunset Lover - Single de Petit Biscuit sur Apple Music]</ref> It got over 62 million views<ref>[https://www.franceinter.fr/emissions/dans-la-playlist-de-france-inter/dans-la-playlist-de-france-inter-19-mars-2018 Le savoureux et précoce Petit Biscuit se déguste sans modération], France Inter, 19 mars 2018</ref> and more than 400 millions streams.

"You", "Palms", "Night Trouble", "Midnight Sky" and "Memories" were released as promotional singles on 15 June 2015.<ref name="iTunesYou">[https://itunes.apple.com/fr/album/you-single/id1084473284 You - Single de Petit Biscuit sur Apple Music]</ref><ref name="iTunesPalms">[https://itunes.apple.com/fr/album/palms-single/id1084456829 Palms - Single de Petit Biscuit sur Apple Music]</ref><ref name="iTunesNightTrouble">[https://itunes.apple.com/fr/album/night-trouble-single/id1084456354 Night Trouble - Single de Petit Biscuit sur Apple Music]</ref><ref name="iTunesMidnightSky">[https://itunes.apple.com/fr/album/midnight-sky-single/id1084457096 Midnight Sky - Single de Petit Biscuit sur Apple Music]</ref><ref name="iTunesMemories">[https://itunes.apple.com/fr/album/memories-single/id1084457598 Memories - Single de Petit Biscuit sur Apple Music]</ref> He released the single "Oceans" on 22 July 2015.<ref>[https://itunes.apple.com/fr/album/oceans-single/id1093533050 Oceans - Single de Petit Biscuit sur Apple Music]</ref> He released his debut eponymous EP, ''Petit Biscuit'' on 13 May 2016.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://stoneyroads.com/2016/09/16-year-old-petit-biscuit-is-off-to-a-roaring-start-with-his-video-for-sunset-lover|title=Petit Biscuit Is Off To A Roaring Start With His Video for 'Sunset Lover'|last=Colquhoun|first=Jack|date=12 September 2016|website=Stoney Roads|access-date=2017-02-04}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.youredm.com/2016/05/24/petit-biscuits-self-titled-debut-ep-review/|title=Petit Biscuit's Self-Titled Debut EP [Review]|last=Andrew Claps|date=2016-05-24|website=Your EDM|access-date=2017-02-04}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://stoneyroads.com/2016/05/listen-to-the-debut-ep-from-16-year-old-french-producer-petit-biscuit|title=Listen to the debut EP from 16 year old French producer Petit Biscuit|last=Morawski|first=Nat|date=20 May 2016|newspaper=Stoney Roads|language=en|access-date=2017-02-04}}</ref><ref>[https://itunes.apple.com/fr/album/petit-biscuit-ep/id1111535116 Petit Biscuit - EP de Petit Biscuit sur Apple Music]</ref>  Sunset Lover became an international breakthrough hit<ref>{{cite web |last1=Jason |first1=Bentley |title=Petit Biscuit Mix and Q+A |url=https://www.kcrw.com/music/shows/metropolis/petit-biscuit-mix |website=KCRW.com |date=11 July 2019 |publisher=KCRW}}</ref> amassing over 350 million streams.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Matt |first1=Murphy |title=Music is a Story of coincidences |url=https://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/music/features/petit-biscuit-interview-real-name-mehdi-benjelloun-music-album-presence-live-shows-tour-france-a8215351.html |website=www.independent.co.uk |date=21 February 2018 |publisher=The Independent |accessdate=21 February 2018}}</ref>

Petit Biscuit's debut album "Presence" was released in December 2017 and was described by [[Earmilk]] as a "powerful, momentous album".<ref>{{cite web |last1=Cooper |first1=Pearson |title=Petit Biscuit releases debut album Presence |url=https://earmilk.com/2017/11/13/petit-biscuit-releases-debut-album-presence/ |website=www.earmilk.com |date=13 November 2017 |publisher=Earmilk |accessdate=1 December 2017}}</ref>  It was [[Triple J]] Album of the week in Australia upon release<ref>{{cite web |title=triple J playlist additions |url=https://jplay.com.au/triple-j-playlist-additions-petit-biscuit-rex-orange-county-jesswar/ |website=www.jplay.com.au |date=10 December 2017 |publisher=JPLAY |accessdate=11 December 2017}}</ref> and features artists [[Lido (musician)|Lido]], [[Bipolar Sunshine]], [[Cautious Clay]] and includes hits "Waterfall" and "Sunset Lover".<ref>{{cite web |last1=Bein |first1=Kat |title=Petit Biscuit proves his creative presence on debut album |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/8031284/petit-biscuit-presence-debut-album-stream |website=www.billboard.com |accessdate=10 November 2017}}</ref> He performed at the [[Victoires de la Musique]], [[Paris]] – the French equivalent of the [[Brit Awards]] – and was nominated for his album, "Presence."<ref>{{cite web |last1=Murphy |first1=Matt |title=Petit Biscuit interview: 'Music is a story of coincidences' |url=https://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/music/features/petit-biscuit-interview-real-name-mehdi-benjelloun-music-album-presence-live-shows-tour-france-a8215351.html |website=Independent.co.uk |date=21 February 2018 |publisher=Independent |accessdate=21 February 2018}}</ref> To stay independent, he created his own label, "Petit Biscuit Music".

On 15 April 2018, he performed at [[Coachella Festival|Coachella]] for the first time. He has also toured multiple countries, including [[United States]], [[United Kingdom]], [[Netherlands]], [[Mexico]] and [[Canada]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Jean-Michel Jarre, Petit Biscuit, Ibeyi... La playlist française de Coachella|website=Le Figaro|date=12 April 2018|url=http://www.lefigaro.fr/musique/2018/04/12/03006-20180412ARTFIG00029-jean-michel-jarre-petit-biscuit-ibeyi-la-playlist-francaise-de-coachella.php|accessdate=2018-04-19}}</ref>{{,}}<ref name=lex>{{cite web |last1=Tournier |first1=Pascale |title=Petit Biscuit, cerise sur le gâteau |url=https://www.lexpress.fr/culture/musique/petit-biscuit-la-cerise-sur-le-gateau_1997244.html |website=L'Express|date=11 April 2018 |accessdate=22 December 2018}}</ref>

At the beginning of 2019, Petit Biscuit released "Wide Awake," which was described by [[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] as a "glitchy bass instrumental", and "We Were Young," featuring [[JP Cooper]], as a "young love daydream".<ref>{{cite web |last1=Bein |first1=Kat |title=Petit Biscuits We Were Young with JP Cooper is a young love daydream |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/8513385/petit-biscuit-we-were-young-jp-cooper |website=www.billboard.com |accessdate=28 May 2019}}</ref>

On October 30, 2020, Petit Biscuit released his second studio album, "Parachute" via Écurie Records.

On 18 April, 2021, Petit Biscuit releases his song "Restez avec Moi" for participating at the national selection of France to Europops Song Contest ("Meilleur en France 2021"). He won the selection with 335 points and was able to represent France at Europops Song Contest 2021 in Reykjavik, Iceland. 

On 19 November, 2021, he won Europops Song Contest 2021 with his song "Restez avec Moi".

==Musical style and influence==
Petit Biscuit produces electronic music with a clean, melodic and atmospheric sound. He describes his music as a call to travel, deep human reflection, natural, and sensual things.<ref>[https://www.letemps.ch/culture/petit-biscuit-douce-transe Petit Biscuit, douce transe], Le Temps, 20 mars 2018</ref>

==== Berlin Music Video Awards ====
Berlin Music Video Awards is an international festival founded in 2013 that puts the talents behind music videos into the spotlight. Supporting both unknown and famous artists, it is a primary networking event for the video and music industries in Europe. Filled with a vast selection of music video marathons, professional judges, live performances, filmmaking workshops and networking events, the festival is not only meeting ground for filmmakers, but also for musicians as well as all music enthusiasts.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Berlin Music Video Awards|date=20 May 2021|title=About Us|url=https://www.berlinmva.com/about-us/|url-status=live|website=www.berlinmva.com}}</ref>
