'''Tobechukwu Dubem "Tobe" Nwigwe''' (born March 8, 1987), is an [[Americans|American]] rapper and singer. He is from the [[Alief, Houston|Alief]] neighborhood of [[Houston]], [[Texas]].He is married to Fat Nwigwe and they have three children,Ivory “Baby Fat”Nwigwe ,Sage Nwigwe and Chukwueze Nwigwe.

==Life==
Nwigwe is of Nigerian descent, and hails from the [[Igbo people|Igbo ethnic group]].

He played high school football and then college football at the [[University of North Texas]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://meangreensports.com/sports/football/roster/tobe-nwigwe/1425|title=Tobe Nwigwe - Football|website=University of North Texas Athletics|access-date=December 26, 2020}}</ref> He was being considered for draft by the [[NFL]]. However, a [[Lisfranc_ligament#:~:text=The%20Lisfranc%20ligament%20is%20one,ligament%20(dorsal%20and%20plantar).|Lisfranc ligament tear]] left him unable to continue to play football.<ref name="houstonchronicle">{{Cite web|url=https://www.houstonchronicle.com/entertainment/music/article/tobe-nwigwe-breakout-rapper-museum-fine-arts-14514653.php|title=Tobe Nwigwe twists himself into Houston's next big star|date=October 11, 2019|website=HoustonChronicle.com|access-date=December 26, 2020}}</ref> He then founded the nonprofit TeamGINI, a name derived from “Gini Bu Nkpa Gi?” which translates to “What’s your trouble?” in the [[Igbo language]]. TeamGINI aims to help families in need by giving them gifts that aid them in gaining financial literacy and independence.<ref>{{Cite web|last=John|first=Caroline|date=October 28, 2020|title=Tobe Nwigwe Wiki: Facts about the Houston Rapper|url=https://www.earnthenecklace.com/tobe-nwigwe-wiki/|website=Earn The Necklace}}</ref> After encouragement from the motivational speaker Eric Thomas, Nwigwe began focusing on music in earnest.<ref name="NYT">{{Cite news|last=Pariseau|first=Leslie|date=August 13, 2020|title=Tobe Nwigwe Never Planned to Go Viral. Then He Rapped About Breonna Taylor.|language=en-US|work=The New York Times|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2020/08/13/arts/music/tobe-nwigwe-breonna-taylor.html|access-date=December 9, 2020|issn=0362-4331}}</ref>

==Music career==
Since August 2016, he has posted an original song and video every Sunday across social media.<ref name="houstonchronicle" /> He began to gain a following through his Instagram and YouTube posts and videos.<ref name="NYT" />

Nwigwe collaborates with his wife, Fat (born Martica Ivory Rogers), and friend and producer/rapper LaNell "Nell" Grant, also known as The Originals.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Tobe Nwigwe And The Originals Discuss Love, Erykah Badu, Breakthroughs & More! [EXCLUSIVE]|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JsfRrK-2JIg|website=YouTube}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.mtv.com/news/3157478/tobe-nwigwe-slow-down-interview/|title=Tobe Nwigwe Is The Transcendent Rapper Making Purpose Popular|author=MTV News Staff|website=MTV News|access-date=December 26, 2020}}</ref> The three came together while working with Nwigwe's nonprofit TeamGINI.

Nwigwe appeared on the BET Hip Hop Awards 2018 Cypher.<ref>{{Cite web|title=BlocBoy JB, YBN Cordae And More Spit Some Fire {{!}} Hip Hop Awards 2018 Cypher|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gdtYhvS-_4M|website=YouTube}}</ref> He is a featured artist on [[PJ Morton]]'s album ''[[Paul (PJ Morton album)|Paul]]'', released on August 9, 2019 by Morton Records and Empire Records.
