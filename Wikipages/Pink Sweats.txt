'''David Bowden''' (born February 14, 1992), known professionally as '''Pink Sweats''' (stylized as '''Pink Sweat$'''), is an American singer and songwriter. He released his first [[Extended play|EP]] ''Volume 1'' on November 2, 2018, led by the single "Honesty". His debut album, ''Pink Planet'', was released in February 2021.

==Early life and career==
Bowden grew up [[Philadelphia]].<ref name="Williams">{{cite web |last1=Williams |first1=Aaron |title=Pink Sweat$ Is Challenging The Conventions Of Modern R&B With Eye-Catching Style And Sensitive Swag |url=https://uproxx.com/music/pink-sweats-honesty-drama-volume-1/ |website=[[Uproxx]] |date=December 7, 2018}}</ref> His father is a minister and his mother is a gospel singer.<ref name="DeLuca">{{cite web |last1=DeLuca |first1=Dan |title=The Philly recording artist Pink Sweat$ is on the cusp of greatness. Here's how it started. |url=https://www.inquirer.com/entertainment/pink-sweat-new-album-the-prelude-20200722.html |website=[[The Philadelphia Inquirer]] |date=July 22, 2020}}</ref> He was raised in a religious household and was only allowed to listen to Christian music until he was 17 years old.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Reese |first1=Alexis |title=Pink Sweat$: 5 Things You Need To Know |url=https://www.bet.com/music/2020/01/08/pink-sweat---5-things-you-need-to-know.html |website=[[BET]] |date=January 8, 2020}}</ref><ref name="Corry">{{cite web |last1=Corry |first1=Kristin |title=Pink Sweat$ Is Putting Love Back in R&B |url=https://www.vice.com/en/article/bj9maq/pink-sweatdollar-is-putting-love-back-in-randb |website=[[Vice (magazine)|Vice]] |date=May 8, 2019}}</ref> The first rap artists he listened to were [[50 Cent]] and [[Kanye West]].<ref name="Lamarre"/> After graduating high school, Bowden had a three-year battle with [[Achalasia]], and his recovery from the disease made him want to pursue music as a full-time career.<ref name="DeLuca"/><ref name="Lamarre">{{cite web | first1=Carl | last1=Lamarre | title=Pink Sweat$ on His Rapid Success In R&B, the Status of 'Volume 2' & How a Near-Death Experience Birthed His Career |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/8493347/pink-sweats-interview-volume-1-album|website=Billboard}}</ref> His stage name was adopted as he always wore pink sweatpants (taking inspiration from [[Cam'ron]]) and was given the nickname "Pink Sweats".<ref>{{cite web |last1=Chesman |first1=Donna-Claire |title=Meet Pink Sweat$, the Most Miraculous Singer of 2018 |url=https://djbooth.net/features/2019-01-02-meet-pink-sweats-interview |website=DJBooth |accessdate=19 January 2019}}</ref> He first started making music at the age of 19 as a demo vocalist at Sound Stigma Studios, where he discovered his love for songwriting and began to develop his career in music. He produced in different genres of music for rapper [[Tierra Whack]] and country duo [[Florida Georgia Line]].<ref>{{cite web |last1=O'Leary |first1=Kathryn |title=Rising R&B Artist Pink Sweat$ May or May Not Lead To Unexpected Pregnancy [NEW ARTIST DISCOVERY] |url=https://www.onestowatch.com/blog/rising-rb-artist-pink-sweat-may-or-may-not-lead |website=Ones To Watch |accessdate=19 January 2019}}</ref>

On July 12, 2018, he released his first single "Honesty", which later started to gain attention on streaming platforms, reaching number 10 on [[Spotify]]'s US Viral Chart.<ref>{{cite web |title=Pink Sweat$ |url=https://thricecookedmedia.com/pink-sweat/ |website=Thrice Cooked Media |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190119230743/https://thricecookedmedia.com/pink-sweat/ |archive-date=January 19, 2019|accessdate=19 January 2019}}</ref> The song's popularity was boosted after it was featured in Spotify's "Are & Be" playlist.<ref name="Williams"/> A remix featuring [[Jessie Reyez]] was released afterwards.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Hare |first1=Anders |title=Pink Sweat$ Releases 'Honesty (Remix)' Featuring Jessie Reyez |url=https://ratedrnb.com/2019/11/pink-sweat-releases-honesty-remix-featuring-jessie-reyez/ |website=Rated RnB |date=November 11, 2019}}</ref> He released the second single "No Replacing You" on July 26.<ref>{{cite web |title=Pink Sweat$ - No Replacing You |url=https://www.bangeroftheday.com/single-post/2018/08/02/Pink-Sweat---No-Replacing-You |website=Banger of the Day |accessdate=19 January 2019}}</ref> His debut EP ''Volume 1'' was released on November 2, 2018, as the singer put out a music video for "Drama" later that month. He later followed this release with the sequel EP ''Volume 2''.<ref>{{cite web |title=Pink Sweat$ Drops 'Volume 1' |url=https://defpen.com/pink-sweat-volume-1/ |website=Def Pen |accessdate=19 January 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Hussein |first1=Wandera |title=Pink Sweat$'s "Drama" video is a triumphant glo-up story |url=https://www.thefader.com/2018/11/30/pink-sweats-drama-video-premiere |website=The Fader |accessdate=19 January 2019}}</ref> Each EP contains 5 songs; Bowden stated it was an intentional decision to make short songs designed for streaming.<ref name="Lamarre"/><ref name="Corry"/>

Due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic]] in 2020, Bowden's album was pushed back, and he released an EP, titled ''The Prelude'', in its place.<ref name="DeLuca"/> In February 2021, Pink Sweats' debut album, ''Pink Planet'', was released.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Pareles |first1=Jon |title=Pink Sweats Harnesses the Power of Niceness |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2021/02/16/arts/music/pink-sweats-pink-planet-review.html |website=[[The New York Times]] |date=February 16, 2021}}</ref> The album work began in the end of the 2019 and continued for roughly a year.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Okon |first1=Wongo |title=Pink Sweats And His 'Pink Planet' Welcome All Visitors To Be Their Most Natural Selves |url=https://uproxx.com/music/pink-sweats-pink-planet-interview/ |website=[[Uproxx]] |date=February 17, 2021}}</ref> In January 2021, a remix of the album's single "At My Worst" was released with [[Kehlani]].<ref>{{cite web |last1=Tigg |first1=Fnr |title=Kehlani Connects With Pink Sweats for "At My Worst" Remix |url=https://www.complex.com/music/2021/01/pink-sweats-kehlani-at-my-worst-remix |website=[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]] |date=January 27, 2021}}</ref> The pair performed the song on ''[[The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon ]]'' on March 10, 2021.<ref>{{cite web |title=Pink Sweats and Kehlani are Fire & Ice for 'At My Worst' Performance on 'Fallon': Watch |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/9537604/pink-sweat-kehlani-at-my-worst-fallon/ |website=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] |date=March 10, 2021}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Bowden became engaged to his girlfriend, JL Bunny, in March 2021.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Mendoza |first1=Mylene |title=Moira Dela Torre and Pink Sweat$ Drop Their Romantic Duet of "17" |url=https://www.candymag.com/all-access/moira-dela-torre-pink-sweats-17-duet-a00306-20210305 |website=Candy Magazine |date=March 5, 2021}}</ref>
