'''Natasha St-Pier''' (born 10 February 1981) is a Canadian singer of [[Acadian]] origin who has spent most of her career in France. She was coach in the second and third season of [[The Voice Belgique]] (''The Voice of [[Belgium]]'').

==Career==
Natasha St-Pier released her first album, ''[[Émergence (Natasha St-Pier album)|Emergence]]'', in 1996, produced by composer/producer [[Steve Barakatt]]. In 2000, she made her international singing debut as ''Fleur-de-Lys'' in the London version of the [[Musical theatre|musical drama]] ''[[Notre Dame de Paris (musical)#Original London Cast|Notre Dame de Paris]]''.

She came fourth in the [[Eurovision Song Contest 2001|2001 Eurovision Song Contest]] in Copenhagen, representing France with the [[power ballad]] ''[[Je n'ai que mon âme]]'', later releasing an English version of the song: "All I Have Is My Soul". By 2010, she had released 7 albums, topped the French album and singles charts, and made it to the top 10 of the [[Eurochart Hot 100]].{{Citation needed|date=August 2010}} St Pier has become popular in [[francophone]] Europe, and in countries such as [[Poland]] and Russia.{{Citation needed|date=August 2010}} Her seventh album, ''Tu Trouveras: 10 Ans De Succès'', which is largely a ''"Best of"'' [[Compilation album|compilation]] of her songs previously recorded, was released in November 2009. During 2010 she toured Canada, [[Belgium]] and France.

In 2004, Sony Music France released a Natasha St-Pier DVD, ''Un Instant Avec Natasha St-Pier'', that included a video tour diary, a personal video dictionary, and seven music videos.<ref>Un Instant Avec Natasha St-Pier: COL 2027359-2027359000</ref>

On 9 March 2012, she married her boyfriend of a year and a half, Gregory Quillacq, a Parisian fireman, at a private ceremony in [[Lit-et-Mixe]].<ref>{{in lang|fr}}[http://people.premiere.fr/News-People/PHOTO-Natasha-St-Pier-a-epouse-son-cheri-francais-3282608 People.premiere.fr ; Natasha St-Pier épouse son chéri français] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131014043443/http://people.premiere.fr/News-People/PHOTO-Natasha-St-Pier-a-epouse-son-cheri-francais-3282608 |date=14 October 2013 }}</ref>

Since September 2013, Natasha is the host of the television show "Les chansons d'abord" (Songs First) to [[France 3]]. She was also one of the commentators of the [[Eurovision Song Contest 2014]] for French television.<ref>{{in lang|fr}}[http://www.chartsinfrance.net/Natasha-St-Pier/news-89034.html Chartsinfrance.net ; Natasha St-Pier News]</ref> In October 2015 St-Pier, signed to the Smart label, released the album ''Mon Acadie'', a collection of covers of traditional folk songs.

== Private life ==
In 2012, she married Grégory Quillacq (a member of the aquatic and subaquatic fire brigade of Paris), she has a child named Bixente Maxime, he underwent a successful heart operation.<ref>{{cite web|title=Natasha St-Pier : nouvelle photo de son fils Bixente, visiblement bien remis de son opération|url=https://www.femmeactuelle.fr/culture/news-culture/photo-du-fils-de-natasha-st-pier-remis-de-son-operation-29343|website=Femme actuelle|date=20 April 2016|access-date=2018-08-03}}</ref> She is a dietitian, vegetarian<ref>{{cite web|title=Interview : découvrez les secrets beauté de Natasha St Pier|url=http://www.plurielles.fr/beaute/beaute-de-star/interview-decouvrez-les-secrets-beaute-de-natasha-st-pier-5628314-402.html|website=Lci|date=9 January 2010|access-date=2018-08-01|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190924064656/http://www.plurielles.fr/beaute/beaute-de-star/interview-decouvrez-les-secrets-beaute-de-natasha-st-pier-5628314-402.html|archive-date=24 September 2019|url-status=dead}}</ref> and a [[Christianity|Christian]] ([[Catholic Church|Catholic]]). She is also a [[yoga]] teacher<ref>{{cite web|title=Exclu – Natasha St-Pier se confie sur sa nouvelle vie " simple et belle ", loin du showbiz|url=https://www.gala.fr/l_actu/news_de_stars/exclu-natasha-st-pier-se-confie-sur-sa-nouvelle-vie-simple-et-belle-loin-du-showbiz_417802|website=Gala|date=31 July 2018|access-date=2018-08-03|quote=le yoga, (...), vise la même prise de conscience.}}</ref> and an occasional [[Scuba diving|scuba diver]]. In 2021 she announced she and her husband were separated.
