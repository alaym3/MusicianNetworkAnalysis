'''Universal Hall Pass''' is a one-person band based in [[Los Angeles]], [[California]] which was first formed in 2001 by Melissa Kaplan, formerly of [[Splashdown (band)|Splashdown]].

Universal Hall Pass' first full-length album, ''Mercury'', was released in 2004, featuring eleven songs. In December, 2006, UHP released a six-song EP entitled ''Subtle Things'', on which Kaplan worked with ex-bandmate [[Kasson Crooker]]. Most recently,  Universal Hall Pass released a self produced single "Lyra"(2019). 

''Mercury'' has topped or been near the top of the "Pop with Electronic Production" chart on [[MP3tunes]] for several months [http://www.mp3tunes.com/musicchart.php?chart=album&genre_id=64].

In 2019, the group released a new single, "Sin Eater." [https://universalhallpass.bandcamp.com/track/sin-eater?fbclid=IwAR2DS2FOG4JjZSRMsF82mXSNFV8GabIhCqS7OSHVibKOnFj6-NrQAVBArtw]
