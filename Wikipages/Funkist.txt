'''FUNKIST''' is a [[Japan]]ese rock band formed in 2001 under the [[POWERPLAY]] label.

==History==
*FUNKIST formed in 2001.
*2005 [[Tokyo]], [[Okinawa]], [[Macau]] doing a live tour of the three largest cities.
*2006 [[South Africa]] tour live dared. Start a live tour 47 prefectures of Japan.
*Release the "white world" indie single in 2007. [[Shibuya Tower Records]] #1 two weeks in a row indies chart, [[Orico]]n indies chart topped the 14. [[China]], [[Macau]] dared live tour. [[SHIBUYA O-EAST]] man live.
*2008 SHIBUYA-AX one-man live. Participate in the live act of the Global Article 9 Conference<ref>"[http://whynot9.jp/program/&usg=ALkJrhhi93m15p_YL4ok2nLnMpiiGPAKqA Program of] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090404134122/http://whynot9.jp/program/ |date=2009-04-04 }} five [http://whynot9.jp/program/&usg=ALkJrhhi93m15p_YL4ok2nLnMpiiGPAKqA 04]  viewed November 5 (April 2008) Japan Executive Committee, "World Conference on Article 9" 2008.</ref>
**July - formed major debut with [[Pony Canyon]]. 
*As a model for the item, "Artist" in the "Images Ashinaru" Images site offers live photos.
*Year 2011
**"ALL TOGETHER" released in August. Charity wrestling "[[Tohoku Earthquake]] [[All Together (professional wrestling)|All Together]]" production as the theme song.
**5:12 p.m, October 13, Yōko Kasugai, the band's flutist, died at the age of 35.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://funkist.info/20111014.html |script-title=ja:重要なお知らせ |publisher=Funkist |language=Japanese |accessdate=2013-08-18 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130814030334/http://funkist.info/20111014.html |archivedate=2013-08-14 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2011-10-16/fairy-tail-funkist-flutist-yoko-kasugai-passes-away|title=Fairy Tail's Funkist Flutist Yōko Kasugai Passes Away|date=16 October 2011|work=[[Anime News Network]]|accessdate=16 May 2014}}</ref>
