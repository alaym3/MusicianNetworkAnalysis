'''Văn Mai Hương''' (born September 27, 1994 in Hanoi) is a [[V-pop|Vietnamese Pop Music]] singer.  She was the runner-up of Season 3 of [[Vietnam Idol]] in 2010, before becoming a mainstay in the industry.

==Career==

===2010: Vietnam Idol===
As a participant of the [[Vietnam Idol (season 3)|third season]] of [[Vietnam Idol]], she was the youngest among the top 10 contestants and was known as a highly technical performer.<ref name="ngoisao.vn 2021">{{cite web | title=Ngắm Văn Mai Hương xinh tươi giữa phố Sài Gòn | website=ngoisao.vn | date=March 9, 2021 | url=https://ngoisao.vn/hau-truong/chuyen-lang-sao/ngam-van-mai-huong-xinh-tuoi-giua-pho-sai-gon-14532.htm | language=vi | access-date=November 7, 2021}}</ref> During the final competitive show, she sang  "Trái tim âm nhạc", "[[Hot n Cold]]", "Cảm ơn Tình Yêu", and "Á quân" and came in second place to [[Uyên Linh]].

===2011-2012: Debut "Nếu như anh đến" and the debut album ''Hãy mỉm cười''===

After Vietnam Idol, she released a sponsored song "Ngày mới trắng hồng" in partnership with an international cosmetics brand.<ref name="Hồ Sơ Nhân Vật 2020">{{cite web | title=Tiểu sử ca sĩ Văn Mai Hương - Hồ Sơ Nhân Vật | website=Hồ Sơ Nhân Vật | date=April 19, 2020 | url=https://hosonhanvat.vn/ho-so-nhan-vat-tieu-su-ca-si-van-mai-huong | language=vi | access-date=November 7, 2021}}</ref>

In June 2011, she released her official single "Nếu như anh đến" (translated: As If You'd Shown Up), a dance-pop/R&B song written by Nguyễn Đức Cường and produced by  Huy Tuấn.  It was released digitally on Zing platforms and as an EP.<ref name="PLO 2011">{{cite web | title=Văn Mai Hương ra album đầu tiên | website=PLO | date=June 1, 2011 | url=https://plo.vn/content/ODg5NTE=.html | language=vi | access-date=November 7, 2021}}</ref>  That August, she release a Music Video directed by Film Ninja Productions that featured both an acoustic and the official electronic version of the songs while also celebrating the burgeoning singer-songwriter cafe scene of Vietnam.<ref name="Online 2011">{{cite web | last=Online | first=Tuoi Tre | title=Dễ thương Văn Mai Hương trong Nếu như anh đến | website=TUOI TRE ONLINE | date=August 11, 2011 | url=https://tuoitre.vn/news-450613.htm | language=vi | access-date=November 7, 2021}}</ref>

The success of "Nếu như anh đến", led to a secong single "Ngày chung đôi" and her first album. "Hãy mỉm cười".<ref name="vnexpress.net 2011">{{cite web | title=Văn Mai Hương ra mắt album đầu tay | website=vnexpress.net | date=December 21, 2011 | url=https://vnexpress.net/van-mai-huong-ra-mat-album-dau-tay-1916523.html | language=la | access-date=November 7, 2021}}</ref> Upon the release of the album, Hương remarked, "At 17 years old, I don't want to perform songs that might feel too old for my character and personality.  I wanted to make music for my fellow teenagers.  I want to make pop music that is dignified and modern to help create my distinct artistic identity.<ref name="vnexpress.net 2011"></ref>

===2013-2017: New Genres with Mười tám+, Mona Lisa, and Ngay Gan Anh===
In 2013, Huong released her second album "Mười tám +", where she released down-tempo ballads and R&B, alongside the dance-pop she had became known for.<ref name="Trí 2013">{{cite web | last=Trí | first=Dân | title=Clip: Văn Mai Hương ra mắt album 18+ | website=Báo điện tử Dân Trí | date=July 24, 2013 | url=https://dantri.com.vn/giai-tri/clip-van-mai-huong-ra-mat-album-18-1375100351.htm | language=vi | access-date=November 7, 2021}}</ref>

In 2014, she collaborated with Dương Triệu Vũ to release ""Ngày gần anh".<ref name="Nam 2016">{{cite web | last=Nam | first=Tin tức Việt | title=Văn Mai Hương là ai và Văn Mai Hương có chồng chưa | website=Tin Tức Việt Nam – Đọc báo tin tức 24h trong ngày | date=September 20, 2016 | url=https://tintucvietnam.vn/van-mai-huong-la-ai-va-van-mai-huong-co-chong-chua-d170567.html | language=vi | access-date=November 7, 2021}}</ref>  She would win "Favorite Female Singer at the 2014 HTV Awards.<ref name="Vân 2014">{{cite web | last=Vân | first=Thu | title=HTV Awards lần tám không nhiều bất ngờ | website=Báo điện tử Tiền Phong | date=April 6, 2014 | url=https://tienphong.vn/post-682641.tpo | language=vi | access-date=November 7, 2021}}</ref>Soon after, she would cancel many shows and withdraw from the industry, later pointing to her struggles with [[Major depressive disorder|Depression]].<ref name="Theo Thủy Nguyễn Đời sống Pháp luật 2015">{{cite web | author=Theo Thủy Nguyễn Đời sống Pháp luật | title=Văn Mai Hương bị trầm cảm : Tạm rời showbiz vì bị bệnh | website=Tin tức 24h | date=March 30, 2015 | url=https://www.24h.com.vn/ca-nhac-mtv/van-mai-huong-huy-nhieu-show-do-tram-cam-c73a699427.html | language=vi | access-date=November 7, 2021}}</ref>  She would come back at the end of 2015 with the single "Mona Lisa", a collaboration with Khắc Hưng and contained elements of [[Swing music|Swing-Jazz]]<ref name="LEO MEDIA 2015">{{cite web | title=Văn Mai Hương ấn tượng với Mona Lisa -  Sao Showbiz | website=LEO MEDIA | date=December 12, 2015 | url=http://saoshowbiz.com/xem.php?urltin=van-mai-huong-an-tuong-voi-mona-lisa | language=vi | access-date=November 7, 2021}}</ref> In 2016, she would feature in Phạm Hồng Phước's song "Thời thanh xuân sẽ qua".<ref name="Như 2021">{{cite web | last=Như | first=Khả | title=Văn Mai Hương gia nhập hội sao Việt có đôi chân "que củi": qua thời mũm mĩm ở Vietnam Idol | website=YAN | date=October 20, 2021 | url=https://www.yan.vn/van-mai-huong-khien-fan-lo-lang-khi-xuat-hien-voi-doi-chan-gay-guoc-280291.html | language=vi | access-date=November 7, 2021}}</ref> She would also serve as a judge on Vietnam Idol Kids. In 2017, she released the ballad "Những khát khao ấy".

===2018-2021:  "Hương (Scent)"===

In 2021, she released her third full album "Hương (Scent)".<ref name="Việt 2021">{{cite web | last=Việt | first=Theo Tiểu Nhi Dân | title=Văn Mai Hương quyết định liều lĩnh giữa mùa dịch và cái kết bất ngờ | website=Tin tức 24h | date=September 20, 2021 | url=https://www.24h.com.vn/giai-tri/van-mai-huong-quyet-dinh-lieu-linh-giua-mua-dich-va-cai-ket-bat-ngo-c731a1290597.html | language=vi | access-date=November 7, 2021}}</ref>
