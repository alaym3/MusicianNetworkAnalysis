{{Short description|American indie rock band}}'''Night Moves''' are an American [[indie rock]] band originating from [[Minneapolis]].

==History==
Night Moves began in 2012, releasing their first full-length record titled ''Colored Emotions'' on [[Domino Recording Company|Domino Records]].<ref name="pitchfork">{{cite web |last1=Brown |first1=Harley |title=Night Moves: Colored Emotions |url=https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/17230-colored-emotions/ |publisher=[[Pitchfork (website)|Pitchfork]] |accessdate=17 June 2019}}</ref><ref name="popmatters">{{cite web |last1=Branson |first1=Scott |title=Night Moves: Colored Emotions |url=https://www.popmatters.com/164333-night-moves-colored-emotions-2495806576.html |publisher=[[PopMatters]] |accessdate=17 June 2019}}</ref> In 2016, Night Moves released their second full-length album on Domino titled ''Pennied Days''.<ref name="huffingtonpost">{{cite web |last1=Epting |first1=Chris |title=Night Moves’ "Pennied Days," one of the best albums you’ve never heard (with new video) |url=https://www.huffpost.com/entry/night-moves-pennied-days-one-of-the-best-albums_b_5939bdf3e4b0b65670e568e7 |publisher=[[Huffington Post]] |accessdate=17 June 2019}}</ref><ref name="brooklynvegan">{{cite web |last1=Pearis |first1=Bill |title=Night Moves ready new LP ‘Pennied Days,’ share "Carl Sagan" single, touring this spring (dates) |url=http://www.brooklynvegan.com/night-moves-rea/ |publisher=[[BrooklynVegan]] |accessdate=17 June 2019}}</ref> In 2019, Night Moves released their third full-length album on Domino titled ''Can You Really Find Me''.<ref name="pastemagazine">{{cite web |last1=Sicurella |first1=Savannah |title=Night Moves Release Electric New Single "Recollections" |url=https://www.pastemagazine.com/articles/2019/06/night-moves-release-new-single-recollections.html |publisher=[[Paste (magazine)|Paste]] |accessdate=17 June 2019}}</ref>

==Discography==
'''Studio albums'''
*''Colored Emotions'' (2012, Domino)
*''Pennied Days'' (2016, Domino)
