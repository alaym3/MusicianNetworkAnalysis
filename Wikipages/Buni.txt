'''Buni''' ({{lang-ur|بونی}}; also spelled '''Booni''') is a town and the headquarters of [[Upper Chitral District]] in [[Khyber Pakhtunkhwa]], [[Pakistan]].

==Demography==
The residents of Buni are [[Kho people|Khos]], and they speak [[Khowar language|Khowar]] which is spoken and understood throughout Chitral valley.
[[Urdu]], the national language of Pakistan, is also spoken and understood.

==Climate==
The climate is considered to be a local [[Mediterranean climate]]. During the year there is abundant rainfall. This climate is considered to be [[Mediterranean climate|Csa]] to the [[Köppen-Geiger]] climate classification. The average annual temperature in Buni is 15.6&nbsp;°C. About 418&nbsp;mm of precipitation falls annually.
