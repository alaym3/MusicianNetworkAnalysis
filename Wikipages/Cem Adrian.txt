'''Cem Filiz''' (born 30 November 1980), better known by his stage name '''Cem Adrian''', is a Turkish musician, singer-songwriter and record producer.

==Early life==
His father was a merchant, while his mother was a housewife. Adrian was born to a family from [[Bosnia]] in [[Edirne]], as the second child of his parents.<ref>{{cite web | url = http://arama.hurriyet.com.tr/arsivnews.aspx?id=296117 | title = Dünyanın bütün sesleri onun sesinde bir arada | publisher = Hürriyet Pazar }}</ref> His real name is Cem Filiz.<ref>{{cite web | title = Sesim Pavorotti'den bile daha güçlü | url = http://haber.gazetevatan.com/0/50086/8/magazin | publisher = gazetevatan | accessdate = 16 August 2013 | date = 28 March 2005 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140107210752/http://haber.gazetevatan.com/0/50086/8/magazin | archivedate = 7 January 2014 | url-status = dead }}</ref> Adrian chose his stage name after Adrianople, the original name of Edirne in ancient times.<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RJzwbzumRhw Video] [[Fazıl Say]]'s the first presentation contains all the information.</ref> He later met [[Fazıl Say]] through [[Demet Sağıroğlu]], and with Say's invitation, he enrolled in [[Bilkent University]]'s Department of Performing Arts.<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.hurriyet.com.tr/kelebek/paparazzi/29979756.asp | title = Ünlüler nasıl keşfedildi? | date = 4 September 2015 | publisher = [[Hürriyet]] | accessdate = 8 September 2015 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150927142818/http://www.hurriyet.com.tr/kelebek/paparazzi/29979756.asp | archivedate = 27 September 2015 | url-status = live }}</ref>

==Career==
===Career beginnings===
In 2003, he worked as a soloist and dancer for the group Mystica. In February 2005, he released his first album, ''[[Ben Bu Şarkıyı Sana Yazdım]]''. This album includes the songs which Adrian had recorded by himself between 1997 and 2003 in Edirne. In January 2006, he started working on his second self-produced album. ''Aşk Bu Gece Şehri Terk Etti'', which contained 13 songs written by himself, was released in December 2006. Artists [[Umay Umay]],<ref>Aşk Bu Gece Şehri Terk Etti. 5th song: Bana Özel</ref> Denizhan<ref>Aşk Bu Gece Şehri Terk Etti. 4th song: Yağmur</ref> and Suicide<ref>Aşk Bu Gece Şehri Terk Etti. 13th song: Merdivenler</ref> were featured on three of the album's songs.

In 2008 he released two albums, ''Essentials / Seçkiler'' and ''Emir'', and produced the music for Abdullah Oğuz's film ''Sıcak'', where he also played the role of a young [[imam]].<ref>[http://www.ansfilmsicak.com/index.html Sicak production credits: Menu Sicak >> Künye] {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090225114020/http://www.ansfilmsicak.com/index.html |date=25 February 2009 }}</ref>

===Recent years===

On 30 December 2015, his first single album ''İlk ve Son Kez'', which contained two songs, was released on internet. One of the songs was "İlk ve Son Kez", which was previously published, while the other was a new version of "Sessizce", which was originally included in the album ''[[Aşk Bu Gece Şehri Terk Etti]]''.<ref>{{cite web | author = Cem Adrian | title = Cem Adrian: "İlk ve Son Kez" albümü | url = https://twitter.com/cemadrian/status/682290129197752320 | publisher = Twitter | accessdate = 30 March 2017 | date = 30 December 2015 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20170401162803/https://twitter.com/cemadrian/status/682290129197752320 | archivedate = 1 April 2017 | url-status = live }}</ref>

On 21 January 2016, his album ''[[Essentials / Seçkiler 2|Seçkiler 2]]'' was released by Dokuz Sekiz Müzik.<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.esenshop.com/detail.aspx?id=96926 | accessdate = 1 February 2016 | title = Cem Adrian / Seçkiler 2 | publisher = Esenshop | date = 1 February 2016 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160205192133/http://www.esenshop.com/detail.aspx?id=96926 | archivedate = 5 February 2016 | url-status = live }}</ref> In September 2016, he announced the release date of his album ''Tuz Buz'' to be September 2017,<ref>{{cite web | author = Cem Adrian | title = Cem Adrian: Seni Seviyorum | url = https://www.instagram.com/p/BQe_OeljXiv/ | publisher = Instagram | accessdate = 30 March 2017 | date = 14 February 2017 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20170401162807/https://www.instagram.com/p/BQe_OeljXiv/ | archivedate = 1 April 2017 | url-status = live }}</ref> but it was eventually released in November 2017.

In 2017, to raise money for a child's surgery, Adrian together with Manuş Baba, [[Ceylan Ertem]], [[Mabel Matiz]] and Derya Köroğlu gave a concert under the title "Bir Çocuk İçin Şarkılar (Songs for a Child)".<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.sozcu.com.tr/hayatim/magazin-haberleri/cem-adrian-ve-dostlari-bir-cocuk-icin-sarkilar-soyleyecek/ | accessdate = 22 June 2017 | title = Cem Adrian ve dostları, ‘Bir çocuk için şarkılar’ söyleyecek | publisher = Sözcü | date = 13 April 2017 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20170419061312/http://www.sozcu.com.tr/hayatim/magazin-haberleri/cem-adrian-ve-dostlari-bir-cocuk-icin-sarkilar-soyleyecek/ | archivedate = 19 April 2017}}</ref>

==Image==
Adrian is among the artists who have publicly spoken about [[censorship in Turkey]], freedom of speech and [[Human rights in Turkey|human rights]]. In response to questions regarding his sexuality, he said: "Is it possible for a man who sings with a high pitched voice to not face such comments?"<ref name="elele">{{cite web | url = http://www.elele.com.tr/unluler/roportajlar/cem-adrian-HaberDetay-449?2.Page#sthash.yyVWeMY4.dpuf | title = Benim İşim Gücüm, Derdim Aşk! | accessdate = 11 May 2014 | work = Elele | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20151208183654/http://www.elele.com.tr/unluler/roportajlar/cem-adrian-HaberDetay-449?2.Page#sthash.yyVWeMY4.dpuf#sthash.yyVWeMY4.dpuf | archivedate = 8 December 2015 | url-status = live }}</ref> His album ''[[Kayıp Çocuk Masalları]]'', which was released in 2010, featured a cover that had a LGBT theme<ref>{{cite web | url = http://arsiv.kadincadergisi.com/%E2%80%9Ckayip-cocuk-masallari%E2%80%9D-cem-adrian-%E2%80%A6-2.html | title = 'Kayıp Çocuk Masalları' Cem Adrian... | accessdate = 11 May 2014 | work = Kadınca | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20151211070837/http://arsiv.kadincadergisi.com/%e2%80%9ckayip-cocuk-masallari%e2%80%9d-cem-adrian-%e2%80%a6-2.html | archivedate = 11 December 2015 | url-status = dead }}</ref> about which he said: 
{{quote|Does it matter whether I'm gay or not? I think people need to learn not to ask questions like that! I think what goes on in my private life is not related to anyone. I'm a musician, that's it.|Cem Adrian, November 2010<ref name="elele" />}}

After facing criticism for supporting the [[LGBT]] community in 2010, he said: "Actually I have a problem with those who have issues with people's sexual identity."<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.kaosgl.com/sayfa.php?id=6117 | title = 'Cinsel Kimliklerle Derdi Olanlarla Derdim Var' | date = 30 December 2012 | accessdate = 11 May 2010 | work = Kaos GL | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20151208162521/http://www.kaosgl.com/sayfa.php?id=6117 | archivedate = 8 December 2015 | url-status = dead }}</ref> In 2012, he recorded a music video for the song "Yalnızlık" from his album ''[[Siyah Bir Veda Öpücüğü]]'', in which a transgender woman was shown.<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.kaosgl.com/sayfa.php?id=12415 | title = Cem Adrian’dan "Farklı" Bir Yalnızlık | date = 3 October 2012 | accessdate = 11 May 2014 | work = Kaos GL | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20151208155202/http://www.kaosgl.com/sayfa.php?id=12415 | archivedate = 8 December 2015 | url-status = dead }}</ref> There was also a scene involving a young guy masturbating, which was meant to imply [[loneliness]], the title of the song.<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.radikal.com.tr/yasam/cem_adrianin_yeni_klibi_sosyal_medyayi_salladi-1102884 | title = Cem Adrian'ın yeni klibi sosyal medyayı salladı | date = 5 October 2012 | accessdate = 11 May 2014 | work = Radikal | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140512220136/http://www.radikal.com.tr/yasam/cem_adrianin_yeni_klibi_sosyal_medyayi_salladi-1102884 | archivedate = 12 May 2014 | url-status = live }}</ref>
