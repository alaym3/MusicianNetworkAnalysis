'''Marguerita Estell "Rita" Springer''' (born April 17, 1967) is an American [[Christian music]]ian, who primarily plays a style of [[contemporary worship music]] and [[contemporary christian music|Christian pop]] music. She has released twelve studio albums, ''Love Covers'' (1995), ''All I Have'' (2000), ''Created to Worship'' (2001), ''Effortless'' (2002), ''Rise Up: Live Worship'' (2004), ''I Have to Believe'' (2005), ''Worth It All'' (2007), ''Beautiful You'' (2008), ''In This Forever'' (2011), ''The Playlist'' (2012), ''Battles'' (2017), and ''Light'' (2020).<ref name="AM Disc">{{cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/rita-springer-mn0000807848/discography|title=Rita Springer : Album Discography|author=AllMusic|publisher=[[AllMusic]]|accessdate=March 27, 2017|author-link=AllMusic}}</ref><ref name="CR AP">{{cite web|url=http://www.crossrhythms.co.uk/artists/Rita_Springer/8057/|title=Rita Springer : Artist Profile|author=Cross Rhythms|publisher=[[Cross Rhythms]]|accessdate=March 27, 2017|author-link=Cross Rhythms}}</ref>
