'''Sleep Token''' are a British rock [[collective]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://substreammagazine.com/2019/12/i-dont-know-if-god-exists-so-im-going-to-worship-sleep-token-instead/|title=I don't know if God exists, so I'm going to worship Sleep Token instead|last=Shotwell|first=James|date=2019-12-18|website=Substream Magazine|language=en-US|access-date=2020-03-14}}</ref>

==Background==

The members of Sleep Token are a masked, anonymous collective of musicians said to be united in their worship of an ancient deity identified as 'Sleep', who appeared to the band's lead singer, 'Vessel', in a dream.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.loudersound.com/features/who-and-what-the-hell-are-sleep-token|title=Who and what the hell are Sleep Token?|last=May 2017|first=Luke Morton19|website=Metal Hammer Magazine|language=en|access-date=2020-03-14}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.knotfestatsea.com/artist/sleep-token/|title=SLEEP TOKEN|last=Sixthman|website=Knotfest at Sea|language=en-US|access-date=2020-03-14}}</ref> 

== Touring ==
The band was scheduled to play at [[Knotfest]] 2020, but the event was cancelled due to concerns regarding the ongoing [[COVID-19 pandemic]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.altpress.com/news/slipknot-cancel-asian-tour-knotfest-japan-coronavirus/|title=Slipknot cancel Asian tour, Knotfest Japan due to coronavirus concerns|last=Greenwood|first=Koltan|date=2020-03-05|website=Alternative Press|language=en-US|access-date=2020-03-14}}</ref> The band has previously toured their home country of the United Kingdom and also opened for [[Issues (band)|Issues]] in the United States.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Sleep Token Surprise Release Deluxe Version Of 'Sundowning' Featuring Whitney Houston & Billie Eilish Covers / the NOISE|url=https://bringthenoise.com/blog/sleep-token-surprise-release-deluxe-version-of|access-date=2020-07-18|website=Sleep Token Surprise Release Deluxe Version Of ‘Sundowning’ Featuring Whitney Houston & Billie Eilish Covers / the NOISE}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Shotwell|first=James|date=2019-12-18|title=I don't know if God exists, so I'm going to worship Sleep Token instead|url=https://substreammagazine.com/2019/12/i-dont-know-if-god-exists-so-im-going-to-worship-sleep-token-instead/|access-date=2020-07-18|website=Substream Magazine|language=en-US}}</ref>On 18 June 2021 the band performed at [[Download Festival|Download Pilot]], where they headlined the second stage.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://downloadfestival.co.uk/download-pilot/|title = Download Pilot}}</ref>

== Musical style ==
Sleep Token's style has been described as [[Alternative rock|alternative]], [[post-metal]], [[ambient music|ambient]], [[progressive metal]] and [[Electronic music|electronic]], among other genres.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.kerrang.com/reviews/album-review-sleep-token-sundowning/|title=Album Review: Sleep Token – Sundowning|website=Kerrang!|access-date=2020-03-14}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thecirclepit.com/2017/07/sleep-token-cult-or-a-band|title=Sleep Token – Cult or a Band? {{!}} The Circle Pit|website=www.thecirclepit.com|language=en-US|access-date=2020-03-14}}</ref>
