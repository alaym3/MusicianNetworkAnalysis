'''Fred John Philip Gibson''',<ref>https://www.ascap.com/repertory#/ace/search/workID/897626370</ref><ref>Robin Murray{{cite news|title=Fred again x Oneman Pay Homage To Wiley With 'Eskimo Disko'|url=https://www.clashmusic.com/news/fred-again-x-oneman-pay-homage-to-wiley-with-eskimo-disko|access-date=28 June 2019|work=[[Clash (magazine)|Clash]]|date=31 May 2019}}</ref> (born 19 July 1993) known professionally as '''Fred Again''' (stylised as '''Fred again..''') or simply '''Fred''' (stylised as '''FRED'''),<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://soundcloud.com/fredagain|title=Fred again..|website=SoundCloud|language=en|access-date=2019-10-02}}</ref> is a British singer, songwriter, multi-instrumentalist, record producer and [[remix]]er.<ref>{{cite news|title=Christine and the Queens – Comme si (Fred again Remix)|url=https://soundcloud.com/christineandthequeens/christine-and-the-queens-19|access-date=28 June 2019|publisher=soundcloud.com}}</ref><ref>Ben Kaye & Alex Young{{cite news|title=Listen: Brian Eno and Karl Hyde's new collaborative single "The Satellites"|url=https://consequenceofsound.net/2014/03/listen-brian-eno-and-karl-hydes-new-collaborative-single-the-satellites/|access-date=5 May 2019|publisher=consequenceofsound.net|date=4 March 2014}}</ref><ref>Andy Beta{{cite news|title=First Listen: Brian Eno & Karl Hyde, 'Someday World'|url=https://www.npr.org/2014/04/27/306161810/first-listen-brian-eno-karl-hyde-someday-world|access-date=5 May 2019|publisher=npr.org|date=27 April 2014}}</ref> Gibson's career began after he joined an a cappella group at [[Brian Eno]]'s studio in London; there, he managed to impress Eno{{citation needed|date=October 2021}} with his skill in using [[Logic Pro]], leading to Gibson gaining the revered musician as a mentor.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Fred Again {{!}} Biography & History|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/fred-again-mn0003882656/biography|access-date=2021-08-04|website=AllMusic|language=en}}</ref>

== Career ==
As a producer and songwriter, Fred is known for the range of artists he has worked with including [[Baxter Dury]], [[Stormzy]], [[Ed Sheeran]], [[Burna Boy]], [[Headie One]], [[Halsey (singer)|Halsey]], Eminem, [[Octavian (rapper)|Octavian]], [[The xx]], [[Underworld (band)|Underworld]], [[Brian Eno]] and [[Stefflon Don]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.popjustice.com/briefing/weve-listened-to-the-clean-bandit-and-demi-lovato-song-ten-times-in-a-row-and-here-are-our-findings/|title=We've listened to the Clean Bandit and Demi Lovato song ten times in a row and here are our findings|date=2018-05-10|work=Popjustice|language=en-US|access-date=2019-05-03}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Universal Music Publishing Australia Signs Robinson To Global Publishing Deal|url=https://www.umusicpub.com/au/News/2018/02/Universal-Music-Publishing-Australia-Signs-Robinson-To-Global-Publishing-umusicpub.com|access-date=5 May 2019|publisher=Universal Music Publishing Group|date=15 February 2018}}{{dead link|date=March 2020}}</ref>
