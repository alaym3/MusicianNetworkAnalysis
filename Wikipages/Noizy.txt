'''Rigels Rajku''' ({{IPA-sq|ɾiˈɡɛls ɾajˈku|lang}}; born 27 September 1986), known professionally as '''Noizy''', is an Albanian rapper, singer, songwriter, actor and entrepreneur.<ref name="TeksteShqip">{{cite web |publisher=TeksteShqip |title=Noizy–Biografia |url=https://www.teksteshqip.com/noizy |access-date=13 February 2021 |language=sq |url-status=live}}</ref>

== Life and career ==

Rigels Rajku was born on 27 September 1986 into a multi-religious Albanian family in the village of [[Sukth]], then part of the [[People's Socialist Republic of Albania|People's Socialist Republic]], present Albania.<ref name="TeksteShqip"/> His father is a [[Islam in Albania|Muslim]] from [[Dibër valley|Dibër]] and his mother a [[Catholic Church in Albania|Catholic]] from [[Krujë]].<ref name="Opinion">{{cite web |publisher=[[Televizioni Klan|Opinion]] |title=Zonë e Lirë/ Noizy: Ja si ishin vitet e mia në Sukth |url=https://opinion.al/zone-e-lire-noizy-ja-si-ishin-vitet-e-mia-ne-sukth/ |access-date=13 February 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191102124352/https://opinion.al/zone-e-lire-noizy-ja-si-ishin-vitet-e-mia-ne-sukth/ |archive-date=2 November 2019 |language=sq |date=1 April 2016 |quote=Ai tha se nënën e ka katolike me orgjinë nga Kruaja, ndërsa babai është i besimit mysliman nga Dibra.}}</ref> In 1997, Rajku and his family fled to [[London]], England, as a refugee to escape the war and conflicts in connection with the [[Albanian Civil War]].<ref name="TeksteShqip"/><ref name="Opinion"/><ref>{{cite web |newspaper=[[Telegrafi]] |title='Largohu se je refugjat', Noizy flet për racizmin me të cilin është përballur në Angli |url=https://telegrafi.com/largohu-se-je-refugjat-noizy-flet-per-racizmin-te-cilin-eshte-perballur-ne-angli/ |access-date=13 February 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210213215249/https://telegrafi.com/largohu-se-je-refugjat-noizy-flet-per-racizmin-te-cilin-eshte-perballur-ne-angli/ |archive-date=13 February 2021 |language=sq |date=2 June 2020}}</ref> He was raised in the [[Woolwich]] neighborhood of [[South London|Southeast London]] and subsequently developed an interest in boxing and street fighting.<ref name="TeksteShqip"/>

In 2006, Rajku returned to his native country and appeared on the third edition of [[Top Fest]], as a solo artist with the song "Jemi OTR" and as part of his group OTR with the song "Kështu e bëjme ne".<ref name="TeksteShqip"/> In May 2007, he collaborated with Albanian rappers J-No and Decee and returned to the competition with the song "Kur vijm na".<ref name="TeksteShqip"/> Later that year in November 2007, he unsuccessfully participated with the song "Veç për ty" in the [[Kënga Magjike 2007|9th edition]] of [[Kënga Magjike]] and failed to qualify for the contest's semi-finals.<ref>{{cite web |publisher=Lajmi |title=Kur Noizy dështoi me paraqitjen e parë në 'Kënga Magjike' |url=https://lajmi.net/kur-noizy-deshtoi-me-paraqitjen-e-pare-ne-kenga-magjike/ |access-date=13 February 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190227040447/http://lajmi.net/kur-noizy-deshtoi-me-paraqitjen-e-pare-ne-kenga-magjike/ |archive-date=5 September 2019 |language=sq |date=9 November 2017}}</ref>
