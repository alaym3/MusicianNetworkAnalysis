'''Jockstrap''' are a musical duo consisting of '''Georgia Ellery''' and '''Taylor Skye'''. They met at London's [[Guildhall School of Music & Drama]] in 2016.<ref>{{Cite news|last=Rogers|first=Jude|date=2020-04-12|title=One to watch: Jockstrap|language=en-GB|work=The Guardian|url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2020/apr/12/one-to-watch-jockstrap|access-date=2020-06-09|issn=0261-3077}}</ref> Their music combines Ellery's half-whispered vocals and string arrangements with Skye's electronic production.<ref>{{Cite news|last=Geoghegan|first=Kev|date=2019-08-06|title=Jockstrap: The music duo defying description|language=en-GB|work=BBC News|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/entertainment-arts-49109262|access-date=2020-06-09}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Meet Jockstrap, an Uncanny Electro-Pop Duo From the Retro Future|url=https://pitchfork.com/features/rising/jockstrap-interview/|access-date=2020-06-09|website=Pitchfork|language=en}}</ref>

They released their first EP ''Love Is the Key to the City'' in 2018. After signing to [[Warp Records]] in early 2020, they released ''[[Wicked City (EP)|Wicked City]]'' in June 2020.
