'''Harris Jung''', known professionally as '''Harris J''', is a [[British people|British]] singer who signed with [[Awakening Music|Awakening]] in 2013. He is a graduate of London's [[BRIT School|BRIT School of Performing Arts]].<ref>{{Cite news | url=http://www.chron.com/life/celebrities/article/The-Muslim-Justin-Bieber-pens-children-s-book-11942348.php| title="Muslim Justin Bieber" pens children's book on peace| work=Houston Chronicle| access-date=2017-10-02}}</ref> In 2017 he published a children's book entitled ''Salam Alaikum: "A Message of Peace''".<ref>{{Cite web | url=https://www.thebookseller.com/news/ss-childrens-launches-muslim-imprint-595416| title=S&S Children's brings 'Salaam Reads' imprint to UK {{!}} The Bookseller| website=www.thebookseller.com| language=en| access-date=2017-10-02}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=-CozDwAAQBAJ&printsec=frontcover&dq=harris+j&hl=en&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwi9p-qR6dLWAhXrDMAKHaZXB3oQ6AEIJDAA#v=onepage&q=harris%20j&f=false| title=Salam Alaikum: A Message of Peace| last=J| first=Harris| date=2017-09-05| publisher=Simon and Schuster| isbn=9781481489386| language=en}}</ref>
