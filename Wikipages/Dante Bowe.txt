'''Dante Bowe''' is an American [[contemporary worship music|Christian worship]] singer, songwriter and worship pastor. Bowe is a member of the [[Bethel Music|Bethel Music collective]] and the [[Maverick City Music]] collective, and serves as a worship pastor at Addereth Church in [[Columbus, Georgia]].<ref name="JRA">{{cite web |url= https://jeffroberts.com/artists/dante-bowe |title= Dante Bowe {{!}} Jeff Roberts & Associates |website= Jeff Roberts & Associates |access-date= April 11, 2021}}</ref><ref name="Bethel Music Bio">{{cite web |url= https://bethelmusic.com/artists/dante-bowe/ |title= Dante Bowe - Bethel Music |website= [[Bethel Music]] |access-date= April 11, 2021}}</ref> In 2017, Bowe released his debut single, "Potter and Friend" featuring Jesse Cline, which was shortly followed with the independent release of his debut studio album, ''Son of a Father''.

Bowe signed with [[Bethel Music]] in June 2019. In 2020, He made his debut appearance with Bethel with the single "[[Champion (Bethel Music and Dante Bowe song)|Champion]]" which was featured on the album ''[[Revival's in the Air]]''. This was also followed by three standalone singles: "Don't Talk About It" featuring Jesse Cline, "Be Alright" featuring [[Amanda Lindsey Cook]], and "[[Voice of God (song)|Voice of God]]" featuring [[Steffany Gretzinger]] and [[Chandler Moore]]. In 2021, he released his second studio album, ''[[Circles (Dante Bowe album)|Circles]]'', which contained the hit single "[[Joyful (song)|Joyful]]". "Joyful" won the [[GMA Dove Award]] for Contemporary Gospel Recorded Song of the Year at the [[52nd GMA Dove Awards|2021 GMA Dove Awards]]. ''Circles'' debuted at No. 7 on ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'''s Top Gospel Albums chart in the United States. Bowe also featured on several collaborations, most notably the song "[[Wait on You]]" alongside [[Elevation Worship]], Maverick City Music and Chandler Moore. Bowe received three nominations for the [[Grammy Award for Best Gospel Performance/Song]] at the [[64th Annual Grammy Awards|2022 Grammy Awards]]: two solo nominations for his singles "Voice of God" and "Joyful", and a songwriting nomination for "Wait on You".

== Career ==
Dante started his career by traveling with Eddie James ministries for many years. In January 2017, Dante Bowe released his debut single, "Potter and Friend" featuring Jesse Cline.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.air1.com/music/artists/dante-bowe |title= Dante Bowe {{!}} Air1 Worship Music |website= Air1 |access-date= April 11, 2021}}</ref> He then independently released his debut studio album, ''Son of a Father'', on March 31, 2017.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.newreleasetoday.com/albumdetail.php?album_id=23721 |title= Son of a Father by Dante Bowe {{!}} CD Reviews And Information {{!}} NewReleaseToday |date= July 16, 2020 |website= NewReleaseToday |publisher= NRT Media, Inc |access-date= April 11, 2021}}</ref>

On January 11, 2019, Dante Bowe released "The Giant Is Dead" featuring [[Travis Greene]], as a standalone single.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.newreleasetoday.com/albumdetail.php?album_id=23722 |title= The Giant Is Dead (feat. Travis Greene) (Single) by Dante Bowe {{!}} CD Reviews And Information {{!}} NewReleaseToday |date= July 16, 2020 |website= NewReleaseToday |publisher= NRT Media, Inc |access-date= April 11, 2021}}</ref> In July 2019, [[Bethel Music]] announced that they signed Dante Bowe, subsequently joining the Bethel Music collective.<ref>{{cite web |url=  https://www.thechristianbeat.org/bethel-music-signs-georgia-based-singer-songwriter-dante-bowe/ |title= Bethel Music Signs Singer/Songwriter Dante Bowe - TCB |last= Longs |first= Herb |date= July 23, 2019 |website= The Christian Beat |access-date= April 11, 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=  https://www.ccmmagazine.com/news/bethel-music-signs-singer-songwriter-dante-bowe/ |title= Bethel Music Signs Singer/Songwriter Dante Bowe – CCM Magazine |date= July 24, 2019 |website= [[CCM Magazine]] |access-date= April 11, 2021}}</ref> On May 15, 2020, Bowe and Bethel Music released "[[Champion (Bethel Music and Dante Bowe song)|Champion]]" as the first promotional single from the album ''[[Revival's in the Air]]'' (2020).<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/index.php/new-music/7676-bethel-music-champion-feat-dante-bowe-live-performance |title= Bethel Music Presents Dante Bowe's "Champion" - On March 1, 2021, Dante Bowe announced that he will be releasing his second studio album, ''[[Circles (Dante Bowe album)|Circles]]'', on March 26.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.ccmmagazine.com/news/dante-bowe-to-release-circles-album-mar-26/ |title= Dante Bowe to Release 'Circles' Album Mar 26 – CCM Magazine |date= March 1, 2021 |website= [[CCM Magazine]] |access-date= April 11, 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=  https://www.thechristianbeat.org/dante-bowe-to-debut-circles-march-26/ |title= Dante Bowe To Debut 'Circles' March 26 -
