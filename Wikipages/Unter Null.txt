'''Erica Dunham''', better known by her stage name '''Unter Null''', founded the solo [[aggrotech]] music project in 1998 at the age of 17, while living in [[Seattle]], United States.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Unter Null + Cyanotic Tour {{!}} Sputnikmusic|url=https://www.sputnikmusic.com/news/12936/Unter-Null--Cyanotic-Tour/|access-date=2021-06-07|website=www.sputnikmusic.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Unter Null|url=https://www.discogs.com/artist/68344-Unter-Null|access-date=2021-06-07|website=Discogs|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=eMinor|title=Unter Null {{!}} Electronica from Hamburg, DE|url=http://www.reverbnation.com/unternull|access-date=2021-06-07|website=ReverbNation|language=en}}</ref> {{As of|2012}}, she is based in [[Portland, Oregon]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.facebook.com/unternullfanpage |title=Unter Null |publisher=Facebook |access-date=2013-11-24}}</ref> Dunham is trained in [[Piano|Classical Piano]], [[Cello]] and [[Guitar]].<ref>[http://www.last.fm/music/Unter+Null Last FM]</ref>

==History==
Unter Null ([[German language|German]] for 'below zero') initially began as a [[powernoise]] project influenced by a number of similar prominent artists, including [[Noisex]], [[Converter (music)|Converter]] and [[P.A.L]]. Her first, self-titled, album was released in 2001.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://unternull.bandcamp.com/album/unter-null |title=Unter Null &#124; Unter Null |publisher=Unternull.bandcamp.com |date=2001-01-01 |access-date=2013-11-24}}</ref> In 2002, she was signed to Leech and NTT/Annihilvs Records to release her second, web-exclusive album ''Neocide''.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2003-10-22|title=/ neocide \|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20031022053131/http://www.annihilvs.org/unter-null/neocide/|access-date=2021-06-07|website=web.archive.org}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://unternull.bandcamp.com/album/neocide |title=Neocide &#124; Unter Null |publisher=Unternull.bandcamp.com |date=2002-06-01 |access-date=2013-11-24}}</ref> In 2005, she signed to label [[Alfa Matrix]]. She has since released a number of EPs and albums, remixed several artists, and appeared on compilation albums.

==Music==
In early 2008, Dunham launched her side project ''Stray''<ref>{{Cite web|date=2013-02-18|title=Unter Null side-project Stray launches debut album 'Abuse by Proxy' - Unter Null news at SIDE-LINE.COM|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130218060150/http://www.side-line.com/news_comments.php?id=31050_0_2_0_C|access-date=2021-06-07|website=web.archive.org}}</ref> with the release of the self-titled a 2-CD album in cooperation with a number other bands including Mothboy, Detritus and Testube. In addition to regular live performances, Unter Null embarked on a short U.S. and European tour in 2005, then a longer European tour in 2006.

In the spring of 2012, Erica relocated to Hamburg, Germany, where at the time of writing, Erica is currently on production of the next Unter Null album.

===House fire===
On December 25, 2008, Dunham's [[studio]], containing her musical, recording and electronic equipment as well as other belongings, was destroyed in a fire. It was feared at first that her newest album, ''Moving On'',<ref>[http://hangout.altsounds.com/news/113647-unter-null-returns-with-2nd-album-moving-on-in-3-different-versions.html  UNTER NULL returns with 2nd album, 'Moving on', in 3 different versions] December 21, 2009 hangout.altsounds.com News</ref> had been lost in the fire, although tracks were able to be recovered from a damaged [[hard drive]].<ref>[http://www.side-line.com/news_comments.php?id=38490_0_2_0_C Fire destroys Unter Null studio and new album]</ref>
