'''Filippo Uttinacci''' (born 12 September 1997), professionally known as '''Fulminacci''', is an Italian singer-songwriter.<ref>{{Cite encyclopedia|url=https://www.treccani.it/enciclopedia/fulminacci|title=Fulminacci|encyclopedia=[[Treccani|Treccani Encyclopedia]]}}</ref>
In April 2019, he debuted with the [[studio album]] ''[[La vita veramente]]'', which was awarded the Targa Tenco for Best Debut Album.<ref>{{Cite news|author=Leonardo Gabriele Stenta|url=https://www.ondarock.it/news.php?&id=6946|title=Premio Tenco 2019: i vincitori|work=OndaRock|date=1 July 2019}}</ref> He also won the prize for Artist of the Year at the Rockol Awards 2019. 
After performing in several Italian music festivals, he enlarged his popularity in 2021, competing for the first time in the [[Sanremo Music Festival]] with the song "[[Santa Marinella (song)|Santa Marinella]]".<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.rainews.it/dl/rainews/articoli/primi-big-amadeus-renga-coma-cose-gaia-0daf331e-cc67-4bd1-8fd5-585839f142a5.html|title=Sanremo 2021, primi Big rivelati da Amadeus: Orietta Berti, Francesco Renga, Coma Cose e Gaia|work=RaiNews|date=17 December 2020|access-date=20 December 2020}}</ref> His second studio album was released shortly after, and became his first top-5 entry on the [[FIMI]] Italian Albums Chart.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fimi.it/top-of-the-music/classifiche.kl#/charts/1/2021/11|publisher=[[Federation of the Italian Music Industry]]|language=it|title=Top Album – Classifica settimanale WK 11 (dal 12.03.2021 al 18.03.2021)|access-date=20 March 2021}}</ref>

== Biography ==
===Early life===
Filippo Uttinacci was born in Rome on 12 September 1997 and grew up in the [[Massimina|Casal Lumbroso]] district.<ref name="Vanity Fair">{{cite web|url=https://www.vanityfair.it/music/storie-music/2020/09/10/fulminacci-io-che-mi-diverto-ad-essere-stesso|work=[[Vanity Fair (magazine)|Vanity Fair]]|language=it|title=Fulminacci: Io che mi diverto a essere me stesso|author=Raffaele Panizza|access-date=19 March 2021|date=10 September 2020}}</ref> 
His father is a graphic designer, while his mother co-owns a furniture store, together with his aunt.<ref name="Riviera24">{{cite web|url=https://www.riviera24.it/2021/03/sanremo-2021-fulminacci-con-santa-marinella-porto-sul-palco-il-mio-sguardo-oggettivo-su-una-storia-damore-678770/|publisher=Riviera24.it|language=it|title=Sanremo 2021, Fulminacci: Con Santa Marinella porto sul palco il mio sguardo oggettivo su una storia d'amore|author=Elisa Colli|date=5 March 2021|access-date=19 March 2021}}</ref> He has a brother.<ref name="Riviera24"/>
He attended primary school and middle school at a Catholic institute run by nuns.<ref name="Vanity Fair"/> He later studied at the ''[[liceo classico]]'' "Dante Alighieri" in the [[Prati|rione Prati]] in Rome.<ref name="Vanity Fair"/> 
At the age of 10, Fulminacci entered a music school and started to play guitar.<ref name="Sentire Ascoltare">{{cite web|url=https://sentireascoltare.com/articoli/intervista-2019-fulminacci-un-artista-che-ha-la-forma-dellacqua/|publisher=SentireAscoltare.com|language=it|title=Intervista a Fulminacci: un artista che ha la forma dell'acqua|author=Giorgia Salerno|date=22 June 2019|access-date=19 March 2021}}</ref> Two years later, he also began studying drums.<ref name="Riviera24"/> He wrote his first songs at the age 16.<ref name="Sentire Ascoltare"/><ref name="Rolling Stone Interview">{{cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.it/musica/interviste-musica/fulminacci-e-un-giovane-vecchio/453072/#Part1|work=[[Rolling Stone (magazine)|Rolling Stone]]|language=it|title=Fulminacci è un giovane vecchio|author=Claudio Biazzetti|access-date=19 March 2021|date=7 April 2019}}</ref> He was initially reluctant at singing, but he decided to reveal himself recording the songs he wrote.<ref name="Sentire Ascoltare"/> Encouraged by the positive reaction received by his family, he sent his demos to independent label Maciste Dischi and signed his first recording deal.<ref name="Sentire Ascoltare"/> 
In 2018, he debuted as an actor, starring in the short film ''Fammi parlare'' by Luca Iacoella.<ref name="Rockol Bio">{{cite web|url=https://www.rockol.it/artista/fulminacci|publisher=Rockol.it|language=it|title=Fulminacci – Biografia}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cinemabreve.org/portal/it/cortometraggio/commedia/fammi-parlare|publisher=CinemaBreve.org|title=Fammi Parlare (2018) - 14' - Luca Iacoella}}</ref> He also appeared in the music video of the song "In My Mind", recorded for Italian TV series ''Immaturi''.<ref name="Rockol Bio"/>

===Music career===
His debut single, "Borghese in borghese", was released in January 2019 by Maciste Dischi.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.it/musica/news-musica/fulminacci-canta-la-sua-opinione-cosicche-si-diffonda/440852/|work=[[Rolling Stone (magazine)|Rolling Stone]]|language=it|title=Fulminacci canta la sua opinione cosicché si diffonda|date=9 January 2019}}</ref> He initially decided not to reveal his real name and surname.<ref name="Rolling Stone Interview"/> For that reason, he decided to adopt the stage name "Fulminacci", chosen after brainstorming for something "unexpected and very personal". His pseudonym creates an assonance with his surname, and is tipically used as an exclamation in comics.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://xl.repubblica.it/articoli/la-vita-veramente-di-fulminacci/87217/|work=XL. [[la Repubblica]].|language=it|title=La vita veramente di Fulminacci|author=Giulia Zanichelli|date=3 April 2019|access-date=19 March 2021}}</ref>

His second single, "La vita veramente", was released in February 2019, and followed by "Una sera", which was one of the first songs he wrote.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.indielife.it/2019/02/17/la-vita-veramente-il-video-del-nuovo-singolo-di-fulminacci/|publisher=IndieLife.it|date=17 February 2019|title=La vita veramente, il video del nuovo singolo di Fulminacci|author=Fabio Tranchina}}</ref><ref name="Sky Canzoni">{{cite web|url=https://tg24.sky.it/spettacolo/musica/2020/04/30/fulminacci-canzoni-famose|publisher=[[Sky Italia]]|language=it|title=Fulminacci, le canzoni più famose del cantautore italiano|date=30 April 2020|access-date=19 March 2021}}</ref>
His debut studio album, also titled ''[[La vita veramente]]'' and featuring all of his previous singles, was released by Maciste Dischi and distributed by Artist First on 9 April 2019. The album was produced by Federico Nardelli and Giordano Colombo.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.it/musica/indie/fulminacci-la-vita-veramente/2019/04/0218891/|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|language=it|title=La Vita Veramente è il disco di esordio di Fulminacci|date=2 April 2019|access-date=19 March 2021}}</ref> Shortly after the album's release, Fulminacci took part in the 2019 International Worker's Day concert in Piazza San Giovanni Laterano, Rome, annually supported by Italian trade unions [[Italian General Confederation of Labour|CGIL]], [[Italian Confederation of Workers' Trade Unions|CISL]] and [[Italian Labour Union|UIL]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://tg24.sky.it/spettacolo/2019/04/30/concerto-primo-maggio-2019-scaletta|publisher=[[Sky Italia]]|language=it|title=Concerto Primo maggio a Roma, la scaletta e tutto quello che c'è da sapere|date=1 May 2019|access-date=19 March 2021}}</ref> He performed at several Italian music festivals, including the Mi Ami Festival in [[Milan]], the Mind Festival in [[Montecosaro]], the Goa-Boa Festival in Genoa and the Flowers Festival in [[Turin]].
<ref>{{cite web|url=https://style.corriere.it/lifestyle/la-nuova-musica-italiana-mi-ami-2019/|work=Style. [[Corriere della Sera]]|language=it|title=Mi Ami 2019, a Milano la nuova musica italiana|author=Valentina Ravizza|date=23 May 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.cronachemaceratesi.it/2019/07/22/al-via-il-mind-festival-con-il-concerto-di-fulminacci/1278647/|publisher=CronaceMaceratesi.it|date=22 July 2019|title=A via il Mind Festival con il concerto di Fulminacci|access-date=19 March 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://tg24.sky.it/spettacolo/musica/2019/07/05/goa-boa-festival-programma-artisti|publisher=[[Sky Italia]]|language=it|title=Goa Boa Festival a Genova: il programma completo e gli artisti in line up|date=5 July 2019|access-date=19 March 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.it/eventi/flowers-festival-2019-line-up-completa/2019/05/2020359/|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|language=it|title=Scopri qui la line up del Flowers Festival 2019|date=20 May 2019|access-date=19 March 2021}}</ref>
During the summer of 2019, Fulminacci released the music videos for the songs "Resistenza" and "Tommaso", both featured in his debut album.<ref>{{cite web|access-date=20 March 2021|url=https://www.allmusicitalia.it/news/fulminacci-resistenza-tour-premio-tenco.html|work=All Music Italia|language=it|title=Fulminacci: un'estate al massimo tra Resistenza, tour e Targa Tenco|date=2 July 2019|author=Simone Zani}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.allmusicitalia.it/news/fulminacci-tommaso.html|work=All Music Italia|language=it|title=Fulminacci lancia il video di Tommaso: un'intreccio d'amore adolescenziale per il vincitore della Targa Tenco 2019|author=Simone Zani|date=8 September 2019|access-date=20 March 2021}}</ref> In July 2019, ''La vita veramente'' received the Targa Tenco for Best Debut Album.<ref name="Targa Tenco">{{cite web|url=https://tg24.sky.it/spettacolo/musica/2019/07/02/targhe-tenco-2019-vincitori|publisher=[[Sky Italia]]|language=it|title=Targhe Tenco 2019: annunciati tutti i vincitori|date=2 July 2019}}</ref>
In December of the same year, he was named Artist of the Year at the Rockol Awards 2019.<ref name="Rockol Awards 2019">{{cite web|url=https://www.rockol.it/news-709641/fulminacci-la-vita-veramente-rockol-awards-2019-artista-dell-anno|publisher=Rockol.it|title=Rockol Awards 2019: è Fulminacci l'artista dell'anno|date=11 December 2019|access-date=20 March 2021}}</ref> On 6 December 2019, he also released the vinyl version of his debut album, featuring the singles "Le ruote, i motori" and "San Giovanni".<ref name="Sky Canzoni"/><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sorrisi.com/musica/canzoni/fulminacci-san-giovanni-e-il-nuovo-singolo/|work=[[TV Sorrisi e Canzoni]]|language=it|title=Fulminacci: San Giovanni è il nuovo singolo|author=Cecilia Esposito|date=6 December 2019|access-date=20 March 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sorrisi.com/musica/canzoni/fulminacci-il-nuovo-singolo-un-fatto-tuo-personale-parla-delle-persone-e-della-societa-che-mi-sta-intorno/|work=[[TV Sorrisi e Canzoni]]|language=it|title=Fulminacci: il nuovo singolo Un fatto tuo personale «Parla delle persone e della società che mi sta intorno|author=Cecilia Esposito|date=4 December 2020|access-date=20 March 2021}}</ref>

On 1 May 2020, he performed for the second time at the International Worker's Day concert. As a consequence of the [[COVID-19 pandemic]], the event was adapted to a televised show without any audience, and Fulminacci performed "San Giovanni" from the Audtiorium Parco della Musica in Rome.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.repubblica.it/spettacoli/musica/2020/05/01/news/concertone_primo_maggio_senza_la_piazza-255397931/|work=[[la Repubblica]]|language=Italian|title=Concertone Primo Maggio senza piazza, Nannini emoziona sui tetti di Milano, Ambra si commuove, Sting rende omaggio all'Italia|author=Chiara Ugolini|access-date=11 October 2020|date=1 May 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rockol.it/news-713445/concerto-primo-maggio-2020-roma-la-diretta-la-scaletta-le-canzoni|publisher=Rockol.it|language=it|title=Concerto del Primo Maggio 2020, Roma: il resoconto completo della serata|date=1 May 2021|access-date=20 March 2021}}</ref>
On 9 September 2020, Fulminacci released the new single "Canguro", followed by "Un fatto tuo personale" in December of the same year.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.leggo.it/spettacoli/musica/fulminacci_torna_canguro_nuovo_singolo_dopo_album_d_esordio_premiato_la_targa_tenco-5438690.html|work=[[Leggo]]|language=it|title=Fulminacci torna con Canguro. Il nuovo singolo dopo l'album d'esordio premiato con la Targa Tenco|date=2 September 2020|access-date=20 March 2021}}</ref> Both songs were included in Fulminacci's second studio album, ''[[Tante care cose]]'', which was released on 12 March 2021, shortly after his appearance at the [[Sanremo Music Festival 2021|71st Sanremo Music Festival]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.mtv.it/musica/fulminacci-santa-marinella-significato-canzone-sanremo/|publisher=[[MTV Italy]]|language=it|title=Fulminacci ha spiegato il significato della canzone di Sanremo Santa Marinella|date=4 March 2021|access-date=20 March 2021}}</ref> Fulminacci competed in the Big Artists section with the song "[[Santa Marinella (song)|Santa Marinella]]", also released as a single from ''Tante care cose'', and placed 16th in a field of 26.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.repubblica.it/dossier/spettacoli/sanremo-2021/2021/03/07/news/sanremo_2021_la_classifica_finale-290707488/|work=[[la Repubblica]]|language=it|title=Sanremo 2021, la classifica finale: vincono i Maneskin|date=7 March 2021|access-date=20 March 2021}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Since 2017, Fulminacci is in a relationship with actress Lia Grieco.<ref name="Chi è"/> Grieco and Ultinacci appeared together on the music video for "In My Mind".<ref name="Rockol Bio"/> She also appeared in the video of Fulminacci's single "Tommaso".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://metropolitanmagazine.it/chi-e-lia-grieco-lattrice-fidanzata-di-fulminacci/|work=Metropolitan Magazine|language=it|title=Chi è Lia Grieco, l'attrice fidanzata di Fulminacci|date=3 March 2021|access-date=19 March 2021}}</ref><ref name="Chi è">{{cite web|url=https://chi-e.com/lia-grieco/|work=Chi è|author=Valentina Meloni|date=1 March 2021|access-date=19 March 2021|language=it|title=Lia Grieco}}</ref>
He is a supporter of [[A.S. Roma]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://metropolitanmagazine.it/sanremo-2021-calcio-in-primo-piano-per-quale-squadra-tifano-i-big/|work=Metropolitan Magazine|access-date=19 March 2021|title=Sanremo 2021, calcio in primo piano: per quale squadra tifano i big?|date=2 March 2021|language=it}}</ref>
