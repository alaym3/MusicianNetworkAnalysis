'''PinkPantheress''' (born 2001) is an English singer, songwriter, and record producer. In 2021, while attending university in London, she posted several songs to [[TikTok]] that went viral, including "[[Break It Off (PinkPantheress song)|Break It Off]]", and she subsequently signed to [[Parlophone]] and [[Elektra Records]]. Her singles "Pain" and "[[Just for Me]]" peaked in the top 40 of the [[UK Singles Chart]]. Her debut mixtape, ''[[To Hell with It]]'', was released in 2021. 

PinkPantheress's music spans a number of genres, including [[Lo-fi music|bedroom pop]], [[drum and bass]], [[Alternative pop|alt-pop]], [[2-step garage]], and [[Jungle music|jungle]], and often uses samples of 1990s and 2000s [[dance music]].

==Artistry==
===Influences===
PinkPantheress's stage name was inspired by her TikTok account of the same name, which was named after a question from the game show ''[[The Chase (British game show)|The Chase]]'', which asked "What is a female panther called?", and the 2006 film ''[[The Pink Panther (2006 film)|The Pink Panther]]''.<ref name="i-d">{{cite web |last1=Greenwood |first1=Douglas |title=PinkPantheress is the musician dominating TikTok's DIY scene |url=https://i-d.vice.com/en_uk/article/m7e3xa/pinkpantheress- |website=[[i-D]] |access-date=19 July 2021 |language=en |date=21 June 2021}}</ref><ref name="vogueint" /> PinkPantheress has listed [[Lily Allen]], [[Just Jack]], [[Michael Jackson]], [[My Chemical Romance]], [[Imogen Heap]], and [[K-pop]] songs as inspirations, also citing [[Blink-182]], [[Good Charlotte]], [[Green Day]], early [[Panic! at the Disco]], [[Linkin Park]], and [[Frou Frou (band)|Frou Frou]] as inspirations for her melodies and beat choices.<ref name="nprint" /><ref name="diyint">{{cite web |last1=Evans |first1=Georgia |title=PinkPantheress: "I always wanted to have an audience" |url=https://diymag.com/2021/10/12/pinkpantheress-just-for-me-october-2021-interview |website=[[DIY (magazine)|DIY]] |access-date=October 13, 2021 |language=en |date=October 12, 2021}}</ref><ref name="dazed" /><ref name=":0" /> She has called [[Hayley Williams]] a "big influence", stating that she wanted to become an artist after seeing her perform as part of [[Paramore]] during [[Reading and Leeds Festivals|Reading Festival]] when she was 14 years old, and that she had "never seen someone have so much fun on stage and look so effortless while doing it".<ref name="nmeint" /><ref name="vogueint" />

==Personal life==
As of 2021, PinkPantheress is based in London, England.<ref name="theguardian">{{cite web |last=Cragg |first=Michael |title=Shygirl, Bad Boy Chiller Crew, PinkPantheress: summer's hottest artists |url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2021/may/29/shygirl-bad-boy-chiller-crew-pinkpantheress-summers-hottest-artists |website=[[The Guardian]] |access-date=18 July 2021 |date=29 May 2021}}</ref> She is studying film at [[University of the Arts London]].<ref name="vogueint" />
