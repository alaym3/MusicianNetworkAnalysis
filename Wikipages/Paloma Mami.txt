'''Paloma Rocío Castillo Astorga''' (born November 11, 1999), known professionally as '''Paloma Mami''', is a Chilean-American singer-songwriter. She began her musical career in 2018, and her first studio album, ''Sueños de Dalí'', was released in March 2021.

== Career ==
Castillo was a participant in the first season of the Chilean talent show, ''[[w:es:Rojo, el color del talento|Rojo, el color del talento]],'' on [[Televisión Nacional de Chile]]. In July 2018, she entered the competition. However, due to the terms of the contract offered by the channel, Castillo left the show after two appearances.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.biobiochile.cl/noticias/espectaculos-y-tv/tv/2018/07/27/exchica-rojo-paloma-castillo-explica-por-que-decidio-renunciar-al-programa.shtml|language=es|title=Exchica Rojo Paloma Castillo explica por qué decidió renunciar al programa|last=Villa|first=Bernardita|date=July 27, 2018|website=BioBioChile|access-date=December 29, 2018}}</ref>

Castillo independently released the single "Not Steady" with a music video in June 2018.<ref name="emol"/><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://culto.latercera.com/2018/12/14/paloma-mami-anuncia-lanzamiento-nuevo-tema/|title=Paloma Mami anuncia lanzamiento de un nuevo tema|date=December 14, 2018|website=Culto|language=es|access-date=December 29, 2018}}</ref> The song was written by Paloma Mami and Lesz. The song mixes styles like [[dancehall]], [[Trap music|trap]], [[Soul music|soul]] and [[Rhythm and blues|R&B.]]<ref name="emol" /> On September 2, 2018, she was the opening act for [[Arcángel (singer)|Arcángel]] at [[Teatro Caupolicán]].<ref name="arcangel">{{Cite web|url=https://noisey.vice.com/es/article/qvq4pd/una-noche-con-paloma-mami-en-la-casa-parlante|title=Una noche con Paloma Mami en la Casa Parlante|website=noisey.vice.com|language=es|access-date=December 25, 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181226035244/https://noisey.vice.com/es/article/qvq4pd/una-noche-con-paloma-mami-en-la-casa-parlante|archive-date=December 26, 2018|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref name="soloartistas">{{Cite web|url=http://soloartistaschilenos.cl/de-rojo-al-teatro-caupolican-el-exitoso-comienzo-de-carrera-de-paloma-mami/|title=De Rojo al Teatro Caupolicán: El exitoso comienzo de carrera de Paloma Mami|website=soloartistaschilenos.cl|language=es|access-date=December 25, 2018}}</ref> The following month, she signed with [[Sony Music Latin]], becoming the first Chilean artist to sign with the label.<ref name="billboard">{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/latin/8481219/paloma-mami-sony-music-us-latin-deal|title=Paloma Mami Joins Sony Music US Latin Roster|last=Fernandez|first=Suzette|date=October 22, 2018|website=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|language=en|access-date=December 17, 2018}}</ref><ref name="emol" /><ref name="rolling">{{Cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-latin/latin-trap-music-r-and-b-alex-rose-paloma-mami-774772/|title=''Latin Artists Changed Trap Music Forever — R&B Is Next''|website=[[Rolling Stone]]|language=en|access-date=January 24, 2019}}</ref>

== Artistry ==
Castillo defines her music as Latin [[R&B]] and [[Urbano music|urbano]]. She incorporates elements of [[Pop music|pop]] and [[Trap music|trap]].<ref name="influencias">{{Cite web|url=https://billboard.com.ar/paloma-mami-la-artista-chilena-que-conquista-con-su-flow-elegante/|title=Paloma Mami: La artista chilena que conquista con su "flow elegante"|website=Billboard Argentina|access-date=April 11, 2019}}</ref> She cites several people as influences, including [[Bad Bunny]], [[Drake (musician)|Drake]], [[Rihanna]], [[Christina Aguilera]], [[Amy Winehouse]], [[Aaliyah]] and [[Ella Fitzgerald]].<ref name="influencias" /><ref name="influenciasmujeres">{{Cite web|url=https://www.ahoranoticias.cl/noticias/tendencias/257620-paloma-mami-mujeres-artistas-christina-aguilera-amy-winehouse-rihanna.html|title=Paloma Mami revela las artistas que la inspiraron en la música|website=Ahora Noticias|access-date=April 14, 2019}}</ref><ref name="upnext">{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/latin/8510779/paloma-mami-up-next-fingias-interview|title=Paloma Mami on Working With Diplo and Making Anthems For 'Girls In The Club'|website=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|language=en|access-date=May 10, 2019}}</ref>

== Awards and nominations ==
Paloma has won 4 awards, including Copihue de Oro for Best Urban Music Singer and Giga Award. Overall, she has been nominated for 21 awards, including nominations for the [[2019 MTV Millennial Awards|MTV Millennial Awards]] for Viral Artist, [[2019 Premios Juventud|Premios Juventud]] for The New Urban Generation and [[Premio Lo Nuestro 2021|Premio Lo Nuestro]] for Female Urban Artist of the Year.
