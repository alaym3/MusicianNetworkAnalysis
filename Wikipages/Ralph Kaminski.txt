'''Rafał Stanisław Kamiński''' (born November 8, 1990), known professionally as '''Ralph Kaminski''', is a [[Poles|Polish]] [[singer-songwriter]] experimenting with [[musical styles]], including [[alternative pop]] and [[chamber music]]. Recipient of the [[Grzegorz Ciechowski|Grzegorz Ciechowski Artistic Award]] of the [[Toruń|City of Toruń]] (2020) and two nominations during [[Fryderyk|26th Fryderyk Awards Gala]]. 

== Early life ==
Kaminski was born and raised in [[Jasło]]. His mother is a [[teacher]]. He has a younger brother Wiktor. His cousin is a fellow singer [[Michał Szpak]], although Ralph himself has never confirmed this.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Michał Szpak i Ralph Kaminski to rodzina! Kim są dla siebie znani wokaliści? EXCLUSIVE|url=https://www.gala.pl/artykul/michal-szpak-i-ralph-kaminski-to-rodzina-kim-sa-dla-siebie-znani-wokalisci-exclusive-180508030503|access-date=2021-10-19|website=www.gala.pl|language=pl-PL}}</ref>

He developed an interest in music as a child. At the age of 11, Kaminski started attending vocal classes at the Jasło Cultural Centre. He studied art and music before embarking on a professional career as a musician in 2010. Kaminski graduated from the Jazz and Popular Music Department at the [[Stanisław Moniuszko Academy of Music]] in [[Gdańsk]]. He also studied at the Pop Department of [[Codarts|Codarts University for the Arts]] in [[Rotterdam]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Ralph Kaminski: Najważniejsze są emocje!|url=https://muzyka.interia.pl/wywiady/news-ralph-kaminski-najwazniejsze-sa-emocje,nId,2380890|access-date=2021-10-19|website=muzyka.interia.pl|language=pl}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Ralph Kamiński - biografia|url=https://muzyka.interia.pl/news-ralph-kaminski-biografia,nId,2321269|access-date=2021-10-19|website=muzyka.interia.pl|language=pl}}</ref> 

== Career ==

=== Talent shows ===
In 2009, he appeared in one of the episodes of [[TVP2]] programme ''[[Szansa na Sukces]]'', performing a song by [[Perfect (Polish band)|Perfect]] "Objazdowe nieme kino". In 2012 he took part in the [[X Factor (Polish season 2)|second edition]] of the [[TVN (Polish TV channel)|TVN]] talent show [[X Factor (Polish TV series)|X Factor]]; he performed [[Norah Jones|Norah Jones']] song "[[Don't Know Why]]" during the auditions. He was eliminated at the [[boot camp|boot camp stage]]{{dn|date=October 2021}}.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Mateusz|date= 21 December 2013|title=PODSŁUCHALNIA: "Młode pokolenie bez kompleksu Zachodu" - Ralph Kaminski (wywiad)|url=http://podsluchalnia.blogspot.com/2013/12/mode-pokolenie-bez-kompleksu-zachodu.html|access-date=2021-10-19|website=PODSŁUCHALNIA}}</ref>

=== Breakthrough ===
On 6 June 2016, he performed at the "Alternative Stage" concert during the [[National Festival of Polish Song in Opole|53rd National Festival of Polish Songs in Opole]]. On 14 November, he released his debut studio album entitled "Morze". The album was promoted with the singles: "Podobno", "Zawsze", and "Meybick Song". In 2017, he performed at the [[Open'er Festival]], and in June 2018, he performed at the [[Orange Warsaw Festival]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Taco Hemingway, Rasmentalism i Ralph Kaminski na Orange Warsaw Festival|url=https://www.wirtualnemedia.pl/artykul/polscy-artysci-w-skladzie-orange-warsaw-festival-2018|access-date=2021-10-19|website=www.wirtualnemedia.pl|language=pl}}</ref> 

In 2019, he won the [[Grand Prix]]{{dn|date=October 2021}} of the 40th Actor Song Review (Polish: Przegląd Piosenki Aktorskiej) in [[Wrocław]], thanks to his "Welcome To Our Fairytale" performance from the musical based on [[Academy of Mr. Kleks]], and "On the Corner" written by [[Agnieszka Osiecka]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Wieliczko|first=Małgorzata|title=40. Przegląd Piosenki Aktorskiej we Wrocławiu. Laureaci. Zloty Tukan|url=https://www.wroclaw.pl/40-przeglad-piosenki-aktorskiej-laureaci|access-date=2021-10-19|website=www.wroclaw.pl|language=pl}}</ref> In August that year, he performed at the [[Pol'and'Rock Festival]]. On 22 November, he released his second [[studio album]] entitled "Youth", which reached number 17 on t[[OLiS|he official sales list in Poland]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Oficjalna lista sprzeda�y :: OLIS - Official Retail Sales Chart|url=http://olis.onyx.pl/listy/index.asp?idlisty=1259|access-date=2021-10-19|website=olis.onyx.pl}}</ref>
