'''Terrell Davis''', known by the stage name '''Ralo''', is an American rapper signed to [[Gucci Mane]]'s 1017 Records via [[Interscope Records]] and his own label Famerica Records.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ue9F59fR4TA |title=Ralo Reveals The Biggest Advice He Received From Young Scooter and Future |work=DJ Smallz Eyes 2 YouTube channel |date=September 7, 2015 |access-date=September 12, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thefader.com/2016/08/29/ralo-diary-of-the-streets-2-ep |title=Atlanta's Ralo Brings The Heat With His EP Diary Of The Streets II |work=[[The Fader]] |date=August 29, 2016 |access-date=September 12, 2016}}</ref> Ralo gained recognition following the release of his single, "Can't Lie" featuring rapper [[Future (rapper)|Future]], which was released in 2015.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://hotspotatl.com/3462986/ralo-talks-about-his-ties-with-future-his-current-single-cant-lie/ |title=Ralo Talks About His Ties With Future & His Current Single Can't Lie |work=Hot 107.9 |date=May 30, 2015 |access-date=September 12, 2016}}</ref> ''[[Spin (magazine)|Spin]]'' magazine placed the song at number 65 on its "Every Future Song of 2015, Ranked" list.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.spin.com/2015/12/every-future-song-of-2015-ranked/ |title=Every Future Song of 2015, Ranked |work=[[Spin (magazine)|Spin]] |date=December 18, 2015 |access-date=October 7, 2016}}</ref> In 2015 Ralo released the mixtapes ''Famerican Gangster'' and ''Diary of the Streets''.<ref name="FG" /><ref name="diary" />

==Personal life==
Ralo is from [[Atlanta|Atlanta, Georgia]].<ref name=vibe>{{Cite web|url=https://www.vibe.com/2018/03/ralo-diary-of-the-streets-interview/|title = Ralo Speaks on Living in Atlanta's Drug-Infested Neighborhood, the Bluff|date = 5 March 2018}}</ref><ref name=djbooth>{{Cite web|url=https://djbooth.net/features/2016-09-22-ralo-interview|title=Ralo Made $12 Million by Selling Drugs, but Rap Pays Him Freedom (Interview)}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://thehundreds.com/blogs/content/ralo-the-bluff-internet-haters-interview|title=Unapologetic :: Atlanta's Ralo Talks About Escaping the Bluff & Internet Haters}}</ref> He practices Islam.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://noisey.vice.com/en_us/article/ralo-future-my-brothers-premiere-interview |title=Ralo Teams with Future and Premieres "My Brothers" |work=[[Vice (magazine)#Website|Noisey]] |date=August 16, 2016 |access-date=September 15, 2016}}</ref> He was arrested for a criminal conspiracy charge in 2018.
