'''Shane Niemi''' (born August 2, 1995 in [[Los Angeles]]), known by his stage name '''Iamnotshane''' (stylized in all [[Letter case|lowercase]]), is an American singer and songwriter.

Known for his flamboyant dance style, his music video for his 2016 song "Insecure" became viral, which includes a version of [[Michael Jackson]]'s [[Michael Jackson#Music videos and choreography|Anti-gravity lean]] and has been viewed over 5 million times<ref>{{Citation|title=iamnotshane - Insecure (Official Music Video)|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fYWZNN4bbz8|language=en|access-date=2021-02-22}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://don411.com/indie-artist-shane-partners-with-acclaimed-animation-dancer-dytto-for-dance-music-video/|title=INDIE ARTIST SHANE PARTNERS WITH ACCLAIMED ANIMATION DANCER DYTTO FOR "DANCE" MUSIC VIDEO|date=January 24, 2017|website=Don411.com Media :: Performing Arts News Unabridged}}</ref>

==History==
Niemi has been performing since the age of 8 and began writing original songs at age 12.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://genius.com/artists/Iamnotshane|title=iamnotshane|website=Genius}}</ref>
In 2014, he posted his first music video for "Sinister". A year later he released his first EP ''Sad''. In 2016, his music video for ''Insecure'' went viral and now has over 5.3 million views on YouTube.<ref>{{Cite web|title=iamnotshane - Insecure (Official Music Video) - YouTube|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fYWZNN4bbz8&feature=youtu.be|access-date=2020-07-30|website=www.youtube.com}}</ref>

He later changed his stage name to iamnotshane and released his self-titled EP ''iamnotshane'' in 2020 through Avant Garden and [[Island Records|Island]]

In 2020, French producer/DJ [[Michael Calfan]] did a remix of ''Afterlife''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://music.apple.com/us/album/afterlife-michael-calfan-remix-single/1502592443|title=Afterlife (Michael Calfan Remix) - Single by iamnotshane|website=Apple Music}}</ref>

For unknown reasons, he left Avant Garden and Island Records and began releasing singles independently in late 2020.

In 2021, he gave an interview for the fashion magazine ''[[Vanity Teen]]'', recounting his beginnings as an artist and his current influence.<ref>[https://www.vanityteen.com/iamnotshane-vanity-teen-x-the-lonely-avocado/ Iamnotshane – Vanity Teen x The Lonely Avocado] 19 December 2020. ''Vanity Teen.''</ref>
