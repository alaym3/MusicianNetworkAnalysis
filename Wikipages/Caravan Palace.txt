'''Caravan Palace''' is a French electronic band based in [[Paris]]. The band's influences include [[Django Reinhardt]], [[Vitalic]], [[Lionel Hampton]], and [[Daft Punk]].<ref name="Allmusic">{{Cite web| url = {{AllMusic|class=artist|id=p1063876/biography|pure_url=yes}}| title = Caravan Palace - Biography| publisher = [[AllMusic]]| accessdate = 21 October 2009}}</ref> The band released their debut studio album, ''[[Caravan Palace (album)|Caravan Palace]]'', on the [[Wagram Music|Wagram]] label in October 2008. The record charted in Switzerland, Belgium, and France, where it reached a peak position of number 11.<ref name="Album">{{cite web | url = http://www.lescharts.com/showitem.asp?interpret=Caravan+Palace&titel=Caravan+Palace&cat=a | title = Caravan Palace - Caravan Palace | website = lescharts.com | accessdate = 22 October 2009}}</ref>

== History ==
The band formed as a three-piece when they were recruited to compose the soundtrack for a silent adult film.<ref>{{cite web | title= Caravan Palace: Swinging To Electro Beats|website = [[NPR]]| url= https://www.npr.org/templates/story/story.php?storyId=129474513 | access-date= 4 March 2019}}</ref> Loïc Barrouk took an interest in the project and booked the band a recording studio and a series of gigs. More performers were required for the live concerts and the other three current band members were found after searching on [[MySpace]].<ref name="Biography">{{cite web | url = http://www.f-cat.de/Caravan-Palace-cv.html | title = Caravan Palace Biography | publisher = f-cat.de | accessdate = 2 October 2009 | url-status = dead | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20120228213957/http://www.f-cat.de/Caravan-Palace-cv.html | archive-date = 28 February 2012 }}</ref>

The band became popular on the internet after releasing a number of demos and promo singles. From 2006 to 2007 they spent a year touring around France, and their first [[music festival|festival]] appearance came at the [[Django Reinhardt Jazz Festival]] in 2007.<ref name="Biography"/> Following this emergence, the group were signed by the Paris-based [[Wagram Music]] record label. They spent the next year recording material for their debut studio album.

On 20 October 2008 the self-titled ''[[Caravan Palace (album)|Caravan Palace]]'' was released, preceded by the single "Jolie Coquine". The album received praise for its [[traditional jazz]] inclinations, and placed in a number of European albums charts. In Switzerland, the album reached number 72 and in Belgium it achieved a position of 42. The album performed best in the band's native France where it attained a peak position of 11 in August 2009, and remained on the [[Syndicat National de l'Édition Phonographique|French albums chart]] for 68 consecutive weeks. The band released their second official single, "Suzy", on 24 February 2009.<ref name="Suzy">{{cite web| url = https://itunes.apple.com/WebObjects/MZStore.woa/wa/viewAlbum?id=305881890&s=143442| title = Suzy - Single| website = [[iTunes]]| accessdate = 22 October 2009}}</ref>

On 3 October 2011, the band released an EP titled ''Clash'', featuring two new songs and four remixes of them. The band's second album, ''[[Panic (Caravan Palace album)|Panic]]'', was released on 5 March 2012.

In 2014, the band remixed [[Drake Bell]]'s single "Bitchcraft".<ref>{{cite web|title=Bitchcraft (Caravan Palace Remix) - Single|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/bitchcraft-caravan-palace/id867920704|website=iTunes|accessdate=5 November 2014}}</ref>

The band released the single "Comics" on 22 June 2015, and their second single "[[Lone Digger]]" on 18 September 2015.

The band's third studio album, ''[[Robot Face|<{{!}}°_°{{!}}>]]'', was released on 16 October 2015.<ref>{{cite web|title=Caravan Palace|url=http://f-cat.de/Caravan-Palace-main.html|website=F-Cat Productions|accessdate=30 December 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141231103445/http://f-cat.de/Caravan-Palace-main.html|archive-date=31 December 2014|url-status=dead}}</ref> They made a video for their track "Mighty"<ref>{{Citation|title=Caravan Palace - Mighty (feat. JFTH)|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eB3RJ1U_MJA|language=en|access-date=2019-10-30}}</ref> and previewed another song, "Wonderland", in a short promotional video.

In 2015, they appeared on ''[[Later... with Jools Holland]]''<ref>http://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/b06myydx</ref> on 27 and 30 October, and on ''[[The Jonathan Ross Show]]'' on 31 October. Subsequently, they appeared on [[Jools' Annual Hootenanny]] on 31 December 2016/1 January 2017 singing a cover of "[[Black Betty]]" and "Lone Digger".

On 4 February 2019 the band announced the release of a new single titled "Miracle" on 8 February 2019.<ref>{{Citation|title=Caravan Palace - Miracle (official audio)|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XRP9k9nlAfE|language=en|access-date=2019-10-30}}</ref>

On 28 June 2019 they released a single titled "Plume".<ref>{{Citation|last=CaravanPalace|title=Caravan Palace - Plume (Official audio)|date=2019-06-28|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cq3fwlZdWhw|access-date=2019-06-29}}</ref>

On 16 August they released a single titled "Supersonics".<ref>{{Citation|title=Caravan Palace - Supersonics (Official audio, with lyrics)|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zpJyjAKDMW4|language=en|access-date=2019-10-30}}</ref>

Their latest album, ''[[Chronologic]]'', was released on 30 August 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://play.google.com/store/music/album/Chronologic?id=Bjd4mk77tbfw36t5pihvnsw76xu&hl=en_US|title=Caravan Palace: Chronologic - Music on Google Play|website=[[Google Play Music]]|access-date=2019-08-30}}</ref>
