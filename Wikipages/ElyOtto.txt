'''Elliott Ferrous-Martin Platt''' (born February 24, 2004), known professionally as '''ElyOtto''', is a Canadian musician. He is best known for his song "[[SugarCrash!]]", which went viral on [[TikTok]] in early 2021.

== Life and career ==
Elliott Ferrous-Martin Platt<ref>{{Cite web|title=ElyOtto on TikTok|url=https://www.tiktok.com/@elpunko/video/6879445494649605377?_r=1&source=h5_m|access-date=2021-07-31|website=TikTok}}</ref> was born in 2004 to parents Mike, a musician and songwriter, and Natasha Sayer, a music teacher and musician, and raised in [[Calgary]], Canada.<ref>{{Cite web|title=High school student ElyOtto an overnight hyperpop sensation with major record deal|url=https://calgaryherald.com/entertainment/music/how-17-year-old-elyottos-lockdown-song-became-one-of-the-biggest-success-stories-of-the-year|access-date=2021-04-23|website=calgaryherald|language=en-CA}}</ref><ref name="Spin" />

After getting fired from his job at a pet store in Calgary in spring 2020, ElyOtto committed his time to working on songs and uploading them to [[SoundCloud]].<ref name="paperint">{{cite web |last1=Moran |first1=Justin |title=ElyOtto on the Algorithm and Autotune |url=https://www.papermag.com/elyotto-profane-2654469124.html?rebelltitem=23#rebelltitem23 |website=[[Paper (magazine)|Paper]] |access-date=October 25, 2021 |language=en |date=August 6, 2021}}</ref> His debut single "[[SugarCrash!]]" was released in August 2020. He followed this with his single "Teeth!".<ref name="Spin" /> "SugarCrash!" went viral on TikTok after ElyOtto posted a video using the song to the platform a week after its release, and he was soon signed to [[RCA Records]].<ref name="gaytimes" />

His debut EP is due for release later in 2021.<ref name="Spin"/> In April 2021, a remix of "SugarCrash!" featuring [[Kim Petras]] and [[Curtis Waters]] was released.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Curto |first1=Justin |title=Admit It, 'SugarCrash!' Was Already Great |url=https://www.vulture.com/2021/04/song-review-sugarcrash-elyotto-remix-kim-petras.html |website=[[New York (magazine)#Vulture|Vulture]] |access-date=October 25, 2021 |language=en-us |date=April 23, 2021}}</ref> His single "Profane" was released in August 2021.<ref name="paperint" />

==Artistry==
ElyOtto's music has been described as [[hyperpop]] and [[Pop music|pop]], and he has described his own music as hyperpop and pop. He has called his own music "sweet flashy runway pop" or "evil crusty I've-been-dead-since-1836 pop", and has described hyperpop as "a very trans sounding genre where you can manipulate your voice and use all these crazy flashy synths" that "still feels authentic because of the lyrics."<ref name="gaytimes">{{cite web |last1=Raza-Sheikh |first1=Zoya |title=ElyOtto is the trans TikTok star taking on the music industry |url=https://www.gaytimes.co.uk/culture/elyotto-is-the-trans-tiktok-star-taking-on-the-music-industry/ |website=[[Gay Times]] |access-date=October 25, 2021 |language=en |date=April 29, 2021}}</ref> His lyrics often revolve around his [[gender dysphoria]] and queer identity, and he regularly uses [[Auto-Tune]] on his vocals.<ref name="bbint" /><ref name="paperint" /> ''[[Spin (magazine)|Spin]]'' referred to ElyOtto as "the face of hyperpop" in April 2021.<ref name="Spin">{{Cite web|date=2021-04-08|title=How ElyOtto Became the New Face of Hyperpop|url=https://www.spin.com/2021/04/elyotto-sugarcrash-tiktok-interview/|access-date=2021-04-23|website=Spin}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Platt is [[transgender]].<ref name="Spin"/> He has described himself as "a little bit on the [[Gender variance|gender noncomforming]] side".<ref name="bbint">{{cite magazine |last1=Lynch |first1=Joe |title=ElyOtto on What Hyperpop Means to the LGBTQ+ Community |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/pride/9581662/elyotto-hyperpop-interview-lgbtq-community-pride-2021 |magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] |access-date=October 25, 2021 |language=en |date=June 3, 2021}}</ref>
