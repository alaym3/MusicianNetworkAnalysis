"'''Then Leave'''" is a song by American rapper and producer BeatKing featuring fellow rapper Queendome Come, released as a single on March 25, 2020, from BeatKing's album ''Gangsta Stripper Music 4'' (2020).<ref>{{cite web|url=https://music.apple.com/us/album/then-leave-feat-queendome-come-single/1519344411|title=Then Leave (feat. Queendome Come) - Single by Beatking on Apple Music|via=[[Apple Music]]|accessdate=July 9, 2020}}</ref> In June 2020, the song went viral on video-sharing app [[TikTok]].

==Background==
"Then Leave" garnered popularity after various choreographed dances were posted on [[TikTok]], based on the user Boujee.Tay's moves.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://stayhipp.com/media/tiktok/the-best-tiktok-trends-to-try-in-july-2020/|title=The Best TikTok Trends To Try In July 2020|work=Stay Hipp|last=Sommer|first=Liz|date=June 30, 2020|accessdate=July 6, 2020}}</ref> As of July 2020, the song's audio was used in over 151,000 videos.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://stayhipp.com/internet/then-leave-tiktok-dance/|title="Then Leave" TikTok Dance|work=Stay Hipp|last=Spence|first=Sade|date=June 19, 2020|accessdate=July 9, 2020}}</ref> In the midst of the song's growing popularity, Columbia Records removed the song's parent album from streaming services. BeatKing announced, however, a new version of the album would be released "soon".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/BEATKINGKONG/status/1275239493570826240?s=19|title=To all my fans y'all probably noticed my album with "Then Leave: got taken down off all the streaming services. @ColumbiaRecords did that lol A new version of GSM4 will be up there soon with new songs on it...|via=BeatKing on [[Twitter]] (confirmed account)|date=June 22, 2020|accessdate=July 9, 2020}}</ref>

==Chart performance==
On the US [[Rolling Stone Top 100|''Rolling Stone'' Top 100]], for the week ending July 9, 2020, the song debuted at number 88, with over four million streams.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/RS_Charts/status/1280229254396084226?s=19|title=.@BEATKINGKONG and @theequeendom_'s "Then Leave" enters this week's RS 100 at Number 88, with over 4 million on-demand audio streams last week...|via=[[Rolling Stone charts|''Rolling Stone'' Charts]] on [[Twitter]] (confirmed account)|date=July 6, 2020|accessdate=July 9, 2020}}</ref> The song also debuted at number 3 on the [[Bubbling Under Hot 100]].<ref name=":0" />

==Music video== 
The song's official video was released on March 27, 2020, directed by OG Visions.
