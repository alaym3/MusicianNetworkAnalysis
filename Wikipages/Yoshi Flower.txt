'''Joshua Smith''' (born January 9, 1995), known professionally as '''Yoshi Flower''',  is an American singer, songwriter and record producer who is signed to [[Interscope Records]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Yoshi Flower|url=https://www.interscope.com/artists/yoshi-flower|date=2019-04-02|website=Interscope Records|language=en|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Yoshi Flower|url=https://www.discogs.com/artist/6451451-Yoshi-Flower|website=Discogs|language=en|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=YOSHI FLOWER biography|url=https://www.last.fm/music/YOSHI+FLOWER/+wiki|website=Last.fm|language=en|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref>

== Early life ==
Joshua Smith was born in [[Detroit|Detroit, Michigan]]. His father gave him his first guitar, which he received in lieu of money from an artist when his son was 6. At age 12 his father began to let him play in local Detroit bars.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Bingham Farms native Yoshi Flower's music career is blooming|url=https://www.theoaklandpress.com/entertainment/bingham-farms-native-yoshi-flowers-music-career-is-blooming/article_ca9e519a-24c8-11e9-afd8-c32cad06446d.html|last=Twitter|first=Gary Graff ggraff@digitalfirstmedia com; @GraffonMusic on|website=The Oakland Press|language=en|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Yoshi Flower Talks Lana Del Rey Comparison, Nailing Shut the Genre 'Coffin'|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/8272245/yoshi-flower-interview|date=2018-03-29|website=Billboard|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref>

== Career ==
'''2014-2019: Career beginnings'''

Smith began releasing music and playing live shows in his hometown Detroit. He played in various bands, and released 2 EPs as part of the duo Gosh Pith to critical acclaim.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Stream Detroit-Based "Cosmic Trap" Duo Gosh Pith's EP, Window|url=https://www.papermag.com/stream-detroit-based-cosmic-trap-duo-gosh-piths-ep-window-1427572286.html|date=2015-05-19|website=PAPER|language=en|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Gosh Pith {{!}} Album Discography|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/gosh-pith-mn0003449142|website=AllMusic|language=en-us|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref> In 2017, he wrote and recorded his first solo demos while living in [[Topanga, California]] with electronic artist [[Elohim (musician)|Elohim]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Yoshi Flower Brings Production to Full Bloom on Debut and Elohim Collaboration|url=https://girlundergroundmusic.com/2018/03/11/yoshi-flower-brings-production-to-full-bloom-on-debut-and-elohim-collaboration/|last=janette|date=2018-03-11|website=Girl Underground Music|language=en|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref> The first of which, “Movies,” surfaced in March 2018 with [[The Fader]] comparing his lyrics to [[Lana Del Rey]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Yoshi Flower goes from 0 to 100 on "Movies," his debut track|url=https://www.thefader.com/2018/03/05/yoshi-flower-movies|website=The FADER|language=en|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref>

After releasing two more songs independently, “Woke” and “Just On Drugs” respectively, Flower signed a record deal with Interscope Records.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Q&A: Yoshi Flower talks record deal, new music at SXSW - The Daily Texan|url=http://www.dailytexanonline.com/2019/03/13/qa-yoshi-flower-talks-record-deal-new-music-at-sxsw|website=www.dailytexanonline.com|language=en|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref> Following his singing, he released the Brown Paper Bag (Maxi) single via GRDN/Interscope Records.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Yoshi Flower Hits Hard With New Single 'Brown Paper Bag' [Listen]|url=https://riverbeats.life/yoshi-flower-hits-hard-with-new-single-brown-paper-bag-listen/|last=Gr|first=Danny|last2=eur|date=2018-08-10|website=RIVER BEATS|language=en-US|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Brown Paper Bag (Original Mix) by Yoshi Flower on Beatport|url=https://www.beatport.com/track/brown-paper-bag-original-mix/11026888|website=www.beatport.com|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref> The lead single of which was featured in the [[Netflix]] show ‘[[On My Block (TV series)|On My Block]].’<ref>{{Cite web|title=On My Block season 2 soundtrack: Every song featured on the Netflix show|url=https://www.popbuzz.com/music/features/on-my-block-season-2-soundtrack/|website=PopBuzz|language=en|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref> The project also included a remix from [[Take a Daytrip|Take A Day Trip]], and a reimagined version of the single featuring [[Rico Nasty]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Rico Nasty joins Yoshi Flower on the flammable "Brown Paper Bag 2.0"|url=https://www.thefader.com/2018/08/30/yoshi-flower-brown-paper-bag-remix-rico-nasty|website=The FADER|language=en|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Interscope artist Yoshi Flower discusses new music video, mixtape (Includes interview)|url=http://www.digitaljournal.com/entertainment/music/interscope-artist-yoshi-flower-discusses-new-music-video-mixtape/article/533780|date=2018-10-04|website=www.digitaljournal.com|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref>

On October 12, 2018, he released “American Raver,” the first of a series of 3 mixtapes. [[I-D|I-D magazine]] labeled the project a “pledge of allegiance gone goth.” The project explores drugs, identity, [[escapism]], and [[capitalism]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=yoshi flower's 'american raver' is the pledge of allegiance gone goth|url=https://i-d.vice.com/en_us/article/yw9ekj/yoshi-flower-american-raver|last=Fulton|first=Nick|date=2018-10-12|website=i-D|language=en|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref> In the summer of 2019 he followed up with a 2nd mixtape titled “Peer Pleasure.” Paper Magazine noted his ability to bend genres in his music.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Yoshi Gives in to 'Peer Pleasure'|url=https://www.papermag.com/yoshi-flower-peer-pleasure-2638733401.html|date=2019-06-07|website=PAPER|language=en|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref> The third and final installment, “I Will Not Let My Love Go To Waste” came in December 2019. The project touches on heart-break, addiction, control and [[Near-death experience|near death experience]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=LADYGUNN|url=https://www.ladygunn.com/music/send-yoshi-flower-s-mixtape-i-will-not-let-my-love-go-to-waste-to-every-single-one-of-your-friends/|date=2019-12-06|website=LADYGUNN|language=en-US|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref> In 2020 he began working on his major-label debut album.<ref>{{Cite web|title=working on my debut album it's going to be truly timeless|url=https://twitter.com/y0shiflower/status/1220552397568667648?s=20|last=FLOWER|first=YOSHI|date=2020-01-23|website=@y0shiflower|language=en|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref>
