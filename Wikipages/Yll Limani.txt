'''Yll Limani''' ({{IPA-sq|yɫ liˈmani|lang}}; born 24 September 1994) is a Kosovo-Albanian singer and songwriter.

== Life and career ==

=== 1994–2012: Early life and career beginnings ===

Yll Limani was born on 24 September 1994 into an [[Albanians|Albanian]] family in the city of [[Pristina]], then part of the [[FR Yugoslavia]], present [[Kosovo]].<ref>{{cite web |publisher=TeksteShqip |title=Yll Limani Biografia |url=https://www.teksteshqip.com/ylli-limani |access-date=2 February 2020 |language=sq}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Top Channel]] |title=Yll Limani #Bio |url=http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/artist/yll-limani/ |access-date=10 February 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210213152038/http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/artist/yll-limani/ |archive-date=13 February 2021 |language=sq}}</ref> His father, Mufail Limani, was a popular guitarist of the Albanian band Minatori.<ref>{{cite web |work=Lajmi |title=A e dini se djali i kujt është këngëtari Yll Limani? |url=https://lajmi.net/e-dini-se-djali-kujt-eshte-kengetari-yll-limani-foto/ |access-date=28 February 2021 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last=Bërbatovci |first=Labinot |work=Kosovarja |title=Yll Limani është djali i këtij analisti të njohur 2018}}</ref>

=== 2013–present: Continued success ===

In December 2016, he participated in the pre-selection competition [[Festivali i Këngës 55|Festivali i Këngës]] to [[Albania in the Eurovision Song Contest 2017|represent Albania]] in the [[Eurovision Song Contest 2017]] eventually finishing third with the song "Shiu".<ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[RTSH]] |title=Fest ’55, publikohen emrat e konkurrentëve |url=https://www.rtsh.al/lajme/fest-55-publikohen-emrat-e-konkurrenteve/ |website=rtsh.al |access-date=10 April 2020 |language=sq |date=1 November 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title='Shiu' i Yll Limanit, ndër këngët pretendente në Festivalin e RTSH-së |url=https://telegrafi.com/shiu-yll-limanit-nder-kenget-pretendente-ne-festivalin-e-rtsh-se-video/ |website=telegrafi.com |access-date=10 April 2020 |language=sq |date=22 December 2016}}</ref> In July 2019, he scored his second number-one single with the release of "Nuk po kalojka", a collaboration with Albanian composers [[Aida Baraku]] and Armend Rexhepagiqi alongside Macedonian producer [[Darko Dimitrov]].<ref>{{cite web |last=Velija |first=Kreizia |publisher=[[Top Channel|Top Albania Radio]] |title=Top Awards: Çfarë nuk po i kalon këtë verë Ylli Limanit? |url=https://www.topalbaniaradio.com/v7/top-awards-cfare-nuk-po-i-kalon-kete-vere-ylli-limanit/ |access-date=5 May 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210505161829/https://www.topalbaniaradio.com/v7/top-awards-cfare-nuk-po-i-kalon-kete-vere-ylli-limanit/ |archive-date=5 May 2021 |language=sq |date=20 July 2019 |url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Top Channel]] |title=Yll Limani zë vendin e Elvana Gjatës dhe Capital T |url=http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/yll-limani-ze-vendin-e-elvana-gjates-dhe-capital-t/ |access-date=5 May 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210505162435/http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/yll-limani-ze-vendin-e-elvana-gjates-dhe-capital-t/ |archive-date=5 May 2021 |language=sq |date=21 July 2019 |url-status=live}}</ref> The Sunny Hill Festival in [[Pristina]] approached Limani a month later, and went on to perform at the festival sharing stage with other acclaimed artists, such as [[Miley Cyrus]], [[Calvin Harris]] and [[Dua Lipa]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Ylli Limani do të performojë gjithashtu në festivalin "Sunny Hill" |url=https://telegrafi.com/ylli-limani-te-performoje-gjithashtu-ne-festivalin-sunny-hill/ |website=telegrafi.com |access-date=10 April 2020 |language=sq |date=3 July 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Nis performanca e Yll Limanit në Sunny Hill |url=https://ballkani.info/nis-performanca-e-yll-limanit-ne-sunny-hill-foto/ |website=ballkani.info |access-date=10 April 2020 |language=sq |date=2 August 2019}}</ref>

In February 2021, Limani was featured on Cricket's singles "Ndoshta" and "Pray" with the former peaking at number one and the latter becoming a top 30 single in Albania.<ref>{{cite web |work=[[Koha Ditore|Koha]] |title=Yll Limani e Cricket sjellin 'Ndoshta' |url=https://www.koha.net/showbiz/257228/ylli-limani-e-cricket-sjellin-ndoshta/ |access-date=28 November 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211128190046/https://www.koha.net/showbiz/257228/ylli-limani-e-cricket-sjellin-ndoshta/ |archive-date=28 November 2021 |language=sq |date=4 February 2021 |url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last=Karabeci |first=Marisa |publisher=[[Top Channel|Top Albania Radio]] |title=Më energjik se kurrë! Ylli Limani zbulon sa këngë do të sjellë këtë vit! |url=https://www.topalbaniaradio.com/v7/me-energjik-se-kurre-ylli-limani-zbulon-sa-kenge-do-te-sjelle-kete-vit/ |access-date=28 November 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211128190619/https://www.topalbaniaradio.com/v7/me-energjik-se-kurre-ylli-limani-zbulon-sa-kenge-do-te-sjelle-kete-vit/ |archive-date=28 November 2021 |language=sq |date=13 February 2021 |url-status=live}}</ref> The singer released "A ki me rrejt", his first single as a lead artist in over six months after "Leje", in April 2021 also reaching number two.<ref>{{cite web |work=[[Telegrafi]] |title=Yll Limani publikon klipin e këngës 'A ki me rrejt' |url=https://telegrafi.com/yll-limani-publikon-klipin-e-kenges-ki-rrejt/ |access-date=28 November 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211128191849/https://telegrafi.com/yll-limani-publikon-klipin-e-kenges-ki-rrejt/ |archive-date=28 November 2021 |language=sq |date=16 April 2021 |url-status=live}}</ref> The follow-up collaborations, "E di" and "Marre", with Albanian musician [[Elvana Gjata]] were released in June 2021 and "E di" peaked at number one for three consecutive weeks.<ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Top Channel]] |title=E di (nim) që do të merrte kreun! |url=http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/e-di-nim-qe-do-te-merrte-kreun/ |access-date=29 November 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210811150555/http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/e-di-nim-qe-do-te-merrte-kreun/ |archive-date=11 August 2021 |language=sq |date=1 August 2021 |url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Top Channel]] |title=Kreu nuk ndryshon por kemi dy surpriza në Top 5! |url=http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/kreu-nuk-ndryshon-por-kemi-dy-surpriza-ne-top-5/ |access-date=29 November 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211026153534/http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/kreu-nuk-ndryshon-por-kemi-dy-surpriza-ne-top-5/ |archive-date=26 October 2021 |language=sq |date=16 August 2021 |url-status=live}}</ref> In October 2021, Limani announced that he had started working on his debut extended play.<ref>{{cite web |work=[[Telegrafi]] |title=Yll Limani paralajmëron albumin e ri |url=https://telegrafi.com/yll-limani-paralajmeron-albumin-e-ri/ |access-date=29 November 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211129134003/https://telegrafi.com/yll-limani-paralajmeron-albumin-e-ri/ |archive-date=29 November 2021 |language=sq |date=11 October 2021 |url-status=live}}</ref> A month later, he was invited to join Swiss-Albanian rapper [[Loredana Zefi|Loredana]]'s [[Red Bull|Red Bull Symphonic]] concert in [[Lucerne]], Switzerland.<ref>{{cite web |last=Seebacher |first=Martin |publisher=[[Red Bull]] |title=8 Momente vom Red Bull Symphonic mit Loredana, die in Erinnerung bleiben Beim Red Bull Symphonic in Luzern stand Deutschrap-Star Loredana mit einem Symphonieorchester auf der Bühne. Wir haben die Highlights der Show für dich zusammengefasst. |url=https://www.redbull.com/de-de/red-bull-symphonic-highlights |access-date=28 November 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211128183124/https://www.redbull.com/de-de/red-bull-symphonic-highlights |archive-date=28 November 2021 |language=de |date=26 November 2021 |url-status=live}}</ref> Throughout the year, the singer contributed to Albanian singer [[Arilena Ara]]'s debut studio album, ''Pop Art The Album''.<ref name="Aligator"/><ref name="Ke me mungu"/><ref name="Murderer"/> His debut extended play, ''Lshoja Zanin'', is scheduled to be released on 5 December 2021.<ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[RTV21|Radio Televizioni 21]] (RTV21) |title='Lshoja Zanin' vjen më 5 dhjetor |url=https://rtv21.tv/lshoja-zanin-vjen-me-5-dhjetor/ |access-date=1 December 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211201175628/https://rtv21.tv/lshoja-zanin-vjen-me-5-dhjetor/ |archive-date=1 December 2021 |language=sq |date=1 December 2021 |url-status=live}}</ref>
