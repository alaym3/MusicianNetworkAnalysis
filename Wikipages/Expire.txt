'''Expire''' was an American [[hardcore punk]] band from [[Milwaukee, Wisconsin]], United States, active from 2009 to 2017. They were signed to [[Bridge 9 Records]].<ref>{{cite web|last1=Kraus|first1=Brian|title=Expire sign to Bridge Nine|url=http://www.altpress.com/news/entry/expire_sign_to_bridge_nine|publisher=Alternative Press|accessdate=30 December 2014}}</ref>

==History==
Expire was formed in 2009. They released three full-length albums, three EPs and one split. They released their first full length, titled ''Pendulum Swings'' in 2012.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Yancey|first1=Bryne|title=Check out the art for Expire's new album "Pendulum Swings"|url=http://www.altpress.com/news/entry/check_out_the_art_for_expires_new_album_pendulum_swings|publisher=Alternative Press|accessdate=30 December 2014}}</ref> They released their second full length, titled ''Pretty Low'' in 2014.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Kraus|first1=Brian|title=Expire - 'Pretty Low' (Album Premiere With Commentary!)|url=http://www.altpress.com/news/entry/expire_pretty_low_album_premiere_with_commentary|publisher=Alternative Press|accessdate=30 December 2014}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.revolvermag.com/news/expire-premiere-new-song-pretty-low.html |title=Archived copy |access-date=2014-12-30 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141230080558/http://www.revolvermag.com/news/expire-premiere-new-song-pretty-low.html |archive-date=2014-12-30 |url-status=dead}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://echoesanddust.com/2014/06/expire-pretty-low/|title=Expire – Pretty Low &#124; Echoes And Dust|date=18 June 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140618021639/http://echoesanddust.com/2014/06/expire-pretty-low/|accessdate=25 September 2020|archive-date=2014-06-18}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://lambgoat.com/albums/3061/Expire-Pretty-Low|title=EXPIRE 'Pretty Low' Album Review|website=Lambgoat.com|accessdate=2020-04-03}}</ref> ''Pretty Low'' peaked at No. 175 on the [[Billboard 200]] chart.<ref>{{cite web|title=Expire - Chart history|url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/1496548/expire/chart?f=305|website=Billboard.com|accessdate=2015-06-09}}</ref> In August 2016, the band announced plans to break-up following the release of a new album, titled ''With Regret'', and a few international tours. On March 11, 2017, they played their final show to a sold-out crowd in their hometown of Milwaukee.
