'''Against the Current''' (often abbreviated as '''ATC''') is an American [[pop rock]] band based in [[Poughkeepsie]], New York , that formed in 2011. The band currently consists of lead vocalist [[Chrissy Costanza]], guitarist Dan Gow, and drummer Will Ferri. The group gained a sizable online following after posting covers of various popular songs on YouTube.

The band's first [[Extended play|EP]], ''Infinity'', was released in May 2014 under their own label. The band followed ''Infinity'' with their next EP, ''[[Gravity (Against the Current album)|Gravity]]'', which was released on February 17, 2015. Shortly afterward, they signed to the record label [[Fueled by Ramen]]. Their debut full-length album, ''[[In Our Bones]]'' was released on May 20, 2016. Their second full-length album, ''[[Past Lives (Against the Current album)|Past Lives]]'' was released on September 28, 2018. Their EP, ''Fever'', was released on July 23, 2021.

== History ==

=== Background and origins ===
Against The Current was formed in early 2011 by Dan Gow, Will Ferri, and Jeremy Rompala and was later joined by [[Chrissy Costanza]] in the summer of 2011 after being introduced to Gow, Ferri, and Rompala through a mutual friend. Joe Simmons was invited to fill in as their bass player but later joined the band after leaving his former band.<ref name="Sweetyhigh">{{cite web | url=https://www.sweetyhigh.com/read/chrissy-costanza | title=CHRISSY COSTANZA TALKS ABOUT AGAINST THE CURRENT! | work=Sweetyhigh | date=2013-06-11 | access-date=2015-08-01 | author=Veronica | url-status=live | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160818163250/https://www.sweetyhigh.com/read/chrissy-costanza | archive-date=2016-08-18 }}</ref>

Will Ferri is the younger brother of [[We Are The In Crowd]] bass player and artist manager Mike Ferri, as the bands manager Mike Ferri utilized his relationships with well known YouTube cover artists such as [[Kurt Hugo Schneider]] and [[Alex Goot]] to help the band achieve a considerable amount of fame via various [[Cover version|covers]] of popular songs posted as collaborations on more established channels.

=== Music history ===
On May 27, 2014, Against The Current released their debut EP, ''Infinity'', independently through online distribution. The ''Infinity'' EP charted at No. 28 on the [[Top Heatseekers|Heatseekers Albums]].<ref name="Billboard Biz Only">{{cite web|title=Infinity by Against The Current|work=Billboard|url=http://www.billboard.com/biz/search/charts?f%5b0%5d=ss_chart_search_title%3A%22Infinity%20%28EP%29%22&f%5b1%5d=itm_field_chart_id%3A324&refine=1|access-date=May 7, 2016|date=June 14, 2014}}</ref> They began touring in support of the release of ''Infinity'' throughout most of 2014. After several tours, they entered the studio to record their second EP, ''Gravity,'' which was released on February 17, 2015. The EP featured six songs, and included a guest appearance from [[Takahiro Moriuchi|Taka]] of [[One OK Rock|ONE OK ROCK]] on the song '"Dreaming Alone." A few weeks after the release of ''[[Gravity (Against The Current album)|''Gravity'']]'',<ref name="Rock Sound Review">{{cite web|first=Andy|last=Ritchie|title=Against The Current – Gravity|work=Rock Sound|date=March 13, 2015|url=http://www.rocksound.tv/reviews/read/against-the-current-gravity|access-date=May 7, 2016|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160406062223/http://www.rocksound.tv/reviews/read/against-the-current-gravity|archive-date=April 6, 2016}}</ref> Against the Current announced that they had been signed by the label [[Fueled By Ramen]] on March 4, 2015.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.rocksound.tv/news/read/against-the-current-just-signed-to-fueled-by-ramen|title=AGAINST THE CURRENT JUST SIGNED TO FUELED BY RAMEN|work=Rocksound|date=2015-03-04|access-date=2015-08-01|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150710084520/http://www.rocksound.tv/news/read/against-the-current-just-signed-to-fueled-by-ramen|archive-date=2015-07-10}}</ref> The ''Gravity'' EP charted on four ''Billboard'' magazine charts, while it peaked on the [[Alternative Albums]], [[Independent Albums]], [[Top Rock Albums|Rock Albums]], and Heatseekers Albums charts, peaking at 23, 27, 36 and 4, respectively.<ref name="AllMusic Awards">{{cite web|title=Against The Current : Awards|publisher=AllMusic|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/against-the-current-mn0003393980/awards|access-date=May 7, 2016}}</ref>

On May 22, 2015, the band announced their first headlining tour in support of the ''Gravity'' EP, called the ''Gravity World Tour''. The tour spanned 4 months (August 2015 to November 2015), and included stops in several continents, including North America, Asia, and Europe.<ref>{{cite web|title=AGAINST THE CURRENT: GRAVITY WORLD TOUR|publisher=Fueled By Ramen|url=http://www.fueledbyramen.com/news/against-current-gravity-world-tour-1198946|date=May 26, 2015|access-date=September 28, 2017}}</ref>

On August 1, 2015, Against The Current announced that they had finished recording their first full-length album. On February 6, 2016, Against The Current announced their upcoming album's name, ''[[In Our Bones]]'', as well as the release date, May 20, 2016.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Against The Current Announce Debut Album, In Our Bones – Kerrang!|url=http://www.kerrang.com/40304/against-the-current-announce-debut-album-in-our-bones/|website=Kerrang!|access-date=2016-02-13|language=en-US|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160214032853/http://www.kerrang.com/40304/against-the-current-announce-debut-album-in-our-bones/|archive-date=2016-02-14}}</ref> Their first [[studio album]], ''In Our Bones'', has been called "pop-rock perfection" by ''[[Alternative Press (music magazine)|Alternative Press]]''.<ref name="AP Review">{{cite magazine|first=Rachel|last=Campbell|title=Against the Current: ''In Our Bones'' – album review|magazine=Alternative Press|volume=June 2016|issue=335|page=99|issn=1065-1667}}<!--|access-date=May 7, 2016-->{{Verify source|date=July 2016}}</ref>

On September 14, 2017, Against The Current collaborated with [[Riot Games]] to create the single "[[Legends Never Die (League of Legends song)|Legends Never Die]]" for the [[2017 League of Legends World Championship|2017 ''League of Legends'' World Championship]], published on Riot Games' YouTube channel.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.gamereactor.eu/esports/591533/The+2017+League+Worlds+official+song+is+here/ |title=The 2017 League Worlds official song is here From rock band Against the Current. |last=James |first=Ford |date=15 September 2017 |website=[[Gamereactor]] |archive-url=https://archive.today/20170915103255/https://www.gamereactor.eu/esports/591533/The+2017+League+Worlds+official+song+is+here/ |archive-date=2017-09-15 |url-status=live |access-date=2017-09-15 }}</ref> On November 4, 2017, Against The Current performed live at the 2017 ''League of Legends'' World Championship opening and closing ceremonies.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.riftherald.com/lol-worlds/2017/11/4/16605868/league-of-legends-worlds-2017-opening-ceremony|title=The opening ceremony for the 2017 World Championship finals had a live performance and a dragon|work=The Rift Herald|access-date=2017-11-05}}</ref>

On May 11, 2018, Against The Current released two singles, "Strangers Again" and "Almost Forgot", from the album ''[[Past Lives (Against the Current album)|Past Lives]]'', released on September 28, 2018. On August 17, 2018, the band released the single "Personal". The single "Voices" was released on September 14, 2018.

On October 28, 2020, Against The Current released their new song "That Won't Save Us," followed by a second single, "Weapon," on March 10, 2021. These were followed up by a third song "again & again" ft. Guardin on June 23, 2021. The bands' first EP since 2015's "Gravity" called "fever" was released on July 23, 2021.
