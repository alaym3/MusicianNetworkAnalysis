Latham was born and brought up in [[Durban]], [[South Africa]] but moved to London aged 18 to pursue a career in music. She wrote her music while working at a Rough Trade music shop in Brick Lane, London. During the [[COVID-19 pandemic]] of 2020, she signed a record deal with Polydor Records over Zoom.

Her first EP, ''Medicine'', was released on 11 November 2020.
