'''Chen Jia-wei '''({{zh|c=陳家偉}}; born 15 January 1985), better known by his stage name '''HUSH''', is a Taiwanese singer-songwriter and lyricist.

He is formerly the vocalist of the Taiwanese band hush! He has worked with artists like [[A-Lin]], Ding Dang, [[Lala Hsu]], and [[Stephanie Sun]].

== Career ==

While at [[National Pingtung Senior High School]], he was the lead singer of the pop music club. His interactions with the guitar club, which included future members of the band Mary See The Future ([[:zh:先知瑪莉|先知瑪莉]]), influenced him to learn guitar.<ref name=interview>{{cite web|title=天文系的憂鬱少年－hush！ – 音樂大人物 – KKBOX|url=https://www.kkbox.com/tw/tc/column/interviews-0-255-1.html|website=KKBOX|accessdate=December 4, 2017|language=zh-TW}}</ref>

HUSH majored in philosophy at [[Fu Jen Catholic University]].

In 2009, he signed with [[HIM International Music]] as a singer-songwriter, but he wasn't able to sell any songs. He also competed in the 3rd season of [[One Million Star]], which was won by Lala Hsu, but he was eliminated before the top 100.<ref name=interview />

In 2010, while working part-time at the coffee shop Kafka by the Sea in [[Gongguan, Taipei|Gongguan]],<ref>{{cite web|last1=公關達人|first1=咖啡館|title=海邊的卡夫卡--音樂藝文空間 @ JuliePR 分享工作與玩樂 :: 痞客邦 PIXNET ::|url=http://juliepr.pixnet.net/blog/post/27620380-%E6%B5%B7%E9%82%8A%E7%9A%84%E5%8D%A1%E5%A4%AB%E5%8D%A1--%E9%9F%B3%E6%A8%82%E8%97%9D%E6%96%87%E7%A9%BA%E9%96%93|website=JuliePR 分享工作與玩樂|accessdate=December 4, 2017|language=zh-TW}}</ref> he met drummer Bearpa and bassist Kabei. Together, they formed the band hush!<ref>{{cite web|title=HUSH|url=https://thewall.tw/artists/34|website=THE WALL MUSIC|accessdate=December 4, 2017|language=zh-TW}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Henley|first1=Joe|title=Live Wire: We’ve got issues|url=http://www.taipeitimes.com/News/feat/archives/2014/01/03/2003580388|accessdate=January 23, 2018|work=Taipei Times|date=January 3, 2014}}</ref> Starting out acoustic, they transitioned into electric after playing many live shows. Usually, Hush records the demo and uploads to StreetVoice, a music-publishing platform for independent songwriters. His songs are inspired by his interests in astronomy, psychology, and tarot.<ref name=interview />

Within a year, they released an EP, ''Astronomical Cachet'' 『天文特徵』, and an album, ''X''. Popular in the indie scene, the band performed at various events and venues in [[Xian|Xi'an]], [[Shenzhen]], [[Taipei]], Hong Kong, and [[Kaohsiung]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.ntdtv.com/xtr/b5/2015/04/05/a1189007.html|title=「天文系創作詩人」「第一人稱」首場大型個唱 33首HUSH詩篇引爆孤獨宇宙！|date=April 4, 2015|publisher=新唐人|language=zh-hant|access-date=7 April 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150414002145/http://www.ntdtv.com/xtr/b5/2015/04/05/a1189007.html|archive-date=April 14, 2015|url-status=dead}}</ref>

After the band disbanded in 2014,<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://playmusic.tw/column_info.php?id=2939&type=news|title=鼓手熊爸閃離 Hush！樂團　樂迷震驚|date=November 16, 2014|publisher=PlayMusic|language=zh-hant|access-date=7 April 2015}}</ref> HUSH started his solo career. His first single was produced by [[Mayday (Taiwanese band)|Mayday]] bassist Masa.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.ettoday.net/news/20150317/479934.htm|title=HUSH回收《克卜勒》自己唱　EP演唱會憑門票免費送|last=蕭采薇|date=March 17, 2015|publisher=東森新聞|language=zh-hant|access-date=7 April 2015}}</ref>

== Personal life ==

He has interests in [[astronomy]] and [[tarot]]. He is a supporter of [[same-sex marriage in Taiwan]],<ref>{{cite news|title=演唱出櫃歌曲 HUSH：沒有人的愛是高尚或卑微的 {{!}} 音樂 {{!}} 三立新聞網 SETN.COM|url=http://www.setn.com/News.aspx?NewsID=212230|accessdate=December 4, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=愛是同一個答案！專訪 HUSH：現在是 2015，我是 GAY 有什麼問題嗎？｜女人迷 Womany|url=http://womany.net/read/article/9415|website=女人迷 womany.net|accessdate=December 4, 2017|language=zh-tw|date=December 17, 2015}}</ref> expressing his views in his song ''Same'' (同一個答案).<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://news.sina.com.tw/article/20150404/14122078.html|title=台灣歌手被曝同性戀 手機軟體上找男伴|date=April 4, 2015|publisher=北京新浪網|language=zh-hant|access-date=5 April 2015}}</ref>
He came out during his concert on April 4, 2015.<ref>{{cite news|title=【我是同性戀】HUSH：在演唱會出櫃是我家的事 {{!}} 蘋果日報|url=https://tw.appledaily.com/new/realtime/20161210/1008929/|accessdate=December 4, 2017|work=Apple Daily |language=zh-TW}}</ref> He's an avid participant of [[Taiwan Pride]].<ref>{{cite news|title=圖／文青男神Hush現身彩虹遊行　挺同志爭婚姻平權 {{!}} ETtoday新聞雲|url=https://www.ettoday.net/news/20161029/801815.htm|accessdate=December 4, 2017|work=ETtoday新聞雲|language=zh-TW}}</ref>

== Controversy ==
In 2015, the music video for his song ''King of Doubt (''我想知道你的一切) was banned from television broadcast for nudity.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://womany.net/read/article/9308|title=寫在 HUSH MV 禁播後：「難道不為藝術脫衣服，我就髒掉了嗎？」|work=女人迷 womany.net|access-date=5 March 2017|language=zh-tw}}</ref> Regarding the ban, HUSH felt that "it's good to open up a discussion about censorship and nudity," adding that "people can find it on the internet anyway." A [[Virtual Reality|VR]] version of the music video was also made.<ref>{{cite news|title=影／不畏禁播壓力 HUSH霸氣「禁一支拍一支！」│娛樂星聞│三立新聞網 SETN.COM|url=http://www.setn.com/E/News.aspx?NewsID=115463&From=Search&Key=hush}}</ref>
