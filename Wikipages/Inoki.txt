'''Inoki''', also known as '''Inoki Ness''', pseudonym of '''Fabiano Ballarin''' ([[Rome]], October 2, 1979), is an [[Italy|Italian]] [[rapper]] and [[record producer]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=INOKI NESS|url=https://www.rappirata.it/inoki-ness/|access-date=2021-02-17|website=Rap Pirata|language=it-IT}}</ref>

It owes its nickname to its old Enok tag, which he later associates with the [[Old Testament]] character [[Enoch (ancestor of Noah)|Enoch]]. "Ness", on the other hand, is a [[Bolognese dialect|Bolognese]] slang expression to indicate who "knows". Furthermore, as he himself stated, the stage name also derives from [[Antonio Inoki]], a well-known Japanese wrestler.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Hano|date=2019-03-21|title=La fierezza di Inoki: "Sono Comunista e Antirazzista"|url=https://www.hano.it/la-fierezza-di-inoki-sono-comunista-e-antirazzista/|access-date=2021-02-17|website=Hano Mnemonic|language=it-IT}}</ref>
