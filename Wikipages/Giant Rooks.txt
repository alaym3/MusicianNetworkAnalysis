'''Giant Rooks''' is an [[Independent music|indie]] band from [[Hamm]], which was founded in 2014.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.wa.de/hamm/band-giant-rooks-hamm-sorgt-ueberregional-aufmerksamkeit-song-smaland-video-5324890.html|title=Hammer Band Giant Rooks: Überall auf der Überholspur|date=2015-08-06|work=wa.de|access-date=2020-08-28}}</ref> In 2019 they won the [[:de:1 Live Krone|1Live Krone Award]]<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www1.wdr.de/radio/1live/events/1live-krone/foerderpreis-giant-rooks-100.html|title=DER 1LIVE KRONE FÖRDERPREIS 2019 GEHT AN GIANT ROOKS|date=2019-12-05|work=wa.de|access-date=2020-08-28|language=de}}</ref> and the [[:de:Preis für Popkultur|Preis für Popkultur]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.musikexpress.de/preis-fuer-popkultur-2019-das-sind-die-gewinner-1350823|title=Hammer Band Giant Rooks: Überall auf der Überholspur|date=2019-10-03|work=wa.de|access-date=2020-08-28|language=de}}</ref> Their debut album ''ROOKERY'' was released on the 28th of August 2020.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.wonderlandmagazine.com/2020/08/28/new-noise-giant-rooks/|title=NEW NOISE: GIANT ROOKS|date=2019-08-28|work=Wonderland Magazine|access-date=2020-08-28|language=en}}</ref>

==History==

The band was founded in 2014 by the cousins Frederik Rabe and Finn Schwieters and the piano player Jonathan Wischniowski and was soon completed by the addition of bass player Luca Göttner and drummer Finn Thomas.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://herzmukke.de/nachgefragt/interview-giant-rooks-band-wir-sind-perfektionisten/|title=Giant Rooks – Wir sind Perfektionisten!|date=2016-01-28|work=herzmukke|access-date=2020-08-28|language=de}}</ref> In 2015 they released their first self-produced [[Extended play|EP]] 'The Times Are Bursting The Lines'. This first release was followed by another EP ''New Estate'' in 2017 which gained them first critical success. The release was supported by hundreds of shows all over Europe eventually landing them a record deal with Irrsinn Tonträger ([[Universal Music Group|Universal Music]]).<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.totalntertainment.com/music/giant-rooks-release-new-single-misinterpretations|title=GIANT ROOKS RELEASE NEW SINGLE MISINTERPRETATIONS|date=2020-07-25|work=TotalNtertainment|access-date=2020-08-28|language=en}}</ref> In 2019 the EP ''Wild Stare'' was released with its title track being a [[top 20]] radio hit in Italy<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.metronews.it/metro-video/19/05/30/i-giant-rooks-dal-vivo-italia.html|title=I Giant Rooks dal vivo in Italia|date=2020-05-30|work=Metro News|access-date=2020-08-28|language=it}}</ref> and the whole EP racking up more than 50,000,000 world-wide streams on [[Spotify]] soon after its release.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.houseofsolo.co.uk/giant-rooks-return-with-candid-video-for-brand-new-single-watershed|title=GIANT ROOKS RETURN WITH CANDID VIDEO FOR BRAND NEW SINGLE 'WATERSHED'|date=2020-01-31|work=House of Solo Magazine|access-date=2020-08-28}}</ref> In 2020 they announced the release of their debut album ''ROOKERY''  for the 28th of August, 2020. Radio X DJ John Kennedy played the band's single 'All We Are' on his X-Posure radio show,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.xposuretracklists.net/2020/08/show-date-28-aug-2020.html|title = Show Date: 28 Aug 2020}}</ref> on the album's release.
