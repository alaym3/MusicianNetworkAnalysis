'''Kim Yong-sun''' ({{Korean|hangul=김용선}}, born February 21, 1991),<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tenasia.hankyung.com/archives/283703|title=My Name, 마마무 (1)|publisher=Ten Asia|language=ko|date=2014-07-15}}</ref> better known by the stage name '''Solar''' ({{Korean|hangul=솔라}}), is a South Korean singer and songwriter signed under RBW. She is the leader of girl group [[Mamamoo]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.wowtv.co.kr/newscenter/news/view.asp?bcode=T30001000&artid=A201406270517|title=마마무 그룹명 무슨뜻? 아이유 존경하는 '4인조 걸그룹'|publisher=Wow TV|language=ko}}</ref> She made her solo debut with the single "[[Spit it Out (Solar song)|Spit it Out]]" on April 23, 2020.

==Biography==
Kim Yong-sun was born in [[Gangseo-gu, Seoul|Gangseo-gu]], [[Seoul]], South Korea,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://people.search.naver.com/search.naver?where=nexearch&query=%EC%86%94%EB%9D%BC&sm=tab_etc&ie=utf8&key=PeopleService&os=1727044|title=솔라 프로필|publisher=Naver|language=ko}}</ref> {{citation needed span|text=where she lived with her parents and elder sister, Yong-hee|date=July 2021}}. She graduated from [[Hanyang Women's University]] with a major in tourism.{{citation needed|date=July 2021}}

==Career==
===2014-present: Debut with Mamamoo===
{{Main|Mamamoo}}
Solar debuted as a member and vocalist of [[Mamamoo]] on June 18, 2014, along with [[Moonbyul]], [[Wheein]], and [[Hwasa]]. On April 24, 2018, she released her first solo EP, ''Solar's Emotion'', which included previously released covers of several Korean pop songs and her solo version of Mamamoo's "[[Yellow Flower|Star Wind Flower Sun]]," which she wrote herself.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Yoon|first=Min-sik|date=April 18, 2018|title=Mamamoo's Solar confirms new releases for solo project|url=http://www.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20180418000618|access-date=June 25, 2020|website=The Korea Herald}}</ref>
[[File:180519 솔라 04.jpg|left|thumb|Solar in 2018]]

===2019-present: Solo activities===
She made her official solo debut with the single, "[[Spit It Out (Solar song)|Spit It Out]]," which was released with a music video on April 23, 2020. The same day, Solar had her debut stage on Mnet's ''[[M Countdown]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Chakraborty|first=Riddhi|date=April 23, 2020|title=MAMAMOO's Solar Makes Powerful Solo Debut With 'Spit It Out'|url=https://rollingstoneindia.com/mamamoo-solar-makes-powerful-solo-debut-with-spit-it-out/|access-date=June 25, 2020|website=Rolling Stone India}}</ref> On April 28, she won her first-ever music show award as a solo artist on SBS MTV's ''[[The Show (South Korean TV series)|The Show]]''.<ref>{{cite web|title=마마무 솔라 '더쇼' 1위→빌보드 월드 디지털송 차트 진입 '겹경사'|trans-title=Mamamoo Solar 'The Show' 1st place → Billboard World Digital Song chart entry 'double slope'|url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=609&aid=0000271945|website=Naver|publisher=Newsen|language=ko|author=Hwang Hye-jin|date=April 29, 2020|access-date=July 30, 2021}}</ref>
