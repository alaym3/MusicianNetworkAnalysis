He is best known for releasing material through [[Diplo]], [[Skrillex]] and [[Steve Aoki]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/valentino-khan-mn0002921833/biography|title=Valentino Khan == Career ==
=== 2012: Beginnings ===
He started his career as a hip-hop producer making music for artists like [[T.I.]], [[2 Chainz]] and [[B.o.B]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.phoenixnewtimes.com/music/valentino-khan-explains-why-music-snobs-are-wrong-7515684|title=Valentino Khan Explains Why Music Snobs Are Wrong|last=Savage|first=Amanda|date=2015-07-25|work=Phoenix New Times|access-date=2017-06-26}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://thebanginbeats.com/2017/06/valentino-khan-interview/|title=Valentino Khan Talks Beginnings, Possibility Of An India Tour & Much More! [Interview] {{!}} The Bangin Beats|date=2017-06-26|work=The Bangin Beats|access-date=2017-06-26|language=en-US}}</ref>

In 2012, some of his remixes were released officially. Most notably, his remix of Gent & Jawns' song "Holler" which was released through Diplo's record label [[Mad Decent]]. He collaborated with Will Bailey for a song, "Rukus" and produced [[B.o.B]].'s single "Play For Keeps" from the album ''[[Strange Clouds (album)|Strange Clouds]]''.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/the-juice/489463/bob-strange-clouds-track-by-track-review|title=B.o.B, 'Strange Clouds': Track-By-Track Review|work=Billboard|access-date=2017-06-26}}</ref> He also co-produced [[Major Lazer]]'s 2013 single "[[Bubble Butt]]".<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/5811922/valentino-khan/biography|title=Valentino Khan – Biography|website=www.billboard.com|language=en|access-date=2017-06-26}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://uk.complex.com/music/2013/05/major-lazer-ft-bruno-mars-2-chainz-tyga-mystic-bubble-butt|title=Major Lazer ft. Bruno Mars, 2 Chainz, Tyga & Mystic – "Bubble Butt"|website=Complex UK|language=en|access-date=2017-06-26}}</ref> Musically, he is [[Trap music (EDM)|trap]]-oriented.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.dance-charts.de/201603317021/valentino-khan-skrillex-slam-dunk-feat-kstylis|title=Valentino Khan & Skrillex – Slam Dunk (feat. Kstylis)|last=Butz|first=Patrick|work=Dance-Charts.de|access-date=2017-06-26|language=de-DE}}</ref>
