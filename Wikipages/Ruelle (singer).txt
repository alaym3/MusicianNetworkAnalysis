'''Maggie Eckford''' or '''Margaret Eckford''' (born November 21, 1985), also known by her stage name '''Ruelle''', is an American [[electropop]] singer-songwriter.<ref name="CultureCollide">{{cite web |url=http://www.culturecollide.com/reviews/detail/ruelle_reps_electro_pop_in_nashville |title=Ruelle Reps Electro-Pop in Nashville |last=Roth |first=Rachael |publisher=Culture Collide |date=March 12, 2015 |accessdate=April 9, 2017}}</ref> Her music has been used on television series such as the opening [[theme song]]s of ''[[Dynasties (2018 TV series)|Dynasties]]'' ("Game of Survival"), ''[[Shadowhunters]]'' ("This Is the Hunt"),<ref name="BeyondTheStage">{{cite web |url=http://www.beyondthestagemagazine.com/new-music-monday-ruelle/ |title=New Music Monday: Ruelle |last=Jones |first=Hannah |publisher=Beyond the Stage |date=June 20, 2016 |accessdate=April 9, 2017}}</ref><ref name="Bookstacked">{{cite web |url=http://bookstacked.com/features/ruelle-songs-shadowhunters/ |title=7 Ruelle Songs That Made Shadowhunters Too Emotional to Handle |last=Zoelling |first=Megan |publisher=Bookstacked |date=September 30, 2016 |accessdate=April 9, 2017}}</ref><ref name="Freeform">{{cite web |url=http://freeform.go.com/shows/shadowhunters/news/ruelle-the-woman-behind-the-music-from-that-clace-kiss |title=Ruelle: The Woman Behind the Music From THAT Clace Kiss |publisher=[[Freeform (TV channel)|Freeform]] |accessdate=April 9, 2017}}</ref> and ''[[The Shannara Chronicles]]'' ("Until We Go Down").<ref name="GeeksOfDoom">{{cite web |url=http://www.geeksofdoom.com/2016/01/05/shannara-chronicles-meet-characters-opening-title |title='The Shannara Chronicles': Meet the Characters; == Background ==

Born the middle child of three sisters, Eckford grew up in [[Hattiesburg, Mississippi]] and attended a private Christian high school. She attended [[music school]] in [[Sydney]], Australia. Ruelle is now based in [[Nashville]], [[Tennessee]].<ref name="CultureCollide"/> Her mother's family owns a legacy music store in Wales, where her interest for music began.

Ruelle's first two albums, ''For What It's Worth'' (2010) and ''Show and Tell'' (2012), released under her birth name, Maggie Eckford, were [[indie pop]].<ref name="CultureCollide"/><ref name="BeyondTheStage"/> She then adopted the stage name, the French word {{lang|fr|Ruelle}}, due to its French roots and its multiple meanings, and moved towards electronic pop with [[extended play|EPs]], ''Up in Flames'' (2015), ''Madness'' (2016), and ''Rival'' (2017).<ref name="CultureCollide"/><ref name="BeyondTheStage"/>

Ruelle's first television placement was a song in a Japanese [[iPad]] commercial. Alongside the theme songs for ''[[Shadowhunters]]'' and ''[[The Shannara Chronicles]]'', her songs have also been included in [[prime time]] television series, including ''[[Arrow (TV series)|Arrow]]'', ''[[Batwoman (TV series)|Batwoman]]'', ''[[Inhumans (TV series)|Inhumans]]'', ''[[Dancing with the Stars (U.S. TV series)|Dancing with the Stars]]'', ''[[The Challenge XXX: Dirty 30]]'', ''[[Cloak & Dagger (TV series)|Cloak & Dagger]]'', ''[[Eyewitness (U.S. TV series)|Eyewitness]]'', ''[[Famous in Love]]'', ''[[Grey's Anatomy]]'', ''[[Guilt (American TV series)|Guilt]]'', ''[[How to Get Away with Murder]]'', ''[[The Leftovers (TV series)|The Leftovers]]'', ''[[Midnight, Texas]]'', ''[[The Originals (TV series)|The Originals]]'', ''[[Pretty Little Liars]]'', ''[[Quantico (TV series)|Quantico]]'', ''[[Reckless (TV series)|Reckless]]'', ''[[Reign (TV series)|Reign]]'', ''[[Revenge (TV series)|Revenge]]'', [[Riverdale (2017 TV series)|''Riverdale'']], ''[[Scream (TV series)|Scream]]'', ''[[Sleepy Hollow (TV series)|Sleepy Hollow]]'', ''[[So You Think You Can Dance]]'', ''[[Teen Wolf (2011 TV series)|Teen Wolf]]'', ''[[Titans (2018 TV series)|Titans]]'', ''[[The Vampire Diaries]]'', ''[[The Walking Dead (TV series)|The Walking Dead]]'', ''[[Wynonna Earp (TV series)|Wynonna Earp]]'', ''[[Doom Patrol (TV series)]] '' and ''[[The Village (2019 TV series)|The Village]]''.<ref name="CultureCollide"/><ref name="BeyondTheStage"/><ref name="RazorAndTie">{{cite web |url=http://razorandtiemusicpublishing.com/writers/maggie-eckford/ |title=Maggie Eckford / Ruelle |publisher=Razor and Tie Music Publishing |date=2014 |accessdate=April 9, 2017}}</ref>

She was included in both the trailer and the soundtrack for ''[[The Loft (film)|The Loft]]'' as well as trailers for ''[[Bad Moms]]'',<ref name="RuelleMusic">{{cite web |url=http://www.ruellemusic.com/about/ |title=About Ruelle Music |publisher=Ruelle Music |accessdate=April 9, 2017}}</ref> ''[[Before I Fall (film)|Before I Fall]]'', ''[[Free State of Jones (film)|Free State of Jones]]'',<ref name="RuelleMusic"/> and ''[[Keeping up with the Joneses (film)|Keeping up with the Joneses]]''.<ref name="RuelleMusic"/> Her song, "Game of Survival", was included in the ''[[13 Reasons Why]]'' trailer, the ''[[Gears 5]]'' trailer, and the trailer for David Attenborough's BBC Earth series ''[[Dynasties (2018 TV series)|Dynasties]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bbcearth.com/dynasties|title=The official Dynasties trailer|website=BBC Earth|language=en-gb|access-date=October 31, 2018}}</ref> Her songs, "Madness" and "Rivals", were included in the TV show, ''[[Siren (TV series)|Siren]]'', and in the trailer for the ''[[The Looming Tower (miniseries)|The Looming Tower]]'' miniseries respectively. In July 2018, the released trailer for the ''[[Titans (2018 TV series)|Titans]]'' series by [[DC Universe (streaming service)|DC Universe]] includes "Madness", "Monsters" and "Revolution". She also released the song, "Take it All" used in a trailer for the British soap, ''[[Emmerdale]]''. The song "Slip Away" was included in a season 2 episode of ''[[Midnight, Texas#Season 2 (2018)|Midnight, Texas]]". In January 2019, "Wake Up World", a collaborative track with Unsecret, was used for the first teaser of the STARZ exclusive limited series, ''[[The Spanish Princess]]''. "I AM", a collaboration with Club Yoko, was in the [[Samsung Galaxy Note 10]] commercials.<ref>[https://twitter.com/SamsungMobile/status/1159826737770856448 Samsung Mobile's commercial featuring "I Am" by Club Yoko]</ref>

She has collaborated with electronic music producer [[Ki Theory]], featuring in his song, "Bringing Me Down". She also provided vocals on [[NF (rapper)|NF]]'s 2017 song, "10 Feet Down", which was part of ''[[Perception (NF album)|Perception]]''.

Her album, ''Earth Glow'', was released on August 7, 2019, with five songs and then another EP soon after with another five songs, choosing to split her album in two in order to draw more attention to all songs. A fellow artist and close friend wrote "Like You Mean It" with Ruelle, alongside mutual friend Jeremy Lutito who also co-produced the EP. The second EP, ''Exodus'', was released on the November 22, 2019.

Her single, "Good Day for Dreaming", was featured in [[Apple Inc|Apple]]'s [[iPhone 12]] lineup launch event in October 2020.

Her song "Madness" was included in the soundtrack of the limited Netflix series, ''[[Behind Her Eyes (TV series)|Behind Her Eyes]]'' in 2021.
