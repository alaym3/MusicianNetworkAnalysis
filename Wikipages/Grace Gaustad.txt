'''Grace Gaustad''' is a [[Los Angeles]]-based singer and songwriter. Gaustad rose to prominence in 2016 when she launched a YouTube channel and her performance of [[Hozier (musician)|Hozier]]'s "[[Take Me to Church]]" received more than 25 million views.<ref name="vitagliano">{{cite news |last1=Vitagliano |first1=Joe |title=Grace Gaustad Honors Her Family And Tells Her Story On New EP, ‘Welcome To Jupiter 1.0’ |url=https://americansongwriter.com/grace-gaustad-honors-her-family-and-tells-her-story-on-new-ep-welcome-to-jupiter-1-0/ |access-date=1 October 2021 |work=American Songwriter}}</ref><ref name="nylon">{{cite news |last1=Valentine |first1=Claire |title=Rising Songwriter Grace Gaustad Premieres "Out Of Time" Video (Exclusive) |url=https://www.nylon.com/entertainment/grace-gaustad-out-of-time-video |access-date=1 October 2021 |work=NYLON}}</ref> 

Gaustad released her debut album, ''BLKBX: wht r u hding'', in August 2021. The album features connections with Gaustad's charity, the BLKBX Project and addresses her experiences with bullying and growing up as part of the LGBTQ+ community.<ref name="thea">{{cite news |last1=Gilchrist |first1=Tracey |title=Singer Grace Gaustad Takes on Queer Identity, Bullying in New Video |url=https://www.advocate.com/music/2021/6/10/singer-grace-gaustad-takes-queer-identity-bullying-new-video |access-date=1 October 2021 |work=The Advocate |date=10 June 2021}}</ref><ref name="mauch">{{cite news |last1=Mauch |first1=Ally |title=Mariska Hargitay on How Starring in Grace Gaustad's Music Video Fits 'Seamlessly' with Her Other Work |url=https://people.com/tv/mariska-hargitay-grace-gaustad-music-video-interview-exclusive/ |access-date=1 October 2021 |work=People |date=16 July 2021}}</ref>

"93 Days", one of the album's singles, featured actor [[Mariska Hargitay]] in its music video.<ref name="dilaura">{{cite news |last1=DiLaura |first1=Cassie |title=Rising Pop Artist Grace Gaustad Talks New Music and Friendship With Mentor Mariska Hargitay (Exclusive) |url=https://www.etonline.com/rising-pop-artist-grace-gaustad-talks-new-music-and-friendship-with-mentor-mariska-hargitay-170784 |access-date=1 October 2021 |work=ET |date=20 August 2021}}</ref>

''Welcome to Jupiter 1.0'', her second [[Extended play|EP]], was released in 2021.<ref name="riff">{{cite news |last1=Gokhman |first1=Roman |title=PREMIERE: Grace Gaustad misses her childhood on “Freedom” |url=https://riffmagazine.com/mp3/premiere-grace-gaustad-freedom/ |access-date=1 October 2021 |work=RIFF Magazine |issue=25 March 2021}}</ref>
