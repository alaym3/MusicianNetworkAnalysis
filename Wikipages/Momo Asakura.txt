{{Nihongo|'''Momo Asakura'''|麻倉 もも|Asakura Momo|born June 25, 1994}} is a Japanese [[Voice acting in Japan|voice actress]] and singer. She is part of the Music Ray'n agency.<ref name="yahoo"/><ref name="profile"/> After passing an audition held by Music Ray'n, she made her voice acting debut as Yumi in the anime ''[[My Little Monster]]''. She played her first main role as Ayumi Otosaka in the anime television series ''[[Charlotte (anime)|Charlotte]]''.<ref name=":0">{{Cite journal|date=March 2014|title= Seiyū Anime Media|issue=April 2014|page=86|language=ja}}</ref> She is a member of the voice acting unit [[TrySail]], alongside [[Sora Amamiya]] and [[Shiina Natsukawa]]. Apart from voice acting, she also performed theme song for various anime she acted in.

==Biography==
Asakura's interest in becoming a voice actress began in middle school and continued through high school.<ref>{{Cite web|title=第35回 麻倉 ももさん {{!}} 先輩から学ぼう！ {{!}} 声優グランプリweb|url=http://seigura.com/senior/future/20140423_6579|website=seigura|access-date=2015-07-11|language=ja|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150629000922/http://seigura.com/senior/future/20140423_6579|archive-date=2015-06-29|url-status=dead}}</ref> She got her break in 2011 when, together with [[Sora Amamiya]] and [[Shiina Natsukawa]], passed an audition held by [[Sony Music Entertainment Japan]]'s sub-label Music Ray'n.<ref name=":0"/> She made her voice acting debut in 2012, voicing the character Yumi in the anime ''[[My Little Monster]]''.<ref name="vadb"/> Since then, she was given minor roles in various anime.<ref name="vadb"/>

In 2014, she was cast as Sumi Otokawa in ''[[Sakura Trick]]'',<ref name="sumi"/> Rin Kazari in ''[[Witch Craft Works]]''.<ref name="rin"/> She and other cast members performed the series' ending theme "Witch☆Activity". On December 21, 2014, it was announced that Asakura, Amamiya, and Natsukawa would be forming the voice acting unit [[TrySail]], which released its first single "Youthful Dreamer", used as the opening theme song for the anime television series ''[[Ultimate Otaku Teacher]]'', on May 13, 2015.<ref>{{cite journal|title=TRYangle harmony presents トライアングルステージ!!! ～STEP～ in 日本青年館|journal=Seiyū Grand Prix|issue=March 2015|language=ja}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.oricon.co.jp/prof/646241/products/1123336/1/|title=Youthful Dreamer|publisher=[[Oricon]]|access-date=July 11, 2015|language=ja}}</ref> That same year, she was cast as Ayumi Otosaka in the anime television series ''[[Charlotte]]'' becoming her first main role.<ref name=":0"/><ref name="ayumi"/> She then played the role of [[List of Monster Musume characters#Manako|Manako]] in ''[[Monster Musume]]'',<ref name="manako"/> and Yuyu Aizawa in ''[[Aria the Scarlet Ammo|Aria the Scarlet Ammo AA]]''.<ref name="vadb"/>

In 2016, she made her official debut as a solo artist for Music Ray'n by releasing her first single {{nihongo|"Ashita wa Kimi to."|明日は君と}} which was used as an insert song for the anime film ''[[Suki ni Naru Sono Shunkan o|Suki ni Naru Sono Shunkan o: Kokuhaku Jikkō Iinkai]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sonymusic.co.jp/artist/asakuramomo/discography/SMCL-456|script-title=ja:明日は君と。|website=Official website|publisher=[[Sony Japan]]|access-date=January 28, 2019|language=ja}}</ref> The song peak at number 8 on the [[Oricon]] weekly charts.<ref name="singles-charts"/> Her first solo album ''Peachy!'' was released on October 3, 2018; the album peaked at number 6 on the Oricon weekly charts.<ref name="albums-charts"/> The following year, she was cast as Rona Pricipa O'Lapanesta in ''[[Endro!]]'',<ref name="rona"/> and Nagisa Kashiwagi in ''[[Kaguya-sama: Love Is War]]''.<ref name="nagisa"/>
