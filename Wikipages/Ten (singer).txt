'''Chittaphon Leechaiyapornkul''' ({{lang-th|ชิตพล ลี้ชัยพรกุล}}, {{lang-ko|이영흠}} {{lang|ko-Latn|I Yeong-heum}}, [[Chinese name]]: {{lang-zh |s=李永欽 |hp=Lǐ Yǒngqīn |labels=no}}; born February 27, 1996), known professionally as '''Ten''' ({{lang-th|เตนล์|links=no}}, {{lang-ko|텐}}), is a Thai singer and dancer active in South Korea and China. He debuted with South Korean [[boy group]] [[NCT (group)|NCT]] in 2016 with its first sub-unit NCT U. Mainly active as a member of the China-based unit [[WayV]]  since 2019, he is also a member of the South Korean supergroup, [[SuperM]].

Ten has released several solo singles through the [[SM Station]] project, "Dream in a Dream" (2017), "New Heroes" (2018) and "Paint Me Naked" (2021).

==Early life==
Ten was born in [[Bangkok]], Thailand, on February 27, 1996, to a [[Thai Chinese]] family.<ref name=sbsau>{{cite web|url=https://www.sbs.com.au/popasia/blog/2019/01/03/beginners-guide-ncts-new-unit-wayv|title=A beginner's guide to NCT's new unit, WayV|website=SBS PopAsia|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190511151645/https://www.sbs.com.au/popasia/blog/2019/01/03/beginners-guide-ncts-new-unit-wayv |archive-date=May 11, 2019|date=January 3, 2019|url-status=live|access-date=May 23, 2021}}</ref> He studied at [[Shrewsbury International School]].{{Citation needed|date=November 2021}}

In January 2011, at the age of 15, Ten competed in the Thai show ''Teen Superstar'', under the stage name TNT. He won and earned an opportunity to sign a contract with the South Korean agency [[Starship Entertainment]], but his family didn't allow him to go.<ref name=pre-debut>{{cite web|url=https://mgronline.com/entertainment/detail/9640000079617|title=Paint Me Naked “เตนล์ NCT” อีกหนึ่งเด็กไทยที่ผงาดบนเวที K-Pop|website=[[Manager Daily]]|date=August 14, 2021|access-date=November 23, 2021|language=th}}</ref> He ultimately joined SM Entertainment in 2013 after passing SM Global Audition in Thailand.<ref name=sbsau/><ref name=pre-debut/> 

==Career==
===2013–2015: Pre-debut and SM Rookies===
{{Main|SM Rookies}}
On December 23, 2013, Ten was introduced as a member of [[SM Rookies]], a pre-debut team of trainees under SM Entertainment.<ref name="tenyutajohnny">{{cite news|url=http://tenasia.hankyung.com/archives/195321|title=SM루키즈, 쟈니·유타·텐 공개 '다국적 글로벌 루키'|date=December 24, 2013|work=Tenasia|language=ko|trans-title=SM Rookies introduce international rookies Johnny, Yuta, Ten|access-date=June 10, 2016|archive-date=March 28, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210328115113/https://tenasia.hankyung.com/topic/article/2013122417024|url-status=live}}</ref>  In 2014, Ten and some of his co-members appeared on the [[Mnet (TV channel)|Mnet]]-produced ''[[Exo 90:2014]]'', a show starring labelmates [[Exo (band)|Exo]], where they did dance performances to K-Pop songs from the '90s. He also participated in the music video remake of [[g.o.d]]'s "[[To Mother (g.o.d song)|To Mother]]".{{Citation needed|date=November 2021}}

In July 2015, Ten and fellow trainee [[Jaehyun (singer)|Jaehyun]] competed in the Hope Basketball All-Star with SM Town, a charity match held by Hope Basketball All-Star Organizing Committee.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2015-06-01|title=슈퍼주니어·샤이니·EXO, 인기가수와 함께 하는 희망농구올스타|url=http://www.jumpball.co.kr/news/newsview.php?ncode=179556516402153|access-date=2020-12-16|website=Jumpball|language=ko|archive-date=May 16, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210516155004/http://www.jumpball.co.kr/news/newsview.php?ncode=179556516402153|url-status=live}}</ref>

=== 2016–2018: Debut and career beginnings  ===
{{Main|NCT (group)}}
On April 3, 2016, Ten was confirmed as a member of boy group [[NCT (group)|NCT]], in the first sub-unit, NCT U, he officially debuted with NCT U's digital single, "The 7th Sense" released on April 8, and it was also the first release of NCT overall.<ref name=":5">{{Cite web|url=https://en.yna.co.kr/view/AEN20160404006300315|title=SM's neo-boy band 'NCT U' to launch this week|date=2016-04-04|website=Yonhap News Agency|language=en|access-date=2019-08-12|archive-date=April 11, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190411103323/https://en.yna.co.kr/view/AEN20160404006300315|url-status=live}}</ref>

He also participated in [[Mnet (TV channel)|Mnet]]'s dance program ''[[Hit the Stage]]'' where he won [[List of Hit the Stage episodes|episode 6]].<ref>{{cite web|title=NCT U confirms lineup, set to unveil singles 'The 7th Sense,' 'Without You'|url=http://kpopherald.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=201604061758581112853_2|last=Son|first=Ji-hyoung|date=April 6, 2016|work=[[The Korea Herald]]|access-date=April 28, 2016|archive-date=June 12, 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180612143554/http://kpopherald.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=201604061758581112853_2|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.koreatimes.co.kr/www/news/culture/2016/07/201_210382.html|title='Hit the Stage' is an idol dance battle|date=July 26, 2016|access-date=July 31, 2016|author=Kim Jae-heun|work=The Korea Times|archive-date=July 30, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160730172541/http://www.koreatimes.co.kr/www/news/culture/2016/07/201_210382.html|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://entertain.naver.com/music/now/read?oid=108&aid=0002535867|title=태민·효연·보라·호야·모모, 엠넷 '힛더 스테이지' 합류|access-date=April 5, 2017|language=ko|archive-date=May 31, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190531125832/https://entertain.naver.com/music/now/read?oid=108&aid=0002535867|url-status=live}}</ref>

In January 2017, he joined the cast of [[Seoul Broadcasting System|SBS]]'s program, ''Elementary School Teacher'', which shows idols from foreign countries improving their Korean language skills as well as becoming more accustomed to Korean culture.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=312&aid=0000235664|title='초등학쌤' 트와이스 모모 "'얼룩말'은 어른이 하는 말?"|access-date=April 5, 2017|language=ko|archive-date=July 22, 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180722041248/https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=312&aid=0000235664|url-status=live}}</ref>

On March 24, 2017, [[SM Entertainment]] released a teaser for Ten's first single through the [[SM Station]] Season 2 project titled "Dream in a Dream". The song was released on April 7.<ref name="DIAD">{{Cite web|url=http://www.sbs.com.au/popasia/blog/2017/03/24/ncts-tens-new-sm-station-teaser-poster-all-trippy-and-colourful|title=NCT's Ten's new SM Station teaser poster is all trippy and colourful|website=SBS PopAsia|access-date=April 5, 2017|archive-date=March 26, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170326212446/http://www.sbs.com.au/popasia/blog/2017/03/24/ncts-tens-new-sm-station-teaser-poster-all-trippy-and-colourful|url-status=live}}</ref> It was announced on the same day that Ten is the new member of SM Entertainment's performance team [[SM The Performance]]. Ten subsequently concluded the project with his single "New Heroes" on April 6, 2018 which peaked at number four on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'''s [[World Digital Song Sales]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Chart Search|url=https://www.billboard.com/charts/search?artistname=ten&charttitle=new+heroes&label=s.m.&chartcode=&chart_date=|website=Billboard|access-date=2020-05-12|archive-date=March 28, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210328115108/https://www.billboard.com/charts/search?artistname=ten&charttitle=new+heroes&label=s.m.&chartcode=&chart_date=|url-status=live}}</ref> Dream in a Dream was then included in NCT's first studio album ''[[NCT 2018 Empathy]]''. For the album, Ten also participated in the release of the NCT U's single "[[Baby Don't Stop]]", a duet which he recorded alongside Taeyong. He also made an appearance in the "Black On Black" music video as a member of NCT 2018.

=== 2019–present: WayV, SuperM ===
{{Main|WayV|SuperM}}
On December 31, 2018, it was announced that [[WayV]], an NCT China-based boy group managed by Label V, a subsidiary of SM Entertainment, would be formed. The group consists of [[Qian Kun|Kun]], Winwin, Ten, [[Lucas Wong|Lucas]], Hendery, Xiaojun, and YangYang. They officially debuted on January 17, 2019, with their debut [[Extended play|EP]] ''[[The Vision (WayV EP)|The Vision]]'' and the Chinese version of [[NCT 127]]'s "Regular" as its lead single.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://m.weibo.cn/profile/6903448926|title=WayV_official|website=Weibo|language=zh|access-date=May 31, 2019|archive-date=December 31, 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181231143710/https://m.weibo.cn/profile/6903448926|url-status=live}}</ref><ref name="entertain.naver.com">{{cite web|url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=109&aid=0003929238|title=SM "中신인 웨이션브이, 2019년 1월 데뷔확정"…NCT 대거 합류 [공식입장]|date=December 31, 2018|website=Osen|language=ko|access-date=May 31, 2019|archive-date=April 11, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190411103306/https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=109&aid=0003929238|url-status=live}}</ref>

The show also got a week-late broadcast on the show's official YouTube channel. It featured both Thai and Korean celebrities such as [[Nam Tae-hyun]], [[Park Ye-eun]], [[Chicha Amatayakul|Kitty Chica]], and [[Teeradon Supapunpinyo|James Teeradon]].<ref>{{cite web |title=สนั่นโซเชียล "เจมส์-เตนล์" แท็กทีมไอดอลไทย-เกาหลี แข่งทำอาหาร "Food Truck Battle" |url=https://www.sanook.com/movie/87649/ |website=Sanook |access-date=15 May 2021 |language=th |date=28 May 2019}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Ten is a [[Multilingualism|multilingual]], and speaks Thai, English, Korean and Mandarin.<ref name="RF210717"/> 

In 2018, Ten received an official exemption from Thailand's mandatory military service due to his failure at passing the physical exam. His past injury and knee surgery had deemed him unfit for military service.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sbs.com.au/popasia/blog/2018/04/03/ncts-ten-officially-exempt-thai-military-release-new-solo-song|title=NCT's Ten officially exempt from Thai military, to release new solo song|access-date=April 29, 2020|archive-date=March 28, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210328115107/https://www.sbs.com.au/popasia/blog/2018/04/03/ncts-ten-officially-exempt-thai-military-release-new-solo-song|url-status=live}}</ref>
