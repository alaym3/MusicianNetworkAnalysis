'''Axwell & Ingrosso''' (stylised as '''Axwell Λ Ingrosso''') is a Swedish DJ duo consisting of [[Swedish House Mafia]] members [[Axwell]] and [[Sebastian Ingrosso]].<ref name=RStonePreview>{{cite news|last1=Blistein|first1=Jon|title=Axwell and Sebastian Ingrosso Form New Band|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/news/axwell-and-sebastian-ingrosso-plan-collaboration-new-album-20140529|access-date=27 August 2014|work=Rolling Stone|date=29 May 2014}}</ref> They made their debut performance at the 2014 [[Governors Ball Music Festival]] in New York City in June.<ref name=GoverBallBilboard>{{cite news|last1=Gale|first1=Alex|title=Governors Ball 2014: Ex-Swedish House Mafia Duo Axwell & Ingrosso Debut New Songs|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/music-festivals/6114031/governors-ball-2014-ex-swedish-house-mafia-duo-axwell|access-date=27 August 2014|work=Billboard|date=9 June 2014}}</ref>

==History==
===Pre-2013: Swedish House Mafia===
[[File:Swedish House Mafia 2012-06-16 001.jpg|thumb|right|Ingrosso (left) and Axwell (right) with [[Steve Angello]] as the [[Swedish House Mafia]].]]
{{main|Swedish House Mafia}}
{{see also|Swedish House Mafia discography}}
[[Axwell]] and [[Sebastian Ingrosso]] were part of the Swedish DJ trio [[Supergroup (music)|supergroup]] [[Swedish House Mafia]], along with [[Steve Angello]]. The group officially formed in late 2008, ranking number ten on the ''[[DJ Mag]]azine'' Top 100 DJ Poll 2011<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://djmag.com/top100dj?year=2011|title=Top 100 DJs – 2011|work=DJMag.com|access-date=2018-02-13}}</ref> and number twelve in 2012.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://djmag.com/top100dj?year=2012|title=Top 100 DJs - 2012|work=DJMag.com|access-date=2018-02-13}}</ref> During this time, the group released two studio/compilation albums, ''[[Until One]]'' and ''[[Until Now (Swedish House Mafia album)|Until Now]]'', and eight singles (with two credit under their individual names). On 24 June 2012, the group announced via their website that they would split up after embarking on "One Last Tour", which ended at [[Ultra Music Festival|Ultra Miami]] 2013 on 24 March 2013.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.huffingtonpost.com/2012/11/13/swedish-house-mafia-ultra-music-festival-2013_n_2122754.html|title=Swedish House Mafia At Ultra: 2013 Fest To Open, Close With Trio's Final Performances Ever|date=13 November 2012|work=HuffPost|access-date=9 February 2018}}</ref>

===2014: Formation and ''X4''===
[[File:Axwell Ingrosso Live.png|thumb|left|Axwell & Ingrosso performing live in 2014]]
Axwell and Ingrosso formed the group Axwell & Ingrosso officially in 2014. During June of that year, the band gained prominence among New Yorkers when they began placing clues about their new album in various locations throughout the city.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Tariq|first1=Sara|title=Song on the Streets: Axwell Ingrosso Premier New Music|url=http://www.vibe.com/article/axwell-ingrosso-premiere-new-music|access-date=27 August 2014|work=Vibe|date=6 June 2014}}</ref><ref name=MTVCluseNYC>{{cite news|last1=Lancaster|first1=Elizabeth|title=Axwell & Ingrosso Post Clues To New Music All Around New York City|url=http://www.mtv.com/news/1837133/axwell-ingrosso-post-clues-to-new-music-all-around-new-york-city/|access-date=27 August 2014|publisher=MTV|date=3 June 2014}}</ref> Their first performance was in 2014 at the 8 June Governors Ball Music Festival in New York City.<ref name=GoverBallBilboard/> The performance debuted music from a new album they announced to release under the name Axwell & Ingrosso the intro music of which premiered on air during [[Zane Lowe]]'s [[BBC Radio 1]] program.<ref name=RStonePreview/> Their performance was also named one of the ''15 Hot Governors Ball Moments'' as well as one of the ''Top 10 Best Performances'' of the Hot Governors Ball by [[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Governors Ball 2014: Top 10 Best Performances|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/music-festivals/6114086/governors-ball-2014-top-10-best-performances|access-date=27 August 2014|work=Billboard|date=9 June 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=15 Hot Governors Ball Moments: Axwell & Ingrosso, Vampire Weekend, Interpol Run Day 3|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/music-festivals/6114025/governors-ball-moments-axwell-ingrosso-vampire-weekend-interpol|access-date=27 August 2014|work=Billboard|date=9 June 2014}}</ref> They also made an appearance at the 2014 [[V Festival]].<ref>{{cite news|last1=Pell|first1=Michael|title=Axwell Ingrosso Literally Set Fire To MTV Stage at V Festival!|url=http://www.mtv.co.uk/axwell-l-ingrosso/news/axwell-ingrosso-literally-set-fire-to-mtv-stage-at-v-festival|access-date=27 August 2014|publisher=MTV|date=18 August 2014}}</ref> The album is also said to be the first for both artists as Swedish House Mafia never released an official full-length studio album.<ref>{{cite news|title=Axwell Ingrosso Confirm New Album Coming, Plus Tour Featuring Ibiza and Festival Dates|url=http://www.musictimes.com/articles/6508/20140602/axwell--ingrosso-confirm-new-album-coming-plus-tour-featuring.htm|access-date=27 August 2014|work=Music Times}}</ref> Axwell and Ingrosso also deleted their personal Facebook pages and created a single page for the group to signify the new partnership.<ref name=RStonePreview/> They toured India, as part of Sunburn Arena.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://themusicessentials.com/event/sunburn-arena-axwell-%CE%BB-ingrosso-bengaluru/ |title=Sunburn Arena with Axwell Λ Ingrosso : Bengaluru |website=T.H.E. Music Essentials |access-date=2018-02-13 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141017011410/http://themusicessentials.com/event/sunburn-arena-axwell-%CE%BB-ingrosso-bengaluru/ |archive-date=17 October 2014 }}</ref> On 14 February 2014, Axwell & Ingrosso released their debut EP ''X4''.<ref name="apple.com">{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/za/album/x4-ep/id964777041|title=X4 – EP by Axwell Λ Ingrosso on Apple Music|work=iTunes Store (ZA)|access-date=9 February 2018}}</ref> The EP contained the songs "Can't Hold Us Down", "We Come, We Rave, We Love" along with their 2007 collaboration "Together". The EP's final song, [[Something New (Axwell & Ingrosso song)|Something New]]", was released as the group's first official single in November that year.<ref>{{cite web|title=Axwell ^ Ingrosso Debut Single "Something New" in Star-Studded Beats by Dre Commercial |first=Marcus |last=Dowling |url=http://www.complex.com/music/2014/11/axwell-ingrosso-something-new-single-debut |date=2014-11-27 |website=[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]] |access-date=2018-02-13 |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150315150139/http://www.complex.com/music/2014/11/axwell-ingrosso-something-new-single-debut |archive-date=2015-03-15 }}</ref>

===2015–2017: Debut studio album and breakthrough===
[[File:Axwell & Ingrosso - Logo.jpg|thumb|right|The Axwell Λ Ingrosso logo in 2015]]

====Further releases====
On 12 March 2015, the duo dropped their next new track, "[[On My Way (Axwell & Ingrosso song)|On My Way]]" which features uncredited vocals from [[Vargas and Lagola]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://wegotthiscovered.com/music/axwell-ingrosso-drop-anthemic-song/|title=Axwell Λ Ingrosso Drop Anthemic New Song on My Way|date=2015-03-12|work=We Got This Covered|access-date=2018-02-13}}</ref> This was followed by a sequel video, directed by [[Christian Larson (director)|Christian Larson]] the next day which continued the story of the music video for "On My Way". This was the official music video for "Can't Hold Us Down",<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/code/6502016/axwell-ingrosso-premiere-cant-hold-us-down-mini-movie|title=Exclusive: Axwell & Ingrosso Premiere 'Can't Hold Us Down' Mini-Movie|work=Billboard|access-date=2018-02-13}}</ref> and was the day which "Can't Hold Us Down" saw an official release as a single.<ref name="apple.com1">{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/gb/album/cant-hold-us-down/id975583913?i=975583914 |title=Can't Hold Us Down - Single by Axwell Λ Ingrosso on Apple Music |publisher=[[iTunes Store]] |access-date=2018-02-13 }}</ref> On 12 June 2015, they released their fourth single "[[Sun Is Shining (Axwell & Ingrosso song)|Sun Is Shining]]" (also featuring uncredited vocals from [[Vargas and Lagola]]) which charted number one in [[Sweden]] giving the group their first number one single on a main singles' chart. In late July, the group made their [[Tomorrowland (festival)|Tomorrowland]] debut under their new collective debuting much new music. The group headlined the event and closed Night Two of the show.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.festicket.com/festivals/tomorrowland/2015/|title=Tomorrowland 2015 in Boom, Belgium – 24-26 July 2015 – Festicket|website=Festicket|access-date=9 February 2018}}</ref>

On 6 November 2015, the duo released their fifth single "[[This Time (Axwell & Ingrosso song)|This Time]]" featuring uncredited vocals from [[Pusha T]] with all proceeds from the song going to charity.<ref name=MT>{{cite news | last = Middleton | first = Ryan | title = Axwell /\ Ingrosso, Pusha T Release 'This Time' For Charity | work = Music Times| date = 2015-11-06 | url = http://www.musictimes.com/articles/53717/20151106/axwell-ingrosso-pusha-t-this-time-charity.htm | access-date = 2015-11-11}}</ref> The instrumental for their new single "Dream Bigger" was released on 31 December 2015,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/6836364/axwell-ingrosso-dream-bigger-instrumental-video|title=Axwell & Ingrosso Release 'Dream Bigger' Instrumental Video|website=Billboard|access-date=9 February 2018}}</ref> with the vocal version being released on 29 April 2016 featuring uncredited vocals from [[Pharrell Williams]].<ref name="apple.com2">{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/gb/album/dream-bigger-radio-edit/id1107756431?i=1107756646 |title=Dream Bigger – Single by Axwell Λ Ingrosso on Apple Music |publisher=[[iTunes Store]] |access-date=2018-02-13 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.beatport.com/release/dream-bigger/1762456|title=Dream Bigger from Axtone Records on Beatport|website=Beatport.com|access-date=9 February 2018}}</ref> The group were to headline [[Tomorrowland (festival)|Tomorrowland]] again for the second time, performing again on Night Two but opening the show this year. [[Axwell]] also performed as a solo artist on the Axtone stage.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.festicket.com/festivals/tomorrowland/2016/|title=Tomorrowland 2016 in Boom, Belgium – 22-24 July 2016 – Festicket|website=Festicket|access-date=9 February 2018}}</ref> On 10 February 2017, the duo released their eighth single "[[I Love You (Axwell & Ingrosso song)|I Love You]]" featuring vocals from [[Kid Ink]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/7686096/axwell-ingrosso-kid-ink-i-love-you|title=Axwell & Ingrosso Finally Drop Kid Ink Collab 'I Love You:' Listen|website=Billboard|access-date=9 February 2018}}</ref>

====''More Than You Know''====
[[File:Axwell Ingrosso - More Than You Know Logo.png|thumb|right|The Axwell Λ Ingrosso logo from the ''More Than You Know'' Album and EP]] 
[[File:Axwell Λ Ingrosso @ Airbeat One 2017.jpg|thumb|left|Axwell & Ingrosso performing at Airbeat One 2017]] 
{{Main|More Than You Know (Axwell & Ingrosso album)}}

On 24 May 2017, Axwell & Ingrosso released their second EP ''More Than You Know'' after releasing their debut single from the EP, "Renegade", on 10 May 2017.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/7808642/axwell-ingrosso-more-than-you-know-ep |title=Axwell & Ingrosso Release Debut 'More Than You Know' EP: Listen |last=Milla |first=Mira |date=2017-05-24 |website=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170730090822/https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/7808642/axwell-ingrosso-more-than-you-know-ep |archive-date=2017-07-30 |url-status=live |access-date=2018-02-09}}</ref> On the day of the EP's release, the duo released the EP's first song "[[More Than You Know (Axwell & Ingrosso song)|More Than You Know]]" as the group's tenth single. It is currently the group's most successful song commercially, gaining certifications in thirteen countries. In July, [[Axwell]] and [[Sebastian Ingrosso]] both performed solo at [[Tomorrowland (festival)|Tomorrowland]] with their collective also performing, headlining for the third consecutive year.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.festicket.com/festivals/tomorrowland/2017/|title=Tomorrowland 2017 in Boom, Belgium – 21-23 July 2017 & 28-30 July 2017 – Festicket|website=Festicket|access-date=9 February 2018}}</ref> 2017 was the first year the festival spanned two weeks (excluding its tenth anniversary in 2014).<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dancingastronaut.com/2016/09/tomorrowland-two-weekends-2017/|title=Tomorrowland to expand to two weekends in 2017 - Dancing Astronaut|date=13 September 2016|website=Dancingastronaut.com|access-date=9 February 2018}}</ref> Axwell & Ingrosso were the penultimate acts for Night One on both weeks. On 8 December 2017, the duo released their debut studio album ''[[More Than You Know (Axwell & Ingrosso album)|More Than You Know]]''.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.youredm.com/2017/12/08/new-axwell-%CE%BB-ingrosso-zomboy-droeloe-marshmello-edm-week-music/|title=Marshmello, Axwell Λ Ingrosso, & Kayzo All Just Dropped New Music|date=2017-12-08|work=Your EDM|access-date=2018-02-13}}</ref> It includes all their previously released singles, two bonus tracks ("Barricade" by Axwell and "Dark River" by Sebastian Ingrosso), as well as their new single "[[Dreamer (Axwell & Ingrosso song)|Dreamer]]" featuring vocals by [[Trevor Guthrie]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/bm/album/more-than-you-know/1321078170|title=More Than You Know by Axwell Λ Ingrosso on Apple Music|date=28 July 2017|publisher=iTunes Store|access-date=9 February 2018}}</ref><ref name="apple.com3">{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/au/album/dreamer-feat-trevor-guthrie-single/1316097495|title=Dreamer – Single by Axwell Λ Ingrosso on Apple Music|work=iTunes Store (AU)|access-date=9 February 2018}}</ref> The album peaked at number 12 on the Swedish album charts.<ref name="sverigetopplistan.se">{{cite web|url=http://www.sverigetopplistan.se/|title=Sverigetopplistan - Sveriges Officiella Topplista|website=Sverigetopplistan.se|access-date=9 February 2018}}</ref>
