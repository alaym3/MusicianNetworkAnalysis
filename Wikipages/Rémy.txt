'''Rémy''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Departments of France|department]] of [[Pas-de-Calais]] in the [[Hauts-de-France]] region of [[France]].

==Geography==
Rémy lies in the valley of the [[Sensée]] river, some {{Convert|10|mi|km|0}} southeast of Arras, on the D9 road.
