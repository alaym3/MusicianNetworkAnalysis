== Career ==

=== 2013–present: BTS ===
{{Main|BTS}}
Pre-debut, he was the "secret member" of BTS. Fans were unaware of his existence as his agency wished to keep him a surprise. He stated the concept had made him feel uneasy and lonely as he thought it was because he might get cut from the team.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Mamo |first1=Heran |title=V Recalls Journey From Breaking Out As BTS' 'Secret Member' to Prepping His Solo Mixtape |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/k-town/9575617/v-bts-secret-member-solo-mixtape/ |website=Billboard |access-date=October 29, 2021 |date=May 20, 2021}}</ref> On June 13, 2013, he made his debut as a member of [[BTS]] on [[Mnet (TV channel)|Mnet]]'s ''[[M Countdown]]'' with the track "No More Dream" from their debut single album, ''2 Cool 4 Skool''. He was first credited for music composition in ''[[The Most Beautiful Moment in Life, Part 1]]'' when he co-wrote and co-produced the song "Hold Me Tight". He also contributed to writing lyrics for the song "Fun Boyz", co-composed by bandmate Suga. For the song "Run", V's melody was used with [[Jungkook]]'s original lyrics for the next album ''[[The Most Beautiful Moment in Life, Part 2]]''. Furthermore, he contributed to music composition and lyrics for a solo song called "Stigma" from the album ''[[Wings (BTS album)|Wings]]''. V has also released, unofficially, a cover of "Hug Me" with bandmate [[J-Hope]] as well as a cover of "[[Someone Like You (Adele song)|Someone Like You]]" by [[Adele]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.fnnews.com/news/201602231940513276|script-title=ko:방탄소년단 뷔 측 "'화랑' 출연 확정, 연기자 데뷔" (공식입장)|last=Yoon|first=Hyo-jin|date=February 23, 2016|work=FN News|language=ko|trans-title=BTS V "'Hwarang' appearance confirmed, Actor Debut" (Official Position)|url-status=live|access-date=August 12, 2019|archive-date=July 8, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170708022210/http://www.fnnews.com/news/201602231940513276}}</ref>

== Personal life ==
In October 2018 during the [[Love Yourself World Tour]] concert in Paris, he had difficulty singing due to an illness and had to abstain from parts of his singing during the concert.<ref>{{cite web |title=BTS 뷔, 파리공연 중 '폭풍 눈물 인사'에 해외언론들 감동 특집기사로 다뤄 |url=https://m.segye.com/view/20181024000503 |website=Segye |access-date=24 October 2021 |language=Korean |date=24 October 2018}}</ref> In July 2019, he bought a luxury apartment worth USD $4.55 million.<ref>{{cite web |title=방탄소년단 뷔, 51억원 강남아파트 無대출 현금 매입 |url=https://theqoo.net/index.php?mid=hot&filter_mode=normal&document_srl=1238684691 |website=theqoo |access-date=24 October 2021 |language=Korean}}</ref> In October 2021 it was announced Taehyung would abstain from choreography during their "[[Permission to Dance on Stage]]" concerts due to a calf injury. Instead, he would remain seated while singing his parts.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Khan |first1=Afreen |title=ARMY trends #GetBetterSoonTae as BTS’ Kim Taehyung injures himself ahead of PTD online concert |url=https://www.sportskeeda.com/pop-culture/news-army-trends-getbettersoontae-bts-kim-taehyung-injures-ahead-ptd-offline-concert |website=[[Sportskeeda]] |access-date=24 October 2021 |date=24 October 2021}}</ref>

Since 2018 he has lived in [[Hannam-dong]], [[Seoul]], South Korea with his bandmates.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.joins.com/article/22766833|script-title=ko:[라이프트렌드] 유명인이 반한 한남동, 최고급 주거지로 뜬 이유|trans-title=[Life trend] The reason why Hannam-dong, which celebrities fall in love with, has emerged as a luxury residence|language=ko|last=Han|first=Jin|date=July 3, 2018|website=[[Korea JoongAng Daily]]|via=Naver|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190421121021/https://news.naver.com/main/read.nhn?mode=LSD&mid=sec&sid1=101&oid=025&aid=0002833392|archive-date=April 21, 2019|access-date=August 17, 2018}}</ref>
