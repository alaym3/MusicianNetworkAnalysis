'''Nils Kurt Erik Einar Grönberg'''{{Need-IPA}} (5 September 2002 – 21 October 2021),<ref name=hant>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hant.se/har-ar-allt-du-vill-vill-veta-om-rapparen-einar/|title=Här är allt du vill vill veta om rapparen Einár|date=16 August 2019|website=Hänt.se|language=sv-SE|access-date=27 August 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190827185136/https://www.hant.se/har-ar-allt-du-vill-vill-veta-om-rapparen-einar/|archive-date=27 August 2019|url-status=live}}</ref> who was commonly known as '''Einár''',{{Need-IPA}} was a Swedish [[Rapping|rapper]]. He released four albums, two of which topped the [[Sverigetopplistan|Swedish album chart]]. In 2019, he won the [[Musikförläggarnas pris]] for Breakthrough of the Year and in 2020 he won two [[Grammis]], Sweden's oldest pop music awards. Two of his singles peaked at number one on the Swedish singles chart and four have been certified platinum by the [[Swedish Recording Industry Association]] (SRIA). He is also featured on the number-one single "Gamora" by the group [[Hov1]]. 

Einár was involved in several high-profile incidents, most notably his kidnap by rival rapper [[Yasin (rapper)|Yasin]] and a [[Huddinge Municipality|Vårby]]-based criminal gang in 2020. A week before he was scheduled to testify in a trial connected to his kidnapping, he was found to have been shot dead in what police described as an execution-style shooting.

== Early life and career ==

Einár was born on 5 September 2002 to the actor [[Lena Nilsson]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.femina.se/i-rampljuset/80-talsstjarnan-lena-nilsson-blev-mamma-till-sveriges-storsta-artist/7634975|title=80-talsstjärnan Lena Nilsson blev mamma till Sveriges största artist|first=Aino|last=Oxblod|date=14 June 2021|website=Femina|access-date=22 October 2021|archive-date=22 October 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211022000458/https://www.femina.se/i-rampljuset/80-talsstjarnan-lena-nilsson-blev-mamma-till-sveriges-storsta-artist/7634975|url-status=live}}</ref> He grew up in [[Enskededalen]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.aftonbladet.se/a/lV9PyG|title=Nils "Einár" Grönberg är död – blev bara 19 år|work=Aftonbladet|access-date=22 October 2021|archive-date=22 November 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211122194517/https://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/musik/a/lV9PyG/einar--underbarnet-som-blev-en-av-sveriges-mest-spelade-artister|url-status=live}}</ref> At the age of 17, Einár was arrested and given an [[anti-social behaviour order]]{{Clarify|reason=linked article is about a UK legal concept, and does not mention Sweden|date=October 2021}} and lived for several months in a [[youth detention center|locked child protection home]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.etc.se/kultur-noje/einar-gjorde-raketkarriar-fran-stokig-uppvaxt-till-topplistorna|title=Einár gjorde raketkarriär – från stökig uppväxt till topplistorna|first=Gustav|last=Gelin|date=22 October 2021|website=ETC|access-date=22 October 2021|archive-date=22 October 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211022082353/https://www.etc.se/kultur-noje/einar-gjorde-raketkarriar-fran-stokig-uppvaxt-till-topplistorna|url-status=live}}</ref> 

Einár's first solo single "Gucci / Duckar Popo" was [[streaming media|streamed]] on [[Spotify]] over one million times.<ref>[http://www.kingsizemag.se/musik/se-musikvideon-till-einars-nya-singel-katten-i-trakten/ Musikvideon till Einars nya singel katten i trakten] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190216153213/http://www.kingsizemag.se/musik/se-musikvideon-till-einars-nya-singel-katten-i-trakten/ |date=16 February 2019}} Kingsize magazine Retrieved 16 February 2019</ref> His second single "Katten i trakten" peaked at number one on the [[Sverigetopplistan|Swedish Singles Chart]] in February 2019.<ref>[http://www.sverigetopplistan.se/ Sverigetopplistan – Einár] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181003131413/http://www.sverigetopplistan.se/ |date=3 October 2018}} Retrieved 16 February 2019</ref> His follow-up single "Rör mig" entered the Sverigetopplistan chart at number three in its first week of release in May 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://dopest.se/einar-tillbaka-med-singeln-ror-mig/|title=Einar är tillbaka efter succén med nya singeln "Rör mig"|date=25 April 2019|website=Dopest|access-date=22 October 2021|archive-date=22 October 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211022005355/https://dopest.se/einar-tillbaka-med-singeln-ror-mig/|url-status=live}}</ref> Einár released the studio albums ''Första klass'', ''Nummer 1'' and ''Welcome to Sweden'';<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.aftonbladet.se/a/lV9PyG|title=Nils "Einár" Grönberg är död – blev bara 19 år|newspaper=Aftonbladet|access-date=22 October 2021|archive-date=26 October 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211026182920/https://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/musik/a/lV9PyG/einar--underbarnet-som-blev-en-av-sveriges-mest-spelade-artister|url-status=live}}</ref> The first two albums were released in 2019 and the third on 15 May 2020. ''Welcome to Sweden'' debuted and peaked at number two on the [[Swedish Albums Chart]].<ref name="auto">{{Cite web|url=https://dopest.se/einar-album-nummer-1/|title=Einár vill visa att han är störst med sitt andra album 'NUMMER 1'|date=5 September 2019|website=Dopest|access-date=22 October 2021|archive-date=22 October 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211022005257/https://dopest.se/einar-album-nummer-1/|url-status=live}}</ref> ''Första klass'' and ''Nummer 1'' peaked at number one.<ref name="auto"/>

Einár won the [[Musikförläggarnas pris]] in 2019 in the Breakthrough of the Year category.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.dn.se/kultur-noje/einar-och-robyn-bland-musikforlaggarnas-pris-2019-vinner-musikforlaggarnas-pris-2019/|title=Einár och Robyn får musikpris|date=8 November 2019|work=Dagens Nyheter|access-date=22 October 2021|archive-date=22 October 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211022005718/https://www.dn.se/kultur-noje/einar-och-robyn-bland-musikforlaggarnas-pris-2019-vinner-musikforlaggarnas-pris-2019/|url-status=live}}</ref> At [[Sveriges Radio]]'s {{ILL|P3 Guld|sv}} gala in 2020, which was broadcast on [[Sveriges Television|SVT]], he won in the Song of the Year category for the song "Katten i trakten."<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://sverigesradio.se/artikel/7387784|title=Här är alla vinnare|date=18 January 2020|newspaper=Sveriges Radio|access-date=22 October 2021|archive-date=24 October 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211024184113/https://sverigesradio.se/artikel/7387784|url-status=live}}</ref> At the 2020 [[Grammis]] awards, Einár won the awards for Newcomer of the Year and Hiphop of the Year.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.kingsizemag.se/noje/erik-lundin-och-einar-tog-hem-statyetter-pa-grammis-2020-se-alla-vinnare-har/|title=Erik Lundin och Einár vann på Grammis 2020|website=Kingsizemag.se &#124; Svensk rap hiphop musik dans kläder artister tv|access-date=22 October 2021|archive-date=24 October 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211024185629/https://www.kingsizemag.se/noje/erik-lundin-och-einar-tog-hem-statyetter-pa-grammis-2020-se-alla-vinnare-har/|url-status=live}}</ref>

== Criminal activity==
Einár was convicted for minor narcotics crime, assault and driving without a permit. In October 2021, he was arrested after a stabbing. He also socialised with known criminals.<ref>{{Cite news|last=Nyheter|first=S. V. T.|last2=Nilsson|first2=Mimmi|date=2021-10-22|title=Hade kopplingar till kriminella gäng – och levde under hot|language=sv|work=SVT Nyheter|url=https://www.svt.se/nyheter/inrikes/rapartisten-einars-liv-i-kriminalitet|access-date=2021-10-25|archive-date=24 October 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211024070842/https://www.svt.se/nyheter/inrikes/rapartisten-einars-liv-i-kriminalitet|url-status=live}}</ref>

== Death and subsequent events ==
Einár was shot dead on the evening of 21 October 2021 in [[Hammarby Sjöstad]], Stockholm.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.expressen.se/nyheter/svenske-rapparen-einar-ihjalskjuten-i-natt/|title=Svenske rapparen Einár ihjälskjuten|work=Expressen|access-date=21 October 2021|archive-date=26 October 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211026182830/https://www.expressen.se/nyheter/krim/svenske-rapparen-einar-ihjalskjuten-i-natt/|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>[https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2021-10-22/swedish-teenage-rapper-killed-in-latest-stockholm-shooting Swedish Teenage Rapper Einar Killed in Stockholm Shooting] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211022121522/https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2021-10-22/swedish-teenage-rapper-killed-in-latest-stockholm-shooting |date=22 October 2021 }} Bloomberg. Retrieved 22 October 2021.</ref> Police described his killing as an execution because he was shot from a distance of {{convert|1.5|m|ft}}.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Swedish rapper Einár, 19, shot to death|url=https://www.aftonbladet.se/a/eEqVxl|publisher=Aftonbladet|access-date=22 October 2021|archive-date=26 October 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211026182832/https://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/a/eEqVxl/swedish-rapper-einar-19-shot-to-death|url-status=live}}</ref> The shooting was part of a trend of rising [[gun violence in Sweden]] that began in the 2010s.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-10-22|title=Swedish rapper Einar shot dead in suspected gang-related attack|url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/2021/oct/22/swedish-rapper-einar-shot-dead-in-suspected-gang-related-attack|access-date=2021-10-25|website=the Guardian|language=en|archive-date=24 October 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211024090458/https://www.theguardian.com/world/2021/oct/22/swedish-rapper-einar-shot-dead-in-suspected-gang-related-attack|url-status=live|quote=One of Sweden’s most popular rappers has been shot and killed in Stockholm, further fuelling public anger over a deadly wave of gang-related violence that has hit the country in recent years.}}</ref>

Einár had been scheduled to testify against  ''[[Vårbynätverket]]'' and Yasin in the ''[[Svea hovrätt]]'' ("Swedish Court of Appeal") the week after his killing.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.svd.se/uppgifter-artist-skjuten-till-dods-i-stockholm|title=Rapparen Einár skjuten till döds i Stockholm|date=22 October 2021|newspaper=Svenska Dagbladet|access-date=22 October 2021|archive-date=22 October 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211022112650/https://www.svd.se/uppgifter-artist-skjuten-till-dods-i-stockholm|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://sverigesradio.se/artikel/nya-uppgifter-einar-skulle-vittna-mot-varbynatverket|title=Nya uppgifter: Einár skulle vittna mot Vårbynätverket|first1=Simon|last1=Andrén|first2=Mariela|last2=Quintana|date=22 October 2021|newspaper=Sveriges Radio|access-date=22 October 2021|archive-date=22 October 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211022083343/https://sverigesradio.se/artikel/nya-uppgifter-einar-skulle-vittna-mot-varbynatverket|url-status=live}}</ref> On the morning after his death, Swedish [[Prime Minister of Sweden|Prime Minister]] [[Stefan Löfven]] and [[Minister for Culture (Sweden)|Minister for Culture]] [[Amanda Lind]] sent their condolences to Einár's family and fans.<ref name="auto2">{{Cite news|url=https://www.svt.se/nyheter/inrikes/lofven-om-dodsskjutningen|title=Löfven: "Han har betytt mycket för många unga människor"|first1=S.
