'''Pa (Patrick) Sheehy''' is an Irish singer-songwriter from [[Dingle]] County Kerry.<ref>{{cite web|url = https://www.echolive.ie/corknews/arid-40351358.html | publisher = Echo Live | title = Former Walking On Cars frontman Pa Sheehy announces Cork show | date = 2 August 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url = https://www.irishcentral.com/opinion/others/patrick-sheehy-walking-cars-songs | publisher = Irish Central | title = WATCH: How COVID quarantine is helping Walking on Cars' Patrick Sheehy to write new songs| date = 25 May 2020}}</ref>

Sheehy achieved some chart success in his native Ireland and across Europe as the lead singer of [[Walking on Cars]].<ref>{{cite web|url = https://www.hotpress.com/music/former-walking-on-cars-frontman-pa-sheehy-on-i-saw-you-a-funeral-theres-a-lot-of-people-out-there-who-will-hopefully-find-healing-from-the-track-22857698 | publisher = Hot Press | title = Former Walking On Cars frontman Pa Sheehy on 'I Saw You A Funeral': "There's a lot of people out there who will hopefully find healing from the track"| date = 18 June 2021}}</ref> Since the 2020 break up of the band, Sheehy began releasing solo material.<ref>{{cite web|url = https://www.buzz.ie/music/former-walking-cars-lead-singer-24398130 | publisher = Buzz.ie | title = Former Walking on Cars lead singer Pa Sheehy releases debut single | date = 25 June 2021}}</ref> His debut single, "Saw You at a Funeral", was released in June 2021 with a follow-up single "Roisin" released in August 2021.<ref>{{cite web|url = https://www.spinsouthwest.com/events/irish-music-1232990 | publisher = Spin South West | title = Pa Sheehy Releases Second Solo Single 'Róisín' And Announces Debut EP 'The Art Of Disappearing' Alongside Irish Tour Dates| date= 30 July 2021}}</ref> His debut EP, ''The Art of Disappearing'', was released in September 2021,<ref>{{cite web|url = https://journalofmusic.com/listing/06-08-21/pa-sheehy-releases-second-solo-single-roisin-announces-debut-ep-art-disappearing | publisher = Journal of Music | title = Pa Sheehy Releases Second Solo Single 'Róisín', Announces Debut EP 'The Art Of Disappearing' Alongside Irish Tour Dates | date = 6 August 2021}}</ref> charting at number 9 in the [[Irish Albums Chart]] on the week of its launch.<ref name="hotpress">{{cite web|url = https://www.hotpress.com/music/dingles-pa-sheehy-enters-the-irish-top-10-with-the-art-of-disappearing-ep-22873826 | publisher = Hot Press | website = hotpress.com | title = Dingle's Pa Sheehy enters the Irish Top 10 with The Art of Disappearing EP | date = 1 October 2021 | accessdate = 3 November 2021 | quote = Landing in the Top 10 at No.9 this week, the former Walking On Cars frontman also nabbed the No. 1 spot on the independent charts }}</ref>
