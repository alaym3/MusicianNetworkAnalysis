'''Elizaveta Igorevna Khripounova''' ([[Russian language|Russian]]: Елизавета Игоревна Хрипунова), now recording as '''Elizaveta''' (Russian: Елизавета), also formerly known as '''Elly K''', is a Russian-American pianist, singer/songwriter and opera singer.

Her debut full-length album ''[[Beatrix Runs]]'' was produced by Grammy Award-winner [[Greg Wells]]<ref>{{cite web|last=Keefe|first=Jonathan|title=Elizaveta Beatrix Runs|url=http://www.slantmagazine.com/music/review/elizaveta-beatrix-runs|publisher=Slant Magazine|access-date=18 April 2014}}</ref>  and was digitally released on [[Universal Republic Records]] 24 January 2012 exclusively via iTunes. The album was physically released through Amazon on 17 July 2012. Also on 17 July 2012, Elizaveta released her iTunes Session EP.<ref>{{cite web|last=Monger|first=James Christopher|title=Elizaveta Biography|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/elizaveta-mn0001944910/biography|publisher=Allmusic.com|access-date=17 April 2014}}</ref>

In 2013 Khripounova performed at the [[TED Global]] 2013.<ref>{{cite web|last=Chung|first=Becky|title=Musical alchemy: Elizaveta at TEDGlobal 2013|url=http://blog.ted.com/2013/06/11/musical-alchemy-elizaveta-at-tedglobal-2013/|publisher=TED|access-date=18 April 2014}}</ref>

On 17 March 2014 the first single from the ''Hero EP''  was remixed by electronic music duo [[Pegboard Nerds]] and featured in the viral video "Superman with a GoPro" which has over 20 million views on YouTube.<ref>{{cite web|last=Harris|first=Brock|title=Pegboard Nerds & Superman with a GoPro|url=http://www.edmsauce.com/2014/03/18/pegboard-nerds-superman-gopro/|publisher=EDM Sauce|access-date=23 April 2014}}</ref> 
 
On 14 April 2014, Khripounova released the ''Hero EP'' which reached #38 on Billboard's Heatseaker's Charts.<ref>{{cite web|title=Heatseekers Week April 26th|url=http://www.billboard.com/biz/charts/2014-04-26/heatseekers|publisher=Billboard.com|access-date=17 April 2014|archive-date=3 October 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151003184229/http://www.billboard.com/biz/charts/2014-04-26/heatseekers|url-status=dead}}</ref> On the same day, [[The A.V. Club]] premiered a video for the song "Sorry" which featured Malece Miller and Nico Greetham from the television show, ''[[So You Think You Can Dance]]''.<ref>{{cite web|last=Eakin|first=Marah|title=Elizaveta looks for heroes with two former So You Think You Can Dance contestants|url=http://www.avclub.com/article/elizaveta-looks-heroes-two-former-so-you-think-you-203378|publisher=A.V. Club|access-date=18 April 2014}}</ref>

Elizaveta's songs have been featured in ABC's ''[[Scandal (TV series)|Scandal]]'',<ref>{{cite news|title=Why the soundtrack on ‘Scandal’ is more like a character|url=https://nypost.com/2013/03/12/why-the-soundtrack-on-scandal-is-more-like-a-character/|website=nypost.com|access-date=12 March 2013|first=Sean|last=Daly|date=12 March 2013}}</ref> ''[[Pretty Little Liars (TV series)|Pretty Little Liars]]''<ref>{{cite web|title=Elizaveta's songs heard on Pretty Little Liars|url=http://www.tvfanatic.com/music/artists/elizaveta/|access-date=18 May 2015}}</ref> and ''[[The Affair (TV series)|The Affair]]''.<ref>{{cite web|title=The Affair|url=http://nameofthesong.blogspot.co.uk/2014/07/the-affair-season-1-teaser-trailer.html|access-date=18 May 2015}}</ref>

Elizaveta also sings the Tavern Songs <ref>{{cite web|title=Dragon Age: Inquisition Tavern Songs|url=http://www.pastemagazine.com/articles/2015/01/dragon-age-inquisition-tavern-songs-available-for.html|website=pastemagazine}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Dragon Age: Inquisition’s Tavern Songs Released for Free Download|url=http://www.gamefront.com/dragon-age-inquisitions-tavern-songs-released-for-free-download/|website=gamefront}}</ref> in the award-winning role playing video game ''[[Dragon Age Inquisition]]'', as the bard Maryden Halewell.<ref>{{cite web|title=Award-winning role playing video game Dragon Age Inquisition|url=http://www.gamespot.com/articles/dragon-age-inquisition-wins-another-game-of-the-ye/1100-6425927/|website=gamespot}}</ref>

Elizaveta's first single from her 2015 album ''Messenger'' is called 'Trap' and is featured in a Russian motion picture "Призрак"(2015).<ref>{{cite web|title=Призрак|url=http://www.kinopoisk.ru/film/839960/|website=kinopoisk}}</ref>

== Biography  ==

Elizaveta was born in New York City and raised in Moscow, Russia.<ref>{{cite web|last=Kristen|first=Houser|title=Interview with Elizaveta|url=http://lamusicblog.com/2009/09/interview/interview-with-elizaveta/|publisher=LA Music Blog|access-date=18 April 2014}}</ref>  She is a pianist, singer/songwriter, and opera singer with classical training.<ref>{{cite web|last=Monger|first=James Christopher|title=Elizaveta Biography|url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/301642/elizaveta/biography|publisher=Billboard|access-date=18 April 2014|archive-date=31 August 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150831203954/http://www.billboard.com/artist/301642/elizaveta/biography|url-status=dead}}</ref>  In 2007 she released a digital EP "Like Water". The title track was featured in the 2008 motion picture'' [[The House Bunny]]''.<ref>{{cite web|title=The House Bunny Soundtrack|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0852713/soundtrack|publisher=IMDb|access-date=18 April 2014}}</ref>

Her song "Gravity" was also featured in the 2010 motion picture ''[[Ice Castles (2010 film)|Ice Castles]]''.<ref>{{cite web|title=Ice Castles|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1411276/}}</ref>
