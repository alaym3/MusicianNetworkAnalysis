'''Leyla The Band''' is a Turkish rock band. Its formation was announced in March 2013 on the social media accounts for the TV series ''[[Leyla and Mecnun]]''.<ref>{{cite web|title=Leyla İle Mecnun Müzik Grubu Oldu|url=http://trtmuzik.net.tr/News/Detail/430881/leyla-the-band|publisher=trtmuzik.net.tr|accessdate=10 March 2014|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6NyTJIRco?url=http://trtmuzik.net.tr/News/Detail/430881/leyla-the-band|archivedate=10 March 2014|date=20 March 2013}}</ref> The series' leading actor [[Ali Atay]] served as the main vocalist for the band. Other members included director [[Onur Ünlü]] on drums, [[Serkan Keskin]] on bass guitar, [[Osman Sonant]] on keyboard, [[Fırat İkisivri]] on guitar, [[Sarp Aydınoğlu]] on percussion and [[Sarper Aksoy]] on clarinet.<ref name="ltb6ea">{{cite web|title=Leyla The Band 6 Ekim'de Arena'da|url=http://www.sabah.com.tr/Egeli/2013/10/04/leyla-the-band-6-ekimde-arenada|publisher=[[Sabah (newspaper)|Sabah]]|accessdate=10 March 2014|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6NyTYl05S?url=http://www.sabah.com.tr/Egeli/2013/10/04/leyla-the-band-6-ekimde-arenada|archivedate=10 March 2014|date=4 October 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Leyla İle Mecnun Müzik Grubu Oldu|url=http://www.trtmuzik.net.tr/Program/Detail/430881|publisher=trtmuzik.net.tr|accessdate=10 March 2014|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6NyTT3qRc?url=http://www.trtmuzik.net.tr/Program/Detail/430881|archivedate=10 March 2014}}</ref> The band's first song and musiv video<ref name="ltb6ea"/> "Yokluğunda" was first played during an episodes of ''Leyla and Mecnun'' and later uploaded on [[YouTube]]. The music video was viewed over 100 million times on YouTube<ref>{{cite web | title = Yokluğunda (Leyla The Band) | url = http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=49Kh1mS4Fhs | publisher = YouTube | accessdate = 27 March 2014 | date = 18 March 2013 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20130501170007/http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=49Kh1mS4Fhs | archivedate = 1 May 2013}}</ref> and became one of the most viewed videos in Turkey in 2013.<ref>{{cite web|title=2013'te YouTube'da en çok ne izledik?|url=http://www.milliyet.com.tr/2013-te-youtube-da-en-cok-ne-internet-1805977/|publisher=[[Milliyet]]|accessdate=10 March 2014|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6NySqxMln?url=http://www.milliyet.com.tr/2013-te-youtube-da-en-cok-ne-internet-1805977/|archivedate=10 March 2014|date=11 December 2013}}</ref> Their second song "Aşk Bitti" expanded their fan base. It was followed by a third song titled "Zaman".
