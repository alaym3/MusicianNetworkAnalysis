'''Mário Cotrim''', mostly known by his [[stage name]] '''ProfJam''' (born 6 June 1991<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://twitter.com/ProfJam6/status/871926985815142400|title=ProfJam on Twitter|work=Twitter|access-date=2018-08-22|language=en}}</ref>), is a Portuguese rapper and [[Hip hop music|hip hop]] singer.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://genius.com/artists/Profjam|title=ProfJam|website=Genius|access-date=2018-08-22}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.profjam.pt/|title=ProfJam - Site Oficial|website=Profjam - Palavras Ritmadas Ouvidas Finitamente|language=pt-PT|access-date=2018-08-22}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://fontesatwork.jimdo.com/m%C3%BAsica/hip-hop/profjam/|title=ProfJam|website=PortuCult|language=en-US|access-date=2018-08-22}}</ref>

== Career ==
Profjam started doing his own rhymes in 2008. His first exposition to the Hip Hop audience occurred when he participated in the Portuguese competition "''Liga Knockout''", where rappers had to make their own rhymes in order to defeat their opponent in a 1v1 punchlines battle.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.hiphoptuga.org/2016/01/entrevista-oficial-profjam.html|title=Entrevista Oficial ProfJam|access-date=2018-12-23}}</ref>

Later on, after dropping from a Computer Science and Information Systems degree at [[Instituto Superior Técnico]], he went to [[London]] to pursue a degree on Audio Production, in the [[SAE Institute]].

In 2016, he created his own record label, [[Think Music Records]]. The label has been growing over the years and has become one of the most popular Hip Hop labels in [[Portugal]]. Think Music Records produced for known Portuguese artists such as [[Think Music Records|Yuzi]], [[Sippinpurpp|SippinPurpp]], [[Think Music Records|Mike El Nite]], [[Think Music Records|Lon3r Johny]], among others.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://hiphopradio.pt/tag/think-music/|title=Arquivo de think music • Hip Hop Rádio|website=Hip Hop Rádio|language=pt-PT|access-date=2018-12-23}}</ref>

In January 2019, it was announced that ProfJam will perform on the [[main stage]] of [[Super Bock Super Rock|Super Bock Super Rock 2019]], on 20 July.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://sicnoticias.sapo.pt/cultura/2019-01-30-ProfJam-e-Masego-atuam-no-Super-Bock-Super-Rock-a-20-de-julho|title=SIC Notícias {{!}} ProfJam e Masego atuam no Super Bock Super Rock a 20 de julho|website=SIC Notícias|language=pt-PT|access-date=2019-01-31}}</ref> In 2018 he performed at the same [[music festival]] but on the secondary stage.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rimasebatidas.pt/profjam-isaura-as-novas-confirmacoes-do-super-bock-super-rock-2018/|title=ProfJam e Isaura entre as novas confirmações do Super Bock Super Rock 2018|date=2018-05-10|website=Rimas e Batidas|language=pt-PT|access-date=2019-01-31}}</ref>

After he released his album #FFFFFF in February 2019, the numbers started to appear, as almost every song on the album got to the top 100 of [[Associação Fonográfica Portuguesa]] singles charts. The song "Tou Bem" featuring Lhast, got to the number 1 spot on the Portuguese charts and on the main social media streaming services, like [[YouTube]] or [[Spotify]]. The records, "Água de Côco" and "Tou Bem" reached the platinum certification as they got over 20,000 sales registered by [[Associação Fonográfica Portuguesa|AFP]]. The album reached the number 1 spot on the Portuguese charts in March 2019.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=https://www.sonymusic.pt/profjam-top-nacional-de-vendas/|title=ProfJam: #1 lugar do TOP Nacional de Vendas com o #FFFFFF|date=2019-03-19|website=Sony Music Entertainment Portugal|language=en-US|access-date=2019-03-21}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rimasebatidas.pt/profjam-ffffff-em-primeiro-lugar-na-tabela-de-discos-mais-vendidos-em-portugal-e-concerto-esgotado-em-lisboa/|title=ProfJam: #FFFFFF em primeiro lugar na tabela de discos mais vendidos em Portugal e concerto esgotado em Lisboa|date=2019-03-20|website=Rimas e Batidas|language=pt-PT|access-date=2019-03-21}}</ref>

On 5 August 2020, ProfJam will perform at the ''MEO Stage'' of [[MEO Sudoeste]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://sudoeste.meo.pt/|title=MEO Sudoeste|last=https://sudoeste.meo.pt|website=MEO Sudoeste|language=pt-PT|access-date=2020-01-15}}</ref>(Canceled due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/COVID-19_pandemic_in_Portugal/|title=COVID-19 pandemic in portugal}}</ref>{{Circular reference|date=November 2020}})

== Personal life ==
On 10 October 2019, he was hospitalized after suffering from a [[psychosis]] due to [[Substance abuse|drug abuse]] while he was writing his new song "''Anjos e Demónios''".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cmjornal.pt/portugal/detalhe/anjos-e-demonios-levam-profjam-ao-inferno-rapper-pode-falhar-premios-mtv-emas|title=Anjos e Demónios levam ProfJam ao inferno|website=www.cmjornal.pt|language=pt|access-date=2019-10-11}}</ref>
