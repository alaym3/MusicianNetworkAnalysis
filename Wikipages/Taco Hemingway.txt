'''Filip Tadeusz Szcześniak''' (born 29 July 1990), better known by the [[stage name]] '''Taco Hemingway''' (earlier '''FV'''), is a Polish rapper, songwriter, and musician who has worked solo and as a part of the duo '''Taconafide'''<ref>{{citation|title = Taconafide|access-date = 17 April 2018|publisher = Discogs|url = https://www.discogs.com/artist/6382713-Taconafide|language = en}}</ref> together with the rapper Quebonafide.

== Early life ==
Filip Szcześniak was born in [[Cairo]], [[Egypt]], in 1990.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://kinkyowl.pl/wywiad-taco-hemingway/|title = Wywiad: Taco Hemingway|last = Bielawski|first = Wojciech|access-date=27 August 2015|publisher=kinkyowl.pl|language = pl}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url = http://wyborcza.pl/1,75475,18412247,taco-hemingway-self-made-raper.html|title = Taco Hemingway: Self made rapper|last = Świąder|first = Jacek|publisher = wyborcza.pl|language = pl|access-date = 27 August 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url = http://hiphopedia.info/index.php?site=show_artist&id=5735&typ=O|title = Taco Hemingway|publisher = hiphopedia.info|language = pl|access-date = 14 March 2016}}</ref> At the age of 2, he moved with his parents from [[Egypt]] to [[China]]. Initially, he attended an English preschool before transferring to a Chinese preschool, where he experienced communication problems due to differences in culture and language. Upon finishing preschool, Szcześniak and his family moved to [[Warsaw]] in 1996,<ref>{{Citation|title=Foodvillain|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=68VQDgDSrOY|author=Barbara Kaczmarczyk|access-date=5 July 2017|date=19 March 2015|language=pl}}</ref> where he experienced little to no difficulties in school while simultaneously developing his bilingualism from his early years. In an interview with ''Metro Warszawa'', Szcześniak stated that he spoke English with his mother and sister and that he spoke in Polish with his father.<ref name=":1">{{citation|title=Taco Hemingway: 'Trójkąt warszawski' jest o pijanych, zakochanych ludziach w mieście [WYWIAD]|journal=Gazetapl|access-date=5 July 2017|url = http://metrowarszawa.gazeta.pl/metrowarszawa/1,141635,17541734,Taco_Hemingway___Trojkat_warszawski__jest_o_pijanych_.html|language = pl}}</ref> He listened to various rap artists with his father, developing his passion for the rap genre.<ref name=":1" /> His parents divorced in 2004.<ref name=":1" />

Szcześniak completed his studies at Prywatne Gimnazjum nr 1 and Copernicus Bilingual High School in Warsaw, where he met his future producer, Maciej Ruszecki. He is also a graduate of cultural studies at the University of Warsaw.<ref>{{cite web|title=Taco Hemingway "Następna stacja"|url=http://www.polskieradio.pl/9/210/Artykul/1474063,Taco-Hemingway-Nastepna-stacja|agency=www.polskieradio.pl|language=pl}}</ref> In 2012, he began studying for a master's degree in anthropology in [[University College London]].<ref>{{citation|title=Nie wiem co robić... W końcu mam ten papier magistra... / Boje się, że branża reklamowa dla mnie za śliska|access-date=5 July 2017|agency=Genius|url=https://genius.com/10770972|language = pl}}</ref>

== Career ==
[[File:Warszawa280919.jpg|left|thumb|Taco Hemingway and [[Dawid Podsiadło]] at a concert at the [[National Stadium, Warsaw|National Stadium]], [[Warsaw]] 2019]]
Szcześniak began his career in 2011, when he released a mixtape in English titled ''Who killed JFK'' under the nickname "Foodvillain".<ref>{{cite web|title=fvkilledjfk|access-date=5 July 2017|publisher=YouTube|url=https://www.youtube.com/user/fvkilledjfk/videos}}</ref> A year later, he changed his stage name to Taco Hemingway and released an EP in English titled ''Young Hems''.<ref>{{cite web|url = http://culture.pl/pl/tworca/taco-hemingway|title = Taco Hemingway|last = Świąder|first = Jacek|publisher = culture.pl|language = pl|access-date = 20 September 2016}}</ref> In 2014, he released a second EP, ''Trójkąt warszawski'', this time recorded in his native language, [[Polish language|Polish]], which has since continued to be the main language in all of his records.<ref>{{cite web|url = http://www.hiphopedia.info/index.php?site=show_album&id=5463|title = Taco Hemingway – "Trójkąt Warszawski"|publisher = www.hiphopedia.info|language = pl|access-date = 2 September 2015}}</ref> The EP's success brought him publicity and granted him the opportunity to sign under [[Asfalt Records]], where he released a new edition of ''Trójkąt Warszawski'' along with a new EP, ''Umowa o dzieło''.<ref>{{cite web|url = http://www.cgm.pl/aktualnosci,41018,taco_hemingway_umowa_o_dzielo_ep_ka_juz_do_odsluchu,news.html|title = Taco Hemingway – "Umowa o dzieło" – EP-ka już do odsłuchu|publisher = www.cgm.pl|language = pl|access-date = 2 September 2015}}</ref> Taco Hemingway's albums appeared on the second and third spots of the [[OLiS|OLiS chart]], selling 15,000 and 10,000 copies respectively, awarding ''Umowa o dzieło'' gold status.<ref name=":2">{{cite web|url=http://olis.onyx.pl/listy/index.asp?idlisty=967&lang=|title=OLiS – sprzedaż w okresie 14.08.2015 – 20.08.2015|publisher=olis.onyx.pl|language=pl|access-date=27 August 2015}}<br />{{cite web|url=http://olis.onyx.pl/listy/index.asp?idlisty=1058&lang=|title=OLiS – sprzedaż w okresie 02.12.2016 – 31.08.2017|publisher=olis.onyx.pl|language=pl|access-date=7 September 2017}}<br />{{cite web|url=http://olis.onyx.pl/listy/index.asp?idlisty=1134&lang=en|title=OLiS – sprzedaż w okresie 13.04.2018 – In 2016, Taco's third EP, ''Wosk'', as well as a studio album, ''Marmur'', were released.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cgm.pl/aktualnosci,47199,taco_hemingway_opublikowal_nowa_plyte,news.html|title = Taco Hemingway opublikował nową płytę|access-date=3 November 2016|publisher=www.cgm.pl|language = pl}}</ref> The two releases debuted on the third and 14th spots on OLiS. Marmur sold 15,000 copies and received gold status.<ref name=":2" /><ref name=":3"/> In 2017, his fifth EP, ''Szprycer'' was released,<ref>{{citation|title = Taco Hemingway "Szprycer" – premiera i odsłuch darmowej EP-ki + teledysk – GLAMRAP.PL – Napędza Hip-Hop'ową gospodarkę|access-date=1 August 2017|publisher=glamrap.pl|url=http://glamrap.pl/pc/43122-taco-hemingway-szprycer-premiera-i-odsluch-darmowej-ep-ki-teledysk|language = pl}}</ref> and became a commercial success, debuting on the 1st spot on OLiS and receiving platinum record status, selling 30,000 copies.<ref name=":2" /><ref name=":0">{{cite web|url=http://bestsellery.zpav.pl/wyroznienia/platynoweplyty/cd/archiwum.php?year=2018|title=Platinum CD 2018|publisher=bestsellery.zpav.pl|language=pl|access-date=8 February 2017}}</ref> On 3 April 2018, Taco cooperated with Polish rapper Quebonafide in recording and releasing ''Soma 0,5 mg'',<ref>{{citation|title = Taconafide - SOMA 0,5 mg|access-date = 17 April 2018|publisher = Discogs|url = https://www.discogs.com/Taconafide-SOMA-05-mg/release/11855023|language = en}}</ref> which debuted on 1st spot of OLiS and received platinum status.<ref name=":4">{{cite web|url=http://olis.onyx.pl/listy/index.asp?idlisty=1134&lang=|title = OLiS – sprzedaż w okresie 13.04.2018 – 19.04.2018|access-date=26 April 2018|publisher=olis.onyx.pl|language = pl}}</ref> A single named ''Tamagotchi'' reached first place in most radio stations in [[Poland]] and landed on spot number 23 on a chart of best-selling singles in Poland, [[Polish music charts|AirPlay]].<ref name=":5">{{cite web|url=http://bestsellery.zpav.pl/airplays/top/archiwum.php?year=2018&idlisty=2702#title|title=Taconafide – Tamagotchi (AirPlay Top: 19.05. – 25.05.2018)|publisher=bestsellery.zpav.pl|language=pl|access-date=28 May 2018}}<br />{{cite web|url=http://bestsellery.zpav.pl/airplays/top/archiwum.php?year=2018&idlisty=2869#title|title=Taco Hemingway - Fiji (AirPlay Top 20.10. – 26.10.2018)|language=pl|accessdate=2018-10-29|agency=[[Związek Producentów Audio-Video]]}}{{cite web|url=http://bestsellery.zpav.pl/airplays/top/archiwum.php?year=2019&idlisty=3133#title|title=Taco Hemingway - W piątki leże w wannie (AirPlay 27.07. - 02.08.2019)|language=pl|accessdate=2019-08-17|agency=[[Związek Producentów Audio-Video]]}}</ref><ref>{{citation|title = Notowanie 7505 - Hop Bęc {{!}} RMF MAXXX|access-date = 7 April 2018|publisher = www.rmfmaxxx.pl|url = https://www.rmfmaxxx.pl/hopbec/archiwalne|language = pl}}</ref><ref>{{citation|title = 2 x Gorąca 20|access-date = 9 April 2018|url = http://www.eska.pl/2xgoraca20?chce_id=17003|language = pl}}</ref><ref>{{citation|title = Szczecińska Lista Przebojów - Radio Szczecin|access-date = 16 May 2018|publisher = radioszczecin.pl|url = http://radioszczecin.pl/index.php?idp=154&idx=1817&idl=1407|language = pl}}</ref>

Taco Hemingway has been nominated three times for the hip hop/R&B album of the year category in the [[Fryderyk|Fryderyk music awards]] in 2016, 2017, and 2018 for his albums ''Umowa o dzieło'', ''Marmur'', and ''Szprycer'' respectively,<ref name="cgm.pl44228">{{cite web|url=http://www.cgm.pl/aktualnosci,44228,fryderyki_2016_sprawdz_kto_otrzymal_nagrode,news.html|title = Fryderyki 2016 – sprawdź, kto otrzymał nagrodę|date=20 April 2016|access-date=21 April 2016|work = cgm.pl|publisher = Codzienna Gazeta Muzyczna|language = pl}}</ref><ref>{{citation|title = Fryderyki 2017: zobacz listę zwycięzców|journal = Onet Muzyka|date = 26 April 2017|access-date = 28 April 2017|url = http://muzyka.onet.pl/newsy/fryderyki-2017-zobacz-liste-zwyciezcow/wf03g7|language = pl}}</ref><ref>{{citation|author = Sergiusz Królak|title = Fryderyki 2018. Ogłoszono wszystkich nominowanych. Fani niezadowoleni|publisher = 6 March 2018|url = http://plejada.pl/newsy/fryderyki-2018-nominacje-kto-wygra-lista-nominowanych/enq6eb|language = pl}}</ref> winning single awards for ''Umowa o dzieło'' and ''Szprycer''.<ref>{{citation|title = Fryderyki 2018: znamy zwycięzców [Wyniki]|journal = Onet Kultura|date = 24 April 2018|access-date = 24 April 2018|url = https://kultura.onet.pl/muzyka/wiadomosci/fryderyki-2018-znamy-zwyciezcow-wyniki/ldf7vqf|language = pl-PL}}</ref><ref name="cgm.pl44228" />

In 2018 he created with Quebonafide hip-hop group called Taconafide. Together they released two music projects: long play - ''Soma 0,5mg'' and mixtape - ''Soma 0,25mg''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://noizz.pl/muzyka/taconafide-soma-05-mg-tracklista-goscie-i-data-premiery/esmp870|title=TACONAFIDE świątecznie odkrywają ostatnie karty. Znamy listę gości i tracklisty albumów|date=2018-04-02|website=Noizz|language=pl|access-date=2019-04-29}}</ref> The Soma 0,5&nbsp;mg reached diamond status.<ref name="platynowe2018" />
