'''The Four Owls''', or simply '''The Owls''' is a [[British hip hop]] group formed in 2011.<ref name="in-reach">{{Cite web|url=http://www.in-reach.co.uk/natural-order-an-interview-with-leaf-dog|title=Natural Order: An Interview with Leaf Dog <nowiki>|</nowiki> In Reach Magazine|author=Hainsey|date=10 March 2015|publisher=In Reach Magazine|access-date=28 November 2019}}</ref>

The group is signed to English label [[High Focus Records]] and is composed entirely of previously established High Focus members under bird-themed aliases: Fliptrix (Big Owl), [[Verb T]] (Bird T), BVA (Rusty Take-Off), and Leaf Dog (Deformed Wing).<ref name="high-focus-biography">{{Cite web|url=https://www.high-focus.com/home/artists/the-four-owls|title=The Four Owls - Official Website of High Focus Records|publisher=High Focus Records|access-date=28 November 2019}}</ref>

The Owls released their third album, ''Nocturnal Instinct'', on the 17th of April 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.blatantlyblunt.com/album-the-four-owls-nocturnal-instinct/|title=Album: The Four Owls - Nocturnal Instinct|last=Wijesinghe|first=James|date=2020-04-17|website=Blatantly Blunt|language=en-GB|access-date=2020-04-20}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://shop.high-focus.com/products/the-four-owls-nocturnal-instinct-digital|title=The Four Owls - Nocturnal Instinct (Digital)|website=High Focus Records|access-date=2020-04-20}}</ref> The album featured US legends [[DJ Premier]], [[R.A. the Rugged Man]] and [[Kool G Rap]], among others.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.iamhiphopmagazine.com/new-music-dj-premier-joins-four-owls-new-single-100/|title=NEW MUSIC {{!}} DJ PREMIER JOINS THE FOUR OWLS ON NEW SINGLE '100%'|last=Dhaliwal|first=Rishma|date=2020-03-27|website=I Am Hip-Hop Magazine|language=en-US|access-date=2020-04-20}}</ref>
