'''Feast''' (stylized as .Feast) is a [[Rock music|rock]] band from Indonesia. The current members include Baskara Putra, Adnan S.P, Dicky Renanda P, F. Fikriawan W, and Adrianus Aristo H.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bandwagon.asia/articles/jakarta-based-rock-band-feast-release-music-video-for-their-latest-single-wives-of-we-belong-dead-watch|title=Jakarta-based rock band Feast release music video for their latest single 'Wives of ゴジラ (We Belong Dead)'—watch|date=24 May 2017|website=Jakarta-based rock band Feast release music video for their latest single 'Wives of ゴジラ (We Belong Dead)'—watch {{!}} Bandwagon {{!}} Music media championing and spotlighting music in Asia.|access-date=1 October 2019}}</ref>

== Career ==
.Feast came into formation when the members were still studying Social and Political Science at the [[University of Indonesia]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.djarumcoklat.com/coklatnews/multiverses-rangkuman-materi-berbahaya-dari-feast|title=Multiverses, Rangkuman Materi Berbahaya dari .Feast|last=Az Zahra|first=Azka|date=23 September 2017|work=Djarum Coklat|accessdate=13 August 2018}}</ref> In 2014, they released their debut album titled "Camkan" which spots Religious Freedom in Indonesia.

After Camkan, they had planned to make another album titled "Convictions", however, they went inactive for a year instead. They later returned with a single, "Wives of ゴジラ/Gojira (We Belong Dead)" featuring Janitra Satriani,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.djarumcoklat.com/coklatnews/setelah-lama-menghilang-grup-musik-feast-kembali-bangkit-|title=Setelah Lama Menghilang, Grup Musik Feast Kembali Bangkit|last=Fadhlan|first=Alfie|date=26 May 2017|work=Djarum Coklat|accessdate=13 August 2018}}</ref> and in July 2017, they released "Sectumsempra", inspired by [[Harry Potter]], featuring Yudhis from Rachun Band.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://supermusic.id/supernews/superbuzz/feast-rilis-video-sectumsempra-lagu-yang-terinspirasi-mantra-harry-potter|title=.FEAST: Rilis Video 'Sectumsempra', Lagu yang Terinspirasi Mantra Harry Potter|author=NTP|date=24 July 2017|work=Super Music|accessdate=13 August 2018}}</ref>

On 18 September 2017, they released an [[Extended play|EP]] titled ''[[Multiverses]]'' featuring many collaborators including the [[rapper]] Ramengvrl, Elephant Kind's vocalist Bam Mastro, Mardial, Oscar Lolang, Haikal Azizi, and many more.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://pophariini.com/feast-merilis-album-studio-perdana-multiverses/|title=Feast Merilis Album Studio Perdana Multiverses|author=Pohan|date=23 September 2017|work=Pophariini|accessdate=13 August 2018}}</ref>

On 13 July 2018, .Feast released a single for an upcoming album titled "Peradaban".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.antaranews.com/berita/727015/feast-rilis-single-baru-peradaban|title=.Feast rilis single baru "Peradaban"|last1=Yuniar|first1=Nanien|last2=Nurcahyani|first2=Ida|date=16 July 2018|work=[[Antaranews]]|accessdate=13 August 2018}}</ref> On 10 August 2018, .Feast released a second single featuring Rayssa Dynta titled "Berita Kehilangan", which points out [[criminalization]], [[rape]], [[rebellion]], and [[terrorism]] cadres in Indonesia.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.djarumcoklat.com/coklatnews/feast-sajikan-berita-kehilangan-dari-peristiwa-kelam-di-indonesia|title=.Feast Sajikan Berita Kehilangan Dari Peristiwa Kelam di Indonesia|last=Suryadi|first=Fatur Rahman|date=10 August 2018|work=Djarum Coklat|accessdate=13 August 2018}}</ref> In late 2018, [[Baskara Putra]] started a solo career under the name "Hindia".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.provoke-online.com/index.php/music/for-your-ears/22550-gunakan-nama-hindia-vokalis-feast-rilis-single-solo-perdana-evaluasi|title=Gunakan Nama Hindia, Vokalis .Feast Rilis Single Solo Perdana, "Evaluasi"|last=Nanda|first=Madava|date=22 March 2019|work=Provoke Online|accessdate=14 May 2019|archive-date=14 May 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190514122748/https://www.provoke-online.com/index.php/music/for-your-ears/22550-gunakan-nama-hindia-vokalis-feast-rilis-single-solo-perdana-evaluasi|url-status=dead}}</ref>

In 2019, .Feast released another EP called "Membangun dan Menghancurkan" containing a single titled "Dalam Hitungan", which satirizes [[political polarization]], [[religious bigotry]], and [[Internet addiction]] in Indonesia, and another single titled "Tarian Penghancur Raya" which spots [[environmental issue|environmental]] and cultural threat issues. Also in 2019, .Feast & The Panturas released a single titled "Gelora" for the [[2019 Southeast Asian Games|2019 SEA Games]] in [[Philippines]].

== Members ==

* [[Baskara Putra]] – vocalist, synthesizer
* Adnan S.P. – guitar
* Dicky Renanda – guitar
* F. Fikriawan – bass
* Adrianus Aristo Haryo (Bodat) – drum
