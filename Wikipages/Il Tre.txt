'''Il Tre''' is an [[Italy|Italian]] [[rapper]]. He is mostly known for his album ''Ali - Per chi non ha un posto in questo mondo'' that debuted at the top of the [[Fimi|FIMI]] leaderbords.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Giometti|first=Eduardo|date=2021-02-26|title=Il Tre debutta in testa alla classifica degli album più venduti con Ali|url=https://www.spettacolo.eu/tre-debutta-testa-alla-classifica-degli-album-piu-venduti-ali/|access-date=2021-03-30|website=Spettacolo.eu|language=it-IT}}</ref>

== Career ==

=== Early years (2015-2018) ===
His career began in the [[2010s in music|2010s]], with his first work "Cataclisma", published on [[YouTube]] in 2015,<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|title=Il Tre: biografia, discografia e contatti ufficiali|url=https://www.bohmagazine.it/il-tre/|access-date=2021-03-30|website=Boh Magazine|language=it-IT}}</ref> but a particular turning point turned when he reached the success of the One Shot Game contest in 2016, surpassing the competition of hundreds of other artists. In 2018 he published the mixtape "Cracovia Vol. 2" from total independent, and despite this he managed to conquer in less than a week over 50 thousand downloads and 1 million streams, also running out of physical copies.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-08-15|title=Chi è Il Tre, il rapper di Te lo prometto|url=https://notiziemusica.it/chi-e-il-tre-rapper/curiosita/|access-date=2021-03-30|website=Notizie Musica|language=it-IT}}</ref>

=== Signing for Atlantic (2018-2019) ===
His first official single, "Bella Guido", was released in June of the same year and immediately went in the "Top Viral" leaderboard on [[Spotify]]. He therefore began to acquire more popularity thanks to the [[YouTube]] Real Talk format, whose video exceeded 3 million views. These results lead him to sign for [[Atlantic Records|Atlantic]] / [[Warner Music Italy]], his new single is "L’importante" produced by Ill Bosca, from Real Talk.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Biografia Il Tre|url=https://www.rockit.it/iltre/biografia|access-date=2021-03-30|website=Rockit.it|language=it}}</ref> with Atlantic he recorded singles such as "Cracovia Pt. 3" ([[Gold Disc|Gold certified]]), "Le vostre madri", "Fight" ft [[Nayt]] and "Te lo prometto" ([[Platinum disc|Platinum certified]]).<ref>{{Cite web|last=Redazione|first=Francesca|date=2021-01-21|title=Chi è Il Tre? Vero nome, età, biografia e discografia|url=https://www.teamworld.it/musica/chi-e-il-tre/|access-date=2021-03-30|website=Team World|language=it-IT}}</ref> He also appears in the "[[Ricercato (album)|Ricercato]]" album by [[Junior Cally]] in the track "Cristiano".<ref name=":0" />

=== ''Ali'' (2020-2021) ===
On February 19, 2021, he released his debut [[studio album]] ''Ali - Per chi non ha un posto in questo mondo'', anticipated by the single "Pioggia".<ref>{{Cite web|last=Redazione|first=Francesca|date=2021-02-15|title=Ali, l'album di debutto di Il Tre: tracklist e versioni|url=https://www.teamworld.it/musica/ali-per-chi-non-ha-un-posto-in-questo-mondo-album-il-tre/|access-date=2021-03-30|website=Team World|language=it-IT}}</ref> After just 1 week by its release,  the album makes its debut at the top of the ranking of the [[Best selling albums|best-selling albums]] of the week, created by the [[Fimi|FIMI]] (Italian Music Industry Federation).<ref>{{Cite web|title=Il Tre, "Ali" vola subito al primo posto: è l’album più venduto|url=https://www.radioitalia.it/news/il_tre/classifica/22884_il_tre_ali_vola_subito_al_primo_posto_e_l_album_piu_venduto.php|access-date=2021-03-30|website=Radio Italia}}</ref>
