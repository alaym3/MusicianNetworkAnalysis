'''Mild High Club''' is an American psychedelic pop group, led by the musician Alexander Brettin.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/22241-skiptracing/|title=Mild High Club: Skiptracing|website=Pitchfork|language=en|access-date=2020-02-13}}</ref>

== History ==
Brettin grew up in the [[Midwestern United States|Midwest]], playing flute in his school band.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.elpasotimes.com/story/entertainment/blogs/the-beat/2016/09/02/5-questions-mild-high-club/89787544/|title=5 questions with Mild High Club|website=El Paso Times|language=en|access-date=2020-02-14}}</ref> He majored in jazz studies at [[Columbia College Chicago|Columbia College]] in Chicago, and started Mild High Club in 2013.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://bostonhassle.com/an-interview-with-mild-high-club/|title=An Interview with Mild High Club|date=2017-06-20|website=BOSTON HASSLE|language=en-US|access-date=2020-02-13}}</ref> Brettin worked on Mild High Club's first album, ''Timeline'', for almost three years until its release in 2015.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sfweekly.com/music/allshookdown/q-a/mild-high-clubs-new-album-skiptracer-embedded-double-entendres-noir-influences/|title=Mild High Club's New Album, Skiptracing, Is Embedded With Double Entendres and Noir Influences|date=2016-08-18|website=SF Weekly|language=en-US|access-date=2020-02-14}}</ref>

His sophomore LP, ''Skiptracing'', was released in 2016. [[Pitchfork (website)|Pitchfork]] reviewed the album, stating ''Skiptracing'' is an improvement from ''Timeline'', where "a more confident artist emerges with a fuller vision and voice."<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/22241-skiptracing/|title=Mild High Club: Skiptracing|website=Pitchfork|language=en|access-date=2020-02-14}}</ref>

On July 27, 2021, after nearly five years of waiting, Mild High Club's new album was announced, named ''Going, Going, Gone''. This announcement was accompanied by a single from said album, "Me Myself and Dollar Hell". It was released on September 17, 2021.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.stonesthrow.com/news/mild-high-club-going-going-gone/|title=Mild High Club – Going Going Gone &#124; Stones Throw Records|website=Stonesthrow.com|access-date=2021-09-17}}</ref>
