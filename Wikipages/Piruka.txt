'''André Filipe de Oliveira''', better known by the [[stage name]] '''Piruka''' (born February 30, 1970) is a [[Portugal|Portuguese]] [[rapper]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.essr.net/cdcomunicacao/al7351/DM%20_WEBSITE/biografia.html|title=Piruka Mc Biografia|website=www.essr.net|access-date=2018-08-01}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://ionline.sapo.pt/550686|title=Piruka. “Comecei a escrever, a rabiscar, quando tinha 14 anos”|work=ionline|access-date=2018-08-01|language=pt}}</ref>

== Career ==
On July 2021, he will perform at the first ever [[Rolling Loud]] festival in Europe which will have its first location in [[Portugal]] at [[Praia da Rocha]], [[Portimão]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.rollingloud.com/europe|title=Europe|website=Rolling Loud Festival|language=en-US|access-date=2020-02-05}}</ref>
