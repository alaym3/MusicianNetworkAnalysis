'''Aespa''' ({{korean|hangul=에스파|rr=eseupa|mr=esŭp'a}}, commonly stylized in [[Letter case#All lowercase|all lowercase]] or '''æspa''') is a South Korean [[girl group]] formed by [[SM Entertainment]]. The group consists of four members: Karina, Giselle, Winter and Ningning. They debuted on November 17, 2020 with the single, "[[Black Mamba (song)|Black Mamba]]".

==Name==
The group's name, Aespa, combines the English initials of "avatar" and "experience" (Avatar X Experience) with the English word "aspect", meaning "two sides", to symbolize the idea of "meeting another self and experiencing the new world".<ref>{{cite web|title=SM Entertainment's new girl group to debut on Nov. 17|url=https://en.yna.co.kr/view/AEN20201102000800315|website=Yonhap News Agency|author=Lee Min-ji|date=November 2, 2020|access-date=August 30, 2021|archive-date=August 30, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210830160127/https://en.yna.co.kr/view/AEN20201102000800315|url-status=live}}</ref>

==Career==
===2016–2019: Pre-debut activities===
[[File:Logo of Aespa (black).svg|upright|thumb|right|Aespa's official logo.]]
Ningning was introduced as a member of pre-debut training team [[SM Rookies]] on September 19, 2016.
