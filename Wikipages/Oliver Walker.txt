'''Oliver Walker''' (born 23 September 1985) is a British actor, known for his role on the TV series ''[[Atlantis (TV series)|Atlantis]]''. He has also made guest appearances on ''[[Doctors (2000 TV series)|Doctors]]'', ''[[Holby City]]'' and ''[[Coronation Street]]'', among others.

==Career==
In 2013, Walker was a key cast member of the fantasy show ''[[Atlantis (TV series)|Atlantis]]'' aired on [[BBC One]] based on Greek myth and the mythological city [[Atlantis]].<ref>{{cite web|last1=Munn|first1=Patrick|title=Oliver Walker Cast In BBC One's 'Atlantis', David Evans Boards 'The Ark'|url=http://www.tvwise.co.uk/2013/03/oliver-walker-joins-bbc-ones-atlantis-david-evans-boards-the-ark/|website=TV Wise|accessdate=16 October 2014}}</ref> He played Heptarian, one of the series' antagonists, who plots along with the main villain, [[Pasiphaë]].

==Awards==
Walker was awarded the Em-Lou Production and Drill Hall Award and the Micheal Mac Liammoir Award for Best Actor at the [[International Dublin Gay Theatre Festival]] in 2011.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Parker|first1=Tim|title=Oliver Walker - Award|url=https://www.flickr.com/photos/timp_photo/5828471504/|website=flikr|accessdate=19 October 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Gala Award Winners 2011|url=http://www.gaytheatre.ie/news/article/gala_award_winners_2011/|website=International Dublin Gay Theatre Festival|accessdate=19 October 2014}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Walker is from [[Plymouth]] and currently lives in [[London]]. He previously studied at [[Arts Educational Schools London]] before becoming an actor.
