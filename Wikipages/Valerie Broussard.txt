'''Valerie Broussard''' ({{IPAc-en|v|ae|l|@r|i|_|b|r|u|'|s|a:r|d}}) is an American singer and songwriter. Her works, in collaboration with other artists, have appeared in [[Theme music|theme tunes]] for television shows. She released the single "Sideways" in collaboration with [[Illenium]] which became a top ten hit on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' Hot Dance / Electronic chart in  May 2021.

==Biography==
Valerie Broussard was born and raised in [[Philadelphia]], regularly performing in theatre.<ref name=bustle/> She moved to [[London]] aged 18,<ref name=hitc/> and became interested in classical opera before beginning songwriting.<ref name=bustle>{{cite magazine|url=https://www.bustle.com/p/how-think-about-you-singer-valerie-broussard-learned-to-enjoy-her-success-not-focus-on-her-flaws-17029153|title=How "Think About You" Singer Valerie Broussard Learned To Enjoy Her Success, Not Focus On Her Flaws|work=Bustle|date=April 18, 2019|accessdate=November 1, 2020}}</ref> One of her early influences was [[Goo Goo Dolls]]' "[[Iris (song)|Iris]]", particularly its music video, which she later covered with her own video. She has since collaborated with the group, singing on "Money, Fame & Fortune" on the album ''[[Miracle Pill]]''.<ref name=as>{{cite magazine|url=https://americansongwriter.com/bringin-it-backwards-interview-with-valerie-broussard/|title=Bringin' It Backwards : Interview with Valeria Broussard|work=American Songwriter|accessdate=1 November 2021}}</ref>

Several of her songs have appeared as theme music for TV shows, including ''[[Luther (TV series)|Luther]]'', ''[[Chilling Adventures of Sabrina]]'' and ''[[Riverdale (2017 TV series)|Riverdale]]''. Her cover of "[[Don't Let Me Be Misunderstood]]", originally released in November 2019, was used as the theme for the [[Alibi (TV channel)|Alibi]] (and later [[BBC One]]) show ''[[Traces (TV series)|Traces]]''.<ref name=hitc>{{cite magazine|url=https://www.hitc.com/en-gb/2021/01/05/traces-theme-song-soundtrack-music-valerie-broussard/|title=Traces theme song: Explore the artist, songs and soundtrack of BBC Crime Drama|work=HITC|date=January 5, 2021|accessdate=November 1, 2021}}</ref> She has also collaborated with DJ and producer [[Kygo]], singing on his compositions "[[Think About You (Kygo song)|Think About You]]" and "[[The Truth (Kygo song)|The Truth]]", and with [[Ray Chen]] on the track "Awaken", used in the 2019 release of ''[[League of Legends]]''.<ref name=as/>

In 2019, she was signed to [[Palm Tree Records]] and [[RCA Records]], and collaborated with [[Lindsey Stirling]] on the song "Deeper".<ref name=bustle/> In May 2021, she released the single "Sideways" in collaboration with [[Illenium]], about a friend facing mental health difficulties during the [[COVID-19 pandemic]]. It became a top ten hit on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' Hot Dance / Electronic chart.<ref name=as/> Her cover of "Iris" includes a video that acknowledges the original, changing themes from being locked in a tower to looking at the world from a spaceship. She has said "I wanted to give an homage to theirs."<ref name=as/> She released her debut EP, ''Voyager'' that summer, with an accompanying single, "Drive".<ref name=as/><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.digitaljournal.com/entertainment/review-valerie-broussard-releases-refreshing-single-drive/article|title=Review: Valerie Broussard releases refreshing single ‘Drive’|work=Digital Journal|date=September 11, 2021|accessdate=November 1, 2021}}</ref>
