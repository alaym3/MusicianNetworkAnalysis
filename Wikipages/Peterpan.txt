'''José Fernandes de Paula''' better known as '''Peterpan''' ([[Maceió]], Brazil 21 January 1911 — [[Rio de Janeiro]], Brazil 28 April 1983) was a Brazilian instrumentalist and composer. His 50-year career began in the 1930s and spanned into the 1980s.<ref name=bio-portuguese>{{cite web|title=Peterpan|url=http://www.dicionariompb.com.br/peterpan/dados-artisticos/biografia|website=Dicionário Música Popular Brasileira|publisher=Dicionário Cravo Albin|accessdate=13 July 2015}}</ref>
