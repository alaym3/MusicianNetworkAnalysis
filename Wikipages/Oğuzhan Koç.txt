'''Oğuzhan Koç''' (born 13 May 1985) is a Turkish singer-songwriter, actor, screenwriter, TV host, composer, music producer and comedian.

== Life and career ==
Koç was born on 13 May 1985 in [[Refahiye]], [[Erzincan Province|Erzincan]]. He studied at the Bursa State Conservatory's Music Department for high school and elementary school. At the age of twelve, Oğuzhan Koç met comedian [[Eser Yenenler]] and writer [[İbrahim Büyükak]] in Bursa and they started their collective career together.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/yazarlar/izzet-capa/3-adam-cocuklugumuzda-cok-dislandik-28708153|title=3 Adam: Çocukluğumuzda çok dışlandık|first=İzzet|last=ÇAPA|website=www.hurriyet.com.tr}}</ref> Also, he and İbrahim Büyükak played in BursaSpor Young Football Team for a short time.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.haberler.com/oguzhan-koc/biyografisi/|title=Oğuzhan Koç Kimdir ? - Oğuzhan Koç Hayatı ve Biyografisi|website=www.haberler.com}}</ref> He simultaneously started to study in the Anthropology Department of [[Istanbul University]] and pursued an online education in the Business Department of [[Anadolu University]] after spending one year in the Labour Economy Department of [[Dokuz Eylül University]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.superhaber.tv/demet-ozdemir-boyu-kac-oguzhan-koc-boyu-kac-demet-ozdemir-yas-haber-320839|title=Demet Özdemir boyu kaç? Oğuzhan Koç boyu kaç?|first=Super|last=Haber|website=www.superhaber.tv}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.biyografi.info/kisi/oguzhan-koc | accessdate = 11 April 2016 | title = Oğuzhan Koç Biyografisi | publisher = biyografi.info | date = 11 April 2016 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20151113040838/http://www.biyografi.info/kisi/oguzhan-koc | archivedate = 13 November 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=Güncellenme: 16:01, 24/07/2020 |url=https://www.sozcu.com.tr/hayatim/magazin-haberleri/oguzhan-koc-ve-ibrahim-buyukak-acun-ilicaliya-karsi-kaybetti/ |title=Oğuzhan Koç ve İbrahim Büyükak, Acun Ilıcalı'ya karşı kaybetti - Magazin haberleri |publisher=Sozcu.com.tr |date=2020-07-24 |accessdate=2021-02-24}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/gundem/oguzhan-koc-kimdir-40109741|title=Oğuzhan Koç kimdir?|website=www.hurriyet.com.tr}}</ref>

Koç, who has been interested in performing arts from a young age, joined the BKM Theatre in 2006, with Eser Yenenler, İbrahim Büyükak and Zeynep Koçak. He and BKM actors worked as screenwriters and performed as actors in Kanal D's theatrical comedy ''[[Çok Güzel Hareketler Bunlar]]'', which was awarded at 36th [[Golden Butterfly Awards]] as the Best Comedy Show.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/kelebek/altin-kelebekte-en-iyiler-11858363|title=Altın Kelebek'te en iyiler|website=www.hurriyet.com.tr}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.haberler.com/oguzhan-koc-kimdir-oguzhan-koc-kac-yasinda-13843395-haberi/|title=Oğuzhan Koç kimdir? Oğuzhan Koç kaç yaşında, nereli? Oğuzhan Koç'un sevgilisi kimdir?|date=January 3, 2021|website=Haberler.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.cumhuriyet.com.tr/haber/neseli-hayat-yarin-seyirciyle-bulusacak-101724|title='Neşeli Hayat' yarın seyirciyle buluşacak|website=www.cumhuriyet.com.tr}}</ref> He wrote and composed the hit song "Gül ki Sevgilim" which was performed by [[Ferhat Göçer]] and later by himself. He similarly gave the hit song "Giden Günlerim Oldu" to [[Gülben Ergen]] for her album ''Uzun Yol Şarkıları''. The song was recorded as a duet and he later appeared in its music video. He received great acclaim for his humour songs "Uzun Hava (Oscar, Fikrimi Çaldı)", "Domuz Gribi", "Unutulmaz ki", "O Benim Arkadaşım (Oscar)", "Uzun Hava (with Eser Yenenler)", "Oscar (with Eser Yenenler)", "Bilmiyorum", "Yatırbank", "Yapma Gülsüm", "Aduket" and other musical performances which were composed by himself and published in Kanal D's ''Çok Güzel Hareketler Bunlar''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.milliyet.com.tr/cadde/galeri/oguzhan-koc-yillarca-calisarak-unlu-oldum-6252446|title=Oğuzhan Koç: Yıllarca çalışarak ünlü oldum!|website=Milliyet}}</ref> In 2009, he took a role in the movie ''Neşeli Hayat'' directed by [[Yılmaz Erdoğan]]. In 2010, Koç starred in the movie titled ''[[Çok Filim Hareketler Bunlar]]'', which was written and directed by BKM actors. He was also awarded the "Best Soundtrack" award at the 17th [[Kral Music Awards]] for composing the songs "El Alem Ne Der" and "Uçanları Vurmasınlar" for the movie's soundtrack. He also wrote and composed the songs "Sebepsiz Aşk" and "Dudaklar Kavuşsun", which released unofficially.<ref>{{cite web | title=Oğuzhan Koç: Kendime sarmak bana iyi geldi | website=Sabah | date=2020-06-07 | url=https://www.sabah.com.tr/pazar/2020/06/07/kendime-sarmak-bana-iyi-geldi | language=tr | access-date=2021-02-24}}</ref><ref name="bio">{{cite web|url=https://www.sozcu.com.tr/hayatim/magazin-haberleri/kimdir-oguzhan-koc-nereli-ve-kac-yasinda-szcu3/|title=Oğuzhan Koç kimdir? Oğuzhan Koç nereli ve kaç yaşında?|newspaper=[[Sözcü]]|date=15 May 2020|accessdate=12 July 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.internethaber.com/oguzhan-koc-bkmye-nasil-girdigini-anlatti-eser-yenenler-kefil-oldu-foto-galerisi-2096304.htm|title=Oğuzhan Koç BKM'ye nasıl girdiğini anlattı! Eser Yenenler kefil oldu|first=Internet|last=Haber|date=April 16, 2020|website=www.internethaber.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/kelebek/televizyon/cok-guzel-hareketler-2nin-ozel-bolumu-ekrana-damga-vuracak-41407454|title=Çok Güzel Hareketler 2'nin özel bölümü ekrana damga vuracak...Yıldızlar geçidi yılbaşı gecesi Kanal D'de|website=www.hurriyet.com.tr}}</ref>

Together with Eser Yenenler and İbrahim Büyükak, Koç presented the talk show ''[[3 Adam]]'' between 2013 and 2017. Koç's first album, ''Ben Hala Rüyada'', was released on 2 December 2013 by Esen Müzik. The album, which was entirely prepared by himself, was awarded at the Golden Butterfly Awards and Turkey Music Awards. In April 2015, he wrote and composed his new duet with Gülben Ergen, titled "Aşkla Aynı Değil", which was released as a single. In 2014, he wrote and composed the song "Bu Nasıl Aşk" for [[Murat Dalkılıç]]'s new album ''Daha Derine''. His songs "3 Adam (jenerik)", "Kuma Yapıyor", "İkimizinde Psikolojisini Sen Bozdun", "Benim Adım Oğuzhan", "3 Adam 1 Kadın", "Saçlar Nerden Link Atsana", "Bezdim Yarim", "Babama Bonus Şarkı" were released in entertainment shows. He then joined the second season of [[TV8 (Turkish TV channel)|TV8]]'s music contest ''[[O Ses Türkiye#O Ses Çocuklar|O Ses Çocuklar]]'' (Turkish version of ''[[The Voice Kids]]'') as a judge and his contestant finished in the first place at the end of the season.<ref>{{cite web|author=Güncellenme: 15:32, 08/05/2015 |url=https://www.sozcu.com.tr/2015/gunun-icinden/gulben-ergen-beyaz-showda-gulben-ergen-kimdir-826787/ |title=Gülben Ergen, Beyaz Show'da! Gülben Ergen kimdir? – Sözcü Gazetesi |publisher=Sozcu.com.tr |date= |accessdate=2021-02-24}}</ref><ref name="auto">{{cite web|url=https://www.star.com.tr/medya/3-adam-yetenek-sizsinizde-rap-soyledi-ibrahim-ve-oguzhan-kirdi-gecirdi-haber-809299/|title=3 Adam Yetenek Sizsiniz'de rap söyledi! İbrahim ve Oğuzhan kırdı geçirdi &#124; STAR|website=Star.com.tr}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.cnnturk.com/magazin/oguzhan-koc-3-adamin-neden-bittigini-acikladi|title=Oğuzhan Koç '3 Adam'ın neden bittiğini açıkladı|website=CNN Türk}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.son.tv/haber-265675/|title=Altın Kelebek ödülleri - Altın Kelebek 2014 ödülleri|date=June 22, 2014|website=SON TV}}</ref>

Koç then had a leading role in the movies ''Yol Arkadaşım'' and ''Yol Arkadaşım 2'' as Onur Güzel, which were written by İbrahim Büyükak. Together with Gülben Ergen and Eser Yenenler, he took part in the Turkish dubbing of musical theatre ''Pinokyo''. He was then cast as a voice actor in the animated sitcom ''[[Fırıldak Ailesi]]'', voicing a single character together with Eser Yenenler and İbrahim Büyükak. He became one of the voice actors chosen for the animated movie ''Doru''.<ref name="bio"/><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sabah.com.tr/oguzhan-koc-kimdir|title=Oğuzhan Koç kimdir?|newspaper=[[Sabah (newspaper)|Sabah]]|date=17 May 2016|accessdate=12 July 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.trendus.com/gulben-ergen-pinokyoyu-seslendirecek-24053|title=Gülben Ergen Pinokyo'yu seslendirecek - Trendus.com|website=www.trendus.com}}</ref>

In 2016, he collaborated on [[Murat Boz]]'s new album ''[[Janti (album)|Janti]]'', by giving him the song "Yüzüm Yok", the acoustic version of which was performed by Oğuzhan Koç on his album ''Ev'' (2020). On 7 October 2016, he released the single "Bulutlara Esir Olduk", which was written and composed by himself, followed by a music video on 17 October. At the same time, he prepared the song "Berhudar Olmak Zor" that was used on the soundtrack of İbrahim Büyükak's movie ''Küçük Esnaf''. In April 2017, Koç released the single "Küsme Aşka", the lyrics and music of which belong to himself. The song was arranged by [[Ozan Çolakoğlu]] and its music video was directed by Bedran Güzel, featuring appearances by [[Sinan Tuzcu]], [[İrem Sak]], Şahin Irmak, Begüm Birgören, Eren Hacısalihoğlu, Serkan Şenalp, [[Alina Boz]] and Dilşad Şimşek. Koç continued his career by releasing singles such as "Aşinayız" (duet with Murat Dalkılıç), "Beni İyi Sanıyorlar", "Takdir-i İlahi", and "Sükut-u Hayal", all of which were written and composed by himself.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.aksam.com.tr/magazin/atakan-celik-kimdir-kac-yasinda-cok-guzel-hareketler-bunlar-2-kadrosunda/haber-821670|title=Atakan Çelik kimdir, kaç yaşında? Çok Güzel Hareketler Bunlar 2 kadorsunda|website=aksam.com.tr}}</ref><ref>[https://teknodiot.com/2020de-en-cok-oguzhan-koc-dinlendi]{{dead link|date=February 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ntv.com.tr/sanat/yildiz-tilbenin-yildizli-sarkilari-cikti-39-sanatci,WiGMeD_iHkiEZT0p8IuloQ|title=Yıldız Tilbe'nin 'Yıldızlı Şarkıları' çıktı (39 sanatçı)|website=www.ntv.com.tr}}</ref> He and 39 singers appeared on the [[Yıldız Tilbe]] tribute album ''Yıldız Tilbe'nin Yıldızlı Şarkıları'', in which he performed the song "Çat Kapı".

In 2020, he released new album ''Ev'', which consisted of 5 new songs and the acoustic version of 11 of his previous songs. He wrote and composed the song "Her Mevsim Yazım" which was performed by [[Zeynep Bastık]] and they later performed it together as a duet. He later released the singles "Kendime Sardım", "Heyecandan" and "Yoksa Yasak". Koç later appeared on ''Mesaj'', a [[tribute album]] to [[Melih Kibar]], and performed the song "Deli Divane".

Koç was also the co-writer of Çağrı Telkıvıran's song "Olur mu?". Together with Telkıvıran, he also wrote and composed Hera's song "Kimse Sormasın" and Zeynep Bastık's "Bir Daha".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.posta.com.tr/yazarlar/bekir-sacar/heranin-bomba-sarkisi-haftaya-cikiyor-2283437|title=Hera'nın bomba şarkısı haftaya çıkıyor|work=Posta|first=Bekir|last=Saçar|date=23 October 2020|accessdate=4 March 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.milliyet.com.tr/zeynep-bastik-bir-daha-ile-neyi-kanitladi--molatik-16349/|title=Bekir Saçar|work=Milliyet|first=Mayk|last=Şişman|date=21 July 2020|accessdate=4 March 2021}}</ref> He also performed the songs "Hepsi Geçiyor", "Küskün" and "Bence de Zor", the lyrics and musics of which belong to himself and Çağrı Telkıvıran.<ref>https://www.muzikonair.com/oguzhan-koctan-yeni-tekli-bence-de-zor/</ref><ref name="auto"/><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.cnnturk.com/magazin/oguzhan-koc-2021-icin-dua-ediyoruz|title=Oğuzhan Koç: 2021 için dua ediyoruz|website=CNN Türk}}</ref> He is reported to be preparing songs for singers [[Bengü]] and Murat Dalkılıç which are set to be released in 2021.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.posta.com.tr/yazarlar/bekir-sacar/heranin-bomba-sarkisi-haftaya-cikiyor-2283437|title=Hera'nın bomba şarkısı haftaya çıkıyor|first1=Bekir|last1=Saçar|website=Posta}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://muzikonair.com/bengu-mujdeyi-verdi-oguzhan-koc-sarkisiyla-gelecek/|title=Bengü Müjdeyi Verdi! Oğuzhan Koç Şarkısıyla Gelecek -|date=October 26, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/kelebek/hurriyet-cumartesi/oguzhan-koc-zaman-gercekten-en-buyuk-acilari-bile-gulerek-anlatma-firsati-veriyor-41733883|title=Oğuzhan Koç: Zaman gerçekten en büyük acıları bile gülerek anlatma fırsatı veriyor|website=www.hurriyet.com.tr}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sabah.com.tr/cumartesi/2020/05/23/istemezsen-soz-butun-kayitlari-sileriz|title=İstemezsen söz bütün kayıtları sileriz|website=Sabah}}</ref> For [[İlginç Bazı Olaylar]] series, İbrahim Büyükak performed the song "Nazlı'ya Veda Bestesi" the lyrics of which belong to İbrahim Büyükak and music of which belong to himself.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.habernediyor.com/kultur-sanat/oguzhan-kocun-kesfi-ibrahim-basaran-uyuyamam-sarkisiyla-dinleyicilerle-h56503.html|title = Oğuzhan Koç'un keşfi İbrahim Başaran, Uyuyamam şarkısıyla dinleyicilerle!}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/kelebek/hurriyet-cumartesi/ibrahim-buyukak-okulun-en-populer-ve-yakisikli-cocuguydum-41790406|title = İbrahim Büyükak: Okulun en popüler ve yakışıklı çocuğuydum}}</ref> He performed duet "Gözlerden Irak", the lyrics and music of which belong to [[İbrahim Başaran]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://muzikonair.com/oguzhan-koctan-yeni-single-surprizi/|title = Oğuzhan Koç'tan Yeni Single Sürprizi -|date = 8 July 2021}}</ref>
