'''Shade 45''' is American rapper [[Eminem]]'s [[hip hop]] music station on [[Sirius XM Satellite Radio|Sirius XM Radio]] channel 45 and [[Dish Network]] channel 6045.

==History==
Eminem established his own channel, Shade 45, that plays uncut [[hip-hop music|hip hop]].<ref>{{cite web|last=Cook|first=Zachary|title=Shade 45-Eminem's Hip-Hop Channel XL|url=http://www.siriusxm.com/shade45|website=Siriusxm.com|publisher=Sirius XM Radio|access-date=20 September 2017}}</ref> Eminem also established a new morning show, ''Sway in the Morning'' with Sway Calloway, a lively morning show that airs at 8:00&nbsp;a.m., Mondayâ€“Friday.<ref>{{cite web|last=Markman|first=Robert|title=EMINEM CALLS SWAY'S FIRST SHADE 45 RADIO SHOW|url=http://www.mtv.com/news/1667443/eminem-calls-sway-in-the-morning/|website=MTV.com|publisher=Celebrity|access-date=18 July 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Gordon|first=Dexter|title=A Complete History of Eminem's Nike Collaborations|url=http://www.complex.com/sneakers/2013/11/a-complete-history-of-eminems-nike-collaborations/|website=Complex.com|publisher=Complex|access-date=8 November 2013}}</ref>

Eminem promoted the station in a 2004 mock national convention (the "Shady National Convention") at the [[Roseland Ballroom]] in New York City,<ref>{{cite web |title=Eminem Confirms Appearance at Shady National Convention |url=http://www.rapbasement.com/eminem/eminem-confirms-appearance-at-shady-national-convention.html |website=Rap Basement |access-date=December 5, 2019 |date=October 15, 2004}}</ref> in which [[Donald Trump]] endorsed him.<ref>{{cite web |last=Bruner |first=Raisa |title=Donald Trump and Eminem Weren't Always Enemies. Here's Proof |url=https://time.com/4978126/trump-eminem-endorsement/ |website=Time |access-date=June 24, 2019 |date=October 11, 2017}}</ref> On his album ''[[Revival (Eminem album)|Revival]]'' (2017), Eminem expressed his regret at having collaborated with Trump, rapping, "wish I would have spit on it before I went to shake his hand at the event".<ref>{{cite web |last=Saponara |first=Michael |title=Eminem's 8 Most Political Lyrics on 'Revival' |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/8070698/eminem-revivial-trump-political-lyrics |website=Billboard |access-date=June 24, 2019 |date=December 15, 2017}}</ref>
