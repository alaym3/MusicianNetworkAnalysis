'''Gramoz Aliu''' ({{IPA-sq|ɡɾaˈmɔz aˈli.u|lang}}; born 29 September 1995), known professionally as '''Mozzik''', is a Kosovo-Albanian rapper, singer and songwriter. Born and raised in [[Ferizaj]], Aliu began to make music at an early age before pursuing a professional career as a rapper. He rose to significant attention in the [[Albanian language|Albanian-speaking]] [[Balkans]] after signing contract with On Records.<ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Top Channel]] |title='On Records' kërkon përjashtimin e këngëve të Mozzik dhe Getinjo nga 'The Top List' |url=http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/on-records-kerkon-perjashtimin-e-kengeve-te-mozzik-dhe-getinjo-nga-the-top-list/ |accessdate=8 December 2019 |language=Albanian |date=10 January 2018 |quote=Prej shumë vitesh, dy reperat e njohur, Getinjo dhe Mozzik, kanë patur një bashkëpunim të fuqishëm me shtëpinë diskografike “On Records”, por gjithçka ka marrë fund ditën e sotme.}}</ref> Aliu later earned popularity in [[German language|German-speaking Europe]], as he collaborated with his wife [[Loredana Zefi|Loredana]]. Their collaborations achieved commercial success in Austria, Germany and Switzerland, and topped the charts in the aforementioned countries.<ref name="Bonnie und Clyde">{{cite web |title=LOREDANA & MOZZIK – BONNIE & CLYDE – SINGLE – GOLD |url=https://ifpi.at/auszeichnungen/loredana-mozzik-bonnie-clyde-single-gold/ |website=ifpi.at |accessdate=8 December 2019 |language=German |date=11 September 2019}}</ref><ref name="Bonnie und Clyde und Eiskalt Gold">{{cite web |title=Zweimal Gold für Loredana von Groove Attack und Believe |url=http://beta.musikwoche.de/details/443773 |website=beta.musikwoche.de |accessdate=8 December 2019 |language=German |date=17 September 2019}}</ref> Due to his success, he signed contract with [[Sony Music|Sony Music Germany]] and announced his debut studio album to be released in 2020.<ref>{{cite web |title=Mozzik me album në vitin 2020 |url=https://rapshqip.com/lajme/mozzik-me-album-ne-vitin-2020/ |website=rapshqip.com |accessdate=10 December 2019 |language=Albanian |date=12 November 2019}}</ref>

== Life and career ==

Gramoz Aliu was born on 29 September 1995 into an Albanian family in the city of [[Ferizaj]], then part of the [[Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia]], present-day [[Kosovo]].<ref name="Biography">{{cite web |publisher=TeksteShqip |title=Mozzik{{spaced ndash}}Biografia |url=https://www.teksteshqip.com/mozzik/biografia |access-date=25 October 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210117065754/https://www.teksteshqip.com/mozzik/biografia |archive-date=17 January 2021 |language=sq |url-status=live}}</ref><ref name="Bio">{{cite web |publisher=[[Top Channel]] |title=Mozzik #Bio |url=http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/artist/mozzik/ |access-date=25 October 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211025210747/http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/artist/mozzik/ |archive-date=25 October 2021 |language=sq |url-status=live}}</ref> He entered a relationship with [[Albanians in Switzerland|Swiss-Albanian]] rapper [[Loredana Zefi]] and married her in 2018.<ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Blick]] |title=Loredana bestätigt Trennung von Mozzik |url=https://www.blick.ch/people-tv/schweiz/loredana-bestaetigt-trennung-von-mozzik-ab-jetzt-bin-ich-eine-alleinerziehende-mum-id15560865.html |accessdate=3 January 2020 |language=German |date=11 October 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Das große Geheimnis von Loredana und ihrem Freund! |url=http://www.deinupdate.de/?p=22353 |website=deinupdate.de |accessdate=8 December 2019 |language=German |date=28 September 2018}}</ref> Loredana gave birth to the couple's first daughter Hana on 18 December 2018 in the city of [[Lucerne]] in [[Switzerland]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Mozzik dhe Loredana bëhen prindër për herë të parë, vjen në jetë Hana |url=https://telegrafi.com/mozzik-dhe-loredana-behen-prinder-per-te-pare-vjen-ne-jete-hana/ |website=telegrafi.com |accessdate=8 December 2019 |language=Albanian |date=18 December 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Ganz heimlich: Rapperin Loredana ist Mama geworden! |url=https://www.promiflash.de/news/2018/12/20/ganz-heimlich-rapperin-loredana-ist-mama-geworden.html |website=promiflash.de |accessdate=8 December 2019 |language=German |date=20 December 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=“BONNIE & CLYDE”-RAPPERIN LOREDANA IST MUTTER GEWORDEN |url=https://www.bigfm.de/news/28017/bonnie-clyde-rapperin-loredana-ist-mutter-geworden |website=bigfm.de |accessdate=8 December 2019 |language=German |date=19 December 2019}}</ref> The couple separated in October 2019.<ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Blick]] |title=Loredana bestätigt Trennung von Mozzik |url=https://www.blick.ch/people-tv/schweiz/loredana-bestaetigt-trennung-von-mozzik-ab-jetzt-bin-ich-eine-alleinerziehende-mum-id15560865.html |website=blick.ch |accessdate=3 January 2020 |language=German |date=11 October 2019}}</ref>

In January 2020, Mozzik announced an upcoming single in collaboration with Albanian rapper [[Tayna]] during a [[social media]] post.<ref>{{cite web |title=Mozzik dhe Tayna flasin lidhur me bashkëpunimin e tyre: Do të jetë hit |url=https://lajmi.net/mozzik-dhe-tayna-flasin-lidhur-me-bashkepunimin-e-tyre-do-te-jete-hit/ |website=lajmi.net |accessdate=29 January 2020 |language=Albanian |date=17 January 2020}}</ref> The aforementioned single, "[[Edhe ti]]", was premiered in the same month and entered the music charts in [[The Top List|Albania]], [[GfK Entertainment charts|Germany]] and [[Swiss Hitparade|Switzerland]].<ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Koha Ditore|Koha]] |title=Tayna e Mozziku publikojnë këngën “Edhe ti” |url=https://www.koha.net/showbiz/205024/tayna-e-mozziku-publikojne-kengen-edhe-ti/ |website=koha.net |accessdate=17 February 2020 |language=Albanian |date=22 January 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Klan Kosova|Privé]] |title=Publikohet dueti tejet i komentuar: Tayna ft Mozzik! |url=https://prive.al/publikohet-dueti-tejet-i-komentuar-tayna-ft-mozzik/ |website=prive.al |accessdate=17 February 2020 |language=Albanian |date=22 January 2020}}</ref> In February 2020, he released his follow-up singles "Auf Widersehen" and "Tom & Jerry", the first marking his first single entirely written in the [[German language]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Mozzik godet me projektin e radhës, “Auf Wiedersehen” |url=https://telegrafi.com/mozzik-godet-projektin-e-radhes-auf-wiedersehen/ |website=telegrafi.com |accessdate=17 February 2020 |language=Albanian |date=14 February 2020}}</ref>
