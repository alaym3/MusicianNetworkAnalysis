'''Zay Hilfigerrr & Zayion McCall''' are an American [[hip hop]] duo from [[Detroit]], Michigan.

Hilfigerrr (born '''Tyjuan Peoples''') began his career on [[social media]] platforms, making videos of raps, dances, and comedic skits.<ref name=allmusic>[https://www.allmusic.com/artist/zay-hilfigerrr-mn0003555783/biography Zay Hilfigerrr] at Allmusic</ref> His [[stage name]] is an homage to [[Tommy Hilfiger]]; "Zay" is a self-applied nickname.<ref name=rollingout/> Hilfigerrr was approached by Timothy McCall, a local film producer, whose son, '''James McCall''', is also a rapper. McCall, who took the alias Zayion, worked with Hilfigerrr to create the song "[[Juju on That Beat]]", whose backing track was based on [[Crime Mob]]'s "[[Knuck If You Buck]]".<ref name=allmusic/> The song went [[viral video|viral]] after members of the dancing group Fresh the Clowns began uploading videos of themselves dancing to it on social media.<ref name=allmusic/> [[Atlantic Records]] picked up the song for official distribution in 2016, and it eventually reached #5 on the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]]. By February 2017, the song had been streamed on [[YouTube]] over 150 million times, by which time Hilfigerrr was 16 years old.<ref name=rollingout>[https://rollingout.com/2017/02/28/zay-hilfigerrr-makes-juju-beat/ Zay Hilfigerrr makes you 'Juju on That Beat']. ''Rolling Out'', February 28, 2017.</ref> The song was also nominated for a [[Kids' Choice Award]] for Favorite Music Video.<ref name=rollingout/> In the version initially released by Hilfigerrr and McCall, the track was only 2:02 in length, one of the shortest songs to hit the ''Billboard'' Hot 100 in the past forty years. However, the Atlantic Records reissue was 2:23 in length.<ref>[https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/8029965/lil-pump-gucci-gang-shortest-hot-100-top-10-song Lil Pump's 'Gucci Gang' Is Shortest Hot 100 Top 10 by Length in 42 Years]. ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'', November 8, 2017.</ref>
