'''Zilo Networks Inc.''' was an entertainment and marketing [[television network]] aimed at college students and young adults. The New York City-based company was founded in 1999.  Zilo Networks the created of Zilo TV, a dorm room campus cable television network, which in Spring 2009 was sold to SirkTV.<ref name="one">[http://www.sirktv/zilo-networks www.sirktv.com/zilo-networks]{{dead link|date=July 2016 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}, SirkTV | Zilo Networks</ref> SirkTV is property of New York City-based film, television and video production company Sirk Productions, a New York City-based production company founded in 1997. Sirk produces films, including the documentaries ''[[Severe Clear]]'' and [[Anytown, USA (film)|''Anytown, USA'']], and television programs, such as ''The Inside Reel'', as well as provides multimedia services to corporate and non-profit clients.
