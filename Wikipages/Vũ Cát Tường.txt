'''Vũ Cát Tường''' (born October 2, 1992) is a Vietnamese singer-songwiter, dancer, record producer, television judge and businesswoman who was a runner-up on the [[The Voice of Vietnam (season 2)|season 2]] of [[The Voice of Vietnam]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://soha.vn/news-20130611052128705.htm|title='Hồ sơ' Vũ Cát Tường – cô gái gây bão tại The Voice 2013|last=VCCorp.vn|website=soha.vn}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://vnexpress.net/giai-tri/vu-thu-phuong-vo-oa-khi-chau-gai-doat-quan-quan-the-voice-2923974.html|title=Vũ Thu Phương vỡ òa khi cháu gái đoạt quán quân The Voice – VnExpress Giải Trí|last=VnExpress|website=vnexpress.net}}</ref> During the music career, she has received 6 nominations, won 2 [[Dedication Music Award]] and an inclusion on [[Forbes|Forbes Vietnam]]{{'s}} 2018 [[Forbes 30 Under 30|30 Under 30]] list, along with many other awards. She is the first Vietnamese artist have an album distributed by [[Universal Music Group]], and VCT is also the first Vietnamese artist having a bilingual album recorded in one of the most famous record studios in the world - United Recordings.

== Early life ==
Tường was born on October 2, 1992, at [[Long Xuyên]], [[An Giang]] Province, [[Vietnam]]. She studied at [[Ho Chi Minh City International University]] from 2010 to 2015 and received a bachelor's degree in Biomedical Engineering.

In 2012, Vũ Cát Tường received attention from [[The Voice of Vietnam|The Voice]] executive producer, Phương Uyên, after performing her self-penned song, "Đông". Phương Uyên bought the song for a contestant of [[The Voice of Vietnam|The Voice]] [[The Voice of Vietnam (season 1)|season 1]] to perform, and had Tường playing the piano during the performance.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://vietnamnet.vn/vn/giai-tri/su-that-ve-co-gai-gay-bao-the-voice-124797.html|title=Sự thật về cô gái gây bão The Voice|date=May 2019|access-date=June 20, 2019|last=vietnamnet.vn|website=vietnamnet.vn}}</ref>

== Life and career ==
=== 2013: The Voice of Vietnam and career beginnings ===
Vũ Cát Tường auditioned for [[The Voice of Vietnam]] [[The Voice of Vietnam (season 2)|season 2]], singing "Đông" in her blind audition. All four coaches, [[Đàm Vĩnh Hưng]], [[Mỹ Linh]], [[Quốc Trung]] and [[Hồng Nhung]] turned her chair for her and Tường eventually chose to join team [[Đàm Vĩnh Hưng]].

Vũ Cát Tường was pitted against fellow team Đàm Vĩnh Hưng member, Song Tú, who defeated her after singing "[[One Night Only (song)|One Night Only]]". Although not chosen as the winner, she received three "steals" from other coaches and opted for [[Hồng Nhung]]. In the Knockout round, Vũ Cát Tường sang "Cám ơn tình yêu tôi" by Phương Uyên and advanced to the Live shows.

In the semi-final round, coach Hồng Nhung gave her all 100% of her point, which advanced her to the finale without any public vote counted. In the live finale broadcast on December 15, 2013, she was declared the second runner-up of the second season, behind team [[Đàm Vĩnh Hưng]]'s Vũ Thảo My and team [[Mỹ Linh]]'s Nguyễn Hoàng Tôn.

===2014–2016: Release MV, Album Vol.1, The Voice Kids ===
{{POV section|date=June 2019}}

In 2014, Vũ Cát Tường released her first products created by herself: "Yêu xa" is a love story of two lovers who must be temporarily apart.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://vnexpress.net/giai-tri/vu-cat-tuong-lac-quan-tro-lai-voi-yeu-xa-3006982.html|title=Vũ Cát Tường lạc quan trở lại với 'Yêu xa'|last=VnExpress|website=vnexpress.net}}</ref> On pop background played by piano, the song has bright colors but still full of narrative feelings. This is the first single from Vũ Cát Tường's debut album, which marked her collaboration with musician Huy Tuấn, who is the music producer of the album. After the debut, this song received a lot of attention and reached the top of the music charts. Vũ Cát Tường also released this music video, the MV uses body language to create a poetic but daring multidimensional way of romance over geographical distances. At the end of 2014, Vũ Cát Tường released her first album [[Giải mã (album)|Giải mã (Decode)]] (December 23).<ref>{{cite web|url=http://baodatviet.vn/the-box/vu-cat-tuong-ra-mat-album-giai-ma-3221339/|title=Vũ Cát Tường ra mắt album Giải mã|last=Báo đất việt|website=baodatviet.vn}}</ref> The songs in this album were all composed by Vũ Cát Tường with her true nostalgia and emotions about life. After that, Vũ Cát Tường also produced an MV for a song in the album [[Giải mã (album)|Giải mã (Decode)]]: "Anh và Anh".

In 2015, Vũ Cát Tường released two singles that were "Góc đa hình" for fans in the summer and single "Phai" with a new [[remix]]. These two years also marked the moments when the name Vũ Cát Tường "bombarded" the awards, the rankings. These are the recognition for Tường's creation, the music products regarded by the audience and highly appreciated by the experts.

After 1 gap year, Vũ Cát Tường returned, the first shot in 2016 was the release of the "Mơ" single – one of the hits that built Vũ Cát Tường's music career.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://tuoitre.vn/vu-cat-tuong-tung-mv-mo-1032550.htm|title=Vũ Cát Tường tung MV Mơ lúc 0g|last=Tuổi trẻ online|website=tuoitre.vn}}</ref> "Mơ" is a soulful version of [[Soul music|Soul]] & [[Contemporary R&B|R&B]] music that had Vũ Cát Tường's hidden minds. The song still holds the style of Tường with the loosened melody and the romantic lyrics. The message that she wants to convey in this song is "the peaceful moments watching the moon on the terrace".

Vũ Cát Tường continued to release a new song "Don't you go".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://tuoitre.vn/vu-cat-tuong-tung-mv-don-t-you-go-1112356.htm|title=Vũ Cát Tường tung MV Don't you go|last=TUỔI TRẺ|website=tuoitre.vn}}</ref> This is a song from the [[Neo Soul]], [[Contemporary R&B|R&B]], [[Pop rock]] genre with the mix of Khắc Hưng. Don't you go has a jubilant melody full of life, different from Vũ Cát Tường's previous romantic rhythmic ballads before.

At the beginning of August, one of Vũ Cát Tường's songs named "Vết mưa" was accused of plagiarism from a Japanese pianist's music. This created a huge scandal and big arguments between different fan-bases as many anti-fans used this chance to voice their opinions online to bring her down. Vũ Cát Tường herself however, tayed out of all these fan-wars and staying level-headed despite all the harsh words and criticism sent her way from both the media and the anti-fans. Later on, Vũ Cát Tường contacted directly such Japanese artist and provided proof of her own demo for the song which was recorded much earlier than the Japanese pianist's song in question. With unquestionable evidence, the Japanese artist announced officially on her Facebook that Vũ Cát Tường's song "Vết mưa" is not related to her song ending the scandals and criticisms of the online community. It is a very clear, direct and straightforward, admirable solution of Tường.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://vnexpress.net/giai-tri/nhac-si-nhat-xac-nhan-vu-cat-tuong-khong-dao-nhac-cua-co-3454122.html|title=Nhạc sĩ Nhật xác nhận Vũ Cát Tường không đạo nhạc của cô|last=VNEXPRESS|website=vnexpress.net}}</ref>

Later in August, Vũ Cát Tường released a new single – "Góc ban công".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://vnexpress.net/giai-tri/vu-cat-tuong-nho-nguoi-yeu-o-goc-ban-cong-3459439.html|title=Vũ Cát Tường nhớ người yêu ở 'Góc ban công'|last=VNEXPRESS|website=vnexpress.net}}</ref>

Beside releasing new products in the year, Vũ Cát Tường accepted to join [[The Voice Kids of Vietnam (season 4)|The Voice Kids]] 2016 with the role of a trainer.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://vtv.vn/truyen-hinh/vu-cat-tuong-xac-nhan-ngoi-ghe-nong-giong-hat-viet-nhi-2016-2016060619494078.htm|title=Vũ Cát Tường xác nhận ngồi ghế nóng Giọng hát Việt nhí 2016|website=vtv.vn}}</ref> Đào Nguyên Thụy Bình – representing the team Vũ Cát Tường entered the final round, won the position of the runner-up of [[The Voice Kids of Vietnam]] 2016. Immediately after [[The Voice Kids of Vietnam (season 4)|The Voice Kids]] 2016 closed, Vũ Cát Tường released a new single called "Tôi" duet with Đào Nguyên Thụy Bình.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://news.zing.vn/vu-cat-tuong-tung-single-moi-voi-tro-cung-thuy-binh-post694155.html|title=Vũ Cát Tường tung single mới với trò cưng Thuỵ Bình|last=ZING|website=Zing.vn}}</ref>

Single "Ngày hôm qua" – The last shot of the year and the opening of Vũ Cát Tường's new musical journey. The MV was released in early 2017. Her composition took [[Funk]] with [[Contemporary R&B|R&B]] as the main theme, the harmony was mixed from the [[electronic music|electronic]] sound played on [[acoustic music|acoustic]] background.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://news.zing.vn/vu-cat-tuong-dien-xuat-cung-hot-girl-lai-trong-mv-moi-post711192.html|title=Vũ Cát Tường diễn xuất cùng hot girl lai trong MV mới|last=ZING|website=Zing.vn}}</ref>

===2017: Igniting and shining===
{{POV section|date=June 2019}}

On the occasion of [[Valentine's Day]], Vũ Cát Tường officially released a new song titled "Em ơi".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://soha.vn/vu-cat-tuong-lay-chuyen-tinh-cua-chinh-minh-lam-cam-hung-sang-tac-20170210085222848.htm|title=Vũ Cát Tường lấy chuyện tình của chính mình làm cảm hứng sáng tác|last=SOHA|website=soha.vn}}</ref> Especially, the song also has the contribution of Hakoota Dũng Hà, Tường's friend from [[The Voice of Vietnam|The Voice]] [[The Voice of Vietnam (season 1)|season 1]].

After releasing the audio version of the song "Cô gái ngày hôm qua" in March, Vũ Cát Tường continued to make the music video for this song. This is the soundtrack for the movie project "Cô gái ngày hôm qua" adapted from the literary same-name work by writer [[Nguyễn Nhật Ánh]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://news.zing.vn/vu-cat-tuong-ra-mat-mv-nhac-phim-co-gai-den-tu-hom-qua-post755304.html|title=Vũ Cát Tường ra mắt MV nhạc phim 'Cô gái đến từ hôm qua'|last=ZING|website=Zing.vn}}</ref>

At the end of July, Vũ Cát Tường produced a new music product called "Vài phút trước" collaborating with musician [[Nguyễn Thanh Bình]] to mix with a gentle and attractive [[R&B]] & [[Soul music|Soul]] melody. Especially in new products, Tường firstly showed off her rapping skills, this was the first step Tường showed her personality in different music genres.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://2sao.vn/vu-cat-tuong-lan-dau-khoe-tai-ban-rap-trong-ca-khuc-moi-n-127834.html|title=Vũ Cát Tường lần đầu khoe tài bắn rap trong ca khúc mới|last=2SAO|website=2sao.vn}}</ref>

In mid-2017, Vũ Cát Tường confirmed the return of the "hot seat" of [[The Voice Kids of Vietnam (season 5)|The Voice Kids]] which promised that it would be a more surprising season than before.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://dantri.com.vn/giai-tri/vu-cat-tuong-quay-tro-lai-the-voice-kids-voi-vai-tro-hlv-mua-thu-2-20170612103416169.htm|title=Vũ Cát Tường quay trở lại The Voice Kids với vai trò HLV mùa thứ 2|last=DÂN TRÍ|website=dantri.com.vn}}</ref> Dương Ngọc Ánh – Vũ Cát Tường team has been the champion of [[The Voice Kids of Vietnam (season 5)|The Voice Kids]] 2017 in the burst of thousands fans.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://dantri.com.vn/giai-tri/adele-nhi-hoc-tro-vu-cat-tuong-dang-quang-the-voice-kids-2017-20171126070541924.htm|title="Adele nhí" – học trò Vũ Cát Tường đăng quang The Voice Kids 2017|last=DÂN TRÍ|website=dantri.com.vn}}</ref>

In parallel with the journey of [[The Voice Kids of Vietnam (season 5)|The Voice Kids]] 2017, Vũ Cát Tường still had her own projects: releasing single "Buổi sáng bình thường" with cute and simple animated MV.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://news.zing.vn/vu-cat-tuong-ke-chuyen-tinh-yeu-mua-va-nang-bang-mv-hoat-hinh-post776029.html|title=Vũ Cát Tường kể chuyện tình yêu mưa và nắng bằng MV hoạt hình|last=ZING|website=Zing.vn}}</ref> The MV conveys the message: Love is not such a great thing to find, it is no need of luxury to be happy, The most meaningful things when being together come from the most normal things.

On October 14, Vũ Cát Tường organized the first "Birthday Concert" with about 5,000 attendants celebrating the 25th anniversary as well as affirming the musical path with her own marks.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://soha.vn/vu-cat-tuong-to-chuc-dai-tiec-sinh-nhat-mung-tuoi-25-20171007115514371.htm|title=Vũ Cát Tường tổ chức đại tiệc sinh nhật mừng tuổi 25|last=SOHA|website=soha.vn}}</ref>

After successful "Birthday Concert", Vũ Cát Tường continued to release the last single of 2017 – "Come Back Home", which was also a special song, which was performed in Birthday Concert before being released official.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://news.zing.vn/vu-cat-tuong-thay-doi-hinh-anh-nu-tinh-trong-san-pham-am-nhac-moi-post803292.html|title=Vũ Cát Tường thay đổi hình ảnh nữ tính trong sản phẩm âm nhạc mới|website=Zing.vn}}</ref> The song has the confidences of lovers and there have been mad moments, missed words hurting each other. The end of the year project 2017 project worked relentlessly of Tường is to release her first perfume brand, entitled "Come Back Home". This is the first "shot" in the collection of perfume called "The Notes" by Vũ Cát Tường, introduced to the public, with this new and spirited child, Tường had the opportunity to collaborate and work together with the world's leading scent-producing company: [[Firmenich]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://thanhnien.vn/van-hoa/vu-cat-tuong-ra-mat-dong-nuoc-hoa-cua-rieng-minh-911062.html|title=Vũ Cát Tường ra mắt dòng nước hoa của riêng mình|last=THANH NIÊN|website=thanhnien.vn}}</ref>

On July 20, Vũ Cát Tường officially became the ambassador of [[Clavinova]] brand of [[Yamaha]] Music Vietnam.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://vn.yamaha.com/vi/artists/v/Vu_Cat_Tuong.html|title=Vũ Cát Tường|last=YAMAHA|website=vn.yamaha.com}}</ref>

One more year, Vũ Cát Tường confirmed to sit on the "hot seat" of [[The Voice Kids of Vietnam (season 6)|The Voice Kids]] 2018, but this time Tường is no longer alone because she had a companion, trainer Soobin Hoàng Sơn, creating Bin – Tường team – one of the three trainers team of [[The Voice Kids of Vietnam (season 6)|The Voice Kids]] 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://vietnammoi.vn/vu-cat-tuong-quay-tro-lai-lam-hlvgiong-hat-viet-nhi-2018-dieu-bi-mat-sap-bat-mi-119967.htm|title=Vũ Cát Tường quay trở lại làm HLV Giọng hát Việt nhí 2018: Điều bí mật sắp bật mí |last=VIỆT NAM MỚI|website=vietnammoi.vn}}</ref> The two young talents Xuân Phương and Anh Tuấn were excellent when they won two official tickets to enter the final round, gaining the title of runner-up of [[The Voice Kids of Vietnam (season 6)|The Voice Kids]] 2018.

At the end of September, Vũ Cát Tường confirmed attending the [[Asia Song Festival]] 2018 as a Vietnamese artist invited to perform in [[Busan]], [[South Korea]] on October 3.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://thanhnien.vn/van-hoa/vu-cat-tuong-gay-bao-khi-bieu-dien-tai-han-quoc-1010042.html|title=Vũ Cát Tường 'gây bão' khi biểu diễn tại Hàn Quốc|last=THANH NIÊN|website=thanhnien.vn}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://vnexpress.net/giai-tri/vu-cat-tuong-dai-dien-viet-nam-du-asia-song-festival-3803893.|title=Vũ Cát Tường đại diện Việt Nam dự Asia Song Festival|last=VNEXPRESS|website=vnexpress.net}}html</ref> [[Asia Song Festival]] is a continental music event and has been held annually in [[South Korea]] since 2004.

On October 2, right on her birthday, Vũ Cát Tường officially released a new song "Leader" – marking a big change in Tường's music. Changing the music style and images is also a way that Vũ Cát Tường wants to assert that her art has a great creativity. MV Leader was invested in the image entirely in [[South Korea]] from A-Z, from image advice for the spirit of the song to the idea of making the MV are carefully taken responsibility by the Korean people.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://vtc.vn/vu-cat-tuong-toi-tu-quyet-nhung-gi-minh-tin-tuong-d437846.html|title=Vũ Cát Tường: 'Tôi tự quyết những gì mình tin tưởng'|last=VTC|website=vtc.vn}}</ref>

On October 18, Vũ Cát Tường released her official Album Vol.2 "[[Stardom (album)|Stardom]]" with the concert to celebrate 5-year singer-songwriter and record producer career.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.elle.vn/sao-va-showbiz/vu-cat-tuong-ra-mat-album-stardom|title=Vũ Cát Tường ra mắt album mới, bật mí về liveshow kỷ niệm 5 năm ca hát|last=ELLE|website=Elle}}</ref> Album [[Stardom (album)|Stardom]] has new materials such as Pop, [[Contemporary R&B|R&B]], [[Neo Soul]], Blue, [[Hip hop music|Hip hop]], [[Rap]], ... and crystallizes Tường's music style in the last 5 years. The whole images of the album were taken by the Korean ekip, and Ms. Lưu Thiên Hương was the main co-producer. The album marked Vũ Cát Tường in music was 5 years old and Vũ Cát Tường in life has turned 26. The 10 songs clearly reflect the path that Tường chose, and her view of [[Creative work|work]], love, [[original]] and [[freedom]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://vtc.vn/sau-2-ngay-ra-mat-album-stardom-cua-vu-cat-tuong-len-so-1-cua-apple-music-d433479.html|title=Sau 2 ngày ra mắt, album 'Stardom' của Vũ Cát Tường lên số 1 của Apple Music|last=VTC|website=vtc.vn}}</ref>

On November 10, Vũ Cát Tường's Stardom Concert took place at Lan Anh stadium with more than 3,000 attendants and friends and colleagues of Tường as guests. Vũ Cát Tường performed with a [[hologram]] effect and her 20 performances consisted of 10 previous songs and all 10 songs in the new album.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://vtc.vn/concert-stardom-cua-vu-cat-tuong-khi-su-sang-tao-khong-gioi-han-di-cung-but-pha-d438359.html|title=Concert Stardom của Vũ Cát Tường: Khi sự sáng tạo không giới hạn đi cùng bứt phá|last=VTC|website=vtc.vn}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://vtc.vn/vu-cat-tuong-tong-duyet-8-tieng-dong-ho-lien-tuc-cho-concert-d438189.html|title=Vũ Cát Tường tổng duyệt 8 tiếng đồng hồ liên tục cho concert|last=VTC|website=vtc.vn}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://tuoitre.vn/vu-cat-tuong-toc-xanh-hat-live-cung-hang-ngan-khan-gia-20181111112553494.htm|title=Vũ Cát Tường tóc xanh, hát live cùng hàng ngàn khán giả|last=TUỔI TRẺ|website=tuoitre.vn}}</ref>

After the grand concert, Vũ Cát Tường was still full of energy and preparing for the album promotion abroad tour.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://vov.vn/van-hoa/am-nhac/vu-cat-tuong-la-ca-si-dau-tien-co-tour-quang-ba-album-tai-nuoc-ngoai-837905.vov|title=Vũ Cát Tường là ca sĩ đầu tiên có tour quảng bá album tại nước ngoài|last=VOV|website=vov.vn}}</ref> [[Universal Music Group]] and Tường accompanied in the promotion campaign in Singapore, performing in the famous Fashion Show Council of Asean Fashion Designer in [[Malaysia]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bandwagon.asia/articles/meet-vietnamese-artist-v%C5%A9-c%C3%A1t-t%C6%B0%E1%BB%9Dng,-the-pop-star-with-a-difference|title=Meet Vietnamese artist Vũ Cát Tường, the pop star with a difference|website=bandwagon.asia}}</ref>

Closing a meaningful 2018, Vũ Cát Tường released a Christmas commemorative song "Christmas Night". This song is a charity fundraiser for the end of the year, all the proceeds from selling this song's download on Tường's website will be donated to babies infected with [[HIV/AIDS]] at Mai Tâm Shelter.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://vtc.vn/vu-cat-tuong-ra-mat-ca-khuc-moi-theo-cach-rieng-biet-d445459.html|title=Vũ Cát Tường ra mắt ca khúc mới theo cách riêng biệt|last=VTC|website=vtc.vn}}</ref>

On June 1, 2019: Vũ Cát Tường officially announced the "[[VCT Tour 2019]]".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://vtc.vn/fan-hao-huc-khi-vu-cat-tuong-tai-xuat-up-mo-tour-dien-hoanh-trang-d478106.html|title=Fan háo hức khi Vũ Cát Tường tái xuất, úp mở tour diễn hoành tráng|last=VTC News|website=vtc.vn}}</ref> After that, Tường revealed the first place to host "VCT Tour 2019" is [[Hanoi]] with a concert titled "Dear Hanoi,".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://saostar.vn/am-nhac/vu-cat-tuong-xac-nhan-ha-noi-la-diem-den-dau-tien-cua-vct-tour-2019-5415623.html|title=#VCTTour2019 – Vũ Cát Tường xác nhận Hà Nội sẽ là điểm đến đầu tiên!|last=Saostar.vn|website=saotar.vn}}</ref>

===2020: During the COVID-19 pandemic===
On April 2, 2020, in the middle of the [[COVID-19 pandemic]] and salt-drought in the west of [[Vietnam]] has affected to the community, Vũ Cát Tường has announced about the Tomorrow Project - a single release and fund-raising to help the community. The project lasted for 2 weeks and collected the amount of 200 million VND (~US$8000).

On November 5, Vu Cat Tuong released the lead single of her new [[Extended play|EP]] - "[[Hành Tinh Ánh Sáng]]" (Planet of light). Then on November 18, her first [[Extended play|EP]] "Một Triệu Năm Ánh Sáng" (One Million Light Years) released in both [[Music download|digital]] and physical versions. Before the EP was released, it was on the No.1 of the most pre-ordered albums on Apple Music Vietnam and the physical merchandise combo has also sold out in 48 hours after on sale.
