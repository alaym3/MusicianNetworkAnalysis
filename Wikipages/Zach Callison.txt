'''Zachry Callison''' (born October 23, 1997) is an American actor and singer. He is known as the voice of [[Steven Universe (character)|Steven Universe]], the [[title character]] of [[Cartoon Network]]'s ''[[Steven Universe]]'' and its follow-ups ''[[Steven Universe: The Movie]]'' and ''[[Steven Universe Future]]''.

==Career==
Callison began acting on television in 2009, with a recurring role on ''[[Diary of a Single Mom]]''. For the next few years he had a variety of one-off television roles. In 2013, he was cast in the supporting voice role of Prince James on ''[[Sofia the First]]'', and in the lead voice role of Steven Universe. He is also known for his role as Billy from ''[[I'm in the Band]]'' and additional voices from ''[[Kinect Disneyland Adventures]]''. He voiced King Tut in ''[[Mr. Peabody & Sherman]]'',<ref name="http://www.com">{{cite web| url = http://www.comingsoon.net/news/movienews.php?id=112739 | title = Mr. Peabody & Sherman Share Christmas Tidings on a New Poster - ComingSoon.net | date = 20 December 2013 | access-date = 2014-01-28 }}</ref> Young Jiro in ''[[The Wind Rises]]'',<ref name="http://www.gotham-news.com">{{cite web| url = http://www.gotham-news.com/news/2013/12/18/joseph-gordon-levitt-emily-blunt-stanley-tucci-werner-herzog-join-the-english-langua | title = Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Emily Blunt, Stanley Tucci & Werner Herzog Lead the English-Language Voice Cast of THE WIND RISES - Gotham News | access-date = 2014-01-28 }}</ref> and [[Billy Batson]] in the direct-to-video movies ''[[Superman/Shazam!: The Return of Black Adam]]''<ref>{{Cite web|last=Harvey|first=James|url=http://www.worldsfinestonline.com/2010/10/main-cast-crew-details-for-supermanshazam-the-return-of-black-adam/|title=Main Cast, Crew Details For "Superman/Shazam: The Return of Black Adam"|publisher=worldsfinestonline.com|date=October 29, 2010|access-date=March 2, 2014}}</ref> and ''[[Justice League: War]]''.<ref name="http://www.galacticnewsone.com">{{cite web| url = http://www.galacticnewsone.com/2013/10/justice-league-war-animation-and-first.html | title = Justice League: War Animation And First Look ~ Galactic News One - Home Of The Latest Entertainment News | access-date = 2014-01-28 }}</ref>

In January 2018, Callison released his debut single, "War!", followed by the singles "Curtain Call" and "She Don't Know". In August 2018, he released his debut EP, ''A Picture Perfect Hollywood Heartbreak''. The EP is a concept album about celebrity life and mental illness revolving around the protagonist's breakup with a mysterious woman named Juanita, as well as his thoughts of suicide and substance abuse.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://themusicismyradar.wordpress.com/2018/07/23/zach-callison-a-picture-perfect-hollywood-heartbreak-ep-preview/|title = Zach Callison: A Picture Perfect Hollywood Heartbreak EP Preview|date = 23 July 2018}}</ref> Callison detailed his inspiration behind the album, saying "It's also a commentary on a lot of young actors and artists who have lost the battle; there's been a lot of dead 20 and 21-year-olds in the industry this year, some of whom I knew or admired. I don't know how we fix the problem, but I needed to say something."<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://rockyourlyrics.com/a-picture-perfect-hollywood-heartbreak-interview-with-zach-callison/v|title=Vagrants Sign to Equal Vision Records in Partnership with Rude Records &#124; Watch "Clarity" Now &#124; New Music Coming in 2019|date=31 December 2018}}</ref>
Also in 2018, he starred in singer/songwriter [[Nathan Sharp]]'s music video for the song "Phantom" playing the role of a magician who learned the wrong spells.

== Personal life ==
Callison can speak [[Italian language|Italian]].{{Citation needed|date=October 2020}} He is also a singer and can play the piano. Callison is a youth representative for the [[Sea Shepherd Conservation Society]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://theezshow.com/whats-next/the-ez-show-with-zack-callison-ez-way-angels/#sthash.u1esFBom.WvwFwQch.dpbs|title=The EZ Show with Zach Callison & EZ WAY ANGELS & EZ TALK LIVE VALENTINES DAY SPECIAL|work=The EZ Show|access-date=2016-11-20}}</ref> Callison frequents anime and cartoon conventions as a guest, such as [[Florida Supercon]] and [[Holiday Matsuri]].

== Awards and nominations ==
{{BLP unsourced section|date=August 2018}}
Callison was nominated for "Best Performance in a Voice-Over Role (Television) - Young Actor" category in the [[34th Young Artist Awards#Best Performance in a Voice-Over Role|34th Young Artist Awards]] which he shared with Jake Sim. He was also nominated in the "Best Performance in a Feature Film - Supporting Young Actor" for his role in "Rock Jocks". In ''[[33rd Young Artist Awards|2012]]'', he was nominated in the "Best Performance in a TV Series - Guest Starring Young Actor 11-13" for his role in Disney's ''[[I'm in the Band]]''.
