'''Victor Leksell''' (born 4 April 1997) is a Swedish singer from [[Torslanda]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ratsit.se/19970404-Lars_Victor_Andreas_Leksell_Torslanda/omMR6S9gqtKhjfKJ_ZPX5K4yTb3TYKi10zV4iMKeTEA|title=Victor Leksell (21 år) Torslanda - Ratsit|website=ratsit.se|access-date=6 December 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181207050138/https://www.ratsit.se/19970404-Lars_Victor_Andreas_Leksell_Torslanda/omMR6S9gqtKhjfKJ_ZPX5K4yTb3TYKi10zV4iMKeTEA|archive-date=7 December 2018|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.jp.se/article/viktor-leksell-framkallade-stort-jubel-sa-harlig-som-manniska/|title=Viktor Leksell framkallade stort jubel: "Så härlig som människa"|last=Cato|first=Julia Löfquist, Lejla|date=25 August 2018|website=JP|language=sv|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181207145645/https://www.jp.se/article/viktor-leksell-framkallade-stort-jubel-sa-harlig-som-manniska/|archive-date=7 December 2018|access-date=6 December 2018}}</ref> Since his first releases in 2018, his most successful single was released in 2020, called "[[Svag]]". The song peaked at number one on the Swedish Singles Chart and it was also certified nonuple platinum in the country. On 17 June 2020, Leksell released his debut [[Album|studio album]], ''[[Fånga mig när jag faller]]''. It peaked at number 1 in Sweden and made it to number 3 in Norway.

== Career ==
Leksell participated in ''[[Idol 2017 (Sweden)|Idol 2017]]'' where he made it to the Top 21 and qualifying week before being voted off.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.expressen.se/noje/har-ar-alla-som-tavlar-i-fredagsfinalerna-i-idol-/|title=De gick vidare i kvalfinalen i "Idol 2017"|website=Expressen|language=sv|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181207102835/https://www.expressen.se/noje/har-ar-alla-som-tavlar-i-fredagsfinalerna-i-idol-/|archive-date=7 December 2018|access-date=6 December 2018}}</ref> In 2018, he released the song "Tappat" which received over 5 million streams on [[Spotify]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.tv4.se/nyhetsmorgon/klipp/victor-leksell-spelar-superhiten-tappat-11927523|title=Victor Leksell spelar superhiten: "Tappat" - Nyhetsmorgon - tv4.se|website=TV4.se|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181207045943/https://www.tv4.se/nyhetsmorgon/klipp/victor-leksell-spelar-superhiten-tappat-11927523|archive-date=7 December 2018|access-date=6 December 2018}}</ref> As of February 2020, the song has amassed over 25 million streams.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://open.spotify.com/artist/6RJXSfbIkEMoCJ8GAg2dVO?si=eTrNUMIpTBS0cBZDYg0sFQ|title=Victor Leksell|website=[[Spotify]]|access-date=28 February 2020}}</ref> His fifth single "[[Svag]]" peaked at number one on the Swedish Singles Chart, thus becoming Leksell's first single to reach that position.<ref name=":0">{{cite web|url=https://www.sverigetopplistan.se/chart/41?dspy=2020&dspp=8|title=Veckolista Singlar, vecka 8|publisher=Sverigetopplistan|access-date=28 January 2020}}</ref> He released the song a few weeks after performing it at [[Musikhjälpen]] in 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.musikindustrin.se/2020/01/26/victor-leksell-allt-annat-an-svag/|title=Victor Leksell allt annat än Svag|last=Nylin|first=Lars|date=26 January 2020|website=Musikindustrin|language=sv|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200128160027/http://www.musikindustrin.se/2020/01/26/victor-leksell-allt-annat-an-svag/|archive-date=28 January 2020|access-date=1 April 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.svtplay.se/video/24800966/musikhjalpen/musikhjalpen-2019-inslag-och-musik-victor-leksell-svag|title=Musikhjälpen - Victor Leksell - Svag|website=[[Sveriges Television|SVT]]|access-date=1 April 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200302014347/https://www.svtplay.se/video/24800966/musikhjalpen/musikhjalpen-2019-inslag-och-musik-victor-leksell-svag|archive-date=2 March 2020|url-status=live}}</ref> The song held the number one spot for seven consecutive weeks, from week four to week eleven, being replaced by [[the Weeknd]]{{'}}s "[[Blinding Lights]]" in the latter week. However, "Svag" returned to number one on the chart in week 12 and stayed there for one week.<ref name=":1" /> On 3 May 2020, the song also placed at number one on [[Sveriges Radio]]{{'}}s chart [[Svensktoppen]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Svensktoppen 2020-05-03|url=https://sverigesradio.se/sida/topplista.aspx?programid=2023&date=2020-05-03|website=[[Sveriges Radio]]|access-date=4 May 2020}}</ref> The song was subsequently certified nonuple platinum in Sweden.<ref name="CERT1"/> It also peaked at number one on the [[VG-lista|Norwegian chart]].<ref name="Svag Norway">{{cite web|url=http://www.vglista.no/topplister/topp-20-single-2020-17/|title=VG-lista – Topp 20 Single uke 17, 2020|publisher=[[VG-lista]]|access-date=24 April 2020}}</ref> He released his sixth single in March 2020, "Fantasi", which peaked at number seven on the Swedish Singles Chart.<ref name=":1">{{cite web|url=https://www.sverigetopplistan.se/chart/41?dspy=2020&dspp=12|title=Veckolista Singlar, vecka 12|publisher=Sverigetopplistan|access-date=21 March 2020}}</ref><ref name=":3" />

On 10 April 2020, Leksell, [[Molly Sandén]] and [[Joakim Berg]] released a cover of [[Kent (band)|Kent]]{{'}}s song "Sverige" and all of its revenue will go to [[Radiohjälpen]]'s fundraising for the [[COVID-19]] risk groups in Sweden.<ref name="MND" /> The song peaked at number 11 on the Swedish Singles Chart.<ref name="Sverige"/> Leksell subsequently performed at the [[Sveriges Television|SVT]] live-show ''En kväll tillsammans'' on 11 April 2020, in support of the fundraising.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.svt.se/kultur/the-ark-och-seinabo-sey-spelar-live-i-svt-s-coronagala|title=The Ark och Seinabo Sey uppträder på SVT:s stödgala i coronakrisen|last=da Silva|first=Tali|date=9 April 2020|website=SVT Nyheter|language=sv|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200411231245/https://www.svt.se/kultur/the-ark-och-seinabo-sey-spelar-live-i-svt-s-coronagala|archive-date=11 April 2020|access-date=28 April 2020}}</ref> In May 2020, Leksell performed the song "Svag" together with Norwegian singer [[Astrid S]] at the border between Norway and Sweden.<ref>{{Cite web|date=29 May 2020|title=ASTRID S OCH VICTOR LEKSELL MÖTS PÅ GRÄNSEN FRAMFÖR LÅTEN SVAG TILLSAMMANS|url=https://tjock.se/sinnet/astrid-s-och-victor-leksell-mots-pa-gransen/411754/|access-date=2 June 2020|website=Tjock|language=sv}}</ref>

On 17 June 2020, Leksell released his debut [[Album|studio album]], titled ''[[Fånga mig när jag faller]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|date=17 June 2020|title=Victor Leksell släpper idag efterlängtat debutalbum - 'Fånga mig när jag faller'|url=https://www.mynewsdesk.com/se/sonybmg/pressreleases/victor-leksell-slaepper-idag-efterlaengtat-debutalbum-faanga-mig-naer-jag-faller-3013934|access-date=17 June 2020|website=MyNewsDesk|language=sv}}</ref> The album contains the previously released singles "Vart du sover", "Tappat", "Allt för mig", "Klär av dig", "Svag" and "Fantasi".<ref name="Spotify"/> The album debuted at number one on the Swedish Albums Chart.

== Awards and nominations ==
In the summer of 2019, Leksell was nominated in three categories at the Swedish magazine ''[[Aftonbladet]]''{{'}}s competition "[[Rockbjörnen]]". Subsequently, he won his first "Rockbjörn" on 13 August 2019 in the category "Årets genombrott".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/musik/rockbjornen/a/zGkWew/victor-leksell-ar-arets-genombrott|title=Victor Leksell är Årets genombrott|last=Hartelius|first=Alex|last2=Zelaya|first2=Maria-Elena|date=13 August 2019|website=[[Aftonbladet]]|language=sv|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191217023153/https://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/musik/rockbjornen/a/zGkWew/victor-leksell-ar-arets-genombrott|archive-date=17 December 2019|access-date=29 February 2020}}</ref> Leksell was also nominated for "Årets nykomling" at the Swedish "Grammisgalan" in 2020.<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|url=https://www.bohuslaningen.se/kultur-n%C3%B6je/victor-leksell-klar-f%C3%B6r-sommarens-amaze-1.24645697|title=Victor Leksell klar för sommarens Amaze|last=Kirudd|first=Sara|date=28 February 2020|website=Bohusläningen|language=sv|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200229112549/https://www.bohuslaningen.se/kultur-n%C3%B6je/victor-leksell-klar-f%C3%B6r-sommarens-amaze-1.24645697|archive-date=29 February 2020|access-date=29 February 2020}}</ref>

== Personal life ==
Leksell grew up in [[Torslanda]]; as of March 2021, he lives in an apartment in [[Gothenburg]] with his girlfriend, Lisa.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.gp.se/kultur/musik/victor-leksell-i-g%C3%B6teborg-vet-ingen-vem-jag-%C3%A4r-1.43616504|title=Victor Leksell: "I Göteborg vet ingen vem jag är"|language=sv|trans-title=Victor Leksell: "In Gothenburg, no one knows who I am"|date=28 March 2021|first=Fanny|last=Wijk|work=[[Göteborgs-Posten]]|access-date=1 May 2021}}</ref>
