'''Edoardo D'Erme''' (born 19 April 1989 in [[Latina, Lazio]]), better known as '''Calcutta''', is an Italian singer-songwriter. 
He debuted with the album ''Forse…'', released by Geograph Records in 2012.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ondarock.it/recensioni/2012_calcutta_forse.htm|work=Onda Rock|language=it|title=Calcutta - Forse…|author=Claudio Lancia, Giuseppe Arduini|date=24 March 2013}}</ref>
Its follow-up, ''Mainstream'', was released by Bomba Dischi on 30 November 2015, preceded by the single "Cosa mi manchi a fare".<ref name="Rockol 2020">{{cite web|url=https://www.rockol.it/news-718425/calcutta-mainstream-5-anni-intervista-produttrice-marta-venturini|publisher=Rockol.it|language=it|title=Calcutta, Mainstream 5 anni dopo: dentro il disco che ha rivoluzionato il pop italiano|author=Mattia Marzi|date=30 November 2020|accessdate=23 January 2021}}</ref> Initially ignored by Italian media, the album started to achieve success after a few months, when its lead single received airplay by Italian radio networks.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rockol.it/recensioni-musicali/album/6925/calcutta-mainstream|publisher=Rockol.it|language=it|title=Il fenomeno Calcutta e la fine della musica italiana: la recensione di Mainstream +|author=Mattia Marzi|date=12 February 2017|accessdate=23 January 2021}}</ref>
The song "Oroscopo", produced by Takagi & Ketra, was released as a stand-alone single in May 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rockit.it/news/calcutta-oroscopo-rullo-tamburo-danza-kuduro|publisher=Rockit.it|language=it|accessdate=23 January 2021|title=Ascolta Oroscopo, la nuova canzone di Calcutta|date=12 May 2016}}</ref> Since its release, Calcutta considered the song as something which did not represent himself; despite this, it became his first commercial success and his first single to be certified gold by the [[Federation of the Italian Music Industry]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.it/musica/interviste-musica/lintervista-a-calcutta-il-mio-pezzo-oroscopo-spacca-bene-a-me-non-piace/316111/|work=[[Rolling Stone (magazine)|Rolling Stone]]|language=it|title=L’intervista a Calcutta: Il mio pezzo Oroscopo spacca? Bene, a me non piace|author=Silvia Danielli|date=31 May 2016}}</ref><ref name="Rockol 2020"/>

In 2018, Calcutta's third studio album, ''Evergreen'', became his first number-one on the [[Italian Albums Chart]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://italiancharts.com/search.asp?search=Calcutta&cat=a|publisher=Italian Charts. Hung Medien|title=Italian Charts – Calcutta – Albums|accessdate=23 January 2021}}</ref> The album spawned singles including "Orgasmo", "Paracetamolo" and "Pesto".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.gqitalia.it/show/musica/2018/05/28/calcutta-evergreen-concerti-pesto-mainstream-paracetamolo|work=[[GQ (magazine)|GQ]]|accessdate=23 January 2021|title=Calcutta, il cantautore ItPop tra Orgasmo, Pesto e Paracetamolo|author=Cecilia Uzzo|date=28 May 2018}}</ref> 
The album was re-released in 2019, with the singles "Punto" and "Sorriso (Milano Dateo)".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.allmusicitalia.it/news/calcutta-sorriso-milano-dateo-e-un-successo-la-tracklist-di-evergreen-e-altre-canzoni.html|publisher=AllMusicItalia.it|language=it|title=Calcutta. Sorriso (Milano Dateo) è un successo. La tracklist di Evergreen… e altre canzoni|date=23 June 2019|accessdate=23 January 2021}}</ref>

In 2019, Calcutta recorded the single "La luna e la gatta" with producers Takagi & Ketra and fellow singer-songwriters [[Jovanotti]] and Tommaso Paradiso.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.it/musica/news-musica/calcutta-jovanotti-e-tommaso-paradiso-insieme-per-takagi-ketra/448085/|work=[[Rolling Stone (magazine)|Rolling Stone]]|language=it|title=Calcutta, Jovanotti e Tommaso Paradiso insieme per Takagi & Ketra|date=1 March 2019}}</ref>
During his career, he also appeared as a featured artist on the singles "[[Se piovesse il tuo nome]]", released in 2018 with [[Elisa (Italian singer)|Elisa]], and "Blue Jeans" (2020), recorded as a duet with Franco126.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rtl.it/musica/elisa-and-calcutta/se-piovesse-il-tuo-nome/|publisher=[[RTL 102.5]]|language=it|title=Elisa & Calcutta – Se piovesse il tuo nome|accessdate=23 January 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sorrisi.com/musica/concerti/franco126-con-calcutta-il-nuovo-singolo-e-blue-jeans/|work=[[TV Sorrisi e Canzoni]]|language=it|title=Franco126 con Calcutta: il nuovo singolo è Blue Jeans|author=Cecilia Esposito|date=15 December 2020|accessdate=23 January 2021}}</ref>
He also penned songs for other Italian recording artists, including [[Francesca Michielin]]'s "[[Io non abito al mare]]", [[Fabri Fibra]]'s "Come mai", [[Emma Marrone|Emma]]'s "Latina", [[Nina Zilli]]'s "Mi hai fatto fare tardi" and [[Luca Carboni]]'s "Io non voglio".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.r101.it/news/le-novita-di-r101-made-in-italy/284501/francesca-michielin-io-non-abito-al-mare.html|publisher=[[R101]]|language=it|title=Francesca Michielin – Io non abito al mare|accessdate=23 January 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.it/musica/pop/emma-calcutta-davide-petrella-e-drd-uniscono-le-forze-arriva-latina/2020/08/2839839/|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|language=it|title=Emma, Calcutta, Davide Petrella e DRD uniscono le forze: arriva Latina|date=28 August 2020|accessdate=23 January 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.radioitalia.it/news/nina_zilli/nuova_uscita/14668_nina_zilli_svelato_il_mistero_di_%22mi_hai_fatto_fare_tardi%22.php|publisher=[[Radio Italia Solo Musica Italiana]]|language=it|title=Nina Zilli, svelato il mistero di Mi hai fatto fare tardi|date=24 May 2017|accessdate=23 January 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.allmusicitalia.it/news/luca-carboni-io-non-vogli-tour-club.html|publisher=AllMusicItalia.it|language=it|title=Luca Carboni lania il singolo Io voglia prima di partire col tour nei club|author=Alessandro Genovese|date=16 September 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rockol.it/news-707989/fabri-fibra-ascolta-come-mai-con-franco126|publisher=Rockol.it|language=it|title=Fabri Fibra, ascolta Come mai con Franco126|date=11 October 2019|accessdate=24 January 2021}}</ref>
