'''CLC''' ({{Korean|씨엘씨}}, an acronym for '''CrystaL Clear''' <ref name="KPOPHerald">{{cite web|last=Lim|first=Jeong-yeo|title=Cube to launch new girl group CLC|url=http://kpopherald.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=201503110929235683076_2|work=KPOP Herald|date=March 11, 2015|access-date=July 16, 2017|archive-date=August 6, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170806061530/http://kpopherald.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=201503110929235683076_2|url-status=live}}</ref>) is a South Korean [[girl group]] formed by [[Cube Entertainment]]. The group consists of five members: Seunghee, [[Choi Yu-jin (singer)|Yujin]], Seungyeon, [[Jang Ye-eun|Yeeun]], and [[Kwon Eun-bin|Eunbin]]. Originally a seven-piece ensemble from February 2016, [[Elkie Chong|Elkie]] departed from the group in February 2021, and [[Sorn (singer)|Sorn]] departed from the group in November 2021.

Their debut EP ''[[First Love (EP)|First Love]]'' was released on March 19, 2015, with five group members: Seunghee, Yujin, Seungyeon, Sorn and Yeeun. The remaining two members, Elkie and Eunbin, were added to the group with the release of their third EP, ''[[Refresh (EP)|Refresh]]'', on February 29, 2016, though Eunbin did not participate in official promotions until the release of their fourth EP, ''[[Nu.Clear|Nu.clear]],'' in May 2016.
