'''Marietta''' was<ref name=sputnikmusic/> an American [[emo]] band from [[Philadelphia]], [[Pennsylvania]].

==History==
Marietta began in 2011. They released a split with [[Modern Baseball]] in 2012 titled ''Couples Therapy''.<ref>{{cite web|title=Marietta / Modern Baseball – Couples Therapy|url=http://www.absolutepunk.net/showthread.php?t=2969462|publisher=AbsolutePunk|accessdate=5 October 2015}}</ref>

In 2013, Marietta released their first full-length album titled ''Summer Death''.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Murray|first1=Katrina|title=Album Review: Experience Marietta’s Summer Death|url=http://thekey.xpn.org/2013/10/23/album-review-experience-mariettas-summer-death/|publisher=The Key (XPN)|accessdate=5 October 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Breslin|first1=Jay|title=Album Review: Marietta – Summer Death|url=http://rockonphilly.com/2013/11/album-review-marietta-summer-death/|publisher=Rock On Philly|accessdate=5 October 2015}}</ref>

In April 2015, Marietta released an EP titled ''Cuts''.<ref>{{cite web|title=MARIETTA’S NEW EP FEATURES ACOUSTIC CUTS|url=http://modern-vinyl.com/2015/04/09/mariettas-new-ep-features-acoustic-cuts/|publisher=Modern-Vinyl|accessdate=5 October 2015}}</ref> On August 17, 2015, Marietta released their second full-length album titled ''As It Were''.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Tate|first1=Nick|title=Marietta clean up nicely for their sophomore effort, As It Were|url=http://thekey.xpn.org/2015/08/19/marietta-returns/|publisher=The Key (XPN)|accessdate=5 October 2015}}</ref>

In December 2015, Marietta announced on their Facebook page that they were breaking up.<ref name=sputnikmusic>{{cite web|title=Marietta Calls it Quits|url=http://www.sputnikmusic.com/news/35413/Marietta-Calls-it-Quits/|publisher=[[SputnikMusic]]|accessdate=31 December 2015}}</ref>

On May 1, 2020, Marietta released an EP called 'Summer Demos 2012' containing demos of songs produced when the band first formed.
