'''Teto''', or "''Teto the Clown''", is among the most popular of the children’s [[toy]] [[clown]] [[puppets]] created by Hazelle Hedges Rollins for her company in [[Kansas City, Missouri]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://americanhistory.si.edu/collections/search/object/nmah_662191|title=Teto the Clown Marionette|website=National Museum of American History|language=en|access-date=2019-01-24}}</ref>

==In other media==
* Teto was seen in ''[[Puppet Master (film)|Puppet Master]]'' and ''[[Puppet Master: Axis of Evil]]'' as a puppet decoration along with the other puppets.{{citation needed|date=March 2016}}
* Teto also made his cameo appearance in the TV series of [[Gumby]], [[List of Gumby episodes|Season 1 of Ep. 18, entitled "''Toying Around''"]] as a puppet decoration along with the other puppets.
