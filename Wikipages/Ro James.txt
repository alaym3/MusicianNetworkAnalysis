'''Ronnie James Tucker''', better known by his stage name '''Ro James''', is an American singer and songwriter. He is known for his single "Permission", which reached number 37 on the US [[Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs]] chart. He is signed to [[ByStorm Entertainment]]/[[RCA Records]] and released ''Eldorado'', his first album under a major label, in 2016.

==Career==
Born in [[Stuttgart]], West Germany, James is of Panamanian<ref>{{Cite magazine|title=Getting To Know Ro James|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/7973659/getting-to-know-ro-james|access-date=2021-06-11|magazine=Billboard|language=en|archive-date=June 11, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210611234315/https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/7973659/getting-to-know-ro-james|url-status=live}}</ref> and African-American<ref>{{Cite web|last=Gold|first=Johnson|title=PAUSE Meets: Ro James|url=https://pausemag.co.uk/2017/04/pause-meets-ro-james/|access-date=2021-06-12|website=PAUSE Online {{!}} Men's Fashion, Street Style, Fashion News & Streetwear|archive-date=June 12, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210612001843/https://pausemag.co.uk/2017/04/pause-meets-ro-james/|url-status=live}}</ref> descent. Throughout his childhood, James would sing regularly, but never in public.<ref name="BillboardRB">{{cite news|last1=Platon|first1=Adelle|title=R&B Bad Boy Ro James On His Influences & Upcoming Debut 'El Dorado'|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/6843753/ro-james-el-dorado-interview|access-date=April 18, 2016|publisher=Billboard|date=January 15, 2016|archive-date=April 21, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160421003153/http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/6843753/ro-james-el-dorado-interview|url-status=live}}</ref> He eventually moved to New York where he was influenced by his aunt [[Rosie Gaines]], a former singer for [[Prince (musician)|Prince]].<ref name="UBPrince">{{cite news|title=New Artist Spotlight: Ro James|url=http://urbanbridgez.com/2016/01/30/new-artist-spotlight-ro-james-video-permission/|access-date=April 18, 2016|publisher=Urban Bridgez|date=January 30, 2016|archive-date=May 2, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160502145245/http://urbanbridgez.com/2016/01/30/new-artist-spotlight-ro-james-video-permission/|url-status=live}}</ref> His love for the album ''[[Purple Rain (album)|Purple Rain]]'' landed him in a studio at the age of 19.<ref name="UBPrince"/> His skills as a songwriter began with the song "Portrait", leading up to a credit on the single "[[Miguel discography|Use Me]]" by R&B singer [[Miguel (singer)|Miguel]]<ref>{{cite news|last1=Leight|first1=Elias|title=jade de LaFleur, '405' Featuring Ro James|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/the-juice/6327646/jade-de-lafleur-405-featuring-ro-james-exclusive-song-premiere|access-date=April 18, 2016|publisher=Billboard|date=November 25, 2014|archive-date=May 5, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160505042852/http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/the-juice/6327646/jade-de-lafleur-405-featuring-ro-james-exclusive-song-premiere|url-status=live}}</ref> after forming a bond with him on [[MySpace]].<ref name="VibeMYspace">{{cite news|title=Ro James Discusses Upcoming EP And Working With Miguel|url=http://www.vibe.com/2013/04/ro-james-discusses-upcoming-ep-and-working-miguel/|access-date=April 18, 2016|publisher=Vibe|date=April 9, 2013|archive-date=March 16, James performed in and around New York with artists such as [[Luke James (singer)|Luke James]] and [[Wynter Gordon]]. In 2013 he released his first EP, a three-part entitled ''Coke, Jack and Cadillacs''. Each part represented important themes in his life - first love, first drink, first car.<ref name="BillboardRB"/> The EP featured songs that included "A.D.I.D.A.S." and "Pledge Allegiance". It also featured [[Asher Roth]] on the song "Lisa".<ref>{{cite news|last1=Platon|first1=Adelle|title=Ro James Makes Magic on Sexy Jam 'Already Knew That'|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/6813548/ro-james-already-knew-that-premiere|access-date=April 18, 2016|publisher=Billboard|date=December 17, 2016|archive-date=April 23, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160423205254/http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/6813548/ro-james-already-knew-that-premiere|url-status=live}}</ref> James signed with [[ByStorm Entertainment]]/[[RCA Records]] in 2015 and subsequently released "Permission", his first single from a major record label, reaching number 37 on the US Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs chart.<ref name="Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs">{{Cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/6857401/ro-james/chart?f=367|title=Ro James - Chart history &#124; Billboard|date=August 11, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160811084832/http://www.billboard.com/artist/6857401/ro-james/chart?f=367|archive-date=August 11, 2016}}</ref> His debut album, ''Eldorado'' was released May 27, 2016. It debuted at number 71 on the US [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]]<ref name="Eldorado BB200">{{Cite web|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160404160907/http://www.billboard.com/artist/6857401/ro-james/chart|title=Ro James - Chart history &#124; Billboard|date=April 4, 2016|website=Web.archive.org|access-date=October 25, 2021}}</ref> and at number eight on the US [[Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums]].<ref name="Eldorado R&B/HH">{{Cite web|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160811080000/http://www.billboard.com/artist/6857401/ro-james/chart?f=333|title=Ro James - Chart history &#124; Billboard|date=August 11, 2016|website=Web.archive.org|access-date=October 25, 2021}}</ref> It subsequently earned him his first nomination for [[Best R&B Performance]] at the [[59th Grammy Awards]] in February 2017.
