'''Jelena Žnidarić''' (born 9 July 1994), known professionally as '''Zsa Zsa''', is a Croatian singer. She rose to fame by participating in the second season of [[X Factor Adria]].<ref name="xfactor">{{cite news | url=http://x-ica.com/zsa-zsa-studentica-koja-zivi-svoj-glazbeni-san/| title=Zsa Zsa: Studentica koja živi svoj san| work=x-ica.com|language=hr|date=1 February 2015| access-date=23 March 2018}}</ref> Zsa Zsa is most known for her collaboration with [[Damir Kedžo]] on the song "Sve u meni se budi".<ref name="sveumeni">{{cite news | url=http://varazdinski.rtl.hr/glazba-i-kultura/najbolji-zsa-zsa-i-kedzo-imaju-najbolju-pjesmu-godine-sve-u-meni-se-budi| title=Zsa Zsa i Kedžo imaju najbolju pjesmu godine| work=varazdinski.rtl.hr|language=hr|date=12 December 2017| access-date=23 March 2018}}</ref>

==Life and career==
===Early life and career beginnings===
Jelena Žnidarić was born in [[Pušćine]], Croatia. Žnidarić began singing at an early age. At age five her parents enrolled her in singing lessons.<ref name="early">{{cite news | url=https://dnevnik.hr/showbuzz/mladi_i_neizbjezni/otkrivamo-tko-je-zsa-zsa-djevojka-koja-je-pokorila-zagrebacki-festival---465325.html| title=O ovoj Hrvatici svi pričaju! Otkrivamo tko je ona!| work=Dnevnik.hr|language=hr|date=1 February 2017| access-date=24 March 2018}}</ref>

===Breakthrough===
In 2015 Žnidarić released her first single "Živa sam".<ref name="zivasam">{{cite news | url=http://www.index.hr/rouge/clanak/zsa-zsa-upoznajte-pjevacicu-ciji-je-glas-pokorio-hrvatsku-scenu/997680.aspx| title=Zsa Zsa: Upoznajte pjevačicu čiji je glas pokorio hrvatsku scenu| work=Index.hr|language=hr|date=29 September 2017| access-date=24 March 2018}}</ref> "Sve u meni se budi", a collaboration with [[Croat]]ian singer [[Damir Kedžo]] was released as Žnidarić's second single. Žnidarić and Kedžo took part with the song at the 54th edition of ''Zagrebački festival'' and won the contest with 144 points.<ref name="zagrebfest">{{cite news | url=https://www.vecernji.hr/showbiz/zsa-zsa-i-kedzo-uvjerljivo-odnijeli-pobjedu-s-pjesmom-sve-u-meni-se-budi-1145558| title=Sjajnom pjesmom 'Sve u meni se budi' pobjedu odnijeli Zsa Zsa i Damir Kedžo| work=Večernji list|language=hr|date=2 January 2017| access-date=27 March 2018}}</ref> Commercially, the song was a success in Croatia. On the [[HR Top 40]] Year-end Chart of 2017, the song was declared the number one song, a career first for both Žnidarić and Kedžo.<ref name="yearend">{{cite news | url=http://www.top-lista.hr/www/hrtop40-godisnja-2017-01-12-2016-30-11-2017/| title=HR Top 40 Year-end Chart 2017| work=top-lista.hr|language=hr| access-date=27 March 2018}}</ref>
