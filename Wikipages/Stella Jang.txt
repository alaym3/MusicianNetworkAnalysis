'''Stella Jang''' or Jang Seong-eun ([[Korean language|Korean]]: 장성은; born on November 18, 1991) is a South Korean musician.
She graduated from [[Lycée Henri-IV]], and [[AgroParisTech]]. She got her name from her French teacher as she thought her Korean name would be hard to pronounce for French people.<ref>{{cite web |title=스텔라장 |url=https://namu.wiki/w/%EC%8A%A4%ED%85%94%EB%9D%BC%EC%9E%A5 | trans-title= | website=namu.wiki |accessdate=September 21, 2020|language=Korean}}</ref>

==Life and career==
===Pre-debut===
Jang moved to [[Paris]] at the age of 12, and graduated from the ''[[grande école]]''.<ref name="lp"/> She also made songs at hiphop platform Jungle Radio, where [[Zico (rapper)|Zico]] and [[RM (rapper)|RM]] were working.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.chosun.com/site/data/html_dir/2017/06/10/2017061000101.html|date=2017-06-09|publisher=[[Chosun Ilbo]]|title=[V라이브] 스텔라 장 "지코·랩몬스터와 같은 곳에서 활동"}}</ref> She also participated in some of the songs from album backpack by South Korean hiphop duo [[Geeks (musical duo)|Geeks]].<ref name="flopp">{{cite web|url=https://www.tvreport.co.kr/570093|date=2014-09-19|website=tvreport|title='리얼 엄친딸' 스텔라장, 작사-작곡한 '어제 차이고' 발매}}</ref>

===2014-2015:Debut and early songs===
She then returned to Korea, where she attempted to become a singer in the [[K-Pop]] genre. Unlike most performers in the genre, she had not trained under one of the various management agencies that typically produce K-Pop stars. Her first single, "Dumped Yesterday", was released in 2014, and was described as a song with bright sounding guitar strings.<ref name="flopp"/> However, it drew little attention.<ref name="lp"/> In March 2015, Stella released her second single "It’s Raining" featuring rapper [[Verbal Jint]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://bntnews.hankyung.com/apps/news?popup=0&nid=04&c1=04&c2=04&c3=00&nkey=201503231355263&mode=sub_view|title=스텔라장, 신곡 ‘It’s Raining’ 발표…버벌진트 랩피처링 참여|website=bntnews}}</ref> In a 2015 interview with BNT News (where she was described as a female hiphop musician), she said her music was influenced by the 90s sound and tried to reflect elements that would bring relatability with her audiences.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://bntnews.hankyung.com/apps/news?popup=0&nid=04&c1=04&c2=04&c3=00&nkey=201507030857353&mode=sub_view|date=2015-07-03|title=[릴레이 인터뷰] 여성 힙합 뮤지션⑥┃스텔라장, 정답을 찾는다면}}</ref> In September 2015, she released her third single "뒷모습(Portrait of you)", a song about her parents.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://bntnews.hankyung.com/apps/news?popup=0&nid=04&c1=04&c2=04&c3=00&nkey=201509151727023&mode=sub_view|title=스텔라장, 부모님께 드리는 작은 선물…신곡 ‘뒷모습’ 발표|date=2015-09-15|website=bntnews}}</ref>

===2016:First EP ''Colors''===
In 2016, She released her first EP ''Colors'' and she was described as an artist loved by hipsters.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://osen.mt.co.kr/article/G1110508503|title='음색여신' 스텔라장, 오늘 데뷔앨범 'Colors' 발표|website=osen|date=2016-10-05}}</ref>
The album was described as "emotional music mixed with rhythmical raps".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mhns.co.kr/news/articleView.html?idxno=26025|title=[비포선라이즈] 힙합과 감성음악의 달콤쌉쌀한 만남, 다채로운 무지개 빛깔의 뮤지션 '스텔라장'|date=2016-10-15}}</ref>

===2017:appearance on Problematic Men and mainstream breakthrough===
A 2017 appearance on the Korean TV show ''[[Problematic Men]]'' brought significant interest to her.<ref name="lp">{{cite web|url=https://star.mt.co.kr/stview.php?no=2017051716203906329|title=[★FULL인터뷰]'佛유학파' 스텔라장 "음악은 내 탈출구였죠"|first1=Sang-geun|last1=Yoon|publisher=starnews|date=May 18, 2017}}</ref> Stella debuted as a radio DJ for Gyeonggi Broadcasting on the 22nd of April.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tvdaily.asiae.co.kr/read.php3?aid=14926556751232986010|website=tvdaily.asiae|title=스텔라장 DJ 도전, 경기방송 심야 시간 책임진다	}}</ref> In April 30,she released the single "vanishing Paycheck" that criticizes the reality of office workers.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://osen.mt.co.kr/article/G1110636468|title=[정오의신곡] "월급은 통장을 스칠 뿐"..스텔라장, '엄친딸'이 들려주는 현실저격송
|date=2017-04-30}}</ref> In June 2017, she did her first solo concert "ddokddok(Knock Knock)" at Hyundaicard Understage.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://star.mt.co.kr/stview.php?no=2017052510245036158&type=1&outlink=1|title='글로벌 뇌섹녀' 스텔라장, 첫 단독콘서트 '똑똑' 개최|website=starmt|date=2017-05-25}}</ref>

===2018-2019:Staples & Substances Dangereuses===
She released her project album ‘staples’ in January 2018, with producer Pleyne and a hip hop and R&B sound.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://sports.chosun.com/news/ntype.htm?id=201801250100184950013192&servicedate=20180124|title=스텔라장, 프로젝트 앨범 'staples' 발매..독보적 감성|website=sportschosun|date=2018-01-24}}</ref> In March 2019, she released 'Substances Dangereuses' an album that uses fine dust as a metaphor for the harsh realities.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://osen.mt.co.kr/article/G1111104425|title=스텔라장, 24일 새 EP앨범 '유해물질' 발표+30일 단독콘서트|date=2019-03-22}}</ref>

===2019-2020:Tiktok fame & STELLA I===
From late 2019, her song ''Colors'' that contained the lyrics 'I could be red or I could be yellow I could be blue or I could be purple I could be green or pink or black or white I could be every color you like' from her 2016 EP ''Colors''  newly gained global popularity in [[TikTok]] around the world and boosted the popularity in Korea.<ref name='tiktok'>{{cite web|url=https://www.chosun.com/site/data/html_dir/2020/03/28/2020032800888.html|title=제시카 알바도 올라탄, 스텔라장 '컬러스' 글로벌 역주행|date=2020-03-28}}</ref> The challenge was followed by [[Jessica Alba]].<ref name='tiktok'/> In 2020, Stella released her first studio album STELLA I.<ref name="lol"/>

Stella Jang also co-wrote the song ‘Friends’ by BTS for their album [[Map of the Soul: 7]].<ref name= 'koreaherald'>{{cite web|url=http://www.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20200427000650|title=‘Stella Jang on her growth as singer-songwriter, co-writing BTS’ ‘Friends’|date=2021-11-17}}</ref>

===2021:Stairs===
In 2021, she released her EP 'Stairs'.<ref name="lkkkkkk"/>

==Personal life==
Stella Jang is fluent in 4 languages, including Korean and French.<ref>{{cite web|url= https://m.koreatimes.co.kr/pages/article.asp?newsIdx=266527|date=2021-11-17|website=koreatimes|title=Stella Jang talks about her new album ‘Hazardous Materials’}}</ref>
In 2021, she revealed she was suffering from a rare disease called [[Rhabdomyolysis]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.donga.com/news/Entertainment/article/all/20210515/106955342/1|date=2021-05-15|title=스텔라장, 희귀병 투병 고백…"횡문근융해증, 억울하다"}}</ref>
