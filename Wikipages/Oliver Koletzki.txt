'''Oliver Koletzki''' (born in [[Braunschweig]] on 5 October 1974) is a [[Germany|German]] [[Dance music|dance]] and [[house music]] [[music producer|producer]] and [[DJ]]. He is currently based in Berlin, Germany.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://mixmag.net/read/oliver-koletzki-in-the-lab-nyc-dj-lab|title=Oliver Koletzki in The Lab NYC|website=Mixmag|access-date=2019-10-31}}</ref>

Koletzki rose to international fame with his track "Der Mückenschwarm" (meaning "the mosquito swarm"), which appeared on the [[Cocoon Recordings]] label.<ref>[http://www.xflo.net/2006/03/20/oliver-der-grosse-koletzki/ Xflo.net Oliver, der große Koletzki] (German)</ref> Many DJs played his track and thus introduced Koletzki to the charts. "Der Mückenschwarm" was remixed and covered by artists such as [[Pig & Dan]] and Dominik Eulberg, which led to worldwide bookings and further releases, notably on [[Martin Eyerer]]'s ''Kling Klong''. Koletzki founded his own label, Stil vor Talent (Style over Talent) in September 2005. His debut album, ''Get Wasted'', was released in 2007, followed by ''Großstadtmärchen'' in 2009, the most successful ''Lovestoned'' in 2010 with [[Fran (singer)|Fran]],<ref>[http://www.timeclash.de/2010/07/13/oliver-koletzki-meldet-sich-mit-videopremiere-von-%E2%80%9Earrow-and-bow%E2%80%9C-zuruck/ TimeClash.de: Oliver Koletzki meldet sich mit Videopremiere von „Arrow and Bow“ zurück] {{Webarchive|url=https://archive.is/20130910203559/http://www.timeclash.de/2010/07/13/oliver-koletzki-meldet-sich-mit-videopremiere-von-%E2%80%9Earrow-and-bow%E2%80%9C-zuruck/ |date=2013-09-10 }} (German)</ref><ref>[http://www.musicline.de/de/chartverfolgung_summary/artist/Koletzki%2COliver+%26+Fran/longplay Musicline.de Oliver Koletzki & Fran discography]</ref> ''Großstadtmärchen 2'' in 2012 and ''I Am OK'' in 2014.

==Awards==
Koletzki was chosen as the ''Best Newcomer 2005'' in the reader's poll of ''[[Groove Magazine]]'', and "Der Mückenschwarm" was chosen as the Track of the Year 2005 by reader's polls in ''Groove'' and in [[Raveline]]'' magazine.
