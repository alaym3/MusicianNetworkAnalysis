'''Young-jae''', also spelled '''Yeong-jae''' or '''Yong-jae''', is a Korean masculine given name. Its meaning differs based on the [[hanja]] used to write each syllable of the name. There are 34 hanja with the reading "[[Young (Korean name)|young]]" and 20 hanja with the reading "[[Jae (Korean name)|jae]]" on the South Korean government's official list of hanja which may be used in given names.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://help.scourt.go.kr/nm/img/hanja/hanja.pdf|title=인명용 한자표|trans-title=Table of hanja for use in personal names|publisher=Supreme Court of the Republic of Korea|publication-place=Seoul|language=ko|date=August 2007|access-date=1 September 2018|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170829123222/http://help.scourt.go.kr/nm/img/hanja/hanja.pdf|archive-date=29 August 2017}}</ref>
