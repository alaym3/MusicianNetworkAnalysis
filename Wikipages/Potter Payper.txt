'''Jamel Bousbaa''' (born 15 November 1990), better known professionally as '''Potter Payper''', is an English rapper and songwriter from [[Barking, London|Barking]], [[London]].<ref>{{cite news |last=Meyler |first=Piers |date=27 October 2017 |title=These are the 100 most influential people from Essex |url=https://www.essexlive.news/news/essex-news/100-most-influential-people-essex-690031 |work=Essex Live |location=Essex |access-date=1 October 2020}}</ref><ref>[https://www.complex.com/music/2020/09/potter-payper-training-day-3-mixtape Potter Payper's 'Training Day 3' Mixtape Has Arrived]</ref><ref>[https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/9455158/24kgoldn-mood-pop-smoke-uk-charts 24kGoldn's 'Mood' Ends 'WAP' Reign In U.K. as Pop Smoke Makes Chart History]</ref> In 2021, he signed to the UK division of [[Def Jam Recordings]].

==Early life==
Jamel Bousbaa was born to a [[British Irish]] mother and an [[Algeria]]n father. He grew up on the Gascoigne Estate in [[Barking, London]].<ref>{{cite news |last=Thiessen |first=Christopher|date=23 July 2019 |title=Breakdown: All 52 Upcoming UK Artists Stormzy Mentions During His Glastonbury Speech |url=https://djbooth.net/features/2019-07-23-stormzy-glastonbury-speech-artist-discovery-list |work=DJ Booth |access-date=1 October 2020}}</ref><ref>[http://wp-mixtapemadness.test-link.co.uk/blog/interviews/not-for-the-radio-nftr-potter-payper/ Not For The Radio &#91;NFTR&#93; – Potter Payper]</ref><ref>[https://www.mixtapemadness.com/blog/interviews/not-for-the-radio-nftr-potter-payper/ Not For The Radio &#91;NFTR&#93; – Potter Payper]</ref><ref>[https://www.beatgogo.com/albums/training-day-3-potter-payper-64998 TRAINING DAY 3 - POTTER PAYPER ALBUM: LIST OF SONGS AND LYRICS TRANSLATION]</ref><ref>[https://estatewatch.london/images/Steve_Lawes_2020.pdf SOCIAL HOUSING AND MOBILITY IN 2017: THE GASCOIGNE ESTATE A CREATIVE INVESTIGATION BY STEVE LAWES]</ref>
