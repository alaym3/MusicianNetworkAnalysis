'''Feine Sahne Fischfilet''' ("Fine Cream Fish Fillet") is a German [[Punk Music|punk]] band formed in 2007 in [[Mecklenburg-Vorpommern]]. The band's members are from [[Jarmen]], [[Greifswald]], [[Rostock]], [[Demmin]], [[Loitz]], [[Hanshagen]] and [[Wismar]].

The band has officially released five studio albums: ''Backstage mit Freunden'' in 2009 and ''Wut im Bauch, Trauer im Herzen'' a year later — both released via [[Diffidati Records]] —, ''Scheitern & Verstehen'' in 2012, ''Bleiben oder Gehen'' in 2015 and ''Sturm & Dreck'' in 2018 via [[Audiolith Records]]. The 2015 released fourth studio album peaked at no. 21 in the [[Media Control Charts|German Albums Charts]]. ''Sturm & Dreck'' reached no. 3 in German Albums Charts, no. 12 in the [[Austrian Music Charts|Austrian Albums Charts]] and no. 30 in the [[Swiss Music Charts|Swiss Albums Charts]].

In 2009 the [[Landeskriminalamt Mecklenburg-Vorpommern]] filed a lawsuit against the band because of the song ''Staatsgewalt'' ("Authority of the State") which is on the tracklist on their debut record ''Backstage mit Freunden'' at the [[Bundesprüfstelle für jugendgefährdende Medien]] for potential violent content which got declined later.<ref name="sponArtikelzumVSBericht">Julia Jüttner: [[Spiegel Online]]: [http://www.spiegel.de/panorama/feine-sahne-fischfilet-im-verfassungsschutzbericht-mecklenburg-vorpommern-a-864974.html Punkband Feine Sahne Fischfilet – Die Staatsfeinde], erschienen am 5. November 2012, abgerufen am 5. November 2012</ref> Two years later the band was mentioned in the [[Verschassungsschutz Mecklenburg-Vorpommern|Verfassungsschutzbericht of Mecklenburg-Vorpommern]] for having an explicit anti-state attitude.<ref>Verfassungsschutzbericht des Landes Mecklenburg-Vorpommern 2011, S. 84, {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130426205054/http://www.verfassungsschutz-mv.de/cms2/Verfassungsschutz_prod/Verfassungsschutz/content_downloads/Verfassungsschutzberichte/Verfassungsschutzbericht_2011.pdf |date=26 April 2013 |title=Onlineversion (PDF; 2,2&nbsp;MB) }}</ref> The band was featured in the Verfassungsschutzbericht again a year later.<ref>{{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170620094901/http://www.verfassungsschutz-mv.de/cms2/Verfassungsschutz_prod/Verfassungsschutz/content_downloads/Verfassungsschutzberichte/VSB_2012_140526.pdf |date=20 June 2017 |title=Verfassungsschutzbericht 2012 des Landes Mecklenburg-Vorpommern }} (PDF; 5,2&nbsp;MB)</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Feine Sahne Fischfilet: Statement zum Verfassungsschutzbericht 2012|periodical=|publisher=|url=http://feinesahnefischfilet.blogsport.de/2013/08/08/statement-zum-verfassungsschutzbericht-2012 |format=|accessdate=1 September 2018|last=|date=|year=|language=|pages=|quote=}}</ref>

Feine Sahne Fischfilet played at major music festivals like [[Rock am Ring]] and [[Rock im Park]], [[Hurricane Festival]], [[Southside Festival|Southside]] and [[Vainstream Rockfest]].<ref>[http://www.rollingstone.de/news/meldungen/article505837/hurricane-und-southside-2014-jetzt-auch-mit-pixies-macklemore-amp-ryan-lewis-und-elbow.html Hurricane und Southside 2014: Jetzt auch mit Pixies, Macklemore & Ryan Lewis und Elbow.] Rolling Stone, 2. Dezember 2013, aufgerufen am 2. April 2015</ref><ref>[https://www.rock-am-ring.com/spielplan ''Rock am Ring – Spielplan 2015''] Website Rock am Ring 2015, abgerufen am 11. Juni 2015</ref><ref>[http://www.rock-im-park.com/spielplan ''Rock im Park – Spielplan 2015''] Website Rock im Park 2015, abgerufen am 11. Juni 2015</ref> As a reaction of the [[2018 Chemnitz protests|protests in Chemnitz]] the band took part at the free-for-all concert under the moniker ''#WirSindMehr'' alongside [[Die Toten Hosen]], [[Kraftklub]], [[Marteria]], [[Casper (rapper)|Casper]] and [[K.I.Z]] against hate and racism. German president [[Frank-Walter Steinmeier]] shared a posting about this concert on his social media and got criticized for supporting left extremists in content of the participation of Feine Sahne Fischfilet.<ref>{{Cite news|title=Werbung für Linksextreme? Kritik an Steinmeier wegen Konzert-Tipp|periodical=[[Welt.de]]|url=https://www.welt.de/politik/deutschland/article181390978/Feine-Sahne-Fischfilet-Steinmeiers-Facebook-Post-in-der-Kritik.html|accessdate=2 September 2018|last=Ansgar Graw, Hannah Lühmann|date=2 September 2018|language=German}}</ref>

The band won several prizes like the [[Preis für Popkultur]] in 2017 for their campaign ''Noch nicht komplett im Arsch''. Feine Sahne Fischfilet ranked second at the 2013s Courage Prize of the Linksfraktion at the [[Landtag of Mecklenburg-Vorpommern]]. Prior to that the band was nominated as ''Best Newcomer'' at the [[VIA! Vut Indie Awards]]. In 2018 the band was nominated at the ''Best German Act'' category at the [[2018 MTV Europe Music Awards]].

== Trivia ==
* Several members, including vocalist Jan Gorkow are part of the [[FC Hansa Rostock]] fan scene.<ref>[http://feinesahnefischfilet.blogsport.de/2008/09/21/2-schoene-freitage/ Sänger ‚Monchi‘ hier in T-Shirts der Suptras zu sehen]</ref>
* The song ''Ostrava'' was dedicated to the FC Hansa fan scene; ''Keine Träne'' describes the estrangement of the fan scene.<ref>[http://yabasta.blogsport.de/2012/11/27/ya-basta-33-out-now/ ''Music from the terraces'' – Interview mit Feine Sahne Fischfilet]</ref>
* Vocalist Jan Gorkow had to face a lawsuit because he was accused to be involved in a confrontation with a neo nazi.<ref>{{cite web|title=Sänger von Feine Sahne Fischfilet vor Gericht|url=https://www.ndr.de/nachrichten/mecklenburg-vorpommern/Saenger-von-Feine-Sahne-Fischfilet-vor-Gericht,prozess4250.html|accessdate=15 December 2017|last=NDR|language=de}}</ref> He was later acquitted by the court.<ref>{{cite web|title=Freispruch für Sänger von Punkband|url=https://www.ndr.de/nachrichten/mecklenburg-vorpommern/Freispruch-fuer-Saenger-von-Punkband,prozess4256.html|accessdate=19 December 2017|last=NDR|date=18 December 2017|language=de}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news | url=http://www.taz.de/!5468333/ | title=Freispruch für Monchi: Loidee, Hammägeil!| newspaper=Die Tageszeitung: Taz| date=20 December 2017| last1=Uthoff| first1=Jens}}</ref>
* [[Charly Hübner]] directed the documentary movie [[Wildes Herz]] which is about the band.<ref>[http://www.bento.de/musik/wildes-herz-hier-ist-der-erste-trailer-zur-feine-sahne-fischfilet-doku-von-charly-huebner-1799519/ Bento: Hier ist der erste Trailer zur "Feine Sahne Fischfilet"-Doku] (in German)</ref>
* In 2017 originally comedian [[Jan Böhmermann]] was receiving the [[VUT Indie Award]] but he didn't attend the Awards ceremony. Feine Sahne Fischfilet were declared as winner afterwards. Böhmermann and the band's members auctioned the prize on [[eBay]] and donated that money to ''Jugend rettet'', an organization which helps rescuing refugees in the [[Mediterranean Sea]].<ref>[https://www.rollingstone.de/preis-gestohlen-jan-boehmermann-wuetet-gegen-feine-sahne-fischfilet-1356639/ Rolling Stone: Preis gestohlen – Jan Böhmermann wütet gegen Feine Sahne Fischfilet] (in German)</ref>
* Jan Gorkow is involved in the campaign ''MV for Kobane''.<ref>[http://www.nordkurier.de/mecklenburg-vorpommern/rostocker-entgeht-nur-knapp-is-anschlag-2116315607.html Helfer aus MV sind wohlauf]</ref><ref>{{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150728042048/http://www.ndr.de/fernsehen/sendungen/nordmagazin/Helfer-zurueck-aus-Suruc,nordmagazin30678.html |date=28 July 2015 |title=Helfer zurück aus Suruc }}</ref>
* In October 2016, Jan Gorkow was part of TV show ″[[Applaus und raus!]]″ on [[Pro Sieben]] moderated by stand-up comedian [[Oliver Polak]].
