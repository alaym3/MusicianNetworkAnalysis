'''Douglas Jack Agu''' (born 19 August 1989), better known by his stage name '''Runtown''', is a Nigerian singer, songwriter and producer.

==Biography==

Runtown was born on 19 August 1989 in [[Enugu]] but grew up partly in [[Abuja]] and Lagos, where he finished his primary education. After the death of his father, he moved to Abuja with his mother, who was working in the Ministry of Health.{{citation needed|date=October 2018}}

In 2014 he released "[[Gallardo (Runtown song)|Gallardo]]", a song which features [[vocal]]s from [[Davido]] and was released as the first single off his debut studio album ''[[Ghetto University]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.praxismagonline.com/album-review-running-runtowns-ghetto-university/|title=Album Review: Running through Runtown's Ghetto University|work=Praxis Magazine|last=Nduka|first=Echezonachukwu|date=7 February 2016|access-date=31 May 2016}}</ref> Gallardo went on to win "Best Collaboration of the Year" at the [[2014 Nigeria Entertainment Awards|2014 edition]] of the [[Nigeria Entertainment Awards]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.vanguardngr.com/2014/12/runtown-dreams-marrying-rihanna/|title=Runtown dreams of marrying Rihanna|work=[[Vanguard (Nigeria)|Vanguard Newspaper]]|last=Sholola|first=Damilola|date=12 December 2014|access-date=31 May 2016}}</ref> On 23 November 2015, Runtown released his debut studio album titled ''Ghetto University'' via MTN Music Plus through Eric Many Entertainment.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tooxclusive.com/download-mp3/runtown-premieres-debut-album-ghetto-university-listen-to-hot-track-picks-from-the-album/|title=Runtown Premieres Debut Album "Ghetto University" + Listen To Hot Track Picks From The Album|publisher=[[tooXclusive]]|last=Donnet|first=Jim|date=23 November 2015|access-date=31 May 2016}}</ref> The album generated over ₦35million on the music portal thus earning him a spot in the list of "Top 5 Most Streamed Artist".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.360nobs.com/2016/04/runtown-rising-profile-africas-next-big-thing/|title=RUNTOWN: The Rising Profile of Africa's Next 'BIG THING'|publisher=360Nobs|last=Oghene|first=Michael|date=6 April 2016|access-date=31 May 2016}}</ref>

On 4 November 2016, Runtown released the ballad "[[Mad Over You]]", which charted at number 38 on the ''Billboard'' Twitter Top Tracks chart.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://soundcity.tv/news/runtowns-mad-over-you-debuts-on-billboard-ranking/|publisher=soundcityTv|title=Runtown's "Mad over You" debuts on Billboard ranking}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.premiumtimesng.com/arts-entertainment/music/219850-wizkid-runtown-make-billboard-twitter-top-tracks-chart.html|title=Wizkid, Runtown make Billboard Twitter Top Tracks chart|last=Salihu|first=Idoko|date=7 January 2017|publisher=Premium Times}}</ref> Joey Akan from ''Pulse Nigeria'' described the song as a "self-produced track that draws inspiration from the Ghanaian Alkayida sound".<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://pulse.ng/buzz/runtown-mad-over-you-remix-should-have-mr-eazi-and-sarkodie-on-it-id6037645.html|title='Mad Over You' remix should have Mr Eazi and Sarkodie on it|last=Akan|first=Joey|date=12 January 2017|publisher=Pulse}}</ref> Runtown told ''[[The Fader]]'' magazine that the song was inspired by African beauty.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.thefader.com/2016/11/04/runtown-mad-over-you-premiere|title=Nigerian Star Runtown Is In Love With Ghanaian Beauty On "Mad Over You"|last=Alexander|first=Amber|date=4 November 2016|publisher=The Fader}}</ref>

==Controversies==

===Ownership of "Aye"===
In 2014, there were reports in the media that Runtown wrote "[[Aye (Davido song)|Aye]]". In an interview with the ''[[Nigerian Tribune]]'', Runtown said, "A lot of people have been asking me that question. Davido has had a number of interviews where he addressed how the song came about. So if you want to learn more about Aye ask David or read his interviews".<ref>{{cite web

===Exit from Eric Manny Entertainment===
Runtown's contract with Eric Manny Entertainment expired in June 2018. He subsequently started his own label, Soundgod Music Group.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.pulse.ng/entertainment/music/runtown-launches-indie-music-company-sound-god-music-group-id8544050.html|publisher= pulseNg|title= Singer launches indie music company, 'Sound God Music Group'|date= 2018-06-26}}</ref>
