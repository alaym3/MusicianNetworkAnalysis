'''Grant Andrew Decouto''' (born 24 March, 1995),<ref name="Genius"/> professionally known as '''Deko''', is an [[United States|American]] rapper and music producer. He is best known for producing [[OG Maco]]'s "Want More"<ref>{{Citation|title=OG Maco â€“ Want More|url=https://genius.com/Og-maco-want-more-lyrics|work=Genius|accessdate=28 February 2018}}</ref> and [[Migos]]' single "One Time".<ref name="RU">{{Cite news|url=http://www.rap-up.com/2015/02/05/new-music-migos-one-time/|title=New Music: Migos â€“ 'One Time'|work=Rap-Up|access-date=28 February 2018}}</ref> He has also worked with [[Gucci Mane]], [[K Camp]],<ref name="George">{{Cite news|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/8099993/og-parker-deko-grammy-nominations-migos-edm-album|title=OG Parker x Deko Talk Recent Grammy Nominations with Migos, EDM Album & Taking Over 2018|work=Billboard|access-date=1 March 2018}}</ref> and, as part of the [[Electronic dance music|EDM]] trio Merge, [[OG Parker]] and Tee Romano. Deko has also collaborated with visual artist Osean to create Yameii Online.<ref>{{Citation|title=Who is Yameii Online|url=https://www.virtualhumans.org/human/yameii-online|work=Virtual Humans|access-date=15 December 2020}}</ref> Yameii Online is a [[Vocaloid]] virtual rapper featured in some of his music.<ref>{{Citation|title=Yameii Online Lyrics, Songs and Albums - Genius|url=https://genius.com/artists/Yameii-online|work=Genius|access-date=15 December 2020}}</ref>
