'''Post Animal''' is an American [[psychedelic rock]] band formed in 2014, originating from [[Chicago, Illinois]]. It consists of Dalton Allison (bass, vocals), Jake Hirshland (guitar/keyboard), Javi Reyes (guitar), Wesley Toledo (drums), and Matt Williams (guitar).<ref name="Bustle">{{cite web|last=Stahler|first=Kelsea|title=What 'Stranger Things' Star Joe Keery Is Really Thinking When You Ask For A Selfie|url=https://www.bustle.com/p/what-stranger-things-star-joe-keery-is-really-thinking-when-you-ask-for-a-selfie-7845978|work=[[Bustle (magazine)|Bustle]]|accessdate=17 February 2018}}</ref> All members share songwriting contributions and sing lead vocals on their respective compositions.

==Career==
The band has self-released an EP (''Post Animal Perform the Most Curious Water Activities'') and a singles collection (''The Garden Series'') and toured with [[White Reaper]], [[Twin Peaks (band)|Twin Peaks]], [[Ron Gallo]], [[J Roddy Walston and the Business|J. Roddy Walston and the Business]], and [[Wavves]].

Post Animal signed to [[Polyvinyl Record Co.|Polyvinyl]] in 2017<ref name="Polyvinyl Signing">{{cite web|title=Post Animal signs to Polyvinyl!!!|url=https://www.polyvinylrecords.com/news/2100|publisher=[[Polyvinyl Record Co.|Polyvinyl]]|accessdate=17 February 2018}}</ref> and played [[Shaky Knees Music Festival|Shaky Knees]]<ref name="Baeble Music">{{cite web|last=Deverell|first=Chris|title=5 Artists You Shouldn't Miss At Shaky Knees This Year|url=https://www.baeblemusic.com/musicblog/1-8-2018/5-artists-you-shouldnt-miss-at-shaky-knees-this-year.html|website=Baeble Music|accessdate=17 February 2018}}</ref> and [[Bonnaroo Music Festival|Bonnaroo]]<ref name="Vanyaland">{{cite web|last=Marotta|first=Michael|title=Aaaaand here's the 2018 Bonnaroo Music and Arts Festival lineup|url=http://www.vanyaland.com/2018/01/09/aaaaaand-heres-the-2018-bonnaroo-music-and-arts-festival-lineup/|website=Vanyaland|accessdate=17 February 2018}}</ref> in 2018.

The band's debut full-length, ''When I Think of You in a Castle'', was released on April 20, 2018.<ref name="Stereogum">{{cite web|title=Post Animal – 'Ralphie'|url=https://www.stereogum.com/1983695/post-animal-ralphie/premiere/|website=[[Stereogum]]|accessdate=20 February 2018}}</ref>

Post Animal are supporting [[Cage the Elephant]] in February 2020 on their United Kingdom tour. The band is the supporting act on dates in Leeds, Glasgow, Manchester, Birmingham, and London.

==Musical style==
The ''[[Chicago Tribune]]'' has described the band's sound as "if [[Tame Impala]] listened to a lot of [[Black Sabbath]] and were signed to [[The Elephant 6 Recording Company|Elephant 6]]."<ref name="Chicago Tribune">{{cite web|last=Terry|first=Josh|title=Steve from 'Stranger Things' is in Chicago band Post Animal|url=http://www.chicagotribune.com/redeye/redeye-steve-stranger-things-joe-keery-chicago-band-post-animal-20160804-htmlstory.html|work=[[Chicago Tribune]]|accessdate=17 February 2018}}</ref>
