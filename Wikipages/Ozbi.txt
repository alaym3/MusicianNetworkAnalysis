'''Onur Dursun''' (born 30 January 1986), better known by his stage name '''Ozbi''', is a Turkish [[rapper]] and [[songwriter]].

== Life and career ==
Dursun was born in 1986 in [[Istanbul]]. His family are from [[Erzincan]].<ref name="solorgtr"/> Ozbi, who has been interested in rap music since the age of 14, has graduated from [[Anadolu University]], School of Turkish Language and Literature. During the [[Gezi Park protests]], he released the song "Asi".<ref name="solorgtr">{{cite news |author1=Sinem Burgu |title=Direnişte tanıştık: Ozbi adında bir rapçi, ‘Asi’ adında bir şarkı |url=https://haber.sol.org.tr/kultur-sanat/direniste-tanistik-ozbi-adinda-bir-rapci-asi-adinda-bir-sarki-haberi-77293 |accessdate=21 February 2020 |publisher=soL News Portal |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190815210531/https://haber.sol.org.tr/kultur-sanat/direniste-tanistik-ozbi-adinda-bir-rapci-asi-adinda-bir-sarki-haberi-77293 |archivedate=15 August 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |author1=Onur Baştürk |title=Gezi sonrasının en asi şarkısı |url=https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/gezi-sonrasinin-en-asi-sarkisi-24401649 |publisher=[[Hürriyet]] |accessdate=21 February 2020 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171221073930/http://www.hurriyet.com.tr/gezi-sonrasinin-en-asi-sarkisi-24401649 |archivedate=21 December 2017 |date=27 July 2013}}</ref> He released his first solo album ''Halk Edebiyatı'' in 2014. Then, in 2016, he started the ''Rakılı Live'' series with Gülce Duru. The first album of the series was released in 2016 and the second in 2017.<ref>{{cite news |title=Ozbi'nin beğenilen çalışması Rakılı Live'ın ikinci serisi başladı |url=https://www.birgun.net/haber/ozbi-nin-begenilen-calismasi-rakili-live-in-ikinci-serisi-basladi-183519 |accessdate=21 February 2020 |publisher=[[BirGün]] |date=9 October 2017 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20200220235533/https://www.birgun.net/haber/ozbi-nin-begenilen-calismasi-rakili-live-in-ikinci-serisi-basladi-183519 |archivedate=20 February 2020}}</ref> In 2018, he released his album called the album ''Serserilik ve Şiir''.<ref>{{cite web | title = Ozbi'nin yeni klibinde yıldızlar geçidi: Popüler Fetiş | url = https://www.birgun.net/haber/ozbi-nin-yeni-klibinde-yildizlar-gecidi-populer-fetis-237785 | publisher = [[BirGün]] | accessdate = 21 February 2020 | date = 23 November 2018 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20200222204439/https://www.birgun.net/haber/ozbi-nin-yeni-klibinde-yildizlar-gecidi-populer-fetis-237785 | archivedate = 22 February 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |author1=Emrah Kolukısa |title=Ozbi: Gezi gençliğine güveniyorum |url=http://www.cumhuriyet.com.tr/haber/ozbi-gezi-gencligine-guveniyorum-1199715 |accessdate=21 February 2020 |publisher=[[Cumhuriyet]] |date=12 January 2019 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20200220233651/http://www.cumhuriyet.com.tr/haber/ozbi-gezi-gencligine-guveniyorum-1199715 |archivedate=20 February 2020}}</ref>
