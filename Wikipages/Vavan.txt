'''Vavan''' ({{lang-fa|واوان}}, also [[Romanize]]d as '''Vāvān''')<ref>{{GEOnet3|-3849456|Vavan}}</ref> is a village in [[Melkari Rural District]], [[Vazineh District]], [[Sardasht County]], [[West Azerbaijan Province]], [[Iran]]. At the 2006 census, its population was 842, in 146 families.<ref>{{IranCensus2006|04}}</ref>
