'''The Outhere Brothers''' are an American [[hip house]] and [[Eurodance]] duo, composed of Keith "Malik" Mayberry and [[record producer]] Lamar "Hula" Mahone. While they achieved only moderate success in their native United States (excluding the hit song [[Summertime (DJ Jazzy Jeff & The Fresh Prince song)|"Summertime"]] by [[DJ Jazzy Jeff & The Fresh Prince]] which they wrote and produced in 1991{{fact|date=November 2020}}), two of their [[Single (music)|singles]], "[[Boom Boom Boom]]" and "[[Don't Stop (Wiggle Wiggle)]]", topped the chart in the United Kingdom in 1995.<ref name=occ/> In the same year, they also contributed to Molella's "If You Wanna Party", which also peaked within the top ten of the charts in the United Kingdom.<ref name=occ/>
