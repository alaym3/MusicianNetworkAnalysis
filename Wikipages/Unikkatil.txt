'''Viktor Palokaj''' (born March 18, 1981), known professionally as '''Unikkatil''' (stylized as '''UniKKatil'''), is an Albanian rapper, singer, songwriter and record producer who lives in [[the Bronx]], [[New York City]]. He was born in [[Prishtina]], [[Kosovo]] and his origin is from [[Kaçinar]], [[Mirditë]]. He was one of the first Albanian rappers and the leader of TBA (The Bloody Alboz). The message that he sends throughout his songs and the fact that he never made a music video during his career make his style of rap unique. Although he isn’t as active in the music industry recently, he's still considered by many the best Albanian rapper of all time.<ref>{{cite web|url= https://gazetablic.com/unikkatil-feston-ditelindjen-ja-sa-vjecar-po-behet-reperi/ |title=Unikkatil feston ditëlindjen ja sa vjeçar po bëhet |publisher=gazetablic.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.botapress.info/rebel-a-k-a-unikatil-pershendet-nga-prekazi/|title=Unikkatil përshëndet nga Prekazi |publisher=botapress.info}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = https://books.google.com/books?id=VL02DwAAQBAJ&pg=PA43|title=Kosovo=Jet|last1=Knaus|first1=Verena|last2=Warrander|first2=Gail|last3=Olenicoff|first3=Larissa|last4=Jennions|first4=Bridget Nurre|page=43|date=Oct 5, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.insajderi.com/unikkatil-feston-ditelindjen/|title=Unikkatil feston ditëlindjen|publisher=insajderi.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.lyrics.al/unikkatil/biografia|title=Unikkatil :: Biography|publisher=lyrics.al}}</ref>

== Career ==
His music career began in 1993 when he recorded his first record.<ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.lajmeshqip.com/showbiz-lifestyle/rikthehet-"shekspiri-shqiptar"-per-ti-mundur-te-gjithe-me-"fitorja-e-sinqerte"|title=Rikthehet "Shekspiri Shqiptarë" për ti mundur të gjithë me "Fitorja e Sinqertë"|publisher=lajmeshqip.com}}</ref> One of the most popular Albanian rappers,<ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.botasot.info/mediale-vip-showbiz/796388/kur-unikkatil-shperthen-me-kuq-e-zi/ |title=Kur Unikatil shpërthen me "Kuq E Zi"|publisher=botasot.info}}</ref> his concert in the Pristina stadium was noted to have no less than 25,000 attendees.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://telegrafi.com/ekskluzive-unikkatili-perseri-ne-stadium/|title=Ekskluzive: Unikkatili, përsëri në stadium – Telegrafi|date=15 July 2013|publisher=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=S88kN9FEVFc|title=UNIKKATIL – KONCERTI NE PRISHTINE – PRIVE KLANKOSOVA|first=|last=PriveKlanKosova|date=21 July 2012|publisher=|via=YouTube}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XQK_sRL15FM|title=Unikkatil dhe TBA – Koncert ne Prishtine RinON – RTK|first=|last=RTKrinon|date=10 June 2013|publisher=|via=YouTube}}</ref> He was commended by internationally acclaimed Albanian writer [[Ismail Kadare]] as an "inspiration to Albanians worldwide".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.albeu.com/showbiz/unikatili-e-cmoj-punen-e-kadarese/77834/|title=Unikatili: E çmoj punën e Kadaresë|publisher=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://periskopi.com/mund-ta-fitoje-unikkatili-nobelin-para-ismail-kadarese/|title=A mund ta fitojë Unikkatili Nobelin para Ismail Kadaresë? - Periskopi|date=19 October 2016|publisher=}}</ref>

Unikkatil has released five albums so far.<ref>{{cite web|url= https://www.teksteshqip.com/unikkatil/albume |title= Albumet e Unikkatil-it |publisher=teksteshqip.com}}</ref> The first album ''Shihemi N'përkujtime'' was released in 2002,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/ca/album/sh%C3%ADhemi-npërkujt%C3%ADme/1202611453|title=Shihemi N'Përkujtime on iTunes|date=4 August 2002|publisher=}}</ref> followed by ''Fjált e Pavdekshme'' in 2004,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/ca/album/fjált-e-pavdekshme/1202601334|title=Fjált E Pavdekshme on iTunes|date=1 May 2004|publisher=}}</ref> ''Prezenton The Bloody Alboz'' in 2005,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/ca/album/prezenton-the-bloody-alboz/473594365|title=Prezenton the Bloody Alboz by Unikkatil on Apple Music|date=5 November 2005|publisher=}}</ref> ''Armiqt Suprem'' in 2006 <ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/ca/album/armiqt-suprem/468211146|title=Armiqt Suprem by Unikkatil on Apple Music|date=28 November 2006|publisher=}}</ref> and ''Kanuni i Katilit'' in 2007.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/ca/album/kanuni-i-katilit/468190977|title=Kanuni I Katilit by Unikkatil on iTunes|date=17 December 2007|publisher=}}</ref>

== Personal life ==
Unikkatil finished primary school with excellent results in Prishtina, Kosovo. During high school, he was involved in fights and was kicked out of school three times. Then, he started having karate lessons. In 1995, his best friend Dren Thaqi died in a crash with a train. He dedicated a song to him called "Shpirt n'paqe". He was against the Serbian invasion in Kosovo and supported the war of KLA (Kosovo Liberation Army). Some of his songs were not played on radio, because he would have been in trouble with the Serbian government. In 1996, with his family, he immigrated to the USA. He established Conqueror Records followed by The Bloody Alboz. Except Unikkatil, the other members are Klepto, Presioni, Jetoni, Cyanide, Miloti, NAG, Rameka, TEE, Z.E.F and B-52.
