'''Insomnium''' is a Finnish [[melodic death metal]] band from [[Joensuu]], Finland. The sound and lyrical themes of their music portray darkness, sorrow, loss, pain, and nature. They are primarily a melodic death metal band with occasional elements of [[doom metal]] and [[progressive metal]].

== History ==
On 9 September 2011 Insomnium released the music video "Through the Shadows" from their 2011 album, ''[[One for Sorrow (album)|One for Sorrow]]''.<ref>{{cite web |title=New Video - Insomnium- Through The Shadows|url=http://www.metalcallout.com/metal-music-videos/new-video-insomnium-through-the-shadows.html |publisher=Metal CallOut |access-date=2011-09-09}}</ref>

In November, 2011 Insomnium embarked on their first headlining European tour with support from [[Before the Dawn (band)|Before the Dawn]] and [[MyGRAIN]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.insomnium.net/news/113/56/Album-release-dates-and-European-headline-tour|title=INSOMNIUM &#124; News|date=10 December 2011|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111210194817/http://www.insomnium.net/news/113/56/Album-release-dates-and-European-headline-tour|access-date=9 October 2020|archive-date=10 December 2011}}</ref> During April, 2012 Insomnium returned to tour the U.K. as the main support act for British [[gothic metal]] band, [[Paradise Lost (band)|Paradise Lost]]. Vreid was also present on the tour as a supporting opening act.

On 19 September 2013 Insomnium released "Ephemeral," a single from their foreshadowed 2014 album, ''[[Shadows of the Dying Sun]]'', via [[Century Media Records]]. It is the first track to feature new guitarist, Markus Vanhala ([[Omnium Gatherum]]), who replaced Ville Vänni in 2011.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.blabbermouth.net/news/insomnium-new-song-ephemeral-released/ |title=Insomnium: New Song 'Ephemeral' Released |publisher=Blabbermouth.net |date=2013-09-19 |access-date=2015-08-19}}</ref>

On 29 April 2014 Insomnium released their sixth studio album, ''[[Shadows of the Dying Sun]]''. On 30 April 2014 they made a guest appearance on [[Epica (band)|Epica's]] CD release show at [[013]] in the Netherlands.

On 19 May 2016, Insomnium announced their seventh studio album, ''[[Winter's Gate]]'' to be released on 23 September. It is a concept album consisting of a single epic 40-minute track, and it is said to be about "a group of Vikings who set out to find a fabled island west of Ireland, despite the treacherous winter drawing near."<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.blabbermouth.net/news/insomnium-to-release-winters-gate-album-book-in-september/|title=INSOMNIUM To Release 'Winter's Gate' Album, Book In September|publisher=Blabbermouth.net|date=May 19, 2016|access-date=May 19, 2016}}</ref> This album was based on the short story by Niilo, "Winter's Gate", or "Talven Portti" in the original Finnish. This story won or was nominated for many Finnish awards.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.blabbermouth.net/news/insomnium-to-release-winters-gate-album-book-in-september|title=Insomnium To Release 'Winter's Gate' Album, Book In September - Blabbermouth.net|date=19 May 2016}}</ref>

In 2019, they announced their eighth album, ''[[Heart Like a Grave]]'', would be released on 4 October 2019. With the album announcement they also confirmed that the guitarist [[Jani Liimatainen]] had joined the band as a full-time member, as he had occasionally filled in for Ville Friman on tours since 2015.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://loudwire.com/insomnium-new-album-heart-like-a-grave/|title=Insomnium Announce New Album 'Heart Like a Grave'|first=Graham|last=Hartmann|website=Loudwire|access-date=2 November 2019}}</ref>

Insomnium released a new EP, titled ''Argent Moon'', on September 17, 2021.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://metalstorm.net/events/news_comments.php?news_id=42724|title=Insomnium - Release 'The Antagonist' Song, Announce EP|publisher=[[Metal Storm (webzine)|Metal Storm]]|access-date=August 8, 2021|date=July 17, 2021}}</ref> The band also plans to release their ninth studio album in 2022.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://metalstorm.net/events/news_comments.php?news_id=42944|title=Insomnium - To Release Ninth Studio Album Next Year|publisher=[[Metal Storm (webzine)|Metal Storm]]|access-date=August 8, 2021|date=August 8, 2021}}</ref>

== Members ==
=== Current members ===
* [[Niilo Sevänen]] − bass, [[Death growl|harsh vocals]] <small>(1997−present)</small>
*[[Ville Friman]] − guitar <small>(1997−present)</small>, clean vocals <small>(2011−present)</small>
* Markus Hirvonen − drums <small>(1997−present)</small>
* Markus Vanhala − guitar <small>(2011−present)</small>
* [[Jani Liimatainen]] − guitar, [[Singing|clean vocals]] <small>(2019−present)</small>

=== Former members ===
* Tapani Pesonen − drums, guitar <small>(1997−1998)</small>
* Timo Partanen − guitar <small>(1998−2001)</small>
* [[Ville Vänni]] − guitar <small>(2001−2011)</small>
