'''EZI''' is the stage name of American singer, songwriter & actress '''Esther Zynn''' who currently resides in Los Angeles California. Before releasing music, EZI starred in the 2016 [[Nickelodeon]] television series ''[[The Other Kingdom]]''<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://theknockturnal.com/exclusive-interview-with-esther-zynn-star-of-the-other-kingdom/|title=Exclusive: Esther Zynn Talks New Show 'The Other Kingdom'|website=theknockturnal.com|accessdate=Dec 9, 2020}}</ref> Shortly after, EZI became the face of [[Steve Madden]]’s “Madden Girl” campaign<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://milled.com/SteveMaddenShoes/introducing-ezi-for-madden-girl-fwQGX82E9nLRvk2p|title=Steve Madden: Introducing EZI for Madden Girl|website=Milled|accessdate=Dec 9, 2020}}</ref> and was the first artist to sign to Madden’s independent record label '''5 Towns Records'''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/7833822/steve-madden-first-5towns-records-signing-ezi|title=Steve Madden Announces First 5Towns Records Signing, Releases First Track: Exclusive|website=Billboard.com|accessdate=8 September 2018}}</ref> Her single "Dancing in a Room" from her 2018 debut EP '''Afraid Of The Dark''' hit #32 on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' US dance charts and peaked at #13.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://earmilk.com/2018/01/11/ezi-finds-sincerity-in-pop-music-through-her-debut-afraid-of-the-dark-ep-exclusive-premiere/|title=EZI finds sincerity in pop music through her debut "Afraid of the Dark" EP|date=Jan 11, 2018|website=EARMILK|accessdate=Dec 9, 2020}}</ref> To date, the EP has accumulated over 30 million streams independently. EZI's music has also been featured in several TV shows & movies including the acclaimed [[Netflix]] original series [[Atypical]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.popsugar.co.uk/node/45258253|title=Atypical: 23 Songs From Season 2 That We Can't Stop Listening To|first=Brittany|last=Natale|date=Sep 12, 2018|website=POPSUGAR Entertainment UK|accessdate=Dec 9, 2020}}</ref> [[Perez Hilton]] is quoted calling her music “ear candy”.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://perezhilton.com/ezi-dancing-in-a-room/|title=Listen To This: Needed You! - Perez Hilton|accessdate=Dec 9, 2020}}</ref> In 2019, EZI signed a publishing deal with [[Tim Blacksmith]] and [[Danny D]]'s '''Stellar Songs''', administered by [[Warner Chappell]]. She has written songs for several other notable artists such as [[Kaskade]], [[Sabrina Claudio]], [[Kiiara]], SadMoney, Stela Cole and [[Party Favor (DJ)|Party Favor]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://genius.com/Sad-money-kaskade-and-sabrina-claudio-come-away-lyrics|title=Sad Money, Kaskade & Sabrina Claudio – Come Away|accessdate=Dec 9, 2020|via=genius.com}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Zynn was born in New York and lives in Los Angeles, as of 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.laweekly.com/music/premiere-ezi-wants-to-be-sober-with-you-9569578|title=Premiere: EZI Wants to Be "SoBer With YoU"|first=Shirley|last=Ju|date=19 June 2018|website=Laweekly.com|accessdate=8 September 2018}}</ref>
