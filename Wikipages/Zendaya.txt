'''Zendaya Maree Stoermer Coleman'''<ref name=usmag/> ({{IPAc-en|z|ə|n|'|d|eɪ|.|ə}} {{Respell|zən|DAY|ə}};<ref>As pronounced by Zendaya in the following:
* {{cite AV media |date=March 13, 2021 |title="A Slate Show" with Stephen Colbert, Feat. Megan Thee Stallion, Tom Hanks and More |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FOvc7mjDQkU |access-date=March 14, 2021 |time=1:10 |location= |publisher=[[The Late Show with Stephen Colbert]] |via=YouTube}}
* {{cite AV media |date=June 25, 2019 |title=Tom Holland, Zendaya & Jacob Batalon Answer the Web's Most Searched Questions |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CA-e5eWB7eg |access-date=March 14, 2021 |publisher=[[Wired (magazine)|Wired]] |via=YouTube}}</ref> born September 1, 1996)<ref name=allmusicbiography>{{cite web |url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/zendaya-mn0002732005/biography |title= Zendaya Biography |first= Heather |last= Phares |publisher= [[AllMusic]] |access-date= June 8, 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161001232427/http://www.allmusic.com/artist/zendaya-mn0002732005/biography |archive-date= October 1, 2016 |url-status= live }}</ref> is an American actress and singer. She is the recipient of [[List of awards and nominations received by Zendaya|numerous accolades]], including a [[Primetime Emmy Award]], a [[Satellite Awards|Satellite Award]], and a [[Saturn Awards|Saturn Award]].

Zendaya began her career as a child model and backup dancer. She rose to mainstream prominence for her role as [[Rocky Blue]] on the [[Disney Channel]] sitcom ''[[Shake It Up (American TV series)|Shake It Up]]'' (2010–2013). In 2013, Zendaya was a contestant on the [[Dancing with the Stars (U.S. season 16)|16th season]] of the dance competition series ''[[Dancing with the Stars (U.S. TV series)|Dancing with the Stars]]''. She produced and starred as the titular spy, K.C. Cooper, in the sitcom ''[[K.C. Undercover]]'' (2015–2018). Her performance as a struggling, [[Addiction|drug addict]] teenager in the [[HBO]] drama series ''[[Euphoria (American TV series)|Euphoria]]'' (2019–present) made her the youngest recipient of the Primetime Emmy Award for [[Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Lead Actress in a Drama Series|Outstanding Lead Actress in a Drama Series]]. Her film roles include the musical drama ''[[The Greatest Showman]]'' (2017), the superhero film ''[[Spider-Man: Homecoming]]'' (2017) and [[Spider-Man in film|its sequels]], the computer-animated musical comedy [[Smallfoot (film)|''Smallfoot'']] (2018), the romantic drama ''[[Malcolm & Marie]]'' (2021), the live-action/animation hybrid sports comedy ''[[Space Jam: A New Legacy]]'' (2021), and the science fiction epic ''[[Dune (2021 film)|Dune]]'' (2021).

In addition to her acting career, Zendaya has ventured into music. In 2011, she released the singles "[[Swag It Out]]" and "[[Watch Me (Bella Thorne and Zendaya song)|Watch Me]]", the latter a collaboration with [[Bella Thorne]]. She signed with [[Hollywood Records]] in 2012 and subsequently released her debut single, "[[Replay (Zendaya song)|Replay]]", which reached the top 40 on the U.S. [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]] chart. She released her [[Zendaya (album)|eponymous debut studio album]] in 2013 to moderate success and critical acclaim. Zendaya's biggest commercial success as a musician came with her [[Zac Efron]] collaboration, "[[Rewrite the Stars]]", from [[The Greatest Showman (soundtrack)|''The Greatest Showman'' soundtrack]] in 2018. The single reached within the top 20 of several [[Record chart|record charts]] and has received multi-platinum sales certifications globally.
