"'''Track Star'''" is a song by American rapper Mooski. It was released on [[TikTok]] on June 17, 2020, and other streaming services on February 19, 2021. The song was later remixed by American singer [[Chris Brown]] and American rappers [[Yung Bleu]] and [[A Boogie Wit Da Hoodie]].

==Background==
The track was released on February 19, 2021, following its success on [[TikTok]]. A remix featuring [[A Boogie wit da Hoodie]], [[Chris Brown]] and [[Yung Bleu]] was released on May 18, 2021.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Mooski drops "Track Star" remix with Chris Brown, A Boogie wit da Hoodie & Yung Bleu|url=https://thesource.com/2021/05/18/mooski-drops-trackstar-remix-with-chris-brown-a-boogie-wit-da-hoodie-yung-bleu/|url-status=live|website=[[The Source]]}}</ref>

==Music video==
A music video was released on April 7, 2021. It was directed by BenMarc and produced by VisionBank.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Mooski - Track Star (Official Video)|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KPfcycMj8BE|url-status=live|via=[[YouTube]]}}</ref> [[Coi Leray]] also stars in the video.<ref>{{Cite web|date=April 8, 2021|title=Mooski's 'Track Star' Video Stars Coi Leray As His On-And-Off Lover|url=https://uproxx.com/music/mooski-track-star-video-coi-leray/|url-status=live|access-date=August 8, 2021|website=[[Uproxx|UPROXX]]|language=en-US}}</ref> 

==Reception and chart success==
Robby Seabrook III of ''[[XXL (magazine)|XXL]]'' wrote that the track is "a melodic ode centered on a woman who has let go of a troubled relationship despite her man's attempts to fix it", and of the artist, "Everything is looking up for the young artist from Alabama, making him a perfect fit for ''XXL''<nowiki/>'s ''The Break'' this week."<ref>{{Cite web|last1=April 7|first1=Robby Seabrook IIIPublished|last2=2021|title=The Break Presents: Mooski|url=https://www.xxlmag.com/mooski-interview-the-break/|access-date=August 8, 2021|website=XXL Mag|language=en}}</ref>

In a ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' article, Mooski spoke on his rise to success: "Where I come from, it's unheard of. I've been counted out my whole life and I've always been successful because I work hard. I didn't need anybody in my corner -- I wanted them in my corner."<ref>{{Cite web|title=Chartbreaker: Mooski Always Believed in His Ability to 'Sing at a Rapper's Pace'|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/9597678/mooski-track-star-july-chartbreaker/|url-status=live|website=Billboard}}</ref>

"Track Star" debuted at No. 21 on the US [[Bubbling Under Hot 100]] on the week of February 26, 2021, before debuting at No. 99 on the main [[Billboard Hot 100|Hot 100]], peaking at No. 31 and spending a total of 20 weeks on the chart. It also peaked at number 53 in the [[United Kingdom]].
