'''Charmer''' is an American [[emo]] band from [[Marquette, Michigan]], formed in 2015. Their self-titled album was released in 2018. Their second album, Ivy, was released in 2020 on [[No Sleep Records]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.getalternative.com/artist-interview-charmer-discuss-releasing-their-album-in-the-time-of-covid/|title=Artist Interview: Charmer Discuss Releasing Their Album in the Age of Coronavirus|publisher=The Alternative|access-date=July 22, 2021}}</ref>

==History==
Charmer released a split-EP in 2021 with Canadian emo band [[Gulfer]] in 2021.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.brooklynvegan.com/stream-gulfer-and-charmers-new-split/|title=Stream Gulfer and Charmer’s new split|publisher=Brooklyn Vegan|access-date=July 22, 2021}}</ref> They will be touring the US with [[Free Throw (band)|Free Throw]] and [[Dogleg (band)|Dogleg]] in 2021 <ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.brooklynvegan.com/free-throw-and-dogleg-announce-tour-with-charmer-and-bad-luck/|title=Free Throw and Dogleg announce tour with Charmer and Bad Luck|publisher=Brooklyn Vegan|access-date=July 22, 2021}}</ref>
