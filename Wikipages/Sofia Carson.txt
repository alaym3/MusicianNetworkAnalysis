'''Sofía Daccarett Char''' (born April 10, 1993<ref name=birthinfo>{{cite web|url=http://www.jetset.com.co/edicion-impresa/temas-revista-jetset/articulo/la-barranquillera-sofia-carson-la-actriz-de-la-familia-char/129990 |title=La barranquillera Sofía Carson, la actriz de la familia Char |work=Revista Jetset |language=es |date=April 2014 |access-date=August 31, 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180612143730/http://www.jetset.com.co/edicion-impresa/temas-revista-jetset/articulo/la-barranquillera-sofia-carson-la-actriz-de-la-familia-char/129990 |archive-date=June 12, 2018 |url-status=dead  }}</ref><ref name=birthinfo2>{{cite web|url=https://www.tvguide.com/celebrities/sofia-carson/835166/|title=Sofia Carson|work=[[TV Guide]]|access-date=July 8, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160722210003/http://www.tvguide.com/celebrities/sofia-carson/835166/|archive-date=July 22, 2016|url-status=live}}</ref>), known professionally as '''Sofia Carson''', is an American actress and singer. Her first appearance on television was as a guest star on the [[Disney Channel]] series ''[[Austin & Ally]]''. In 2015, she appeared as Evie, the daughter of the [[Evil Queen (Disney)|Evil Queen]], in the Disney Channel Original Movie ''[[Descendants (2015 film)|Descendants]]'', later reprising her role in ''[[Descendants 2]]'' and ''[[Descendants 3]]''. In 2016, she appeared as Lola Perez in ''[[Adventures in Babysitting (2016 film)|Adventures in Babysitting]]'', Melanie Sanchez in ''[[Tini: The Movie]]'', and Tessa in ''[[A Cinderella Story: If the Shoe Fits]]''. In 2019, Carson starred as Ava Jalali in the [[Freeform (TV channel)|Freeform]] drama series ''[[Pretty Little Liars: The Perfectionists]]''. In June 2020, she starred in the [[Netflix]] film ''[[Feel the Beat (film)|Feel the Beat]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|author=Lauren Rearick|title=Sofia Carson's Upcoming Netflix Movie Is "Step Up" Meets "Footloose"|url=https://www.teenvogue.com/story/sofia-carson-feel-the-beat-netflix|access-date=June 21, 2020|website=Teen Vogue|language=en-us|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200622101308/https://www.teenvogue.com/story/sofia-carson-feel-the-beat-netflix|archive-date=June 22, 2020|url-status=live}}</ref>

Carson made her singing debut in 2015 with the soundtrack album [[Descendants (soundtrack)|''Descendants'']], and in the same year released the single "[[Rotten to the Core (song)|Rotten to the Core]]". In 2016, she signed to [[Hollywood Records]], and released her debut single, "[[Love Is the Name]]".

==Early life==
Carson was born Sofia Daccarett Char in [[Fort Lauderdale, Florida]],<ref name=birthinfo2 /><ref name="fame" /> to José F. Daccarett and Laura Char Carson, who both moved to Florida from [[Colombia]].<ref name=birthinfo/><ref name="barranquillera">{{cite web|date=August 3, 2015|title=Actriz barranquillera Sofía Carson vive un "cuento de hadas" en la película de Disney "Descendants"|url=http://www.elheraldo.co/tendencias/actriz-barranquillera-sofia-carson-vive-un-cuento-de-hadas-en-la-pelicula-de-disney|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150824004224/http://www.elheraldo.co/tendencias/actriz-barranquillera-sofia-carson-vive-un-cuento-de-hadas-en-la-pelicula-de-disney|archive-date=August 24, 2015|access-date=September 2, 2015|work=El Heraldo|language=es}}</ref> Sofia chose the artistic name "Carson" after her American maternal grandmother, Lauraine Carson.<ref>{{cite web|date=December 24, 2015|title=Actriz de Disney, Sofía Carson, pasa Navidad en Barranquilla|url=http://www.elheraldo.co/tendencias/actriz-de-disney-sofia-carson-pasa-navidad-en-barranquilla-235329|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160914215848/http://www.elheraldo.co/tendencias/actriz-de-disney-sofia-carson-pasa-navidad-en-barranquilla-235329|archive-date=September 14, 2016|access-date=September 6, 2016|work=El Heraldo|language=es}}</ref> Through her mother, Carson is related to the [[Fuad Char|Char family]] of Colombian politicians.<ref name=birthinfo/><ref>{{cite web|date=December 9, 1988|title="Declaro abiertas las fiestas desde ya": Laura Char, reina del Carnaval|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1706&dat=19881209&id=SK4cAAAAIBAJ&sjid=PFMEAAAAIBAJ&pg=6884,3560477&hl=en|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151120135226/https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1706&dat=19881209&id=SK4cAAAAIBAJ&sjid=PFMEAAAAIBAJ&pg=6884,3560477&hl=en|archive-date=November 20, 2015|access-date=September 1, 2015|work=[[El Tiempo (Colombia)|El Tiempo]]|language=es}}</ref>

==Career==

=== Music ===
[[File:Sofia Carson at Dress Rehearsal for "A Capitol Fourth" Concert and Celebration.jpg|alt=|left|thumb|Carson in 2017]]
