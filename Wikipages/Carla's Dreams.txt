'''Carla's Dreams''' are a [[Moldova]]n musical project.<ref>{{Cite web|url = http://www.europafm.ro/interviu-carlas-dreams-despre-controversatul-proiect-muzical-si-despre-noul-single-te-rog-video/|title = EXCLUSIV: Interviu Carla's Dreams. Despre controversatul proiect muzical și noul single, "Te rog" – VIDEO|date = 31 October 2015|website = Europa FM}}</ref> Starting in 2012, the band is an anonymous group of singers and composers who sing in [[Moldavian language|Romanian]], English, and [[Russian language|Russian]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://adevarul.ro/entertainment/muzica/cine-ascunde-masca-solistului-carlas-dreams-anonimatul-nu-a-fost-optiune-fost-unica-metoda-pastram-identitatea-1_5593f83dcfbe376e35c23f2e/index.html |title=Cine se ascunde sub masca solistului Carla's Dreams: "Anonimatul nu a fost o opţiune, a fost unica metodă să ne păstrăm identitatea" |work=Adevărul |author=Victor Arvunescu |date=1 July 2015}}</ref>

Carla's Dreams combines several musical styles, including [[hip hop]], [[jazz]], rock and pop. The first song produced by Carla's Dreams was ''Dă-te'' ("Get Off"), an "underground" song with obscene content.

Carla's Dreams has launched in [[Romania]] in 2013, along with [[Inna]] with the song P.O.H.U.I., later to sing with [[Loredana Groza|Loredana]] ''Lumea ta'' ("Your World"), and in 2015 with [[Delia Matache|Delia]], releasing songs ''Cum ne noi'' ("How We Us") and ''Da, mamă'' ("Yes, Mom").<ref>{{Cite web|url = http://a1.ro/timp-liber/muzica/carlas-dreams-si-delia-una-dintre-cele-mai-spectaculoase-colaborari-din-romania-lanseaza-singleul-cum-ne-noi-id396559.html|title = Carla's Dreams și Delia, una dintre cele mai spectaculoase colaborări din România, lansează single-ul "Cum ne noi"|date = 14 May 2015|website = Antena 1}}</ref> With nine number-one singles on the Romanian [[Airplay 100 (Romania)|Airplay 100]], "Carla's Dreams" is the most-successful act in [[List of Airplay 100 number ones of the 2010s|the chart's history]]. Their latest number-one was "N-aud" in October 2021, their ninth. In June 2020, the group's singles "Seară de seară" and "Secrete" were both in the Airplay 100's top ten, sitting at numbers seven and ten respectively.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.unsitedemuzica.ro/topuri/Top-Airplay-100-Vara-asta-are-alt-ritm-cu-piesa-de-pe-primul-loc-p10270|title=Top Airplay 100 - Vara asta are alt ritm cu piesa de pe primul loc!|trans-title=Top Airplay 100 – The song at number ones gives this summer another rhythm!|author=Arvunescu, Victor|date=22 June 2020|accessdate=22 June 2020|language=Romanian|publisher=Un site de muzică}}</ref>

They were chosen by Disney to provide the Romanian dub of a character in the animated movie Frankenweenie. <ref>{{Cite web|url=https://m.cinemagia.ro/stiri/20-de-ani-de-magie-de-la-primul-film-disney-dublat-n-limba-romn-45661/|title=20 de ani de magie de la primul film Disney dublat în limba română - CineMagia.ro}}</ref>
