'''Hanna Emilia Rydberg-Mitiku''', known simply as '''Emilia''' (born 5 January 1978),<ref>[http://www.allmusic.com/artist/p355006/charts-awards/billboard-singles Emilia Rydberg Charts], Allmusic</ref> is a [[Swedish popular music|Swedish pop music]] and [[soul music|soul]] singer.<ref>Her father TESHOME MITIKU is also popular Amharic musician in Ethiopia.[http://www.discogs.com/artist/74162-Emilia Emilia Rydberg] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141023195736/http://www.discogs.com/artist/74162-Emilia |date=23 October 2014}}</ref> Her 1998 hit single "[[Big Big World (song)|Big Big World]]" reached number one in the [[music charts]] in several countries.<ref>[http://www.br-online.de/bayern3/musik-center/ohw-emilia-big-big-world-ID1254388014057.xml One Hit Wonder: Emilia: "Big Big World" | Musik-Center | BAYERN 3 | BR] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110604233130/http://www.br-online.de/bayern3/musik-center/ohw-emilia-big-big-world-ID1254388014057.xml |date=4 June 2011 }}. Google Books (12 December 1998). Retrieved on 2011-05-30.</ref>

==Life and career==
Rydberg's father is the [[Ethiopia]]n popular singer {{ill|Tèshomè Mitiku|fr|Tèshomè Meteku}}. Her mother is Swedish, Malena Rydberg. As a student she attended the [[Adolf Fredrik's Music School]] in [[Stockholm]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.dn.se/kultur-noje/emilia-mitiku-for-forsta-gangen-i-livet-har-jag-en-plan |title=Emilia Mitiku: "För första gången i livet har jag en plan" |author=Hans Kronbrink |publisher=Dagens Nyheter (Swedish daily newspaper) |date=17 June 2012 |access-date=2014-11-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150326141326/http://www.dn.se/kultur-noje/emilia-mitiku-for-forsta-gangen-i-livet-har-jag-en-plan/ |archive-date=26 March 2015 |url-status=live}}</ref>

Rydberg was discovered in 1996 by Lars Anderson, son of [[ABBA]]'s manager, [[Stig Anderson]].  She used the [[mononym]] of Emilia in the first years of her career, but more recently she has started to perform under the name of Emilia Mitiku, using her father's surname. In 2009, she competed in [[Melodifestivalen 2009|Melodifestivalen]], the Swedish national selection for [[Eurovision Song Contest]], with the song "[[You're My World (Emilia Rydberg song)|You're My World]]". She advanced directly from her heat; however, she placed ninth in the final. She released her album ''My World'' in 2009. Her single "Teardrops" was released to radio stations on 2 June 2009.<ref>[http://www.jnytt.se/nojesnyhet/21526/emilia-ar-tillbaka-i-hetluften-igen Jönköping | Jnytt.se | Nöje | Melodifestivalen | Emilia är tillbaka i hetluften igen] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110721160426/http://www.jnytt.se/nojesnyhet/21526/emilia-ar-tillbaka-i-hetluften-igen |date=21 July 2011 }}. Jnytt.se. Retrieved on 2011-05-30.</ref><ref>[http://svt.se/svt/jsp/Crosslink.jsp?d=100325&a=1316256&lid=puff_1316166&lpos=lasMer Melodifestivalen 2009] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090218135116/http://svt.se/svt/jsp/Crosslink.jsp?d=100325&a=1316256&lid=puff_1316166&lpos=lasMer |date=18 February 2009 }}. svt.se (4 November 2008). Retrieved on 2011-05-30.</ref> Her duet with {{ill|Ákos Dobrády|hu|Dobrády Ákos}}, "Side By Side" (Szerelemre hangolva), was recorded in both [[Hungarian language|Hungarian]] and English languages and issued the same year.<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cBH20t6WEwM Dobrády Ákos feat Emilia – Szerelemre hangolva (official, HD)] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161219135005/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cBH20t6WEwM |date=19 December 2016 }}. YouTube (12 November 2009). Retrieved on 2011-05-30.</ref>

Emilia recorded a [[cover version]] of [[Rihanna]]'s "[[We Found Love]]" for a studio session in Stockholm which was published on [[YouTube]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PHCJZh0xmEw&feature=player_embedded |title=Emilia Mitiku &#124; We Found Love (Live) |via=YouTube |access-date=2013-02-02 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160414101012/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PHCJZh0xmEw&feature=player_embedded |archive-date=14 April 2016 |url-status=live }}</ref> On 25 June 2012 she released the single "Lost Inside".<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.digitalspy.co.uk/music/thesound/a385601/ones-to-watch-emilia-mitiku.html |title=Ones to watch: Emilia Mitiku - Music Blog |website=Digital Spy |date=2012-06-07 |access-date=2013-02-02 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120608203850/http://www.digitalspy.co.uk/music/thesound/a385601/ones-to-watch-emilia-mitiku.html |archive-date=8 June 2012 |url-status=live }}</ref>
