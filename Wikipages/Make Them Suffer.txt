'''Make Them Suffer''' is an Australian [[symphonic]] [[deathcore]]/[[metalcore]] band from [[Perth]],<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.loudersound.com/features/the-50-best-metal-albums-of-2020/2|title=The best metal albums of 2020|publisher=Loudersound|accessdate=8 January 2020}}</ref><ref name="KillYourStereo">{{cite web|url=https://killyourstereo.com/reviews/1109255/make-them-suffer-how-to-survive-a-funeral/|title=Album Review: Make Them Suffer - 'How To Survive A Funeral'|date=18 June 2020|website=killyourstereo.com}}</ref> currently signed to [[Rise Records]]. Their debut album ''Neverbloom'' peaked at No.&nbsp;56 on the [[ARIA Charts|ARIA Album charts]] in June 2012, while its follow up ''Old Souls'' debuted at No.&nbsp;30 upon release in May 2015. After the band signed with Rise, the label released an expanded version of ''Old Souls'' in August 2016, containing also the EP ''Lord of Woe'' (originally self-released in 2010) and a new track, "Ether".

==History==
They self-released their debut EP, ''Lord of Woe'', on 27 September 2010.<ref name="iTunes Store Lord of Woe Page">[https://itunes.apple.com/gb/album/lord-of-woe/id421186809] {{dead link|date=October 2020}}</ref>

In February 2012, the group signed with [[Roadrunner Records]] and in May that year released their debut album ''Neverbloom''.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.blabbermouth.net/news/make-them-suffer-signs-with-roadrunner-records/ |title= MAKE THEM SUFFER Signs With ROADRUNNER RECORDS |website=Blabbermouth.net |date=8 February 2012}}</ref>

They released a single entitled "Let Me In" on 12 May 2014. 

On 29 May 2015, Make Them Suffer released their second full-length album entitled ''Old Souls''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://newnoisemagazine.com/album-review-suffer-old-souls/|title=Album Review: Make Them Suffer - "Old Souls" - New Noise Magazine|date=2 June 2015|website=Newnoisemagazine.com}}</ref>

The band signed to [[Rise Records]] internationally in January 2016.<ref name="sign">{{cite web|url=http://themusic.com.au/news/all/2016/01/07/perth-band-make-them-suffer-sign-international-deal-with-rise-records/|title=Perth Band Make Them Suffer Sign International Deal With Rise Records|website=Themusic.com.au}}</ref>

On 14 June 2016, they released a new track titled "Ether".<ref name="synth">{{cite web|url=http://www.metalinjection.net/video/make-them-suffers-new-song-ether-is-pretty-damn-catchy|title=MAKE THEM SUFFER's New Song "Ether" Is Pretty Damn Catchy |website=Metalinjection.net}}</ref>

On 7 June 2017, the band released the single "Fireworks" and announced their third album ''[[Worlds Apart (Make Them Suffer album)|Worlds Apart]]'', released in July of that year.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.metalsucks.net/2017/07/31/make-them-suffers-worlds-apart-will-not-make-you-suffer/|title=Make Them Suffer's Worlds Apart Will Not Make You Suffer |website=Metalsucks.net|date=31 July 2017}}</ref>

On 9 June 2017, it was announced that Chris Arias-Real, Lachlan Monty, and Louisa Burton were no longer in the band. Jaya Jeffery was announced as the new bassist and Booka Nile was announced as keyboardist/clean vocals.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.huffingtonpost.com/entry/australian-deathcore-band-make-them-suffer-aim-to_us_5970b177e4b0d72667b05e97|title=Australian Deathcore Band 'Make Them Suffer' Aim To Progress Their Unique, Exciting Heavy Music With New Album and Upcoming Headline Tour|first=Kelly|last=Frazier|date=22 July 2017|website=Huffingtonpost.com}}</ref>

On 24 July 2018, a new single "27" was released.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.metalsucks.net/2018/07/27/make-yourself-listen-to-make-them-suffers-27/|title=Make Yourself Listen to Make Them Suffer's "27" |website=Metalsucks.net|date=27 July 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://killyourstereo.com/news/1103582/make-them-suffer-reset-the-system-with-27|title=Make Them Suffer Reset The System With '27' |website=Killyourstereo.com}}</ref>

In September of 2018, the band toured America with After the Burial and the Acacia Strain.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.altpress.com/news/after-the-burial-the-acacia-strain-north-american-tour-2018/|title=After The Burial and the Acacia Strain announce North American tour|date=18 July 2018|website=Altpress.com}}</ref>

In December 2018, a North American tour with Kingdom of Giants, [[Chelsea Grin]], and [[Born of Osiris]] was announced to take place in 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://metalinjection.net/tour-dates/born-of-osiris-announce-2019-north-american-tour-dates-with-chelsea-grin-make-them-suffer|title=BORN OF OSIRIS Announce 2019 North American Tour Dates with CHELSEA GRIN, MAKE THEM SUFFER|first=Robert|last=Pasbani|date=17 December 2018|website=Metalinjection.net|accessdate=10 October 2020}}</ref>

They have toured with many notable bands, including [[Architects (British band)|Architects]], [[Thy Art Is Murder]], [[Parkway Drive]], [[The Amity Affliction]], [[Pvris]], [[Bleeding Through]], [[Stick to Your Guns (band)|Stick to Your Guns]], [[Northlane]], [[August Burns Red]], [[Oceano (band)|Oceano]], [[Job for a Cowboy]], [[Whitechapel (band)|Whitechapel]], and [[War from a Harlots Mouth]]. The band completed a headlining world tour in 2017 in support of the Worlds Apart album through Canada, USA, Europe, UK and Australia with supporting acts [[Wage War]], [[Enterprise Earth]], [[Novelists (band)|Novelists]], Spite, and [[Alpha Wolf (band)|Alpha Wolf]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.metalobsession.net/2011/12/18/thy-art-is-murder-the-corner-hotel-live/ |title=THY ART IS MURDER, WAR FROM A HARLOTS MOUTH (GERMANY), THE BRIDE, MAKE THEM SUFFER, TRAINWRECK @ THE CORNER HOTEL, MELBOURNE |publisher=Metalobsession.net |date= 12 October 2011}}</ref>

In 2020, keyboardist, clean vocalist Booka Nile took time away from the band during COVID-19 to appear on the eighth season of [[Married at First Sight (Australian TV series)|Married At First Sight Australia]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=brownypaul|date=2020-10-04|title=Make Them Suffer’s Booka Nile Is Getting Married… At First Sight!|url=https://wallofsoundau.com/2020/10/04/make-them-suffers-booka-nile-is-getting-married-at-first-sight/|access-date=2020-11-11|website=Wall Of Sound|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=brownypaul|date=2020-11-11|title=Here’s Your First Look At Booka Nile On Married At First Sight 2021….LOL|url=https://wallofsoundau.com/2020/11/11/heres-your-first-look-at-booka-nile-on-married-at-first-sight-2021-lol/|access-date=2020-11-11|website=Wall Of Sound|language=en-US}}</ref>
