'''Annalisa Scarrone''' (born 5 August 1985)<ref>http://www.annalisaofficial.it/bio</ref> better known as simply '''Annalisa '''or''' Nali''', is an Italian singer-songwriter, Record producer and TV personality. After being part of two bands, ''Elaphe Guttata'' and ''leNoire'' <small>(Ex Malvasia)</small>, she became famous after participating in the tenth edition of the talent show [[Amici di Maria De Filippi]] in 2010–2011, where she finished in second place.<ref>{{cite news|last=Valentina Scionti|title=Annalisa Scarrone: preferisco il premio della critica|url=http://music.fanpage.it/annalisa-scarrone-preferisco-il-premio-della-critica/|accessdate=21 March 2012|newspaper=FanPage Music|date=13 April 2011}}</ref> Subsequently, Annalisa participated in the [[Festival of Sanremo]] 2013 with the song "[[Scintille]]" and finished ninth.<ref name="sanremo">{{Cite web|url=http://www.davidemaggio.it/archives/70799/sanremo-2013-la-classifica-finale|title=SANREMO 2013: LA CLASSIFICA FINALE COMPLETA|website=DavideMaggio.it|access-date=23 August 2016}}</ref>

Annalisa participated at the [[Sanremo Music Festival 2015]] with the song "[[Una finestra tra le stelle]]" and finished fourth.<ref name="sanremo2015">{{Cite web|url=http://www.rsvn.it/festival-di-sanremo-la-classifica-completa.lits6c54384.htm|title=Radio Savona News – Leggi le News più aggiornate di Savona e Provincia|website=www.rsvn.it|access-date=23 August 2016}}</ref> In 2016, she returned to the [[Sanremo Music Festival 2016|Sanremo Music Festival]] with the single "[[Il diluvio universale (song)|Il diluvio universale]]" finishing eleventh, and again in the [[Sanremo Music Festival 2018|2018 edition]] with "[[Il mondo prima di te]]", finishing third, and most recently she finished seventh with "[[Dieci (Annalisa song)|Dieci]]" in [[Sanremo Music Festival 2021|Sanremo 2021]]''.'' She received several awards, including a [[MTV Europe Music Award]], a [[Wind Music Awards|Wind Music Award]] and a Lutezia Award for her songwriting works, and nominations at the [[Kid's Choice Award]] and [[World Music Awards]].

She has recorded 6 albums and 25 singles, receiving 8 Platinum disc and 10 [[Gold disc]]s, and selling over 1 million copies.<ref name="naliottantamila"/><ref name=diamantegold/><ref name=senzariservagold/><ref name="senzariservaplatinum">{{Cite web|url=http://www.fimi.it/archivio_certificazioni.php |title=FIMI – Pagina non trovata – FIMI |website=www.fimi.it |access-date=23 August 2016 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141006100124/http://www.fimi.it/archivio_certificazioni.php |archivedate=6 October 2014 }}</ref><ref name=unafinestragold>{{Cite web|url=http://www.fimi.it/certificazioni|title=Certificazioni Digital Settimana 10 del 2015|newspaper=[[Federazione Industria Musicale Italiana]]|date=13 March 2015|accessdate=13 March 2015}}</ref><ref name=finestraplat>{{Cite web|url=http://www.fimi.it/certificazioni|title=Certificazioni Digital Settimana 16 del 2015|newspaper=[[Federazione Industria Musicale Italiana]]|date=8 May 2015|accessdate=8 May 2015}}</ref><ref name=vincegold/> She also wrote songs for other artists including [[Gianna Nannini]], [[Fedez]], Benji & Fede and collaborated with [[Antonello Venditti]], [[Gianluca Grignani]], [[Marcella Bella]] and [[Nomadi]].

From 2015 to 2019 she hosted the [[science divulgation]] tv program ''Tutta colpa di ... – Quelli del Cern'' on [[Italia 1]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sorrisi.com/tv/news-e-anticipazioni/annalisa-porta-la-fisica-tv-tutta-colpa-di-einstein/|title=Annalisa porta la fisica in tv: "Tutta colpa di Einstein"|date=27 November 2015|website=TV Sorrisi e Canzoni|language=it-IT|access-date=13 January 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.tvblog.it/post/1635730/tutta-colpa-di-leonardo-italia-1-annalisa-torna-conduttrice|title=Tutta colpa di Leonardo, Annalisa torna conduttrice su Italia 1 (Anteprima Blogo)|date=18 February 2019|website=TVBlog.it|access-date=13 January 2020}}</ref>

== Early life ==
She grew up in [[Carcare]] and studied music and singing from an early age. From the age of 13, she worked as a bartender and musician.<ref name="workgraduated">{{Cite web|url=http://www.mondoreality.com/amici-11-annalisa-scarrone-per-sfondare-oltre-al-talento-ci-vuole-fortuna/|title=Amici 11, Annalisa Scarrone: "Per sfondare, oltre al talento, ci vuole fortuna" |publisher=MondoReality|date=28 March 2012|language=it-IT|access-date=23 August 2016}}</ref> In 2009 she graduated from the [[University of Turin]] in [[Physics]].<ref name="workgraduated"/>

The song peaked at number 8 on the [[FIMI]] Singles Chart and was certified double platinum in Italy.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-06-08|title=Annalisa e Federico Rossi: fuori ora il videoclip di "Movimento lento"|url=http://news.mtv.it/musica/annalisa-federico-rossi-video-movimento-lento/|access-date=2021-10-18|website=News Mtv Italia}}</ref>
