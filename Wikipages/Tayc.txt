'''Julien Bouadjie'''<ref>[https://www.blk-sqr.com/cameroun-phenomene-tayc/ Cameroun - Le phénomène Tayc] {{In lang|fr}}</ref> (born 2 May 1996 in [[Marseille]]), better known as '''Tayc''' (pronounced {{respell|tye|KEE}}), is a Cameroonian-French singer songwriter. He is the lyricist of a triple album title ''Nyxia'' in 2019, two mixtapes and a number of singles.

==Career==
Born in Marseille to Cameroonian parents, he launched his musical career in 2012 after relocating to Paris. He studied theatre and dance before moving to song and writing. His first release was a10-track mixtape, ''Alchemy'' on 30 May 2017 followed by a second mixtape ''H.E.L.I.O.S'' on 27 July 2018.<ref name=rfi>{{cite news|url=https://musique.rfi.fr/musique-africaine/20190425-afro-club-tayc-wizkid-poison-mobutu-bgmfk|author=|title=Afro-club, le hit des platines avec Tayc, Wizkid, Poison Mobutu, et BGMFK|publisher=rfi.fr|date=25 April 2019|accessdate=11 January 2021|language=fr}}</ref>

Signing with the label H24 founded by [[Barack Adama]] of Sexion D'assaut, he worked on his debut studio album. In February 2019, Tayc released debut album ''NYXIA''<ref name=rfi/> which reissued two more times with, the Tome II on 21 June 2019 Tome III on 13 December 2019 all under the title ''Nyxia''. Tom II particularly concentrated on his Cameroonian roots. He featured songs from his triple album with a big concert in Trianon de Paris on 19 October 2019.<ref>[https://www.raprnb.com/2019/06/27/tayc-nous-fait-voyager-au-cameroun-dans-ewondo-ou-bami-clip/ RapRB: Tayc nous fait voyager au Cameroun dans Ewondo ou Bami] {{In lang|fr}}</ref>
