'''Marwa Outamghart''' (born 12 December 1996), commonly known as '''Marwa Loud''', is a French singer of [[Morocco|Moroccan]] origin. Her parents come from a [[Berbers|Berber]] village near Beni Mellal, Morocco. It is said that her religion is Christianity, but it is yet to be confirmed. In 2017, she was signed to [[Lartiste]]'s record label Purple Money Purple (PMP).<ref name=snepcertification>{{cite web|url=https://rapunchline.fr/rap-francais/interviews/marwa-loud-parle-de-rencontre-jul-album-signature-label-de-lartiste.html|website=Rapunchline|title=Marwa Loud parle de sa rencontre avec Jul, son album, sa signature dans le label de Lartiste|language=fr|accessdate=25 July 2019}}</ref> She is best known for a number of singles, notably "Fallait pas". Her debut album ''Loud'' was certified gold in 2018<ref>[http://www.snepmusique.com/les-disques-dor/?awards_cat=65&awards_awd=0&awards_year=0&awards_artist=LOUD&awards_title=&awards_edit_distrib=&awards_sort=date_certif-desc&awards_nb=30&submitAdvanced=Rechercher SNEP Musique: Les Certifications - Base de Données] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180408210200/http://www.snepmusique.com/les-disques-dor/?awards_cat=65&awards_awd=0&awards_year=0&awards_artist=LOUD&awards_title=&awards_edit_distrib=&awards_sort=date_certif-desc&awards_nb=30&submitAdvanced=Rechercher |date=2018-04-08 }} {{in lang|fr}}</ref> reaching number 2 on the French Albums Chart and also charting in Belgium, Netherlands and Switzerland. Marwa Loud is married and keeps her husband away from the public’s eye. Her song "Bad boy" rose to popularity after several videos on social media used the song  over clips of English footballer [[Mason Mount]] doing his signature goal celebration.<ref>{{Cite web|last=publimetro|title=Estos son los challenges de TikTok que todos hacen a inicios de año|url=https://www.metroworldnews.com/hub/entretenimiento/2021/01/18/estos-los-challenges-tiktok-todos-hacen-inicios-ano/|access-date=2021-02-03|website=Publimetro Test|language=en}}</ref>
