'''Jaden Isaiah Hossler''' (born February 8, 2001),<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.seventeen.com/celebrity/a32106354/jaden-hossler-tiktok/|title=Here's Everything You Need to Know About TikTok Star Jaden Hossler|work=[[Seventeen (American magazine)|Seventeen]]|last1=Williams|first1=Kori|date=April 10, 2020|access-date=January 18, 2021}}</ref><ref>{{Cite tweet |author=Jxdn |user=jadenhossler |number=1358674943265832960 |date=February 8, 2021 |title=Entering into my 20th birthday it felt appropriate to say this! Regardless of how you feel about me thank you for an amazing year! Blessings to you all. |access-date=February 10, 2021 |link=https://twitter.com/jadenhossler/status/1358674943265832960 |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210210210232/https://twitter.com/jadenhossler/status/1358674943265832960 |archive-date=February 10, 2021}}</ref> known professionally as '''Jxdn''' (stylized in all lowercase and pronounced "Jaden"), is an American singer, songwriter and [[TikTok]] personality from [[Chattanooga, Tennessee]].<ref name="billboard1">{{cite news |last1=Havens |first1=Lyndsey |title=Chartbreaker: jxdn Is Bringing Rock Into the TikTok Era, With Travis Barker's Help |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/rock/9428765/chartbreaker-jxdn-tiktok-comatose-travis-barker-interview |access-date=August 24, 2020 |work=Billboard |date=August 4, 2020}}</ref>  Hossler rose to prominence on the video sharing app TikTok in 2019 before beginning a music career in early 2020 with the release of his debut single "Comatose".<ref name="billboard1"/>

==Early life==
Jaden Isaiah Hossler was born in Texas and lived there until he moved to Tennessee at age 14.<ref name="forbes">{{cite news |last1=Baltin |first1=Steve |title=Q&A: Rising Star Jxdn On His Friendship With Travis Barker And Overcoming Depression |url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/stevebaltin/2020/07/19/qa-rising-star-jxdn-on-his-friendship-with-travis-barker-and-overcoming-depression/#5d5e25143d7f |access-date=August 24, 2020 |work=[[Forbes#Forbes.com|Forbes.com]] |date=July 19, 2020}}</ref> He attended Grace Baptist Academy for high school.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Jaden Hossler's High School Timeline|url=https://www.maxpreps.com/athlete/jaden-hossler/i7gwJI5EEeeT-Oz0u-e-FA/default.htm|access-date=November 15, 2020|website=MaxPreps.com}}</ref> Hossler was raised in a Christian household and grew up listening to classic rock and pop music.<ref name="people">{{cite news |last1=Merrett |first1=Robyn |title=Jaden Hossler Reveals Battle with Depression: I'd 'Pray' to 'Die in My Sleep' |url=https://people.com/tv/tiktok-jaden-hossler-reveals-battle-with-depression-song-pray/ |access-date=August 28, 2020 |work=[[People (magazine)|People]] |date=August 11, 2020}}</ref><ref name="billboard1"/>  While in high school, he had performed in multiple school plays, and during his senior year of high school, Hossler listened to artists such as [[Juice Wrld]] and [[XXXTentacion]] as he battled depression.<ref name="billboard1"/>  Hossler stated that he attempted suicide twice during the same year.<ref name="forbes"/>

==Career==
===2019–2020: Career beginnings===
In 2019, Hossler joined social media platform [[TikTok]]. Over the course of a few months, he gained millions of followers on the app and following his success on the platform, Hossler was invited to join talent agency Talent X and join the [[Sway House]], a Los Angeles-based collective of content creators.<ref name="billboard1"/>  Hossler would later leave the Sway House in May 2020.<ref name="people-hiatus">{{cite news |last1=Guglielmi |first1=Jodi |title=TikTok Star Jaden Hossler's 'Hiatus' from Sway House Was Planned Before Arrest, Source Says |url=https://people.com/tv/tiktok-jaden-hossler-taking-a-hiatus-from-sway-house-after-arrest/ |access-date=March 4, 2021 |work=People |date=June 1, 2020}}</ref> Despite this, Hossler would continue to see success on the platform, reaching 7.6 million followers in August 2020 and by December 2020, he had accumulated over 9 million followers on the platform.<ref name="billboard1"/><ref name="variety"/>

=== 2020–present: Shift to music, "La Di Die," and ''Tell Me About Tomorrow'' ===
Hossler began his music career in February 2020, when he self-released his debut single "Comatose".<ref name="billboard2">{{cite news |last1=Eggertsen |first1=Chris |title=The Deals: Travis Barker's DTA Records Signs TikTok Star Jxdn, CEEK Pacts With UMe for VR Concerts |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/business/9391617/deals-travis-barker-dta-records-ceek-virtual-reality-ume |access-date=August 28, 2020 |work=Billboard |date=May 28, 2020}}</ref>  The single caught the attention of American musician [[Travis Barker]], who signed him to his label DTA Records in a joint deal with [[Elektra Records]].<ref name="billboard2"/><ref name="forbes"/>  In May and July 2020, respectively, Hossler released the singles "Angels & Demons" and "So What".<ref name="billboard1"/>  In August 2020, Hossler released the song "Pray" in which he opens up about his depression and suicidal thoughts, using the song to fundraise for suicide prevention.<ref name="e">{{cite news |last1=Cohen |first1=Jess |title=Jaden Hossler Shares Battle With Depression, Prayed to 'Die in My Sleep' |url=https://www.eonline.com/news/1178392/tiktoks-jaden-hossler-shares-battle-with-depression-prayed-to-die-in-my-sleep |access-date=August 28, 2020 |work=[[E! Online]] |date=August 12, 2020}}</ref>  In October 2020, it was announced that Hossler was signed into a global co-publishing agreement with [[Warner Chappell Music]].<ref name="billboard-publish"/>  On October 21, 2020, he released a collaboration with [[Iann Dior]], titled "Tonight".<ref name="dujour">{{cite news |last1=Caminiti |first1=Kasey |title=Sound Bite: FINNEAS, jxdn, and Bright Eyes |url=https://dujour.com/culture/sound-bite-finneas-jxdn-bright-eyes-new-songs/ |access-date=March 4, 2021 |work=[[DuJour]] |date=October 23, 2020}}</ref>  On December 18, 2020, Hossler released the single "Better Off Dead", co-written by [[Lauv]] and [[Blackbear (musician)|Blackbear]] with co-production from Travis Barker.<ref name="clash">{{cite news |last1=Murray |first1=Robin |title=jxdn Shares New Stunner 'Better Off Dead' |url=https://www.clashmusic.com/news/jxdn-shares-new-stunner-better-off-dead |access-date=December 18, 2020 |work=[[Clash (magazine)|Clash]] |date=December 18, 2020}}</ref> Jxdn also revealed the title of his debut studio album ''Tell Me About Tomorrow''.<ref name="clash"/>

==Legal issues==
On May 25, 2020, while on a road trip in Lee County, Texas, Hossler and fellow Sway House member [[Bryce Hall]] were arrested on drug possession charges.<ref name="e-arrest">{{cite news |last1=Cohen |first1=Jess |title=TikTok Stars Bryce Hall and Jaden Hossler Arrested for Drug Offenses During Road Trip |url=https://www.eonline.com/news/1155808/tiktok-stars-bryce-hall-and-jaden-hossler-arrested-for-drug-offenses-during-road-trip |access-date=September 1, 2020 |work=E! Online |date=May 26, 2020}}</ref>  Hossler was charged with possession of a controlled substance between 4 and 400 grams and was released on bond.<ref name="e-arrest"/>
