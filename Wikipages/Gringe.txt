'''Guillaume Tranchant''' ({{IPA-fr|ɡijom tʁɑ̃ʃɑ̃}}; born 20 February 1980), better known by his stage name '''Gringe''' ({{IPA-fr|ɡʁɛ̃ʒ|}}), is a French rapper, songwriter and actor. He is one half of the French hip hop duo [[Casseurs Flowters]], along with [[Orelsan]], with whom he has released two studio albums: ''[[Orelsan et Gringe sont les Casseurs Flowters]]'' in 2013, and the [[Comment c'est loin (soundtrack)|original soundtrack]] for their 2015 film ''[[Comment c'est loin]]''.

==Career==
In 2004, Gringe formed the hip hop duo Casseurs Flowters with his colleague [[Orelsan]], and with help from French record producer [[Skread]], they released their first [[mixtape]] ''Fantasy: Episode 1'' in 2004 with 11 tracks.<ref name=charts1>[http://www.chartsinfrance.net/Orelsan/news-85758.html Charts in France: Orelsan : un nouvel album des Casseurs Flowters avec Gringe!] {{in lang|fr}}</ref> Afterwards, the two decided to focus on building their solo careers; Gringe collaborated with a number of artists including El Matador, [[Brasco]], Pit Baccardi, La Province, Jamal and Nubi, while he also appeared on the song "[[Ils sont cools]]", from Orelsan's second studio album ''[[Le chant des sirènes]]'', which peaked at number 66 on the French ([[Syndicat National de l'Édition Phonographique|SNEP]]) Singles Chart.<ref name="Ils sont cools Lescharts.com">{{cite web|url=http://lescharts.com/showitem.asp?interpret=Orelsan+feat%2E+Gringe&titel=Ils+sont+cools&cat=s |title=Lescharts.com – Orelsan feat. Gringe – Ils sont cools|publisher=[[Syndicat National de l'Édition Phonographique|Les classements single]]|language=French|accessdate=22 March 2014}}</ref>

On 3 July 2013, Gringe released his first collaborative single as [[Casseurs Flowters]] with Orelsan, "[[Bloqué]]", as a pre-release for their upcoming debut studio album,<ref name=figaro>{{cite news|url=http://www.lefigaro.fr/musique/2013/07/03/03006-20130703ARTFIG00476-orelsan-et-gringe-signent-le-retour-de-casseurs-flowters-avec-bloque.php|author=Morgane Giuliani|title=Orelsan et Gringe signent le retour de Casseurs Flowters avec Bloqué|date=3 July 2013|accessdate=13 July 2013|language=French}}</ref> ''[[Orelsan et Gringe sont les Casseurs Flowters]]'', which was released on 15 November 2013 to generally positive reviews.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/casseurs-flowters-mw0002605419 |title=Orelsan et Gringe sont les Casseurs Flowters |website=[[Allmusic]].com |accessdate=22 March 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/fr/album/orelsan-et-gringe-sont-les/id732848459 |title=Orelsan et Gringe sont les Casseurs Flowters – iTunes |language=French |publisher=[[iTunes]] |date=18 November 2013 |accessdate=22 March 2014}}</ref> On 9 December 2015, Orelsan and Gringe released their second collective studio album ''[[Comment c'est loin (soundtrack)|Comment c'est loin]]'', which also served as the soundtrack for their comedy [[Comment c'est loin|film of the same name]], peaking at number 24 on the French Albums Chart and earning a [[List of music recording certifications|platinum certification]] in France 13 months later.

In April 2017, Gringe announced that he was working on his debut solo album, but did not provide any exact date for its release.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.booska-p.com/new-les-casseurs-flowters-annoncent-3-surprises-video-n73127.html |title=LES CASSEURS FLOWTERS ANNONCENT 3 SURPRISES ! [VIDEO] |language=French |date=3 April 2017 |publisher=BOOSKA-P |accessdate=5 April 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://rap2tess.fr/rap-francais/rapgame/casseurs-flowters-ont-deux-grandes-annonces-a-faire.html |title=Les Casseurs Flowters ont deux grandes annonces à faire ! |language=French |date=4 April 2017 |website=Rap2Tess.fr |accessdate=5 April 2017}}</ref>

On 3 November 2018, Gringe released his first solo album ''Enfant lune''.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.mouv.fr/rap-fr/gringe-son-album-enfant-lune-est-enfin-dehors-et-en-ecoute-345439|title=Gringe : son album "Enfant Lune" est enfin dehors et en écoute !|work=Mouv|access-date=8 November 2018|language=fr-FR}}</ref>
