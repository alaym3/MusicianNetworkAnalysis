'''Alexander García''' is a Mexican singer-songwriter of [[regional Mexican music]] known professionally as '''El Fantasma'''.
Mide 1.94

== Life ==
Alexander García was born in Las Cañas, [[Durango]]. He worked as a gardener for six years before becoming a singer.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|last=Thorpe|first=JeriLynn|date=2019-02-11|title=Regional Mexican artist El Fantasma to perform in Laredo for the first time|url=https://www.lmtonline.com/local/article/Regional-Mexican-artist-El-Fantasma-to-perform-in-13607680.php|url-status=live|access-date=2021-11-11|website=Laredo Morning Times|language=en-US}}</ref>

He performs [[regional Mexican music]]<ref>{{Cite web|last=Balbastro|first=Gladis|date=2021-10-26|title=La vez que "El Fantasma" compró una camioneta en una borrachera; así fue el momento|url=https://heraldodemexico.com.mx/espectaculos/2021/10/26/la-vez-que-el-fantasma-compro-una-camioneta-en-una-borrachera-asi-fue-el-momento-video-348019.html|url-status=live|access-date=2021-11-11|website=El Heraldo de México|language=es}}</ref> and [[corrido]] songs about hardworking people. On social media, fans nickname him the "King from the Underground."<ref name=":0" />

His nickname began because his friends and family call him El Fanta.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|last=Roiz|first=Jessica|date=2019-04-26|title=El Fantasma Reveals Story Behind His Artistic Name at 2019 Billboard Latin Music Awards: Watch|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/latin/8508937/el-fantasma-billboard-latin-music-awards-red-carpet|url-status=live|access-date=2021-11-11|website=Billboard|language=en}}</ref> He was finalist at the 2019 [[Billboard Latin Music Awards]].<ref name=":1" />

In 2019, El Fantasma's single "Encantadora" charted number 1 on [[Regional Mexican Airplay]]. On August 14, 2021, his single "Soy Buen Amigo" charted number 1 on Regional Mexican Airplay. It also charted 27 on [[Hot Latin Songs]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Zellner|first=Xander|date=2021-08-11|title=El Fantasma Scores Second Regional Mexican Airplay No. 1 With 'Soy Buen Amigo'|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/business/chart-beat/9613019/el-fantasma-soy-buen-amigo-tops-regional-mexican-airplay/|url-status=live|access-date=2021-11-11|website=Billboard|language=en}}</ref>
