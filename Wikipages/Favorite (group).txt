'''Favorite''' ({{korean|hangul=페이버릿|rr=Peibeorit}}) is a [[South Korea]]n [[girl group]] formed by Astory Entertainment in 2017.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://people.search.naver.com/search.naver?where=nexearch&sm=tab_ppn&query=%EA%B7%B8%EB%A3%B9favorite&os=6062487&ie=utf8&key=PeopleService|title=페이버릿 프로필|publisher=Naver|language=ko}}</ref> They debuted on July 5, 2017, with extended play ''My Favorite''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tenasia.hankyung.com/archives/1250399|title='V앱' 페이버릿, 데뷔곡 '파티 타임' 응원법 영상 공개|publisher=Ten Asia|language=ko|date=2017-07-07}}</ref> The group debuted in Japan with single album ''Catch Me'', released on November 6, 2019.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://favorite-official.jp/2019/11/01/%e3%80%8cfavorite%e3%80%8d%e3%80%80japan-1st-single-catch-me%e3%80%80%e7%99%ba%e5%a3%b2%e8%a8%98%e5%bf%b5%e3%83%8d%e3%83%83%e3%83%88%e3%82%b5%e3%82%a4%e3%83%b3%e4%bc%9a%e3%80%80/|title=「FAVORITE」 JAPAN 1st Single "Catch Me" 発売記念ネットサイン会 開催！|publisher=Favorite Jp Official|language=ja|date=2019-11-01}}</ref>
