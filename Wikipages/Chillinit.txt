'''Blake James Turnell''' (born 1993),<ref name="Thai">{{cite web |url=https://www.thaiexaminer.com/thai-news-foreigners/2019/08/11/young-australian-rapper-star-chillinit420-hotel-thai-police-marijuana-use-thailand/ |title=Aussie rap star taken in by Thai police after raid on his hotel following online posts of party drug use |work=Thai Examiner |date=11 August 2019 |access-date=4 September 2019 |author1=Boonkong, Carla |author2=Nguyen, Son |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190904085738/https://www.thaiexaminer.com/thai-news-foreigners/2019/08/11/young-australian-rapper-star-chillinit420-hotel-thai-police-marijuana-use-thailand/ |archive-date=4 September 2019 |url-status=live}}</ref> known professionally as '''Chillinit''' (stylised as '''ChillinIT'''), is a rapper from [[Sydney]], [[New South Wales]]. He released his debut studio album ''Women Weed & Wordplay'' in October 2018.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://thesource.com/2018/11/08/australia-artist-chillinits-independent-debut-album-goes-1/ |title=Australia artist Chillinit's independent debut album goes #1 |work=[[The Source]] |date=8 November 2018 |access-date=20 August 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190819234227/http://thesource.com/2018/11/08/australia-artist-chillinits-independent-debut-album-goes-1/ |archive-date=19 August 2019 |url-status=live}}</ref>

==Career==
In August 2019, Turnell released the standalone single "Freedom", which became his first charting single in Australia, which peaked at number 87 on the [[ARIA Charts|ARIA Singles Chart]].<ref name="auspOp2"/>

His second studio album ''The Octagon'' was released on 31 January 2020, and peaked at number 2 on the [[ARIA Charts#Charts|ARIA Albums Chart]] the week following its release.<ref name="Octagon"/><ref name="auspOp1"/> The album's [[lead single]], "Laying Low", peaked at number 72 on the ARIA Singles Chart in February 2020.<ref name="auspOp2"/>

In October 2021, ChillinIT released "Susan’s Son", the lead single from his forthcoming album ''Family Ties'', scheduled for release in November 2021.<ref name="FamiyTies announcement"/>
