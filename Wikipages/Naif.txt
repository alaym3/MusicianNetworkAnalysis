'''Naif''' ({{IPA-ar|naːˈiːf|pron}}, {{lang-ar|نايف}}) is a [[Human settlement|locality]] in [[Deira, Dubai|Deira]] side of [[Dubai]], [[United Arab Emirates]]. It is a commercial and residential locality. Considering the nearby localities, Naif is larger in size. It is one of the oldest localities. The residents of this locality are mainly [[South Asia]]ns. The coordinates of Naif are N 25.27153° and E 55.30512°.

Naif has the second-highest population density of all communities in Dubai (after [[Ayil Nasir]]).<ref name=dhcc>[http://cpq.dhcc.ae/PDF%20Bank/Healthcare%20in%20Dubai/PopulationDistribution.pdf Existing Population and Future Holding Capacities in Dubai Urban Area] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080911062819/http://cpq.dhcc.ae/PDF%20Bank/Healthcare%20in%20Dubai/PopulationDistribution.pdf |date=2008-09-11 }}. Dubai Healthcare City. 2000</ref>

== Fire in Naif Souq ==
In 2008, A fire broke out in the Naif Souq and around 183 shops were burned down. The Dubai Municipality built a new building which has almost double the capacity of the old building.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.constructionweekonline.com/article-2768-naif-souq-to-be-rebuilt-in-just-eight-months/|title=Naif Souq to be rebuilt in just eight months {{!}} ConstructionWeekOnline.com|website=www.constructionweekonline.com|access-date=2018-10-16}}</ref>
