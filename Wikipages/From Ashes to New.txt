'''From Ashes to New''' is an American [[Rock music|rock]] band from [[Lancaster, Pennsylvania]]. The band's lineup frequently shifted in it's early years, musician Matt Brandyberry being the founding and sole constant member of the group. The band has released three studio albums, ''[[Day One (From Ashes to New album)|Day One]]'' (2016), ''[[The Future (From Ashes to New album)|The Future]]'' (2018), and ''[[Panic (From Ashes to New album)|Panic]]'' (2020). They have found moderate success with some of their singles, including "[[Through It All]]", "[[Crazy (From Ashes to New song)|Crazy]]", and "[[Panic (From Ashes to New song)|Panic]]", which reached peaks of 6, 3, and 11, respectively, on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Mainstream Rock (chart)|Mainstream Rock]] chart.<ref name="BillboardMainRock">{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/from-ashes-to-new/chart-history/hot-mainstream-rock-tracks|title=From Ashes To New Chart History|access-date=September 3, 2018}}</ref>

== History ==
From Ashes to New was formed in 2013 in Lancaster, Pennsylvania, releasing their debut single, "My Fight", followed by an eponymous [[extended play]].<ref name="AllMusicBio"/> In 2015, they released a second extended play, ''Downfall'', as a taste of their debut album, set to be released in 2016.<ref name="roques-interview">{{cite web|last1=Roques|first1=Alice|title=INTERVIEW: FROM ASHES TO NEW|url=http://rockrevoltmagazine.com/interview-from-ashes-to-new/|website=Rock Revolt Magazine|access-date=October 23, 2015}}</ref> ''[[Day One (From Ashes to New album)|Day One]]'' was released on February 26, 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/day-one/id1053939663#|title=Day One|work=iTunes}}</ref> A deluxe edition of the album was released exclusively online on November 18, 2016. It includes an acoustic version of "Lost and Alone" as well as other new tracks.<ref>{{cite tweet|user=FromAshestoNew|author=FATN|number=799635181183188992|date=November 18, 2016|title=The 'Day One' digital deluxe version is officially out now!  iTunes:   Stream:…|access-date=October 31, 2017}}</ref> This deluxe edition was exclusively digital, with no [[Compact Disc]] or [[LP Record]] versions available.

The band has also recorded a song with [[CFO$]], "Hail the Crown", the main theme song for [[WWE 205 Live]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BBmTHAcJnbM|title=WWE 205 Live - Hail The Crown feat. From Ashes to New (Official Theme)|last=WWEMusic|date=December 6, 2016|access-date=October 31, 2017|via=YouTube}}</ref>

In December 2016, they began recording for their second studio album. On March 11, 2017, it was announced that drummer Tim D'onofrio and lead vocalist Chris Musser had decided to leave the band, and that [[Mat Madiro]] had stepped in as their new drummer.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/FromAshesToNew/posts/633447086854030|title=From Ashes to New|website=www.facebook.com|access-date=October 31, 2017 |quote=As we mentioned in our live stream, there are some changes happening with From Ashes to New. Unfortunately, Tim and Chris decided this wasn't for them anymore and have ultimately stepped away from the band.}}</ref> The band began looking for a new lead singer through social media websites. After a series of auditions submitted by fans, Danny Case (formerly of Vanity Strikes) was revealed to have been chosen as the new lead singer on July 13, 2017.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://musicmayhemmagazine.com/ashes-new-announce-new-vocalist-danny-case/|title=From Ashes To New Announce New Vocalist Danny Case|publisher=Loudwire|author=Andrew Wendowski|date=February 6, 2018}}</ref>

On February 1, 2018, the band released "Crazy", the first single from their upcoming album ''[[The Future (From Ashes to New album)|The Future]]''. The full album was released on April 20, 2018.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://loudwire.com/from-ashes-to-new-revamped-lineup-new-song-crazy/ |title=FROM ASHES TO NEW RETURN WITH REVAMPED LINEUP, NEW SONG ‘CRAZY’!|publisher=Loudwire|author=SethM |date=February 6, 2018}}</ref> The album debuted at number 163 on the [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]] chart.<ref name="AllMusicBio">{{cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/from-ashes-to-new-mn0003377221/biography|title=From Ashes to New - Biography & History - AllMusic|website=AllMusic|access-date=September 3, 2018}}</ref>

On October 4, 2019, a remix of [[The Hu]]'s "Yuve Yuve Yu" was released, featuring new vocals by Case.<ref name="The HU">{{cite magazine |url=https://www.loudersound.com/news/the-hu-release-new-version-of-yuve-yuve-yu-with-english-vocal-from-danny-case |title=The Hu release new version of Yuve Yuve Yu with English vocal from Danny Case |first=Fraser |last=Lewry |magazine=[[Metal Hammer]] |date=October 4, 2019 |access-date=December 14, 2019}}</ref>
