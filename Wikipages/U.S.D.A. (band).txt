'''U.S.D.A.''' ('''United Street Dopeboyz of America''') is an American hip hop group from [[Atlanta, Georgia]], United States. The group's founder, Atlanta-based rapper [[Jeezy]] (formerly known as Young Jeezy), officially brought the group mainstream after his departure from [[Boyz n da Hood]]. The original group consisted of Young Jeezy, Slick Pulla and [[Blood Raw]]. Blood Raw has since left the group.
