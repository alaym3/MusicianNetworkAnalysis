'''Marz''' ({{lang-fa|مرز}}; also known as '''Ramz''')<ref>{{GEOnet3|-3746794|Marz}}</ref> is a village in [[Geshmiran Rural District]], in the [[Central District (Manujan County)|Central District]] of [[Manujan County]], [[Kerman Province]], [[Iran]]. At the 2006 census, its population was 247, in 50 families.<ref>{{IranCensus2006|08}}</ref>
