'''Kim Hyun-ah''' (born June 6, 1992), better known by the [[Mononymous person|mononym]] '''Hyuna''', is a South Korean singer-songwriter, rapper and model. She debuted as a member of the girl group [[Wonder Girls]] in February 2007. After leaving the ensemble shortly after, Hyuna subsequently left [[JYP Entertainment]] and joined the girl group [[4Minute]], under [[Cube Entertainment]]. 4Minute debuted in June 2009 and went on to become one of the most popular girl groups in the country. In 2010, Hyuna began a solo career with a style she described as "performance-oriented music".<ref name="Interview">{{cite news|url=http://www.interviewmagazine.com/music/hey-sexy-lady-hyuna-k-pops-gangnam-girl/|title=HEY, SEXY LADY: HYUNA, KPOP'S GANGNAM GIRL|author=Robert Michael Poole|work=[[Interview (magazine)|Interview]]|access-date=November 21, 2012|date=November 16, 2012|archive-date=June 22, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170622075027/http://www.interviewmagazine.com/music/hey-sexy-lady-hyuna-k-pops-gangnam-girl|url-status=live}}</ref> Her solo debut single "[[Change (Hyuna song)|Change]]" charted at number-two on South Korea's [[Gaon Digital Chart]].

In 2011, Hyuna gained wider public recognition with the release of her first extended play ''[[Bubble Pop!]]''. The [[Bubble Pop! (song)|title track]] sold more than three million digital copies and she became the first female [[K-pop]] solo artist to reach 100 million views on a single music video on [[YouTube]]. Later the same year, she formed the duo [[Trouble Maker (duo)|Trouble Maker]] with former [[Highlight (band)|Beast]] member [[Jang Hyun-seung|Hyunseung]], releasing the hit single "Trouble Maker". In 2012, she appeared in a successful duet rendition of [[Psy]]'s worldwide hit "[[Gangnam Style]]" and later released her second extended play ''[[Melting (EP)|Melting]]'', which featured the number-one single "[[Ice Cream (Hyuna song)|Ice Cream]]". Her next two extended plays ''[[A Talk]]'' and ''[[A+ (EP)|A+]]'' yielded the successful singles "[[Red (Hyuna song)|Red]]" and "[[Roll Deep (Hyuna song)|Roll Deep]]", respectively. Following 4Minute's disbandment in mid-2016, she released her fifth extended play ''[[A'wesome]]'', which was supported by her first concert tour The Queen's Back. In 2017, she was involved in the trio [[Triple H (South Korean band)|Triple H]] with [[Pentagon (South Korean band)|Pentagon]] members [[Hui (singer)|Hui]] and [[Dawn (rapper)|E'Dawn]] before releasing her sixth extended play ''[[Following (EP)|Following]]''.

In 2018, Hyuna ended her contract with Cube Entertainment after several internal conflicts,<ref name="auto">{{Cite web|url=http://www.kukinews.com/news/article.html?no=585307=%20and%20http://news.imaeil.com/Entertainments/2018091313543902075|title=큐브 "현아-이던, 신뢰 회복 불가능" 계약만료 전 방출 결정|last=이|first=은진|date=September 13, 2013|website=kukinews|access-date=January 19, 2021|archive-date=March 27, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190327091043/http://www.kukinews.com/news/article.html?no=585307%3D%20and%20http%3A%2F%2Fnews.imaeil.com%2FEntertainments%2F2018091313543902075|url-status=live}}</ref><ref name="auto1">{{Cite news|url=http://star.mk.co.kr/new/view.php?mc=ST&year=2018&no=645998|title=[MK이슈]큐브 떠난 현아 자필편지 후폭풍…평행선은 좁혀진 적 없었다|work=star.mk|access-date=November 6, 2018|language=ko|archive-date=November 8, 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181108025929/http://star.mk.co.kr/new/view.php?mc=ST&year=2018&no=645998|url-status=live}}</ref> and signed with Psy's [[P Nation]] the following year.<ref name="auto2">{{Cite web|url=https://m.entertain.naver.com/read?oid=213&aid=0001084781|title=[단독] 현아♥이던, 싸이와 계약…2019년 활동 시작|date=January 27, 2019|access-date=January 27, 2019|archive-date=January 27, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190127205646/https://m.entertain.naver.com/read?oid=213&aid=0001084781|url-status=live}}</ref> Her first digital single under [[P Nation]] "[[Flower Shower]]" became her seventh top-ten entry on the US ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[World Digital Song Sales]]. On January 27, 2019, she signed with [[P Nation]], a new agency founded by Psy since he left [[YG Entertainment]] in May 2018.<ref name="auto2"/> On November 5, 2019, she released a single titled ''[[Flower Shower]]'', her first release under P Nation.<ref>{{cite web|title=HyunA Returns Triumphant With 'Flower Shower' Video|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/international/8542760/hyuna-flower-shower-video|website=Billboard|access-date=November 5, 2019|archive-date=November 6, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191106090541/https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/international/8542760/hyuna-flower-shower-video|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title= HyunA is a flower with attitude in her gorgeous solo comeback teaser photos |url= https://www.allkpop.com/article/2019/10/hyuna-is-a-flower-with-attitude-in-her-gorgeous-solo-comeback-teaser-photos |website= allkpop |access-date= November 5, 2019 |archive-date= November 5, 2019 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20191105021637/https://www.allkpop.com/article/2019/10/hyuna-is-a-flower-with-attitude-in-her-gorgeous-solo-comeback-teaser-photos |url-status= live }}</ref>

In January 2021, P Nation announced Hyuna's first comeback since "Flower Shower", with the release of her seventh EP ''[[I'm Not Cool]]'' and its [[I'm Not Cool (song)|title track]] of the same name, which was released on January 28.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://sports.donga.com/article/all/20210108/104831653/1|title=현아, 28일 컴백 확정|website=Sports Dong-a|language=ko|date=January 8, 2021|access-date=January 20, 2021}}</ref>

On September 9, 2021, Hyuna and [[Dawn (rapper)|Dawn]] collaborated to release their duet EP ''[[1+1=1]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=311&aid=0001341639|title=현아♥던, 9월 9일 듀엣 컴백…새 앨범 '1+1=1' 로고 모션 공개|website=Xports News|language=ko|date=August 31, 2021|access-date=August 31, 2021}}</ref>

==Personal life==
===Relationships and health===
On August 2, 2018, Hyuna revealed that she has been in a relationship with [[Pentagon (South Korean band)|Pentagon]] member [[Dawn (rapper)|E'Dawn]] since May 2016.<ref name="naver1">{{cite web|script-title=ko:현아·펜타곤 이던, 당당한 열애 인정 "2년째 열애중, 속이고 싶지 않아" |url=https://m.entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=011&aid=0003364263|website=Sedaily|date=August 3, 2018|language=ko}}</ref>

On November 28, 2019, HyunA posted a letter on Instagram where she opened up about her health. She revealed that, in 2016, she was diagnosed with depression and panic disorder. Additionally, she revealed she experienced foggy vision, causing her to collapse. Thinking they were symptoms of her panic disorder, she ignored it. However, after seeing a doctor, she received a brain wave test that concluded a diagnosis of [[vasovagal syncope]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=HyunA Reveals She Deals With Depression, Panic Disorder, And Vasovagal Syncope|url=https://www.soompi.com/article/1368410wpp/hyuna-reveals-she-deals-with-depression-panic-disorder-and-vasovagal-syncope|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191128204821/http://www.soompi.com/article/1368410wpp/hyuna-reveals-she-deals-with-depression-panic-disorder-and-vasovagal-syncope|archive-date=November 28, 2019|access-date=2021-01-08|website=Soompi|language=en}}</ref>{{unreliable source?|date=January 2021}}
