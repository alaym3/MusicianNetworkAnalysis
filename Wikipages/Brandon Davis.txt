'''Brandon Michael Davis'''<ref name=fullname /> (born May 8, 1990) is an American [[Mixed martial arts|mixed martial artist]] who competes in the [[Bantamweight (MMA)|Bantamweight]] division of the [[Ultimate Fighting Championship]] (UFC).<ref name="UFCProfile" />

==Background==
Davis began training in [[Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu]] at the age of 20, while attending [[Mississippi State University]]. His first fight came after training just for four months of training.  He later transitioned to mixed martial arts (MMA) and joined Dana White's Contender Series, where he earned his contract with UFC.<ref name="Receptra">{{Cite news|url=https://receptranaturals.com/brandon-killerb-davis/|title=Brandon "KillerB" Davis- Receptra MMA|work=Receptra|access-date=2018-06-03|language=en-US}}</ref><ref name="UFCProfile" />

== Mixed martial arts career ==

=== Early career ===
Davis fought mostly in the southern region especially his adopted state of [[Mississippi]].<ref name="Sherdog.com">{{Cite web|url=http://www.sherdog.com/fighter/Brandon-Davis-67782|title=Brandon "Killer B" Davis|last=Sherdog.com|website=Sherdog|access-date=2018-06-03}}</ref> He participated in [[Dana_White%27s_Contender_Series_Season_One#Week_4_-_August_1|Dana White's Contender Series 4]] in 2017<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.bloodyelbow.com/2018/5/6/17325150/dana-white-contender-series-returns-june-12th-mma-ufc-news|title=Dana White's Contender Series returns on June 12th|work=Bloody Elbow|access-date=2018-06-03}}</ref> where he won the fight against Austin Arnett and he was signed by UFC.<ref name="Receptra"/> He amassed a record of 8—2, on a seven winning streak of prior joining UFC.<ref name="Sherdog.com"/>{{ntsn|date=December 2018}}

===Ultimate Fighting Championship===
Davis made his promotion debut on January 20, 2018, facing [[Kyle Bochniak]], at [[UFC 220]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://mmadna.nl/thuisgevecht-voor-kyle-bochniak-tegen-brandon-michael-davis-tijdens-ufc-220-in-boston/|title=Thuisgevecht voor Kyle Bochniak tegen Brandon Michael Davis tijdens UFC 220 in Boston MMA DNA|last=DNA|first=MMA|website=mmadna.nl|language=en-US|access-date=2018-06-03}}</ref>  He lost the fight via unanimous decision.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://mmajunkie.com/2018/01/ufc-220-results-kyle-bochniak-frustrates-brandon-davis-for-unanimous-decision|title=UFC 220 results: Kyle Bochniak frustrates Brandon Davis for unanimous decision|date=2018-01-21|work=MMAjunkie|access-date=2018-06-03|language=en-US}}</ref>

On February 18, 2018, Davis was scheduled to meet [[Humberto Bandenay]]  at [[UFC Fight Night: Cowboy vs. Medeiros]]. However, due to visa issue, Bandenay was pulled from the card and was replaced by [[Steven Peterson]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.sherdog.com/news/news/Visa-Issues-KO-Humberto-Bandenay-Brandon-Davis-Now-Faces-Steven-Peterson-at-UFC-Austin-132011|title=Visa Issues KO Humberto Bandenay; Brandon Davis Now Faces Steven Peterson at UFC Austin|last=Sherdog.com|website=Sherdog|access-date=2018-06-03}}</ref>  He won the fight via unanimous decision.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.lowkickmma.com/brandon-davisoutguns-steven-peterson-in-austin/|title=Brandon Davis Outguns Steven Peterson In Austin|last=Jr.|first=Fernando Quiles|date=2018-02-18|work=LowKickMMA.com|access-date=2018-06-03|language=en-US}}</ref>  This fight earned him the [[List of UFC bonus award recipients|Fight of the Night]] award.<ref name=ufn126b>{{Cite news|url=https://mmajunkie.com/2018/02/ufc-fight-night-126-bonuses-brandon-davis-steven-peterson-get-50000-for-slugfest|title=UFC Fight Night 126 bonuses: Brandon Davis, Steven Peterson get $50,000 for slugfest|date=2018-02-19|work=MMAjunkie|access-date=2018-11-15|language=en-US}}</ref>

His next fight came three months later on May 19, 2018.  He faced [[Enrique Barzola]] at [[UFC Fight Night: Maia vs. Usman]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.mmafighting.com/2018/4/12/17227642/enrique-barzola-meets-brandon-davis-at-ufc-chile|title=Enrique Barzola meets Brandon Davis at UFC Chile|work=MMA Fighting|access-date=2018-06-03}}</ref> He lost his second UFC fight via unanimous decision.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://mmajunkie.com/2018/05/ufc-fight-night-129-results-enrique-barzolas-wrestling-too-much-for-brandon-davis|title=UFC Fight Night 129 results: Enrique Barzola's wrestling too much for Brandon Davis|date=2018-05-20|work=MMAjunkie|access-date=2018-06-03|language=en-US}}</ref>

Davis faced [[Zabit Magomedsharipov]] on September 8, 2018 at [[UFC 228]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://mmajunkie.com/2018/08/report-brandon-davis-to-face-zabit-magomedsharipov-ufc-228|title=Brandon Davis steps in to face Zabit Magomedsharipov at UFC 228|work=mmajunkie.com|author=Staff|date=2018-08-28|access-date=2018-08-29}}</ref> He lost the fight via submission in the second round.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://mmajunkie.com/2018/09/ufc-228-results-zabit-magomedsharipov-stuns-brandon-davis-with-brilliant-finish|title=UFC 228 results: Zabit Magomedsharipov stuns Brandon Davis with brilliant finish|date=2018-09-09|work=MMAjunkie|access-date=2018-09-09|language=en-US}}</ref>

Davis faced touted newcomer [[Randy Costa]] on April 13, 2019 at [[UFC 236]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://mmadna.nl/brandon-davis-treft-ongeslagen-nieuwkomer-randy-costa-tijdens-ufc-236-in-atlanta/|title=Brandon Davis meets undefeated newcomer Randy Costa during UFC 236 in Atlanta|author=Marcel Dorff|publisher=mmadnanl.com|date=2019-03-20|access-date=2019-03-21|language=nl}}</ref> He won the fight via a rear-naked choke submission in the second round.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://mmajunkie.com/2019/04/ufc-236-results-brandon-davis-throws-down-randy-costa-submission-win|title=UFC 236 results: Brandon Davis throws down with Randy Costa before scoring submission win|date=2019-04-13|website=MMA Junkie|language=en|access-date=2019-04-14}}</ref>

Davis faced [[Kang Kyung-ho]] on August 17, 2019 at [[UFC 241]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://mmajunkie.com/2019/06/brandon-davis-vs-kyung-ho-kang-ufc-241-anaheim-california|title=Brandon Davis vs. Kyung Ho Kang added to UFC 241 in Anaheim|author=Farah Hannoun|website=mmajunkie.com|date=2019-06-21|access-date=2019-06-21}}</ref> He lost the fight via split decision.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://cagesidepress.com/2019/08/17/ufc-241-kyung-ho-kang-brandon-davis/|title=UFC 241 Results: Kyung Ho Kang Overcomes Adversity, Edges Out Brandon Davis|last=Fortune|first=Ryan|date=2019-08-17|website=Cageside Press|language=en-US|access-date=2019-08-18}}</ref>

Davis faced promotional newcomer [[Giga Chikadze]] on September 28, 2019 at [[UFC on ESPN+ 18]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bloodyelbow.com/2019/9/18/20871025/former-glory-kickboxer-giga-chikadze-talks-ufc-debut-against-brandon-davis-denmark-georgian|title=Former GLORY kickboxer Giga Chikadze talks UFC debut against Brandon Davis|last=Mercado|first=Eddie|date=2019-09-18|website=bloodyelbow.com|access-date=2019-09-18}}</ref> He lost the fight via split decision.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://cagesidepress.com/2019/09/28/ufc-copenhagen-results-chikadze-vs-davis-ends-draw/|title=UFC Copenhagen Results: Chikadze vs. Davis Ends In A Draw|last=Evanoff|first=Josh|date=2019-09-28|website=Cageside Press|language=en-US|access-date=2019-09-28}}</ref> Davis was subsequently released from the UFC.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://mmajunkie.usatoday.com/2020/02/ufc-roster-update-ben-saunders-brandon-davis-among-eight-fighters-released|title=UFC roster update: Ben Saunders, Brandon Davis among eight fighters released|author=Nolan King|date=February 11, 2020|publisher=mmajunkie.com}}</ref>

===Post-UFC career===
After being released from the UFC, Davis signed with Gulf Coast MMA. He won three fights in a row meanwhile capturing the organization's Bantamweight Championship. In his first title defense attempt, he faced Josh Huber for the Gulf Coast MMA Bantamweight Championship at Gulf Coast MMA 11 on August 14, 2021.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://mmajunkie.usatoday.com/lists/ufc-bellator-on-the-doorstep-august-2021-mma-prospects|title=On the Doorstep: 5 fighters who could make UFC or Bellator with August wins|date=August 4, 2021|author=Nolan King|work=[[MMAjunkie.com]]}}</ref> He won the fight via first-round knockout.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://mmajunkie.usatoday.com/2021/08/ufc-news-brandon-davis-extends-regional-winning-streak-pitches-dana-white|title=Video: Former UFC fighter Brandon Davis wins again, asks Dana White for second shot|date=August 15, 2021|work=[[MMAjunkie.com]]|author=Nolan King}}</ref>

===Return to UFC===
Davis faced [[Danaa Batgerel]] on October 16, 2021 at [[UFC Fight Night 195]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Dorff|first=Marcel|date=2021-10-04|title=Brandon Davis keert terug in de UFC tegen Batgerel Danaa op 16 oktober in Las Vegas|url=https://mmadna.nl/brandon-davis-keert-terug-in-de-ufc-tegen-batgerel-danaa-op-16-oktober-in-las-vegas/|access-date=2021-10-04|website=MMA DNA|language=en-US}}</ref> Davis lost the fight via technical knockout in round one.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Evanoff|first=Josh|date=2021-10-16|title=UFC Vegas 40 Results: Danaa Batgerel Finishes Returning Brandon Davis|url=https://cagesidepress.com/2021/10/16/ufc-vegas-40-results-danaa-batgerel-kos-returning-brandon-davis/|access-date=2021-10-16|website=Cageside Press|language=en-US}}</ref>
 
==Personal life==
Davis earned an Athletic Training degree from [[Itawamba Community College]] and a degree in Biological Sciences at [[Mississippi State University]].<ref name="UFCProfile" />
