'''Pedro David Daleccio Torres''' (born October 7, 1990), better known as '''Dalex''', is a Puerto Rican [[Urbano music|Urbano]] singer. He was formerly part of the duo Jayma y Dalex.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://elgenero.com/jayma-dalex-biografia/|title=Jayma y Dalex - Biografia|date=2014-05-13|work=Elgenero.com|access-date=2017-07-31|language=es-ES}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.buenamusica.com/jayma-y-dalex/biografia|title=Jayma y Dalex - Biografía, historia y legado musical {{!}} BuenaMusica.com|last=BuenaMusica.com|website=www.buenamusica.com|language=es|access-date=2017-07-31}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.dalexmusic.com/about/|title=ABOUT|website=Dalex|language=en-US|access-date=2017-07-31}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.xflowofficial.com/jayma-dalex-como-tu-no-hay-lyric-video/|title=Jayma & Dalex – Como Tu No Hay {{!}} Lyric Video {{!}} XFLOW|website=www.xflowofficial.com|language=en-CA|access-date=2017-08-01}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://elgenero.com/jayma-dalex-presentan-su-album-gravedad/|title=Jayma & Dalex presentan su álbum "Gravedad"|date=2015-10-05|work=Elgenero.com|access-date=2017-08-01|language=es-ES}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.fogoneo.com/jayma-dalex-continuan-ganando-territorio-en-estados-unidos/|title=Jayma & Dalex continúa ganando territorio en Estados Unidos|date=2015-06-03|work=Fogoneo.Com|access-date=2017-08-01|language=es-ES}}</ref>

Currently, Dalex is signed to the [[Rich Music]] record label.

== Early life and career beginnings ==
At age 11, the son of divorced parents, Dalex moved to [[Allentown, Pennsylvania]]. At age 12, he returned to [[Puerto Rico]].

At age 18, while studying to become a barber, Dalex began to write songs. When he finished his studies, he moved to [[Orlando, Florida]], looking for better opportunities in the music industry.

== Jayma y Dalex ==
After signing with Anakin Ent, Dalex started singing in the duo Jayma and Dalex. Their first single "Me Encantas" drew attention. Subsequently, the duo collaborated with urban artist [[Cosculluela]] on the song "Lento," which surpassed a million views on [[YouTube]].

Jayma and Dalex's first album, "Gravedad", was released in October 2015.  It featured collaborations with [[Justin Quiles]], [[Jory Boy]] and [[Pusho]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.fogoneo.com/jayma-y-dalex-lanzan-su-disco-gravedad/|title=Jayma y Dalex lanzan su disco "Gravedad"|date=2015-10-07|work=Fogoneo.Com|access-date=2017-08-01|language=es-ES}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.holaciudad.com/Reggaeton-Jayma-Dalex-Feria-Familia_0_845915718.html|title=El Reggaeton de Jayma y Dalex en La Feria de la Familia 2015|work=holaciudad|access-date=2017-08-01|language=es}}</ref>
