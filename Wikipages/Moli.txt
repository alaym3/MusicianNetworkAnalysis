'''Moli''' is a [[Village development committee (Nepal)|village development committee]] in [[Okhaldhunga District]] in the [[Sagarmatha Zone]] of mid-eastern [[Nepal]]. At the time of the [[1991 Nepal census]] it had a population of 3447 living in 597 individual households.<ref>{{Citation| url=http://www.digitalhimalaya.com/collections/nepalcensus/form.php?selection=1|title=Nepal Census 2001|magazine=Nepal's Village Development Committees|publisher=[[Digital Himalaya]]|accessdate=15 November 2009 }}.</ref>
