'''TV Girl''' is an American [[indie pop]] band from [[San Diego]], California, consisting of Brad Petering, Jason Wyman, and Wyatt Harmon.<ref name="Interview with Interview Magazine">{{cite web|url=http://www.interviewmagazine.com/music/discovery-tv-girl#_ |title=Discovery: TV Girl|last=Taylor|first=John|publisher=Interview Magazine |date= |accessdate=November 7, 2014}}</ref><ref name="Interview with Rocksucker">{{cite web|url=http://rocksucker.co.uk/2011/10/interview-tv-girl.html |title=Interview: TV Girl|last=Abrams|first=Jonny|publisher=Rocksucker |date= |accessdate=November 7, 2014}}</ref> The group is now based in [[Los Angeles]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://pitchfork.com/news/11253-if-you-want-it/|title=If You Want It|website=Pitchfork|language=en|access-date=2020-03-07}}</ref>
