'''Jazmin Bean''' (born 2002/2003) is an English singer, songwriter, social media personality, and makeup artist. Based in [[London]], they first gained attention online for their "extreme" makeup looks. They self-released their debut [[extended play]] ''Worldwide Torture'' in 2019, which was later reissued in 2020 through [[Interscope Records]].

==Early life==
Bean's mother, Angie Adams, is a drummer and former member of the [[punk rock]] band [[Fluffy (band)|Fluffy]].<ref name="vogue">{{cite web |last1=Andrews |first1=Arden Fanning |title=Watch Jazmin Bean's "Hybrid Creature" Extreme Beauty Tutorial |url=https://www.vogue.com/article/jazmin-bean-extreme-beauty-tutorial-tim-burton-cult-candy-cosmetics |website=[[Vogue (magazine)|Vogue]] |access-date=8 November 2021 |language=en |date=18 October 2019}}</ref> Their father was also in a [[Rock music|rock]] band.<ref name="diyclass">{{cite web |last1=Wright |first1=Lisa |title=Class of 2021: Jazmin Bean |url=https://diymag.com/2020/12/17/class-of-2021-jazmin-bean-interview |website=[[DIY (magazine)|DIY]] |access-date=8 November 2021 |language=en |date=17 December 2020}}</ref> They have [[Philippines|Filipino]] heritage.<ref name="nmereading">{{cite web |last1=Williams |first1=Kyann-Sian |title=Jazmin Bean at Reading Festival 2021: "This is all I ever wanted to do" |url=https://www.nme.com/blogs/nme-blogs/jazmin-bean-interview-reading-festival-liver-worldwide-torture-debut-album-aswangs-3031974 |website=[[NME]] |access-date=8 November 2021 |language=en |date=29 August 2021}}</ref> They have stated that they had "minimal to no friends" and were "often isolated" during high school.<ref name="dazed100">{{cite web |last1=Cadogan |first1=Dominic |title=Meet Jazmin Bean, the paradoxical pop star with an otherworldly look |url=https://www.dazeddigital.com/beauty/head/article/49385/1/jazmin-bean-musician-artist-dazed-100-2020 |website=[[Dazed (magazine)|Dazed]] |access-date=8 November 2021 |language=en |date=29 May 2020}}</ref>

==Career==
===Music===
Bean began performing music at age 15. Their first live performance involved them singing while rubbing chicken liver on themselves at a bar.<ref name="nmereading" /><ref name="diyclass" />

Bean released their debut single, "Worldwide Torture", in 2019.<ref name="vogue" /> They self-released their debut [[extended play]], ''Worldwide Torture'', in October 2019, which was later reissued by [[Interscope Records]] in November 2020 with four additional tracks.<ref name="clashpc" /><ref name="diyclass" /> A music video for their song "Saccharine", taken from ''Worldwide Torture'', was also released in October 2019.<ref name="papersack" /> Their single "Hello Kitty" was released in December 2019.<ref name="wonderlandhk" /> They released their single "Yandere" in October 2020, and released their single "Princess Castle" in November 2020.<ref name="paperyandere">{{cite web |last1=Matozzo |first1=Marissa |title=Jazmin Bean Buries Their Enemies on 'Yandere' |url=https://www.papermag.com/jazmin-bean-yandere-2648554661.html?rebelltitem=15#rebelltitem15 |website=[[Paper (magazine)|Paper]] |access-date=7 November 2021 |language=en |date=30 October 2020}}</ref><ref name="clashpc" /> They released the single "Monster Truck" featuring Zheani in November 2020.<ref name="dbmt" />

===Makeup===
Bean became known on social media for their "extreme", "doll-like" makeup looks and pastel goth style, which they have described as "genderless monster".<ref name="contactpc">{{cite web |title=Jazmin Bean - Princess Castle ft. Cottontail Video |url=https://www.contactmusic.com/video/jazmin-bean-princess-castle-ft-cottontail-video |website=[[Contactmusic.com]] |access-date=November 8, 2021 |language=en |date=November 12, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Marines |first1=Mauro |title='Super Slaughter': La ternura y el gore se unen en el Cerdo de Babel |url=https://vanguardia.com.mx/show/artes/super-slaughter-la-ternura-y-el-gore-se-unen-en-el-cerdo-de-babel-ESVG3570703 |website=Vanguardia |access-date=8 November 2021 |language=es-MX |date=29 January 2021}}</ref><ref name="paperyandere"/> They first started doing makeup as an early teenager while trying to recreate the looks of their ''[[Monster High]]'' dolls.<ref name="diyclass" /> They run Cult Candy Cosmetics, a cruelty-free, [[vegan]] makeup brand.<ref name="contactpc" /><ref name="wonderlandhk" />

==Artistry==
Bean's music has been described as [[Glam metal|pop metal]], [[trap metal]], [[hyperpop]], [[Alternative pop|alt-pop]], [[grunge]], and "horror pop".<ref name="diyclass" /><ref>{{cite web |last1=Matulaityte |first1=Giedre |title=11 artists who are redefining everything we know about the trap metal genre |url=https://www.altpress.com/features/next-gen-trap-metal-artists/ |website=[[Alternative Press (magazine)|Alternative Press]] |access-date=8 November 2021 |language=en |date=8 June 2021}}</ref><ref name="wonderlandhk">{{cite web |title=London-based Musician Jazmin Bean Drops "Hello Kitty" |url=https://www.wonderlandmagazine.com/2019/12/10/premiere-jazmin-bean-hello-kitty/ |website=[[Wonderland (magazine)|Wonderland]] |access-date=8 November 2021 |language=en |date=10 December 2019}}</ref><ref name="clashpc">{{cite web |last1=Murray |first1=Robin |title=Jazmin Bean Shares New Single 'Princess Castle' |url=https://www.clashmusic.com/news/jazmin-bean-shares-new-single-princess-castle |website=[[Clash (magazine)|Clash]] |access-date=8 November 2021 |language=en |date=11 November 2020}}</ref><ref name="nmereading" /><ref>{{cite web |last1=Enis |first1=Eli |title=Why are so many pop artists borrowing from metal? |url=https://www.redbull.com/int-en/pop-artists-finding-inspiration-in-metal |website=Red Bull |access-date=8 November 2021 |language=en |date=27 February 2020}}</ref> ''[[Paper (magazine)|Paper]]''{{'}}s Jael Goldfine described their music as "freaky", "eerie", and "genre-bending".<ref name="papersack">{{cite web |last1=Goldfine |first1=Jael |title=Welcome to the Bloody, Pastel Pink World of Jazmin Bean |url=https://www.papermag.com/jazmin-bean-saccharine-video-2641055492.html?rebelltitem=16#rebelltitem16 |website=[[Paper (magazine)|Paper]] |access-date=8 November 2021 |language=en |date=22 October 2019}}</ref> They have stated that their music is inspired by film [[soundtrack]]s, including those of the 2019 horror film ''[[Midsommar]]'' and the 2005 fantasy film ''[[Corpse Bride]]''.<ref name="paperyandere" />

Bean has stated that their style is inspired by [[Hello Kitty]], [[Aswang]]s, ''[[Fraggle Rock]]'', and [[Tim Burton]] films, and have described it as a form of escapism.<ref name="nmereading" /><ref name="vogue" /> They often wear a [[Breast binding|chest binder]].<ref name="metrogender" />

==Personal life==
Bean has [[Asperger syndrome]]. They identify as [[Non-binary gender|non-binary]] and use [[Singular they|they/them]] pronouns.<ref name="metrogender">{{cite web |last1=Bakar |first1=Faima |title='I'm a genderless monster': Non-binary Londoner loves dressing up as an 'alien' |url=https://metro.co.uk/2019/05/02/im-a-genderless-monster-non-binary-londoner-loves-dressing-up-as-an-alien-9376059/ |website=[[Metro (British newspaper)|Metro]] |access-date=8 November 2021 |language=en |date=2 May 2019}}</ref><ref name="diyclass" /> They are based in [[London]].<ref name="nmereading" />
