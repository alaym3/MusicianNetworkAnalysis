In 2018 Hutson met [[Phoebe Bridgers]], and the two have since collaborated on a number of projects.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://consequenceofsound.net/2019/03/christian-lee-hutson-phoebe-bridgers-produced-album-northsiders-stream/|title=Christian Lee Hutson previews Phoebe Bridgers-produced album with "Northsiders": Stream|date=2019-03-06|website=Consequence of Sound|language=en-US|access-date=2019-06-04}}</ref> Christian co-wrote and played on the self-titled EP from the supergroup '[[Boygenius]]' as well as Bridgers' 2019 release with [[Conor Oberst]], ''[[Better Oblivion Community Center]]'' and sophomore album ''[[Punisher (album)|Punisher]]''. Christian's third studio album ''Beginners'', produced by Bridgers, was released on [[Anti- (record label)|ANTI- Records]] on May 29, 2020. Its first single, "Northsiders," was released on March 8, 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-news/christian-lee-hutson-northsiders-808600/|title=Song You Need to Know: Christian Lee Hutson, ‘Northsiders’|last=Bernstein|first=Jonathan|date=2019-03-15|website=Rolling Stone|language=en-US|access-date=2019-06-04}}</ref>
