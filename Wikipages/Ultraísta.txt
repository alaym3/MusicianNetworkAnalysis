'''Ultraísta''' is an English-American [[experimental rock]] trio, formed in 2008. The band consists of [[Atoms for Peace (band)|Atoms for Peace]] members [[Nigel Godrich]] and [[Joey Waronker]], alongside [[Laura Bettinson]]. [[Ultraísta (album)|The band's self-titled debut album]] was released on 2 October 2012.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://pitchfork.com/news/47184-nigel-godrichs-band-ultraista-confirm-debut-album/|website=[[Pitchfork (website)|Pitchfork]]|title=Nigel Godrich's Band Ultraísta Confirm Debut Album|date=17 July 2012|first=Jenn|last=Pelly}}</ref> The band's biography notes that it was "conceived from a love of [[afrobeat]], [[electronica]], art and inspired by [[tequila]]".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/pg/UltraistaMusic/about/|title=ultraísta - About|website=[[Facebook]]|access-date=27 September 2020|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200928010115/https://www.facebook.com/UltraistaMusic/about/|archive-date=28 September 2020}}</ref>

==History==
Initially a collaboration between [[Nigel Godrich]] and [[Joey Waronker]], Ultraísta became a three-piece with the addition of [[Laura Bettinson]] in 2010. The band's name is taken from the Spanish [[literary movement]] [[Ultraist movement|ultraism]].

The band released [[Ultraísta (album)|their first album]] in 2012, followed by a remix album. In 2019, the band announced via social media that it would be returning, releasing a deluxe edition of their self-titled debut on 22 November.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.instagram.com/p/B5Ix1pxHcNj/|title=Ultraísta on Instagram: "Is there anybody out there? "|website=Instagram|language=en|access-date=21 November 2019}}</ref>

They released their first single, "Tin King," from their second album on BBC Radio 6 on 14 January 2020. The album, ''[[Sister (Ultraísta album)|Sister]]'', released on 13 March 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://twitter.com/partisanrecords/status/1216823312044773376|title=Tune in to @BBC6Music at 8am UTC tomorrow to hear the new single from @UltraistaMusic + the band members in conversation with @laurenlaverne pic.twitter.com/7Nm73ss6aT|last=Records|first=Partisan|date=13 January 2020|website=@partisanrecords|language=en|access-date=14 January 2020}}</ref>
