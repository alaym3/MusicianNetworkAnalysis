'''Bakhtiyar Mammadov''' ({{lang-az|Bəxtiyar Məmmədov}}, {{lang-ru|Бахтияр Мамедов}}; born 29 September 1993), known professionally as '''Jah Khalib''' is a Kazakh rapper, singer and record producer.

== Biography ==
Bakhtiyar Mammadov was born on September 29, 1993, in [[Almaty]] to an [[Azerbaijanis|Azerbaijani]] father and [[Kazakhs|Kazakh]] mother.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://news.day.az/culture/1100821.html |title=Jah Khalib рассказал об азербайджанском характере и джаду |author=<!--Not stated--> |date=11 March 2019 |website=news.day.az |publisher= |access-date=20 February 2021 |quote=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://1news.az/news/20190403023403113-Jah-Khalib-V-Azerbaidzhane-moya-krov-nachinaet-bushevat-FOTO-VIDEO |title=Jah Khalib: "В Азербайджане моя кровь начинает бушевать" |author=<!--Not stated--> |date=3 April 2019 |website=1news.az |publisher= |access-date=20 February 2021 |quote=}}</ref> He studied musicology and management in the [[Kazakh National Conservatory]].<ref name=ztb>{{cite web |url=https://ztb.kz/persona/jah-khalib-mne-dostatocno-odnogo-lejbla-eto-moj-kazahstan |title=Jah Khalib: "Мне достаточно одного лейбла - это мой Казахстан" |author=<!--Not stated--> |date= |website=ztb.kz |publisher= |access-date=20 February 2021 |quote=}}</ref>

When starting his solo career, he took on the stage name of ''Jah Khalib''. According to him, Khalib is a fictitious name, while Jah is the name of God in [[Rastafari]].<ref name=ztb /> Among his early works, the most popular were "Твои сонные глаза", "SnD", "Сжигая дотла" and "Ты для меня". Soon after, his popularity grew outside Kazakhstan.<ref name=forbeskz>{{cite web |url=https://forbes.kz/ranking/object/819 |title=Jah Khalib |author=<!--Not stated--> |date= |website=forbes.kz |publisher=[[Forbes]] Kazakhstan |access-date=20 February 2021 |quote=}}</ref> He released his first solo album in 2016, named "Если че, я Баха".<ref name=forbeskz>{{cite web |url=https://forbes.kz/ranking/object/819 |title=Jah Khalib |author=<!--Not stated--> |date= |website=forbes.kz |publisher=[[Forbes]] Kazakhstan |access-date=20 February 2021 |quote=}}</ref>

In 2015 and 2016 he was included in the list of stars of show business and sports of the [[Forbes]] Kazakhstan magazine.<ref name=forbeskz /> In June 2017, he won the [[Muz-TV]] 2017 Prize for the "Breakthrough of the Year" nomination.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://informburo.kz/novosti/kazahstanec-jah-khalib-poluchil-premiyu-muz-tv-v-nominacii-proryv-goda.html |title=Казахстанец Jah Khalib получил премию Муз-ТВ в номинации "Прорыв года" |author=<!--Not stated--> |date=10 June 2017 |website=informburo.kz |publisher= |access-date=20 February 2021 |quote=}}</ref> In 2018, he received his first [[Golden Gramophone Award]] for the song "Медина" in Moscow.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://tengrinews.kz/music/jah-khalib-poluchil-pervyiy-zolotoy-grammofon-358347/ |title=Jah Khalib получил первый "Золотой граммофон" |author=<!--Not stated--> |date=25 November 2018 |website=tengrinews.kz |publisher= |access-date=20 February 2021 |quote=}}</ref>

In the summer of 2019, he moved to [[Kyiv]], where he continued to work on his album "Выход в свет".<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nDwf0Olv6eY&ab_channel=%D0%A5%D0%BE%D0%B4%D1%8F%D1%82%D0%A1%D0%BB%D1%83%D1%85%D0%B8 |title=Куда пропал JAH KHALIB ?! ВЫХОД В СВЕТ. Трек с Джамалой. Ходят слухи #51 |author=<!--Not stated--> |date=5 September 2019 |website=youtube.com |publisher= |access-date=20 February 2021 |quote=}}</ref> He released the album on 29 October 2019, which contained 14 tracks.

Bakhtiyar Mammadov got married in the summer of 2020.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://tengrinews.kz/music/jah-khalib-jenilsya-426732/ |title=Jah Khalib женился |author=<!--Not stated--> |date=22 January 2021 |website=tengrinews.kz |publisher= |access-date=20 February 2021 |quote=}}</ref>
