'''Vertical Worship''', previously known as  '''Vertical Church Band''', is a [[Contemporary Christian music|contemporary Christian]] and [[Contemporary worship music|worship]] band from [[Chicago]], Illinois as part of the [[Harvest Bible Chapel]]. They are signed to [[Provident Label Group]] and have released seven albums through [[Essential Records (Christian)|Essential Records]]: ''Live Worship from Vertical Church'' in 2012, ''Rock Won't Move'' in 2013, ''[[Church Songs (Vertical Church Band album)|Church Songs]]'' on January 16, 2015,<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/artist/vertical-church-band/id543692468?ign-mpt=uo%3D4 |title=iTunes - Music - Vertical Church Band |website=apple.com |access-date=May 19, 2015}}</ref> ''Frontiers'' on July 29, 2016, the self-titled album, ''Vertical Worship'', in 2017, ''Bright Faith Bold Future'' on April 6, 2018, and ''Grace Is On Our Side'' on March 13, 2020.

==Background==
The music collective is from [[Chicago]], Illinois, where they form [[Harvest Bible Chapel]], and they started making music in 2012.<ref name="AM Bio">{{Cite web |url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/vertical-church-band-mn0002955566/biography |title=Vertical Church Band : Biography |last=Leggett |first=Steve |publisher=[[AllMusic]] |access-date=May 19, 2015}}</ref> They count as their membership, [[Meredith Andrews]], Andi Rozier, Jake France, [[Jon Guerra]], Tara Stutes and Lauren Sweeney-Smith.<ref name="AM Bio" /><ref name="NRT Review One" /><ref name="NRT Review Two" /><ref name="IVM Review One" /><ref name="Frontiers- Vertical Worship">{{Cite web |url=https://www.allmusic.com/album/frontiers-mw0002954264/credits |title=Vertical Church Band : Frontiers |publisher=[[AllMusic]] |access-date=February 1, 2017}}</ref>

The band released ''Live Worship from Vertical Church'' on July 31, 2012 on [[Essential Records (Christian)|Essential Records]]<ref name="AM Disc">{{Cite web |url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/vertical-church-band-mn0002955566/discography |title=Vertical Church Band : Discography |last=[[AllMusic]] |publisher=[[AllMusic]] |access-date=May 19, 2015}}</ref> and it peaked on ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]''{{'}}s [[Christian Albums]] chart at No. 10 and No. 9 on the [[Top Heatseekers|Heetseekers Albums]] chart.<ref name="AM Awards" /> ''Vertical EP'' was released on May 7, 2013.<ref name="IVM EP Review" /> It was followed by ''The Rock Won't Move'' on October 29, 2013<ref name="AM Disc" /> and charted at No. 58 on the [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]], No. 2 on the Christian Albums chart.<ref name="AM Awards" /> ''White EP'' was released on November 4, 2014.<ref name="365 EP Review" /> Their third album, ''[[Church Songs (Vertical Church Band album)|Church Songs]]'', was released on January 20, 2015<ref name="AM Disc" /> and peaked at No. 31 on the ''Billboard'' 200 and topped the Christian Albums chart<ref name="AM Awards" /> and was followed by the "Church Songs Tour & Workshop" national tour concluding in June, 2015.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://verticalchurchband.com/tour/ |title=TOUR |website=Vertical Church Band |access-date=May 19, 2015}}</ref>
