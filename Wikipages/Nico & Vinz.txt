'''Nico & Vinz''' are a Norwegian duo consisting of Kahouly Nicolay "Nico" Sereba from [[Holmlia]], Oslo and Vincent "Vinzy V" Dery from [[Lambertseter]], Oslo.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/nico-vinz-mn0003228134/biography|title=Nico & Vinz {{!}} Biography & History|last=Kellman|first=Andy|website=AllMusic|access-date=2016-12-01}}</ref> They formed in 2010 as '''Envy''' but changed their name to Nico & Vinz by the end of 2012.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/nico-vinz-mn0003228134/biography|title=Nico & Vinz - Biography & History - AllMusic|website=AllMusic}}</ref> They are mostly famous for their biggest hit single "[[Am I Wrong]]", which peaked at number 2 in Norway, Denmark, Sweden, and the United Kingdom.

== Music career ==

=== 2010–11: Formation and debut mixtape ===
'''Nicolay Sereba''' of [[Norway|Norwegian]]-[[Ivory Coast|Ivorian]] origin and '''Vincent Dery''' of [[Ghana]]ian origin created their music as a fusion of genres as diverse as pop to reggae to soul,<ref name=":0" /> having been exposed by the various musical influences of their parents with whom they did recurring trips to the West Coast of Africa.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://eurochannel.com/en/Nico-Vinz-Norway.html|title=Nico & Vinz - Norway - Eurochannel|last=Eurochannel|newspaper=Eurochannel: The European TV channel - European movies, TV series and music|language=en-GB|access-date=2016-12-01}}</ref> The duo was launched as '''Envy''' with its main debut appearance at Emergenza Festival in 2011.<ref name="MTV">[http://www.mtv.com/artists/nico-vinz/biography/ Nico & Vinz Biography | MTV.com] MTV</ref> They won first place at the Taubertal Open Air Festival World's "Emergenza Final" for new emerging artists. Following that success, the duo released the mixtape ''Dreamworks: Why Not Me'' under the name Envy. The materials were also made available online through [[WiMP]]. Sereba speaks [[English language|English]], [[Norwegian language|Norwegian]] and [[French language|French]]. Dery speaks English, Norwegian and [[Dagaare]].<ref>{{cite web|title="We both speak English and Norwegian. Vinz also speaks Dagarra (a language from Ghana) and Nico also speaks French;)"|url=https://twitter.com/nicoandvinz/status/547110246813302785|publisher=[[Twitter]]|date=December 22, 2014}}</ref>

In June 2011, the duo released their debut single "[[One Song (Envy song)|One Song]]" under the name Envy. The song peaked to number 19 on the [[VG-lista|Norwegian Singles Chart]].<ref name="MTV"/>

=== 2011: ''The Magic Soup and the Bittersweet Faces'' ===
Envy released their debut [[studio album]] ''[[The Magic Soup and the Bittersweet Faces]]'' on April 27, 2012, peaking to number 37 on the [[VG-lista|Norwegian Albums Chart]]. "[[One Song (Envy song)|One Song]]" was released as the lead single from the album peaking at number 19 on the [[VG-lista|Norwegian Singles Chart]]. It was followed by "Go Loud" released as a follow-up second and last single from the album.

=== 2013–14: Breakthrough and "Am I Wrong" international success ===
In April 2013, they released "[[Am I Wrong]]" after the duo changed their name from Envy to Nico & Vinz, in coordination with their signing to [[Warner Bros. Records]] in the [[United States]] to avoid being confused with other artists with a similar name.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.nicoandvinz.com/post/81329914691/exit-envy-hello-nico-vinz|title=Nico & Vinz|website=The Official Website for Nico & Vinz|access-date=2016-12-01|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160915193956/http://www.nicoandvinz.com/post/81329914691/exit-envy-hello-nico-vinz|archive-date=2016-09-15|url-status=dead}}</ref> With international success of the single, the duo changed the credits of the single to the new adopted name. The song peaked to number 2 on the [[VG-lista|Norwegian Singles Chart]] and also peaked to number 2 on the [[Tracklisten|Danish Singles Chart]] and number 2 on the [[Sverigetopplistan|Swedish Singles Chart]].<ref name="MTV"/> It also reached number one on the [[Mainstream Top 40]] chart. It was followed up with another release "[[In Your Arms (Envy song)|In Your Arms]]" which also charted in Norway, Denmark and Sweden.<ref name="MTV"/>

The [[music video]] for "Am I Wrong" was directed and edited by African Folk music composer Kavar Singh. The video for "Am I Wrong" was filmed in [[Botswana]] and at the [[Victoria Falls, Zambia|Victoria Falls]] between [[Zambia]] and [[Zimbabwe]]. It was put together in a deliberate effort to present a positive side of [[Africa]] when the image of the continent is too often mired in negative news stories.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://top40.about.com/od/reviews/fl/Review-Nico-amp-Vinz-Am-I-Wrong.htm|title=Nico & Vinz - "Am I Wrong" - Review|author=Bill Lamb|work=About|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160605125656/http://top40.about.com/od/reviews/fl/Review-Nico-amp-Vinz-Am-I-Wrong.htm|archive-date=2016-06-05}}</ref> The music video was released via [[YouTube]] on June 20, 2013.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bg1sT4ILG0w|title=Nico & Vinz - Am I Wrong [Official Music Video]|work=YouTube}}</ref>

In early 2014, Nico & Vinz received the European Border Breakers Award (EBBA) at Eurosonic Festival, performed at the Spellemann Awards, completed a [[Scandinavia]]n tour, and launched "Am I Wrong" worldwide.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.europeanborderbreakersawards.eu/en/the-winners/nico-vinz-envy/|title=The winners - EBBA, European Border Breakers Awards, the awards for best European new music|website=www.europeanborderbreakersawards.eu|access-date=2016-12-01|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150101040200/http://www.europeanborderbreakersawards.eu/en/the-winners/nico-vinz-envy/|archive-date=2015-01-01|url-status=dead}}</ref> When "Am I Wrong" made its American radio debut in April 2014, it was the number one most added record at the [[Contemporary hit radio|mainstream radio]] format and it peaked at number 4 on the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/5915842/nico-vinz/chart|title=Nico & Vinz - Chart history {{!}} Billboard|website=www.billboard.com|access-date=2016-12-01}}</ref> They performed "Am I Wrong" at the [[iHeartRadio Music Festival]] in [[Las Vegas]], as well as for the television show ''[[Dancing with the Stars (U.S. TV series)|Dancing with the Stars]]'' in [[Los Angeles]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.axs.com/uk/nico-vinz-headline-iheartradio-after-party-at-haze-nightclub-in-las-ve-19712|title=Nico & Vinz headline iHeartRadio after party at HAZE Nightclub in Las Vegas|newspaper=AXS|access-date=2016-12-01}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.idolator.com/7564128/nico-vinz-am-i-wrong-dancing-with-the-stars-watch|title=Nico & Vinz Perform "Am I Wrong" On 'Dancing With The Stars': Watch {{!}} Idolator|date=2014-09-24|newspaper=Music News, Reviews, and Gossip on Idolator.com|access-date=2016-12-01}}</ref>

Their album ''[[Black Star Elephant]]'' was released in September 2014, followed by the single "[[When the Day Comes]]" in October 2014.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.idolator.com/7564720/nico-vinz-when-the-day-comes-video|title=Nico & Vinz Release New Video For "When The Day Comes": Watch The Inspiring Clip|date=30 September 2014|publisher=idolator.com|author=Bianca Gracie|access-date=2 October 2014}}</ref>

Nico & Vinz were featured on a track of [[David Guetta]]'s 2014 album ''[[Listen (David Guetta album)|Listen]]'', named "[[Lift Me Up (David Guetta song)|Lift Me Up]]" along with South African group [[Ladysmith Black Mambazo]].

Their song "Find a Way", featuring [[Emmanuel Jal]], is featured in the film ''[[The Good Lie]]''. "When the Day Comes" is featured the [[EA Sports]] game ''[[FIFA 15]]''.

===2015–present: Extended plays ''Cornerstone'' & ''Elephant in the Room''===
In October 2015, the duo released the EP ''Cornerstone''. "[[That's How You Know (Nico & Vinz song)|That's How You Know]]", featuring [[Kid Ink]] and [[Bebe Rexha]], was released as the first single from the EP and peaked at number 2 in both Norway and Australia. They also featured in Alesso's song "I Wanna Know".

Nico & Vinz then released "Hold It Together" in January 2016, which is taken from their third studio album, due later in 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.auspop.com.au/2016/01/video-nico-vinz-hold-it-together/|title=Video: Nico & Vinz "Hold it Together"|publisher=www.auspop.com.au|date=11 January 2016|access-date=15 January 2016}}</ref>

The second single from ''Cornerstone'' was "Praying to a God".

In November 2017, Nico & Vinz released their new EP ''Elephant In The Room'' including their new single "Intrigued" and "Listen".

They are working on a new album, as they told in 2019 in a podcast of LipRoll.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=46q6hWwrDek|title=Video: LipRoll: Nico & Vinz|publisher=www.youtube.com|date=9 April 2019|access-date=27 April 2019}}</ref>
