'''Olvi plc''' ({{lang-fi|Olvi Oyj}}, {{OMX|HEX24317|OLVAS}}) is a [[Finland|Finnish]] [[brewery]] and [[soft drink]]s company founded in 1878. It currently holds 18.7% of Finland's market share in beverages,<ref>[http://www.virallinenolvi.fi/documentindex.asp?id=391&type=1 Document Viewer<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref> making it the largest Finnish-owned business in its sector. Among its subsidiaries, [[A. Le Coq]] is the second largest beverage company in [[Estonia]] and [[Volfas Engelman]] is the third largest beverage company in [[Lithuania]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://eng.ichacha.net/zaoju/volfas%20engelman.html|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20171111103040/https://eng.ichacha.net/zaoju/volfas%20engelman.html|archive-date = 2017-11-11|title = Volfas engelman in a sentence - volfas engelman sentence}}</ref> Olvi also has businesses in [[Latvia]] ([[Cēsu Alus]]) and [[Belarus]] (Lidskаe Pivа).
