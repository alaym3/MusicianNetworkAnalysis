'''Bad Boy Chiller Crew''' (often abbreviated as '''BBCC''') is a British [[Bassline (music genre)|bassline]] collective from [[Bradford]] in West Yorkshire. The group began by uploading comedy and [[prank]] videos online before making music influenced by the UK bassline scene which rose to prominence, particularly in the [[Yorkshire]] region, during the 2000s.  <ref>{{cite interview |subject-link= BBCC |interviewer= Will Chalker|title= Bad Boy Chiller Crew: Today Bradford, Tomorrow we take over the world|url= https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/newsbeat-53663601|publisher= BBC Newsbeat|access-date= 21 November 2020}}</ref><ref name="pitchfork">{{cite web|url=https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/bad-boy-chiller-crew-full-wack-no-brakes/|title=Bad Boy Chiller Crew: Full Wack No Brakes Album Review|website=Pitchfork|accessdate=21 November 2020}}</ref> In November 2021, the band could be seen in their own reality TV documentary, broadcast on ITV2.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.radiotimes.com/programme/b-ds012o/bad-boy-chiller-crew-season-1/?episode=b-egv91r|title=Bad Boy Chiller Crew Season 1}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.nme.com/news/music/bad-boy-chiller-crew-are-getting-their-own-tv-show-on-itv-3031467|title=Bad Boy Chiller Crew are getting their own TV show on ITV|website=[[NME]]|date=28 August 2021}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.nme.com/big-reads/bad-boy-chiller-crew-cover-interview-2021-reality-tv-show-documentary-3081833|title = Bad Boy Chiller Crew: "We're not a proper boyband. There's nothing normal about us"|website = [[NME]]|date = 29 October 2021}}</ref>

The group is a threesome: Gareth Kelly (GK) , Kane Welsh and Sam (Clive) Robinson.<ref name="thetelegraphandargus">{{cite web|url=https://www.thetelegraphandargus.co.uk/news/18137197.bradfords-bad-boy-chiller-crew-nationals-bassline/|title=Bradford's Bad Boy Chiller Crew in nationals over its bassline|website=Bradford Telegraph and Argus|accessdate=21 November 2020}}</ref> In early 2020, the group were signed by the London-based record label House Anxiety and have been managed by Darren Booth (commonly known as Dr Google).<ref name="theguardian">{{cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2020/jul/04/bad-boy-chiller-crew-were-like-the-explicit-venga-boys|title='We're the explicit Vengaboys': meet Bad Boy Chiller Crew, Bradford's bassline stars &#124; Music|website=The Guardian|date=4 July 2020|accessdate=21 November 2020}}</ref> More recently, it has been confirmed that Booth had negotiated a major recording contract with [[Relentless Records|Relentless]].{{citation needed|date=August 2021}}

The group released their debut album ''Full Wack No Brakes'' on 25 September 2020.<ref>{{cite interview |subject-link= BBCC |interviewer= Jenessa Williams|title= Bad Boy Chiller Crew: Yorkshire Boys joyriding their way to viral success |url= https://www.nme.com/blogs/nme-radar/bad-boy-chiller-crew-yorkshire-lads-joyriding-their-way-to-viral-success-2752482
 |publisher= NME|access-date= 21 November 2020}}</ref>
