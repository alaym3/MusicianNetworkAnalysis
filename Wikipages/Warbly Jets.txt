'''Warbly Jets''' (sometimes stylized in all caps) is an American music duo formed in 2015 in [[Los Angeles, California]].<ref name="LA band warbly jets releasing debut single listen touring with the mystery lights">{{cite web|last = Pearis|first = Bill|title = Article: LA band warbly jets releasing debut single listen touring with the mystery lights|publisher = Brooklyn Vegan|date = October 2016|url = https://www.brooklynvegan.com/la-band-warbly-jets-releasing-debut-single-listen-touring-with-the-mystery-lights/|accessdate = 2016-10-24}}</ref> The group consists of founding members Samuel Shea (vocals, instruments)  and Julien O'Neill (synthesizers, instruments) and has a rotating cast of touring and in-studio members.<ref name="Purple presents an exclusive video of Warbly Jets">{{cite web|title  =Article: Purple presents an exclusive video of Warbly Jets |publisher = purple.fr |date = May 2018|url = https://purple.fr/television/purple-presents-an-exclusive-video-of-warbly-jets/|accessdate = 2018-05-26}}</ref> The group's self-titled debut album was released in late 2017.<ref name="Warbly Jets to release self-titled debut album on Oct 20th">{{cite web|last = Wilkerson|first = Jameelah|title = Warbly Jets to release self-titled debut album on Oct 20th |publisher = The Hype Magazine |date = July 2017 |url = https://www.thehypemagazine.com/2017/07/warbly-jets-to-release-self-titled-debut-album-on-oct-20th/|accessdate = 2017-07-26}}</ref>

==History==
Shea and O'Neill first met while living in [[Brooklyn, NY]].<ref name="A moment with the Warbly Jets">{{cite web|last = Maliwan|first = Kanya|title = Article: A moment with the Warbly Jets |publisher = Topper Radio|date = November 2016|url = https://topperradio.com/blog/2018/5/8/pqu3obi26o5yehk66r8as8juqbd0su|accessdate = 2016-11-29}}</ref> and soon moved to Los Angeles, CA to write and record demos in early 2015.<ref name="Couch surfing critiques: Warbly Jets attacks majors in new single,">{{cite web|last = Rucker|first = Matt|title = Article: Couch Surfing Critiques: Warbly Jets attacks majors in new single, "The Lowdown" |publisher = Ones to Watch|date = July 2017|url = https://www.onestowatch.com/blog/couch-surfing-critiques-warbly-jets-attacks|accessdate = 2017-07-20}}</ref> 

In 2017, they released their debut studio album under the name Warbly Jets.<ref name="Video premiere: by Warbly">{{cite web|title = Article: Video premiere: "Propaganda" by Warbly Jets|publisher = The Big Takeover|date = February 2019|url = https://bigtakeover.com/news/VideoPremierePropagandabyWarblyJets|accessdate = 2019-02-06}}</ref>
Self-produced, recorded, and released, songs from the album such as "Alive," "Fast Change," and "The Lowdown"<ref name="Our take: catchy pop meets '60s Brit rock on Warbly Jets' debut">{{cite web|last = Graber |first = Alexandra|title = Article: Our take: Catchy Pop Meets '60s Brit Rock On Warbly Jets' Debut |publisher = Atwood Magazine|date = October 2017|url = https://atwoodmagazine.com/wj-warbly-jets-debut-review/|accessdate = 2017-10-23}}</ref> have been used in numerous video games and television series such as [[Spider-Man (2018 video game)]],<ref name="American rockers Warbly Jets' 'Alive' announced as intro theme song to 'Spiderman' PS4">{{cite web|title = Article: American Rockers Warbly Jets' 'Alive' Announced as Intro Theme Song to 'Spiderman' PS4 |publisher = The Rockpit|date = September 2018|url = https://www.therockpit.net/2018/american-rockers-warbly-jets-alive-announced-as-intro-theme-song-to-spiderman-ps4/#:~:text=Spider%2DMan%2C%20the%20latest%20game,ask%2C%20'What's%20that%20song%3F|accessdate = 2018-09-20}}</ref> [[Need for Speed Payback]],<ref name="Need for speed payback soundtrack">{{cite web|title = Article: Need for Speed Payback Soundtrack |publisher = EA|url = https://www.ea.com/games/need-for-speed/need-for-speed-payback/news/nfs-payback-music-soundtrack}}</ref> and [[Lucifer (TV series)]].<ref name="Lucifer - S3E21 soundtrack">{{cite web|title = Article: Lucifer- S3E21 Soundtrack |publisher = WhatSong|date = April 2018|url = https://www.what-song.com/Tvshow/145/Lucifer/e/111512/|accessdate = 2018-04-21}}</ref> 

In late 2017, Warbly Jets made their US television debut appearing on NBC's [[Last Call with Carson Daly]].<ref name="Last call with Carson Daly">{{cite web|title = Article: Last Call With Carson Daly |publisher = TV Guide|date = December 2017|url = https://www.tvguide.com/tvshows/last-call-with-carson-daly/episode-47-season-17/paul-reiser-warbly-jets-vicky-krieps/192264/|accessdate = 2017-12-21}}</ref>

They have done multiple international tours as an independent band  <ref name="Warbly Jets tour China and we gave them a camera to prove it">{{cite web|last = Hobbs|first = Leah|title = Article: Warbly Jets Tour China and We Gave Them a Camera to Prove It |publisher = Culture Collide|url = https://culturecollide.com/warbly-jets-tour-china-gave-camera-prove//}}</ref> and have toured as support acts alongside artists such as [[Liam Gallagher]],<ref name="Interview with warbly jets: keeping the rock 'n' roll scene alive">{{cite web|last = Martin|first = Erin|title = Article: Interview with Warbly Jets: Keeping the Rock 'n' Roll Scene Alive |publisher = Teal Magazine|url = https://tealmagazine.com/articles/interview-with-warbly-jets/}}</ref> [[The Dandy Warhols]],<ref name="The Dandy Warhols + Telegram + Warbly Jets @ The Fonda Theatre">{{cite web|title = Article: The Dandy Warhols + Telegram + Warbly Jets @ The Fonda Theatre |publisher = L.A. Record|date = December 2016|url = https://larecord.com/photos/2016/12/19/the-dandy-warhols-telegram-warbly-jets-the-fonda-theatre|accessdate = 2016-12-19}}</ref> [[Stone Temple Pilots]]<ref name="Stone Temple Pilots stun at the fillmore in new orleans">{{cite web|last = Cilluffo|first = Kim|title = Article: Stone Temple Pilots Stun at The Fillmore in New Orleans|publisher = Music Mayhem Magazine|date = October 2019|url = https://musicmayhemmagazine.com/stone-temple-pilots-stun-at-the-fillmore-in-new-orleans/|accessdate = 2019-10-02}}</ref> and [[Rival Sons]].<ref name="Stone Temple Pilots and Rival Sons">{{cite web|title = Article: Stone Temple Pilots and Rival Sons|publisher = 313 Presents|url = https://www.313presents.com/events/detail/stone-temple-pilots-rival-sons}}</ref> 

In 2019, they released a three-song EP entitled "Propaganda."<ref name="Warbly jets craft an innovative new sound in 'propaganda'">{{cite web|last = Robinson|first = Chloe|title = Article: Warbly jets craft an innovative new sound in 'propaganda'|publisher = High Road Touring|url = https://www.highroadtouring.com/warbly-jets-craft-an-innovative-new-sound-in-propaganda/}}</ref> 

On August 26, 2021, Warbly Jets announced their second studio album, ''MONSTERHOUSE'', due out November 12, 2021 by Rebel Union Recordings.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://flaunt.com/content/warbly-jets-monsterhouse |title=Warbly Jets Announcing the New Studio Album Monsterhouse |work=Flaunt |date=August 26, 2021 |access-date=September 22, 2021 |author=Ju, Shirley}}</ref>
