'''Édouard Baer''' (born 1 December 1966) is a French actor, director, screenwriter, film producer and radio personality.<ref name="PallisterHottell2005">{{cite book|last1=Pallister|first1=Janis L.|last2=Hottell|first2=Ruth A.|title=Francophone women film directors: a guide|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=P15tyiOSRz0C&pg=PA79|access-date=25 September 2011|year=2005|publisher=Fairleigh Dickinson Univ Press|isbn=978-0-8386-4046-3|pages=79–}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Edouard Baer|url=http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0046347/|access-date=2021-01-12|website=IMDb}}</ref>

In 2009, he participated in the French television programme ''[[Rendez-vous en terre inconnue]]''. On 5 March 2015 he appeared with [[Sandrine Kiberlain]] on the cover of ''[[Paris Match]]'' .<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.lexpress.fr/styles/vip/sandrine-kiberlain-et-edouard-baer-en-couple_1658546.html#xWvH1wT23fIMtIRm.99|title=Sandrine Kiberlain et Edouard Baer en couple|last=Dormoy|first=Géraldine|date=6 March 2015|website=L'Express|language=fr}}</ref>
