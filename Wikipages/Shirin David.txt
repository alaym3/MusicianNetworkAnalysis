'''Barbara Shirin Davidavičius''' (born 11 April 1995), known professionally as '''Shirin David''', is a German rapper, singer and former YouTuber.

== Early life ==
Shirin David was born in [[Hamburg]], Germany, to an Iranian father and Lithuanian mother<ref>{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/shirindavid/status/546776808658837504|title=Shirin David on Twitter|work=twitter.com|access-date=12 February 2017}}</ref> and has one younger sister. She graduated from a school of opera singing, dancing and acting in Hamburg. David currently lives in [[Berlin]]. She was raised by a single mother after her father abandoned the family.

==Career==
David started her [[YouTube]] channel in March 2014. As of July 2021, she was the 63rd most subscribed channel in Germany with over 2.7 million subscribers.<ref>[http://socialblade.com/youtube/top/country/de/mostsubscribed Top 100 German Youtubers by subscribers]</ref> In 2015, she reached the top 10 of the [[GfK Entertainment charts|German Charts]] as a feature on German [[Contemporary R&B|R&B]] singer Ado Kojo's song "Du liebst mich nicht", a cover of the song with the same name by German rapper [[Sabrina Setlur]]. In February 2016, it was reported that David had cancelled her contract with YouTube network TubeOne Networks, who had made her famous.<ref>{{cite web|title=Auch Shirin David verlässt TubeOne|website=celepedia.de|url=http://www.celepedia.de/article/verlaesst-auch-shirin-david-tubeone|access-date=5 January 2019|archive-url=https://archive.today/20161017100257/http://www.celepedia.de/article/verlaesst-auch-shirin-david-tubeone|archive-date=17 October 2016|url-status=dead}}</ref> In 2017, she was one of four judges at ''[[Deutschland sucht den Superstar]]'', the German version of ''[[American Idol]]''.<ref>{{cite web|title=DSDS 2017: YouTube-Star Shirin David kommt für Vanessa Mai in die DSDS-Jury|periodical=|publisher=[[RTL Television]]|url=https://www.rtl.de/cms/dsds-2017-youtube-star-shirin-david-ersetzt-vanessa-mai-in-der-in-der-14-staffel-dsds-4017191.html|access-date=5 October 2016|last=|date=5 October 2016}}</ref> She moved to [[Berlin]] in 2018, having lived in [[Cologne]] for more than three years previously. 

In January 2019, David released her first official single, "Orbit", which was later one of the tracks of her debut album ''Supersize''.<ref>{{cite web|title=Shirin David geht Gold & gibt Release Date von "Supersize" bekannt|periodical=Hiphop.de|publisher=|url=https://hiphop.de/magazin/news/shirin-david-gold-album-supersize-release-date-319948|format=|access-date=28 June 2019|last=|date=|year=|language=|pages=|quote=}}</ref> In February, her second single, "Gib ihm", was released, reaching number one in the German Charts. Five weeks after the release of "Gib ihm", David was featured on the track "Affalterbach" by German rapper [[Shindy]]. About two weeks later, David released the song "ICE", which was followed by "Fliegst du mit" in May. At the end of June 2019, she published the single "On Off" featuring [[Maître Gims]],<ref>{{cite web|title=Shirin David feat. Gims – On Off|periodical=16bars.de|publisher=|url=http://www.16bars.de/media/247121/shirin-david-gims-on-off/|format=|access-date=28 June 2019|last=|date=|year=|language=|pages=|quote=}}</ref> which was followed by the single "Brillis" in August. On 20 September 2019, she released both the single "Nur mit dir" featuring German singer [[Xavier Naidoo]] and her album ''Supersize'', which peaked at number one on the German Album Charts. She became the first national female solo [[Hip hop|hip-hop]] artist to reach number one with an album as well as the first female hip hop act to top the charts with her debut album.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.offiziellecharts.de/news/item/700-shirin-david-als-erste-hiphopperin-mit-debuetalbum-an-chartspitze|title=Shirin David als erste Hiphopperin mit Debütalbum an Chartspitze|publisher=GFK Entertainment|date=27 September 2019|access-date=27 September 2019}}</ref>

In a YouTube video released in September 2019, David announced her second perfume was set to come out in October 2019, after she had already sold a perfume as part of a collaboration with [[Dm-drogerie markt|dm-drogerie]] since 2014.<ref>{{cite web|title=Shirin David mir eigener Kosmetik-Marke|periodical=News.de|publisher=|url=http://www.news.de/promis/855662945/shirin-david-parfum-bei-dm-ab-07-04-2017-youtube-star-bibisbeautypalace-dagi-bee-mit-eigener-kosmetik-marke/1/|format=|access-date=20 September 2019|last=|date=|year=|language=|pages=|quote=}}</ref> The newest fragrance is a collaboration with [[Douglas Holding|Douglas]] and a first sample of it is found in the limited content box to her album ''Supersize''.<ref>{{cite web|title=Shirin David: Kritik wegen ihrer Supersize-Box|periodical=Starzip.de|publisher=|url=https://www.starzip.de/shirin-david-kritik-wegen-ihrer-supersize-box|format=|access-date=20 September 2019|last=|date=|year=|language=|pages=|quote=}}</ref>

After David had already released two merchandising collections in November 2016 and June 2017 in collaboration with Holymesh,<ref>{{cite web |last=Maier |first=Hanna |date=29 June 2017 |url=https://www.starzip.de/shirin-david-shop-jetzt-kommt-ihr-merch |title=Shirin David Shop: Hier gibt es ihren neuen Shirizzle-Merch! |publisher=StarZip.de |access-date=13 April 2020}}</ref> she released a pair of slippers with "GIB IHM" printed on in April 2020.

David released the single "90-60-111" on 24 April 2020<ref>{{cite web |last= |first= |date=24 April 2020 |url=https://www.16bars.de/newsartikel/260491/shirin-david-teilt-gegen-shindy-und-loredana-aus/ |title= Shirin David teilt gegen Shindy und Loredana aus |publisher=16bars.de |access-date=9 May 2020}}</ref> which turned being her second number one-hit in Germany, as well as her first number one-single in Austria. She also released a limited merchandise collection for the single. Two weeks after the release of "90-60-111", David was featured on the track "Conan x Xenia" by German rapper [[Haftbefehl]].<ref>{{cite web |last= |first= |date=8 May 2020 |url=https://www.focus.de/kultur/musik/musik-news-im-ticker-haftbefehl-veroeffentlicht-song-mit-shirin-david-kann-das-funktionieren_id_11923378.html |title= Haftbefehl veröffentlicht Song mit Shirin David: Kann das funktionieren? |publisher=Focus.de |access-date=9 May 2020}}</ref> Later in 2020, she released her song "Hoe's Up G's Down". Later, she released the song "Never Know" with German rapper [[Luciano (rapper)|Luciano]], which debuted in the top 10 in Germany, Austria, and Switzerland.

== Perfume line ==
In 2017, David brought her own perfume called "''Shirin David — Created by the Community''" in cooperation with [[Dm-drogerie markt|dm-drogerie]]. Her fans had the chance to vote for their personal preferences and tastes on a perfume during a specific time and created the perfume as a community. For her second perfume, "''Shirin David — Created by Shirin''", she cooperated with [[Douglas Holding|Douglas]].

* 2017: Shirin David — ''Created by the Community'' (in cooperation with ''dm-drogerie'')
* 2019: Shirin David — ''Created by Shirin'' (in cooperation with ''Douglas'')
