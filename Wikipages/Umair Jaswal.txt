'''Umair Jaswal''' is a Pakistani [[film actor|film]], television actor, singer-songwriter and music producer from [[Islamabad]]. He was also lead vocalist of the rock band [[Qayaas]]. Umair is the brother of singers [[Yasir Jaswal]] and [[Uzair Jaswal]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Hottie of the week: Umair Jaswal|url=http://tribune.com.pk/story/513889/hottie-of-the-week-umair-jaswal/|website=[[The Express Tribune]]|access-date=31 August 2015|date=3 March 2013}}</ref>

== Life and career ==
Jaswal started his career in 2008 by joining the rock band [[Qayaas]]. Umair gained popularity in 2009 with the song ‘Tanha’ and ‘Umeed’. He joined [[Coke Studio (Pakistan season 5)|Coke Studio season 5]] with his band in 2012 and performed the song Charkha Nolakha with [[Atif Aslam]]. [[Qayaas]] released their debut album [[Uss Paar]] in 2011 with Umair as a lead vocalist. The songs ‘Halaak’ and ‘Inquilaab’ from the album were featured in the film [[Waar (film)|Waar]]. In [[Coke Studio Pakistan (Season 6)|Coke Studio season 6]], Umair decided to go solo. He performed the song ‘Khayal’ on the first episode of [[Coke Studio Pakistan (Season 6)|Coke Studio season 6]] which was also featured in film [[Waar (film)|Waar]].<ref>{{cite web|author1=Mariam Shafiq|title=Umair Jaswal – The Man Who Mesmerized Us All!|url=http://reviewit.pk/umair-jaswal-the-man-who-mesmerized-us-all/|website=Reviewit.pk|access-date=31 August 2015}}</ref>
In 2013 Umair was nominated as Pakistan's [[youth ambassador]] by the [[International Human Rights]] Commission.<ref>{{cite web|author1=Maha Mussadaq|title=Meet Umair Jaswal, the youth ambassador|url=http://tribune.com.pk/story/621458/meet-umair-jaswal-the-youth-ambassador/|website=[[The Express Tribune]]|access-date=31 August 2015|date=23 October 2013}}</ref>
In [[Coke Studio Pakistan (season 8)|Coke Studio season 8]] Umair performed the song "Sammi Meri Waar" with [[QB (singer)|QB]] which received more than two million views in two weeks.<ref>{{cite web|author1=Maheen Shah|title=A place among the stars|url=http://tns.thenews.com.pk/place-among-stars-coke-studio-season-8/#.VePbhiWqqko|website=TNS – The News on Sunday|access-date=31 August 2015|date=30 August 2015}}</ref> In an interview to [[The Express Tribune]] he told that he also received hate mails after release of the song. Umair said: "Man! I’ve received audio messages from places like Iraq. The main idea behind doing this song was to penetrate a bigger market and fortunately that is exactly what happened".<ref>{{cite web|author1=Hassa Ansari|title=Sammi Meri Waar: 'I received hate mail after the song'|url=http://tribune.com.pk/story/953099/sammi-meri-waar-i-received-hate-mail-after-the-song/|website=[[The Express Tribune]]|access-date=14 October 2015|date=8 September 2015}}</ref>
Umair made his [[Lollywood]] debut in the [[epic film|war epic]] film [[Yalghaar]] as Captain Umair.<ref>{{cite web|author1=Huda|title=The Jaswal Brothers: Taking Pakistani Music Industry Forward|url=http://blush.com.pk/the-jaswal-brothers-taking-pakistani-music-industry-forward/|website=Blush.com.pk|access-date=31 August 2015|date=17 April 2013}}</ref> He also made his television debut by appearing in the serial ''[[Mor Mahal]]'' (2016), playing the lead role of a [[nawab]] alongside [[Meesha Shafi]]. In an interview Jaswal said, "I'm honored to be a part of the biggest Pakistani television production to date. I’m sharing the screen with some of the best actors in the industry. Sarmad is a phenomenal storyteller and Sarmad Sehbai a wordsmith second to none. I can’t wait to share the project with the viewers."<ref>{{cite web|author1=Satish Umair|title=Mor Mahal: Umair Jaswal's upcoming attraction!|url=http://www.ibexmag.com/pakistan/lifestyle/mor-mahal-umair-jaswals-upcoming-attraction/|website=IBEX|access-date=9 February 2016|date=21 January 2016}}</ref>

On October 20, 2020 it was announced that he has married the Pakistani female actor [[Sana Javed]]<ref>{{Cite web|title=Umair Jaswal, Sana Javed tie the knot {{!}} SAMAA|url=https://www.samaa.tv/entertainment/2020/10/umair-jaswal-sana-javed-tie-the-knot/|access-date=2020-10-20|website=Samaa TV|language=en-US}}</ref>
.
