'''Oceana Mahlmann''' (born January 23, 1982<ref>[https://www.welt.de/newsticker/dpa_nt/infoline_nt/boulevard_nt/musik_nt/article106254175/Oceana-singt-offiziellen-Song-der-Fussball-EM.html Oceana singt offiziellen Song der Fußball-EM]</ref>) is a German singer. Her musical roots are embedded in [[Soul music|soul]], [[reggae]], [[hip hop]], and [[funk]].

==Biography==
Oceana was raised by her mother, a German [[Haute Couture]] designer.  Her father was a [[disc jockey|DJ]] from [[Martinique]]. The funk legend [[Maceo Parker]] is a close family friend and as a young child she performed with him on stage.

In her teens, she established herself as dancer and choreographer. She performed in the role of Aida in the musical ''[[Aida (musical)|Aida]]'' by [[Tim Rice]] and [[Elton John]]. She was also the vocal soloist in the musical version of ''[[Dirty Dancing]]'' by [[Eleanor Bergstein]].<ref>{{Citation|title=Dirty dancing das Original|date=2006-01-01|publisher=Sony BMG Music Entertainment (Germany)|oclc=475348129}}</ref> In 2006, she was the choreographer of the German chart-breaking band [[Seeed]].

In 2008, she signed to [[Warner Bros. Records|Warner]] to establish herself as a singer-songwriter.

Her solo career took off in 2009 with the release of the single "Cry Cry" from her debut album ''Love Supply'', which was also released on the US-market by the label [[Ultra Records]]. This was a breakthrough in her career: the song was the highest selling single in [[Poland]] that year and peaked in several other European countries in the charts.  Following this success Oceana appeared in numerous European TV shows.

In March 2010, she appeared in the [[Taniec z gwiazdami|Polish version]] of ''[[Dancing with the Stars]]'', but she withdrew, because she was in an advanced state of pregnancy and she finished in 6th place. In May 2010, she hosted the TV show ''Arte Lounge'', which aired on the acclaimed European channel [[Arte]].  She has also appeared as a musical guest on the [[Greece|Greek]] ''[[The X Factor (Greece)|X Factor]]'' and performed on some of the greatest festivals in Europe. On her tour for ''Love Supply'', she performed festivals such as [[Rock En Seine]] in [[France]] and the [[Sopot International Song Festival]] in Poland. Throughout her career, she has supported musicians such as [[Lionel Richie]], [[Raphael Saadiq]], and [[Annie Lennox]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.rap2soul.de/2012/oceana-als-vorgruppe-von-lionel-richie-eigene-tour-abgesagt-18502|title=Oceana als Vorgruppe von Lionel Richie, eigene Tour abgesagt|website=rap2soul}}</ref>

Due to her success the Union of European Football Associations ([[UEFA]]) chose her to follow [[Nelly Furtado]] (2004) and [[Enrique Iglesias]] (2008) to write and perform the official 2012 UEFA song "[[Endless Summer (Oceana song)|Endless Summer]]" for the [[UEFA European Football Championship]], one of the biggest sports events in the world.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://de.uefa.com/news/newsid=1837437.html|title=UEFA Euro 2016 - News – UEFA.com|last=uefa.com|date=2012-07-01}}</ref> At the closing ceremony on July 1, 2012 Oceana performed the song live to a television audience of approximately one billion people. The video of Endless Summer" has been watched by more than 80 million people.<ref>{{Citation|last=Oceanaofficial|title=Oceana - Endless Summer (Official Video UEFA Euro 2012)|date=2012-05-04|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5EVhiBGvVFc|access-date=2012-05-04}}</ref> The song received numerous gold and platinum awards throughout Europe (Germany, [[Poland]], [[Russia]], [[Ukraine]], and Italy).<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.merkur-online.de/nachrichten/stars/em-song-goldene-schallplatte-oceana-2492897.html |title=Oceana: Goldene Schallplatte für EM-Song |date=2012-09-07 }}; {{Cite web|url=http://www.fimi.it/temp/cert_gfk_download_022013.pdf |title=FIMI - Federazione Industria Musicale Italiana - Benvenuto! |date=2014-01-01 |url-status=unfit |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140101131831/http://www.fimi.it/temp/cert_gfk_download_022013.pdf |archive-date=January 1, 2014 }}</ref>

Her second album, ''[[My House (album)|My House]]'', was released in Europe on July 22, 2012.

Oceana is working on her third album in [[Los Angeles]], which was scheduled for release in late 2014 or 2015. After long anticipation, her third studio album "Can't Stop Thinking About You" was released in 2018.
