'''Senta-Sofia Delliponti''' (born 16 April 1990 in [[Wolfsburg]], Germany) is a German singer, songwriter and actress. Since January 2014, she uses the stage name '''Oonagh''' ({{IPA-de|ˈuːnäːɣ}}).<ref name="gala.de">{{Cite web|url=https://www.gala.de/stars/starportraets/senta-sofia-delliponti-20557114.html|title=Senta-Sofia Delliponti|website=Gala.de|access-date=1 December 2021}}</ref><ref name="imdb.com">{{Cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/name/nm2353870/|title=Senta-Sofia Delliponti|website=IMDb.com|access-date=1 December 2021}}</ref> Her signature musical style is inspired by the mystical lore of [[J. R. R. Tolkien]]'s universe and by ethnic sounds throughout the world.

She had her breakthrough in ''[[Star Search]]'',<ref name="gala.de"/><ref name="imdb.com"/> a television talent show. She released a Christmas album with TV Allstars in 2003.{{Citation needed|date=April 2020}}

In 2015, she was a guest singer on the [[Celtic Woman]] album ''[[Celtic Woman: Destiny]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://austriancharts.at/showitem.asp?interpret=Celtic%20Woman&titel=Destiny&cat=a|title=Celtic Woman – Destiny|first=Steffen|last=Hung|website=Austriancharts.at}}</ref> She sang the theme song to ''Big Brother'' in Germany in 2007.

==Biography==
Senta-Sofia Delliponti is the daughter of an Italian father and a [[Bulgary|Bulgarian]] mother and grew up in [[Gifhorn]], Lower Saxony. Her father Maik Delliponti is a pizza maker and her mother Elena Delliponti a music teacher. Her names were inspired by the actress [[Senta Berger]] and the capital of her mother's homeland, [[Sofia]]. She has a younger brother. She participated in [[music competition]]s and was on stage in Musical ''[[Jim Button and Luke the Engine Driver|Jim Button]]''. In 2006, she completed her [[Mittlere Reife|secondary school diploma]]. From 2007 to 2010, she completed training in drama and vocals at [[Drama School Charlottenburg]] in Berlin. In December 2017, she gave birth to a daughter.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.t-online.de/-/82957706|title=Ex-GZSZ-Star ist erstmals Mutter geworden|website=T-online.de|access-date=1 December 2021}}</ref>

===Career===
On 12 July 2003, Delliponti made her first appearance on the Talent Show ''[[Star Search (Germany)|Star Search]]'' on [[Sat.1]]. She participated in the category ''Music Act of 10 to 15 years'' and was able to prevail until the finale. Behind her associate act [[Daniel Siegert]], she finished second on 10 August 2003.
