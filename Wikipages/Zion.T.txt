'''Kim Hae-sol''' (born April 13, 1989),<ref>{{cite web|url=http://people.search.naver.com/search.naver?where=nexearch&sm=tab_ppn&query=%EC%9E%90%EC%9D%B4%EC%96%B8%ED%8B%B0&os=262893&ie=utf8&key=PeopleService |script-title=ko:자이언티 프로필|publisher=Naver|language=ko}}</ref> known as '''Zion.T''' ({{Korean|hangul=자이언티|rr=}}), is a South Korean [[Hip Hop Music|hip hop]] and [[Contemporary R&B|R&B]] singer signed to The Black Label, a subsidiary label of [[YG Entertainment]] managed by [[Teddy Park]] and Kush.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://www.sbs.com.au/popasia/blog/2016/03/17/zion-t-becomes-family-bigbang-last|title=Zion . T becomes family with BIGBANG at last|website=SBS PopAsia|access-date=May 5, 2016}}</ref> He has released a studio album, ''Red Light'' (2013)<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/33499/zionts-new-album-red-light-is-released-on-april-9|title=Zion.T Releases New Solo Album with Dynamic Duo and Other Hip-Hop Artists |website=Mwave|access-date=May 5, 2016}}{{dead link|date=October 2021}}</ref> and two extended plays. He has also been featured on tracks by well-known [[K-pop]] artists such as [[G-Dragon]] and [[Zico (rapper)|Zico]].

==Personal life==
In an interview with ''Kpopeurope'' in 2014, Zion.T explained that he chose his stage name because "I am a Christian and so the "T" in my name is representative of the cross."<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.kpopeurope.eu/endeplhurofrsknlsv-exclusive-interview-with-zion-t-part-1/?lang=en/|title=Exclusive Interview with Zion.T – Part 1|website=Kpopeurope|access-date=January 15, 2018|date=January 29, 2014}}</ref>

In a 2017 CBS ''No Cut News'' interview, he said he was influenced by the American musician [[T-Pain]]. "There was a time when T-Pain's style of hip hop vocals were in. I really like T-Pain, so I used to emulate him a lot. [Evident in his debut single 'Click Me'.] This is something only my friends know, but I even had the nickname 'Zion.T-Pain.'"<ref>{{cite web|url=http://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=079&aid=0002926116|title=자이언티가 직접 밝힌 개성 강한 창법의 비결 |trans-title=The secret of unique singing method revealed by Zion.|publisher=Naver|access-date=February 26, 2018|date=February 4, 2017|language=ko}}</ref>

==Career==
Zion.T made his musical debut in 2011, collaborating with Korean hip hop artists such as [[Dok2]], [[Crucial Star]], [[Simon Dominic|Simon D]], [[Primary (musician)|Primary]] and [[Gray (singer)|Gray]]. His first single, "Click Me", featuring Dok2, was released in April 2011.

On April 9, 2013, Zion.T's first studio album, ''Red Light'', was released with the title track "Babay", featuring [[Gaeko]].<ref name=":1" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://music.naver.com/promotion/specialContent.nhn?articleId=3823#cbox_module |script-title=ko:네이버 뮤직 :: Zion.T [Red Light] 작업 노트|website=Naver|access-date=May 5, 2016|language=ko}}</ref> The album was critically well-received.{{cn|date=October 2021}}

That year, he featured on tracks by [[Infinite H]], [[Dynamic Duo (South Korean duo)|Dynamic Duo]], [[Swings (rapper)|Swings]] and [[G-Dragon]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/47407/zion-t-talks-about-working-with-g-dragon|title=Zion T was Grateful when G-Dragon Sent a Love Call|date=October 4, 2013|website=Mwave|access-date=January 26, 2014}}{{dead link|date=October 2021}}</ref> In December, he released the EP ''Mirrorball'' ("{{lang|ko|미러볼}}") with the title track "Miss Kim" ({{lang|ko|미스 김}}).<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mtvk.com/blog-posts/zion-t-teases-fans-with-a-trot-dub-comeback/|title=Zion T. Teases Fans with a "Trot-Dub" Comeback|website=MTV|access-date=January 26, 2014|date=December 16, 2013}}{{dead link|date=October 2021}}</ref>

In 2014, he released the digital single "Yanghwa BRDG", which became a hit and contributed to his rising mainstream success as an artist in Korea.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/77276/ziont-releases-mv|title=Zion.T Gets Emotional And Real With 'Yanghwa BRDG' MV|website=Mwave|access-date=May 5, 2016}}{{dead link|date=October 2021}}</ref> The song's honest, personal nature connected with a wide range of audiences.{{cn|date=October 2021}}

He also appeared on the soundtrack of the television drama series ''[[Pinocchio (2014 TV series)|Pinocchio]]'' with the song "Kiss Me", and featured on songs by [[Seo In-young]] and [[The Quiett]].{{cn|date=October 2021}}

On February 1, 2015, he collaborated with the Korean R&B singer [[Crush (singer)|Crush]] on the single "Just" ({{lang|ko|그냥}}).<ref>{{cite web|title=Zion.T & Crush Drop Silky R&B Jam 'Just' |last=Oak | first=Jessica |website=Billboard |url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/k-town/6458506/zion-t-crush-just-mv |access-date=August 10, 2015 |date=February 4, 2015}}</ref> The same year, he released the digital singles "Zero Gravity" ({{lang|ko|무중력}}), "Eat" ({{lang|ko|꺼내 먹어요}})<ref>{{cite web|title=Zion.T Tells You to ′Eat′ in New MV |last=Hong | first=Grace Danbi |website=Mwave |url=http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/92780/video-ziont-tells-you-to-eat-in-new-mv |access-date=August 10, 2015 |date=May 19, 2015}}{{dead link|date=October 2021}}</ref> and "No Make Up" ({{lang|ko|노메이크업}}) and featured on singles by [[Shinee|SHINee]]'s [[Kim Jong-hyun (singer)|Jonghyun]], Yankie and [[Psy]]. That July, he participated in the biennial music festival event hosted by the [[Munhwa Broadcasting Corporation|MBC]] variety show ''[[Infinite Challenge]]'', which resulted in a spike in domestic sales of his previously released singles.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=109&aid=0003116918 |title=[뮤직톡톡] '무한도전'이 뒤흔든 음원차트…이건 예고편|trans-title=Music chart shaken by 'Infinite Challenge'... this is a preview|website=Naver|access-date=May 5, 2016}}</ref> For the event, he partnered with the ''Infinite Challenge'' cast member [[Haha (entertainer)|Haha]] to perform under the name Eutteugeottasi ({{lang|ko|으뜨거따시}}) with the song "$ponsor", later released in an album of the songs performed at the event.<ref>{{Cite web|title = ′Infinity Challenge′ Song Festival Team Profile Photos Released|url = http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/96952/infinity-challenge-song-festival-team-profile-photos-released|website = Mwave|access-date =January 1, 2016}}{{dead link|date=October 2021}}<br>- {{Cite web|title = 'Infinite Challenge' songs sweeping charts|url = http://kpopherald.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=201508231335326955253_2|website = Kpop Herald|author=Kim Yu-jin|date=August 23, 2015|access-date =January 1, 2016}}</ref>

Zion.T finished the year with two wins at the annual [[Mnet Asian Music Awards]],: Best Vocal Performance (Male) for "Eat " {{lang|ko|꺼내먹어요}} and Best Collaboration, with Crush, for the song "Just" {{lang|ko|그냥}}, beating more well-established artistes in an idol-focused industry.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.mwave.me/kr/mama/history/winners/list?years=2015|title=MAMA History Winner 2015|website=Mwave|language=ko|access-date=May 25, 2018}}{{dead link|date=October 2021}}</ref>

In 2016, following the end of his contract with the hip hop label [[Amoeba Culture]], he signed with [[YG Entertainment#THE BLACK LABEL|The Black Label]], a sub-label of [[YG Entertainment]].<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.yonhapnews.co.kr/bulletin/2016/03/17/0200000000AKR20160317046100005.HTML?input=1195m|title=자이언티, 아메바컬쳐 떠난다…YG '테디 레이블'로 이적|trans-title=Zion.T leaves Amoeba Culture... Transfer to YG 'Teddy Label'|last=Lee|first=Eun Jung|website=Yonhap News|date=March 17, 2016|language=ko}}</ref> On February 1, 2017, he released his first EP under The Black Label, ''[[OO (EP)|OO]].''<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.soompi.com/2017/01/31/watch-zion-t-drops-eccentric-charming-mv-self-composed-track-the-song/|title=Watch: Zion.T Drops Eccentric And Charming MV For Self-Composed Track "The Song"|last=K|first=J.|date=January 31, 2017|website=Soompi|access-date=February 1, 2017}}</ref>

In December 2017, he released his collaboration with the veteran singer Lee Moon-se, the song "Snow". It topped all major music charts in South Korea. Lee Moon-se said he listened to Zion.T's song more than 200 times to faithfully represent his song, and showed great affection towards Zion.T and his musical career.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://news.chosun.com/site/data/html_dir/2017/12/05/2017120501736.html|title=이문세, 자이언티와 컬래버.."'눈' 200번 이상 들었다|trans-title=Lee Moon-se and Zion.|website=Chosun|date=January 14, 2018|language=ko}}</ref>
