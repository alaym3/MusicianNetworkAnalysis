'''Ben Cramer''', better known as '''Old Sea Brigade''', is an American songwriter and producer, signed to [[Nettwerk Music Group]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.wherethemusicmeets.com/2016/09/20/old-sea-brigade-all-the-same/ |title=Old Sea Brigade – All The Same |website=Where the Music Meets|last=Baptista Coelho|first=José|accessdate=December 31, 2016|date=September 20, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.nettwerk.com/news/2016/old-sea-brigade-signs-nettwerk-listen-all-same |title=Old Sea Brigade Signs To Nettwerk, Listen To "All The Same" |work=[[Nettwerk|Nettwerk Music Group]] |publication-date=September 19, 2016|accessdate=December 31, 2016}}</ref> Originally from Atlanta, Georgia, Cramer currently resides in [[Nashville, Tennessee]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.globalyodel.com/yodels/a-guide-to-atlanta-with-old-sea-brigade/ |title=A Guide to Atlanta with Old Sea Brigade |website=Global Yodal |publication-date=January 13, 2016|accessdate=December 31, 2016|last=Bushati|first=Era}}</ref>

==Career==
Old Sea Brigade's debut self-titled EP was released in January 2016 to much critical acclaim.<ref>{{Cite web |website=Mother Church Pew|accessdate=December 31, 2016}}</ref> Cramer spent the following year touring the United States and Europe, including support shows with [[Joseph]], [[Julien Baker]], and [[Benjamin Francis Leftwich]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://hearnebraska.org/feature/the-comprehensive-guide-to-nebraska-folk-roots-festival-2016/ |title=The Comprehensive Guide to the 2016 Nebraska
