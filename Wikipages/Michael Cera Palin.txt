'''Michael Cera Palin''' are an American [[Emo]] band from [[Atlanta]]. The band's name is a [[portmanteau]] of actor [[Michael Cera]] and former Alaska governor [[Sarah Palin]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://modern-vinyl.com/2018/02/01/exclusive-spin-michael-cera-palin-i-dont-know-how-to-explain-it/|title=Exclusive Spin: Michael Cera Palin — I Don’t Know How to Explain It|website=modern-vinyl.com|language=en-US|access-date=2020-03-28}}</ref> 

== Background ==
Active since 2015, the band has performed with [[Math the Band]], [[Prince Daddy & The Hyena]], [[Mikey Erg]], and others. In October 2015, the band released their debut EP, ''Growing Pains'', followed by their 2018 sophomore release, ''I Don't Know How To Explain It,'' which featured a cover of "[[If It Makes You Happy]]" by [[Sheryl Crow]]''.''<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.getalternative.com/interivew-michael-cera-palin/|title=Interview: Michael Cera Palin|date=2018-05-29|website=The Alternative|language=en|access-date=2020-03-28}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.georgialocalspotlight.com/home/2018/5/3/michael-cera-palin-hits-the-road|title=Michael Cera Palin Kicks Off Their First Major Tour|website=Georgia Local Spotlight|language=en-US|access-date=2020-03-28}}</ref> The band has received airplay on [[KDVS]], a college radio station based on the campus of the [[University of California, Davis]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://kdvs.org/playlist-details/48182/|title=Anyway, Here's Wonderwall|website=kdvs.org|language=en-US|access-date=2020-03-28}}</ref>
