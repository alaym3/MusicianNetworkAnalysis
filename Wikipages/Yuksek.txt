'''Pierre-Alexandre Busson''' (born 3 June 1977) better known as '''Yuksek''', is a [[French people|French]] electronic music producer, remixer, singer and [[Disc Jockey|DJ]]. Yüksek means "high" in Turkish.

==Biography==
Yuksek was born in [[Reims]], France. Yuksek played the piano at the Conservatoire of Music until the age of 17 – the time he decided to give up his studies. After playing the piano for ten years at a conservatoire, he took part in several bands such as ''Klanguage'', whose first album was released in 2007.

He created his first tracks as Yuksek in 2002 and then started to perform successfully as a DJ all over the world. At the same time, his first EPs and remixes were released on labels such as Relish, I'm a Cliché, [[Kitsuné]], Exploited and Citizen.

His debut album, ''[[Away from the Sea]]'', was released on February 9, 2009. Featuring the singles ''Tonight'' and ''Extraball'', this album received a lot of media attention, including an interview on the popular French TV talk show [[Le Grand Journal (Canal+)|Le Grand Journal]] on premium pay television channel [[Canal+]]. ''Tonight'' was also selected as the soundtrack for the show in January 2009. His track ''Extraball'' was featured in a commercial for the [[Airness]] "Attraction" deodorant, as well as [[Lacoste Essential (fragrance)|Lacoste Red !]] collection in 2009. His track ''Tonight'' has also been chosen for the [[Peugeot]] commercial "Alchemy", part of a major brand revival, that was broadcast on French TV from January 2010.

Yuksek has also been involved in many other musical projects such as The Krays (with Brodinski) and Peter & The Magician (with Stephen Fasano, ex-[[Aeroplane (musician)|Aeroplane]]).

After touring for 2 years, Yuksek released his second album, ''Living on the Edge of Time'', on June 13, 2011.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://next.liberation.fr/musique/2011/06/24/yuksek-voix-devant-et-virage-pop_744856|title=Yuksek, voix devant et virage pop|date=June 24, 2011|website=Next.liberation.fr}}</ref> This album – like the debut single "On a Train" – took a pop direction while retaining Yuksek's electronic flow.

In December 2011, Yuksek played the renowned New Year [[Rhythm and Vines]] festival in [[Gisborne, New Zealand]], then headed to the [[Field Day (Sydney festival)|Field Day]] music festival in [[Sydney]], [[Australia]] on [[New Year's Day]] 2012.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://fuzzy.com.au/coming-up/252/info |title=Archived copy |access-date=June 4, 2012 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120426001111/http://fuzzy.com.au/coming-up/252/info |archive-date=April 26, 2012 }}</ref>

In April 2013, Yuksek launched his own label, Partyfine.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://france3-regions.francetvinfo.fr/grand-est/marne/reims-metropole/reims/artiste-remois-yuksek-lance-son-label-partyfine-sort-premier-ep-240949.html|title=L'artiste rémois Yuksek lance son label "Partyfine" et un sort un premier EP|website=France3-regions.francetvinfo.fr}}</ref> The first EP, ''EP#1'' was released May 13, 2013. It includes featurings with Juveniles and [[Oh Land]]. The second one, with Peter & The Magician, was released June 24, 2013 and is titled ''On My Brain''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.allpointsmusic.fr/|title=AllPoints France|website=AllPointsmusic.fr}}</ref>

==Collaborations==
===The Krays===
Yuksek is a longtime friend of Brodinski, a French [[Disc jockey|DJ]] who also comes from Reims.<ref>''[[Le Monde]]'', 8 April 2008</ref> The Krays is the musical collaboration between Yuksek and French producer Brodinski. Their first EP titled ''We’re Ready When You Are'' was released on May 17, 2010. (Abracada).

===Peter & The Magician===
Since 2011, Yuksek has been collaborating with The Magician (aka Stephane Fassano, former member of [[Aeroplane (musician)|Aeroplane]]) under the stage name '''Peter & The Magician'''. Their first song "Twist" has been recorded on a [[Kitsuné]] compilation (Kitsuné Maison Compilation 11: "The Indie Dance Issue") released on May 26, [[2011 in music|2011]].

The three-track EP also called ''Twist'' was released one month later on the [[Kitsuné]] label.

=== The Alexanders ===
The Alexanders is a collaboration between Yuksek and English DJ and producer [[Alex Metric]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.residentadvisor.net/event.aspx?574430|title=RA: The Alexanders (Yuksek + Alex Metric) + Mighty Mouse at The Nest, London|publisher=[[Resident Advisor]]|date=17 April 2014}}</ref><ref name="Mixmag">{{cite magazine|url=http://www.mixmag.net/music/in-session/in-session-alexanders|title=IN SESSION: THE ALEXANDERS|magazine=[[Mixmag]]|date=19 March 2014}}</ref> The Alexanders released their debut single "Don't Miss" through Yuksek's label Partyfine on 24 February 2014.<ref name="Mixmag"/> The song features vocals from Anna Lunoe.<ref name="Mixmag"/> The duo remixed [[Porter Robinson]]'s "Lionhearted" (featuring [[Urban Cone]]), the third single from his debut studio album ''[[Worlds (Porter Robinson album)|Worlds]]''. The remix features on the extended play as well as the limited edition album box set.
