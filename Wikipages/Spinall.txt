'''Oluseye Desmond Sodamola''', professionally known as '''Spinall''' (formerly '''DJ Spinall'''), is a Nigerian disc jockey, record producer, songwriter, label executive, and media personality. He first gained prominence in 2004, while working<ref>{{cite web |title=DJ Spinall speaks to Pulse on his journey, losing out at the SMWA's and meeting Killertunes |url=https://www.pulse.ng/entertainment/music/dj-spinall-on-wizkid-losing-out-on-smvas-and-career-pulse-exclusive-interview/m3c981m |website=Pulse Nigeria |accessdate=16 January 2020 |date=21 March 2019}}</ref> as a radio DJ at [[Raypower 100.5 FM]]. He later joined Industry Nite<ref>{{cite web |last1=Magazine |first1=Tush |title=DJ SPINALL: Spinning Around the World |url=http://tushmagazine.com.ng/dj-spinall-spinning-around-the-world/ |website=[[Tush Magazine]] |accessdate=14 January 2020 |date=25 November 2015}}</ref> where he promoted underground talents at the weekly platform. He was the official DJ to [[Wande Coal]] while he was affiliated with [[Mavin Records]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bellanaija.com/2013/02/06/activated-dj-spinall-joins-mavin-records/|title=Dj Spinall joins mavin's|publisher=bellanija |accessdate=}}</ref> He created a style edge to his outfit known as TheCAP, tracing his roots back to his culture. TheCAP, which fans tags as "TheCrazyAzzParty"<ref>{{cite web |title=DJ Spinall, Ajebutter 22 are a year older today |url=https://www.pulse.ng/entertainment/celebrities/celebrity-birthday-dj-spinall-ajebutter-22-are-a-year-older-today/yg2ess9 |website=Pulse Nigeria |accessdate=15 January 2020 |date=2 June 2015}}</ref> has since then been his brand power and identity. Prior to April 2021, he went by the stage name DJ Spinall. Same month, he started an Afromix series on [[Apple Music]], on 15 April 2021, Spinall released an Afromix, EP 1<ref>{{cite web |title=Afromix, Ep. 1 (DJ Mix) by Spinall |url=https://music.apple.com/ng/album/afromix-ep-1-dj-mix/1561009000 |website=Apple Music |access-date=18 May 2021}}</ref> and hosted a television show, Smirnoff Infamous Mix<ref>{{cite web |title="Women Are More Talented Than Men", Niniola on Smirnoff InfamousMix |url=https://notjustok.com/article/niniola-brags-on-smirnoff-infamousmix-with-djspinall/ |website=NotjustOk |access-date=18 May 2021 |date=17 April 2021}}</ref> on [[MTV Base]], [[Soundcity TV]] with a podcast series on [[Audiomack]] and various radio stations. On 6 April 2021, Smirnoff Infamous Mix first episode featured guest appearance from [[Wurld (musician)|Wurld]].<ref>{{cite web |last1=Nwankpa |first1=Adaugo |title=Smirnoff InfamousMix pilot episode hits airwaves & You should look out for It |url=https://www.bellanaija.com/2021/04/smirnoff-infamous-mix/ |website=BellaNaija |access-date=18 May 2021 |date=12 April 2021}}</ref>

In 2014, he launched TheCAPMusic, a record label/management company. In the following year, he signed producers<ref>{{cite web |title=DJ Spinall speaks to Pulse on his journey, losing out at the SMWA's and meeting Killertunes |url=https://www.pulse.ng/entertainment/music/dj-spinall-on-wizkid-losing-out-on-smvas-and-career-pulse-exclusive-interview/m3c981m |website=Pulse Nigeria |accessdate=14 January 2020 |date=21 March 2019}}</ref> to the TheCAPMusic. Top Boy as he is popularly called, released his first hit single "GbaGbe E"<ref>{{cite web |last1=S |first1=Deji |title=New Video: DJ Spinall Feat. Burna Boy – Gba Gbe è |url=https://www.bellanaija.com/2014/06/new-video-dj-spinall-feat-burna-boy-gba-gbe-e/ |website=[[BellaNaija]] |accessdate=16 January 2020 |date=5 June 2014}}</ref> in 2014 featuring [[Burna Boy]]. He has collaborated with other acts such as; [[2Baba]], [[Wizkid]], [[Wande Coal]], [[Davido]], [[Mr Eazi]], [[Tiwa Savage]], [[Kizz Daniel]], and many more.

Spinall's debut album ''My Story: The Album''<ref>{{cite web |title=Album Review: DJ Spinall – My Story |url=https://www.360nobs.com/2016/08/album-review-dj-spinall-story/ |website=360Nobs |accessdate=14 January 2020 |date=9 August 2016}}</ref>&nbsp;(2015) with breakthrough singles "Oluwa", "Gba Gbe E", and "Money". In the following year, on 10 October 2016, DJ Spinall released his second album titled ''TEN''.<ref>{{cite web |title=Religion and science inspired my new "Ten" album |url=https://www.pulse.ng/dj-spinall-religion-and-science-inspired-my-new-ten-album/wq86djj |website=Pulse Nigeria |accessdate=14 January 2020 |date=11 October 2016}}</ref> The album features [[Davido]], [[Sauti Sol]], [[Mr Eazi]] among others, with lead singles "Package" featuring Davido & "Ohema" featuring [[Mr Eazi]]. His third studio albums, ''Dreams''<ref>{{cite web |title=DJ Spinall Features Top Nigerian Artistes For Third Album ‘Dreams’ |url=https://guardian.ng/life/whatsnew/dj-spinall-features-top-nigerian-artistes-for-third-album-dreams/ |website=[[The Guardian (Nigeria)|The Guardia]] |accessdate=14 January 2020}}</ref>&nbsp;was released on 8 October 2017 with singles "Opoju", "Calm Down", and "Gimme Luv". On 26 October 2018, he released his 4th studio album ''Iyanu''.<ref>{{cite web |title=DJ Spinall Drops Tracklist For "Iyanu" Album |url=https://www.pmnewsnigeria.com/2018/10/24/dj-spinall-drops-tracklist-for-iyanu-album/ |website=[[P.M. News]] |accessdate=14 January 2020 |date=24 October 2018}}</ref> The album features two lead singles including "Nowo",<ref>{{cite web |title=The 15 Best Nigerian Songs of the Year So Far |url=https://www.okayafrica.com/nigerian-songs-music-2018-best-download/ |website=[[OkayAfrica]] |accessdate=16 January 2020  |date=21 June 2018}}</ref> [[OkayAfrica]] refers to as one of 15 biggest Nigerian songs of 2018 featuring [[Wizkid]], which also peaked on [[MTV Base (Africa)|MTV Base]] Official Naija Top 10 Countdown<ref>{{cite web |title=Official Naija Top 10 Countdown |url=https://guardian.ng/saturday-magazine/weekend-beats/official-naija-top-10-countdown/ |website=[[The Guardian (Nigeria)|Saturday Magazine]] |accessdate=16 January 2020}}</ref> and "Baba" featuring [[Kizz Daniel]], which made it to [[MTV Base (Africa)|MTV Base]] Top 20 Hottest Naija<ref>{{cite web |title=MTV Base Top 20 Hottest Naija Tracks of 2018 |url=https://www.informationng.com/2018/12/mtv-base-top-20-hottest-naija-tracks-of-2018-full-list.html |website=Information Nigeria |accessdate=16 January 2020}}</ref> tracks of 2018. On 11 December 2020, he released his 5th studio album ''Grace'',<ref>{{cite web |title=DJ Spinall drops Star-Studded Album "Grace" featuring Tiwa Savage, Minz & Omah Lay |url=https://www.bellanaija.com/2020/12/dj-spinall-grace-album/ |website=BellaNaija |access-date=5 February 2021 |date=11 December 2020}}</ref> under TheCAP Music<ref>{{cite web |title=DJ Spinall’s ‘Grace’ feels like a playlist [Pulse Album Review] |url=https://www.pulse.ng/entertainment/music/dj-spinall-grace-album-review/tbzt73f |website=Pulse Nigeria |access-date=5 February 2021 |date=17 December 2020}}</ref> with 5 self-produced songs.<ref>{{cite web |title=Here’s the Star-Studded Tracklist for DJ Spinall’s forthcoming Album "Grace" |url=https://www.bellanaija.com/2020/12/dj-spinall-grace/ |website=BellaNaija |access-date=5 February 2021 |date=7 December 2020}}</ref> The album features the top-six songs, "Dis Love" featuring [[Wizkid]] & [[Tiwa Savage]], "Sere" featuring [[Fireboy DML]], "Pressure" featuring [[Dice Ailes]], "Everytime" featuring [[Kranium]], "Tonight" featuring [[Omah Lay]], and "EDI" featuring [[Reminisce (rapper)|Reminisce]].

In 2015, Spinall became the first African DJ to perform at the [[SXSW]] festival<ref>{{cite web |title=Photos of African stars at SXSWest Africa, Texas |url=https://www.pulse.ng/entertainment/music/ice-prince-dj-spinall-sarkodie-photos-of-african-stars-at-sxswest-africa-texas/zp1jbv8 |website=Pulse Nigeria |accessdate=14 January 2020 |date=25 March 2015}}</ref> in [[Austin]], [[Texas]], with other supporting acts to join Spinall on stage including;&nbsp;[[Davido]], [[Sarkodie (rapper)|Sarkodie]], [[R2Bees]] with host [[Eddie Kadi]]. He is also the first Nigerian DJ to tour 5 cities in United States, including [[New York City]], [[Miami]], [[Houston]], [[Austin, Texas|Austin]], and [[Washington, D.C.]] In 2017, he was the official DJ<ref>{{cite web |title=‘DJ Spinall’ Official DJ For BET International Awards 2017 |url=https://www.channelstv.com/2017/06/23/dj-spinall-official-dj-bet-international-awards-2017/ |publisher=[[Channels TV|Channels Television]] |accessdate=14 January 2020}}</ref> for [[BET Awards 2017|BET Awards]] in Los Angeles, California. The award was aired across the globe, which also gained him worldwide attention as a Disc Jockey pioneering [[Afrobeat]] to the world. On 21 April 2019, Spinall became the first Nigerian DJ to headline<ref>{{cite web |title=Complex Sessions 033: DJ Spinall |url=https://www.complex.com/music/2019/04/complex-sessions-033-dj-spinall |website=[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]] |accessdate=14 January 2020 }}</ref>&nbsp;a show at&nbsp;[[XOYO]]&nbsp;in&nbsp;London&nbsp;and the first Nigerian DJ to perform at the&nbsp;[[Glastonbury Festival]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://guardian.ng/saturday-magazine/dj-spinall-to-perform-at-glastonbury-festival-today/|title=DJ Spinall to perform at Glastonbury festival today|date=29 June 2019|website=[[The Guardian {Nigeria)|The Guardian]]|access-date=30 June 2019}}</ref>&nbsp;on 29 June 2019.

==Music career==
===Early life and TheCAP Music===
Oluseye Desmond Sodamola was born on June 2, 1984, in [[Lagos]] and he is the third<ref>{{cite web |last1=Kool |first1=C. J. |title=Colourful Radio |url=https://www.colourfulradio.com/content/guest/39587 |website=[[Colourful Radio]] |accessdate=15 January 2020 }}</ref> of five siblings. He attained his primary and secondary school education in [[Lagos State]], then moved to [[Ogun State]], to study [[Electrical engineering|Electrical and Electronics Engineering]] at [[Olabisi Onabanjo University]],<ref>{{cite news|title=Educational Background – DJ Spinall|url=http://thenet.ng/2016/07/10-things-you-didnt-know-about-nigerias-fast-rising-jockey-dj-spinall/|accessdate=1 August 2016|work=Nigerian Entertainment Today – Nigeria's Number 1 Entertainment Daily|date=29 July 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160801083437/http://thenet.ng/2016/07/10-things-you-didnt-know-about-nigerias-fast-rising-jockey-dj-spinall/|archive-date=1 August 2016|url-status=dead}}</ref> where he received his BSc degree.

Spinall's first musical experience<ref>{{cite web |title=The Artist Is Present: DJ Spinall Wants to Push Afrobeats to the World, Without Forgetting the Culture |url=https://www.okayafrica.com/dj-spinall-artist-is-present/ |website=[[OkayAfrica]] |accessdate=14 January 2020  |date=9 June 2017}}</ref> as a kid, was listening to some vinyl records been played by his parents and according to him "In the morning my dad would wake up, and open the drawers, and start playing some [[Fela Kuti]], and [[King Sunny Adé]] records". This shaped him and fueled his passion for music after watching a DJ he hired to play in high school.

===2017–18: ''Dreams'', and ''Iyanu''===
His third studio album ''Dreams'' was released independently by TheCAP Music on 8 October 2017. The album featured guest appearances from [[Niniola]], [[Wurld (musician)|Wurld]], [[Ycee]], [[Wizkid]], [[Simi (singer)|Simi]], [[Mr Eazi]], [[Harrysong]], [[Davido]], [[Wande Coal]], [[Reekado Banks]], and [[Olamide]]. The album lead singles include: "Opoju" featuring [[Wizkid]], "Calm Down" featuring [[Mr Eazi]], and "Gimme Luv" featuring [[Olamide]]. On 18 November 2017, Spinall announced Party Of Your Dreams album launch<ref>{{cite web |title=#PartyOfYourDreams: We’ve got the Juice on how you can attend DJ Spinall’s Album Launch Party |url=https://www.bellanaija.com/2017/11/dj-spinalls-album-launch/ |website=[[BellaNaija]] |accessdate=15 January 2020 |date=18 November 2017}}</ref> on 26 November at Grand Ballroom of Oriental Hotel, [[Victoria Island, Lagos|Victoria Island]], Lagos and in 2018, Spinall announced the second edition of Party of Your Dreams<ref>{{cite web |last1=Idowu |first1=Tomi |title=Your Definitive Event Guide to Christmas 2018 in Lagos |url=http://culturecustodian.com/your-definitive-event-guide-to-christmas-2018-in-lagos/ |website=The Culture Custodian |accessdate=15 January 2020 |date=3 December 2018}}</ref> in [[Lagos]] on 7 December 2018, with supporting DJs; [[DJ Big N]], DJ Consequence, [[DJ Enimoney]], [[DJ Lambo]], and more to play at the event. In 2019, Sodamola had his first UK headline concert in London tagged Party of Your Dreams<ref>{{cite web |title=DJ Spinall Presents: Party Of Your Dreams, London – Notjustok |url=https://notjustok.com/news/dj-spinall-presents-party-of-your-dreams-london/ |website=[[NotJustOk]] |accessdate=15 January 2020 |date=24 February 2019}}</ref> on 21 April 2019 at [[XOYO]]!.

On 24 October 2018, DJ Spinall shared featured acts and producers on his fourth studio album Iyanu.<ref>{{cite web |title=DJ Spinall Drops Tracklist For "Iyanu" Album |url=https://www.pmnewsnigeria.com/2018/10/24/dj-spinall-drops-tracklist-for-iyanu-album/ |website=[[P.M. News]] |accessdate=15 January 2020 |date=24 October 2018}}</ref> Guests on the album are [[Wizkid]], [[Kizz Daniel]], [[Davido]], [[Burna Boy]], [[Tekno (musician)|Tekno]], [[Nonso Amadi]], [[Wurld (musician)|Wurld]], [[Dotman]], and more. The album was exclusively produced by DJ Spinall and co-produced by Killertunes, CKAY, [[Nonso Amadi]], Benie Macaulay and Stg. The album lead singles<ref>{{cite web |title=DJ Spinall's New Album 'Iyanu' Is Here |url=https://www.okayafrica.com/listen-stream-dj-spinall-iyanu/ |website=[[OkayAfrica]] |accessdate=15 January 2020  |date=26 October 2018}}</ref> include: "Nowo" featuring [[Wizkid]] and "Baba" featuring [[Kizz Daniel]]. The album was released independently by TheCAP Music on 26 October 2018.

===2019-2020: ''Beyoncé Presents: Making The Gift, and The Recording Academy''===
{{main|Beyoncé Presents: Making The Gift}}
In 2019, [[Beyoncé]] released an hour-long documentary special entitled ''[[Beyoncé Presents: Making The Gift]]''.<ref>{{cite web |title=Watch "Beyoncé Presents: Making The Gift" {{!}} ABC Updates |url=https://abc.com/news/insider/watch-beyonce-presents-making-the-gift-on-abc-and-the-abc-app |website=[[American Broadcasting Company|ABC]] |accessdate=16 January 2020 }}</ref> The television film was shot in [[Egypt]], [[Nigeria]], South Africa, and United States. The documentary was aired 16 September on [[American Broadcasting Company|ABC]], where DJ Spinall appeared and spoke about the album ''[[The Lion King: The Gift]]'', the basis of the documentary.<ref>{{cite web |title=Beyoncé Making The Gift – Documentary 2019 |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cZDVZJX4FDQ |via=YouTube |accessdate=16 January 2020 }}</ref>

In 2020, Spinall was among the 2,300 music executive inductees into [[The Recording Academy]]'s<ref>{{cite web |title=Nigeria: Bankulli, DJ Spinall join Recording Academy |url=https://www.musicinafrica.net/magazine/nigeria-bankulli-dj-spinall-join-recording-academy |website=Music In Africa |access-date=5 February 2021 |language=en |date=15 July 2020}}</ref> class of 2020, alongside [[Bankulli]], [[Lil Nas X]], [[Gunna (rapper)|Gunna]], [[Victoria Monét]], [[Juls]], among others for the [[63rd Annual Grammy Awards]]. On 26 July 2020, Spinall spoke about [[Grammy Award|The Grammy Award]] at This Sunday<ref>{{cite web |last1=Gitau |first1=Susan |title=Apple Music's Africa Now Radio With Cuppy Features DJ Spinall This Sunday |url=https://africa.com/apple-musics-africa-now-radio-with-cuppy-features-dj-spinall-this-sunday/ |website=Africa.com |access-date=5 February 2021 |date=29 July 2020}}</ref> show with [[DJ Cuppy]] on Africa Now Radio via [[Apple Music]]. He was also among several celebrities such as [[D'banj]], [[Praiz]] and Olisa to attend an exclusive party hosted by MTN for the data-bundled music service MusicTour.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-01-11|title=D’Banj, DJ Spinall, Praiz, Olisa, others turned up for MTN’s MusicTime|url=https://www.vanguardngr.com/2020/01/dbanj-dj-spinall-praiz-olisa-others-turned-up-for-mtns-musictime/|access-date=2021-02-26|website=Vanguard News|language=en-US}}</ref>
