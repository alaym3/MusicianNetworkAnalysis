'''Mirko Manuele Martorana''' (born 19 April 1994), known professionally as '''Rkomi''', is an Italian rapper.<ref>{{Cite web|url = https://www.vanityfair.it/music/storie-music/2021/05/13/rkomi-intervista-taxi-driver-album-boxe-thailandese-fidanzata-famiglia?refresh_ce=|title= Rkomi: "Grazie alla mia cazzimma"|date=13 May 2021|first=Valentina|last=Colosimo|work= [[Vanity Fair (magazine)|Vanity Fair]]|accessdate= 15 June 2021|language=it}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.it/musica/interviste-musica/rkomi-in-viaggio-oltre-il-rap/556733/#Part1|title=Rkomi - In viaggio oltre il rap|work=[[Rolling Stone]]|date=6 April 2021|first=Marta|last=Blumi Tripodi|access-date=15 July 2021|language=it}}</ref>

In September 2017, he released his first [[studio album]] ''Io in terra'', which peaked number 1 of [[Federazione Industria Musicale Italiana|FIMI]]'s albums chart.<ref>{{Cite web|url =https://www.goaboa.it/17-11-17-rkomi-bangarang-genova/|title= Il primo album ufficiale del nuovo fenomeno rap milanese subito ai vertici delle classifiche FIMI|date= 17 November 2017|work=Goaboa|access-date=15 July 2021|language=it}}</ref> Also his subsequent albums, ''Dove gli occhi non arrivano'' (2019) and ''Taxi Driver'' (2021), reached the top of the Italian albums chart.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.iwebradio.fm/rkomi-lalbum-dove-gli-occhi-non-arrivano-si-posiziona-al-1-posto-della-classifica-album-fimi-gfk/|title=Rkomi: l'album 'Dove gli occhi non arrivano' si posiziona al 1° posto della classifica album FIMI-GFK|date=31 March 2019|work=Iwebradio|access-date=15 July 2021|language=it}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://recensiamomusica.com/classifiche-fimi-settimana-18-del-2021-rkomi-il-piu-venduto-tra-album-e-singoli/|title= Classifiche FIMI, settimana 18 del 2021: Rkomi il più venduto tra album e singoli|first=Ilario |last=Luisetto|date=7 May 2021|work=Recensiamo Musica|access-date=15 July 2021|language=it}}</ref>
