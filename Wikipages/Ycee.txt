'''Oludemilade Martin Alejo''' (born January 29, 1993), known professionally as '''Ycee''' (also stylized as '''YCee'''), is a [[Nigerian]] rapper, singer and songwriter.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.bellanaija.com/2015/10/23/olamide-jumps-on-the-remix-to-jagaban-by-ycee/|title=Olamide Jumps on the Remix to ‘Jagaban’ by Ycee|work=[[BellaNaija]]|date=23 October 2015|access-date=30 October 2015}}</ref> He gained popularity after his single "Condo", which features vocals by [[Patoranking]], earned him two nominations at the [[2015 Nigeria Entertainment Awards]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.naij.com/544827-exclusive-fresh-hot-fast-rising-rapper-releases-new-video.html|title=Fresh: Ycee Releases Jagaban Video|work=[[Naij]]|last=Bodunrin|first=Sola|date=8 September 2015|access-date=30 October 2015}}</ref> He was also nominated for Best Artist in African Pop at the 2015 [[All Africa Music Awards]]<ref>{{cite news|url=http://expressng.com/2015/08/olamide-davido-wizkid-sarkodie-dominate-all-africa-music-awards-afrima-categories-full-list/|title=Olamide, Davido, Wizkid, Sarkodie Dominate All Africa Music Awards (AFRIMA) Categories [FULL LIST]|work=[[Entertainment Express]]|last=Shola|first=Ayeotan|date=22 August 2015|access-date=30 October 2015}}</ref> and for [[The Headies Award for Next Rated|Next Rated]] at [[The Headies 2016]].<ref>{{cite news|url=http://culturecustodian.com/headies-awards-2016-complete-list-nominees/|title=Headies Awards 2016 : Complete List of Nominees|work=Culture Custodian|author=Tomi|date=19 December 2016|access-date=21 September 2017}}</ref> Ycee signed a record deal with [[Sony Music]] in October 2016, but terminated his contract with the label in February 2018.<ref>{{cite web |author1=Jayne Augoye |title=Tinny Entertainment announces termination of Ycee’s deal with Sony Music |url=https://www.premiumtimesng.com/entertainment/music/257546-tinny-entertainment-announces-termination-ycees-deal-sony-music.html |access-date=21 February 2019 |date=5 February 2018}}</ref>

==Early life and education==
Ycee was born in [[Festac Town]], a housing estate in Lagos State, Nigeria. He completed his [[primary school|basic education]] at Dr. Soyemi Memorial Nursery and Primary School. He attended the Nigerian Navy Secondary School in [[Ojo, Lagos State|Ojo]] for his secondary education. Ycee studied [[marine biology]] at the [[University of Lagos]].<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.encomium.ng/im-not-a-womanizer-ycee/|title=‘I’m not a womanizer’ – YCEE|work=Ecomium|author=Ecomium Weekly|date=28 July 2015|access-date=30 October 2015}}</ref>

==Career==
Ycee started his music career in 2012 as an underground rapper. He signed a record deal with Tinny Entertainment. Due to his educational engagements, he took a brief hiatus from music after gaining admission to the [[University of Lagos]].<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.naij.com/598957-exclusive-1-year-2-singles-3-award-nominations-upcoming-artist-shares-winning-secrets.html|title=Exclusive: Ycee The Jagaban Speaks On His Success|work=[[Naij]]|last=Bodunrin|first=Sola|access-date=30 October 2015}}</ref> Ycee released the critically acclaimed song "Condo" in May 2015; it was nominated for Best Collaboration of the Year and Best Music Video of the Year at the [[2015 Nigeria Entertainment Awards]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.bellanaija.com/2015/06/12/kiss-daniel-korede-bello-falz-wizkid-olamide-davido-more-nominated-for-the-10th-annual-nigerian-entertainment-awards/|title=Kiss Daniel, Korede Bello, Falz, Wizkid, Olamide, Davido & More Nominated for the 10th Annual Nigerian Entertainment Awards|work=[[BellaNaija]]|last=Ade-Unuigbe|first=Adesola|date=19 October 2015|access-date=30 October 2015}}</ref> On 20 July 2015, he released the hit single "Jagaban". Nigerian rapper [[Olamide]] asked him to be featured on the remix.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.naij.com/609767-exclusive-olamide-teams-jagaban-master.html|title=Exclusive: Olamide Teams Up With Jagaban Master, Ycee|work=[[Naij]]|last=Bodunrin|first=Sola|date=19 October 2015|access-date=30 October 2015}}</ref> He gained further exposure after receiving a nomination for Revelation of the Year at the 2015 [[MTV Africa Music Awards]]. Moreover, he was nominated for Best Artist in African Pop at the 2015 [[All Africa Music Awards]].<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.ngrguardiannews.com/2015/07/ycee-makes-a-come-back-to-the-music-scene-with-condo/|title=Ycee Makes A Come Back To The Music Scene With ‘Condo’|work=[[The Guardian (Nigeria)|The Guardian]]|last=Anazia|first=Daniel|date=25 July 2015|access-date=30 October 2015}}</ref>

Ycee sold out a headline gig at the [[O2 Academy Islington]] on 12 January 2018. The gig featured additional performances from his label mates [[Bella Alubo]], Sona, [[Eugy]], [[Ms. Banks]] and Flosha.<ref>{{cite web |author1=Toyin Owoseje |title=YCEE brings the Afrobeats Juice and plenty of sauce with surprise guests at sold-out London show |url=https://www.ibtimes.co.uk/ycee-brings-afrobeats-juice-plenty-sauce-surprise-guests-sold-out-london-show-1655054 |website=International Business Times |access-date=4 April 2020 |date=January 13, 2018}}</ref> On 15 January 2019, he announced on Instagram that he left Tinny Entertainment.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bellanaija.com/2019/01/new-year-new-record-label-ycee-parts-ways-with-tinny-entertainment/|title=New Year, New Record Label? YCee parts ways with Tinny Entertainment|last=BellaNaija|date=2019-01-17|website=bellanaija.com|language=en-US|access-date=2019-01-15}}</ref> He also announced the launch of his record label ANBT, an [[acronym]] for Ain't Nobody Badder Than.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.freeinstrumental.com.ng/2019/01/ycee-launches-his-own-label/|title=Ycee Announces His Own Record Label|last=admin|date=2019-01-17|website=Freeinstrumental.com.ng|language=en-US|access-date=2019-01-24}}</ref>

Ycee released his debut studio album ''Ycee vs Zaheer'' on 8 November 2019.<ref name="Pulse Nigeria 12">{{cite web |author1=Motolani Alake |title=YCee releases debut album, 'YceevsZaheer' |url=https://www.pulse.ng/entertainment/music/ycee-ycee-vs-zaheer-new-album-davido-niniola-phyno-ms-banks-feature/x8lvlgj |publisher=Pulse Nigeria |access-date=9 November 2019 |date=8 November 2019}}</ref> It is a mixture of [[Hip hop music|hip-hop]], [[Trap music|trap]], [[Afrobeats]], Afro-house, hyphy, R&B and alte.<ref name="Pulse Nigeria 12" /> The album serves as the follow-up to his 8-track debut EP ''First Wave'' (2017) and his collaborative EP with [[Bella Alubo]], titled ''Late Night Vibrations'' (2018).<ref name="Pulse Nigeria 12" /> The album was produced by Adey, Jay Bidz, Ballertosh, Fanatix, BeatsbyKarma, Syn X, Krizbeatz, Jay Popping, Buzzin Producer, AP Da Don, Two Seven, Elmore, Willis Give Dem and KO. It features collaborations with [[Davido]], [[Niniola]], [[Phyno]], Ms Banks and Dapo Tuburna.<ref name="Pulse Nigeria 12" />

Over the course of his career and since his rise to prominence, Ycee has featured on notable records to break upcoming artists. He featured on records to break his label mate Dapo Turbuna "[https://www.bellanaija.com/2017/07/dapo-tuburna-nothing-remix/ Nothing]" Remix in 2016,<ref>{{Cite web|last=BellaNaija.com|date=2017-07-07|title=New Music: Dapo Tuburna feat. Olamide & Ycee – Nothing (Remix)|url=https://www.bellanaija.com/2017/07/dapo-tuburna-nothing-remix/|access-date=2020-06-11|website=BellaNaija|language=en-US}}</ref> Label Mate [[Bella Alubo|Bella]] on [https://thenativemag.com/music/bella-ycee-radio/ Radio] in 2017 before going ahead to create a joint EP with her [https://thenativemag.com/music/essentials-bella-alubo-ycee-get-feels-late-night-vibration-ep/ (Late Night Vibrations]).<ref>{{Cite web|date=2018-01-31|title=Essentials: Bella Alubo and YCee get in their feels for 'Late Night Vibrations' EP|url=https://thenativemag.com/music/essentials-bella-alubo-ycee-get-feels-late-night-vibration-ep/|access-date=2020-06-11|website=The Native|language=en-US}}</ref> Ycee also featured on [https://tooxclusive.com/arosino-bigman-doingz-ft-ycee-dapo-tuburna-karma/ Big Man Doingz]<ref>{{Cite web|date=2018-10-04|title=Arosino - BigMan Doingz ft. Ycee, Dapo Tuburna & Karma « tooXclusive|url=https://tooxclusive.com/arosino-bigman-doingz-ft-ycee-dapo-tuburna-karma/|access-date=2020-06-11|website=tooXclusive|language=en-US}}</ref> by Arosino alongside Dapo Tuburna and Karma. Some Say it was his way of giving back to emerging artistes as he was once on the receiving end of such impact through Olamide on his remix to [https://tooxclusive.com/ycee-jagaban-remix-ft-olamide/ Jagaban], his breakthrough single.
