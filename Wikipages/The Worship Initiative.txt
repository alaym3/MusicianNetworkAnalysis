'''''The Worship Initiative''''' is the ninth studio album by [[Shane & Shane]], released on April 28, 2015 through [[Fair Trade Services]]. The album was funded via a [[Kickstarter]] project.

Grace S. Aspinwall, awarding the album three stars at ''[[CCM Magazine]]'', writes, "Delivering a set of worship favorites can be tricky, as some will revert to favoring the original writer/performer. Shane and Shane, in their own way, cover this chosen batch with ease."<ref name="CCM"/> In a three and a half star review by Jesus Freak Hideout, Christopher Smith describes, "If the intention of ''The Worship Initiative'' was to simplify these songs to make them easier for a worship team to reproduce, then the duo accomplished this without sacrificing too much creativity."<ref name="JFH"/> Kevin Davis, writing in a four and a half star review from New Release Tuesday, replies, "the songs get deeper into the recesses of your heart with the challenging messages."<ref name="NRT"/> In a 4.7 out of five rated review at Christian Music Review, Kelly Meade says, "From start to finish, this album is full of well written, reflective lyrics calling our hearts to worship."<ref name="CMR"/> Joshua Andre, awarding the album four stars for 365 Days of Inspiring Media, writes, "A thoroughly enjoyable worship album, Shane & Shane's ''The Worship Initiative'' is one to enjoy if you want to hear a worship album full of recognizable old and new favourites."<ref name="365DoIM"/> In a four star review from ''[[Worship Leader (magazine)|Worship Leader]]'', Jeremy Armstrong says, "plied with the Shane & Shane artistry full of soaring melodies, pristine guitar work, and emotive musicality, these songs are given new breath with this outstanding collection."<ref name="WL"/>
