'''Holden''' is a French pop duo that began recording in 1997. The band members are Armelle Pioline (vocals) and Dominique Dépret (a.k.a. Mocke, guitar), with help from Pierre-Jean Grapin (drums), Evan Evans (keyboard) and Richard Cousin (bass).

Holden released "L'arrière monde" in 1998. In 2001, they met the producer Señor Coconut a.k.a. Atom a.k.a. [[Uwe Schmidt]], who mixed their second [[album]], 'Pedrolira' (2002). Thanks to him, they became famous in [[Chile]], where they recorded their third album, "Chevrotine" (2006). [http://www.last.fm/music/Holden/+wiki]
