'''Stray Kids''' ({{ko-hhrm|스트레이 키즈|rr=Seuteurei kijeu}}; often abbreviated as '''SKZ''') is a South Korean [[boy band]] formed by [[JYP Entertainment]] through the 2017 [[Stray Kids (TV series)|reality show of the same name]]. The group is composed of eight members: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N. Originally a nine-piece group, member [[Kim Woo-jin (singer)|Woojin]] left Stray Kids due to undisclosed personal reasons in October 2019. The group released their pre-debut [[extended play]] (EP) ''[[Mixtape (Stray Kids EP)|Mixtape]]'' in January 2018 and officially debuted on March 25<!--Please do NOT edit the date. I Am Not was released on March 25, on their unveil event. --> with the EP ''[[I Am Not]]''.<ref name="Mixtape release" /><ref>{{cite news|last=Jeong|first=Ji-won|title=[Oh!쎈 초점] SM·YG·JYP, 3대 기획사의 2018년|url=http://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=109&aid=0003699641|website=Naver|publisher=Osen|access-date=January 17, 2018|language=ko}}</ref>

=== 2019: Showcase touring, ''Clé'' series and Woojin's departure ===

Stray Kids started the year with the continuation of their showcase tour Unveil Tour "I Am..." in the Asia-Pacific, starting on January 19 in [[Bangkok]].<ref>{{cite news |last1=Hwang |first1=Ji-young |title=스트레이 키즈, 2019년 해외 쇼케이스 투어 돌입 |url=https://news.joins.com/article/23155761 |access-date=February 22, 2021 |work=JoongAng Ilbo |publisher=JTBC |date=November 26, 2018 |language=ko}}</ref> The group also hold the national promotional tour Hi Stay Tour in Korea in [[Busan]], [[Daejeon]] and [[Incheon]] in March and the Finale in Seoul was hold in [[Olympic Park, Seoul|Olympic Park]] on April 20.<ref name=histay>{{cite news |last1=Kim |first1=Ha-jin |title=스트레이 키즈, 전국 프로모션 투어 '하이 스테이 투어' 연다 |url=https://www.hankyung.com/entertainment/article/2019030831514 |access-date=February 22, 2021 |work=Korea Economic Daily |publisher=Tenasia |date=March 8, 2019 |language=ko}}</ref>

On March 5, JYPE announced the group's third comeback on March 25, 2019, with the release of their fourth extended play ''[[Clé 1: Miroh]]'', in commemoration of the first anniversary of the group's debut.<ref>{{cite news|last=Seon|first=Mi-kyung|title=스트레이키즈, 3월25일 컴백 확정.."데뷔 1주년 기념 앨범"[공식입장]|url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=109&aid=0003963639|website=Naver|publisher=Osen|access-date=May 14, 2019|language=ko}}</ref> The group earned their first music show win on 4 April on ''[[M Countdown]]'' for the album's lead single, "Miroh."<ref>{{cite news|last=Seon|first=Mi-kyung|title=스트레이키즈, '엠카'서 데뷔 1년 만에 음악방송 첫 1위.."초심 잃지 않을 것" 눈물|url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=109&aid=0003983457|website=Naver|publisher=Osen|access-date=May 14, 2019|language=ko}}</ref> On June 19, the group released their first special album ''[[Clé 2: Yellow Wood]]'', along with title track "Side Effects" ({{lang-ko|부작용}}) between the American leg and the European leg of their first world tour.<ref>{{cite news|last=Benjamin|first=Jeff|title=Stray Kids Detail Their 'Darker,' Special 'Yellow Wood' Album & How They Travel the 'Road Not Taken'|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8517475/stray-kids-interview-cle-2-yellow-wood-the-road-not-taken|website=Billboard|publisher=Billboard Music|access-date=June 26, 2019|date=June 24, 2019}}</ref>

Stray Kids released a digital single titled "Double Knot" on October 9 and also announced their ''District 9 Unlock World Tour'' starting from November 23–24 at Olympic Hall, Seoul, South Korea, with their fifth extended play ''[[Clé: Levanter]]'', initially announced for release on November 25. However, on October 28, JYPE announced that [[Kim Woo-jin (singer)|Woojin]] had left the group due to personal circumstances, and the release of ''Clé: Levanter'' was delayed to December 9 as a result.<ref>{{cite web|title=안녕하세요, JYPE입니다.|url=https://fans.jype.com/BoardView?BoardName=NOTICE_SK&Num=434&DivisionId=&SearchField=&SearchQuery=|website=JYP Fans|publisher=JYP Fans|access-date=October 27, 2019}}</ref> On November 13, the group released the music video for "Astronaut," their first single without Woojin and as an eight-member group.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/international/8543480/stray-kids-astronaut-video |title=Stray Kids Are Ready to Take to the Skies With Uplifting 'Astronaut' Video: Watch |last=Herman |first=Tamar |date=November 13, 2019 |website=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] |access-date=November 15, 2019}}</ref> On December 9, Stray Kids released ''Cle: Levanter''.

Stray Kids hold the Japanese showcase Hi Stay on December 19 at [[Yoyogi National Gymnasium]], Tokyo with 8,000 audience.<ref name=jpnshowcase>{{cite news |last1=Kim |first1=Joo-hee |title=스트레이 키즈, 일본 쇼케이스 성료..2020년 3월 일본 정식 데뷔 |url=https://www.sedaily.com/NewsVIew/1VRYEIJ002 |access-date=February 22, 2021 |work=Seoul Economic Daily |publisher=Seoul Economic Daily |date=December 5, 2019 |language=ko}}</ref> The group released the digital single titled "Mixtape: Gone Days", the first single of Mixtape Project on December 26.<ref>{{cite news|last=Lee|first=Deok-haeng|title=스트레이 키즈, 신곡 'Mixtape : Gone Days'에 담긴 센스만점 메시지|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/311/0001091836|website=Naver|publisher=Xportsnews|date=December 27, 2019|access-date=April 6, 2020|language=ko}}</ref>
