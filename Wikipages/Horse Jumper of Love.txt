'''Horse Jumper of Love''' is an American [[rock music|rock]] band from [[Boston]], [[Massachusetts]].

==History==
Horse Jumper of Love released their self-titled first studio album in 2016 to mixed reviews.<ref name="noiseyvice">{{cite web |last1=Domenighini |first1=Annalise |title=Listen to Horse Jumper of Love's Self-Titled Debut and Enjoy the Meta-Weirdness |url=https://noisey.vice.com/en_au/article/r3zepk/listen-to-horse-jumper-of-loves-self-titled-debut |publisher=[[Vice (magazine)|Vice]] |accessdate=7 August 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/21630-horse-jumper-of-love/|title=Horse Jumper of Love: Horse Jumper of Love|website=Pitchfork|language=en|access-date=2019-05-01}}</ref> The single “Ugly Brunette” from the album premiered on [[Stereogum]]<ref name="stereogum">{{cite web|url=https://www.stereogum.com/1854234/horse-jumper-of-love-ugly-brunette-stereogum-premiere/mp3s/|title=Horse Jumper Of Love – “Ugly Brunette” (Stereogum Premiere)|last1=Robinson|first1=Collin|publisher=[[Stereogum]]|accessdate=7 August 2018}}</ref> and was later featured on [[NPR Music]]’s series ''Songs We Missed''.<ref name="nprmusic">{{cite web |last1=Lorusso |first1=Marissa |title=Songs We Love: Horse Jumper Of Love, 'Ugly Brunette' |url=https://www.npr.org/2016/12/27/506468984/songs-we-love-horse-jumper-of-love-ugly-brunette |publisher=[[NPR Music]] |accessdate=7 August 2018}}</ref> On March 8, 2017 they performed an [[Audiotree]] live session in which they played tracks from their, at that point untitled, 2019 album ahead of its announcement.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://audiotree.tv/session/horse-jumper-of-love|title=Horse Jumper of Love|website=Audiotree|language=en-US|access-date=2019-05-01}}</ref> In April 2017, Joy Void reissued ''Horse Jumper of Love'' on 12" vinyl along with the previously unreleased bonus track "Orange Peeler."<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://joyvoid.bandcamp.com/album/horse-jumper-of-love-2|title=Horse Jumper of Love, by Horse Jumper of Love|website=Joy Void|access-date=2019-05-01}}</ref> On April 22, 2019 Horse Jumper of Love announced their new album ''So Divine'', set to release on June 28th through [[Run for Cover Records]]. The band also premiered the first single from the album, "Poison," via [[The Fader]] – along with a music video directed by Ty Ueda.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thefader.com/2019/04/22/horse-jumper-of-love-poison-video-premiere-so-divine-run-for-cover|title=Horse Jumper of Love’s new “Poison” video is a master class in pizza making|website=The FADER|language=en|access-date=2019-05-01}}</ref> It was also announced in April 2019 that, that July, HJOL would be opening up at several dates of seminal [[slowcore]] band [[Duster (band)|Duster]]'s United States east coast reunion tour.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.brooklynvegan.com/17-new-songs-out-today-7/|title=17 New Songs Out Today|website=BrooklynVegan|language=en-US|access-date=2019-05-01}}</ref>
