'''Joseph''' is an American [[folk music|folk]] band from [[Portland, Oregon]] made up of three sisters: Natalie Closner Schepman, and [[Twin|twins]] Allison Closner and Meegan Closner. Their first album, ''Native Dreamer Kin'', was self-released in early 2014.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.npr.org/2016/05/24/478723192/songs-we-love-joseph-white-flag|title=Songs We Love: Joseph, 'White Flag'|website=NPR|last=Christensen|first=David|date=2016-05-24|access-date=2016-06-01}}</ref> Their second album ''I'm Alone, No You're Not'' was produced by [[Mike Mogis]] and was released on August 26, 2016 by [[ATO Records]].<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web|title=I'm Alone, No You're Not by Joseph|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/im-alone-no-youre-not/id1115881921|website=Apple Music|access-date=2016-08-26}}</ref> Their third album ''Stay Awake'' was released on September 15, 2017 by ATO Records.<ref>{{Citation|title=Stay Awake by Joseph|date=2017-09-15|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/stay-awake/1278397113|work=Apple Music|language=en-US|access-date=2018-04-14}}</ref>

==History==

===Founding and ''Native Dreamer Kin''===
Joseph was formed when Natalie Closner Schepman, who had been pursuing a solo singer-songwriter career, recruited her younger twin sisters Meegan and Allison to join her on vocals for a new project.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://response.spu.edu/alumni/natalie-closner-schepman/|title=Natalie Closner Schepman|website=Response|publisher=Seattle Pacific University|date=2016|access-date=2016-12-17}}</ref> They chose their name as a tribute to the town of [[Joseph, Oregon]] and their grandfather Jo.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=https://shorefire.com/releases/entry/sibling-trio-joseph-ato-records-debut-im-alone-no-youre-not-august-26|title=Sibling Trio Joseph Conjure Vocal Alchemy On ATO Records Debut 'I'm Alone, No You're Not' Out August|website=Shore Fire Media|access-date=2016-06-02|date=2016-05-24}}</ref> Originally performing under a different name, a label representative said the name did not match the gravitas of their songwriting; they had just been camping in Joseph, a regular activity for them, and decided on the name.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Joseph • The Kate|url=https://thekate.tv/artist/joseph/|website=[[The Kate]]|language=en-US|type=As shared on the TV special.|access-date=May 17, 2020}}</ref> They self-released their debut album ''Native Dreamer Kin'' in 2014, and built a following playing intimate house shows as a trio accompanied by acoustic guitar and [[foot drum]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://mindequalsblown.net/interviews/interview-joseph|title=Interview: Joseph|date=2015-06-03|website=Mind Equals Blown|language=en-US|last=Guido|first=Emma|access-date=2016-06-02}}</ref>

===''I'm Alone, No You're Not''===
Joseph were signed to [[ATO Records]] in 2015, releasing an acoustic two-song digital ''ATO Sessions'' EP and accompanying video series.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://atorecords.com/featured/joseph-signs-to-ato-watch-their-ato-session-now/|title=Joseph signs to ATO – watch their ATO Session now!|website=ATO Records|access-date=2016-06-02|date=2015-09-16}}</ref> In 2015, they recorded ''I'm Alone, No You're Not'' with producer [[Mike Mogis]]. The album was released on August 26, 2016.<ref name=":1" /> It debuted at #1 on [[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'s [[Heatseekers Albums|Heatseekers]] chart and remained at the top of the chart for two weeks.<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/archive/charts/2016/heatseekers-albums|title=Heatseekers Albums - 2016 Archive|work=Billboard Charts Archive|access-date=2016-09-15}}</ref> They released their first single, "White Flag", on May 24, 2016 with a premiere on [[NPR|NPR's]] Songs We Love.<ref name=":0" /> "White Flag" reached No. 1 on the [[Adult Alternative Songs]] chart in October 2016,<ref>{{Cite magazine|url=https://www.billboard.com/charts/triple-a/2016-10-15|title=Alternative Music: Top Alternative Songs Chart|magazine=Billboard|date=28 November 2013|access-date=2016-10-04}}</ref> and charted on [[Spotify]]'s US Viral Top Ten days after its release.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://shorefire.com/releases/entry/joseph-unveil-music-video-for-white-flag-fallon-performance-spotify-success|title=Joseph Unveil Music Video For "White Flag" Following Fallon Performance and Spotify Viral Success|date=2016-07-07|work=Shore Fire Media|access-date=2016-09-15}}</ref> In Fall 2016, Joseph launched an a cappella covers contest,<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://site.thebandjoseph.com/news/2016/9/20/its-an-a-cappella-contest|title=It's an a cappella contest!!|work=Joseph|last=Closner|first=Natalie|access-date=2017-07-21|date=2016-09-20|language=en-US}}</ref> which included submissions from fans, amateur musicians, and college a cappella groups.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=joseph+a+cappella|title=Joseph A Cappella|website=YouTube|language=en|access-date=2017-07-21}}</ref>

The band made their television debut performing "White Flag" on ''[[The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon]]'',<ref>{{Citation|last=NBC.com|title=Joseph: White Flag - The Tonight Show|date=2016-06-24|url=http://www.nbc.com/the-tonight-show/video/joseph-white-flag/3056772|language=en-us|access-date=2016-06-27|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160627054301/http://www.nbc.com/the-tonight-show/video/joseph-white-flag/3056772|archive-date=2016-06-27}}</ref> followed by appearances on ''[[Later... with Jools Holland]]'', ''[[The Ellen DeGeneres Show|Ellen]]'', ''[[Conan (talk show)|CONAN]]'', ''[[CBS This Morning]]'', and NBC's ''[[Today (U.S. TV program)|TODAY]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://shorefire.com/releases/entry/mesmerizing-sibling-trio-joseph-perform-live-with-ny-times-from-electric-la|title=Mesmerizing Sibling Trio Joseph Perform Live With NY Times From Electric Lady Studios At Noon ET Tod|date=2016-08-26|access-date=2016-09-15}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last=TheEllenShow|title=Joseph Performs 'White Flag'!|date=2016-10-25|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qO-yBIbCTLo|work=YouTube|access-date=2018-09-09}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://teamcoco.com/schedule/2016-08-29/thursday/joseph|title=Joseph will be appearing on CONAN Thursday, September 1st, 2016|website=TeamCoco|language=en|access-date=2018-09-09}}</ref> Prior to the album's release, the band was named a [[Spotify]] Spotlight Artist and toured with [[James Bay (singer)|James Bay]]. The band re-joined Bay as support on a sold-out arena tour in Fall 2016.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.redlightmanagement.com/sibling-trio-joseph-conjure-vocal-alchemy-on-ato-records-debut-im-alone-no-youre-not-out-august-26/|title=Sibling Trio Joseph Conjure Vocal Alchemy On ATO Records Debut 'I'm Alone, No You're Not' Out August 26|date=2016-05-24|work=Red Light Management|access-date=2018-09-09|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.musiclifemagazine.net/james-bay-live-at-the-fillmore-detroit-michigan-october-4-2016/|title=James Bay with Joseph live at the Fillmore - Detroit, Michigan - October 4, 2016|last=Hartling|first=B.|date=2016-10-08|work=Music Life Magazine|access-date=2018-09-09|language=en-US}}</ref> Joseph has performed both as an acoustic trio and a full band at music festivals across the globe including [[Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival|Coachella]], [[Lollapalooza]], [[Bonnaroo Music Festival|Bonnaroo]], [[Newport Folk Festival]], [[Sasquatch! Music Festival|Sasquatch Festival]], [[Glastonbury Festival|Glastonbury]], [[Outside Lands Music and Arts Festival]], [https://pilgrimagefestival.com/ Pilgrimage Music Festival] and more.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://site.thebandjoseph.com/shows/|title=Shows|website=Joseph|language=en-US|access-date=2017-07-21}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.chicagotribune.com/entertainment/lollapalooza/94274610-132.html|title=Video: Joseph is having more fun than anyone at Lollapalooza 2017|last=Morales|first=Roger|date=2017|work=Chicago Tribune|access-date=2018-09-09|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.npr.org/2017/08/11/539346196/joseph-live-in-concert-newport-folk-2017|title=Joseph, Live In Concert: Newport Folk 2017|last=Horn|first=Rachel|date=2017-08-11|work=NPR|access-date=2018-09-09|language=en}}</ref>

=== ''Good Luck, Kid'' ===
On July 9, 2019 the band announced on social media and their website their new album ''[[Good Luck, Kid]]'' would be released September 13, 2019. The first single, ''Fighter'', was described on Twitter by Meegan  as "a song looking you in the eye and asking you to come alive and fight.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://inews.co.uk/culture/television/jools-holland-hootenanny-line-up-2020-whos-on-when-time-tonight-filmed-1337350|title=Here's who is appearing on the Jools Holland Hootenanny on New Year's Eve|website=inews.co.uk|date=31 December 2019|language=en|access-date=2020-01-01}}</ref> It’s asking you to wake up and make a choice to have presence for what and who is in front of you." The title track, “Good Luck Kid,” was released as a single on August 13, 2019.
