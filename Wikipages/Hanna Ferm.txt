'''Hanna Alma Beata Ferm''' (born 23 October 2000) is a Swedish singer. She competed in ''[[Idol 2017 (Sweden)|Idol 2017]]'' where she placed second.

== Career ==
Ferm participated in 2014 in the [[TV4 (Sweden)|TV4]] talent show ''[[Talang Sverige 2014]]'', which was broadcast on [[TV3 (Sweden)|TV3]].<ref name="nyheter24.se">{{cite web|url=https://nyheter24.se/modette/livsstil/895404-5-saker-du-inte-visste-om-hanna-ferm-fran-idol|title=5 saker du inte visste om Hanna Ferm från Idol|date=12 October 2017|publisher=Nyheter24|access-date=27 February 2018}}</ref> She made it to the second semifinal of the competition.<ref name="nyheter24.se"/> Three years later, she participated in ''Idol 2017'' where she made it to the final held at [[Globen Arena]]. She ended up in second place, behind the winner [[Chris Kläfford]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/a/7l0zyW/hanna-ferm-jag-kanner-mig-som-en-vinnare|title=Hanna Ferm efter Idol-finalen: "Känner mig som en vinnare"|publisher=Aftonbladet|access-date=27 February 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171209204049/https://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/a/7l0zyW/hanna-ferm-jag-kanner-mig-som-en-vinnare|archive-date=9 December 2017|url-status=live}}</ref>

On 2 February 2018, Ferm released her first single called "Never Mine" after signing a record deal with [[Universal Music Group|Universal Music]]. It was written and produced by [[Jimmy Jansson]], Isak Alveus Bornebusch, and ISELIN.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://sverigesradio.se/sida/artikel.aspx?programid=104&artikel=6892062|title=Hanna Ferm premiärspelar sin första singel - P4 Göteborg|first=Sveriges|last=Radio|publisher=Sveriges Radio|access-date=27 February 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180228161730/http://sverigesradio.se/sida/artikel.aspx?programid=104&artikel=6892062|archive-date=28 February 2018|url-status=live}}</ref> In July, she released her second single called "Bad Habit", which was written and produced by Herman Gardarfve, [[Patrik Jean]], Melanie Wehbe, and Hanna herself.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://nyheter24.se/noje/922216-kanner-du-igen-hanna-ferm-i-mello-det-ar-inte-sa-konstigt|title=Känner du igen Hanna Ferm i Mello? Det är inte så konstigt|publisher=Nyheter24|access-date=1 December 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190824221432/https://nyheter24.se/noje/922216-kanner-du-igen-hanna-ferm-i-mello-det-ar-inte-sa-konstigt|archive-date=24 August 2019|url-status=live}}</ref>

On 16 November 2018, Ferm returned as a guest performer to [[Idol 2018]] on [[TV4 (Sweden)|TV4]] to duet with contestant [[Bragi Bergsson]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.tv4.se/idol/klipp/hanna-ferm-bragi-up-where-we-belong-joe-cocker-jennifer-warnes-idol-2018-11927305|title=Hanna Ferm & Bragi: Up Where We Belong – Joe Cocker, Jennifer Warnes – Idol 2018 - Idol - tv4.se|access-date=2018-11-17|language=sv|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181118035239/https://www.tv4.se/idol/klipp/hanna-ferm-bragi-up-where-we-belong-joe-cocker-jennifer-warnes-idol-2018-11927305|archive-date=2018-11-18|url-status=live}}</ref>

Ferm participated in ''[[Melodifestivalen 2019]]'' in a duet with [[LIAMOO]] with the song "Hold You". They had sung together while Hanna was in Idol 2017. LIAMOO had won the competition the year before.<ref>{{cite web|last=Dahlander|first=Gustav|url=https://blogg.svt.se/melodifestivalen-expertbloggen/artisterna-melodifestivalen-2019-hela-listan-s%C3%A5-blir-delt%C3%A4vlingarna/|title=Artisterna i Melodifestivalen 2019: Hela listan – så blir deltävlingarna|work=Gustavs expertblogg|publisher=[[Sveriges Television|SVT]]|language=sv|date=27 November 2018|access-date=27 November 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181127092702/https://blogg.svt.se/melodifestivalen-expertbloggen/artisterna-melodifestivalen-2019-hela-listan-s%C3%A5-blir-delt%C3%A4vlingarna/|archive-date=27 November 2018|url-status=live}}</ref> On 9 February 2019, in semi-final 2 of the competition, they qualified for the final, where they finished in 3rd place.

Outta Breath, which was released as a single in October 2019, was written and produced by Jakob Redtzer, Sorana, William Jerner, and Hanna.

Hanna Ferm competed as a solo singer in ''[[Melodifestivalen 2020]]'' with the song "[[Brave (Hanna Ferm song)|Brave]]". She performed in the fourth semi-final at the [[Malmö Arena]] in [[Malmö]] on 22 February 2020<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.aftonbladet.se/tv/a/302531/hanna-ferm-klart-for-melodifestivalen-2020|title=Hanna Ferm klar för Melodifestivalen 2020|publisher=Aftonbladet|access-date=1 December 2019}}</ref> and went directly to the final, hosted at [[Friends Arena]] in [[Solna Municipality|Solna]] on 7 March 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.aftonbladet.se/a/Jo4XQP|title=STORT jubel när Hanna Ferm och Victor Crone gick direkt till final|website=Aftonbladet|language=sv|access-date=2020-02-22}}</ref> She ended up in fourth place (out of 12 finalists in total), scoring a total of 94 points. She received a higher score by the television votes (third-most votes) than by the international jury (ninth-most points).<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/melodifestivalen/a/K39LxE/the-mamas-vinner-melodifestivalen-2020|title=The Mamas vinner Melodifestivalen 2020|last=Thornéus|first=Ebba|date=7 March 2020|website=[[Aftonbladet]]|access-date=8 March 2020}}</ref>

The single Sweet Temptation was released on 12 June 2020. It was written and produced by [[Dotter (singer)|Dotter]], Dino Medanhodzic, and Hanna. It was accompanied by her first music video as a professional artist.
