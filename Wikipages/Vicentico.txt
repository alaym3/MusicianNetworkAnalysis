'''Gabriel Julio Fernández Capello''' (born {{birth date|1964|7|24}} in [[Buenos Aires]], [[Argentina]]) is a musician and composer better known by his stage name '''Vicentico'''. Co-founder and vocalist of the band [[Los Fabulosos Cadillacs]] along with [[Flavio Cianciarulo]]. He was part of the group since its creation in 1984 to the year 2001, when he began a solo career as a singer.<ref>{{cite web|url={{Allmusic|class=artist|id=p562051|pure_url=yes}}|title=Biography: Vincentico|last=Birchmeier|first=Jason|publisher=[[Allmusic]]|access-date=28 May 2010}}</ref> Vicentico won the [[Latin Grammy Award for Best Rock Album]] in 2021 for ''El Pozo Brillante'' and [[Latin Grammy Award for Best Rock Song|Best Rock Song]] for "Ahora 1".<ref>{{cite web |last1=Flores |first1=Griselda |last2=Cobo |first2=Leila |title=Camilo Is Top Winner, Cuban Anthem ‘Patria y Vida’ Wins Song of the Year at 2021 Latin Grammys: Winners List |url=https://www.billboard.com/music/latin/2021-latin-grammys-winners-list-1234999583/ |website=Billboard |access-date=19 November 2021 |date=19 November 2021}}</ref>

He lives with his wife, actress Valeria Bertucelli, and their sons Florián and Vicente.
