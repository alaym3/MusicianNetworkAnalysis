'''Ishmael 262 Ali''' (known professionally as '''IshDARR''') is an American rapper, hip hop artist, and actor from [[Milwaukee]], [[Wisconsin]]. He has released 2 EPs and 3 LPs. His most recent project, the mixtape ''Slow Down, KID'', was released in November 2018 and was distributed by [[Empire Distribution|EMPIRE]]. IshDARR is also an actor who had a role in the 2018 film, ''[[White Boy Rick]]'' and was managed between 2013-2017 by [[Enrique "Mag" Rodriguez]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=https://hiphopdx.com|first=HipHopDX-|date=2016-03-24|title=The Wisconsin Soul: The Best Rappers Out Of The Badger State|url=https://hiphopdx.com/editorials/id.3257/title.the-wisconsin-soul-the-best-rappers-out-of-the-badger-state|access-date=2020-07-05|website=HipHopDX}}</ref>

==Early life and education==

Ishmael Ali was born in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. He and his family moved to [[Waukegan, Illinois|Waukegan]], [[Illinois]] for a few years before moving back to Milwaukee when Ali was 7 or 8.<ref name="RadioM2014">{{cite news | last=Gonderman| first=Brenna| title =Interview with IshDARR: A Young Milwaukee Emcee| work=Radio Milwaukee| date =5 September 2014 | url =https://radiomilwaukee.org/discover-music/music-news/interview-with-ish-darr-a-young-milwaukee-emcee/| accessdate =30 August 2019}}</ref> He began making music, freestyling, and writing lyrics in 7th grade.<ref name="XXLBreak">{{cite news | last=C.M.| first=Emmanuel| title =The Break Presents: IshDARR| work=XXL| date =21 October 2016 | url =https://www.xxlmag.com/news/2016/10/ishdarr-interview-the-break/| accessdate =30 August 2019}}</ref> As a freshman at [[Messmer High School (Milwaukee)|Messmer High School]], Ali met his future manager ([[Enrique "Mag" Rodriguez]]) who was friends with his older brother and fellow rapper, Isaiah "BoodahDARR" Ali. The three (along with another Messmer rapper, Wave Chappelle) formed a group called the iLL Collective which released three songs before disbanding when BoodahDARR went to college. Rodriguez began working with IshDARR on music in late 2013 and early 2014 using a studio in the high school.<ref name="MJS2015">{{cite news | last=Levy| first=Piet| title =Milwaukee rapper Ish Darr, 18, getting national noise| newspaper=Milwaukee Journal Sentinel| date =3 June 2015 | url =http://archive.jsonline.com/entertainment/musicandnightlife/milwaukee-rapper-ish-darr-18-is-rising-fast-thanks-to-national-press-for-his-recent-mixtape-b9950936-306068791.html| accessdate =30 August 2019}}</ref><ref name="WIG2016">{{cite news | last=Grihalva| first=Joey| title =Meet the man behind Milwaukee's rap phenom IshDARR | newspaper=Wisconsin Gazette| date =20 October 2016 | url =https://www.wisconsingazette.com/entertainment/meet-the-man-behind-milwaukee-s-rap-phenom-ishdarr/article_4942ee08-d0e1-5bbf-9d38-20864d1d7193.html| accessdate =30 August 2019}}</ref> IshDARR graduated from Messmer in 2014.<ref name="Shep2014">{{cite news | last=Rytlewski| first=Evan| title =IshDARR and the New Face of Conscious Rap| work=Shepherd Express| date =16 April 2014 | url =https://shepherdexpress.com/music/music-feature/ishdarr-new-face-conscious-rap/| accessdate =30 August 2019}}</ref>

==Career==

In early 2014 as IshDARR was preparing to release his debut solo EP, he was contacted by an [[Artists and repertoire|A&R]] representative at [[Epic Records]], Amanda Berkowitz. She would later become IshDARR's mentor and co-manager.<ref name="WIG2016"/> He released his EP, ''The Better Life'', in April 2014. The day after the collection's release, he opened for [[Schoolboy Q]], [[Isaiah Rashad]], [[Vince Staples]], and [[Audio Push]] at [[The Rave/Eagles Club|The Rave]] in Milwaukee.<ref name="Shep2014"/> In the summer of that year, IshDARR and his manager [[Enrique "Mag" Rodriguez]] were flown to [[Los Angeles]] to have a meeting with [[Mike Caren]], the Vice President of A&R at [[Atlantic Records]] at the time. Although the two decided against signing a deal, IshDARR received interest from several record labels afterward,<ref name="WIG2016"/> and joined The Agency Group (a talent booking firm) in December 2014.<ref name="MJS2015"/> He also opened up for [[Ab-Soul]] on his "These Days" tour in the fall of 2014.<ref name="Complex2015">{{cite news | last=Nostro| first=Lauren| title =Premiere: Stream Milwaukee Rapper IshDARR's "Old Soul Young Spirit" Album| work=Complex| date =4 March 2015 | url =https://www.complex.com/music/2015/03/ishdarr-old-soul-young-spirit-album-premiere| accessdate =30 August 2019}}</ref>

In March 2015, he released his first mixtape, ''Old Soul, Young Spirit'', which featured the single "Too Bad".<ref name="Complex2015"/> The song gained increased popularity after actress [[Chloë Grace Moretz]] tweeted it out to her followers. It also reached the top 10 on [[Hype Machine]]<ref name="MJS2015"/> and garnered over 7 million streams by the following year.<ref name="WIG2016"/> IshDARR performed at [[South by Southwest]] in 2015<ref name="MJS2015"/> and toured Europe later that year and in early 2016.<ref name="DX2016">{{cite news | last=Grefe| first=Cliff| title =The Wisconsin Soul: The Best Rappers Out Of The Badger State| work=HipHopDX| date =24 March 2016 | url =https://hiphopdx.com/editorials/id.3257/title.the-wisconsin-soul-the-best-rappers-out-of-the-badger-state| accessdate =30 August 2019}}</ref> In the summer of 2016, he performed at [[Summerfest]] in Milwaukee opening for [[Jason Derulo]] on the main stage.<ref name="XXLBreak"/>

In October 2016, he released his second mixtape, ''Broken Hearts & Bankrolls'',<ref name="XXLBreak"/> which featured the single, "Sugar".<ref name="WIG2016"/> In November of that year, IshDARR was featured on the [[Jacob Latimore]] track, "The Real", and its accompanying video.<ref name="ComplexJacob">{{cite news | last=Ortiz| first=Edwin| title =Premiere: Watch Jacob Latimore's "The Real" Video With Ishdarr| work=Complex| date =10 November 2016 | url =https://www.complex.com/music/2016/11/jacob-latimore-new-video-premiere-the-real-feat-ishdar| accessdate =30 August 2019}}</ref> Throughout the year, he opened for a variety of acts including [[Anderson Paak]], [[PartyNextDoor]], and [[Jeremih]].<ref name="MJS2017">{{cite news | last=Levy| first=Piet| title =Wisconsin Bands to Watch 2016: Where are they now?| newspaper=Milwaukee Journal Sentinel| date =5 June 2017 | url =https://www.jsonline.com/story/entertainment/music/2017/01/05/wisconsin-bands-watch-2016-where-they-now/96052038/| accessdate =30 August 2019}}</ref> In April 2017, it was announced that IshDARR had been cast as Steven "Freaky Steve" Roussell in the film, ''White Boy Rick'', starring [[Matthew McConaughey]].<ref name="MJSWBR">{{cite news | last=Levy| first=Piet| title =Milwaukee rapper IshDARR cast in 'White Boy Rick,' starring Matthew McConaughey| newspaper=Milwaukee Journal Sentinel| date =5 April 2017 | url =https://www.jsonline.com/story/entertainment/music/2017/04/05/milwaukee-rapper-ishdarr-cast-white-boy-rick-starring-matthew-mcconaughey/100068498/| accessdate =30 August 2019}}</ref> The film, which also featured IshDARR's brother BoodahDARR, had its wide release in September 2018.<ref name="MJSWBR2018">{{cite news | last=Levy| first=Piet| title =Milwaukee rappers (and brothers) hit the big screen with Matthew McConaughey in 'White Boy Rick'| newspaper=Milwaukee Journal Sentinel| date =7 September 2018 | url Later in April 2017, IshDARR released an EP, ''Four The Better'', which featured the singles "Free"<ref name="MJSBreak">{{cite news | last=Levy| first=Piet| title =Breakout Milwaukee rapper IshDARR shows real star potential at Turner Hall Ballroom| newspaper=Milwaukee Journal Sentinel| date =13 April 2017 | url =https://www.jsonline.com/story/entertainment/music/2017/04/13/breakout-milwaukee-rapper-ishdarr-shows-real-star-potential-turner-hall-ballroom/100406194/| accessdate =30 August 2019}}</ref> and "Mucho Mango".<ref name="ComplexFour">{{cite news | last=Moore| first=Jacob| title =Listen to Milwaukee Rapper IshDARR's New Project 'Four The Better'| work=Complex| date =13 April 2017 | url =https://www.complex.com/pigeons-and-planes/2017/04/ishdarr-four-the-better| accessdate =30 August 2019}}</ref> Later that year, he again appeared at Summerfest,<ref name="Bill2017">{{cite news | last=Lamarre| first=Carl| title =Rising Rapper IshDARR Talks Milwaukee Roots & Acting Alongside Matthew McConaughey in Upcoming Movie 'White Boy Rick'| work=Billboard| date =31 May 2017 | url =https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/7809762/ishdarr-milwaukee-white-boy-rick-interview| accessdate =30 August 2019}}</ref> this time in a headlining role. His performance was recorded for the music video for his single, "Sanity".<ref name="MR2017">{{cite news | last=Wild| first=Matt| title =Watch IshDARR dominate Summerfest 2017 in video for "Sanity"| work=Milwaukee Record| date =14 August 2017 | url =https://milwaukeerecord.com/music/watch-ishdarr-dominate-summerfest-2017-in-video-for-sanity/| accessdate =30 August 2019}}</ref> In 2018, he began releasing several new tracks, starting with "They Lost Me 2"<ref name="HNHHTLM2">{{cite news | last=P.| first=Milca| title =IshDARR Previews Debut Album With "They Lost Me 2"| work=HotNewHipHop| date =20 January 2018 | url =https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/ishdarr-previews-debut-album-with-they-lost-me-2-new-song.1977062.html| accessdate =30 August 2019}}</ref> and "Fangs" in January.<ref name="EarFangs">{{cite news | last=Steindorf| first=Jack| title =IshDARR unleashes a dynamic hip-hop flow in latest single "Fangs"| work=Earmilk| date =29 January 2018 | url =https://earmilk.com/2018/01/29/ishdarr-is-unleashes-a-dynamic-hip-hop-flow-in-latest-single-fangs/| accessdate =30 August 2019}}</ref> He followed that with "Critical" in May<ref name="ComplexCritical">{{cite news | last=Ortiz| first=Edwin| title =Premiere: IshDARR Shares New Song "Critical"| work=Complex| date =24 May 2018 | url =https://www.complex.com/music/2018/05/ishdarr-critical| accessdate =30 August 2019}}</ref> and "Slow Down" in October.<ref name="BillSlow">{{cite news | last=Saponara| first=Michael| title =IshDARR Shows No Signs Of Stopping With New Single 'Slow Down': Premiere| work=Billboard| date =29 October 2018 | url =https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/8482072/ishdarr-slow-down| accessdate =30 August 2019}}</ref>

Album "Slow Down, KID"| work=HotNewHipHop| date =2 November 2018 | url =https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/ishdarr-comes-through-strong-on-his-sophomore-album-slow-down-kid-new-mixtape.118584.html| accessdate =30 August 2019}}</ref> and distributed by EMPIRE.<ref name="Source2018">{{cite news | last=Van Sol| first=Matt| title =Milwaukee's Newest Hometown Hero Hits Over a Million Streams in 20 days with "Slow Down Kid"| work=The Source| date =5 December 2018 | url =http://thesource.com/2018/12/05/milwaukees-newest-hometown-hero-hits-over-a-million-streams-in-20-days-with-slow-down-kid/| accessdate =30 August 2019}}</ref> A deluxe edition of the LP was released in April 2019 and featured 4 additional tracks.<ref name="Shep2019">{{cite news | last=Rytlewski| first=Evan| title "Checks & Balances".<ref name="Radio2019">{{cite news | last=Baghai| first=Stephanie| title =Two Milwaukee producers call on IshDARR and BoodahDARR for an amped-up breakout single| work=Radio
