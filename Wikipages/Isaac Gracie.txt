'''Isaac Joseph Gracie-Burrow''' (born 28 October 1994),<ref>[https://www.facebook.com/isaacgraciemusic/posts/happy-birthday-to-you-isaac-gracie-/911174572367230/ "Isaac Gracie - Happy birthday to..."] Facebook. Retrieved 20 December 2018</ref> known professionally as simply '''Isaac Gracie''', is a British singer-songwriter from [[Ealing]], [[West London]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/isaac-gracie-mn0003495578/biography|title=Isaac Gracie {{!}} Biography & History {{!}} AllMusic|website=AllMusic|access-date=2018-06-04}}</ref> As a child, Gracie sang in the Ealing Abbey Choir and started writing songs at the age of 14. Gracie signed a record deal with [[Virgin EMI Records]] in 2016<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/entertainment-arts-43667393|title=Isaac Gracie: From choir boy to the charts|last=Savage|first=Mark|date=2018-04-10|work=BBC News|access-date=2018-06-04|language=en-GB}}</ref> and released his self titled debut album in 2018.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.readdork.com/news/have-a-listen-to-isaac-gracies-debut-album-out-now/|title=Have a listen to Isaac Gracie's debut album, out now {{!}} Dork|date=2018-04-13|work=Dork|access-date=2018-06-04|language=en-GB}}</ref> Gracie has been compared with the likes of [[Jeff Buckley]], [[Nick Drake]], and [[Bob Dylan]], amongst others.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.standard.co.uk/go/london/music/isaac-gracie-review-former-choirboys-voice-is-an-instrument-to-be-proud-of-a3825116.html|title=Isaac Gracie's voice is an instrument to be proud of|work=Evening Standard|access-date=2018-06-04|language=en-GB}}</ref>
