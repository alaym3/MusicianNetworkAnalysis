'''The Key of Awesome''' (formerly '''Barely Productions''' and '''Barely Political''') was a [[YouTube]] channel that produced comedy videos starring writer/performer Mark Douglass. ""The Key of Awesome" was created by Mark Douglas and [[Ben Relles]] and is the channel's most popular series, mainly producing [[Viral video|viral music videos]] and parodies.{{citation needed|date=June 2015}} Barely Political was created in June 2007 by [[Ben Relles]] and subsequently debuted its first music video, "[[Crush on Obama]]", starring [[Amber Lee Ettinger|Amber Lee "Obama Girl" Ettinger]], created by [[Ben Relles]] and Jake Chudnow.<ref>http://www.jakechudnow.com</ref> Videos on the channel have been seen over 3.2 billion times online. "The Key of Awesome" became the central identity of the channel.

The Key of Awesome created numerous videos covering political topics, the military, and news media. During the [[2008 United States presidential election|2008 presidential primaries]], the channel invited some of the [[List of candidates in the 2008 United States presidential election|candidates]] to be featured in a video, including former Senator [[Mike Gravel]].

In October 2007, Barely Political was bought by [[Next New Networks]] for an unknown amount which in turn was acquired by YouTube in spring 2011. Herb Scannell, co-founder and CEO of Next New Networks, stated, "With Barely Political, we've added a team that can grow our reach to a very important audience &mdash; one that likes their politics with a healthy serving of humor &mdash; timed perfectly with the upcoming 2008 election season."<ref>{{cite web|first=Mark |last=Hefflinger |url=http://www.dmwmedia.com/news/2007/10/17/next-new-networks-buys-obama-girl-creator-barelypolitical |title=Next New Networks Buys "Obama Girl" Creator BarelyPolitical |publisher=Digital Media Wire |date=2007-10-17 |accessdate=2008-11-16 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080706042931/http://www.dmwmedia.com/news/2007/10/17/next-new-networks-buys-obama-girl-creator-barelypolitical |archivedate=2008-07-06 }}</ref>

Its videos were mainly directed by Tom Small and feature writing and performances by Mark Douglas, [[Todd Womack]], Doug Larsen, Anastasia Douglas, Bryan Olsen, and occasionally Lauren Francesca, [[Andrea Feczko]], [[Amber Lee Ettinger|Amber Lee "Obama Girl" Ettinger]], [[Ewan Gotfryd]], and [[Michael Stevens (educator)|Michael Stevens]].

In 2014, the channel was listed on [[New Media Rockstars]] Top 100 Channels, ranked at #29.<ref>{{cite web|title=The NMR Top 100 YouTube Channels: 50-26!|url= http://newmediarockstars.com/2014/12/the-nmr-top-100-youtube-channels-50-26/|website=New Media Rockstars|accessdate=6 January 2015}}</ref>

On February 4, 2018, in form of a song, Mark Douglas announced that he would be taking an indefinite break from YouTube, thanking his fans for their support over the years.<ref>{{Citation|last=The Key of Awesome|title=THE LAST KEY OF AWESOME|date=2018-02-04|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4FkEYUPr2gg|accessdate=2018-02-04}}</ref>

==Response to Obama Girl==
When asked about the video by the ''[[Des Moines Register]]'' on June 18, 2007, Obama said, "It's just one more example of the fertile imagination of the Internet. More stuff like this will be popping up all the time."<ref>{{cite web|first=Jason|last=Clayworth|url=http://blogs.dmregister.com/?p=6506|title=Obama responds to 'crush'|date=2007-06-19|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080211114524/http://blogs.dmregister.com/?p=6506|archivedate=2008-02-11}}</ref> Obama told the [[Associated Press]] that the Obama Girl video had upset his daughters and said, "You do wish people would think about what impact their actions have on kids and families."<ref name="cnn">{{cite news|title='Obama girl' may become Clinton's girl|url=http://politicalticker.blogs.cnn.com/2007/08/23/obama-girl-may-become-clintons-girl/ | publisher=[[CNN]]|date=2007-08-23|accessdate=2008-11-05| archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20081122044008/http://politicalticker.blogs.cnn.com/2007/08/23/obama-girl-may-become-clintons-girl/| archivedate= 22 November 2008 | url-status= live}}</ref>
