'''Chelsea Nikkel''', better known by her [[stage name]] '''Princess Chelsea''', is a producer and musician from [[Auckland]], [[New Zealand]], and a previous member of [[twee pop]] band [[The Brunettes]] and Auckland band Teenwolf. Nikkel is associated with the [[Lil' Chief Records]] collective and is a part-time member of [[Auckland]] soul group The Cosbys and Disciples of Macca, a [[Paul McCartney]] covers band featuring members of [[The Brunettes]], [[Ruby Suns]], [[Bressa Creeting Cake]] and [[Lawrence Arabia]] and more recently performs as bass player in three piece rock n roll band 'Hang Loose'. Nikkel works in Auckland as a composer.<ref name="facebook">{{cite web |url=https://www.facebook.com/wonderfulprincesschelsea/info |title= Princess Chelsea, About |work=[[Facebook]] |publisher=[[Facebook.com]] |accessdate=6 January 2013}}</ref>

Her baroque musical style has been attributed to her classical training, and the ''[[New Zealand Herald]]'' praised her "angelic vocals and acerbic wit".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://lilchiefrecords.com/epk/princess-chelsea/#bio|title=Princess Chelsea - EPK|website=Lilchiefrecords.com|accessdate=9 October 2018}}</ref> Her best known song, "[[The Cigarette Duet]]" received significant press exposure after its video<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4TV_128Fz2g|title=Princess Chelsea - The Cigarette Duet|date=7 June 2011|accessdate=9 October 2018|publisher=[[YouTube]]}}</ref> went viral on [[YouTube]] in early 2012, recently reaching over 62 million views. This led to her being featured on ''[[The Guardian]]''<nowiki>'</nowiki>s "New Band of the Day".<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2012/jun/01/new-band-princess-chelsea |title= New Band of the Day: Princess Chelsea (No 1,281) |newspaper=[[The Guardian]] |date=1 June 2012 |accessdate=6 January 2013}}</ref>

Chelsea's song "World Turns Grey" was remixed in a collaboration with the German electronic music producer, [[Robin Schulz]]. The remix is on the album ''Sugar'' released by Schulz in September 2015.

In August 2016, she made a cover of a song called "Cold Glass Tube" by [[The Reduction Agents]], which is available on the album ''Waiting for Your Love: A Tribute to The Reduction Agents''. The album was released on iTunes on 19 August.
