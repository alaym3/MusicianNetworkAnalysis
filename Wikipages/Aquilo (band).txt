'''Aquilo''' are an English musical duo from [[Silverdale, Lancashire]], consisting of Tom Higham and Ben Fletcher. They began gaining recognition in 2013 for their singles such as "Calling Me" and "You There", in addition to their five EPs.

The duo's debut album, ''[[Silhouettes (Aquilo album)|Silhouettes]]'', was released on 27 January 2017. Their second studio album, ''[[II (Aquilo album)|ii]]'', was released on 4 May 2018, and the third studio album ''A Safe Place To Be'' was released on 15 October 2021.

== History ==
=== Career beginnings ===
Members Tom Higham and Ben Fletcher grew up as neighbours in [[Silverdale, Lancashire]]. They were both part of rivaling rock bands in their hometown.<ref name="Guardian14apr7" /><ref name="Guardian13apr29" /> Higham studied music production in university.<ref name="Guardian14apr7" /> The two worked on their musical projects individually before collaborating as Aquilo.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/culture/music/new-music/11161160/Aquilo-New-Music.html|title=Aquilo – New Music|website=Telegraph.co.uk|access-date=2016-04-15}}</ref>

=== 2013–present: ''Aquilo'', ''Human'', and ''Calling Me'' ===
[[Image:Aquilo-NY-1.jpg|thumb|left|160px|Tom Higham]]
Aquilo began receiving attention in 2013 for their debut tracks "You There" and "Calling Me".<ref name="Guardian14apr7">{{cite news|last1=Lester|first1=Paul|title=New Band of the Week: Aquilo (No 1,736)|url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2014/apr/07/aquilo|access-date=18 June 2014|publisher=The Guardian|date=7 April 2014}}</ref><ref name="Guardian13apr29">{{cite news|last1=Brinnand|first1=Emily|title=Aquilo – New Band Up North #24|url=https://www.theguardian.com/uk/the-northerner/2013/apr/29/aquilo-new-band-up-north|access-date=18 June 2014|publisher=The Guardian|date=29 April 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Aquilo – "You There"|url=http://pigeonsandplanes.com/2014/02/aquilo/|website=[[Pigeons & Planes]]|access-date=18 June 2014}}</ref> "You There" was included in [[BBC Radio 1]]'s playlist on 12 June 2014 and the "Best Of BBC Introducing Stage" choices.<ref>{{cite web|title=Radio 1 Playlist|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/radio1/playlist#introducing|publisher=BBC|access-date=18 June 2014|date=12 June 2014}}</ref> The song was also featured in the trailer for the 2014 independent drama ''[[Camp X-Ray (film)|Camp X-Ray]]''.<ref>{{cite web|title=Best of BBC Introducing|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/events/errnc8/videos/p021rb8k#p021tl01|website=Glastonbury|publisher=BBC|access-date=29 June 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Reilly|first1=Travis|title=Kristen Stewart Shakes Bella Swan Stigma in First ‘Camp X-Ray’ Trailer|url=http://www.thewrap.com/kristen-stewart-tries-to-shake-bella-swan-stigma-in-first-camp-x-ray-trailer-video/|access-date=13 August 2014|work=The Wrap|date=8 August 2014|quote=The trailer contains  ... Aquilo's haunting song “You There” ...}}</ref> Additionally, they performed "Calling Me" and "You There" on the [[BBC Introducing]] stage at the 2014 [[Glastonbury Festival 2014]] on 28 June 2014.<ref>{{cite web|title=Aquilo|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/events/errnc8/acts/a92q2m#p021trm8|website=Glastonbury|publisher=BBC|access-date=29 June 2014|date=28 June 2014}}</ref> They also played at the 2014 [[Kendal Calling]] festival.<ref name="kendal">{{cite web|title=Lineup 2014|url=http://www.kendalcalling.co.uk/lineup/2014/|publisher=Kendal Calling|access-date=18 June 2014}}</ref> Their first EP, ''Aquilo'', was released on 3 March 2014.

On 8 December 2014, they released their second EP, ''Human''.

On 5 March 2015, their collaboration with popular disc jockey [[Vanic]], "Losing You", was released onto [[YouTube]] and [[SoundCloud]]. It has so far accumulated over 7.5 million 'plays' on SoundCloud as of 10 April 2017.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://soundcloud.com/djvanic/vanic-aquilo-losing-you|title=Vanic x Aquilo – Losing You|website=SoundCloud}}</ref> They also collaborated with French DJ and record producer [[Madeon]] on "Innocence" from his debut album, ''[[Adventure (Madeon album)|Adventure]]'', on 27 March. Besides collaborations, their third EP, ''Calling Me'', was released at the start of June that year. Another EP, ''Painting Picture Of A War,'' was later released on 4 December.

On 17 March 2016, they played alongside alt-pop duo [[Oh Wonder]], at O2 Forum Kentish Town, London.

In November 2016, the band appeared on [[Enigma (German band)|Enigma's]] eighth studio album ''[[The Fall of a Rebel Angel]]''. They provided vocals to the album's closing track and second single, respectively, "[[Amen (Enigma song)|Amen]]".<ref>[https://www.discogs.com/Enigma-Amen/release/9370505 ''Amen'' by Enigma on Discogs.] Discogs. Retrieved 22 November 2016.</ref>

On 27 January 2017, the duo released their debut album, ''[[Silhouettes (Aquilo album)|Silhouettes]]''.<ref name="billboard">{{cite web|last1=Havens|first1=Lyndsey|title=English Duo AQUILO On Their Atmospheric Debut Album, Anxiety & More|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/7668958/english-duo-aquilo-on-their-atmospheric-debut-album-silhouettes|website=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|access-date=28 January 2017}}</ref> Their second studio album, ''[[II (Aquilo album)|ii]]'', was released on 4 May 2018.<ref>{{cite web| url=https://www.allmusic.com/album/ii-mw0003163858|title=ii – Aquilo – Songs, Reviews, Credits|publisher=AllMusic|access-date=6 August 2018}}</ref>

== Musical style ==
Aquilo's music have been described mainly as [[Alternative rock|alternative]], as well as [[electronica]], [[dream pop]], [[Ambient music|ambient]] and [[Independent music|indie]]. They are known for producing "soft, calm [[Electropop|electro-pop]]",<ref name="Guardian13apr29" /> "chilled electronic",<ref>{{cite web | url=http://lifeisabanana.com/2013/05/03/aquilo-interview/ | date=5 March 2013 | website=Banana | title=Aquilo Interview|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140522023214/http://lifeisabanana.com/2013/05/03/aquilo-interview/|archive-date=22 May 2014}}</ref> "ravishingly refined sort of dreamily sad [[electronica]]",<ref name="Guardian14apr7" /> and "fragile dream pop laced with subtle electronica and soulful vocals."<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20171120190946/https://zebrix.abconcerts.be/en/agenda/events/aquilo/20581/ Aquilo – Agenda – Ancienne Belgique – Official Website] zebrix.abconcerts.be</ref>
