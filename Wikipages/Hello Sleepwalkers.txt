{{nihongo|'''Hello Sleepwalkers'''|ハロー・スリープウォーカーズ}} is a five-member Japanese [[alternative rock]] band from [[Okinawa]] formed in 2008. The band is signed to [[Amuse, Inc.|A-Sketch Music Label]] and have currently released five studio albums, two extended plays, four single albums, and ten singles. They are best known for performing the opening theme song of the [[anime]] ''[[Noragami]]'', {{nihongo|"Goya no Machiawase"|午夜の待ち合わせ|Midnight's Appointment}}. In November 2018, the band announced their hiatus.

Hello Sleepwalkers consists of:<ref>{{cite web|url=http://hellosleepwalkers.com/profile|title=Hello Sleepwalkers Official Profile|date=2016-12-09|website=hellosleepwalkers.com|accessdate=2016-12-09}}</ref>

* {{nihongo|Narumi|ナルミ}} — [[Electric guitar|guitar]], [[Backing vocalist|vocals]]
* {{nihongo|Tasoko|タソコ}} — [[Electric guitar|guitar]]
* {{nihongo|Makoto|マコト}} — [[Bass guitar|bass]]
* {{nihongo|Yūki|ユウキ}} — [[Drum kit|drums]]
