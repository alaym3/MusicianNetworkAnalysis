'''Thelumusa Samuel Owen''' (born 14 April 1991), better known by his stage name '''Tellaman''' is a South African singer, songwriter and record producer. Growing up in a musical background, he is most known for a number one charting single by [[DJ Speedsta]] titled "[[Mayo (song)|Mayo]]"<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.zkhiphani.co.za/speedstas-mayo-hits-number-one-spot/|title=Speedsta's mayo hits number ONE spot|date=24 October 2016|work=Zkhiphani.com|access-date=11 February 2018|url-status=live|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180212083511/https://www.zkhiphani.co.za/speedstas-mayo-hits-number-one-spot/|archivedate=12 February 2018}}</ref> which features himself, [[Yung Swiss]], Frank Casino & [[Shane Eagle]].

== Early life and career ==
Tellaman was born and grew up in [[Durban]], South Africa. He is a self-taught musician whose interest in music was sparked by his mother, who had him singing at home, at church and in a youth choir.<ref name=aab/> At the age of fifteen he decided to pursue music as a career and began writing and recording his own music.<ref name=aaa/>

Tellaman was signed and featured in Soul Candi Record's compilation album titled ''Sould Candi Sessions 15'', alongside MoFlava, Lulo Café, Cueber and Hareal Salko. Tellaman's disc, titled ''Ntsikelelo'', included hit singles "Drinks and Music" featuring [[Okmalumkoolkat]]<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=http://yuledark.co.za/drink-music/|title=Drink & Music|date=26 September 2014|work=Yule Dark|access-date=11 February 2018|url-status=live|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180212083412/http://yuledark.co.za/drink-music/|archivedate=12 February 2018}}</ref> and "Intoxicated" featuring Lastee.
