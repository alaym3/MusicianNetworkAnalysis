'''Zuhura Othman Soud''' (born 22 November 1993<ref>{{Cite web|title=Zuchu – YouTube|url=https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCOn8zDM533kqzhIA8c3NFbQ/about|access-date=6 November 2020|website=www.youtube.com}}</ref>), better known by her stage name '''Zuchu''', is a [[Tanzanian]] singer<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://eafeed.com/zuchu-biography/|title=Zuchu Bio – Age, Career, Education, Songs, WCB, Boyfriend, Net Worth|website=www.eafeed.com|language=en|access-date=3 June 2020}}</ref> and songwriter born in [[Zanzibar]] and currently based in [[Dar es Salaam]] and signed to [[WCB Wasafi]] record label.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thecitizen.co.tz/news/entertainment/Zuchu-is-the-new-face-on-Diamond-s-record-label-WCB/1840560-5519064-8da1n4z/index.html|title=Zuchu is the new face on Diamond's record label WCB |newspaper=[[The Citizen (Tanzania)|The Citizen]] |access-date=4 June 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=17 July 2020|title=Zuchu set to rise under the auspices of Diamond Platnumz|url=https://www.musicinafrica.net/magazine/zuchu-set-rise-under-auspices-diamond-platnumz|access-date=6 November 2020|website=Music in Africa|language=en}}</ref> She was awarded the Silver Plaque Button by YouTube for hitting 100,000 subscribers within a week.<ref>{{Cite web|date=17 July 2020|title=Zuchu set to rise under the auspices of Diamond Platnumz|url=https://www.musicinafrica.net/magazine/zuchu-set-rise-under-auspices-diamond-platnumz|access-date=6 November 2020|website=Music in Africa|language=en}}</ref> She  became the first East African female artist to reach that milestone within a week. She also became the first East African female artist to reach 1 million subscribers on YouTube 11 months later.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Muli|first=Davis|title=We have reached 1M subscribers on YouTube I can't thank you enough, Zuchu - Entertainment News|url=https://www.sde.co.ke/article/2001407744/we-have-reached-1m-subscribers-on-youtube-i-can-t-thank-you-enough-zuchu|access-date=2021-04-05|website=Standard Digital News}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-03-27|title=WCB's Zuchu makes history as she becomes first female artiste in East Africa to hit 1 Million subscribers|url=https://www.pulselive.co.ke/entertainment/celebrities/wcbs-zuchu-makes-history-as-she-becomes-first-female-artiste-in-east-africa-to-hit-1/0h562kd|access-date=2021-04-05|website=Pulse Live Kenya|language=en-US}}</ref> In 2020 Zuchu was named by AFRIMMA as the winner of the Emerging Artist Award.<ref>{{Cite web|date=16 November 2020|title=Check out full list of winners at the 2020 AFRIMMA Awards|url=https://www.pulselive.co.ke/entertainment/celebrities/zuchu-nandy-and-diamond-win-big-at-the-2020-afrimma-awards-full-list/vcs43m6|access-date=24 November 2020|website=Pulse Live Kenya|language=en-US}}</ref>

== Personal life ==
Zuchu comes from a musical [[Swahili people|Swahili]] [[Zanzibari]] family and background. She is a daughter of the revered and famous female Zanzibari [[Taarab]] musician [[:sw:Khadija Kopa|Khadija Kopa]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Diamond's new signee Zuchu features her mum, legendary Khadija Kopa, in new song days after joining WCB |url=https://www.tuko.co.ke/351303-diamonds-signee-zuchu-features-mum-legendary-khadija-kopa-song-barely-days-joining-wcb.html |website=TUKO |access-date=10 June 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Kati ya wasanii hawa mmoja atajiunga WCB |url=https://www.mwananchi.co.tz/habari/kitaifa/Kati-ya-wasanii-hawa-mmoja-atajiunga-WCB/1597296-5201332-puqk78z/index.html |website=Mwananchi |access-date=10 June 2020}}</ref> Zuchu started singing at a tender age and later collaborated with her mother Khadija Kopa on a song titled [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t_vfSmbdw34 Mauzauza] from her debut EP titled [https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLiq9qeF916uzV0TnziekW62P2942YT5dY I am Zuchu EP.]<ref>{{cite web |title=ZUCHU – Biography – Age, Family, Early life, Record Label, Music, Net Worth |url=https://daily4mative.com/zuchu-biography-age-family-early-life-music-net-worth/ |website=Daily4mative |access-date=28 June 2020}}</ref> Among her first appearances as a musician to the public date back to 2015 in the first edition of TECNO OWN THE STAGE in [[Lagos]], [[Nigeria]].<ref>{{Citation|title=EP4 Zouchu Performance|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ahdgDqFJUdY|language=en|access-date=2021-03-14}}</ref>
