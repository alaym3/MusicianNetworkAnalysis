'''Slander''' (stylized as '''SLANDER''') is an American DJ duo consisting of Derek Andersen and Scott Land based in [[Los Angeles]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.billboard.com/photos/7431186/djs-real-names-edm|title=DJs' Real Names|work=Billboard|access-date=June 10, 2016}}</ref><ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=http://dancemusicnw.com/5-questions-with-slander-a-lucky-favorite/|title=5 Questions With Slander, A Lucky Favorite|date=March 9, 2015|work=Dance Music NW|access-date=June 10, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://stoneyroads.com/2016/10/interview-slander-talk-australian-tour-and-upcoming-collaborations|title=Interview: Slander Talk Australian Tour And Upcoming Collaborations|work=Stoney Roads|access-date=June 10, 2016}}</ref> They are best known for their unique sound and subgenre "heaven trap".<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://edm.com/articles/2016/4/12/heres-12-wacky-sub-genres-that-will-leave-you-scratching-your-head|title=Here's 12 Wacky Sub Genres That Will Leave You Scratching Your Head|date=April 12, 2016|publisher=EDM.com|access-date=June 10, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160817181807/http://edm.com/articles/2016/4/12/heres-12-wacky-sub-genres-that-will-leave-you-scratching-your-head#|archive-date=August 17, 2016|url-status=dead}}</ref> They attribute most of their success to their close friend Nathaniel Bates, who they say “single handedly gave them the inspiration and confidence to become full time DJ’s”.

== Early life ==
Andersen and Land met at their [[fraternity]], [[Kappa Sigma]], in [[University of California, Irvine]].<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.ocweekly.com/music/slander-two-former-oc-frat-boys-help-take-trapstyle-worldwide-6570452|title=Slander: Two Former OC Frat Boys Help Take TrapStyle Worldwide|last=Loera|first=Alejandra|date=October 5, 2012|work=OC Weekly|access-date=June 10, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160613202551/http://www.ocweekly.com/music/slander-two-former-oc-frat-boys-help-take-trapstyle-worldwide-6570452|archive-date=June 13, 2016|url-status=dead}}</ref> They later went to and graduated from Icon Collective, a music production school together with [[Nghtmre]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.broadwayworld.com/bwwmusic/article/Slanders-New-Remix-Package-of-Duality-EP-Out-Now-20161121|title=Slander's New Remix Package of 'Duality' EP Out Now|author=BWW News Desk|access-date=June 10, 2016}}</ref> Andersen said: "My relationship with Tyler (Nghtmre) came out of nowhere when we were put in the same classroom at Icon Collective together."<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://runthetrap.com/2017/01/25/exploring-sounds-with-slander-interview-diplo-friends-mix/|title=Exploring Sounds with Slander [Interview + Diplo & Friends Mix]|date=January 25, 2017|website=Run The Trap|access-date=June 10, 2016}}</ref> They initially played gigs for their college friends and at local venues. The name Slander was combined from the duo's real names: "S(cott) Land" and "Ander(sen)". They have performed at [[Electric Daisy Carnival|EDC]] (Las Vegas, Orlando and Mexico), [[Nocturnal Wonderland]], [[Electric Zoo]], [[Sun City Music Festival]], and [[Ultra Music Festival]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.insomniac.com/artist/slander|title=Slander|website=Insomniac.com|access-date=June 10, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.ocweekly.com/music/dj-duo-slander-put-an-oc-bred-spin-on-an-international-edm-craze-6424405|title=DJ Duo Slander Put an OC-bred Spin on an International EDM Craze|last=Loera|first=Alejandra|date=October 25, 2012|work=OC Weekly|access-date=June 10, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171020135207/http://www.ocweekly.com/music/dj-duo-slander-put-an-oc-bred-spin-on-an-international-edm-craze-6424405|archive-date=October 20, 2017|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.redbull.com/au-en/the-records-that-shaped-slander|title=Slander: The records that shaped the duo|website=www.redbull.com|access-date=June 10, 2016}}</ref>
