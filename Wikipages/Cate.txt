'''Cate''' is a feminine given name. It is a variant of [[Kate (given name)|Kate]]. The name comes from Latin, French, English, and Welsh origins. The name literally means either 'pure' or 'blessed', used in different context.  The name Catherine is popular in Christian countries, as it was the name of one of the first Christian saints, [[Catherine of Alexandria]]. Its variants are also widely used around the world.
