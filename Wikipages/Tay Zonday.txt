==Early life==
Zonday was born Adam Nyerere Bahner in [[Minneapolis]], [[Minnesota]]. He is of half African-American descent. He is the youngest of three children, and his parents are both teachers.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://soundcloud.com/earbiscuits/ep-67-tay-zonday-ear-biscuits|title=Ep. 67 Tay Zonday - Ear Biscuits|access-date=August 13, 2016|archive-date=September 14, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160914195000/https://soundcloud.com/earbiscuits/ep-67-tay-zonday-ear-biscuits|url-status=live}}</ref> He attended the [[Illinois Mathematics and Science Academy]] but dropped out before graduating.{{Citation needed|date=October 2020}} Zonday graduated from [[The Evergreen State College]] in 2004.<ref>{{cite web |title=Congratulations to The Evergreen State College Class of 2021! |url=https://www.evergreen.edu/news/post/2021/congratulations-evergreen-state-college-class-2021 |website=Evergreen State College |publisher=Evergreen State College |access-date=16 July 2021}}</ref>

Zonday enrolled in the [[University of Minnesota]] American studies [[PhD]] program in 2004, studying the history of theater and social change, with the intention of becoming a professor. After the viral success of "Chocolate Rain", he left university with a [[master's degree]] in 2008 and moved to [[Los Angeles]], [[California]].<ref name=":0">{{Cite video|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kUexle4jktU| archive-url=https://ghostarchive.org/varchive/youtube/20211024/kUexle4jktU| archive-date=2021-10-24|title=I spent a day with VIRAL MEME STARS (Kombucha Girl, Hit or Miss, Chocolate Rain)|date=February 4, 2020|last=Padilla|first=Anthony|type=YouTube video|language=en-US|minutes=6:04}}{{cbignore}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=C.J.|date=June 13, 2017|title='Chocolate Rain' and its many memes bring sweet success to Tay Zonday|url=https://www.startribune.com/c-j-chocolate-rain-and-its-many-memes-bring-sweet-success-to-tay-zonday/428271353/|access-date=October 11, 2020|website=Star Tribune|archive-date=December 31, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171231210526/http://www.startribune.com/c-j-chocolate-rain-and-its-many-memes-bring-sweet-success-to-tay-zonday/428271353/|url-status=live}}</ref> He used a stage name because he wanted a separation between his academic and entertainment career. Bahner chose "Tay Zonday" because he thought it was catchy and nobody had claimed the name yet, according to a Google search.<ref name=":0" />

==Career==
While a PhD student in [[Minneapolis]], Minnesota, Zonday began performing at [[open mic night]]s in 2006. Looking for more feedback, he began uploading his performances online the next year.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Kale|first=Sirin|date=August 24, 2016|title=Tay Zonday Does Not Want You to Understand the Meaning Of 'Chocolate Rain'|url=https://www.vice.com/en/article/d3ggvk/tay-zonday-does-not-want-you-to-understand-the-meaning-of-chocolate-rain|access-date=October 11, 2020|website=www.vice.com|language=en|archive-date=November 11, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201111180500/https://www.vice.com/en/article/d3ggvk/tay-zonday-does-not-want-you-to-understand-the-meaning-of-chocolate-rain|url-status=live}}</ref>

Zonday released "Chocolate Rain", a viral song on race and race politics, on April 22, 2007.<ref name="OpAnt">{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ONpjOyzgnYY|title=Tay Zonday on the Opie & Anthony show|date=July 19, 2007|access-date=July 20, 2007|archive-date=May 17, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140517085907/http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ONpjOyzgnYY|url-status=live}}</ref> He was soon making appearances on national television. He appeared on the ''[[Opie and Anthony|Opie & Anthony Show]]'',<ref>{{YouTube|ONpjOyzgnYY|Tay Zonday on the Opie & Anthony Show}}</ref> [[G4 (U.S. TV channel)|G4TV's]] ''[[Attack of the Show!]]'', [[VH1]]'s ''[[Best Week Ever]]'',<ref>{{YouTube|ckWvAEQEWRw|Chocolate Rain on Best Week Ever}}</ref> ''[[Lily Allen and Friends]]'', ''[[Jimmy Kimmel Live!]]'',<ref>[http://www.citypages.com/2007-08-22/unknown/rain-man Minneapolis City Pages: Rain Man] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111128211642/http://www.citypages.com/2007-08-22/unknown/rain-man/|date=November 28, 2011}}</ref> ''[[Tosh.0]]'' and ''[[Maury (TV series)|Maury]]''<ref>{{YouTube|aDe1TUa-XQE|Tay Zonday On Jimmy Kimmel}}</ref><ref name="People" /> where he performed "Chocolate Rain" on national television a little over three months after he posted his composition on YouTube. He made the front page of Sunday's ''[[Los Angeles Times]]'' on August 12, 2007 with additional features in The ''[[Toronto Star]]'', The ''[[Chicago Tribune]]''<nowiki/>'s ''[[RedEye]]'', The ''[[Star Tribune]]''<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.startribune.com/457/story/1468848.html|title=The latest from the StarTribune|access-date=August 13, 2016|archive-date=January 7, 2009|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090107174121/http://www.startribune.com/457/story/1468848.html|url-status=live}}</ref> and ''[[People (magazine)|People]]''<ref name="People" /> and has appeared on [[CNN]]<ref>{{cite news | url=http://transcripts.cnn.com/TRANSCRIPTS/0708/18/cnr.01.html | work=CNN | title=CNN.com - Transcripts | access-date=November 13, 2007 | archive-date=March 3, 2016 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303185609/http://transcripts.cnn.com/TRANSCRIPTS/0708/18/cnr.01.html | url-status=live }}</ref> for a televised interview. On February 12, 2008, he appeared on the television show ''Lily Allen and Friends'' on BBC Three, and performed a cover of [[Lily Allen]]'s debut "[[Smile (Lily Allen song)|Smile]]". ==Personal life==
Zonday has stated that he has been diagnosed with [[Asperger syndrome]] as a teenager, resumed psychiatric care at age 37, and has been re-diagnosed with [[autism spectrum disorder]].<ref>{{cite web |last1=Zonday |first1=Tay |url=https://twitter.com/tayzonday/status/1205143811527065600?lang=en |website=Twitter |access-date=September 13, 2020 |date=December 12, 2019 |quote="I'm diagnosed by multiple practitioners as having high-functioning autism with aspergers." |title=Archived copy |archive-date=April 17, 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210417193631/https://twitter.com/tayzonday/status/1205143811527065600?lang=en |url-status=live }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/tayzonday/posts/436389271185541|last1=Zonday|first1=Tay| website=Facebook|title=Facebook post|date=October 24, 2021}}</ref> In April 2021, he stated that he suffers from debilitating [[hyperacusis]].<ref>{{cite web |last1=Zonday |first1=Tay |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ybPgmjTRvMo&lc=UgwFCqfNwp8wBeZ5-IV4AaABAg | archive-url=https://ghostarchive.org/varchive/youtube/20211024/ybPgmjTRvMo| archive-date=2021-10-24|website=YouTube|access-date=April 19, 2021 |date=April 17, 2021 |quote="As an autistic adult with debilitating hyperacusis, I appreciate this." |title=The Truth About my Son}}{{cbignore}}</ref>
