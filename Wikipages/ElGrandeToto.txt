'''Taha Fahssi''' ({{lang-ar|طه فحصي}}, born 3 august 1996 in [[Casablanca]]), known by his stage name '''ElGrandeToto''', is a [[Moroccan Hip-Hop|Moroccan rapper]], performing in [[Arabic]], [[French language|French]] and [[English language|English]] (usually mixing between them). He is the most listened Moroccan rap artist on [[Spotify]], with more than 50 million streams for his album Caméléon in 92 countries.<ref>[https://www.lemonde.fr/afrique/article/2021/03/15/maroc-la-nouvelle-scene-rap-a-la-conquete-du-monde_6073205_3212.html Maroc : la nouvelle scène rap à la conquête du monde - Le Monde (2021) - in ''French'']</ref>

In 2020, Fahssi was the eighth most streamed Arab artist in [[MENA]] on [[Deezer]], and his song “Hors Série” was the seventh most streamed song on the platform.<ref>[https://www.forbesmiddleeast.com/lists/arab-music-stars-2021/elgrandetoto/ Arab Music Starts 2021 - ElGrandeToto - Forbes Lists]</ref> In the same year, he won the title of "Best Hip-Hop/Rap Artist" in the [[2020 African Entertainment Awards USA]].<ref>[https://aeausa.net/awards/ African Entertainment Awards USA - 2020 Winners]</ref>

In march 2021, Toto's second studio album "Caméléon" was ranked among the top 10 global album debuts worldwide by [[Spotify]].<ref>[https://www.africanews.com/2021/03/17/moroccan-rap-blossoms-thanks-to-social-media/ Moroccan rap blossoms thanks to social media - Africanews (2021)]</ref>
