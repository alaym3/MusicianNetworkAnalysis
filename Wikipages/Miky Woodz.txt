'''Miguel Ángel Rodríguez Rivera''', known as '''Miky Woodz''', is a Puerto Rican [[Latin trap]] rapper born in [[Carolina, Puerto Rico]].

To date, Woodz has released three studio albums, ''Before Famous'' in 2017, ''El OG'' in 2018, and most recently, in 2020, ''Los 90 Piketes'', which are all commercial successes.

==Personal life==
According to Woodz, he sacrificed a job in a warehouse that paid him minimal wage to continue with his passion for music. Woodz also has a young son named "Meek".<ref name="auto">{{cite news|url=https://www.elvocero.com/escenario/miky-woodz-las-canta-como-las-ve/article_daefc10e-8492-11e8-ba70-ff950527b79e.html|title=Miky Woodz las canta como las ve|first=Yomaris|last=Rodríguez|newspaper=El Vocero de Puerto Rico}}</ref>

==Musical career==
His debut album was ''Before Famous''<ref name="primerahora.com">{{cite web|url=https://m.primerahora.com/entretenimiento/musica/nota/mikywoodzledaalagenteloquequiere-1291983/|title=Miky Woodz le da a la gente lo que quiere|website=Primera Hora}}</ref> released in 2017, which reached number four in ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' and number 20 in Latin Album Sales.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://elnuevodiario.com.do/miky-woodz-posicion-4-billboards-before-famous/|title=Miky Woodz en posición 4 de Billboards con "Before Famous"|website=El Nuevo Diario}}</ref> He then released his second studio album through Gol2 Records, SoldOut Records, and GLAD Empire Records<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/8459661/miky-woodz-el-og-no-6-latin-albums-chart|title=Miky Woodz Debuts in Top 10 on Latin Albums Chart With 'El OG'|website=Billboard}}</ref> in 2018, entitled ''El OG''.<ref name="allmusic.com">{{cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/miky-woodz-mn0003551224|title=Miky Woodz - Biography, Albums, Streaming Links|website=AllMusic}}</ref> Highlighted by the song "Estamos Clear" with [[Bad Bunny]], it debuted at number six on ''Billboard''{{'}}s [[Top Latin Albums]] chart<ref name="allmusic.com"/> and had collaborations with [[Noriel (rapper)|Noriel]], [[Farruko]], [[De La Ghetto]], [[Pusho]], Juhn, Ñejo, [[Darkiel]], and [[Bad Bunny]].<ref name="primerahora.com"/> The song "Estamos Clear" was censored in the Dominican Republic.<ref name="primerahora.com"/>

Miky Woodz gained commercial ground in the mid-2010s with featured roles on tracks by artists such as [[Farruko]], Benny Benni, and Juhn.<ref name="allmusic.com"/> He signed to the Florida-based label Gol2 Latin Music, where he released songs such as "Alcoba [Remix]", "En Lo Oscuro Sin Perse", and "Tarde o Temprano", all three of which amassed streams and millions of views on [[YouTube]].<ref name="allmusic.com"/>
