'''Ghoul''' is an American [[thrash metal]] band from [[Oakland, California]]. Though the members' actual identities are meant to be concealed, it is known that some members or past members of Ghoul also play or have played in other bands such as [[Impaled (band)|Impaled]], [[Dystopia (band)|Dystopia]], [[Wolves in the Throne Room]], [[Exhumed (band)|Exhumed]], [[Phobia (band)|Phobia]], [[Morbid Angel]], Asunder and Morbosidad. Ghoul released their debut album in 2002 and since then have released five studio albums.

== History ==
The masked members go by the stage names Cremator, Fermentor, Digestor, and Dissector.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.roadrunnerrecords.com/BLABBERMOUTH.net/showreview.aspx?reviewID%3D761 |title=Archived copy |accessdate=February 5, 2009 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090917084524/http://www.roadrunnerrecords.com/BLABBERMOUTH.net/showreview.aspx?reviewID=761 |archivedate=September 17, 2009 }}</ref> The band's lyrics describe a large cast of characters, including themselves. Much of the known information concerning the band comes from the "Curio Shop Owner" one of the many characters mentioned in their songs. They claim to be mutants that come from Creepsylvania.<ref>{{cite web|last=Butcher |first=The |url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/36360/scare-riff-eyeing-vol-2-ghoul |title=SCARE-RIFF-EYEING Vol. 2: GHOUL |publisher=Dread Central |date=March 11, 2010 |accessdate=August 11, 2016}}</ref> Ghoul was signed after self-releasing a demo. Their musical style is best described as a blend of thrash and [[grindcore]] with [[Death growl|death metal vocals]] and lyrics. In 2008, Ghoul released a split 7-inch with Brody's Militia.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thrashyourface.com/brodysmilitia/news.html |title=Archived copy |accessdate=February 5, 2009 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090105161504/http://thrashyourface.com/brodysmilitia/news.html |archivedate=January 5, 2009 }}</ref> In November 2011, the band released their fourth album, ''[[Transmission Zero]]'', and embarked on a North American tour opening for shock rockers [[Gwar]].<ref>{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.brooklynvegan.com/archives/2011/10/ghoul_releasing.html |title=Ghoul releasing 'Transmission Zero' (song premiere), now on tour with Gwar (dates) |website=Brooklynvegan.com |date= |accessdate=August 11, 2016}}</ref> In early 2012, Ghoul once again toured the United States, opening for Gwar.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.metalsucks.net/2012/01/24/dates-for-sweet-gwarmunicipal-wasteghoul-tour-announced/ |title=Dates For Sweet Gwar/Municipal Waste/Ghoul Tour Announced |website=MetalSucks.net |date=January 24, 2012 |accessdate=August 11, 2016}}</ref> In mid-2012, the band toured the U.S. on a headlining run, playing festivals such as [[Maryland Deathfest]], Chaos in Tejas, and the third annual GWAR B-Q.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://riotfest.org/heres-the-final-lineup-of-the-2012-gwar-b-q/ |title=Here's the final lineup of the 2012 Gwar-B-Q |publisher=Riot Fest |date= July 3, 2012|accessdate=August 11, 2016}}</ref> The band teamed up with [[Cannabis Corpse]] for a brief tour of Europe in January/February 2013.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.creepsylvania.com/cannabis-corpseghoul-euro-tour-2013/ |title=Ghoul – Official Website » Cannabis Corpse/Ghoul Euro tour 2013! |website=Creepsylvania.com |date=December 21, 2012 |accessdate=August 11, 2016}}</ref>
