'''Dan Romer''' is an American music producer, songwriter and [[Film score|film composer]] based in Los Angeles.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://rupertneve.com/archive/dan-romer-keeps-it-simple-with-the-portico-5012/ |title=Dan Romer Keeps it Simple with the Portico 5012 |publisher=Rupertneve.com |date=October 22, 2009 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100114165637/http://rupertneve.com/archive/dan-romer-keeps-it-simple-with-the-portico-5012/ |archivedate=January 14, 2010 }}</ref>

==Career==
As a film composer, Romer's scores include four-time Oscar-nominated ''[[Beasts of the Southern Wild]]'', ''[[Beasts of No Nation (film)|Beasts of No Nation]]'', ''[[Chasing Coral]]'', ''[[Gleason (2016 film)|Gleason]]'', ''[[The Good Doctor (TV series)|Good Doctor]]'' and the Emmy-winning ''[[Jim: The James Foley Story]]''.<ref name=aaa>Wacey, Rob. [http://www.allmusic.com/artist/dan-romer-mn0001775784/biography "Dan Romer Biography"], AllMusic. Retrieved May 30, 2017.</ref> Romer scored the Ubisoft video game ''[[Far Cry 5]]'' released on March 27, 2018.

He has produced music for numerous artists including [[A Great Big World]] and [[Christina Aguilera]], whose single "[[Say Something (A Great Big World song)|Say Something]]," topped charts around the world, hit 6x Platinum, sold over seven million copies, and won a 2015 Grammy. Dan co-produced "[[Treat You Better (Shawn Mendes song)|Treat You Better]]" by [[Shawn Mendes]] which reached No. 1 on the [[iTunes]] chart and No. 3 on the US Top 40 pop radio charts in September 2016.<ref name=aaa /> He was set to compose the [[James Bond]] film ''[[No Time to Die]]'', but was reportedly replaced by [[Hans Zimmer]] due to "creative differences".<ref>[https://variety.com/2020/film/news/hans-zimmer-james-bond-composer-no-time-to-die-1203458908/ ‘No Time to Die’: Hans Zimmer Takes Over as Composer on Bond Movie (EXCLUSIVE)]</ref> Romer composed the score to the [[Pixar]] animated feature film, ''[[Luca (2021 film)|Luca]]''<ref>{{Cite web|title=We announce today Luca's music composer: the amazing Dan Romer|url=https://twitter.com/sketchcrawl/status/1377789977019310081|access-date=2021-04-02|website=Twitter|language=en}}</ref> and worked with [[Justin Paul]] on the incidental underscore for [[Stephen Chbosky]]'s [[Dear Evan Hansen (film)|2021 film adaptation]] of Broadway musical ''[[Dear Evan Hansen]]''.<ref>https://filmmusicreporter.com/2021/08/24/dan-romer-justin-paul-scoring-stephen-chboskys-dear-evan-hansen/</ref>
