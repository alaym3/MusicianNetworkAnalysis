'''Eduard Marian Ilie''' ({{IPA-ro|ˈedu.ard mariˈan iˈli.e}}; born 29 June 1986), better known by his stage name '''Edward Maya''', is a Romanian DJ, musician, record producer, performer, singer and songwriter, most famous for his 2009 smash hit single "[[Stereo Love]]".

==Career==
In and around 2006, Maya worked with many other Romanian artists such as [[Akcent]] and [[Vika Jigulina]]. He also worked on the song [[Tornerò (Mihai Trăistariu song)|Tornerò]], which was performed by [[Mihai Trăistariu]] in the [[Eurovision Song Contest 2006]]. He added in an interview on Beat-Town.com that this song "was the beginning of my career"<ref>{{cite web |title=Edward Maya speaks to Beat-Town|url=http://beat-town.com/2010/04/09/edward-maya-beat-town/ |publisher=Beat-Town |language=en, el |access-date=9 April 2010}}</ref>

In the summer of 2009, Maya launched his first song as an artist, "[[Stereo Love]]", reaching No. 2 in the Romanian singles charts. Later that year, "Stereo Love" became an international hit in clubs.<ref>[http://www.antenna.gr/webtv/uservideo?cid=_smyqm_n2gn3g%3D ANT1 WEB TV / VIDEO ΧΡΗΣΤΩΝ / Stereo Love – Edward Maya & Vika Jigulina / ΜΟΥΣΙΚΗ<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref> While this success was followed by concerts worldwide, the song entered the top-5 singles charts in Austria, Denmark, Finland, France, Germany, On 13 May 2010, Maya released his second single "[[This Is My Life (Edward Maya song)|This Is My Life]]". To help maintain control of his career and provide opportunities for other artists, Maya has formed his own record label, Mayavin Records.<ref>{{cite web|url={{Allmusic|class=artist|id=edward-maya-p2111631/biography|pure_url=yes}}|title=Edward Maya > Biography|last=Deming|first=Mark|publisher=[[Allmusic]]. [[Rovi Corporation]]}}</ref>

Maya released the song and video for his third single "[[Desert Rain (Edward Maya song)|Desert Rain]]" on Christmas night worldwide.<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.mizozo.com/entertainment/12/2010/24/exclusive-interview-with-edward-maya-composer-of-t....html | title=Exclusive Interview with Edward Maya | work=Mizzo | access-date=23 July 2011 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110714103549/http://www.mizozo.com/entertainment/12/2010/24/exclusive-interview-with-edward-maya-composer-of-t....html | archive-date=14 July 2011 | url-status=dead }}</ref> In an interview Edward Maya talked about his Desert Rain tour he mentions he'll start off with India and later he will be touring Europe, US, South Africa and Pakistan.<ref>{{cite web | url=http://edwardmaya.com/home/361-my-first-time-in-pakistan | title=Edward Maya's concert in Pakistan | access-date=13 February 2012 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120203225756/http://edwardmaya.com/home/361-my-first-time-in-pakistan | archive-date=3 February 2012 | url-status=dead }}</ref> His fourth single 'Mono in Love' was released on 29 November 2012 also featuring [[Vika Jigulina]].

He graduated from [[George Enescu]] Music High School in [[Bucharest]] and is currently{{when|date=June 2020}} a final year student at the [[Bucharest Conservatory]].

Maya was chosen to dub Grug voice in the animated movie ''Onward''.<ref> {{Cite web|title=20 de ani de magie de la primul film Disney dublat în limba română - CineMagia.ro|url=https://m.cinemagia.ro/stiri/20-de-ani-de-magie-de-la-primul-film-disney-dublat-n-limba-romn-45661/|access-date=2021-06-08|website=m.cinemagia.ro}}</ref>
