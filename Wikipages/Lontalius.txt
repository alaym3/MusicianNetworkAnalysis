'''''Lontalius''''' is a [[monotypic]] [[butterfly]] genus in the family [[Lycaenidae]]. Its single species, '''''Lontalius eltus''''', is found in [[Borneo]] and the [[Philippines]]. Both the genus and species were [[Species description|first described]] by [[John Nevill Eliot]] in 1986.<ref>{{cite web |last=Savela |first=Markku |date=November 3, 2009 |url=https://www.nic.funet.fi/pub/sci/bio/life/insecta/lepidoptera/ditrysia/papilionoidea/lycaenidae/miletinae/lontalius/ |title=''Lontalius'' Eliot, 1986 |website=Lepidoptera and Some Other Life Forms |accessdate=January 13, 2020}}</ref>
