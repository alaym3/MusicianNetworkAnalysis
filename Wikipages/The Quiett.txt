'''Shin Dong-gab''' ([[Hangul]]: 신동갑; born January 29, 1985), better known by his stage name '''The Quiett''' ([[Hangul]]: 더콰이엇), is a [[South Korea]]n [[rapping|rapper]], [[composer]], [[lyricist]] and [[record producer]]. He is widely known within the South Korean [[Hip hop music|hip hop]] community for founding several major hip hop record labels, including co-founding record labels [[Soul Company]] in 2004, [[Illionaire Records]] in 2011,<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://www.complex.com/music/2015/06/the-rise-of-illionaire-records-korean-rap|title=The Rise of Illionaire Records, the Independent Label Taking Korean Hip-Hop to the Next Level|date=2015-06-30|website=Complex|access-date=2016-04-23}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.mnet.com/artist/35419|title=더 콰이엇|website=Mnet|language=ko|trans-title=The Quiett|access-date=2016-06-06}}</ref> and [[Ambition Musik]] in 2016, the latter two with fellow rapper [[Dok2]].<ref>{{Cite web |date=2016-10-11 |title=The Quiett, 'Ambition Musik was an idea sprung up after Kim Hyo-Eun was Eliminated' |url=https://hiphopkr.com/interviews/the-quiett-ambition-musik-was-an-idea-sprung-up-after-kim-hyo-eun-was-eliminated-l-commentary/}}</ref> He founded label Daytona Entertainment in 2020 and has remained since after Illionaire Records closed its doors.

==Career==
===2004–2010: Soul Company and indie popularity===
In 2004, The Quiett co-founded indie Korean hip hop record label and talent agency, [[Soul Company]], alongside rappers [[Kebee]], [[Jerry.K]], and several other artists.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://news.khan.co.kr/kh_news/khan_art_view.html?artid=200807301451395&code=900315|title=[한국의 인디레이블]④ 소울컴퍼니(Soul Company)|date=2008-07-30|website=The Kyunghyang Shinmun|language=ko|access-date=2016-04-24}}</ref> He released his first album, ''Music,'' the following year.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://magazine.hiphopplaya.com/magazine/article/view.html?num=1859&category=&category2=&mode=&sort=&page=|title=소울컴퍼니의 메인 프로듀서, 'The Quiett'|date=2005-09-01|website=Hiphop Playa|language=ko|trans-title=The main producer of Soul Company, 'The Quiett'|access-date=2016-04-24}}</ref> In 2006, he released a solo album, ''Q Train,'' and a collaboration with rapper [[Paloalto (rapper)|Paloalto]] called ''Supremacy.''<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://magazine.hiphopplaya.com/magazine/article/view.html?num=1961&category=&category2=&mode=&sort=&page=|title='P&Q' Paloalto & The Quiett 인터뷰, 1부 – Supremacy|date=2006-07-28|website=Hiphop Playa|language=ko|trans-title='P&Q' Paloalto & The Quiett Interview, Part 1 – Supremacy|access-date=2016-04-24}}</ref> His 2010 album, ''The Real Me,'' went gold, selling 7,000 copies and solidifying his reputation as one of the most popular indie rappers in Korea.<ref name=":0" />

The Quiett left Soul Company in 2010 to start a new label.<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.frontiertimes.co.kr/news/htmls/2011/01/2011011163632.html|title=더콰이엇, 2011년 힙합계를 흔든다|date=2011-01-11|website=Frontier Times|language=ko|trans-title=The Quiett shakes up the hip hop scene in 2011|access-date=2016-04-24}}</ref>

===2011–present: Illionaire Records, Television Appearances and Daytona Entertainment===
[[File:The Quiett 2017.png|thumb|The Quiett in 2017]]
The Quiett and rapper [[Dok2]] founded [[Illionaire Records]] on January 1, 2011. Rapper [[Beenzino]] joined the label later that year.<ref name=":0" /> The record label lasted for almost a decade in which they contributed to the rise of Korean Hip-Hop in the country.

The Quiett has made several appearances in popular Korean hip-hop competitions. He appeared as a judge on the third season of the TV rap competition ''[[Show Me the Money (South Korean TV series)|Show Me the Money]]'' with Dok2 in 2014, taking first place with contestant [[Bobby (rapper)|Bobby]] of the group [[IKON (South Korean band)|IKON]]. He reappeared in the show's fifth season with Dok2<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.kpopstarz.com/articles/269430/20160222/show-me-the-money-dok2-the-quiett.htm|title=Rappers Dok And The Quiett Confirmed As 'Show Me the Money 5' Producer-Judges|date=2016-02-22|website=KpopStarz|access-date=2016-04-24}}</ref> and in the seventh season with [[Changmo]]. He also made two appearances as a judge in ''[[High School Rapper (TV series)|High School Rapper]]'' in season three with producer [[Code Kunst]] and in season four with [[Yumdda]] after establishing his new label Daytona Entertainment.

On July 6, 2020, Illionaire Records announced through their official Instagram account that their record label will close after 10 years of operation.<ref>{{Cite web|title=1LLIONAIRE RECORDS OFFICIAL (@1llionaire_records) • Instagram photos and videos|url=https://www.instagram.com/1llionaire_records/|access-date=2020-07-06|website=instagram.com|language=en}}</ref> and Twitter<ref>{{Cite web|title=https://twitter.com/1llionaire|url=https://twitter.com/1llionaire|access-date=2020-07-06|website=Twitter|language=en}}</ref> account. The Quiett was the sole artist signed at the time the company closed, as former artists Dok2 and [[Beenzino]] left prior to that year. On November 11, 2020, he released his final two singles "Bentley 2" which featured [[Yumdda]], and Abu Dhabi which featured Skinny Brown, Leellamarz, and [[Sik-K]] as a tribute to the record company.

On November 25, 2020, The Quiett and fellow rapper [[Yumdda]] established their new record label Daytona Entertainment.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.instagram.com/daytona.ent/|title = Login • Instagram}}</ref> He remains CEO of Ambition Musik, a former sub-label under Illionaire Records, although not an artist under the label.
