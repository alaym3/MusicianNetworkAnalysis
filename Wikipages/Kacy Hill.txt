'''Kacy Anne Hill''' (born May 1, 1994)<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/music/artists/f33e8fd2-8536-42e8-9169-fae50f850dd9 | title=Kacy Hill - New Songs, Playlists & Latest News | work=[[BBC Music]] | date= | accessdate=July 30, 2017}}</ref> is an American singer-songwriter, producer, and former model.

She was born and raised in [[Phoenix, Arizona]] and had previously worked as a model for [[American Apparel]]. She released "Experience", her first non-album single independently in 2014.<ref>{{cite news|last1=R|first1=Kaya|date=31 July 2015|title=Kacy Hill is Feeling "Bloo" With New EP|publisher=Next2Shine|url=http://next2shine.com/kacy-hill-is-feeling-bloo-with-new-ep|accessdate=24 August 2015}}</ref> She additionally worked as a backup dancer on [[The Yeezus Tour]] for American rapper [[Kanye West]], where he discovered her music and signed her to his GOOD Music label "immediately".<ref name=BillboardGood>{{cite news|last1=Leight|first1=Elias|title=G.O.O.D. Music Signs Former Kanye West Backup Dancer Kacy Hill|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/6406666/good-music-signs-kacy-hill|accessdate=24 August 2015|publisher=Billboard|date=17 December 2014}}</ref>

Hill released "Foreign Fields", in 2015,<ref>{{cite web|title=Single Review: Kacy Hill's Foreign Fields|url=https://www.michigandaily.com/section/music/single-review-kacy-hills-foreign-fields|website=The Michigan Daily|accessdate=14 September 2015}}</ref> which was later included on the ''[[Bloo (EP)|Bloo]]'' EP, via GOOD Music. Hill was also #6 on [[Dazed|Dazed and Confused]]'s Readers' 100 list and #29 on the [[Dazed|Dazed 100]] list in 2014.<ref name="Dazed100">{{cite web|title=The Dazed 100|url=http://www.dazeddigital.com/projects/article/22568/1/29-kacy-hill|website=[[Dazed]]|accessdate=25 August 2015}}</ref> She released her debut album ''[[Like a Woman]]'' in 2017.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|title=Listen To Kacy Hill’s Debut Album Like A Woman|url=https://www.thefader.com/2017/07/01/kacy-hill-like-a-woman-album|access-date=2021-09-24|website=The FADER|language=en}}</ref>

Hill announced that she had left GOOD Music in 2019,<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|date=2019-05-22|title=Kacy Hill Has Left Kanye West's G.O.O.D Music Label|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/kacy-hill-has-left-kanye-wests-good-music-label-news.80941.html|access-date=2021-09-24|website=HotNewHipHop|language=en}}</ref> and independently released her second and third studio albums, ''[[Is It Selfish If We Talk About Me Again]]'' in 2020,<ref name="Howard">{{Cite web|last=Howard|first=Lindsay|title=Listen to Kacy Hill's incredible new album 'Is It Selfish If We Talk About Me Again'|url=https://variancemagazine.com/sounds/11368-kacy-hill-album-is-it-selfish-if-we-talk-about-me-again|access-date=2021-09-24|website=Variance Magazine|language=en-gb}}</ref> and ''[[Simple, Sweet, and Smiling]]'' in 2021.<ref name=":2" />

==Early life==
Hill was raised in [[Phoenix, Arizona]] and attended [[Arizona School for the Arts]].<ref name=DJBoothUncover>{{cite news|title=Uncovering The Hidden Past of Kacy Hill, Kanye's New G.O.O.D. Protege|url=http://www.djbooth.net/index/news/entry/kacy-hill-kanye-good-music|accessdate=24 August 2015|publisher=DJ Booth}}</ref> Hill has a younger sister, Emma. At an early age, she was influenced by music, playing the oboe and saxophone and singing in choir.<ref name=DJBoothUncover/>

==Career==
Hill began modelling at the age of 16 after being discovered by a wedding photographer.<ref name=NYMagApril>{{cite news|last1=Kaplan|first1=Ilana|title=Kacy Hill's Kanye approved pop|url=http://nymag.com/thecut/2015/04/kacy-hills-kanye-approved-pop.html|accessdate=24 August 2015|publisher=New York Magazine|date=April 2015}}</ref> She moved to [[Los Angeles]] after high school to pursue a modelling career, which led to her becoming the face for [[American Apparel]].<ref name=DJBoothUncover/> She got into music when she met a director named Stephen Garnett, who later directed the video for her first non-album single release, ''Experience''.<ref name="DJBoothUncover"/><ref>{{cite news|last1=Walker|first1=Lily|title=Homecoming Queen|url=http://www.wonderlandmagazine.com/2015/05/homecoming-queen/|accessdate=24 August 2015|publisher=Wonderland Magazine|date=22 May 2015}}</ref> Regarding her modelling career, Hill stated that she never felt passionate about it. In an interview with [[New York (magazine)|NY Mag]], she was quoted as saying, "It wasn't something I really wanted to do; it was something that was there, so I went after it."<ref name=NYMagApril/>

Hill was a backup dancer for Kanye West during his 2013–2014 [[The Yeezus Tour]].<ref name=EvenStand>{{cite news|last1=Foster|first1=Alistair|title=Kanye West's been great, says new star Kacy Hill - he told me to be myself|url=https://www.standard.co.uk/showbiz/celebrity-news/kanye-west-s-been-great-says-new-star-kacy-hill-he-told-me-to-be-myself-a2919586.html|accessdate=24 August 2015|publisher=Evening Standard|date=24 August 2015}}</ref> She had released ''Experience'', a non-album single at the beginning of the tour. The song was produced by Jaylien Wesley, also known as JAYLIEN. The video for the song was shot in ten minutes between her getting off a plane and going to a tour rehearsal. During the tour, West was provided with a copy of the song<ref>{{cite news|title=Kanye West Signs Kacy Hill to G.O.O.D. Music After Pusha T Suggests Lil Wayne Join the Label|url=http://www.musictimes.com/articles/20670/20141217/kanye-west-signs-kacy-hill-good-music-pusha-t-lil-wayne.htm|accessdate=24 August 2015|publisher=Music Times|date=17 December 2014}}</ref> and he immediately signed Hill to his GOOD Music label after his show in [[Atlantic City, New Jersey|Atlantic City]].<ref name=NYMagApril/>

Her song "Experience" received international media attention, with [[Digital Spy]] naming it a "track you need to hear"<ref name=DigitalSply>{{cite news|last1=Corner|first1=Lewis|last2=Davidson|first2=Amy|title=Playlist: 10 tracks you need to hear-Olly Murs, Cheryl, Clean Bandit|url=http://www.digitalspy.com/music/feature/a601802/playlist-10-tracks-you-need-to-hear-olly-murs-cheryl-clean-bandit.html#~pmlsA0GyDToIs7|accessdate=24 August 2015|publisher=[[Digital Spy]]|date=7 October 2014}}</ref> as well as The BoomBox naming Hill an artist to watch in 2015.<ref name=BoomBox>{{cite news|last1=Josephs|first1=Brian|title=10 Singers You Need to Pay Attention to in 2015|url=http://theboombox.com/10-singers-you-need-to-pay-attention-to-in-2015/|accessdate=24 August 2015|publisher=The BoomBox|date=16 January 2015}}</ref> Hill was also #6 on [[Dazed|''Dazed and Confused'']]'s Readers' 100 list and #29 on the [[Dazed|Dazed 100]] list in 2014.<ref name=Dazed100/> Since the release of "Experience", Hill has written songs that will become part of her debut EP with GOOD Music. Entitled ''Bloo'', the songs are said to be a collection of what Hill wants to accomplish musically.<ref name=Fader>{{cite news|last1=Golden|first1=Zara|title=Kacy Hill Announces Bloo EP, Download "Experience"|url=http://www.thefader.com/2015/07/28/kacy-hill-experience-download-bloo-ep|accessdate=24 August 2015|publisher=The Fader|date=28 July 2015}}</ref> The ''[[Express Tribune]]'' named her one of 10 new music artists you are bound to have on repeat.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Abdul|first1=Haseeb Sultan|title=10 new music artists you are bound to have on repeat|url=http://blogs.tribune.com.pk/story/26003/10-new-music-artists-you-are-bound-to-have-on-repeat/|accessdate=24 August 2015|publisher=Express Tribune|date=7 March 2015}}</ref> Additional accolades have come from [[GQ]] who stated "Hill's barrelling towards the big time" and NY Magazine who stated she was "ethereal yet catchy" and "one to watch."<ref>{{cite news|last1=Cheslaw|first1=Louis|title=How to get signed by Kanye with only one song|url=http://www.gq-magazine.co.uk/entertainment/articles/2014-12/18/how-to-get-signed-by-kanye-with-only-one-song-kacy-hill|accessdate=21 September 2015|publisher=GQ Magazine|date=18 December 2014|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150912041831/http://www.gq-magazine.co.uk/entertainment/articles/2014-12/18/how-to-get-signed-by-kanye-with-only-one-song-kacy-hill|archivedate=12 September 2015}}</ref> Her first major feature was on the song "90210" on labelmate [[Travis Scott (musician)|Travis Scott's]] [[Rodeo (Travis Scott album)|''Rodeo'']].

Hill finished recording her debut album in December 2016. In early May 2017, she released two singles, entitled "Hard to Love", produced by [[Stuart Price]] and [[Oskar Sikow]], and "Like a Woman", produced by [[DJ Mustard]], [[Terrace Martin]] and [[DJ Dodger Stadium]] (DJDS). Both singles were announced as the lead cuts from Hill's debut studio album, which is titled ''[[Like a Woman]]'', released on 30 June 2017.<ref name=":0" />

In August 2018, Hill independently released the single entitled, "Dinner". In May 2019, Hill announced that she had left GOOD Music on Twitter.<ref name=":1" />

In July 2020, Hill released her second album ''[[Is It Selfish If We Talk About Me Again]]'' independently,<ref name="Howard"/> featuring co-production from Hill herself, [[Francis and the Lights]], and [[BJ Burton]], as well as [[Cashmere Cat]], and Hill's now-partner, [[Jim-E Stack]], who inspired a lot of the album's tracks. According to Hill, the album was "turned down by every label but it means the world to me."<ref>{{Citation|title=Kacy Hill – Is It Selfish If We Talk About Me Again (2020, Vinyl)|url=https://www.discogs.com/Kacy-Hill-Is-It-Selfish-If-We-Talk-About-Me-Again/release/16163226|language=en|access-date=2021-07-31}}</ref>  The album was met with favourable reviews from critics with music publication [[Pitchfork (website)|Pitchfork]] describing it as "Bright and open, built with sounds that move and breathe with the artist".<ref>{{Cite news|title=Kacy Hill: Is It Selfish If We Talk About Me Again|language=en|url=https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/kacy-hill-is-it-selfish-if-we-talk-about-me-again/|access-date=2021-10-19}}</ref> The album has surpassed 10 million streams as of September 2021.<ref>{{Citation|title=Is It Selfish If We Talk About Me Again|date=2020-07-10|url=https://open.spotify.com/album/6MeHJ39jMIA4niDn0jhJBx|language=en|access-date=2021-10-19}}</ref> She released a remix EP for the album in December 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Pandya|first=Hershal|title=Just Circling Back with Kacy Hill: Interview|url=https://djbooth.net/features/2021-03-02-kacy-hill-interview-audiomack|access-date=2021-09-24|website=DJBooth|language=en}}</ref> Hill also contributed to Japanese-Australian singer-songwriter [[Joji (musician)|Joji]]'s ''[[Nectar (Joji album)|Nectar]]'' album in September 2020, co-writing "Like You Do".

Hill began teasing her third album via social media, working on the record alongside Jim-E Stack and [[John Carroll Kirby]]. She finished production on the album in July 2021.<ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|title=Kacy Hill on Twitter|url=https://twitter.com/kacyhill/status/1421350351471403012|url-status=live|access-date=2021-08-02|website=Twitter|language=en}}</ref> She released the lead single, "Seasons Bloom", on 27 August 2021, featuring production from [[Ariel Rechtshaid]], Jim-E Stack, and Hill.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Wang|first=Steffanee|title=Hear Kacy Hill's New Song "Seasons Bloom" Before Summer Ends|url=https://www.nylon.com/entertainment/kacy-hill-seasons-bloom-new-song|access-date=2021-09-20|website=Nylon|language=en}}</ref> Another single, titled "Easy Going" with production from Ethan Gruska, Jim-E Stack, and Hill was released on the 24th September. The album, titled ''[[Simple, Sweet, and Smiling]]'', was announced for release on 15 October 2021 the same day.<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|title=Sacks & Co. /// Kacy Hill|url=https://sacksco.com/pr/kacy_hill.html|access-date=2021-09-24|website=sacksco.com}}</ref> Hill will also embark on her first headlining tour, the Simple, Sweet and Selfish Tour, in promotion of the album, as well as her previous album.
