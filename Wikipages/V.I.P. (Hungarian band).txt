'''V.I.P.''' were a [[Hungarian pop]] [[boy band]] founded in 1997 and dissolved in 2001, consisted of 4 members including 2 brothers.

== Members ==
* Józsa Alex
* Imre Rakonczai (b. 1972)
* Viktor Rakonczai (b. 1976)
* Gergő Rácz (b. 1976)

==History==
They represented Hungary in the [[Eurovision Song Contest 1997]] with the song "Miért kell, hogy elmenj?" ("Why Do You Have To Go?"). They tied for twelfth place with Greece with 39 points.
One of them, Viktor Rakonczai composed the song [[Szívverés]] for [[Csézy]], who represented Hungary in the [[Eurovision Song Contest 2008]]. Another member, Gergő Rácz, won [[A Dal]] in 2020 together with Reni Orsovai.
