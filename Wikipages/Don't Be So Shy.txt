"'''Don't Be So Shy'''" is a song by French singer [[Imany]]. The song was written by Imany and music by Nadia Mladjao (Imany) and Stéfane Goldman. A remixed version by Filatov & Karas became a pan-European and international hit for Imany and her biggest hit.

It was used in the soundtrack of the 2014 film ''[[French Women (film)|Sous les jupes des filles]]'' (also known by the English title ''[[French Women (film)|French Women]]'') where two versions are used, one by Sherika Sherard and the other by Imany dubbed "Don't Be so Shy (Work in Progress)".

==Filatov & Karas remix==
{{Hatnote|1="[https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Filatov_%26_Karas&redirect=no Filatov & Karas]" redirects here. On this topic you need to [https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Filatov_%26_Karas&action=edit create a separate article].}}
In August 2015, the song was remixed by the Russian DJ duo {{ill|Filatov & Karas|ru}}. This version became a hit across Europe, topping the charts in Slovenia, Russia, Poland, France, Germany and Austria and charting highly on other European singles charts.

This version later appeared on Imany's album ''The Wrong Kind of War'', which was released on 26 August 2016.

==''Don't Be So Shy Remixes''==
Imany also released a separate EP of remixes of the song.
