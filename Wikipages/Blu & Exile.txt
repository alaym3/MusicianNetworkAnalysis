'''Blu & Exile''' are an American [[hip hop music|hip hop]] duo, formed in 2007, composed of rapper [[Blu (rapper)|Blu]] and hip hop producer [[Exile (producer)|Exile]].<ref name=allmusic>{{cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/blu-exile-mn0001597819/biography|title=Blu & Exile - Biography|work=[[AllMusic]]|first=Cyril|last=Cordor|accessdate=October 12, 2014}}</ref>

==History==
Blu and Exile first met in the mid-2000s while Blu was serving as a [[hype man]] for various hip hop groups including [[Slum Village]], [[Platinum Pied Pipers]], and Exile's own group, [[Emanon]].<ref name=allmusic/> He was personally introduced to Exile through Emanon's other member, [[Aloe Blacc]].<ref name=allmusic/> In 2007, Blu & Exile released their debut album, ''[[Below the Heavens]]'', which was described by ''[[XXL (magazine)|XXL]]'' as "one of 2007's most celebrated hip-hop releases".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2012/07/the-creation-of-blu-exiles-below-the-heavens-five-year-anniversary/|title=The Making of Blu & Exile’s Below the Heavens|work=[[XXL (magazine)|XXL]]|first=Jaeki|last=Cho|date=July 18, 2012|accessdate=October 12, 2014}}</ref> In 2012, the duo released their second album, ''[[Give Me My Flowers While I Can Still Smell Them]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/17017-give-me-my-flowers-while-i-can-still-smell-them/|title=Blu & Exile: Give Me My Flowers While I Can Still Smell Them|work=[[Pitchfork (website)|Pitchfork]]|first=Jonah|last=Bromwich|date=August 30, 2012|accessdate=October 12, 2014}}</ref> In 2017, the duo released ''In the Beginning: Before the Heavens'', deleted tracks from ''Below the Heavens''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/8006597/blu-and-exile-interview-in-the-beginning-before-the-heavens-album|title=Blu & Exile Talk Comeback Album 'In the Beginning: Before the Heavens,' Stream New Project: Exclusive|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|first=Eric|last=Diep|date=October 19, 2017|accessdate=October 21, 2017}}</ref> Their next release, the True & Livin' EP was released May 24, 2019.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://thedirtyscience.com/blu/blu-exile-true-livin/|title=Blu & Exile True & Livin'|work=[[Dirty Science.]]|date=May 24, 2019|accessdate=June 10, 2019}}</ref> On July 17, 2020, Blu & Exile released their third album, ''[[Miles: From An Interlude Called Life]]'' (or ''Miles'' for short), coinciding with the 13th anniversary of ''Below the Heavens''.
