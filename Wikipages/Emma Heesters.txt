'''Emma Heesters''' (born 8 January 1996) (pronunciation: emā hīsṭars) is a Dutch singer and television personality, mainly known for performing covers of popular songs on her own YouTube channel.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-06-16|title=Emma Heesters Is Ready To Sing At Eurovision 2022|url=https://wiwibloggs.com/2020/06/16/emma-heesters-is-ready-to-sing-at-eurovision-2022/255032/|access-date=2021-07-03|website=wiwibloggs|language=en-US}}{{unreliable source|date=July 2021}}</ref> She began her music career in 2014 and currently has over 4 million subscribers on YouTube.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Emma Heesters - YouTube|url=https://www.youtube.com/c/EmmaHeesters/videos|access-date=2021-07-03|website=www.youtube.com}}{{unreliable source|date=July 2021}}</ref><ref name=":1" />

== Biography ==
Emma Heesters was born in the Netherlands, and being the daughter of a music teacher, she was surrounded by music in her childhood. Her mother is Willian Heesters, her father is Ludo Heesters, and she had a brother. Her religion is [[Christianity]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Gautam|first=Umesh|title=Emma Heesters Wiki - Age, Husband, Net worth, Family, Biography & More|url=https://www.newsunzip.com/wiki/emma-heesters/|access-date=2021-07-03|language=en-US}}{{unreliable source|date=July 2021}}</ref> She had an early talent for music and practiced to be a singer under the supervision of her mother.<ref name=":1">{{Cite news|date=2017-08-02|title=Nederlandse Emma Heesters krijgt groot Amerikaans platencontract|url=https://jeugdjournaal.nl/artikel/2186074-nederlandse-emma-heesters-krijgt-groot-amerikaans-platencontract.html?ext=html|access-date=2021-07-02}}</ref> After her education, she started consistently uploading her covers to [[YouTube]] and has amassed over 4 million subscribers on her YouTube channel.<ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|title=Emma Heesters on Apple Music|url=https://music.apple.com/us/artist/emma-heesters/958624262|url-status=live|access-date=2021-07-03|website=AppleMusic|language=en-US}}{{primary source inline|date=July 2021}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-06-14|title=Metejoor neemt opnieuw duet op met Nederlandse: "Haar stijl past perfect bij de zomerse song"|url=https://www.hln.be/muziek/metejoor-neemt-opnieuw-duet-op-met-nederlandse-haar-stijl-past-perfect-bij-de-zomerse-song~a79edff5/|url-status=live|access-date=2021-08-06|website=[[Het Laatste Nieuws]]|language=nl}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-06-16|title=Emma Heesters Is Ready To Sing At Eurovision 2022|url=https://wiwibloggs.com/2020/06/16/emma-heesters-is-ready-to-sing-at-eurovision-2022/255032/|access-date=2021-07-03|website=wiwibloggs|language=en-US}}{{unreliable source|date=July 2021}}</ref>

== Career ==
In 2019 she was the first Dutch winner of MTV's Push Award and gained Platinum status for her single "Pa Olvidarte" with Rolf Sanchez,<ref>{{Cite web|last=Choudhury|first=Nila|date=2020-04-19|title=You Can't Miss Emma Heesters' Remake Of 'Shayad'|url=https://www.dissdash.com/2020/04/19/you-cant-miss-emma-heesters-remake-of-shayad/|access-date=2021-07-03|website=DissDash|language=en-US}}</ref> she featured in a song by the band Boyce Avenue plus collaborated with them on their European tour, and took to television performing in ''[[Beste Zangers]]'' ''a'' Dutch television program, the [[web series]] Jachtseason of [[Stukov|StukTV]], and the game show, [[I Can See Your Voice]].<ref>{{Cite news|date=2020-09-09|title=ViacomCBS to produce MTV's first live 5G-enabled remote production|url=https://www.rapidtvnews.com/2020090959012/viacomcbs-to-produce-mtv-s-first-live-5g-enabled-remote-production.html#axzz6zTKlt6NZ|access-date=2021-07-02}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=van Leeuwen|first=Marlies|date=2021-06-14|title=Davina Michelle en Douwe Bob zingen hun favoriete guilty pleasure|url=https://www.ad.nl/show/davina-michelle-en-douwe-bob-zingen-hun-favoriete-guilty-pleasure~ae27cc18/|url-status=live|access-date=2021-08-06|website=[[Algemeen Dagblad]]|language=nl}}</ref><ref name=":0">{{Cite web|last=Slot|first=Aileen|date=2021-06-15|title=Dit zijn de nieuwe namen van Freshtival: 'Het gaat door het dak'|url=https://www.tubantia.nl/enschede/dit-zijn-de-nieuwe-namen-van-freshtival-het-gaat-door-het-dak~ac14ffb4/|url-status=live|access-date=2021-07-03|website=[[Tubantia (newspaper)|Tubantia]]|language=nl}}</ref>

Heesters was named by Top40 NL<ref>{{Cite web|title=Emma Heesters zingt duet met populaire Vlaamse zanger Metejoor|url=https://www.omroepzeeland.nl/nieuws/127844/Emma-Heesters-zingt-duet-met-populaire-Vlaamse-zanger-Metejoor|access-date=2021-07-03|website=Omroep Zeeland|language=NL}}</ref> as the most successful Dutch artist of the year so far in 2020 and later in November was named winner of MTV EMA's Best Dutch Act.<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web|last=September 2020|first=Dan Oliver 08|title=Emma Heesters joins MTV for its first 5G-enabled remote broadcast|url=https://www.5gradar.com/news/emma-heesters-joins-mtv-for-its-first-5g-enabled-remote-broadcast|access-date=2021-07-03|website=www.5gradar.com|date=8 September 2020|language=en}}</ref>
