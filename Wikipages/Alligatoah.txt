'''Lukas Strobel''' (born 28 September 1989), better known by his [[stage name]] '''Alligatoah''', is a German rapper, singer, [[record producer|producer]] and [[Disc jockey|DJ]] signed to the [[Independent music label|independent]] German [[Hip hop music|hip hop]] [[record label]] Trailerpark. He also publishes music for his two fictional characters Kaliba 69 (rapper) and DJ Deagle (producer) under his stage name. His albums ''Triebwerke'' and ''Musik ist keine Lösung'' were certified gold while the single "[[Willst du]]" was certified platinum.<ref name="musikindustrie.de">{{cite web|url=http://www.musikindustrie.de/no_cache/gold_platin_datenbank/ |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2017-05-07 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131202022835/http://www.musikindustrie.de/no_cache/gold_platin_datenbank/ |archive-date=2013-12-02 }}</ref>

== Biography ==
Lukas Strobel grew up in [[Neuenwalde]], a town near [[Bremerhaven]] in [[Lower Saxony]]. During his time at school he produced a number of short films and attended a film festival where he won 750 Euros for one of his films. Strobel continues to produce his videos. On April 10, 2006 Strobel founded the band Alligatoah and created two alter egos, to separate his function as a rapper (Kaliba 69) and as a producer (DJ Deagle). According to Strobel himself, another reason for the creation of the two characters is that he does not always "want to be alone" when making music.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://alligatoah.de/category/alligatoah/|title = Alligatoah &#124; Alligatoah}}</ref> The greatest influence for his music was German battle rap like Aggro Berlin. In 2006 he released his first album ''ATTNTAAT'' and a mixtape called ''Schlaftabletten, Rotwein Teil I''. Both albums, along with his music film ''Goldfieber'', were downloadable for free. In 2007 he published the mixtape ''Schlaftabletten, Rotwein Teil II''. In March 2008 he signed a contract with the German hip hop label rappers.in. Under this label he released the album ''In Gottes Namen'', which was awarded the best demo of the month in the January/February issue of the German hip hop magazine ''Juice''. For this issue Alligatoah produced an exclusive track.<ref>in: ''Juice'' published February 13th 2009.</ref> After successfully graduating from high school, Strobel moved to [[Berlin]]. In 2011 he released the third part of his mixtape named ''Schlaftabletten, Rotwein Teil III''. In August of the same year he left rappers.in and joined the label Trailerpark after Timi Hendrix, a member of this label, recommended Alligatoah.<ref>[http://www.16bars.de/newsartikel/2644/alligatoah-unterschreibt-bei-trailer-park/ Alligatoah unterschreibt bei Trailer Park] August 8, 2011. Retrieved November 22, 2015.</ref> From September 2011 until March 2012 he attended together with Pimpulsiv, Sudden and DNP the PeppNoseDay Tour in Germany.<ref>[http://meinrap.de/2011/08/18/trailerpark-geht-auf-peppnosedays-tour-2011-tourflyer/ Trailerpark geht auf “Peppnosedays Tour 2011″] August 18, 2011. Retrieved November 22, 2015</ref>

In August 2013 Alligatoah released his third album ''Triebwerke'', which debuted at number 1 on the German albums chart.<ref>[http://www.focus.de/kultur/musik/alligatoah-alligatoah-rauscht-mit-triebwerke-an-die-spitze-der-album-charts_aid_1070389.html Focus: Alligatoah rauscht mit Triebwerke an die Spitze der Album-Charts] August 13, 2013. Retrieved November 22, 2015</ref> The album received a gold certification in 2014 and the single "Willst du" reached platinum status.<ref name="musikindustrie.de"/> In September 2014 a remix of the song "Willst du" by the German DJ [[Robin Schulz]] was released and charted on the German [[iTunes]] singles chart just a few hours after its release. On 31 August 2015, Alligatoah announced his fourth album ''Musik ist keine Lösung'', which was released on 27 November.<ref name="MikL">[https://www.facebook.com/alligatoah/photos/a.269489129757512.66813.132841826755577/1020911791281905/?type=1&theater Announcement of ''Musik ist keine Lösung'' on Facebook] August 13, 2015. Retrieved November 22, 2015.</ref> On December 3, Strobel won the award "1Live Krone 2015" for the best hip-hop act.<ref>[http://www.presseportal.de/pm/7899/3193512 einslive Krone: Gewinner 2015]. 1Live Krone winners 2015. Retrieved December 5, 2015</ref> In February 2016, Alligatoah embarked on his Himmelfahrtskommando tour and played in 11 German cities, as well as in [[Graz]] and [[Vienna]]. On April 5 his album ''Musik ist keine Lösung'' was certified gold.<ref name="musikindustrie.de"/>
