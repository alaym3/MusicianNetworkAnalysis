'''Ali Haider''' ([[Urdu]]:علی حیدر, born 22 September 1967) is a Pakistani singer.<ref name=Dawn>{{cite news|url=https://images.dawn.com/news/1187053/singer-ali-haider-is-back-in-action-with-a-new-show |author=Irfan Ul Haq|date=21 April 2021|title=Singer Ali Haider is back in action with a new show (Ali Haider Show for NTV Houston)|newspaper=Dawn (newspaper)|access-date=12 June 2021}}</ref> He had a number of songs in the 1990s, including "Poorani Jeans", which appears on his 1993 album ''Sandesa''.<ref name=Dawn/><ref name=TOI>{{cite news|url=https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/music/news/I-dont-mind-being-called-a-one-hit-wonder-Ali-Haider/articleshow/19524911.cms|title=I don't mind being called a 'one-hit wonder': Ali Haider|author=Debarati S Sen|newspaper=The Times of India|date=13 April 2013|accessdate=12 June 2021}}</ref>

==Career==
He acted in a Pakistani film ''[[Chalo Ishq Larain]]'', released in 2002.

In 2012, he returned to music after a three-year break following the death of his son.<ref name=TOI2>{{cite news|url=https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/music/news/I-lost-music-after-the-loss-of-my-son-Ali-Haider/articleshow/13956161.cms|title=I lost music after the loss of my son: Ali Haider|date=9 June 2012|newspaper=The Times of India|accessdate=12 June 2021}}</ref><ref name=TOI/>

In 2013, he returned to TV with the sitcom ''Ek aur Ek Dhai''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://reviewit.pk/ali-haider-makes-a-comeback-in-acting-with-new-sitcom-ek-aur-ek-dhai/|title=Ali Haider makes a comeback in acting with new sitcom Ek aur Ek Dhai!|date=24 June 2013|work=Reviewit.pk website|accessdate=12 June 2021}}</ref><ref name=TV/>

===Personal life===
Ali Haider was born in an [[Urdu]]-speaking family of Karachi. He married in 2006.<ref name=Dawn2>{{cite news|url=https://www.dawn.com/news/187891/karachi-ali-haider-decides-to-marry|title=KARACHI: Ali Haider decides to marry|newspaper=Dawn (newspaper)|date=16 April 2006|accessdate=12 June 2021}}</ref> After the death of his young son in 2009, Ali Haider quit making music for some time.<ref name=TOI/><ref name=TOI2/>
