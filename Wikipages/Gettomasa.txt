'''Aleksi Lehikoinen''' (born November 22, 1993), better known by his stage name '''Gettomasa''', is a [[Finland|Finnish]] rapper. He won the Finnish battle rap championship in 2012.<ref name=":0" /> He released his debut album ''Vellamo LP'' in 2014 with Finnish music producer Ruuben.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.ksml.fi/kulttuuri/Gettomasa-Ruuben-Vellamo-LP-KPC-Records/252928|title=Gettomasa & Ruuben: Vellamo LP (KPC Records)|last=Kivelä|first=Heikki|work=KSML.fi – Keskisuomalainen|access-date=2018-01-23|language=fi-FI}}</ref> His first solo project ''Chosen One'' was released in 2016.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://rosvot.fi/maitohotelli/suomirapin-uusi-ruhtinas-gettomasa-chosen-one|title=Suomiräpin uusi ruhtinas: Gettomasa – Chosen one|date=2016-03-15|work=Rosvot|access-date=2018-01-23|language=en}}</ref>

== Personal life ==
Lehikoinen was born in [[Montreal]], [[Canada]] in 1993 to [[Finland|Finnish]] parents.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.is.fi/musiikki/art-2000001164846.html|title=22-vuotias rap-artisti Gettomasa kerää kehuja – VilleGalle: "Suomen paras"|date=2016-04-23|work=Ilta-Sanomat|access-date=2018-01-23|language=fi-FI}}</ref> He moved to Finland as a child, and spent most of his childhood in the [[Kortepohja|Kortepohja district]] of [[Jyväskylä]].<ref name=":1">{{Cite news|url=https://www.nalka.fi/artikkeli/gettomasa-pelasi-suomirapin-lapi/|title=Gettomasa pelasi suomirapin läpi - Nälkä|work=Nälkä|access-date=2018-01-23|language=fi-FI}}</ref> He has been very open about his troubled childhood and growing up with his abusive alcoholic father. He discussed their problematic father-son relationship on the track ''Pelkuri'' (The Coward)

Lehikoinen was six years old when he was introduced to rap music by his cousin.<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=https://www.jylkkari.fi/2013/01/gettomasa-pakenee-arkea-riimeihin/|title=Gettomasa pakenee arkea riimeihin - Jyväskylän Ylioppilaslehti|date=2013-01-28|work=Jyväskylän Ylioppilaslehti|access-date=2018-01-23|language=fi-FI}}</ref> 

Lehikoinen is a [[High school|high school]] dropout but graduated from a [[vocational school]].<ref name=":1" />
