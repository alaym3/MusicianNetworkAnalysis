'''Haley Hannah Heynderickx''' (born May 28, 1993)<ref>{{cite web|work=[[California Birth Index]]|title=Birth of Haley Heynderickx|url=https://www.californiabirthindex.org/birth/haley_hannah_heynderickx_born_1993_22219646|access-date=February 1, 2019}}</ref> is an American singer-songwriter from [[Portland, Oregon]]. In 2016, she released her debut EP, ''Fish Eyes''. Her first full-length album, ''[[I Need to Start a Garden]]'', was released in March 2018 on Mama Bird Recording Co.

==Early life==
Heynderickx was born in [[Stockton, California]] to a [[Filipino Americans|Filipino-American]] family and grew up in [[Forest Grove, Oregon]], where she learned to sing in church. She graduated from [[Portland State University]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.wweek.com/music/2015/12/08/introducing-haley-heynderickx/|title=Introducing: Haley Heynderickx}}</ref>

==Career==
She released her debut EP, ''Fish Eyes'', in 2016. In March 2018, she released her first full-length recording, ''[[I Need to Start a Garden]]'', on Mama Bird Recording Co. The album received positive reviews, including a 7.3 rating from ''[[Pitchfork (website)|Pitchfork]]'',<ref name=pitchfork>{{cite news | last1=Horn | first1=Olivia | title=Haley Heynderickx – I Need to Start a Garden | url=https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/haley-heynderickx-i-need-to-start-a-garden/ | accessdate=March 29, 2018 | work=Pitchfork | date=March 12, 2018}}</ref> while [[NPR Music]] wrote that the album "captivates"<ref>{{cite news | last1=Walker | first1=Jerad | title=Review: Haley Heynderickx, 'I Need To Start A Garden' | url=https://www.npr.org/2018/02/22/587238388/first-listen-haley-heynderickx-i-need-to-start-a-garden | accessdate=March 29, 2018 | work=NPR Music | date=February 22, 2018}}</ref> and [[Uproxx]] called it "an utterly brilliant folk debut".<ref>{{cite news | last1=White | first1=Caitlin | title=Haley Heynderickx's 'I Need To Start A Garden' Is An Utterly Brilliant Folk Debut | url=https://uproxx.com/music/haley-heynderickx-i-need-to-start-a-garden-interview/ | accessdate=March 29, 2018 | work=Uproxx | date=March 6, 2018}}</ref> In January 2018, she was named an "Artist to Watch" by ''[[Stereogum]]''.<ref>{{cite news | last1=Deusner | first1=Stephen | title=Artist To Watch: Haley Heynderickx | url=https://www.stereogum.com/1978242/artist-to-watch-haley-heynderickx/franchises/interview/ | accessdate=March 29, 2018 | work=Stereogum | date=January 16, 2018}}</ref>

==Style==
Heynderickx's music features a [[Fingerstyle guitar|fingerstyle]] acoustic guitar technique inspired by [[Leo Kottke]] and [[John Fahey (musician)|John Fahey]]<ref>{{cite news | last1=Walker | first1=Jerad | title=How Haley Heynderickx Found Her Confidence For Her Debut Album | url=http://nprillinois.org/post/how-haley-heynderickx-found-her-confidence-her-debut-album | accessdate=March 29, 2018 | work=NPR Illinois | date=March 29, 2018}}</ref> paired with introspective lyrics punctuated with "well-timed levity".<ref name=pitchfork/>
