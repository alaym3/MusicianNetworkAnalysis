'''Cecilia Krull''' (born 29 June 1986) is a Spanish singer best known as the singer of "Something's Triggered", the theme song of [[Three Steps Above Heaven|''Three Steps Above Heaven'']], and "[[My Life Is Going On]]", the main theme song of ''[[Money Heist]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://open.spotify.com/playlist/2ihK63yWyrMYmdDONdjwif|title=La Casa de Papel / Money Heist – The Soundtrack on Spotify|publisher=spotify.com|accessdate=24 April 2020}}</ref>

==Biography==
Born to a pianist father from France and a singer mother from Spain, Krull's career began at the age of 7 in [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]] productions and continued until the age of 14. She reached international popularity in 2017 with the song "[[My Life Is Going On]]", the theme from the [[Netflix]] TV series ''[[Money Heist]]''.<ref name=CECILIA>{{cite web|url=https://www.rockol.it/artista/cecilia-krull/biografia?refresh_ce|title=Cecilia Krull Biografia|publisher=rockol.it|language=it|accessdate=24 April 2020}}</ref>

==Selected discography==
The Spanish singer has often worked with author Manel Santisteban, interpreting the following songs.<ref name=CECILIA/>

===Singles===
* "All My Fears", for the film ''[[Brain Drain (film)|Fuga de Cérebros]]'' (2009)
* "Something's Triggered", for the film ''[[Three Steps Above Heaven|Três Metros Sobre o Céu]]'' (2010)
* "[[My Life Is Going On]]", the main theme song of ''[[Money Heist]]'' (2017)
* '"La verdad", for the TV series ''[[El Accidente]]'' (2018)
* "Agnus Dei", the main theme song of ''[[Locked Up (TV series)|Vis a Vis]]'' (2019), with [[Mala Rodríguez]]
