'''Guendoline Rome Viray Gomez''' (born August 30, 1997), better known by his stage name '''No Rome''', is a [[London]]-based Filipino musician. Gomez grew up in [[Manila]] before settling in London. He is currently signed to [[Dirty Hit]], the same label as collaborators [[The 1975]].<ref>{{cite web |last1=Smith |first1=Thomas |title=The 1975 collaborator No Rome shares lush new single 'Cashmoney' |url=https://www.nme.com/blogs/nme-radar/1975-collaborater-no-rome-shares-lush-new-single-cashmoney-2478011 |website=NME |accessdate=19 April 2019 |date=17 April 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Diez |first1=Grace |title=In Focus: 6 Things You Need to Know About London-Based Filipino Musician No Rome |url=https://lifestyle.abs-cbn.com/articles/6628/chalk/in-focus-6-things-you-need-to-know-about-london-based-filipino-musician-no-rome/|website=Lifestyle.abs-cbn.com |accessdate=19 April 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=No Rome drops new track, 'Cashmoney', unveils tracklist for new EP – listen |url=https://www.bandwagon.asia/articles/no-rome-drops-new-track-cashmoney-announces-tracklist-ep |website=Bandwagon.asia |accessdate=19 April 2019}}</ref> His debut studio album, ''It's All Smiles'', will be released in December 2021.
