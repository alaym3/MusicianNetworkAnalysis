'''Last Child''' is an Indonesian pop punk/ alternative rock band formed in 2006. The band's current members are Virgoun Teguh (vocals and guitar), Rachmad Firdaus (guitar), Dimas Rangga (vocals and bass). It has released one mini album and two studio albums.

==Name==
The name ''Last Child'' was chosen because the band founders are the last (youngest) children of their respective families.<ref name="is"/>

==Career==
The band was formed in 2006 in [[East Jakarta]]<ref name="rn">{{cite web |url=http://rollingstone.co.id/read/2011/04/07/142645/1610936/1093/besok-rolling-stone-release-party-edisi-april-2011 |title=Besok. Rolling Stone Release Party Edisi April 2011 |first=Reno |last=Nismara |date=7 April 2011 |work=Rolling Stone Indonesia |accessdate=6 May 2012 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110410160241/http://rollingstone.co.id/read/2011/04/07/142645/1610936/1093/besok-rolling-stone-release-party-edisi-april-2011 |archive-date=10 April 2011 |url-status=dead }}</ref> by schoolmates Virgoun Teguh (guitar and vocals), Dimas Rangga (bass), and Ary Ceper (drums). Later Ary left the band, while Rachmad Firdaus joined as a guitarist.<ref name="is">{{cite web |url=http://www.thejakartapost.com/news/2012/03/04/last-child-wants-have-a-say.html |title=Last Child wants to have a say |first=Indah |last=Setiawati |date=4 March 2012 |work=The Jakarta Post |accessdate=6 May 2012}}</ref>

The group's first album was ''Grow Up'', a mini album. It was released independently.<ref name="rn"/> Virgoun described it as having many socially-themed protests.<ref name="is"/>

In 2008,<ref name="yd">{{cite web |url=http://hot.detik.com/music/read/2012/03/21/105307/1873026/228/perjalanan-4-tahun-last-child-untuk-album-kedua |title=Perjalanan 4 Tahun Last Child untuk Album Kedua |first=Yulia |last=Dian |date=21 March 2012 |work=detikcom |accessdate=6 May 2012}}</ref> Last Child released their first studio album, ''Everything We Are Everything'', was released by Fake Records.<ref name="rn"/> Virgoun said that the album touches different issues from the independent album, "from love to spiritualism".<ref name="is"/>

In 2009, the band signed a contract with the local studio dr.m to promote the ringtone for "Diary Depresiku" ("My Depression Diary"), about a kid wrought with emotional turmoil after a divorce, which reached 300,000 downloads. Indah Setiawati wrote that the song has bitter lyrics and a slow melody.<ref name="is"/> The second single, "Pedih" ("Sore"), was in a [[rock music|rock]] style but had melancholic lyrics.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://showbiz.vivanews.com/news/read/215260-last-child-coba-peruntungan-di-musik-mellow |title=Last Child, Adu Untung Rock Melankolis |first=Muhammad |last=Firman |first2=Beno |last2=Junianto |date=17 April 2011 |work=VIVAnews |accessdate=6 May 2012}}</ref> The third single released by dr.m was "Percayalah" ("Trust").<ref>{{cite web |url=http://music.okezone.com/read/2012/01/24/386/562712/last-child-rilis-album-hasil-doa |title=Last Child Rilis Album Hasil Doa |first=Rama Narada |last=Putra |work=Okezone |date=24 January 2012 |accessdate=6 May 2012 |archive-url=https://archive.is/20120716064013/http://music.okezone.com/read/2012/01/24/386/562712/last-child-rilis-album-hasil-doa |archive-date=16 July 2012 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

The band released ''[[Our Biggest Thing Ever]]'', its second album, in January 2012 through dr.m.<ref name="yd"/> The songs were mostly written by Virgoun. One of the songs, "Seluruh Nafas Ini" ("This Whole Breath") features Giselle, who was a finalist in the 2008 series of [[Indonesian Idol]].<ref name="is"/>

==Influences==
The band members said that their musical style is influenced by [[Blink 182]], [[MxPx|MXPX]], [[Angels and Airwaves]], [[Rancid (band)|Rancid]], [[Green Day]], [[We the Kings]], and [[Sheila on 7]]. Morin Chandra, the chief commercial officer of dr.m, said that the band has "strong lyrics, which reflect real life, not merely something out of [their] imagination".<ref name="is"/>
