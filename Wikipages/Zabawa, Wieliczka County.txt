'''Zabawa''' {{IPAc-pl|z|a|'|b|a|w|a}} ([[translation]]: ''Fun'') is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Wieliczka]], within [[Wieliczka County]], [[Lesser Poland Voivodeship]], in southern Poland.<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) &ndash; TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> It lies approximately {{convert|2|km|mi|0}} east of [[Wieliczka]] and {{convert|14|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} southeast of the regional capital [[Kraków]].

The village has a population of 812.
