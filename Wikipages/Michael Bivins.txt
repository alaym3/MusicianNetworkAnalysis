'''Michael Lamont Bivins''' (born August 10, 1968) is an American singer, rapper, manager, actor, and producer, and a founding member of [[New Edition]] and [[Bell Biv DeVoe]].<ref name="George2001">{{cite book|last=George|first=Nelson|title=Buppies, b-boys, baps & bohos: notes on post-soul Black culture|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=E1XR7Fz-7gIC&pg=PA63|access-date=13 July 2011|date=2001-07-11|publisher=Da Capo Press|isbn=978-0-306-81027-5|pages=63–}}</ref>

==Biography==
Bivins was born in [[Boston, Massachusetts]]. He and his wife, Teasha, have four daughters: Savannah, Shilah, Starlah, and Sanaah Bivins.{{cn|date=November 2021}}

==Career==
Michael Bivins is one of the founding members of [[New Edition]]. When the group broke up he formed [[Bell Biv DeVoe]] with [[Ricky Bell (singer)|Ricky Bell]] and [[Ronnie DeVoe]]. The group is credited with being one of the pioneers of the [[new jack swing]] sound. Their debut album, ''[[Poison (Bell Biv DeVoe album)|Poison]]'', peaked at number five on the [[Billboard 200]] chart in 1990.

===Music Manager===
He also discovers, manages, and produces for other acts, most notably [[Another Bad Creation]], [[MC Brains]], [[Boyz II Men]], and [[702 (band)|702]], all of whom were signed to his [[Motown Records|Motown]] distributed label [[Biv 10 Records]]. He serves as the music entrepreneur and [[A&R]] man of both of his own acts. Bivins also produced [[The New Edition Story]], a biographical miniseries that ran on BET in 2017.

===Acting===
Bivins' credits include [[Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas soundtrack#Contemporary Soul Radio .28CSR-103.9.29|CSR 103.9]] in the hit [[video game]] ''[[Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas]]''. Most recently, he appeared in the basketball film ''[[Crossover (2006 film)|Crossover]]'' as the character, Heart Attack. Bivins also managed Artist Development on the reality competition show ''[[Making the Band|Making the Band 4]]'' and is CEO and founder of Sporty Rich Enterprises record label.
