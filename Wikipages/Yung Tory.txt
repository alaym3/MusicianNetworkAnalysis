'''Tory Lester Zellars''' (born April 24, 1996), better known by his stage name '''Yung Tory''' and nickname, '''Mr. Yaw Yaw<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://sidedoormag.com/blog/2018/10/12/yung-tory-|title = Yung Tory &#124; Mr.YawwYaww}}</ref>''', is a Canadian rapper from [[Toronto]], [[Ontario]]. Tory initially signed to [[Lil Durk]]’s record label [[OTF (record label)|OTF]] in 2015, but after gaining recognition in 2018 he signed into a joint venture deal with [[Timbaland]]’s [[Mosley Music Group]] and [[Def Jam Recordings]] a year later. He released his debut album ''[[Rastar (album)|Rastar]]'' in early 2019.

==Early life==
Tory was born in Toronto, Canada, and spent most of his childhood in Atlanta or Toronto. He is of Jamaican descent and is one of the few artists to combine the rap and the rasta lifestyle. He is a lifelong vegan and is a committed [[Rastafarian]]. He was influenced in his rap style by listening to [[Lil Wayne]], [[Dipset]] and [[50 Cent]]. He first found his love for music as a toddler, rapping over beats on his father's [[Xbox]] console.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://mosleymusicgroup.com/#artists|title=Artists: Yung Tory|website=Mosley Music Group|language=en-US|access-date=2020-01-12}}</ref>

==Career==
Yung Tory comes from Toronto, Ontario, and has been rapping music since 2015. He was noticed by Lil Durk who signed Yung Tory in 2017.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://audibletreats.com/yung-tory_pr1/|title=Lil Durk Co-Signed Trap Rasta Yung Tory is the Drippiest Rapper You Haven't Heard Yet|date=2017-09-27|website=Audible Treats|language=en-US|access-date=2020-01-05}}</ref>

He was signed to [[Def Jam Recordings]] in 2019 and subsequently released his debut album ''Rastar'' in March.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.worldwideentertainmenttv.com/tdot-fest-headliner-yung-tory-inks-deal-with-def-jam/|title=TDot Fest Headliner Yung Tory Inks Deal with Def Jam|last=WWETV-admin|website=WWETV|language=en-US|access-date=2020-01-05}}</ref> The song of the same name was sampled by [[Jorja Smith]] on "[[Be Honest (song)|Be Honest]]" featuring [[Burna Boy]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://exclaim.ca/music/article/yung_tory-rastar|title=Yung Tory Rastar|website=exclaim.ca}}</ref> In the same year he released the single "Friends," a song he which he used a sample from the artist Anne Marie's Marshmello, and his biggest personal single "Netflix & Chill" featuring Shoreline Mafia's Fenix Flexin.<ref>https://www.defjam.com/music/yung-tory</ref>

He released his debut EP ''Still Here'' in September 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://hiphopdx.com/reviews/id.3535/title.review-timbalands-artist-yung-tory-doesnt-live-up-to-his-influences-on-still-here-ep|title=Review: Timbaland's Artist Yung Tory Doesn't Live Up To His Influences On "Still Here EP"|first=HipHopDX-|last=https://hiphopdx.com|date=September 18, 2019|website=HipHopDX}}</ref> The album was released on [[Timbaland]]'s label [[Mosley Music Group]].

Yung Tory released the single "2020" on January 24, 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/yung-tory-is-ready-to-tackle-the-year-with-2020-new-song.1985940.html|title=Yung Tory Is Ready To Tackle The Year With "2020"|website=HotNewHipHop|access-date=2020-01-29}}</ref>
