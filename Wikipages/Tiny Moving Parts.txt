'''Tiny Moving Parts''' is an American [[Rock music|rock]] band from [[Benson, Minnesota]]. Formed by brothers William and Matthew Chevalier and cousin Dylan Mattheisen, the band has released seven studio albums since their 2008 formation in junior high. They signed to [[Hopeless Records]] in 2019 for their most recent album, ''[[Breathe (Tiny Moving Parts album)|breathe]]''.

== History ==
Tiny Moving Parts is a self-described family band consisting of brothers William Chevalier (drums) and Matthew Chevalier (bass, vocals), and their cousin Dylan Mattheisen (guitar, vocals) who were also all students at [[Benson High School (Minnesota)|Benson High School]]. The trio started playing together in junior high and have been together since then.<ref name="tumblr">{{cite web|url=http://carlculationinfinity.tumblr.com/TINY%20MOVING%20PARTS|title=Carlculationinfinity – TINY MOVING PARTS INTERVIEW|publisher=carlculationinfinity.tumblr.com|accessdate=2014-08-18|url-status=live|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140819102726/http://carlculationinfinity.tumblr.com/TINY%20MOVING%20PARTS|archivedate=2014-08-19}}</ref> After releasing their first album ''Waves Rise, Waves Recede, the Ocean Is Full of Waves'', they followed up with their second, ''Moving to Antarctica'' on June 19, 2010. Their third album, ''This Couch Is Long & Full of Friendship'', was released through Kind of Like Records on January 13, 2013.<ref name="punknews">{{cite web|url=http://www.punknews.org/review/12017/tiny-moving-parts-this-couch-is-long-and-full-of-friendship|title=Tiny Moving Parts - This Couch Is Long &amp; Full of Friendship &#124; Punknews.org|publisher=punknews.org|accessdate=2014-08-18|url-status=live|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140819083108/http://www.punknews.org/review/12017/tiny-moving-parts-this-couch-is-long-and-full-of-friendship|archivedate=2014-08-19}}</ref> On June 12, "Clouds Above My Head" was released as a free download. On July 9, the album was released physically. Later in the month, the group toured alongside [[The Front Bottoms]], [[Frameworks (band)|Frameworks]] and [[Hostage Calm]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.altpress.com/news/entry/free_download_tiny_moving_parts_clouds_above_my_head|title=Free Download: Tiny Moving Parts, "Clouds Above My Head"|work=Alternative Press|publisher=Alternative Press Magazine, Inc|author=Whitt, Cassie|date=June 12, 2013|accessdate=December 21, 2017|url-status=live|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171221130813/https://www.altpress.com/news/entry/free_download_tiny_moving_parts_clouds_above_my_head|archivedate=December 21, 2017}}</ref>
