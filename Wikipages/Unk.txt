'''Anthony Leonard Platt''' (born November 28, 1981)  commonly known by his stage name '''Unk''', is an American [[DJ]], [[hype man]], and rapper. Some of his work has featured on the [[2K Sports]] ''[[NBA 2K9]]'' game. He is best known for the mid 2000s [[snap music|snap]] hit "[[Walk It Out (Unk song)|Walk It Out]]".<ref name="US100" />

== Career ==
He began spinning records in 1998, and, after meeting DJ Jelly and [[DJ Montay]], joined their DJ entourage, the Southern Style DJs. They performed for high school parties, proms, [[pep rallies]], and other events around the state of Georgia. In 2000, Big Oomp signed Unk to his label, Big Oomp Records. He released the album ''Beat'n Down Yo Block!'' in 2006 led by the single "Walk It Out" that reached Billboard Hot 100 top 10.<ref name="US100" /> In 2008 he released the album 2econd Season supported by the single "Show Out" to moderate success.<ref name="USR&B" /> In 2009 he suffered health-issues that resulted in a reduced profile.<ref>{{Cite web|title = DJ Unk Gives Up Weed, Liquor After Heart Attack|url = http://allhiphop.com/2009/08/31/dj-unk-gives-up-weed-liquor-after-heart-attack/|website = Hip-Hop News, Rumors, Rap Music & Videos {{!}}AllHipHop|access-date = 2016-02-15|date = August 31, 2009|last = Wolfe|first = Roman}}</ref> In 2013 he released the single "Have A Toast" followed by "Wait" in 2014,<ref>{{Cite web|title = #FreshHeat: DJ Unk "Wait"|url = http://allhiphop.com/2014/01/24/freshheat-dj-unk-wait/|website = Hip-Hop News, Rumors, Rap Music & Videos {{!}}AllHipHop|access-date = 2016-02-15}}</ref> while he continues to tour the college circuit.<ref>{{Cite web|title = What the Fuck Happened to...DJ Unk and Chingy? - Notmad Notmad|url = http://notmad.us/2015/02/what-the-fuck-happened-to-dj-unk-and-chingy/#|website = notmad.us|access-date = 2016-02-15}}</ref>
