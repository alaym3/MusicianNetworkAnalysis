'''Palace''' is a [[British people|British]] [[alternative rock]] and [[blues rock]] band from [[London]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Subscribe|title=Palace: "We thought we might be the shittest band in the world" {{!}} Features|url=http://diymag.com/2014/10/14/palace-we-thought-we-might-be-the-shittest-band-in-the-world|access-date=2021-05-01|website=diymag.com|language=en}}</ref> The band has released two albums to date.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Palace {{!}} Biography & History|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/palace-mn0003303810/biography|access-date=2021-05-01|website=AllMusic|language=en}}</ref>

==History==
Palace began in 2014, releasing their debut EP titled ''Lost in the Night''.<ref name="clashmagazine">{{cite web |last1=Murray |first1=Robin |title=Premiere: Palace - 'Lost In The Night' |url=https://www.clashmusic.com/news/premiere-palace-lost-in-the-night |publisher=[[Clash (magazine)|Clash]] |accessdate=27 February 2020}}</ref> In 2015, Palace released their second EP titled ''Chase The Light''.<ref name="diymagazine">{{cite web |last1=Milton |first1=Jamie |title=Palace step up with new EP ‘Chase The Light’ |url=https://diymag.com/2015/06/03/palace-step-up-with-new-ep-chase-the-light |publisher=[[DIY (magazine)|DIY]] |accessdate=27 February 2020}}</ref> In 2016, Palace released their first full-length album titled ''So Long Forever''.<ref name="diymagazine1">{{cite web |title=Palace announce debut album ‘So Long Forever’ |url=https://diymag.com/2016/06/14/palace-announce-debut-album-so-long-forever |publisher=DIY |accessdate=27 February 2020}}</ref><ref name="dorkmagazine">{{cite web |title=Palace - So Long Forever |url=https://www.readdork.com/reviews/palace-so-long-forever/ |publisher=[[Dork (magazine)|Dork]] |accessdate=27 February 2020}}</ref>  In 2019, Palace released their second full-length album titled ''Life After''.<ref name="theguardian">{{cite web |last1=Mongredien |first1=Phil |title=Palace: Life After review – indie trio find new force |url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2019/jul/14/palace-life-after-review |publisher=[[The Guardian]] |accessdate=27 February 2020}}</ref><ref name="diymagazine2">{{cite web |last1=Bentley |first1=James |title=Palace - Life After |url=https://diymag.com/2019/07/12/palace-life-after |publisher=DIY |accessdate=27 February 2020}}</ref>
