'''Ungu''' (literally means 'Purple') is an [[Indonesian rock]] band formed in [[Jakarta]] in 1996 with Makki as the only remaining original member. The group comprises vocalist Pasha, guitarists Enda & Onci, bassist Makki, and drummer Rowman.<ref name="ST"/><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.nst.com.my/lifestyle/groove/2017/09/277477/all-set-afgan-and-ungu|title=All set for Afgan and Ungu|newspaper=[[New Straits Times]]|first=Syahirah|last=Mokhtazar|date=September 8, 2017|access-date=February 8, 2018}}</ref>

Ungu are known for their hit singles, such as "Demi Waktu", "Tercipta Untukku", "Andai Ku Tahu" and "Kekasih Gelapku".<ref name="ST"/><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.esplanade.com/events/2017/ungu-20th-anniversary-concert|title=Ungu 20th Anniversary Concert Live in Singapore 2017|website=[[Esplanade, Singapore|Esplanade]]|access-date=February 8, 2018}}</ref>

Ungu gained mainstream popularity in [[Malaysia]] around 2006 with the release of their single "Demi Waktu". followed by their third album ''Melayang'' (2006).<ref>{{cite news|url=http://ww1.utusan.com.my/utusan/info.asp?y=2006&dt=0620&pub=Utusan_Malaysia&sec=Hiburan&pg=hi_02.htm|title=Ungu atasi rekod jualan album di Indonesia|trans-title=Ungu to overcome record of album selling in Indonesia|newspaper=[[Utusan Malaysia]]|language=ms|first=Mohammad Arif Nizam|last=Abdullah|date=June 20, 2006|access-date=February 8, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://musik.kapanlagi.com/berita/ungu-gelar-konser-di-malaysia-9dvx60s.html|title=Ungu Gelar Konser di Malaysia|trans-title=Ungu held a concert in Malaysia|website=KapanLagi.com|language=id|first=Darmadi|last=Sasongko|date=April 5, 2006|access-date=February 8, 2018}}</ref>

In February 2016, Pasha was elected as [[Deputy Mayor]] of [[Palu]], [[Central Sulawesi]]. However, Pasha is still a member of the band. The vocalist position will be filled by both Enda and Onci for a while during Pasha served as Deputy Mayor.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.star2.com/entertainment/2017/09/07/indonesian-rock-band-ungu-denies-break/|title=Despite their singer's politics, Indonesian rock band Ungu denies split|website=[[Star Media Group|Star2.com]]|first=Angelin|last=Yeoh|date=September 7, 2017|access-date=February 8, 2018}}</ref>

==History==
===Formation and early years (1996–1999)===
The original members of the band were guitarist Ekky, bassist Makki, drummer Pasha Akbar Firmasnyah and vocalist Ariyo Wahab. In 1998, Pasha Akbar left the band and was replaced by Rowman.

===''Laguku'' (2000–2002)===
In 2000, Ungu focused efforts on their debut album. Ungu has also released "[[Hasrat]]" and "Bunga" for a compilation album, ''[[Klik (album)|Klik]]''.

Ungu released their first full-length album, ''Laguku'' on July 6, 2002. Their first single "Bayang Semu" was used for a soundtrack in ''ABG'' ([[RCTI]]). Even though the album was considered success, it reached Platinum Award after two years of its release date.<ref name="Album 1 & 2">{{cite news|url=https://musik.kapanlagi.com/berita/ungu-memilih-suara-hati-sebagai-single-kedua-crfqoba.html|title=Ungu Memilih 'Suara Hati' Sebagai Single Kedua|trans-title=Ungu chose 'Suara Hati' as the second single|website=KapanLagi.com|language=id|first=Darmadi|last=Sasongko|date=March 3, 2004|access-date=February 8, 2018}}</ref>

===''Tempat Terindah'' (2002–2005)===
Just before starting the new album, Ekky decided to leave the band in 2003. His position was replaced by Onci who had just left Funky Kopral.

Ungu released its second album ''Tempat Terindah'' in December 2003. The album spawned a hit single "Karena Dia Kamu" and "Suara Hati". The album sold 150,000 copies.<ref name="Album 1 & 2"/>

Ungu has also collaborated with [[Chrisye]] in his 2005 album, ''[[Senyawa]]''.

===''Melayang'' (2005–2007)===
Ungu successfully recorded new materials for their next album in 2005 and released their next album ''Melayang'' in December 2005 in [[Indonesia]] alone.

This new album had put Ungu into the spotlight with their hit single "Demi Waktu". The single received massive airplay in both Indonesia and Malaysia which causes 4 record labels in Malaysia to own Ungu's copyright to distribute the album in Malaysia. Malaysian's [[Suria Records]] who owns [[Siti Nurhaliza]], won the copyright.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://musik.kapanlagi.com/berita/ungu-melayang-ke-negeri-jiran-xcczajj.html|title=Ungu 'Melayang' ke Negeri Jiran|trans-title=Ungu 'Melayang' to Malaysia|website=KapanLagi.com|language=id|first=Moch. Indra|last=Kurniawan|date=April 13, 2006|access-date=February 8, 2018}}</ref> The album received good reviews from both Malaysian and Indonesian critics. With the successful airplay from another hits likes Seperti Yang Dulu, Tercipta Untukku, Sejauh Mungkin, and Aku Bukan Pilihan Hatimu, this album able to got double platinum certification.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://ww1.kosmo.com.my/kosmo/content.asp?y=2014&dt=0118&pub=Kosmo&sec=Hiburan&pg=hi_03.htm|title=Konsert Ungu 28 Februari di Malaysia|trans-title=Ungu's concert on February 28 in Malaysia|newspaper=[[Kosmo!]]|language=ms|first=Faten Saphilla Mohamed|last=Isa|date=January 17, 2014|access-date=February 8, 2018}}</ref>

===Other albums and activity (2005–present)===

Ungu released a mini album, ''SurgaMu'' to celebrate [[Ramadhan]] in December 2006.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://musik.kapanlagi.com/berita/surgamu-mini-album-ungu-sambut-ramadhan-fpfn73y.html|title='Surgamu', Mini Album Ungu Sambut Ramadhan|trans-title='Surgamu', Ungu's mini album welcomes Ramadan|website=KapanLagi.com|language=id|first=Moch. Indra|last=Kurniawan|date=September 28, 2006|access-date=February 8, 2018}}</ref> 150,000 copies sold in 15 days. The album received an inspiring recognition from [[Yusuf Kalla]] but the band didn't receive it as the band's appearance in [[Presidential Palace]], didn't follow the correct procession protocol.

In 2006, Ungu was nominated for Most Favorite Band/Duo in 2006 [[MTV Indonesia]] Awards, Best Video Director "Demi Waktu" Abimael Gandy and Video of the Year "Demi Waktu".<ref name="MTVI 2006">{{cite news|url=https://hot.detik.com/music/691998/nominasi-penghargaan-mtv-indonesia-2006/228|title=Nominasi Penghargaan MTV Indonesia 2006|trans-title=Nominees of the 2006 MTV Indonesia Awards|website=[[DetikCom|Detik.com]]|language=id|date=October 9, 2006|access-date=February 8, 2018}}</ref>

In 2012, Ungu released a compilation album, ''[[Timeless (Ungu album)|Timeless]]''. The album was only sold at [[KFC]] outlets in Indonesia, and has sold 500.000 in two month. The album compiled top hit singles and four new songs, "Apa Sih Maumu", "Kau Anggap Apa", "Sayang" and "Puing Kenangan". Ungu also includes musicians who merged their albums sold in KFC stores like [[Cinta Laura]], [[Indah Dewi Pertiwi]], [[Noah]], [[Agnez Mo|Agnes Monica]], [[SM*SH]], T.R.I.A.D, [[Rossa (singer)|Rossa]], [[Slank]], [[Last Child (band)|Last Child]], Ello, Sammy Simorangkir and Armada.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.radarjakarta.com/berita-619-ungu-rilis-album-timeless.html|title=Ungu Rilis Album "Timeless"|trans-title=Ungu released "Timeless" album|website=Radar Jakarta|language=id|date=May 10, 2012|access-date=February 8, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://hot.detik.com/music/d-1913438/ungu-jualan-album-timeless-di-gerai-kfc|title=Ungu Jualan Album 'Timeless' di Gerai KFC|trans-title=Ungu selling album 'Timeless' at KFC outlets|website=[[DetikCom|Detik.com]]|language=id|date=May 9, 2012|access-date=February 8, 2018}}</ref>

In 2015, Ungu launched an album called Mozaik. Released on March 18, 2015. This album featuring 2 singles is "Terbaik" and "Aku Tahu". This album feels different from their previous albums, because this album has various musical genres in each song. This album is only sold in all KFC outlets in Indonesia. On July 7, 2015, the Mozaik album received a multiplatinum award for selling more than 200 thousand copies.

In 2015, main vocalist Pasha announced that he will become a candidate to being a [[Deputy mayor|Vice Major]] of [[Palu]] in the 2015 simultaneous regional head election alongside major candidate Hidayat. They currently have the most votes.

==Case==
===Riots on concerts===
During a concert in [[Mojokerto]], [[East Java]], on March 30, 2006, dozens of female fans fainted. Nine months later, on December 19, 2006, the concert "Popcoholic with Purple" at Widya Mandala Krida Stadium, Kedungwuni, [[Pekalongan]] ended in riots that resulted in 10 deaths and six others seriously injured because of being trampled and lack of oxygen when tens of thousands crowded out after watching their concert.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.antaranews.com/berita/49105/10-penonton-tewas-dalam-konser-musik-ungu-di-pekalongan|title=10 Penonton Tewas dalam Konser Musik "Ungu" di Pekalongan|trans-title=10 Spectators died at "Ungu" concert in Pekalongan|website=[[Antara (news agency)|Antara News]]|language=id|author=Priya|date=December 20, 2006|access-date=February 8, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://news.liputan6.com/read/134732/pelajaran-berharga-dari-tragedi-konser-ungu|title=Pelajaran Berharga dari Tragedi Konser Ungu|trans-title=A valuable lesson from Ungu concert tragedy|website=[[Liputan 6]]|language=id|date=December 26, 2006|access-date=February 8, 2018}}</ref>
