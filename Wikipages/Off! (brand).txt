'''Off!''' (styled as '''OFF!''') is an [[insect repellent]] [[brand]] from [[S. C. Johnson & Son]], produced in [[Finland]]. Its active ingredient is [[DEET]] (''N'',''N''-diethyl-''m''-toluamide) or [[metofluthrin]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://pmep.cce.cornell.edu/profiles/insect-mite/fenitrothion-methylpara/metofluthrin/metoflu_reg_0608.pdf|title=Registration of OFF! }}</ref> It was first sold in 1957.<ref>{{cite web|title=Johnson Wax Company: Personal Care & Home Products|url=http://www.hbs.edu/leadership/database/leaders/herbert_f_johnson.html|publisher=[[Harvard Business School]]|access-date=May 30, 2011}}</ref>
