'''Vassendgutane''' is a Norwegian [[country music|country]] and [[danseband]] that have defined their own genre, 'party-country', a combination of the two.{{cn|date=April 2021}}

The band was formed in [[Ørsta]] in 1996, and have since released seven albums and two DVDs.{{cn|date=April 2021}}

==Members==
*Sindre Aam – vocals, bass and lyrics
*Arthur Johan Bjørdal – vocals, guitar and lyrics
*Rune S. Brautaset – guitar, vocals and lyrics
*Ernst O. Brune – accordion
*Lars Egil Vaagseter – steel
*Eivind Skjerdal – electric guitar, background vocals
*Bjørn Marius Øyehaug – drums
