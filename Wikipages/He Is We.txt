'''He Is We''' is an [[indie pop]] band from [[Tacoma, Washington]], fronted by lead singer, Rachel Taylor.<ref name=verbicidemagazine>{{cite web|last=Rose|first=Hanna|title=Interview: He Is We|url=http://www.verbicidemagazine.com/2011/01/04/interview-he-is-we/|publisher=Scissor Press|access-date=January 22, 2011}}</ref>

==Career==
===2008–2011: Formation and early years===
{{quote box|width=280px|align=right|quote=Trevor and I would see these local bands with these cocky auras about them. They didn't want to communicate with fans or acknowledge them in any way. He Is We gets its meaning from us wanting to be like everyone else. We are so blessed and want to stand with the crowd and make someone go, 'Dude, she was standing right next to me earlier, and now she's on stage.'<ref>{{cite web |first=Charlee |last=Monroe |url=http://www.spinner.com/2010/03/12/he-is-we-interview-sxsw-2010/ |title=He Is We Interview: SXSW 2010 |publisher=Spinner |date=March 15, 2010 |access-date=December 11, 2011}}</ref>|source=Rachel Taylor}}

Rachel Taylor and Trevor Kelly met while working at Ted Brown Music Company, a music store in their hometown of [[Tacoma, Washington]].<ref>{{cite web|first=Corey|last=Apar|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/he-is-we-mn0002424448|title=He Is We|work=AllMusic}}</ref> Rachel and Trevor were the founding duo that comprised He Is We. The touring members initially included Harrison Allen (drums), Carman Kubanda (guitar), and Aaron Campbell (keyboard/guitar).<!--I had a source for this, but it was from the Examiner, and that's a blocked site. Need a new one!--> Later, Jake Randle (bass guitar) replaced Aaron Campbell.<ref>{{cite web|title=Optimism Encounters Frustration|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2011/07/09/arts/music/he-is-we-at-the-studio-at-webster-hall-review.html?_r=0|work=The New York Times|date=July 8, 2011 |access-date=January 12, 2013}}</ref>{{Better source needed|date=April 2014}} He Is We initially relied on social networking, using websites such as [[Myspace]] and [[Facebook]]. In 2009, they were named [[PureVolume]]'s number one unsigned band of the year.<ref>{{cite web|title=He Is We|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/artist/he-is-we/id330688951|website=iTunes Preview|publisher=[[Apple Inc.]]|access-date=April 22, 2012}}</ref> This online presence and the release of an album of old demos in February 2010 led to their signing with [[Universal Motown Records]]. Prior to the release of ''[[My Forever (album)|My Forever]]'', He Is We toured with [[The Rocket Summer]].<ref>{{cite web|last=Boyer|first=Travis|title=He Is We Interview: SXSW 2010|url=http://www.spinner.com/2010/03/12/he-is-we-interview-sxsw-2010/|publisher=AOL Music|access-date=January 22, 2011}}</ref>  Their debut album ''[[My Forever (album)|My Forever]]'' hit number 6 on ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]''{{'}}s Heatseekers Album Charts.<ref>{{cite web|title=THE WORKING GROUP PRESENTS: THE LOVE IS ALL AROUND US TOUR W/ THE SCENE AESTHETIC ...|url=http://hangout.altsounds.com/news/125037-the-working-group-presents-the-love-is-all-around-us-tour-w-the-scene-aesthetic.html|publisher=Alt sounds|access-date=January 22, 2011|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110121230905/http://hangout.altsounds.com/news/125037-the-working-group-presents-the-love-is-all-around-us-tour-w-the-scene-aesthetic.html|archive-date=January 21, 2011|df=mdy-all}}</ref>  On their website, He Is We says that the start of the band came from "the idea that someone who you pass everyday can influence the rest of your life ... and you would never know."<ref>{{cite web|title=Story|url=http://www.heiswemusic.com/story/|website=He Is We|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100624111001/http://www.heiswemusic.com/story/|archive-date=June 24, 2010|access-date=April 22, 2012}}</ref>

===2012–2017: Lineup changes, She Is We, and other releases===
In early 2012, Rachel Taylor was diagnosed with [[ankylosing spondylitis]], and Stevie Scott filled in for the remainder of the tour.<ref>{{cite web|title=He Is We Lead Singer No Longer Able To Tour; Bringing In Touring Replacement|url=http://propertyofzack.com/post/21941153994/he-is-we-lead-singer-no-longer-able-to-tour-bringing|publisher=Property Of Zack|access-date=September 3, 2012}}</ref> Despite being officially replaced by Scott in August,<ref>{{cite web|title=He Is We announces new singer|url=http://www.indiependentmusic.net/2012/05/from-earlier-he-is-we-announces-new-singer/|publisher=indiependentmusic|access-date=September 3, 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.underthegunreview.net/2012/08/30/he-is-we-announce-new-vocalist-comment-on-rachel-taylors-departure/|title=He Is We Announce New Vocalist, Comment On Rachel Taylor's Departure|work=Under the Gun Review}}</ref> and having stated "Walking away from the label would mean that I would be walking away from the He Is We name but I would regain my freedom to be me. I would regain my ability to connect with you fans 100% with my words. I wouldn't feel like I can only release what THEY want me to. I could be the soul that longs to escape this dreary vessel.",<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.underthegunreview.net/2012/08/28/rachel-taylor-blogs-about-leaving-he-is-we-discusses-future-plans/|title=Rachel Taylor Blogs About Leaving He Is We, Discusses Future Plans|work=Under the Gun Review}}</ref> she returned to the band at the end of October 2012,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.altpress.com/news/entry/rachel_taylor_rejoins_he_is_we|title=Rachel Taylor rejoins He Is We|work=Alternative Press}}</ref> while Trevor Kelly departed to form his own band.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://propertyofzack.com/post/34743025000/rachel-taylor-is-back-in-he-is-we-trevor-kelly-is-out|title=Rachel Taylor Is Back In He Is We; Trevor Kelly Is ...|work=propertyofzack.com}}</ref>

After Kelly left with Stevie Scott in 2012, Rachel continued to pursue the project herself. She said that she planned to release new music and tour in 2013. In 2013, their song "All About Us", with [[Aaron Gillespie]], was included on ''[[The Mortal Instruments: City of Bones (Music from the Motion Picture)|The Mortal Instruments: City of Bones]]'' soundtrack. Despite rumors surfaced of her changing the name of He Is We and moving in a new direction, she insisted that the rumors were completely untrue and she would do no such thing. However, on June 23, 2014, Rachel Taylor broke away as a solo artist with her debut EP, ''Come Alive''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.altpress.com/news/entry/rachel_taylor_he_is_we_announes_new_ep_come_alive|title=Rachel Taylor (He Is We) announces new EP, 'Come Alive'|work=Alternative Press|date=May 19, 2014}}</ref>

In August 2015, Taylor announced she was launching "a He Is We 2.0" with Adamm Mitchell entitled She Is We. On October 22, 2015, She Is We released their first single "Boomerang" and announced that their debut album ''War'' would be available for pre-order on October 23, 2015.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.altpress.com/features/entry/she_is_we_formerly_he_is_we_premieres_new_single_boomerang_exclusive|title=She Is We (formerly He Is We) premieres new single, "Boomerang" (exclusive)|work=Alternative Press|date=October 22, 2015}}</ref> It was released by [[Vanguard Records]] on March 18, 2016. ''War'' was made available to stream prior to release on Billboard's website on March 11, 2016.<ref>{{cite magazine|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/rock/7120583/she-is-we-album-war-exclusive-premiere|title=Former He Is We Singer Rachel Taylor Debuts New She Is We Project With 'WAR' Album: Exclusive Premiere|magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]}}</ref>

In August 2016, Taylor announced that she had reunited with bandmate Trevor Kelly and that they were writing new songs.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/heiswe.official/videos/10154303286925977/|title=He Is We|website=www.facebook.com}}</ref> In September 2016, Taylor and Kelly announced that they would be touring together.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/heiswe.official/photos/a.422296600976.209955.157556445976/10154359942850977/?type=3&theater|title=He Is We|website=www.facebook.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/heiswe.official/videos/10154334750295977/|title=He Is We|website=www.facebook.com}}</ref>

In August 2017, touring members McBride, Mitchell and Chamberlain left the band and in September 2017, touring guitarist Macy Santa Maria accused Taylor of sexually assaulting her.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.altpress.com/news/entry/macy_santa_maria_he_is_we_rachel_taylor_statements|title=He Is We sexual assault allegations: Macy Santa Maria, Rachel Taylor release statements - News - Alternative Press|website=Alternative Press|date=September 22, 2017}}</ref> Taylor responded with her own statement, denying that a sexual assault had occurred and alleging that Santa Maria's statement mischaracterized her actions and the nature of sexual assault.<ref>"It disgusts me that the true allegation is "poking her butt while you were asleep in the hotel bed" and the words being used, imply sexual assault. ... I would not and did not do anything illegal and nothing was ever communicated implying anything like this." [https://substreammagazine.com/2017/09/exclusive-rachel-taylor-assault/ Exclusive: He Is We’s Rachel Taylor further comments on sexual assault allegations]. Substream Magazine, September 23, 2007.</ref> Following Santa Maria's allegations, He Is We were removed as support for [[Secondhand Serenade]]'s fall 2017 tour.<ref>[https://substreammagazine.com/2017/09/he-is-we-assault-tour/ He Is We removed from Secondhand Serenade tour following assault allegations]. Substream Magazine, September 29, 2017.</ref> On June 4, 2019, Rachel Taylor was found not guilty of sexual assault following a one day trial in Jamestown, North Dakota.<ref>{{cite web |title=HE IS WE SINGER RACHEL TAYLOR FOUND NOT GUILTY OF SEXUAL ASSAULT ALLEGATIONS |url=https://www.altpress.com/features/he-is-we-rachel-taylor-not-guilty-allegations/ |website=altpress |date=June 28, 2019 |publisher=Alt Press |access-date=14 July 2019}}</ref> Taylor was found guilty of a separate disorderly conduct misdemeanor charge, which carries up to a $1,500 fine and potentially 30 days in jail.<ref>{{cite web |title=Rachel Taylor of He Is We Found Not Guilty of Sexual Assault by a Jury Trial |url=https://ventsmagazine.com/2019/06/25/rachel-taylor-of-he-is-we-found-not-guilty-of-sexual-assault-by-a-jury-trial/ |website=ventsmagazine |date=June 25, 2019 |publisher=Vents Magazine |access-date=14 July 2019}}</ref>
 
In March 2017, He is We released ''Fall out of Line'' to [[iTunes]], which contains old demos and re-released songs. The band's "For the Runaways" tour (March and April 2017) promoted the album by focusing on older He Is We songs. Trevor Kelly did not tour with the band during the "For the Runaways" tour, with Hans Hessburg filling in on acoustic guitar.{{citation needed|date=October 2018}}

===2018–present: ''He Is We Chapter One'' and ''Hold My Heart''===

In March 2018, He is We released ''He Is We Chapter One'' digitally, which contains re-released songs. Rachel Taylor announced in February 2018 that she was back in studio and that she would release a new He Is We EP in March or April 2018.

He Is We announced they would be touring with Vendetta Red in March 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sidestagemagazine.com/launch-us-tour-vendetta-red/|title=He Is We Launch US Tour with Vendetta Red|website=www.sidestagemagazine.com|date=February 9, 2018}}</ref>

He Is We's new EP, ''Hold My Heart'', was released on April 10, 2018.<ref>{{cite tweet|number=982388836146675712|user=HeIsWE|title="Hold My Heart" EP out in four days, April 10th! #Everyotherman<!-- full text of tweet that Twitter returned to the bot (excluding links) added by TweetCiteBot. This may be better truncated or may need expanding (TW limits responses to 140 characters) or case changes. --> |date=April 6, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/hold-my-heart-ep/1366355856|title = Hold My Heart - EP by He is We|website = [[iTunes]]|date = April 10, 2018}}</ref>

Rachel Taylor has recently announced that He Is We will be back with the new single "Amazing Grace", The single will come out on February 14, 2020.
