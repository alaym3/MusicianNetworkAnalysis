'''Waking the Cadaver''' is an American [[death metal]] band from [[Jersey Shore]], New Jersey, formed in 2006. Originally called '''Death to Honor''', the band released one demo in 2004 before changing their name to Waking the Cadaver in 2006. The band has released a total of three albums within a six-year span; ''[[Perverse Recollections of a Necromangler]]'' (2007), ''Beyond Cops, Beyond God'' (2010) and ''Real-Life Death'' (2013). They are now signed to Unique Leader Records and are set to release their new album ''Authority Through Intimidation'' on October 22nd this year.

==History==
The band formed in January 2006 in Shore Points, New Jersey. They released a two-song demo, ''Demo 2006''. Its two tracks, "Chased Through the Woods by a Rapist" and "Blood Splattered Satisfaction", were both later re-recorded.

On November 21, 2007 their debut album, ''[[Perverse Recollections of a Necromangler]]'', was released on Necrohamonic Records.<ref name="allmusic_a">{{cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/beyond-cops-beyond-god-mw0002016974|title=Beyond Cops, Beyond God - Waking the Cadaver - Songs, Reviews, Credits|website=[[AllMusic]]|accessdate=August 10, 2016}}</ref>

On August 10, 2010, they released their second full-length album, ''Beyond Cops. Beyond God.'' and supported it with tours with [[death metal]] bands such as [[Napalm Death]] and [[Immolation (band)|Immolation]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.centurymedia.com/newsdetailed.aspx?IdNews=9132&IdCompany=3|title=Century Media Records - Napalm Death: : touring Europe with Immolation, Macabre and Waking The Cadaver in November/December! Working on a new album!|website=Centurymedia.com|accessdate=August 10, 2016}}</ref>

On January 19, 2013, the band released a new single, "Lumped Up". They announced plans to release a new album, ''Real-Life Death''.<ref>{{cite magazine |url=http://www.altpress.com/news/entry/waking_the_cadaver_stream_new_single_from_pre_production_lumped_up |title=Waking the Cadaver stream new single from pre-production, "Lumped Up" |magazine=[[Alternative Press (music magazine)|Alternative Press]] |date=January 19, 2012}}</ref> ''Real-Life Death'' was released digitally and sold at the band's live concert shows on October 5, 2013 and was released in stores November 5, 2013.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://amputatedveinrecords.bandcamp.com/album/real-life-death|title=Real-Life Death, by Waking The Cadaver|website=Amputatedveinrecords.bandcamp.com|accessdate=August 10, 2016}}</ref>

Waking the Cadaver’s fourth album Authority Through Intimidation is set for release for October 2021.{{cn|date=November 2021}}

==Musical style==
Waking the Cadaver has been described as [[brutal death metal]],<ref name="auto">{{Cite web|url=http://fullforcestudio.com/client/waking-the-cadaver/|title=Waking the Cadaver|website=Fullforcestudio.com}}</ref> particularly its [[slam death metal]] offshoot.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://noisecreep.com/waking-the-cadaver-beyond-cops-beyond-god-new-album/|title=Waking the Cadaver Bring 'Slamming Gore Groove' in New Album|first=Justina|last=Villanueva|website=Noisecreep.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.metalsucks.net/2009/09/18/axl-rosenberg-should-just-go-join-waking-the-cadaver/|title=AXL ROSENBERG SHOULD JUST GO JOIN WAKING THE CADAVER|date=September 18, 2009|website=MetalSucks}}</ref> The band has also been described as [[deathcore]].<ref>{{cite magazine|author=Yardley, Miranda|title=Waking the Cadaver to release 'Beyond Cops, Beyond God' in August|url=http://www.terrorizer.com/news/news-news/waking-the-cadaver-to-release-beyond-cops-beyond-god-in-august/|magazine=[[Terrorizer (magazine)|Terrorizer]]|accessdate=2014-08-03}}</ref><ref>{{cite magazine|author=Kraus, Brian|title=Waking the Cadaver stream new single from pre-production, 'Lumped Up'|url=http://www.altpress.com/news/entry/waking_the_cadaver_stream_new_single_from_pre_production_lumped_up|magazine=[[Alternative Press (music magazine)|Alternative Press]]|accessdate=2014-08-03}}</ref><ref>{{cite book |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=PxWasD0U6pcC&pg=PA104 |pages=103–104 |title=Studies in Symbolic Interaction |editor=Norman K. Denzin |editor2=Christopher J. Schneider |editor3=Robert Owen Gardner |editor4=John Bryce Merrill |editor5=Dong Han |chapter=Music Genre as a Gendered Process: Authenticity in Extreme Metal |last1=Hutcherson |first1=Ben |last2=Haenfler |first2=Ross |publisher=Emerald Group Publishing |year=2010 |isbn=9780857243614}}</ref> Their later material features influence from [[hardcore punk|New Jersey hardcore]].<ref name=BTSTY>{{Cite web|url=http://bringingthescenetoyou.blogspot.com/2010/07/waking-cadaver-interview.html|website=Bringingthescenetoyou.blogspot.com|title=WAKING THE CADAVER Interview|date=July 21, 2010}}</ref><ref name="auto"/>

Waking the Cadaver's influences include [[Cannibal Corpse]], [[Devourment]], [[Slayer]], [[Napalm Death]], [[Disgorge (American band)|Disgorge]], and Cinerary.<ref name=BTSTY/>
