'''Dawid Henryk Podsiadło''' (born 23 May 1993) is a Polish singer-songwriter who won [[X Factor (Poland series 2)|the second series]] of ''[[X Factor (Poland)|X Factor]]'' in 2012. He received [[Polish złoty|PLN]] 100,000 and a recording contract with [[Sony Music]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Dawid Podsiadło zwycięzcą drugiej edycji programu "X Factor"|url=http://xfactor.tvn.pl/aktualnosci/47259,dawid-podsiadlo-zwyciezca-drugiej-edycji-programu-x-factor,0.html|publisher=xfactor.tvn.pl|language=Polish|accessdate=2 June 2012}}</ref> On the show, he was mentored by [[Tatiana Okupnik]].

His debut album titled ''[[Comfort and Happiness]]'' was released on 28 May 2013.<ref name="debutalbum">{{cite web |title=Dawid Podsiadło wraca do "X Factora" |url=http://muzyka.interia.pl/raport/x-factor/news/dawid-podsiadlo-wraca-do-x-factora,1909710,7927 |publisher=[[Interia.pl|Interia]] |language=Polish |accessdate=12 April 2013 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130416003750/http://muzyka.interia.pl/raport/x-factor/news/dawid-podsiadlo-wraca-do-x-factora%2C1909710%2C7927 |archivedate=16 April 2013 }}</ref> It debuted at number one on the [[OLiS|Polish Albums Chart]] and was certified triple Platinum the same year,<ref name="OLiS"/><ref name="Platinum 2013">{{cite web|url=http://bestsellery.zpav.pl/wyroznienia/platynoweplyty/cd/archiwum.php?year=2013#title |title=Albums and singles certified Platinum in 2013 |publisher=[[Polish Society of the Phonographic Industry]] |language=Polish |accessdate=31 January 2016}}</ref> becoming the best-selling album of 2013 in Poland.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://zpav.pl/rankingi/aktualnosci.php?idaktualnosci=802 |title=Najchętniej kupowane płyty roku 2013 – podsumowanie listy OLiS |language=Polish |publisher=[[Polish Society of the Phonographic Industry|ZPAV]] |accessdate=12 February 2014 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140222014414/http://zpav.pl/rankingi/aktualnosci.php?idaktualnosci=802 |archivedate=22 February 2014 }}</ref> The album received Diamond certification in 2015.<ref name="Diamond 2015"/>

In October 2014, Podsiadło released an album with his band Curly Heads. The album, titled ''Ruby Dress Skinny Dog'' and produced by Daniel Walczak,<ref name="Fryderyk Curly Heads"/> debuted at number four on the Polish Albums Chart and was certified Gold.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://olis.onyx.pl/listy/index.asp?idlisty=893&lang=en |title= OLiS – sales for the period 20.10.2014 – 26.10.2014 |publisher=[[OLiS]] |accessdate=31 January 2016}}</ref><ref name="Gold 2015">{{cite web|url=http://bestsellery.zpav.pl/wyroznienia/zloteplyty/cd/archiwum.php?year=2015#title |title=Albums and singles certified Gold in 2015 |language=Polish |publisher=[[Polish Society of the Phonographic Industry]] |accessdate=31 January 2016}}</ref>
The album was nominated for the 2015 [[Fryderyk]] award in the Rock Album of the Year category.<ref name="Fryderyk Curly Heads">{{cite web|url=http://zpav.pl/pliki/aktualnosci/Fryderyk2015/Nominacje_do_Nagrody_FRYDERYK_2015.pdf |title=Nominacje do Nagrody FRYDERYK 2015 |format=pdf |publisher=[[Polish Society of the Phonographic Industry]] |language=Polish |accessdate=31 January 2016}}</ref>

Podsiadło's second solo album, ''Annoyance and Disappointment'', was released on 6 November 2015. The album debuted at number one in Poland and was certified Diamond.<ref name="OLiS"/><ref name="Diamond 2018"/>
