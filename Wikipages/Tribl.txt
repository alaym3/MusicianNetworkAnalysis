'''Tribl''' (pronounced "tribal") is an American [[contemporary worship music]] collective originating from [[Atlanta|Atlanta, Georgia]]. The collective made its debut in 2021 with the release of ''Tribl I'', a collaborative live album with [[Maverick City Music]]. ''[[Tribl I]]'' debuted at number ten on the [[Top Christian Albums Chart]] and number three on the Top Gospel Albums Chart in the United States. Tribl went on to release their second live album, ''[[Tribl Nights Atlanta]]'' (2021). ''Tribl Nights Atlanta'' debuted at number 46 on the Top Christian Albums Chart and number six on the Top Gospel Albums Chart.

==Career==
On May 28, Tribl released their debut promotional single, "Still Holy" featuring Ryan Oféi and Naomi Raine, announcing that they will release their debut collaborative album with [[Maverick City Music]] titled ''[[Tribl I]]'', initially slated for June 11, 2021.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/tribl-artists-release-still-holy/ |title= TRIBL Artists Release "Still Holy" - TCB |last= Clarks |first= Jessie |date= May 28, 2021 |website= The Christian Beat |access-date= November 27, 2021}}</ref> ''Tribl I'' was ultimately released on July 23, 2021.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/tribl-records-releases-first-compilation-album-tribl-1-ft-maverick-city-music/ |title= Tribl Records Releases First Compilation Album 'Tribl 1' Ft. Maverick City Music - TCB |last= Clarks |first= Jessie |date= July 23, 2021 |website= The Christian Beat |access-date= November 27, 2021}}</ref> ''Tribl I'' debuted at number ten on the [[Top Christian Albums Chart]],<ref name="Tribl I Christian Chart" /> and number three on the Top Gospel Albums Chart in the United States.<ref name="Tribl I Gospel Chart" /> Tribl then went on to release their second live album, ''Tribl Nights Atlanta'' on November 12, 2021.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/tribl-nights-atlanta-available-now/ |title= 'TRIBL Nights Atlanta' Available Now - TCB |last= Clarks |first= Jessie |date= November 12, 2021 |website= The Christian Beat |access-date= November 27, 2021}}</ref> ''Tribl Nights Atlanta'' debuted at number 46 on the Top Christian Albums Chart,<ref name="TNA Christian Chart" /> and number six on the Top Gospel Albums Chart.<ref name="TNA Gospel Chart" />
