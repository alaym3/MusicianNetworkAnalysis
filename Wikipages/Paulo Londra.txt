'''Paulo Ezequiel Londra Farías''' (born 12 April 1998) is an Argentine rapper and singer. His music has topped Argentine charts and been highly successful across Argentina and Latin America.

== Early life ==
Londra was inspired to become a rap artist by watching [[Eminem]]'s ''[[8 Mile (film)|8 Mile]]'' film. The urge to interpret his lyrics outside of a recording studio led him to improvise and rap in Argentinian squares with friends, gradually creating a local buzz. Leaving his comfort zone and freestyling in squares helped with his shyness, and launched a competitive period spanning around five years.<ref name=Homerum>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/latin/8472080/paulo-londra-latin-artist-on-the-rise|title=Latin Artist On the Rise: Meet Paulo Londra|first=Pamela|last=Bustios|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|date=24 August 2018|access-date=24 November 2018}}</ref> He forged his style battling in freestyle contests as the so-called ''El Quinto Escalón'', and rapidly stepped up to [[Latin trap]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/latin/8455315/paulo-londra-argentina-rapper-trap-interview|title=Argentina's Paulo Londra on Keeping Positive in Trap: 'It's Cool Being the Good Guy'|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|date=9 May 2018|access-date=24 November 2018}}</ref>

== Career ==
His professional career kicked off with "Relax", a song he uploaded in early 2017. Londra's music avoids themes prominent in urban music, such as violence and drugs.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/latin/8492198/paulo-londra-argentina-hot-100-song-adan-y-eva-interview|title=Paulo Londra Reveals How He Created Argentina's No. 1 Song, 'Adan y Eva'Interview|website=Billboard|access-date=15 January 2019}}</ref>

Londra became well known through his hit songs "Nena Maldición" (featuring Lenny Tavarez), "[[Te Amo (Piso 21 and Paulo Londra song)|Te Amo]]" (with [[Piso 21]]), and "[[Cuando Te Besé]]" (with [[Becky G]]). The latter peaked at number 45 on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Hot Latin Songs]] and became the first song to ever top the ''[[Billboard Argentina]]'' [[Argentina Hot 100|Hot 100]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/8480409/argentina-hot-100-chart-debuts-becky-g-paulo-londra|title=Billboard Argentina Hot 100 Premieres with Becky G and Paulo Londra at No. 1|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|date=17 October 2018|access-date=24 November 2018}}</ref> His song "[[Adán y Eva]]" also topped Billboard's Argentina Hot 100.<ref name=":0" />

In 2019, he was featured on [[Ed Sheeran]]'s ''[[No.6 Collaborations Project]]'', performing on the song "Nothing on You" along with British rapper Dave. On 26 September of the same year, Londra released the song "Party", a collaboration with the American rapper and producer [[A Boogie wit da Hoodie]]. In May 2020, a conflict between Paulo and his label Big Ligas was made public through Instagram, where Ovy on the Drums and Kristoman were accused of manipulating Londra's contract and defrauding him to have the rights to his songs without the consent of the latter. Londra would later publish an open letter in which he would disclose the reasons for the conflict, claiming that they made him sign with Warner Music without his consent to record his first album, and that they scammed him into signing a contract that linked him to Big Ligas until 2025. This resulted in Paulo not being able to release more music until he could solve the court case that his lawyers are carrying out to be able to terminate his contract with Warner and Big Ligas.
