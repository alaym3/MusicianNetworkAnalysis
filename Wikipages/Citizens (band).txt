'''Citizens''', formerly known as '''Citizens & Saints''', are an American [[Christian rock]] band from [[Seattle]], [[Washington (state)|Washington]].

==Background==
Citizens & Saints were formerly based at [[Mars Hill Church]], which was one of their record labels.<ref name=MARS/> Zach Bolen leads the band.<ref name=Jesus>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vyrU2YX7dK0|title=Gospel|author= |publisher= |accessdate=February 18, 2014}}</ref> Bolen resigned from Mars Hill in 2014, where he served as elder and worship leader.<ref>{{cite web|title=Controlling Christianity Kills Creativity|url=http://zionica.com/2014/06/20/controlling-christianity-kills-creativity/|website=zionica.com|accessdate=20 March 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160401082527/http://zionica.com/2014/06/20/controlling-christianity-kills-creativity/|archive-date=1 April 2016|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.patheos.com/blogs/warrenthrockmorton/2014/06/06/zach-bolen-resigns-from-mars-hill-ballard/|title=Zach Bolen Resigns from Mars Hill Ballard|work=Patheos|access-date=2016-12-25|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160925062332/http://www.patheos.com/blogs/warrenthrockmorton/2014/06/06/zach-bolen-resigns-from-mars-hill-ballard/|archive-date=2016-09-25|url-status=dead}}</ref>

Their album, ''Citizens,'' has seen critical and commercial success since its 2013 release.

After changing their name, the band released their second studio album, ''[[Join the Triumph]]'', in November 2014. This album incorporated more electronic sounds and synthesizers can be heard on several of the tracks. The album is still in keeping with their fundamental indie/alternative rock sound.

==Music==
The band released an EP called ''Already / Not Yet'' on May 8, 2012, which was done only on the Mars Hill label.<ref name=JESUS/>

Their first full-length studio album under the band name Citizens & Saints was ''[[Citizens (album)|Citizens]]'', released on March 12, 2013. This album was released on both the [[BEC Recordings]] and Mars Hill Records labels, and was produced by Brian Eichelberger.<ref name=NRT/><ref name=JESUS/><ref name=ALLMUSIC/>

Their second full-length studio album, ''[[Join the Triumph]]'', was released on November 11, 2014, by [[BEC Recordings]] and was again produced by Eichelberger.

A third album, titled ''A Mirror Dimly'', was released on September 16, 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.citizensandsaints.com/news/2016/8/1/u7pj7afekrinak3wb039yr6pnfuig7|website=Citizens & Saints|title=News|accessdate=11 August 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160919031409/http://www.citizensandsaints.com/news/2016/8/1/u7pj7afekrinak3wb039yr6pnfuig7|archive-date=19 September 2016|url-status=dead}}</ref>
