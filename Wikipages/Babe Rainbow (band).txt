'''Babe Rainbow''' is an Australian [[psychedelic rock]] band from [[Byron Bay, New South Wales|Byron Bay]], [[New South Wales]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://flightlessrecords.com/collections/the-babe-rainbow|title=The Babe Rainbow|website=Flightless Records}}</ref> The band formed in 2014 and they released their first single later that year, Evolution 1964.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.speakertv.com/videos/music-clips/babe-rainbow-evolution-1964/|title=The Babe Rainbow – Evolution 1964|date=May 2, 2014}}</ref>  The band is noted for their "throw-back" [[1960s]] style of psychedelic rock along with their [[surf culture]] imagery.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://clunkmag.com/babe-rainbow-interview/|title=Interview &#124; Babe Rainbow|date=September 2, 2019|website=Clunk Magazine}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.beat.com.au/the-babe-rainbow-evoked-a-60s-fever-dream-when-they-came-to-the-croxton/|title=The Babe Rainbow evoked a ‘60s fever dream when they came to The Croxton|date=May 20, 2019}}</ref> As of 2021, the band has released four full length albums, along with one EP.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.discogs.com/artist/4454908-The-Babe-Rainbow|title=The Babe Rainbow|website=Discogs}}</ref> The band's later work ventures more into [[soft rock]] with influences from [[latin music]], with Domingo being from Venezuela.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/the-babe-rainbow-mn0003653168/biography|title=The Babe Rainbow &#124; Biography & History|website=AllMusic}}</ref> The band has worked with fellow [[Flightless (record label)|Flightless]] alumni Stu Mackenzie of [[King Gizzard & The Lizard Wizard]] to produce their self-titled debut album, ''The Babe Rainbow''. In July 2018, The Babe Rainbow's second studio album, ''Double Rainbow'', debuted at no. 18 on the [[ARIA Albums Chart]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.aria.com.au/charts/news/amy-shark-debuts-at-1-on-the-aria-albums-chart|title=Amy Shark debuts at #1 on the ARIA Albums Chart|website=www.aria.com.au}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/8466482/amy-shark-snags-top-spot-on-australia-albums-chart-love-monster|title=Amy Shark Snags Top Spot on Australia's Albums Chart With 'Love Monster'|website=Billboard}}</ref>
