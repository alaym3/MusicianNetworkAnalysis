'''Giveon Dezmann Evans''' (born February 21, 1995<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|title=Giveon Dezmann Evans, Born 02/21/1995 in California|url=https://www.californiabirthindex.org/birth/giveon_dezmann_evans_born_1995_23599744|access-date=August 27, 2020|website=[[California Birth Index]]}}</ref>), known as '''Giveon''', is an American R&B singer and songwriter. He rose to prominence with his collaboration with [[Drake (musician)|Drake]] on their 2020 single, "[[Chicago Freestyle]]". That same year, Giveon released the EPs, ''[[Take Time (Giveon EP)|Take Time]]'' and ''[[When It's All Said and Done]]'', the former being nominated for a [[Grammy Award for Best R&B Album]], and the latter reaching the Top 10 on the US [[Top R&B Albums]] chart. He released "[[Heartbreak Anniversary]]", as the second single from ''Take Time,'' which reached the [[Top 40]] in the US and was [[Music recording certification|certified Platinum]] by [[Recording Industry Association of America|RIAA]]. In 2021, Giveon was featured alongside [[Daniel Caesar]] on the [[Justin Bieber]] single "[[Peaches (Justin Bieber song)|Peaches]]", which debuted at number one on the [[Billboard Global 200|''Billboard'' Global 200]] and US [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]] charts.

== Early life ==
Evans was born on February 21, 1995, in [[Los Angeles County, California|Los Angeles County]]<ref name=":2" /> (likely in the city of [[Long Beach, California]]).<ref>{{Cite web|last=Polo|first=Maxamillion|date=March 3, 2020|title=Get to Know Giveon, a Haunting and Angelic Voice in R&B|url=http://www.onestowatch.com/blog/get-to-know-giveon-a-haunting-and-angelic-voice|access-date=January 2, 2021|website=[[Ones to Watch]]}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Pastore|first=Alexandra|date=September 18, 2019|title=Just Being Giveon|url=https://wwd.com/business-news/business-features/sony-just-being-giveon-1234592274/|access-date=January 2, 2021|website=[[Women's Wear Daily]]}}</ref> One of three brothers raised by a single mother, he realized his passion for music at a young age, often singing at birthday parties. He credits his mother with pushing him to explore this early love for music and protecting him and his brothers from the pressures of gang culture and poverty.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-02-07|title=R&B Singer Giveon Details His California Upbringing & Breaks Down His Latest Single "Like I Want You" [Interview + Premiere]|url=https://www.okayplayer.com/music/giveon-like-i-want-you-video-interview.html|access-date=2021-02-17|website=Okayplayer|language=en-US}}</ref> He attended [[Long Beach Polytechnic High School]],{{Citation needed|date=December 2020}} and took a music education program at the [[Grammy Museum at L.A. Live|Grammy Museum]] at age 18, where he fell in love with [[Frank Sinatra]], whose bellowing voice was inspiring to the teenager.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|title=R&B Singer Giveon Details His California Upbringing & Breaks Down His Latest Single "Like I Want You" [Interview + Premiere]|url=https://www.okayplayer.com/music/giveon-like-i-want-you-video-interview.html|website=[[OkayPlayer]]|access-date=May 25, 2020}}</ref><ref name="BET">{{Cite web|date=May 20, 2020|title=Rising R&B Singer Giveon Makes Pain Sound Beautiful|url=https://www.bet.com/music/2020/05/20/giveon-106-and-park-mic-check.html|access-date=May 25, 2020|website=[[BET]]}}</ref>

== Career ==
=== 2018–2019: Career beginnings ===
Giveon self-released his debut single "Garden Kisses" in August 2018.<ref name=":3" /> Shortly after, he was discovered by Canadian record producer [[Sevn Thomas]], who found him on a random playlist on [[SoundCloud]], and later signed him to his record label Not So Fast and [[Epic Records]].<ref>https://6ix.buzz/exclusive-scarboroughs-sevn-thomas-chats-working-with-drake-rihanna-travis-scott-and-discovering-giveon/</ref> Upon signing, he started to perform original songs at live venues, including "Like I Want You", before recording them professionally. In November 2019, he released the single "Like I Want You" shortly before serving as an opening act for [[Snoh Aalegra]] on her ''[[Ugh, Those Feels Again|Ugh, A Mini Tour Again]]'' of Europe and North America''.''<ref>{{Cite tweet|user=giveon|number=1197380089270407170|title=LIKE I WANT YOU is finally out now on all platforms! i appreciate you all for waiting ever so patiently. thanks to everyone involved in helping me put this song together. i hope you all fall in love with it! more music coming your way very soon.|author=Giveon|date=November 21, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|first=DeMicia|last=Inman|date=September 24, 2019|title=Snoh Aalegra Announces North American Tour With Baby Rose|url=https://defpen.com/snoh-aalegra-north-american-tour/|accessdate=May 25, 2020|website=Def Pen}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Okon|first=Wongo|date=December 29, 2020|title=Giveon’s Heart Opened And Closed With The World In 2020|url=https://uproxx.com/music/giveon-2020-review-heart-opened-closed/|access-date=January 2, 2021|website=[[Uproxx]]|quote=Giveon introduced himself to a small part of the world as the opening act on Snoh Aalegra's 2019 tour, 'Ugh, A Mini Tour Again.' Here he graced fans with eyebrow-raising performances of songs like "Garden Kisses" and "Like I Want You" and left his newfound supporters to declare him as their "crush" and request he sing at their wedding reception.}}</ref>

==Artistry==
Giveon is known for his [[baritone]] vocals.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|last=Ju|first=Shirley|date=April 9, 2020|title=Giveon: Get To Know Baritone Singer-Songwriter|url=https://flaunt.com/content/giveon|access-date=December 18, 2020|website=[[Flaunt]]|issn=1523-5726}}</ref> He is inspired by jazz music from the 1960s and 1970s.<ref name=":1" /> He cites Frank Sinatra, [[Frank Ocean]], [[Drake (musician)|Drake]], [[Adele]] and [[Sampha]] as some of his biggest influences.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Giveon Explains His Quick Rise, Songwriting Approach and The Impact of a Drake Collaboration|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/9391774/giveon-take-time-interview|access-date=2021-02-17|website=Billboard|language=en}}</ref>
