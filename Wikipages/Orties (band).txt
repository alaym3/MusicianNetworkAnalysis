'''Orties''' is a French band{{when|date=August 2018}} composed of twin sisters, Kincy and Antha.

==Biography==
Based from Paris suburb's city, [[Bures-sur-Yvette]], they started musical careers at the age of 15. Their name Orties is a reference to [[Baudelaire]]’s ''[[Les Fleurs du mal]]''. They started as a rock Gothic band but soon the project evolved toward rap music combined with French lyrics. The two sisters described the mix of genre, as the ''Nouvelle Chanson Francaise'' (New French Song).<ref>{{cite web|author=Laurent David Samama |url=http://www.lesinrocks.com/2013/02/24/musique/orties-linterview-fleuve-11365113/ |title=Orties, l'interview fleuve |publisher=Les Inrocks |date=2013-02-24 |accessdate=2016-12-04}}</ref> Orties released their first EP ''La Boum'' October 2010 on Believe Music. Their controversial track "Plus Putes Que Toutes Les Putes" (Bitchier than any bitches), remixed by Lecter ( Booty Call Records) was playlisted by [[Skrillex]] in his essential mix on [[BBC Radio 1|BBC Radio]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5rghsWzG7ag |title=Orties - Plus Pute Que Toutes Les Putes (Lecter Remix) BCR022 |publisher=[[YouTube]] |date=2012-10-29 |accessdate=2016-12-04}}</ref> In February 2013, ''Sextape'' their first album was released on Nuun Records.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.discogs.com/Orties-Sextape/master/450662 |title=Orties - Sextape |website=Discogs.com |date=2015-09-14 |accessdate=2016-12-04}}</ref>

==Critical reception==
They received support from ''[[Technikart]]''<ref>{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.technikart.com/orties-ou-le-rap-assassin-quon-attendait/ |title=Orties ou le rap assassin qu'on attendait |publisher=Technikart |date=2015-06-03 |accessdate=2016-12-04}}</ref> and mainstream press periodic such as ''Elle Magazine'', and ''Glamour'' etc.<ref>{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.dailyelle.fr/pourquoi-cest-important/elle-dor-a-orties-77692 |title=Elle d'Or à Orties - dailyELLE |website=Dailyelle.fr |date= |accessdate=2016-12-04}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.lesinrocks.com/2016/06/22/musique/orties-rappeuses-jumelles-11848058/ |title=ORTIES, les rappeuses jumelles |publisher=Les Inrocks |date= |accessdate=2016-12-04}}</ref>
In June 2016, they featured four pages on Les Inrockuptibles’ special issue with [[Michel Houellebecq]] as main contributor. They released a new single on June 23, 2016, : "SEXEDROGUEHORREUR" as well as a music video directed by Jean Bocheux. Their second Album ''Nouvelle Chanson Francaise'' which is scheduled to be released mid 2017.<ref>{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.technikart.com/orties-peuvent-elles-piquer-tous-les-publics/ |title=Orties peuvent-elles piquer tous les publics ? |publisher=Technikart |date=2015-06-03 |accessdate=2016-12-04}}</ref>
