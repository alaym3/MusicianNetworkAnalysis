'''Burak Kaçar''' (born 18 November 1994), better known by his stage name '''Zen-G''', is a Turkish [[rapper]] and [[songwriter]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sabah.com.tr/yazarlar/gunaydin/hakan-uc/2020/09/23/titiz-rapci-zen-g|title=Titiz rap’çı Zen-G!|website=Sabah}}</ref>

== Life and career ==
He was born in 1994, in [[Istanbul]]. He founded a band with his friend Tahribad-ı İsyan in 2008.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://m.haberturk.com/zen-g-para-rap-i-degil-karakteri-bozar-2808868-amp|title=Zen - G: Para Rap'i değil karakteri bozar|website=m.haberturk.com}}</ref>
He has made a song for a Toyota ad.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://uzmanparam.milliyet.com.tr/kap-haberi/basin-bulteni-toyotanin-katkilariyla-zen-g-tarafindan-yazilan-imkansiz-yok-sarkisinin-klibi-gosterime-girdi/1726065/|title=BASIN BÜLTENİ-Toyota'nın katkılarıyla Zen-G tarafından yazılan|website=Uzmanpara.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.haberler.com/zen-g-delale-ile-adindan-soz-ettiriyor-13570417-haberi/|title=Zen- G, 'Delalê' ile adından söz ettiriyor|date=September 4, 2020|website=Haberler.com}}</ref>
