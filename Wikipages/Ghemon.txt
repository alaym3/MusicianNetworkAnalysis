'''Ghemon''' (born 1 April 1982) is an Italian rapper and singer-songwriter.

His stage name is a tribute to the character [[Ishikawa Goemon XIII]] from [[Monkey Punch]]'s manga series ''[[Lupin the Third]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://music.fanpage.it/ghemon-il-rapper-svela-il-significato-del-suo-nome-mi-sono-ispirato-a-goemon-del-cartoon-lupin/|title=Ghemon, il rapper svela il significato del suo nome: "Mi sono ispirato a Goemon del cartoon Lupin"|website=Fanpage|date=6 February 2019|access-date=6 February 2019}}</ref> His debut album ''La rivincita dei buoni'' was released in 2007.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.rockit.it/album/8163/ghemon-scienz-la-rivincita-dei-buoni|title=La rivincita dei buoni|website=rockit.it}}</ref> In 2017 his single "Adesso sono qui" was included in the soundtrack of the videogame ''[[NBA 2K17]]''.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.sportfair.it/2016/12/basket-e-rap-laccoppiata-vincente-il-rapper-ghemon-fa-impazzire-i-fans-di-nba-2k17-con-adesso-sono-qui-video/466055/|title=Basket e Rap, l'accoppiata vincente: il rapper Ghemon fa impazzire i fans di NBA 2k17 con "Adesso sono qui" [VIDEO]|date=2 December 2016|publisher=Sport Fair|access-date=20 June 2020}}</ref>

Ghemon participated at the [[Sanremo Music Festival 2019]] with the song "Rose viola"<ref>{{Cite web|title=Italy: Sanremo 2019 finalists unveiled; Il Volo are back in the competition!|url=http://esctoday.com/170516/italy-sanremo-2019-finalists-unveiled-il-volo-are-back-in-the-competition/|work=Esctoday.com|date=22 December 2018}}</ref> and at the [[Sanremo Music Festival 2021]] with "Momento perfetto".<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://tg24.sky.it/spettacolo/musica/2021/03/02/sanremo-2021-ghemon-momento-perfetto-testo|title=Ghemon a Sanremo 2021 con Momento perfetto: il testo|date=2 March 2021|work=[[Sky TG24]]|access-date=3 March 2021}}</ref>
