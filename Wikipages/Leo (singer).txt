'''Jung Taek-woon''' ({{Korean|정택운}}; born on November 10, 1990),<ref name=Leoprofile /> better known by his stage name '''Leo''' ({{Korean|레오}}), is a South Korean singer, songwriter and musical theatre actor, signed under [[Jellyfish Entertainment]]. Leo debuted as a member of the South Korean [[boy group]] [[VIXX]] in May 2012,<ref name=vixxdebut>{{cite web|title=Upcoming group VIXX releases 3rd video teaser for "Super Hero"|url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2012/05/upcoming-group-vixx-releases-3rd-video-teaser-for-super-hero|work=allkpop|date=20 May 2012}}</ref> and began his acting career in 2014 in the musical ''[[Full House (2004 TV series)#Musical theatre|Full House]]'' as Lee Young-jae.<ref name=fullhouse>{{cite web|last=Hong|first=Grace Danbi|title=B2ST Yang Yo Seop, A Pink Jung Eun Ji, VIXX Leo, Joo, and More to Star in Musical ''Full House''|url=http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/60213/idols-to-star-in-musical|work=enewsWorld|accessdate=2014-03-06|date=5 March 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303220048/http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/60213/idols-to-star-in-musical|archive-date=3 March 2016|url-status=dead}}</ref> In 2015 he began his songwriting career,<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|title = A Bitter Serenade|date = September 2014|work = Ceci Magazine|language = ko}}</ref> and with VIXX member [[Ravi (rapper)|Ravi]] formed the group's first official sub-unit [[VIXX LR]].<ref>{{cite web|title=VIXX's First Subunit LR Composed of Leo and Ravi, Releases Teasers and More|url=http://www.soompi.com/2015/08/13/vixx-announces-leo-and-ravi-subunit-lr-in-new-trailer/|website=soompi.com |accessdate=16 August 2015}}</ref>

==Early life==
Born in [[Yangjae-dong]], [[Seoul]], [[South Korea]], his family consists of himself, his parents and three older sisters. Leo studied music composition at [[Baekseok University]] and was a member of the National Youth Soccer Players from 2004 to 2007. Growing up, Leo was actively involved in swimming, boxing, taekwondo (blue belt), and soccer.<ref>{{Cite web|title = VIXX's Leo revealed to be a sports prodigy|url = http://www.allkpop.com/article/2012/06/vixxs-leo-revealed-to-be-a-sports-prodigy|accessdate = 2015-06-20|website = Allkpop|date = 2012-06-25}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.koreaboo.com/buzz/idols-completely-different-careers-made-debut/|title=Idols That Had Completely Different Careers Before They Made Their Debut — Koreaboo|date=2016-12-31|newspaper=Koreaboo|language=en-US|access-date=2017-01-01}}</ref> While recovering from an injury, he developed an interest in becoming a singer after listening to [[Wheesung]]'s "Walking in the Sky".<ref>{{Cite web|title = [My Name is] 빅스(2) 레오텐아시아 {{!}} 텐아시아|url = http://tenasia.hankyung.com/archives/129718|accessdate = 2015-06-20|date = 2013-05-02|website = 10Asia|language = ko}}</ref>

==Career==

===2012–2014: Career beginnings===
{{Main|VIXX}}
Leo was one of ten trainees who were contestants in [[Mnet (TV channel)|Mnet]]'s survival reality show ''[[MyDOL]].'' Leo was one of the six contestants who were chosen to be a part of the final line-up of the new boy group VIXX.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Yoo|first=Ji-yoon|date=May 15, 2012|title=젤리피쉬 男 아이돌 빅스, 티저 공개 '데뷔 임박'|url=http://biz.heraldcorp.com/view.php?ud=20120515000109|url-status=live|access-date=May 14, 2021|website=Herald Corp|language=ko}}</ref> The group debuted with "[[Super Hero (EP)|Super Hero]]"<ref>{{cite web|title=VIXX to watch the last episode of 'Mydol' with their fans at a movie theater|url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2012/05/vixx-to-watch-the-last-episode-of-mydol-with-their-fans-at-a-movie-theater|work=allkpop|date=31 May 2012}}</ref> on May 24, 2012<ref name=vixxdebut /> on [[M Countdown|M! Countdown]].<ref>{{cite web|title=VIXX to share download links for their music and teasers on Twitter|url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2012/05/vixx-to-share-download-links-for-their-music-and-teasers-on-twitter|work=allkpop|date=20 May 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Finalists from 'Mydol' reveal official group name, Vixx|url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2012/05/finalists-from-mydol-reveal-official-group-name-vixx|work=allkpop|date=10 May 2012}}</ref> During ''MyDOL,'' Leo was featured in [[Brian Joo]]'s "Let This Die" and [[Seo In-guk]]'s "Shake It Up" music videos.<ref name=brianjooletthisdiemv>{{cite video|title=Brian Joo (브라이언) - Let This Die (Extended Eng Ver.)|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9c9Z4vQO_hI|date=31 Jan 2012}}</ref><ref name=seoingukshakeitupmv>{{cite video|title=Seo In Guk (서인국) "SHAKE IT UP" Music Video|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GIaTgs0ypg4|date=8 Aug 2011}}</ref> After his debut with VIXX, Leo appeared in episode 4 of [[Seoul Broadcasting System|SBS]]' television drama ''[[The Heirs]]'' alongside his group members.

As an avid sports fan, Leo has appeared in several athletic variety shows with other idols such as ''Dream Team'' and ''Idol Athletics Championship''.<ref>{{Cite web|title = 빅스 레오 '아육대' 부상현장 포착, 깁스후 목발짚고 절뚝절뚝|url = http://news.nate.com/view/20130904n14837|website = Nate|accessdate = 2015-06-20|date = 2013-09-04|language = ko}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title = Kangin's basketball team defeats Yonghwa's team with Michael Jordan style|url = http://www.allkpop.com/article/2015/02/kangins-basketball-team-defeats-yonghwas-team-with-michael-jordan-style|accessdate = 2015-06-20|date = 2015-02-19|website = Allkpop}}</ref>

In 2014, Leo had a cameo role in SBS' television drama ''[[Glorious Day (TV series)|Glorious Day]]'' alongside Hyuk and was cast in the musical ''[[Full House (2004 TV series)#Musical theatre|Full House]]'' in the lead role of Lee Young-jae from April to June at the Hongik Daehakro Art Center, Grand Theater in Seoul.<ref name=fullhouse /> Later that year, Leo also participated in the ''[[Y.Bird from Jellyfish Island]]'' project created by Jellyfish Entertainment CEO Hwang Se-jun by collaborating with [[Lyn (singer)|Lyn]] on her self-composed single "Blossom Tears" ({{Korean|꽃잎놀이}}); their single ''[[Y.Bird from Jellyfish Island#Y.BIRD from Jellyfish with LYn X Leo|Y.BIRD from Jellyfish with LYn X Leo]]'' was the fourth in the series. In the haunting music video, he played a psychopath who killed the women he loved so that he could keep them.<ref name=":0"/>

===2015–2016: Composing, VIXX LR and musical===
{{Main|VIXX LR}}
In 2015, Leo composed the song "On a Cold Night" ({{Korean|차가운 밤에}}) for VIXX's fifth single album ''[[Boys' Record (single)|Boys' Record]]''. Originally written as a duet between him and [[Ken (singer)|Ken]], a studio version was released with the rest of the members performing for that album.<ref name=":0" />

On August 7, 2015, Jellyfish Entertainment released a video trailer on VIXX's official website after a mysterious countdown with a silhouette of VIXX's last special album ''[[Boys Record (EP)|Boys' Record]]''. As time went by, members of VIXX disappeared until finally only Leo and [[Ravi (rapper)|Ravi]] were left behind, which caused fans to speculate that it meant another comeback for all six members. A video trailer of [[VIXX LR]] was then revealed.<ref>{{cite web|title=VIXX starts a mysterious countdown - perhaps to a comeback?|url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2015/08/vixx-starts-a-mysterious-countdown-perhaps-to-a-comeback|website=allkpop.com|accessdate=2015-08-08}}</ref>

VIXX LR was confirmed by Jellyfish Entertainment to be VIXX's first official sub-unit composed of rapper Ravi and vocalist Leo. Their debut EP, ''[[Beautiful Liar (EP)|Beautiful Liar]]'', was released on August 17, 2015.<ref>{{cite web|title=VIXX′s First Unit LR to Debut on August 17|url=http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/96502/vixxs-first-unit-lr-to-debut-on-august-17|website=mwave.interest.me|accessdate=2015-08-08|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150808213814/http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/96502/vixxs-first-unit-lr-to-debut-on-august-17|archive-date=2015-08-08|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title= VIXX's sub-unit LR unveils teaser photo for their upcoming debut!|url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2015/08/vixxs-sub-unit-lr-unveils-teaser-photo-for-their-upcoming-debut|website= allkpop.com |accessdate=2015-08-08}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title= VIXX's first subunit LR releases tracklist for 'Beautiful Liar' mini album|url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2015/08/vixxs-first-subunit-lr-releases-tracklist-for-beautiful-liar-mini-album|website= allkpop.com |accessdate=2015-08-08}}</ref> On the same day VIXX LR held their first showcase for ''Beautiful Liar'' at Yes24 Muv Hall in [[Seoul]]′s [[Mapo-gu]].<ref>{{cite web|title=VIXX LR Holds Its First Showcase|url=http://www.bntnews.co.uk/app/news.php?nid=24648|website= bntnews.co.uk |accessdate=2015-08-17}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=LR′s Leo and Ravi Reveal Why They Formed VIXX′s First-Ever Unit|url=http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/96820/lrs-leo-and-ravi-reveal-why-they-formed-vixxs-first-ever-unit|website=mwave.interest.me|accessdate=2015-08-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150907133608/http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/96820/lrs-leo-and-ravi-reveal-why-they-formed-vixxs-first-ever-unit|archive-date=2015-09-07|url-status=dead}}</ref>

In 2016 Leo was cast in the musical ''[[Mata Hari (Wildhorn musical)|Mata Hari]]'' in the lead role of Armand from March 25 to June 12 at the Blue Square in Seoul. It was confirmed that he would be shedding his stage name and using his birth name Jung Taek Woon for the role instead, unlike his previous musical role in ''Full House'' where he was credited as Leo.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Kim|first=Ye-na|date=December 16, 2016|title=빅스 레오 아니, 뮤지컬 배우 정택운…'마타하리' 낙점|url=https://news.nate.com/view/20151216n14864?mid=e0101|url-status=live|access-date=May 9, 2021|website=TV Report|language=ko}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=VIXX Leo to star in musical 'Mata Hari'|url=http://kpopherald.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=201512161549235039313_2|website=koreaherald.com|accessdate=19 November 2015}}</ref>

On September 20, 2016, Leo collaborated with actress and DJ [[Park So-hyun]] to celebrate [[SBS Power FM]]’s 20th Anniversary and released the song "That's All" ({{Korean|그뿐야}}) as part of SBS Power FM's 20th Anniversary song project.<ref>{{cite video|title=[SBS]'파워FM 20주년 쏭 프로젝트' 2탄 소현이레오(박소현X빅스 레오), 스페셜 음원 ‘그뿐야’ 티저 |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h0HFmV1zg90|date=Sep 12, 2016}}</ref>

Leo was cast in the musical ''[[The Count of Monte Cristo (musical)|Monte Cristo]]'' in the supporting role of Albert from November 19, 2016 to February 12, 2017 at the Chungmu Arts Center Grand Theater.<ref>{{cite web|title=빅스 레오, 뮤지컬 '몬테크리스토' 캐스팅|url=http://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=109&aid=0003382618|date=Aug 24, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title='몬테크리스토', 류정한-엄기준-신성록-카이-조정은-린아-정택운 등 초특급 캐스팅의 완결판 공개|url=http://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=215&aid=0000489697|date=Aug 24, 2016}}</ref>

===2017–present: Solo debut===
On October 16, 2017, it was announced that Leo had been cast in the musical ''[[Rudolf (musical)|The Last Kiss]]'' as Crown Prince Rudolf.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Hwang|first=Seo-yoon|date=October 16, 2017|title=[단독] 루나, 뮤지컬 '더 라스트 키스' 출연…수호·레오·카이 '연인 호흡'|url=http://tvdaily.asiae.co.kr/read.php3?aid=15081398401286843016|url-status=live|access-date=May 9, 2021|website=TV Daily}}</ref>

On July 21, 2018, it was announced that Leo will be releasing his first solo EP titled ''Canvas'' on July 31, 2018.<ref name="OSEN">{{cite web|url=http://osen.mt.co.kr/article/G1110950655|title=[공식입장] 빅스 레오, 31일 첫 솔로 미니앨범 발매...티저 기습 공개|language=ko|work=OSEN|date=July 21, 2018}}</ref>

From August 31 to September 2, Leo had his first ever solo concert "LEO 1st SOLO CONCERT [CANVAS]" which was held at Blue Square Eye Market Hall<ref>http://www.jelly-fish.co.kr/sub/artist_singer08.php?prof_id=26&num=#!</ref>

On May 14, 2019, Leo announced about his "2nd Solo Concert [MUSE]" which was on June 2019.<ref>http://officiallykmusic.com/vixxs-leo-unveils-coming-soon-poster-second-concert-muse/</ref> Leo's second EP ''Muse'' was released on June 17.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.soompi.com/article/1324204wpp/vixxs-leo-surprises-with-teaser-for-muse|title=Update: VIXX's Leo Sings New Song "The Flower" Featuring Maximilian Hecker In Studio Clip}}</ref>

Leo enlisted for his [[conscription in South Korea|mandatory military service]] on December 2, 2019, serving as a public service worker due to severe panic disorder and depression.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=469&aid=0000439872 |title=빅스 레오, 12월 2일 사회복무요원 입대 "공황장애 치료 중" [전문] |language=ko |date=November 14, 2019 |website=Naver |access-date=November 14, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://koogle.tv/media/news/vixxs-leo-to-enlist-as-public-service-worker-reveals-he-is-suffering-from-panic-disorder-depression/|title=VIXX's Leo to Enlist as Public Service Worker, Reveals He is Suffering from Panic Disorder & Depression|date=November 14, 2019|work=KoogleTV|access-date=November 14, 2019}}</ref>  He was discharged on September 9, 2021.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=311&aid=0001345975 |title=빅스 레오, 오늘(9일) 사회복무요원 소집해제 [공식입장] |language=ko |date=September 9, 2021 |website=Naver |access-date=September 9, 2021}}</ref>
