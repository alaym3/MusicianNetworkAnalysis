'''Eefje de Visser''' ([[Voorburg]], 8 February 1986) is a Dutch singer/songwriter. She won the [[Grote Prijs van Nederland]], a national competition for musicians, in 2009.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.maxazine.nl/muziek/nieuws/603-grote-prijs-van-nederland-voor-eefje-de-visser |title=Grote prijs van Nederland voor Eefje de Visser |last=Wander |first=Wil |date=12 December 2009 |publisher=Maxazine.nl |accessdate=31 August 2011 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120331121434/http://www.maxazine.nl/muziek/nieuws/603-grote-prijs-van-nederland-voor-eefje-de-visser |archivedate=31 March 2012 }}</ref> She released her first album, ''De Koek'', in 2011, a breakthrough year in which she performed (accompanied by a four-man band called Most Unpleasant Men) at three major Dutch festivals:<ref name=pittig/> the [[Oerol Festival]], [[A Campingflight to Lowlands Paradise|Lowlands]],<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.volkskrant.nl/vk/nl/7424/Lowlands/article/detail/2857248/2011/08/20/Eefje-de-Visser-groot-succes-op-Lowlands.dhtml|title=Eefje de Visser groot succes op Lowlands|last=Lier|first=Heleen van|date=20 August 2011|work=[[de Volkskrant]]|accessdate=31 August 2011}}</ref> and the [[Uitmarkt]].<ref name=pittig>{{cite news|url=http://www.volkskrant.nl/vk/nl/3380/muziek/article/detail/2878743/2011/08/31/Eefje-de-Visser-Een-fladderig-pittig-schattig-muziekmeisje.dhtml|title=Eefje de Visser: Een fladderig, pittig, schattig muziekmeisje|last=Lindhout|first=Sterre|date=31 August 2011|work=[[de Volkskrant]]|accessdate=31 August 2011}}</ref>

On December 14, 2015, Eefje de Visser released a new song, "Scheef", on [[YouTube]]. The album ''Nachtlicht'' followed the next year. In 2020 she released her fourth album ''Bitterzoet'', followed by a European tour supporting [[Balthazar (band)|Balthazar]].<ref>https://www.nrc.nl/nieuws/2020/01/29/eefje-de-visser-ik-hou-mijn-teksten-graag-beeldend-en-fluide-a3988578</ref> She currently resides in [[Ghent]].<ref>https://focus.knack.be/entertainment/muziek/wij-hebben-gelukkig-nog-geen-echte-hit-eefje-de-visser-en-luwten-wuiven-de-festivalzomer-uit/article-longread-1189499.html</ref>
