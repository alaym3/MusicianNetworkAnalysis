'''Hayden James''' is an Australian DJ, songwriter and record producer from [[Sydney|Sydney, Australia]]. He is signed to the [[Future Classic]] label.

==Personal life==
{{expand section|date=July 2020}}
Hayden James is married to Sydney artist Jennifer Lia. James became a father in 2020.<ref name="WoG">{{cite web |url=https://www.abc.net.au/triplej/news/musicnews/hayden-james-waves-of-gold-compilation-livestream/12440222 |title=Hayden James talks new compilation Waves of Gold and special streaming event |work=[[Australian Broadcasting Corporation|ABC]] |date=9 July 2020 |access-date=9 July 2020 |author=Fuamoli, Sose}}</ref>

==Career==
===2013-present: ''Between Us''===
{{main|Hayden James (EP)|Between Us (Hayden James album)}}
James released his first single "Permission to Love" in June 2013 with remixes from label mates [[Touch Sensitive (producer)|Touch Sensitive]] and [[Charles Murdoch (record producer)|Charles Murdoch]].

His debut EP, ''[[Hayden James (EP)|Hayden James]]'' was released on 30 August 2013. Since then, James has supported [[Disclosure (band)|Disclosure]] and [[Odesza]] on their US and Australian tours. He has also played at major Australian music festivals [[Splendour in the Grass]] and [[Groovin' the Moo|Groovin The Moo]].

His single "[[Something About You (Hayden James song)|Something About You]]" was released in December 2014 and was certified gold in Australia and has been streamed over 68 million times.

In June 2016, James released "[[Just a Lover]]" which received an [[Australian Recording Industry Association|ARIA]] nomination for Best Dance Release and was followed by a national tour with producer Dena Amy as a support.

In 2016–17, James toured across Europe and North America and co-wrote a number of tracks for [[Katy Perry]]'s ''[[Witness (Katy Perry album)|Witness]]'' album, as well as working on his own debut studio album.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://amnplify.com.au/hayden-james-returns-with-numb-ft-graace/ |title=HAYDEN JAMES Returns With New Single "Numb" Feat. GRAACE| publisher=Anmplify |date= 8 October 2017|access-date=21 January 2018}}</ref> "[[Numb (Hayden James song)|Numb]]" featuring Graace was released in October 2017.

In May 2019, James announced he would release his debut studio album ''[[Between Us (Hayden James album)|Between Us]]'' on 14 June 2019.<ref name="Between Us">{{cite web|url=https://www.abc.net.au/triplej/news/musicnews/first-spin-hayden-james-debut-album-between-us-nowhere-to-go/11074240|title=First Spin: Hayden James gives an insight into his long-awaited debut album with 'Nowhere To Go'|last=Fuamoli|first=Sose|date=2 May 2019|work=Triple J|access-date=5 May 2019}}</ref>
