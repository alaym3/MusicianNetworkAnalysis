'''Branden Ratcliff''', also known as '''Vindata''' is a producer/DJ based in [[Los Angeles]]. The project was formerly a duo with '''Jared Poythress'''. In March 2017, they were featured on ''Rolling Stone''{{'}}s list of 10 New Artists You Need to Know Now.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/lists/10-new-artists-you-need-to-know-march-2017-w471328/vindata-w471329|title=10 New Artists You Need to Know Now|date=March 15, 2017|website=Rolling Stone|access-date=2017-06-03}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.insomniac.com/artist/vindata|title=Vindata|website=Insomniac.com|language=en|access-date=2017-06-03}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.thatdrop.com/vindata-bad-dreams-original-mix/|title=Vindata - Bad Dreams (Original Mix)|date=2014-02-28|work=Thatdrop|access-date=2017-06-03|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.themusicninja.com/electronic-vindata-through-time-and-space-ep-stream/|title=[Electronic] Vindata – Through Time and Space (EP Stream)|last=El Gamal|first=Ash|date=August 15, 2015|website=www.themusicninja.com|language=en-US|access-date=2017-06-03}}</ref> They gained recognition with a single featuring Kenzie May, "All I Really Need."<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://earmilk.com/2014/02/27/vindata-teams-with-vocalist-kenzie-may-for-instant-hit-all-i-really-need/|title=Vindata teams with vocalist Kenzie May for instant hit "All I Really Need" - EARMILK|last=Beer|first=Nathan|date=2014-02-27|work=EARMILK|access-date=2017-06-03|language=en-US}}</ref> Shortly after, [[Skrillex]] signed them to a record deal at [[OWSLA]] where they released Through Time and Space featuring Kenzie May again as well as [[Anderson .Paak]], [[Chuck Ellis]] and Kaleena Zanders and Mack.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/vindata-mn0002804146/biography|title=Vindata Biography & History|last=Simpson|first=Paul|website=AllMusic|access-date=2017-06-03}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/shawn-russell-johnson/video-premiere-vindata-ge_b_9577628.html|title=Video Premiere: Vindata - "Getting Away"|last=Johnson|first=Shawn Russell|date=2016-03-31|website=Huffington Post|language=en-US|access-date=2017-06-03}}</ref>

Their first official artistic collaboration with [[Skrillex]] came in August 2017 with the "uplifting, vibrant",<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://hypebeast.com/2017/8/vindata-skrillex-nstasia-favor|title=Skrillex, Vindata & NSTASIA Collide for New Single "Favor"|work=HYPEBEAST|access-date=2017-08-19|language=en}}</ref> "Favor."
