'''U-Piter''' ({{lang-ru|Ю-Питер|links=no}}) was a [[Russia]]n [[rock music|rock]] band from [[Saint-Petersburg]]. The group was formed by [[Vyacheslav Butusov]] in 2001.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2003-08-22|title=Ю-ПИТЕР - Неземная география|url=https://www.zvuki.ru/R/P/10041/|url-status=live|access-date=2021-04-28|website={{ill|Zvuki.ru|ru|Звуки.ру}}|language=ru}}</ref> The group disbanded in 2017.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2017-03-04|title=Ю-ПИТЕР - Нет жизни на Марсе|url=https://www.zvuki.ru/R/P/76940/|url-status=live|access-date=2021-04-28|website={{ill|Zvuki.ru|ru|Звуки.ру}}|language=ru}}</ref>
