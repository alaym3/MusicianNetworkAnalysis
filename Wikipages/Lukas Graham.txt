Falgren.<ref name="EB01">{{cite news | last=Winther| first=Emil| title =I aften spiller Graham gratis på hjemmebane| newspaper =[[Ekstra Bladet]]| language =da | date =10 June 2012 | url ==Career==
[[File:Lukas Graham Stavernfestivalen (180541).jpg|thumb|Lukas Graham performing at [[Stavernfestivalen]] 2016]]

===2011–2012: Career beginnings===
The band formed in Denmark in 2011 and initially uploaded homemade videos of the songs "[[Drunk in the Morning]]" and "Criminal Mind".<ref name="EB01"/><ref name="Polit02">{{cite news | last=Lund| first=Simon| title =Christianitten i wifebeateren er lidt af en soulsensation| newspaper =[[Politiken]]| language =da | date =23 March 2012 | url =http://politiken.dk/kultur/musik/cd-beat/premium/ECE1580315/christianitten-i-wifebeateren-er-lidt-af-en-soulsensation/| access-date =4 April 2016}}</ref> The songs were also shared on Facebook and accrued several hundred thousand views.<ref name="GAFFA2">{{cite news | last=Pallesen| first=Randi| title =Lukas Graham udgiver album i hele Europa| language =da | work=Gaffa| date =10 July 2012 | url =http://gaffa.dk/nyhed/60369| access-date =4 April 2016}}</ref> The band signed a record deal in late 2011 with the Danish record label Copenhagen Records which is owned by [[Universal Music|Universal Music Denmark]].<ref name="GAFFA"/> In a later interview Lukas Graham has revealed that the band already had a contract with Copenhagen Records and the self-made videos were the label's idea to create an underground buzz.

Prior to officially releasing any music, the band had sold 17,000 tickets for an upcoming tour.<ref name="Polit02" />
 
Their first release, available only in Europe and selected other nations,<ref name="Polit01"/> became certified five-times platinum in Denmark.<ref name="Platinum">{{cite web |url=http://www.ifpi.dk/?q=content/lukas-graham-lukas-graham-cph |title=Lukas Graham 'Lukas Graham' (CPH) |publisher=[[IFPI Denmark]] |access-date=4 April 2016}}</ref> Their first release also featured four charting songs such as "Drunk in the Morning", "[[Better Than Yourself (Criminal Mind Pt 2)]]" and "[[Ordinary Things (song)|Ordinary Things]]".<ref name="DanishSongs">{{cite web |url=https://danishcharts.dk/showinterpret.asp?interpret=Lukas+Graham |title=Lukas Graham |publisher=danishcharts.dk |access-date=3 April 2016}}</ref> Over the course of 2012, Lukas Graham played 107 concerts throughout Europe, selling 40,000 tickets in Denmark alone. They sold 80,000 albums and 150,000 singles while also earning 5&nbsp;million YouTube views and 27&nbsp;million streams. Their tour took them across Europe to countries like England, Netherlands, Norway, Sweden, Germany, Austria, France, and Spain.<ref name="Polit01"/>

==Musical style==
Lukas Graham's music has been described as a [[Pop music|pop]]-[[Soul music|soul]] hybrid.<ref name="Polit01"/> In a review of the band's new album, journalist [[Jon Pareles]] of ''[[The New York Times]]'' described their sound as the place "where pop meets [[Contemporary R&B|R&B]]".<ref name="NYT01"/> Patrick Ryan of ''[[USA Today]]'' wrote that the band's songs "effortlessly blend elements of [[Hip hop music|hip hop]] and [[Folk music|folk]]". Lyrically, the songs often deal with relatable experiences like growing up poor ("Mama Said") or drinking ("Drunk in the Morning").<ref name="USA"/> The band's most popular song, "7 Years", describes growing up and aging at specific points in life (from seven years old to 60).<ref name="Huff">{{cite news | last=Moraski| first=Lauren| title =If You Don't Know Lukas Graham Yet, You Should| work =[[HuffPost]]| date =4 April 2016| url =http://www.huffingtonpost.com/entry/if-you-dont-know-lukas-graham-yet-you-should_us_56feea7de4b0a06d5805c960| access-date =4 April 2016}}</ref>
 
Lukas grew up in a self-governing artistic community in the middle of [[Copenhagen]] called [[Freetown Christiania|Christiania]].<ref name="USA"/> The area is known for its creative atmosphere, relative poverty and crime. His experiences growing up in the community have shaped both the sound of his music and the lyrics he writes.<ref name="Indie"/><ref name="Meyers"/>
