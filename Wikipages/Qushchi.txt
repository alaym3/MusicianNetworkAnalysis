'''Qushchi''' ({{lang-fa|قوشچی}}; Romanized as Qūshchī; also known as '''Ghoshchi''', '''Gūchi''', and '''Kushi''')<ref>{{GEOnet3|-3080533}}</ref> is a city and capital of [[Anzal District]], in [[Urmia County]], [[West Azerbaijan Province]], [[Iran]].  At the 2006 census, its population was 2,832, in 828 families.<ref>{{IranCensus2006|04}}</ref>
