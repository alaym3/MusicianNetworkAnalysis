'''Zack Tabudlo''' (born December 6, 2000) is a Filipino singer-songwriter, musician and producer. He was first seen in [[The Voice Kids (Philippine season 1)|''The Voice Kids (Philippines)'' season 1]] as a member of Team Bamboo.<ref>{{cite web |title=[INTERVIEW] Zack Tabudlo: From The Voice Kids To 16M Views! |url=https://www.hypnoticasia.com/zack-tabudlo-from-the-voice-kids-to-16m-views/ |website=HypnoticAsia |access-date=8 October 2021}}</ref> He later began his musical career in 2018 before he rose to fame in 2020 when he joined [[MCA Music (Philippines)|MCA Music]]/[[Universal Music Group|Universal]] through sublabel [[Island Records Philippines]].<ref>{{cite news |last1=Custodio |first1=Arlo |title=‘The Voice Kids’ alumnus finds success in writing, producing music |url=https://www.manilatimes.net/2020/01/02/lifestyle-entertainment/show-times/the-voice-kids-alumnus-finds-success-in-writing-producing-music/669855 |work=The Manila Times |date=2 January 2020}}</ref> In a [[Rolling Stone]] magazine article, Zack Tabudlo was described as having a soaring soprano voice that brings to mind [[Bruno Mars]] and [[Adam Levine]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Brown|first=Ted|date=2021-07-28|title=Here Are 10 Songs From International Hitmakers You Should Have on Your Radar|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-news/international-music-spotify-radar-1199758/|access-date=2021-10-27|website=Rolling Stone|language=en-US}}</ref>

His debut single "Nangangamba" was released in 2020.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Olivares |first1=Rick |title=How Island Records PH's Zack Tabudlo wrote debut single during math class |url=https://news.abs-cbn.com/entertainment/08/10/20/how-island-records-phs-zack-tabudlo-wrote-debut-single-during-math-class |work=ABS-CBN News |date=10 August 2020}}</ref> His song "Binibini", released in 2021, broke a record as the top local song on [[Spotify]]'s Philippines charts.<ref>{{cite news |title=Young Pinoys on top: Zack Tabudlo’s 'Binibini' breaks record on Spotify |url=https://manilastandard.net/lifestyle/youth/356357/young-pinoys-on-top-zack-tabudlo-s-binibini-breaks-record-on-spotify.html |work=Manila Standard |date=5 June 2021}}</ref><ref name="binibini">{{cite news |title=Zack Tabudlo’s 'Binibini' tops Spotify’s PHL’s Top 50 Chart for six straight weeks |url=https://businessmirror.com.ph/2021/05/28/zack-tabudlos-binibini-tops-spotifys-phls-top-50-chart-for-six-straight-weeks/ |access-date=8 October 2021 |work=BusinessMirror |date=28 May 2021}}</ref> His debut album, ''Episode'', was released on October 15, 2021.<ref name="episode">{{cite news |last1=Peters |first1=Daniel |title=Zack Tabudlo announces debut album ‘Episode’ |url=https://www.nme.com/en_asia/news/music/zack-tabudlo-announces-debut-album-episode-3063588 |work=NME |date=6 October 2021}}</ref>
