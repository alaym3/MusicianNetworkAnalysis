'''Sofien Hakim Manessour''' (born 21 March 1987 in [[Marseille]]), known professionally as '''Soso Maness''', is a French rapper of Algerian descent.<ref>{{Cite news|date=2020-06-17|title=Soso Maness, nouveau héros du rap marseillais|language=fr|work=Le Monde.fr|url=https://www.lemonde.fr/culture/article/2020/06/17/soso-maness-nouveau-heros-du-rap-marseillais_6043123_3246.html|access-date=2021-08-26}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Le franc-parler de Soso Maness fait plaisir à voir|url=https://www.vice.com/fr/article/bj9xz3/le-franc-parler-de-soso-maness-fait-plaisir-a-voir|access-date=2021-08-26|website=www.vice.com|language=fr}}</ref> He has released three albums, ''Rescapé'' in 2019, ''Mistral'' in 2020<ref>{{Cite web|last=|title=Quand Soso Maness était placé en garde à vue pour avoir défendu Jul|url=https://www.booska-p.com/new-quand-soso-maness-etait-place-en-garde-vue-pour-avoir-defendu-jul-n152084.html|url-status=live|access-date=2021-08-26|website=www.booska-p.com|language=en}}</ref> and ''Avec le temps'' in 2021.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Soso Maness révèle le nom et la tracklist de son nouvel album|url=https://www.mouv.fr/musique/rap-fr/soso-maness-revele-le-nom-et-le-tracklist-de-son-nouvel-album-368084|access-date=2021-08-26|website=Mouv|language=fr}}</ref> ''Mistral'' reached number 2 on the French Albums Chart. His song "Petrouchka" featuring rapper [[PLK (rapper)|PLK]] reached number 1 on the French Singles Chart.
