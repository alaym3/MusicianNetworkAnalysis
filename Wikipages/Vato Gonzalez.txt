'''Vato Gonzalez''' (born 6 June 1983 as '''Björn Franken''') is a Dutch record producer, songwriter, artist, DJ, who is signed to [[Spinnin' Records]] and [[DOORN Records]]. Best known for his single [[Badman Riddim (Jump)]] (featuring [[Foreign Beggars]]), which reached No. 7 on the [[UK Singles Chart]] after a release on 19 June 2011.<ref name="theofficialcharts.com">{{cite web|url=https://www.officialcharts.com/charts/singles-chart/20110626/7501/|title=2011-07-02 Top 40 Official Singles Chart UK Archive – Official Charts|access-date=23 September 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://top40.about.com/od/onthecharts/a/Uk-Pop-Music-Charts-June-26-2011.htm|title=UK Pop Music Charts June 26, 2011|author=Bill Lamb|work=About|access-date=23 September 2014}}</ref>

==Biography==

===Early life===
Franken was born in [[Spijkenisse]], Netherlands. As a teenager he developed an interest in graphic design before he started to focus on making music.

===2007–2009: Dirty House===
Between 2007 and 2009 Franken released 5 [[mixtapes]] in the 'Dirty House' series, It was around the time of this release he and this song started to gain mainstream attention.

===2010–present: Badman Riddim===
Badman Riddim, in addition to a vocal version which featured UK hip-hop/grime/dubstep group [[Foreign Beggars]] and was given the new title [[Badman Riddim (Jump)|Badman Riddim]], began to start gaining airplay from some [[BBC Radio 1]] DJs in the UK in late 2010. As attention for the single started to grow Franken was signed to [[Ministry of Sound]] and a music video was made for the song. The single was released in the UK on 19 June 2011 and entered the UK Singles Chart at No. 7,<ref name="theofficialcharts.com"/><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/469770/bon-iver-debuts-at-no-4-on-uk-charts-derulo-has-top-single|title=Bon Iver|date=27 June 2011|work=Billboard|access-date=23 September 2014}}</ref> the first hit in the country for both acts involved.

On 7 January 2013, [[Not a Saint]] was released. It features [[Lethal Bizzle]] and [[Donae'o]]. It is a remix of the same song by Bizzle and Donae'o. The remix featured positive feedback and was released as a collaborative single. It peaked at number 20 in the UK Singles Chart.

Since late 2013, Franken signed to [[DOORN Records]]. He released his first song under the label, "Triplets Riddim" on 30 December 2013. There is a scheduled upcoming single "Bio Riddim" of which the release date will soon be announced.
