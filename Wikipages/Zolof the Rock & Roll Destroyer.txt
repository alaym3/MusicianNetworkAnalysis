'''Zolof the Rock & Roll Destroyer''' (often referred to as simply '''Zolof''') is an American [[pop rock]] band that originated in [[Philadelphia]], Pennsylvania through core members Vince Ratti and [[Rachel Minton]] alongside [[Anthony Green (musician)|Anthony Green]]. Their music, defined by the band members as "[[Wikt:spunk|spunk]] rock", is heavily structured and influenced by [[pop music]] by way of catchy rhythms and bright lyrics.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://thepunksite.com/reviews/zolof-the-rock-and-roll-destroyer-schematics |title=Zolof The Rock And Roll Destroyer |last=Gorman |first=Bobby |publisher=Thepunksite |access-date=2014-04-18}}</ref>

The band competed in 2006's Mountain Dew Circuit Breakout challenge on [[MTV2]], but was beaten by California band [[Halifax (band)|Halifax]].
