'''Kalu''' ({{lang-fa|كلو}}, also [[Romanize]]d as '''Kalū''', '''Kaloo''', and '''Kalow'''; also known as '''Chalow''', '''Chelou''', '''Chelow''', '''Chelū''', '''Chilov''', and '''Chīlowv''')<ref>{{GEOnet3|-3069222|Kalu}}</ref> is a village in [[Dizmar-e Markazi Rural District]], [[Kharvana District]], [[Varzaqan County]], [[East Azerbaijan Province]], [[Iran]]. At the 2006 census, its population was 77, in 24 families.<ref>{{IranCensus2006|03}}</ref>
