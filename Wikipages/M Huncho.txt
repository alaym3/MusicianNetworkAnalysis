'''M Huncho''' (born 17 November 1993) is a British rapper, singer and songwriter from Kilburn, North West [[London]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/m-huncho-mn0003736651/biography|title=M Huncho {{!}} Biography & History|last=Buchanan|first=John D.|date=|website=[[AllMusic]]|access-date=March 19, 2020}}</ref> He performed a Mad About Bars online freestyle session in 2017, and releasing his first [[Extended play|EP]] ''Get Out'' that October, with the [[Single (music)|single]] "Mediocre" and another EP, ''48 Hours'', following in 2018. 2019 debut studio mixtape ''Utopia'' has been streamed over 77 million times globally, and reached number 13 in the [[UK Albums Chart]].<ref name="OCC"/> His second mixtape, ''[[Huncholini the 1st]]'', was released in January 2020 and peaked at number 5 in the UK. M Huncho has also worked with [[D-Block Europe]], [[Nafe Smallz]], [[Yxng Bane]], [[Headie One]], [[Dutchavelli]] and [[Unknown T]].
