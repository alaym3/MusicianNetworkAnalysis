'''Carlo Luigi Coraggio''' (born 23 September 1989), better known as '''Carl Brave''', is an Italian artist and [[record producer]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://24ilmagazine.ilsole24ore.com/2018/12/come-andare-dallo-psicologo-ma-gratis/|work=[[Il Sole 24 Ore]]|language=Italian|access-date=2 May 2020|date=19 December 2018|title=Come dallo psicologo, ma gratis|author=Francesca Milano}}</ref> 

He rose to fame in 2017, as part of the duo Carl Brave x Franco126, with the album ''Polaroid''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.it/recensioni/in-polaroid-di-carl-brave-x-franco-126-ce-tutto-il-friccicorio-di-primavera/|work=[[Rolling Stone (magazine)|Rolling Stone]]|language=Italian|title=Polaroid di Carl Brave x Franco 126 è un friccicorio di primavera}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rockit.it/recensione/38267/carl-brave-franco126-polaroid|publisher=Rockit.it|language=Italian|title=Polaroid - Recensione|author= Enzo Baruffaldi}}</ref> As a solo artist he released two album, ''Notti Brave'' (2018), which topped the Italian Albums Chart, and ''Coraggio'' (2020).<ref>{{cite web|url=https://deerwaves.com/storie/notti-brave-disco-esordio-carl-brave|title=Notti Brave, il disco dell'esordio in nazionale di Carl Brave|publisher=DeerWaves.com|author=Luca Montesi|date=11 May 2018|language=Italian}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=s.r.l|first=Rockol com|title=√ Niente più "polaroid": ora le canzoni di Carl Brave sono piccoli film di vita romana|url=https://www.rockol.it/recensioni-musicali/album/9572/carl-brave-coraggio|access-date=2020-10-18|website=Rockol|language=it}}</ref> As of 2020, the singer has sold over 2 million copies in Italy, thanks to successful singles and collaborations "[[Fotografia (Carl Brave song)|Fotografia]]" with [[Francesca Michielin]] and [[Fabri Fibra]], [[Elisa (Italian singer)|Elisa]]'s single "Vivere tutte le vite" , "Posso" with [[Max Gazzè]] and "Spigoli" with Tha Supreme.<ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=|title=Carl Brave - Biography, Albums and Singles|url=https://www.universalmusic.it/popular-music/artista/carl-brave_32258855864/|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=|website=[[Universal Music Group]]}}</ref>

== Career ==
In 2017 Carl Brave and Franco126 published their collaborative album ''Polaroid'' and embarked on a tour across Italy.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Carl Brave e Franco 126: Istantanee di successo, live a Roma il duo rap del successo "Polaroid"|url=https://www.leggo.it/italia/roma/carl_brave_franco_126_roma_rock_in_roma_capannelle_concerto_musica-3850303.html|access-date=2020-10-18|website=www.leggo.it|language=it}}</ref> At the end of the tour, the duo announced a hiatus to pursue solo projects.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rockol.it/news-697906/carl-brave-franco126-si-separano-dopo-notti-brave-after-tour-solista?refresh_ce|publisher=Rockol.it|language=Italian|access-date=3 May 2020|title=Carl Brave si separa da Franco126 (per ora): In tour come solista. Palasport? Mo' vediamo - Intervista|date=30 November 2018}}</ref> His debut solo album, ''Notti Brave'', was released on 11 May 2018. Its lead single, "[[Fotografia (Carl Brave song)|Fotografia]]", features vocals by singer-songwriter [[Francesca Michielin]] and rapper [[Fabri Fibra]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.deejay.it/articoli/carl-brave-video-fotografia-simpson/|work=[[Radio DeeJay]]|language=Italian|access-date=3 May 2020|title=Carl Brave, Fabri Fibra e Francesca Michielin diventano Simpson per il video Fotografia|date=14 May 2018}}</ref>
In November 2018, Carl Brave released a new single, "Posso", featuring [[Max Gazzè]] and preceding a repack of the album, titled ''Notti Brave After''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sorrisi.com/musica/canzoni/carl-brave-posso-e-il-nuovo-singolo-con-max-gazze/|work=[[TV Sorrisi e Canzoni]]|language=Italian|access-date=3 May 2020|title=Carl Brave: Posso è il nuovo singolo con Max Gazzè|author=Giulia Ciavarelli|date=16 November 2018}}</ref>

In 2019, he appeared as a featured artist on the single "Vivere tutte le vite" by [[Elisa Toffoli|Elisa]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://tg24.sky.it/spettacolo/musica/2019/06/13/elisa-carl-brave-vivere-tutte-le-vite-singolo-video.html|work=TG24|publisher=[[Sky Italia]]|title=Elisa e Carl Brave: fuori il video del singolo Vivere tutte le vite|date=13 June 2019|language=Italian}}</ref> The singles "Che poi" and "Regina Coeli", both preceding his second studio album, were released in January 2020 and in March 2020, respectively.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rockol.it/news-710694/carl-brave-che-poi-nuovo-singolo-ascolta|publisher=Rockol.it|language=Italian|access-date=3 May 2020|title=Carl Brave: il singolo 'Che poi' anticipa il nuovo album. Ascolta|date=22 January 2020}}</ref>The second studio album Coraggio was published on 9 October 2020, with the third single "Parli Parli" with italian singer [[Elodie (singer)|Elodie]].<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-10-13|title=Elodie e Carl Brave insieme nel video di "Parli parli"|url=https://www.corrierenazionale.it/2020/10/13/elodie-e-carl-brave-insieme-nel-video-di-parli-parli/|access-date=2020-10-18|website=Corriere Nazionale|language=it-IT}}</ref>
