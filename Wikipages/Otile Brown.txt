'''Jacob Obunga''' popularly known as '''Otile Brown''' is a [[Kenyan]] independent [[R&B]] singer, songwriter, guitarist and actor.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.pulselive.co.ke/entertainment/otile-brown-advises-fan-following-remarks-on-dating-an-artiste/rgsrt70|title=Otile Brown advises fan following remarks on dating an artiste|website=Pulselive.co.ke|accessdate=29 October 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sde.co.ke/article/2001338397/otile-brown-parts-ways-with-ethiopian-girlfriend|title=Otile Brown parts ways with Ethiopian girlfriend?|website=Sde.co.ke|accessdate=29 October 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.pulselive.co.ke/entertainment/otile-brown-apologizes-to-ex-girlfriend-nabayet-nabbi-in-new-song-named-after-her/734dfl7|title=Otile Brown apologizes to ex-girlfriend Nabayet in new song [Video]|website=Pulselive.co.ke|accessdate=29 October 2019}}</ref> He gained media attention after the release of  his hit single "Imaginary Love" which features Khaligraph Jones.He released his debut studio album, Best of Otile Brown, in April 2017.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.hungama.com/album/best-of-otile-brown/26036007/|title=Best of Otile Brown|website=hungama.com|accessdate=23 May 2019}}</ref> The album was supported by the singles "Basi", "Alivyonipenda", "Shujaa Wako", "DeJavu" and "Aiyolela". Brown had also collaborated with artists such as [[Sanaipei Tande]], and Barakah The Prince, King Kaka among others.

==Early life==
Otile was born in [[Kisumu]] and raised in [[Miritini]], [[Mombasa]]. He is the last born in a family of four- three brothers and one sister.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://kenyanmusik.co.ke/blog/pakate-otile-brown-mombasa-star-dreamland-signee-continues-shine/|title=Pakate by Otile Brown - Mombasa star and Dreamland signee continues to shine.|date=13 August 2016|website=Kenyanmusic.co.ke|accessdate=22 April 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://hivisasa.com/posts/the-musical-journey-of-otile-brown|title=The musical journey of Otile Brown|website=Hivisasa.com|accessdate=22 April 2019}}</ref> Otile started singing and writing songs at age 13.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.tuko.co.ke/274283-who-otile-brown-9-interesting-facts.html#274283|title=Who is Otile Brown? 9 interesting|website=Kuko.co.ke|accessdate=17 February 2019}}</ref>

==Music career==
===2017:''Best of Otile Brown''===
Otile's debut album Best of Otile Brown was released in April 2017. The lead single "Imaginary Love" featuring Kenyan rapper, Khaligraph Jones was released on June 3, 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.varcity.co.ke/exclusive-new-release-from-otile-brown-featuring-khaligraph-jones-imaginary-love/|title=#EXCLUSIVE New Release From Otile Brown Featuring Khaligraph Jones 'Imaginary Love.' - VarCity|first=Terry|last=Muchemi|date=18 March 2015|accessdate=22 April 2019|website=Varcity.co.ke}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.capitalfm.co.ke/tv/video/qwv7fyz|title=Otile Brown – Imaginary Love Feat. Khaligraph Jones 2015 New Kenyan Music|website=Capitalfm.co.ke|accessdate=17 February 2019}}</ref>  The song was played on several radio stations in Kenya. He parted ways with Dreamland Music label in March 2017 and became an Independent artist.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://howkenya.com/popular-kenyan-singer-otile-brown-cancelled-music-contract-record-label/|title=Popular Kenyan Singer, Otile Brown Cancelled His Music Contract With His Record Label|date=10 March 2017|accessdate=23 May 2019|website=howkenya.com}}</ref>

Since his breakthrough into the Kenyan music industry, Otile has garnered several awards including "Male Video of the Year" at Pulse Music Awards 2018 for his single "Baby Love".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.kenyanvibe.com/pulse-music-video-awards-are-back-check-out-the-nominees/|title=Pulse Music Video Awards Are Back! Check Out The Nominees|website=Kenyanvibe.com|accessdate=2019-02-23}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Otile is known for his affairs with [[Vera Sidika]], a Kenyan Socialite. Their relationship ended in August 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://nairobinews.nation.co.ke/chillax/vera-sidikas-love-story-ends-tears-break-otile-brown|title=Vera Sidika’s love story ends in tearful break up with Otile Brown|website=Nairobinews.nation.co.ke|accessdate=2019-02-21}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nation.co.ke/lifestyle/showbiz/Celeb-Buzz-Vera-Sidika-and-Otile-Brown/1950810-4833686-dw3owv/index.html|title=CELEB BUZZ: Why Vera Sidika and Otile Brown’s break-up is bad for their careers|accessdate=2019-02-23}}</ref>
