'''Itzy''' (stylized in all caps; {{korean|hangul=있지|hanja=|rr=Itji|mr=Itchi}}) is a South Korean [[girl group]] formed by [[JYP Entertainment]], consisting of members Yeji, Lia, Ryujin, Chaeryeong, and Yuna. The group debuted on February 12, 2019, with the release of their [[single album]], ''[[It'z Different]]''. The group's accolades include Rookie of the Year at the 34th [[Golden Disc Awards]], New Artist of the Year at the 9th [[Gaon Chart Music Awards]] and 2019 [[Melon Music Awards]], Best New Female Artist at the 2019 [[Mnet Asian Music Awards]], and the New Artist Award at the 29th [[Seoul Music Awards]]; making them the first and only K-pop girl group to achieve a "Rookie Grand Slam".<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1" />

== Career ==
=== 2015–2019: Pre-debut activities ===
After failing to pass the auditions held by [[Fantagio]], Chaeryeong participated in [[JYP Entertainment]]'s survival show ''[[Sixteen (TV series)|Sixteen]]'' in 2015.<ref>{{cite web|date=January 21, 2019|title=채령, 채연과 'K팝스타3'서 박진영 평가 눈길 "이 집안 어떡하냐"|url=http://www.nextdaily.co.kr/news/article.html?id=20190121800036|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190216153620/http://www.nextdaily.co.kr/news/article.html?id=20190121800036|archive-date=February 16, 2019|access-date=February 16, 2019|website=Next Daily|language=ko}}</ref> She did not make the final lineup of the winning girl group, [[Twice]],<ref>{{cite news|last=Jang|first=Hye-won|date=February 14, 2019|title=걸그룹 친자매 채연·채령…아이보리톤 대리석으로 꾸며진 궁궐 같은 자택 '화제' 본문듣|language=ko|website=World Daily|url=http://www.segye.com/content/html/2019/02/14/20190214004140.html?OutUrl=naver|access-date=February 16, 2019}}</ref> but was cast by the company through the ''[[K-pop Star 3]]'' television series, along with her sister. The first member of Itzy to join JYP, Chaeryeong trained for five years. Ryujin was scouted at a [[Got7]] concert and trained with JYP for four years prior to Itzy's debut. Both Yuna and Yeji joined JYP in 2015 and trained for three years, Yuna after being discovered by company staff and Yeji having successfully auditioned. The final member Lia initially auditioned for [[SM Entertainment]] and passed but was forced to back out at the last minute due to a disagreement with her parents. Several years later, Lia passed the JYP auditions and trained for two years.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://stylecaster.com/feature/how-itzy-members-discovered-1145110|title=Itzy Scouting Stories|website=Style Caster|access-date=May 1, 2021}}</ref>

In 2017, Yuna and Ryujin appeared in [[BTS]]'s "Love Yourself" Highlight Reel.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.kpopmap.com/why-itzy-ryujin-yuna-are-making-bts-fans-fume-in-jealousy|title=Ryujin and Yuna BTS Highlight Reel|website=Kpop Map|access-date=May 1, 2021}}</ref> The same year, the members (with the exception of Lia) appeared on the [[Mnet (TV channel)|Mnet]] reality show ''[[Stray Kids (TV series)|Stray Kids]]'' as a project group alongside the boy group that would eventually be named [[Stray Kids]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://biz.heraldcorp.com/view.php?ud=201902060812233995203_1|title="있지는 달라달라" Itzy, 데뷔곡 '달라달라' 트랙 리스트 공개|website=Herald Pop|access-date=February 16, 2019|language=ko|date=February 6, 2019}}</ref>

In 2017, Ryujin competed on [[JTBC]]'s survival show ''[[Mix Nine]]''. She placed first among the female contestants but lost to the male contestants overall.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://osen.mt.co.kr/article/G1110822703|title=종영 '믹스나인' 소년팀, 최종 우승 '데뷔'‥男 1등 우진영|website=OSEN|access-date=February 16, 2019|language=ko|date=January 27, 2018|archive-date=September 30, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200930063709/http://osen.mt.co.kr/article/G1110822703|url-status=live}}</ref> Meanwhile, Yeji participated in [[Seoul Broadcasting System|SBS]]' ''The Fan'' but was eliminated in the fifth episode.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.newsen.com/news_view.php?uid=201902122128459410|title=있지 예지 '앉아서도 카리스마 폭발'|last=Pyo|first=Myung-joong|website=Newsen|access-date=February 16, 2019|language=ko|date=February 13, 2019}}</ref>

On February 12, the group released their debut single album, ''[[It'z Different]]'', led by the single "[[Dalla Dalla]]". === 2020: US showcase tour and continued commercial success ===
Itzy began the year with the US leg of their showcase tour.
