'''Raised Fist''' is a Swedish [[hardcore punk]] band formed in 1993 in [[Luleå]]. It currently consists of guitarists Jimmy Tikkanen and Daniel Holmgren, bassist Andreas Johansson, vocalist [[Alexander Hagman]] and drummer Robert Wiiand.

==History==
Raised Fist was formed in 1993 in the Swedish city [[Luleå]], with Alexander Hagman as the lead singer.<ref>[http://rollingstoneaus.com/reviews/post/raised-fist-from-the-north/1050 "Raised Fist From The North Epitaph"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151117002119/http://rollingstoneaus.com/reviews/post/raised-fist-from-the-north/1050 |date=17 November 2015 }}. ''Rolling Stone'', by Simon Jones | 14 January 2015.</ref> The name "Raised Fist" refers to the [[Rage Against the Machine]] song "Know Your Enemy", which include the lyrics: "Born with an insight and a raised fist..."

The band played at the  [[Scandinavia]]n [[Roskilde Festival]] in 2004. At one time they were signed to [[Burning Heart Records]], which houses a number of Swedish [[punk rock]] and [[hardcore punk]] bands.

In 2010 Hagman received a serious electrical shock during a concert in Sundsvall when he touched a power cable which had been punctured by a riot fence. He collapsed on the stage.<ref>[http://www.expressen.se/noje/musik/rockstjarnan-kollapsade-av-elchock---pa-scenen/ "'Rockstjärnan kollapsade av elchock – på scenen"]. ''Expressen'', 6 September 2010. Article in Expressen about Alexander Hagman collapsing of an electric shock</ref>

The band's album ''[[Veil of Ignorance (album)|Veil of Ignorance]]'', released in 2009, placed on the Swedish charts<ref>[http://www.sverigetopplistan.se/netdata/ghl002.mbr/lista?liid=54&dfom=20090909&newi=0&mode=list&navi=no&platform=Win32&browser=MSIE/ Raised Fists "Veil Of Ignorance" enters at nr: 22 on the Swedish albumchart]</ref> and was nominated for a Swedish Grammy Award.<ref>[http://www.expressen.se/noje/musik/de-ar-nominerade-till-grammis-2010/ Raised Fist nominated for a Swedish Grammy award]</ref>  Many of the songs on this album were written by Hagman.

In 2013 Raised Fist signed a two-album contract with [[Epitaph Records]]. In 2015, after working on the songs for five years, Raised Fist released an album ''[[From the North (Raised Fist album)|From the North]]''.<ref>[http://www.soundspheremag.com/reviews/album-review-raised-fist-from-the-north/ "Album Review: Raised Fist – ‘From The North’"]. ''SoundSphere'' By Danielle Barge– 19 January 2015</ref>

==Appearances==
Vocalist Alexander appeared in an interview on Swedish television [[TV4 (Sweden)|TV4]] where he criticizes the EU [[Directive on the enforcement of intellectual property rights]] in EU countries.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pVnBBTtwItc |title=Raised Fist Alexander Hagman om IPRED-lagen |publisher=YouTube |date=14 April 2009 |accessdate=2 March 2015}}</ref>
