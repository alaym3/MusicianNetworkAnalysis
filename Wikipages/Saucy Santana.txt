==Early life and career==
Spain was born to Adrianne Spain in 1993.<ref name=":0" /> He started his career in the music industry through working as a makeup artist for [[City Girls]].<ref name=":7">{{Cite web|title=Rapper Saucy Santana Sits Down With ESSENCE For A Fun Q&A|url=https://www.essence.com/fashion/rapper-saucy-santana-sits-down-with-essence/|access-date=2021-06-27|website=[[Essence (magazine)|Essence]]}}</ref> He had his breakthrough with his debut single "Walk Em Like A Dog" in 2019, which he released through [[SoundCloud]].<ref name=":0" /> Spain released an [[Extended play|EP]] ''Dog Walkers'' in September 2019. In January 2020 he released mixtape ''Imma Celebrity''.<ref name=":5">{{Cite web|date=2020-01-15|title=There's a New 'Material Girl' in Town|url=https://www.papermag.com/saucy-santana-material-girl-premiere-2644822687.html?rebelltitem=14#rebelltitem14?rebelltitem=14|access-date=2021-06-27|website=[[Paper (magazine)|Paper]]}}</ref> On the mixtape's closing track, "You Can't Kill Me", Spain responds to an incident in December 2019 when he was wounded in a drive-by shooting in Miami.<ref name=":5" />

In July 2020 Spain released ''Pretty Little Gangsta'', featuring the single "Up & Down" with guest rapper [[Latto]], who used the stage name Mulatto at the time.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-08-21|title=Mulatto Goes From Teen TV Star To Rap Royalty With 'Queen Of Da Souf'|url=https://uproxx.com/music/mulatto-queen-of-da-souf-interview/|access-date=2021-06-27|website=[[Uproxx]]}}</ref> Spains' 2020 single "Walk" went viral in 2021 after being sampled in the popular [[TikTok]] #WalkChallenge.<ref>{{Cite web|title=The Best TikTok Trends and Challenges Right Now|url=https://www.complex.com/life/best-tiktok-trends-challenges-right-now/|access-date=2021-06-27|website=[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]]}}</ref>

He has since collaborated with artists including [[Sukihana]],<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-03-10|title=Sukihana And Saucy Santana’s ’Food Stamp H*e’ Video Is A Risque Effort|url=https://uproxx.com/music/sukihana-saucy-santana-food-stamp-hoe/|access-date=2021-06-27|website=[[Uproxx]]}}</ref> LightSkinKeisha,<ref name=":6" /> and [[Ivorian Doll]].

==Personal life==
Spain was shot in a [[drive-by shooting]] in Miami in December 2019, along with two others.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|last=Dwork|first=Christina Vazquez, David|date=2019-12-11|title=Celebrity rapper Saucy Santana shot in arm after leaving Miami-Dade strip club|url=https://www.local10.com/news/local/2019/12/11/celebrity-rapper-saucy-santana-shot-in-arm-after-leaving-miami-dade-strip-club/|access-date=2021-06-27|website=[[WPLG]]}}</ref> Spain, who is openly gay and [[Gender variance|gender non-conforming]], stated that he believes the shooting was motivated by [[homophobia]].<ref name=":6">{{Cite web|date=2020-08-13|title=Saucy Santana's 'Back It Up' Video With LightSkinKeisha Gets Messy|url=https://uproxx.com/music/saucy-santana-back-it-up-video-lightskinkeisha/|access-date=2021-06-27|website=[[Uproxx]]}}</ref> Spain was hospitalized with his injuries.<ref name=":4">{{Cite web|title=Rapper Saucy Santana Among 3 Hurt in Shooting on Miami Highway|url=https://www.nbcconnecticut.com/news/national-international/saucy-santana-shooting-miami-highway/2193473/|access-date=2021-06-27|website=[[NBC Connecticut]]}}</ref> He said of the shooting, "I got shot in the top of my shoulder, you was aiming at my face or at my head, that's an instant kill."<ref name=":4" /> He discussed the incident on ''[[Angela Yee]]'s Lip Service''.<ref>{{Citation|title=Angela Yee's Lip Service Feat. Saucy Santana|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KNyb9ho-oOM|via=YouTube|access-date=2021-06-27}}</ref> Spain came out as gay at age 17.<ref name=":2" /> He is based in [[Tallahassee, Florida]].
