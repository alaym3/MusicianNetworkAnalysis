'''Anders Lee Ly''' (born July 14, 1995), known mononymously as '''Anders''' (stylized as '''anders'''), is a Canadian singer, rapper and songwriter. He is best known for his second EP ''Twos'' (2017) and for his collaboration with Canadian DJ duo [[Loud Luxury]] titled "[[Love No More]]". Anders initially gained recognition for his single "Diamonds". Anders then released a single produced by fellow Canadian record producer [[FrancisGotHeat]] and featuring American rapper [[Rich the Kid]] titled "Sticky Situation" in 2019. Anders released his fourth EP, ''Honest'' on July 29, 2021.<ref name="Complex">{{Cite web|title=anders Gets Honest About Fatherhood, Asian Identity, and Racism on Social Media|url=https://www.complex.com/music/anders-interview-honest-ep|access-date=August 5, 2021|website=Complex|language=en}}</ref>

==Early life==
Anders Lee Ly was born on July 14, 1995 in [[Mississauga]], [[Ontario]].<ref name="Complex"/> He attended [[Rick Hansen Secondary School (Mississauga)|Rick Hansen Secondary School]].

==Career==
Anders released his first song "Choosy" on October 6, 2016, produced by fellow Canadian record producer S.L.M.N. His debut EP ''669'' was released on May 25, 2017, featuring the singles "You for You" and "Diamonds".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.vice.com/en/article/j5e3wd/anders-669-ep-combines-toronto-randbs-past-and-present/|title = Anders' '669' EP Combines Toronto R&B's Past and Present}}</ref> Anders released his second EP ''Twos'', an eight-track project on March 6, 2018, it featured the hit single "Bad Guy."<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hypefresh.co/anders-drops-new-single-bad-guy/|title=Rising Toronto Singer - Songwriter Anders Drops "Bad Guy" from His Forthcoming EP 'Twos'|date=March 4, 2018}}</ref><ref name="GNC">{{cite web|title=8 emerging Canadian artists to look for in 2018|url=https://globalnews.ca/news/4191495/8-emerging-canadian-artists-2018/|author=Scott, Katie|date=May 8, 2018|work=[[Global News]]|access-date=December 16, 2018}}</ref> For his EP "Twos", Anders collaborated with Canadian record producers Luca and [[FrancisGotHeat]], as well as S.L.M.N., Jordon Manswell and Bizness Boi.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.hypefresh.co/toronto-anders-releases-new-ep-twos/ |title=Toronto Native ANDERS Displays His Melodic Genius & Affinity For Songwriting With New EP 'Twos'- |date=March 7, 2018 |language=en-US |access-date=December 16, 2018}}</ref>

Following his EP "Twos" Anders released the single "Bad Habits" on March 29, 2019,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ntrsly.com/anders-bad-habits/|title = Anders Confesses His "Bad Habits" in New Single|date = March 29, 2019}}</ref> the single "Bossy" on July 29, 2019,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://hiphopcanada.com/anders-nst-bossy-single/|title = Mississauga's anders returns with his latest single "Bossy"|date = July 29, 2019}}</ref> and the single "My Side of the Bed" on October 7, 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ntrsly.com/anders-my-side-the-bed/|title=Anders is Looking for a Sign on "My Side of the Bed"|date=October 8, 2019}}</ref> Anders also dropped the song "Nauseous" exclusively on [[SoundCloud]] on October 2, 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.canadiandope.com/anders-released-new-single-nauseous/|title = Anders Releases New Single "Nauseous"|date = October 3, 2019}}</ref> Anders then collaborated with American rapper [[Rich The Kid]] for the single "Sticky Situation" released on October 17, 2019, and produced by FrancisGotHeat. <ref name="thefader.com">{{Cite web|url=https://www.thefader.com/2019/10/20/anders-rich-the-kid-sticky-situation/|title = Anders connects with Rich the Kid for the FrancisGotHeat-produced "Sticky Situation"}}</ref>

On December 11, 2020, Anders released the single "Don't Play", which was produced by fellow Canadian record producers [[WondaGurl]], Jenius and London Cyr.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/anders-unveils-new-single-dont-play-new-song.1989651.html/|title=Anders Unveils New Single "Don't Play"}}</ref> Then on January 21, 2021, anders released his third EP "Chaos"; a six-track project, including previously released singles "On Me", and "Bad Habits".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/anders-returns-with-chaos-new-mixtape.121160.html/|title = Anders Returns with "Chaos"}}</ref> On March 26, 2021, Anders released "What I Like", featuring Canadian singer and rapper FRVRFRIDAY and [[6ixBuzz]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.complex.com/music/anders-frvrfriday-what-i-like/|title=Anders and FRVRFRIDAY Link up for New Track "What I Like"}}</ref> On July 29, 2021, Anders released his second EP ''Honest'' a nine-track project.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/anders-gets-honest-on-electrifying-new-ep-new-mixtape.121799.html|title = Anders Gets "Honest" on Electrifying New EP}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://sidedoormag.com/blog/2021/7/31/with-his-new-ep-honest-anders-makes-a-comeback-to-the-rampbpop-scene|title = With His New EP "Honest," Anders Makes a Comeback to the R&B/Pop Scene}}</ref> anders once again collaborated with record producers Luca and [[FrancisGotHeat]]; as well as S.L.M.N., Jenius, Mark Raggio, Rhys, and Emerson Brooks.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.complex.com/music/anders-interview-honest-ep|title = Anders Gets Honest About Fatherhood, Asian Identity, and Racism on Social Media}}</ref>

Anders has had strategic partnerships with brands in Canada such as [[Nike, Inc.|Nike]], [[Remy Martin]], [[BMW]], [[Adidas]], [[McDonald's]] and Get Fresh Company.<ref>https://www.paradigmagency.com/music/anders/</ref>

==Concerts==
===2018===
Anders held his first-ever concert in his hometown of [[Toronto]] at the [[Phoenix Concert Theatre]] on May 22, 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.melodicmag.com/2019/06/22/live-photos-review-nxne-presents-anders-toronto-on/|title = LIVE PHOTOS + REVIEW: NXNE presents anders // Toronto, ON|date = June 22, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.youngcalifornia.com/2018/12/slidin-thru-anders/|title = Slidin' Thru: Anders : Young California}}</ref> The concert was originally to be held at the [[Mod Club Theatre]] but was moved after tickets quickly sold out due to demand.<ref name="JUNO2019">{{cite web |title=2019 R&B/SOUL RECORDING OF THE YEAR {{!}} Anders |url=https://junoawards.ca/nomination/2019-rbsoul-recording-of-the-year-anders/ |website=The JUNO Awards}}</ref> Anders has had many other performances in Toronto in 2018. On November 30, 2018, Anders appeared as a surprise guest at [[88rising]]'s joint "88 Degrees and Rising" tour at Toronto venue RBC Echo Beach.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thecomeupshow.com/2018/10/03/88rising-rbc-echo-beach-on-the-88degrees-and-rising-tour-09-30-2018/|title = 88Rising @ RBC Echo Beach on the 88Degrees and Rising tour. 09/30/2018|date = October 3, 2018}}</ref> He also performed "[[Love No More]]" with [[Loud Luxury]] on December 2, 2018, at the 2018 [[iHeartRadio]] Jingle Ball in Toronto. Anders ended the year headlining the 2019 New Year's Eve (NYE) event at Celebration Square in [[Mississauga]], [[Ontario]] on December 31, 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://culture.mississauga.ca/event/celebration-square/nye-2019/|title = Nye 2019|date = November 28, 2018}}</ref>

===2019===
Anders performed at the [[Juno Award]] side stage 2019 in [[London, Ontario|London]], [[Ontario]], alongside Canadian artists [[Killy (rapper)|Killy]] and [[88Glam]] on March 16, 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://canadianbeats.ca/2019/03/13/london-calling-introducing-juno-side-stage/|title = London Calling - Introducing JUNO Side Stage &#124; Canadian Beats Media}}</ref> Anders was then featured on [[Ones to Watch]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.onestowatch.com/artist/anders/|title=I'm watching anders at Ones to Watch!}}</ref> and performed at the Northside Sessions inside The Lounge at [[Live Nation]] in Toronto, ON on May 8, 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thecomeupshow.com/2019/05/09/anders-live-at-the-live-nation-lounge-for-ones-to-watch-at-cmw-may-7th-2019/|title=Anders Live at the Live Nation Lounge for Ones to Watch at CMW - May 8th, 2019|date=May 9, 2019}}</ref>

Anders performed at the [[NXNE]] music and gaming festival in Toronto on June 16, 2019.<ref>https://nowtoronto.com/music/concert-reviews/nxne-2019-festival-village/</ref> On July 6, 2019, Anders performed at "FVDED In The Park" in [[Surrey, British Columbia|Surrey]], [[British Columbia]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://dailyhive.com/vancouver/fvded-in-the-park-single-day-2019/|title=Single-day lineups announced for FVDED in the Park 2019 &#124; Listed}}</ref> and on August 4, 2019, Anders performed at the [[Osheaga Festival]] in [[Montreal]], [[Quebec]].<ref>http://theconcordian.com/2019/09/anders-platinum-plaque/</ref>

On November 20, 2019, Anders performed a free concert for the displaced students and staff of York Memorial Collegiate Institute at Scarlett Heights Entrepreneurial Academy.<ref>https://nowtoronto.com/music/anders-york-memorial</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://schoolweb.tdsb.on.ca/yorkmemorial/Home/ctl/Detail/mid/180890/ItemID/2338|title = York Memorial Collegiate Institute}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.andersnst.com/blog/2019/9/23/first-annual-nst-bbq-x-cold-tea-nstforyorkmemo|title=Andersnst BLOG FIRST ANNUAL NST BBQ: COLD TEA x #NSTFORYORKMEMO}}</ref>

==Music videos==
Anders has released ten music videos over the years collaborating with many top Canadian music video directors. On October 11, 2017, Anders released the music video to his song "Diamonds."<ref>https://www.vice.com/en/article/qvjqmm/anders-steps-out-of-the-shadows-in-diamonds-video/</ref> The video was directed by Elliot Clancy-Osberg. After the music video of "Diamonds," Anders released the "You For You" music video directed by [[Karena Evans]] on November 28, 2017.<ref>https://www.vice.com/en/article/zmymd5/anders-hangs-out-around-pleasing-architecture-in-you-for-you-video/</ref> His most viewed video to date is "[[Love No More]]", which features DJ duo [[Loud Luxury]], released by [[Armada Music]] on September 4, 2018. The video for "Love No More" has over a millions of views. Anders and Elliot Clancy-Osberg collaborated again for the music video for the single "Sticky Situation" ft [[Rich the Kid]].<ref name="thefader.com"/> Anders released the music video "My Side of the Bed" shot entirely on a [[Samsung Galaxy Note 10|Samsung Galaxy Note 10+]] on October 21, 2019.<ref>https://www.andersnst.com/blog/2019/10/20/my-side-of-the-bed-bts</ref>
