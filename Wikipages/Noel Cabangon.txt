'''Noel G. Cabangon''' (born December 25, 1963) is a Filipino [[Folk music|folk]] singer and composer, known for composing socially-relevant songs. In 1987, he formed the group ''Buklod'' together with Rene Boncocan and Rom Dongeto.

==Early life==
Noel G. Cabangon was born on December 25, 1963, in the town of [[Rosario, La Union]]. At the age of ten, he began his musical career and learned to play the guitar that he borrowed from a neighbor. He made a name for himself in the local music scene in 1982 beginning in little-known folk houses and bars.<ref>{{cite web |title=Noel Cabangon biography |url=https://www.last.fm/music/Noel+Cabangon/+wiki |website=Last.fm |access-date=16 October 2019 |language=en}}</ref>

==Music career==
In 1987, he formed the group ''Buklod'' together with Rene Boncocan and Rom Dongeto. They produced three albums: ''Bukid at Buhay, [[Tatsulok (song)|Tatsulok]],'' and ''Sa Kandungan ng Kalikasan''.<ref>{{cite web |title=Noel Cabangon |url=https://www.goodreads.com/author/show/6995913.Noel_Cabangon |website=www.goodreads.com |access-date=16 October 2019}}</ref> The band wrote and performed songs about the environment, human rights, and politics.

After the group disbanded, Noel Cabangon went on to record six solo albums; perform solo at music bars and private events;<ref>{{cite news |title=Noel Cabangon and company: Older, more mature, but still wielding the power of music |url=https://lifestyle.inquirer.net/325353/noel-cabangon-and-company-older-more-mature-but-still-wielding-the-power-of-music/ |access-date=16 October 2019 |work=Inquirer Lifestyle |date=23 February 2019}}</ref> compose songs for the movies and for other artists; and enter the local theater industry as a musical director, as a composer or sometimes as an actor.

He wrote the book ''Ang Bayan Ko at Lupa: Awit ng Diwa'' in 2005,<ref>{{cite book |last1=Cabangon |first1=Noel G. |title=Ang bayan ko at lupa: awit ng diwa |date=2005 |publisher=Megabooks Co. |isbn=9789718380208 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=ugUJAQAAMAAJ&q=who+is+noel+cabangon |access-date=16 October 2019 |language=tl}}</ref> and co-authored ''Ako'y Isang Mabuting Pilipino (I Am A Good Filipino)'' in 2012.<ref>{{cite web |title=Ako'y Isang Mabuting Pilipino (I Am A Good Filipino) |url=https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/17612145-ako-y-isang-mabuting-pilipino |website=www.goodreads.com |access-date=16 October 2019 |language=en}}</ref>

Cabangon played Jesus Christ in a local production of ''[[Jesus Christ Superstar]]'' in 2000.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Jesus Christ, Superstar|url=https://www.philstar.com/entertainment/2000/05/20/91725/jesus-christ-superstar|access-date=2021-11-12|website=Philstar.com}}</ref>
