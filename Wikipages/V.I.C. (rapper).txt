'''Victor Grimmy Owusu''' (born July 20, 1987), better known by his stage name '''V.I.C.''', is an [[United States of America|American]] [[rapper]]. His single, "[[Get Silly]]", reached #29 on the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]] chart in 2008.

==Early life==
V.I.C. was born Victor Grimmy Owusu in 1987 in [[Corona, Queens]] to a mother from [[New York City]] and a father from [[Ghanaian American|Ghana]].<ref name="2008 bio">{{cite web |url=http://www.vicgetsilly.com/v-i-c |title=Info |author=<!--Not stated--> |website=V.I.C. - GET SILLY |publisher=Warner Bros. Records |access-date=2008-06-18 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080613143518/http://www.vicgetsilly.com:80/v-i-c |archive-date=13 June 2008}}</ref>  At ten he wrote his first song, a drug awareness rap for his [[elementary school]].<ref name="2008 bio"/> Later that year he moved with his family to the [[Atlanta, Georgia|Atlanta]] suburb of [[College Park, Georgia]].<ref name="2008 bio"/> After graduating from [[high school]], he worked as a freelance [[barber]] while pursuing a music career.<ref name="2008 bio"/>

==Music career==
His first album ''[[Beast (V.I.C. album)|Beast]]'' (2008) charted at number 73 on the Billboard 200.<ref name="LP200"/> He recorded a couple of songs<ref name="g5">{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hxSD-ZBQJn0|title=V.I.C "G5" ft. Rich Kids 2009 (Prod. by Mr Hanky)|last=AMRHANKYBEAT|date=10 May 2009|via=YouTube}}</ref><ref name="haveitall">{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=prNhf3qWyjY|title=V.I.C.-Have It All(feat Bobby Valentino)|last=VICmusic100|date=5 June 2010|via=YouTube}}</ref> for a second album ''Revenge of the Beast'' that was never completed.

He released a single called "Twerk It" in 2012<ref name="USR&B"/> and another called "Snapchat (Pose For The Camera)" in 2015.<ref name="snapchat"/>
