'''Enemy Inside''' is a [[heavy metal music|heavy metal]] band founded in [[Aschaffenburg, Germany]] in 2017.

== Background ==
The band was formed by singer Nastassja Giulia and guitar player [[Evan K]] ([[Mystic Prophecy]], [[Exit Eden]], ex-[[Cypecore]]) who have found their own sound, somewhere between [[gothic rock|dark rock]] and modern metal, through a wide range of musical experimentation.<ref>metal-aschaffenburg.de | [https://www.metal-aschaffenburg.de/?p=21139/ Band Of The Month-Interviews-Regionale Bands]</ref><ref>konzert-nerd.de | [https://www.konzert-nerd.de/enemy-inside-und-concept-insomnia-am-17-02-2018-im-nachtleben-frankfurt-das-erste-konzert-von-enemy-inside-im-vollen-frankfurter-kellerclub/ Enemy Inside - Nachtleben]</ref> Their music strikes hard guitar riffs with melodic vocals and melodic guitar solos.

In June 2018, Enemy Inside announced their debut album ''Phoenix'' to be released by [[ROAR! Rock of Angels Records|ROAR]] in September 2018.

<ref>rockandroll.gr [https://rockandroll.gr/music/rock-pages/new-releases/item/9702-enemy-inside-phoenix/ Enemy Inside - Album release PHOENIX]</ref>

In August 2018, the band released their first singles & video clips "Falling Away",<ref>toughmagazine.de | [http://toughmagazine.de/news/enemy-inside-video-zu-falling-away/ Enemy Inside „Falling Away“]</ref> "Oblivion" and "Lullaby"<ref>truemetal.it | [http://www.truemetal.it/news/enemy-inside-il-lyric-video-ufficiale-di-oblivion-102179 ENEMY INSIDE - Video ufficiale di oblivion ]</ref><ref>ffm-rock.de | [https://www.ffm-rock.de/33797-roar-enemy-inside-und-outloud-mit-lyric-videos.html   Enemy Inside verröffentlichen Lyric Videos]</ref> on YouTube.<ref>YouTube | [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xc2YEysPTKE/ ENEMY INSIDE - Falling Away (Official Video)]</ref><ref>YouTube | [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V_7ICHDClSY/ ENEMY INSIDE - Oblivion (Official Lyric Video)]</ref><ref>YouTube | [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=j89ChkaWpi0/ ENEMY INSIDE - Lullaby (Official Lyric Video)]</ref>

[[Georg Neuhauser]], the singer of the symphonic/progressive power metal band [[Serenity (band)|Serenity]], appears also as a guest and performs alongside Giulia on the song "Doorway to Salvation". The album reached after few days of its release Nr.28 in the German iTunes Metal Top releases and Nr.74 in the US.<ref>iTunes | [https://www.facebook.com/RockofAngelsRecords/photos/a.593895880667413/2039388122784841/?type=3&theater/ Enemy Inside iTunes charts]</ref>
