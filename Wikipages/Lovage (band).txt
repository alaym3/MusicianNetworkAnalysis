'''Lovage''' was a collaborative project headed by [[Dan the Automator]],<ref name= "avclub">{{cite news |last=Rabin |first=Nathan |url= https://music.avclub.com/nathaniel-merriweather-presents-lovage-music-to-make-l-1798197000 |title=Nathaniel Merriweather Presents Lovage: ''Music To Make Love To Your Old Lady By'' |work=[[The A.V. Club]] |date=April 19, 2002 |accessdate=April 19, 2020}}</ref> under his pseudonym "Nathaniel Merriweather" (a persona he created for the project [[Handsome Boy Modeling School]]).<ref name= "faces">{{cite news |last=Harvilla |first=Rob |url= https://www.theringer.com/music/2019/8/1/20749404/handsome-boy-modeling-school-20-years-prince-paul-dan-automator |title=Look at These Faces: How Handsome Boy Modeling School Created the Perfect Album for ’99 |work=[[The Ringer (website)|The Ringer]] |date=August 1, 2019 |accessdate=April 19, 2020}}</ref> Their only album is titled ''[[Music to Make Love to Your Old Lady By]]'', which was created in team with [[Mike Patton]] and [[Jennifer Charles]], who both provide vocals.<ref name= "Patton">{{cite news |last=Rolli |first=Bryan |url= https://consequenceofsound.net/2020/03/outside-the-spotlight-mike-patton/ |title=Faith No More’s Mike Patton Is the Busiest Man in Metal |work=[[Consequence of Sound]] |date=March 13, 2020 |accessdate=April 19, 2020}}</ref>  [[Kid Koala]] plays turntables and samples and toured with the band for their 13-city U.S. tour.
