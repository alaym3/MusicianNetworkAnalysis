'''Alvin Lamar Worthy''' (born July 27, 1982), known professionally as '''Westside Gunn''', is an American [[rapping|rapper]]. He is the brother of fellow rapper [[Conway the Machine|Conway The Machine]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.passionweiss.com/2016/02/15/i-love-fly-street-shit-an-interview-with-westside-gunn/|title=I Love Fly Street Shit: An Interview With Westside Gunn|date=March 11, 2016|publisher=Passionweiss|access-date=March 11, 2016}}</ref> In 2014, he founded the record label [[Griselda Records]],<ref name="Shady-Griselda-deal" /> and signed a deal with [[Eminem]] and [[Paul Rosenberg (music manager)|Paul Rosenberg]]’s [[Shady Records]] in 2017. On November 14, 2020, in an interview with [[Joe Budden]], Gunn announced he had fulfilled his contractual obligations to Shady Records and had become an independent artist.<ref>{{cite web |title=Westside Gunn Tells Joe Budden He's Left Eminem's Shady Records |url=https://hiphopdx.com/news/id.59013/title.westside-gunn-tells-joe-budden-hes-left-eminems-shady-records |first=Kyle |last=Eustice |date=2020-11-14 |website=[[HipHopDX]] |access-date=2021-08-30}}</ref>

==Musical career==
In 2016, he released his first commercial album, ''Flygod''.

On March 3, 2017, Griselda Records signed a deal with [[Eminem]]'s [[Shady Records]], making Westside Gunn and Conway the first rappers from Buffalo to sign with a major label.<ref name="Shady-Griselda-deal">{{Cite web|url=http://shadyrecords.com/2017/03/03/shady-x-griselda-ink-deal-for-westside-gunn-and-conway/|title=Shady X Griselda Ink Deal for WestsideGunn and Conway|website=Shady Records|access-date=2017-03-03}}</ref> After signing with Shady, Gunn played at [[Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival|Coachella]], [[Firefly Music Festival|Firefly]] and [[Governors Ball Music Festival|Governors Ball]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/garysuarez/2018/06/22/westside-gunn-griselda/|title=At The Nexus Of Rap And Wrestling, Westside Gunn Reigns Supreme|last=Suarez|first=Gary|website=Forbes|language=en|access-date=2019-06-01}}</ref> On October 2, 2020, Westside Gunn released his [[Aftermath Entertainment|Shady Records]] debut, ''[[Who Made the Sunshine]]'', his only solo project with [[Shady Records]]. On 14 November 2020 he announced he had left Shady.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.hypebeast.com/2020/10/westside-gunn-who-made-the-sunshine-album-stream%3famp=1|title=Westside Gunn Asks All the Right Questions on New Album 'WHO MADE THE SUNSHINE'|work=Hypebeast|last=Caraan|first=Sophie|date=October 2, 2020|access-date=October 2, 2020}}</ref>

Shortly after signing with Shady Records, Westside released ''Flygod Is Good... All the Time'', as a collaborative EP with producer [[Mr. Green (record producer)|Mr. Green]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://hypebeast.com/2018/3/westside-gunn-mr-green-flygod-is-good-all-the-time-ep-stream?amp=1|website=Hypebeast|title=Westside Gunn & Mr. Green Unite for 'FLYGOD Is Good... All The Time' EP}}</ref>  Ambrosia For Heads named 2018's ''[[Supreme Blientele]]'' one of the best albums of 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ambrosiaforheads.com/2018/12/best-album-2018-pusha-t-westside-gunn/|title=Who Had The Best Rap Album Of 2018 (Battle 3): Pusha-T vs. Westside Gunn|date=2018-12-07|website=Ambrosia For Heads|language=en-US|access-date=2019-06-01}}</ref> He also signed with [[Roc Nation]] alongside label mate Benny the Butcher on August 6, 2019, under a management deal.<ref>{{cite web |title=Benny the Butcher and Westside Gunn Sign Roc Nation Management Deal |url=https://www.complex.com/music/2019/08/benny-the-butcher-westside-gunn-sign-to-roc-nation-management |website=[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]] |date=2019-08-06 |first=Edwin |last=Ortiz |access-date=2021-08-27}}</ref>

On April 17, 2020, he released his third studio album, ''[[Pray for Paris]]''. The album has features from artists such as [[Tyler, the Creator]], [[Wale (rapper)|Wale]], [[Joey Badass]] and [[Freddie Gibbs]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://hypebeast.com/2020/4/westside-gunn-pray-for-paris-album-stream|title=Westside Gunn Expands His Rap Dynasty With 'Pray for Paris'|website=HYPEBEAST|access-date=2020-04-21}}</ref>
On July 3, 2020, Westside Gunn released his tenth mixtape ''Flygod Is an Awesome God II'', the sequel to his July 2019 mixtape ''Flygod Is an Awesome God''. The album features guest appearances by Benny The Butcher, as well as frequent collaborators like Keisha Plum, [[Boldy James]], and Daringer.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://pitchfork.com/news/westside-gunn-details-new-album-flygod-is-an-awesome-god-ii/|title=Westside Gunn Details New Album Flygod Is an Awesome God II|work=[[Pitchfork (magazine)|Pitchfork]]|last=Strauss|first=Matthew|date=July 1, 2020|access-date=July 3, 2020}}</ref>
