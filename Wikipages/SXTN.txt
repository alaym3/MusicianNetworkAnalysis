'''SXTN''' was a German [[hip hop]] duo from [[Berlin]] consisting of [[Rapping|rapper]]s [[Juju (German rapper)|Juju]] and [[Nura (German rapper)|Nura]]. They are known for their somewhat-mockingly provocative lyrics, that often play on stereotypical rap themes such as sex, weed-smoking, money and party lifestyles.<ref name="me01112018">{{cite web |author=<!--not stated--> |url=https://www.musikexpress.de/sxtn-juju-bestaetigt-trennungsgeruechte-1148491/ |title=SXTN: Juju bestätigt Trennungsgerüchte |trans-title=SXTN: Juju confirms separation rumors |language=German |date=November 1, 2018 |website=musikexpress.de |publisher=Axel Springer Mediahouse Berlin |access-date=February 9, 2019 |quote=}}</ref>

==Members==
The duo consists of rappers Juju and Nura. Nura Habib Omer<ref name="spiegel01112018"/><ref name="me01112018"/><ref name="ts10092018">{{cite web |author=<!--not stated--> |url=https://www.tagesspiegel.de/kultur/lollapalooza-festival-das-leben-feiern-forever/23017976.html |title=Das Leben feiern, forever |trans-title=Celebrating life, forever |language=German |date=September 10, 2018 |work=[[Der Tagesspiegel]] |access-date=February 10, 2019 |quote=}}</ref> left Saudi-Arabia at the age of three, when her mother fled to Germany with Nura and her three siblings.<ref name="faz12032016">{{cite web |last=Baum |first=Antonia |url=https://www.faz.net/aktuell/feuilleton/pop/berliner-rapperinnen-sxtn-entfesselt-laut-und-unverschaemt-14107847.html |title=Entfesselt, laut und unverschämt |trans-title=Unleashed, loud and outrageous |language=German |date=March 12, 2016 |website=faz.net |publisher=[[Frankfurter Allgemeine Zeitung]] |access-date=February 8, 2019 |quote=}}</ref> After two years in a refugee home, they moved to [[Wuppertal]]. At the age of 18, Nura moved to Berlin.<ref name="faz12032016"/> Juju, whose real name is Judith Wessendorf,<ref name="spiegel01112018">{{cite web |author=<!--not stated--> |url=http://www.spiegel.de/kultur/musik/sxtn-juju-und-nura-haben-sich-offenbar-getrennt-a-1236314.html |title=SXTN haben sich offenbar getrennt |trans-title=SXTN have apparently separated |language=German |date=November 1, 2018 |website=spiegel.de |publisher=[[Spiegel Online]] |access-date=February 8, 2019 |quote=}}</ref><ref name="me01112018"/><ref name="ts10092018"/> grew up in [[Neukölln]] with a German mother and a Moroccan father.<ref name="faz12032016"/> She met Nura at the age of 17.<ref name="zeit26082016">{{cite web |last= |first= |url=https://www.zeit.de/zeit-magazin/2016/34/jugend-rueckblick-fehler-erwachsene/seite-5 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180704010037/https://www.zeit.de/zeit-magazin/2016/34/jugend-rueckblick-fehler-erwachsene/seite-5 |title=Ich sehe was, was du nicht siehst |trans-title=I can see what you can not see |language=German |date=August 26, 2016 |page=5 |archive-date=July 4, 2018 |website=zeit.de |publisher=[[Zeit Online]] |access-date=February 9, 2019 |url-status=live |quote=}}</ref>

==History==
SXTN was founded in 2014.<ref name="me01112018"/> Their first EP, titled ''Asozialisierungsprogramm'', was released in 2016. Their debut studio album, titled ''Leben am Limit'', was released in 2017 and peaked at 8 on the German charts,<ref name="dlf14072017">{{cite web |last=Peşmen |first=Azadê |url=https://www.deutschlandfunkkultur.de/musikalbum-leben-am-limit-von-sxtn-keine-lust-auf-feminimus.2177.de.html?dram:article_id=391041 |title=Musikalbum "Leben am Limit" von SXTN - Keine Lust auf Feminimus-Debatten |trans-title=Music album „Leben am Limit“ by SXTN - No desire for feminism debates |language=German |date=July 14, 2017 |website=deutschlandfunkkultur.de |publisher=[[Deutschlandradio]] |access-date=February 10, 2019 |quote=}}</ref><ref name="me01112018"/> and also peaked at 1 on the German hip-hop charts.<ref name="gfk">{{cite web |url=https://www.offiziellecharts.de/news/item/379-sxtn-mit-debuetalbum-auf-rang-eins-der-hiphop-charts |title=SXTN mit Debütalbum auf Rang eins der HipHop-Charts |trans-title=SXTN with debut album at number one on the HipHop charts |language=German |date=June 13, 2017 |accessdate=February 10, 2019 |publisher=[[GfK Entertainment Charts|GfK Entertainment]]}}</ref> In a late-2018 Instagram post, Juju revealed that she and Nura intended to focus on their own individual solo careers going forward, and that they had become otherwise estranged aside from their time performing on-stage together, which resulted in speculations regarding a possible disbandment of SXTN.<ref name="spiegel01112018"/>

==Lyrical content==
SXTN's lyrics often utilise male clichés,<ref name="fnp03032018">{{cite web |last= |first= |url=https://www.fnp.de/rhein-main/frauen-mischen-gangster-rap-10432387.html |title=Frauen mischen den Gangster-Rap auf |trans-title=Women rough up the gangster-rap |language=German |date=March 3, 2018 |website=fnp.de |publisher=Frankfurter Societäts-Medien |access-date=February 8, 2019 |quote=}}</ref> and rap about prostitution, drugs and partying.<ref name="bento30102016">{{cite web |last=Siggelkow |first=Pascal |url=https://www.bento.de/musik/sxtn-diese-frauen-treten-dem-deutschen-hiphop-maechtig-in-den-arsch-a-00000000-0003-0001-0000-000000952239 |title=Diese Frauen treten dem deutschen HipHop mächtig in den Arsch |trans-title=These women really kick German hip-hop's ass |language=German |date=October 30, 2016 |website=bento.de |publisher=[[Spiegel Online]] |access-date=February 8, 2019 |quote=}}</ref>
