'''Zeynep Tanyalçın''' (born 28 June 1994), better known by her stage name '''Lil Zey''', is a Turkish [[rapper]], [[songwriter]] and [[DJ]].<ref name="milliyet.com.tr">{{Cite web|url=https://www.milliyet.com.tr/kultur-sanat/lil-zeyden-yeni-calismalar-6193091|title=Lil Zey'den yeni çalışmalar|website=Milliyet}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/kesfet/mix-festivalda-sira-disi-bir-muzik-deneyimine-hazir-misiniz-41358049|title=MIX Festival'da sıra dışı bir müzik deneyimine hazır mısınız?|website=www.hurriyet.com.tr}}</ref>

==Biography==
Tanyalçın studied music management and songwriting at [[Berklee College of Music]]. In 2019, she voiced two songs in the album ''METFLIX'', released under the label RedKeys Music.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://genius.com/albums/Metth/Metflix|title=METFLIX by Metth|website=Genius}}</ref> In the same year, she attracted the attention of the music market and the press with the song "Ötede Dur", released as a duet with KÖK$VL. She continued her career with the song "Yolumuz Yol Değil", which is a duet song with [[Khontkar]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sabah.com.tr/cumartesi/2019/11/23/biraz-yukaridan-bakmaktan-sakinca-yok|title=Biraz yukarıdan bakmaktan sakınca yok|website=Sabah}}</ref> Towards the last months of 2019, she made a duet with Metth in the song "Marina".<ref name="ntv.com.tr">{{Cite web|url=https://www.ntv.com.tr//sanat/turkcetrap-sarkicisi-lil-zey-ile-metthin-yeni-dueti-marina,DcKKAzwcjkeFdCuFdy-pcQ|title=Türkçe trap şarkıcısı Lil Zey ile Metth'in yeni düeti: Marina|website=www.ntv.com.tr}}</ref> She released her first solo single named "Heveslenmem" in 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.powerapp.com.tr/powerplus/muzik/muzik-haber/lil-zey-den-yepyeni-bir-single-heveslenmem.html|title=Lil Zey'den Yepyeni Bir Single 'Heveslenmem'|website=www.powerapp.com.tr}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/yazarlar/tolga-akyildiz/para-diye-bir-sey-olmasa-yine-muzik-yapardim-41462952|title=Para diye bir şey olmasa yine müzik yapardım|first=Tolga|last=AKYILDIZ|website=www.hurriyet.com.tr}}</ref> Tanyalçın signed a contract with "Universal Music Production" in 2020. Lil Zey was a nominee in the "Best Debut" category at the "17th Radyo Boğaziçi Music Awards".
