'''Layton Greene'''  is an American singer and songwriter from [[East St. Louis, Illinois]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/8532404/layton-greene-tell-ya-story-interview|title=Quality Control's Layton Greene Wants to Share Her Pain to Help Herself and Others Heal|website=Billboard|access-date=2020-02-09}}</ref> She is signed to [[Quality Control Music]] and [[Universal Music Group]]. Her debut EP, ''Tell Ya Story,'' was released on September 9, 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/quality-controls-layton-greene-releases-tell-ya-story-debut-ep-new-mixtape.119615.html|title=Quality Control's Layton Greene Releases "Tell Ya Story" Debut EP|website=HotNewHipHop|access-date=2020-02-09}}</ref>

Greene is [[Quality Control Music]]'s first [[Rhythm and blues|R&B]] artist.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.11alive.com/article/news/local/outreach/atl-culture/layton-greene-quality-control-atlanta/85-3b9f1c88-f0bb-4e98-976b-de6a50f6efd0|title=Atlanta-based record label Quality Control signs first R&B artist Layton Greene|website=WXIA|access-date=2020-02-09}}</ref>

== Early life ==
Greene was born on December 4, 1998 in East St.Louis, Missouri.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://genius.com/a/who-is-20-year-old-quality-control-singer-layton-greene-with-viral-hits-roll-in-peace-leave-em-alone-i-love-you|title=In Search Of: 20-Year-Old Singer Layton Greene Is Quality Control's First R&B Artist|website=Genius|language=en|access-date=2020-02-09}}</ref>  She was raised by her biological mother and her step father, who eventually separated when she was 14. Greene began singing at seven years old, reciting [[Keyshia Cole]]'s "[[Love (Keyshia Cole song)|Love]]" to her mother.<ref>{{Citation|title=Layton Greene Talks Family Upbringing, Not Fitting In, New Music + More|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ve5iFC5HNpo|language=en|access-date=2020-02-09}}</ref>

== Personal life ==
Greene is in a relationship with music producer, GStylesOnTheTrack, who worked with her on “Open Wounds.”<ref>{{Cite web|last=Rôkour|first=Javoe|date=2020-05-01|title=GStylesOnTheTrack|url=https://www.javoerokour.com/post/gstylesonthetrack|access-date=2021-09-02|website=JAVOE ROKOUR|language=en}}</ref> They are expecting their first child together as of August 31, 2021.

== Artistry ==
Greene names [[Keyshia Cole]], [[Aaliyah]], [[Mary J. Blige]], [[India Arie]], [[Monica (singer)|Monica]], [[Prince (musician)|Prince]], [[Lauryn Hill]], [[Chris Brown]] and [[Fantasia (singer)|Fantasia]] as her musical influences.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/pop/layton-greene-remix-viral-sensation-interview-8095428/|title=R&B Singer Layton Greene Talks Going Viral With Kodak Black Remix, Making Her Billboard Chart Debut & New Music|website=www.billboard.com|access-date=2021-11-18}}</ref>
