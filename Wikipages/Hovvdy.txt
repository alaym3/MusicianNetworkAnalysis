'''Hovvdy''' is an American [[Indie pop]] duo from [[Austin, Texas]]. 

==History==
The duo began their career releasing an EP in 2014. The following year, they released a split with the band Loafer. Hovvdy released their debut album, ''Taster'', in 2016.<ref name="pitchfork">{{cite web |last1=Joyce |first1=Colin |title=Hovvdy: Taster |url=https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/23175-taster/ |publisher=[[Pitchfork (website)|Pitchfork]] |accessdate=10 September 2019}}</ref> In 2018, Hovvdy released their second full-length album titled ''Cranberry''.<ref name="slugmagazine">{{cite web |last1=Sanford |first1=Ryan |title=Review: Hovvdy – Cranberry |url=https://www.slugmag.com/national-music-reviews/review-hovvdy-cranberry/ |publisher=[[SLUG Magazine]] |accessdate=10 September 2019}}</ref><ref name="thefader">{{cite web |last1=Maicki |first1=Salvatore |title=Listen to Hovvdy’s new album, “Cranberry” |url=https://www.thefader.com/2018/02/05/listen-to-hovvdys-new-album-cranberry |publisher=[[The Fader]] |accessdate=10 September 2019}}</ref><ref name="stereogum">{{cite web |last1=DeVille |first1=Chris |title=Stream Hovvdy Cranberry |url=https://www.stereogum.com/1981758/stream-hovvdy-cranberry/music/album-stream/ |publisher=[[Stereogum]] |accessdate=10 September 2019}}</ref> In 2019, the duo released a split with musician Hannah Read, known as [[Lomelda]]. The split found Hovvdy and Lomelda covering each others songs. The two also went on a summer tour to promote the split.<ref name="paste1">{{cite web |last1=Todd |first1=Harry |title=Hovvdy and Lomelda Share Surprise Split EP Covers, Announce Tour Dates Together |url=https://www.pastemagazine.com/articles/2019/06/hovvdy-lomelda-new-ep-covers-split.html |publisher=Paste |accessdate=10 September 2019}}</ref> The duo's third album, ''Heavy Lifter'', was released in 2019.<ref name="thefader1">{{cite web |last1=Gooding |first1=Sarah |title=Hovvdy discuss their new album Heavy Lifter and premiere a new single, “Cathedral” |url=https://www.thefader.com/2019/08/07/hovvdy-heavy-lifter-new-album-announce-cathedral-new-single |publisher=The Fader |accessdate=10 September 2019}}</ref><ref name="pastemagazine">{{cite web |last1=Todd |first1=Harry |title=Hovvdy Announce New Album Heavy Lifter, Share Lead Single "Cathedral" |url=https://www.pastemagazine.com/articles/2019/08/hovvdy-new-album-new-song-cathedral.html |publisher=[[Paste (magazine)|Paste]] |accessdate=10 September 2019}}</ref> The bands fourth album, ''True Love'', was released on October 1, 2021.<ref name="pitchforkwebsite">{{cite web |last1=Ruiz |first1=Matthew |title=Hovvdy Announces New Album True Love, Shares Video for New Song: Watch |url=https://pitchfork.com/news/hovvdy-announces-new-album-true-love-shares-video-for-new-song-watch/ |publisher=Pitchfork |access-date=1 October 2021}}</ref>
