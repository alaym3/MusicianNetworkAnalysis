'''Trần Huyền My''',<ref>{{Cite web|title=Hóa ra, Amee 'Anh nhà ở đâu thế?' là một tiểu thư 'rich kid' chính hiệu|url=https://saostar.vn/doi-song/amee-anh-nha-o-dau-the-la-mot-tieu-thu-rich-kid-chinh-hieu-5292227.html|url-status=live|website=saostar.vn}}</ref> also known as '''Amee''' (born 23 March 2000) is a Vietnamese singer. She first gained audiences attention in 2019 when she released her debut song "Anh Nhà Ở Đâu Thế" ("Boy, Where do you live?").<ref>{{Cite web|title=The Rookie Girl of ST. 319 – AMEE Officially Debuts with "Anh Nha O Dau The?"|url= https://www.vpopwire.com/music/the-rookie-girl-of-st-319-amee-officially-debuts-with-anh-nha-o-dau-the/|url-status=live|website=vpopwire.com}}</ref> She is best known for being the first solo female artist from St.319 Entertainment.<ref>{{Cite web|title=AMEE: 'Tôi và ERIK vào St.319 cùng ngày, người đó đi khéo lại là cơ hội của tôi'|url=https://amp.saostar.vn/am-nhac/amee-toi-va-erik-vao-st-319-cung-ngay-nguoi-do-di-kheo-lai-la-co-hoi-cua-toi-4896067.html|url-status=live|website=saostar.vn}}</ref><ref>{{cite tweet|author=vpopwire |user=vpopwire|number= 1137704943538884608|title=The Rookie Girl of ST. 319 – AMEE Officially Debuts with 'Anh Nha O Dau The?' https://vpopwire.com/2019/06/the-rookie-girl-of-st-319-amee-officially-debuts-with-anh-nha-o-dau-the/|date=9 June 2019|accessdate=5 March 2021}}</ref> She is the youngest Vietnamese artist to have won at the [[2020 Mnet Asian Music Awards|MAMA Awards]] for "Best New Asian Artist in Vietnam".<ref name="auto">{{Cite web|url=https://www.mwave.me/kr/mama/history/winners/list?years=2020|title=Enjoy Mnet K·POP, Mwave|website=www.mwave.me}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Vietnamese artists win awards at MAMA 2020
|url=https://en.nhandan.org.vn/culture/lifestyle/item/9419002-vietnamese-artists-win-awards-at-mama-2020.html|url-status=live|website=[[Nhân Dân]]}}</ref>

Engaged in a musical career since 2018, Amee has a clear voice, and managed to quickly reach young audiences and become popular on social networks. The artist notably proposes a combination of tradition and modernity in her songs with the desire to bring Vietnamese music into world trends.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Trois artistes vietnamiens primés aux MAMA 2020 |url=https://www.lecourrier.vn/trois-artistes-vietnamiens-primes-aux-mama-2020/883335.html|language=fr|url-status=live|website=lecourrier.vn}}</ref>

In addition to "Anh nhà đâu thế?", Amee is also known for her songs "Đen đá không đường" ("Black-Iced-Coffee"), "Sao anh chưa về nhà?" ("Why don't you come home?"),<ref>{{Cite web|title=Vietnamese singer accused of copying Taylor Swift's 'Blank Space' in new music video|url=https://tuoitrenews.vn/news/lifestyle/20200307/vietnamese-singer-accused-of-copying-taylor-swifts-blank-space-in-new-music-video/53348.html|url-status=live|website=[[Tuổi Trẻ]]}}</ref> and "Mama Boy". In December 2020, she released her first album ''[[dreAMEE]]'' and performed the first live [[Acoustic music|acoustic]] show of the same name with the idea of "healing" her fans with her soft music during the anxious period of the [[COVID-19 pandemic]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=AMEE chính thức ra mắt "Dreamee live acoustic show", tin tưởng âm nhạc từ trái tim sẽ chạm đến trái tim|url=https://billboardvn.vn/amee-chinh-thuc-ra-mat-dreamee-live-acoustic-show-tin-tuong-am-nhac-tu-trai-tim-se-cham-den-trai-tim/|language=vi|url-status=live|website=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] Vietnam}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Amee đưa đến trải nghiệm không gian âm nhạc trong lành, thư giãn trong "Dreamee – Live Acoustic Show" |url=https://yeah1music.net/vpop/amee-dua-den-trai-nghiem-khong-gian-am-nhac-trong-lanh-thu-gian-trong-dreamee-live-acoustic-show.html|language=vi|url-status=live|website=Yeah1 Music}}</ref>

Before winning an award at the [[2020 Mnet Asian Music Awards|MAMA 2020]], Amee had already been honored with several national awards such as "Best Video of the Year" at the Metub WebTV Asia Awards 2019, "Best Song" at the [[Green Wave (radio show)|Green Wave Awards 2019]], "Top 10 Music Video in Vietnam" at [[YouTube Rewind 2019]],<ref>{{Cite web|title=K-ICM và Jack, Amee 'phá đảo' bảng trending YouTube Việt Nam 2019|url=https://m.vlive.tv/post/0-12350854 |language=vi|url-status=live|website=[[Vlive]]}}</ref> "Best New Artist" at the [[Zing Music Awards]] 2019 and "New Revelation of the Year" at the [[Dedication Music Award]] 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Trois jeunes artistes vietnamiens primés aux MAMA 2020 |url= https://fr.vietnamplus.vn/trois-jeunes-artistes-vietnamiens-primes-aux-mama-2020/154203.vnp|language=fr|url-status=live|website=vietnamplus.vn}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Amee khóc khi bị ép cân làm ca sĩ|url= https://vnexpress.net/amee-khoc-khi-bi-ep-can-lam-ca-si-4078063.html|language=vi|url-status=live|archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20210312095931/https://vnexpress.net/amee-khoc-khi-bi-ep-can-lam-ca-si-4078063.html |archive-date=12 March 2021|website=[[VnExpress]]}}</ref>

== Life and career ==

=== Early life ===
Amee was born on 23 March 2000 in [[Hanoi]]. She was recruited by St.319 Entertainment as a trainee when she was 15 and was one of the first idols in Vietnam to have undergone a training period of four years. During her trainee period, she made an appearance in Nguyễn Trọng Tài, San Ji and Double X's music video "HongKong1".<ref>{{Cite web|title=Nhan sắc ngọt ngào của nữ chính MV 'Hongkong1' sinh năm 2000|url=https://zingnews.vn/nhan-sac-ngot-ngao-cua-nu-chinh-mv-hongkong1-sinh-nam-2000-post884104.html|url-status=live|access-date=28 June 2020|website=zingnews.vn}}</ref>

=== Career beginnings (2018–2019) ===
On 15 October 2018, Amee made her official label debut with her appearance in fellow label group Monstar's "Nếu Mai Chia Tay".<ref>{{Cite web|date=16 October 2018|title=Tung sản phẩm hoàn chỉnh đội hình, MONSTAR còn ẩn ý hé lộ một điều…|url=https://saostar.vn/am-nhac/tung-san-pham-hoan-chinh-doi-hinh-monstar-con-an-y-he-lo-mot-dieu-3875488.html|access-date=28 June 2020|website=SaoStar.vn}}</ref>

Amee then was formally introduced as a solo artist on 13 March 2019 with her solo debut teaser "dreAMEE (intro)"<ref>{{Cite web|title=YouTube|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UFTXVhjOHgw|access-date=28 June 2020|via=YouTube}}</ref> releasing on 1 April 2019. Her first title track "Anh Nhà Ở Đâu Thế" featuring B Ray released on 3 April 2019 which after two days, garnered 2.5&nbsp;million views on YouTube and was trending second on YouTube Vietnam.<ref>{{Cite web|title=AMEE|url=https://www.facebook.com/ameest319/posts/435617580601601|access-date=28 June 2020|publisher=Facebook}}</ref>

On 14 April 2019, Amee again collaborated with rapper B Ray with song "Ex's Hate Me" which gained her popularity within the Vietnamese community as the song garnered popularity on the YouTube charts five days after the song's release.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Cặp đôi rapper và hot girl 10X trong MV 'Ex's Hate Me' là ai?|url=https://zingnews.vn/cap-doi-rapper-va-hot-girl-10x-trong-mv-ex-s-hate-me-la-ai-post917618.html|url-status=live|access-date=28 June 2020|website=zingnews.vn}}</ref> She then collaborated with Kay Tran on 22 April 2019 with Phố Hàng Nóng.

On 9 May 2019, Amee's second single "Đen Đá Không Đường" which featured B Ray in the Music Video. The video was trending 3rd on YouTube Vietnam. Nine days after on 18 May 2019 also marked the first audio release of Amee's with her single "dreAMEE" releasing as a timed exclusive on [[VNG Corporation]]'s music streaming service Zing MP3.<ref>{{Cite web|title=AMEE Album|url=https://zingmp3.vn/AMEE|url-status=live|access-date=28 June 2020|website=zingmp3.vn}}</ref> The single was then eventually distributed on international streaming services and music storefronts [[Spotify]], [[iTunes]] and [[Apple Music]] on 30 July 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Dreamee – Single by AMEE|url=https://music.apple.com/au/album/dreamee-single/1474983166|access-date=28 June 2020|website=Apple Music}}</ref>

Amee marked her first [[Soundtrack|OST]] debut with Andiez, on 15 July 2019 with Anh Đánh Rơi Người Yêu Này for movie Thật Tuyệt Vời Khi Ở Bên Em. Her fourth collaboration was also released on 12 September 2019 with OSad for "Dấu Yêu Vô Hình".

Amee released her fourth title track on 13 October 2019 with "Trời Giấu Trời Mang Đi". The song featured retired professional ''[[League of Legends]]'' player and popular Vietnamese internet streamer ViruSs, who wrote the song's music and lyrics. This marked ViruSs' first composed track to be released to the public. "Trời Giấu Trời Mang Đi" was also Amee's biggest breakthrough, garnering 4.5&nbsp;million views in two days and being Amee's first song to top YouTube trending in Vietnam.<ref>{{Cite web|date=16 October 2019|title='Trời giấu trời mang đi' thẳng tiến top 1 trending Youtube, AMEE tung vũ đạo 'thả thính' cực ngọt|url=https://saostar.vn/am-nhac/troi-giau-troi-mang-di-cua-amee-thang-tien-top-1-trending-youtube-6257818.html|access-date=28 June 2020|website=SaoStar.vn}}</ref>

On 5 December 2019, Amee marked her first win in the Vietnamese Music Awards industry for Best Collaboration Music Video of The Year at the METUB WebTVAsia Awards 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|title=METUB Network|url=https://www.facebook.com/metub.net/posts/2481491348789449|access-date=28 June 2020|publisher=Facebook}}</ref>

=== Award wins, first album debut ''dreAMEE'' (2020–present) ===
Amee continued her award-winning streak on 9 January 2020 at the annual Zing Music Awards (ZMA) for the Best Rookie of the Year award. A week later, on 18 January 2020, her second appearance in an OST was released with Huynh James, titled "Giận Muốn Chết Đến Tết Cũng Quên" for "30 Chưa Phải Là Tết'. During Lunar New Year festivities, Amee also appeared with rookies Han Sara and Xesi to sing Tết Đón Xuân Về at the annual Lunar New Year "Gala Nhac Viet" music show.<ref>{{Cite web|title=YouTube|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QrhgwPaPOhc|access-date=28 June 2020|via=YouTube}}</ref>

On 14 February 2020, Amee was featured on B Ray's album ''Loser2Love'' in the track "Do For Love". The music video was a hit, with the video trending 2nd on YouTube Vietnam for two days. Another featuring track with Amee was released two weeks later, on 25 February 2020 with Lou Hoang and Rhymastic for "1000x(Ngàn Lần)".

Amee scored her third win at a music award show on 3 March 2020 at SCTV2's Làn Sóng Xanh awards for the "Top 10 Most Liked Songs in 2019".<ref>{{Cite web|last=ONLINE|first=TUOI TRE|date=3 March 2020|title=Làn Sóng Xanh 2019: Hoàng Thùy Linh thắng lịch sử tất cả các hạng mục được đề cử|url=https://tuoitre.vn/news-20200303085609819.htm|access-date=28 June 2020|website=Tuoi Tre Online|language=vi}}</ref>

Amee then released her fourth single "Sao Anh Chưa Về Nhà" featuring labelmate and rapper Ricky Star. This music video trended at number 1 on YouTube Vietnam.<ref>{{Cite news|date=7 March 2020|title=Amee đưa 'Sao anh chưa về nhà' #1 trending sau hơn 1 ngày ra mắt|language=vi-VN|work=SaoStar.vn|url=https://saostar.vn/am-nhac/sao-anh-chua-ve-nha-cua-amee-1-trending-youtube-7116903.html|access-date=28 June 2020}}</ref>

Her fifth award was given at the "Giải thưởng Âm nhạc Cống hiến" event (translated as "Prize for Music Devotees", somewhat equivalent to Vietnamese Grammys) for Best New Artist of the Year.

On 18 June 2020, Amee released her fifth single "Yêu Thì Yêu Không Yêu Thì Yêu" which was also announced to be the main title track for her new album ''[[Dreamee]]''. The title track is Amee's first promoted track not featuring any other artist, and was trending 1st on YouTube Vietnam for two days. ''DreAMEE'' was released on 28 June 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|date=22 June 2020|title=Sau MV cực dễ thương và thành công, AMEE chuẩn bị ra mắt album đầu tay|url=http://emagazine24.net/sau-mv-cuc-de-thuong-va-thanh-cong-amee-chuan-bi-ra-mat-album-dau-tay/|access-date=28 June 2020|website=Emagazine24.net|language=vi}}</ref>

On 6 December 2020, Amee was awarded the "Best New Asian Artist Vietnam" in MAMA 2020.<ref name="auto"/>
