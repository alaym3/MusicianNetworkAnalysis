'''Frej Larsson''' (born 9 September 1983 in [[Ronneby]], Sweden) is a Swedish musician and rapper. He is a member of [[Slagsmålsklubben]], [[Maskinen]] and Far & Son.

Frej Larsson joined [[Slagsmålsklubben]] in 2002, after he met them at the 2002 Emmabodafestivalen.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://djurandmir.se/smk/wp-content/themes/smk_frames/index.php?page_id=12 |title=Archived copy |access-date=2010-06-12 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100827060659/http://djurandmir.se/smk/wp-content/themes/smk_frames/index.php?page_id=12 |archive-date=2010-08-27 |url-status=dead }}</ref>
