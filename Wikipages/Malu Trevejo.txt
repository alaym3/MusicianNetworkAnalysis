== Early life and career beginnings ==
Mar√≠a Luisa Trevejo was born in [[Havana]], [[Cuba]]. She is of Cuban and Spanish descent.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Moore|first=Camille|date=2020-07-02|title=10 Things You Didn't Know about Malu Trevejo|url=https://tvovermind.com/malu-trevejo/|access-date=2021-11-12|website=TVOvermind|language=en-US}}</ref> She moved to [[Madrid]], [[Spain]] with her parents when she was still a baby and lived there for 12 years.<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|last=Malu|first=Trevejo|date=|title=What you don't know about Malu Trevejo|url=https://thenetline.com/people/malu-trevejo/|url-status=live|website=TheNetline}}</ref> During her time in [[Spain]], Trevejo showed a lot of interest in [[acting]] especially with skit videos she did with her friends, recorded by her parents.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Malu|first=Trevejo|date=23 January 2021|title=Malu trevejo acting as a kid|url=https://www.tiktok.com/@malutrevejo/video/6920770041919130886?lang=fr&is_copy_url=0&is_from_webapp=v1&sender_device=pc&sender_web_id=6968354854091621894|url-status=live|website=TikTok}}</ref> She began [[Belly dance|belly dancing]] at an early age, and uploaded videos of her engaging in this activity to the Internet,<ref>{{Cite web|last=Malu|first=Trevejo|date=23 January 2021|title=Malu belly dancing as a kid|url=https://www.tiktok.com/@malutrevejo/video/6921063457466895621?lang=fr&is_copy_url=0&is_from_webapp=v1&sender_device=pc&sender_web_id=6968354854091621894|url-status=live|website=Tiktok}}</ref> which garnered her first public attention. She stayed in [[Spain]] until she was 13 then moved to [[Miami]], [[Florida]] with her mother.<ref name=":2" />

Shortly after, she opened a [[Musical.ly]] (today known as [[TikTok]]) account, and gathered a following the eventually numbered in the millions. She posted [[Lip sync|lip-syncing]] videos, and then became a verified user.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Malu|first=Trevejo|title=Malu Trevejo's Tiktok|url=https://www.tiktok.com/@malutrevejo?lang=fr|url-status=live|website=TikTok}}</ref>

Trevejo signed a [[Recording studio|recording]] agreement and [[Fisheries co-management|co-management]] agreement with In-Tu Linea and [[Universal Music Latin]] at the age of 14.<ref>{{Cite magazine|last=Colin|first=Stutz|date=7 May 2018|title=Malu Trevejo Seeks to Void Universal Music Latin Deal, Claims Contract Signed as Minor Was Never Certified|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/latin/8464173/malu-trevejo-voids-deal-universal-music-latin-underage|url-status=live|magazine=Billboard}}</ref> On September 22,  2017, she released her debut single "[[Luna Llena (song)|Luna Llena]]" which accumulated over 113 million views on [[YouTube]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Malu|first=Trevejo|title=Malu Trevejo - Luna Llena|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E-zcNpZJX78|url-status=live|website=YouTube}}</ref>
