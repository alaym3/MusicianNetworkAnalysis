'''Isyana Sarasvati''' (born 2 May 1993) is an Indonesian singer-[[songwriter]]. She is a graduate of [[Singapore]]'s [[Nanyang Academy of Fine Arts]] and [[London]]'s [[Royal College of Music]].<ref name="Jawapos.com">{{Cite web|url=http://www.jawapos.com/baca/artikel/10275/Isyana-Sarasvati-Bersinar-karena-Bakat-Musik|title=Isyana Sarasvati, Bersinar karena Bakat Musik}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.galamedianews.com/?arsip=5952&judul=isyana-sarasvati-totally-untuk-musik|title=Isyana Sarasvati, Totally untuk Musik|date=January 10, 2015|website=galamedianews.com }}</ref> Known for her original compositions, she wrote all of the songs on her 2015 debut pop album, [[Explore!]] and on her two subsequent albums, [[Paradox (Isyana Sarasvati album)|Paradox]] (2017) and [[Lexicon (Isyana Sarasvati album)|Lexicon]] (2019).<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cnnindonesia.com/hiburan/20210428090831-240-635732/jalan-isyana-mencapai-titik-kulminasi-seorang-musisi/2|title= a Journey of Isyana Reaches The Culmination Point of a Musician|date=May 29, 2021|website=cnnindonesia.com}}</ref> She has also performed as an opera singer in Singapore.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.singaporeopera.com.sg/profiles/isyana-sarasvati.html|title=Isyana Sarasvati â€“ Singapore Lyric Opera}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.liputan6.com/showbiz/read/2146770/isyana-sarasvati-mariah-carey-dari-indonesia|title=Isyana Sarasvati, 'Mariah Carey' dari Indonesia|date=December 13, 2014|website=liputan6.com}}</ref> She is the recipient of numerous Indonesian and international awards.

Before gaining fame as a pop singer and songwriter, she had built a reputation as a classical music soloist and recitalist. Isyana also participated in numerous musical competitions. At age 15, she won a "best composer" award. Her song ''Wings of Your Shadow'' was selected as the 12th best composition of 3,500 entries in the International Junior Original Concert (IJOC). As a result, she performed the [[electone]] at the IJOC 2008 concert in [[Bunkamura]] hall, Tokyo, [[Japan]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://musik.kapanlagi.com/berita/isyana-sarasvati-sukses-jadi-12-pianis-muda-terbaik-di-dunia-ccd3b8.html|title=Isyana Sarasvati Pernah Menjadi Top 12 Electonis Terbaik di Dunia|website=Kapanlagi.com}}</ref> Isyana signed a recording deal with [[Sony Music Entertainment|Sony Music Indonesia]] in 2014 and released two singles, ''Keep Being You'' (2014) and ''Tetap Dalam Jiwa'' (2015). Her albums have received positive reviews and commercial success.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://indonesiatatler.com/generation-t/2017-list/isyana-sarasvati|title=Isyana Sarasvati Profile|website=Indonesia Tatler}}</ref>

On 2 September 2018, Isyana sang the first song at the closing ceremony of the 18th [[2018 Asian Games|Asian Games]] in Jakarta, [[Indonesia]], performing ''Asia's Who We Are'' before an audience of more than 55,000 people at [[Bung Karno Stadium|Gelora Bung Karno Stadium]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.liputan6.com/showbiz/read/3634711/manggung-di-closing-ceremony-asian-games-2018-isyana-sarasvati-bikin-orang-nangis|title=Manggung di Closing Ceremony Asian Games 2018, Isyana Sarasvati Bikin Orang Nangis|date=September 3, 2018|website=liputan6.com}}</ref>

In May 2019, [[Walt Disney Pictures]] selected Isyana and {{ill|Gamaliel Tapiheru|id}} to sing ''[[A Whole New World]]'' on the soundtrack for the Indonesian version of the 2019 remake of ''[[Aladdin (2019 film)|Aladdin]]''. They were interviewed in Tokyo by [[Alan Menken]], the man behind many [[Walt Disney Animation Studios]] film scores.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.jpnn.com/news/isyana-dan-gamaliel-bikin-pencipta-a-whole-new-world-terpukau|title=JPNN|date=May 19, 2019|website=jpnn.com}}</ref>
In July 2019, Isyana joined the coaching panel of Season 4 of [[The Voice Indonesia (season 4)|The Voice Indonesia]] with {{ill|Armand Maulana|id}}, [[Titi DJ]], Nino [[RAN (Indonesian group)|RAN]], and [[Vidi Aldiano]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://lifestyle.sindonews.com/read/1416639/157/isyana-sarasvati-gantikan-anggun-di-the-voice-indonesia-1562040737|title=Isyana Sarasvati Gantikan Anggun di The Voice Indonesia|website=SINDOnews.com|date=2 July 2019|language=id-ID|access-date=2019-07-03}}</ref> Isyana was also a panelist for Season 4 of [[The Voice Kids Indonesia (TV series)|The Voice Kids Indonesia]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.suara.com/entertainment/2021/01/22/215035/juri-the-voice-kids-indonesia-season-4-resmi-diumumkan|title=Juri The Voice Kids Indonesia Season 4 Resmi Diumumkan|website=suara.com|date=22 January 2021|language=id-ID|access-date=2021-02-03}}</ref> 

In 2019, she was listed on [[Forbes 30 Under 30|Forbes Indonesia's 30 Under 30]] list of inspiring young achievers, in the art, style and entertainment category.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://keepo.me/news/sukses-dan-berprestasi-ini-9-milenial-indonesia-yang-masuk-forbes-2019-30-under-30/|title=Sukses dan Berprestasi, Ini 9 Milenial Indonesia yang Masuk Forbes 2019 "30 Under 30"|date=February 7, 2019|website=Keepo.me}}</ref> In April 2020, [[Forbes Asia]] included Isyana on its 30 Under 30 list in the Entertainment and Sports, and Celebrity categories.<ref name="forbes.com">{{Cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/profile/isyana-sarasvati/?list=30under30-asia-entertainment-sports#72e5a7ce6c25|title=Isyana Sarasvati listed on Forbes 30 under 30 Asia in Entertainment & Sports category|website=Forbes|access-date=2020-04-03}}</ref><ref name="ReferenceA">{{Cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/30-under-30/2020/asia/celebrities/#70ccf721336b|title=Isyana Srasvati listed on Forbes 30 under 30 Asia in Celebrities category|website=Forbes|access-date=2020-04-03}}</ref> 

After six years with [[Sony Music Entertainment|Sony Music Indonesia]], Isyana on 20 October 2020 announced she had established a music label called [[REDROSE RECORDS]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.pramborsfm.com/entertainment/keren-isyana-sarasvati-dirikan-label-musik-sendiri/all|title=Isyana Sarasvati Dirikan Label Musik Sendiri|website=pramborsfm|date=21 October 2020|language=id-ID|access-date=2020-10-27}}</ref> On 2 February 2021, Isyana and her husband [[Rayhan Maditra Indrayanto|Rayhan]] released a single titled ''1+1'' to mark their first wedding anniversary.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://kumparan.com/kumparanhits/rayakan-wedding-anniversary-isyana-sarasvati-dan-rayhan-maditra-rilis-lagu-1-1-1v66fKO1uOA/full|title=Rayakan Wedding Anniversary, Isyana Sarasvati dan Rayhan Maditra Rilis Lagu 1+1|website=kumparan.com|language=id-ID|access-date=2021-02-03}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K2_X8YyYbNs|title=Official Music Video 1+1|website=YouTube.com|language=id-EN|access-date=2021-02-03}}</ref>

==About==
Isyana was born in Bandung on 2 May 1993. She spent part of her early childhood outside Indonesia because her parents worked and studied in [[Belgium]]. When she was seven, her family moved to Bandung, West Java. Isyana is the youngest daughter of Luana Marpanda, a music teacher, and Sapta Dwikardana, a lecturer and graphologist. She has an older sister, {{ill|Rara Sekar Larasati|id}}, a cultural anthropologist, who also sang in a called Banda Neira. Raised in a family of educators, Isyana was introduced to music at the age of four by her mother. Isyana plays piano, [[electone]], [[flute]], violin and saxophone.

Isyana's music career in Indonesia began with several covers of popular songs of 2012 and 2013, uploaded on her [[YouTube]] and [[SoundCloud]] channels. After turning down initial offers of recording contracts because she wanted to focus on her studies, she signed with [[Sony Music Entertainment|Sony Music Indonesia]] in 2014.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://literaturnegeri.id/isyana-sarasvati-a-new-star-is-born/|title=Awal Karir Bermusik Isyana Sarasvati|website=Literature Negeri|access-date=2020-04-11}}</ref>

===Education===
At age 16, Isyana received a scholarship from the Singapore Government to study Music Performance at the [[Nanyang Academy of Fine Arts]] (NAFA). in 2012, she was an exchange student at [[Tainan National University of the Arts]], Tainan, [[Taiwan]]. In 2013, she obtained a Diploma in Music Performance. Isyana was awarded a full scholarship to study further at NAFA in collaboration with the [[Royal College of Music]] (RCM) in London, under the Bachelor of Music with Honours Funded Degree Program. In her final year of study, she received the RCM Excellence Award Scholarship (2015). On 26 September 2015, Isyana graduated from NAFA with a Bachelor of Music and received the Best Graduate and Embassy of [[Peru]] awards.
