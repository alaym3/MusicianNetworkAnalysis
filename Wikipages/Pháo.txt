'''Nguyễn Diệu Huyền''' (born 28 March 2003), commonly known by her stage name '''Pháo''',  is a Vietnamese [[Rapping|rapper]] and producer.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|date=2021-07-22|title=Rapper Mỹ có MV một tỷ view mời Pháo hợp tác|url=https://zingnews.vn/zingnews-post1241812.html|access-date=2021-07-24|website=ZingNews.vn|language=vi}}</ref> She grew up in [[Tuyên Quang Province]], and lives in Hanoi as of 2021. The name [[wiktionary:pháo|Pháo]] was chosen by her as it symbolizes passion,<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|title=Nữ sinh 17 tuổi đang gây điên đảo cộng đồng mạng tại "King of Rap" là ai?|url=https://laodong.vn/giai-tri/nu-sinh-17-tuoi-dang-gay-dien-dao-cong-dong-mang-tai-king-of-rap-la-ai-826766.ldo|access-date=2021-07-24|website=laodong.vn|language=vi}}</ref> although in an earlier interview she stated that it was derived from the name of a character in the sitcom ''Kim Chi Cà Pháo'' (''Eggplant Kimchi'').<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|last=|date=2020-08-14|title=Pháo - rapper 17 tuổi đang gây bão: Thi King Of Rap vì tên 'ngầu' hơn Rap Việt, nếu không theo âm nhạc nhất định sẽ đi bán trà đá!|url=https://kenh14.vn/phao-co-nang-rapper-17-tuoi-tham-du-king-of-rap-vi-ten-show-ngau-hon-rap-viet-neu-khong-theo-nghe-thuat-nhat-dinh-se-di-ban-tra-da-20200813120722589.chn|url-status=live|access-date=2021-07-24|website=kenh14.vn|language=vi}}</ref> As of 2020, she was a student at the [[Hanoi College of Art]].<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-03-06|title=Nữ sinh 10X sáng tác 'Hai phút hơn' có bạn trai là rapper|url=https://zingnews.vn/zingnews-post1055472.html|access-date=2021-07-24|website=ZingNews.vn|language=vi}}</ref>

Pháo started rapping around 2018.<ref name=":2" /> She has said she admires [[Cardi B]]<ref name=":2" /> and fellow Vietnamese rapper [[Kimmese]].<ref name=":1" /> She wasn't known to the wider public until the song "[[2 Phút Hơn]]" with Vietnamese producer and DJ [[Masew]] <small>[<nowiki/>[[:vi:Masew|:vi:Masew]]]</small>, which garnered over 30 million views. The KAIZ remix of the latter song gained global popularity and topped the Shazam music chart.<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web|last=|title=Tyga collaborates with Vietnamese rapper for mixtape|url=https://e.vnexpress.net/news/life/culture/tyga-collaborates-with-vietnamese-rapper-for-mixtape-4329687.html|url-status=live|access-date=2021-07-24|website=VnExpress International|language=en}}</ref> She also participated in the Vietnamese TV show "[[:vi:Thế giới Rap – King of Rap|King of Rap]]".<ref>{{Cite web|last=|date=2020-08-09|title=Rap Việt gây sốt vì Ricky Star, King of Rap không thua kém với Pháo|url=https://nld.com.vn/news-20200809092606456.htm|url-status=live|access-date=2021-07-24|website=nld.com.vn|language=vi}}</ref> In 2021, she released a new version of "2 Phút Hơn" with the American rapper [[Tyga]], who is of partial Vietnamese descent.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-07-22|title=Rapper Mỹ có MV một tỷ view mời Pháo hợp tác|url=https://zingnews.vn/zingnews-post1241812.html|access-date=2021-07-24|website=ZingNews.vn|language=vi}}</ref>
