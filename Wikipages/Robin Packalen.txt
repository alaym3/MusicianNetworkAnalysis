'''Robin Packalen''' (born 24 August 1998), previously known by his stage name '''Robin''', is a Finnish singer who started as a [[teen pop]] artist. During his career, Packalen's [[List of music recording certifications|certified]] albums and singles have sold over 370,000&nbsp;copies, which makes him one of the 70 best-selling artists of all-time in his country.{{r|list of best-sellers}}

==Music career==

===2008–2009: ''Staraskaba'' and ''New Wave Junior Contest''===
In 2008, Packalen won the Finnish nationwide singing contest for youth, ''Staraskaba'', when he was just 10.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.stara.fi/2008/10/29/kymmenvuotias-robin-packalen-staraskaban-voittajaksi/ |title=Stara: Kymmenvuotias Robin Packalen Staraskaban voittajaksi |access-date=27 January 2012 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130927082459/http://www.stara.fi/2008/10/29/kymmenvuotias-robin-packalen-staraskaban-voittajaksi/ |archive-date=27 September 2013 |url-status=dead }}</ref> In 2009, he represented Finland at the ''New Wave Junior Contest'' in Moscow, which was followed by about 100 million viewers.<ref>[http://www.stara.fi/2012/01/17/robin-on-turkulainen-teini-idoli/ Stara: Kuka ihmeen Robin? Hän on teini-idoli suomalaisen nettihitin taustalla] (in Finnish)</ref>

===2012: ''Koodi''===
{{Main|Koodi}}
On 16 January 2012, Packalen, described as "The [[Justin Bieber]] of Finland" by his label,<ref>[http://www.ess.fi/?article=356840 Turkulaispojan sinkusta nettihitti] (in Finnish)</ref> released his first official single "[[Frontside Ollie]]".  It received instant success, huge following online, and based on digital downloads debuted at number five on the [[The Official Finnish Charts|Finnish Singles Chart]] before reaching the top position.<ref name="ollie-fin-charts">{{Cite web|url=http://www.ifpi.fi/tilastot/virallinen-lista/artistit/robin/frontside+ollie|title=Robin: Frontside Ollie|publisher=[[Musiikkituottajat – IFPI Finland]]|language=Finnish|accessdate=29 January 2012}}</ref><ref name="FIN">{{cite web|url=http://finnishcharts.com/showinterpret.asp?interpret=Robin+%5BRobin+Packalen%5D|title=Discography Robin Packalen|publisher=Finnish Charts Portal. Hung Medien (Steffen Hung)|last=Hung|first=Steffen}}</ref> On 22 February 2012,  his first album ''[[Koodi]]'' was released. It sold over 100,000 records in Finland, earning quintuple platinum.

===2012–2013: ''Chillaa''===
{{Main|Chillaa}}
On 5 October 2012, Packalen released his second studio album ''[[Chillaa]]''. The album topped the [[The Official Finnish Charts|Finnish Albums Chart]] in its first week of release.

===2013: ''Boom Kah''===
{{Main|Boom Kah}}
On 30 August 2013, Packalen released "[[Boom Kah (song)|Boom Kah]]", featuring [[Mikael Gabriel]] and [[Uniikki]], as the lead single from his third studio album ''[[Boom Kah]]''. The song peaked at number four on the Finnish Singles Chart. On 2 October 2013, he released the album ''[[Boom Kah]]'', which also topped the [[The Official Finnish Charts|Finnish Albums Chart]]. The chart topping second single, "[[Erilaiset]]", was released on 16 October 2013.

===2014: ''16''===
{{Main|16 (Robin album)}}
Packalen released his fourth studio album ''[[16 (Robin album)|16]]'' on 22 September 2014.

===2015: ''Yhdessä''===
{{Main|Yhdessä}}
All-duets album ''Yhdessä'' was released on 9 October 2015.

===2018: Hiatus===
Packalen went on a hiatus in 2018 to serve in the military, which is mandatory for every Finnish male citizen.

===2019: Comeback===
On 2 February, during the Emma Gaala awards ceremony, Packalen announced his comeback with a live performance of "I'll Be with You".
