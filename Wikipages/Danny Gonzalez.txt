'''Daniel James Gonzalez'''<ref>{{Cite web|title=Help Let Me Go by Danny Gonzalez|url=https://www.pandora.com/artist/danny-gonzalez/help-let-me-go-single/help-let-me-go/TRvtkmvtgrfZdX2|url-status=live|access-date=February 22, 2021|website=|via=[[Pandora (streaming service)|Pandora]]}}</ref> (born June 12, 1994)<ref name="Gonzalez birthdate">{{cite AV media |last=Gonzalez |first=Danny |title=I Googled Myself And Hated What I Found |type=video |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JR4CanmB_d0 |time=3:45 |via=[[YouTube]] |accessdate=October 16, 2020 |date=June 28, 2018 |quote=I went on it one time and it said my birthday was, like, June 13, 1994, which is one day off of my actual birthday, June 12.}}</ref> is an American [[YouTuber]], musician, and former [[Vine (service)|Viner]].

== Career ==
Gonzalez began his career on Vine making six-second [[Sketch comedy|comedy sketches]]. He later participated in ''Camp Unplug'', a 2016 Vine [[mini-series]], during which he met [[Drew Gooden (comedian)|Drew Gooden]]. Gonzalez accumulated about 2.9 million Vine followers before the service shut down.<ref name="KatieRogers">{{Cite news|last=Rogers|first=Katie|date=October 28, 2016|title=5 Vine Stars Share Why They Loved, and Outgrew, Platform|work=[[The New York Times]]|publisher=[[A. G. Sulzberger]]|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2016/10/29/technology/5-vine-stars-share-why-they-loved-and-outgrew-platform.html|url-status=live|access-date=October 16, 2020|archive-url=https://archive.today/20161028223020/http://www.nytimes.com/2016/10/29/technology/5-vine-stars-share-why-they-loved-and-outgrew-platform.html|archive-date=October 28, 2016}}</ref><ref name="Duffin">{{cite news|last1=Duffin|first1=Michael|date=September 11, 2019|title=Triangle Talks: YouTube stars Danny Gonzalez and Drew Gooden|work=[[Drexel University#Publications|The Triangle]]|publisher=[[Drexel University]]|url=https://www.thetriangle.org/entertainment/triangle-talks-youtube-stars-danny-gonzalez-and-drew-gooden/|accessdate=October 16, 2020}}</ref> In 2014, while still on Vine, he created and started uploading videos to his YouTube channel. 

In 2019, he and Drew Gooden headlined the "We are Two Different People" comedy tour with [[Kurtis Conner]] as the opener.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Guzman|first1=Richard|date=September 23, 2019|title=Why these YouTube stars are going from the internet to real life|work=[[Orange County Register]]|publisher=Ron Hasse|url=https://www.ocregister.com/2019/09/23/why-these-youtube-stars-are-going-from-the-internet-to-real-life/|accessdate=October 16, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|last=Wynne|first=Kelly|date=September 5, 2019|title=Danny Gonzalez and Drew Gooden talk tour, comedy and YouTube careers|website=[[Newsweek]]|publisher=[[Dev Pragad]]|url=https://www.newsweek.com/comedians-danny-gonzalez-drew-gooden-talk-tour-individuality-why-youtube-best-career-1457886|accessdate=October 16, 2020}}</ref> The tour was named as such due to the similarities between Gooden and Gonzalez, both being self-described "skinny white guys on the internet".<ref>{{cite web|last1=McDaniel|first1=Taylor|date=September 11, 2019|title=YouTubers Danny Gonzalez and Drew Gooden captivate fans on comedy show tour|url=https://oaklandpostonline.com/28465/opinion/youtubers-danny-gonzalez-and-drew-gooden-captivate-fans-on-comedy-show-tour/|url-status=live|website=[[The Oakland Post (Michigan)|The Oakland Post]]|publisher=[[Oakland University]]|accessdate=April 29, 2021}}</ref>

=== YouTube content ===
Gonzalez's videos are often commentary on different aspects of [[Internet culture]], [[criticism of YouTube]], [[B movie]]s and general [[cultural criticism]];<ref name="paste">{{Cite web|last=Wilbur|first=Brock|date=September 9, 2019|title=Drew Gooden and Danny Gonzalez: The John Olivers of YouTube Culture|url=https://www.pastemagazine.com/comedy/youtube/drew-gooden-and-danny-gonzalez-the-john-olivers-of/|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=October 16, 2020|website=[[Paste (magazine)|Paste]]|publisher=Paste Media Group}}</ref> his 2018 Troom Troom reaction videos popularized the channel.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Jennings|first1=Rebecca|date=November 12, 2018|title=Why YouTube is riddled with bizarre DIY videos|work=[[Vox (website)|Vox]]|url=https://www.vox.com/the-goods/2018/11/12/18065662/troom-troom-5-minute-crafts-youtube-diy-prank|accessdate=October 16, 2020}}</ref> He is known for his criticisms of [[Jake Paul|Jake]] and [[Logan Paul]]<ref name="paste" /> and for his commentaries on [[Musical.ly]] (later [[TikTok]]) stars.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Alexander|first1=Julia|date=March 17, 2019|title=YouTube creators are using a hilarious tactic to combat copyright policies|work=[[The Verge]]|url=https://www.theverge.com/2019/3/17/18265484/tiktok-youtube-danny-gonzalez-kurtis-conner-react-commentary|accessdate=October 16, 2020}}</ref> In addition to commentary videos, he is also known for his [[parody music]]. Gonzalez calls his fanbase "Greg".<ref>{{cite AV media|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=117DwB70Q_M|title=Why Does Danny Gonzalez Call His Fans Greg?|date=September 10, 2020|author1=Klein, Ethan|publisher=[[h3h3Productions|H3 Podcast Highlights]]|access-date=June 6, 2021|author2=Klein, Hila|via=[[YouTube]]}}</ref>

He is associated with fellow YouTube comedians [[Drew Gooden (comedian)|Drew Gooden]], [[Kurtis Conner]], and [[Cody Ko]].<ref name="Daily Star">{{cite news|last=Faruk|first=Joyita|date=January 31, 2020|title=The shift in YouTube comedy|work=[[The Daily Star (Bangladesh)|The Daily Star]]|location=[[Bangladesh]]|url=https://www.thedailystar.net/toggle/news/the-shift-youtube-comedy-1861306|accessdate=October 16, 2020}}</ref>

== Personal life ==
Gonzalez attended [[Wheaton North High School]] where he was on the school's speech team. In 2016, he graduated with a degree in computer science from the [[Georgia Tech|Georgia Institute of Technology]].<ref>{{cite tweet|user=dannygonzalez|number=826214027176992768|title=My college just roasted me I think??|date=January 30, 2017|access-date=August 29, 2021|first=Danny|last=Gonzalez}}</ref><ref>{{Cite AV media|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-W0wK0srvUE|title=Kanye's Weirdest Tweets w/ Danny Gonzalez|date=September 4, 2020|type=video|publisher=Sad Boyz|access-date=August 29, 2021|via=[[YouTube]]}}</ref> He married Laura Fuechsl in 2017.<ref>{{Cite web|last=JaaniKay|title=A Secret Marriage and Other Things to Know about Danny Gonzalez and His Wife Laura|url=https://hollywoodmask.com/p/danny-gonzalez-wife.html|access-date=2021-11-15|website=hollywoodmask|language=en}}</ref>
