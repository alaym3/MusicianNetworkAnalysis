'''Van She''' are an [[electropop]] band formed in 2002 in [[Sydney]], Australia, with Matt Van Schie (pronounced Van Ski) on [[bass guitar]]/[[vocals]], Tomek Archer on [[drum kit|drums]]/[[music sequencer|sequencer]], [[Michael Di Francesco]] on [[synthesizer|synth]]/[[guitar]] and Nicholas Routledge on vocals/guitar. The band's debut album, ''[[V (Van She album)|V]]'' was released on the Australian label [[Modular Recordings]] in August 2008, and peaked at number 10 on the [[ARIA Charts]].<ref name="AusCharts">{{cite web|url=http://australian-charts.com/showinterpret.asp?interpret=Van+She|title=Discography Van She|publisher=Australian charts portal|access-date=21 March 2021}}</ref> 

Van She [[remix album|remix]] other artists' work both under the name ''Van She'' and as ''Van She Technologic''.<ref name="MusicBrainz">{{cite web|url=http://musicbrainz.org/artist/a05b1b41-e959-4867-9f04-ee7ae15fa047.html|title=Artist: Van She – MusicBrainz|publisher=[[MusicBrainz]]|date=19 June 2007|access-date=28 April 2009}}</ref><ref name="MusicBrainzVST">{{cite web|url=http://musicbrainz.org/artist/d57823ff-e9a0-456a-a6c4-8519ba99f1ca.html|title=Artist: Vanshe Technologic – MusicBrainz|publisher=MusicBrainz|date=19 June 2007|access-date=28 April 2009}}</ref>

==History==
Van She began in 2002 when band members Nick Routledge, Matt Van Schie, Michael Di Francesco, and Tomek Archer all met through mutual friends at a club called ''Bang Gang'' in Sydney. In 2005. Van She released their [[Van She (EP)|self-titled, debut EP]]. It included the single "Sex City".

After touring with [[Yeah Yeah Yeahs]], [[New Young Pony Club]] and playing shows with the likes of [[Daft Punk]], [[Phoenix (band)|Phoenix]] and [[Bloc Party]], Van She released their debut studio album, ''V'' in 2008, which debuted at number 10 on the [[ARIA Charts]]. Their label introduced them as a "new band from Sydney fresh on ideas, fresher than [[Flavor Flav]], fresh like coriander, fresher than the [[Will Smith|Fresh Prince]], fresher than fresh eggs."<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.modularpeople.com/vanshe/|title=Van She|publisher=[[Modular Records]]|access-date=16 June 2007}}</ref>

In June 2009, they released a remixed version of ''V'', called ''[[Ze Vemixes]]''.

The band's second studio album, ''Idea of Happiness'' was released in 2012.

===Solo projects===
*Matt Van Schie has released songs under his own name and makes up half of the electronic music duo, Du Tonc, with Mighty Mouse. Michael Di Francesco has remixed songs under the title Arithmatix!, and released songs under the moniker ''[[Touch Sensitive (producer)|Touch Sensitive]]''. 

*Nicholas Routledge has released solo music under the moniker '[[Nicky Night Time]]'.
