'''Natalia Weronika Szroeder''' (born 20 April 1995) is a Polish singer-songwriter and TV presenter. She is most known for her works with rapper [[Liber (rapper)|Liber]], of which she appeared with in his songs ''Wszystkiego na raz'' and ''Nie patrzę w dół'' in 2013. The singer also has a solo career: her debut single titled ''Jane '' was released in 2012.

== Biography ==
Szroeder was born on 20 April 1995 in [[Bytów]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://dziendobry.tvn.pl/wideo,2064,n/natalia-szroeder-tutaj-postawie-dom,204902.html|title=Natalia Szroeder: "Tutaj postawię dom"|publisher=dziendobry.tvn.pl|language=pl|access-date=22 January 2017}}</ref> She grew up in the Pomeranian village of [[Parchowo]] in a musically inclined family.<ref name="allaboutmusic.pl">{{cite web|title=RISING STARS: KIM JEST NATALIA SZROEDER?|url=http://allaboutmusic.pl/rising-stars-natalia-szroeder/|last=Mantiuk|first=Łukasz|agency=allaboutmusic.pl|accessdate=11 July 2016|language=pl}}</ref> Her mother, Joanna, is a singer for the folk band Modraki, which was founded by her grandfather Waldemar Kapiszka. Three of her siblings were part of the Rusland Trio. Her father Jaromir was the founder of the Dialogus theater, in 2004/2005.<ref>{{cite web|title=Natalia Szroeder: nie mam czasu na smutek|url=http://muzyka.onet.pl/wywiady/natalia-szroeder-nie-mam-czasu-na-smutek/vrp8x|last=Gzyl|first=Paweł|agency=muzyka.onet.pl|accessdate=11 July 2016|language=pl}}</ref> In childhood, Szroeder appeared in the [[TVP1]] program, Od przedszkola do Opola.<ref name="allaboutmusic.pl" />

In 2011, she was a part of an episode of ''[[Szansa na sukces]]''. She sang the song "Cicha woda" by [[Maryla Rodowicz]].<ref name="szansa">{{cite web|title=Natalia z Parchowa wygrała "Szansę na sukces" (wideo). Zaśpiewa latem z Marylą Rodowicz|url=http://www.gp24.pl/apps/pbcs.dll/article?AID=/20110314/POWIATBYTOWSKI/476421408|last=Gurba|first=Andrzej|agency=www.gp24.pl|accessdate=11 July 2016|language=pl}}</ref> On 24 March 2012, she released a video on YouTube for the song "Potrzebny je drech" (in the [[Kashubian language]]), of which she wrote {{ill|Weronika Korthals|pl}}, as advised by Tomasz Fopke.<ref name="potrzebnejedrech">{{cite web|url= https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0K540JmUc48|title= Natalia Szroeder – "Potrzebny je drech"|publisher= www.youtube.com|language= pl|access-date= 7 February 2018}}</ref> In July of that year, she released their debut single ''Jane''.<ref name="janesingel">{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/pl/album/jane-single/id689110899|title=Jane – Single Natalia Szroeder|publisher=itunes.apple.com|access-date=22 January 2015}}</ref> The music and the lyrics were written by {{ill|Marek Kościkiewicz|pl}}, a former member of {{ill|De Mono|pl}}. In the same year, she teamed up with rapper Liber, with whom she recorded the song ''Wszystkiego na raz''.<ref name="wszyskiegosingel">{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/pl/album/wszystkiego-na-raz-single/id594859748|title=Natalia Szroeder, Liber – Wszystkiego na raz – Single|publisher=itunes.apple.com|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141218023224/https://itunes.apple.com/pl/album/wszystkiego-na-raz-single/id594859748|archivedate=18 December 2014|url-status=bot: unknown|access-date=7 February 2018}}</ref> The composition was ranked 5th in AirPlay – Top,<ref>{{cite web|url= http://bestsellery.zpav.pl/airplays/top/archiwum.php?year=2013&idlisty=907#title|title= Liber & Natalia Szroeder (Airplay – Top: 09.02. – 15.02.2013)|access-date= 7 February 2018|publisher= bestsellery.zpav.pl|language= pl}}</ref> and also hit the charts of radio stations such as [[RMF FM]] and [[Radio ZET|Radio Zet]]. The song "Wszystkiego na raz" was the second single released in May of the same year in the Liber album Duety. The album also shared the second track of the two, ''Nie patrzę w dół,'' whose music video was produced by Cam-L Studio DL Promotion. On 3 August of that year, Szroeder performed a duet with Liber at the Eska Music Awards 2013 gala, during which they were nominated for Best Debut.<ref>{{cite web |url= http://muzyka.onet.pl/pop/znamy-nominacje-eska-music-awards/v7177 |title= Znamy nominacje ESKA Music Awards |publisher= muzyka.onet.pl |language= pl |access-date= 7 February 2018 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20131021000630/http://muzyka.onet.pl/pop/znamy-nominacje-eska-music-awards/v7177 |archive-date= 21 October 2013 |url-status= dead }}</ref> In the same month, Szroeder appeared on the Russian Song Festival in [[Zielona Góra]], where she performed the song "Kukuszka" from the repertoire of [[Zemfira]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://muzyka.onet.pl/koncerty/festiwal-piosenki-rosyjskiej-michal-szpak-natalia-sikora-i-natalia-szroeder-powalcza/r7yly |title=Festiwal Piosenki Rosyjskiej: Michał Szpak, Natalia Sikora i Natalia Szroeder powalczą o Złoty Samowar |publisher= muzyka.onet.pl|language= pl |access-date=7 February 2018}}</ref> On 2 December 2013, she released her second hit single, ''Tęczowy''.<ref name="tęczowy">{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/pl/album/teczowy-single/id766522637|title=Teczowy – Single Natalia Szroeder|publisher=itunes.apple.com|language=en|access-date=7 February 2018}}</ref>

On 11 February 2014, she released her third single "''Nie pytaj jak''  (Young Stars Festival)".<ref name="niepytajsingel">{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/pl/album/nie-pytaj-jak-young-stars/id808696999|title=Nie Pytaj Jak (Young Stars Festival) – Single Natalia Szroeder|publisher=itunes.apple.com|access-date=7 February 2018}}</ref> In April, she appeared alongside {{ill|Mezo|pl}} and [[Dawid Kwiatkowski]] at the Young Stars Festival 2014, at the [[HWS Arena]] in [[Poznań]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.eska.pl/news/young_stars_festival_2014_kto_wystapi_bilety_i_jakie_atrakcje_czekaja_w_poznaniu_video_zdjecia/93064|title= Young Stars Festival 2014: kto wystąpi, bilety i jakie atrakcje czekają w Poznaniu|access-date=7 February 2018|publisher= eska.pl|language= pl}}</ref> In June of that year, she took part in a concert to celebrate the 25th anniversary of the careers of {{ill|Jacek Cygan|pl}} and [[Ryszard Rynkowski]], which took place in the framework of LI Festival of Polish Songs in [[Opole]]. During the concert, she sang the song "Za młodzi, za starzy", in a duet with Cygan. In June, she took part in Carpathia Festival 2014, organized in [[Rzeszow|Rzeszów]].<ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.carpathia.rzeszow.pl/lang-pl/carpathia-2005-2014/carpathia-2014.html|title= Carpathia 2014 – finał|access-date= 7 February 2018|publisher= carpathia.rzeszow.pl|language= pl|url-status= bot: unknown|archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20150609024200/http://www.carpathia.rzeszow.pl/lang-pl/carpathia-2005-2014/carpathia-2014.html|archivedate= 9 June 2015}}</ref> On 27 July 2014, during a concert at Lata ZET i Dwójki in [[Toruń]], together with Liber, she won in the category of Best Performance for the performance of the song ''Nie patrzę w dół''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://vod.tvp.pl/audycje/rozrywka/lato-zet-i-dwojki/wideo/lato-zet-i-dwojki-2014-torun-cz-2/16214097|title=Lato Zet i Dwójki 2014 – Toruń cz. 2|publisher=vod.tvp.pl|language=pl|access-date=7 February 2018}}</ref> In the same year, on 24 August, they released their first single, ''Teraz ty'', which heralds the two as a duo.<ref name="teraztysingel">{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/pl/album/teraz-ty-feat.-natalia-szroeder/id910661114|title=Natalia Szroeder, Liber – Teraz Ty – Single|publisher=itunes.apple.com|access-date=7 February 2018}}</ref>

On 9 February 2016, Szroeder confirmed on the radio show RMF FM that she would attempt to represent [[Poland in the Eurovision Song Contest 2016|Poland]] via a national final for the 61st edition of the [[Eurovision Song Contest 2016|Eurovision Song Contest]] in 2016 with the song ''Lustra''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.rmf.fm/magazyn/news,2461,eurowizja-2016-kolejna-gwiazda-w-polskim-finale-natalia-szroeder-zglosila-piosenke-lustra.html|title=Eurowizja 2016: Kolejna gwiazda w polskim finale! Natalia Szroeder zgłosiła piosenkę "Lustra"|website=www.rmf.fm|publisher=[[RMF FM]]|date=9 February 2016|access-date=18 February 2018|language=Polish}}</ref> She had been a jury member for [[Poland in the Eurovision Song Contest 2015]]. A few days later, she was on the list of artists qualified for the final. She got 5th place with 4.20% of the votes.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://eurowizja2015.tvp.pl/24375297/wyniki-polskich-preselekcji|title=Wyniki polskich preselekcji|website=eurowizja2015.tvp.pl|language=Polish|access-date=18 February 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160311011346/http://eurowizja2015.tvp.pl/24375297/wyniki-polskich-preselekcji|archive-date=11 March 2016|url-status=dead}}</ref> In August 2016, she released her second single ''Domek z kart'' for her debut album ''NATinterpretacje''. Szroeder is a co-author of lyrics and music from the album. The album debuted at the 15th Place of the Polish OLiS charts.<ref>{{cite web|title=Natalia Szroeder – strona oficjalna|access-date=18 February 2018|website=www.nataliaszroeder.pl|url=http://www.nataliaszroeder.pl/|language=Polish}}</ref>

From 3 March to 12 May 2017, she took part in the seventh edition of the Polish version of [[Dancing with the Stars]], [[Dancing with the Stars: Taniec z gwiazdami]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://plejada.pl/newsy/taniec-z-gwiazdami-7-lista-uczestnikow-kto-wystapi/9dz5tf|title=Taniec z gwiazdami 7 – lista uczestników. Kto zatańczy w Polsacie?|website=plejada.pl|publisher=Plejada.pl|first=Julia|last=Tryzno|date=31 January 2017|access-date=18 February 2018|language=Polish}}</ref> with her dance partner [[Jan Kliment]]. The couple won the competition, beating Cichosz and {{ill|Stefano Terrazzino|pl}} in the finals.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://plejada.pl/newsy/taniec-z-gwiazdami-7-wygrala-natalia-szroeder-i-jan-kliment/qj3em2|title="Taniec z gwiazdami": wielki finał za nami! Kto wygrał? Co działo się w ostatnim odcinku show?|website=plejada.pl|publisher=Plejada.pl|first=Milena|last=Majek|date=12 May 2017|access-date=18 February 2018|language=Polish}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Natalia Szroeder i Jan Kliment wygrali Dancing with the Stars. Taniec z Gwiazdami – Polsat News|website=polsatnews.pl|access-date=18 February 2018|url=http://www.polsatnews.pl/wiadomosc/2017-05-12/natalia-szroeder-i-jan-kliment-wygrali-dancing-with-the-stars-taniec-z-gwiazdamidancing-with-the-stars-taniec-z-gwiazdami/|language=Polish}}</ref> Along with [[Rafał Brzozowski]] and {{ill|Sylwia Lipka|pl}}, she was an interval act for the national selection for the [[Poland in the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2017|Polish national final]] for the [[Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2017]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://plejada.pl/newsy/eurowizja-junior-2017-eliminacje-kto-prowadzacym-kto-w-jury/zgsvp6|title=Eurowizja Junior 2017 – krajowe eliminacje: Rafał Brzozowski prowadzącym. Kto w jury?|website=plejada.pl|publisher=Plejada.pl|access-date=18 February 2018|language=Polish}}</ref>

In January 2018, in an interview with [[Plejada.pl]], she confirmed the beginning of work on a second studio album, which she plans to premiere in autumn of 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://plejada.pl/newsy/eurowizja-2018-natalia-szroeder-zdecyduje-sie-na-krajowe-eliminacje/n2zv84|title=Eurowizja 2018. Natalia Szroeder zawalczy o udział w konkursie?|website=plejada.pl|publisher=Plejada.pl|access-date=18 February 2018|language=Polish}}</ref>

In January 2020, the premiere of the movie Jak zostałem gangsterem. Historia prawdziwa, took part in which she played Magda, the wife of the main character. She was the host of a TV program of the talent show broadcast on [[Polsat]] The Four. Bitwa o sławę.

== Inspirations ==
Among her inspirations, Szroeder mentions above all [[Michael Jackson]]. She is also inspired by the music of artists such as [[Justin Timberlake]] and [[Rihanna]].<ref name="allaboutmusic.pl" />
