'''Prince Daddy & The Hyena''' is an American [[rock music|rock]] band from [[Albany, New York]], formed in 2014. They have released two full-length albums and several extended plays.

==History==
===Formation and early years===
Prince Daddy and the Hyena was formed in 2014 when Kory Gregory, Alex Ziembiec and Zakariya Houacine were inspired to start a band after seeing [[Rozwell Kid]] live. According to Gregory, Rozwell Kid was a substantial influence on much of his early songwriting.<ref name="Poorly Edited Podcast">{{cite podcast |host=Chandler Davis |title=Poorly Edited Podcast |website=YouTube |publisher=Lab To Twelve |date=24 February 2020 |url=https://www.facebook.com/Labtwotwelve/ |access-date=3 March 2020}}</ref> The band's name is derived from a game that Gregory played with the band's merch person, Luis Wiest, at recess in elementary school.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.reddit.com/r/indieheads/comments/cg17nz/my_name_is_kornie_boi_from_prince_daddy_the_hyena/eudvwnf|title = My name is Kornie Boi from Prince Daddy & the Hyena. We just released our second album COSMIC THRILL SEEKERS and are currently on a 6 week tour supporting it. ASK US ANYTHING|date = 21 July 2019}}</ref> 
Prince Daddy & The Hyena began in 2015 with the release of an extended play titled ''Adult Summers''.<ref name="stereogum">{{cite web |last1=Rettig |first1=James |title=Prince Daddy & The Hyena – "Lauren (Track 2)" Video |date=26 April 2019 |url=https://www.stereogum.com/2041450/prince-daddy-the-hyena-lauren-track-2-video/video/ |publisher=[[Stereogum]] |accessdate=19 July 2019}}</ref> They later released their first full-length album titled ''I Thought You Didn't Even Like Leaving''<ref name="punknews" /> in 2016 on [[Broken World Media]].<ref name="punknews">{{cite web |title=Prince Daddy and The Hyena: I Thought You Didn't Even Like Leaving (2016) |publisher=PunkNews |url=https://www.punknews.org/review/14863/prince-daddy-and-the-hyena-i-thought-you-didnt-even-like-leaving |accessdate=19 July 2019}}</ref>

===''Cosmic Thrill Seekers''===
In 2019, the band released its second full-length album titled ''Cosmic Thrill Seekers'' on [[Counter Intuitive Records]], and embarked on several extensive tours in support of the album.<ref name="thefader">{{cite web |last1=Ross |first1=Alex |title=Hear Prince Daddy & The Hyena's post-acid pop-punk concept album, Cosmic Thrill Seekers |url=https://www.thefader.com/2019/06/26/prince-daddy-and-the-hyena-cosmic-thrill-seekers-album-stream-premiere-interview |publisher=[[The Fader]] |accessdate=19 July 2019}}</ref><ref name="uproxx">{{cite web |last1=Gelfand |first1=Zac |title=Indie Mixtape 20: Prince Daddy And The Hyena Turn Mania Into Rock And Roll On 'Cosmic Thrill Seekers' |date=3 July 2019 |url=https://uproxx.com/indie/prince-daddy-and-the-hyena-indie-mixtape-20-interview/ |publisher=[[Uproxx]] |accessdate=19 July 2019}}</ref><ref name="floodmagazine">{{cite web |last1=Ysteboe |first1=Taylor |title=Prince Daddy & the Hyena Search for Home |url=http://floodmagazine.com/63670/prince-daddy-the-hyena-search-for-home/ |publisher=Flood Magazine |accessdate=19 July 2019}}</ref><ref name="brooklynvegan">{{cite web |last1=Sacher |first1=Andrew |title=Prince Daddy & the Hyena announce 'Cosmic Thrill Seekers' (stream a track) |url=http://www.brooklynvegan.com/prince-daddy-the-hyena-announce-cosmic-thrill-seekers-stream-a-track/ |publisher=[[BrooklynVegan]] |accessdate=19 July 2019}}</ref> In July 2019, Taco Bell announced they were adding Prince Daddy to their Feed the Beat roster. The band recorded a cover of the Nacho Fries song from the Taco Bell commercial.{{citation needed|date=September 2020}} The band went on tour Europe with [[Oso Oso]] in further support of the record in the fall of 2019. In 2020, the band embarked on their co-headlining tour with Oso Oso with support from [[Just Friends (band)|Just Friends]].

==Musical style==
The band's sound has been described as [[indie rock]] with punk and "slacker" influences.{{citation needed|date=October 2020}} Frontman Kory Gregory cites [[The Strokes]], [[Jeff Rosenstock]], and [[Green Day]] as major influences on the sound. In an interview with [[The Fader]], Greogory described the sound on the band's sophomore album ''Cosmic Thrill Seekers'' as "the soundtrack to a Disney film played by a punk rock band."<ref>{{cite web |last1=Ross |first1=Alex Robert |title=Hear Prince Daddy & The Hyena's post-acid pop-punk concept album, Cosmic Thrill Seekers |url=https://www.thefader.com/2019/06/26/prince-daddy-and-the-hyena-cosmic-thrill-seekers-album-stream-premiere-interview |website=The Fader |publisher=The Fader |accessdate=5 March 2020}}</ref>
