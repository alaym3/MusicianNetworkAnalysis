'''Nedeljko Bajić''', known as '''Baja''' (born 9 June 1968), is a Serbian [[pop-folk]] singer.

==Personal life==
Bajić was born into a [[Serbs of Bosnia and Herzegovina|Bosnian-Serb]] family in [[Šipovo]], near [[Jajce]] in [[SR Bosnia and Herzegovina]], Yugoslavia, and was brought up with two brothers and two sisters. His father died in his early childhood. He moved to [[Serbia]] in 1988, and lived in [[Novi Sad]] until 1999, when he moved to [[Belgrade]], where he currently lives. Since 1991 he has also lived at times in [[Austria]].
