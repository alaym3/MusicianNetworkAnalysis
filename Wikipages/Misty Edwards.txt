'''Misty Dawn Edwards''' (born January 19, 1979), is an American [[contemporary Christian music]]ian. [[Cross Rhythms]] has reviewed seven of her albums, 2003's ''Eternity'', 2005's ''Always on His Mind'', 2007's ''Relentless'', 2009's ''Fling Wide'', 2010's ''Point of Life'', 2013's ''Only a Shadow'', and 2014's ''Little Bird'', while reviewing the [[extended play]], 2011's ''Measure of Love'', whereas Forerunner Music released these musical works. New Release Today has done five ''Behind the Song'' features on her songs. Edwards has seen five of her albums appear on the [[Billboard (magazine)|''Billboard'' magazine]] charts, where they have achieved thirteen peak positions; the best was ''Only a Shadow'', where it placed on the [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]]. The albums, ''Point of Love'', ''Fling Wide'', ''Only a Shadow'', and ''Little Bird'' placed on the [[Christian Albums]] chart, while the extended play, ''Measure of Love'', charted on it and the [[Top Heatseekers|Heatseekers Albums]] chart, likewise, her previous albums placed on that chart, ''Point of Life'' and ''Fling Wide''. There has been three placements on the [[Independent Albums]] chart, for, ''Fling Wide'', ''Only a Shadow'', and ''Little Bird''.

==Music career==
Edwards' music recording career commenced with the release ''Eternity'', a [[studio album]], on December 5, 2003,<ref>{{cite web|title=Eternity by Misty Edwards|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/eternity/id903928234|publisher=[[iTunes Store]]|accessdate=July 1, 2015}}</ref> by Forerunner Music.<ref name="CR One">{{cite web|first=Nigel|last=Bayley|title=Review: Eternity - Misty Edwards|url=http://www.crossrhythms.co.uk/products/Misty_Edwards/Eternity/28770/|publisher=[[Cross Rhythms]]|accessdate=July 1, 2015|date=November 29, 2007}}</ref> Her second album, “Always on His Mind,” was released by Forerunner Music on December 1, 2005.<ref>{{cite web|title=Always on His Mind by Misty Edwards|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/always-on-his-mind/id903436198|publisher=[[iTunes Store]]|accessdate=July 1, 2015}}</ref><ref name="CR Two">{{cite web|first=Tony|last=Cummings|title=Review: Always on His Mind - Misty Edwards|url=http://www.crossrhythms.co.uk/products/Misty_Edwards__Team/Always_On_His_Mind/25247/|publisher=[[Cross Rhythms]]|accessdate=July 1, 2015|date=October 22, 2007}}</ref> The subsequent album, “Relentless,” was released on December 20, 2007, from Forerunner Music.<ref>{{cite web|title=Relentless by Misty Edwards|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/relentless/id899630204|publisher=[[iTunes Store]]|accessdate=July 1, 2015}}</ref><ref name="CR Three">{{cite web|first=Tom|last=Lennie|title=Review: Relentless - Misty Edwards|url=http://www.crossrhythms.co.uk/products/Misty_Edwards/Relentless/45772/|publisher=[[Cross Rhythms]]|accessdate=July 1, 2015|date=July 16, 2008}}</ref>

She released “Fling Wide” on December 28, 2009, with Forerunner Music.<ref>{{cite web|title=Fling Wide (live) by Misty Edwards|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/fling-wide-live/id911060047|publisher=[[iTunes Store]]|accessdate=July 1, 2015}}</ref><ref name="CR Four">{{cite web|first=Tom|last=Lennie|title=Review: Fling Wide - Misty Edwards|url=http://www.crossrhythms.co.uk/products/Misty_Edwards/Fling_Wide/93840/|publisher=[[Cross Rhythms]]|accessdate=July 1, 2015|date=August 20, 2010}}</ref> The album placed on three [[Billboard (magazine)|''Billboard'' magazine]] charts, where it peaked at No. 21 on the [[Christian Albums]], No. 46 on the [[Independent Albums]], and No. 10 on the [[Top Heatseekers|Heatseekers Albums]] chart.<ref name="AM Charts">{{cite web|title=Misty Edwards : Awards|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/misty-edwards-mn0001469865/awards|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|publisher=[[AllMusic]]|accessdate=July 1, 2015}}</ref>

Her next album, Point of Life, was released by Forerunner Music on April 6, 2010.<ref name="CR Five">{{cite web|first=Tom|last=Lennie|title=Review: Point of Life - Misty Edwards|url=http://www.crossrhythms.co.uk/products/Misty_Edwards/Point_Of_Life/93841/|publisher=[[Cross Rhythms]]|accessdate=July 1, 2015|date=June 20, 2010}}</ref> This album saw placements on two ''Billboard'' magazine charts, where it peaked at No. 45 on the Christian Albums chart, and hitting a placement of No. 27 on the Heatseekers Albums.<ref name="AM Charts"/>

She released an [[extended play]] with David Brymer, ''Measure of Love'', and this was released on December 21, 2011,<ref>{{cite web|title=Measure of Love by Misty Edwards and David Brymer|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/measure-of-love/id490890925|publisher=[[iTunes Store]]|accessdate=July 1, 2015}}</ref> from Forerunner Music.<ref name="CR EP One">{{cite web|first=Doug|last=Holland|title=Review: Measure of Love - Misty Edwards  & David Brymer |url=http://www.crossrhythms.co.uk/products/Misty_Edwards__David_Brymer/Measure_Of_Love/132011/|publisher=[[Cross Rhythms]]|accessdate=July 1, 2015|date=January 27, 2013}}</ref> The extended play charted on two ''Billboard'' magazine charts, with it placing on the Christian Albums at No. 24 and No. 16 on the Heatseekers Albums.<ref name="AM Charts"/>

The follow-up album, ''[[Only a Shadow]],''was released on March 19, 2013,<ref>{{cite web|title=Only a Shadow (Live) by Misty Edwards |url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/only-a-shadow-live/id948764253|publisher=[[iTunes Store]]|accessdate=July 1, 2015}}</ref> by Forerunner Music.<ref name="CCM One">{{cite web|first=Matt|last=Conner|title=Review: Only a Shadow (Live) |url=http://www.ccmmagazine.com/article/review-only-a-shadow-live/|journal=[[CCM Magazine]]|accessdate=July 1, 2015|date=July 1, 2013}}</ref><ref name="CR Six">{{cite web|first=Stephen|last=Curry|title=Review: Only a Shadow: Misty Edwards Live - Misty Edwards|url=http://www.crossrhythms.co.uk/products/Misty_Edwards/Only_A_Shadow_Misty_Edwards_Live/137524/|publisher=[[Cross Rhythms]]|accessdate=July 1, 2015|date=June 24, 2013}}</ref><ref name="NRT One">{{cite web|first=Kevin|last=Davis|title=Misty Edwards (Only a Shadow) |url=http://www.newreleasetoday.com/userprofile_reviewssinglepost.php?review_id=21059&user_id=140|publisher=New Release Today|accessdate=July 1, 2015|date=March 31, 2013}}</ref> The album placed on three ''Billboard'' magazine charts, the [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]] at a peak of No. 107, while placing on the Christian Albums at No. 7, and Independent Albums at No. 21.<ref name="AM Charts"/> She sat down with Kevin Davis in two ''Behind the Song'' features for New Release Today, where she discussed her songs "Shine Like the Stars" and "When You Think of Me" from this album.<ref>{{cite web|first=Kevin|last=Davis|title=#416 - "Shine Like the Stars" by Misty Edwards|url=http://www.newreleasetoday.com/article.php?article_id=1100|publisher=New Release Today|accessdate=July 18, 2015|date=April 30, 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|first=Kevin|last=Davis|title=#474 - "When You Think of Me" by Misty Edwards|url=http://www.newreleasetoday.com/article.php?article_id=1220|publisher=New Release Today|accessdate=July 18, 2015|date=November 19, 2013}}</ref>
