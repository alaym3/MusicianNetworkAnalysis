'''Lily Ki''' (born November 20, 1991), better known by her online alias '''LilyPichu''', is an American [[internet personality]], musician and voice actress. She is a member of [[OfflineTV]], an [[online social entertainment]] group of [[Content creation|content creators]].

==Early life==
Ki was born on November 20, 1991 in California. She is of [[Koreans|Korean]] descent and has a brother named Daniel.<ref name="lilysbrother1"></ref><ref name="lilysbiography1"></ref> Ki started learning piano at the age of five after her parents discovered her interest for music.<ref name="lilysbio2">[https://answersafrica.com/lilypichu-height-boyfriend-brother.html LilyPichu bio, family and love life]. Retrieved on 2021-11-23.</ref><ref name="lilysbiography1"></ref>

==Career==
Ki first gained popularity in 2011 when her parody song "I'll Quit LoL" went viral on YouTube.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Favie III |first1=Carmelo |title=Offline TV: The story of one of the most successful streaming houses ever |url=https://clutchpoints.com/offline-tv-the-story-of-one-of-the-most-successful-streaming-houses-ever/ |website=ClutchPoints |date=16 November 2020}}</ref> In July 2017, a video of Ki playing the [[melodica]] to unsuspecting cosplayers at [[Indy PopCon]] also went viral, becoming her most viewed YouTube video to date.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Johnson |first1=Mia |title=Cosplayers receive a lovely surprise when a talented melodica player belts out their themes |url=https://mashable.com/2017/08/03/melodica-player-surprises-cosplayers/ |website=Mashable |language=en |date=3 August 2017}}</ref> That same month, she joined [[OfflineTV]].<ref>{{cite web |last1=Hong |first1=James |last2=Jang |first2=David |title=League of Legends: [A Deep Discussion With Scarra] Scarra Reveals His Thoughts On DL vs qt, Harry Potter, and OfflineTV. |url=https://www.invenglobal.com/articles/6205/a-deep-discussion-with-scarra-scarra-reveals-his-thoughts-on-dl-vs-qt-harry-potter-and-offlinetv |website=InvenGlobal |language=en |date=25 September 2018}}</ref> Ki's internet content include gaming, art, and music.<ref>{{cite web |title=LilyPichu - The Shorty Awards |url=https://shortyawards.com/12th/lilypichu |website=shortyawards.com}}</ref>

In September 2018, [[Riot Games]] launched a new series of ads on YouTube for ''League of Legends'' including one featuring Ki and former OfflineTV member Albert Chang titled "LilyPichu and Sleightlymusical: Duo."<ref>{{cite news |last1=Lee |first1=Julia |title=New League ads celebrate your average player |url=https://www.riftherald.com/culture/2018/9/6/17828468/its-on-league-ads-cinematic |date=January 26, 2019 |work=[[The Rift Herald]]}}</ref>

Ki was the 5th most watched female Twitch streamer in 2020.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Lee |first1=Helen A. |title=The Top Female Twitch Streamer Of 2020 May Surprise You |url=https://www.svg.com/315285/the-top-female-twitch-streamer-of-2020-may-surprise-you/ |website=SVG |date=19 January 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Belous |first1=Daria |title=The most popular female Twitch streamers in 2020 |url=https://streamscharts.com/news/most-popular-female-twitch-streamers-2020 |website=StreamsCharts |language=en |date=14 January 2021}}</ref> In late 2020, Ki appeared in an advertisement for [[5 (gum)|5 Gum]].<ref>{{cite web |title=5 GUM X AVENGERS (FT. LILYPICHU) |url=https://sawhorsela.com/portfolio/5-gum-x-avengers-ft-lilypichu/ |website=Sawhorse |publisher=Sawhorse LA |access-date=18 February 2021}}</ref>

On August 12, 2021, Ki starred in the music video for [[Sub Urban (musician)|Sub Urban]] and Bella Poarch's song, "INFERNO".<ref name=":inferno">{{Cite web|last=Piersey|first=Shonele|date=August 13, 2021|title=Who is in Bella Poarch's new music video? TommyInnit, Valkyrae, Pokimane, Bretman Rock, and more take Twitter by storm in "Inferno"|url=https://www.sportskeeda.com/pop-culture/news-who-bella-poarch-s-new-music-video-tommyinnit-valkyrae-pokimane-bretman-rock-take-twitter-storm-inferno|url-status=live|access-date=November 21, 2021|website=[[Sportskeeda]]|language=en}}</ref> She also participated in a stream promoting the video.

==Personal life==
In 2018, Ki started dating Albert Chang, better known as '''Sleightlymusical'''. They later broke up in 2019 after he cheated on her.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Asarch|first=Steven|date=November 11, 2019|title=Members of OfflineTV are embroiled in an alleged cheating scandal over Twitter and Reddit|url=https://www.newsweek.com/offlinetv-drama-explained-alleged-cheating-scandal-subtweets-fill-reddit-1471048|url-status=live|access-date=November 21, 2021|website=[[Newsweek]]|language=en}}</ref> In March 2020, Ki confirmed on a livestream that she and [[Michael Reeves (Internet personality)|Michael Reeves]] were in a relationship.<ref>[https://boardgamestips.com/wow/who-is-xchocobars-boyfriend/#Are_Michael_and_Lily_together Are Michael and Lily together?] Retrieved on 2021-11-23.</ref><ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Nm8Spw8gv24&t=227s WE'RE DATING NOW, RIGHT?! Ft. Michael Reeves - Road Trip | Crunchyroll Awards]. ''{{youtube|Nm8Spw8gv24}}''. Published on 2020-03-01. Retrieved on 2021-11-23.</ref>
