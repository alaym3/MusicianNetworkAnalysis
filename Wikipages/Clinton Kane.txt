'''Clinton Kane''' is a Filipino singer-songwriter born and raised in Bandar Seri Begawan, Brunei signed to [[Columbia Records]]. His 2021 singles "[[Chicken Tendies]]" and "[[I Guess I'm in Love]]" reached numbers 88 and 90, respectively, on the US [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]], with the song also peaking at number 25 in Australia and number 22 in the UK.

==Early life==
Clinton Kane Talabis Concon was born on November 26, 1998 to Filipino parents and was raised in Bandar Seri Begawan, Brunei Darussalam. His dad Lilioso Concon (deceased) was an Aircraft Mechanic in Royal Brunei Airlines, and his mom Edna Concon was a Christian preacher. He grew up singing, and dancing in church.
He has two brothers who are also singers namely Gyver Jude Talabis Concon “Jude Concon” a Brunei pop star known for his “Time Machine” single, and 
“Marcos Jay” Talabis Concon who graduated as an Engineer in Curtin University - Malaysia, also has original songs posted in Soundcloud. <ref name="TheBragAug21">{{cite web|url=https://theindustryobserver.thebrag.com/clinton-kane-crazy-voice-uk-chart/|title=Clinton Kane, the kid with the "crazy voice" discovered after making a YouTube cover of James Arthur’s “Can I Be Him”, is flying up the U.K. chart |website=the industry observer|date=24 August 2021|access-date=24 August 2021|author=Brangle, Lars}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.officialcharts.com/chart-news/who-is-clinton-kane-the-youtube-music-star-making-a-big-first-impression-on-the-official-charts__33859/|title=Who Is Clinton Kane? The Youtube music star making a big first impression on the Official Charts|website=[[Official Charts]]|date=23 August 2021|access-date=24 August 2021}}</ref>

Kane is a self-taught singer and multi-instrumentalist, and grew up singing in Church. He studied in Seri Mulia Sarjana School in Brunei and also in Menglait Secondary School. He was in Year 11 in 2016. <ref>{{cite web|url=https://btarchive.org/news/national/2016/06/03/program-rakan-remaja-entertains-menglait-students|title='Program Rakan Remaja' entertains Menglait students|website=btarchive.org|date=June 2016|access-date=10 October 2021}}</ref>

He qualified to study in 'A' Level College, but pursued a path that blended music and travel. Kane quelled growing anxiety and stress into song and in 2016, began uploading covers and subsequently original songs onto his [[YouTube]] channel.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://lost-lake.com/event/clinton-kane-im-not-toxic-i-swear-tour/|title=I'm Not Toxic I Swear|website=lost-lake.com|date=August 2021|access-date=24 August 2021}}</ref><ref name="NME radar">{{cite web|url=https://www.nme.com/en_au/blogs/nme-radar/clinton-kane-get-to-know-the-rising-star-working-with-martin-garrix-2638729/|title=Clinton Kane: Get to know the rising star working with Martin Garrix|website=[[NME Australia]]|date=27 February 2020|access-date=24 August 2021|author=Smith, Thomas}}</ref>

He performed in local music events in Brunei and recorded a cover on a local Youtube channel. <ref>{{cite web|url=https://borneoinsidersguide.com/soulful-tunes-and-beat-boxing-at-the-dream-project-music-nite/|title=Soulful Tunes and Beat-boxing at The DREAM Project Music Nite|website=borneoinsidersguide.com|date=February 2017|access-date=10 October 2021}}</ref>
<ref>{{cite web|url=https://m.facebook.com/rewind.asia/videos/say-you-wont-let-go-james-arthur-clinton-concon-cover-/366573703724600/?refsrc=deprecated&_rdr|title=Say You Won't Let Go - James Arthur (Clinton Concon Cover)|website=m.facebook.com|date=January 2017|access-date=10 October 2021}}</ref>

==Career==
===2019–present: Columbia Records===
Kane was signed to [[Columbia Records]] in 2019, with the label declaring the artist a "huge talent" with "powerful vocals".<ref name="TheBragAug21"/> Kane relocated to Los Angeles and released the 5-track EP ''This Is What It Feels Like''.<ref name="TheBragAug21"/>

In December 2019, Kane released "So I Don't Let Me Down". Upon its release, Kane said that song is about having to accept the fact that life is constantly changing – for better or worse.<ref name="NME radar"/> 

In 2020, Kane provided vocals for Dutch DJ [[Martin Garrix]]'s single "[[Drown (Martin Garrix song)|Drown]]". 

On 19 February 2021, Kane released "[[Chicken Tendies]]",<ref>{{cite web |url=https://music.apple.com/au/album/chicken-tendies-single/1552789478/ |title=Chicken Tendies by Clinton Kane on Apple Music |publisher=[[Apple Music]] |date=19 February 2021 |access-date=24 August 2021}}</ref> which peaked at number 83 on the [[UK Singles Chart]] and 88 on the US [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]] chart. Kane told ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' that the song is "about accepting things and relationships I can't change".<ref name="TheBragAug21"/><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/9535578/clinton-kane-interview-chicken-tendies-emerging-artists-spotlight|title=When Words Fail Clinton Kane, His Music Speaks: Emerging Artists Spotlight|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|date=5 March 2021|access-date=25 August 2021}}</ref>

On 20 August 2021, Kane released "[[I Guess I'm in Love]]", which peaked at number 22 on the UK Singles Chart and 90 on the US ''Billboard'' Hot 100 chart.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://music.apple.com/au/album/i-guess-im-in-love-single/1576261616/ |title=I Guess I'm in Love by Clinton Kane on Apple Music |publisher=[[Apple Music]] |date=20 August 2021 |access-date=24 August 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite tweet|user=billboardcharts|number=1432415301329735687|title=@clintonkane's "I Guess I'm In Love" debuts at No. 90 on this week's #Hot100.|date=30 August 2021|access-date=31 August 2021}}</ref>
