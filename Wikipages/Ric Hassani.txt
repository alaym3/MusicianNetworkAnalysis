'''Ikechukwu Eric Ahiauzu''' better known as '''Ric Hassani''' (born  on 6 January 1989, in [[Port Harcourt]]<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.naija.ng/1147833-ric-hassani-biography.html#1147833|title=Ric Hassani bio and interesting facts|last=Agu|first=Zain|work=Naija.ng - Nigeria news|date=February 14, 2018|accessdate=July 14, 2018}}</ref>) is a Nigerian singer, songwriter and musician. <ref name="guardian">{{Cite news|url=https://guardian.ng/life/on-the-cover/africas-rising-star-ric-hassani/|title=Africa's Rising Star: Ric Hassani|newspaper=[[The Guardian (Nigeria)|The Guardian]]|date=December 20, 2016|accessdate=July 14, 2018}}</ref> 

== Education ==
Hassani graduated from [[Covenant University]] in [[Nigeria]].<ref>{{Cite web|date=2018-01-27|title=SPOTLIGHT: Simi, Bez... five alternative artistes that are Covenant University alumni|url=https://lifestyle.thecable.ng/covenant-university-artistes/|access-date=2021-03-27|website=TheCable Lifestyle|language=en-US}}</ref> He attended the [[University of Surrey]], where he earned a master's degree in energy economics.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.tribuneonlineng.com/105198/|title=Artiste of the week: Ric Hassani|newspaper=[[Nigerian Tribune]]|date=August 4, 2017|accessdate=July 14, 2018}}</ref>

==Musical career==
Hassani released his debut album ''The African Gentleman'' in 2016. He is said to have a dress style that is influenced by his self-acclaimed stance as a gentleman.<ref>{{Cite web|first=Emeka|last=Nwankwo|url=https://lifestyle.thecable.ng/ric-hassani-port-harcourt-gentleman/|title=FACE-TO-FACE: How the nerdy Port Harcourt boy — Ric Hassani — lost and found himself|work=[[TheCable]] Lifestyle|date=November 25, 2017|accessdate=July 14, 2018}}</ref>

== Awards and Nominations ==
Hassani was nominated in three categories at the 2016 [[All Africa Music Awards]]: Best Artiste in African [[Rhythm and blues|R&B]] and [[Soul music|Soul]], Video of the Year, and Most Promising Artiste.<ref>{{cite web |first=Abiola |last=Solanke |title=Wizkid, Tekno, Brymo lead regional nominees list |url=https://www.pulse.ng/buzz/afrima-2016-wizkid-tekno-brymo-lead-regional-nominees-list-id5416687.html |website=Pulse.ng |date=August 24, 2016 |accessdate=July 20, 2018}}</ref> He also received two nominations at [[The Headies 2016]], including Best Alternative Song for "Gentleman".<ref name="guardian" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.dailytrust.com.ng/news/weekend-mag/why-i-prefer-to-sing-love-songs-ric-hassani/208693.html|title=Why I prefer to sing love songs – Ric Hassani|last=Bivan|first=Nathaniel|date=August 5, 2017|website=[[Media Trust|The Daily Trust]]|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=July 16, 2018}}</ref>

In December 2018, the [[Falz]] and [[Olamide]]-assisted "Believe" won Best Collaboration at the Galaxy Music Awards.<ref>{{cite web |first=Gist |last=Always |title= GMA2018: Full list of winners at Galaxy Music Awards, facial reactions and more |url= https://gistalways.ng/gma2018-full-list-of-winners-at-galaxy-music-awards-facial-reactions-and-more |website=Gist Always |date=December 3, 2018 |accessdate=December 3, 2018}}</ref>
