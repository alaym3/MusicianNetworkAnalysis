'''Jose Villanueva III''' (born January 12, 1982), better known as '''Quest''', is a [[Filipino people|Filipino]] singer, [[rapper]]<ref>[https://www.bandwagon.asia/articles/watch-filipino-rapper-quest-spits-fire-during-his-live-performance-at-barber-shop-by-timbre Filipino rapper QUEST spits fire during his live performance at Barber Shop by Timbre]</ref> and songwriter, known for his song "Sige Lang" in 2012 which is the theme song for the basketball team [[Gilas Pilipinas]].<ref>[http://hollywoodinvasian.com/quest-mv-sige-lang/ Quest, M/V “Sige Lang” ], Hollywood Invasian-Magazine Premium ''Retrieved September 2012''</ref><ref>[http://www.gannsdeen.com/2012/06/14/quest-sige-lang/ Quest, “Sige Lang”-Filipino recording artist Jose Villanueva III, better known by his stage name, Quest], Filipino-Christian Blogger Ganns Deen ''Retrieved September 2012</ref> Quest is a [[Pinoy hip hop|hip-hop]] and [[Contemporary R&B|R&B]] singer wherein he won 'Best Urban Music Video for 2010' at the MYX Awards Philippines for his first single "Back to Love".<ref>[http://www.philstar.com/entertainment/2012/08/01/833618/quests-sige-lang-climbs-charts Quest's Sige Lang climbs the charts], The Philippine Star ''Retrieved August 1, 2012''</ref>

==Early life==
Born '''Jose Villanueva III''' on January 12, 1982 in [[Quezon City]], and he is the son of Joel Villanueva (father) and grandson of Jose Villanueva Sr.
Sibling; Ana, Mary Adelene, Ana Cassandra, Michaela, and Nalina Sky. Mother: Diana Villanueva.

==Career==
Quest honed his craft as a lead vocalist and music ministry director at the Fort location of the [[Congregational church|Christian church]] [[Victory (church)|Victory]] in [[Manila]].<ref>Bonifacio, Tinna (July 2010)[http://www.abs-cbnnews.com/entertainment/07/27/10/new-rb-artist-collaborates-sam-milby New R&B artist collaborates with Sam Milby ], ABS-CBN Publishing ''Retrieved September 2012''</ref> He left in 2007 to pursue a full-time career in music. In 2009, Quest started recording Flip Music with Jumbo de Belen without abandoning his [[contemporary Christian music]] roots. He soon collaborated with Filipino actor and singer [[Sam Milby]], whom he met in church, in marketing and producing his debut album.<ref>(November 1, 2010 )[http://www.gannsdeen.com/2010/11/01/quest-back-to-love/ Quest, “Back to Love”-Filipino Contemporary Christian rapper and vocalist], Filipino-Christian Blogger Ganns Deen. ''Retrieved 2012''</ref>

In 2015, Quest signed a recording deal with [[Warner Music Group|Warner Music Philippines]]. His second album, ''Life of a Champion'', was released on May 20, 2016.
