{{nihongo|'''Jin Hashimoto'''|橋本 仁|Hashimoto Jin}} is a Japanese singer and vocal trainer. He is best known for his contributions to Japanese television series' theme songs, such as his debut as the vocalist for the ending theme of ''[[Kamen Rider Kuuga]]''.

After joining an [[Earth, Wind & Fire]] tribute band at age 22, he formed the band BETCHIN' with [[m.c.A.T|Akio Togashi]] and Hiroaki "Tommy" Tominaga in 1998 and together they released two albums. He made his solo debut in 2000 with the song "Aozora ni Naru" for ''[[Kamen Rider Kuuga]]''{{'}}s soundtrack. He performed a series of other songs on the ''Kuuga'' soundtrack, as well as its successor ''[[Kamen Rider Agito]]'' and ''[[Kamen Rider Hibiki]]''. His performance is one of the influences of [[Project.R|Hideyuki Takahashi]], who had success in other theme song performances.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.animate.tv/news/detail.php?id=atv080626d|title=オリコンチャート初登場4位を達成した『炎神戦隊ゴーオンジャー』のOP主題歌を歌う高橋秀幸さんに直撃インタビュー！|publisher=アニメイト|accessdate=2012-05-05|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080804071757/http://www.animate.tv/news/detail.php?id=atv080626d|archivedate=2008-08-04}}</ref>

In 2014, Hashimoto performed what might be his most well known performance, the vocals on "[[Stand Proud]]" and "[[JoJo Sono Chi no Kioku ~end of THE WORLD~|JoJo Sono Chi no Kioku]]," the opening theme songs for ''[[JoJo's Bizarre Adventure: Stardust Crusaders]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://jojo-animation.com/contents/cd/op.html|title=CD -TVアニメ『ジョジョの奇妙な冒険 スターダストクルセイダース』公式サイト|publisher=Jojo-animation.com|date=|accessdate=2014-04-20|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150415064400/http://jojo-animation.com/contents/cd/op.html|archive-date=2015-04-15|url-status=dead}}</ref>
