'''Charlotte Sarah Lawrence'''<ref name="VarietyCharlotte"/><ref name="BrightonHall"/> (born June 8, 2000)<ref name="VarietyCharlotte"/> is an American singer-songwriter and model.

== Early life ==
Lawrence was born and raised in [[Los Angeles]].<ref name="VarietyCharlotte"/><ref name=":1">{{Cite news|url=https://wwd.com/eye/people/charlotte-lawrence-young-ep-music-instagram1202726708-1202726708/|title=Milkshake Break With Charlotte Lawrence|last=Nordstrom|first=Leigh|date=2018-06-22|work=WWD|access-date=2018-07-16|language=en-US|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180716165656/https://wwd.com/eye/people/charlotte-lawrence-young-ep-music-instagram1202726708-1202726708/|archive-date=2018-07-16|url-status=live}}</ref> She is the daughter of actress [[Christa Miller]] and television producer [[Bill Lawrence (TV producer)|Bill Lawrence]].<ref name="w magazine">{{cite web |last1=Marine |first1=Brooke |title=Slide Into Charlotte Lawrence's DMs If You Must, But Make It Funny |url=https://www.wmagazine.com/story/charlotte-lawrence-instagram-musician-model |website=W Magazine |accessdate=16 April 2019 |language=en |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190321054003/https://www.wmagazine.com/story/charlotte-lawrence-instagram-musician-model |archive-date=21 March 2019 |url-status=live }}</ref> Lawrence is the great-niece of actress [[Susan Saint James]]. Her paternal great-great-great grandfather was the real estate mogul [[William Van Duzer Lawrence]], who was of Dutch descent.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1999/12/05/style/weddings-vows-christa-miller-william-lawrence-iv.html|title=WEDDINGS: VOWS; Christa Miller, William Lawrence IV|first=Lois Smith|last=Brady|date=December 5, 1999|via=NYTimes.com|access-date=November 11, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191111194420/https://www.nytimes.com/1999/12/05/style/weddings-vows-christa-miller-william-lawrence-iv.html|archive-date=November 11, 2019|url-status=live}}</ref> She began singing as a child.<ref name="coveteur" /> Lawrence attended [[Marymount High School]] in Los Angeles, where she was a member of volleyball and basketball teams.<ref>{{cite web| url = https://www.mhs-la.org/page/news-detail?pk=758665| title = MARYMOUNT ATHLETICS HONORS STANDOUT SAILORS| date = 24 November 2014| website = Marymount Los Angeles| access-date = 5 January 2019| archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20190106010726/https://www.mhs-la.org/page/news-detail?pk=758665| archive-date = 6 January 2019| url-status = live}}</ref>

== Career ==
Lawrence is signed to [[IMG Models]] and has appeared in magazines such as ''[[Teen Vogue]]'' and ''[[Harper's Bazaar]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.fendi.com/us/fisfor/categories/faces/meet-charlotte-lawrence|title=FENDI {{!}} Meet Charlotte Lawrence|last=Fendi|website=Fendi Online Store|language=en-US|access-date=2018-06-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180616030156/https://www.fendi.com/us/fisfor/categories/faces/meet-charlotte-lawrence|archive-date=2018-06-16|url-status=live}}</ref> In March 2018, she featured on [[Kaskade|Kaskade's]] single "Cold as Stone".<ref name="kaskade - billboard">{{cite web |last1=Schiller |first1=Rebecca |title=Kaskade Talks New Single 'Cold as Stone,' Being Musically Inspired by Brazil: Watch |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/8260996/kaskade-cold-as-stone-ultra-interview |website=Billboard |accessdate=16 April 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190426061837/https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/8260996/kaskade-cold-as-stone-ultra-interview |archive-date=26 April 2019 |url-status=live }}</ref> In June 2018, she released her debut [[Extended play|EP]] ''Young''.<ref name="coveteur">{{cite web |last1=Taylor |first1=Jodi |title=Charlotte Lawrence Is About to Have a Meteoric Rise to the Top of the Charts |url=http://coveteur.com/2018/09/14/new-wave-indie-pop-singer-charlotte-lawrence/ |website=Coveteur |accessdate=16 April 2019 |date=14 September 2018 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190416153758/http://coveteur.com/2018/09/14/new-wave-indie-pop-singer-charlotte-lawrence/ |archive-date=16 April 2019 |url-status=live }}</ref>

In January 2021, Lawrence announced her second EP titled ''Charlotte'', which was subsequently released on March 5, 2021.<ref name=CharlotteEP>{{cite news |last1=Richards, Will |title=Charlotte Lawrence shares 'Talk You Down' video and details new EP |url=https://www.nme.com/news/music/charlotte-lawrence-shares-talk-you-down-video-and-details-new-ep-2868517 |access-date=31 January 2021 |work=[[NME]] |date=January 30, 2021}}</ref>
