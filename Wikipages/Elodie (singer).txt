'''Elodie Di Patrizi''' (born 3 May 1990), known professionally as simply '''Elodie''' ({{IPA-it|eloˈdi|lang}}), is an Italian singer and model. She first came to prominence as the runner-up of the fifteenth season of the show ''[[Amici di Maria De Filippi]]''.

Since 2015 she had published three studio albums, which entered the top 10 of the Italian Albums Chart, and numerous successful singles. She collaborated with numerous Italian artists including [[Emma Marrone]], [[Michele Bravi]], [[Fabri Fibra]], [[Mahmood (singer)|Mahmood]], [[The Kolors]] and [[Marracash]] and with [[Daddy's Groove]], as a producer. She has sold over 965.000 copies certified in Italy and received a [[MTV Europe Music Award for Best Italian Act]] nomination. Elodie participated at [[Sanremo Music Festival]] two times with "Tutta colpa mia" (2017) and "[[Andromeda (Elodie song)|Andromeda]]" (2020).

Throughout her career, Elodie has also become the face of numerous fashion and beauty brands, including [[Versace]], [[Sephora]], [[Puma (brand)|Puma]], [[Lancome]], [[Calvin Klein (company)|Calvin Klein]], [[Bulgari]] and [[OVS (company)|OVS]].

==Biography and career==
Elodie was born on 3 May 1990 in [[Rome]], to an Italian father and a [[French people|French]] mother of [[Creole peoples|Creole]] descent from [[Guadeloupe]]. After ending her modelling career, she took part in season three of ''[[X Factor (Italian TV series)|X Factor Italy]]''. She was eliminated in the early stages of the competition, and then moved to [[Apulia]].<ref name=":0">{{cite web |last=Traversa|first=Fabio |url=http://realityshow.blogosfere.it/post/710383/elodie-di-patrizi-amici-2016-passato-difficile-esclusa-provini-xfactor-papa-senza-lavoro-mamma-malata-storia-damore-finita|title=Elodie di Patrizi e il passato difficile: esclusa ai provini di X Factor, papà senza lavoro, mamma malata, storia d'amore finita... |website=RealityShow |date=17 May 2016 |accessdate=1 February 2017|language=Italian}}</ref> In 2015, she took part in season fifteen of ''[[Amici di Maria De Filippi]]'', where she placed second.<ref name=":1">{{cite web |last=Presicce|first=Claudia |url=http://www.quotidianodipuglia.it/spettacoli/amici_talent_sergio_elodie_vittoria-1756009.html |title=Amici: ha vinto Sergione "big boy". Seconda Elodie, a lei il premio della critica |publisher=Quotidiano di Puglia |date=26 May 2016 |accessdate=1 February 2017|language=Italian}}</ref>

After her stint on ''Amici di Maria De Filippi'', Elodie signed with [[Universal Music Group|Universal Music Italy]] and released the single "Un'altra vita". The song peaked within the Top 40 in Italy, and was certified gold. It was followed by her debut album, also titled ''Un'altra vita'', which peaked at number-two in Italy.<ref name="Elodie album peak positions in Italy">{{cite web|last=|first=|date=|title=Italian Charts - Elodie - Albums|url=http://www.italiancharts.com/search.asp?cat=a&search=Elodie|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|accessdate=17 October 2020|website=|publisher=Italiancharts.com. Hung Medien}}</ref> Her second studio album, ''Tutta colpa mia'', was released on 17 February 2017.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.radioitalia.it/news/elodie/nuova_uscita/13795_elodie_ecco_la_copertina_di_“tutta_colpa_mia”_in_uscita_il_17_febbraio.php#|title=Elodie: ecco la copertina di "Tutta colpa mia", in uscita il 17 febbraio |website=Radio Italia |date=24 January 2017|accessdate=1 February 2017|language=Italian}}</ref> It was produced by the singer and songwriter [[Emma Marrone]] and peaked at number six on [[Fimi]]'s Chart, making her second consecutive top 10 album.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://italiancharts.com/showitem.asp?interpret=Elodie&titel=Tutta+colpa+mia&cat=a|title=italiancharts.com - Elodie - Tutta colpa mia|website=italiancharts.com|access-date=3 January 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rockol.it/recensioni-musicali/album/6900/elodie-di-patrizi-tutta-colpa-mia|title=√ Quando le canzoni fanno la differenza: la recensione del nuovo album di Elodie|last=s.r.l|first=Rockol com|website=Rockol|language=it|access-date=3 January 2020}}</ref> She also competed in the [[Sanremo Music Festival 2017]] with the song "Tutta colpa mia", which peaked at number 12 on the Italian Singles Chart and was certified platinum.<ref name=":2">{{cite web|url=http://www.ilfattoquotidiano.it/2016/12/13/festival-di-sanremo-2017-al-bano-mannoia-eloide-dai-talent-ai-veterani-ecco-i-big-che-saliranno-sul-palco-dellariston/3255212/|work=[[Il Fatto Quotidiano]]|language=Italian|accessdate=7 January 2017|title=Festival di Sanremo 2017: i cantanti in gara. In May 2018 Elodie collaborated with [[Michele Bravi]] and [[Gué Pequeno]] on the song "Nero Bali".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.radioitalia.it/news/elodie/nuova_uscita/17139_%E2%80%9Cnero_bali%E2%80%9D_il_nuovo_singolo_di_elodie_con_gue_pequeno_e_michele_bravi.php|title="Nero Bali": il nuovo singolo di Elodie con Gué Pequeno e Michele Bravi|website=Radio Italia|access-date=3 January 2020}}</ref> The song becomes the singer's first top ten in the Italian Charts, reaching number 10.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://recensiamomusica.com/classifiche-fimi-settimana-34-del-2018-ultimo-piglia-tutto/|title=Classifiche FIMI, settimana 34 del 2018: Ultimo piglia tutto|last=Luisetto|first=Ilario|date=24 August 2018|website=Recensiamo Musica|language=it-IT|access-date=3 January 2020}}</ref> Subsequently, the collaboration received a double platinum certification.<ref name="Richiedi targhetta">{{Cite web|url=https://www.fimi.it/top-of-the-music/certification-form.kl|title=Richiedi targhetta|website=www.fimi.it|language=it-IT|access-date=3 January 2020}}</ref>

The song was cerfied platinum by [[Fimi]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.earone.it/classifiche/classifica_settimanale_storico/|title=EarOne {{!}} Classifica Airplay Radio Settimanale|website=EarOne|access-date=3 January 2020}}</ref> In June 2019 the collaboration "Margarita" with Italian rapper [[Marracash]] was published.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Elodie e Marracash insieme nel nuovo singolo "Margarita"|url=http://news.mtv.it/musica/elodie-e-marracash-insieme-nel-nuovo-singolo-margarita/|last=June 2019|first=12|date=12 June 2019|website=News Mtv Italia|access-date=29 May 2020}}</ref> The song became one of the most successful songs of the 2019 summer in Italy, receiving two platinum certifications and debuting at the number six on the Italian Singles Chart and number one on the Italian Airplay Chart.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.fimi.it/top-of-the-music/classifiche.kl|title=Classifiche - FIMI|website=www.fimi.it|language=it-IT|access-date=3 January 2020}}</ref><ref name="Richiedi targhetta"/>

In January 2020 was announced that Elodie would be in [[Sanremo Music Festival|Sanremo Music Festival 2020]]'s cast with the song "[[Andromeda (Elodie song)|Andromeda]]", which debuted at number 35 after only two days on [[streaming music services]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.corriere.it/spettacoli/cards/sanremo-2020-lista-22-cantanti-gara/achille-lauro_principale.shtml|title=Achille Lauro, Elodie e Piero Pelù: i 22 cantanti in gara a Sanremo|last=Online|first=Redazione|date=31 December 2019|website=Corriere della Sera|language=it|access-date=3 January 2020}}</ref> On 7 January 2020, the singer announced her third studio album "[[This Is Elodie]]", which was released only by streaming music services on 31 January 2020 and in [[CD]] format on 7 February 2020 by [[Island Records]]. The new music project widespread different music genres, from [[R&B]], [[Pop music|pop]], [[funk]] to [[Rapping|rap]] and [[reggae]], and over fifty songwriters and producers, including [[Michele Bravi]], [[Takagi & Ketra]], [[Levante (singer)|Levante]], [[Carl Brave]], [[Mahmood]], [[Neffa]], and [[Daddy's Groove]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rockol.it/recensioni-musicali/album/9072/elodie-this-is-elodie?refresh_ce=|title=√ C'è una nuova Elodie tutta da scoprire|last=s.r.l|first=Rockol com|website=Rockol|language=it|access-date=31 January 2020}}</ref> With only streams, the album debuted at number seven on [[Fimi]]'s chart and was anticipated by two songs "Non è la fine" with [[Gemitaiz]] and "Mal di testa" with [[Fabri Fibra]].<ref name=":3">{{Cite web|url=https://www.rockol.it/news-710309/elodie-nuovo-album-31-gennaio-prima-del-festival-dettagli|title=√ Elodie, mossa inusuale: l'album esce quattro giorni prima del Festival|last=s.r.l|first=Rockol com|website=Rockol|language=it|access-date=8 January 2020}}</ref> The week after the closing of the Sanremo Festival, both the album and the competing song "Andromeda" reached the sixth position of the relative FIMI's charts.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.fimi.it/top-of-the-music/classifiche.kl|title=Classifiche - FIMI|website=www.fimi.it|language=it-IT|access-date=14 February 2020}}</ref>

== Personal life ==
Prior to joining ''Amici di Maria De Filippi'', Elodie was engaged to dj-producer Andrea Maggino.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.radioitalia.it/news/elodie/gossip/17267_elodie_e_lele_si_sono_lasciati%3F_lei_pizzicata_in_discoteca....php#|title=Elodie Relationship|last=|first=|date=|website=|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=}}</ref> During her participation on the show ''Amici'' she met the singer Lele. The two began a relationship in June 2016, which ended two years later.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9xmR3Ikc1r4|title=YouTube|website=www.youtube.com|access-date=1 February 2020}}</ref>

In the summer of 2019, after the release of the hit "Margarita", she started a relationship with the Italian rapper [[Marracash]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://tg24.sky.it/spettacolo/2019/12/31/elodie-marracash-coppia.html|title=Elodie e Marracash, la storia della coppia|last=Cinema|first=Sky|date=31 December 2019|website=tg24.sky.it|access-date=1 February 2020}}</ref>

== Music style and influences ==
In an interview on Fanpage.it Elodie affirms "I'm a girl born in a low-income housing project, that's why rap, naked and raw, is a good representation of where I grew up, and I like that." and cites young Italian rappers Babe, Chadia and [[Madame (rapper)|Madame]] as examples of "women of my generation who are not afraid to show this side. We are fierce."<ref>{{Citation|title=This is Elodie: "È un momento importante per noi donne, quello di Amadeus solo un errore"|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bWF01ghUu9A|language=en|access-date=1 February 2020}}</ref> In an interview with [[Rolling Stone|Rolling Stone Italy]], Elodie speaks about her influences: [[Etta James]], [[Raf (singer)|Raf]], [[Kaytranada]], [[Nina Simone]], [[Beyoncé]] and italian singers [[Giorgia (singer)|Giorgia]], [[Mina (Italian singer)|Mina]] and [[Lucio Battisti]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.it/digital-cover/elodie-niente-canzoni-damore/499500/|title=Elodie, niente canzoni d’amore|date=30 January 2020|website=Rolling Stone Italia|language=it-IT|access-date=1 February 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|title=Sanremo 2020, Elodie: "Sono una tosta e rivendico la mia indipendenza"|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=doUc62b2X2w|language=en|access-date=2021-04-06}}</ref> She also cities ''Amici'''s coaches [[Emma Marrone]] and [[Elisa (Italian singer)|Elisa]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.novella2000.it/emma-marrone-elisa-ed-elodie-sono-state-strepitose-ad-amici-con-la-loro-interepretazione-di-la-cura-pazzesche/|title=Emma Marrone, Elisa ed Elodie sono state strepitose ad Amici con la loro interpretazione di La cura di Battiato|last=Alessi|first=Roberto|date=3 April 2016|website=Novella 2000|language=it-IT|access-date=1 February 2020}}</ref>

Her music is inspired by [[pop music|pop]], [[R&B]], [[rap music|rap]], [[reggae]], and [[urban music|urban]] music.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rockol.it/recensioni-musicali/album/9072/elodie-this-is-elodie?refresh_ce=|title=√ C'è una nuova Elodie tutta da scoprire|last=s.r.l|first=Rockol com|website=Rockol|language=it|access-date=1 February 2020}}</ref>
