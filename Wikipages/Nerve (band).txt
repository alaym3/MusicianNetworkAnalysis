'''Nerve''' is an American, New York based band, founded by the drummer [[Jojo Mayer]].

== History ==
From the 1990s, Jojo Mayer participated actively in party events called "Prohibited Beatz" which was a platform for experimental interaction with DJ's and many kind of artists.<ref name="auto1">{{Cite web|url=http://www.jojomayer.com/about/|title=ABOUT|date=2 June 2014|website=Jojomayer.com|access-date=17 November 2021}}</ref><ref>(Filmmaker and DJ) Julie Covello's movie "Prohibited Beats" which was shown in [[2002 New York Underground Film Festival]]</ref> The band Nerve initially emerged from these parties,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1999/01/10/archives/pulse-dancing-in-their-seats.html|title=PULSE; Dancing in Their Seats|first=Kevin|last=Bisch|date=10 January 1999|access-date=17 November 2021|website=Nytimes.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.villagevoice.com/1999/01/19/doing-it-live/|title=Doing It Live|date=19 January 1999|website=Villagevoice.com|access-date=17 November 2021}}</ref> described as an "endeavor in reverse engineering the textures and rhythms of the current stream of computer generated music into a live performed, improvisational format".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.drummerworld.com/drummers/JOJO_Mayer.html|title=Jojo Mayer|website=Drummerworld.com|access-date=17 November 2021}}</ref> While the line-up of musicians in Nerve was initially fluid, it solidified in the early 2000s around Jojo Mayer, Takuya Nakamura, John Davis and [[Roli Mosimann]], and then again in 2014 around Mayer, Davis, Jacob Bergson and Aaron Nevezie.<ref name="auto1"/><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://drummagazine.com/jojo-mayer-john-davis-of-nerve/|title=Jojo Mayer & John Davis of Nerve|website=Drummagazine.com|date=13 March 2018|access-date=17 November 2021}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://drummagazine.com/jojo-mayer-and-nerve-go-acoustic-on-after-the-flare/|title=Jojo Mayer And Nerve Go Acoustic On 'After The Flare'|website=Drummagazine.com|date=12 April 2019|access-date=17 November 2021}}</ref><ref name="auto">{{cite AV media |date=May 31, 2018 |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_mdZDp3yuq4 |title=Jojo Mayer – Changing Time (Documentary in English) {{!}} Sternstunde Musik {{!}} SRF Kultur |publisher=SRF Kultur |via=[[YouTube]] |access-date=August 24, 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210812160022/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_mdZDp3yuq4&gl=US&hl=en |archive-date=August 12, 2021 |url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://jazzineurope.mfmmedia.nl/2018/04/prince-of-drums-jojo-mayernerve-euro-tour/|title=Prince of Drums – Jojo Mayer/Nerve – Euro Tour. |website=Jazzineurope.mfmmedis.nl|access-date=17 November 2021}}</ref>

In 2016, [[Schweizer Radio und Fernsehen]] produced and televised a documentary film which chronicles several Nerve concerts in Zurich, London, Tokyo and Hong Kong, and which features interview footage with Mayer and others pertaining to the origins and history of Nerve.<ref name="auto"/><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.srf.ch/sendungen/sternstunde-musik/alexis-amitirigala|title=Sternstunde Musik - Alexis Amitirigala|date=30 October 2017|website=Srf.ch|access-date=17 November 2021}}</ref>

With the current lineup of Mayer, Davis, Bergson and Nevezie, Nerve has maintained a constant touring schedule across the United States, Europe, Asia and Canada.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.timeout.com/croatia/music/jojo-mayer-nerve|title=Jojo Mayer / Nerve|website=Timeout.com|access-date=17 November 2021}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.mcall.com/entertainment/mc-allentown-jazz-fest-jojo-mayer-symphony-hall-20150430-story.html|title=Spacey new sounds ground drummer Jojo Mayer and Nerve|first=Dave|last=Howell|website=Mcall.com|access-date=17 November 2021}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thehindu.com/entertainment/music/jojo-mayer-and-the-nerve-come-to-town/article21076875.ece|title=Hitting a nerve|first=Anurag|last=Tagat|date=29 November 2017|accessdate=17 November 2021|website=Thehindu.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.vervemagazine.in/arts-and-culture/jojo-mayer-drums-his-way-to-the-percussion-loving-shores-of-india|title=Jojo Mayer Drums His Way To The Percussion-Loving Shores Of India &#124; Verve Magazine|date=8 December 2017|website=Vervemagazine.in|access-date=17 November 2021}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thewildcity.com/features/14702-jojo-mayer-nothing-new-can-be-created-without-deviating-from-the-norm|title=Jojo Mayer: ‘Nothing New Can Be Created Without Deviating From The Norm’|website=Thewildcity.com|access-date=17 November 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite AV media |date=March 9, 2016 |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YQa1TSlsl5c |title=Jojo Mayer & Nerve - Full Performance I Babylon Performance |publisher=Babylon |via=[[YouTube]] |access-date=August 24, 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210131111821/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YQa1TSlsl5c |archive-date=January 31, 2021 |url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite AV media |date=June 11, 2018 |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SIahbUYxdfE |title=Jojo Mayer Nerve - Live at A38 Ship 2018 |publisher=A38 Hajó |via=[[YouTube]] |access-date=August 24, 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201101165114/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SIahbUYxdfE |archive-date=November 1, 2020 |url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite AV media |date=September 18, 2020 |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6muS5lg-9B8 |title=Jojo Mayer & Nerve LIVE - Drumeo Festival 2020 |publisher=Drumeo |via=[[YouTube]] |access-date=August 24, 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210131140557/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6muS5lg-9B8 |archive-date=January 31, 2021 |url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite AV media |date=September 8, 2017 |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pfVIiln27gk |title=Jojo Mayer/Nerve - Terminal Music&Arts Festival, Sombor 2017 // Full concert |publisher=Terminal Music&Arts Festival Sombor |via=[[YouTube]] |access-date=August 24, 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210205101103/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pfVIiln27gk |archive-date=February 5, 2021 |url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.katjazz.com.np/jojo-mayer-nerve/|title=Jojo Mayer &#124;
