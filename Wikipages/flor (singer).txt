'''Florencia "Flor" Caserta''' (born April 13, 1984) is an [[Argentina|Argentine]] female [[pop music|pop]] and [[rock music|rock]] singer.

==Biography==
Caserta was born in [[El Palomar, Buenos Aires|El Palomar]], [[Buenos Aires Province|Buenos Aires]]. She met Pablo Romero, singer of the band {[ill|Árbol (band)|lt=Árbol|es|Árbol (banda)}}, who heard her singing and invited her to make a disc of [[cover versions|covers]] from different bands from El Oeste (the western zone of [[Greater Buenos Aires]]). It was there that she recorded her first song, “Mañana en el Abasto”, by [[Sumo (band)|Sumo]].

After that, they proposed that she record some demos of pop music. The process lasted two years, until they obtained five songs which they presented to producer [[Gustavo Santaolalla]].

==Discography==
<!-- Deleted image removed: [[Image:Flor disco.jpg|thumb|right|Flor, first album by this argentine female singer|200px]] -->
Flor's first album is the self-titled ''Flor'' (2006). Tracks include:

*"La Respuesta" (The Answer)
*"No, nada" (No, nothing)
*"Aire" (Air)
*"Igual" (Same One)
*"Sal" (Get Out)
*"Girar" (To Turn)
*"Así" (Like This)
*"¿Qué será?" (What Will Be?)
*"Jazmín"

"Jazmín" was the album's first single. "La Respuesta" was its second single.
