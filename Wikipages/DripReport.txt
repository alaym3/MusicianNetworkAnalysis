== Early life and career ==
''DripReport'' Report's parents emigrated from [[Pakistan]] to [[America]]. His age, real name, and face remain unknown. He has completed his high school and later dropped out of college to focus on his music career. 

He started his music career in 2018 by making an [[Instagram]] page. He started with making remixes of popular rap/hip-hop songs and eventually launched his debut single in January 2020. His song [[Skechers (song)|Skechers]] was a big hit. The song is very catchy and it went on social media platforms especially on [[TikTok]], where more than 2.7 million videos were made using this track. The song has also crossed 100 million streams on [[Spotify]]. == CEO of Remixes ==
He is also known as CEO of Remixes, because he can turn any song into a funny meme song. Most of the videos on his channel are remixes. He started his [[YouTube channel]] with the name of '''SoleDrip'''. On this channel, he started uploading remixes of popular songs. His popular remix of all is the funny remix of [[The Box (Roddy Ricch song)|The Box]] song by [[Roddy Ricch]].
