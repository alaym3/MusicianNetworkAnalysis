'''Up10tion''' ({{ko-hhrm|업텐션|}}; commonly stylized as '''UP10TION'''; pronounced ''eobtensyeon'' for "up tension", [[acronym]] for Unbelievable Perfect 10 members Teenager Idol Open Now<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bandwagon.asia/articles/a-guide-to-the-best-artist-names-in-k-pop|title=A guide to the best artist names in K-Pop|date=2018-04-01|website=Bandwagon|language=en|access-date=2019-09-26}}</ref>) is a South Korean [[boy band]] formed by [[TOP Media]] in 2015. The group consists of ten members: Jinhoo, Kuhn, Kogyeol, [[Lee Jin-hyuk]], Bitto, [[Kim Woo-seok (singer)|Kim Woo-seok]], Sunyoul, Gyujin, Hwanhee, and Xiao. The group debuted with an [[extended play]] ''[[Top Secret (Up10tion EP)|Top Secret]]''. In 2017, they made their Japanese debut with EP ''ID''. 

==History==

=== Pre-debut ===
On July 2015, Up10tion introduced its members through a parody of [[Munhwa Broadcasting Corporation|MBC]]'s [[King of Mask Singer]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://japakomusic.com/uptention-debut-through-king-of-mask-singer-parody/|title=Up10tion debut through 'King of Mask Singer' parody|website=Japako Music|author=Martina|date=July 28, 2015|access-date= 2016-07-27|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160807015724/http://japakomusic.com/uptention-debut-through-king-of-mask-singer-parody/|archive-date=August 7, 2016|url-status=dead|df=mdy-all}}</ref>

=== 2015: Debut with ''Top Secret'' and ''Bravo!'' ===
On September 9, Up10tion held a debut showcase at AX Concert Hall in [[Seoul]] which 500 fans attended.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Park|first=So-young|date=2017-05-18|title=500 fans attend "So Dangerous" showcase|url=http://www.itopgroup.com/bbs/board.php?bo_table=up10tion_news&wr_id=8&page=52|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2021-01-09|website=itopgroup}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Park|first=So-young|date=2015-09-10|title=Up10tion held "So Dangerous" showcase|url=http://osen.mt.co.kr/article/G1110243944|access-date=2021-01-09|website=Osen}}</ref> Up10tion's debut mini album ''Top Secret'', which includes the debut single "So, Dangerous", was released on September 11, 2015.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://vibe.naver.com/album/583152|title=一級秘密 (일급비밀) VIBE(바이브)|website=Naver Vibe|access-date=2016-05-12}}</ref> The album debut at number 9 and peaked at number 7 on Gaon Album Chart.<ref>{{Cite web|title=국내 대표 음악 차트 가온차트!|url=http://gaonchart.co.kr/main/section/chart/album.gaon?nationGbn=T&serviceGbn=&targetTime=39&hitYear=2015&termGbn=week|website=gaonchart.co.kr|access-date=2016-01-30}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=국내 대표 음악 차트 가온차트!|url=http://gaonchart.co.kr/main/section/chart/album.gaon?nationGbn=T&serviceGbn=&targetTime=42&hitYear=2015&termGbn=week|website=gaonchart.co.kr|access-date=2016-01-30}}</ref> They made their official music program debut on September 10 through [[Mnet (TV channel)|Mnet]]'s [[M Countdown|M! Countdown]], performing their debut single "So, Dangerous".<ref>{{Cite web|title=Girls' Generation win #1 + Performances from September 10th 'M! Countdown'! {{!}} allkpop.com|url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2015/09/performances-from-september-10th-m-countdown|website=www.allkpop.com|access-date=2016-01-30}}</ref>{{Unreliable source?|date=December 2020|certain=y}} They also had their debut album showcase in Beijing, China on September 22.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Kim|first=Ji-yeon|date=2015-09-23|title=Up10tion debuts in China|url=http://enews24.tving.com/news/article?nsID=880468|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2021-01-27|website=news24}}</ref> The "So, Dangerous" music video was ranked 9th place in [[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'s Most Viewed K-pop Videos in America for the month of September 2015.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Benjamin|first=Jeff|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/k-town/6729434/most-viewed-k-pop-videos-mvs-america-world-september-2015|title=Most Viewed K-Pop Videos in America & Around the World: September 2015|website=Billboard|date=October 13, 2015|access-date=2016-05-18}}</ref>

On November 26, Up10tion released their second mini album ''Bravo!'' with the lead single "Catch Me!", which was produced by Iggy and Yong Bae, who produced Ailee's "Heaven" and [[GFriend|GFriend's]] "Me Gustas Tu". It also includes the track "Party2nite", which was written and composed by [[Teen Top]]'s [[Changjo]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://vibe.naver.com/album/601157|title=BRAVO! VIBE (바이브)|website=Naver Vibe|access-date=2016-05-12}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/100242/up10tion-displays-affection-for-teen-tops-changjo|title=KPOP NEWS - Up10tion Displays Affection for Teen Top′s Changjo {{!}} Mwave|website=mwave.interest.me|access-date=2016-05-18|url-status=dead}}</ref> The album peaked at number 5 on Gaon Album Chart.<ref>{{Cite web|title=국내 대표 음악 차트 가온차트!|url=http://gaonchart.co.kr/main/section/chart/album.gaon?nationGbn=T&serviceGbn=&targetTime=50&hitYear=2015&termGbn=week|website=Gaon Chart|access-date=2016-01-30}}</ref>

=== 2016: ''Spotlight'', ''Summer Go!'' and ''Burst''===
Up10tion released their third mini album ''[[Spotlight (EP)|Spotlight]]'' with the lead single "Attention" on April 18.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://vibe.naver.com/album/631648|title=SPOTLIGHT VIBE(바이브)|website=Naver Vibe|access-date=2016-05-12}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Son|first=Ji-hyoung|url=http://kpopherald.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=201604181447441201106_2|title=Up10Tion releases 'Attention'|website=Kpopherald|date=April 18, 2016|access-date=2016-05-18}}</ref>

On August 5, Up10tion released their fourth mini album ''[[Summer Go!]]'' with the lead single "Tonight".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://vibe.naver.com/album/654636|title=Summer go! VIBE(바이브)|website=Naver Vibe|access-date=October 12, 2021}}</ref>

Up10tion released their fifth mini album ''[[Burst (Up10tion EP)|Burst]]'' with the lead single "White Night" on November 21.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Jeon|first=Hyo-jin|url=https://sports.donga.com/3/all/20161110/81258939/2|title=업텐션, 오늘만 사는 강렬 카리스마 눈빛…'버스트' 발표|website=Sports Donga|access-date=October 12, 2021|date=10 November 2016}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Park|first=Sae-jin|url=http://eng.ajunews.com/view/20161121093318104|title=Boy group UP10TION releases music video for "White Night"|website=Aju Business Daily|date=21 November 2016|access-date=October 12, 2021}}</ref>

=== 2017: Japanese debut, Wooseok's hiatus, ''Star;dom'' and ''2017 Special Photo Edition'' ===
Up10tion released their Japanese debut single "ID" (アイディー) on February 27.<ref>https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=c3H-mz3PzkA</ref> They launched their first Japan tour titled "Zepp Tour 2017 ID" in May.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Top|first=Media|date=2017-05-10|title=Up10tion 1st Japan tour!|url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=420&aid=0000010768|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2021-02-06|website=Naver}}</ref>

On June 6, TOP Media issued a statement regarding Wooseok's hiatus.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://itopgroup.com/bbs/board.php?bo_table=up10tion_notice&wr_id=189|title=&#91;공지&#93; 업텐션 우신군 활동에 대한 공지}}</ref> It was stated that his psychological condition had worsened due to the mental stress that he had been receiving since the end of 2016, due to a controversy that alleged he had inappropriately touched his "The Show" co-host, [[Jeon So-mi]], near her chest area during a video. There were official statements released by both parties' agencies that denied these claims,<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://m.entertain.naver.com/read?oid=382&aid=0000529607|title = 전소미·우신 측 "논란이 될 스킨십 일체 이뤄지지 않았다"}}</ref> along with the production staff from "The Show".<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://m.entertain.naver.com/read?oid=144&aid=0000468354|title = '더쇼' 측 "우신-소미에 큰 상처…진심으로 사과"&#91;전문 포함&#93;}}</ref>

With Wooseok on hiatus, Up10tion continued their activities as nine members. They released their sixth mini album ''[[Stardom (EP)|Star;dom]]'' on June 29. The EP contained six tracks, including lead single "Runner". On October 12, the group released ''[[2017 Special Photo Edition]]'', containing two tracks, including lead single "Going Crazy".<ref>http://www.newsinside.kr/news/articleView.html?idxno=474553</ref>

=== 2018: "Wild Love", Wooseok's return, first album, concert tours, Chaser, 2018 Special Photo Edition and ''Laberinto''===
On January 20, Up10tion released their second Japanese single album "Wild Love".

On March 15, Up10tion released their first studio album ''[[Invitation (Up10tion album)|Invitation]]'' with title track "Candyland" which contain 10 other tracks. Wooseok returned to the group which ended his 8 month hiatus.<ref>http://www.topstarnews.net/news/articleView.html?idxno=379067</ref>

In May, Up10tion started their first tour "Up10tion Japan Live Tour 2018".<ref>https://sports.donga.com/3/all/20180507/89966075/1</ref> In June, the group started their second tour, "Up10tion 1st US Meet & Live Tour "Candyland" in 8 cities.<ref>{{cite tweet|number=974535605844459521|user=studio_pav|title=StudioPAV is proud to present...|date=16 March 2018}}</ref>

On August 8, Up10tion released third japan single album "Chaser".<ref>https://sports.donga.com/3/all/20180807/91403280/1</ref> On August 20, Up10tion released "[[2018 Special Photo Edition]]" with the single "So Beautiful".<ref>http://www.topstarnews.net/news/articleView.html?idxno=467985</ref>

On September 19, Up10tion started their third tour, "Up10tion Europe Tour".<ref>https://tenasia.hankyung.com/topic/article/2018080194374</ref>

On December 6, Up10tion released their seventh mini-album ''[[Laberinto (UP10TION EP)|Laberinto]]'', with the lead single "Blue Rose".<ref>https://sports.donga.com/3/all/20181203/93125056/1</ref>

=== 2019: ''Produce X 101'' Participation, Jinhyuk and Wooseok's hiatus, and ''The Moment of Illusion'' ===
On May 31, Up10tion started their 4th tour "Laberinto Tour in North America" without Wooseok and Jinhyuk.<ref>https://www.instagram.com/p/BvYX73_g7nS/?hl=en</ref>
Jinhyuk and Wooseok were contestants in [[Produce X 101]], and on July 19, Wooseok became a member of the new boy group of [[X1 (band)|X1]]<ref>{{Cite web|date=2019-07-19|title=Produce X 101 confirm X1's line-up as 11 new K-pop idols are revealed|url=https://metro.co.uk/2019/07/19/produce-x-101-confirm-x1s-line-11-new-k-pop-idols-revealed-10427832/|access-date=2019-07-19|website=Metro|language=en-US}}</ref> with its debut in August but since then X1 disbanded due to Mnet's voting manipulation.<ref>https://koreajoongangdaily.joins.com/news/article/article.aspx?aid=3072300</ref>

On August 22, Up10tion released their eighth mini-album ''[[The Moment of Illusion (EP)|The Moment of Illusion]]'' with the lead single "Your Gravity",<ref>http://news.kmib.co.kr/article/view.asp?arcid=0013630805&code=61181111&cp=nv</ref> which was promoted without Wooseok and Jinhyuk.<ref>https://www.vop.co.kr/A00001429973.html</ref> Jinhyuk meanwhile debuted solo in November.

=== 2020: Jinhoo's hiatus, ''Light Up'' and member's COVID-19 positive cases ===
On April 7, TOP Media announced Jinhoo will go on a hiatus due to health concerns. They also announced the group will be having a summer album but it didn't happen until the comeback was announced late August.<ref>https://www.soompi.com/article/1392855wpp/up10tion-announces-plans-for-7-member-comeback-jinhoo-to-take-break-due-to-health-concerns</ref>

Up10tion's ninth mini-album, ''[[Light Up (EP)|Light Up]]'' with the lead single "Light", was released on September 24.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/311/0001197470|title=업텐션, 새 앨범 'Light UP' 1차 스펙트럼 포토 공개…7인조 활동 개시|website=entertain.naver.com|language=ko|access-date=2020-09-03}}</ref> On November 23, it was announced that Jinhoo would begin his [[Conscription in South Korea|mandatory military service]] on the same day.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=311&aid=0001231270|title=업텐션 측 "진후, 오늘(23일) 육군 현역으로 입대…응원 부탁드린다" [공식입장]|website=entertain.naver.com|language=ko|access-date=2020-11-23}}</ref>

On November 27, Up10tion released the single "Destiny" which was part of their ''Light Up'' album.<ref>http://www.m-i.kr/news/articleView.html?idxno=769833</ref>

On November 29, Bitto tested positive for [[Coronavirus disease 2019|COVID-19]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Son|first=Jin-ah|date=November 30, 2020|script-title=ko:업텐션 비토, 코로나19 양성 판정…확진자와 접촉|trans-title=Up10tion Bitto tested positive for Corona 19... Contact with confirmed patient|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/410/0000746527|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201130063604/https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/410/0000746527|archive-date=November 30, 2020|access-date=November 30, 2020|website=My Daily|language=ko|via=Naver}}</ref> On December 1, Kogyeol also tested positive for COVID-19.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Kim|first=Minji|date=December 1, 2020|title=업텐션 고결, 비토 이어 코로나19 확진…몬스타엑스 민혁·아이즈원 '음성'(종합)|trans-title=Up10tion Kogyeol, Bitto followed Corona 19 confirmed... Monsta X's Minhyuk and Iz*one 'Voice' (general)|url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=421&aid=0005023193|access-date=December 1, 2020|website=News1|language=ko|via=Naver}}</ref> On December 15, Xiao tested positive for COVID-19.<ref>https://www.anewsa.com/detail.php?number=2306243&thread=05</ref>

As of December 30, all 3 members have recovered from COVID-19.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://post.naver.com/viewer/postView.nhn?volumeNo=30233579&memberNo=38506&vType=VERTICAL| title= Bitto tested negative for COVID-19|date=2020-12-15|website=Naver|language=ko|access-date=2020-12-30}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.joynews24.com/view/1331147| title= Xiao and Kogyeol recover from COVID-19, return to normal life|date=2020-12-30|website=Joy News 24|language=ko|access-date=2020-12-30}}</ref>

=== 2021–present: ''Connection'' ===
On June 14, Up10tion returned with their second studio album ''[[Connection (Up10tion album)|Connection]]'' and its lead single "Spin Off".<ref>{{Cite web|last=Kim|first=Na-young|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/now/article/410/0000788981|title=업텐션, 6월 14일 정규 앨범 'CONNECTION'으로 컴백 (공식)|work=Sports MK|publisher=Naver|language=ko|date=May 27, 2021|access-date=2021-05-27}}</ref>
