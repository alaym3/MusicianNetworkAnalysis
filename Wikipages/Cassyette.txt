'''Cassyette''' is a British rock singer and songwriter
Her debut single "Jean" was released 6 September 2019. She has been described by [[Clash Magazine]] as a [[Nu Metal]] "pop provocateur" <ref>https://www.clashmusic.com/news/pop-provocateur-cassyette-says-suck-it</ref> and "The electro-pop princess to keep on your radar".<ref>[http://www.hungertv.com/feature/the-muse-meet-cassyette-the-electro-pop-princess-to-keep-on-your-radar/ www.hungertv.com/feature/the-muse-meet-cassyette-the-electro-pop-princess-to-keep-on-your-radar/] – Hunger Magazine – Retrieved 15 March 2018. "The Muse"</ref>
In [[Rankin (photographer)|Rankin]]'s Hunger Magazine. [[MTV]] say "her style is hardcore, women-fuelled rock". "Cassyette is the brand new up and coming rockstar".<ref>http://www.mtv.co.uk/cassyette/news/get-to-know-cassyette</ref>

 
== Early life ==
Cassyette was born in [[Chelmsford]] [[Essex]]. She attended [[Brentwood Ursuline Convent High School]], later graduating with a degree in commercial music.
Cassyette began her career as a DJ and songwriter. "After graduating from [[University of Westminster]] Cassyette became a staple on the London live circuit".<ref>[http://www.thelineofbestfit.com/new-music/song-of-the-day/cassyette-push-n-pull www.thelineofbestfit.com/new-music/song-of-the-day/cassyette-push-n-pull] l – The Line Of Best Fit – Retrieved 5 March 2018. Cassyette unveils stunning ‘80s-tinged pop debut "Push N Pull"</ref>

== Career ==
<ref>[http://www.galoremag.com/casseyette-new-music-zone/ https://galoremag.com/casseyette-new-music-zone/] – Galore Magazine – Retrieved 6 April 2015. "Cassyette talks music and getting in the zone"</ref>

Songwriting for various artists and bands alongside her own music, Cassyette merges pop and [[Rock music|rock]] inspired sounds with modern-day [[electronic pop]]. Cassyette's first solo debut was labelled "A seriously well-crafted, addictive gem that should underline Cassyette's reputation as not only one of London's most relevant new artists, but a consummate beat maker and songwriter".<ref>https://nijimagazine.com/2018/03/push-n-pull-is-the-amazing-debut-release-from-london-based-producer-dj-cassyette/{{Dead link|date=July 2020 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} – Niji Magazine – Retrieved 15 March 2018.</ref>

Cassyette has most recently appeared at [[Download Festival|Download]] Pilot festival,<ref>{{Citation|title=Cassyette - Download Pilot Festival 2021|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wic_tNRXsFM|language=en|access-date=2021-06-21}}</ref> as her first festival debut as well as making a guest appearance alongside [[Frank Carter and the Rattlesnakes|Frank carter and the rattlesnakes]] on their headlining slot.<ref>{{Cite web|title=What happened when Frank Carter & The Rattlesnakes headlined Download Pilot|url=https://www.kerrang.com/features/what-happened-when-frank-carter-the-rattlesnakes-headlined-download-pilot/|access-date=2021-06-21|website=Kerrang!}}</ref>
