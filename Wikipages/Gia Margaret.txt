'''Gia Margaret''' is an American singer-songwriter and producer from Chicago, Illinois. She was trained in classical piano as a child and began pursuing a degree in music before ending both endeavors because they were “creatively limiting”.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Julious |first1=Britt |title=Gia Margaret's Notoriety Is a Happy Accident |url=https://www.chicagotribune.com/entertainment/music/ct-chicago-music-local-sounds-ott-0310-20170307-story.html. |website=Chicago Tribune |accessdate=7 March 2020}}</ref> Margaret has had various jobs prior to becoming a musician, including a nanny, book store clerk, and dental assistant.<ref name="chicagomagazine">{{cite web |last1=Hyman |first1=Dan |title=You Should Know Gia Margaret |url=http://www.chicagomag.com/Chicago-Magazine/November-2018/You-Should-Know-Gia-Margaret/ |work=[[Chicago (magazine)|Chicago]] |accessdate=August 11, 2019}}</ref> She released her first full length album, There’s Always Glimmer, in July of 2018. She describes the genre of her music as “sleep rock,” and has been compared to everyone from Imogen Heap to Postal Service.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Farrell |first1=Margaret |title=Gia Margaret: There's Always Glimmer |url=https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/gia-margaret-theres-always-glimmer/|website=Pitchfork |accessdate=7 March 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Geffen |first1=Sasha |title=Listen to 'Birthday' by Gia Margaret |url=https://pitchfork.com/reviews/tracks/gia-margaret-birthday/
 |website=Pitchfork |publisher=Pitchfork |accessdate=7 March 2020}}</ref> Margaret has been steadily rising in popularity in the music industry and has 464,994 monthly listeners on Spotify as of March 7, 2020.<ref>{{cite web |title=Gia Margaret |url=https://open.spotify.com/artist/7fnMav7xXJwwjbyWbSjF4C 
 |website=Spotify |publisher=Spotify Technology S.A. |accessdate=7 March 2020}}</ref>

== Touring ==
In 2019, she toured around the United States with Welsh alternative folk singer-songwriter [[Novo Amor]].<ref>{{cite web |last1=Kenneally |first1=Cerys |title=Gia Margaret and Novo Amor Team up for Collaborative Tracks 'No Fun' and 'Lucky for You.' |url=https://www.thelineofbestfit.com/news/latest-news/gia-margaret-novo-amor-collaborate-on-two-tracks-no-fun-and-lucky-for-you |website=The Line of Best Fit |accessdate=7 March 2020}}</ref> In September of that year, she went on her first headlining tour in the UK.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Kenneally |first1=Cerys |title=Gia Margaret Announces First Headline UK Tour |url=https://www.thelineofbestfit.com/news/latest-news/gia-margaret-announces-first-headline-uk-tour |website=The Line of Best Fit |accessdate=7 March 2020}}</ref> Margaret was selected to perform at [[SXSW]] 2019, a music festival in Austin, Texas, and was later selected by NPR to be part of the Austin 100.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Thompson |first1=Stephen |title=The Austin 100: Gia Margaret |url=https://www.npr.org/2019/03/05/696688221/the-austin-100-gia-margaret |website=NPR |publisher=NPR |accessdate=7 March 2020}}</ref> This is a playlist of “a hundred handpicked highlights from among the thousands of acts playing SXSW 2019”.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Thompson |first1=Stephen |title=The Austin 100 |url=https://www.npr.org/2019/03/05/695993809/the-austin-100-a-2019-sxsw-mixtape |website=NPR |publisher=NPR |accessdate=7 March 2020}}</ref>

== Discography ==

Margaret has released one full length album, 2018’s There’s Always Glimmer. This album was re-released on May 24, 2019 with two additional tracks, “Smoke (Acoustic)” and “Babies”.<ref>{{cite web |title=There's Always Glimmer (Deluxe Version) |url=https://genius.com/albums/Gia-margaret/There-s-always-glimmer-deluxe-version |website=Genius |publisher=Genius Media Group Inc. |accessdate=7 March 2020}}</ref> Beyond her LP, she released an [[Audiotree]] live album on November 14, 2018 that included live performances of six songs.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Margaret |first1=Gia |title=Gia Margaret on Audiotree Live |url=https://audiotree.bandcamp.com/album/gia-margaret-on-audiotree-live |website=Audiotree |publisher=Bandcamp |accessdate=7 March 2020}}</ref> Margaret’s song “Birthday” was remixed by artists Ryan Hemsworth and swim good now and released on streaming services on September 3, 2019.<ref>{{cite web |title=Gia Margaret 'Birthday' Remix |url=http://m-chicago.thedelimagazine.com/41685/gia-margaret-birthday-remix?external=1 |website=The Deli Magazine |publisher=Disqus |accessdate=7 March 2020}}</ref> Margaret has two record labels, Orindal in the United States and Dalliance in the UK.<ref>{{cite web |title=GIA MARGARET - There's Always Glimmer |url=http://orindal.limitedrun.com/products/619361 |website=Orindal Records USA |publisher=Orindal |accessdate=7 March 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Artists |url=https://www.dalliancerecordings.com/artists |website=Dalliance Recordings |publisher=Dalliance Recordings |accessdate=7 March 2020}}</ref> While touring with Novo Amor, the two artists recorded and released two songs on February 28 2019, “Lucky for You” and “No Fun”.<ref>{{cite web |title=New Release: Novo Amor And Gia Margaret - No Fun / Lucky For You |url=https://thankfolkforthat.com/new-release-novo-amor-and-gia-margaret-no-fun-lucky-for-you/ |website=Thank Folk For That |accessdate=7 March 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Kenneally |first1=Cerys |title=Gia Margaret and Novo Amor Team up for Collaborative Tracks 'No Fun' and 'Lucky For You" |url=https://www.thelineofbestfit.com/news/latest-news/gia-margaret-novo-amor-collaborate-on-two-tracks-no-fun-and-lucky-for-you |website=The Line of Best Fit |accessdate=7 March 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Sadler |first1=Adam |title=Novo Amor & Gia Margaret: No Fun / Lucky For You |url=http://streamingdept.com/song-reviews/novo-amor-gia-margaret-no-fun-lucky-for-you/ |website=Streaming Dept. |publisher=Streaming Dept. |accessdate=7 March 2020}}</ref> On Spotify, “Lucky for You” has 3.8 million streams and “No Fun” has 2.6 million streams as of March 7, 2020 and remain Margaret’s most streamed songs on the platform. In 2020, she released her second full-length record, "Mia Gargaret"<ref>{{cite web |title=Gia Margaret |url=https://open.spotify.com/artist/7fnMav7xXJwwjbyWbSjF4C |website=Spotify |publisher=Spotify Technology S.A. |accessdate=7 March 2020}}</ref>
