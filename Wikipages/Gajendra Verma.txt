== Early life ==
Gajendra Verma was born on 20 April 1990 in [[Sirsa]], [[Haryana]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://in.bookmyshow.com/person/gajendra-verma/1056471|title=Gajendra Verma – Movies, biography, News, Age & Photos|access-date=19 July 2018}} by his father Surender Verma, a well-known poet and theatre personality.</ref> He was raised in [[Jaipur]], [[Rajasthan]]. His father Surender Verma is a well-known poet and theatre personality.<ref name=":2" />

His elder brother [[Vikram Singh (composer)|Vikram Singh]] is a music producer, music director and composer. His first song "Tune Mere Jaana" was released with support from SONOTEK (Haryana Famous Music Channel).<ref name=":3">{{Cite web|title=Gajendra Verma : Whose "Emptiness" Gave Us Fulfillment|url=https://www.allaboutmen.co.in/gajendra-verma-whose-emptiness-gave-us-fulfillment/|date=2019-08-22|website=AllAboutMen|language=en-US|access-date=2020-05-11}}</ref>

Gajendra Varma started his career in 2008, with the song, "Tune Mere Jaana" also called 'Emptiness'.<ref name=":3" /><ref name=":0" />
