'''In Strict Confidence''' is a [[Germany|German]] [[electro-industrial]] band. Formed in 1989, they were known as ''Seal of Secrecy'' until 1992.<ref name=Bio2001>{{cite web|url=http://www.minuswelt.de/isc/english/band/biografie.shtml|title=In Strict Confidence Official Biography 2001|publisher=In Strict Confidence|access-date=23 March 2018}}</ref>  In Strict Confidence has been signed to several record labels, including [[Metropolis Records]] and [[WTII Records]].<ref>[http://www.wtiirecords.com/artists/artist.php?id=isc/ WTII Records Official Website - In Strict Confidence]</ref><ref>[http://www.facebook.com/pages/WTII-Records-LLC/118624595174 WTII Records Official Facebook page]</ref>

==History==
===Founding 1989–2000===
In Strict Confidence started out as a quartet, but stripped down to a duo (Dennis Ostermann and Jörg Schelte) in 1992,<ref name=Bio2001 /> releasing two demos tapes on the way to their debut release '[[Cryogenix]]' on [[Zoth Ommog]] in 1996 (the initial pressing of this album sold out in one week), shortly followed by an EP ''Collapse''.<ref name=Bio2001 /> The band left Zoth Ommog after releasing follow-up effort ''[[Face the Fear]]'' in 1998, due to payment disputes. The third album ''[[Love Kills!]]'' was released on the newly formed and short lived Bloodline label two years later.<ref name=Bio2001 />

===Band Growth 2001–2010===
Founding member Stefan Vesper then re-joined the group in time for ''[[Mistrust the Angels]]'' in 2002 with subsequent albums ''[[Holy (In Strict Confidence album)|Holy]]'' and ''[[Exile Paradise]]'' appearing at two-year intervals. These albums saw the project's sound diversify further, notably with increasing use of female vocals from Antje Schulz, vocalist of the band [[Chandeen]], and the addition of female vocalist Nina de Lianin<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.minuswelt.de/isc/english/neues/index.shtml|title=In Strict Confidence - NEW FEMALE SINGER "NINA DE LIANIN" |publisher=In Strict Confidence|access-date=22 March 2018}}</ref>

==Side Projects==
Dennis Ostermann has a side project called Controlled Fusion. Stefan Vesper has produced material under his own name and also as Steve Dragon.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.minuswelt.de/isc/english/musik/seitenprojekte.shtml|title=In Strict Confidence Side Projects|publisher=In Strict Confidence|access-date=23 March 2018}}</ref>
