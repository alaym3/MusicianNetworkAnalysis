'''Ion Chirinciuc''', better known by stage name '''Vanotek''', is a Moldovan-born Romanian [[record producer]] and [[disc jockey]].<ref name="global"/> He started producing music for native artists before releasing his debut single "My Heart is Gone" in 2015. The song became successful in Romania, and also brought him a [[MTV Europe Music Award for Best Romanian Act|nomination for Best Romanian Act]] at the [[2016 MTV Europe Music Awards]]. In November 2017, [[Global Records]] distributed his debut studio album ''No Sleep''. Two of its singles, "Tell Me Who" and "Back to Me", reached the charts in Romania and Russia, among other countries.

==Career==
Ion Chirinciuc was born in [[Ungheni]], Moldova.<ref name="interview">{{cite news|url=https://www.unsitedemuzica.ro/special/L-am-descusut-pe-Vanotek-DJ-ul-care-se-teme-de-camera-dar-se-pricepe-la-hituri-p4754|title=L-am descusut pe Vanotek, DJ-ul care se teme de cameră, dar se pricepe la hituri|trans-title=In-depth interview with Vanotek, the DJ who's afraid of the camera but is good at making hits|language=Romanian|publisher=Unsitedemuzica.ro|date=26 March 2018|accessdate=4 March 2019|author=Popescu, Luiza}}</ref> He moved to Romania to study at the Octav Băncilă Art School in [[Iași]], and later relocated to [[Bucharest]] to pursue a career in music. 17 years old at that time, Vanotek went on to produce material for notable artists such as [[Dan Balan]], [[Antonia Iacobescu|Antonia]] and [[Tom Boxer]].<ref name="global">{{cite news|url=https://globalbooking.ro/vanotek-lanseaza-primul-album-no-sleep/|title=Vanotek lanseaza primul album "No Sleep"|trans-title=Vanotek releases his first album "No Sleep"|language=Romanian|publisher=Global Records|date=21 November 2017|accessdate=4 March 2019}}</ref><ref>
{{cite news|url=http://www.europaplus.ru/index.php?go=Performer&id=2246|title=Vanotek feat. Eneli|publisher=Europa Plus|language=Russian|accessdate=4 March 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180630041830/http://www.europaplus.ru/index.php?go=Performer&id=2246|archive-date=2018-06-30|url-status=live}}
</ref> In 2015 he released his debut single, "My Heart is Gone", featuring vocals from Yanka. The song went on to be commercially successful in Romania,<ref name="interview"/> and brought him a [[MTV Europe Music Award for Best Romanian Act|nomination for Best Romanian Act]] at the [[2016 MTV Europe Music Awards]].<ref name="MTV">
{{cite news|url=https://www.mediafax.ro/life-inedit/mtv-europe-music-awards-hahaha-production-intra-cu-3-piese-in-cursa-pentru-best-romanian-act-15744688|title=MTV Europe Music Awards: HaHaHa Production intră cu 3 piese în cursa pentru "Best Romanian Act"|trans-title=MTV Europe Music Awards: HaHaHa Production has three entries for "Best Romanian Act"|language=Romanian|publisher=[[Mediafax]]|date=24 September 2016|accessdate=4 March 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180707124609/http://www.mediafax.ro/life-inedit/mtv-europe-music-awards-hahaha-production-intra-cu-3-piese-in-cursa-pentru-best-romanian-act-15744688|archive-date=2018-07-07|url-status=live}}
</ref> In the same year, Vanotek attempted to represent Romania at the [[Eurovision Song Contest 2016]] in [[Stockholm]], Sweden with "I'm Coming Home", featuring the Code and Georgian. He ultimately [[Romania in the Eurovision Song Contest 2016|finished in second place]] in Romania's selection show ''Selecția Națională''.<ref name="interview"/><ref>{{cite news|url=https://eurovision.tv/story/ovidiu-anton-wins-selectia-nationala-in-romania|title=Ovidiu Anton wins Selecţia Naţionala in Romania!|publisher=[[European Broadcasting Union]]|date=6 March 2016|accessdate=13 February 2019|author=Leon, Jakov|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180513215912/https://eurovision.tv/story/ovidiu-anton-wins-selectia-nationala-in-romania|archive-date=2018-05-13|url-status=live}}</ref> In November 2017, Vanotek released his debut album, ''No Sleep'', distributed by Global Records.<ref name="no sleep">{{cite news|url=https://1music.ro/news/vanotek-se-pregateste-sa-isi-lanseze-album/|title=Vanotek se pregătește să își lanseze album|trans-title=Vanotek releases his debut album|language=Romanian|publisher=1Music|date=21 November 2017|accessdate=4 March 2019|author=Furdui, Mihaela}}</ref> Alongside the latter label, he is also signed by [[Ultra Music]].<ref name="interview"/><ref name="no1"/> The album spawned a string of singles, including "Tell Me Who" and "Back to Me" with Eneli, which attained success in countries such as Romania and Russia.<ref name="no1">

</ref><ref name="tophit"/>

In October 2019 Vanotek released single "Take Me" in collaboration with Ukrainian singer Myata.
