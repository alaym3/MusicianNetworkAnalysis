'''Adel Salah Mahmoud Eid El-Tawil''' (born 15 August 1978) is a German singer, songwriter and producer. Besides his solo career, he is part of the popular German duo [[Ich + Ich]] and a former member of the [[boy band]] [[The Boyz (German band)|The Boyz]].

==Early life==
Tawil was born in Berlin. His father Salah Tawil is [[Egypt|Egyptian]] and his mother is [[Tunisia|Tunisian]]. He is the first born of three, having a younger brother, Hatem, and a sister, Rascha.

==Musical career==
During the end of the 1990s, Tawil was part of the German boyband The Boyz. Since 2004, he and [[Annette Humpe]] formed the duo known as [[Ich + Ich]]. They have released three albums, ''Ich + Ich'' (2005), ''Vom Selben Stern'' (2007) and ''Gute Reise'' (2009).

In 2007, Tawil teamed up with rapper [[Azad (rapper)|Azad]] and released the song "[[Prison Break Anthem (Ich glaub' an dich)]]",<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vDbHYfyPDcM Music video for "Ich Glaub an Dich"]</ref> which is the opening theme for the German version of ''[[Prison Break]]''. He made another solo appearance with [[Tobias Schenke]] for the song "Niemand hat gesagt".

In 2007, Tawil held three spots in the top ten with two released singles and one album.

On 8 November 2013, he released his first solo album, ''Lieder'', in Germany, and also toured in 2014.<ref>[http://www.vorverkaufstarts.de/adel-tawil-tour-tickets/ Adel Tawil Tourdates]. Vorverkaufstarts.de. Retrieved Oktober 24. 2013.</ref>

== Personal life ==
[[File:1 Live Krone 2013 Adel Tawil 1.jpg|thumb|Tavil in 2013]]
In 2011, Tawil married his longtime girlfriend Jasmin Weber in a private ceremony in Berlin.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.stern.de/lifestyle/leute/heimliche-hochzeit-ichich-saenger-adel-tawil-heiratet-1726529.html|title="Ich+Ich"-Sänger Adel Tawil heiratet}}</ref>
In 2014, they were separated. He currently lives with his girlfriend Lena.
