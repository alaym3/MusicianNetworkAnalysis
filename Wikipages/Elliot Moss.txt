'''Elliot Moss''' is a multi-instrumentalist songwriter, producer, and visual artist from [[New York City]]. He slowly released multiple singles from his debut album ''Highspeeds'', released in 2015, including tracks like "Big Bad Wolf", "Slip" and "Highspeeds", which was played, recorded, and mixed primarily by himself.<ref>[http://www.mtv.com/artists/elliot-moss/ Elliot Moss | New Music And Songs | MTV<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref> Moss worked on this specific album since his sophomore year of high school.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://rutvnews.com/2016/10/11/exclusive-elliot-moss-talks-travelling-at-highspeeds/|title=EXCLUSIVE: Elliot Moss talks travelling at 'Highspeeds'|last=News|first=RUtv|date=2016-10-11|website=RUtv News|access-date=2017-02-22}}</ref>

In 2014, Moss started booking shows with his five-man band, covering material from both ''Highspeeds'' and songs from a new album scheduled for release in 2015. Moss has played in support of [[Cold War Kids]], [[Digitalism (band)|Digitalism]], and [[Nada Surf]], and has performed at venues such as [[CMJ]], [[Music Hall of Williamsburg]], and he played at [[SXSW]] in March 2015.<ref>http://www.brooklynvegan.com/tag/Digitalism</ref>

Sean Moeller's ''[[Paste (magazine)|Paste]]'' magazine review of the 2014 CMJ fest called Moss "as great as I wanted him to be," and New Music Nashville described his music as "smooth-as-they-come alternative electronic beats."<ref>[http://www.musicnewsnashville.com/elliot-moss-highspeeds/ Elliot Moss – Highspeeds - Music News Nashville<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>

Hilly Dilly said that the song "just might be the very definition of a hidden gem," and The Musicality described the song as the "definition of a gem. The electro effects on his voice and the dark synths combine to make a track that is a laid back, slow jam with elements of cool jazz."<ref>[http://www.hillydilly.com/2014/04/elliot-moss-slip/ Elliot Moss – 'Slip' | Hillydilly<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref><ref>[http://themusicality.com/chill/2014/4/9/slip Slip — Musicality<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>

In 2014, his music was featured in an episode of the [[MTV]] series ''[[Finding Carter]]''.<ref>[http://www.earmilk.com/2014/10/15/elliot-moss-proves-simple-is-effective-in-new-visuals-for-highspeeds-video-premiere/ Elliot Moss proves simple is effective in new visuals for "Highspeeds" [Video Premiere&#93; - EARMILK<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref> In October, 2014 Moss signed with the booking company AM Only.<ref>[http://www.billboard.com/articles/business/6281470/new-signings-kobalt-pitbull-orchard-donna-summer New Signings: Kobalt Signs Pitbull, Orchard Adds Donna Summer, Blue Note Nabs Jamie Cullum | Billboard<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>

In 2020, "99" was featured in STARZ popular TV show, 'Power Book II: Ghost', Season 1, Episode 2, Exceeding Expectations. '99' plays during Ghost’s funeral scene. It reached Top 50 on SHAZAM following the episode.

In 2021, "Slip" was featured in popular Netflix show, 'Elite'.
