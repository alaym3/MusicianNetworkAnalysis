'''Vianney Bureau''' ({{IPA-fr|vjanɛ byʁo}}; born in [[Pau, Pyrénées-Atlantiques|Pau]] on 13 February 1991), better known by the mononym '''Vianney''', is a French singer-songwriter.<ref>[http://www.la-croix.com/Culture/Actualite/Vianney-un-petit-vent-de-fraicheur-dans-la-chanson-francaise-2015-06-27-1328640 AFP / La Croix: Vianney, un petit vent de fraîcheur dans la chanson française] {{in lang|fr}}</ref> At 24 years old he won the award for performing artist of the year at the "Victoires de la musique 2016"<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.leparisien.fr/musique/en-direct-suivez-la-ceremonie-des-victoires-de-la-musique-a-partir-de-20-h-55-12-02-2016-5540013.php|title=VIDEOS. Victoires de la musique : la jeunesse au palmarès|date=2017-04-26|work=leparisien.fr|access-date=2017-04-26}}</ref> one year after having been named in the up and coming category  of the "Victoires de la musique 2015". His debut album ''Idées blanches'' was certified platinum. His second album was released on 25 November 2016<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.virginradio.fr/vianney-un-second-album-organique-reussi-notre-critique-a575904.html|title=Avec son deuxième album éponyme, Vianney signe un disque organique réussi !|date=2016-11-25|work=virginradio.fr|access-date=2017-04-26|language=fr-FR}}</ref> and was certified double platinum.

==Beginnings==
Born to a father who was a helicopter pilot in the army and a mother who worked in air traffic control, Vianney comes from a family of three brothers.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://next.liberation.fr/musique/2016/12/30/vianney-voix-parallele_1538307|title=Vianney : Voix parallèle|work=Libération.fr|access-date=2017-04-26|language=fr}}</ref> Vianney grew up in music-loving family. Listening to his father sing while playing songs on the guitar by artists such as [[Georges Brassens]], [[François Béranger]], [[Maxime Le Forestier]] and [[Rickie Lee Jones]],<ref name="La Croix">{{Cite news|url=http://www.la-croix.com/Culture/Actualite/Vianney-un-petit-vent-de-fraicheur-dans-la-chanson-francaise-2015-06-27-1328640|title=Vianney, un petit vent de fraîcheur dans la chanson française|date=2015-06-27|work=La Croix|access-date=2017-04-26|language=fr-FR|issn=0242-6056}}</ref> Vianney discovered a passion for music from the age of 12 and began to write songs from then on.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.leparisien.fr/informations/vous-allez-adorer-vianney-02-11-2014-4259475.php|title=VIDEO. Chanson : vous allez adorer Vianney|date=2017-04-26|work=leparisien.fr|access-date=2017-04-26}}</ref> In 2005, his father introduced him to some musician friends with whom he recorded his first track along with his older brother. In 2007, he held his first concert in an auditorium, the New Morning in Paris.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/le-roue-tourne-bertrand-dalle|title=Il y a 10 ans, pour lui, c'était nous les stars... La roue tourne !|last=Dalle|first=Bertrand|date=February 11, 2017|website=LinkedIn}}</ref> He grew up in Paris and studied at Notre-Dame-des-Oiseaux College in the 16th district; where he would write a song of the same name, present on his first album ''Idées blanches''<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.lci.fr/musique/vianney-le-genial-petit-canard-de-la-chanson-francaise-1559497.html|title=Vianney, le génial petit canard de la chanson française|work=LCI|access-date=2017-04-26|language=fr-FR}}</ref>''.'' He then attended the [[Lycée militaire de Saint-Cyr]], where he would spend three years. After his baccalaureate, in 2009, he studied at [[Paris School of Business]], formerly the ESG Management School de Paris, for three more years and later earned a business diploma in London. Finally, he studied styling at [[ESMOD|École supérieure des arts et techniques de la mode]] (ESMOD), graduating in June 2014. He explained his school life by his "desire to live in different ways",<ref name="La Croix"/> and believes that these experiences had "opened [his] mind, socially and artistically".<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.esmod.com/sites/default/files/2014-10-281589l_express.pdf|title=2014-10-281589l_express.pdf|last=Médioni|first=Gilles|date=28 January 2015|work=L'Express|access-date=25 April 2017}}</ref>

==Career==
In 2011, Vianney met Isabelle Vaudey, who became his manager. It was Vaudey who encouraged Vianney to reveal his songs to the general public, and she arranged a meeting with vocalist Antoine Essertier, who offered to produce his debut album, ''Idées blanches'', which was recorded throughout two weeks during summer of 2013 in the [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes|Auvergne]] mountains, not far from [[Vichy]]. The album was realised on 20 October 2014. With the album, he was signed to the "Tôt ou tard" record label in February 2014. His first charting single from the album was "Je te déteste" with the music video directed by Nicolas Davenel. The follow up single "Pas là" became his biggest chart success. The music video was directed by Nicolas Bary and featured [[Lola Bessis]].

He took part in the 30-event tour of [[Florent Pagny]] entitled Vieillir Ensemble, and has recorded with other artists, including Samaha of [[Shaka Ponk]], and then with [[Joyce Jonathan]] in "Les filles d'aujourd'hui". He also recorded an acoustic version of his album ''Idées blanches''.

On 27 January 2015, he won the W9 Talent Award over other nominated acts [[Christine and the Queens]], [[Indila]], [[Black M]], Irma, [[The Dø]], Camélia Jordana, Peter Peter, Brigitte and Adrien Gallo. He was nominated for "Revelation Album of the Year" during [[Victoires de la musique]] 2015. In 2016, he won the "Artiste interprète of the Year" at the same event.
