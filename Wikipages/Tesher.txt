'''Hitesh Sharma''', known professionally as '''Tesher''', is a [[Canadians|Canadian]]<ref>{{Cite tweet |user=teshermusic |number=1402747633433661447 |title=I think I gotta finally clear this up I, Tesher, am a Canadian singer @Wikipedia you can cite this tweet|access-date=2021-08-23|language=en}}</ref> singer. He is best known for his singles "Young Shahrukh" and "[[Jalebi Baby]]", both released in 2020. A remix version of "Jalebi Baby" was released in 2021 with [[Jason Derulo]].

== Life and career ==
===Upbringing===
Raised in [[Regina, Saskatchewan|Regina]], [[Saskatchewan]],<ref name="cbc"/> Tesher was influenced by his Indian heritage as well as his Canadian upbringing.<ref name="regina">{{cite news |title=U of R Business grad scores #1 song, viral hit on TikTok, and major music label partnership |url=https://www.uregina.ca/external/communications/feature-stories/current/2020/07-24.html |access-date=16 August 2021 |publisher=uregina.ca |date=23 July 2020}}</ref> Growing up listening to Indian as well as Western [[hip hop music]], Tesher began to fuse [[Bollywood]] and hip-hop songs together and create mashups from a young age.<ref name="mans world">{{cite web|last1=Mathew|first1=Rajeev|title=Tesher Talks To Us About 'Young Shahrukh,' TikTok Stardom, Bollywood Mashups And More|url=https://www.mansworldindia.com/currentedition/tesher-talks-about-combining-bollywood-classics-with-hip-hop-music-and-more/|website=[[Man's World (magazine)|Man's World]]|date=21 November 2020|access-date=17 May 2021}}</ref> Later, he also began to incorporate [[Bhangra (music)|bhangra]], [[R&B]], [[Pop music|pop]], [[reggaeton]], [[House music|house]], and [[Salsa music|salsa]] music into his songs.<ref name="regina"/><ref name="strand">{{cite web|last1=Mahabaleshwarkar|first1=Ketki|title=Kabhi Khushi, Kabhi Viral - In Conversation with Tesher|url=https://www.thestrandmagazine.com/single-post/2020/10/13/kabhi-khushi-kabhi-viral-in-conversation-with-tesher|website=[[The Strand Magazine]]|date=13 October 2020|access-date=17 May 2021}}</ref>

=== Early mashups and remixes ===
Tesher began uploading his remixes to [[YouTube]] and [[SoundCloud]] when he was in high school.<ref name="regina"/> Around the years 2012 to 2014, he was known in particular for making country remixes.<ref name="strand"/>

In 2016, Tesher produced the official Bhangra mix of "Kar Gayi Chull" from the soundtrack of Bollywood film ''[[Kapoor and Sons]]'' for [[Sony Music India]]. The remix, featuring a snippet of vocals from Tesher's sister, was performed by actress [[Alia Bhatt]] at [[Femina Miss India 2017]].<ref name="strand"/>

Some early mashups he created were [[Drake (musician)|Drake]]'s "[[Back to Back (Drake song)|Back to Back]]" with [[Florence and the Machine]]'s "[[Delilah (Florence and the Machine song)|Delilah]]" (2015),<ref>{{cite news |last1=Frydenlund |first1=Zach |title=This Drake and Florence + The Machine Mashup Makes "Back To Back" Sound Even Better |url=https://www.complex.com/music/2015/08/drake-back-to-back-florence-and-the-machine-mashup-video |access-date=16 August 2021 |publisher=Complex |date=18 August 2015}}</ref> Drake's "KMT" with [[XXXTentacion]]'s "[[Look at Me (XXXTentacion song)|Look At Me]]" (2017),<ref>{{cite news |title=Someone Mashed Up Drake's "KMT" and XXXTentacion's "Look at Me!" |url=https://www.complex.com/music/2017/03/drake-kmt-xxxtentacion-look-at-me-mashup |access-date=16 August 2021 |publisher=Complex |date=22 March 2017}}</ref> and [[Ariana Grande]]'s "[[7 Rings]]" with songs including "[[My Favorite Things (song)|My Favorite Things]]" from ''[[The Sound of Music (film)|The Sound of Music]]'', [[Notorious B.I.G.]]'s "Gimme the Loot" and [[Soulja Boy]]'s "[[Pretty Boy Swag]]" (2010).<ref>{{cite news |last1=Fitzgerald |first1=Trent |title=Here Are the Rap Songs People Say Ariana Grande Stole for Her Song '7 Rings' |url=https://www.xxlmag.com/ariana-grande-rappers-stole-from-mashup-7-rings/ |access-date=16 August 2021 |publisher=XXL |date=23 January 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last1=Darville |first1=Jordan |title=This "7 rings" mashup combines Ariana with Soulja Boy with Princess Nokia with 2 Chainz |url=https://www.thefader.com/2019/01/23/tesher-7-rings-mashup-ariana-grande-soulja-boy-2-chainz-princess-nokia |access-date=16 August 2021 |publisher=The Fader |date=23 January 2019}}</ref>

In 2020, his 2019 remix "Old Town Road vs Ramta Jogi" combining [[Sukhwinder Singh]]‚Äôs "Ramta Jogi" and [[Lil Nas X]]‚Äôs "[[Old Town Road]]" went viral on [[TikTok]] as a sound accompanying a milk drinking challenge.<ref name="regina"/><ref name="cbc">{{cite web|last1=Sciarpelletti|first1=Laura|title=From Prairies to Bollywood: Record deal shows power of TikTok to get diverse artists noticed|url=https://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/saskatchewan/hitesh-tesher-sharma-viral-tiktok-song-young-shahrukh-1.5878975|website=[[CBC.ca]]|date=7 March 2021|access-date=17 May 2021}}</ref>

=== 2020‚Äìpresent: Debut original singles ===
In February 2020, Tesher released "Young Shahrukh", an original rap song he wrote, produced, and performed, with a beat that sampled "Bole Chudiyan" from the soundtrack of the Bollywood film ''[[Kabhi Khushi Kabhie Gham...]]'' According to Tesher, the song's title references [[Shahrukh Khan]], an Indian actor commonly dubbed the "King of Bollywood".<ref name="ctv">{{cite web|last1=Lourenco|first1=Denio|title=Hit TikTok remix elevates Canadian musician Tesher to new heights|url=https://www.ctvnews.ca/entertainment/hit-tiktok-remix-elevates-canadian-musician-tesher-to-new-heights-1.5342557|website=[[CTV News]]|date=10 March 2021|access-date=17 May 2021}}</ref> After the song went viral on social media platforms such as YouTube, Instagram and TikTok, it was picked up by [[Sony Music India]] in May 2020, becoming Tesher's debut major label single.<ref name="cbc"/> "Young Shahrukh" peaked at number one on the BBC [[Official Charts Company|Asian Music Chart]] on the week of July 10, 2020, and at number four on ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'''s Top Triller Global chart on the week of July 25, 2020.<ref>{{cite web|title=Tesher celebrates No.1 spot in UK chart with ''Young Shahrukh''|url=https://www.bizasialive.com/teshar-celebrates-no-1-spot-in-uk-chart-with-young-shahrukh/|website=Biz Asia|date=14 July 2020|access-date=17 May 2021}}</ref><ref name="triller 1">{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/business/chart-beat/9422082/ty-dolla-igns-ego-death-vaults-to-top-triller-u-s-no-1/|title=Ty Dolla $ign's 'Ego Death' Vaults to Top Triller U.S. No. 1|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210525153628/https://www.billboard.com/charts/world-digital-song-sales/2021-05-29|archive-date=May 25, 2021|url-status=live|access-date=July 24, 2020|url-access=subscription}}</ref> Indian celebrities such as [[Ranveer Singh]] and [[Sunny Singh (actor)|Sunny Singh]] praised the song for its fusion of Bollywood with hip-hop.<ref>{{cite web|title="When Bollywood Meets Hip-Hop ü§ôüèª @tesherrrr üëàüèª song killed it üî•" - streams on Spotify.<ref name="cbc"/>

In 2020, Tesher released "Yummy Jalebi", a remix of [[Justin Bieber]]'s "[[Yummy (Justin Bieber song)|Yummy]]", with new vocals and production from Tesher. The song went viral on social media platforms including TikTok and Instagram. In response to popular demand, he released a version that removed "Yummy" and extended his verse to a full original song with his own [[English language|English]] and [[Punjabi language|Punjabi]] vocals.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Baddhan|first1=Lakh|title=In Video: Tesher returns with groovy second single|url=https://www.bizasialive.com/in-video-tesher-returns-with-groovy-second-single/|website=Biz Asia|date=17 November 2020|access-date=17 May 2021}}</ref><ref name="billboard1">{{cite news |last1=Frankenberg |first1=Eric |title=The Jason Derulo Effect: Tesher's 'Jalebi Baby' Scales Global Charts Thanks to Remix |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/business/chart-beat/9604474/tesher-jalebi-baby-scales-global-charts-remix |access-date=16 August 2021 |publisher=Billboard |date=21 July 2021}}</ref> The new song, entitled "[[Jalebi Baby]]", was released as his second single on November 13, 2020. The single amassed over 100 million streams and peaked at number one on the UK Asian Music Chart for seven consecutive weeks.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.officialcharts.com/charts/asian-music-chart/20210514/asian/|title=Asian Music Chart Top 40|publisher=[[Official Charts Company]]|date=14 May 2021|access-date=17 May 2021}}</ref> It also topped the [[Shazam (application)|Shazam]] charts in over 25 countries.<ref name="360derulo">{{cite news |title=Tesher x Jason DeRulo |url=https://www.the360mag.com/tesher-x-jason-derulo-jalebi-baby/ |access-date=16 August 2021 |publisher=360}}</ref>

In 2021, Tesher signed a worldwide deal with [[Capitol Records]] in partnership with [[Universal Music India]], which saw his music released through a unique partnership between Capitol in the U.S. and Universal Music India, who will focus on building his popularity across South Asia and its diaspora.<ref name="360derulo"/> On May 28, Tesher released a remix of "Jalebi Baby" with American singer [[Jason Derulo]].<ref name="360derulo"/><ref name="capitol">{{cite web|last1=Mathew|first1=Rajeev|date=1 June 2021|title=Tesher and Jason Derulo Release Reimagined 'Jalebi Baby'|url=https://www.capitolrecords.com/tesher-and-jason-derulo-release-reimagined-jalebi-baby/|access-date=9 June 2021|website=[[Capitol Records]]}}</ref> Following the release of the remix, the track reached number 3 on Billboard's [[World Digital Song Sales]] chart, and reached the US Top 40 pop radio chart.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Cantor |first1=Brian |title=Songs By Lil Nas X & Jack Harlow, Maneskin, Bazzi, Twenty One Pilots, Tesher & Jason Derulo Make Top 40 At Pop Radio |url=https://headlineplanet.com/home/2021/08/01/songs-by-lil-nas-x-jack-harlow-maneskin-bazzi-twenty-one-pilots-tesher-jason-derulo-make-top-40-at-pop-radio/ |access-date=16 August 2021 |publisher=Headline Planet}}</ref> Tesher and Derulo performed the song live on ''[[Today (American TV program)|Today]]'' on NBC on July 7, 2021,<ref>{{cite news |last1=Sciarpelletti |first1=Laura |title=Saskatchewan musician Tesher launches music video with Jason Derulo as 'Bollywood Hollywood duo' |url=https://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/saskatchewan/hitesh-tesher-sharma-viral-song-jason-derulo-jalebi-baby-1.6099738 |access-date=16 August 2021 |publisher=CBC |date=13 July 2021}}</ref> and released the music video on July 13, 2021.<ref name="billboard1"/>
