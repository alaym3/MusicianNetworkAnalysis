'''Edgar Wasser''' (born September 22, 1990 in [[Chicago]]) is a German [[rapper]] from Munich.<ref name="erziehungskunst.de">{{cite web |url=http://www.erziehungskunst.de/artikel/stockholmsyndrom-und-naechstenliebe-gespraech-mit-einem-rapper/ |title=Stockholmsyndrom und Nächstenliebe. Gespräch mit einem Rapper |publisher=erziehungskunst.de |access-date=2015-06-13}}</ref>

== Early life ==
Edgar Wasser was born in Chicago but moved to Munich with his parents at the age of three. He completed his [[Abitur]] at a [[Waldorf school]].<ref name="erziehungskunst.de" /> He has publicly performed as a rapper since 2007 <ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.klatsch-tratsch.de/2013/05/24/edgar-wasser-ist-die-neue-deutsche-hip-hop-hoffnung/164002 |title=Edgar Wasser ist die neue deutsche Hip-Hop-Hoffnung |publisher=Klatsch-tratsch.de |date=2013-05-24 |access-date=2014-12-25}}</ref> and has published a series of free [[extended play|EP]]s and [[mixtape]]s online.

== Music career ==
[[Blumentopf]] booked him as support for their tour. Wasser has released most of his music free of charge, collaborating with other members of the German rap scene, most notably Cap Kendricks. In 2013 he joined Cap Kendricks, X, Phil Harmony, Paul and DJ Mic-e to form the band Dopeboy.<ref name="laut">{{cite web|title=Edgar Wasser: Portrait|url=http://www.laut.de/Edgar-Wasser|website=laut.de|accessdate=24 August 2015}}</ref>

After releasing the third installment of his ''Edgar Wasser Freetrack Collection'', in 2013 he published the album ''Nocebo'' with {{ill|Fatoni|de}} of Creme Fresh.<ref name="laut" /> Furthermore, he appeared at the 2013 [[Splash! (festival)|Splash! Festival]].

On November 14, 2014, he released his debut commercial album, the ''Tourette-Syndrom EP'', with the German label ''Regenbogenpinguin''. The record was distributed by [[Soulfood]] and peaked at #45 on the German album charts.<ref name=m1>{{cite web|last1=Winkel|first1=M.C.|title=Edgar Wasser – "Tourette Syndrom EP" (Album Stream + Videos)|url=http://www.whudat.de/edgar-wasser-tourette-syndrom-ep-album-stream-videos/|website=whudat.de|accessdate=24 August 2015|date=25 November 2014}}</ref>

== Musical style ==
Edgar Wasser utilizes elements of [[old-school hip hop]] as well as new-wave German rap. Unlike many German rappers, Edgar Wasser puts little emphasis on image or street credibility, revealing little of his private life. His texts are influenced by [[Conscious Rap]] and stand out due to his humour, which employs [[cynicism (contemporary)|cynicism]], [[sarcasm]] and [[irony]]. His texts often allude to [[hip hop|Hip-Hop-culture]] and various hip hop cultures, ranging from German student rap to [[Gangsta rap]].<ref name="laut" />
