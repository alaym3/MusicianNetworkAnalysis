'''Gerard Jan Joling''' (born 29 April 1960 in [[Schagen]]) is a Dutch singer and television presenter. Known for his high [[tenor]] voice, he rose to fame in the late 1980s and released a string of singles including "Ticket to the Tropics" and "Love Is in Your Eyes". He also achieved success in Asia{{citation needed|date=September 2020}} and received more than 20 gold and platinum records.

In 1988, he was the Dutch participant in the [[Eurovision Song Contest 1988]], with the song "[[Shangri-La (Gerard Joling song)|Shangri-La]]".

His biggest hit was "[[No More Boleros]]" that reached the top 10 in several countries in mainland Europe. The song was recorded by different artists such as Clemente (Portugal), Sula Mazurenga and As Marcianas (both in Brazil), [[Karel Gott]] (in German and Czech), [[Demis Roussos]], [[Semino Rossi]], [[Oliver Thomas (singer)|Oliver Thomas]] and [[George Meiring]] (in 2011 in South Africa).

''Joling & Gordon Over de Vloer'' is a television program Gerard Joling made with singer [[Gordon Heuckeroth|Gordon]] in 2005. There were 3 seasons of the series which featured the duo during work and having a laugh.

In 2007, he was the host of ''[[Sterren dansen op het ijs]]'' and ''[[So You Wanna Be a Popstar (Netherlands)|So You Wanna Be a Popstar]]'' for the television channel [[SBS 6]]. 2007 was also the year that sparked Joling's renewed success, with two number 1 songs, a number 1 album, and 11 gold and platinum awards.

In 2008, Joling became team captain on the Dutch TV show ''Wie ben ik?'' ("''Who am I?''"), in which the team captains, together with both two guests, have to guess who they are, based on hints and questions they can ask. The other team captain was [[Patty Brard]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://radio.nl/54048/gerard-joling-in-vernieuwde-wie-ben-ik|title=Gerard Joling in vernieuwde Wie Ben Ik?|website=Radio.NL|language=nl-NL|access-date=28 September 2019}}</ref>

Joling was scheduled to represent The Netherlands at the [[Eurovision Song Contest 2009]] in Moscow as a member of [[De Toppers]], but temporarily left the group after a conflict with Gordon.<ref>https://eurovisionary.com/eurovision-news/gordon-leaves-toppers {{Bare URL inline|date=May 2021}}</ref> Joling was replaced in The Toppers with [[Jeroen van der Boom]]. In December 2009, De Toppers manager Benno de Leeuw announced in Dutch newspaper ''[[De Telegraaf]]'' that Gerard Joling and Gordon were to rejoin the group, turning the trio into a foursome once more.<ref>Gerard Joling and Gordon back in De Topper. [http://www.dutchnews.nl/news/archives/2009/12/gerard_joling_and_gordon_back.php Dutchnews.nl]</ref>

Joling can also regularly be seen as a member of juries on a myriad of Dutch television programs. In 2018 he was seen in ''[[The Voice Senior (Dutch TV series)|The Voice Senior]]''<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.telegraaf.nl/entertainment/2147236/gerard-joling-en-gordon-coaches-bij-the-voice-senior|title=Gerard Joling en Gordon coaches bij The Voice Senior|date=8 June 2018|website=De Telegraaf|language=nl|access-date=28 September 2019}}</ref> and in 2019 in the Dutch version of the program ''[[The Masked Singer]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rtlboulevard.nl/entertainment/tv/artikel/4648161/gerard-joling-het-nieuwe-jurylid-van-masked-singer|title=Gerard Joling het nieuwe jurylid van The Masked Singer|date=19 March 2019|website=RTL Boulevard|language=nl|access-date=28 September 2019}}</ref>
