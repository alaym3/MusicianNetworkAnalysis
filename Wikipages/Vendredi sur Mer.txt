'''Charline Mignot''' (born 20 April 1995), better known as '''Vendredi sur Mer''' (French for "Friday by the Sea"), is a [[Swiss people|Swiss]] singer-songwriter and photographer.

== Biography ==
Charline Mignot was born in [[Romandy]], a [[Francophone]] part of [[Switzerland]], where she lived until she moved to [[Lyon]] where she attended an art school. She began her career as a [[photographer]], working notably with a French luxury goods manufacturer, [[Hermès]]. Inspired by a meeting with her manager who appraised her homemade song made with a friend, she decided to put her first steps in the music industry.<ref name="Lesinrocks"/> She settled in [[Paris]]<ref name="Lesinrocks"/> and adopted a pseudonym ''Vendredi sur Mer''. "I wanted a name that invites you to a voyage, to poetry, to reverie," she explained, adding that it's the sea that she draws most inspiration from.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://i-d.vice.com/fr/article/xwyg9z/vendredi-sur-mer-quand-lelectro-pop-francaise-fait-de-la-prose|title=vendredi sur mer, quand l'électro-pop française fait de la prose|first=Antoine|last=Mbemba|date=July 13, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://magazineantidote.com/musique/qui-est-vendredi-sur-mer-jeune-groupe-electro-pop-suisse/|title=Qui est Vendredi sur Mer, jeune groupe électro-pop suisse ?|date=November 15, 2017}}</ref>

Composed by [[Lewis OfMan]],<ref name="Lesinrocks">{{Cite web|url=https://www.lesinrocks.com/actu/rencontre-avec-charline-du-beau-projet-vendredi-sur-mer-120979-10-10-2017/|title=Rencontre avec Charline du beau projet Vendredi sur Mer – Les Inrocks|first=Contenu|last=Partenaire}}</ref> her first EP ''Marée basse'' was released in November 2017. In 2019, she released her first studio album called ''Premiers émois''.

== Reception ==
The cultural magazine ''[[PopMatters]]'' likened Mignot's work to that of the French band [[La Femme (band)|La Femme]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.popmatters.com/vendredi-sur-mer-premiers-emois-2632861529.html|title=Vendredi sur Mer Brings a Breath of Summer with 'Premier Émois'|access-date=2021-11-21|website=[[PopMatters]]}}</ref>
