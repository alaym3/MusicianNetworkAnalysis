'''Ocean Park Standoff''' is an American pop band made up of [[Samantha Ronson]], Pete Nappi and Ethan Thompson. Their debut EP was released on [[Hollywood Records]] on March 3, 2017.

== History ==
The three band members originally met at a songwriting session in 2014.<ref name="hli">Huimin Li, [http://monkeygoosemag.com/2017/01/album-review-ocean-park-standoff/ "Album Review – Ocean Park Standoff"], ''Monkeygoose Magazine'', January 21, 2017.</ref> Ronson is an English musician/DJ and the sister of DJ/producer [[Mark Ronson]]; Ethan Thompson is an American vocalist and songwriter, and Pete Nappi is an American drummer/producer.<ref>[http://www.billboard.com/artist/7542532/ocean-park-standoff/biography Ocean Park Standoff Biography], ''Billboard''. Accessed March 3, 2017.</ref> The band was formed in 2016.<ref>Mark Small, [https://www.berklee.edu/berklee-today/summer-2016/pop-hit-makers "Pop Hit Makers"], ''Berklee Today'', Summer 2016.</ref><ref>{{cite news |last1=Nordstrom |first1=Leigh |title=Ocean Park Standoff Doesn’t Need to Release an Album |url=https://wwd.com/eye/people/ocean-park-standoff-samantha-ronson-music-group1202739363-1202739363/ |accessdate=17 July 2018 |publisher=Women's Wear Daily |date=28 June 2018}}</ref>

Their debut single "[[Good News (Ocean Park Standoff song)|Good News]]" was released on October 7, 2016,<ref>[http://www.stitchkingdom.com/disney-samantha-ronsons-new-band-ocean-park-standoff-teases-good-news-87430/ "Samantha Ronson's New Band, 'Ocean Park Standoff' Teases 'Good News' for October 7, 2016"], Stitch Kingdom, October 7, 2016.</ref> along with its B-side, "Photos & Liquor".<ref name="hwilliams">Holly Williams, [http://www.contactmusic.com/dj-samantha-ronson/news/samantha-ronson-ocean-park-standoff_5547268 "What We Know So Far About Samantha Ronson's New Band Ocean Park Standoff"], Contact Music, January 26, 2017.</ref> "Good News" reached number 13 on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Adult Top 40|Adult Pop Songs]] chart,<ref name="billboardchart">[http://www.billboard.com/music/ocean-park-standoff/chart-history/adult-pop-songs Ocean Park Standoff Chart History], ''Billboard''. Accessed May 22, 2018.</ref> and was on [[Amazon (company)|Amazon]]'s Most Requested Lyrics list of 2017.<ref>[https://www.billboard.com/biz/articles/news/digital-and-mobile/8070385/amazon-music-alexas-top-lyric-request-for-2017-was-for "Amazon Music: Alexa's Top Lyric Request in 2017 Was For a 'Roley, Roley, Roley'"], ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'', December 13, 2017.</ref>

Their debut EP was released on [[Hollywood Records]] on March 3, 2017.<ref>RJ Frometa, [http://ventsmagazine.com/2017/01/27/ocean-park-standoff-announces-upcoming-tour-dates/ "Ocean Park Standoff Announces Upcoming Tour Dates"], ''Vents Magazine'', January 27, 2017.</ref> On February 2, 2018, they released their second single, "Lost Boys (Ocean Park Standoff vs Seeb)", in collaboration with [[Seeb (music producers)|Seeb]].<ref>[http://weraveyou.com/2018/02/seeb-lost-boys/ "Ocean Park Standoff vs. Seeb - Lost Boys"], weraveyou.com, February 2, 2018.</ref> On April 27, 2018, they released the single "If You Were Mine" featuring [[Lil Yachty]].<ref>Abby Jones, [https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8390292/ocean-park-standoff-lil-yachty-if-you-were-mine-premiere "Ocean Park Standoff Blends Genres With Lil Yachty-Assisted Banger 'If You Were Mine'"], ''Billboard'', April 26, 2018.</ref> The video for "If You Were Mine" premiered on July 12, 2018.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Skelton |first1=Eric |title=Premiere: Lil Yachty Joins Ocean Park Standoff at a Dive Bar in "If You Were Mine" Video |url=https://www.complex.com/music/2018/07/ocean-park-standoff-lil-yachty-video |accessdate=17 July 2018 |publisher=Complex |date=12 July 2018}}</ref>

==Band members==
* [[Samantha Ronson]] - keys, guitar, vocals 
* Pete Nappi - drums 
* Ethan Thompson - vocals

==Performances==
Ocean Park Standoff embarked on a US tour starting in February 2017.<ref name="hwilliams"/> In February 2017, the band was chosen as [[Elvis Duran]]'s [[Elvis Duran's Artist of the Month|Artist of the Month]], and performed their hit "Good News" live on February 22 on [[NBC]]'s ''[[Today (U.S. TV program)|Today]]'' show hosted by [[Kathie Lee Gifford]].<ref>[http://www.today.com/video/watch-ocean-park-standoff-perform-new-single-good-news-live-on-today-882413635708 "Watch Ocean Park Standoff perform new single ‘Good News’ live on TODAY"], ''[[Today (U.S. TV program)|Today]]'', February 22, 2017.</ref> In the summer of 2017, the band went on a US tour with [[Third Eye Blind]] and [[Silversun Pickups]].<ref>Vrinda Jagota, [http://www.papermag.com/chatting-with-ocean-park-standoff-about-their-new-ep-playing-live-and--2377007456.html "Samantha Ronson's New Band, Ocean Park Standoff, on Learning to Play Live and Hearing the Good News"], ''[[Paper (magazine)|Paper]]'', April 27, 2017.</ref> The band performed on June 29, 2017 at Bear's Den Showroom inside [[Seneca Niagara Casino & Hotel]] for listeners of Star 102.5 ([[WTSS]]) in Buffalo, New York. Starting in February 2018, the band opened for [[AJR (band)|AJR]] on The Click Tour.<ref>[https://www.etix.com/ticket/p/2551093/ajr-the-click-tourwith-ocean-park-standoffhundred-handed-portland-roseland-theater&cobrand=cascadetickets "AJR - The Click Tour, with Ocean Park Standoff, Hundred Handed"] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180207005459/https://www.etix.com/ticket/p/2551093/ajr-the-click-tourwith-ocean-park-standoffhundred-handed-portland-roseland-theater%26cobrand%3Dcascadetickets |date=2018-02-07 }}, etix.com. Retrieved February 6, 2018.</ref> On April 30, 2018, the band performed "If You Were Mine" on ''[[Conan (talk show)|Conan]]''.<ref>[http://www.fltimes.com/ocean-park-standoff-if-you-were-mine---conan/youtube_a7ca32d6-11fd-5f35-838b-6f2727d86f30.html "Ocean Park Standoff 'If You Were Mine' 04/30/18 - CONAN on TBS"], ''Finger Lakes Times'', May 1, 2018.</ref> The band went on tour with [[Plain White T's]] in the summer of 2018.<ref>Lauren Tullio, [https://www.altpress.com/news/entry/august_burns_red_european_2018_tour_dates "Catch up on news from August Burns Red, Seaway, Emo Nite and more"], ''[[Alternative Press (magazine)|Alternative Press]]'', April 16, 2018.</ref>

On October 9, 2018, the band did a warm up performance before the [[American Music Awards of 2018|2018 American Music Awards]].<ref>{{cite news |last1=Cerio |first1=Michael |title=Behind The Scenes At The AMAs: A Fireside Chat With Ocean Park Standoff |url=https://www.radio.com/blogs/michael-cerio/behind-scenes-amas-fireside-chat-ocean-park-standoff |accessdate=16 October 2018 |publisher=Radio.com |date=October 8, 2018}}</ref> On October 10, 2018 the band returned to Buffalo, New York as a part of their fall tour to perform at Pink Party 2018<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://mystar1025.radio.com/events/pink-party-2018 |title=Archived copy |access-date=2018-10-12 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181012214541/https://mystar1025.radio.com/events/pink-party-2018 |archive-date=2018-10-12 |url-status=dead }}</ref> for listeners of Star 102.5 ([[WTSS]]). The annual concert benefits [[Roswell Park Comprehensive Cancer Center]].  They also performed their single "If You Were Mine" in-studio on ''Rob Lucas In The Morning'' during their visit.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://mystar1025.radio.com/media/audio-channel/ocean-park-standoff-performs-and-talks-pink-party-w-rob-lucas-morning-0|title=Ocean Park Standoff Performs And Talks Pink Party w Rob Lucas In The Morning!|website=Mystar1025.radio.com|accessdate=March 28, 2020}}</ref>
