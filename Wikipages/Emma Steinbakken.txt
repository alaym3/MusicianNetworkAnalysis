'''Emma Steinbakken''' (born 2003) is a Norwegian singer. She has released the songs "Not Gonna Cry" (2018) and "Without You".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://arenan.yle.fi/1-50156533|title=Skavlan &#124; Avsnitt 8: Skådespelaren Malin Åkerman och hotellkungen Petter Stordalen bland gästerna|date=8 November 2019|accessdate=19 February 2021|via=arenan.yle.fi}}</ref> She performed on Swedish and Norwegian teleivison as a guest on [[Fredrik Skavlan]]'s talk show ''[[Skavlan]]'' in 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://stadsevent.se/nyheter/16-%C3%A5riga-popkometen-emma-steinbakken-g%C3%A4star-skavlan-ikv%C3%A4ll/|title=16-Åriga Popkometen Emma Steinbakken Gästar Skavlan Ikväll|first=Digital sun|website=StadsEvent.se|accessdate=19 February 2021}}</ref>
