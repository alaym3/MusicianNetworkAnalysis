'''Ison and Fille''' more commonly '''Ison & Fille''' is a Swedish hip hop duo consisting of Ison Glasgow ("Ison") and Felipe Leiva Wenger ("Fille").

The two met in 1994 in JKS basketball club in Bredäng, Sweden.<ref name="dn">{{cite news|author=Gustavsson|first=Matilda|url=http://www.dn.se/kultur-noje/musik/ison-fille-vanskap-respekt/|title=Ison & Fille – Vänskap & respekt|date=March 2, 2014|work=[[Dagens Nyheter]]|accessdate=27 March 2020|url-status=live|language=sv|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200118172520/https://www.dn.se/kultur-noje/musik/ison-fille-vanskap-respekt/|archive-date=18 January 2020}}</ref> Their initial songs were included in a 2000 compilation album ''Den svenska underjorden'' (meaning the Swedish underground). 
Media attention on the duo came with [[ZTV]], a Swedish television channel putting out Ison & Fille 2006 release "När vi glider".

The duo have cooperated with various artists like Swedish rapper [[Petter (rapper)|Petter]], Dekan Ahmed and the rap crew Highwon.

==Members==
*Ison Glasgow ("Ison"), born October 8, 1980, is a US-born rapper, but grew up in [[Bredäng]]. He immigrated to Sweden in 1986. His father died when he was just 13.
*Felipe Leiva Wenger ("Fille), born April 25, 1980, comes from Chile and grew up in [[Vårberg]]. He immigrated to Sweden in 1985 with his parents and two other siblings. Soon after the father left to Barbados where he died.

==In popular culture==
The duo also took part in the Swedish TV series ''Leende guldbruna ögon'' broadcast on [[Sveriges Television]] (SVT) where they played the roles of Carlos and Mike. This was followed by appearances in another television series, ''Lilla Al-Fadji & Co'' broadcast on [[Kanal 5 (Swedish TV channel)|Kanal 5]] and in comedy series ''Sverige dansar och ler''. Ison and Fille has toured Sweden extensively since the early 2000s. Ison served as one of four judges in the inaugural season of the Swedish ''[[X Factor (Swedish TV series)|The X Factor]]'' in 2012, broadcast on TV4. He coached the "Boys" category. Ison & Fille subsequently took part in the Music Aid 2013 event with their songs "Vår sida av stan" and "Galen". In 2015, the duo participated in season six of the Swedish TV-serie ''[[Så mycket bättre]]'', broadcast on [[TV4 (Sweden)|TV4]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.kingsizemag.se/noje/sa-mycket-battre-med-ison-fille-drar-igang-i-tv4-pa-lordag-datum-artister/|title="SÅ MYCKET BÄTTRE"-PREMIÄR MED ISON & FILLE PÅ LÖRDAG – HÄR ÄR DATUMEN & ARTISTERNA DUON TOLKAR!|website=KingSizeMag|url-status=live|access-date=27 April 2020}}</ref>

==Grillat & Grändy==
Fille and Sabo also run the hip hop musical project Grillat & Grändy with occasional collaborations with Ison as well. In 2015, Grillat & Grändy released the album ''Kör vi tills att dör vi''. The album charted on [[Sverigetopplistan]], the official Swedish albums chart.

==Awards==
In 2012, Ison & Fille won the P3 Gold Award in the category "Best Hip Hop / Soul Act of the Year".<ref>[http://sverigesradio.se/sida/gruppsida.aspx?programid=3964&grupp=16962&artikel=4912991 Sverige Radio: Ison & Fille gjorde årets Hiphop/Soul] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200210135951/https://sverigesradio.se/sida/gruppsida.aspx?programid=3964&grupp=16962&artikel=4912991 |date=2020-02-10 }} {{in lang|sv}}</ref>
