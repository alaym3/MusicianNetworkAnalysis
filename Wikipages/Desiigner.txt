'''Sidney Royel Selby III''' (born May 3, 1997), better known by his stage name '''Desiigner''', is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. In December 2015, his debut single "[[Panda (Desiigner song)|Panda]]" reached number one on the U.S. [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]]. In 2019, he was released from Def Jam and is currently an independent artist.

== Early life ==
Selby was born on May 3, 1997,<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/7341870/desiigner-panda-billboard-hot-100-number-1 | title=Desiigner's 'Panda' Claws to No. 1 on Hot 100, Bringing Americans Back to the Top | work=Billboard | date=April 25, 2016 | access-date=April 26, 2016 | last=Trust | first=Gary | quote=Desiigner is 18 years and 11 months old; he'll turn 19 on May 3.}}</ref> in [[Brooklyn]], [[New York City]].<ref>{{cite web| url=http://pigeonsandplanes.com/2016/02/desiigner-panda/ |title=The New Wave: Desiigner - "Panda"|date=9 February 2016|work=Pigeons & Planes}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2016/02/the-break-presents-desiigner/ |title=The Break Presents: Desiigner |author=emmanuel |date=19 February 2016 |work=XXL Mag}}</ref> He is of [[Afro-Barbadian]] and [[African American]] descent,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://wwd.com/eye/people/panda-desiigner-rapper-interview-10431787/|title=Meet Desiigner: The Elusive and Kanye West-Mentored Rapper|first=Aria|last=Hughes|date=17 May 2016|access-date=13 May 2017}}</ref> and is the grandson of blues musician [[Guitar Crusher|Sidney "Guitar Crusher" Selby]]. He grew up in the [[Louis Armstrong]] housing projects in the [[Bedford–Stuyvesant, Brooklyn|Bedford–Stuyvesant]] neighborhood.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/magazine-feature/7400285/desiigner-on-panda-difficult-past-kanye-west-advice|title=Desiigner on His Difficult Past and How His No. 1 Hit 'Panda' Gave Him a Way Out}}</ref> He began practicing vocals at school choir and church. At the age of 14, he embarked on his musical career.<ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.hotnewhiphop.com/everything-you-need-to-know-about-desiigner-news.20299.html?gallery=1 |title=Everything You Need To Know About Desiigner |work=HotNewHipHop |date=February 22, 2016 |access-date=April 26, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9YXCsqOFi20|title=Desiigner Freestyle and talks Panda & Kanye|work=KISS FM UK|date=May 3, 2016|access-date=May 24, 2016}}</ref>

== Career ==
<!--===2014–present: Beginnings and breakthrough===-->
Desiigner embarked on his music career under his original [[nickname]] Dezolo,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mtv.com/artists/desiigner/|title=Desiigner – Music Videos, News, Photos, Tour Dates – MTV|access-date=1 July 2016}}</ref> before he began putting out work on these tracks under the alias of Designer Royel, especially with Royel (his middle name). His sister convinced him to drop the "Royel" part and then keep the "Designer" part as his new [[stage name]], doubling the "I"{{'s}}. On December 3, 2015, under his new [[pseudonym]], Desiigner released his debut track, called "Zombie Walk". The song was [[Record production|produced]] by The Shell God.<ref>{{cite AV media |url= https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Uz8MzFisPnM |title= Desiigner Ft. King Savage- "Zombie Walk" (Prod. By: @TheShellGod) |date=3 December 2015|via=YouTube}}</ref> On December 15, 2015, Desiigner released his commercial debut [[single (music)|single]], titled "[[Panda (Desiigner song)|Panda]]" on SoundCloud.<ref>{{cite AV media|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lsJLLEwUYZM|title=Desiigner- Panda (OFFICIAL SONG) Prod. By: Menace|date=20 December 2015|via=YouTube}}</ref> The song released on [[iTunes]] within five days later, before being re-released as of February 2016.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/panda-single/id1088507971 | title=Panda – Single | work=iTunes Store (US) | access-date=April 18, 2016}}</ref> On February 11, 2016, Desiigner signed a [[recording contract]] with [[Kanye West]]'s [[GOOD Music]] imprint, under the aegis of [[Def Jam Recordings]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hotnewhiphop.com/brooklyn-rapper-desiigner-signs-to-good-music-news.20126.html|title=Brooklyn Rapper Desiigner Signs To G.O.O.D. Music|author=Danny Schwartz|date=11 February 2016|work=HotNewHipHop}}</ref> After signing a record deal, Desiigner subsequently appeared on two tracks — "[[Pt. 2]]" (which features an [[Interpolation (popular music)|interpolation]] of "Panda") and "Freestyle 4" — that was taken from Kanye West's seventh album ''[[The Life of Pablo]]''. Desiigner then went on to perform at the 2016's [[South by Southwest]] (SXSW) music festival.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.complex.com/music/2016/03/desiigner-courts-more-future-comparisons-debuts-new-song-pluto-at-sxsw|title=Desiigner Courts More Future Comparisons, Debuts New Song "Pluto'' at SXSW|website=www.complex.com|author=Chris Mench|date=March 16, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://hypetrak.com/2016/03/desiigner-pluto-panda-sxsw/|title=Desiigner Debuts New Song "Pluto" & Performs "Panda" at SXSW}}</ref> In an interview with ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'', Desiigner confirmed the release of his first upcoming [[mixtape]], titled ''Trap History Month''.<ref name="bbinterview">{{cite web | url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/magazine-feature/7326380/panda-rapper-desiigner-on-working-with-kanye-west-panda-hit-single-upcoming-music | title='Panda' Rapper Desiigner on Why His Mind Operates in a 'Crazy' Way and Working With Kanye West | work=Billboard | date=April 12, 2016 | access-date=April 17, 2016 | author=Peltz, Jonathan}}</ref>

== Legal issues ==
On September 8, 2016, Desiigner and three other people were arrested in [[New York City]], after a 911 caller claimed that he pulled a gun on them. Desiigner was later found in an [[SUV]] with four other people and the vehicle was searched. The police allegedly found [[oxycodone]] and guns, for which he and the other individuals received drug and weapon charges. They were all detained in custody.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/nyc-crime/desiigner-arrested-gun-drugs-menacing-midtown-driver-article-1.2784606|title=Desiigner charged in gun-pointing road rage incident in Midtown}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tmz.com/2016/09/09/desiigner-arrested-drugs-sell/|title=Desiigner -- Arrested for Drugs, Intent to Sell and Loaded Gun (UPDATED PHOTOS + VIDEO)}}</ref> On September 10, 2016, he was acquitted of the felony [[gun charge]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.vibe.com/2016/09/desiigner-felony-gun-charge-dropped/ |title=Desiigner acquitted of gun charge |last=Augustin |first=Camille |date=September 10, 2016 |website=[[Vibe (magazine)|Vibe]] |access-date=September 10, 2016}}</ref> He was cleared of the charges on September 12, 2016, when it was found that the pills were [[anabolic steroids]] belonging to his driver.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2016/09/desiigner-steroids-not-oxycontin/ |title=Drugs found in desiigner's car were steroids |last=Simmons |first=Ted |date=September 12, 2016 |website=[[XXL (magazine)|XXL]] |access-date=September 12, 2016}}</ref>

== Musical style ==
Desiigner's musical style is influenced by [[Southern hip hop]], namely [[Trap music (hip hop)|trap music]] which originated in [[Atlanta, Georgia]].<ref name="bbinterview" /> His [[rapping]] technique and vocals have been heavily compared to that of Atlanta-based rapper [[Future (rapper)|Future]]. When asked about the frequent comparisons, he told ''[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]]'': "God gave him a blessing, but he gave me a blessing too. I ain't gonna doubt the man's music. He make beautiful music too. Music is made every day. Big ups to him, big ups to Future. I actually like Future's music. I like his music, you feel me. I’m not a hater or a critic on him, you know, I do me. God bless him, God bless me."<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.complex.com/music/2016/02/desiigner-talks-about-the-success-of-panda-and-the-comparisons-to-future|title=Interview: Desiigner Talks About the Success of "Panda" and Comparisons to Future
|work=[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]]|date=February 19, 2016|access-date=March 29, 2016}}</ref>
