'''Antoine Valentinelli''' (born 4 December 1991 in [[Paris]]), better known by his stage name '''Lomepal''', is a French rapper and singer from the [[13th arrondissement of Paris]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Lomepal - Chute libre|url=http://www.artfelicis.com/2014/08/01/lomepal-chute-libre/|website=artfelicis.com|language=French|date=2014-08-01|accessdate=2018-12-17}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Retailleau|first=Maxime|title=Lomepal en animateur télé sadique dans son nouveau clip|url=http://www.lesinrocks.com/2017/06/28/musique/lomepal-en-animateur-tele-sadique-dans-son-nouveau-clip-11960215/|website=Les Inrocks|date=2017-06-28|accessdate=2018-12-17}}</ref> He has released five [[Extended play|EPs]], and two studio albums: ''Flip'', released in June 2017, and ''Jeannine'', released in December 2018, were certified double platinum by the [[Syndicat national de l'édition phonographique|SNEP]].<ref name="SNEP">{{cite web|url=http://www.snepmusique.com/les-disques-dor/?awards_cat=0&awards_awd=0&awards_year=0&awards_artist=LOMEPAL&awards_title=&awards_edit_distrib=&awards_sort=date_certif-desc&awards_nb=30&submitAdvanced=Rechercher|title=Les certifications : Notre base de données|publisher=[[Syndicat national de l'édition phonographique|SNEP]]|accessdate=2018-12-17|date=November 2013}}</ref>

His stage name comes from the fact that his friends always thought he was sick because of his pale complexion, hence Lomepal (for "the pale man"). His first stage name was Jo Pump.<ref name="RF_2532013">{{cite web|last=Haddad|first=Julien|title=[Interview] Lomepal (1/2) : Le rap c'est un jeu de feinte.|url=http://lerapenfrance.fr/entretien-avec-lomepal-12/|website=Le Rap en France|date=2013-03-25|accessdate=2018-12-17}}</ref> He is friends with the Belgian rapper [[Roméo Elvis]], with whom he collaborated on the song "1000°C" for Lomepal's album ''Jeannine''.

In 2017, he performed more than a hundred live shows and received over 100 million views on his YouTube channel.<ref>{{cite web|title=''Flip'' premier album du jeune rappeur Lomepal|website=[[France Culture]]|date=2017-06-16|url=https://www.franceculture.fr/emissions/les-carnets-de-la-creation/flippremier-album-du-jeune-rappeur-lomepal|accessdate=2018-12-17}}</ref>

On 25 October 2019 Antoine released the album, ''Amina''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.booska-p.com/new-lomepal-sa-reedition-dehors-minuit-n113680.html|title=Les certifications : Lomepal releases a new album at midnight! |date=October 2019}}</ref> The album is essentially a deluxe version of his album ''Jeannine'' including all of its songs, with some having been remixed, replaced with a live/acoustic version or somehow modified.
