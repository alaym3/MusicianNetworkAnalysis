'''Mark Mbogo''' (born September 23, 1997),<ref name="minnesotan-to-meet">{{cite tweet|last=Mbogo|first=Mark|user=kaycyy______|number=1043932735834861569|date=September 23, 2018|title=On this day a real nigga was born ðŸ’« Happy Birthday to myself ðŸ›¸ðŸš€ 21|access-date=July 27, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210727180124/https://twitter.com/kaycyy______/status/1043932735834861569|archive-date=July 27, 2021|url-status=live|link=no}}</ref> known professionally as '''KayCyy''' or '''KayCyy Pluto''', is an American rapper and singer.

Born in Kenya, Mbogo moved with his family to [[Minnesota]] at an early age. Throughout his career, he has released three mixtapes, two albums, and several singles. KayCyy has worked as a writer and credited vocalist alongside several major artists, including [[Kanye West]], [[Lil Baby]], [[Lil Wayne]], and [[Travis Scott]] amongst others.

== Early life ==
Mark Mbogo was born on September 23, 1997,<ref name="minnesotan-to-meet" /> in Kenya. At the age of nine, he and his family moved to the United States, specifically to the state of [[Minnesota]]. He attended high school in the city of [[Saint Paul, Minnesota|Saint Paul]], where he graduated from in 2016.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://minnesota.cbslocal.com/2016/05/17/minnesotan-to-meet-kaycyy-pluto/|title=Minnesotan To Meet: KayCYY Pluto|last=Lucia|first=Ali|work=[[WCCO-TV]]|publisher=[[CBS]]|date=May 17, 2016|access-date=July 27, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210727191655/https://minnesota.cbslocal.com/2016/05/17/minnesotan-to-meet-kaycyy-pluto/|archive-date=July 27, 2021|url-status=live}}</ref> After high school, he left home and ran off to New York to pursue his music career.<ref>{{Cite web|date=|title=From Kenya to Kanye West, KayCyy's unique journey has Minnesota roots|url=https://www.kare11.com/article/news/local/from-kenya-to-kanye-west-kaycyys-unique-journey-has-minnesota-roots/89-af24ea02-7ef0-4120-8d6f-c9e0542754fb|url-status=live|access-date=2021-11-12|website=kare11.com|language=en-US}}</ref>
