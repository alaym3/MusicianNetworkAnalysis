'''Weeekly''' ({{lang-ko|위클리}})<ref>{{cite web|url=https://people.search.naver.com/search.naver?where=nexearch&query=%EC%9C%84%ED%81%B4%EB%A6%AC&sm=tab_etc&ie=utf8&key=PeopleService&os=14250321|title=위클리 (Weeekly) 프로필|publisher=Naver|language=ko|access-date=July 2, 2020|archive-date=August 24, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200824190218/https://people.search.naver.com/search.naver?where=nexearch&query=%EC%9C%84%ED%81%B4%EB%A6%AC&sm=tab_etc&ie=utf8&key=PeopleService&os=14250321|url-status=live}}</ref> is a South Korean girl group under [[IST Entertainment]] (formerly Play M Entertainment).<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hankyung.com/entertainment/article/202007019436H|title=위클리, 평균나이 만 17세 '완성형 비주얼'|date=July 1, 2020|website=Hankuk Kyungje|access-date=July 2, 2020|archive-date=July 1, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200701155834/https://www.hankyung.com/entertainment/article/202007019436H|url-status=live}}</ref> The group is Play M Entertainment's second girl group in 10 years, after [[Apink]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.mydaily.co.kr/new_yk/html/read.php?newsid=202006301715754312&ext=na|title="평균 17세, 실력은 안 어려"…위클리, 10년만에 탄생한 에이핑크 동생 걸그룹 [MD현장 종합]|date=June 30, 2020|website=Mydaily|access-date=July 2, 2020|archive-date=July 4, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200704143100/http://www.mydaily.co.kr/new_yk/html/read.php?newsid=202006301715754312&ext=na|url-status=live}}</ref> The group consists of seven members: Lee Soo-jin, Shin Ji-yoon, Monday, Park So-eun, Lee Jae-hee, Jihan and Zoa. The group debuted on June 30, 2020, with their debut EP, ''We Are''.<ref name=debutalbum>{{Cite web|url=http://www.breaknews.com/739436|title=신예 위클리, 첫 미니앨범 '위 아(We are)'로 데뷔..걸그룹 시장 '차세대 돌풍'|date=June 30, 2020|website=Break News|access-date=July 2, 2020|archive-date=July 2, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200702041945/http://www.breaknews.com/739436|url-status=live}}</ref>

==History==
===Pre-debut===
Soojin, Jiyoon and Soeun were contestants of [[YG Entertainment]] and [[JTBC]]'s survival show ''[[Mix Nine]]'', with Soojin being the "prominent face" among the female contestants during the initial stages of the show's launch.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.mydaily.co.kr/new_yk/html/read.php?newsid=202006301714308966&ext=na|title=[MD포토] 위클리 이수진 '믹스나인 출신으로 위클리로 데뷔'|date=June 30, 2020|website=Mydaily|access-date=July 2, 2020|archive-date=July 3, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200703081911/http://www.mydaily.co.kr/new_yk/html/read.php?newsid=202006301714308966&ext=na|url-status=live}}</ref> However, on January 8, 2018, Soojin withdrew from the show following injury from a traffic accident and had received emergency surgery.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://pop.heraldcorp.com/view.php?ud=201801081411211260641_1|title='믹스나인' 이수진 측 "교통사고 부상 치료가 먼저…하차 결정"(공식)|date=January 8, 2018|website=HeraldPOP|access-date=July 2, 2020|archive-date=July 2, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200702085800/http://pop.heraldcorp.com/view.php?ud=201801081411211260641_1|url-status=live}}</ref>

In October 2018, Fave Entertainment had announced its plans to debut a new girl group, temporarily named Fave Girls (페이브 걸즈, stylised as FAVE GIRLS).<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://star.mt.co.kr/stview.php?no=2018101908483094943|title=페이브 엔터, 新 걸그룹 '페이브 걸스' 론칭..11월 첫 선|date=October 19, 2018|website=Star News|access-date=July 2, 2020|archive-date=July 2, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200702181747/http://star.mt.co.kr/stview.php?no=2018101908483094943|url-status=live}}</ref> The lineup included Soojin, Jiyoon, Monday, Soeun and Jaehee.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://pop.heraldcorp.com/view.php?ud=201811220852540295569_1|title=페이브걸스, 12월 8일 두 번째 '프리쇼' 개최‥티켓 연속매진 '소녀파워'|date=November 22, 2018|website=Star News|access-date=July 2, 2020|archive-date=July 4, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200704025036/http://pop.heraldcorp.com/view.php?ud=201811220852540295569_1|url-status=live}}</ref> The group was subsequently named PlayM Girls (플레이엠 걸즈) following the merger of Plan A Entertainment and Fave Entertainment, to form Play M Entertainment, on April 1, 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.news1.kr/articles/?3583791|title=[공식입장] 플랜에이·페이브 합병…'플레이 M' 이름으로 새 출발|date=March 29, 2019|website=News1|access-date=July 2, 2020|archive-date=July 2, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200702103750/https://www.news1.kr/articles/?3583791|url-status=live}}</ref>

===2020: Debut with ''We Are'' and ''We Can''===
On May 8, 2020, Play M Entertainment announced that PlayM Girls were confirmed to make their debut in June.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://sports.donga.com/article/all/20200508/100957836/1|title=에이핑크 후 10년만…플레이엠 걸즈, 6월 데뷔 확정 [공식]|date=May 8, 2020|website=Sports DongA|access-date=July 2, 2020|archive-date=July 2, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200702085758/https://sports.donga.com/article/all/20200508/100957836/1|url-status=live}}</ref> On May 11, the group name was revealed to be Weeekly, with all seven members of the group and their profile pictures revealed.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.wowtv.co.kr/NewsCenter/News/Read?articleId=A202005120152&t=NN|title=플레이엠 新걸그룹, 데뷔 팀명 '위클리(Weeekly)'로 확정…'6월 첫 선'|date=May 12, 2020|website=Hankuk Kyungje TV|access-date=July 2, 2020|archive-date=July 4, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200704004311/http://www.wowtv.co.kr/NewsCenter/News/Read?articleId=A202005120152&t=NN|url-status=live}}</ref> On June 12, the group's debut [[extended play]] ''We Are'' was announced to be released on June 30, together with a release schedule.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.xportsnews.com/?ac=article_view&entry_id=1284271|title=플레이엠 新걸그룹 위클리, 30일 첫 미니앨범 'We are'로 데뷔 확정|date=June 12, 2020|website=XportsNews|access-date=July 2, 2020|archive-date=November 24, 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181124163746/http://www.xportsnews.com/?ac=article_view&entry_id=1284271|url-status=live}}</ref>

The group released their second EP ''[[We Can (EP)|We Can]]'' on October 13, featuring the lead single "Zig Zag".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.dispatch.co.kr/2104132|title="보랏빛 컬러의, We can"…위클리, 10월 13일 컴백|date=September 21, 2020|website=Dispatch|access-date=September 21, 2020|archive-date=October 11, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201011172616/https://www.dispatch.co.kr/2104132|url-status=live}}</ref> On October 15, they had their debut stage for the song on [[M Countdown]].<ref>{{cite web|title=M COUNTDOWN 201015 EP.686|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tnl-wmfZdeE|website=YouTube|publisher=Mnet Kpop|access-date=1 December 2020|archive-date=November 3, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201103155500/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tnl-wmfZdeE|url-status=live}}</ref> The choreography was distinct for its integration of 10-pound cubes into the dance, which the members pushed around the stage.<ref name="ZigZag">{{cite web|title=위클리, 'Zig Zag(지그재그)' 퍼포먼스 뮤직비디오 공개...큐브 퍼포먼스에 눈길|url=https://www.hankyung.com/life/article/202011063846k|website=hankyung.com|publisher=Hankyung|access-date=1 December 2020|language=ko|date=6 November 2020|archive-date=December 27, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201227015611/https://www.hankyung.com/life/article/202011063846k|url-status=live}}</ref> The song achieved 10 million views on YouTube within 4 days, beating their previous record.<ref name=ZigZag/>

===2021: ''We Play'' and ''Play Game: Holiday''===
The group released their third EP ''[[We Play (EP)|We Play]]'', featuring "After School" as the lead single on March 17.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Yoo|first1=Byung-chul|title=위클리, 17일 신곡 'After School' 음원과 뮤직비디오 발표…신인상 6관왕 넘어 대세돌 노린다|url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=215&aid=0000944360|website=Hankook Kyungje TV via Naver|access-date=17 March 2021|language=ko|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Jang|first1=Jin-ri|title=[단독]위클리, 라이언전 손잡고 3월 컴백 '대세의 귀환'|url=http://www.spotvnews.co.kr/?mod=news&act=articleView&idxno=405859|website=SPOTV|access-date=22 February 2021|language=ko|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Son|first1=Jin-ah|title=위클리, 3월 17일 컴백 확정…미니 3집 'We play' 발매(공식)|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/now/article/410/0000764623|website=MK Sports via Naver|access-date=23 February 2021|language=ko|url-status=live}}</ref>

The group released a single "7Days Tension", in collaboration with South Korean eyewear brand Davich, on May 28.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Jung|first1=Hye-yeon|title=위클리, 신곡 '텐션업' 오늘(28일) 발매..청량한 여름 분위기|url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=112&aid=0003438483|website=Herald Pop via Naver|access-date=28 May 2021|language=ko|url-status=live}}</ref>

The group released their fourth EP ''[[Play Game: Holiday]]'' on August 4, featuring "Holiday Party" as the lead single.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Yoon|first1=Sang-geun|title=위클리, 8월 4일 'Play Game : Holiday' 컴백 확정[공식]|url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=108&aid=0002972205|website=Star News via Naver|access-date=15 July 2021|language=ko|url-status=live}}</ref>
