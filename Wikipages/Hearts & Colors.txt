'''Hearts & Colors''' is a singer-songwriter duo from Gothenburg, Sweden, consisting of [[Philip Tillström]] & [[Nicolai Kjellberg]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.bornmusiconline.com/podcast/hearts-colors-share-the-feel-good-and-vibrant-too-many-friends/|title=Hearts & Colors share the feel-good and vibrant 'Too Many Friends' – Born Music|work=Born Music|access-date=2018-07-27|language=en-US}}</ref> The band formed in 2014, and started off as an [[Indie folk|indie-folk]] duo, covering artists like [[One Direction]]<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y0PbTHwU5OM|title=One Direction – Steal My Girl (Hearts & Colors Cover)|work=YouTube|access-date=2018-06-13|language=en}}</ref> and [[Bruno Mars]]<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6r1e1Q--8gY|title=Bruno Mars – The Lazy Song (Hearts & Colors Cover)|work=YouTube|access-date=2018-06-13|language=en}}</ref> on [[YouTube]]. They were initially signed to independent label Firefly entertainment,<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://hellofirefly.com/?p=763|title=Hearts & Colors – FIREFLY ENTERTAINMENT|website=hellofirefly.com|language=en-US|access-date=2018-07-27}}</ref> with their track "[[Lighthouse (Hearts & Colors song)|Lighthouse]]" by Swedish producer [[Andrelli]], amassing over 1 million views on [[YouTube]].<ref>{{Citation|last=play & cue music|title=Hearts & Colors – Lighthouse (Andrelli Remix)|date=2016-11-30|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yVE1NUwmk-Q|access-date=2018-07-27}}</ref> They later moved on to writing their own material,<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://nervemedia.org.uk/nerve/bournemouths-an-inspiring-place-qa-with-hearts-and-colors/|title="Bournemouth’s an inspiring place" – Q&A with Hearts and Colors|work=Nerve Media|access-date=2018-06-26|language=en}}</ref> and are signed with Island Records.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.islandrecords.co.uk/island-artists/hearts-colors/|title=Hearts & Colors|work=Island records|access-date=2018-06-13|language=en}}</ref>

The band has released four songs: "Too Many Friends" in February 2018,<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.clashmusic.com/news/track-of-the-day-13-hearts-colors|title=Track Of The Day – Hearts & Colors|work=Clash Magazine|access-date=2018-06-26|language=en}}</ref> and "Lying to Myself" and "Waterbed" simultaneously in June of the same year.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://themusicsite.com/music-news/news/hearts-colors-unveil-two-new-singles|title=Hearts & Colors Unveil Two New Singles for Summer  {{!}} The Music Site|website=themusicsite.com|language=en-GB|access-date=2018-07-27}}</ref> Their fourth song "LA On A Saturday Night" was released in October 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/la-on-a-saturday-night-single/1438180997|title=LA on a Saturday Night – Single|publisher=Itunes|access-date=2019-01-29}}</ref>
