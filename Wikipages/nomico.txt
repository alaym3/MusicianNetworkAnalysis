'''Nomico''' (stylized in all-lowercase) is a Japanese singer and voice actress<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.generasia.com/wiki/nomico|title=nomico – generasia|website=generasia.com|access-date=4 June 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://rateyourmusic.com/artist/のみこ|title=のみこ discography|website=RateYourMusic|access-date=4 June 2019}}</ref> who is signed to the [[Lantis (company)|Lantis]] record label. She is known for her [[cover songs]],<ref name=":0"/> and has also released works which have been used in both [[anime]] and  [[visual novel]]s.<ref name=":0" />

== Career ==
Nomico debuted in 2004 as the lead singer of the musical circle LOOPCUBE.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=https://www.last.fm/music/Nomico/+wiki|title=Nomico biography|website=Last.fm|access-date=5 June 2019}}</ref> They released their debut album, entitled {{nihongo|"Nomico no Ōzora"|のみこの大空}} in 2004.<ref>{{Citation|title=のみこの大空 on OTOTOY Music Store|url=https://ototoy.jp/_/default/p/948|language=ja|access-date=6 June 2019}}</ref> 

In 2006, she left LOOPCUBE to pursue a solo career; her debut single, "Shichaimashou" was used as the ending theme in the anime ''[[Renkin 3-kyū Magical? Pokān]]'',<ref name=":1" /> in which she also acted, providing the voices of Keimi, an [[Griffin (The Invisible Man)|invisible woman]] who serves as a chaperone of the protagonists, and Jun, a talking rabbit.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Nomico|url=https://www.imdb.com/name/nm3145374/bio|access-date=5 June 2019|publisher=IMDb}}</ref> She released her solo debut album, ''Nomico no Mi'' in 2006.<ref name=":3">{{Citation|title=のみこの実 [Nomico no Mi] by のみこ [Nomico]|url=https://rateyourmusic.com/release/album/のみこ/のみこの実__nomico_no_mi_/|access-date=6 June 2019}}</ref> 

Nomico has sung "Koisora Recycling" for ''[[Akikan!]]'', "Skip!" for ''Moetan'', "Randoseling" for ''[[Mitsudomoe Zouryouchuu!]]'', "Karakuri Nemuri Dan" for ''[[Katanagatari]]'', "Hoshikaze no Horoscope" for ''[[Hoshizora e Kakaru Hashi]]''. 

Although she has worked extensively in the [[visual novel]] and [[Cover Song|song cover]] scenes, Nomico is known for her single "[[Bad Apple!!]]"<ref name=":6">{{Cite web|url=https://radiovolna.net/en/artist/722585-nomico.html|title=nomico – Videos, Songs, Discography, Lyrics|website=RadioVolna.net|access-date=4 June 2019}}</ref> which was a collaboration with Alstroemeria Records. Released in 2007,<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|url=https://knowyourmeme.com/memes/bad-apple|title=Bad Apple!!|website=Know Your Meme|access-date=5 June 2019}}</ref> the song went [[Viral video|viral]] both on YouTube and [[Nico Nico Douga]], and spawned a massive number of remixes and fanworks.<ref name=":2" /> As of July 2021, the "Bad Apple!!" YouTube music video has gained 47 million views. The song also charted on Nico Nico Douga's rankings around the time of its release.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://dic.nicovideo.jp/id/|title=【東方】Bad Apple!! ＰＶ【影絵】 (sm8628149) [動画記事]|website=ニコニコ大百科|language=ja|access-date=6 June 2019}}</ref>
