'''Golden Child''' ({{Korean|골든차일드}}; abbreviated as '''GNCD''' or '''GolCha''') is a South Korean [[boy band]] formed by [[Woollim Entertainment]] in 2017.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://people.search.naver.com/search.naver?where=nexearch&sm=tab_ppn&query=%EA%B7%B8%EB%A3%B9%EA%B3%A8%EB%93%A0%EC%B0%A8%EC%9D%BC%EB%93%9C&os=5913702&ie=utf8&key=PeopleService|title=골든 차일드|publisher=Naver|language=ko}}</ref> The group debuted on August 28, 2017, with their EP, ''Gol-Cha!''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tenasia.hankyung.com/archives/1280004|title=골든차일드, 데뷔 앨범 미리보기 공개..본격 신호탄|publisher=Ten Asia|language=ko}}</ref> The group consists of ten members: Daeyeol, Y, Jangjun, Tag, Seungmin, Jaehyun, Jibeom, Donghyun, Joochan and [[Choi Bo-min (singer)|Bomin]], former member Jaeseok officially left the group due to health issues in early 2018.

On August 9, Hanteo Chart announced that the album ''Game Changer'' sold more than 126,000 copies in the first week and became the album with highest 1st week sales under Woollim Ent surpassing 'Yes' by Golden Child.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-08-10|title=[Initial Chodong Record] Golden Child’s ‘Game Changer’ Sold More Than 126,000 Copies in the First Week…Achieves Outstanding Career High! (Hanteo Chart Official)|url=https://hanteonews.com/en/article/chart?fc=23877|access-date=2021-08-19|website=HANTEONEW|language=ko}}</ref> Hanteo Chart, the world's one-and-only real-time music chart, congratulated Golden Child and issued an official certificate which proves the Initial Chodong sales amount over 100,000 copies. Thus Golden Child proved themselves as one of the main group representing K-pop by showing 1.8 times growth compared to the previous album YES 's Initial Chodong record (71,485 copies).<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-08-20|title=[Exclusive] Golden Child Receives Hanteo Chart Official Certificate! (Hanteo Chart Official)“All thanks to dear Goldenness…let’s support and love each other forever!”|url=https://hanteonews.com/en/article/chart?fc=24229|access-date=2021-08-21|website=HANTEONEW|language=ko}}</ref>

The title track 'Ra Pam Pam' debuted at #2 on Billboard World Digital Song Sales Chart and #37 on Billboard Digital Song Sales Chart.<ref>{{Cite web|last=McIntyre|first=Hugh|title=BTS, Golden Child, Sunmi, Oneus And Hyo: Hits Making Moves On The World Songs Chart|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/hughmcintyre/2021/08/17/bts-golden-child-sunmi-oneus-and-hyo-hits-making-moves-on-the-world-songs-chart/|access-date=2021-08-18|website=Forbes|language=en}}</ref> Golden Child is the third K-pop Boy Group to be named on the Chart and holding the title for the highest debut record.<ref>{{Cite web|title=골든차일드, 빌보드 월드 디지털송 세일즈 차트 데뷔 최고 기록..美 포브스 관심집중|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/112/0003467906|access-date=2021-08-19|language=ko}}</ref> 'Ra Pam Pam' was the First Mongolian iTunes top song after its release at 6 p.m. on August 2. In addition, the 'Game Changer' ranked First in Turkey on the iTunes top K-pop album chart, Second in Israel, Thailand, Australia, Singapore, United States, Spain, Indonesia and Malaysia, Third in Hong Kong and Canada, and thus ranked within TOP5 in 11 countries and regions.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Moon|first=Wan-sik|title=골든차일드 '라팜팜' 빌보드 월드 디지털 송 세일즈 차트 2위..글로벌 막강 파워|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/now/article/108/0002981501|access-date=2021-08-18|website=n.news.naver.com|language=ko}}</ref>

On October 5, Golden Child released the repackaged version of their second studio album titled ''Ddara'', and its lead single of the same name.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Lee|first=Jae-eun|title=골든차일드 10월 5일 컴백, 포스터 깜짝 공개 |url=http://sports.khan.co.kr/entertainment/sk_index.html?art_id=202109230926003&sec_id=540301&pt=nv#csidx6cfefea380fb7c5a33a2eae1e46f629|access-date=2021-09-23|website=Sports Khan|language=ko}}</ref>
