'''İrem Derici''' (born 21 March 1987) is a Turkish singer and songwriter. From the early 2010s she became famous in Turkey through her hit singles "Zorun Ne Sevgilim", "Kalbimin Tek Sahibine" and "Dantel".

== Life and career ==
İrem Derici was born on 21 March 1987, the daughter of Hulusi Derici and Jale Ediz.<ref>{{cite news | title = İrem Derici'den doğum günü mesajı | url = http://www.sabah.com.tr/magazin/2016/03/21/irem-dericiden-dogum-gunu-mesaji | date = 21 March 2016 | newspaper = [[Sabah (newspaper)|Sabah]] | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20170219133834/http://www.sabah.com.tr/magazin/2016/03/21/irem-dericiden-dogum-gunu-mesaji | archivedate = 19 February 2017}}</ref><ref name="biyo">{{cite web | url = http://iremderici.com/biyografi | title = Biyografi <nowiki> | </nowiki> İrem Derici | publisher = iremderici.com | accessdate = 2 January 2017 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20161029160652/http://iremderici.com/biyografi | archivedate = 29 October 2016}}</ref> At the age of four she started to play piano,<ref>{{cite news | url = http://www.milliyet.com.tr/irem-derici-kimdir--gundem-2214482/ | accessdate = 14 April 2016 | title = İrem Derici kimdir? | newspaper= Milliyet | date = 23 March 2016 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160705053803/http://www.milliyet.com.tr/irem-derici-kimdir--gundem-2214482/ | archivedate = 5 July 2016}}</ref> and later had piano lessons at [[Mimar Sinan Fine Arts University]] State Conservatory High School. Derici studied sociology at [[Istanbul Bilgi University]], and while she was studying Marketing Communication she participated in the singing competition ''[[O Ses Türkiye]]'' and advanced to the semi-final stage. Meanwhile, she began to perform at different places in Turkey with a group named Monopop.<ref name="biyo"/>

With the release of the single "Bensiz Yapamazsın" in 2012 Derici started her professional music career. In May 2013, her second single "Düşler Ülkesinin Gelgit Akıllısı" was released. In September 2013, her first [[maxi single]] ''İki'' was released. The album's first music video was made for "Sevgi Olsun Taştan Olsun", followed by another music video for "Zorun Ne Sevgilim". "Zorun Ne Sevgilim" remained number two on [[Türkçe Top 20]] for three weeks. Derici then released a new single titled "Neredesin Sen?", written by [[Neşet Ertaş]].

In 2014, her single "Kalbimin Tek Sahibine" became a massive hit in Turkey and was viewed by millions on YouTube.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.iha.com.tr/haber-irem-dericiden-tiklanma-rekoru-430407/|title=İrem Derici'den tıklanma rekoru|agency=İhlas Haber Ajansı|date=17 January 2015|accessdate=1 September 2017}}</ref> In the same year her second maxi single ''Üç'' was released, and two music videos were made for the songs "Bir miyiz?" and "Nabza Göre Şerbet".<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.powerturk.com/muzik/muzik-haber/irem-derici-nin-yeni-maxi-single-i-uc-cikti.html | accessdate = 14 April 2016 | title = İrem Derici'nin Yeni Maxi Single'ı ''Üç'' Çıktı! | publisher = Powertürk TV | date = 17 June 2014 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140723122538/http://www.powerturk.com/muzik/muzik-haber/irem-derici-nin-yeni-maxi-single-i-uc-cikti.html | archivedate = 23 July 2014}}</ref> "Nabza Göre Şerbet" ranked third on Turkey's official music chart.<ref>{{cite web | url = https://twitter.com/MusicTopTR/status/568409789560168448 | title = @MusicTopTR on Twitter | date = 20 February 2015 | publisher = Twitter | accessdate = 10 March 2015 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160310163312/https://twitter.com/MusicTopTR/status/568409789560168448 | archivedate = 10 March 2016}}</ref> In 2015, two more singles "Değmezsin Ağlamaya" and "Aşk Eşittir Biz" were released in March and September respectively. "Aşk Eşittir Biz" became a number one hit in Turkey.<ref>{{cite web | url = https://twitter.com/MusicTopTR/status/685468688498167808 | title = MusicTopTR Resmi Listesinde 2016 1. haftanın ikincisi... AŞK EŞİTTİR BİZ | accessdate = 8 January 2016 | publisher = Twitter | date = 8 January 2016 }}</ref> In the same year, Derici collaborated with [[Emrah Karaduman]] in his album ''Tozduman'' and was featured in the song "Nerden Bilecekmiş".

Derici, released her first studio album ''Dantel'' in February 2016 under the label GNL Entertainment. The song "Dantel" from the album, was the number-one hit in Turkey for four continuous weeks.<ref>"Dantel" on Turkey's official music chart:

* {{cite web | url = https://twitter.com/MusicTopTR/status/720967551400939520 | title = MusicTopTR Resmi Listesinde 15. haftanın Lideri... DANTEL | accessdate = 28 April 2016 | publisher = Twitter | date = 15 April 2016 }}

Subsequently, a third and forth music video were made for the songs "Dur Yavaş" and "Bana Hiçbir Şey Olmaz".

From 13 September 2014 to 22 March 2016, Derici was married to the arranger Rıza Esendemir.<ref>{{cite news | url = http://www.sabah.com.tr/magazin/2014/09/14/irem-derici-evlendi | title = İrem Derici evlendi | date = 14 September 2014 |newspaper = Sabah | accessdate = 18 October 2014 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160415163218/http://www.sabah.com.tr/magazin/2014/09/14/irem-derici-evlendi | archivedate = 15 April 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | url = http://www.milliyet.com.tr/irem-derici-ile-riza-esendemir-tek-magazin-2214247/ | accessdate = 23 March 2016 | title = İrem Derici ile Rıza Esendemir tek celsede boşandı | newspaper=Milliyet| date = 23 March 2016 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160629220727/http://www.milliyet.com.tr/irem-derici-ile-riza-esendemir-tek-magazin-2214247/ | archivedate = 29 June 2016}}</ref> From 11 July to 21 September 2016, she served as a judge on the second season of the singing competition ''Rising Star Türkiye''.

In 2017 she collaborated with [[Mustafa Ceceli]] in his album ''Zincirimi Kırdı Aşk'' and was featured in the song "Kıymetlim".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.milliyet.com.tr/cadde/7-subat-kiymetlim-mujdesi-2384691|title='7 Şubat Kıymetlim' müjdesi|newspaper=Milliyet|date=25 January 2017|accessdate=5 July 2019}}</ref> Together with [[Gökçe (singer)|Gökçe]], Derici was featured in the music video for [[Yonca Evcimik]]'s new single "Kendine Gel".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.cnnturk.com/magazin/yoncanin-melekleri|title=Yonca’nın melekleri|publisher=CNN Türk|date=25 July 2017|accessdate=5 July 2019}}</ref> In the same year, she released a new single titled "Tektaş", which ranked eighth on Turkey's official music chart.<ref>{{cite web | url = https://twitter.com/MusicTopTR/status/860475475122106369 | title = @MusicTopTR on Twitter | date = 5 May 2017 | publisher = Twitter | accessdate = 5 May 2017 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20170507170918/https://twitter.com/MusicTopTR/status/860475475122106369 | archivedate = 7 May 2017}}</ref> Later she voiced the song "Sevimli" for the soundtrack of the movie ''Bekâr Bekir''. In November, Derici released the single "Bazı Aşklar Yarım Kalmalı", which rose to the third position on the official chart in Turkey.<ref>{{cite web | url = https://twitter.com/MusicTopTR/status/949219096368951296 | title = MusicTopTR Resmi Listesinde 2018 1. haftanın üçüncüsü... BAZI AŞKLAR YARIM KALMALI | date = 5 January 2018 | publisher = Twitter | accessdate = 18 June 2019 }}</ref>

In August 2018, her second studio album, ''Sabıka Kaydı'', was released. A music video was published on the same day for the album's lead single "Ben Tek Siz Hepiniz".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.haberturk.com/irem-derici-nin-sabika-kaydi-cikti-magazin-haberleri-2096131-magazin|title=İrem Derici'nin 'Sabıka Kaydı' çıktı|newspaper=Habertürk|date=10 August 2018|accessdate=5 July 2019}}</ref> In February 2019, Derici released her tenth single "Meftun",<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.milliyet.com.tr/yeni-sarkisi--meftun---%C4%B1rem-derici-yi-iyilestirir-mi--molatik-11102/|title=Yeni şarkısı 'Meftun', İrem Derici'yi iyileştirir mi?|newspaper=Milliyet|first=Yağız|last=Güzel|date=26 February 2019|accessdate=5 July 2019}}</ref> and on 31 May 2019 she released her first cover album ''Mest Of'', which contains eight popular Turkish songs from the 90s.<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.takvim.com.tr/saklambac/2019/05/31/irem-dericinin-yeni-albumu-mest-of-raflardaki-yerini-aldi | accessdate = 4 July 2019 | title = İrem Derici'nin yeni albümü 'Mest Of' raflardaki yerini aldı | newspaper = [[Takvim]] | date =31 May 2019}}</ref> Mayk Şişman from ''[[Milliyet]]'' believed that Derici's new album was 'successful as an idea' and named "Bende Hüküm Sür" as its best piece.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.milliyet.com.tr/%C4%B1rem-derici-nin--mest-of--albumunun-yildizi---bende-hukum-sur--molatik-12077/|title=İrem Derici'nin 'Mest Of' albümünün yıldızı: 'Bende Hüküm Sür'|newspaper=[[Milliyet]]|first=Mayk|last=Şişman|date=10 June 2019|accessdate=4 July 2019}}</ref>
