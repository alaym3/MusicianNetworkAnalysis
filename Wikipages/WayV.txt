'''WayV''' ({{lang-zh |s=威神V |hp=WēiShén V }}) is a Chinese [[boy band]] which serves as the fourth sub-unit and China-based unit of the South Korean boy band [[NCT (group)|NCT]] managed by [[SM Entertainment]]'s Chinese sub-label, Label V.<ref name=NCT>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/international/8526172/chris-lee-sm-entertainments-kpop-interview|title=SM Entertainment A&R Chris Lee Talks 'Cultural Technology' & Creating K-Pop Hits|last=Herman|first=Tamar|date=August 5, 2019|website=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|access-date=November 12, 2019|archive-date=November 19, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191119102256/https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/international/8526172/chris-lee-sm-entertainments-kpop-interview|url-status=live}}</ref><ref name=billboard2/> The group debuted on January 17, 2019, with their digital extended play ''[[The Vision (single album)|The Vision]]'', and is composed of seven members: Kun, [[Ten (singer)|Ten]], Winwin, [[Lucas Wong|Lucas]], Xiaojun, Hendery and Yangyang.

The group have released one [[studio album]] and three [[extended play]]s and made regular appearances on [[Billboard (magazine)|''Billboard'']]'s [[Billboard charts#International charts|world charts]].

==History==
===2016–2018: Pre-debut activities, formation===
Prior to joining SM Entertainment, some of the members were already involved in the entertainment industry. In 2011, Ten participated as a contestant in the Thai TV show ''Teen Superstar'' under the name TNT.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://mgronline.com/entertainment/detail/9640000079617|title=Paint Me Naked “เตนล์ NCT” อีกหนึ่งเด็กไทยที่ผงาดบนเวที K-Pop|website=[[Manager Daily]]|date=August 14, 2021|access-date=November 23, 2021|language=th}}</ref> Xiaojun was a singing contestant in [[Zhejiang Television|Zhejiang TV]] survival program ''X-Fire'' in November 2015.{{Citation needed|date=September 2021}}

In 2013, Ten was the first member to join the label through an SM Global audition in Bangkok, Thailand.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sbs.com.au/popasia/blog/2019/01/03/beginners-guide-ncts-new-unit-wayv|title=A beginner's guide to NCT's new unit, WayV|website=SBS PopAsia|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190511151645/https://www.sbs.com.au/popasia/blog/2019/01/03/beginners-guide-ncts-new-unit-wayv|archive-date=May 11, 2019|date=January 3, 2019|url-status=live|access-date=May 23, 2021}}</ref> In 2015, Lucas was cast through SM Global Audition in Hong Kong,<ref>{{Cite web|title='아는 형님' NCT 루카스, SM 오디션 합격 비결은?|url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=011&aid=0003374039|access-date=13 August 2020|website=entertain.naver.com|language=ko|archive-date=6 November 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191106144858/https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=011&aid=0003374039|url-status=live}}</ref> in July of the same year, Kun at Beijing, China,{{Citation needed|date=September 2021}} while Winwin was street-cast in his school by an SM Casting Manager and passed the audition.{{Citation needed|date=September 2021}} In February 2016, YangYang passed an audition in Taiwan.{{Citation needed|date=September 2021}} Hendery was street-cast in 2017. Xiaojun was cast in 2018.{{Citation needed|date=September 2021}}

All seven members of WayV were originally part of [[SM Rookies]], a pre-debut team of trainees under [[SM Entertainment]]. In 2014, Ten alongside some of his co-members appeared on the [[Mnet (TV channel)|Mnet]]-produced ''[[Exo 90:2014]]'', a show starring labelmates [[Exo (group)|Exo]], where they get to do dance performances to 90s K-pop songs. In April 2016, Kun sang with NCT U's in the Chinese version of "Without You" and joined them for the song's promotional stages,{{Citation needed|date=September 2021}} while Winwin joined as dancer for NCT U's "The 7th Sense" at their first live performance in China at the 16th Music Feng Yun Bang Awards at the same month.{{Citation needed|date=September 2021}}

On January 28, 2016, SM founder and producer [[Lee Soo-man]] announced plans for a new boy group NCT with several different units to be based in cities around the world.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://kpopherald.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=201602231707438293945_2&ACE_SEARCH=1|title=SM Entertainment continues expansion with New Culture Technology|website=Kpop Herald|date=November 23, 2018|access-date=November 23, 2018|archive-date=June 14, 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180614171928/http://kpopherald.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=201602231707438293945_2&ACE_SEARCH=1|url-status=live}}</ref>

Ten debuted with the first unit, NCT U, on April 9, 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://english.yonhapnews.co.kr/culturesports/2016/04/04/19/0701000000AEN20160404006300315F.html|title=SM's neo-boy band 'NCT U' to launch this week|date=April 4, 2016|website=[[Yonhap]]|access-date=November 23, 2018|archive-date=September 20, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160920032511/http://english.yonhapnews.co.kr/culturesports/2016/04/04/19/0701000000AEN20160404006300315F.html|url-status=live}}</ref> Although Kun participated in the Chinese version of the NCT U song "Without You" in 2016 he was not considered as a member until he and Lucas debuted in NCT 2018, a project involving all members of NCT, and promotions of their album ''[[NCT 2018 Empathy|Empathy]]''.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://m.chosun.com/svc/article.html?sname=news&contid=2016040900704|title=SM 신규 보이그룹 NCT, 출격 준비 완료…10일 자정 'WITHOUT YOU'발표|last=김|first=준석|date=April 9, 2016|work=Chosun Ilbo|access-date=December 4, 2019}}{{Dead link|date=May 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://news.sbs.co.kr/news/endPage.do?news_id=N1004599958&plink=SEARCH&cooper=SBSNEWSSEARCH|title=NCT Unveils 2 New Members; On August 13, 2018, SM announced their plan to launch a China-oriented unit under the project name, NCT China.<ref name=billboard1>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/k-town/8469964/k-pop-label-sm-entertainment-to-launch-china-oriented-nct-group|title=K-pop Label SM Entertainment to Launch China-Oriented NCT Group|date=August 13, 2018|access-date=November 23, 2018|website=Billboard|archive-date=November 16, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201116082819/https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/k-town/8469964/k-pop-label-sm-entertainment-to-launch-china-oriented-nct-group|url-status=live}}</ref>

The formation of WayV was announced through the group's official Weibo account on December 31.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=109&aid=0003929238|title=SM "中신인 웨이션브이, 2019년 1월 데뷔확정"…NCT 대거 합류 [공식입장]|date=December 31, 2018|website=Osen|language=ko|access-date=January 3, 2019|archive-date=April 11, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190411103306/https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=109&aid=0003929238|url-status=live}}</ref> The group consists of seven members: the four preexisting NCT members - Kun, Winwin, Ten and Lucas - along with three new members who were just introduced through SM Rookies earlier that year - Hendery, Xiaojun, and Yangyang. The group was announced to be under SM Entertainment's new China-exclusive label, Label V.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://koreajoongangdaily.joins.com/news/article/Article.aspx?aid=3057566|title=SM’s latest boy band is set to debut|date=January 1, 2019|website=Korea JoongAng Daily|access-date=January 3, 2019|archive-date=January 3, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190103215823/http://koreajoongangdaily.joins.com/news/article/Article.aspx?aid=3057566|url-status=live}}</ref><ref name=billboard2>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/international/8492077/wayv-nct-associated-boy-band-china|title=New NCT-Associated Boy Band WayV Set to Debut in China|date=January 2, 2019|website=Billboard|access-date=January 3, 2019|archive-date=January 3, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190103155703/https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/international/8492077/wayv-nct-associated-boy-band-china|url-status=live}}</ref>

The group is a sub-unit of NCT, although it was not promoted as an NCT group until the announcement of NCT 2020.<ref name="Forbes" /><ref name="nct 2020"/> Journalists theorize that the distancing of WayV from the NCT label was due to [[China–South Korea relations#Deployment of THAAD in South Korea|tense relations between China and South Korea]].<ref name="Forbes" /><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.mtv.com/news/3109825/wayv-debut-c-pop-guide/ |title=With WayV, All Eyes are on C-Pop |last=Assis |first=Tássia |date=January 17, 2019 |website=[[MTV News]] |access-date=November 12, 2019 |archive-date=December 7, 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191207231655/http://www.mtv.com/news/3109825/wayv-debut-c-pop-guide/ |url-status=live }}</ref> Despite this, since their formation, SM Entertainment CEO Chris Lee had acknowledged WayV as NCT's China-based unit.<ref name=NCT/>

=== 2020: ''Awaken the World'' and NCT 2020 ===
In 2020, WayV was the second group from SM Entertainment to hold a live online concert - jointly organized by SM Entertainment and [[Naver]], as part of the world's first online-dedicated live concert series ''[[Beyond LIVE]]''. The live concert, which was WayV first-ever concert and titled ''WayV - Beyond the Vision'', was held on May 3.<ref>{{cite web|access-date=2020-05-01|title=SuperM, 26일 '비욘드 라이브' 스트리밍 서비스 첫 공연 주자..특급 라인업 예고|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/now/article/109/0004194920|website=n.news.naver.com|archive-date=2020-06-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200617013320/https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/now/article/109/0004194920|url-status=live}}</ref> The group performed songs from their prior albums as well as a [[Mashup (music)|mashup]] of "Turn Back Time" and "Bad Alive", two tracks on their first studio album ''[[Awaken the World]]'' which was released five weeks later on June 9, 2020.<ref>{{cite web|access-date=2020-05-04|title='비욘드 라이브' WayV, 'Yeah Yeah Yeah' 최초 공개 "어제 한숨도 못자..잊지 못할 것"[V라이브]|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/109/0004200087|website=n.news.naver.com|archive-date=2020-06-09|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200609222107/https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/109/0004200087|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |author1=Alex Kusumapradja |title=WayV Sukses Menyapa Fans Global Melalui Konser Virtual "Beyond The Vision" |url=https://billboardid.com/wayv-sukses-menyapa-fans-global-melalui-konser-virtual-beyond-the-vision/ |website=Billboard Indonesia |access-date=23 June 2020 |language=id |date=4 May 2020 |archive-date=16 May 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200516060057/https://billboardid.com/wayv-sukses-menyapa-fans-global-melalui-konser-virtual-beyond-the-vision/ |url-status=dead }}</ref>

===2021–present: ''Kick Back''===

== Sub-units ==
In June 2021, SM Entertainment formed the group's first sub-unit named WayV-Kun & Xiaojun, composed of members Kun and Xiaojun. The sub-unit debuted by releasing a pop-ballad [[Single (music)#In South Korea|single album]] titled ''Back to You''.<ref>{{cite web |title=WayV 쿤-샤오쥔, 첫 유닛 출격..16일 새 싱글 'Back To You' 발매[공식] |url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/112/0003443753 |website=Naver |publisher=HeraldPop |access-date=12 June 2021 |language=ko |date=11 June 2021 |archive-date=12 June 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210612070202/https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/112/0003443753 |url-status=live }}</ref> In August 17, Ten and Yangyang formed a second sub-unit named WayV-Ten & Yangyang and debuted with a hip-hop single titled "Low Low".<ref>{{cite web |title=威神V第二支小分队出击威神V（WayV）- TEN&YANGYANG单曲《Low Low》17日发行 娱乐中国 |url=http://chinayule.cn/music/china/20210813/15637.html |website=chinayule |access-date=13 August 2021 |language=zh |date=13 August 2021 |archive-date=13 August 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210813061900/http://chinayule.cn/music/china/20210813/15637.html |url-status=live }}</ref> A third unit WayV-Lucas & Hendery was scheduled to release a debut digital single titled "Jalapeño" on August 25 but canceled due to the controversy surrounding Lucas' private life.<ref>{{cite web |title=루카스 사생활 논란 인정…"심려 끼쳐 죄송" |url=https://www.hankyung.com/entertainment/article/202108258310H |website=Hankyung |access-date=30 August 2021 |language=ko |date=25 August 2021 |archive-date=30 August 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210830095445/https://www.hankyung.com/entertainment/article/202108258310H |url-status=live }}</ref>
