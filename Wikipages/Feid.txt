'''Salomón Alejandro Villada Hoyos''', better known as '''Feid''' (born 19 August 1992), is a Colombian singer and songwriter.<ref name="elpalpitar.com">{{cite web|last1=Castañeda|first1=Krystell|title=Feid, un músico y compositor que eligió el género urbano como proyecto de vida|url=http://www.elpalpitar.com/noticias/entretenimiento/2015/07/feid-un-musico-y-compositor-que-eligio-el-genero-urbano-como-proyecto-de-vida/|website=elpalpitar.com|publisher=El Palpitar|accessdate=2 May 2017|language=Spanish}}</ref><ref name="BB">{{Cite web |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/latin/7800962/feid-billboard-latin-artist-on-the-rise |title=Feid: Billboard Latin Artist On The Rise |last=Cobo |first=Leila |date=19 April 2017 |website=Billboard |access-date=2018-01-09}}</ref> Born in [[Medellín, Colombia]],<ref name="buenamusica.com" /> he has worked with several well-known singers such as [[J Balvin]], [[Sebastian Yatra]], [[Nacho (singer)|Nacho]] and [[Yaga & Mackie|Mackie]].<ref name="elpalpitar.com" /><ref name="buenamusica.com" /><ref>{{cite web|last1=Pimiento|first1=Alexandra|title=El compositor detrás de 'Ginza', el último éxito de J Balvin|url=http://www.eltiempo.com/archivo/documento/CMS-16517022|website=eltiempo.com|publisher=El Tiempo|accessdate=2 May 2017|language=Spanish}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Kerguelen|first1=María|title=FEID, el compositor estrella|url=http://alo.co/entretenimiento/feid-el-compositor-de-jbalvin-reikon-y-otros|website=alo.co|accessdate=2 May 2017|language=Spanish}}</ref><ref name="BB" />

== Biography ==
Feid was born on August 19, 1992 in Medellín, Colombia.<ref name="buenamusica.com">{{cite web|title=Biografía de Feid|url=https://www.buenamusica.com/feid/biografia|website=buenamusica.com|accessdate=2 May 2017|language=Spanish}}</ref> He is son of Berta Lucía Hoyos, a preschool teacher and psychologist.<ref name="musica.com">{{cite web|title=Biografía de Feid - Música.com|url=http://www.musica.com/letras.asp?info=75754&biografia=40546&idf=5|website=musica.com|accessdate=2 May 2017|language=Spanish}}</ref> His father, Jorge Mario Villada, is a teacher in Arts and university teacher. His sister Manuela Villada Hoyos is currently a graphic design student.<ref name="musica.com" /> He attended the Music Extension Courses offered by the University of [[Antioquia Department|Antioquia]] in the city of [[Medellín]].<ref name="musica.com" />

In his childhood, he learned to play clarinet, which he then later abandoned after he decided he wanted to start singing. When he later went to college, he took singing classes and sung in front of his friends. He also was in the college choir. He finally decided to start focusing on making reggaeton music. He also sung at college parties, birthday parties, and reunions. Even though he is a reggaeton artist, he prefers hip-hop and R&B and follows artists like Drake and Chris Brown. He became a reggaeton artist because he saw that his generation liked reggaeton a lot and decided to go down that path.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.eltiempo.com/archivo/documento/CMS-16517022|title=El compositor detrás de 'Ginza', el último éxito de J Balvin|first=Casa Editorial El|last=Tiempo|website=El Tiempo}}</ref>

He has recently worked with several well-known artists like [[J Balvin]] and [[Sebastian Yatra]]. He also helped write the popular reggaeton song by [[J Balvin]] called [[Ginza (song)|Ginza]].<ref name="BB" /> His most popular song, Sigueme (Ft. [[Sech (singer)|Sech]]), has over 115 million views on YouTube.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Feid, Sech - Sígueme (Remix) (Video Oficial) - YouTube|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dTbONq0zxRA|access-date=2020-07-28|website=www.youtube.com}}</ref>

The song "[[Dorado (song)|Dorado]]", in which he has collaborated with the italian rappers [[Mahmood (singer)|Mahmood]] and [[Sfera Ebbasta]], will be released on 10 July 2020.
