'''Leland Sanford Austin''' (born March 26, 1986), better known by his [[stage name]] '''Yung L.A.''' or '''Da Boi Lay''', is an American [[Hip hop music|hip hop]] recording artist from [[Atlanta, Georgia]]. He is perhaps best known for his debut single "[[Ain't I]]", which peaked at number 47 on the US [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]] chart and at number seven on ''Billboard''{{'}}s [[Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs]] chart. Austin's second single, titled "[[Futuristic Love (Elroy)]]", features American singer [[Ricco Barrino]] and was released in 2009.

==Music career==

===2008–2011: Mixtapes and ''Futuristic Leland''===
In 2008, Austin was signed to American record executive [[T.I.]]'s [[Grand Hustle Records]], after fellow [[Atlanta]] artist [[Young Dro]] found and brought Austin to the label. On April 12, 2008, Austin released his debut mixtape ''Offset Shawty''.<ref>[http://www.livemixtapes.com/mixtapes/3003/yung_la_offset_shawty.html Yung LA – Offset Shawty – DJ Spinz]. Livemixtapes.com. Retrieved on February 13, 2016.</ref> On November 25, 2008, Austin released his debut single, titled "[[Ain't I]]"; it features appearances from his fellow Grand Hustle label artist Young Dro and T.I., it went on to chart on the [[Billboard Hot 100]] at number 47 and be [[platinum certification|certified platinum]] by the [[Recording Industry Association of America|RIAA]]. On December 13, 2008, it was announced and confirmed that Austin had signed a label deal with [[Interscope Records]] and he was working on his debut album, titled ''Futuristic Leland''.<ref>[http://www.ballerstatus.com/2008/12/13/ti-grand-hustle-team-up-with-interscope-to-release-yung-las-debut/ T.I., Grand Hustle Team Up With Interscope To Release Yung LA's Debut]. BallerStatus.com (December 13, 2008). Retrieved on 2016-02-13.</ref> On December 28, 2008, Austin released his mixtape titled ''The Matrix''.<ref>[http://www.livemixtapes.com/mixtapes/3652/yung_la_the_matrix.html Yung L.A. – The Matrix – DJ Coolbreeze]. Livemixtapes.com. Retrieved on February 13, 2016.</ref>

On January 10, 2009, Austin released his first collaboration mixtape titled ''Black Boy Swag, White Boy Tags'' with Young Dro.<ref>[http://www.livemixtapes.com/mixtapes/3687/young_dro_yung_la_black_boy_swag_white_boy_tags.html Young Dro & Yung LA – Black Boy Swag, White Boy Tags – DJ Infamous]. Livemixtapes.com. Retrieved on February 13, 2016.</ref> On April 7, 2009, Austin released his second single titled "[[Futuristic Love (Elroy)]]"; it features [[Ricco Barrino]], though it did not match the success of "Ain't I" it charted on the [[Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs|Billboard R&B/Hip-Hop]] chart at number 55. On June 2, 2009, Austin released his mixtape titled ''Lamborghini Leland''.<ref>[http://www.datpiff.com/Yung-LA-Lamborghini-Leland-mixtape.48684.html Yung LA – Lamborghini Leland Hosted by Drama Mixtape – Stream & Download]. Datpiff.com. Retrieved on February 13, 2016.</ref><ref>[http://www.livemixtapes.com/mixtapes/10378/yung_la_lamborghini_leland.html Yung L.A. – Lamborghini Leland – DJ Drama]. Livemixtapes.com. Retrieved on February 13, 2016.</ref> On November 5, 2009, Austin released his mixtape titled ''I Think I Can Sing''.<ref>[http://www.datpiff.com/Yung-LA-I-Think-I-Can-Sang-mixtape.76110.html Yung LA – I Think I Can Sang Hosted by Holiday Mixtape – Stream & Download]. Datpiff.com. Retrieved on February 13, 2016.</ref> On December 14, 2009, Austin released his second collaboration mixtape, titled ''Batman & Robin (Superhero Language)'', with fellow Atlanta artist J Money aka J.Futuristic.<ref>[http://www.livemixtapes.com/mixtapes/10828/jfuturistic_yung_la_batman_robin.html J.Futuristic & Yung L.A. – Batman & Robin (Superhero Language) – DJ Spinz]. Livemixtapes.com. Retrieved on February 13, 2016.</ref>

On February 24, 2010, Austin released his mixtape titled ''Crush Da Block''.<ref>[http://www.datpiff.com/Yung-LA-Crush-Da-Block-mixtape.100843.html Yung LA – Crush Da Block Hosted by DJ Infamous & Don Cannon Mixtape – Stream & Download]. Datpiff.com (February 24, 2010). Retrieved on 2016-02-13.</ref><ref>[http://www.livemixtapes.com/mixtapes/11379/yung_la_crush_da_block.html Yung L.A. – Crush Da Block – DJ Infamous, DJ Don Cannon]. Livemixtapes.com. Retrieved on February 13, 2016.</ref> On February 24, 2010, Austin released his mixtape titled ''Suntrust Leland''.<ref>[http://www.datpiff.com/Yung-LA-Suntrust-Leland-mixtape.146941.html Yung LA – Suntrust Leland Hosted by Dj Smallz Mixtape – Stream & Download]. Datpiff.com (August 25, 2010). Retrieved on 2016-02-13.</ref><ref>Kerry, Steve. Hotnewhiphop.com. Retrieved on 2016-02-13.</ref><ref>[http://www.livemixtapes.com/mixtapes/12182/yung_la_suntrust_leland.html Yung L.A. – Suntrust Leland – DJ Smallz]. Livemixtapes.com. Retrieved on February 13, 2016.</ref>

In early 2011 it was revealed that Austin had parted ways from Grand Hustle and was dropped from [[Interscope Records|Interscope]]. It was also the shelving of his debut album ''Futuristic Leland''. On September 16, 2011, Austin released his mixtape titled ''Crush Da Block 2''.<ref>[http://www.datpiff.com/Yung-LA-Crush-Da-Block-2-mixtape.262894.html Yung LA – Crush Da Block 2 Hosted by DJ P Exclusivez Mixtape – Stream & Download]. Datpiff.com (September 16, 2011). Retrieved on 2016-02-13.</ref>

===2012: ''Check''===
In a March 27, 2012 interview Austin said he was "dropped from [[Interscope Records|Interscope]] because my [second] single didn't match the success of the first one". He also said that he wished to leave [[Grand Hustle Records]], that the label had failed him professionally by not following up his hit "Ain't I" with the release of his album, and failed him personally by ignoring him when he was hurt and had asked for their help and by entangling him in disputes that didn't involve him and had begun before he joined the label.<ref name="YungL.A.interview1">[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nV9j1aPW_K4 Yung LA Speaks Out On Alley Boy Fight, Walking Away From Grand Hustle Because Of Beef]. YouTube (March 27, 2012). Retrieved on 2016-02-13.</ref><ref>[http://www.worldstarhiphop.com/videos/video.php?v=wshh08WjWH8qS0pV0YIs Yung LA Speaks Out On Alley Boy Fight, Getting Dropped From Interscope, Walking Away From Grand Hustle Because Of Beef, & More! | New Video]. Worldstarhiphop.com (March 27, 2012). Retrieved on 2016-02-13.</ref><ref name="xxlmag.com">[http://www.xxlmag.com/xxl-magazine/2011/02/yung-la-explains-face-tattoo/ Yung LA Explains Face Tattoo – XXL]. Xxlmag.com. Retrieved on February 13, 2016.</ref>

On November 27, 2012, Austin released his debut album titled ''Check''; it was distributed by independent [[Atlanta]] based record label U-Digg Music Group.<ref>[http://www.mtv.com/artists/yung-la/discography/3000934/ Check by Yung L.A.]. MTV (January 29, 2013). Retrieved on 2016-02-13.</ref>

==Controversy==
In February 2011 Austin had the logo of a local record label tattooed on his face after a conversation with the label's [[Chief executive officer|CEO]] Big Bank Black.<ref name="xxlmag.com"/> Black's brother Alley Boy, the label's co-CEO, demanded that Austin remove the tattoo because he wasn't signed to the label and wasn't given permission to use the logo.<ref>[http://www.xxlmag.com/xxl-magazine/2011/02/alley-boy-speaks-on-yung-las-face-tattoo/ Alley Boy Speaks on Yung LA’s Face Tattoo – XXL]. Xxlmag.com. Retrieved on February 13, 2016.</ref> Austin countered that Alley Boy lacked the authority to make such a demand because Big Bank Black, not Alley Boy, was "the real CEO" of the label.<ref name="xxlmag.com"/> He did though have a [[Los Angeles Dodgers]] logo tattooed over it two weeks later.<ref>[http://www.ballerstatus.com/2011/03/09/yung-la-covers-up-duck-tattoo-says-real-ns-do-real-sh/ Yung LA Covers Up Duck Tattoo, Says 'Real N****s Do Real Sh**']. BallerStatus.com. Retrieved on February 13, 2016.</ref> Austin was set up and viciously assaulted by Alley Boy and members of his label; whether he was beaten before covering the tattoo or after wasn't known.<ref>{{cite web |title=Yung LA Catches Beat Down At Hands Of Alley Boy |url=https://www.ballerstatus.com/2011/04/19/yung-la-catches-beat-down-at-hands-of-alley-boy-for-tough-talk-duck-tattoo/ |date=2011-04-19 |website=BallerStatus.com |access-date=2021-09-24}}</ref>

In April 2011, Austin was arrested for criminal damage to property and released on bail.<ref>[http://hiphopwired.com/2011/04/25/yung-l-a-arrested/ Yung L.A. Arrested]. Hip-Hop Wired (April 25, 2011). Retrieved on 2016-02-13.</ref>
