'''Victor "Vic" Terrell Spencer''' (born September 23, 1981), also known by his stage name, "'''Vic Spencer'''" is an American rapper from Chicago, Illinois.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/pictures/10-new-artists-you-need-to-know-february-2015-20150219/vic-spencer-20150217|title=10 Artists You Need to Know Now|work=[[Rolling Stone]]}}</ref> He has worked [[Sean Price]],<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.illanoize.co/home/vic-spencer-on-being-underrated-signing-to-sean-price-and-dropping-6-albums-in-2020-on-illanoize-radio|title=Vic Spencer on Being Underrated, Signing to Sean Price, and Dropping 6 Albums in 2020 on iLLANOiZE Radio}} ''iLLANOiZE Radio''</ref> [[Ghostface Killah]],<ref>{{cite news|url=https://insomniacmagazine.com/ghostface-killah-sheek-louch-vic-spencer-reignwolf-luke-holland-say-i-think-i-saw-a-ghost/|title=GhostfaceKillah, Sheek Louch, Vic Spencer, Reignwolf & Luke Holland Say "I Think I Saw A Ghost"}} ''Insomniac Magazine''</ref> [[Twista]],<ref>{{cite news|url=http://thehiphopdemocrat.com/2019/07/26/chicago-emcees-vic-spencer-twista-show-off-their-impeccable-bars-in-new-video-hardcore-rap-theory/|title=Chicago Emcees Vic Spencer & Twista Show Off Their Impeccable Bars in New Video, Hardcore Rap Theory}} ''Hip Hop Democrat''</ref> [[Roc Marciano]],<ref>{{cite news|url=https://insomniacmagazine.com/vic-spencer-x-original-super-legend-deliver-legend-laws-of-poweralbumft-roc-marciano-ransom-joe-fresh-goods/|title=Vic Spencer x Original Super Legend Deliver "Legend Laws of Power"(Album)ft. Roc Marciano, Ransom, Joe Fresh Goods}} ''Insomniac Magazine''</ref> [[Denmark Vessey]],<ref>{{cite news|url=https://westcheddar.com/2018/04/21/sellout/|title=Sellout (music)}} ''Westcheddar''</ref> and many other artists/musicians. In 2017, he was featured on the [[Twista]] album ''Crook County'',<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/twista-mortuary-feat-vic-spencer-new-song.1974818.html|title=Twista – Mortuary Feat. Vic Spencer|work=HotNewHipHop|accessdate=28 March 2018}}</ref> and [[Sean Price]]'s album ''[[Imperius Rex]]''.{{citation needed|date=March 2018}} In April, 2018 he contributed a track on a [[Denmark Vessey]] album, produced by [[Earl Sweatshirt]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://uproxx.com/hiphop/denmark-vessey-sun-go-nova-knxwledge/|title=Detroit's Denmark Vessey Drops The Knxwledge-Produced 'Sun Go Nova'|date=22 February 2018|work=[[Uproxx]]|accessdate=28 March 2018}}</ref>
