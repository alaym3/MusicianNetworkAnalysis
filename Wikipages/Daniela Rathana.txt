'''Daniela Sörensen''', better known by her stage name '''Daniela Rathana''' is a Swedish singer and songwriter. She was born and grew up in [[Akalla]] and was a backup singer for [[Sabina Ddumba]], [[Kent (band)|Kent]], [[Zara Larsson]] and [[Seinabo Sey]] before embarking on a solo career.<ref name="red">{{Cite web|url=https://www.redbull.com/se-en/music/7-swedish-acts-to-discover|title=Pandemic Playlist: 7 Swedish Acts To Discover At Home|date=20 May 2020|first=Özgür|last=Kurtoğlu|publisher=[[Red Bull]]|access-date=16 May 2021}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://gaffa.se/artikel/141187/jag-fistar-folk-i-oeronen-med-mina-budskap|title="Jag fistar folk i öronen med mina budskap"|website=gaffa.se|accessdate=19 February 2021}}</ref> In 2020, she released her debut EP, ''Halva vägen fri''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://popmani.se/daniela-rathana-en-kvinna-kan-gora-allt/|title=Daniela Rathana: "En kvinna kan göra allt"|first=Josefin|last=Östby|date=29 September 2020|accessdate=19 February 2021}}</ref> In February 2021, she released the song "Testamente", a duet with [[Oskar Linnros]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://sverigesradio.se/artikel/daniela-rathana-oskar-linnros-testamente|title=Daniela Rathana och Oskar Linnros sluter cirkeln i "Testamente" - P3 Din Gata|first=Sveriges|last=Radio|agency=Sveriges Radio|accessdate=19 February 2021}}</ref> Her debut album is set for release in 2021 via Linnros' label Dundra; it will include the singles "Ansikte" and "Testamente".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://gaffa.se/nyhet/147451/daniela-rathana-och-oskar-linnros-slaepper-singel-helt-oplanerat-och-spontant|title=Daniela Rathana och Oskar Linnros släpper singel – "helt oplanerat och spontant"|date=5 February 2021|first=Daniel|last=Horn|language=sv|work=[[Gaffa (magazine)|Gaffa]]|access-date=16 May 2021}}</ref>
