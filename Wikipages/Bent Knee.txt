'''Bent Knee''' is an American art rock band formed in [[Boston]], [[Massachusetts]], in 2009. The band performs in multiple genres and draws from multiple influences, including [[pop music|pop]], [[industrial rock]], [[progressive rock]],<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/say-so-by-bent-knee-review-1464125585|title=‘Say So’ by Bent Knee Review|last=Fusilli|first=Jim|date=2016-05-24|newspaper=Wall Street Journal|issn=0099-9660|access-date=2016-07-25}}</ref> and [[avant-garde music|avant-garde]]. The band is known for unpredictable dynamic contrast and the wide vocal range of singer Courtney Swain.<ref name="DPRP SEB Review">{{cite web|url=http://www.dprp.net/reviews/2016-010.php#bentknee|title=Bent Knee - Shiny Eyed Babies|first=Owen|last=Davies|publisher=DPRP|access-date=3 September 2016}}</ref>

Bent Knee has toured with [[The Dillinger Escape Plan]],<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.blabbermouth.net/news/the-dillinger-escape-plan-announces-fall-u-s-tour/|title=THE DILLINGER ESCAPE PLAN Announces Fall U.S. Tour|date=2016-08-22|publisher=Blabbermouth}}</ref> [[Haken (band)|Haken]],<ref name="haken1">{{cite web |title=Haken & Leprous Join Forces for North American Tour |url=https://www.hakenmusic.com/blog/post/us-tour-haken-leprous-bent-knee |website=hakenmusic.com |publisher=[[Haken (band)|Haken]] |access-date=December 7, 2018}}</ref> [[Leprous]],<ref name="MI1">{{cite web |last=Kennelty |first=Greg |title=Haken, Leprous & Bent Knee to Tour North America This Fall |url=http://www.metalinjection.net/tour-dates/haken-leprous-bent-knee-to-tour-north-america-this-fall |work=Metal Injection |access-date=December 7, 2018}}</ref> [[Thank You Scientist]],<ref>{{cite web|url=http://progreport.com/thank-scientist-tour-dates-bent-knee-announced/|title=Thank You Scientist tour dates with Bent Knee announced|date=2017-03-10|publisher=The Prog Report}}</ref> and [[Mike Keneally]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/mike.keneally/posts/10152156348416593|title=School For Robots and Bent Knee open for us tonight in Cambridge, MA.|author=Mike Keneally|publisher=Facebook|date=27 May 2014|access-date=3 September 2016}}</ref> In 2016, they signed to [[Cuneiform Records]] for the release of their third album, ''[[Say So (album)|Say So]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cuneiformrecords.com/bandshtml/bentknee.html|title=Cuneiform Records - Bent Knee|publisher=Cuneiform Records|date=2015-12-01}}</ref> In 2017, they signed a deal with [[Inside Out Music]] and released their fourth album, ''Land Animal''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.insideoutmusic.com/artist.aspx?IdArtist=1947|title=Inside Out - Bent Knee|date=2017-04-19|publisher=Inside Out Music}}</ref>

==History==
Bent Knee was formed at the [[Berklee College of Music]] in 2009, when guitarist Ben Levin and vocalist Courtney Swain began writing music together. The band's name is a compound of the founders' names ("Ben" + "[Cour]tney").

The band self-released their first LP, ''Bent Knee'' in 2011, then self-released their follow-up, ''Shiny Eyed Babies'' in 2014.<ref name="Bent Knee bids">{{cite time|first=Steve|last=Smith|date=2016-01-24|website=Boston Globe|access-date=2016-07-25}}</ref> In late 2015, they were signed to [[Cuneiform Records]], an American independent record label,<ref Knee Signs With Cuneiform Records|date=2015-12-05|archive-date=2016-08-13|website=Bent Knee Music|url-status=dead|df=}}</ref> on which they released ''[[Say So (album)|Say So]]'' in 2016.<ref>{{Cite
