'''Surf Curse''' is an American [[Surf music|surf rock]] band formed in 2013 in [[Reno, Nevada]]. It currently consists of [[Nick Rattigan]] (lead vocals, drums) and Jacob Rubeck (guitars).<ref name="allindependentreno">{{cite web|url=https://allindependentreno.com/2016/09/15/surf-curse/|title=Episode 11: Surf Curse &#124; All Independent Reno Podcast|website=allindependentreno.com|accessdate=2017-08-13}}</ref>

==History==
Since the band's formation, they have released three full-length albums and one extended play.<ref name="bandcamp">{{cite web|url=https://surfcurse.bandcamp.com/music|title=Music|website=Surf Curse|accessdate=2017-08-13}}</ref> Though they are originally from [[Reno, Nevada]], they have since moved to [[Los Angeles, California]], where they have become a part of the local DIY, all-ages punk scene. In 2016, the band played at the annual Beach Goth festival, hosted by [[Eric Andre]] and [[The Growlers]].<ref name="atwoodmagazine">{{cite web|url=http://atwoodmagazine.com/strange-kind-surf-curse/|title=Surf Curse Run Through Life Dazed and Confused on &quot;The Strange and the Kind&quot;|website=Atwood Magazine|accessdate=2017-08-13}}</ref>

In May 2021, after becoming popular on [[TikTok]], "[[Freaks (Surf Curse song)|Freaks]]" reached number 64 on the [[UK Singles Chart]], eight years after it first appeared on their debut album ''Buds''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.musicweek.com/analysis/read/charts-analysis-body-s-sales-up-for-tion-wayne-russ-millions/083272|title=Charts analysis: Body's sales up for Tion Wayne & Russ Millions|work=[[Music Week]]|last=Masterton|first=James|date=14 May 2021|access-date=16 May 2021|url-access=subscription}}</ref>
