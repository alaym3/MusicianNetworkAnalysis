'''Stefan Đurić''' ({{lang-sr-cyr|Стефан Ђурић}}; born 16 November 1989), better known as '''Rasta''' ({{lang-sr-cyr|Раста}}), is a [[Serbia]]n [[recording artist]], [[record producer|producer]] and founder of the record label imprint Balkaton. Regarded as one of the most popular artists from his generation, he is best known for using and popularizing the [[Auto-Tune]] [[pitch correction]] effect on the regional music scene and is also noted for often promoting [[marijuana]] in his songs.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.novosti.rs/vesti/scena.147.html:768939-Dzala-Buba-Maja-i-Rasta-tinejdzerska-bozanstva|title=Džala, Buba, Maja i Rasta tinejdžerska božanstva|date=December 30, 2018|work=[[Večernje novosti]]|language=sr}}</ref>

==Early life==
Stefan Đurić was born on 16 November 1989 in [[Pristina|Priština]] to parents Slađana and Drago Đurić, who worked as professors at the [[University of Belgrade]].<ref name="Balkanskom ulicom">{{Cite news|author=Jovović|first=Pero|date=June 24, 2020|title=Treća epizoda: Balkanskom ulicom sa Stefanom Đurićem Rastom|language=sr|work=[[Nova S|Nova.rs]]|url=https://nova.rs/zabava/showbiz/treca-epizoda-balkanskom-ulicom-sa-stefanom-duricem-rastom/}}</ref> When the [[Kosovo War]] began, he relocated to [[Srebrenica]] where he lived with his grandmother.<ref name="Balkanskom ulicom"/> Đurić eventually settled in [[Belgrade]] after several years, where he was reunited with his parents.<ref name="Balkanskom ulicom"/>

==Career==
Rasta began his career as a teenager hosting a show about [[reggae]] on the [[Radio Belgrade]]. He later focused on creating music and released his first mixtape ''Sensemilia Mixtape'' featuring LMR in 2009.<ref name="Beforafter.rs">{{Cite news|url=https://www.beforeafter.rs/muzika/rasta-10-godina-na-sceni/|title=Rasta: 10 godina na sceni|website=beforeafter.rs|accessdate=February 3, 2021|language=sr}}</ref> The following year, Rasta released his succeeding bodies of work, [[R&B]]-influenced ''One i lova'' (''Them and Money'') in collaboration with Cvija and [[crunk]]-influenced ''Kaseta'' (''The Cassette'') featuring hip-hop collective Unija.<ref name="Beforafter.rs"/> Same year in April, he also served as the opening act for [[50 Cent]] in Belgrade.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.b92.net/kultura/vesti.php?nav_category=271&yyyy=2010&mm=04&dd=02&nav_id=421955|title=50 Cent za 2.500 najvernijih|website=[[B92|b92.net]]|date=April 2, 2010|language=sr}}</ref>

In December 2011, Rasta released his first album, titled ''Superstar'', through Mascom Records.<ref name="Beforafter.rs"/> The album, produced by [[Slobodan Veljković Coby|Coby]], was also created in collaboration with Serbian hip-hop label Bassivity Music, whom he continued working with afterwards. In July 2014, Rasta broke through [[mainstream]] with his [[Viral video|viral hit]] "Kavasaki" (''[[Kawasaki motorcycles|Kawasaki]]''), collecting over 60 million views on [[YouTube]].<ref name="Beforafter.rs"/> It was followed by even more successful "Kavali" ([[Roberto Cavalli|''Cavalli'']]) in June the following year.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.blic.rs/zabava/vesti/necete-verovati-ovo-su-najgledaniji-jutjub-video-snimci-u-srbiji-2015-godine/r6dmtln|title=Nećete verovati! Ovo su najgledaniji Jutjub video snimci u Srbiji 2015. godine|website=[[Blic (newspaper)|Blic.rs]]|date=11 December 2015|language=sr|access-date=11 December 2019|archive-date=7 October 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201007190200/https://www.blic.rs/zabava/vesti/necete-verovati-ovo-su-najgledaniji-jutjub-video-snimci-u-srbiji-2015-godine/r6dmtln|url-status=live}}</ref> Also in 2015, he established a record label in partnership with Bassivity, called Balkaton. With singles "Euforija" (''Euphoria'') and "Hotel", released during the summer of 2016, he became one of the most popular regional artists and also saw significant popularity in [[Serbian diaspora]].<ref name="Beforafter.rs"/> In February 2017, Rasta released album ''Indigo'', which was divided into two parts. Same year, Đurić, alongside his then-wife [[Ana Nikolić]], served as a judge on the fifth season of ''[[Ja imam talenat!]]'', Serbian version of ''[[Got Talent]]''.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://tracara.com/potpisali-ugovor-ana-nikolic-i-rasta-u-ziriju-muzickog-takmicenja/|title=50 Potpisali ugovor! Ana Nikolić i Rasta u žiriju muzičkog takmičenja|publisher=Tracara.com|language=sr}}</ref> Then in 2018, he released ''Don Reggaeton'', which was followed by a concert at the [[Štark Arena|Belgrade Arena]] on 4 September, making him the second Serbian rap act to achieve so.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://starkarena.co.rs/cir/event-7750/|title=Rasta: Štark arena|website=starkarena.co.rs|accessdate=February 3, 2021|language=sr}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.vice.com/sr/article/9kvya5/vice-sa-rastom-u-bekstejdzu-koncerta-u-areni|title=Vice sa Rastom u bekstejdžu koncerta u Areni|website=[[Vice (magazine)|vice.com]]|author=S. Radojković|date=September 16, 2018|language=sr}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Đurić and singer [[Ana Nikolić]] married on 28 July 2016, after dating in secret for several months during their collaboration on the album ''[[Labilna]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.blic.rs/zabava/vesti/neverovatno-ana-nikolic-se-udala-za-11-godina-mladeg-rastu/tx7h4v6|title=Neverovatno! Ana Nikolić se udala za 11 godina mlađeg Rastu|website=[[Blic (newspaper)|Blic.rs]]|date=July 28, 2016|language=sr}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.blic.rs/zabava/ludi-od-ljubavi-ana-nikolic-zaljubljena-do-usiju/jqzz9v9|title=Ludi od ljubavi: Ana Nikolić zaljubljena do ušiju|date=March 13, 2016|website=[[Blic (newspaper)|Blic.rs]]|language=sr}}</ref> On 7 August 2017, Nikolić gave birth to daughter Tara.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.blic.rs/zabava/vesti/mama-i-beba-otisle-kuci-pogledajte-kako-ana-nikolic-izgleda-posle-porodaja-video/9wvges5|title=Mama i beba otišle kući, pogledajte kako Ana Nikolić izgleda posle porođaja|language=sr|access-date=7 August 2017|website=[[Blic (newspaper)|Blic.rs]]}}</ref> The couple eventually separated a year later.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tracara.com/prijateljica-potvrdila-ana-je-zauvek-zavrsila-sa-rastom/|title=Prijateljica potvrdila: Ana je zauvek zavrišla sa Rastom!|date=September 2018|language=sr|publisher=Tracara.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.blic.rs/zabava/odredili-visinu-alimentacije-za-cerku-ana-i-rasta-se-razvode-otkriven-jedan-od/tnnt8lf|title=Odredili visinu alimentacije za ćerku, Ana i Rasta se razvode!|year=2018|language=sr|work=[[Blic (newspaper)|Blic.rs]]}}</ref>

In April 2017, Đurić was accused of assaulting the owner of the label Mascom, Slobodan Nešović, due to unpaid wages of his 2012 release.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.novosti.rs/vesti/scena.147.html:750236-Rasta-Lokin-dug-veci-od-30000-evra|title=Rasta: Lokin dug veci od 30.000€|date=September 18, 2018|author=M. Ćunković|language=sr|publisher=Večernje Novosti}}</ref> In May, Rasta denied the accusation at court and was eventually released of charge two years later in March.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.novosti.rs/vesti/naslovna/hronika/aktuelno.291.html:666660-Rasta-negirao-da-je-napao-Loku|title=Rasta negirao da je napao Loku|date=May 22, 2018|language=sr|publisher=Večernje Novosti}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://tracara.com/sud-odlucio-rasta-nije-pretukao-loku/|title=Sud odlučio, Rasta nije pretukao Loku!|date=March 4, 2019|language=sr|publisher=Tracara.com}}</ref>
 
On October 2, 2020, he was also arrested on a charge of possessing [[illegal substance]]s.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://nova.rs/zabava/rasti-produzen-pritvor-zbog-droge-jos-30-dana-iza-resetaka/|title=Rasti produžen pritvor: Zbog droge još 30 dana iza rešetakka|website=nova.rs|language=sr|date=November 2, 2020}}</ref> After spending close to four months in jail, he pleaded guilty and was sentenced to a year of house arrest.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://nova.rs/zabava/stefan-duric-rasta-pusten-iz-pritvora-sacekao-ga-prijatelj/|title=Stefan Đurić Rasta pušten iz pritvora, sačekao ga prijatelj|website=nova.rs|author=M. Dejanović|language=sr|date=January 27, 2021}}</ref>
