}}'''Field Medic''' is the stage name of American [[lo-fi music|lo-fi]] musician Kevin Patrick Sullivan.<ref name="sfgate">{{cite web|author=[[Tony DuShane]]|title=Kevin Sullivan unleashes his emotions in Field Medic|url=http://www.sfgate.com/music/bandwidth/article/Kevin-Sullivan-unleashes-his-emotions-in-Field-5562551.php|publisher=[[San Francisco Chronicle]]|accessdate=November 24, 2017}}</ref>

==History==
He began releasing music with his older brother Sean in 2009, initially as Westwood & Willow and then with drummer Andrew Skewes-Cox as Rin Tin Tiger.<ref name="SF Gate">{{cite web|author=[[Tony DuShane]]|title=Rin Tin Tiger mixes folkie and fierce|url=https://www.sfgate.com/music/bandwidth/article/Rin-Tin-Tiger-mixes-folkie-and-fierce-3735359.php|publisher=SF Gate|accessdate=February 13, 2018}}</ref> He debuted as Field Medic with his first EP titled ''Crushed Pennies''.<ref name="insomniaradio">{{cite web|last1=Stepczyk|first1=Charles|title=Field Medic: Mongols Pasture|url=http://insomniaradio.net/2017/11/18/field-medic-mongols-pasture/|publisher=The Insomnia Radio Network|accessdate=November 23, 2017}}</ref> In 2015, Sullivan released his first full-length album titled ''Light Is Gone''.<ref name="varioussmallflames">{{cite web|last1=Doyle|first1=Liam|title=Field Medic – Light is Gone|url=https://varioussmallflames.co.uk/2015/09/29/field-medic-light-is-gone/|publisher=Various Small Flames|accessdate=November 23, 2017}}</ref> In 2017, Sullivan released an EP titled '' if i shout that the revolutions in my blind heart have left me on the mend, would i still have to surrender to the tides to exorcise this possession?'' on [[Bandcamp]].<ref name="thefader">{{cite web|last1=Mandel|first1=Leah|title=Listen To Field Medic’s Freaky Bedroom Folk Lament, "I AM SATAN"|url=http://www.thefader.com/2017/04/11/field-medic-i-am-satan|publisher=[[The Fader]]|accessdate=November 24, 2017}}</ref> Also in 2017, Sullivan signed to [[Run for Cover Records]] and released his first album on the label titled ''Songs From The Sunroom''.<ref
