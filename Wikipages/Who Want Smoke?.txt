"'''Who Want Smoke?'''" is a song by American rapper Nardo Wick. It was released through Flawless Entertainment as a single on January 22, 2021. A remix, which features [[G Herbo]], [[Lil Durk]], and [[21 Savage]], was released on October 8, 2021.<ref>{{cite web |title=Nardo Wick Drops "Who Want Smoke??" Ft. 21 Savage, Lil Durk, G Herbo|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/nardo-wick-drops-who-want-smoke-ft-21-savage-lil-durk-g-herbo-new-song.1992898.html?_amp|website=HotNewHipHop|last=Marie|first=Erika|date=October 8, 2021|accessdate=October 8, 2021}}</ref> The song will be included on Wick's debut album, ''Who Is Nardo Wick?''<ref>{{cite web|url=https://hypebeast.com/2021/11/nardo-wick-lil-baby-future-me-or-sum-stream|title=Future and Lil Baby Join Nardo Wick for New Single "Me or Sum"|work=Hypebeast|last=Caraan|first=Sophie|date=November 30, 2021|access-date=December 1, 2021}}</ref>

The song gained attention for its lyrics "Who want smoke with me?" and "What the fuck is that?", and a stomping dance to the song went viral on [[TikTok]].<ref>{{cite news |last1=Seabrook III |first1=Robby |title=The Break Presents â€“ Nardo Wick |url=https://www.xxlmag.com/nardo-wick-interview-the-break/ |date=October 13, 2021 |ref=[[XXL (magazine)|XXL]]}}</ref>

==Remix==
On October 8, 2021, Nardo Wick released a remix of the song, featuring fellow American rappers [[G Herbo]], [[Lil Durk]], and [[21 Savage]].<ref>{{cite web |title=G Herbo, Lil Durk, and 21 Savage Hop on Nardo Wick's "Who Want Smoke" Remix|url=https://thesource.com/2021/10/09/g-herbo-lil-durk-and-21-savage-hop-on-nardo-wicks-who-want-smoke-remix/|website=[[The Source]]|author=Mira B|date=October 9, 2021|accessdate=October 9, 2021}}</ref> The remix adds another question mark to the title and was released on G Herbo's 26th birthday. An accompanying music video, directed by [[Cole Bennett]] from Lyrical Lemonade, premiered on the same day.<ref>{{cite web |title=Lil Durk, 21 Savage & G Herbo Bolster Nardo Wick's 'Who Want Smoke?' Remix|url=https://hiphopdx.com/videos/id.29425/title.nardo-wick-g-herbo-lil-durk-21-savage-wanna-know-who-want-smoke|website=[[HipHopDX]]|last=Saponara|first=Michael|date=October 8, 2021|accessdate=October 8, 2021}}</ref>
