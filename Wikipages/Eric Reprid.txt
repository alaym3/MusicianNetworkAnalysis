'''Shi Yi Liu''', known professionally as '''Eric Reprid''', is a Canadian independent rapper and songwriter based in Vancouver.<ref>[https://hiphopcanada.com/eric-reprid-cold-world/ "Cold World: Budding Vancouver star Eric Reprid returns with new EP"]. ''HipHopCanada'', December 26, 2020.</ref><ref name=Tik>[https://www.coastmountainnews.com/entertainment/young-juno-nominees-on-why-theyre-moving-to-the-algorithm-of-tiktok/ "Young Juno nominees on why they’re moving to the algorithm of TikTok"], ''The Canadian Press'', via ''Coast Mountain News'', June 2, 2021.</ref> His 2020 single "Cold World" reached 24&nbsp;million plays<ref name=Tik/> and was nominated for [[Juno Award for Rap Recording of the Year|Rap Recording of the Year]] at the [[Juno Awards of 2021]];<ref name="cbc">Holly Gordon, [https://www.cbc.ca/music/junos/news/the-weeknd-jp-saxe-jessie-reyez-and-justin-bieber-lead-2021-juno-award-nominations-1.5940551 "The Weeknd, JP Saxe, Jessie Reyez and Justin Bieber lead 2021 Juno Award nominations"]. [[CBC Music]], March 9, 2021.</ref><ref>Rick Mele, [https://www.complex.com/music/inside-the-push-to-fix-the-juno-awards-rap-category "Inside the Push to Fix the Juno Awards’ Rap Category"], ''Complex Canada'', June 3, 2021.</ref> he was the only independent nominated in that category.<ref>Richard Trapunski, [https://www.straight.com/music/what-2021-juno-awards-nominations-say-about-state-of-canadian-music "What the 2021 Juno Awards nominations say about the state of Canadian music"], ''Straight.com'', March 9, 2021.</ref>
