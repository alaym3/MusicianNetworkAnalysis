'''Antwan Afram'''<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.dn.se/kultur-noje/ant-wan-beror-med-sin-halvt-dolda-sorgsenhet/|title=Ant Wan berör med sin halvt dolda sorgsenhet|date=6 April 2020|work=[[Dagens Nyheter]]|language=sv|url-access=subscription|access-date=28 February 2021}}</ref> (born 1 January 1998{{Citation needed|date=February 2021}}), known professionally as '''Ant Wan''', is a Swedish rapper of [[Arameans|Aramean]] descent from [[Västerås]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Ant Wan släpper nytt album om uppväxten i Västerås|url=https://www.kingsizemag.se/musik/ant-wan-slapper-nytt-album-om-uppvaxten-i-vasteras/|work=Kingsizemag.se|date=3 April 2020|access-date=6 October 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Ant Wan har inte glömt Råby på senaste albumet ’724’|url=https://dopest.se/ant-wan-album-724-bando-kids-music/|work=Dopest|access-date=6 October 2021}}</ref> He started rapping in [[Råby]] and released his first songs in early 2016.<ref>{{cite web|title=Ant Wan: "Ingen bar mig, jag kom hit själv"|url=https://gaffa.se/artikel/136473/ant-wan-ingen-bar-mig-jag-kom-hit-sjaelv|work=[[Gaffa (magazine)|Gaffa]]|date=4 May 2019|access-date=6 October 2021}}</ref> His songs gained popularity in late 2016. He made a few songs in 2017 and later in 2018 changed his artist name to Ant Wan.<ref name=dopest/> His five-track EP ''Wow'', which included the tracks "Wow", "Kall", "Rikare", "Fendi" and "Boo",<ref name=dopest>[https://dopest.se/ant-wan-ep-wow/ Dopest.se Ant Wan släpper sin debut-EP "WOW"]</ref> reached the top of the [[Swedish Albums Chart]].

In December 2019, Ant Wan founded his own record label, Bando Kids Music. It had signed the rappers Christian "10an" Homsi and 2M, but was closed down in March 2021 after the two signed artists were arrested and later convicted of serious crimes.<ref>{{cite web|title=10an gästas av Ant Wan på debutsingeln ”Mills”|url=https://dopest.se/10an-ant-wan-mills-debutsingel/|work=Dopest|access-date=6 October 2021}}</ref>
