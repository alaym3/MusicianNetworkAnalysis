'''Teniola Apata''', born 23 December (1992) better known as '''Teni''', is a Nigerian singer, songwriter and [[The Entertainer (play)|entertainer.]]<ref name="Fader">{{cite news |last1=Kochhar |first1=Nazuk |title=Teni rules, here's the proof |url= https://www.thefader.com/2018/10/18/teni-rules-askamaya-fake-jersey-african-pop |access-date=11 November 2018 |work=The FADER |date=18 October 2018}}</ref><ref name="TD">{{cite news |last1=Olatunbosun |first1=Yinka |title=Teni Makanaki's Strides to Stardom |url=https://www.thisdaylive.com/index.php/2018/10/26/teni-makanakis-strides-to-stardom/ |access-date=5 November 2018 |work=This Day |date=26 October 2018}}</ref>

==Life and music career==

=== Early life and career beginnings ===

Teni was born on 23 December 1992 in [[Lagos State|Lagos state]]. She is the younger sister of Nigerian singer [[Niniola]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://punchng.com/my-love-for-niniola-stronger-than-music-teni-entertainer/|title=My love for Niniola stronger than music ‚ÄìTeni Entertainer|work=Punch Newspapers|access-date=2018-09-23|language=en-US}}</ref> She attended [[Apata Memorial High School]] and later preceded to [[American InterContinental University]] where she obtained degree in business administration.<ref name="TC">{{cite news |last1=Machunga |first1=Saminu |title=SPOTLIGHT: My late father will be proud that his legacy is untarnished, says Teni |url=https://lifestyle.thecable.ng/spotlight-teniola-the-entertainer |access-date=5 November 2018 |work=The Cable |date=4 August 2018}}</ref>

== Career ==

=== 2016-2019: ''Fargin, Askamaya and Other Recordings'' ===

Teni released her debut single "Amen" while signed to [[Shizzi]]'s Magic Fingers Records.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://tooxclusive.com/download-mp3/shizzis-magic-fingers-records-presents-teni-amen/|title=Shizzi's Magic Fingers Records Presents: Teni - "Amen"|date=2016-12-23|website=tooXclusive|language=en-US|access-date=2019-01-13}}</ref> She left the record label and signed with Dr. Dolor Entertainment in 2017.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.pulse.ng/buzz/teni-entertainer-is-one-of-nigeria-s-fastest-rising-musicians-id6076858.html|title=Teni Entertainer is one of Nigeria's fastest rising musicians|last=Akan|first=Joey|access-date=2018-09-23|language=en-US}}</ref> Teni started gaining prominence after releasing the single "[[Fargin]]" in September 2017. She made her breakthrough after releasing the hit singles "Askamaya", "[[Case (Teni song)|Case]]" and "[[Uyo Meyo]]".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thefader.com/2018/10/18/teni-rules-askamaya-fake-jersey-african-pop|title=Teni rules, and here's the proof|website=The FADER|language=en|access-date=2019-01-13}}</ref> "Askamaya" was ranked 15th on [[MTV Base]]'s year-end list of the Top 20 Hottest Naija Tracks of 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://tooxclusive.com/news/mtv-base-top-20-hottest-naija-tracks-of-2018/|title=MTV Base Top 20 Hottest Naija Tracks of 2018" tooXclusive|date=2018-12-23|website=tooXclusive|language=en-US|access-date=2019-01-13}}</ref>

On 20 February 2019, Teni was featured on [[YouTube Music]]'s Trending Artist on the Rise.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://notjustok.com/news/teni-becomes-the-first-nigerian-artist-featured-as-youtubes-trending-artist-on-the-rise/|title=Teni Gets Featured As YouTube's 'Trending Artist On The Rise'|date=2019-02-20|language=en-US|access-date=2019-02-20}}</ref> On May 3, 2019, she released the video for "Sugar Mummy". On June 14, 2019, she released a new single titled "Power Rangers".<ref>{{cite news |title=Teni drops new single, "Power Rangers" |url=https://oak.tv/newstrack/teni-drops-new-single-power-rangers/ |access-date=25 June 2019 |work=Oak TV Newstrack |agency=Oak Tv |date=14 June 2019}}</ref>

=== 2019-2020: ''Billionaire E.P and Quarantine Playlist'' ===

Teni released her debut E.P, Billionaire on the 11th of October 2019. The ‚ÄúBillionaire EP‚Äù was complied with six (6) interesting tracks and with no guest appearance. The E.P was produced by Jaysynths Beatz and [[Pheelz]].<ref>{{cite web|last=Akinyoade|first=Akinwale|title=Teni The Entertainer Releases Highly-Anticipated EP ‚ÄúBillionaire‚Äù|url=https://guardian.ng/life/teni-the-entertainer-drops-highly-anticipated-ep-billionaire/ |publisher=Pulse|date=October 11, 2019|access-date=October 11, 2019}}</ref>

In April 2020, Teni partnered with Nigerian Disk Jockey, DJ Neptune to release an EP called the Quarantine Playlist, which sees her playing out different stages of being in quarantine and talking about the strains of being on lock down due to the coronavirus pandemic. ==Endorsements==
In March, 2019, Teni signed an endorsement deal with Tom Tom, a candy brand produced by [[Cadbury]] Nigeria.<ref>{{cite web |first1=Yvonne |last1=Ugwuezuoha |title=Five Nigerian celebrities who bagged major endorsement deals in 2019 |url=https://www.dailytrust.com.ng/five-nigerian-celebrities-who-bagged-major-endorsement-deals-in-2019.html |website=Daily Trust |access-date=11 April 2020 |date=April 16, 2019}}</ref> On October 6, 2020, PM News reported that Teni had signed an endorsement deal with Telecommunications Giant, [[Globacom]] Nigeria.<ref>{{cite web |first1=Jennifer |last1==Okundia |title=Don Jazzy, Teni become Globacom Ambassadors |url=https://www.pmnewsnigeria.com/2020/10/06/don-jazzy-teni-become-globacom-ambassadors/ |website=PM News |access-date=6 October 2020 |date=October 6, 2020}}</ref>

==Awards and nominations==
Teni won Rookie of the Year at the [[The Headies 2018|2018 Headies Awards]], and Most Promising Act to Watch at the 2018 [[Nigeria Entertainment Awards]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://thenet.ng/headies-2018teni-entertainer-wins-rookie-year-award/|title=HEADIES 2018:Teni Entertainer Wins 'Rookie Of The Year' Award|date=2018-05-05|website=Nigerian Entertainment Today|language=en-GB|access-date=2019-01-13}}</ref> She also won Best New Artist at the 2018 [[Soundcity MVP Awards Festival]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thisdaylive.com/index.php/2019/01/11/teni-gets-emotional-after-winning-best-new-act-at-soundcity-mvp-awards/|title=Teni Gets Emotional after Winning Best New Act at Soundcity MVP Awards|last=editor|date=2019-01-11|website=THISDAYLIVE|language=en-US|access-date=2019-01-13}}</ref> [[NotJustOk]] ranked her eighth on its list of the 10 Hottest Artists In Nigeria.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://notjustok.com/lists/the-10-hottest-artists-in-nigeria-thelist2018-8-teni/|title=The 10 Hottest Artists In Nigeria #TheList2018: #8 ‚Äì Teni|date=2018-12-27|website=NotJustOk|language=en-US|access-date=2019-01-13}}</ref> She was listed on ''[[Premium Times]]''{{'}} list of the Six Nigerian breakout stars, viral sensations of 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.premiumtimesng.com/entertainment/naija-fashion/302975-six-nigerian-breakout-stars-viral-sensations-of-2018.html|title=Six Nigerian breakout stars, viral sensations of 2018|last=Augoye|first=Jayne|date=2018-12-27|website=Premium Times Nigeria|language=en-GB|access-date=2019-01-13}}</ref>
