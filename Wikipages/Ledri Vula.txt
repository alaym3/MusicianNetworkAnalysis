'''Ledri Vula''' ({{IPA-sq|lɛˈdɾi vuˈla|lang}}; born 10 July 1986) is a Kosovo-Albanian rapper, singer and songwriter.

== Life and career ==

Ledri Vula was born on 10 July 1986 to Agron and Xheraldina Vula in the city of [[Pristina]], then part of the [[Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia]], present [[Kosovo]].<ref name="Biography">{{cite web |publisher=TeksteShqip |title=Ledri Vula–Biografia |url=https://www.teksteshqip.com/ledri-vula |access-date=12 February 2020 |language=sq}}</ref><ref name="Top Channel">{{cite web |publisher=[[Top Channel]] |title=Ledri Vula–Bio |url=http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/artist/ledri-vula/ |access-date=12 February 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201025164139/http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/artist/ledri-vula/ |archive-date=25 October 2020 |language=sq}}</ref> His father is an Albanian television producer Agron Vula, his mother is journalist and television personality Xheraldina Vula, who originally comes from [[Ulcinj]], Montenegro.<ref>{{cite web |title=Nënën e Ledrit e njihni por ky është babai |url=https://www.gazetaexpress.com/roze-nenen-e-ledrit-e-njihni-por-ky-eshte-babai-foto-199324/ |website=gazetaexpress.com |access-date=12 February 2020 |language=Albanian |date=12 May 2016}}</ref> In 2005, the rapper rose to prominence as one of the member of the hip hop duo Skillz.<ref>{{cite web |title=Skillz–Biografia |url=https://www.teksteshqip.com/skillz |website=teksteshqip.com |access-date=13 February 2020 |language=Albanian}}</ref> In 2012, the rise of his own stardom eventually led to the dissolution of the duo.<ref>{{cite web |title=Ledri Vula largohet nga muzika |url=https://telegrafi.com/ledri-vula-largohet-nga-muzika/ |website=telegrafi.com |access-date=13 February 2020 |language=Albanian |date=13 July 2012}}</ref> In 2014, Vula launched his solo career starting off in December that year with the release of his debut single "100 Probleme".<ref>{{cite web |title=Ledri Vula shënoi rikthimin në muzikë tre vite më parë në një datë si kjo |url=https://telegrafi.com/ledri-vula-shenoi-rikthimin-ne-muziken-tre-vite-pare-ne-nje-date-si-kjo-video/ |website=telegrafi.com |access-date=14 February 2020 |date=1 February 2017}}</ref>

In 2016, he won the My Music Hit of the Year Award at the [[Top Music Awards 2016]]. In 2019, he performed at the Sunny Hill Festival in [[Pristina]] sharing a stage with other acclaimed artists such as [[Calvin Harris]], [[Miley Cyrus]] and [[Dua Lipa]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Ledri Vula do performojë në Sunny Hill Festival |url=https://klankosova.tv/ledri-vula-do-performoje-ne-sunny-hill-festival/ |website=klankosova.tv |access-date=14 February 2020 |language=Albanian |date=22 June 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Skenën e "Sunny Hill Festival" këtë vit do ta 'ndez' edhe Ledri Vula |url=https://www.botasot.info/mediale-vip-showbiz/1109646/skenen-e-sunny-hill-festival-kete-vit-do-ta-ndez-edhe-ledri-vula/ |website=botasot.info |access-date=14 February 2020 |language=Albanian |date=22 June 2019}}</ref>

In January 2020, Vula announced his debut studio album, ''10/10'', to be released later that year.<ref name="album">{{cite web |title=Ledri Vula së shpejti vjen me albumin e ri "X" |url=https://telegrafi.com/ledri-vula-se-shpejti-vjen-albumin-e-ri-x/ |website=telegrafi.com |access-date=12 February 2020 |language=Albanian |date=12 January 2020}}</ref> In February 2020, he premiered his follow-up singles "[[Piano Rap]]" and "[[Prej Inati]]",<ref>{{cite web |title=Ledri Vula sjell projektin e ri "Piano Rap" |url=https://telegrafi.com/ledri-vula-sjell-projektin-e-ri-piano-rap/ |website=telegrafi.com |access-date=14 February 2020 |language=Albanian |date=1 February 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Koha Ditore|Koha]] |title=Ledri Vula publikon këngën "Prej inati" |url=https://www.koha.net/showbiz/209944/ledri-vula-publikon-kengen-prej-inati/ |website=koha.net |access-date=23 February 2020 |language=Albanian |date=22 February 2020}}</ref> whereas during the summer he premiered two other songs where both of them where features, the first one was "DMP<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-06-19|title=Capital T dhe Ledri Vula publikojnë projektin "DMP"|url=https://telegrafi.com/capital-t-dhe-ledri-vula-publikojne-projektin-dmp/|access-date=2020-07-15|website=Telegrafi|language=en-US}}</ref>" featuring Capital T, and "Aman<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-07-01|title=Publikohet kënga "Aman" nga Dafina Zeqiri, Ledri Vula dhe Lumi B|url=https://telegrafi.com/publikohet-kenga-aman-nga-dafina-zeqiri-ledri-vula-dhe-lumi-b/|access-date=2020-07-15|website=Telegrafi|language=en-US}}</ref>" featuring Lumi B & Dafina Zeqiri.
