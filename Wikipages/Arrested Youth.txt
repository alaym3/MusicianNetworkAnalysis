'''''Arrested Youth''''' is a digital [[Extended play|EP]] by the American [[pop punk]] band [[The Dollyrots]].<ref>[http://thedollyrots.bandcamp.com/album/arrested-youth Arrested Youth download page]</ref>  It was released on August 9, 2011 on the band's own "Arrested Youth Records".<ref>[http://the-adventurers-club.typepad.com/the_adventurers_club/2011/08/the-dollyrots-arrested-youth.html Arrested Youth @ the Adventures Club]</ref> 30 physical [[Compact Disc|CD]] copies were made, these were initially made available on their 2011 summer tour, with then the remaining copies were sold via their web site.<ref>[http://www.dollyrots.com/web/arrested-youth-cd-order/ Dollyrots.com]</ref>
