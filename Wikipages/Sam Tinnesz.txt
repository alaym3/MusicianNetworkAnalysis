'''Sam Tinnesz''' is an American singer and songwriter.

==Biography==
Sam Tinnesz is based in Nashville, Tennessee and signed through Warner Music Group’s ADA.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/pro/features/the-music-industry-wants-happy-songs-now-990033/|title=The Music Industry Is About to Put Out a Lot of Very, Very Uplifting Songs|website=RollingStone|language=en|access-date=2020-12-16}}</ref> Tinnesz's songs have been streamed over 100 million times. His Spotify reach each month is over 2.1 million listeners.<ref name="auto1">{{Cite web|url=https://geekinsider.com/new-music-from-sam-tinnesz-is-coming-with-a-chance-to-win-something-cool/|title=New Music from Sam Tinnesz is Coming... With a Chance to Win Something Cool|website=Geek Insider|language=en|access-date=2020-12-16}}</ref>

Tinnesz was featured in the song “Don't Give Up On Love” with Kygo which reached a Billboard top position of 35, spending 2 weeks on the charts with a peak date of June 12, 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/sam-tinnesz/chart-history/DAN/song/1206300|title=Billboard Chart History|website=Billboard|language=en|access-date=2020-12-16}}</ref>

Tinnesz wrote and recorded the song “Better Together” with Unsecret via Zoom and during quarantine on April 8, 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/anniereuter/2020/06/12/12-country-songs-written-in-quarantine-about-the-coronavirus-pandemic/?sh=24349b7665ac|title=12 Country Songs Written In Quarantine About The Coronavirus Pandemic|website=Forbes|language=en|access-date=2020-12-16}}</ref>

His music has been featured in Charmed, Batwoman, Love Island, American Ninja Warrior, The Hills: New Beginnings, So You Think You Can Dance, The Society, The Challenge, World of Dance, Siesta Key, Riverdale, Quantico, and others.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.tunefind.com/artist/sam-tinnesz|title=Sam Tinnesz Sync Placements|website=TuneFind|language=en|access-date=2020-12-16}}</ref>

Tinnesz is a gamer. His song “Wolves” was used in a commercial for Apex Legends and his song “Fire It Up” (feat. Ruelle) was used in the 2019 Xbox E3 trailer.<ref name="auto1"/>

==Collaborations==
Sam Tinnesz has collaborated with [[Ruelle (singer)|Ruelle]], [[Fleurie]], [[Tommee Profitt]], [[Kygo]], Banners, Daniella Mason, [[Manafest]] and [[Our Last Night]].<ref name="auto1"/>
