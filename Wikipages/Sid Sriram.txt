'''Sidharth Sriram''' (born 19 May 1990<ref>https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JzE_T1LewNM</ref>) is a Carnatic musician, an Indian-American music producer, [[playback singer]], and songwriter. He is an [[Contemporary R&B|R&B]] songwriter<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thefader.com/2014/02/05/video-sid-sriram-sagetron|title=Video: Sid Sriram, "SAGETRON"|website=The Fader |date=5 February 2015}}</ref> and has been working in the [[Tamil language|Tamil]] and [[Telugu language|Telugu]] music industry and also in [[Kannada language|Kannada]] and [[Malayalam cinema|Malayalam]] film productions.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.thehindu.com/society/history-and-culture/%E2%80%98Margazhi-here-I-come%E2%80%99/article16858487.ece|title=Margazhi, here I come |website=The Hindu |date=16 December 2016|last1=Sriram |first1=Sid }}</ref> He regularly collaborates with [[Bharatanatyam|Bharata Natyam]] dancer, his sister Pallavi Sriram, and music directors.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.thehindu.com/entertainment/dance/Music-struck-a-dominant-note/article16992678.ece|title= Music struck a dominant note|website=The Hindu |date=5 January 2017|last1= Srikanth|first1= Rupa}}</ref>

==Early life==

Sid Sriram was born in [[Chennai]], [[Tamil Nadu]], India to [[Tamils|Tamil]] family. He moved with his parents to [[California]] when he was a year old, and he grew up in [[Fremont, California|Fremont]]. His musical skills were nurtured by his mother, Latha Sriram, a [[Carnatic music]] teacher in the [[San Francisco Bay Area]].<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.thehindu.com/features/cinema/He%E2%80%99s-on-song-and-loving-it/article12291397.ece|title='He's on song and loving it'|newspaper=The Hindu|date=5 January 2013|last1=Dundoo|first1=Sangeetha Devi}}</ref> He started learning Carnatic music since he was 3 years old; he continues to be an avid student of Carnatic music. Carnatic music has been a foundation for his musical journey and source of inspiration and vocal timbre. His concerts in Carnatic music was a springboard for his career in playback singing.

He simultaneously started picking up [[Rhythm and blues|R&B]]. After graduating from [[Mission San Jose High School]] in 2008, he joined the [[Berklee College of Music]] and graduated in music production and engineering.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.rediff.com/movies/report/slide-show-1-south-interview-with-a-r-rahman-on-kadal/20130124.htm#2|title=Being an Indian kid in the USA, I was exposed to so many kinds of music |website=[[Rediff.com]]|date=24 January 2013}}</ref> Since his graduation, he has been regularly visiting [[India]] and performing Carnatic concerts, including as a part of the December Music season - ''Marghazhi Utsavam''. Since then, he moved back to India and currently resides in the town of [[Mylapore]].<ref>{{cite web | author=Jyothsna | url=http://www.behindwoods.com/tamil-singer/sid-sriram/sid-sriram-interview.html | title=The Environment Ar Rahman Sir Creates Is Spiritually Uplifting and Calm | website=Behindwoods | access-date=12 October 2016}}</ref>

==Career==
His strong foundation in Carnatic music and expertise in R&B genre brought him to the attention of the legendary Indian musical genius AR Rahman. AR Rahman has been and continues to be his mentor

Thalli Pogathey in ''[[Achcham Yenbadhu Madamaiyada]]'',<ref>{{cite web|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/tamil/music/I-knew-Thalli-Pogathey-was-going-to-be-special/articleshow/50578435.cms|title=I knew Thalli Pogathey was going to be special|newspaper=[[The Times of India]]|date=14 January 2017}}</ref> Vellipomaake in ''[[Sahasam Swasaga Sagipo]]'', Maruvaarthai in ''[[Enai Noki Paayum Thota]]'', Undiporaadhey in ''[[Husharu]]'', Arerey Manasa in ''[[Falaknuma Das]]'', Adiga Adiga in ''[[Ninnu Kori]]'' and Inkem Inkem Inkem Kaavaale in ''[[Geetha Govindam]]'', High On Love in ''[[Pyaar Prema Kaadhal]]'' , Ennadi Maayavi Nee in ''[[Vada Chennai]]'' , Ennodu Nee Irundhaal in [[I (film)|''I'']], Adiye from [[Kadal (2013 film)|''Kadal'']] Maate Vinadhuga in ''[[Taxiwaala]]'', Kadalalle in [[Dear Comrade (soundtrack)|''Dear Comrade'']], Parayuvaan from [[Ishq (2019 film)|''Ishq'']] , [[Samajavaragamana (2020 single)|Samajavaragamana]] in ''[[Ala Vaikunthapurramuloo]]'' , Visiri in ''[[Enai Noki Paayum Thota]]'',<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.behindwoods.com/tamil-movies-cinema-news-16/teaser-of-maru-varthai-pesathey-song-from-enai-noki-paayum-thota-released.html|title=Who Is Enpt's Music Director? Any Guesses?|website=Behindwoods |date=31 December 2016}}</ref> Maguva Maguva in ''[[Vakeel Saab]]'', Naa Kanulu Yepudu of ''[[Rang De]]'' have all been notable songs. He also recently worked in some Malayalam songs like Uyire from ''[[Gauthamante Radham]]''.

He would later making his foray into music composition, where he would compose for ace director [[Mani Ratnam]]'s production, ''[[Vaanam Kottatum]]'', which is directed by Mani's former assistant and director of [[Padaiveeran]], Dhana Sekharan.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.thehindu.com/entertainment/movies/the-new-sid-on-the-block/article29242826.ece|title=Sid Sriram on turning composer with Mani Ratnam's Vaanam Kottatum and much more|last=Ramanujam|first=Srinivasa|date=2019-08-24|work=The Hindu |language=en-IN |issn=0971-751X |access-date=2019-08-28}}</ref>
