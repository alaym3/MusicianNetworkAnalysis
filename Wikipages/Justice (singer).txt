'''Justice''', born '''Lauren Justice''', is an American female pop recording artist.<ref>"Lights on San Diego Red Carpet Event Benefiting Being Alive San Diego" Melissa Anderson, Lake Elsinore Examiner</ref>

==Music==
Her 2012 debut single, "Find a Way", a pop dance track featuring Sean-Gemini,<ref>"JUSTICE Releases "Find A Way" Her Debut Single Produced by Jean-Michel Soupraya" – March 22, 2012 – Staff, CW Place</ref> reached No. 30 on the ''Billboard'' Indicator Chart and was featured for nine consecutive weeks.<ref>"Lauren Justice to Make Tour Stop in Winfield" – May 8, 2012 – Staff, The Winfield Courier</ref><ref>Billboard Mid-Week Update, March 8, 2012</ref><ref>Billboard Mid-Week Update, May 2, 2012</ref>

Justice's second radio single, "By My Side" (2013),<ref>"JMWest Entertainment Announces Single Release Party for JUSTICE's 'By My Side'" – March 18, 2013 – Christopher Simmons, www.bx.business.com</ref><ref>"JMWest Entertainment Announces Single Release Of JUSTICE’s “By My Side"" – March 18,2 2013 – Staff, Mi2N.com</ref><ref>"Vie D'ailleurs Vue D'ailleurs" – March 18, 2013 – Christophe Cuby, Guadeloupe 1ère</ref><ref>"Vie D'ailleurs Vue D'ailleurs" – March 19, 2013 – Christophe Cuby, Guadeloupe 1ère</ref><ref>"Vie D'ailleurs Vue D'ailleurs" – March 20, 2013 – Christophe Cuby, Guadeloupe 1ère</ref><ref>"Vie D'ailleurs Vue D'ailleurs" – March 21, 2013 – Christophe Cuby, Guadeloupe 1ère</ref><ref>"Vie D'ailleurs Vue D'ailleurs" – March 22, 2013 – Christophe Cuby, Guadeloupe 1ère</ref> reached No. 34 on the ''Billboard'' Indicator Chart.<ref>"Billboard TOP 40 Mid-Week Update" – June 13, 2012 -</ref><ref>BDSRadio.com – June 22, 2013 – Issue Date 6/22/2012</ref>  The music video was released March 19, 2013 and has received over 900,000 YouTube views.<ref>"MusicDishTV Presents Justice "By My Side" – May 29, 2013 – Staff, Music Industry News Network</ref>
