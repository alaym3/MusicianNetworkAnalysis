'''Kim Ah-joong''' (born October 16, 1982) is a South Korean actress, model and singer. She is best known for playing the main character in the romantic comedy ''[[200 Pounds Beauty]]''.

==Education==
Kim graduated from [[Korea University]] with a master's degree in journalism.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://english.chosun.com/site/data/html_dir/2011/01/06/2011010600835.html?related_all|title=Kim A-joong Receives Master's Degree from Korea University|date=January 6, 2011|work=The Chosun Ilbo}}</ref>

==Career==
Kim made her film debut in the romance film ''[[When Romance Meets Destiny]]'' (2005).<ref>{{cite web|url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=117&aid=0000006116|title=김아중, '광식이 동생 광태'로 스크린데뷔|date=March 16, 2005|website=My Daily|language=ko}}</ref> She appeared in several commercials and landed first leading role in daily soap opera ''[[Bizarre Bunch]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=005&aid=0000219189|title=김아중 "나도 이젠 주인공"…KBS 새 일일극서 첫 주연 맡아|date=September 21, 2005|publisher=KMIB|language=ko}}</ref>

Her breakthrough came in the comedy film ''[[200 Pounds Beauty]]'', where she played an overweight girl who undergoes cosmetic surgery makeover to become a pop sensation. She was awarded the Best Actress award at the 2007 [[Grand Bell Awards]]. She released soundtracks of the film, "Beautiful Girl" and the Korean version of the song "[[Maria (Blondie song)|Maria]]". "Beautiful Girl" won Digital Music Awards' "Song of the Month"  by Cyworld.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://english.chosun.com/site/data/html_dir/2007/01/13/2007011361002.html|title=Propelled to the Top by 200 Pounds|date=January 13, 2007|work=The Chosun Ilbo}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://english.chosun.com/site/data/html_dir/2005/12/09/2005120961016.html|title=Kim A-joong Brings Living Beauty to Barbie Exhibition|date=December 9, 2005|work=The Chosun Ilbo}}</ref>

Kim returned to acting three years later, starring alongside [[Hwang Jung-min]] in romantic comedy ''[[The Accidental Couple]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://koreatimes.co.kr/www/news/nation/2009/04/398_43978.html|title=Kim and Hwang Team up as Accidental Couple|date=April 28, 2009|work=The Korea Times}}</ref> 
In 2010, Kim was cast in a U.S.-Chinese film project ''[[Amazing (film)|Amazing]]'', a sci-fi film about the development of a virtual-reality basketball game.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://english.chosun.com/site/data/html_dir/2010/08/28/2010082800197.html|title=Kim A-joong to Star in U.S.-Chinese Film|date=August 20, 2010|work=The Chosun Ilbo}}</ref> The same year, she was cast in medical drama ''[[Sign (TV series)|Sign]]'' by [[Kim Eun-hee]], playing a forensic scientist.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.asiae.co.kr/news/view.htm?sec=ent5&idxno=2010110809335729993|title=Park Shin-yang, Kim Ah-joong starrer drama title set as "Sign"|date=November 8, 2010|work=10Asia}}</ref>

In September 2011, Kim was found guilty of tax evasion. She also had to pay an additional $600,000 fine. She and her management at the time parted ways due to the verdict. Due to this scandal she was banned from TV work in South Korea from late 2011 to late 2014, and she focused on films.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hancinema.net/exclusive-kim-ah-joong-tax-evasion-nts-imposes-6-hundred-million-32910.html|title=[Exclusive] Kim Ah-joong tax evasion, NTS imposes 6 hundred million|date=September 5, 2011|work=Hancinema}}</ref> In December 2012, Kim co-starred with [[Ji Sung]] in the romantic comedy film ''[[My PS Partner]]''.<ref>{{cite news|last=Ho|first=Stewart|title=Ji Sung and Kim Ah Joong's Release Movie Poster for Phone Sex Comedy|url=http://enewsworld.mnet.com/enews/contents.asp?idx=17981|access-date=November 28, 2012|newspaper=enewsWorld|date=October 17, 2012|agency=CJ E&M}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=Lee|first=Nancy|title=Ji Sung and Kim Ah Joong Dial Up for ''My PS Partner''|url=http://enewsworld.mnet.com/enews/contents.asp?idx=20294|access-date=November 28, 2012|newspaper=enewsWorld|date=November 9, 2012|agency=CJ E&M}}</ref> 
This was followed by another romantic comedy film ''[[Steal My Heart (film)|Steal My Heart]]'' in 2013.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://english.chosun.com/site/data/html_dir/2014/01/18/2014011800462.html|title=Kim A-joong Steals Audiences' Hearts in New Romantic Comedy|date=January 18, 2014|work=The Chosun Ilbo}}</ref>

She returned to TV in legal drama ''[[Punch (2014 TV series)|Punch]]'', where she received positive reviews for her portrayal of a prosecutor.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://english.chosun.com/site/data/html_dir/2014/12/13/2014121300399.html|title=Kim A-joong Returns to TV in Legal Drama|date=November 13, 2014|website = The Chosun Ilbo}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title = Kim A-joong Rediscovers the Fun of Acting|url = http://english.chosun.com/site/data/html_dir/2015/03/12/2015031200799.html|website = The Chosun Ilbo|access-date =November 5, 2015}}</ref> 
In 2016, Kim starred in the crime thriller ''[[Wanted (South Korean TV series)|Wanted]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://sports.donga.com/3/all/20160516/78115254/1|title=김아중·엄태웅·지현우, '원티드' 출연확정…6월22일 첫방 [공식입장]|website=Sports Donga|date=May 16, 2016|language=ko}}</ref> 
In 2017, she starred in time-slip romantic comedy ''[[Live Up to Your Name]]'' alongside [[Kim Nam-gil]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://kpopherald.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=201709031748448661572_2|title=Kim Nam-gil, Kim Ah-joong slip through time in tvN drama|date=September 3, 2017|work=Kpop Herald}}</ref>

In 2018, Kim was cast in the film adaptation of OCN's crime drama ''[[Bad Guys (TV series)|Bad Guys]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://star.mt.co.kr/foreign/starViewEng.html?no=2018061809016938342|title=Kim Ah Joong For 'Bad Guy'|date=June 19, 2018|website=Star News}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://koreanfilm.or.kr/eng/news/news.jsp?pageIndex=1&blbdComCd=601006&seq=4924&mode=VIEW&returnUrl=&searchKeyword=|title=KIM Sang-joong, Don LEE and KANG Ye-won Return for BAD GUYS: THE MOVIE KIM A-joong and JANG Ki-yong Join OCN Action Drama Screen Adaptation|date=August 7, 2018 |website=Korean Film Biz Zone}}</ref>
