'''Carl Erik Friman''', better known by his stage name '''Fricky''' (born 9 March 1992),<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.ratsit.se/19920309-Carl_Erik_Friman_Ekero/8vjbs-tkBjIoX5UWepuTaJkeUbjJR_P8FaTlbhwfSnE|title=Erik Friman (28 år) Ekerö {{!}} Ratsit|website=Ratsit|language=sv|url-status=live|access-date=24 April 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://modette.se/fricky|title=Fricky - Nyheter, artiklar, reportage och video - Modette|website=Modette|language=sv|url-status=live|access-date=24 April 2020}}</ref> is a Swedish rapper and musician. In 2018, he released his debut EP ''Aqua Aura'', which peaked at number five on the Swedish Albums Chart. The EP contained songs like "Aqua Aura" and "Hon Få Mig", with the first being certified platinum in Sweden. "Hon få mig" was released as a remix, that featured Ozzy, Leslie Tay and Blizzy. It was subsequently certified platinum in Sweden as well.

== Career ==
Fricky started doing [[hip-hop]] in his hometown of [[Umeå]] (often referred in his music as UÅ) with his friend Johan Bäckström ([[Broder John]]) and they made an album together called ''Mauro'', which was released under the name "Broder John & Friman".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.kingsizemag.se/karusellen/intervju-broder-john-friman/|title=Intervju – Broder John & Friman|last=Kihlström|first=Alexander|date=27 June 2013|website=Kingsizemag|language=Sv|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200329121414/https://www.kingsizemag.se/karusellen/intervju-broder-john-friman/|archive-date=29 March 2020|access-date=29 March 2020}}</ref> They also co-founded the record label [[Hoopdiggas]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://dopest.se/broder-john-bokad-till-amerikanska-festivalen-sxsw/|title=Broder John bokad till amerikanska festivalen SXSW|last=Birkemose|first=Amina|website=Dopest|language=sv|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200329213432/https://dopest.se/broder-john-bokad-till-amerikanska-festivalen-sxsw/|archive-date=29 March 2020|access-date=29 March 2020}}</ref>  Friman released the song "Hon få mig" in 2017 which became a hit in Sweden that later paved the way for his [[Extended play|EP]] ''Aqua Aura'', which was released in 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://dopest.se/fricky-ep-aqua-aura/|title=Frickys debut-EP "Aqua Aura" är här!|last=|first=|date=23 March 2018|website=Dopest|language=sv|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200330043637/https://dopest.se/fricky-ep-aqua-aura/|archive-date=30 March 2020|access-date=29 March 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://open.spotify.com/album/7aJi5qJQTI6mQV194aMuwj?si=JrXWSOn6SkmnnHQYsJuk4A|title=Aqua Aura by Fricky|website=[[Spotify]]|url-status=live|access-date=30 March 2020}}</ref> Aqua Aura featured songs as "Mästerort", "Je T'aime" and "Aqua Aura". The latter became one of the most streamed songs on [[Spotify]] in Sweden that year. Majority of the songs from the EP was produced by fellow collective artist and close friend Academics.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://umeax.se/16354/fricky-avslojar-bakgrunden-till-laten-hon-fa-mig|title=Fricky avslöjar bakgrunden till låten "Hon få mig"|last=Burman|first=Nicolina|date=18 September 2019|website=Umeax|language=sv|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200329122209/https://umeax.se/16354/fricky-avslojar-bakgrunden-till-laten-hon-fa-mig|archive-date=29 March 2020|access-date=29 March 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://dopest.se/fricky-academics-ar-tillbaka-med-nya-singeln-man/|title=Fricky & Academics är tillbaka med nya singeln "Man"|date=5 January 2018|website=Dopest|language=sv|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200329122321/https://dopest.se/fricky-academics-ar-tillbaka-med-nya-singeln-man/|archive-date=29 March 2020|access-date=29 March 2020}}</ref> The EP also charted for 82 weeks in Sweden, peaking at number 5 on the [[Swedish Albums Chart]].<ref name="SWE" /> Subsequently, Fricky was given the award for Artist of the Year from P3 Guld 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.vk.se/2019-01-19/fricky-arets-artist|title=Fricky Årets artist|last=Forsgren|first=Helena|date=5 April 2019|website=VK|language=sv|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200329004346/https://www.vk.se/2019-01-19/fricky-arets-artist|archive-date=29 March 2020|access-date=29 March 2020}}</ref> Fricky released the single "Kungsliften", featuring [[Cherrie (singer)|Cherrie]], in 2018. The song peaked at number 54 on the Swedish Singles Chart. He has also been featured on numerous tracks; "Cali" (with Näääk and Denz), "Flyger Högt" (by BENNETT) and "Chocolate" (by K27). "Cali" peaked at number 18 on the Swedish Singles Chart.<ref name="Cali"/> He has released three solo singles in 2020, "Beemer" and "Axlar", with the first peaking at number 41 on the Swedish Singles Chart. "Axlar" charted on the Swedish Heatseeker Chart, where it peaked at number six. The third single, "Kär Pt. 2", was released on 10 July.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Kär Pt. 2 by Fricky|url=https://open.spotify.com/album/3TwcHt4FKpVmuxLlrAImXw?si=Atu6neNxTwOURLtohrkThg|access-date=16 September 2020|website=Spotify}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Fricky är "Kär Pt. 2" på nya singeln|url=https://dopest.se/fricky-kar-pt-2-ny-singel/|url-status=live|access-date=16 September 2020|website=Dopest|language=sv}}</ref> It marks the follow-up to Fricky's single "Kär Pt. 1", released in 2016.

== Personal life ==
Friman is married to Swedish rapper [[Cleo (Swedish singer)|Cleo]],<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.svd.se/det-har-ar-stjarnskottet-fricky|title=Det här är stjärnskottet Fricky|last1=TT|first=|date=|website=[[Svenska Dagbladet]]|language=sv|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181125072147/https://www.svd.se/det-har-ar-stjarnskottet-fricky|archive-date=25 November 2018|accessdate=6 January 2019}}</ref> who gave birth to their daughter Elda in early 2019.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://mobil.vt.se/kultur-noje/cleo-och-fricky-har-fatt-barn-om5656553.aspx|title=Cleo och Fricky har fått barn|date=3 January 2019|website=VT|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|accessdate=24 April 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/a/3ja9kX/bebislycka-for-cleo-och-fricky|title=Bebislycka för "Cleo" och "Fricky"|last=Hartelius|first=Alex|date=3 January 2019|website=[[Aftonbladet]]|url-status=live|access-date=24 April 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190512203959/https://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/a/3ja9kX/bebislycka-for-cleo-och-fricky|archive-date=12 May 2019}}</ref>
