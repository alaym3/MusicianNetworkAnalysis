'''Marcus Füreder''' (born 27 November 1974), better known by his stage name '''Parov Stelar''', is an Austrian musician, composer, [[Record producer|producer]], [[DJ]] and designer.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.wohndesigners.at/trapa-stelarbloom-moebel-linie-voltaire/|title=Kunst trifft Handwerk|last=Pilar|first=Sylvia|website=Wohndesigners}}</ref> His musical style is based on a combination of [[jazz]], [[House music|house]], [[Electro (music)|electro]], [[Hip hop music|hip hop]], and [[Pop music|pop]].

==Biography==
Marcus Füreder first appeared as a DJ at nightclubs during the mid-late 1990s. He became involved in producing and publishing in 2000. Having released his first maxi singles on Bushido using his real name and his stage name Plasma, Fuereder founded the record label ''Etage Noir Recordings'' in 2003.<ref>{{cite web| url = https://www.faz.net/aktuell/feuilleton/pop/album-der-woche/cd-der-woche-parov-stelar-extravaganz-als-genuss-11763622.html | title = Parov Stelar: Extravaganz ale Genuss | access-date = 2014-10-08 | website = faz.net | year = 2012 | last = Seibold | first = Ulrike }}</ref> A year later, under the stage name Parov Stelar, the EP ''Kiss Kiss'' was released, followed by the LP ''Rough Cuts''.<ref name="rab1">{{cite web | url = http://www.rhythm-and-booze.co.uk/?p=8533 | title = Parov Stelar Release New Single | access-date = 2014-09-10 | date = 2013-08-29 | website = Rhythm & Booze | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20160321212036/http://rhythm-and-booze.co.uk/?p=8533 | archive-date = 2016-03-21 | url-status = dead }}</ref> Parov Stelar has worked with [[Lana Del Rey]],<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6FJ7F6trvBc|title=Lana Del Rey - Dark Paradise (Parov Stelar Official Remix)}}</ref> [[Bryan Ferry]], and [[Lady Gaga]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BVYfOwLqyck|title=I Can't Give You Anything But Love - Parov Stelar Mix}}</ref>

[[Cleo Panther]] was the lead singer for Parov Stelar since 2011. In 2019, Cleo Panther left the Parov Stelar Band. She was replaced with [[Elena Karafizi]], who "brings a versatile musical background" from her hometown of [[Chisinau]], [[Moldova]]. Soul singer Anduze co-inhabits the lead singer position alongside Elena. He officially joined the band in 2018, but has been working with Parov Stelar since 2012 as a writer and featured artist.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/parovstelar/posts/our-long-time-singer-cleo-panther-has-informed-us-that-due-to-life-choices-she-w/10156799902100622/|title=Welcome, Elena Karafizi|date=2019-04-11}}</ref> In the summer of 2021 Parov Stelar gave the first insight into his work as a visual artist in his hometown of Linz. The Francisco Carolinum Museum presented 25 large-format paintings such as "HELLO Mom" - a homage to his mother Margit Füreder, who is also active as an artist - "I'll be OK soon" as well as "Toxic Lover" in which he receives and artistically processes autobiographical events and fears. <ref>{{Cite web|title=Parov Stelar. I'll be OK soon|url=https://www.ooelkg.at/de/ausstellungen/detail/parov-stelar.html|url-status=live}}</ref> <ref>{{Cite web|title=Parov Stelar mischt mit seiner Kunst das Francisco Carolinum auf|url=https://www.nachrichten.at/kultur/parov-stelar-mischt-mit-seiner-kunst-das-francisco-carolinum-auf;art16,3404877|url-status=live}}</ref>

==Critical reception==
Parov Stelar won 10 Amadeus Austrian Music Awards: Electronic/Dance (2012, 2013, 2015 and 2016, 2019, 2020, 2021),<ref>{{Cite web|last=Zeidler|first=Thomas|date=2021-09-09|title=Amadeus Awards 2021: Die Sieger|url=https://www.oe24.at/leute/oesterreich/amadeus-awards-2021-die-sieger/491161828|url-status=live|website=oe24}}</ref> Album of the Year for ''The Princess'' (2013), and Best Live Act (2013<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.tips.at/news/altenfelden/land-leute/271688-abgeraeumt-parov-stelar-holt-drei-amadeus-awards|title=Abgeräumt! Parov Stelar holt drei Amadeus Awards|website=Tips Online|access-date=2019-04-12}}</ref> and 2014<ref>{{Citation|title=Amadeus-Verleihung 2014|date=2018-12-12|url=https://de.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Amadeus-Verleihung_2014&oldid=183649504|work=Wikipedia|language=de|access-date=2019-04-12}}</ref>). The track ''Booty Swing'' charted positions in the electronic US and Canadian iTunes charts.<ref>{{cite web| url = http://bbpiratesradio.over-blog.com/article-parov-stelar-the-princess-104265986.html | title = Parov Stelar - The Princess - DJ DemonAngel & Siouxxx aka DJ HighKik | access-date = 2014-09-10 }}</ref> ''All Night'' reached double platinum status for 100,000 tracks sold in Italy in May 2017. In May 2018 the song ''The Sun'' featuring Graham Candy and in April 2020 the release ''Voodoo Sonic Part 2'' reached the No. 1 chart position in the US iTunes Electronic Charts.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://oe3.orf.at/stories/2911551|title=oe3.orf.at|date=2018-05-08|work=Hitradio Ö3|access-date=2018-05-08}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://oe3.orf.at/stories/3001843/|title=Parov Stelar wieder Nummer 1 in den USA|access-date=2020-04-27}}</ref>

==Personal life==
He was married to Barbara Lichtenauer (better known by her stage name Lilja Bloom), with whom he co-founded the design label Stelarbloom.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://competition.adesignaward.com/press-release.php?ID=63360|title=Stelarbloom has been granted the celebrated Golden A' Design Award|date=2018-04-14}}</ref> Stelar and Bloom divorced in early 2020. <ref>{{Cite web|title=Der Maler Parov Stelar|url=https://oe3.orf.at/stories/3016192/|url-status=live}}</ref>
