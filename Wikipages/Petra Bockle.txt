'''Petra  Bockle''' (born 14 May 1995) known professionally as '''Petra''', is a rapper, songwriter and singer of Kenyan and [[Seychelles|Seychellois]] descent.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://softkenya.com/kenya/petra/|title=Petra Biography|work=Softkenya|access-date=16 April 2018}}</ref> She performs in both [[Swahili language|Swahili]] and English, sometimes switching back and forth.<ref name="HapaKenya">{{cite web|url=https://hapakenya.com/2018/03/14/song-review-khali-cartel-khaligraph-jones/|title=Song review; Khali Cartel by Khaligraph Jones|last=Anyuolo|first=Lena|date=14 March 2018|website=HapaKenya|access-date=6 May 2018}}</ref> She cites [[Tupac Shakur|2Pac]], [[Eminem]], [[MC Lyte]] and [[Lil' Kim]] as her musical influences.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sde.co.ke/pulse/article/2000112141/petra-s-originality|title=Petra's Originality|last=Kwamboka|first=Rose|website=SDE News|access-date=23 April 2018}}</ref>

==Early life==
Petra Bockle was born to a Kenyan mother and [[Seychelles|Seychellois]] father.  Her father died in 2004, when Petra was nine years old, She then started becoming active with music around this time as a form of therapy.<ref name="Ghafla">{{Cite web|url=http://www.ghafla.com/photos-exposing-the-super-hot-young-femcee-who-killed-it-in-ligi-soo-remix-2/|title=Photos: Exposing the Super Hot Young Femcee Who Killed It in "Ligi Soo Remix" – Ghafla!|last=Omondi|first=Jeff|date= 13 August 2013|website=[[Ghafla!]]|access-date=30 April 2018}}</ref>

==Career==
Petra first appeared on the "Ligi Soo Remix" which featured her along with other Kenyan artists.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.ghafla.com/thanks-to-ligi-soo-remix-rabbit-is-now-the-artist-to-beat/|title=Thanks To 'Ligi Soo Remix' Rabbit is Now The Artist To Beat – Ghafla!|last=Omondi|first=Jeff|website=www.ghafla.com|access-date=16 April 2018}}</ref> This includes: [[Kaka Sungura]], Madtraxx, MwendaMchizi Jay A and Mejja among others.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.bottomline.co.ke/ligi-soo-remix-rabbit-ft-various-artists/|title=Ligi Soo (Remix) – Rabbit ft. Various Artists (Jay A, Mejja, Chiwawa, Madtraxx, Petra, Shikow FemOne, Razz, Lyra Aoko & Wangechi)|date=1 July 2013|work=Bottom Line Kenya|access-date=16 April 2018}}</ref> She then disappeared from the music scene for a while. In 2018 she collaborated with Kenyan hip hop artist Khaligraph Jones on a song titled "Rider".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://nairobiwire.com/2018/02/khaligraph-jones-femcee-petra-trade-mad-bars-hot-new-joint-rider-watch.html|title=Khaligraph Jones and Femcee Petra Trade Mad Bars in Hot New Joint 'Rider' [Watch]|last=Kamau|first=Richard|date=13 February 2018|website=NairobiWire|access-date=6 May 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.kenyanvibe.com/exclusive-khaligraph-jones-speaks-new-jam-petra/|title=Exclusive: Khaligraph Jones Speaks Of New Jam With Petra|last=Nyota|first=Maxwell|date=26 February 2018|website=KenyanVibe|access-date=6 May 2018}}</ref> In the music video for "Rider", which features Khaligraph Jones, Petra is seen eating raw meat, which was actual raw liver, caused controversy.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.the-star.co.ke/news/2018/03/01/i-enjoyed-raw-liver-and-didnt-deworm-petra_c1722294|title=I enjoyed raw liver and didn't deworm – Petra|last=Ngigi|first=Elizabeth|date=1 March 2018|website=[[The Star (Kenya)]]|publisher=Radio Africa Group|access-date=6 May 2018}}</ref>

A month later she was featured on another song titled "Khali Kartel Cypher".<ref name="HapaKenya"/> Petra performed alongside [[Mr Eazi]] at the Hypefest concert during his Kenya tour.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.pulselive.co.ke/entertainment/photos-of-mr-eazi-s-nairobi-concert-id8228284.html|title=In case you missed it, this is what went down during Mr Eazi's Nairobi concert (Photos)|last=Watiri|first=Sue|access-date=16 April 2018}}</ref> Her latest single "i got that" features singer [[Victoria Kimani]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://kenyannews.co.ke/entertainment/petra-taps-victoria-kimani-for-banging-new-joint-i-got-that/|title=Petra Taps Victoria Kimani For Banging New Joint 'I Got That' — kenyan News|date=1 April 2018|work=kenyan News|access-date=22 May 2018|language=en-US}}</ref>

==Awards and nominations==
In 2012, Petra won an award for best female artist at the Kenya Coast Music Awards.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.ghafla.com/full-list-all-the-winners-at-the-kenya-coast-music-awards/|title=Full List: All the Winners at the Kenya Coast Music Awards – Ghafla!|last=Wagwau|first=Adam|website=Ghafla!|access-date=16 April 2018}}</ref> Later on in 2015 she received an award for best female hip hop artist at the Pwani Celebrity Awards.
