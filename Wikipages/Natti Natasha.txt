'''Natalia Alexandra Gutiérrez Batista''' (born December 10, 1986),  better known by her stage name '''Natti Natasha''', is a Dominican singer and songwriter. She was signed to [[Don Omar]]'s label [[Orfanato Music Group]]. Her debut EP, ''All About Me'', was released on March 28, 2012, by Orfanato Music Group.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.hispanicallyspeakingnews.com/latino-daily-news/details/natti-natasha-finds-her-place-among-the-men-of-reggaeton/14450/ |title=Natti Natasha Finds Her Place Among the Men of Reggaeton {{!}} HispanicallySpeakingNews.com |date=October 20, 2013 |url-status=unfit |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131020060807/http://www.hispanicallyspeakingnews.com/latino-daily-news/details/natti-natasha-finds-her-place-among-the-men-of-reggaeton/14450/ |archive-date=October 20, 2013}}</ref> Her debut album, ''[[Iluminatti]]'', was released on February 15, 2019, by [[Pina Records]] and [[Sony Music Latin]].

==Early life==
Gutiérrez was born on December 10, 1986<ref>{{cite web|access-date=October 22, 2019|language=es|url=https://historia-biografia.com/natti-natasha/|title=Historia y biografía de Natti Natasha|first=Leydy |last=Montoya|website=Historia y biografía|date=October 20, 2018}}</ref> in [[Santiago de los Caballeros]], Dominican Republic<ref name="RS 2019">{{cite magazine |last=Exposito |first=Suzy |title=Latin Pop Superstar Natti Natasha Taps Into the Power of Sisterhood|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-latin/natti-natasha-iluminatti-interview-807179/|magazine=Rolling Stone Magazine |location= United States|date=March 15, 2019}}</ref> where she started her involvement in music. When she was seven, Natasha registered at the school of Fine Arts in Santiago, where she took classes for singing and developed her singing abilities, which she has described as one of the best times in her life. From a very young age, she had admired artists such as [[Bob Marley]] and [[Jerry Rivera]], and especially [[Lauryn Hill]], whom she still names as one of her favorites.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://hastaabajobel.com/en/content/biography-natti-natasha |title=Biography Natti Natasha {{!}} Hasta Abajo Belgica |date=May 24, 2012 |url-status=unfit |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120524113435/http://hastaabajobel.com/en/content/biography-natti-natasha |archive-date=May 24, 2012 }}</ref> Natasha also expressed that she has followed and admired [[Ivy Queen]] since the beginning of Queen's career,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.listin.com.do/entretenimiento/2012/3/7/224341/|title=Dominicana Natti Natasha se abre espacio en reguetón de la mano de Don Omar|work=Listin Diario|language=es|author=Muñiz Ortiz, Jorge|date=March 7, 2012|access-date=May 2, 2013}}</ref> with Queen responding via [[Twitter]]: "Thanks for the respect, admiration. That's having class and more than anything intelligence".<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://wikiton.net/ivy-queen-elogia-a-natti-natasha|title=Ivy Queen elogia a Natti Natasha|work=Wikiton|date=March 8, 2012|author=Dajer, Joan|access-date=May 2, 2013|language=es}}</ref> When she was 18, she moved to the Bronx hoping to make it in the "City of Dreams". There she met reggaeton veteran [[Don Omar]] who then signed her to his record label. She's also an engineer and worked at a [[Makeup]] factory.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thefader.com/2018/12/05/natti-natasha-la-baby-criminal-interview|title=Natti Natasha is ushering in a brighter future for reggaeton|website=The FADER}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Univision|title=Karol G y Natti Natasha, las mujeres más nominadas de Premio Lo Nuestro 2021|url=https://www.univision.com/shows/premio-lo-nuestro/karol-g-natti-natasha-mas-nominadas-premio-lo-nuestro-2021-fotos|access-date=February 21, 2021|website=Univision|language=spanish}}</ref> 

At the age of 18, Natalia began to compose and record her own songs, performing different musical performances that took place in her native Santiago and together with her friends, she decided to form the musical group "D'Style", arriving to record some musical themes, the grouping did not achieve the expected results and eventually disintegrated. As a result, Natalia decides to put in a brief pause her short musical career.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Trome|first=Redacción|date=August 27, 2018|title=Natti Natasha: Biografía de la cantante de "Criminal", "Sin Pijama" y "Buena vida"|url=http://trome.pe/celebrities/natti-natasha-biografia-completa-cantante-criminal-pijama-nnda-92733|access-date=April 9, 2019|website=trome.pe|language=es}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=January 9, 2018|title=Los inicios de Natti Natasha: La historia de la Cri-criminal|url=https://heabbi.com/natti-natasha-la-mas-cri-criminal|access-date=April 9, 2019|website=Heabbi.com|language=es}}</ref>

As of 2021, Natasha has been a [[vegan]] for more than six years.<ref>{{cite web|access-date=September 11, 2018|language=es|url=https://www.laprensagrafica.com/farandula/Natti-Natasha-criticada-por-un-inocente-comentario-20180724-0116.html|title=Natti Natasha, criticada por un "inocente" comentario|date=July 25, 2018|website=[[La Prensa Gráfica]]|url-status=live|archive-url=https://archive.today/20180809061832/https://www.laprensagrafica.com/farandula/Natti-Natasha-criticada-por-un-inocente-comentario-20180724-0116.html|archive-date=August 9, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|access-date=September 11, 2018|language=es|url=http://alo.co/entretenimiento/entrevista-natti-natasha-2018|title=NATTI NATASHA, LA #POWERGIRL DE LA MÚSICA URBANA|first=Johanna|last=Muñoz|website=Alo.co|date=April 3, 2018|url-status=dead|archive-date=August 9, 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180809061316/http://alo.co/entretenimiento/entrevista-natti-natasha-2018}}</ref>

==Career==

=== 2010–2016: Career beginnings ===
Natasha was signed to [[Orfanato Music Group]] for a few songs, in 2010 released "Hold Ya (Remix)" with [[Gyptian]] and [[Don Omar]]. She was featured in a song by [[Don Omar]], called "[[Dutty Love (Don Omar song)|Dutty Love]]", recorded in 2011 and released in March 2012 and worked with [[Farruko]] on "Crazy in Love". The former's success led to another collaboration with Omar, "Tus Movimientos". Both songs are featured on Omar's album ''[[Don Omar Presents MTO²: New Generation]]'', released in 2012.

=== 2017–2018: Breakthrough and collaborations ===
In 2017, Puerto Rican singer [[Daddy Yankee]] released the song "Otra Cosa" with Natasha. She released the single "[[Criminal (Natti Natasha and Ozuna song)|Criminal]]", with [[Ozuna (singer)|Ozuna]], which became an instant hit, reaching the top five of the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Hot Latin Songs]] chart and being the most watched music video overall in 2017. In April 2018, [[Becky G]] released "[[Sin Pijama]]", a collaboration with Natasha. The song was Natasha's second global hit, peaking at number three on the Hot Latin Songs chart, being certified platinum within three weeks of release, and reaching one billion views in six months, being the most watched female song of the year. She has also collaborated with [[Bad Bunny]], [[R.K.M & Ken-Y]], [[Thalía]], and [[Cosculluela]]. Natasha is currently signed to [[Pina Records]].

=== 2019–present: ''Iluminatti'' and ''Nattividad'' ===
She released her debut album, ''[[Iluminatti]]'' on February 15, 2019, by Pina Records and [[Sony Music Latin]].The album has a total of 15 songs, with two of them being features, "Te Lo Dije" with [[Anitta (singer)|Anitta]] and "Soy Mía" with [[Kany Garcia]]. 
In 2019, she collaborated with Daddy Yankee, the [[Jonas Brothers]] and [[Sebastián Yatra]] on the song "[[Runaway (Sebastián Yatra, Daddy Yankee and Natti Natasha song)|Runaway]]". In 2020, she collaborated with [[CNCO]] on the song “Honey Boo”.

On September 24, 2021, at Billboard’s 2021 Latin Music Week, Natasha returned to the stage for the first time in two years, performing a multitude of her top tracks.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Roiz|first=Jessica|title=Natti Natasha Makes Concert Comeback at 2021 Latin Music Week's Billboard En Vivo|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/latin/9636296/latin-music-week-2021-natti-natasha-j-quiles-concert-recap/|date=September 25, 2021|access-date=September 27, 2021|website=Billboard}}</ref>

Her six-episode docuseries ''[[Everybody Loves Natti]]'' premiered on November 19, 2021.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Avila |first1=Daniela |title=Natti Natasha Tells Younger Self to Not 'Be Scared' in Trailer for Upcoming Docuseries |url=https://people.com/music/watch-natti-natasha-upcoming-docuseries-everybody-loves-natti-trailer/ |access-date=18 November 2021 |publisher=People |date=20 October 2021}}</ref>

== Personal life ==
In 2021, she became engaged to her longtime manager, Raphy Pina, founder of Pina Records.{{Citation needed|date=May 2021}} On February 18, 2021, she announced that she's pregnant with Pina's child. On May 22, 2021, she gave birth to their daughter, Vida Isabelle Pina Gutiérrez.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.eonline.com/news/1272498/natti-natasha-gives-birth-welcomes-first-baby-with-fiance-raphy-pina|title=Natti Natasha Gives Birth, Welcomes First Baby with Fiancé Raphy Pina|date=May 22, 2021}}</ref>
