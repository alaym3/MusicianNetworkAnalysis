'''Geowulf''' are an Australian [[dream pop]] duo formed in [[Sunshine Coast, Queensland]] in 2016. Their debut [[Album#Studio|studio album]], ''Great Big Blue'', was released in 2018, and their second album, ''My Resignation'', was released on October 25, 2019.<ref name="paste">{{cite news |last1=Thapliyal |first1=Substitute |title=Geowulf Announce New Album, Channel the Dark Side of '60s Girl Group Pop in "He's 31" |url=https://www.pastemagazine.com/articles/2019/07/geowulf-channels-the-dark-side-of-60s-girl-group-p.html |accessdate=12 September 2019 |publisher=Paste |date=8 July 2019}}</ref>

==History==
In 2017, Geowulf released their debut [[Extended play|EP]], ''Relapse'', containing four songs including "Saltwater", their first single.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Day |first1=Laurence |title=Track By Track: Geowulf on the Relapse EP |url=https://www.thelineofbestfit.com/features/track-by-track/track-by-track-geowulf-on-the-relapse-ep |accessdate=16 September 2019 |publisher=The Line of Best Fit |date=1 June 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last1=Aikins |first1=Gabriel |title=Listen to “Get You,” a fantastic new track from Geowulf |url=https://substreammagazine.com/2017/05/get-you-geowulf/ |accessdate=16 September 2019 |publisher=Substream Magazine |date=30 May 2017}}</ref> "Saltwater" was originally released as a single in 2016, and was a part of the 37 Adventures [[compilation album]] ''Odd Numbers Volume 1''.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Hackett |first1=Andrew |title=Song of the Day: Geowulf - Saltwater |url=https://www.kexp.org/read/2016/10/10/song-of-the-day-geowulf-saltwater/ |accessdate=16 September 2019 |publisher=KEXP.org |date=10 October 2016}}</ref>

Geowulf's debut studio album, ''Great Big Blue'', was released on February 16, 2018.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Yung |first1=Ben |title=Geowulf – ‘Great Big Blue’ (album review) |url=https://therevue.ca/2018/02/20/geowulf-great-big-blue/ |accessdate=16 September 2019 |publisher=The Revue |date=20 February 2018}}</ref> Their second album, ''My Resignation'', was released on October 25, 2019. The singles "I See Red", "He's 31", "Lonely" and "My Resignation" were all released in advance of the album.<ref name="paste"/><ref>{{cite news |title=Premiere: Geowulf - "Lonely" |url=https://www.wonderlandmagazine.com/2019/09/02/premiere-geowulf-lonely/ |accessdate=20 September 2019 |publisher=Wonderland |date=2 September 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last1=Lowe |first1=Nick |title=Geowulf - My Resignation |url=https://www.clashmusic.com/reviews/geowulf-my-resignation |accessdate=28 October 2019 |publisher=Clash Music |date=25 October 2019}}</ref>

They are currently based in [[London]], England.<ref name="wonderland">{{cite news |title=New Noise: Geowulf |url=https://www.wonderlandmagazine.com/2017/09/06/new-noise-geowulf/ |accessdate=12 September 2019 |publisher=Wonderland |date=6 September 2017}}</ref>
