'''Félix Danilo Gómez Bosquez''' (born August 29, 1980),<ref name="mun2">{{Cite web|url=http://www.holamun2.com/news/versus-flex-vs-eddy-lover|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100128131046/http://www.holamun2.com/news/versus-flex-vs-eddy-lover|url-status=dead|title=Flex vs. Eddy Lover on mun2|archive-date=January 28, 2010}}</ref> known by his [[stage name]]s '''Flex''' and '''Nigga''',<ref name="mun2"/> is a [[Panamanian]] [[reggaeton]] singer and songwriter. He adopted the name '' Nigga'' after being told by another Panamanian artist that he "sings like a black guy from Jamaica."<ref name="name">{{Cite web|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/music-nigga-dc-idUSN1031367620071211|title=Panama singer changes dubious nickname for U.S.|date=December 11, 2007|website=Reuters.com}}</ref> Before releasing an album in the United States in 2008, Flex removed references to his nickname in songs, and his CD packages were reprinted with the name "Flex."<ref name="name"/>

==Awards and accomplishments==
Flex has received nominations for a variety of awards shows including Premios Billboard, Premios lo Nuestro, Latin Grammys, Premios Juventud, Premios Oye, and Premios Monitor Latino.<ref name="bio">{{Cite web|url=http://www.latinrapper.com/flex-biography.html|title=Flex Biography|website=Latinrapper.com}}</ref> Flex has won an Oye award, a Latin Grammy, and eight Billboard Latin Music awards.<ref name="bio"/>

His accomplishments include a number one position in Mexican and American radio for over 20 weeks, as well as Gold and Platinum album sales worldwide.<ref name="bio"/>

==Personal life==
After five years of dating, Flex married his long-time girlfriend Osiris Vega on August 26, 2008 and got divorced in 2011.<ref name="osiris">[https://web.archive.org/web/20101205130712/http://www.latingossip.com/celebrity-weddings/flex-marries-in-panama.html] </ref>  Flex continues to live in Panama.<ref name="flex"/>
