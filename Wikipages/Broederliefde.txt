'''Broederliefde''' are a Dutch [[hip-hop]] [[musical group|group]], originating from [[Rotterdam]]. The group is composed of five rappers: Emms (Emerson Akachar), Jerr (Jerzy Rocha Livramento), Sjaf (Javiensley Dams), Edson (Edson Cesar) and Mella (Melvin Silberie). In 2016, their project ''Hard Work Pays Off 2'' sat at the top of the album charts in the Netherlands for three months.

== Biography ==

The group members originated from [[Cape Verde]], the [[Dominican Republic]] and [[Curaçao]], and grew up in the [[Netherlands]]. They started off playing football, then moved into the studio. The group's name translates from [[Dutch language|Dutch]] as ''brotherly love'' (literally ''brotherlove'').{{Citation needed|date=August 2019}}

Their first album, ''Gevoelig feestje'', released in 2014, reached the top of the Album Top 100. They were nominated for Edison Awards.<ref>{{in lang|nl}} [http://www.funx.nl/news/music/23300-broederliefde-ronnie-flex-en-typhoon-genomineerd-voor-edison Broederliefde, Ronnie Flex en Typhoon genomineerd voor Edison], FunX (28 January 2015)</ref> The group also won awards at the FunX Awards in the same year.

Their second album, ''Hard Work Pays Off'', was released at the end of 2015, and contained the single ''Alaka''. The single was the first released by Broederliefde, and was a certified [[platinum record]] reaching the [[Dutch Top 40]] and [[Single Top 100]]. At the end of April 2016, the sequel to ''Hard Work Pays Off'', named ''Hard Work Pays Off 2'', was released. With its digital release, the album reached almost 700,000 listens on [[Spotify]]. Broederliefde again reached the Single Top 100.<ref>{{in lang|nl}} [http://top-notch.nl/artiesten/broederliefde Broederliefde], Top Notch.</ref> ''Hard Work Pays Off 2'' remained for longer at number one than any record for 13 years, since [[Frans Bauer]]. It became the first Dutch-language album to reach number one for 13 weeks.<ref>{{in lang|nl}} [http://nos.nl/op3/artikel/2129572-broederliefde-pakt-13-jaar-oud-record-van-frans-bauer-af.html Broerderliefde pakt 13 jaar oud record van Frans Bauer af], Nederlandse Omroep Stichting (NOS) (2 September 2016).</ref> In November, the album was also nominated for a [[3voor12]] Award, and the group was nominated for an [[MTV EMA|MTV Award]] for best Dutch group.<ref>{{in lang|nl}} [https://www.televizier.nl/categorie/muziek/douwe-bob-en-broederliefde-in-de-running-voor.2327968.lynkx Douwe Bob en Broerderliefde in de running voor MTV Award], Televizier (27 September 2016).</ref><ref>{{in lang|nl}} [http://3voor12.vpro.nl/nieuws/2016/3voor12-Award/Recensie-Broederliefde.html 3voor12 Award Genomineerde: Broederliefde], ''[[3voor12]]'', (9 September 2016).</ref>

After taking part in the [[Lowlands (festival)|Lowlands Festival]],<ref>{{in lang|nl}} [http://top-notch.nl/nieuws/lucky-fonz-iii-en-broederliefde-op-pinkpop-2016 Lucky Fonz III en Broederliefde op Pinkpop 2016], Top Notch (10 March 2016).</ref><ref>{{in lang|nl}} [http://go.3fm.nl/festivals-lowlands/nieuws-detail/7748/Tom-Odell,-Chase-?-Status,-Klangstof-en-meer-naar-Lowlands= Tom Odell, Chase & Status, Klangstof en meer naar Lowlands] {{Webarchive|url=https://archive.is/20160609013535/http://go.3fm.nl/festivals-lowlands/nieuws-detail/7748/Tom-Odell,-Chase-?-Status,-Klangstof-en-meer-naar-Lowlands= |date=9 June 2016 }}, 3FM (4 February 2016).</ref> Broederliefde made a number of significant public appearances, including at Uitmarkt in [[Amsterdam]], and on the local TV shows ''RTL Late Night'' and ''DINO''. A collaboration with Jan Smit, named ''Kom dichterbij me'', was especially written for their performance on ''DINO''.<ref>{{in lang|nl}} [http://www.funx.nl/news/music/29403-broederliefde-en-jan-smit-over-hun-samenwerking-in-rtl-late-night Broederliefde en Jan Smit over hun samenwerking in RTL Late Night], FunX (30 September 2016).</ref> Their single ''Jungle'' became the most played Dutch song on [[Spotify]] in 2016.<ref>{{cite web|language=nl|url=https://www.538.nl/broederliefde/2016-is-het-jaar-van-broederliefde|title=2016 is het jaar van Broederliefde|date=1 December 2016|website=Radio 538}}.</ref> In 2017, Broederliefde became ambassador to Bevrijdingsdag (Dutch Liberation Day).<ref>{{in lang|nl}} [http://www.ad.nl/show/broederliefde-geen-ambassadeur-van-de-vrijheid~a0434d3c/ Broederliefde geen Ambassadeur van de Vrijheid] Algemeen Dagblad, 9 March 2017.</ref>
