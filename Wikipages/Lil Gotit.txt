'''Semaja Zair Render'''<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://mugshots.com/US-Counties/Georgia/Fulton-County-GA/Semaja-Zair-Render.176876829.html|title=Semaja Zair Render Mugshot 176876829 - Semaja Zair Render Arrest - Fulton County, GA - Booked on 4/25/2019|website=mugshots.com|language=en-US|access-date=2019-10-07}}</ref> (born August 6, 1999), known professionally as '''Lil Gotit''', is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter.<ref name="Signed to ysl. Dandridge-Lemco 2018">{{Cite news|url=https://www.thefader.com/2018/11/16/lil-gotit-hood-baby-interview|title=Lil Gotit is ready to rise to the top of Atlanta's rap food chain|last=Dandridge-Lemco|first=Ben|date=November 16, 2018|work=[[The Fader]]|access-date=2018-11-17}}</ref>

==Early life==
Semaja Zair Render was born in [[Atlanta]], [[Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia]], the youngest of seven children. Render was initially raised in [[Forest Park, Georgia|Forest Park]] on Southside Atlanta's Conley Road. After dropping out of high school during his freshman year, Render moved to Cleveland Avenue. Like his brother and fellow rapper Lil Keed, who is one year older, Gotit did not start taking rap seriously until his friend Rudy was killed a few days before the release of the group compilation mixtape ''Young Slime Season'' in 2016.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thefader.com/2019/03/06/lil-gotit-hood-baby-gen-f|title=Lil Gotit is the heir to Atlanta's rap throne|website=The FADER|language=en|access-date=2019-09-26}}</ref>

Gotit then began releasing songs with his brother Lil Keed, whom he collaborated with on the songs: "Trap Bunkin", "Dirty Dancer" and "All Season".<ref name="Signed to ysl. Dandridge-Lemco 2018" /> Described as one of Atlanta's next rising rap stars, his music was starting to be played in clubs.<ref name="Signed to ysl. Dandridge-Lemco 2018" /> The name Lil Gotit was derived from Maja Gotit, a nickname inspired by him "having everything". 
He has cited Young Thug as his mentor.<ref name="Zidel 2018">{{Cite news|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/lil-gotit-is-an-atlanta-secret-not-for-long-the-hood-baby-talks-co-signs-from-young-thug-lil-uzi-ver-news.64695.html|title=Lil Gotit Is An Atlanta Secret, Not For Long: The "Hood Baby" Talks Co-Signs From Young Thug, Lil Uzi Vert & More|last=Zidel|first=Alex|date=November 16, 2018|work=HotNewHipHop|access-date=2018-11-17}}</ref>
