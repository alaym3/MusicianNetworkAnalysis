'''Egotrippi''' is a [[Finnish people|Finnish]] pop group, founded in the early 1990s. Among their hits are "Älä koskaan ikinä", which is featured on the soundtrack of the Finnish movie ''[[Nousukausi]]'' (2003), "Unihiekkaa" and "Matkustaja", which reached #11 on the Finnish Top 20 and featured on the soundtrack of the movie ''[[Kukkia ja sidontaa]]'' (2004).
