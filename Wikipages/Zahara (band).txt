'''Zahara''' were a [[jazz fusion]] ensemble who released one album for [[Antilles Records]].<ref>[{{AllMusic|class=artist|id=p26044|pure_url=yes}} Allmusic discography]</ref>

They consisted of several notable members including [[Reebop Kwaku Baah]] (percussion), [[Paul Delph]] (keyboards), [[Bryson Graham]] (drums), [[Rosko Gee]] (bass).<ref>[http://www.discogs.com/Zahara-Flight-Of-The-Spirit/release/1358534 Personnel]</ref>
