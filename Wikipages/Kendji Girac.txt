'''Kendji''' "'''Girac'''" '''Jason Maillié''' ({{IPA-fr|kɛndʒi ʒiʁak dʒɛzɔn maje}}<!--Pronunciation of "Jason" is correct. Dot not change it to /ʒazɔ̃/.-->; born 3 July 1996), also known mononymously as '''Kendji''', is a [[French people|French]] singer and guitarist. He is the winner of [[The Voice: la plus belle voix (season 3)|Season 3]] of the music competition ''[[The Voice: la plus belle voix]]'' as part of Team [[Mika (singer)|Mika]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/6251770/kendji-girac/biography|title=Kendji Girac - Biography {{!}} Billboard|website=www.billboard.com|language=en|access-date=2017-04-10}}</ref> He has released three studio albums, ''[[Kendji (album)|Kendji]],'' ''[[Ensemble (album)|Ensemble]]'' and ''[[Amigo (Kendji Girac album)|Amigo]]'' as well as a string of hit singles.

==Biography==
===Beginnings===
The youngest of a [[Catalonia|Catalan]] [[Romani people in Spain|Gitano]] family, Kendji grew up in [[Saint-Astier, Dordogne|Saint-Astier]] and was taught to play the guitar and sing by his grandfather. He left school at 16 to follow the family business as an [[arborist]].<ref>[http://www.leparisien.fr/tv/video-kendji-avant-the-voice-j-avais-une-vie-de-gitan-12-05-2014-3834509.php VIDEO. Kendji: « Avant The Voice, j'avais une vie de gitan »] {{in lang|fr}}</ref> He speaks Catalan with his parents.<ref>[http://www.purebreak.com/news/kendji-girac-et-le-francais-parfois-c-est-un-peu-complique-pour-moi/75252#lt_source=external,manual Kendji Girac et le français : "Parfois, c'est un peu compliqué pour moi"] {{in lang|fr}}</ref> He gained some fame through publishing materials online. His Romanes version of "Bella" by [[Maître Gims]] filmed by a friend went viral. His video garnered 5.8 million views.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=INTK-HvRznA|title=Bella (maître Gims) version Gitane par Kendji Girac|last=fugitif73|date=10 August 2013|via=YouTube}}</ref> Another popular song he uploaded was "Si j'étais président" a cover of [[Gérard Lenorman]].

===''The Voice''===
For [[The Voice: la plus belle voix (season 3)|season 3]] of ''[[The Voice: la plus belle voix]]'', he auditioned with the song "Bella" by [[Maître Gims]] in the second episode of season 3 broadcast on January 18, with only judge [[Mika (singer)|Mika]] turning his chair, whereas the three others, [[Florent Pagny]], [[Jenifer (singer)|Jenifer]] and [[Garou (singer)|Garou]] refrained.

In the Musical Battles Round broadcast on 8 March 2014, Mika put him against Youness both singing "[[Tous les mêmes]]" by [[Stromae]]. Kendji moved to "L'épreuve ultime" round where he performed "[[Hotel California]] by [[Eagles (band)|The Eagles]] in the 29 March 2014 emission. 
 
In the live rounds he performed "Ma philosophie" by [[Amel Bent]] (5 April 2014), "[[Mad World]]" from [[Tears for Fears]] (19 April 2014) and "Allumer le feu" by [[Johnny Hallyday]] (19 April 2014). In the semi-final on 3 May 2014 he sang "Belle" by [[Garou (singer)|Garou]] and [[Daniel Lavoie]] qualifying for the finals as Team Mika's finalist.

In the final broadcast on Final, on 10 May 2014, he sang "Amor de mis amores" / "[[Volare (song)|Volare]]" by [[Gipsy Kings]], "Temps à nouveau" by [[Jean-Louis Aubert]] and "[[L'Aigle noir]]" by [[Barbara (singer)|Barbara]]. He won ''The Voice'' title for season 3 with 51% of the public vote against 21% for runner up Maximilien Philippe, with [[Amir Haddad]] finishing third with 18% and Wesley with only 10% fourth.

===Career===
[[Image:Kendji Montauban.jpg|thumb|right|240px|Kendji Girac live]]
His first official single  the bilingual French/Spanish "[[Color Gitano]]", which was released in June 2014 from his eponymous EP,<ref>[http://www.metronews.fr/culture/the-voice-decouvrez-color-gitano-le-premier-single-de-kendji-girac/mnfn!5uANILEA5jAr/ ''MetroNews'' (Paris): "The Voice" : Découvrez Color Gitano, le premier single de Kendji Girac] {{in lang|fr}}</ref><ref>[http://www.chartsinfrance.net/Kendji/news-92520.html Charts in France: Clip de "Color Gitano" : Kendji Girac ("The Voice") profite de la vie et impose son style] {{in lang|fr}}</ref> followed by his official album release in September. In December 2014, he won two [[NRJ Music Award]]s for Francophone Breakthrough of the Year and "Color Gitano" as Francophone Song of the Year.<ref>[http://www.leparisien.fr/musique/en-images-les-grands-vainqueurs-des-nrj-music-awards-sont-13-12-2014-4371905.php ''Les grands vainqueurs des NRJ Music Awards sont...''] sur le site ''leparisien.fr''</ref> His album ''Kendji'' sold 600,000 copies by January 2015,<ref>[http://www.chartsinfrance.net/Kendji-Girac/news-95599.html Kendji franchit le seuil des 600.000 ventes avec son premier album] {{in lang|fr}}</ref> and over 1 million copies by October 2015,<ref>{{cite web|title = Kendji Girac franchit le cap du million d'albums vendus|url = http://www.lefigaro.fr/musique/2015/10/13/03006-20151013ARTFIG00148-kendji-girac-franchit-le-cap-du-million-d-albums-vendus.php|website = Le Figaro|access-date = 2015-10-15}}</ref> certifying [[List of best-selling albums in France|Diamond]] and becoming France's second highest selling album of 2014 after ''[[Racine carrée]]'' by [[Stromae]]. He is currently the biggest selling ''[[The Voice (TV series)|The Voice]]'' alumnus worldwide. His follow-up single "[[Andalouse (song)|Andalouse]]" proved even more successful commercially. Other notable singles included "[[Conmigo (song)|Conmigo]]" from the repackaged version of ''Kendji'' released on 4 May 2015.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.melty.fr/kendji-girac-bientot-une-nouvelle-reedition-de-son-premier-album-a396227.html|title=Kendji Girac : Bientôt une nouvelle réédition de son premier album !|date=31 March 2015}}</ref>

A second album titled ''[[Ensemble (album)|Ensemble]]'' was released on 30 October 2015.<ref>{{cite web|title = Kendji : "Ensemble", son deuxième album, bientôt dans les bacs|url = http://www.chartsinfrance.net/Kendji-Girac/news-98933.html|website = chartsinfrance.net|access-date = 2015-10-15}}</ref> Notable singles from his second album include "[[Me Quemo]]", "[[Les yeux de la mama]]" and "[[No Me Mirès Màs]]" (jointly with [[Soprano (rapper)|Soprano]]).

On 31 August 2018 his third album, titled ''[[Amigo (Kendji Girac album)|Amigo]]'' was released. It started at number one on the French sales chart with 48,408 sales.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chartsinfrance.net/Kendji-Girac/news-107974.html|title="Amigo" : Kendji Girac numéro un des ventes avec son nouvel album|website=chartsinfrance.net|access-date=2018-09-07}}</ref>

On 22 August 2020 he joined the cast of [[The Voice Kids (French TV series)|The Voice Kids France]], becoming the first French former contestant to integrate the jury of the French franchise of the show, alongside [[Jenifer (singer)|Jenifer]], [[Patrick Fiori]] and [[Soprano (singer)|Soprano]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Média|first=Prisma|title=The Voice Kids : Kendji Girac fera ses premiers pas de coach le… - Voici|url=https://www.voici.fr/news-people/actu-people/the-voice-kids-kendji-girac-fera-ses-premiers-pas-de-coach-le-685601|access-date=2020-08-16|website=Voici.fr|language=fr}}</ref>

=== Charity work ===
In 2017, Kendji joined the [[Les Enfoirés|Enfoirés]], a team of high-grossing artists involved in the hugely popular charity mission [[Restaurants du Cœur|Restos du Coeur]], which hosts yearly concerts to raise money for one of France's most respected soup kitchen networks.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Kendji Girac rejoint la troupe des Enfoirés - Vidéo Dailymotion|url=https://www.dailymotion.com/video/x54f6w8|website=Dailymotion|language=en|access-date=2020-05-04}}</ref>
