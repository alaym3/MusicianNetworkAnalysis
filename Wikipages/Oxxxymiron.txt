'''Miron Yanovich Fyodorov''' ({{Lang-ru|Мирон Янович Фёдоров}}; born 31 January 1985), known by stage name '''Oxxxymiron''', is a Russian [[Hip hop music|hip-hop]] artist and former executive director of the Booking Machine [[Talent agent|booking]] agency,<ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=9 February 2017|title=Oxxxymiron возглавил компанию Booking Machine|trans-title=Oxxxymiron takes over the leadership of Booking Machine|url=https://the-flow.ru/news/oxxxymiron-booking-machine-ceo|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=21 November 2020|website=The-Flow.ru|language=ru}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=24 August 2019|title=Oxxxymiron объявил об уходе c Booking Machine|trans-title=Oxxxymiron announced his departure from Booking Machine|url=https://the-flow.ru/news/oxxxy-bm-vsyo|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190824193304/https://the-flow.ru/news/oxxxy-bm-vsyo|archive-date=24 August 2019|access-date=21 November 2020|website=The-Flow.ru|language=ru}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=24 August 2019|title=Оксимирон объявил об уходе из лейбла Booking Machine и намекнул на отказ от исполнения старых песен|trans-title=Oxxxymiron announced his departure from the Booking Machine label and hinted at abandoning the performance of old songs|url=https://meduza.io/news/2019/08/24/oksimiron-ob-yavil-ob-uhode-iz-leybla-booking-machine-i-nameknul-na-otkaz-ot-ispolneniya-staryh-pesen|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=24 August 2019|access-date=21 November 2020|website=[[Meduza]]|language=ru}}</ref> as well as a co-founder and former member of the record [[Record label|label]] Vagabund. == Biography ==
Miron Yanovich Fyodorov was born on 31 January 1985 in [[Leningrad]], in a family of [[History of the Jews in Russia|Russian Jews]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=M|first=Tuyara|date=24 January 2013|title=Интервью с Луперкалем|trans-title=Interview with Lupercal|url=https://dystopia.me/luperkal|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=22 November 2020|website=dystopia.me}}</ref> His father is a [[Theoretical physics|theoretical physicist]] of [[Boris Konstantinov|Konstantinov]] Institute of Nuclear Physics in St. Petersburg and his mother is a [[librarian]]. In St. Peterburg Miron attended secondary school No. 185. When Fyodorov was 9 years old, his family emigrated to [[Essen]], [[Germany]]. During his studies in Maria Wächtler School, Miron had a 'tense relationship' with his classmates because of his social standing and poor German. At the age of 13, Fyodorov began to write lyrics and rap in [[German language|German]] and [[Russian language|Russian]] under the name MC Mif (from '''Mi'''ron '''F'''yodorov). When he was 15, the Fyodorovs moved to [[Slough]], [[United Kingdom]]. In 2004, Miron Fyodorov began studying at the [[University of Oxford]] in the faculty of English. During his studies Fyodorov was the  president of the Russian community of Oxford. In 2006 he was diagnosed with [[bipolar disorder]] and had to stop studying, however he managed to continue studying after a break. In June 2008 he graduated from the university with a degree in [[Middle English literature]]. 

After university, Fyodorov moved to [[East End of London|East End]] where he restarted his musical career under the name Oxxxymiron. The name is derived from his first name Miron, rhetorical device [[oxymoron]] and [[X rating|triple X]], representing the large amount of profanity in his lyrics. He was not able to find a job because of his perceived [[overqualification]] and had to work as a cashier, a translator, a loader, a guide, a stallman, a tutor, an MC and an office clerk. Fyodorov's life in London formed the basis of the script of the Russian 2015 TV series ''[[Londongrad (TV series)|Londongrad]]''.

=== Optik Russia (2008–2010) ===
In 2008, after a seven-year break, Oxxxymiron published a new track ''London Against All'' ({{Lang-ru|Лондон против всех}}). In the same year he joined a German label [[Optik Records|Optik Russia]] where he met Dmitry Khinter ({{Lang-ru|Дмитрий Хинтер}}), known by his stage name [[w:ru:Schokk|Schokk]]. In 2008 Oxxxymiron published his first music video, ''I'm a Hater'' ({{Lang-ru|Я xeйтep}}).

In 2009 Oxxxymiron participated in the 14th independent battle on Hip-Hop.ru and reached the semi-finals. One of the main intrigues of the battle was the correspondence confrontation of Oxxxymiron and underground anonymous rapper [[w:ru:Babangida|Babangida]], which was expressed in mutual disses against each other in the tracks of both performers. However, due to the departure of Miron after the 8th round, the face-to-face confrontation between them never took place. At the end of the year Oxxxymiron won several nominations (the Best Battle MC, the Breakthrough of the Battle, the Best Sparring of the Battle, the Best Track of the Battle) in the Hip-Hop.ru Awards 2009 public voting and also became the Invention of 2009. 

On July 26 and 31 of the same year, Miron's first concerts took place in [[Kyiv]] and [[Odessa]]. In August 2010 Oxxxymiron and Schokk left Optik Russia.

=== Vagabund (2011) ===
In autumn 2010 Oxxxymiron and Schokk toured countries of [[Commonwealth of Independent States|CIS]]. In a year, Oxxxymiron, Schokk and manager Ivan Karoy aka Vanya Lenin founded an independent label ''Vagabund'' (''Vagabond'' in German). On 15 September 2011, Vagabund published Fyodorov's first album ''[[w:ru:Вечный жид (альбом)|The Wandering Jew]]'' ({{Lang-ru|Вечный жид}}), which received a lot of positive reviews. On 30 October 2011 ''Vagabund'' gave their last concert. On 1 November 2011 Oxxxymiron left ''Vagabund'' due to conflict between the members of the label and [[Moscow]] rapper [[w:ru:Жиган, Рома|Roma Zhigan]]. On November 6 he gave a free [[a cappella]] concert in [[Moscow]] and moved back to [[London]].

=== miXXXtape (2012–2014) ===
[[File:Oxxxymiron at Afisha's Picnic-1.jpg|thumb|Oxxxymiron performing in [[Kolomenskoye]], 24 July 2012]]
On 12 March 2012 Oxxxymiron published ''miXXXtape I'' which includes the best tracks recorded from 2008 to 2011. On 18 October 2013 ''[[w:ru:MiXXXtape II: Долгий путь домой|miXXXtape II: Long way home]]'' ({{Lang-ru|miXXXtape II: Долгий путь домой}}) was published. It included the best tracks of 2012-2013 and remixed Oxxxymiron's bars from his battle against [[w:ru:Крип-а-Крип|Krip-a-Krip]] in [[w:ru:Versus Battle|Versus Battle]] league. On 8 August 2014 Oxxxymiron extended the deadline of the release of his second album for an indefinite period despite it having been planned to be released on 14 August 2014.

=== Gorgorod (2015–2016) ===
On 25 August 2015 music video  ''Londongrad'' ({{Lang-ru|Лондонград}}) was published as a teaser trailer for TV series ''[[Londongrad (TV series)|Londongrad]]'' based on Miron's life in [[London]] before restarting his rapper career. On September 21 Oxxxymiron published the music video ''City under the Sole'' ({{Lang-ru|Город под подошвой}}) which was watched 2 million times in a week.

On 13 November 2015 Oxxxymiron released his second album, ''[[w:ru:Горгород|Gorgorod]]'' ({{Lang-ru|Горгород}}) which became the most popular Russian music album of 2015.

In 2017 Oxxxymiron became [[Chief executive officer|CEO]] of the Booking Machine (BM) booking agency.

On 28 June 2016 Oxxxymiron became the first ambassador of [[Reebok Classic]] in Russia.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.gq.ru/style/oxxxymiron-stal-licom-reebok-classic-vnezapno|title=OXXXYMIRON СТАЛ ЛИЦОМ REEBOK CLASSIC (ВНЕЗАПНО)}}</ref>

=== 2017-present ===
[[File:Выступление Oxxxymiron в Ледовом (171028) (10).jpg|thumb|left|Oxxxymiron's concert in the [[Ice Palace (Saint Petersburg)|Ice Palace]] of [[St. Petersburg]], 28 October 2017]]
On 28 September 2017 it was announced that Oxxxymiron will star in the film adaptation of [[Victor Pelevin]]’s novel ''[[Empire V]]''. The film's [[Film director|director]] is [[Victor Ginzburg (director)|Victor Ginzburg]], famous for the [[Generation P (film)|screen version]] of the novel ''[[Generation "П"]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E-RMCNqzS80|title=Oxxxymiron сыграет Митру в фильме Empire V (Анонс 2017)}}</ref> In 2021 Oxxxymiron also portrayed composer [[Nikolai Rubinstein]] in [[Kirill Serebrennikov]]'s ''[[w:ru:Жена Чайковского|Tchaikovsky's wife]]''.

In August 2019 Oxxxymiron left his CEO position in Booking Machine agency.

In September 2019, Oxxxymiron unexpectedly took part in the 17th Independent Battle on Hip-Hop.ru and passed the track for the first qualifying round. After that, a certain wave of excitement began around the event, and many famous hip-hop artists also paticipated ([[Egor Kreed]], [[Noize MC]], [[L'One]], [[ST1M]], [[Yury Khovansky]], etc.). On 2 March 2020 Oxxxymiron announced his rejection from the 5th round of the battle on [[Twitter]].

On 1 November 2021 Oxxxymiron published a nine-minute-long music video ''[[w:ru:Кто убил Марка?|Who killed Mark?]]''. On 12 November Fyodorov's third mixtape, ''[[w:ru:miXXXtape III: Смутное время|miXXXtape III: Time of Troubles]]'', was released. It included 36 songs from 2014 to 2021 and four new tracks among them. The nine-minute single ''Who killed Mark?'', which preceded the release of the mixtape, was not included in the playlist of the record.

=== Battle rap ===
Oxxxymiron is the most viewed battle rapper in the world. Amount of views of his rap battles exceed 100 million on [[YouTube]].{{Citation needed|date=June 2021}}

Oxxxymiron participated in the Russian battle rap league [[w:ru:Versus Battle|Versus Battle]] five times and won the first four of them.

* 2013 – Oxxxymiron vs. [[w:ru:Крип-а-Крип|Krip-a-Krip]] (3:0){{Citation needed|date=June 2021}}
* 2014 – Oxxxymiron vs. Dunya (won by vote of crowd){{Citation needed|date=June 2021}}
* 2015 – Oxxxymiron vs. [[w:ru:Johnyboy|Johnyboy]] (5:0).<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rlZ1go7PhAI|title=VERSUS #1 season III Oxxxymiron VS Johnyboy (English subs)}}</ref> This battle has been watched 1 million times during one day and became the most popular battle in the world.{{Citation needed|date=June 2021}} Oxxxymiron and Versus Battle league attracted the [[King of the Dot]] host [[Organik (musician)|Organik]]'s and Dizaster's attention.
* 2016 – Oxxxymiron vs. [[w:ru:ST (рэпер)|ST]] (3:0). This battle has been watched 4 million times during one day.{{Citation needed|date=June 2021}}
* 2017 – Oxxxymiron vs. [[Slava KPSS]] (Versus Battle x #SLOVOSPB) (0:5).<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dwNPU-Yt99o|title=VERSUS X #SLOVOSPB: Oxxxymiron VS Slava KPSS / Gnoyny (English subtitles)}}</ref> This battle has been watched over 9 million times during the fist day and has become one of the most watched battles in the world.{{Citation needed|date=June 2021}}

* 2017 – Oxxxymiron vs. Dizaster is scheduled as a Main Event for [[King of the Dot]]'s World Domination 7 event (without judges).<ref>{{Citation|last=King of the Dot Entertainment|title=KOTD - Oxxxymiron (RUS) vs Dizaster (USA) {{!}} #WDVII|date=16 October 2017|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ldK9qvRBkMI|accessdate=16 October 2017}}</ref> This battle became highest viewed on YouTube channel [[King of the Dot]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=King of the Dot Entertainment - YouTube|url=https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCIuFtIO8i_XqA8lM7q4B1FQ|access-date=8 June 2021|website=www.youtube.com}}</ref> Oxxxymiron after the battle with Dizaster thanked [[Slava KPSS]] for an opportunity to work on the mistakes.
