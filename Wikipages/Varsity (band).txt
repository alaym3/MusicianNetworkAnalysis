'''Varsity''' is an American [[indie rock]] band from [[Chicago, Illinois]].

==History==
Varsity released a song on their Bandcamp page in 2013 titled "Turns Out", with a B-Side titled "Downtown".<ref name="thedeli1">{{cite web|title=Varsity|url=http://chicago.thedelimagazine.com/15123/varsity|publisher=The Deli Magazine|accessdate=18 December 2016}}</ref> Varsity released their first EP titled ''Thanks For Nothing'' in 2014.<ref name="thedeli">{{cite web|title=Varsity|url=http://chicago.thedelimagazine.com/17354/varsity|publisher=[[The Deli Magazine]]|accessdate=18 December 2016}}</ref> Varsity released their self-titled first full-length album in 2015.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Julious|first1=Britt|title=Varsity is ready for the next level|url=http://www.chicagotribune.com/entertainment/ct-varsity-chicago-music-hairpool-coles-bar-20150319-story.html|publisher=[[Chicago Tribune]]|accessdate=18 December 2016}}</ref><ref name="redeye">{{cite web|last1=Terry|first1=Josh|title=Exclusive: Varsity announces debut album, makes blissful indie pop on 'Hairpool'|url=http://www.redeyechicago.com/music/redeye-varsity-song-premiere-hairpool-20150304-htmlstory.html|publisher=[[RedEye]]|accessdate=18 December 2016}}</ref> In 2015, Varsity released a new song titled "Cult of Personality", with a B-Side titled "So Sad, So Sad".<ref name="redeye1">{{cite web|last1=Terry|first1=Josh|title=Exclusive: Varsity premieres new single 'Cult of Personality'|url=http://www.redeyechicago.com/music/redeye-song-of-the-day-varsity-premieres-new-track-cult-of-personality-20150824-htmlstory.html|publisher=RedEye|accessdate=18 December 2016}}</ref> In mid 2016, Varsity released a song titled "Smash", with a B-Side titled "Still Apart".<ref name="avclub">{{cite web|last1=Anthony|first1=David|title=Varsity strides forward with newfound confidence on its latest single|url=http://www.avclub.com/article/varsity-strides-forward-newfound-confidence-its-la-238830|publisher=[[The A.V. Club]]|accessdate=18 December 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Doyle|first1=Liam|title=VARSITY – SMASH / STILL APART|url=http://www.wakethedeaf.co.uk/2016/07/27/varsity-smash-still-apart/|publisher=Wake The Deaf|accessdate=18 December 2016}}</ref> On April 27, 2018 Varsity released their sophomore record, ''Parallel Person'', on Babe City Records.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://store.babecityrecords.com/products/610550-varsity-parallel-person|title=Varsity - Parallel Person|website=Babe City Records|language=en|access-date=2019-05-07}}</ref>

On October 30, 2018, Varsity announced their signing with Boston-based independent label [[Run for Cover Records|Run For Cover Records]] and released two new singles, "The Dogs Only Listen To Him" and "UFO".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.pastemagazine.com/articles/2018/10/varsity-sign-to-run-for-cover-records-release-two.html|title=Varsity Sign to Run for Cover Records, Release Two New Songs|website=pastemagazine.com|language=en|access-date=2019-05-07}}</ref>

*Dylan Weschler – Guitar / Backing Vocals

*Paul Stolz – Bass Guitar
*Jake Stolz – Drums
