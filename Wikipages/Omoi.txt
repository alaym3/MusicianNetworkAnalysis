"'''Omoi'''" is the first single of Japanese singer and [[Voice acting in Japan|voice actress]] [[Nana Mizuki]]. It was released on December 6, 2000, by [[King Records (Japan)|King Records]].<ref>http://www.mizukinana.jp/discography/single01.html</ref> It is her first single under the name Nana Mizuki.
