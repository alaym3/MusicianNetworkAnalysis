'''Mat Kerekes''' (born April 22, 1994) is an American singer and songwriter, best known as the lead vocalist of [[rock music|rock]] band [[Citizen (band)|Citizen]], of which he is a founding member. He is also a solo artist and a member of The Flats.

==Biography==
Kerekes was born and grew up in the [[Bedford Township, Monroe County, Michigan|Bedford Township]] area of Michigan, and attended Bedford High School. Before Citizen, Kerekes was the drummer of a metalcore band called The Sound of Glory at the age of 14. They had a large local following as well as somewhat of a regional following during their years of existence. This band included members of Citizen Nick Hamm and Eric Hamm on their same respective instruments. Additional members of the band were vocalist Josh Childress (guitarist of [[The Plot in You]] and a second guitarist to The Sound of Glory, Joey Chester. Kerekes formed the band Citizen shortly after the breakup of The Sound of Glory and stuck with a melodic hardcore sound in their early self-released material.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://newnoisemagazine.com/interview-citizen-talks-youth-memorable-band-moments/|title=Interview: Citizen talks about 'Youth' and Memorable Band Moments|publisher=New Noise Magazine|author=Joe Fitzpatrick|date=February 12, 2014|access-date=November 26, 2017}}</ref> Citizen played their first show on January 28, 2010 at Frankie's Inner City in Toledo, Ohio where they opened for [[Set Your Goals (band)|Set Your Goals]]. They have released three studio albums and two EPs.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/citizen-mn0001383557/discography/all|title=Citizen Discography|publisher=AllMusic|author=Jason Lymangrover|date=October 6, 2017|access-date=November 26, 2017}}</ref> Additionally, he has been an active member of his brother Chris Kerekes's band The Flats, playing drums and occasionally contributing vocals.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.altpress.com/news/entry/the_flats_and_northbound_announce_spring_tour|title=The Flats (Mat/Chris Kerekes) and Northbound announce spring tour|publisher=Altpress|author=Brian Kraus|date=March 14, 2015|access-date=November 26, 2017}}</ref>

Kerekes has also released solo music online since 2011.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://matkerekes.bandcamp.com/album/2011-2014|title=2011 - 2014|publisher=Bandcamp|author=Mat Kerekes|date=August 2, 2015|access-date=November 26, 2017}}</ref> In 2014 he released a six-song self-titled LP, and in 2016 released a full solo studio album, ''Luna & The Wild Blue Everything''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://exclaim.ca/music/article/mat_kerekes-luna_and_the_wild_blue_everything|title=Mat Kerekes Luna & the Wild Blue Everything|publisher=Exclaim!|author=Adam Feibel|date=August 31, 2016|access-date=November 26, 2017}}</ref> He wrote and performed everything on this record with the exception of some percussion, which was done by producer [[Will Yip]], and the violin.

Kerekes has cited [[Third Eye Blind]] among his greatest musical influences.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://matkerekes.com/about/|title=About|publisher=Mat Kerekes|date=2016|access-date=November 26, 2017}}</ref>

Kerekes follows a [[straight edge]] lifestyle.<ref>{{cite web|access-date=December 23, 2017|url=http://highwiredaze.com/citizenint1|title=Making a Connection with Citizen|publication-date=July 11, 2013|location=Pomona, California|first=Kenneth|last=Morton|website=highwiredaze.com|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://archive.today/20171102030806/http://highwiredaze.com/citizenint1|archive-date=November 2, 2017|df=mdy-all}}</ref> He is an advocate of animal rescues and has used his music before for fundraising efforts, specifically Toledo's PET Bull Project.<ref name="twitter">{{cite web|last=Kerekes|first=Mat|url=https://twitter.com/MatKerekes/status/859107665334743040|website=twitter.com|title=All donations given to Toledo Pet Bull dog rescue|date=May 1, 2017|access-date=November 26, 2017}}<!-- Proof of its officiality: https://www.facebook.com/Citizentheband/posts/242704455803910 --></ref> He owns two pit bulls.
