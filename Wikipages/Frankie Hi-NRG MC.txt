'''Francesco Di Gesù''' ({{IPA-it|franˈtʃesko di dʒeˈzu}}; born 18 July 1969), better known by his [[stage name]] '''Frankie Hi-NRG MC''', is an Italian [[rapper]]. He has been active since the early stages of the [[Italian hip hop]] movement in the early 1990s and is regarded as one of the pioneers of the genre.<ref>{{citation|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=KRAEAAAAMBAJ&pg=RA1-PA24&dq=Frankie+hi-nrg+mc#q=Frankie%20hi-nrg%20mc|title=Rapping the world|pages=16–22|last=Sinclair|first=David|publisher=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|date=1992-11-28|accessdate=2017-12-30}}</ref><ref name=italia>{{citation|url=https://italiapost.it/frankie-hi-nrg-mc-compleanno/|title=FRANKIE HI-NRG MC: OGGI IL COMPLEANNO, COMPIE 48 ANNI|publisher=[[Italia Post]]|date=2017-07-17|accessdate=2017-12-30}}</ref> He predominantly discusses politics and societal issues in his music, his debut single "Fight da faida" (Irma Records, 1992) complains about various issues, including the [[Mafia]] and its ties with Italian politicians, the [[Itavia Flight 870|Ustica massacre]], the [[Bologna massacre]] and the [[Operation Gladio]].<ref name=italia/> He has also participated in the [[Sanremo Music Festival]] in 2008 with his song "Rivoluzione" and again in 2014 with "Pedala" and "Un uomo è vivo".<ref name=italia/>

He has released six albums, 5 of those albums with [[RCA]] and the single "Quelli che benpensano" from his second album ''La morte dei miracoli'' was awarded Italian Song Of The Year 1998.<ref>{{citation|url=https://www.notizie.it/frankie-hi-nrg-mc-una-carriera-di-successi/|title=Frankie hi-nrg mc: una carriera di successi|publisher=[[Notizie]]|accessdate=2017-12-30|first= Emanuela|last=Crosetti}}</ref> In addition he has written for and performed with Italian artists like [[Fiorella Mannoia]], [[Giorgia (singer)|Giorgia]] and international stars like [[RZA]], [[Nas]], [[Beastie Boys]] and [[Run DMC]].

Frankie has expressed himself in other fields, writing and directing music videos. Since 2009 he has been an independent artist, releasing his latest album ''Essere Umani'' in 2014 on the Materie Prime Circolari snc label.
