'''Xibalba''' is an American [[hardcore punk]] band from [[Pomona, California]], formed in 2007. The band plays hardcore with influences from [[death metal]] and [[doom metal]], with lyrics in English and Spanish. Their debut album, ''[[Madre Mia Gracias Por Los Dias]]'' (2010) was released through A389 Recordings.<ref name="chuckingamosh.com">{{cite web|url=http://www.chuckingamosh.com/2012/03/20/xibalba-australian-tour-local-supports-announced/|title=Xibalba Australian Tour Local Supports Announced|work=chuckingamosh.com|access-date=30 March 2014}}</ref>  Their second album, ''[[Hasta La Muerte]]'' (2012), was released by [[Southern Lord Records|Southern Lord]].<ref name="stosuy">{{cite news|url=http://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/17005-hasta-la-muerte/|title=Rev. of Xibalba, ''Hasta La Muerte''|last=Stosuy|first=Brandon|date=20 August 2012|work=[[Pitchfork Media]]|access-date=4 March 2014}}</ref>

==History==
The band started in 2007 in Pomona, California when long time friends Nate, Brian, Jason and Bryan wanted to play dark and heavy music with eccentric influences. The friends previously performed music in hardcore punk bands together prior to forming Xibalba. In 2012, the band went on an Australian tour with Warbrain and [[Incendiary (band)|Incendiary]].<ref name="chuckingamosh.com"/> In 2013, they traveled with the "No Way Out" U.S. tour with headliners [[The Acacia Strain]], and [[Within the Ruins]], [[Fit for an Autopsy]], and [[American Me (band)|American Me]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://lambgoat.com/news/20710/The-Acacia-Strain-Within-The-Ruins-Xibalba-tour|work=lambgoat.com|title=The Acacia Strain, Within The Ruins, Xibalba tour|date=2 July 2013|access-date=30 March 2014}}</ref> 
Xibalba was toured on the "Die Knowing" U.S. tour alongside [[Comeback Kid (band)|Comeback Kid]], [[Backtrack (band)|Backtrack]], Downpresser, and [[To the Wind]].
