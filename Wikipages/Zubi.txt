'''Andrés González Jaén''' (born 21 May 1993), commonly known as '''Zubi''', is a Spanish [[Association football|footballer]] who plays for [[CP Cacereño|Cacereño]] as a [[Midfielder#Winger|left winger]].

==Club career==
Zubi was born in [[Cáceres, Spain|Cáceres]], [[Extremadura]]. He played youth football with [[Real Valladolid]], making his debut as a senior in the [[2012–13 Tercera División|2012–13 season]] with [[Real Valladolid B|the reserves]] in [[Tercera División]]. 

On 30 October 2013 Zubi made his first team – and [[La Liga]] – debut, playing the last two minutes in a 2–2 home draw against [[Real Sociedad]].<ref>[http://espnfc.com/en/report/373115/report.html?soccernet=true&cc=3888 Bravo denies valiant Valladolid]{{dead link|date=July 2016 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}; [[ESPN FC]], 30 October 2013</ref> On 17 July of the following year he moved to another [[reserve team]], [[Atlético Madrid B]] in [[Segunda División B]].<ref>[http://www.elperiodicoextremadura.com/noticias/deportes/zubi-salto-atletico_815864.html Zubi, salto al Atlético (Zubi, jump to Atlético)]; El Periódico de Extremadura, 17 July 2014 {{in lang|es}}</ref>
