'''Kevin Manthei''' (born 1970 in [[Maplewood, Minnesota]]) is an American [[composer]] for [[Film score|film]], television, and video games.

==Biography==
Manthei grew up in Minnesota. He played the piano in his youth and played the trumpet in high school. He graduated from the University of Minnesota with a Bachelor of Music degree in Theory and Composition. He also studied at the University of Southern California under [[Jerry Goldsmith]]. He has collaborated with other graduates of the program, [[Christophe Beck]] and [[Marco Beltrami]], writing additional music for Beck on the TV series ''[[Buffy the Vampire Slayer (TV series)|Buffy the Vampire Slayer]]'' and other shows, and composing additional music for Beltrami on films including  ''[[Scream 2]]'', ''[[Scream 3]]'', ''[[The Faculty]]'', ''[[Scary Movie 2]]'' and ''[[Resident Evil (2002 film)|Resident Evil]]''.<ref>[http://kmmproductions.com/meet_us.html] Kmmproductions.com</ref>

Manthei began his game writing career working on titles such as ''[[Panzer General II]]'', ''[[Vampire: The Masquerade - Redemption]]'', ''[[Sacrifice (video game)|Sacrifice]]'', ''[[Wizardry 8]]'', ''[[Twisted Metal: Black]]''. His other work on video game titles includes ''[[Kung Fu Panda (video game)|Kung Fu Panda]]'', ''[[Marvel Universe Online]]'', ''[[Upshift Strikeracer]]'', ''[[Xiaolin Showdown (video game)|Xiaolin Showdown]]'', ''[[Ultimate Spider-Man (video game)|Ultimate Spider-Man]]'', ''[[City of Villains]]'', ''[[The Sims 2]]'', ''[[StarCraft: Ghost]]'', ''[[Shrek 2 (video game)|Shrek 2]],'' ''[[Silent Hunter II]]'', and ''[[Shark Tale (video game)|Shark Tale]]''.

For his work on ''[[Invader Zim]]'', Manthei received an [[Annie Award]] nomination for Outstanding Achievement in Music for an animated series.<ref>[http://www.dailymotion.com/video/xafcmt_annie-awards-red-carpet-part-5_shortfilms] DailyMotion.com August 9, 2009</ref> ''Invader Zim'' also led to ''[[Xiaolin Showdown]]'' for Warner Brothers Animation as well as ''[[Brandy & Mr. Whiskers]]'' for Disney. In scoring with Warner Brothers' television series ''[[Johnny Test]]'', he wrote the emo punk inspired main title and score to the first season. Manthei also created music for shows like Cartoon Network's ''[[Generator Rex]]'' and Adult Swim's ''[[Robot Chicken]]''.

Manthei has also worked on scores for independent filmmakers such as ''[[The 24th Day]]''. He wrote the music for the feature films ''[[Justice League: The New Frontier]]'' and ''[[Batman: Gotham Knight]]'' and scored the animated series ''[[Ultimate Spider-Man (TV series)|Ultimate Spider-Man]]''.<ref>[http://kmmproductions.com/meet_us.html] Official Bio</ref>
