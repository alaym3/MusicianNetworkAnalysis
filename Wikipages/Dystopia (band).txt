'''Dystopia''' was an American [[crust punk]] band that formed in [[Orange County, California]]<ref>{{cite web |url=http://kzsu.stanford.edu/pguide/1995Summer/dystopia.html |title=Archived copy |accessdate=December 23, 2014 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141224012041/http://kzsu.stanford.edu/pguide/1995Summer/dystopia.html |archivedate=December 24, 2014 }}</ref> in 1991. They were popular in both the [[Heavy metal music|heavy metal]] and [[crust punk]] scenes, due in large part to the band's bleak, [[misanthropic]] imagery.<ref name="TM">{{cite web|last1=Matthew (February 20, 2013)|title=Miniature History of Dystopia|url=http://www.terrorizer.com/news/features-2/miniature-history-of-dystopia/|website=terrorizer.com|date=February 20, 2013|publisher=Terrorizer|accessdate=July 3, 2017}}</ref> Dystopia's lyrics often dealt with human emotions and social or political issues such as [[environmentalism]], [[racial equality]], [[substance abuse]], and [[animal rights]].<ref name="vice">{{cite web|last1=Hill|first1=John (August 6, 2014)|title=Dystopia's Landmark "Human=Garbage"|url=https://noisey.vice.com/en_us/article/rqy3dr/dystopias-landmark-human-garbage-twenty-years-old-so-celebrate-with-the-reissue|website=noisy.vice.com|publisher=Vice|accessdate=July 3, 2017}}</ref>  Their music utilized the slow, heavy sound of sludge metal whilst including more up-beat elements drawing from crust punk, [[grindcore]], and [[noise rock]].
