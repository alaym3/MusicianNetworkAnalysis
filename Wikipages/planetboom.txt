'''Planetboom''' or Boom is the [[contemporary worship music]] band, youth ministry of [[Planetshakers Church]] in [[Melbourne]], Australia.<ref>{{cite web |title=Planetboom Planetshakers Church Youth Ministry|url=https://www.planetshakers.com/planetboom/|publisher=[[Planetshakers]]}}</ref> They have released their first album, ''[[Jesus Over Everything]]'',<ref>{{cite web |author1=Hoganson Media |title=Planetshakers' Youth Band planetboom Releases 1st Full-Length Album 'Jesus Over Everything' |url=https://www.newreleasetoday.com/news_detail.php?newsid=3718 |publisher=New Release Today |accessdate=27 September 2019}}</ref> on 22 March 2019 by Venture3Media.<ref>{{cite web |title=planetboom - Venture3Media |url=https://venture3media.com/planetboom/ |website=Venture3Media |accessdate=27 September 2019}}</ref>

==History==
Youth ministry branch of [[Planetshakers]] primarily targeting high school students, ministering to teenagers via channels like school programs, youth camps and Friday meetups.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.theaustralian.com.au/life/weekend-australian-magazine/inside-planetshakers-melbournes-biggest-pentecostal-church/news-story/966521d9c6e4cba8e7ea61533411c6dc|title=Australian News Article}}</ref> They created the band called Planetboom, composed of young musicians, songwriters and vocalists, the team lead thousands of teenagers in breakthrough praise and worship every week,<ref>{{cite web |title=Planetboom {{!}} Planetshakers Church Youth Ministry|url=http://www.planetshakers.com/planetboom/ |publisher=[[Planetshakers]] |accessdate=27 September 2019}}</ref> they started doing [[Christian music]] since 2016 at the [[Planetshakers Church]]. Their debut single called, "Here Comes the Revival", was for the album ''[[Overflow: Live]]'' by the band [[Planetshakers]] in 2016.<ref>{{cite web |author1=Jessie Clarks |title= Planetshakers New Album "Overflow" Arrives September 30|url=http://www.thechristianbeat.org/index.php/news/2591-planetshakers-new-album-overflow-sept-30|website=The Christian Beat}}</ref> In the year 2017 they released two singles: "New Levels" and "Praise Over Problems".<ref>{{cite web |author1=Courtney Simpson |title=Planetboom Releases New Single Run to You November 9th |url=https://jesuswired.com/2018/10/30/planetboom-releases-new-single-run-to-you-november-9th/ |publisher=Jesus Wired |accessdate=27 September 2019}}</ref> In 2018 they released two singles: the first single released in June "[[Jesus Over Everything (song)|Jesus Over Everything]]",<ref>{{cite web |author1=GMA |title=Planetshakers’ Youth Band Planetboom Releases "Jesus Over Everything" June 1 |url=http://www.gospelmusic.org/planetshakers-youth-band-planetboom-releases-jesus-over-everything-june-1/ |publisher=Gospel Music Association |accessdate=27 September 2019}}</ref> and another in August called "[[Lemme Tell Ya]]"<ref>{{cite web |author1=CCM News |title=Planetshakers’ Planetboom Releases New Single, "Lemme Tellya" |url=https://www.ccmmagazine.com/news/planetshakers-planetboom-releases-new-single-lemme-tellya/ |publisher=[[CCM Magazine]] |accessdate=27 September 2019}}</ref> On 9 November they announced that they are releasing a new single called "[[Run to You (Planetboom song)|Run to You]]".<ref>{{cite web |title=PLANETSHAKERS' YOUTH BAND PLANETBOOM RELEASING 'RUN TO YOU' |url=http://www.louderthanthemusic.com/document.php?id=7382 |publisher=LOUDER THAN THE MUSIC |accessdate=27 September 2019}}</ref> They announced the release of their first album, ''[[Jesus Over Everything]]'', released on 22 March 2019.<ref>{{cite web |title=Jesus Over Everything: Planetboom weigh in with debut full-length album and Andy Harrison-penned book |url=http://www.crossrhythms.co.uk/articles/news/Jesus_Over_Everything/64901/p1/ |publisher=[[Cross Rhythms]] |accessdate=27 September 2019}}</ref>
