'''Anna Clendening''' (born March 12, 1993) is an American [[singer]], [[actress]], and [[Internet personality]], known originally for being one of the most followed people on the now-defunct video sharing service [[Vine (service)|Vine]],<ref>{{cite web |last1=Ulanoff |first1=Lance |title=The Ultimate Breakdown of the Weird World of Vine |url=https://mashable.com/2014/07/15/the-weird-world-of-vine/#EzbihYPKzPqW |website=Mashable |date=July 16, 2014 |publisher=Mashable |accessdate=March 17, 2019}}</ref> and later as a contestant on season 9 of ''[[America's Got Talent]]''.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Filipek |first1=Claire |title='America's Got Talent' Contestant Sings Louder Than Her Anxiety Disorder |url=https://bust.com/tv/12393-americas-got-talent-contestant-sings-louder-than-her-anxiety-disorder.html |website=Bust |publisher=Bust}}</ref><ref name="Reilly">{{cite news |last1=Reilly |first1=Kaitlin |title=Anna Clendening On How Justin Bieber Inspired Her New Track "Boys Like You" |url=https://www.refinery29.com/en-us/2018/06/200775/anna-clendening-boys-like-u-lyric-meaning |accessdate=March 23, 2019 |work=Refinery29 |date=June 4, 2018}}</ref> Now focusing primarily on her music career, Clendening has amassed over 89 million views on her YouTube channel and over 50 million streams on Spotify as of March 2019.<ref>{{cite web |title=Anna Clendening's Official Youtube Channel |url=https://www.youtube.com/user/annaclendening08/about |website=Youtube |publisher=Youtube |accessdate=March 17, 2019}}</ref>

==Early life==
Clendening was born in [[Chapel Hill, North Carolina]],<ref name="Collar">{{cite web |last1=Collar |first1=Matt |title=Anna Clendening Artist Biography by Matt Collar |url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/anna-clendening-mn0003701900/biography |website=AllMusic |accessdate=March 23, 2019}}</ref> to parents Vickie Moore Clendening and Michael Clendening.<ref>{{cite web |title=Anna Clendening's Personal Facebook Page |url=https://www.facebook.com/annawrenncee/about?lst=580486759%3A650084552%3A1552833713&section=relationship |website=Facebook |accessdate=March 17, 2019}}</ref> At age 14, Anna was discovered to have severe anxiety and depression. While suffering from panic attacks due to her anxiety, Anna turned to music and began writing and performing songs in her bedroom.<ref>{{cite web |title=Contestant With Crippling Anxiety Disorder Gives Stunning Performance on 'America's Got Talent' |url=https://themighty.com/2014/06/contestant-with-crippling-anxiety-disorder-gives-stunning-performance-on-americas-got-talent/ |website=The Mighty |publisher=The Mighty}}</ref>

==Career==
Clendening's debut EP, ''"waves",'' was released on February 22, 2019, on the [[East West Records]] label.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/waves-ep/1449867298 |title=Waves - EP by Anna Clendening |date=February 22, 2019 |publisher=Apple Music |accessdate=April 7, 2019 |via=iTunes}}</ref> In April 2019, Clendening embarked on ''The Waves Tour,'' performing in eight U.S. cities.<ref name="Tour2019">{{cite web |url= https://www.axs.com/anna-clendening-announces-the-waves-tour-2019-137155 |title= Anna Clendening announces The Waves Tour 2019 |last1= Robinson |first1= Dominiq |date= March 4, 2019 |website= axs.com |publisher= AXS Group |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20190304202352/https://www.axs.com/anna-clendening-announces-the-waves-tour-2019-137155 |archive-date= March 4, 2019  |url-status= live |access-date=April 27, 2019}}</ref>

In 2019, Clendening is a member of the roster at Sony/ATV's new joint venture, TwentySeven Music Publishing.<ref name="billboard_190405">{{cite web |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/business/8505822/sonyatv-signs-little-mix-jade-thirlwall-leigh-anne-pinnock-twenty-seven-jv |title=Sony/ATV Signs Little Mix's Jade Thirlwall & Leigh-Anne Pinnock |last=Cirisano |first=Tatiana |date= April 5, 2019 |department=Business News |website= billboard.com |publisher=Billboard Magazine |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20190407203030/https://www.billboard.com/articles/business/8505822/sonyatv-signs-little-mix-jade-thirlwall-leigh-anne-pinnock-twenty-seven-jv |archive-date=April 7, 2019 |url-status=live |access-date=April 7, 2019}}</ref>

She signed the record deal with [[Atlantic Records|Atlantic]] as of June 12, 2020

==Personal life==
Clendening resides in Los Angeles, California.<ref>{{cite web |title=Singer Anna Clendening Talks Cutting, Female Role Model, Girls Staying Strong & Music! |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BiYqmn0LOHY |publisher=PacificRimVideoPress}}</ref>
