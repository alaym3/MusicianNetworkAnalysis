'''Violeta Miljković''' ({{lang-sr-cyr|Виолета Миљковић}}; born 18 December 1974), better known as '''Viki''', is a [[Serbian people|Serbian]] singer and television personality.<ref name="Biography">{{cite news|url=https://www.grand.online/zvezde-granda/jury/viki-miljkovic|title=Viki Miljković - Žiri - Zvezde Granda|website=Grand.online|access-date=9 September 2021|language=sr}}</ref>

== Life and career ==
Born and raised in [[Niš]], Viki started performing as teenager alongside her brother who played [[accordion]]. After getting discovered by song-writing duo [[Marina Tucaković]] and Milan Radulović, she released her debut album, ''Loša sreća'' (1992), under [[Diskos (record label)|Diskos]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.hellomagazin.rs/2017/04/20/viki-miljkovic-surovi-sam-profesionalac-ali-karijera-ne-sme-da-bude-ispred-porodice/|title=Viki Miljković: Surovi sam profesionalac, ali karijera ne sme da bude ispred porodice|author=Tabašević, M.|date=2017-04-20|website=Hellomagazine.rs|language=sr}}</ref> However, it was only the following year that Viki saw significant commercial success with her second release and breakout hit "Nikom nije lepše nego nama". Her music career peaked in the early 2000s with albums ''Mariš li'' (2003) and ''Mahi, mahi'' (2005), released through [[Grand Production]]. In 2006, Viki held her first solo concert in [[Belgrade]].<ref name="Biography"/>

Since 2015 she has appeared on the televised singing competition ''[[Zvezde Granda]]'' as a judge and mentor.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://tracara.com/vatreni-okrsaj-izmedu-karleuse-i-viki-u-emisiji-video/|title=Vatreni okršaj između Karleuše i Viki u emisiji|website=Tracara.rs|date=2016-01-14|language=sr}}</ref>

In 2007, Miljković married to accordion player Dragan Tašković "Taške", with whom she has a son, Andrej, born in October the same year.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.blic.rs/zabava/viki-taske-brak-roditelji/3klztcr|title=Daleko od idiličnog! Odnos Viki Miljković i njenog muža je godinama turbulentan|website=[[Blic (newspaper)|Blic.rs]]|date=September 20, 2020|access-date=9 September 2021|author=T., Lj.|language=sr}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.story.rs/celebrity-news/vesti/nakon-jezive-porodicne-drame-sada-su-srecna-porodica-viki-miljkovic-ovako-cestitala-sinu-rodendan/|title=Nakon jezive porodične drame sada su srećna porodica: Viki Miljković ovako čestitala sinu rođendan|website=Story.rs|date=2018-10-06|language=sr}}</ref> She graduated with a degree in musical arts at the [[Universiteti i Prishtinës Faculty of Arts|Facuty of Arts]], [[University of Pristina]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.hellomagazin.rs/2020/11/11/violeta-viki-miljkovic-ima-zavrsen-ovaj-fakultet/|title=Violeta Viki Miljković ima zavrsen ovaj fakultet|website=Hellomagazine.rs|date=November 11, 2020|access-date=9 September 2021|author=Luković, S.|language=sr}}</ref>
