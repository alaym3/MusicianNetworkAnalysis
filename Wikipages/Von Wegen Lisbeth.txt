'''Von Wegen Lisbeth''' (English: "Because of Lisbeth" or "As if, Lisbeth!") ([[Help:IPA/Standard German|IPA]]: /fɔn veːgən̩ liːzbɛt/) is a German [[Indie pop|indie-pop]] band from [[Berlin]] that was founded in 2006 under the name Fluchtweg. The group sings in German; a special feature is the use of unusual musical instruments such as a children's [[Glockenspiel|ɡlockenspiel]].

== History ==
The band was founded when the members were in seventh grade at the Beethoven-Gymnasium in Berlin, after sports lessons were cancelled due to the teacher having called in sick. They have described their earlier musical style as "from [[hardcore punk]] to 8-bit Gameboy Casio music". After high school, all band members started studying subjects like [[architecture]], [[film music]], [[mathematics]], [[electrical engineering]] and [[art]]. However, they took a break from university in order to focus on their first album and the associated tour.

In 2014, Von Wegen Lisbeth went on tour as the supporting act to [[AnnenMayKantereit]], and in November they released their EP “Und plötzlich der Lachs”. This was followed by 18 more concerts with AnnenMayKantereit at the beginning of 2015, and their first tour as headliner called "Störung im Betriebsablauf" at the end of the year.

After they went on tour with [[Element of Crime]] and again AnnenMayKantereit in early 2016.,<ref>{{cite web|title=Konzerte|periodical=vonwegenlisbeth.de|publisher=Von Wegen Lisbeth|url=https://www.vonwegenlisbeth.de/konzerte/|url-status=|format=|access-date=2020-07-12|archive-url=|archive-date=|last=|date=|year=|language=|pages=|quote=}}</ref> they released their debut album ''Grande'' on July 15, 2016, which was followed by a tour in September and October of the same year. They also played at several music festivals in 2016, such as the [[:de:Appletree Garden Festival|Appletree Garden Festival]], the [[:de:Kosmonaut Festival|Kosmonaut Festival]], [[:de:Rocken am Brocken|Rocken am Brocken]] and the [[Fusion Festival]]. In 2018, they performed at the Campus Festival in Bielefeld<ref>{{cite web|title=Campus Festival Bielefeld · 21-06-2018|periodical=campusfestival-bielefeld.de|publisher=Vibra Agency|url=http://www.campusfestival-bielefeld.de/|url-status=|format=|access-date=2018-07-11|archive-url=|archive-date=|last=|date=|year=|language=|pages=|quote=}}</ref> as well as at the [[Deichbrand]] in Nordholz.

In 2019, the band released their second album ''[[:de:Sweetlilly93@hotmail.com|sweetlilly93@hotmail.com]]''.<ref>{{citation|surname1=Jonas Leppin|periodical=Spiegel Online|title=Deutsche Popmusik: Bernstein über Berlin|date=2019-05-03|language=German|url=https://www.spiegel.de/kultur/musik/von-wegen-lisbeth-neues-album-sweetlilly93-hotmail-com-kritik-a-1265510.html|access-date=2019-05-19
}}</ref> In the summer of 2019, they performed at numerous festivals like the [[:de:Open Air Werden|Open Air Werden]], the [[:de:Taubertal-Festival|Taubertal-Festival]] and others.<ref>{{cite web|title=Impressionen vom Taubertal Festival 2019|periodical=taubertal-festival.de|publisher=KARO Konzert-Agentur Rothenburg GmbH|url=https://taubertal-festival.de/impressionen-2019/|url-status=|format=|access-date=|archive-url=|archive-date=|last=|date=2019|year=|language=|pages=|quote=}}</ref> That same year, they also toured Germany, Austria and Switzerland with their “Britz-California”-tour, their fourth tour as headliners<ref>{{cite web|title=Von Wegen Lisbeth kündigen „Britz-California“-Tour 2019 an|periodical=eventim.de|publisher=CTS EVENTIM AG & Co. KGaA|url=https://www.eventim.de/magazin/rock-pop/von-wegen-lisbeth-kuendigen-britz-california-tour-2019-an/|url-status=|format=|access-date=|archive-url=|archive-date=|last=Felix Goth|date=2018-09-10|year=|language=|pages=|quote=}}</ref>

== Style ==

Their song lyrics are mainly written by the band’s singer Matthias Rohde, who integrates everyday situations and conversations into his songs.

“These are all everyday experiences that you unconsciously pick up. The basic ideas are usually autobiographical, but when developing the lyrics, I add a lot of stuff.”<ref>{{cite web|title=Musik: Von Wegen Lisbeth im Interview|periodical=szenerostock.de|publisher=Rostocker Medienverlag|url=https://szenerostock.de/musik/musik/2016-08-30_von-wegen-lisbeth-im-interview/|url-status=|format=|access-date=2020-07-13|archive-url=|archive-date=|last=Olaf Neumann|date=2016-08-30|year=|language=|pages=|quote=}}</ref>

Characteristic for their musical style are instruments such as an electronic [[Casio]]-Keyboard or a [[Steel Drum]], which the band has partly borrowed or bought from an opera house.
