== Early life ==
Adriana Livia Opri»ô was born in [[Bucharest]], [[Romania]], to a lawyer and an accountant. She lived in the [[Obor]] district under the care of her parents and grandmothers.<ref name=":0" />

Olivia Addams grew up surrounded by music, even though no one in her family was singing. She started singing at the age of about 3 in the Alegretto choir.<ref name=":0" /> As she says herself, "she could sing before she could talk".<ref>{{Cite web|date=10 September 2020|title=Interviu Olivia Addams: "Cred ca inainte sa invat sa vorbesc am invatat sa cant"|url=https://bravonet.ro/interviu-olivia-addams-cred-ca-inainte-sa-invat-sa-vorbesc-am-invatat-sa-cant/|access-date=20 November 2020|website=BRAVO net|language=ro-RO}}</ref>

Already as a child, she performed, among others in Japan, China or the United States.<ref name=":0" />

Addams studied in Germany.<ref name=":0" />
