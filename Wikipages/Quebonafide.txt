'''Kuba Grabowski''' (born 7 July 1991 in Ciechanów), better known by his stage name '''Quebonafide''', is a [[Poland|Polish]] [[Rapping|rapper]], [[Singing|singer]] and [[songwriter]]. Member of the [[Duet|duos]] Yochimu and Taconafide. Founder of the record label QueQuality. With over 350 thousand [[album]]s sold, he is one of Poland's best-selling rappers. His biggest success thus far - the Soma 0.5&nbsp;mg album, recorded with the rapper [[Taco Hemingway]] - has sold more than 150 thousand copies. Also his solo album Egzotyka proved successful, selling over 150 thousand copies. His albums have repeatedly won the status of platinum, gold and diamond records. The rapper has authored such hits as 'Candy', 'Bubbletea' or 'Tamagotchi'. Five-time nominee for the [[Fryderyk]] award, with one victory for Soma 0,5&nbsp;mg<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=http://fryderyki.pl/fryderyk-2019/|access-date=2020-11-29|website=fryderyki.pl|title=Fryderyk 2019 – FRYDERYK 2020}}</ref> in the [[Hip hop|Hip-Hop]] Album of the Year category. In 2018, his album Egzotyka was nominated for [[Empik]] Bestseller award in the Polish Music category.<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|title=ZPAV :: Bestsellery i wyróżnienia - Aktualności|url=http://bestsellery.zpav.pl/aktualnosci.php?idaktualnosci=1605|access-date=2020-11-29|website=bestsellery.zpav.pl}}</ref>

== Education ==
He studied [[philosophy]] at the [[University of Warsaw]].

== Music career ==

=== 2008–2014: Career beginnings ===
Initially, he was a member of the duo Yochimu, together with the rapper and producer Fuso. Since 2008, they have released three albums: Pierwsze cięcie [First Cut], Drugie cięcie [Second Cut] and Flow witam [Welcome Flow].<ref>{{Cite web|date=2012-08-02|title=HiPHOPedia :: e-Ncyklopedia polskiego hip-hopu|url=http://archive.is/YhP8|access-date=2020-11-29|website=archive.is}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=2014-11-30|title=HiPHOPedia :: e-Ncyklopedia polskiego hip-hopu|url=http://archive.is/oivap|access-date=2020-11-29|website=archive.is}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=2014-11-30|title=HiPHOPedia :: e-Ncyklopedia polskiego hip-hopu|url=http://archive.is/cwuYq|access-date=2020-11-29|website=archive.is}}</ref> In 2012, he took part in several freestyle battles. Over time, he captured the attention of the hip-hop community.<ref name=":4">{{Cite web|date=2016-04-11|title=WBW.PL - QUEBONAFIDE|url=http://wbw.pl/zawodnicy/uczestnicy-wbw-freestyle/individual/410-quebonafide|access-date=2020-11-29|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160411081730/http://wbw.pl/zawodnicy/uczestnicy-wbw-freestyle/individual/410-quebonafide|archive-date=11 April 2016}}</ref> He won the Battle for the End of the World and was a finalist in the Grand Battle of Warsaw. He also ranked second in the Raciąż Freestyle Battle and the Battle of September.<ref name=":4" /> In 2013, he earned popularity by releasing Eklektyka [Eclectics] mixtape on 31 July, with the SB Maffija label.<ref name="archive.is">{{Cite web|date=2014-11-30|title=HiPHOPedia :: e-Ncyklopedia polskiego hip-hopu|url=http://archive.is/MTay4|access-date=2020-11-29|website=archive.is}}</ref> The mixtape gave Quebo recognition in the Polish hip-hop community, and translated into invitations to concert tours with SB Maffija. More and more often he was featured as a guest rapper, and eventually on 18 November 2013 he released the single Tarot with the guest appearance of Justyna Kuśmierczyk, followed by the unlicensed 'Album of the Year' released on 31 December 2013 together with the rapper Eripe. The album enjoyed a positive response from the audience.<ref name="archive.is"/> In 2014, Quebonafide became even more popular after appearing on Pawbeats's Utopia, on the track 'Euforia'.<ref name="archive.is"/> The song was broadcast on [[RMF FM|RMF]], [[Eska Rock|Eska]], [[Polskie Radio Szczecin]] and [[Polskie Radio|Polskie Radio Trójka]] radio stations, reached the 3rd position on the RMF Hop Bęc chart.

They also revealed the names of producers and guests on the bonus CD: Bedoes, Kękę, Paluch, Kaz Bałagane, Dawid Podsiadło and Białas. On 4 April, they released another single, 'Metallica 808', and five days later, a single ';Kryptowaluty' [Cryptocurrencies] with a video. On 13 April, the album Soma 0,5&nbsp;mg premiered. A few days later, the bonus material entitled 0,25&nbsp;mg was also made available on streaming services<ref>{{Citation|title=0,25 mg|url=https://open.spotify.com/album/1OSThsZztBE3D9TTG7bm0s|language=en|access-date=2020-11-30}}</ref> and artists' YouTube channels.

On 13 October, during the Quefestival in Katowice, he received a triple platinum certificate for selling over 90 000 copies of Egzotyka.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Kmiecik|first=Mikołaj|date=2018-10-16|title=Znamy wyniki sprzedaży "Egzotyki" Quebonafide|url=https://glamrap.pl/znamy-wyniki-sprzedazy-egzotyki-quebonafide/|access-date=2020-11-30|website=GlamRap.pl|language=pl-PL}}</ref> On 19 October 2019, during the Quefestival in Katowice, he received a diamond status certificate for selling over 150 000 copies of Egzotyka. === Since 2020: Romantic Psycho and an original documentary ===
On 7 February 2020, the first single entitled Romantic Psycho was released, featuring Taco Hemingway, where the rapper comes to terms with the past.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-02-07|title=Quebonafide po 2 latach wraca z nowym singlem. W "ROMANTICPSYCHO" rozlicza się ze swojej przeszłości|url=https://www.spidersweb.pl/rozrywka/2020/02/07/quebonafide-romanticpsycho-spotify-singiel-premiera/|access-date=2020-11-30|website=rozrywka.blog - Cyfrowy styl życia|language=pl-PL}}</ref> On 10 February, another single 'Jesień' [Autumn] was released, accompanied by a video - in a similarly depressive mood, with the guest appearance of [[Natalia Szroeder]]. It also launched the pre-sale of the album Romantic Psycho.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Co oznacza stylówka Quebonafide na okładce i w klipie "Jesień"? {{!}} ...|url=https://www.popkiller.pl/2020-02-11,co-oznacza-stylowka-quebonafide-na-okladce-i-w-klipie-jesien|access-date=2020-11-30|website=popkiller.pl}}</ref> In the video and on the cover of the album, the rapper showed a completely different image - the characteristic tattoos disappeared from his body, and the overall appearance resembled that of his college days. On 15 February 2020, the musician performed on the morning show Dzień Dobry TVN, where he presented the new image. Usually known for his crazy and explosive character, the rapper introduced himself as a quiet and shy guy.<ref>{{Cite web|last=TVN|first=Dzień Dobry|date=2020-02-15|title=Quebonafide w nowej odsłonie: "To jestem dawny ja. I teraźniejszy". Dlaczego raper zmienił wizerunek?|url=https://dziendobry.tvn.pl/a/tylko-u-nas-to-jestem-dawny-ja-i-terazniejszy-quebonafide-w-nowej-odslonie-dlaczego-raper-zmienil-styl|access-date=2020-11-30|website=dziendobry.tvn.pl|language=pl}}</ref> He also announced his tour promoting the album Romantic Psycho Experience, calling it an 'experience' rather than a tour.<ref>{{Cite web|date=21 February 2020|title=Koncert Quebonafide 2020 - kiedy, gdzie się odbędzie i kiedy bilety? [DATA, MIEJSCE] - Radio ZET|url=https://www.radiozet.pl/Co-gdzie-kiedy-jak/Koncert-Quebonafide-2020-kiedy-gdzie-sie-odbedzie-i-kiedy-bilety-DATA-MIEJSCE|access-date=2020-11-30|website=radiozet.pl|language=pl}}</ref> The artist also announced his participation in Ten to One show and an appearance in Kuba Wojewódzki's talk show.<ref>{{Cite web|date=3 April 2020|title=Quebonafide u Kuby Wojewódzkiego - kiedy premiera odcinka z raperem? - Radio ZET|url=https://www.radiozet.pl/Co-gdzie-kiedy-jak/Quebonafide-u-Kuby-Wojewodzkiego-kiedy-premiera-odcinka-z-raperem|access-date=2020-11-30|website=radiozet.pl|language=pl}}</ref> Due to the coronavirus epidemic, the tour as well as the production of shows featuring the rapper were postponed.<ref>{{Cite web|last=4FUN.TV|title=Koronawirus w Polsce: co z koncertami Harry'ego Stylesa i trasą Quebonafide?|url=https://4fun.tv/news/koronawirus-w-polsce-koncerty-imprezy-odwolane|access-date=2020-11-30|website=4FUN.TV - Pierwsza muzyczna telewizja społecznościowa}}</ref> The musician also posted his new image on the Instagram, with comical photos contrasting with his former style.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Quebonafide z Lewandowskimi i bramkarzem Bayernu. Relacja rapera na Instagramie podbija internet|url=https://natemat.pl/300273,co-sie-dzieje-z-quebonafide-raper-pokazal-zdjecia-z-lewandowskimi|access-date=2020-11-30|website=naTemat.pl|language=PL-pl}}</ref>

On 13 March, the third single Przytobie was released, where the rapper criticised Polish stand-up comedians, celebrities and divisions in the Polish hip-hop community.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Quebonafide ostro o obecnym hip-hopie i nie tylko! O czym jest piosenka PRZYTOBIE?|url=https://www.eska.pl/news/quebonafide-piosenka-przy-tobie-o-czym-jest-tekst-znaczenie-quebonafide-2020-aa-Cbcs-nJfx-epti.html|access-date=2020-11-30|website=eska.pl}}</ref> The premiere of the album was originally announced for 20 March, but after a few days Quebo admitted a mistake and announced that the album would be released on 27 March 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Quebonafide się pomylił! Premiera płyty Romantic Psycho przesunięta: 'Gapa ze mnie'|url=https://www.eska.pl/news/quebonafide-sie-pomylil-premiera-plyty-romantic-psycho-przesunieta-gapa-ze-mnie-aa-kMv3-sCQ2-uUJm.html|access-date=2020-11-30|website=eska.pl}}</ref> On 27 March, the album was issued, receiving very negative reviews, while the tracks sounded as if they had not been properly completed.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-03-26|title=Stary nowy Quebonafide! "Romantic Psycho" [RECENZJA]|url=https://skrr.pl/stary-nowy-quebonafide-romantic-psycho-recenzja/|access-date=2020-11-30|website=Skrr.pl {{!}} Dla kultury.|language=pl-PL}}</ref>

On 1 April 2020, a single entitled Szubienicapestycydybroń was released, with a music video in which Quebonafide abandons the shy boy's style and tattoos reappear on his body. The single addresses actor Sebastian Fabijański, among others, and between the second verse and the chorus there is a fragment mocking talk shows. Janusz Chabior, as the host, tried interview Quebo.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-04-01|title=Prima aprilis! Quebo wraca do starego wizerunku z nowym numerem|url=https://noizz.pl/muzyka/quebonafide-wraca-do-starego-wizerunku-mowi-na-co-przeznaczyl-milion/q5mm989|access-date=2020-11-30|website=Noizz|language=pl}}</ref> Thus, it is revealed that the first album was only a limited bonus and the actual album began to reach the audience and appeared on streaming platforms. The album was published in two versions. The first one - Japanese<ref>{{Cite web|last=Hozer|first=Maxymilian|date=2020-04-01|title=Quebonafide "Romantic Psycho (Japan Edition)"|url=https://glamrap.pl/quebonafide-romantic-psycho-japan-edition/|access-date=2020-11-30|website=GlamRap.pl|language=pl-PL}}</ref> - with three additional tracks, available to order from the rapper's website. The second - European - available for purchase in all music shops.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-04-01|title="Romantic Psycho" - obok edycji japońskiej, jest jeszcze europejska. Czym się różnią? - CGM.pl|url=https://www.cgm.pl/news/romantic-psycho-obok-edycji-japonskiej-jest-jeszcze-europejska-czym-sie-roznia/|access-date=2020-11-30|website=CGM|language=pl-PL}}</ref>

Romantic Psycho made its debut on the first place of the OLiS chart, and Quebo scooped top three positions, with Egzotyka in second place and Ezoteryka ranking third. A total of Quebo's five albums made it to the OLiS charts at the same time. Only Taco Hemingway has had more - seven.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Quebonafide z wyjątkowym osiągnięciem na OLiS! {{!}} Popkiller|url=https://www.popkiller.pl/2020-04-16,quebonafide-z-wyjatkowym-osiagnieciem-na-olis|access-date=2020-11-30|website=popkiller.pl}}</ref> Romantic Psycho topped the charts five times, which is a historical result in Polish hip-hop.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Historyczny rekord Quebonafide - żaden inny rapowy album nie wylądował tyle razy na szczycie OLiS|url=https://newonce.net/artykul/historyczny-rekord-quebonafide-zaden-inny-rapowy-album-wyladowal-tyle-razy-na-szczycie-olis|access-date=2020-11-30|website=newonce.net}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Oficjalna lista sprzedaży |publisher= OLIS - Olis miesięczny - Archiwum|url=http://bestsellery.zpav.pl/olis/miesieczny/archiwum.php?year=2020&idlisty=3400#title|access-date=2020-11-30|website=bestsellery.zpav.pl}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=ZPAV :: Bestsellery i wyróżnienia - As part of the promotion campaign, the rapper released a documentary entitled Quebonafide: Romantic Psycho Film, presenting the creative process behind the new album and the backstage of the promotional campaign.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Redakcja|date=2020-08-31|title=Film o Quebonafide: bilety wyprzedane, dodatkowe pokazy|url=https://glamrap.pl/film-o-quebonafide-bilety-wyprzedane-dodatkowe-pokazy/|access-date=2020-11-30|website=GlamRap.pl|language=pl-PL}}</ref> The film was screened in cinemas and tickets sold out in pre-orders.

== Activities outside music ==
On 12 September 2016, he acted as a sports commentator on the [[Canal+]] Sport channel.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Quebonafide komentatorem Canal+ Sport?! {{!}} Popkiller|url=https://www.popkiller.pl/2016-09-12,quebonafide-komentatorem-canal-sport|access-date=2020-11-30|website=popkiller.pl}}</ref> In August 2018, he joined the [[KTS Weszło Warsaw]] football club.<ref>{{Cite web|date=24 August 2018|title=Quebonafide został piłkarzem! Zagrał w barwach KTS Weszło [WIDEO] - Sport|url=https://sport.radiozet.pl/Pilka-nozna/Quebonafide-zostal-pilkarzem-Zagral-w-barwach-KTS-Weszlo-WIDEO|access-date=2020-11-30|website=sport.radiozet.pl|language=pl}}</ref> He appeared in an ad promoting a new clothing campaign of Nike (2017). In spring 2019, together with Kuba Stemplowski, he published a book entitled 'Egzotyka. Wywiad-rzeka', describing the rapper's journey through the 70 countries he visited while working on his album Egzotyka.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Quebonafide, Kuba Stemplowski - Egzotyka. Wywiad-rzeka.|url=https://www.queshop.pl/quequality-2/quebonafide-kuba-stemplowski-egzotyka-wywiad-rzeka|access-date=2020-11-30|website=Queshop - sklep hip-hopowy z odzieżą streetwearową.|language=pl}}</ref>

On 1 April, Quebonafide was a guest of [[Kuba Wojewódzki]] in his show '250m² of Kuba Wojewódzki'. In an interview, Kuba talked about his career break, his life in quarantine and about the album Romantic Psycho. On 3 April, he was a guest of Tomasz Smokowski on the YouTube 'Sports Channel, where he answered questions from the host and from the audience - mainly about his musical activity and passion for sports.

He appeared in the show 'Creeps' of Ciechanowska Telewizja Kablowa (CTK), where he earned 1 million PLN from the [[Allegro (website)|Allegro]] platform. All the income from the campaign and the income from the sale of his clothing collection at allegro.pl was allocated for the purchase of instruments for children from a brass band in Ciechanów, as well as for supporting an animal shelter in Pawłowo, for the purchase of equipment for non-invasive breathing support in newborns at the Specialist Hospital in Ciechanów, for renovation and furnishing of the Society of Children's Friends in Ciechanów, and for supporting the foundation 'Giving strength to children'.
