'''Irina Rimes''' (born 22 August 1991),<ref name="jurnal" /> also known as '''Irina Remesh''' or '''Irra''', is a [[Moldova]]n singer and songwriter, who is one of the four coaches of the Romanian talent show ''[[Vocea României]]''.<ref name="Știrile Pro TV">{{cite web |url=https://stirileprotv.ro/show-buzz/entertainment/irina-rimes-va-fi-antrenor-la-vocea-romaniei-ce-schimbari-mai-urmeaza.html |title=Irina Rimes va fi antrenor la "Vocea României". Ce schimbări mai urmează |work=Știrile Pro TV |date=2 June 2018}}</ref> She works and lives in [[Bucharest]], Romania.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://1music.ro/news/muzica/exclusiv-am-tacut-vreo-cateva-luni-aproape-un-an-ca-sa-am-vocea-asta-irina-rimes/ |title=#Exclusiv – "Am tăcut vreo câteva luni, aproape un an, ca să am vocea asta", Irina Rimes |website=1music.ro|date=27 July 2016}}</ref>

In 2019 the artist was chosen by [[Warner Bros Animation]] to provide the Romanian voice of Whatevra Wanabi in the animated movie [[The Lego Movie 2]]
<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.protv.ro/filme/irina-rimes-este-o-regina-extravaganta-pe-marile-ecrane.html|title=Irina Rimes este o regină extravagantă pe marile ecrane}}</ref>

== Life and career ==
=== 1991–2015: Childhood and beginnings in music ===
Irina was born on 22 August 1991 in the [[Florești District]] and is the first child of Valentina and Fiodor Rîmeș;<ref>{{cite web |url=https://ok.ru/profile/271468756741|title=Irina Rimes' profile |website=Ok.ru|date=22 November 2017}}</ref> she has a younger brother, Vitali.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://ok.ru/vitali.remesh|title=Irina Rimes' brother's profile|website=Ok.ru|date=22 November 2017}}</ref> Irina has stated in interviews that her family nourished her interest for music, especially her father and paternal grandmother, the latter of which had a very good voice, according to Irina — "grandma used to be a part of a church choir and I remember how she would sit on the stove and sing carols to us".<ref>{{cite web |url=http://a1.ro/video/antenastars/star-news/irina-rimes-primul-interviu-alaturi-de-mama-ei-id698634.html|title=Irina Rimes, primul interviu alături de mama ei|website=A1.ro|date=22 November 2017}}</ref> Rimes manifested an interest towards writing and composing from an early age: "I started writing poetry when I was in grade 0. I remember that I learned to sing, write and read at home."<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.click.ro/vedete/romanesti/irina-rimes-cu-mama-de-mana-la-avanpremiera-filmului-despre-albumul-ei-seamana|title=Irina Rimes, cu mama de mâna la avanpremiera filmului despre albumul ei! Seamănă?|website=Click.ro|date=22 November 2017}}</ref> The artist moved to [[Soroca]] when she started studying at the "Constantin Stere" Theoretical High School until 2010; she later graduated from the Academy of Music, Theater and Plastic Arts in Chișinău.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.facebook.com/profile.php?id=100000058664484|title=Irina Rimes' Facebook profile|access-date=22 November 2017}}{{Primary source inline|date=October 2019}}</ref> In 2012, Irina Rimes participated in the talent show ''Fabrica de staruri'', where she was a finalist.<ref name="Mihaela Costache">{{cite web |url=http://bucurestifm.ro/2016/05/23/irina-rimes-un-nou-artist-in-romania/ |title=Irina Rimes un nou artist în România! |work=București FM |author=Mihaela Costache |date=23 May 2016}}</ref>

=== 2016–present: "Visele" and ''Despre el'' ===
Rimes moved to [[Romania]] in 2016, when she signed with Quantum Music. Her first single, "Visele" (''The dreams''), peaked at #1 in Romania Airplay 100 in 2016<ref name="kissfm">[https://api.kissfm.ro/api/podcasturi/download/4319] {{dead link|date=March 2020}}</ref> and got her an award for the best début of the year at the Radio România Music Awards in 2017.<ref name="adevarul">{{cite web |url=http://adevarul.ro/entertainment/muzica/exslusiv-irina-rimes-semnat-cele-mai-importante-case-discuri-franta-presupune-noul-proiect-primele-declaratii-video-1_58ebac425ab6550cb82380c8/index.html |title=Irina Rimes a semnat cu una dintre cele mai importante case de discuri din Franța: ce presupune noul proiect și primele declarații |work=[[Adevărul]] |author=Alexandra Constanda |date=10 April 2017}}</ref> Rimes has also penned songs for various Romanian artists, such as [[Inna]], [[Raluka]], [[Andra (singer)|Andra]], [[Alina Eremia]], [[Nicoleta Nucă]] and [[Antonia Iacobescu|Antonia]].<ref name="adevarul" /><ref>{{cite web |url=http://zutv.ro/muzica/top-4-piese-compuse-de-irina-rimes-pentru-alti-artisti-id27925.html |title=TOP 4 piese compuse de Irina Rimes pentru alți artiști |work=[[ZU TV]]}}</ref> The Romanian branch of ''[[Cosmopolitan (magazine)|Cosmopolitan]]'' named her "the revelation of 2016".<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.cosmopolitan.ro/cosmo-fun/muzica/irina-rimes-revelatia-anului-2016 |title=Irina Rimes, revelația anului 2016 |work=Cosmopolitan |author=Alina Neamțu |date=28 December 2016}}</ref> In 2017, Rimes signed a contract with [[Universal Music Group|Universal Music]] France and released her first album, ''Despre el'' (''About him'').<ref name="adevarul" /> In 2018, she became one of the four coaches of ''[[Vocea României]]''.<ref name="Știrile Pro TV"/>
