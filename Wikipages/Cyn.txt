'''Cynthia Nabozny''' (born May 13, 1993), known professionally as '''Cyn''', is an American singer and songwriter.

== Life and career ==
Cyn was born on May 13, 1993 in [[Michigan]]. By the age of 16 she was recording her own vocals and then doubling over it.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|last=Vitagliano|first=Joe|date=September 18, 2020|title=Cyn Reimagines Debut EP With Follow-Up, "Mood Swing (even moodier)"|url=https://americansongwriter.com/cyn-reimagines-debut-ep-with-follow-up-mood-swing-even-moodier/|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=February 6, 2021|website=American Songwriter|language=en-US}}</ref> After failing to get into [[DePaul University]]'s music program, she graduated from the university in [[Management information system|Management Information Systems]].<ref name=":7">{{Cite web|last=Santino|first=Catherine|date=February 11, 2020|title=Armed with old school charm and midwestern grit, cyn is carving out a new genre within pop|url=http://www.ladygunn.com/music/interviews-music/armed-with-old-school-charm-and-midwestern-grit-cyn-is-carving-out-a-new-genre-within-pop/|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=February 7, 2021|website=LadyGunn|language=en-US}}</ref>

She found a mentor in [[Katy Perry]] and went on to be signed by her record label.<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|last=Kim|first=Soey|date=4 February 2019|title=Meet CYN – Katy Perry's Dark Horse|url=https://www.vogue.co.uk/article/meet-cyn-katy-perrys-dark-horse|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2021-02-06|website=British Vogue|language=en-GB}}</ref><ref name=":8">{{Cite web|last=Cusumano|first=Katherine|date=28 September 2017|title=Premiere: Newcomer Cyn Finally Releases the Song That Made Katy Perry Take Notice (Exclusive)|url=https://www.wmagazine.com/story/cyn-only-with-you-katy-perry-label-exclusive/|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2021-02-06|website=W Magazine|language=en-US}}</ref> The song that had made Perry and her team take note of her was ''Only With You'', which she had written only two months back in September 2015.<ref name=":8" /> It would be another two years before ''Only With You'' was released as a single.<ref name=":8" /> Her debut single under Unsub was ''Together'' released in July 2017.<ref name=":3">{{Cite web|last=Molina|first=Jasely|date=July 13, 2017|title=CYN’s "Together" Is A Poppy Breath Of Fresh Air|url=https://www.thefader.com/2017/07/13/cyn-together-unsub-premiere|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=February 6, 2021|website=The Fader|language=en}}</ref> She also opened for Perry's [[Witness: The Tour]] on the [[Mexico]] dates.

Her debut album "Mood Swing" released in September 2019.<ref name=":4">{{Cite web|date=September 20, 2019|title=CYN Releases Debut EP "Mood Swing" Today|url=https://www.elicitmagazine.com/cyn-mood-swing/|access-date=February 6, 2021|website=Elicit Magazine|language=en-US}}</ref> The seven track album includes production by [[Lars Stalfors]] and Matias Mora.<ref name=":4" /> Cyn describes the album as a "Parisian park" and that most of the songs in that album, including lyrics and melody, are built around a single sentence, the "thesis statement".<ref name=":0" /> Following this, Cyn then came out with a reimagined version of her debut album titled "Mood Swing (even moodier)" in September 2020.<ref name=":1" /> Her singles ''Drinks'' and ''Uh-Oh'' were written and recorded for the film ''[[Promising Young Woman]].<ref name=":6">{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=2020-11-12|title=Cyn - "Uh Oh"|url=https://www.the360mag.com/cyn-uh-oh/|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2021-02-06|website=360 MAGAZINE|language=en-US}}</ref>'' The single ''Moment of Truth'' was part of the animated film [[Smallfoot (film)|''Smallfoot'']].<ref name=":2" /> Her latest single ''New York'' came out in October 2020.<ref name=":5">{{Cite web|last=Restaino|first=Amanda|date=October 20, 2020|title=Cyn's "New York"|url=https://www.beyondthestagemagazine.com/cyn-new-york-beyond-the-stage/|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=February 6, 2021|website=Beyond The Stage Magazine|language=en-US}}</ref>

Her single ''Lonely Gun'' was part of the [[Birds of Prey (soundtrack)|''Birds of Prey'']] soundtrack,<ref>{{Cite web|last=Marotta|first=Michael|date=2020-02-07|title=Cyn delivers thunder to the 'Birds of Prey' soundtrack with 'Lonely Gun'|url=https://vanyaland.com/2020/02/07/cyn-delivers-thunder-to-the-birds-of-prey-soundtrack-with-lonely-gun/|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2021-02-07|website=Vanyaland|language=en-US}}</ref> while ''I Can't Believe'' was part of the soundtrack of ''[[To All the Boys: P.S. I Still Love You (Music from the Netflix Film)|To All the Boys: P.S. I Still Love You]].''<ref>{{Cite web|last=Frometa|first=RJ|date=February 19, 2020|title=Cyn Unveils Music Video For ‘I Can’t Believe.’|url=https://ventsmagazine.com/2020/02/19/cyn-unveils-music-video-for-i-cant-believe/|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=February 7, 2021|website=Vents Magazine|language=en-US}}</ref>

On February 17, 2021, a son, Etienne Noel Newman, was born to Cyn and director and author [[Kyle Newman]].

In August 2021, Cyn recorded a cover version of [[Sixpence None the Richer]]'s "[[Kiss Me (Sixpence None the Richer song)|Kiss Me]]" for the [[Netflix]] film soundtrack of ''[[He's All That]]'' (2021).<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/movies/9610453/cyn-sixpence-none-the-richer-kiss-me-cover-hes-all-that-netflix-film|title=Cyn Covers Sixpence None the Richer's 'Kiss Me' for 'He's All That' Soundtrack: Listen|publisher=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|first=Heran|last=Mamo|date=August 4, 2021|access-date=August 5, 2021}}</ref>

== Artistry and influences ==
One of the earliest influences Cyn remembers is listening to her mother playing "[[Foolish Games]]" by [[Jewel (singer)|Jewel]] when she was three or four.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|last=Klonowski|first=Lauren|date=5 February 2020|title=Cyn: Not Your Average Pop Artist|url=https://www.beyondthestagemagazine.com/cyn-not-average-pop-artist/|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=February 6, 2021|website=Beyond The Stage Magazine|pages=6–9|language=en-US}}</ref> Her grandmother would listen to [[Doris Day]].<ref name=":7" /> Having been born in Detroit, [[Motown (music style)|Motown]] inspired her a lot.<ref name=":0" /> [[Sheryl Crow]] and [[Rosalía (singer)|Rosalita]] are some of her favorites.<ref name=":0" /> [[Lorde]]'s influence made her realize that she could create an entirely new persona to go along with her stage name.<ref name=":1" /> A new phase of inspiration came from [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]] classics such as ''[[Sleeping Beauty (1959 film)|Sleeping Beauty]].<ref name=":1" />''
