'''Girls' Generation-TTS''' ({{ko-hhrm|소녀시대-태티서}}, also known as '''TTS''', '''TaeTiSeo''', or '''Girls' Generation-TaeTiSeo''') is the first sub-unit of South Korean girl group [[Girls' Generation]], formed by [[SM Entertainment]] in 2012. It is composed of three Girls' Generation members: [[Kim Tae-yeon|Taeyeon]], [[Tiffany Young|Tiffany]], and [[Seohyun]]. The sub-group has released three extended plays: ''[[Twinkle (EP)|Twinkle]]'', ''[[Holler (EP)|Holler]]'', and ''[[Dear Santa (EP)|Dear Santa]]''.

==History==
During the year 2012, South Korean girl group [[Girls' Generation]] went through an unannounced hiatus as the majority of the group's members were focusing on individual activities. Some acted in television dramas while others participated in various television programs. The subgroup TTS was born because members Taeyeon, Tiffany, and Seohyun wanted to carry on with their music activities. All three were hosts of the weekly music program ''[[Show! Music Core]]'' at the time.<ref>{{cite episode|title=Kiss The Radio - May 4, 2012|series=[[Kiss the Radio]]|network=[[KBS Cool FM]] |date=May 4, 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/7073/seohyun-reveals-which-snsd-member-she-would-pick-to-join-taetiseo|title=Seohyun Reveals Which SNSD Member She Would Pick to Join Taetiseo|date=May 23, 2012|access-date=March 20, 2016|publisher=eNewsWorld|first=Stewart|last=Ho|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150717040547/http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/7073/seohyun-reveals-which-snsd-member-she-would-pick-to-join-taetiseo|archive-date=July 17, 2015|url-status=dead}}</ref> On April 19, 2012, SM Entertainment officially announced the formation of the subgroup and hinted at the possibility of other future subgroups, explaining, "the members of the unit [are rotated] according to the [unique] music and concept of each new subgroup album."<ref name="daum">[http://media.daum.net/entertain/enews/view?cateid=1033&newsid=20120419083904397 Subgroup "TTS", formed by Girls' Generation's Taeyeon, Tiffany & Seohyun, debuts in May (in Korean)] Daum.net (April 19, 2012). Retrieved on April 20, 2012.</ref> Compared to Girls' Generation, TTS's main focus is on each member's vocal ability while the original group places more emphasis on the overall songs and uniform group performances.<ref name=focus>{{cite journal |date=August 2014 |title=Tiffany's Interview|url=http://mnbmagazine.joins.com/magazine/Narticle.asp?magazine=202&articleId=GRT736ZPRWXXEF|journal=Korea InStyle Magazine }}</ref> The name "TaeTiSeo" or "TTS", is made up of the first syllables of Taeyeon, Tiffany, and Seohyun.<ref>{{cite web|title=Guerilla Date with TTS (Entertainment Weekly / 2014.10.11)|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OpBzP4g661M|website=[[KBS World (TV channel)|KBS World]] on YouTube|publisher=Google Inc|access-date=March 18, 2016|format=Video upload|date=October 11, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/6382/taetiseo-twinkles-in-live-wide-entertainment-news-broadcast|title=Taetiseo Twinkles in Live 'Wide Entertainment News' Broadcast|date=May 8, 2012|access-date=March 20, 2016|publisher=eNewsWorld|first=Erika|last=Kim|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160402142804/http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/6382/taetiseo-twinkles-in-live-wide-entertainment-news-broadcast|archive-date=April 2, 2016|url-status=dead}}</ref>

Their debut EP, ''[[Twinkle (EP)|Twinkle]]'', was released on April 29, 2012. It was the first album by a Korean artist to rank number 1 on the ''Billboard'' World Albums chart.<ref name=billboard09-2014>{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/k-town/6266529/girls-generation-tts-holler-2nd-consecutive-no-1-world-albums|title=Girls' Generation-TTS Earns 2nd Consecutive No. 1 on World Albums Chart|first=Jeff|last=Benjamin|work=Billboard|publisher=Prometheus Global Media|date=September 26, 2014|access-date=September 26, 2014}}</ref> At the time, it was also the highest charting K-pop album on the [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]], reaching number 126.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/489102/girls-generation-splinter-group-enters-billboard-200|title=Girls' Generation Splinter Group Enters Billboard 200|first=Jeff|last=Benjamin|work=Billboard|publisher=Prometheus Global Media|date=May 4, 2012|access-date=August 1, 2014}}</ref> The title track of the same name sold 2,520,485 digital copies in South Korea as of July 2014,<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.gaonchart.co.kr/chart/download.php?f_chart_kind_cd=T&nationGbn=&f_month=3&f_year=2012&f_type=year|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140716100633/http://www.gaonchart.co.kr/chart/download.php?f_chart_kind_cd=T&nationGbn=&f_month=3&f_year=2012&f_type=year|title=South Korea's Gaon chart's overall download sales of 2012|language=ko|work=Gaon Digital Chart|archive-date=July 16, 2014|access-date=July 23, 2014}}</ref> and the album has sold 155,521 copies in South Korea.<ref name=twinkle>Cumulative sales for "Twinkle":
