'''Hannah Cohen''' (born October 25, 1986) is an American singer and model. She has released three albums and toured internationally.<ref name=":0" /><ref>[http://www.7digital.com/features/interviews/hannah-cohen Interview] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141111203028/http://www.7digital.com/features/interviews/hannah-cohen |date=2014-11-11 }} for 7Digital, accessed November 11, 2014.</ref>

==Family background==
Hannah Cohen was born in San Francisco and comes from a family of musicians and booksellers. Her grandfather, [[Bertie Rodgers]], was a poet.<ref name="Bertie Rodgers">{{cite web|title=WR Rodgers|url=http://www.culturenorthernireland.org/article.aspx?art_id=579|publisher=Culture Northern Ireland|access-date=2 March 2012|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120224043050/http://www.culturenorthernireland.org/article.aspx?art_id=579|archive-date=24 February 2012|df=}}</ref> Her father is the Jazz drummer Myron Cohen.

Hannah left home as a teenager, soon finding herself in New York and becoming something of a muse to the city's art scene, posing for [[Richard Prince]], [[Terry Richardson]], [[David Salle]], [[Will Cotton]],<ref name="Will Cotton">{{cite web|last=Prose|first=Francine|title=Will Cotton's Food Art: Buche de Noel|url=http://www.foodandwine.com/articles/will-cottons-food-art-buche-de-noel|publisher=Food & Wine|accessdate=2 March 2012}}</ref>  and [[Ryan McGinley]].

Hannah immersed herself in New York's music scene, working at the [[Village Vanguard]].<ref name=":0">{{cite web|first=Alex|title=Introducing Hannah Cohen|url=http://www.folkradio.co.uk/2012/01/introducing-hannah-cohen/|publisher=Folk Radio UK|accessdate=2 March 2012|last = Gallacher|website = Folkradio}}</ref>

== Music career ==
===Child Bride===
Hannah's debut record, ''Child Bride'', was produced by Thomas Bartlett, aka [[Doveman]], known for his keyboard work with artists like [[The National (band)|The National]] and [[Antony and the Johnsons]].<ref>{{Cite web|url = http://www.thelineofbestfit.com/reviews/albums/hannah-cohen-child-bride-89704|title = Hannah Cohen - Child Bride|date = April 25, 2014|accessdate = November 11, 2014|website = The Line Best Fit|publisher = |last = Conner|first = Matt}}</ref> Drawn from Hannah and Thomas's mutual friends, the core band is a small group of New York musicians, including [[Sam Amidon]], [[Rob Moose]], Brad Albetta, Doug Wieselman and Kenny Wollesen. Their sessions were recorded by engineer [[Patrick Dillett]]. ''Child Bride'' was released by [[Bella Union]] on April 23, 2012.<ref name="Daytrotter">{{cite web|last=Moeller|first=Sean|title=Cold Smoke Finds Loves Losses|url=http://www.daytrotter.com/#!/concert/hannah-cohen/20055833-37383093|work=Daytrotter.com|publisher=|accessdate=June 21, 2012}}</ref>

===Pleasure Boy===
A second album, ''Pleasure Boy'' was released March 2015.

===Welcome Home===
A third album, ''Welcome Home'' was released April 2019.
