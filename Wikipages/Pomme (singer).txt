'''Claire Pommet''' ({{IPA-fr|klɛʁ pɔmɛ}}; born August 2, 1996), known professionally as '''Pomme''' ({{IPA-fr|pɔm|lang}}), is a French singer, songwriter and musician.

== Biography ==
Claire Pommet grew up in the [[Lyon]] area. She learned [[music theory]] from the age of 6, joined a children's choir, La Cigale de Lyon, at age 7, and learned to play the cello at age 8.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://www.leparisien.fr/culture-loisirs/musique/concert-on-craque-pour-pomme-13-01-2019-7987392.php|title=Concert: on craque pour Pomme|last=Bonnaud|first=Maguelone|date=January 13, 2019|website=[[Le Parisien]]|language=fr-FR|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190329234059/http://www.leparisien.fr/culture-loisirs/musique/concert-on-craque-pour-pomme-13-01-2019-7987392.php|archive-date=March 29, 2019|access-date=2019-11-22}}</ref> Her mother plays the flute while her father, a real estate agent, listened to [[Michel Polnareff]], [[Serge Reggiani]], and [[Charles Aznavour]]. One of her father's friend introduced her to American folk and country music.<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=https://next.liberation.fr/musique/2017/10/09/pomme-le-fruit-detendu_1601926|title=Pomme, le fruit détendu|last=Renault|first=Gilles|date=October 9, 2017|website=[[Libération]]|language=fr|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190730052159/https://next.liberation.fr/musique/2017/10/09/pomme-le-fruit-detendu_1601926|archive-date=July 30, 2019|access-date=2019-11-22}}</ref> She is self-taught and publishes videos on the [[YouTube]] web platform.

In September 2017, at the age of 21, she performed for the first time at La Boule Noire in [[Paris]]. In October she released her first [[Chanson|chanson française]] album, titled ''[[À peu près]]''; it was described as a mixture of pop and folk by Salome Rouzerol-Douglas in ''[[Le Figaro]]''. It was "very encouraging" according to Gilles Renault in ''[[Libération]]'', while Marie-Catherine Mardi of [[Radio France Internationale|RFI]] said that "the lyrics do not convince [in their entirety]" but praised Pommet's voice.<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1" /><ref name=":2">{{Cite web|url=https://www.lefigaro.fr/musique/2018/02/24/03006-20180224ARTFIG00032-la-voix-est-libre-pour-la-chanteuse-pomme.php|title=La voix est libre pour la chanteuse Pomme|last=Rouzerol-Douglas|first=Salomé|date=February 24, 2018|website=[[Le Figaro]]|language=fr|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190502190909/https://www.lefigaro.fr/musique/2018/02/24/03006-20180224ARTFIG00032-la-voix-est-libre-pour-la-chanteuse-pomme.php|archive-date=May 2, 2019|access-date=2019-11-22}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://rocknfool.net/2017/09/19/pomme-nord-boule-noire/|title=Onirisme et grands frissons avec Pomme à la Boule Noire|last=|first=|date=September 19, 2017|website=RockNfool|language=fr-FR|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190330005818/http://rocknfool.net/2017/09/19/pomme-nord-boule-noire/|archive-date=March 30, 2019|access-date=2019-11-22}}</ref><ref name=":3">{{Cite web|url=https://musique.rfi.fr/chanson-francophone/20171212-pomme-peu-pres|title=L’ascension soyeuse de Pomme|last=Mardi|first=Marie-Catherine|date=December 12, 2017|website=[[Radio France Internationale|RFI Musique]]|language=fr|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190329234239/https://musique.rfi.fr/chanson-francophone/20171212-pomme-peu-pres|archive-date=March 29, 2019|access-date=2019-11-22}}</ref> The journalists of ''Libération'' and ''Le Figaro'' emphasize the quality of her live performances, during which she particularly plays the [[autoharp]] and the [[guitar]].

Pomme performed in the first part of the [[Asaf Avidan]] tour in autumn 2017.<ref name=":3" /> In February 2018, she performed at the Café de la Danse in Paris, after having performed as an opening act for [[Louane (singer)|Louane]] and [[Vianney (singer)|Vianney]],<ref name=":2" /> then took the stage in [[La Cigale]] in mid-2018 and in La Trianon in early 2019.<ref name=":4">{{Cite web|url=https://www.telerama.fr/musique/pomme-ado,-jaurais-aime-pouvoir-me-reconnaitre-dans-des-chanteuses-lesbiennes,n6098042.php|title=Pomme: "Ado, j’aurais aimé pouvoir me reconnaître dans des chanteuses lesbiennes"|last=Lehoux|first=Valérie|date=January 28, 2019|website=[[Télérama]]|language=fr|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190921060605/https://www.telerama.fr/musique/pomme-ado,-jaurais-aime-pouvoir-me-reconnaitre-dans-des-chanteuses-lesbiennes,n6098042.php|archive-date=September 21, 2019|access-date=2019-11-22}}</ref>

The lyrics she writes often evoke themes of love, death, and "everyday situations that resort to romanticism".<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":2" /> Love, in her lyrics, is not only heterosexual, but also bisexual or homosexual; she dedicates a song to Quebec singer [[Safia Nolin]], who used to be her partner.<ref name=":0" /> She explained to ''[[Télérama]]'': "I am naturally comfortable with my homosexuality, for example, using female pronouns in my songs. And I think it's important, given the amount of thank you messages I get. As a teenager, I would have also liked to recognize myself in lesbian singers".<ref name=":4" />

In 2020, she won the [[Victoires de la Musique]] award for ''album révélation'' ("''newcomer album''") of the year with her second studio album ''[[Les failles]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=2020-02-14|title=Victoires de la musique : Les Failles de Pomme, album révélation|url=https://www.lefigaro.fr/culture/victoires-de-la-musique-les-failles-de-pomme-album-revelation-20200215|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200220014506/https://www.lefigaro.fr/culture/victoires-de-la-musique-les-failles-de-pomme-album-revelation-20200215|archive-date=2020-02-20|access-date=2020-06-22|website=[[Le Figaro]]|language=fr}}</ref> In 2021, she was crowned ''artiste féminine'' ("''female artist''") of the year.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-02-13|title=Victoires de la Musique : Pomme sacrée meilleure artiste féminine de l'année|url=https://www.lefigaro.fr/musique/victoires-de-la-musique-pomme-sacree-meilleure-artiste-feminine-20210213|url-status=live|access-date=2021-07-12|website=[[Le Figaro]]|language=fr}}</ref>
