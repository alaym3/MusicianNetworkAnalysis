'''Francesca Michielin''' ({{IPA-it|franˈtʃeska mikjeˈlin}}; born 25 February 1995) is an [[Italians|Italian]] singer and songwriter. She rose to fame after winning the [[X Factor (Italian season 5)|fifth season]] of the Italian [[talent show]] ''[[X Factor (Italian TV series)|X Factor]]''. Her first single, "[[Distratto]]", debuted atop the Italian [[Italian Singles Chart|FIMI Top Digital Downloads]] chart and was certified double platinum by the [[Federation of the Italian Music Industry]]. Michielin's debut studio album, ''[[Riflessi di me]]'', was released in October 2012, preceded by the single "[[Sola (Francesca Michielin song)|Sola]]".

During the following years, she released the albums ''[[di20]]'' (2015) and ''[[2640 (album)|2640]]'' (2018), both certified gold by the Federation of the Italian Music Industry, and several hit singles, including "[[L'amore esiste]]" (2015), "[[Nessun grado di separazione]]" (2016), "[[Vulcano (song)|Vulcano]]" (2018), "[[Io non abito al mare]]" (2018) and "[[Cheyenne (Francesca Michielin song)|Cheyenne]]" (2019). 

She recorded three commercially successful singles with Italian rapper [[Fedez]]—"[[Cigno nero]]" (2013), "[[Magnifico (song)|Magnifico]]" (2014) and "[[Chiamami per nome]]" (2021)—and she released collaborations with several artists spanning different genres, including rappers [[Fabri Fibra]] and [[Don Joe]], [[indie pop]] acts Giorgio Poi, [[Coma Cose]], Vasco Brondi and [[Carl Brave]], rock brand [[Måneskin]], and [[pop music|pop]] singers [[Elisa (Italian singer)|Elisa]] and [[Max Gazzè]]. Her fourth studio album, ''[[Feat (stato di natura)]]'', was conceived as a collection of previously unreleased collaborations.

During her career, Michielin competed twice in the [[Sanremo Music Festival]], placing second both in [[Sanremo Music Festival 2016|2016]] with "Nessun grado di separazione" and in [[Sanremo Music Festival 2021|2021]] with "Chiamami per nome", performed with Fedez. With "Nessun grado di separazione", she went on representing [[Italy in the Eurovision Song Contest 2016]].

==Music career==

===2011–2012: ''X Factor'' and ''Riflessi di me''===

===2015–2016: ''di20'', Sanremo Music Festival and Eurovision Song Contest===

In April 2014, Michielin confirmed she was working on new material.<ref name=RockolApril2014/> The single "[[L'amore esiste]]", produced by [[Fortunato Zampaglione]] and [[Michele Canova]], was released on 6 March 2015, ahead of her second studio album, ''[[di20]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ansa.it/sito/notizie/cultura/musica/2015/03/05/nuovo-album-per-francesca-michielin_95d8d0e2-5cf0-4387-8beb-d8763acf386b.html |publisher=[[Agenzia Nazionale Stampa Associata]] |date=5 March 2015 |title=Nuovo album per Francesca Michielin |archive-date=23 March 2015 |archive-url=https://www.webcitation.org/6XFlTnWFM?url=http://www.ansa.it/sito/notizie/cultura/musica/2015/03/05/nuovo-album-per-francesca-michielin_95d8d0e2-5cf0-4387-8beb-d8763acf386b.html |url-status=dead }}</ref> The song achieved commercial success, entering the top ten of the Italian [[FIMI]] Top Digital chart, and receiving the Lunezia Pop Award.<ref name="FIMISingle"/><ref name=Lunezia2015/>
"L'amore esiste" was followed by "[[Battito di ciglia]]", released on 10 July 2015, and "[[Lontano (Francesca Michielin song)|Lontano]]", released on 25 September 2015.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.rockol.it/news-645229/francesca-michielin-battito-di-ciglia---nuovo-singolo-disco-settembre|publisher=Rockol.it|access-date=20 July 2015|title=Francesca Michielin, esce il singolo Battito di ciglia e a settembre il nuovo album|date=10 July 2015}}</ref>

In February 2016, she competed in the [[Sanremo Music Festival 2016|66th Sanremo Music Festival]], performing the song "[[Nessun grado di separazione]]".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.lastampa.it/2015/12/13/spettacoli/chi-sono-i-big-del-festival-di-sanremo-ThPJABJbzRAWdgO7bNuepK/pagina.html|work=[[La Stampa]]|language=it|date=13 December 2015|title=Chi sono i 20 big del Festival di Sanremo 2016|archive-date=14 December 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151214214237/http://www.lastampa.it/2015/12/13/spettacoli/chi-sono-i-big-del-festival-di-sanremo-ThPJABJbzRAWdgO7bNuepK/pagina.html|access-date=14 December 2015}}</ref> The song placed second in the competition, behind [[Stadio]]'s "[[Un giorno mi dirai]]".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.repubblica.it/speciali/sanremo/edizione2016/2016/02/13/news/sanremo_serata_finale_vincitore-133365775/?ref=HRER3-1|work=[[la Repubblica]]|language=it|date=14 February 2016|title=Sanremo, trionfo Stadio, seconda Michielin, terzi Caccamo&Iurato|author=Alessandra Vitale|access-date=14 February 2016}}</ref> The band was then eligible to represent Italy in the [[Eurovision Song Contest 2016]], but they declined, stating they would be on a previously planned tour in May 2016. ===2017–2018: ''2640''===
In January 2017, Michielin announced through her [[Twitter]] account that she had started working in the studio on her third album, the title of which was later announced as ''[[2640 (album)|2640]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.radioitalia.it/news/francesca_michielin/evento/13820_francesca_michielin_inizia_i_lavori_sul_nuovo_album_%E2%80%9Csono_felice%E2%80%9D.php|publisher=[[Radio Italia Solo Musica Italiana]]|language=it|access-date=29 January 2017|title=Francesca Michielin inizia i lavori sul nuovo album: sono felice|date=28 January 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.radiogelosa.it/12-gennaio-2018-esce-album-francesca-michielin/ |title=12 Gennaio 2018: Esce Il Nuovo ALbum Di Francesca Michielin |work=Radio Gelosa |language=it |date=5 December 2017 |access-date=15 December 2017}}</ref> The album was recorded in [[Los Angeles]] with producer [[Michele Canova]].<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|url=https://www.radioitalia.it/news/francesca_michielin/nuova_uscita/15570_francesca_michielin_annuncia_la_data_d_uscita_del_suo_nuovo_album.php|title=Francesca Michielin annuncia la data d'uscita del suo nuovo album|website=Radio Italia|access-date=2019-02-12}}</ref> 
The album's lead single "Vulcano" was released on 21 July 2017,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.radioitalia.it/news/francesca_michielin/nuova_uscita/15110_francesca_michielin_e_un_%E2%80%9Cvulcano%E2%80%9D._svelato_il_suo_nuovo_singolo.php|publisher=[[Radio Italia Solo Musica Italiana]]|language=it|access-date=15 July 2017|title=Francesca Michielin è un vulcano. === 2019–present: ''FEAT (Stato di Natura)'' ===
Michelin began teasing her musical return on her social media accounts on 8 November 2019,<ref>{{cite tweet |user=francescacheeks |number=1192758662571233280 |date=2019-11-08|title=postare con copy prova 1 |access-date=2020-02-17}}</ref> culminating in the announcement of the single "Cheyenne" featuring [[Charlie Charles]] on 11 November 2019.<ref name=":4">{{Cite web|url=https://www.radioitalia.it/news/francesca_michielin/nuova_uscita/20166_cheyenne_il_nuovo_singolo_di_francesca_michielin_con_charlie_charles.php|title=Cheyenne: il nuovo singolo di Francesca Michielin con Charlie Charles|website=Radio Italia|access-date=2020-02-17}}</ref><ref>{{cite tweet |user=francescacheeks |number=1193966645829652481 |date=2019-11-11|title=CHEYENNE feat Charlie Charles dal 15 novembre il mio nuovo singolo in tutti i digital store. |access-date=2020-02-17}}</ref> On 15 November 2019, "Cheyenne" was released as the lead single from Michielin's fourth studio album.<ref>{{Citation|title=Cheyenne - Single by Francesca Michielin & Charlie Charles|url=https://music.apple.com/us/album/cheyenne-single/1487156162|language=en-us|access-date=2020-02-17}}</ref> It was written by [[Alessandro Raina]], Davide Simonetta, [[Mahmood (singer)|Mahmood]] and Charlie Charles, the latter of which also produced the song. It is a romantic and nostalgic song that focuses on the melancholy of memory and the illusion of having experienced something great.<ref name=":3">{{Cite web|url=https://tg24.sky.it/spettacolo/musica/2020/02/17/francesca-michielin-album-feat.html|title=Francesca Michielin, il nuovo album è "Feat"|last=Cinema|first=Sky|date=2020-02-17|website=tg24.sky.it|access-date=2020-02-17}}</ref> Of Charles' production, Michielin said "he knew how to produce for the nature of the song: without superstructures, getting straight to the heart like an arrow".<ref name=":4" /> An accompanying music video for "Cheyenne" was released on 21 November 2019, it was directed by Jacopo Farina.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.soundsblog.it/post/582333/francesca-michielin-charlie-charles-cheyenne-testo-video-significato-canzone|title=Francesca Michielin feat. Charlie Charles, Cheyenne: testo e video ufficiale|date=2019-11-22|website=Soundsblog.it|access-date=2020-02-17}}</ref> On 13 November 2019, Michielin announced a concert at Carroponte in Milan on 20 September 2020, which she described as a "super party".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.radioitalia.it/news/francesca_michielin/intervista/20192_francesca_michielin_presenta_a_radio_italia_il_nuovo_singolo_cheyenne.php|title=Francesca Michielin presenta a Radio Italia il nuovo singolo Cheyenne|website=Radio Italia|access-date=2020-02-17}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.onstageweb.com/notizie/francesca-michielin-prezzi-biglietti-milano-carroponte-2020/|title=Francesca Michielin in concerto al Carroponte di Milano|date=2019-11-13|website=Onstage|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-02-17}}</ref>

On 6 February 2020, Michielin performed a cover of [[Gianni Morandi]], [[Enrico Ruggeri]] & [[Umberto Tozzi]]'s "Si può dare di più" with [[Levante (singer)|Levante]] and Maria Antonietta for the third night of the [[Sanremo Music Festival 2020]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://tg24.sky.it/spettacolo/musica/2020/02/06/tozzi-ruggeri-morandi-si-puo-dare-di-piu-testo.html|title=Sanremo 2020, Levante canta Si può dare di più: il testo|last=Cinema|first=Sky|date=2020-02-06|website=tg24.sky.it|access-date=2020-02-17}}</ref> On 12 February 2020, Michielin announced that her fourth album ''FEAT (Stato di Natura)'' would be released on 12 March 2020.<ref>{{cite tweet |user=francescacheeks |number=1227683796612349952 |date=2020-02-12|title=FEAT fuori il 13 marzo. |access-date=2020-02-17}}</ref> Michielin also revealed that the album would include 11 tracks, all of which featured other artists, with the main theme being the contrast and union between urban and natural environments. On the following day, Michielin announced that the album would be preceded by three concerts, each with a different location, theme and arrangement, to take place on 20 February, 27 February and 5 March 2020 in Milan. A new song from ''[[Feat (stato di natura)]]'' was premiered at each concert and released on the following day. It was announced at the same time that the first concert would take place at the Rocket [[nightclub]], with a vintage electro theme and the premiere of the song "[[Gange]]", featuring the Italian rapper [[Shiva (rapper)|Shiva]].<ref name=":3" /> "Gange" was released on the following day, 21 February, with the announcement that the second concert would take place at the Serraglio nightclub, with an urban orchestral theme and the premiere of the song "Riserva naturale", featuring Italian duo [[Coma Cose]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rockol.it/news-711666/francesca-michielin-gange-nuovo-singolo-feat-shiva-ascolta|title=√ Francesca Michielin costruisce dal vivo il suo nuovo album. Ascolta|last=s.r.l|first=Rockol com|website=Rockol|language=it|access-date=2020-02-24}}</ref> On 24 February, the event was cancelled in accordance with measures intended to prevent the spread of the [[Coronavirus disease 2019|coronavirus]] in [[Lombardy]],<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.vivoconcerti.com/mag/annullato-il-concerto-al-serraglio-718306|title=annullato il concerto al Serraglio - Vivo Concerti News|website=Vivo Concerti Official Website|language=en|access-date=2020-02-24}}</ref> with Michielin later announcing that the concert would be [[Live streaming|live-streamed]] on [[Facebook]] instead.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.corriere.it/tecnologia/20_febbraio_27/coronavirus-non-ferma-francesca-michielin-concerto-spostato-facebook-diretta-gratuita-da03588e-5946-11ea-af71-899699a3d6d8.shtml|title=Il coronavirus non ferma Francesca Michielin: concerto spostato su Facebook in diretta gratuita|last=Vinci|first=Alessandro|date=2020-02-27|website=Corriere della Sera|language=it|access-date=2020-02-27}}</ref> The third concert was broadcast on [[RaiPlay]] on 5 March 2020 and included the live debut of the song "Monolocale" featuring [[Fabri Fibra]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.lopinionista.it/michielin-con-fabri-fibra-a-milano-multietnica-set-concerto-su-raiplay-53008.html|title=Michielin con Fabri Fibra a Milano Multietnica Set, concerto su Raiplay|last=Redazione|date=2020-03-05|website=L'Opinionista|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-03-15}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.radioitalia.it/news/francesca_michielin/nuova_uscita/20795_il_nuovo_feat_di_francesca_michielin_e_monolocale_con_fabri_fibra.php|title=Il nuovo Feat di Francesca Michielin è Monolocale con Fabri Fibra|website=Radio Italia|access-date=2020-03-15}}</ref> The tracklist of ''[[Feat (stato di natura)]]'' was announced on 12 March 2020, one day before the album's release.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://tg24.sky.it/spettacolo/musica/2020/03/13/francesca-michielin-feat-album.html|title=Francesca Michielin, esce l'album Feat: le collaborazioni|last=Cinema|first=Sky|date=2020-03-13|website=tg24.sky.it|access-date=2020-03-15}}</ref>

On 13 March 2020, Michielin's fourth studio album, ''[[Feat (stato di natura)]]'', was released. The album debuted and peaked at number 7 in Italy.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://italiancharts.com/showitem.asp?interpret=Francesca+Michielin&titel=Feat+-+stato+di+natura&cat=a|title=italiancharts.com - Francesca Michielin - Feat - Stato di natura|website=italiancharts.com|access-date=2020-04-09}}</ref> On the same day, the song "Stato di natura" featuring [[Måneskin]] was released as the album's second single.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.earone.it/news/francesca_michelin_stato_di_natura_feat_maneskin_radio_date_13_03_2020_40752366/|title=Francesca Michelin - Stato di natura (feat. Måneskin) (Radio Date: 13-03-2020)|website=EarOne|access-date=2020-04-09}}</ref> An accompanying music video was released on 19 March 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.newsic.it/video-francesca-michielin-stato-di-natura-feat-maneskin/|title=Video: FRANCESCA MICHIELIN - "Stato di natura" feat. Måneskin|date=2020-03-19|website=Newsic.it|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-04-09}}</ref> On 3 April 2020, "Monolocale" featuring [[Fabri Fibra]] was released as the album's third single.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.earone.it/news/francesca_michielin_monolocale_radio_date_03_04_2020_41234607/|title=Francesca Michielin - MONOLOCALE (Radio Date: 03-04-2020)|website=EarOne|access-date=2020-04-09}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.radiopico.it/monolocale-il-nuovo-singolo-di-francesca-michielin-feat-fabri-fibra/|title="Monolocale" il nuovo singolo di Francesca Michielin feat. Fabri Fibra|date=2020-04-03|website=Radio Pico|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-04-09}}</ref> An accompanying music video was released on 7 April 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://tg24.sky.it/spettacolo/musica/2020/04/07/francesca-michielin-fabri-fibra-monolocale-video.html|title=Francesca Michielin apre il suo Monolocale su New York|last=Cinema|first=Sky|date=2020-04-07|website=tg24.sky.it|access-date=2020-04-09}}</ref>

On 18 December 2020, it was announced that Francesca Michielin would perform the song "[[Chiamami per nome]]" with [[Fedez]] at the [[Sanremo Music Festival 2021|Sanremo Music Festival]] in March 2021.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-12-18|title=Sanremo 2021, Fedez e Francesca Michielin di nuovo insieme: «Siamo cresciuti, ma siamo sempre noi»|url=https://www.vanityfair.it/music/concerti-eventi/2020/12/18/sanremo-2021-fedez-francesca-michielin-insieme-big-scaletta|access-date=2021-01-20|website=VanityFair.it|language=it}}</ref> This marked the pair's third collaboration, following 2013's "[[Cigno nero]]" and 2014's "[[Magnifico (song)|Magnifico]]", which reached number one in Italy. In January 2021, the song was the bookmaker's favourite to be victorious in the contest.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-01-17|title=Sanremo Festival 2021 Odds: Francesca Michielin & Fedez favourite to win|url=http://wiwibloggs.com/2021/01/17/sanremo-festival-2021-odds-francesca-michielin-fedez-favourite-to-win/260552/|access-date=2021-01-20|website=wiwibloggs|language=en-US}}</ref> On 18 January 2021, Michielin announced that she would release the song "Cattive stelle" with Vasco Brondi on 22 January, as an addition to her previous album ''Feat (stato di natura)''.<ref>{{Cite web|last=TG24|first=Sky|title=Francesca Michielin aggiunge un capitolo a Feat: è Cattive Stelle|url=https://tg24.sky.it/spettacolo/musica/2021/01/18/francesca-michelin-aggiuge-un-captiolo-a-feat-e-cattive-stelle|access-date=2021-01-20|website=tg24.sky.it|language=it}}</ref>
Her song "Chiamami per nome" with Fedez placed second at the 71st festival of Sanremo.
