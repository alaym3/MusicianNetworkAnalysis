'''Butrint Imeri''' ({{IPA-sq|buˈtɾint iˈmɛɾi|lang}}; born 3 July 1996) is a Kosovo-Albanian singer, songwriter and dancer.

== Life and career ==

Imeri was born on 3 July 1996 into an [[Albanians|Albanian]] family in the city of [[Lörrach]], Germany.<ref name="Biography">{{cite web |publisher=TeksteShqip |title=Butrint Imeri–Biografia |url=https://www.teksteshqip.com/butrint |access-date=3 February 2020 |language=Albanian}}</ref><ref name="Bio">{{cite web |publisher=[[Top Channel]] |title=Butrint Imeri #Bio |url=http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/artist/butrint-imeri/ |access-date=3 February 2020 the age of 8 shortly after he enrolled in dance classes in his hometown.<ref name="Early years">{{cite web |last=Zettler |first=Veronika |publisher=Verlagshaus Jaumann |title=Mit acht in die Tanzschule: Heute ein Star |url=https://www.verlagshaus-jaumann.de/inhalt.loerrach-mit-acht-in-die-tanzschule-heute-ein-star.45f61395-19d6-4da3-ad07-7edf64ef7051.html |access-date=14 January 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200311140959/https://www.verlagshaus-jaumann.de/inhalt.loerrach-mit-acht-in-die-tanzschule-heute-ein-star.45f61395-19d6-4da3-ad07-7edf64ef7051.html |archive-date=11 March 2020 |language=German |date=10 March 2020}}</ref> Imeri first rose to fame as he debuted with the singles, "Ki me lyp", "Merre zemrën tem" and "Eja Eja", with whom he received significant recognition in the [[Albanian language|Albanian-speaking]] [[Balkans]].<ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Telegrafi]] |title=Lansohet klipi i Butrintit me Real dhe Blunt |access-date=3 February 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201112223031/https://telegrafi.com/ylli-i-ri-butrint-imeri-thote-merre-zemren-tem-video/ |archive-date=12 November 2020 Albania.<ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Klan Kosova|Prive]] |title=Vie 'E Jona' nga Butrint Imeri! |url=https://priveshow.tv/vie-e-jona-nga-butrinti/ |access-date=3 February 2020 == Artistry ==

Imeri's musical work is often defined as [[Contemporary R&B|R&B]] and [[Pop music|Pop]].<ref name="Biography"/><ref name="Bio"/><ref name="Early years"/> He has cited [[Justin Timberlake]] as his major musical influence and stated that he is a fan of [[Elvana Gjata]].<ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Klan Kosova|Prive]] |title=Cilën këngëtare shqiptare e ka të preferuar Butrinti |url=https://prive.al/cilen-kengetare-shqiptare-e-ka-te-preferuar-butrinti/ |access-date=10 February 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201231135344/https://prive.al/cilen-kengetare-shqiptare-e-ka-te-preferuar-butrinti/ |archive-date=31 December 2020 |language=Albanian |date=20 April 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |publisher=Daily Xing |title=Butrint Imeri e thotë hapur: Unë dhe Elvana Gjata do ishim çifti perfekt |url=https://www.dailyxing.com/news/32143/butrint-imeri-says-it-openly-elvana-gjata-and-i-would-be-the-perfect-couple |access-date=10 February 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201231135505/https://www.dailyxing.com/news/32143/butrint-imeri-says-it-openly-elvana-gjata-and-i-would-be-the-perfect-couple |archive-date=31 October 2020 |language=Albanian |date=1 January 2020}}</ref>
