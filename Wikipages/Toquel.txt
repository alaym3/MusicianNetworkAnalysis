'''Klaudjo Dhespo''' ({{IPA-sq|klauˈdjɔ ðɛsˈpɔ|lang}}; born 6 December 1994), known professionally as '''Toquel''', is a Greek-Albanian rapper and songwriter.

== Life and career ==

Toquel was born as Klaudjo Dhespo on 6 December 1994 into an Albanian family in the city of [[Tepelenë]], [[Albania]].<ref>{{cite web |last=Konstantaras |first=Antonis |publisher=[[Vice == Artistry ==

Dhespo's stage name, Toquel, was inspired by a therapist from [[Chania]], Greece, who helped him to manage his [[panic attack]]s.<ref>{{cite web |last=Καπέλλα |first=Ναστάζια |publisher=OneMan |title=O Toquel εμπνεύστηκε το όνομά του από έναν ψυχίατρο |url=https://www.oneman.gr/synentefxeis/toquel-777-interview/ |access-date=2 July 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201130135937/https://www.oneman.gr/synentefxeis/toquel-777-interview/ |archive-date=30 November 2020 |language=el |date=23 May 2019 |url-status=live}}</ref> He grew up listening to artists such as [[50 Cent]], [[Audio Two]], [[Big L]], [[Bone Thugs-n-Harmony]], [[Dr. Dre]], [[Eminem]], [[Mobb Deep]], [[Rakim]], [[Snoop Dogg]], [[The Notorious B.I.G.]], [[Tupac Shakur|Tupac]] and [[Xzibit]].<ref>{{cite web |last=Kouka |first=Anastasia |publisher=Ethnos |title=0 Toquel στο Εθνος: «Απευθύνομαι σε εκείνους που η ζωή τούς ζόρισε» |url=https://www.ethnos.gr/politismos/moysiki/50577_toquel-sto-ethnos-apeythynomai-se-ekeinoys-poy-i-zoi-toys-zorise |access-date=2 July 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190830052100/https://www.ethnos.gr/politismos/moysiki/50577_toquel-sto-ethnos-apeythynomai-se-ekeinoys-poy-i-zoi-toys-zorise |archive-date=30 August 2019 |language=el |date=15 July 2019 |url-status=live}}</ref>
