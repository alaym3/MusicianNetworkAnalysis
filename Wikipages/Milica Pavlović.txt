'''Milica Pavlović''' ({{lang-sr-cyr|Милица Павловић}}, {{IPA-sh|mîlitsa pǎːʋloʋitɕ|pron}}; born 11 August 1991) is a [[Serbs in Switzerland|Swiss-born Serbian]] singer, model and actress who received regional recognition on the singing competition show ''[[Zvezde Granda]]'' in 2011.

==Life and career==
She was born August 11, 1991 in [[Einsiedeln]], [[Switzerland]], but spent most of her childhood with her grandparents in [[Gornji Bunibrod]], near [[Leskovac]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.zvezdegranda.rs/zvezde-granda-2011/milica-pavlovic-imala-tuzno-detinjstvo|title=Milica Pavlović imala tužno detinjstvo|publisher=Zvezde Granda|date=27 November 2011|access-date=29 December 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.svet.rs/najnovije-vesti/milica-pavlovic-nastupam-poput-svetskih-zvezda|title=Milica Pavlović: Nastupam poput svetskih zvezda|publisher=Svet|date=7 June 2012|access-date=29 December 2014}}</ref> Pavlović graduated from a music high school "Stanisal Binički" in Leskovac and also attended the school of singing of [[Aleksandra Radović]].

She rose to prominence in 2011 as a contestant on the televised singing show ''[[Zvezde Granda]]'', where she attracted interest of viewers and judges with her showmanship. Pavlović managed to reach the final, where she was ultimately placed 8th, giving her the chance to sign a recording contract with [[Grand Production]]. Her first single, titled "Tango", was released subsequently in June 2012.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.svet.rs/svetplus/milica-pavlovic-ima-cime-da-se-pohvali-na-fejsbuku/|title=Milica Pavlović ima čime da se pohvali na Fejsbuku!|publisher=Svet|date=22 November 2014|access-date=29 December 2014}}</ref> After several other singles, such as her signature hit "Sexy Senorita" from June 2013, Pavlović released her debut album ''[[Govor tela]]'' (''Body Language'') in June 2014 to commercial success.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.alo.rs/v-i-p/estrada/milica-predstavlja-novi-album-u-zvezdama-granda/59582|title=ČETVOROSTRUKA PREMIJERA: Milica predstavlja novi album u "Zvezdama Granda"|publisher=Alo!|date=27 June 2014|access-date=29 December 2014}}</ref> Her second album, titled ''Boginja'' (''Goddess''), with  was then released in December 2016 featuring stand-out hits like "Mogla sam", "Boginja" and "Ljubi, Ljubi". On her third body of work ''Zauvek'' (''Forever''), which was released in December 2018, Milica collaborated with [[Phoebus (songwriter)|Phoebus]].

In addition to her music career, since 2019 she has also served as the brand ambassador for German retail brand [[Deichmann SE|Deichmann]] in [[Serbia]] and [[Bosnia and Herzegovina]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://tracara.com/posle-rite-ore-i-demi-lovato-milica-pavlovic-postala-ambasador-deichmanna/|title=Posle Rite Ore i Demi Lovato, Milica Pavlović postala ambasador Deichmanna|publisher=Tracara|language=sr}}</ref> Pavlović also scored the lead role in a television drama series ''Pevačica'' in 2021.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.telegraf.rs/pop-i-kultura/film-tv/3381459-milica-pavlovic-u-svom-glumackom-debiju-prvi-trejler-za-seriju-pevacica|title=Milica Pavlović u svom glumačkom debiju: prvi trejler za seriju "Pevačica"|publisher=Telegraf.rs|date= August 28, 2021|language=sr}}</ref>
