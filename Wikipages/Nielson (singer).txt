'''Niels Littooij''' (born 28 December 1989), better known by his stage name '''Nielson''', is a Dutch singer-songwriter. He was a member of the Dutch music trio Zirkus Zirkus in the early 2010s, but rose to national fame in 2012 due to his participation in the television programme ''[[De beste singer-songwriter van Nederland]]''.

==Career==

===1989–2008: Early life and career beginnings===
Niels Littooij was born on 28 December 1989 in [[Dordrecht]]. His passion for music started when he got a drum kit for his birthday. He also started to play the guitar. As an adolescent, Littooij began to explore his interest for music and participated in several local talent shows.<ref name=avrotros>{{cite web|url=http://sterren.avrotros.nl/artiesten/artiesten-pagina-s/nielson/nielson-artiest-biografie/|title=Nielson artiest biografie|language=Dutch|work=[[AVROTROS]]|accessdate=24 November 2015}}</ref> Littooij attended the [[Herman Brood Academy]] in [[Utrecht]], an academy that offers education in various fields of profession related to the music industry,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hermanbroodacademie.nl/english/the-herman-brood-academie/525|title=The Herman Brood Academy|accessdate=24 November 2015}}</ref> where he specialised in [[rap music]].<ref name=3fm>{{cite web|url=http://www.3fm.nl/artiest/7147/nielson/biografie|title=Nielson biografie|work=[[NPO 3FM]]|language=Dutch|accessdate=24 November 2015}}</ref> In 2008, Littooij participated in the ''Kunstbende'' project, where he won the [[Rotterdam]] preliminaries with a rap act. Due to this success, Littooij was asked to perform in the supporting programme of [[The Opposites]].<ref name=3fm/>

===2009–2011: Part of Zirkus Zirkus===
In 2009, [[Nickelodeon (Netherlands)|Nickelodeon]] asked Littooij to be part of a new-formed dance and music trio called Zirkus Zirkus.<ref name=avrotros/> With Zirkus Zirkus, he released several singles and one studio album called ''In de tent''. The trio was aimed at children and they appeared on TV in several Nickelodeon programmes. The trio had some moderate success: three singles charted on the [[Single Top 100]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dutchcharts.nl/showinterpret.asp?interpret=Zirkus+Zirkus|title=Zirkus Zirkus dicografie|language=Dutch|work=dutchcharts.nl|accessdate=24 November 2015}}</ref> In 2011, they split up.

===2012–2013: Rise to fame===
During his education at the Herman Brood Academy, Littooij developed a passion for songwriting, especially for writing Dutch songs. Littooij participated in the 2012 talent show ''De beste singer-songwriter van Nederland'' (English: "The best singer-songwriter of the Netherlands"), broadcast by [[Omroepvereniging VARA|VARA]], [[BNN (Dutch broadcaster)|BNN]] and [[NPO 3FM|3FM]]. This was a great opportunity to reach a broader, national, audience for his self-written songs.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.artiestenwereld.nl/details_biografie.php?id=2477|title=Biografie van Nielson|language=Dutch|work=artiestenwereld.nl|accessdate=24 November 2015}}</ref> In the first episode, Littooij performed his song "Beauty & de brains", which became an instant hit. Although Littooij lost the competition in favor of [[Douwe Bob]], he had achieved national fame and popularity.<ref name=avrotros/>

===2013–present: ''Zo van ah yeah'' and ''Met de tijd mee''===
His song "Beauty & de brains" was released as single and was certified Platinum by the [[NVPI]].<ref name=platinabeauty>{{cite web|url=http://www.3fm.nl/nieuws/detail/354636/Nielson-krijgt-platina-single-voor-'Beauty-%26-The-Brains'|title=Nielson krijgt platina single voor 'Beauty & The Brains'|work=[[NPO 3FM]]|date=17 December 2012|language=Dutch|accessdate=24 November 2015}}</ref> Littooij started working on his first studio album, ''Zo van ah yeah''. It was released in March 2014 through Pacemaker Music. Several singles preceded the album, including "Hoe", a Platinum-certified<ref name=platinahoe>{{cite web|url=http://www.nu.nl/muziek/3535299/nielson-en-miss-montreal-krijgen-platina-plaat-duet.html|last=Oitmann|first=Pierre|title=Nielson en Miss Montreal krijgen platina plaat voor duet|language=Dutch|work=[[NU.nl]]|date=25 July 2013|accessdate=24 November 2015}}</ref> collaboration with [[Miss Montreal]] and "Mannenharten", a collaboration with [[BLØF]] (the title song of the movie of the same name). The album reached the second position on the Album Top 100.<ref name=Top100>{{cite web|url=https://dutchcharts.nl/search.asp?search=nielson&cat=s |title=Nielson in the Mega Single Top 100|language=Dutch |website=Dutchcharts.nl|accessdate=17 November 2018}}</ref>

With the 2014 single "Sexy als ik dans", Littooij achieved his first success in Belgium. It reached the peak position in the [[Ultratop 50]] and was certified Platinum by the [[Belgian Entertainment Association]].<ref name=Ultratop>{{cite web|url=http://www.ultratop.be/nl/showinterpret.asp?interpret=Nielson |title=Nielson discografie|language=Dutch |publisher=Ultratop.be|accessdate=24 November 2015}}</ref><ref name=goldbel>{{cite web|url=http://www.ultratop.be/nl/goud-platina/2015|title=Goud en platina 2015|work=ultratop.be|language=Dutch|accessdate=1 December 2015}}</ref> The single also performed well in his own country, where it became his third Platinum-certified single.<ref name=platinasexy>{{cite web|url=http://www.nu.nl/muziek/3923514/nielson-krijgt-platina-sexy-als-dans.html|work=[[NU.nl]]|title=Nielson krijgt platina voor Sexy als ik dans|language=Dutch|date=7 November 2014|accessdate=24 November 2015}}</ref> When King [[Willem-Alexander of the Netherlands|Willem-Alexander]] visited Littooij's home city of [[Dordrecht]] on [[Koningsdag]] 2015, Littooij was chosen to end the festivities with a performance of "Sexy als ik dans".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://nos.nl/artikel/2032686-koningsdag-in-dordrecht-afgesloten-met-muziekspektakel.html|title=Koningsdag in Dordrecht afgesloten met muziekspektakel|language=Dutch|date=27 April 2015|accessdate=24 November 2015|work=[[Nederlandse Omroep Stichting]]}}</ref>

In 2015, Littooij released two extended plays called ''Met de tijd mee, deel 1'' en ''Met de tijd mee, deel 2''. Included on the EPs is the single "Sexy als ik dans", among others.
