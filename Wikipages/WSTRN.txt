'''WSTRN''' (a [[disemvoweling]] of the word '''Western''', which is also pronounced), is a British [[afroswing]] [[musical collective|collective]] from [[West London]] consisting of Akelle Charles, Haile (born Ras Haile Alexander), and Louis Rei (born Louis-Rae Beadle). Their debut single, "[[In2]]", was released on 20 July 2015,<ref>{{citation|title=WSTRN - IN2 (Original Video) {{!}} Link Up TV|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4k3g4VGRn8E|access-date=22 January 2020}}</ref> and peaked at number 4 on the [[UK Singles Chart]]. Louis Rei was formerly known as LB, Akelle was formerly known as A-Star and is Haile's cousin. Haile has featured on several tracks as a solo artist, including "Can't Blame Me" by [[Nines (rapper)|Nines]].

==Background==
WSTRN consists of Akelle Charles, Haile and Louis Rei.<ref name="4music">{{cite episode|title=This Week's Fresh Music Top 20|network=[[4Music]]|airdate=22 October 2015}}</ref> The name bears no relevance to [[western (genre)|western]]s; according to the band, the name represents positive vibrations<ref name="vice">{{cite web|url=http://noisey.vice.com/en_uk/blog/is-wstrns-in2-the-underground-smash-hit-of-the-year|title=Is WSTRN'S "In2" The Underground Smash Hit of The Year|publisher=Noisey.vice.com}}</ref> and to them means that it is "[[West London]]'s turn to shut down the scene".<ref name="introducing">{{cite web|url=http://www.officialcharts.com/chart-news/introducing-wstrn-playing-live-is-the-best-feeling__11246/|title=Introducing WSTRN: 'Playing live is the best feeling'|publisher=[[The Official Charts Company]]}}</ref> Akelle and Haile are cousins who know Louis, a former footballer who last played for Staines Town F.C., through their inhabitance of [[West London]], while Akelle is the brother of singer [[Angel (British musician)|Angel]]<ref name="4music"/> and was part of a band with him and his three other siblings called the Charles Family, co-managed by the late reggae singer [[Smiley Culture]] and Akelle's father, Tendai Charles, who was in the same reggae group as Haile's father. Rei's father is [[Gary Beadle]]. The group failed to achieve mainstream success despite being the subject of a BBC documentary ''One Life''. Akelle Charles had two main followers, which were his nephews, who encouraged him to keep producing songs as well as the will to be signed.<ref name="guardian">{{cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2013/mar/24/angel-turned-fortunes-after-jail|title=Meet Angel, the new R&B star who turned his fortunes around after jail|first=Eva |last=Simpson|date=24 March 2013|work=[[The Guardian]]}}</ref>

==Career==
===2015–present: Formation and "In2"===
Their debut single, "[[In2 (song)|In2]]", was released on 20 July 2015,<ref name="mtv">{{cite web|url=http://www.mtv.co.uk/the-wrap-up/blog/WSTRN-In2-music-video|title=WSTRN Drop Brand New Video For 'In2'|publisher=[[MTV]]}}</ref> through [[Link Up TV]]<ref>{{citation|title=WSTRN - IN2 (Original Video) {{!}} Link Up TV|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4k3g4VGRn8E|access-date=22 January 2020}}</ref> and came together as a result of a jam session, instrumental first; Akelle and Haile had written a track and had asked Louis to feature on it.<ref name="introducing"/> It was recorded in one of the group's first sessions<ref name="gigwise">{{cite web|url=http://www.gigwise.com/features/103496/wstrn-interview-and-session-london-trio-in2|title=Introducing the biggest and smallest act in the UK right now, WSTRN|publisher=Gigwise}}</ref> and has appeared on the [[Radio 1Xtra]] and [[Kiss (UK radio station)|Kiss FM]] playlists.<ref name="the405">{{cite web|url=http://www.thefourohfive.com/music/article/listen-to-wstrn-s-new-track-in2-144|title=Listen to WSTRN's new track 'In2'|publisher=The 405}}</ref> Two music videos were produced for the song – the first was pulled after the band signed to Atlantic Records, while the second was produced by Sally Sibbet and sees the group loitering across West London.<ref name="capital">{{cite web|url=http://www.capitalxtra.com/news/wstrn-unveil-official-video-for-in2/#Gz8V7CJc94YG0id0.97|title=WSTRN Unveil Official Video For New Song 'In2'|publisher=[[Capital Xtra]]}}</ref> Prior to the release of the latter video, Atlantic Records pulled down 43 unofficial YouTube and [[SoundCloud]] links before its submission.<ref name="vice"/>

On 30 October, "In2" debuted at number 85 in the top 100 of the [[UK Singles Chart]], and climbed to number 60 on 6 November, the day of its digital download release. For these two weeks, it charted on streams alone. Upon its digital release, it peaked at number 4 on 13 November 2015, earning the group its first top 40 and general chart entry.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.officialcharts.com/search/artists/wstrn|title=WSTRN {{!}} full Official Chart History|publisher=[[Official Charts Company]]}}</ref>

On 2 December 2015, the trio released the song "[[Got Love (WSTRN song)|Got Love]]",<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.capitalxtra.com/new-music/lists/songs-week/4th-december-2015/#6pB8Ujuzkjp2oK11.97|title=The 5 Biggest New Songs To Drop this Week (4th December 2015)|work=[[Capital Xtra]]|date=4 December 2015}}</ref> produced by Parallel (a collective made up of Akelle's brother, [[Angel (British musician)|Angel]], and PRGRSHN).<ref>{{cite web|url=http://grmdaily.com/video/wstrn-release-new-video-got-love/|title=WSTRN release brand new video 'Got Love'|work=GRM Daily|date=3 December 2015}}</ref> The song was available to download for free when subscribing to their mailing list,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://rwdmag.com/wstrn-show-that-theyve-got-love-for-the-fans-with-free-download/|title=WSTRN show that they've 'Got Love' for the fans with free download|work=[[RWD Magazine]]|author=Brydon, Grant|date=3 December 2015}}</ref> and was also released via digital retailers on 4 December. The group expressed via Twitter that the song was not a single.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mobo.com/wstrn-return-new-track-got-love|title=WSTRN return with new track 'Got Love'|work=[[MOBO Awards]]|author=Gboyega, Adenike|date=3 December 2015}}</ref> According to ''[[Clash (magazine)|Clash Music]]'', the song is about "appreciating the simple things in life".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.clashmusic.com/news/listen-wstrn-share-got-love|title=Listen: WSTRN Share 'Got Love'|work=[[Clash (magazine)|Clash]]|author=Murray, Robin|date=3 December 2015}}</ref>

On 28 July 2018, Akelle was convicted alongside his brother of violent disorder following an attack on a 60-year-old woman and a 40-year-old man in their own flat. He received a {{frac|4|1|2}}-year sentence of imprisonment. <ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ibtimes.co.uk/wstrn-singer-akelle-charles-jailed-after-grandmothers-teeth-knocked-out-assault-1632391|title=WSTRN singer Akelle Charles jailed after grandmother's teeth knocked out in assault|work=[[International Business Times]]|author=Adejobe, Alicia|date=28 July 2017}}</ref> he also recently released a new song featuring fireboy dml titled <ref>{{Cite web|last=Vibejamz|date=27 August 2021|title=WSTRN Ft. Fireboy DML – Be My Guest|url=https://vibejamz.com/download-mp3-wstrn-ft-fireboy-dml-be-my-guest/|url-status=live|website=Vibejamz}}</ref>
