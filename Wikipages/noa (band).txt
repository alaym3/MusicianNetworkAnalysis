'''Noa''' was a French [[Magma_(band)#Zeuhl|zeuhl]] group.<ref name="arch">{{cite web|title=Noa|url=http://www.progarchives.com/artist.asp?id=6674|website=progarchives.com}}</ref> The band claims influences from the progressive rock bands [[Henry Cow]] and [[Art Zoyd]].<ref>{{cite web|title=NOA|url=http://www.soleilzeuhl.com/en/catalogue/reeditions/noa/|website=soleilzeuhl.com}}</ref>

The group's lineup was Philippe Vincendeau on saxophones, Christian Robard on drums, vibraphone and xylophone, Bernard Nicolas on flute and soprano saxophone, Alain Gaubert on guitar and bass, and Claudie Nicolas on vocals.<ref name="arch"/>

In 2011, the band's only album, ''Noa,'' was re-released by the label [[Soleil Zeuhl]].<ref>{{cite web|last1=Davis|first1=Jon|title=Review of Noa - Noa|url=http://www.expose.org/index.php/articles/display/noa-noa-2.html|website=expose.org}}</ref>
