'''''199X''''' is the debut [[extended play]] by South Korean co-ed group, [[Triple H (band)|Triple H]], composed of solo singer [[Hyuna]] and [[Pentagon (South Korean band)|Pentagon]] members Hui and E'Dawn. It was released by [[Cube Entertainment]] and [[LOEN Entertainment]] on May 1, 2017. The song "365 Fresh" was released as the title track.

== Background and release ==
On April 13, 2017, the first teaser images were released, revealing the three members of Triple H. A few days later, a concept preview video was released setting a release date for May 1, 2017, at 12 pm. On April 20, the full track list was released, revealing the name of the title track as "365 Fresh" and for the EP of five songs as ''199X''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.soompi.com/2017/04/11/cube-entertainments-new-triple-h-unit-drops-first-concept-photo/|title=Update: Triple H Releases Track List And Title Track Details For Upcoming Mini Album|first=C.|last=Hong|date=2017-04-11|website=Soompi|access-date=2017-05-18}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last=United CUBE (CUBE Entertainment Official YouTube Channel)|title=트리플 H(Triple H) - Concept Preview|date=2017-04-16|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FhXg8M0ldCs|accessdate=2017-05-18}}</ref> On April 26, an audio teaser of the upcoming EP was released, revealing a short version of each song.<ref>{{Citation|last=United CUBE (CUBE Entertainment Official YouTube Channel)|title=트리플 H(Triple H) - 1st Mini Album "199X" Audio Snippet|date=2017-04-26|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6OElhnbOmLU|accessdate=2017-05-18}}</ref> From April 27 to April 29, individual teasers were released, starting with Hyuna, followed by Hui and E'Dawn.<ref>{{Citation|last=United CUBE (CUBE Entertainment Official YouTube Channel)|title=트리플 H(Triple H) - '365 FRESH' M/V Teaser (HyunA)|date=2017-04-27|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_daZpcdfUiY|accessdate=2017-05-18}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last=United CUBE (CUBE Entertainment Official YouTube Channel)|title=트리플 H(Triple H) - '365 FRESH' M/V Teaser (Hui)|date=2017-04-28|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gC56lAHnWpA|accessdate=2017-05-18}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last=United CUBE (CUBE Entertainment Official YouTube Channel)|title=트리플 H(Triple H) - '365 FRESH' M/V Teaser (E'dawn)|date=2017-04-29|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rZP0WSOJs3o|accessdate=2017-05-18}}</ref>

The EP was released on May 1, 2017, through several music portals, including [[Melon (online music service)|Melon]] in South Korea and [[ITunes Store|iTunes]] for the global market.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.melon.com/album/detail.htm?albumId=10059017|title=199X|website=www.melon.com|language=ko|access-date=2017-05-18}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/199x-ep/id1231900105|title=199X - EP by Triple H on Apple Music|website=iTunes|language=en|access-date=2017-05-18}}</ref>

== Promotion ==
=== Single ===
"365 Fresh" was released as the title track from the EP. The song debuted at number 81 on the [[Gaon Digital Chart]] with 26,199 digitals downloads sold.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://gaonchart.co.kr/main/section/chart/online.gaon?nationGbn=T&serviceGbn=ALL&targetTime=18&hitYear=2017&termGbn=week|title=국내 대표 음악 차트 가온차트!|website=gaonchart.co.kr|access-date=2017-05-18}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://gaonchart.co.kr/main/section/chart/online.gaon?nationGbn=T&serviceGbn=S1020&targetTime=18&hitYear=2017&termGbn=week|title=국내 대표 음악 차트 가온차트!|website=gaonchart.co.kr|access-date=2017-05-18}}</ref> A music video was released in conjunction with the EP on May 1. The story follows as each member committed a crime that leads them to find each other and be followed by the police.<ref>{{Citation|last=United CUBE (CUBE Entertainment Official YouTube Channel)|title=트리플 H(Triple H) - '365 FRESH' Official Music Video|date=2017-04-30|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qsJd9Ixa-ow|accessdate=2017-05-18}}</ref>

== Commercial performance ==
''199X'' debuted at number 4 on the [[Gaon Album Chart]] on the chart issue dated April 30 - May 6, 2017.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://gaonchart.co.kr/main/section/chart/album.gaon?nationGbn=T&serviceGbn=&targetTime=18&hitYear=2017&termGbn=week|title=국내 대표 음악 차트 가온차트!|website=gaonchart.co.kr|access-date=2017-05-18}}</ref> In its second week, the EP fell to number 32.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://gaonchart.co.kr/main/section/chart/album.gaon?nationGbn=T&serviceGbn=&targetTime=19&hitYear=2017&termGbn=week|title=국내 대표 음악 차트 가온차트!|website=gaonchart.co.kr|access-date=2017-05-18}}</ref>

The EP also charted at number 10 on [[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'s [[Billboard charts|World Albums]] on the week ending May 20, 2017.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.billboard.com/charts/world-albums/2017-05-20|title=World Music: Top World Albums Chart|work=Billboard|access-date=2017-05-18}}</ref>

The EP entered at number 22 on the Gaon Album Chart for the month of May 2017 for 5,157 physical copies sold.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://gaonchart.co.kr/main/section/chart/album.gaon?nationGbn=T&serviceGbn=&targetTime=05&hitYear=2017&termGbn=month|title=국내 대표 음악 차트 가온차트!|website=gaonchart.co.kr|access-date=2017-06-08}}</ref>
