'''Wild Ones''' were<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.portlandmercury.com/music/2018/08/22/22317936/wild-ones-calls-it-quits|title=Wild Ones Calls It Quits|date=2018-08-22|access-date=2020-04-27|publisher=The Portland Mercury}}</ref> an American [[dream pop]] band from [[Portland, Oregon]].

==History==
Wild Ones began in 2010 with the release of an EP titled ''You're A Winner''.<ref name="partydamage">{{cite web|title=Wild Ones|url=http://partydamagerecords.com/wild_ones/|publisher=Party Damage Records|accessdate=6 January 2017}}</ref><ref name="oregonlive"/> In 2014, they released their first full-length album on [[Topshelf Records]].<ref name="punknews">{{cite web|title=Wild Ones - Keep It Safe (2014)|url=https://www.punknews.org/review/12503/wild-ones-keep-it-safe|publisher=Punknews|accessdate=6 January 2017}}</ref><ref name="popmatters">{{cite web|last1=Fennell|first1=Sean|title=Wild Ones - Keep It Safe|url=http://www.popmatters.com/review/180943-wild-ones-keep-it-safe/|publisher=[[PopMatters]]|accessdate=6 January 2017}}</ref><ref name="spin">{{cite web|last1=Martins|first1=Chris|title=Wild Ones Douse Dream-Pop in Synth Beauty on 'From Nothing'|url=http://www.spin.com/2013/09/wild-ones-from-nothing-stream-keep-it-safe-album/|publisher=[[Spin (magazine)|Spin]]|accessdate=6 January 2017}}</ref><ref name="oregonlive">{{cite web|last1=Frochtzwajg|first1=Jonathan|title=Portland's Wild Ones' promise -- authenticity|url=http://www.oregonlive.com/music/index.ssf/2013/07/portlands_wild_ones_promise_--.html|publisher=[[The Oregonian]]|accessdate=6 January 2017}}</ref>

In 2015, Wild Ones released an EP titled ''Heatwave'' on Topshelf Records.<ref name="consequenceofsound">{{cite web|last1=Henry|first1=Dusty|title=Stream: Wild Ones' new EP Heatwave|url=http://consequenceofsound.net/2015/08/stream-wild-ones-new-ep-heatwave/|publisher=[[Consequence of Sound]]|accessdate=6 January 2017}}</ref><ref name="noisey">{{cite web|last1=Bennett|first1=Kim|title=PREMIERE: Wild Ones - "Dim the Lights"|url=https://noisey.vice.com/en_uk/article/premiere-wild-ones---dim-the-lights|publisher=[[Vice (magazine)|Vice]]|accessdate=6 January 2017}}</ref>
