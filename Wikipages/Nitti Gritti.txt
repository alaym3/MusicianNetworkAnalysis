== Early life and career ==

Raised in [[Pennsylvania]], Mears moved with his family to [[Haiti]] at the age of six where his parents served in Christian missionary work—his father as a soccer coach and his mother, a teacher.<ref name="Rolland"/> After living In Haiti for eight years, he moved to [[Miami|Miami, Florida]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Adamchak|first=Jason|title=Getting Down to the Nitti Gritti [Interview]|url=https://edm.com/interviews/getting-down-to-the-nitti-gritti-interview|access-date=2021-04-10|website=EDM.com - The Latest Electronic Dance Music News, Reviews & Artists|language=en}}</ref> He started his professional music career under the name Nitti Gritti in the year 2016 as he released ''It's Nit!''.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Nitti Gritti Discography at CD Universe|url=https://www.cduniverse.com/search/xx/music/artist/Nitti+Gritti/a/albums.htm|access-date=2021-04-10|website=www.cduniverse.com}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|title=Nitti Gritti - Music Rankings|url=http://lyricsodus.com/music/MusicRankings.html?id=266758200&lang=es-ES|language=es|access-date=2021-04-10}}</ref> In 2018, he attended his first world tour and [[Ghastly (DJ)|Ghastly]]'s The Mystifying Oracle Tour. In the same year he produced Enrique's “''[[Move to Miami|Move To Miami]]''” featuring [[Pitbull (rapper)|Pitbull]]<ref name=":1" /> and Bad Bunny's [[200 MPH (song)|200 MPH]], with [[Diplo]].<ref name=":2" /> His 2018 release ''Crack'' was the 7th most played song at 2018 [[Ultra Music Festival]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Longhurst|first=Courtney|title=Nitti Gritti Collabs with Vlien Boy on ‘Bailando’|url=http://relentlessbeats.com/2018/04/nitti-gritti-collabs-with-vlein-boy-on-bailando-2/|access-date=2021-04-10|website=Relentless Beats|language=en-US}}</ref> Mears performed in [[Audiotistic Music Festival]],<ref>{{Cite web|last=Anika Anderson|date=2019-12-07|title=Nitti Gritti & Wuki Team Up For 2020 Tour|url=https://www.edmtunes.com/2019/12/nitti-gritti-wuki-team-up-for-2020-tour/|access-date=2021-04-10|website=EDMTunes|language=en-US}}</ref> [[Electric Daisy Carnival]],<ref>{{Cite web|title=Nitti Gritti performers at the Circuit Grounds stage during day two of Electric Daisy Carnival …|url=https://www.reviewjournal.com/entertainment/a-look-at-night-2-of-edc-las-vegas-photos-1667878/attachment/nitti-gritti-performers-at-the-circuit-grounds-stage-during-day-two-of-electric-daisy-carnival/|access-date=2021-04-10|website=Las Vegas Review-Journal|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Mixing.DJ|date=2019-11-12|title=Nitti Gritti -
