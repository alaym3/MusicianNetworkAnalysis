'''Leisure''' are a [[Soul music|soul]], [[Rhythm and blues|RnB]] and [[Pop music|pop]] collective from [[Auckland]], formed in [[Muriwai]] in 2015.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2019-07-24|title=LEISURE prioritise good vibes first, music second|url=https://www.beat.com.au/leisure-prioritise-good-vibes-first-music-second/|access-date=2021-02-24|website=Beat Magazine|language=en-AU}}</ref> The band consists of [[Djeisan Suskov]], [[Goodnight Nurse|Jaden Parkes]], [[Josh Fountain]], [[Tom Young (musician)|Tom Young]], and [[Jordan Arts]], all established New Zealand musicians who have worked in the Auckland scene since the 2000s.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Concert review: LEISURE, at Crystal Palace|url=https://www.nzherald.co.nz/aucklander/news/concert-review-leisure-at-crystal-palace/MOZF7DZEJNCEPRLZOG7BSHPZ4M/|access-date=2021-02-24|website=NZ Herald|language=en-NZ}}</ref> 

Since their formation the band have produced two albums (''Leisure'' in 2016<ref>{{Cite web|last=Saw|first=Yadana|date=2016-10-21|title=Leisure: yacht rockers set sail|url=https://www.rnz.co.nz/national/programmes/nat-music/audio/201820961/leisure-yacht-rockers-set-sail|access-date=2021-02-24|website=RNZ|language=en-nz}}</ref> and ''Twister'' in 2019<ref>{{Cite web|date=2019-07-20|title=What To Listen To: Leisure get down and deep on sophomore album|url=https://www.stuff.co.nz/entertainment/music/114262886/what-to-listen-to-leisure-get-down-and-deep-on-sophomore-album|access-date=2021-02-24|website=Stuff|language=en}}</ref>), one EP (''Side A'' in 2020) and various singles, such as "Spark It Up", "Money" and "Got It Bad" (which was featured in the American television drama ''[[You (TV series)|You]]'').<ref>{{Cite web|title=At your Leisure: The Auckland band with more listeners in New York and Paris than NZ|url=https://www.metromag.co.nz/arts/arts-music/leisure-nz-music-auckland-metro|access-date=2021-01-27|website=www.metromag.co.nz|language=en}}</ref>

The band's members established themselves in the early 2000s, with the debut album by Jaden Parkes' band [[Goodnight Nurse]], ''[[Always and Never]]'', being certified [[Music recording certification|gold]] after its 2006 release. Furthermore, Jordan Art's [[Kids of 88]] had various hits from their debut album ''[[Sugarpills]]'', such as "[[My House (Kids of 88 song)|My House]]", "[[Just a Little Bit (Kids of 88 song)|Just a Little Bit]]" and "[[Downtown (Kids of 88 song)|Downtown]]". Fountain, who is also known for being [[Benee]]'s producer, recalled getting the band together after having casual music sessions during a spontaneous holiday at a [[Bach (New Zealand)|bach]] on the West Coast of the [[South Island]], subsequently forming a [[Supergroup (music)|supergroup]].
