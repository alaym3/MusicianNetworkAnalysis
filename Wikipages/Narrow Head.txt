'''Narrow Head''' is an American rock band from Texas. The group was founded by vocalist Jacob Duarte in Dallas, but the band is now based in Houston.<ref name=do>[https://www.dallasobserver.com/music/texas-band-narrow-head-new-album-11927850 Texas Band Narrow Head Is Kind of Shoegaze, Even If by Accident]. ''[[Dallas Observer]]'', July 21, 2020.</ref>

==History==
Narrow Head formed in 2013 and released their debut album, ''Satisfaction'', in 2016.<ref name=newnoise>[https://newnoisemagazine.com/news-narrow-head-sign-to-run-for-cover-announce-album-first-single/ News: Narrow Head Sign to Run For Cover, Announce Album + First Single]. ''New Noise'', June 22, 2020.</ref> By this time, the group's membership had coalesced into the trio of Jacob Duarte on vocals, William Menjivar on guitar, and Carson Wilcox on drums.<ref name=newnoise/> In 2018, the group released the single "Bulma" and did a tour of the western United States.<ref>[https://www.houstonpress.com/music/narrow-head-returns-with-a-sound-from-long-ago-10125204 Narrow Head Returns With Killer New Track]. ''[[Houston Press]]'', January 22, 2018.</ref> In 2020, Narrow Head signed with [[Run For Cover Records]] and released the singles "Night Tryst", "Stuttering Stanley, and "Hard to Swallow" in preparation for their sophomore album, ''12th House Rock''.<ref>[https://uproxx.com/indie/narrow-head-night-tryst-12th-house-rock/ Narrow Head’s Noisy ‘Night Tryst’ Heralds Their Indie Label Debut ’12th House Rock’]. ''[[Uproxx]]'', June 22, 2020.</ref><ref>[https://www.stereogum.com/2093780/narrow-head-hard-to-swallow-12th-house-rock/music/ Narrow Head – “Hard To Swallow”]. ''[[Stereogum]]'', August 5, 2020.</ref>
