'''NoCopyrightSounds''' ('''NCS''') is a British record label that releases royalty-free [[electronic dance music]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.edmsauce.com/2017/11/09/copyright-free-music/ |title=Copyright Free Music: These Labels Are Changing Up Royalty Free Music |last=Stevo |date=9 November 2017 |website=EDM Sauce |language=en-US |access-date=26 January 2018}}</ref> Starting as a [[YouTube]] channel,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://sites.google.com/nocopyrightsounds.co.uk/ncs|title=FAQ / Usage Policy|website=NoCopyrightSounds|access-date=16 February 2019}}</ref> it reached 1 million paid downloads in 2017.

==History==
NoCopyrightSounds was started as a YouTube channel in 2011 by an "avid gamer"<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://startups.co.uk/young-entrepreneurs-billy-woodford-nocopyrightsounds/ |title=Young Entrepreneurs: Billy Woodford, NoCopyrightSounds |date=18 March 2015 |website=Startups.co.uk: Starting a business advice and business ideas |language=en-GB |access-date=26 January 2018}}</ref> named Billy Woodford.<ref name="YEDM1"/><ref name="BB"/> The label was described by ''[[Forbes]]'' as "a YouTube-first label that allows indie creators to use and even monetize its music freely as long as they give due credit back to the content owners."<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/cheriehu/2017/10/25/how-youtube-entrepreneurs-in-their-20s-are-disrupting-traditional-record-labels/#5a36e540182a |title=How YouTube Entrepreneurs In Their 20s Are Disrupting Traditional Record Labels |last=Hu |first=Cherie |website=Forbes |language=en |access-date=26 January 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.influencerupdate.biz/news/66698/nocopyrightsounds-youtube-channel-has-138m-subscribers-and-1m-paid-itunes-downloads/ |title=NoCopyrightSounds has 13.8m subscribers... and 1m paid iTunes downloads |website=influencerupdate.biz |access-date=26 January 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://weraveyou.com/2017/12/ncs-1-million-paid-downloads/ |title=Alan Walker's success is owed to this label and it just reached a major milestone |date=18 December 2017 |website=We Rave You |language=en-US |access-date=26 January 2018}}</ref>

In 2017, NoCopyrightSounds achieved a milestone of over a million sales of digital downloads, despite releasing music for free.<ref name="YEDM1">{{Cite web |url=http://www.youredm.com/2017/12/31/nocopyrightsounds-hits-huge-milestone-1-million-digital-sales/ |title=This EDM Label Just Hit 1 Million Sales Despite Giving Away Its Music Free |date=31 December 2017 |website=Your EDM |language=en-US |access-date=26 January 2018}}</ref> The label manager Daniel J. Lee said "Similar to services with a freemium model, NCS provides both the option to enjoy music for free but at the same time also enables fans to support the music across paid services", asserting that the label releases music for free and also sells them.<ref name="BB">{{Cite web |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/8070591/ncs-label-free-music-1-million-paid-downloads |title=NCS Celebrates 1 Million Paid Downloads, Despite Giving Music Away for Free: Exclusive |website=Billboard |access-date=26 January 2018}}</ref> Woodford, about the milestone, stated, "I’m immensely proud to have reached 1 million paid downloads, and believe this shows the growth and strength NCS has built over the years."<ref name="YEDM1"/> He also commented that "The demand for fresh original music from Creators is enormous and growing daily."<ref name="DA">{{Cite web |url=http://www.dancingastronaut.com/2017/12/youtube-record-label-nocopyrightsounds-hits-milestone-1-million-record-sales-despite-free-downloads/ |title=YouTube record label NCS hits $1 million in sales |date=17 December 2017 |website=Dancing Astronaut |language=en-US |access-date=26 January 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.edmsauce.com/2017/12/15/nocopyrightsounds-million-sales/ |title=Popular YouTube Label Reaches One Million Sales Despite Offering Free Downloads |last=Stevo |date=15 December 2017 |website=EDM Sauce |language=en-US |access-date=26 January 2018}}</ref>
