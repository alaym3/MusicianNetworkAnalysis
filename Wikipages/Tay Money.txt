'''Taylor Noelle Watson''' (born April 6, 1993), known professionally as '''Tay Money''', is an American rapper. She started her career in 2017, and gained fame after the release of her single "Trappers Delight" in June 2018. She then released her debut EP, ''DUH!''. Her debut mixtape, ''Hurricane Tay'' was released in March 2019. In 2020, her single, "Bussin" became a viral sensation on the app [[TikTok]]<ref>{{Cite web|last=Garrity|first=Caitlyn|date=December 1, 2020|title=Tay Money Bussin' Her Way Through the Rap Industry|url=http://www.valleymagazinepsu.com/tay-money-bussin-her-way-through-the-rap-industry/|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=January 21, 2021|website=Valley|language=en-US}}</ref> and reached #41 on the U.S. Viral 50 on February 9, 2020. In 2021 she signed a record deal with [[Geffen Records]] and [[Interscope Records]].

==Career==
Tay Money made her debut with the single "Lewis & Clark" in 2017, she later released the single "Na Na".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://m.youtube.com/watch?list=PL8Etq0CAZvwQ2wbhRE0oF9df4Uwdq1JjG&v=jaBQMg4cuIQ|title=Tay Money - Na Na (Official Video) ( Directed by @DontHypeMe )}}</ref> Her breakthrough came with the 2018 single "Trapper's Delight" which was released on WorldStarHipHop's YouTube channel, The music video received over 1.2 million views in less than a month.<ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=|title=Tay Money: Bubble Rap Barbie|url=https://www.inkedmag.com/.amp/original-news/taymoney|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=January 23, 2021|website=inkedmag.com}}</ref> She released her debut EP, ''DUH!'', on November of that year.<ref>{{Citation|title=Duh! - EP by Tay Money|url=https://music.apple.com/us/album/duh-ep/1521944559|language=en-us|access-date=January 23, 2021}}</ref>
