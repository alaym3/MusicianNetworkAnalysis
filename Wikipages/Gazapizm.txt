'''Anıl Acar''', (born 20 July 1988) better known by his stage name '''Gazapizm''', is a Turkish rapper. He began making [[Turkish hip hop]] music in 2003, and in 2012 he found Argo İzmir in [[Konak, İzmir]]. In 2014, he published the album ''Yeraltı Edebiyatı''. He attracted attention with his street-like music compositions. In 2016, he released his second studio album ''Bir Gün Her Şey''. The song's release coincided with a period in which Turkish rap songs were more visible and used in the media and television.<ref name=":TVGazetesi">{{cite web | title = Dizilerdeki yeni trendi siz de fark ettiniz mi?
