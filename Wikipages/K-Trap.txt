'''Devonte Perkins''', better known by his stage name '''K-Trap''', is a British rapper and songwriter from [[South London]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://trenchtrenchtrench.com/features/ktrap-interview|title=K-Trap Has Dreams Bigger Than The Bando|website=Trenchtrenchtrench.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2019/jul/20/one-to-watch-k-trap-drill-big-mood|title=One to watch: K Trap|first=Tara|last=Joshi|date=20 July 2019|website=Theguardian.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://mixmag.net/read/ktrap-no-magic-news|title=K-Trap removes his mask on new music video for 'Big Mood'|website=Mixmag.net}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.factmag.com/2018/01/04/grime-drill-uk-rap-2018/|title=10 UK rap artists to watch in 2018|website=Factmag.com|date=4 January 2018}}</ref> He is known for his prominence in the [[UK drill]] scene, releasing notable mixtapes such as ''The Last Whip'' (2017), ''The Re-Up'' (2018) and ''No Magic'' (2019).

His debut studio album, ''Street Side Effects'', was released on 13 November 2020.
