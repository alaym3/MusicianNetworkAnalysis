'''Zuzu''' is an administrative ward in the [[Dodoma Urban]] district of the [[Dodoma Region]] of [[Tanzania]]. According to the 2002 and 2012 census,<ref>https://www.nbs.go.tz/nbs/takwimu/majimbo/MAJIMBOApril_Kisw.pdf</ref> the ward has a total population of 7,048.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tanzania.go.tz/census/census/districts/dodomaurban.htm|title=2002 Population and Housing Census General Report|publisher=Government of [[Tanzania]]|access-date=2008-08-22 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20060608223755/http://www.tanzania.go.tz/census/census/districts/dodomaurban.htm |archive-date=2006-06-08}}</ref>
