'''Tatjana Savić''' ({{lang-sr-cyr|Татјана Савић}}; born 20 March 1985), better known as '''Tanja Savić''', is a Serbian pop-folk singer who rose to prominence in the first season of ''[[Zvezde Granda]]'' in 2004, finishing third.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.story.rs/poznati/vesti/71191/audicija-pre-13-godina-ovako-je-tanja-savic-izgledala-na-pocetku-karijere-video|title=Audicija pre 13 godina: Ovako je Tanja Savić izgledala na početku karijere|website=story.rs|language=sr|date=9 June 2016|access-date=25 June 2016}}</ref><ref name="Hello!">{{cite web|url=https://www.hellomagazin.rs/2020/08/24/tanja-savic-u-iskrenoj-ispovesti-za-hello-otkriva-svoj-bol/|title=Tanja Savić u iskrenoj ispovesti za Hello|publisher=Hello!|language=sr|date=24 August 2020|access-date=2 December 2020}}</ref> Following the show, Savić was signed to [[Grand Production]] through which she released her debut album, ''[[Tako mlada]]'', to general success.<ref name="Hello!"/> With songs "Suknjica" and "Gde ljubav putuje" which were featured in movies ''Guča!'' (2006) and ''Srpski ožiljci'' (2009), respectively, she received further notice. 

After facing career setbacks, Savić managed to retrieve popularity with her fourth album ''Stranci'' (2019), on which she collaborated with hip-hop duo Corona and Rimski.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.hellomagazin.rs/celebrity-news/domace-vesti/tanja-savic-se-vraca-sceni-snimam-nove-pesme-za-svoj-cetvrti-album-nakon-10-godina/|title=Tanja Savić se vraća sceni, snima nove pesme za svoj četvrti album nakon 10 godina|website=hellomagazine.rs|language=sr}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://nova.rs/zabava/kako-su-rasta-cobi-buba-i-jala-pevace-spasili-od-zaborava/|title=Kako su Rasta, Cobi, Buba i Jala pevače spasili od zaborava|website=Nova.rs|language=sr|date=February 8, 2021|access-date=November 19, 2021|author=Dejanović, M.}}</ref>

In 2011, she married Serbian-Australian businessman from [[Queensland]], Dušan Jovaničević, with whom she has two sons.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.story.rs/poznati/vesti/14941/tanja-savic-istina-je-udala-sam-se|title=Tanja Savić: Istina je, udala sam se|website=story.rs|language=sr|date=17 April 2011|access-date=25 June 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://nova.rs/zabava/showbiz/decenija-zlostavljanja-i-ucena-zivotna-agonija-tanje-savic/|title=Decenija zlostavljanja i ucena: Životna agonija Tanje Savić|date=August 10, 2020|access-date=November 19, 2021|author=Stoisavljević, L.}}</ref> The couple separated in 2020, following a highly publicized custody battle, in which Savić accused her then-husband of relocating their children to Australia against her will. In March 2021, she was eventually reunited with her sons after close to a year of separation.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://nova.rs/zabava/showbiz/kraj-agonije-tanji-savic-muz-vratio-decu-nakon-godinu-dana/|title=Kraj agonije: Tanji Savić muž vratio decu nakon godinu dana|website=Nova.rs|language=sr|date=March 17, 2021|access-date=November 19, 2021|author=Stoisavljević, L.}}</ref>
