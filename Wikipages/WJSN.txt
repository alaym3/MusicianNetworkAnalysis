'''WJSN''' ({{korean|hangul=우주소녀|rr=Uju Sonyeo}}; {{zh|s=宇宙少女}}), also known as '''Cosmic Girls''', is a South Korean-Chinese [[girl group]] formed by [[Starship Entertainment]] and [[Yuehua Entertainment]]. The group debuted on February 25, 2016 with the extended play ''[[Would You Like?]]'' and with twelve members: [[Seola (singer)|Seola]], [[Wu Xuanyi|Xuanyi]], [[Bona (singer)|Bona]], [[Exy (rapper)|Exy]], Soobin, [[Luda (singer)|Luda]], [[Dawon (singer)|Dawon]], Eunseo, [[Cheng Xiao]], [[Meng Meiqi|Meiqi]], Yeoreum and Dayoung. In July 2016, WJSN added a thirteenth member, [[Yoo Yeon-jung|Yeonjung]], to the group.

=== 2020: Third concert, ''Neverland'', and first sub-unit WJSN Chocome ===
In 2020, WJSN had planned to hold their third concert titled ''WJSN Concert "Obliviate,"'' starting on February 22, 2020 at the [[Olympic Hall]] in Seoul, South Korea and finishing on March 22, 2020 at the Toyosu PIT in Tokyo, Japan.<ref name=3rdconcert/> However, due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic]], the concert was postponed.

WJSN released their eighth mini album ''[[Neverland (WJSN EP)|Neverland]]'', including the title track "Butterfly," on June 9.<ref>{{cite web|title=우주소녀, 6월 9일 '네버랜드' 컴백 확정[공식]|trans-title=Cosmic Girls, 'Neverland' comeback confirmed on June 9 [Official]|url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=108&aid=0002861938|website=Naver|publisher=MT Star News|language=ko|author=Yoon Sang-geun|date=May 11, 2020|access-date=July 26, 2021|archive-date=July 26, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210726140642/https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=108&aid=0002861938|url-status=live}}</ref>

On September 23, WJSN announced the formation of the new sub-unit WJSN Chocome, featuring members Soobin, Luda, Yeoreum, and Dayoung.  They released their debut single album ''Hmph!'', and its title track with the same name on October 7.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=609&aid=0000330612|title=우주소녀, 데뷔 4년6개월만 첫 유닛 '쪼꼬미' 결성 "10월7일 출격"(공식)|website=entertain.naver.com|language=ko|access-date=2020-09-23|archive-date=2020-11-01|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201101045551/https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=609&aid=0000330612|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=408&aid=0000102735|title='인간 비타민' 수빈-루다-여름-다영, 우주소녀 첫 유닛 '쪼꼬미'로 대활약 예고|website=entertain.naver.com|language=ko|access-date=2020-09-26|archive-date=2020-11-12|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201112031133/https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=408&aid=0000102735|url-status=live}}</ref>

=== 2021–present: ''Unnatural'' and second sub-unit WJSN The Black ===
WJSN released their ninth mini album ''[[Unnatural (EP)|Unnatural]],'' with the title track of the same name on March 31.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/108/0002939442|title=우주소녀, 9개월만 미니앨범 '언내추럴' 컴백 확정[공식]|website=news.naver.com|language=ko|access-date=2021-03-15|archive-date=2021-03-15|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210315002045/https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/108/0002939442|url-status=live}}</ref>

On April 26, WJSN announced the formation of their second sub-unit WJSN The Black, featuring members Seola, Exy, Bona, and Eunseo, along with announcing their first single album ''My Attitude'' and its title track "Easy", which was released on May 12.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=108&aid=0002950514|title=우주소녀, 5월 12일 新 유닛'우주소녀 더 블랙' 출격 [공식]|website=entertain.naver.com|language=ko|access-date=2021-04-27|archive-date=2021-04-27|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210427051551/https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=108&aid=0002950514|url-status=live}}</ref>

On September 23, WJSN released the [[Promotional recording|promotional single]] "Let Me In" through [[Universe (platform)#Universe Music|Universe Music]] for the mobile application Universe.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=144&aid=0000761503|title=우주소녀, 23일 신곡 '너의 세계로' 발매 [공식]|website=entertain.naver.com|language=ko|access-date=2021-09-13}}</ref>
