'''Lee Tae-yong''' ({{korean|이태용}}), professionally known by the mononym '''Taeyong''', is a South Korean rapper, singer, dancer, and songwriter. He is best known as a member and leader of the boy group [[NCT (group)|NCT]].<ref name=":12">{{Cite web|last=Aversa|first=Ralphie|title='I really listened to Eminem a lot': SuperM, K-pop's 'Avengers,' talk musical influences, new album|url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/entertainment/music/2020/09/24/superm-k-pop-avengers-interview-super-one-album/3503551001/|access-date=2020-09-24|website=USA TODAY|language=en|archive-date=September 25, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200925051751/https://www.usatoday.com/story/entertainment/music/2020/09/24/superm-k-pop-avengers-interview-super-one-album/3503551001/|url-status=live}}</ref> In 2016, he debuted in the group's first sub-unit, NCT U, and later that year went on to become the leader of the second sub-unit, [[NCT 127]]. As a songwriter, Taeyong has participated in writing over 30 songs in four languages, released mostly by NCT's various units.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=https://www.komca.or.kr/srch2/srch_01_popup_mem_right.jsp?S_MB_CD=10012932|title=Korea Music Copyright Association. "태용 on KOMCA"|website=KOMCA|access-date=2019-08-12|archive-date=June 5, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200605183648/https://www.komca.or.kr/srch2/srch_01_popup_mem_right.jsp?S_MB_CD=10012932|url-status=live}}</ref> In 2019, he debuted as a member of the South Korean supergroup [[SuperM]], which is produced by [[SM Entertainment]] in collaboration with [[Capitol Records]].<ref name=":3">{{Cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/jeffbenjamin/2019/08/07/super-m-the-avengers-of-k-pop-unveiled-by-sm-entertainment-and-capitol-music-group/|title=Super M, 'The Avengers Of K-Pop,' Unveiled By SM Entertainment And Capitol Music Group|last=Benjamin|first=Jeff|website=Forbes|language=en|access-date=2019-08-10|archive-date=August 9, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190809181715/https://www.forbes.com/sites/jeffbenjamin/2019/08/07/super-m-the-avengers-of-k-pop-unveiled-by-sm-entertainment-and-capitol-music-group/|url-status=live}}</ref> He also released his first solo single, "Long Flight", in 2019.<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/international/8521004/nct-taeyong-long-flight-video|title=NCT's Taeyong Takes to the Skies in Music Video for Solo Track 'Long Flight'|website=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|access-date=2019-08-10|archive-date=August 14, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190814084859/https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/international/8521004/nct-taeyong-long-flight-video|url-status=live}}</ref>

== Career ==

=== Early life ===
Lee Taeyong was born on July 1, 1995 in [[Gwanak District|Gwanak-gu]], [[Seoul]], [[South Korea]]. He graduated from the [[School of Performing Arts Seoul]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cosmo.ph/entertainment/super-m-kpop-supergroup-members-guide-a2520-20190808|title=The Beginner's Guide To All The Members Of New K-Pop Supergroup, Super M|last=Tabanera|first=Lily Grace|date=2019-08-09|website=Cosmpolitan|language=en-ph|access-date=2019-08-12|archive-date=August 12, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190812211806/https://www.cosmo.ph/entertainment/super-m-kpop-supergroup-members-guide-a2520-20190808|url-status=live}}</ref> 

=== 2012–2016: Pre-debut ===
In 2012, he was scouted on the street by an [[SM Entertainment]] recruiter and joined the company after successfully passing the audition by singing the national anthem.<ref name=":11">{{Cite web|url=https://www.dispatch.co.kr/1629279|title=SM 춤 선생님조차 "넌 안된다"며 포기했던 'NCT' 태용|date=2019-01-06|website=Dispatch.co.kr|language=ko-KR|access-date=2019-10-02|archive-date=June 5, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200605184001/https://www.dispatch.co.kr/1629279|url-status=live}}</ref> In the same year, he participated in the poster shooting of the SBS TV series "To the Beautiful You". 

On December 2, 2013, He was introduced as a member of [[SM Rookies]], a pre-debut team of trainees under SM Entertainment.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://news.chosun.com/site/data/html_dir/2013/12/03/2013120302934.html|title=SM루키즈 공개… 슈퍼주니어 규현의 그녀(?) As part of the SM Rookies program, in January 2014, he alongside now-NCT co-member Johnny, as well as now-[[Red Velvet (group)|Red Velvet]] members [[Irene (singer)|Irene]] and [[Seulgi (singer)|Seulgi]] appeared in 'The Celebrity magazine. 2014.02.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://kpopn.niusnews.com/=K2ujiu68|title=SMROOKIES 攜手拍畫報|newspaper=Kpopn 韓娛最前線 X Niusnews 妞新聞|accessdate=2017-02-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170112165655/http://kpopn.niusnews.com/=K2ujiu68|archive-date=2017-01-12|url-status=dead}}</ref> Later that month, he alongside Irene and Seulgi appeared in the 'OhBoy!' Magazine.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.koreastardaily.com/tc/news/38379|title=SM ROOKIES瑟琪、Irene及Tae Yong畫報公開|newspaper=韓星網|accessdate=2017-02-21|language=zh-hant|archive-date=2017-01-12|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170112160110/http://www.koreastardaily.com/tc/news/38379|url-status=live}}</ref> 

In July 2014, a video of Taeyong performing a fragment of his song "Open the Door" was published on SMTown's YouTube channel.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.allkpop.com/article/2014/07/smrookies-sr14bs-taeyong-impresses-with-his-rapping-skills-in-open-the-door-performance-clip|title=S.M.ROOKIES (SR14B)'s Taeyong impresses with his rapping skills in 'Open the Door' performance clip|date=2014-07-22|website=allkpop|access-date=2019-08-12|archive-date=August 9, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190809004212/https://www.allkpop.com/article/2014/07/smrookies-sr14bs-taeyong-impresses-with-his-rapping-skills-in-open-the-door-performance-clip|url-status=live}}</ref> 

Later that year, he alongside some of his co-members appeared on the [[Mnet (TV channel)|Mnet]]-produced ''[[Exo 90:2014]]'', a show starring labelmates [[Exo (band)|Exo]], where they get to do dance performances to 90s K-Pop songs, he also participated in the MV remake of Shinhwa 's "Yo!" and Fly To The Sky 's "Missing You".<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.osen.co.kr/article/G1109972619|title=[어저께TV] '엑소902014' K팝 시험, 90 반갑고 2014 새롭고|last=Kim|first=Sara|date=October 4, 2014|publisher=Osen|access-date=July 18, 2019|language=ko|archive-date=July 21, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190721150008/http://www.osen.co.kr/article/G1109972619|url-status=live}}</ref> 

In October of the same year, Taeyong featured on [[Red Velvet (group)|Red Velvet]]'s song "Be Natural", credited as "SR14B Taeyong".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.kpopstarz.com/articles/122550/20141009/red-velvet-be-natural-taeyong.htm|title=Red Velvet's 'Be Natural' Music Video Features Taeyong Of SMRookies|last=Kuang|first=Robert|date=2014-10-09|website=KpopStarz.com|access-date=August 13, 2019|archive-date=February 23, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200223063750/http://www.kpopstarz.com/articles/122550/20141009/red-velvet-be-natural-taeyong.htm|url-status=live}}</ref>

=== 2016–18: Debut with NCT, NCT U, NCT 127 ===
{{Main|NCT (group)|NCT 127}}
On April 3, 2016, Taeyong was confirmed as a member of boy group [[NCT (group)|NCT]], in the first sub-unit, NCT U, he officially debuted with NCT U's digital single, "[[The 7th Sense]]" released on April 8, in which he and Mark participated in songwriting and it was also the first release of NCT overall.<ref name=":5">{{Cite web|url=https://en.yna.co.kr/view/AEN20160404006300315|title=SM's neo-boy band 'NCT U' to launch this week|date=2016-04-04|website=Yonhap News Agency|language=en|access-date=2019-08-12|archive-date=April 11, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190411103323/https://en.yna.co.kr/view/AEN20160404006300315|url-status=live}}</ref>

On July 1, 2016, Taeyong was confirmed as a member and the leader of NCT's second sub-unit, [[NCT 127]], on July 6, 2016, he officially made his second debut with NCT 127's "Fire Truck", 3 days later, they released their first mini-album, ''[[NCT 127 (EP)|NCT #127]]'', in which Taeyong co-wrote 3 of the songs.<ref name=":6">{{Cite web|url=http://www.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20160705000588|title=New era of multinational K-pop groups dawns|last=Doo|first=Rumy|date=2016-07-05|website=Korea Herald|language=en|access-date=2019-08-12|archive-date=June 5, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200605183824/http://www.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20160705000588|url-status=live}}</ref><ref name=":7">{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/k-town/7445875/nct-127-world-heatseekers-album-charts|title=NCT Debuts on World, Heatseekers Albums Charts With 'NCT #127'|website=Billboard|access-date=2019-08-12|archive-date=July 14, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200714112712/https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/k-town/7445875/nct-127-world-heatseekers-album-charts|url-status=live}}</ref>

In January 2017, NCT 127 returned with the EP ''[[Limitless (NCT 127 EP)|Limitless]].''<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/k-town/7661729/nct-127-limitless-no-1-world-albums-chart|title=NCT 127 Scores No. 1 Spot on World Albums Chart With 'Limitless'|last=Benjamin|first=Jeff|date=2017-01-18|website=Billboard|access-date=2019-08-12|archive-date=July 25, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200725224053/https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/k-town/7661729/nct-127-limitless-no-1-world-albums-chart|url-status=live}}</ref> Taeyong participated in writing four songs for the album, with the song "Baby Don't Like It" marking the first time he was credited as a composer.<ref name=":1" /> In June, the group released their third EP, ''[[Cherry Bomb (EP)|Cherry Bomb]]''. All of the songs on the album, save one, were co-written by Taeyong.<ref name=":1" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20170614000936|title=Rising K-pop rookie NCT 127 drops powerful ‘Cherry Bomb’|last=Hong|first=Dam-young|date=2017-06-14|website=Korea Herald|language=en|access-date=2019-08-12|archive-date=June 27, 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180627005843/http://www.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20170614000936|url-status=live}}</ref> The title track, "Cherry Bomb", was later named as one of the best K-pop songs of the year by ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' and ''[[Idolator (website)|Idolator]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/events/year-in-music-2017/8070355/best-k-pop-songs-of-2017-top-20|title=The Best K-pop Songs of 2017: Critics' Picks|website=Billboard|access-date=2019-08-12|archive-date=April 13, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190413151648/https://www.billboard.com/articles/events/year-in-music-2017/8070355/best-k-pop-songs-of-2017-top-20|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.idolator.com/7672844/best-k-pop-songs-2017|title=From BTS To HyunA, The Best K-Pop Songs Of 2017|date=2017-12-22|website=idolator|access-date=2019-08-12|archive-date=March 28, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210328064540/https://www.idolator.com/7672844/best-k-pop-songs-2017|url-status=live}}</ref>

=== 2019–present: Solo work, SuperM ===
In April, NCT 127 released their first full-length Japanese album, ''[[Awaken (NCT 127 album)|Awaken]]'', for which Taeyong co-wrote the song "Lips".<ref name=":1" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/k-town/8502969/nct-127-wakey-wakey-japanese-music-video|title=NCT 127 'Wakey Wakey' Up the World in New Japanese Music Video: Watch|last=Herman|first=Tamar|date=2019-03-18|website=Billboard|access-date=2019-08-12|archive-date=March 30, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190330072352/https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/k-town/8502969/nct-127-wakey-wakey-japanese-music-video|url-status=live}}</ref>

In July 2019, Taeyong released his first solo song, "Long Flight", which he had written and co-composed, with an accompanying music video. The track served as the finale of Season 3 of the SM Station project.<ref name=":2" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/STATION.smtown/posts/625757737916042|title="SM 'STATION' season 3 will have its grand finale with 'Long Flight' by NCT TAEYONG..."|date=2019-07-18|website=SM Station on Facebook|language=en|access-date=2019-08-12|archive-date=September 27, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190927025416/https://www.facebook.com/STATION.smtown/posts/625757737916042|url-status=live}}</ref> The song debuted at #6 on Billboard World Digital Song Sales chart.<ref>{{cite web |title=Chart history: Taeyong |url=https://www.billboard.com/music/taeyong/chart-history |website=Billboard |access-date=2 July 2020 |archive-date=October 10, 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201010110022/https://www.billboard.com/music/taeyong/chart-history |url-status=live }}</ref>

On August 8, 2019, Taeyong was revealed as a member of [[SuperM]], a K-pop supergroup created by SM Entertainment in collaboration with Capitol Records. The group's promotions began in October, and were aimed at the American market.<ref name=":3" /><ref name=":4">{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/video/bbnews080819tiffanykpop2-8526949|title=SM Entertainment and Capitol Records Team Up to Launch Super M {{!}} Billboard News|website=Billboard|access-date=2019-08-10|archive-date=August 14, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190814084930/https://www.billboard.com/video/bbnews080819tiffanykpop2-8526949|url-status=live}}</ref> The group released their first EP, ''[[SuperM (EP)|SuperM]]'', for which Taeyong co-wrote the song "No Manners". The album debuted at #1 on Billboard 200 Album chart.<ref>{{cite web |first=Keith |last=Caulfield |title=SuperM Debuts at No. 1 on Billboard 200 Albums Chart With 'The 1st Mini Album' |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/business/chart-beat/8532879/superm-the-1st-mini-album-billboard-200-no-1 |website=Billboard |access-date=2 July 2020 |date=October 13, 2019 |archive-date=October 14, 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191014061116/https://www.billboard.com/articles/business/chart-beat/8532879/superm-the-1st-mini-album-billboard-200-no-1 |url-status=live }}</ref>

That same month, Taeyong and [[Punch (singer)|Punch]] collaborated on an OST song for ''[[Hotel del Luna]]'' , titled "Love del Luna", is the soundtrack's thirteenth (and final) part.<ref name=":10">{{Cite web|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/109/0004073447|title=NCT 태용X펀치, 오늘 '호텔 델루나' OST '러브 델루나' 공개.."대세들의 러브송"[공식]|website=OSEN|language=ko|access-date=2019-08-28|archive-date=August 28, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190828144050/https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/109/0004073447|url-status=live}}</ref>

In November 2019, Taeyong featured on [[Marteen]]'s "Mood".<ref>{{cite web |title=Marteen Releases MIXTAPE 8 Today |url=https://www.broadwayworld.com/bwwmusic/article/Marteen-Releases-MIXTAPE-8-Today-20191108 |website=Broadway World |access-date=2 July 2020 |archive-date=December 12, 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191212164557/https://www.broadwayworld.com/bwwmusic/article/Marteen-Releases-MIXTAPE-8-Today-20191108 |url-status=live }}</ref>

In March 2020, NCT 127 released their second full album, ''[[Neo Zone]]''. Taeyong participated in co-writing three songs for the album.<ref>{{cite web |title=NCT 127, 멤버 태용, 마크, 쟈니 정규 2집 수록곡 작사 참여! |url=https://smentertainment.com/PressCenter/Details/4139 |website=SM Entertainment |access-date=2 July 2020 |language=ko |date=February 21, 2020 |archive-date=July 2, 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200702163038/https://smentertainment.com/PressCenter/Details/4139 |url-status=live }}</ref> The album was commercially successful. It debuted at number five on the US [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]] and together with the repackage, ''Neo Zone: The Final Round'', sold more than one million copies in South Korea.<ref>{{cite web |first=Hugh |last=McIntyre |title=XXXTentacion, NCT 127 And Post Malone: 5 Big Moves On This Week’s Billboard 200 |url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/hughmcintyre/2020/03/20/xxxtentacion-nct-127-and-post-malone-5-big-moves-on-this-weeks-billboard-200/#45dff2b855cd |website=Forbes |access-date=2 July 2020 |date=March 20, 2020 |archive-date=July 2, 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200702222656/https://www.forbes.com/sites/hughmcintyre/2020/03/20/xxxtentacion-nct-127-and-post-malone-5-big-moves-on-this-weeks-billboard-200/#45dff2b855cd |url-status=live }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=NCT127, 新밀리언셀러 등극‥정규 2집 음반 판매량 121만장 돌파(공식) |url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=112&aid=0003300304 |website=Kpop Herald |access-date=2 July 2020 |language=ko |date=May 21, 2020 |archive-date=June 5, 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200605195431/https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=112&aid=0003300304 |url-status=live }}</ref>

In September 2020, SuperM released their first full length album, ''[[Super One (album)|Super One]]'', for which Taeyong co-wrote the song "Together At Home".<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-09-20|title=Press Release: TAEYONG & MARK participate in the lyrics of "Together At Home", one of the B-sides of SuperM's 1st Album!|url=https://smentertainment.com/PressCenter/Details/4943|access-date=2020-09-24|website=smentertainment.com|archive-date=March 27, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210327172239/https://smentertainment.com/PressCenter/Details/4943|url-status=live}}</ref> That same month, NCT's second large scale project uniting all of its subgroups, ''[[NCT 2020 Resonance|NCT 2020: Resonance]]'', was announced, and Taeyong was confirmed as the overall leader of the group.<ref name=":12" />

On March 15 2021, Taeyong opened his own SoundCloud account and released a new demo single "Dark Clouds"<ref>{{Citation|title=먹구름 (Dark Clouds)|url=https://soundcloud.com/taeoxo/dark-clouds|language=en|access-date=2021-04-18|archive-date=April 5, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210405023842/https://soundcloud.com/taeoxo/dark-clouds|url-status=live}}</ref> and "Dark Clouds (Remix)".<ref>{{Citation|title=먹구름 (Dark Clouds) (Remix)|url=https://soundcloud.com/taeoxo/dark-clouds-remix|language=en|access-date=2021-04-18|archive-date=April 18, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210418062246/https://soundcloud.com/taeoxo/dark-clouds-remix|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=NCT’s Taeyong opens SoundCloud account, releases new song |url=https://www.nme.com/news/music/nct-taeyong-soundcloud-dark-clouds-2900837 |website=NME.com |access-date=6 June 2021}}</ref> Since then, he has released further demo singles such as "GTA1" and "GTA2", "Blue", "Monroe" (featuring [[Baekhyun]] of [[Exo (band)|Exo]]), "Rose" (featuring [[Seulgi (singer)|Seulgi]] of [[Red Velvet (group)|Red Velvet]]) and "Swimming Pool".
