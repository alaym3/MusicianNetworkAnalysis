'''Will Gold'''<ref name="20Min">{{cite web |url=https://www.20min.ch/fr/story/un-hit-avec-une-captivante-romance-a-sens-unique-826679741000 |title=Wilbur Soot: Un hit avec une captivante romance à sens unique |website=[[20 minutes (France)|20 minutes]] |language=French |publisher=Schibsted |date=January 6, 2021 |access-date=20 January 2021}}</ref> (born 14 September  1996), known professionally as '''Wilbur Soot''', is a British [[Internet celebrity|internet personality]], [[Twitch (service)|Twitch]] [[Online streamer|streamer]], and singer-songwriter. He first became known in 2017 for his work with the group comedy YouTube channel SootHouse, where he made recurring appearances and was the lead editor and a co-founder. He later started his own channel, Wilbur Soot, on 29 March 2019. He released his first single, "The 'Nice Guy' Ballad", in January 2018.<ref name="TheVersed">{{cite web |last=Bovey |first=LJ |url=https://www.theversed.com/97844/gamer-focus-wilbur-soot/ |title=Gamer Focus: Who Is Wilbur Soot? |website=The Versed |publisher=Clickon Media |date=23 October 2019 |access-date=28 December 2020}}</ref> His sixth single, "[[Your New Boyfriend]]", peaked at No. 65 on the [[UK Singles Chart]].<ref name="OCCLead">{{cite web |url=https://www.officialcharts.com/artist/59206/wilbur-soot/ |title=Wilbur Soot – Full Official Chart History |publisher=[[Official Charts Company]] |access-date=26 January 2021}}</ref> He is also part of the English rock band [[Lovejoy (band)|Lovejoy]].

== YouTube career ==
Gold became well known as an editor for the YouTube group channel SootHouse, which was founded by Gold and some of his friends. The channel consisted mostly of [[reaction video]]s, with its members discussing [[meme]]s and [[life hack]]s, among other topics.<ref name="TheVersed" />

He created his main channel, Wilbur Soot, on 29 March 2019. The channel features content pertaining to video games, most commonly, the 2011 [[sandbox game|sandbox]] game ''[[Minecraft]]''. Gold also actively live streams on Twitch,<ref name="20Min" /> where he has amassed over 4 million followers as of September 2021, making him the 37th [[List of most-followed Twitch channels|most-followed channel]] on the platform.<ref name="SocialBlade">{{cite web |url=https://socialblade.com/twitch/top/100 |title=Top 100 Most Followed Twitch Accounts (Sorted By Followers Count) |website=[[Social Blade]] |access-date=12 March 2021}}</ref>

In 2020, Gold joined the [[Role-playing|roleplay]]-focused ''Minecraft'' server [[Dream SMP]], run by the eponymous YouTuber [[Dream (YouTuber)|Dream]]. There, Gold founded the fictional nation of L'Manberg and eventually became a lead writer for the stories featured in the server.<ref name="Insider">{{cite web |last=Asarch |first=Steven |url=https://www.insider.com/dream-changed-face-of-minecraft-content-with-his-smp-series-2021-2 |title=Inside YouTube's Minecraft boom: how an exclusive roleplay server helped a group of streamers dominate the platform's charts |website=[[Insider Inc.|Insider]] |date=February 16, 2021 |access-date=March 2, 2021}}</ref> Gold has also competed in multiple ''Minecraft'' tournaments, including MC Championships and [[Minecraft Monday]].<ref name="MCC">{{cite web |last=Michael |first=Cale |url=https://dotesports.com/streaming/news/every-mc-championship-winner |title=Every MC Championship winner |website=[[Dot Esports]] |publisher=[[Gamurs]] |date=26 September 2020 |access-date=19 January 2021}}</ref><ref name="MCMonday">{{cite web |last=Michael |first=Cale |url=https://dotesports.com/streaming/news/minecraft-monday-week-12-scores-and-standings |title=Minecraft Monday week 12 scores and standings |website=[[Dot Esports]] |publisher=[[Gamurs]] |date=9 September 2019 |access-date=21 January 2020}}</ref> In January 2021, Gold was one of the eight participants of the chess tournament BlockChamps, hosted by WFM [[Alexandra Botez]] and her sister, Andrea. Gold was eliminated in the tournament's first round, when he lost to fellow YouTuber and Twitch streamer GeorgeNotFound.<ref name="Chess.com">{{cite web |last=Copeland |first=Sam |url=https://www.chess.com/news/view/blockchamps-sets-chess-viewership-record |title=BlockChamps Sets Chess Viewership Record |website=[[Chess.com]] |date=17 January 2021 |access-date=21 January 2020}}</ref>

On 3 April 2021, Gold started a podcast entitled ''Hey and Stuff''.<ref name="HeyAndStuff">{{cite AV media |date=3 April 2021 |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SFsY9yobwXE |title=Roleplay |publisher=[[Hey and Stuff]] |via=[[YouTube]] |access-date=19 September 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210520003447/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SFsY9yobwXE |archive-date=20 May 2021 |url-status=live}}</ref>

== Musical career ==
Gold released his first single, "The 'Nice Guy' Ballad",<ref name="TheVersed" /> in January 2018. Gold first charted with his sixth single, "[[Your New Boyfriend]]", released in December 2020, which peaked on the UK Singles Chart at number 65.<ref name="OCCLead" /> The song also appeared on the [[UK Indie Chart]] and the [[Irish Singles Chart]], where it peaked at numbers 10 and 100, respectively.<ref name="Indie">{{cite web |url=https://www.officialcharts.com/charts/independent-singles-chart/20201218/130/ |title=Official Independent Singles Chart Top 50: 18 December 2020 – 24 December 2020 |publisher=[[Official Charts Company]] |access-date=19 January 2020}}</ref><ref name="IRMA">{{cite web |url=http://irish-charts.com/showinterpret.asp?interpret=Wilbur+Soot |title=Discography Wilbur Soot |publisher=[[Irish Recorded Music Association]] |access-date=23 October 2021}}</ref> Gold has also appeared on several artist charts, including ''[[Billboard charts|Billboard]]''{{'s}} [[Emerging Artists]] Chart and ''[[Rolling Stone]]''{{'s}} Top Breakthrough Chart.<ref name="EmergingArtists">{{cite web |url=https://www.billboard.com/charts/emerging-artists/2020-12-26 |title=Emerging Artists: The Week of December 26, 2020 |website=[[Billboard charts|Billboard]] |access-date=19 January 2020}}</ref><ref name="RollingStone">{{cite web |last=Blake |first=Emily |url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-news/vory-dj-scheme-trey-lewis-breakthrough-chart-1110882/ |title=RS Charts: Vory, DJ Scheme and Trey Lewis Lead Breakthrough 25 Chart |website=[[Rolling Stone]] |date=7 January 2021 |access-date=19 January 2020}}</ref>

He formed the band [[Lovejoy (band)|Lovejoy]] in 2021. Gold is the singer, songwriter, and guitarist; Joe Goldsmith the guitarist and songwriter; Mark Boardman the drummer; and Ash Kabosu the bassist. Their first release, on 9 May 2021, was the EP ''Are You Alright?''<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.sportskeeda.com/minecraft/tommyinnit-karl-jacobs-join-minecraft-community-celebrating-launch-wilbur-soot-s-band-lovejoy|title=TommyInnit, Karl Jacobs, and more join the Minecraft community in celebrating launch of Wilbur Soot's band, 'Lovejoy'|website=[[Sportskeeda]]|last=Periwal|first=Saahil Agnelo|date=2021-05-10|access-date=2021-05-15}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title="What a legend!": Wilbur Soot praises Minecraft streamer GeorgeNotFound for his "one-take" performance in "Taunt" music video|url=https://www.sportskeeda.com/minecraft/what-legend-wilbur-soot-praises-minecraft-streamer-georgenotfound-one-take-performance-taunt-music-video|website=[[Sportskeeda]]|last=Periwal|first=Saahil Agnelo|date=2021-05-22|access-date=2021-05-24}}</ref> On 20 May 2021, they debuted on ''[[Billboard charts|Billboard]]''{{'}}s Emerging Artists chart at number 10.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Emerging Artists Chart|url=https://www.billboard.com/charts/emerging-artists/2021-05-22|access-date=2021-05-29|website=[[Billboard charts|Billboard]]|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210529103528/https://www.billboard.com/charts/emerging-artists/2021-05-22|archive-date=2021-05-29|url-status=live}}</ref>
