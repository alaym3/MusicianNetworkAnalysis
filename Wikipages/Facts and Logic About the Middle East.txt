'''Facts and Logic About the Middle East''' ('''FLAME''') is a [[Non-profit organization|non-profit]] pro-[[Israel]] organization based in [[San Francisco, California|San Francisco]], [[California]]. ''[[The Los Angeles Times]]'' described ''FLAME'' as "an organization that must literally buy its way into print."<ref>{{cite news |newspaper=[[The Los Angeles Times]]
|url=https://www.latimes.com/archives/la-xpm-2005-aug-06-oe-miles6-story.html
|title=A peace no one wins |date=August 6, 2006}}</ref> Founder Gerardo Joffe's opinion letters are periodically printed (at no charge).<ref>{{cite news |newspaper=[[Wall Street Journal]]
   |url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/SB863043583466329000
   |title=Israel Isn't on 'Welare'}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |newspaper=[[Wall Street Journal]]
|url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/SB863043583466329000
|title=When Silence Equaled Death |date=May 8, 1997}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |newspaper=[[Wall Street Journal]]
        |url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/SB898893109620916000
        |title=What's to Understand? |date=June 29, 1998}}</ref>

Their ads, they state, are meant as an explanation for "the proverbial man from Mars" regarding various aspects of Arab/Israeli issues.
