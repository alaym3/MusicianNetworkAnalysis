'''Zachary "Zach" James''' (born December 7, 1981) is an American actor and singer. He is best known for creating the role of [[Lurch (The Addams Family)|Lurch]] in [[The Addams Family (musical)|''The Addams Family'']] on Broadway, Abraham Lincoln in the world premiere Philip Glass opera, ''[[The Perfect American]]'', Amenhotep III in the Olivier Award-winning production of [[Akhnaten (opera)|''Akhnaten'']] and for appearances on the television shows, ''[[30 Rock]]'' and ''[[Murphy Brown]]''. Zachary was named the Most Innovative Opera Singer of 2019 by The Classical Post, Breakout Opera Artist of 2019 by Verismo Magazine and was identified as an industry leader and invited to be an official ambassador for [[Opera America]]. He has sung with some of the world's top opera companies and orchestras including [[English National Opera]], [[Los Angeles Opera|LA Opera]], [[Teatro Real]], [[New York Philharmonic|The New York Philharmonic]], [[Philadelphia Orchestra]], [[Opera Philadelphia]] and [[NHK Symphony Orchestra|NHK Symphony Orchestra Tokyo]] and made his [[Metropolitan Opera]] debut in 2019 in ''[[Akhnaten (opera)|Akhnaten]].'' His debut visual album, ''Call Out'', has played 31 film festivals worldwide and received the highly commended designation from London’s Classical Music Digital Awards. His solo show, ''On Broadway'', won him [[BroadwayWorld|BroadwayWorld’s]] Vocalist of the Decade and Performer of the Decade awards for the years 2010-2020. The show was filmed live at Chris’s Jazz Cafe in Philadelphia, produced by [[Des Moines Metro Opera]], and released as an album in 2021. Zachary is the host and creator of the comedy talkshow, ''What Happened Was...'' airing every Friday on TDO Network, produced by [[Dallas Opera]].

==Biography==

===Early life===
James was born in [[Providence, Rhode Island]] and grew up in [[Spring Hill, Florida]]. The son of a professional jazz guitarist, James played percussion and served as drum major in his high school marching band. Although his first goal was to become a band director, he had a longing for musical theater.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.zachjames.com/news.html |title=Zachary James · News |publisher=Zachjames.com |access-date=2010-06-18}}</ref> James, inspired by actor [[Nathan Lane]],<ref name="Mike">Mellini, Michael. [http://www.broadway.com/shows/the-addams-family/buzz/152800/the-addams-familys-zachary-james-on-serving-up-laughs-as-lurch/ "The Addams Family's Zachary James on Serving Up Laughs as Lurch"] broadway.com</ref> majored in [[musical theatre]] at [[Ithaca College]] and later moved to New York City.<ref name="play"/><ref>[http://moviedearest.blogspot.com/2010/04/reverends-interview-lurch-speaksand.html "Lurch Speaks... and sings!"], moviedearest.com</ref>

===Career===
Zachary James created the role of [[Lurch (The Addams Family)|Lurch]] in the Broadway musical production of ''[[The Addams Family (musical)|The Addams Family]]''. James appeared in the Original Broadway Cast of ''[[Coram Boy (play)|Coram Boy]]'' and also in the original cast of the [[Tony Award]]-winning [[Broadway theatre|Broadway]] revival of ''[[South Pacific (musical)|South Pacific]]''.<ref>{{cite web|author=The Broadway League |url=http://www.ibdb.com/person.php?id=457605 |title=Zachary James &#124; IBDB: The official source for Broadway Information |publisher=IBDB |access-date=2010-06-18}}</ref> New York credits include the role of [[Oberon]] in the world premiere of Kristin Hevner's ''Il Sogno''. As an operatic singer, he created the role of [[Abraham Lincoln]] in [[Philip Glass]]' opera ''[[The Perfect American]]'' at the [[Teatro Real]] in Madrid which was broadcast on Medici TV and created the role of Terry in the world premiere of ''[[Breaking the Waves (opera)|Breaking the Waves]]'' by [[Missy Mazzoli]] for [[Opera Philadelphia]]. He created the speaking role of The Scribe/[[Amenhotep III]] in Phelim McDermott's [[Laurence Olivier Award]] winning 2016 production of [[Philip Glass]]’s ''[[Akhnaten (opera)|Akhnaten]]'' at [[English National Opera]]. He reprised the role in the production’s revivals at the [[Los Angeles Opera]], English National Opera in 2019 and the [[Metropolitan Opera]] in November and December 2019. James played the role of John Claggart in ''[[Billy Budd (opera)|Billy Budd]]'' at [[Des Moines Metro Opera]] in 2017. The production was broadcast on [[Iowa Public Television]] and won an [[Emmy Award]]. He also performed with English National Opera, Los Angeles Opera, The Philadelphia Orchestra, NHK Symphony Orchestra Tokyo, The New York Philharmonic, Des Moines Metro Opera, Arizona Opera, Virginia Opera, Nashville Opera, Fort Worth Opera, Ash Lawn Opera, Knoxville Opera, Central City Opera, Anchorage Opera, Opera Roanoke, Union Avenue Opera, Opera Ithaca, Shreveport Opera, American Lyric Theater, New York City Ballet and Australia's Opera Queensland.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://chicago.broadwayworld.com/article/THE_ADDAMS_FAMILYs_Zachary_James_Shares_His_IMBECILE_DAMOUR_Oct_25th_20091018 |title='THE ADDAMS FAMILY's' Zachary James Shares His 'IMBECILE D'AMOUR' Oct. 25th 2009/10/18 |publisher=Chicago.broadwayworld.com |access-date=2010-06-18}}</ref>
