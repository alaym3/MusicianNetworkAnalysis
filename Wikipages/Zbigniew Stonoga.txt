'''Zbigniew Stonoga''' ({{IPA-pl|ˈzbiɡɲɛf stɔˈnɔɡa|pron}}; born 17 October 1974 in [[Ozimek]])<ref name="dziennik1">{{cite news|title=Już się powygłupiałem, pokląłem. Tak wojuje Zbigniew Stonoga. SYLWETKA |url = http://wiadomosci.dziennik.pl/wydarzenia/artykuly/475076,zbigniew-stonoga-kontra-sokolowski-i-sld-wojny-zbigniewa-stonogi.html |access-date = 18 June 2015| date = 9 June 2015}}</ref> is a Polish entrepreneur, businessman, activist and philanthropist who gained notoriety as a [[whistleblower]] for the publication of the investigation files concerning the [[Wprost#Role in the 2014 government bugging scandal|Polish bugging scandal (2014–2015)]], known as Waitergate, and for anti-government activity. Founder of the short-lived [[Stonoga Party Poland]] (also known in English as The Centipede Party, since ''Stonoga'' translates as "centipede").<ref>{{cite news| url = http://telewizjarepublika.pl/stonoga-partia-polska-rejestracja-nowej-formacji-politycznej-moze-nastapic-lada-dzien,20590.htm| title = "Stonoga Partia Polska. Rejestracja nowej formacji politycznej może nastąpić lada dzień". | date = 10 June 2015 | access-date  = 18 June 2015 }}</ref> Founder of ''Gazeta Stonoga''. Convicted for financial fraud.

==Biography ==

=== Academic background ===
After graduating from an electrotechnical college, he started higher education which he did not complete. Claims to have studied law at the [[University of Warsaw]]; however, the University denies such person has ever attended the university.<ref name="dziennik1"/>

=== Political career ===
In 2015 he ran in Polish parliamentary elections in which his party, Stonoga Party Poland, scored 0.4% (40 000 voters). After the results were announced, furious Stonoga uploaded a video to the internet in which he expressed his frustration with those who did not vote for him or for the [[Coalition for the Renewal of the Republic - Liberty and Hope]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4qARYM_ACOk|title=Zbigniew Stonoga obraża Polaków: "WAS POLACZKI TYLKO DO AUSCHWITZ WYPROWADZIĆ"|date=2015-08-31|publisher=YouTube|access-date=2017-04-20}}</ref>

His language is vulgar and direct, attracting the attention of young audiences.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://kontrrewolucja.net/wiadomosci/stonoga-szokuje-wulgarnie-wyzywa-politykow-tym-prezydenta|title=Stonoga znowu szokuje. Wulgarnie wyzywa polityków, w tym prezydenta (WIDEO)|date=2017-04-06|website=Kontrrewolucja.net|language=pl-PL|access-date=2019-12-30}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.pch24.pl/glupie-polaczki--was-tylko-do-auschwitz-zaprowadzac---sfrustrowany-stonoga-o-porazce-wyborczej--w-kolejnym-nagraniu-przeprosil-za-slowa,39007,i.html|title="Głupie polaczki, was tylko do Auschwitz zaprowadzać" – sfrustrowany Stonoga o porażce wyborczej. W kolejnym nagraniu przeprosił za słowa|website=Pch24.pl|language=pl|access-date=2019-12-30}}</ref>

==  Lawsuits ==
In the 2000s Stonoga was named in a total of 89 criminal lawsuits.

In 2004 he was sentenced to 3.5 years' imprisonment for financial irregularities. In 2005 his sentence was quashed.<ref>{{cite news|title=Już się powygłupiałem, pokląłem. Tak wojuje Zbigniew Stonoga. SYLWETKA |url = http://wiadomosci.dziennik.pl/wydarzenia/artykuly/475076,zbigniew-stonoga-kontra-sokolowski-i-sld-wojny-zbigniewa-stonogi.html |language=pl| access-date = 18 June 2015| date = 9 June 2015}}</ref>

By 2015, he was named in 61 criminal cases related mostly to financial fraud and insulting public servants; he was sentenced to a year in prison for fraud on a private car sale.<ref>{{cite news| url=http://www.polityka.pl/tygodnikpolityka/kraj/1623573,1,stonoga-raczej-do-wiezienia-niz-do-sejmu.read | title = Stonoga raczej do więzienia niż do Sejmu | language=pl}}</ref>
