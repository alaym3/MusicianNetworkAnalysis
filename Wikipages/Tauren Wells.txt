'''Tauren Gabriel Wells''' is an American [[Contemporary Christian music|Christian pop]]-[[Christian rock|rock]] and [[Christian contemporary R&B|R&B]] artist and a [[worship leader]] at [[Lakewood Church]], [[Houston, Texas]]. He is the former frontman for the American Christian pop rock band [[Royal Tailor]], which produced two albums over a five-year career, garnering two [[Grammy]] nominations and a New Artist of the Year award from the [[Gospel Music Association Dove Awards]]. In 2014, Wells, along with his wife, launched the Prisma Worship Arts School, a private music academy with multiple locations in the Houston area.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.ccmmagazine.com/news/royal-tailor-front-man-tauren-wells-announces-solo-project/|title=Royal Tailor front man Tauren Wells announces solo project|last=News|first=CCM|date=August 19, 2016|work=CCM Magazine|access-date=June 18, 2017}}</ref>

== Biography ==
Wells was a founding member of [[Royal Tailor]] along with DJ Cox and Blake Hubbard while they were attending [[Indiana Bible College]] in 2004.<ref name="AllMusic">{{cite web|title=Royal Tailor : Biography & History|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/royal-tailor-mn0001599368/biography|first=Chrysta|last=Cherrie|publisher=[[AllMusic]]|accessdate=February 2, 2017}}</ref> Jarred Ingram later joined.

In 2016, Wells signed to [[Provident Label Group]] - [[Reunion Records]] as a solo artist,<ref>{{cite web|title=Tauren Wells Steps into New Solo Project, Out of Royal Tailor|url=http://www.newreleasetoday.com/news_detail.php?newsid=1533/|publisher=NewReleaseToday.com}}</ref> and he released his first solo single, "Undefeated", in May 2016.<ref>{{Cite web|title=News: Royal Tailor Frontman Tauren Wells Announces Solo Project, Signs Deal With Provident Label Group {{!}} Freeccm.com|url=https://freeccm.com/2016/08/25/news-royal-tailor-frontman-tauren-wells-announces-solo-project-signs-deal-with-provident-label-group/|access-date=August 7, 2020}}</ref> It marked a distinct shift from the glossy pop rock sound of Royal Tailor to a more electronic sound.{{citation needed|date=June 2017}} It also featured Reach Records rapper [[KB (rapper)|KB]] and served as the soundtrack for [[Dude Perfect]]'s "World Records Edition" video on [[YouTube]].{{citation needed|date=June 2017}} Wells released his second solo single, "Love Is Action", in August 2016<ref>{{cite web|title=Former Royal Tailor Frontman TAUREN WELLS Goes Solo with New Single Love Is Action|url=https://pathmegazine.com/news/christian-contemporary/former-royal-tailor-frontman-tauren-wells-goes-solo-with-new-single-love-is-action/|publisher=PathMegazine.com|date=August 22, 2016}}</ref> and his first solo EP, ''Undefeated EP'', on September 12, 2016.<ref>{{cite web|title=Tauren Wells Releases Debut Solo EP Undefeated|url=http://www.newreleasetoday.com/news_detail.php?newsid=1776/|publisher=NewReleaseToday.com}}</ref> "Love Is Action" was No. 1 on Christian Hot AC/CHR Radio for nine weeks.

In January 2017, Wells released "[[Hills and Valleys]]", which reached No. 8 on the Christian Airplay Chart and No. 3 on the Hot Christian Songs chart.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/7800163/american-idol-christian-airplay-mandisa-jasmine-murray|title='Idol' Alums Mandisa & Jasmine Murray Shine in Christian Airplay Chart's Top 10|work=Billboard|date=May 17, 2017|first=Jim|last=Asker|accessdate=December 7, 2018}}</ref> The full-length ''Hills and Valleys'' was released digitally on June 23 and was released physically on July 21.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/hills-and-valleys-deluxe-edition/1249506638|title=Hills and Valleys (Deluxe Edition) - Tauren Wells|date=June 23, 2017|publisher=iTunes Store|accessdate=December 7, 2018}}</ref> The fourth single, "When We Pray", was released on October 20, 2017;<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9YZZzgJB33E|title=Tauren Wells - When We Pray (Official Music Video)|date=October 20, 2017|publisher=iTunes Store|accessdate=December 7, 2018}}</ref> it peaked at No. 7.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/8096330/mandisa-tobymac-top-10-hot-christian-songs-chart|title=Mandisa & tobyMac Hit Top 10 on Hot Christian Songs Chart|date=January 25, 2018|work=Billboard|accessdate=December 7, 2018}}</ref> He was featured on [[Crowder (musician)|Crowder]]'s "[[All My Hope]]".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7tElvdnId4Q|title=Crowder - All My Hope ft. Tauren Wells|date=October 19, 2018|via=YouTube|accessdate=December 7, 2018}}</ref> The single peaked at No. 3 on the Christian Songs chart and No. 1 on the Christian Airplay chart, becoming his first number one.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/8248053/crowder-tauren-wells-koryn-hawthorne-christian-gospel-charts|title=Crowder, Tauren Wells & Koryn Hawthorne Hit No. 1 on Christian/Gospel Charts|date=March 15, 2018|work=Billboard|accessdate=December 7, 2018}}</ref>

In 2018, Wells received [[Grammy]] nominations for Best Contemporary Christian Performance/Song and Contemporary Christian Album of the Year, but did not win either.

In 2018, he became a [[worship leader]] at [[Lakewood Church]], [[Houston, Texas]].<ref>Jeannie Law, [https://www.christianpost.com/news/singer-tauren-wells-stepping-into-call-to-preach-leading-worship-at-joel-osteens-lakewood-church.html Singer Tauren Wells Stepping Into Call to Preach, Begins Speaking at Joel Osteen's Lakewood Church], christianpost.com, USA, September 26, 2018</ref>

The fifth single, "[[Known (song)|Known]]", was released on June 23, 2018.<ref name="Genius">{{cite web |url= https://genius.com/Tauren-wells-known-lyrics |title= Known - Tauren Wells|date= June 23, 2018 |publisher=iTunes Store|access-date = December 7, 2018}}</ref> The song peaked at No. 3 on the US [[Hot Christian Songs]] chart, becoming his fourth Top 10 single from that chart.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/8479477/elevation-worship-houghton-debut-christian-gospel-charts |title= Elevation Worship & Houghton Debut on Christian, Gospel Charts|date= October 11, 2018 |work= Billboard|access-date = December 7, 2018}}</ref> It was a huge success on Christian radio, reaching number one on multiple charts including Christian Airplay and Christian AC Songs. It was nominated for the [[61st Annual Grammy Awards|2019 Grammy Award]] for [[Grammy Award for Best Contemporary Christian Music Performance/Song|Best Contemporary Christian Music Performance/Song]].<ref name=Grammy2019>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/grammys/8489045/2019-grammy-nominees-full-list|title=Grammys 2019 Nominees: The Complete List|work=Billboard|date=December 7, 2018|first=Joe|last=Lynch|accessdate=December 7, 2018}}</ref> It was also nominated for Top Christian Song at the [[2019 Billboard Music Awards]].

In late 2019, he released "Miracle", "Perfect Peace", and "Like You Love Me" leading up to ''Citizen of Heaven'', which was released in 2020. It also featured the songs "Famous for (I Believe)", "Millionare (Good Like That)", and "Close".{{citation needed|date=October 2020}}

== Personal life ==

Wells served on the staff of his father-in-law's church in Houston (Royalwood) until 2018. Wells and his wife started the Prisma Worship Arts School, to train musicians.<ref>{{cite web|title=Royal Tailor Frontman Tauren Wells Announces Solo Project, Signs Deal With Provident Label Group|url=http://www.gospelmusic.org/news-royal-tailor-frontman-tauren-wells-announces-solo-project-signs-deal-with-provident-label-group/|publisher=GospelMusic.org|date=August 19, 2016}}</ref> Through the school, Prisma offers private coaching in piano, drums, guitar, bass, voice, violin, viola, and cello.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.prismaworship.com|title=prisma|website=prisma|access-date=June 18, 2017}}</ref>
