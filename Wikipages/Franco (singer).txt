'''Franco Javier Iglesias''', better known simply as '''Franco''' (born 14 December 1959) is a Cuban Latin pop singer.<ref>{{cite web |title=Franco Iglesias llega a Guadalajara para promocionar sus nuevos sencillos |url=http://udgtv.com/noticias/franco-iglesias-llega-gdl-promocionar-nuevos-sencillos/ |website=udgtv.com |accessdate=19 January 2020 |language=es |date=22 October 2019}}</ref> He achieved two number-one songs on the [[Hot Latin Songs|''Billboard'' Hot Latin Songs]] chart with his cover of "[[Tutta la vita|Toda la Vida]] in 1986 and "[[María (Franco song)|María]]" in 1988.<ref>{{cite web |title=Franco Chart History |url=https://www.billboard.com/music/franco/chart-history |website=Billboard |accessdate=19 January 2020}}</ref> The latter song received a [[Premio Lo Nuestro|Lo Nuestro]] nomination for [[Lo Nuestro Award for Pop Song of the Year|Pop Song of the Year]] in 1989.<ref name="Nominees-1989">{{Cite news | last1 = Coto | first1 = Juan Carlos | title = Univision Launches Latin Music Awards | newspaper = [[The Miami Herald]] |publisher = [[The McClatchy Company]] | date = 28 May 1989  }}</ref>
