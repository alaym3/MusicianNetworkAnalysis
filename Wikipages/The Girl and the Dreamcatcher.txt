'''The Girl and the Dreamcatcher''' was an American pop duo from [[Los Angeles, California]], which consisted of ''[[Liv and Maddie]]'' co-stars [[Dove Cameron]] ("the Girl") and [[Ryan McCartan]] ("the Dreamcatcher"). Their debut EP, ''Negatives'', was released on GDC Records on July 29, 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/7502827/the-girl-and-the-dreamcatcher/biography|title=The Girl and the Dreamcatcher ‚Äì Biography|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|author=Timothy Monger|accessdate=September 14, 2016}}</ref>

=== 2016: ''Negatives'' ===
In January 2016, Cameron and McCartan announced that their next single, titled "Glowing in the Dark", would be released on January 29, 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.twistmagazine.com/posts/the-girl-and-the-dreamcatcher-announce-new-single-music-video-87614|title=The Girl and the Dreamcatcher Announce New Single & Music Video|work=Twist|author=Jennifer Maldonado|date=January 15, 2016|access-date=August 20, 2016}}</ref> On April 8, 2016, the band officially released their song "Someone You Like", the acoustic version of which was first released on their YouTube channel in 2014.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.j-14.com/posts/the-girl-and-the-dreamcatcher-drop-someone-you-like-98274|title=The Girl And The Dreamcatcher Drop "Someone You Like"|work=[[J-14 (magazine)|J-14]]|author=Gisselle Gaitan|date=April 9, 2016|access-date=August 20, 2016}}</ref> On June 16, 2016, the music video for their next single, "Make You Stay", was exclusively released on ''[[People (magazine)|People]]'',<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.people.com/article/dove-cameron-ryan-mccartan-make-you-stay-music-video-premiere|title=The Girl and the Dreamcatcher (Dove Cameron and Fianc√© Ryan McCartan) Debut Adorable 'Make You Stay' Music Video|work=[[People (magazine)|People]]|author=Jeff Nelson|date=June 16, 2016|access-date=August 20, 2016}}</ref> followed by the song's official release the next day.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.j-14.com/posts/the-girl-and-the-dreamcatcher-s-make-you-stay-music-video-105589|title=The Girl and the Dreamcatcher's "Make You Stay" Video Features a Cameo From One of Your Disney Channel Favorites|work=J-14|author=Brie Hiramine|date=June 17, 2016|access-date=August 20, 2016}}</ref>

In May 2016, the band announced that they would be releasing their first EP, titled ''Negatives'',<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.j-14.com/posts/the-girl-and-the-dreamcatcher-are-releasing-a-new-ep-101428|title=The Girl and the Dreamcatcher Are Releasing a New EP|work=J-14|author=Gisselle Gaitan|date=May 7, 2016|access-date=August 20, 2016}}</ref> whose release date was later revealed to be July 29, 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://popcrush.com/the-girl-and-the-dreamcatcher-make-you-stay-behind-the-scenes-video/|title=The Girl and The Dreamcatcher‚Äôs ‚ÄòMake You Stay': Behind The Scenes (Exclusive)|work=PopCrush|author=Bradley Stern|date=July 6, 2016|access-date=August 20, 2016}}</ref> It was made available for pre-order on [[iTunes]] on July 9, 2016.<ref>{{cite tweet|user=tgatdc|author=The Girl and the Dreamcatcher|number=751204082380005377|title=Pre order for the #NegativesEP is avail TOMORROW on @iTunes! In October 2016, McCartan announced the breakup of their band, as well as their relationship, as he said, "Dove has decided this relationship isn't what she wants. We still love each other very much. Please be sensitive, as this is painful." It was confirmed by McCartan that he and Cameron will not continue the band together.
