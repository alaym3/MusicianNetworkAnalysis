'''Anıl Piyancı''' (born 17 July 1990) is a Turkish rapper, musician and songwriter.

== Career ==
He started his career at the age of 16 by founding his studio "Yeşil Oda". Piyancı started making hip hop music in 2010<ref>{{cite web | title = Serbest Atış: Anıl Piyancı vs. Can Volkan | url = https://www.redbull.com/tr-tr/serbest-atis-anil-piyanci-can-volkan-izle | publisher = Redbull | accessdate = 1 June 2020 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20200606164628/https://www.redbull.com/tr-tr/serbest-atis-anil-piyanci-can-volkan-izle | archivedate = 6 June 2020}}</ref> and was featured in [[Ezhel]]'s song ''Sekiz'' in 2011.<ref>{{cite web | title = Ünlü rapçi Ezhel hakkında 10 yıl hapis istemi | url = https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/gundem/unlu-rapci-ezhel-hakkinda-10-yil-hapis-istemi-40858195 | newspaper = [[Hürriyet]] | accessdate = 1 June 2020 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20180606181710/https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/gundem/unlu-rapci-ezhel-hakkinda-10-yil-hapis-istemi-40858195 | archivedate = 6 June 2018}}</ref> His breakthrough came in May 2014 with the song ''Bi Duman Hip-Hop'' which was published on [[YouTube]]. The song was well received by the audience and soon products and T-shirts branded with the song's title were produced and sold.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.anilpiyanci.com/ |title=Archived copy |accessdate=12 February 2016 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160206232633/http://www.anilpiyanci.com/ |archivedate=6 February 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.respectshop.com.tr/index.php?route=product%2Fproduct&product_id=69 |title=Archived copy |accessdate=5 April 2020 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160223151637/http://www.respectshop.com.tr/index.php?route=product%2Fproduct&product_id=69 |archivedate=23 February 2016}}</ref>

In 2019, he voiced an acoustic version of the song "Bırakman Doğru mu?" together with [[Zeynep Bastık]].<ref>{{cite web | last = Şişman | first = Can | title = Anıl Piyancı ve Zeynep Bastık'ın The song was among the most-listened songs on [[Spotify]] in Turkey in 2019.<ref>{{cite web | title = Spotify, bu yaz dünyada ve Türkiye’de en çok dinlenen şarkıları açıkladı | url =
