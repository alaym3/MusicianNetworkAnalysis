'''Neiked''' (stylised as '''NEIKED''') is a Swedish artist collective<ref>{{Cite web|last=Blair|first=Lindsay|date=2019-03-03|title=Stockholm collective NEIKED release fun new single "Lifestyle" Feat. Husky|url=https://earmilk.com/2019/03/03/stockholm-collective-neiked-release-fun-new-single-lifestyle-feat-husky/|access-date=2021-11-11|website=EARMILK|language=en-US}}</ref> of singers, songwriters and record producers. '''Victor Rådström'''<ref>{{cite web|title=Victor Rådström {{!}} Musik {{!}} DR|url=http://www.dr.dk/musik/kunstner/victor%20r%C3%A5dstr%C3%B6m/10571751|publisher=[[DR (broadcaster)|Dr.dk]]|accessdate=9 September 2016}}</ref> and '''Mikael Rabus'''<ref>{{Cite web|title=Neiked på besök! {{!}} Musikmakarna|url=https://www.musikmakarna.se/sv/nyheter/neiked-pa-besok|access-date=2021-11-11|website=www.musikmakarna.se}}</ref> founded '''NEIKED''', a group that comprises rising artists, producers, writers and vocalists, creating a sound that is equal parts mainstream and DIY.<ref name="Blair">{{Cite web|url=https://earmilk.com/2019/04/23/stockholm-collective-neiked-break-down-debut-album-best-of-hard-drive-exclusive/|title=Stockholm collective NEIKED break down debut album 'Best Of Hard Drive' [Exclusive]|last=Blair|first=Lindsay|date=2019-04-23|website=EARMILK|language=en-US|access-date=2020-02-05}}</ref>

==Career==
Rådström got his start writing songs in the basement of his home. Upon hearing his music, his friends were impressed by his creativity and encouraged him to reach out to various record labels in order to share his music with a larger audience.

Impatient with the pace of the labels and with an eagerness to share the project with the world, Rådström and his friend and collaborator Rabus took matters into their own hands, formed the Neiked Collective, and released the music independently.

The two of them are the founding parts of the independent label Neiked Collective. The collective has since appeared on shows such as [[BBC Radio 1|BBC Live Lounge]] and on Scandinavian talk shows, showcasing the young producer's work in an unorthodox way.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/pages/category/Artist/NEIKEDMusic/about/|title=NEIKED|website=www.facebook.com|language=en|access-date=2020-02-05}}</ref>

NEIKED's third single "[[Sexual (song)|Sexual]]" (2016) reached a peak position of number four on the [[ARIA Charts|Australian Singles Chart]]<ref>{{Cite web|title=australian-charts.com - Neiked feat. Dyo - Sexual|url=https://australian-charts.com/showitem.asp?interpret=Neiked+feat.+Dyo&titel=Sexual&cat=s|access-date=2021-11-11|website=australian-charts.com}}</ref> and number five on the [[UK Singles Chart]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.swedishcharts.com/showitem.asp?interpret=Neiked&titel=Sexual&cat=s|title=swedishcharts.com - NEIKED - Sexual|publisher=Hung Medien|accessdate=29 October 2016}}</ref> "Sexual" was influenced by [[Marvin Gaye]]'s "[[Sexual Healing]]"; Rådström came up with the line "I'm feeling sexual so we should be sexual", with that thought forming the base of the song.<ref name="Blair"/>

In 2018 they released the single "How Do I Find You" featuring Swedish singer and childhood friend [[Miriam Bryant]],<ref>{{cite web|url=https://thevogue.com/artists/miriam-bryant/#bio|title=Miriam Bryant|website=Vogue}}</ref> which features "a woozy, dusk-drenched pulse".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://amnplify.com.au/miriam-bryant-and-neiked-launch-video-for-how-did-i-find-you/|title=Miriam Bryant and NEIKED launch video for How Do I Find You|last=Bruce|first=Dave|website=Amplify|accessdate=14 November 2018}}</ref>

NEIKED released the single "Sometimes" in November 2019, which was called a "big and brassy declaration, [with] engaging production and west-coast warmth".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.scandipop.co.uk/song-neiked-feat-kes-kross-jackson-penn-sometimes/|title=Song: NEIKED feat. Kes Kross & Jackson Penn- 'Sometimes'|website=Scandipop|accessdate=6 November 2019}}</ref>

NEIKED created the collaboration with [[Mae Muller]] and [[Polo G]] on the single "[[Better Days (Neiked, Mae Muller and Polo G song)|Better Days]]" in 2021,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://outnowmagazine.com/neiked-mae-muller-polog-better-days/|title=NEIKED, Mae Muller and Polo G Goes for a Euphoric New Single "Better Days"|date=26 September 2021}}</ref><ref>https://www.maemuller.com</ref> a song which was a new entry at number 57 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart of October 30.<ref>{{Cite magazine|url=https://www.billboard.com/charts/hot-100|title = The Hot 100 Chart|magazine = [[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]}}</ref> "Better Days" also charted on the British Official Singles Chart Top 100, debuting at number 52 on the chart of 4 November 2021 after accumulating a sales total of 8,007.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.musicweek.com/analysis/read/charts-analysis-adele-takes-it-easy-at-singles-summit/084519|title = Charts analysis: Adele takes it Easy at singles summit &#124; Analysis &#124; Music Week}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.officialcharts.com/charts/singles-chart/|title = Official Singles Chart Top 100 &#124; Official Charts Company|website = [[OfficialCharts.com]]}}</ref>
