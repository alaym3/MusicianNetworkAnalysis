'''Oso Oso''' is an American [[Rock music|rock]] band from [[Long Beach, New York]]. Jade Lilitri (vocals, guitar), formerly of [https://statelines.bandcamp.com/releases State Lines], is the only permanent member. The last name Lilitri is a pen name.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.reddit.com/r/indieheads/comments/cnbzgu/ama_announcement_oso_oso_on_thursday_august_15th/ex0cpxh|title=r/indieheads - [AMA ANNOUNCEMENT] oso oso on Thursday, August 15th @ 3 PM PST / 6 PM EST / 10 PM UTC!|website=reddit|language=en|access-date=2019-09-02}}</ref> The band has released three studio albums and two EPs. Their most recent release, ''Basking in the Glow'', was named "Best New Music" by online music magazine [[Pitchfork (website)|Pitchfork]].
