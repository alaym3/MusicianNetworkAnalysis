'''Shahriyar Atababayev''' ({{lang-az|Şəhriyar Zahid oğlu Atababayev}}), better known by his [[stage name]] '''Xpert''', is an Azerbaijani [[Rapping|rapper]], [[songwriter]].<ref name="a"/><ref name="b"/><ref name="c"/><ref>{{cite web |title=Azərbaycanlı reper 100 min manat qazanmaq istəyir - FOTO |url=https://baku.ws/show-business/78402 |website=BAKU.WS - Azərbaycanın xəbər saytı |access-date=19 July 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210719181658/https://baku.ws/show-business/78402 |archive-date=19 July 2021 |language=az}}</ref>

== Biography ==
Shahriyar Atababayev was born in September 1990 in Baku, Azerbaijan.
 
He graduated from the Faculty of Turkish Language and Literature ([[Qafqaz University]]) in 2011 with a bachelor's degree and in 2014 with a master's degree.<ref name="c">{{cite web |title=Məşhur reper SİRLƏRİNİ AÇDI: “Dinləyicilər XPERTİ rahatlıqla həzm edib” - MÜSAHİBƏ/VİDEO |url=https://publika.az/projects/xususi/220510.html |website=Publika.Az |access-date=19 July 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180605094723/http://publika.az/projects/xususi/220510.html |archive-date=6 May 2018 |language=az |date=13 November 2017}}</ref>
 
Shahriyar met a rapper and producer nicknamed A4 and started his rap career in 2009.
 
Xpert is one of the founders of Synaps Production. Azerbaijan's most famous rap company.
 
He worked with many local and foreign artists as a musician, producer and director.
 
He is currently CEO of the record label "101".
