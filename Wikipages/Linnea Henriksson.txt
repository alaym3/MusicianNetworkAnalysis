'''Ellen 'Linnea' Petrea Henriksson''' (born 9 November 1986) is a [[Swedes|Swedish]] singer and songwriter who participated in ''[[Idol 2010]]'' in which she placed fourth.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://poplight.zitiz.se/artikel/idol-linnea-henrikssons-idol-2010-slutade-i-kvaell-my-work-done-here |title=Idol: Linnea Henrikssons Idol 2010 slutade i kväll – "My work is done here" |publisher=Poplight.zitiz.se |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110211141008/http://poplight.zitiz.se/artikel/idol-linnea-henrikssons-idol-2010-slutade-i-kvaell-my-work-done-here |archive-date=11 February 2011}}</ref> {{As of|April 2018}}, she is also the lead singer of the jazz band Prylf. In 2012 Henriksson released her first music album, which she co-wrote and co-produced with singer [[Orup]].<ref name="autogenerated1">{{cite web|author=Tweet Stockholm TT Spektra |url=http://www.svd.se/kultur/linnea-henriksson-har-stadat-klart_7238667.svd |archive-url=https://archive.today/20130730152826/http://www.svd.se/kultur/linnea-henriksson-har-stadat-klart_7238667.svd |url-status=dead |archive-date=30 July 2013 |title=Linnea Henriksson har städat klart |language=sv |work=Svenska Dagbladet |date=29 May 2012}}</ref> During mid-2013 Henriksson toured Sweden with the band [[Gyllene Tider]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://hallandsposten.se/nojekultur/musik/1.1913790-linnea-drar-pa-turne-med-gyllene-tider?articleRenderMode=image&image=bigTop |archive-url=https://archive.today/20130730152854/http://hallandsposten.se/nojekultur/musik/1.1913790-linnea-drar-pa-turne-med-gyllene-tider?articleRenderMode=image&image=bigTop |url-status=dead |archive-date=2013-07-30 |title=Linnea drar på turné med Gyllene Tider |language=sv |publisher=hallandsposten.se}}</ref>

==Early life==
Henriksson was born in [[Halmstad]], Sweden. Her public debut occurred in 1995 when she participated in the [[TV4 (Sweden)|TV4]] televised singing show ''Småstjärnorna''.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.jnytt.se/hoppas-det-blir-basta-kvallen-pa-aret |title="Hoppas bjuda publiken på bästa kvällen på året" |language=sv |publisher=Jnytt.se |date=14 July 2013 |access-date=30 July 2013 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130718040007/http://www.jnytt.se/hoppas-det-blir-basta-kvallen-pa-aret |archive-date=18 July 2013 |url-status=dead}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://sverigesradio.se/sida/artikel.aspx?programid=2907&artikel=5165069 |title=Familjen besökte studion och så träffade vi Linnea Henriksson – P3 Star |publisher=Sveriges Radio}}</ref> She studied music at Skurups folkhögskola, and in 2007 she met other singers and musicians, started the jazz band Prylf and became the band's lead singer.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.vf.se/kultur-noje/noje/toorell-idol-ar-inte-riktigt-det-forum-vi-ar-vana-vid-i-prylf |title=Toorell: "Idol är inte riktigt det forum vi är vana vid i Prylf" |publisher=Värmlands Folkblad |date=19 November 2010}}</ref> In 2010 Prylf released their only, music album, which is called ''Kind of Green''.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.sydsvenskan.se/kultur-och-nojen/article640968/Behoven-styr-Prylf.html |archive-url=https://archive.today/20120525002911/http://www.sydsvenskan.se/kultur-och-nojen/article640968/Behoven-styr-Prylf.html |url-status=dead |archive-date=2012-05-25 |title=Behoven styr Prylf |work=Sydsvenskan}}</ref>

==2010–present: ''Idol 2010'' and Gyllene Tider==
[[Image:Linnea Henriksson crop.jpg|thumb|Linnea Henriksson during Idol 2010.]]

From August to December 2010, Henriksson participated in the singing competition ''[[Idol 2010]]'', which was broadcast on TV4. She finished in fourth place and later that year she signed a record deal with Sony Music and Epic.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://sonymusic.se/nyheter/linnea-henriksson-ny-talang-pa-sony |title=Linnea Henriksson – Ny talang på Sony |publisher=Sony Music Entertainment Sweden |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://archive.today/20130730141902/http://sonymusic.se/nyheter/linnea-henriksson-ny-talang-pa-sony |archive-date=30 July 2013 |df=dmy-all}}</ref> During her time on ''Idol'' she was among the final twelve contestants during a qualification week and was voted into the Top 12 along with singer [[Geir Rönning]] during the broadcast.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nyhetskollen.com/nyheter/130388/linna-henriksson-till-kvalfinal |title=Linna Henriksson till kvalfinal » Inrikes |publisher=Nyhetskollen |date=1 October 2010}}</ref> Henriksson was one of the least-popular two contestants during her time on ''Idol 2010''; she had to [[sing-off]] with [[Elin Blom]] and retained her place in the competition by attracting more public votes than Blom.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://nyheter24.se/noje/idol/487971-elin-blom-ute-ur-idol-2010 |title=Elin Blom ute ur Idol 2010 |publisher=Nyheter24.se}}</ref>

In 2011, Henriksson released her first solo music single "Väldigt kär/Obegripligt ensam", which she co-wrote and co-produced with [[Orup]].<ref>{{cite web|author=Av Maria Zandihn|url=http://www.skanskan.se/article/20120815/MALMOFESTIVALEN/120819799/-/linnea-henriksson-om-sina-latar |title=Linnea Henriksson om sina låtar |publisher=Skånskan.se |date=15 August 2012}}</ref><ref name="autogenerated1"/>  Parts of Henriksson's first solo music album ''Till mina älskade och älskare'', which was released on 30 May 2012, were also co-written with Orup.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.mynewsdesk.com/se/pressroom/sonybmg/pressrelease/view/till-mina-aelskade-och-aelskare-idag-slaepps-linnea-henrikssons-debutalbum-767172 |title=Sony Music Entertainment Sweden – Till mina älskade och älskare – Idag släpps Linnea Henrikssons... |publisher=Mynewsdesk |date=30 May 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.expressen.se/noje/recensioner/musik/linnea-henriksson---till-mina-alskade-och-alskare/ |title=Linnea Henriksson – "Till mina älskade och älskare" |work=Expressen |date=4 June 2012}}</ref> During mid-2012 Henriksson went on a summer tour around Sweden; later that year she embarked upon an extended music tour to several Swedish cities.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://hd.se/noje/2012/09/06/linnea-henriksson-aker-pa-turne |title=Linnea Henriksson åker på turné |language=sv |publisher=hd.se |date=6 September 2012}}</ref>

On 19 January 2013, Henriksson was voted "Artist of the Year 2012" by listeners to Swedish radio station [[Sveriges Radio P3|P3:s]] for its yearly P3 Gold gala, receiving the most votes. From June to August 2013 Henriksson joined the music tour of the band [[Gyllene Tider]], which toured around Sweden, as their opening act.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.livenation.se/artist/linnea-henriksson-tickets |title=Linnea Henrikssonbiljetter &#124; Linnea Henriksson turné & evenemangsinformation |publisher=Livenation.se}}</ref> In late July 2013, Henriksson became the first live act to perform at the new [[Tele2 Arena]] in Stockholm; she was the opening act for Gyllene Tider.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.expressen.se/noje/linnea-invigde-arenan---inte-gyllene-tider/ |title=Linnea invigde arenan – inte Gyllene Tider|work=Expressen}}</ref> Henriksson's songs have been included on several music compilation albums.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/se/artist/linnea-henriksson/id399007993 |title=Linnea Henriksson |publisher=iTunes Store}}</ref>

Henriksson's song "Enastående" was included in the soundtrack to the Swedish movie ''[[Once Upon a Time in Phuket]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/se/album/en-gang-i-phuket-original/id498498545 |title=En gång i Phuket Original Soundtrack av Various Artists |publisher=iTunes Store |date=3 February 2012}}</ref> She collaborated with the Swedish DJ [[Avicii]] in his song "Hope There's Someone", which is included in his debut album ''[[True (Avicii album)|True]]''. On 8 December 2014, Henriksson was one of the hosts of [[Musikhjälpen]] [[Musikhjälpen#Musikhj.C3.A4lpen 2014|2014]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://sverigesradio.se/sida/artikel.aspx?programid=3946&artikel=6010050|title=Linnea Henriksson|author=Sveriges Radio|access-date=2014-11-29}}</ref> In 2018, she appears on [[Så mycket bättre]] which is broadcast on TV4.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.expressen.se/noje/har-ar-artisterna-i-sa-mycket-battre-2018/|title=Här är artisterna i ”Så mycket bättre” 2018|publisher=Expressen|access-date=5 December 2018}}</ref>

On 3 September 2019, Henriksson was announced as a presenter of [[Melodifestivalen 2020]] along with [[Lina Hedlund]] and [[David Sundin]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.expressen.se/noje/de-programleder-melodifestivalen-2019/|title=De programleder Melodifestivalen 2020|website=Expressen}} Retrieved 3 September 2019</ref>
