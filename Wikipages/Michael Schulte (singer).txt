'''Michael Anthony Schulte''' ({{IPA-de|ˈʃʊltə}}; born 30 April 1990) is a German singer and songwriter. He began his singing career in 2008, posting covers on his [[YouTube]] channel and later placed third on [[The Voice of Germany (season 1)|season one]] of ''[[The Voice of Germany]]'' in 2012. His debut studio album ''Wide Awake'' was released later that year. He [[Germany in the Eurovision Song Contest 2018|represented Germany]] in the [[Eurovision Song Contest 2018]] in [[Lisbon]], [[Portugal]], with the song "[[You Let Me Walk Alone]]". He finished in fourth place with 340 points.

==Early life==
Schulte was born on 30 April 1990 in [[Eckernförde]] and grew up first in [[Boren, Germany|Lindau]], a part of ''Boren'' at the [[Schlei]] and later in [[Dollerup]], a small municipality near [[Flensburg]]. He attended [[Duborg-Skolen]], a [[Danish language|Danish]] school and graduated in 2009. Afterwards, he completed his [[Alternative civilian service|civil service]].<ref>{{cite web| url=http://www.shz.de/nachrichten/top-thema/article//mit-acoustic-pop-zum-erfolg-dolleruper-moechte-the-voice-of-germa.html| archive-url=https://archive.today/20120913011753/http://www.shz.de/nachrichten/top-thema/article//mit-acoustic-pop-zum-erfolg-dolleruper-moechte-the-voice-of-germa.html| url-status=dead| archive-date=2012-09-13| title=Dolleruper möchte "The Voice of Germany" werden| publisher=Schleswig-Holsteinischer Zeitungsverlag| date=2012-03-27| language=German}}</ref><ref>{{cite web| url=http://www.fla.de/?UNF=1&G=a6c1| title=Michael Schulte synger for ligestilling| publisher=Flensborg Avis| date=2012-03-27| language=German| access-date=2018-02-22| archive-date=2018-02-21| archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180221222542/https://www.fla.de/?UNF=1&G=a6c1| url-status=live}}</ref> His father died when he was only fourteen years old.

==Career==

===2012–2017: ''The Voice of Germany''===
Schulte began his career in 2008 by publishing covers of popular songs on his [[YouTube]] channel. Through his YouTube channel, he was able to sign with a management company and eventually signed with [http://weinstein-media.com/ Weinstein Media] founded by Singer/Songwriter Andy Weinstein in Berlin.


In 2011, Schulte became a contestant on [[The Voice of Germany (season 1)|season one]] of ''[[The Voice of Germany]]''.<ref name="Mercereau">{{cite news|last1=Mercereau|first1=Damien|title=Eurovision 2018 : Michael Schulte, la douceur allemande|url=http://tvmag.lefigaro.fr/programme-tv/eurovision-2018-michael-schulte-la-douceur-allemande_e6b5ab92-187d-11e8-8f49-e734b611bc3e/|access-date=24 February 2018|publisher=Le Figaro|date=23 February 2018|language=fr|archive-date=23 February 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180223164400/http://tvmag.lefigaro.fr/programme-tv/eurovision-2018-michael-schulte-la-douceur-allemande_e6b5ab92-187d-11e8-8f49-e734b611bc3e/|url-status=live}}</ref> He auditioned with the song "[[Set Fire to the Rain]]" by [[Adele]] and joined the team of [[Rea Garvey]]. Schulte progressed through the competition, eventually placing third behind winner [[Ivy Quainoo]] and runner-up [[Kim Sanders]]. Following the competition, he signed with German independent label [[Edel AG]]. His debut studio album ''Wide Awake'' was released in 2012.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.musikmarkt.de/Aktuell/News/News/Charts-Deutschland-KW-3-The-Voice-Kandidaten-entern-Single-Top-100-mit-Videos|title=Charts Deutschland KW 3: "The Voice"-Kandidaten entern Single Top 100|publisher=Musikmarkt.de|date=17 January 2012|language=German|access-date=22 February 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120119215510/http://www.musikmarkt.de/Aktuell/News/News/Charts-Deutschland-KW-3-The-Voice-Kandidaten-entern-Single-Top-100-mit-Videos|archive-date=19 January 2012|url-status=dead}}</ref>

===2017–present: Eurovision Song Contest and other projects===
On 29 December 2017, Schulte was confirmed to be one of the six acts competing in {{lang|de|[[Germany in the Eurovision Song Contest 2018|Unser Lied für Lissabon]]}}, the German national selection for the [[Eurovision Song Contest 2018]], with the song "[[You Let Me Walk Alone]]".<ref name="Mercereau" /><ref>{{cite web|url=https://eurovoix.com/2018/02/13/germany-titles-unser-lied-fur-lissabon/|title=Germany: Song Titles for Unser Lied für Lissabon Revealed|date=13 February 2018|publisher=Eurovoix|last=Herbert|first=Emily|access-date=22 February 2018|archive-date=20 February 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180220163452/https://eurovoix.com/2018/02/13/germany-titles-unser-lied-fur-lissabon/|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://eurovoix.com/2018/02/20/germany-unser-lied-fur-lissabon-entries-revealed/|title=Germany: Unser Lied für Lissabon Entries Revealed|date=20 February 2018|publisher=Eurovoix|last=Farren|first=Neil|access-date=22 February 2018|archive-date=3 March 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180303181726/https://eurovoix.com/2018/02/20/germany-unser-lied-fur-lissabon-entries-revealed/|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.eurovision.de/news/Diese-sechs-Acts-sind-im-ESC-Vorentscheid-2018,kandidaten568.html|title=Diese sechs Acts sind im ESC-Vorentscheid 2018|work=ARD|date=29 December 2017|access-date=29 December 2017|language=German|archive-date=12 June 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180612165906/https://www.eurovision.de/news/Diese-sechs-Acts-sind-im-ESC-Vorentscheid-2018,kandidaten568.html|url-status=live}}</ref> The song was later released on 20 February 2018. Schulte won the final, held on 22 February, becoming Germany's entry to the 2018 Eurovision Song Contest. In Lisbon, Schulte received 340 points, ending up in fourth place. In 2020, Schulte provided commentary for Germany in the ''[[Eurovision: Europe Shine a Light]]'' broadcast on {{lang|de|[[Das Erste]]|italic=no}} alongside [[Peter Urban (presenter)|Peter Urban]]. During the show, Michael also sang a duet version of "{{lang|de|[[Ein bißchen Frieden]]|italic=no}}" with [[Ilse DeLange]] set in the [[Peace Palace]] ({{lang-nl|Vredespaleis}}) in [[The Hague]]. He later released a single with DeLange entitled "Wrong Direction".  

Michael also recently became a father to his son, Luis.{{when |date=August 2021}}{{cn |date=August 2021}}
