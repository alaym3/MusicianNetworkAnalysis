'''SOB X RBE''' (an [[acronym]] of '''Strictly Only Brothers x Real Boi Entertainment''') is an American [[Hip hop music|hip hop]] group.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/sob-x-rbe-gangin/|title=SOB X RBE: Gangin Album Review|website=Pitchfork|language=en|access-date=2018-04-20|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180421031019/https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/sob-x-rbe-gangin/|archive-date=2018-04-21|url-status=live}}</ref> The group consists of two active members, DaBoii and Slimmy B. The group was founded in 2016 by Slimmy B (Jabbar Brown), Yhung T.O. (Juwon Lee), DaBoii (Wayman Barrow), and Lul G (George Harris).<ref>{{cite web | title = SOB X RBE | url =https://music.apple.com/us/artist/sob-x-rbe/1192174117 | website = music.apple.com | access-date =22 January 2020 }}</ref> The group was formed during their teenage years and they worked together to create music.<ref>{{cite web | title = SOB X RBE | url =https://music.apple.com/us/artist/sob-x-rbe/1192174117 | website = music.apple.com | access-date =22 January 2020 }}</ref> Their sound is highly influenced by the Bay Area and various artists that make up the Hip-Hop and Rap culture of Northern California.<ref>{{cite web | title = SOB X RBE | url =https://music.apple.com/us/artist/sob-x-rbe/1192174117 | website = music.apple.com | access-date =22 January 2020 }}</ref>

== Music career ==
As SOB x RBE gained momentum and exposure, in 2017 they caught the eye of Bay Area rapper [[Sage the Gemini]] who took the group under apprenticeship and had them be the opening act for his tour.<ref>{{cite web | title = SOB X RBE | url =https://music.apple.com/us/artist/sob-x-rbe/1192174117 | website = music.apple.com | access-date =22 January 2020 }}</ref> He introduced the group to more prominent names in the music industry to help them gain more momentum.<ref>{{cite web | title = SOB X RBE | url =https://music.apple.com/us/artist/sob-x-rbe/1192174117 | website = music.apple.com | access-date =22 January 2020 }}</ref> The group has released six complete projects: a self-titled mixtape in 2017,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/sob-x-rbe/1198398937|title=SOB X RBE by SOB X RBE on Apple Music|website=iTunes|access-date=6 June 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180606083647/https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/sob-x-rbe/1198398937|archive-date=6 June 2018|url-status=live}}</ref> a debut album ''Gangin'' in 2018, an EP with Big Money, a second album ''Gangin II'' in late 2018, an EP with Marshmello, and an album "Family Not A Group" with producer Hit Boy.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/album/gangin-mw0003157792|title=Gangin - SOB X RBE - Songs, Reviews, Credits - AllMusic|website=AllMusic|access-date=18 July 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180718234642/https://www.allmusic.com/album/gangin-mw0003157792|archive-date=18 July 2018|url-status=live}}</ref> The group found mainstream attention after being featured on the track "Paramedic!" for the 2018 superhero film ''[[Black Panther (film)|Black Panther]]''.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/lyrics/8223821/sob-x-rbe-paramedic-lyrics-black-panther-kendrick-lamar |title=Here are the Lyrics to SOB x RBE's 'Paramedic!,' Feat. == Biographies ==
'''Juwon Lee (Yhung T.O.)'''

Born on December 7, 1998 in Vallejo, California, Yhung T.O. has had a passion for making music since he was in middle school and essentially brought the group together as he was the one with the studio the men began recording their very first hits in.<ref>{{Citation|title=The SOB x RBE Interview Pt. 1 {{!}}{{!}} Thizzler.com Interview|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fboV36_Pu6U|language=en|access-date=2020-01-31}}</ref> SOB x RBE was a merge of two pre-existing groups, Real Boi Entertainment (RBE) was Yhung T.O.'s musical group, starting as early as ninth grade.<ref>{{Citation|title=SOB X RBE Speak On Being Underdog Black Rappers & Struggles As A Group From The Bay|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8sn92C4Sffs|language=en|access-date=2020-02-07}}</ref>  Yhung T.O. had gotten married at the age of nineteen and is still currently married.<ref>{{cite AV media| people = DJ Vlad| title =SOB X RBE: Slimmy B Robbed a House, Returned the Money 6 Months Later (Part 6)| medium = Youtube | publisher = VladTV}}</ref> In March 2018 Yhung T.O. revealed his signing to the record label [[Interscope Records|Interscope]], a record label who acquired Dr. Dre's [[Death Row Records]] in the late 1990s.Yhung T.O. was signed by [[Joie Manda]] in December 2017. The group expressed that they are still a family, though Yhung T.O. was signed as a solo artist to the label.<ref>{{cite AV media|people = DJ Vlad|title=SOB x RBE on Break-Up Rumors, Why Yhung T.O. Got a Solo Deal| medium = Vlad TV| publisher =VladTV}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.kqed.org/arts/13828024/what-happens-to-sob-x-rbe-now-that-yhung-t-o-has-an-interscope-deal|title=What Happens to SOB x RBE Now That Yhung T.O. Has An Interscope Deal?|date=2018-03-27|website=KQED|language=en-us|access-date=2020-01-31}}</ref> His signing left fans with unanswered questions as to what is going to happen to the musical group in the long run.<ref>{{cite AV media| people = DJ Vlad| title =SOB X RBE: Slimmy B Robbed a House, Returned the Money 6 Months Later (Part 6)| medium = Youtube | publisher = VladTV}}</ref>

'''Wayan Barrow (DaBoii)'''

Born on August 24, 1997 in Vallejo, California, DaBoii also was making music since he could remember and at a very young age joined Yhung T.O. in his studio where they began to make music.<ref>{{Citation|title=The SOB x RBE Interview Pt. 1 {{!}}{{!}} Thizzler.com Interview|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fboV36_Pu6U|language=en|access-date=2020-01-31}}</ref> The second half of the musical group's final merge was Strictly Only Brothers (SOB) and this was DaBoii's initial group.<ref>{{Citation|title=SOB X RBE Speak On Being Underdog Black Rappers & Struggles As A Group From The Bay|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8sn92C4Sffs|language=en|access-date=2020-02-07}}</ref> During an interview with the musical group, DaBoii revealed  that he is known to be the most soft-spoken among the three and keeps very quiet around those he does not know well.<ref>{{Citation|title=SOB x RBE on growing up in Vallejo, recruiting Swaggy P, and tour life|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DoHkoTmp6us|language=en|access-date=2020-01-31}}</ref> While the group is known for their collaborative music, each member identifies themselves as an independent artist, with DaBoii also taking steps to sign with a record label.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.spin.com/featured/yhung-t-o-interview-profile/|title=Yhung T.O. Interview: Can He Be the Bay's New Star?|date=2019-03-28|website=Spin|access-date=2020-01-31}}</ref> In December 2019, DaBoii announced on Instagram he has a son.

'''Jabbar Brown''' '''(Slimmy B)'''

Born on February 26, 1997 in Vallejo, California Slimmy B is the third active member of the musical group. Slimmy B's passion to pursue music also started very early on, just like the rest of the group. He attended Vallejo Middle School where he and DaBoii met and formed their relationship. In an interview, Slimmy B revealed that he had a son and before the group had gained exposure he was working at various wineries and warehouses in Vallejo, California to take care of his family.<ref>{{cite AV media| people = DJ Vlad| title =SOB X RBE: Slimmy B Robbed a House, Returned the Money 6 Months Later (Part 6)| medium = Youtube | publisher = VladTV}}</ref>  In an interview with the artists, it was revealed that among the three, Slimmy B seems to be the artist with all the jokes.<ref>{{Citation|title=SOB x RBE on growing up in Vallejo, recruiting Swaggy P, and tour life|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DoHkoTmp6us|language=en|access-date=2020-01-31}}</ref>

'''George Harris (Lul G)'''

Born on November 14, 1998 in Vallejo, California Lul G is currently not an active member in the group as of late 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/8531980/former-sob-x-rbe-rapper-lul-g-arrested-murderl|title=Former SOB x RBE Rapper Lul G Arrested for Murder|date=2019-02-10|website=Billboard|language=en-us|access-date=2020-01-31}}</ref> Lul G signed to Def Jam records in the summer of 2018 as a solo artist.<ref>{{cite web | title = Can Yhung T.O. Be the Bay's Pensive New Star?| url =https://www.spin.com/featured/yhung-t-o-interview-profile/| website = Spin |language=en-us| access-date =22 January 2020 }}</ref> KQUED, reported that the Fairfield Police Department, stated that a Lul G (George Harris) was charged for murder and arrested On September 21, 2019.<ref>{{cite web | title = Shooting Victim in Lul G's Murder Trial Was Close Friend of SOB x RBE| url =https://www.kqed.org/arts/13868326/sob-rbe-lul-g-murder-trial-arrest| website = KQUED |language=en-us| access-date =22 January 2020 }}</ref>
He is currently being held in the Stanton Correction Facility in Fairfield, California. Harris is being charged for in connection of the murder of Rashield Flowers a friend to the SOB x RBE and co-worker.<ref>{{cite web | title = Shooting Victim in Lul G's Murder Trial Was Close Friend of SOB x RBE| url =https://www.kqed.org/arts/13868326/sob-rbe-lul-g-murder-trial-arrest| website = KQUED |language=en-us| access-date =22 January 2020}}</ref>
