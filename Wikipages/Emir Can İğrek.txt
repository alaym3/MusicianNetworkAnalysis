'''Emir Can İğrek''' (born 2 April 1993, [[Çerkezköy]]<ref name=":0">{{cite web | last1 = Şayan | first1 = Rana | title = EMİR CAN İĞREK İLE TEPETAKLAK ÖZEL RÖPORTAJI | url = https://medium.com/tepetaklak-dergisi/emi%CC%87r-can-i%CC%87%C4%9Frek-i%CC%87le-tepetaklak-%C3%B6zel-r%C3%B6portaji-45962f7e357f | publisher = Tepetaklak | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20200405172854/https://medium.com/tepetaklak-dergisi/emi%CC%87r-can-i%CC%87%C4%9Frek-i%CC%87le-tepetaklak-%C3%B6zel-r%C3%B6portaji-45962f7e357f | archivedate = 5 April 2020}}</ref>) is a Turkish singer and songwriter. He started making music by setting up small stage groups in the city where he was born. In 2019, he had his breakthrough with the song "Nalan".<ref>{{cite web | last1 = Akyıldız | first1 = Tolga | title = Bu sene onları dinledik... 2019'un en iyileri | url = https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/yazarlar/tolga-akyildiz/bu-sene-onlari-dinledik-2019un-en-iyileri-41407407 | publisher = [[Hürriyet]] | accessdate = 1 April 2020 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20200105134609/https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/yazarlar/tolga-akyildiz/bu-sene-onlari-dinledik-2019un-en-iyileri-41407407 | archivedate = 5 January 2020}}</ref> At the 2020 PowerTürk Music Awards, he was awarded as the "Most Powerful Newcomer of the Year".<ref name="Power">{{cite web | title = İşte popun en güçlü isimleri | url = https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/kelebek/magazin/iste-popun-en-guclu-isimleri-41443839 | publisher = Hürriyet | accessdate = 1 April 2020 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20200319081438/https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/kelebek/magazin/iste-popun-en-guclu-isimleri-41443839 | archivedate = 19 March 2020}}</ref> He has also composed songs for artists such as [[Funda Arar]] and [[Ferhat Göçer]].<ref>{{cite web | last1 = Hazar | first1 = Furkan Can | title = Funda Arar İstanbul'u stüdyoya sığdırdı | url = https://www.milliyet.com.tr/funda-arar-istanbul-u-studyoya-sigdirdi-molatik-14447/ | publisher = [[Milliyet]] Molatik | accessdate = 1 April 2020 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20200405172904/https://www.milliyet.com.tr/funda-arar-istanbul-u-studyoya-sigdirdi-molatik-14447/ | archivedate = 5 April 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | title = Ferhat Göçer "Bu Kalp İçinde Teksin" | url = https://www.gazetemag.com/ferhat-gocer-bu-kalp-icinde-teksin/ | publisher = gazetemag | accessdate = 1 April 2020 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20171217173949/https://www.gazetemag.com/ferhat-gocer-bu-kalp-icinde-teksin/ | archivedate = 17 December 2017}}</ref>

As of 2020, he is studying at [[Yıldız Technical University]].<ref name=":0" />
