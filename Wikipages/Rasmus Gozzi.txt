'''Rasmus Gozzi''' is a Swedish singer and songwriter.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://unt.se/kultur-noje/han-skriver-musik-at-varldsartister-4865727.aspx|title=Han skriver musik åt världsartister |first=Jenny|last=Högblad|date=12 January 2018|website=Uppsala Nya Tidning|accessdate=19 February 2021}}</ref> He started his music career in 2016, and has co-written songs with Louise Andersson Bodin. He has also worked with  [[Lazee]] and [[Sean Kingston]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.aftonbladet.se/a/KVgwM|title=Rasmus Gozzi gör låt med Sean Kingston och Lazee|website=Aftonbladet|accessdate=19 February 2021}}</ref>
