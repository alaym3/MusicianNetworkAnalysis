{{nihongo|'''Yuzu'''|ゆず}} is a [[Japanese people|Japanese]] [[pop rock]] duo who debuted in 1997.<ref name="AllMusic"/> Its members are {{nihongo|Yūjin Kitagawa|北川悠仁|Kitagawa Yūjin}} and {{nihongo|Kōji Iwasawa|岩沢厚治|Iwasawa Kōji}}. Both of the band members come from [[Yokohama]] in [[Kanagawa Prefecture]], and attended 
[[:ja:横浜市立岡村中学校|Okamura Junior High School]].<ref>{{cite web|publisher=JpopAsia|url=https://www.jpopasia.com/yuzu/|title=Yuzu Biography|access-date=September 22, 2019}}</ref> Their songs {{nihongo|"Hyōri Ittai"|表裏一体|Two sides of the same coin}},<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eKoD2CRr_KA|title=ゆず「表裏一体」|trans-title=Yuzu – "Hyori Ittai"|date=December 10, 2013|access-date=September 22, 2019|publisher=Yuzu Official [[YouTube]]}}</ref> "Reason",<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cyq5bUXpnYI|title=ゆず「REASON」|trans-title=Yuzu – "Reason"|date=December 24, 2012|access-date=September 22, 2019|publisher=[[Toy's Factory]] Official YouTube}}</ref> and {{nihongo|"Nagareboshi Kirari"|流れ星キラリ|Shooting star}} were used as ending themes of the 2011 [[anime]] series ''[[Hunter × Hunter]]''.<ref>{{cite web|last=Green|first=Scott|publisher=[[Crunchyroll]]|url=https://www.crunchyroll.com/anime-news/2013/04/04-1/hunter-x-hunter-chimera-ant-arc-to-feature-new-ending|title='Hunter x Hunter' Chimera Ant Arc to Feature New Ending|date=April 4, 2013|access-date=September 22, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Loveridge|first=Lynzee|publisher=[[Anime News Network]]|url=https://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2013-09-24/yuzu-hyadain-make-their-2nd-hunter-x-hunter-film-theme-song|title=Yuzu, Hyadain Make Their 2nd Hunter x Hunter Film Theme Song|date=September 24, 2013|access-date=September 22, 2019}}</ref>

Yuzu started out as street musicians. At first, the band's name was "Light's", but Kitagawa disliked it, so they changed the name to "Yuzu". At that time, Kitagawa was eating a [[Citrus junos|yuzu]] sherbet, so he named the band after the fruit (incidentally, Iwasawa was eating vanilla ice cream so the band's name might have been "Vanilla" - sometimes Iwasawa tells this story). The fans of Yuzu are called "Yuzukko (ゆずっ子)".

==Members==
* Yūjin Kitagawa (''born January 14, 1977'') – [[vocals]], [[guitar]], [[Bandleader|leader]]
* Kōji Iwasawa (''born October 14, 1976'') – vocals, guitar
