'''Thomas Conning''' (born 11 January 1981), better known by the stage name '''Verb T''' is a  [[UK hip-hop]] artist based in [[London]].

==Biography==
Verb T has released 10 albums and six EPs as well as numerous singles.

His first release was the 12-track EP ''Backhand Slap Talk / Technical Illness'' which was a shared release featuring songs from fellow London-based rapper Kashmere.<ref>[http://www.ukhh.com/bhh/artists/verb_t-kashmere-lp.htm] {{dead link|date=May 2020}}</ref> It was released on [[Low Life Records]].<ref>[http://www.lowliferecords.co.uk/Release.asp?ID=52] {{dead link|date=May 2020}}</ref>

This was followed by his debut album ''Bring It Back To Basics'' which was a collaborative release featuring production by former Scratch Perverts DJ Harry Love.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.britishhiphop.co.uk/reviews/album_reviews/verb_t_harry_love_-_bring_it_back_to_basics_cd_silent_soundz_.html |title=Sam Krats featuring Gee Bag and El Da Sensei - Annihilate MP3 [In the Line] |website=Britishhiphop.co.uk |access-date=2020-05-21}}</ref>

In 2011, Verb T formed a group with artists Fliptrix, Leaf Dog and BVA MC, known as The Four Owls. The lead single ''Not Like Before'' and following album ''Nature's Greatest Mystery'' have seen The Owls touring solidly in the UK throughout 2012 with DJ Madnice. The Verb T album ''Morning Process (Waking Up)'' was released in September 2012 on [[High Focus Records]].

Verb T's first self produced album ''I Remain'' was released in 2013 on High Focus Records.

The Four Owls released an album in 2015 called ''The Natural Order'' which featured the well-known US producer [[DJ Premier]].<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JYOYqn4No1w] {{dead link|date=May 2020}}</ref> 

In 2018, Verb T and Pitch 92 created four original tracks for the programme ''[[Just Another Immigrant]]'' which aired in the USA on [[Showtime (TV network)|Showtime]] and in the UK on [[Sky Atlantic]] starring [[Romesh Ranganathan]].

In the same year Verb T and Pitch 92 also composed and performed the title track for the [[Sky One]] sitcom ''[[The Reluctant Landlord]]''.

In 2019, the second series of ''Reluctant Landlord'' was released and in addition to the title track, Verb T and Pitch 92 created original music used throughout the series and in the Christmas Special which aired on 23 December 2019 on Sky One.
