'''Stanley Stevenson Byrne''' (born 25 January 1993),<ref name="leedsmusicscene">{{cite web |url=http://www.leedsmusicscene.net/article/14079/ |title=Stan SB – Biography |website=Leeds Music Scene |date=2011 |accessdate=23 June 2014}}</ref> better known by his stage name '''Fox Stevenson''' (formerly '''Stan SB'''),<ref name="whiteraverrafting2">{{cite web|url=http://whiteraverrafting.com/fox-stevenson-nyc-slake-wrr-interview/2014/03/28/|title=Underground legend Fox Stevenson to make NYC debut|last=Downs|first=Chad|date=28 March 2014|website=White Raver Rafting|accessdate=16 June 2014}}</ref> is an English singer-songwriter, DJ and producer of [[bass music]], mostly [[drum and bass]] and [[Glitch (music)|glitch hop]].<ref name="youredm-mix">{{cite web |url=http://www.youredm.com/2014/05/28/edm-mix-fox-stevenson-volume-10/ |title=Your EDM Mix with Fox Stevenson – Volume 10 |date=28 May 2014 |first=Miguel |last=Tost |website=Your EDM |accessdate=16 June 2014}}</ref> He has released ten [[Extended play|EP]]s under Fox Stevenson, one under Stan SB, and has been featured on two [[Compilation album|compilations]] under Stan SB and eighteen under the name of Fox Stevenson.

==Music career==
Stevenson's interest in electronic music production began in the early 2000s, when he made his first release on the website [[Newgrounds]].<ref name="friedmylittlebrain2">{{cite web|url=http://www.friedmylittlebrain.com/fox-stevenson-interview/|title=Fox Stevenson interview|last=B.|first=Damian|date=22 March 2014|website=Friedmylittlebrain|accessdate=16 June 2014}}</ref> He was 15{{ndash}}16 years old when he created his first vocal track.<ref name="whiteraverrafting2"/> He later rose to prominence through the YouTube-based music community and nowadays an influential drum and bass label [[Liquicity]], where "Cloudhead", one of his earliest tracks as Stan SB, was featured.<ref name="Stan SB - Cloudhead2">{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=coKBa7nNJyg|title=Stan SB – Cloudhead|date=26 April 2009|website=YouTube|publisher=Liquicity|accessdate=3 September 2014}}</ref>

His works are also published to the audio distribution platform [[SoundCloud]] since 2012, under the alias Stan SB and from 2013 under the alias Fox Stevenson.<ref name="whiteraverrafting2" /> In 2013, he released an EP titled ''Endless'',<ref name="youredm-endless2">{{cite web|url=http://www.youredm.com/2013/10/15/fox-stevenson-endless-ep-firepower-records/|title=Fox Stevenson – Endless EP from Firepower Records|last=Trew|first=Tyler|date=15 October 2013|website=Your EDM|accessdate=16 June 2014}}</ref> the title track of which has since gained over 1.6&nbsp;million listens on SoundCloud.<ref name="soundcloud endless2">{{cite web|url=https://soundcloud.com/foxstevenson/fox-stevenson-endless|title=Fox Stevenson – Endless|date=15 October 2014|website=SoundCloud|accessdate=16 June 2014}}</ref>

In 2014, he announced the launch of his own record label, Cloudhead Records, under which he released an EP titled ''All This Time'' in the same year.<ref name="whiteraverrafting2" /><ref name="ukf2">{{cite web|url=http://ukf.com/words/exclusive-fox-stevenson-announces-cloudhead-records-brand-new-track-time/|title=EXCLUSIVE: Fox Stevenson announces Cloudhead Records and brand new track All This Time|date=10 March 2014|website=[[UKF Music|UKF]]|publisher=AEI Media|accessdate=16 June 2014}}</ref> In July 2014, Stevenson released ''Throwdown'', an EP accompanied by a live online premiere.<ref name="thump2">{{cite web|url=http://thump.vice.com/words/stream-fox-stevensons-blockbuster-new-ep-throwdown-before-its-official-release|title=Stream the Exclusive Premiere of Fox Stevenson’s Blockbuster New EP ‘Throwdown’|last=Schwartzberg|first=Lauren|date=14 July 2014|publisher=[[Vice (magazine)|Vice]]|work=Thump|accessdate=14 July 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140714231241/http://thump.vice.com/words/stream-fox-stevensons-blockbuster-new-ep-throwdown-before-its-official-release|archive-date=14 July 2014|url-status=dead}}</ref>

In August 2015, he released a collaboration on Spinnin' Records with UK EDM artist Curbi, titled ''Hoohah''.<ref name="soundcloud-curbi">{{cite web |url=https://soundcloud.com/curbiofficial/fox-stevenson-curbi-hoohah-original-mix |title=Hoohah – Fox Stevenson and Curbi |website=SoundCloud |date=24 August 2015 |accessdate=28 December 2016}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.edmsauce.com/2015/07/28/fox-stevenson-curbi-hoohah/|title=Fox Stevenson & Curbi – Hoohah [EDM Sauce Premiere]|last=BWizzy|date=28 July 2015|website=EDM Sauce|access-date=29 July 2017}}</ref> He signed with the dubstep label Disciple in March 2016 and in April 2016 released his first EP on the label, ''No Fox Given''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.youredm.com/2016/04/13/edm-premiere-fox-stevenson-get-strange-disciple/|title=Your EDM Premiere: Fox Stevenson – Get Strange [Disciple]|last=Meadow|first=Matthew|date=13 April 2016|website=Your EDM|access-date=29 July 2017}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ukf.com/news/fox-stevenson-signs-to-disciple/15718|title=UKF: Fox Stevenson Signs to Disciple|website=UKF}}</ref> On 12 September 2016, Fox Stevenson released a single titled "Rocket" on Disciple for the ''Alliance, Vol. 3'' EP.<ref name="edmchicago rocket2">{{cite web|url=http://edmchicago.com/2016/09/11/new-release-fox-stevenson-rocket/|title=New Release: Fox Stevenson – Rocket|last=Anthony|first=Cameron|date=11 September 2016|website=EDM Chicago|accessdate=15 September 2016}}</ref>

His debut album, ''[[Killjoy (Fox Stevenson album)|Killjoy]]'', was released on 18 October 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/FoxStevensonMusic/photos/a.715761061781977/2652250224799708/?type=1&theater|title=Debut album Killjoy presave link|last=Byrne|first=Stanley|date=22 August 2019|website=Facebook|access-date=12 October 2019}}</ref>

==Genres==
Stevenson is particularly known for creating [[liquid drum & bass]], [[glitch hop]], [[House music|house]] and [[dubstep]] tracks.<ref name="whiteraverrafting2"/> However, he is keen to experiment with many musical genres, stating that "there are tonnes of intricacies to each and every genre."<ref name="youredm-mix"/>
