'''Helena Deland''' is a Canadian singer-songwriter. To date she has released five [[Extended play|EPs]]: ''Drawing Room'' (2016<ref name="stereogum">{{cite web |last1=Robinson |first1=Collin |title=Helena Deland – "Aix" |url=https://www.stereogum.com/1891909/helena-deland-aix/mp3s/ |publisher=[[Stereogum]] |accessdate=12 November 2018}}</ref>) and ''From the Series of Songs "Altogether Unaccompanied"'' Vol. I, II, III & IV (2018) that she has written and composed. Her first one-piece album ''Someone New'' was released on 15 October 2020. Deland is currently signed to Luminelle Recordings (label founded by [[Gorilla vs. Bear]] & [[Fat Possum Records]]) for the world as well as Chivi Chivi for Canada. She has toured consistently since the beginning of her career in Canada, United States and Europe.

==Biography==
Born in [[Vancouver]], [[British Columbia]], Deland grew up in Quebec City and currently lives in [[Montreal]]. Her native languages are French and English; however, she primarily writes and sings in English.<ref>Werner, Mackenzie. [http://www.hooliganmagazine.com/blog/2018/12/15/interview-a-conversation-with-helena-deland "INTERVIEW: A conversation with Helena Deland"], ''[[Hooligan Magazine]]'', 15 December 2018. Retrieved on 6 March 2019.</ref> She began playing piano and guitar as a child and started singing when she moved to Montreal.<ref>Kidwell, Zoe. [https://milk.xyz/articles/getting-personal-with-helena-deland/ "Getting personal with Helena Deland"], Milk Agency, 19 October 2018. Retrieved on 6 March 2019.</ref> Deland's inspirations are diverse,<ref>Finn, Rachel. [http://diymag.com/2018/08/28/get-to-know-helena-deland-interview/ "Get to know… Helena Deland"], [[DIY Mag]], 28 August 2018. Retrieved on 6 March 2019.</ref> including [[Jessica Pratt (musician)|Jessica Pratt]], [[Joni Mitchell]], and Sea Oleena.

Her 2020 album ''Someone New'' was longlisted for the [[2021 Polaris Music Prize]],<ref>David Friend, [https://www.theglobeandmail.com/arts/music/article-daniel-lanois-savannah-re-charlotte-cardin-make-long-list-of-polaris/ "Daniel Lanois, Savannah Ré, Charlotte Cardin make long list of Polaris Music Prize"]. ''[[The Globe and Mail]]'', June 16, 2021.</ref> and was a [[Felix Award]] nominee for Anglophone Album of the Year at the [[43rd Félix Awards]].<ref>Louis-Philippe Labrèche, [https://lecanalauditif.ca/actualites/nominations-premier-gala-adisq-2021/ "Les nominations du Premier gala de l’ADISQ 2021"]. ''Le Canal Auditif'', September 22, 2021.</ref>
