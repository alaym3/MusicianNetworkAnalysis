'''Christopher Brent Wood''' (born September 19, 1995), better known by his stage name '''Brent Faiyaz''', is an American singer from [[Columbia, Maryland]].

== Early life ==
Faiyaz started making music at the age of 12. His parents were not always understanding of his dreams as a musician, which Faiyaz said was warranted as he was "always distracted" by his love for music.<ref name="Vogue" />

== Career ==
=== 2013–2016: Early career and ''A.M. Paradox'' ===
Faiyaz began uploading his experimental music (Black Child EP) onto [[SoundCloud]] in 2014 and moved from his hometown [[Columbia, Maryland]]  to [[Charlotte, North Carolina]] before settling in [[Los Angeles]], California to further his music career.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://artisticmanifesto.com/2016/09/19/brent-faiyaz-am-paradox/ |title=Stream Brent Faiyaz's fervid, soulful 'A.M. Paradox' debut |date=September 19, 2016 |work=Artistic Manifesto |access-date=October 12, 2017 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171013013831/http://artisticmanifesto.com/2016/09/19/brent-faiyaz-am-paradox/ |archive-date=October 13, 2017 |url-status=dead }}</ref> On January 19, 2015, he released his debut single "Allure".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://pigeonsandplanes.com/music/2015/01/premiere-brent-faiyaz-allure |title=Premiere: Brent Faiyaz – "Allure" |date=January 22, 2015 |first=Jon |last=Tanners |work=[[Pigeons and Planes]] |access-date=October 12, 2017}}</ref> On June 1, 2016, Faiyaz released "Invite Me", the lead single from his upcoming debut EP.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.hillydilly.com/2016/06/brent-faiyaz-invite-me-prod-by-pablo-dylan-james-harte/ |title=Brent Faiyaz – "Invite Me" (prod. by Pablo Dylan & James Harte) |date=June 1, 2016 |first=Tyler |last=Switsky |work=HillyDilly |access-date=October 12, 2017 |archive-date=October 13, 2017 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171013014209/https://www.hillydilly.com/2016/06/brent-faiyaz-invite-me-prod-by-pablo-dylan-james-harte/ |url-status=dead }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://artisticmanifesto.com/2016/06/06/brent-faiyaz-invite-me/ |title=Brent Faiyaz embraces his vulnerability on 'Invite Me' |date=June 6, 2016 |work=Artistic Manifesto |access-date=October 12, 2017 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171013014010/http://artisticmanifesto.com/2016/06/06/brent-faiyaz-invite-me/ |archive-date=October 13, 2017 |url-status=dead }}</ref> On September 19, 2016, his 21st birthday, the EP entitled ''A.M. Paradox'' was released and received positive reviews from music critics.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://pigeonsandplanes.com/music/2016/09/brent-faiyaz-am-paradox-ep |title=Brent Faiyaz Delivers Delicate Soul on "A.M. Paradox |date=September 19, 2016 |first=Nishant |last=Karvinkop |work=HillyDilly |access-date=October 12, 2017 |archive-date=October 13, 2017 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171013013717/https://www.hillydilly.com/2016/09/brent-faiyaz-a-m-paradox/ |url-status=dead }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://dailychiefers.com/brent-faiyaz-am-paradox-ep/ |title=Brent Faiyaz – AM Paradox [EP] |date=September 19, 2016 |work=Daily Chiefers |access-date=October 12, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thisisrnb.com/2016/09/listen-emerging-artist-brent-faiyaz-releases-buzz-worthy-a-m-paradox-ep/ |title=Listen: Emerging Artist Brent Faiyaz Releases Buzz-Worthy 'A.M. In October 2016, Faiyaz formed a group named Sonder with record producers Dpat and Atu.<ref name="pigeonsandplanes-1" /> The group released their debut single, "One night only," on October 25.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://pigeonsandplanes.com/music/2016/10/sonder-too-fast |title=Brent Faiyaz, Atu, and Dpat Share Sultry New Single as Sonder |date=October 25, 2016 |first=Adrienne |last=Black |work=[[Pigeons and Planes]] |access-date=October 12, 2017}}</ref> On December 16, 2016, Faiyaz was featured alongside rapper [[Shy Glizzy]] on the single "[[Crew (song)|Crew]]" by rapper [[GoldLink]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.xxlmag.com/rap-music/new-music/2016/12/goldlink-shy-glizzy-brent-faiyaz-crew/ |title=GoldLink, Shy Glizzy and Brent Faiyaz Start a "Crew" on New Single |date=December 16, 2016 |work=[[XXL (magazine)|XXL]] |access-date=October 12, 2017}}</ref>

=== 2017–present: ''Into'', ''Sonder Son'', ''Fuck the World'' ===
On January 26, 2017, Sonder released their debut EP ''Into'',<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thefader.com/2017/01/26/sonder-into-ep-premiere |title=Sonder's Sweet-Sounding into EP Is A Sincere Testament To Intimacy |date=January 26, 2017 |first=Lakin |last=Starling |work=[[The Fader]]|access-date=October 12, 2017}}</ref> for which ''Duplion'' named them their "Artist of the Month" for January 2017.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.duplion.com/sonder-into-ep/ |title=Sonder, Into EP (Brent Faiyaz, Atu, and Dpat): Artist of the Month |date=February 2, 2017 |work=Duplion |access-date=October 12, 2017 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171013074637/https://www.duplion.com/sonder-into-ep/ |archive-date=October 13, 2017 |url-status=dead }}</ref> The EP was also ranked 23rd by ''[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]]'' on its list of "The Best Albums of 2017 (So Far)" on June 6, 2017.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.complex.com/music/best-albums-2017/ |title=The Best Albums of 2017 (So Far) |date=June 6, 2017 |work=[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]] |access-date=October 12, 2017}}</ref> On June 21, 2017, Faiyaz was featured in the remix for GoldLink's "[[Crew (song)|Crew]]," which also featured Shy Glizzy and included rapper [[Gucci Mane]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2017/06/gucci-mane-goldlink-crew-remix/ |title=Gucci Mane Joins GoldLink for 'Crew (Remix)' |date=June 21, 2016 |first=Bryan |last=Hahn |work=[[XXL (magazine)|XXL]] |access-date=October 12, 2017}}</ref> On March 8, 2019, Faiyaz featured on the song "Demonz (Interlude)" from [[Juice Wrld]]'s second studio album ''[[Death Race for Love]]''.

Faiyaz released his second EP, ''Fuck the World'', on February 7, 2020.<ref name="Vogue">{{cite web|url=https://www.vogue.com/article/brent-faiyaz-on-his-new-music-and-staying-motivated/|title=Brent Faiyaz on His New Music and Staying Motivated in 2021|work=[[Vogue (magazine)|Vogue]]|last=Akili|first=King|date=January 29, 2021|accessdate=February 6, 2021}}</ref> It peaked at number 20 on the US [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]]. On September 18, 2020, a day before his birthday, he released the single "Dead Man Walking".<ref name="DMW">{{cite web|url=https://music.apple.com/nz/album/dead-man-walking-single/1529524254|title=Dead Man Walking – Single by Brent Faiyaz on Apple Music|via=[[Apple Music]]|access-date=September 17, 2020}}</ref> He returned with a new single, "[[Gravity (Brent Faiyaz and DJ Dahi song)|Gravity]]", on January 29, 2021. Produced by [[DJ Dahi]], the song features [[Tyler, the Creator]], who provides a "spacey" verse, while Faiyaz sings of "uncertain love and raps in a distilled voice praising loyalty from his female companion".<ref name="Gravity">{{cite web|url=https://rapradar.com/2021/01/28/new-music-brent-faiyaz-ft-dj-dahi-tyler-the-creator-gravity/|title=New Music: Brent Faiyaz Ft. DJ Dahi, Tyler, The Creator "Gravity"|work=[[Rap Radar]]|last=Duong|first=Paul|date=January 28, 2020|access-date=January 28, 2021}}</ref> On February 8 , he released a new song titled "Circles", alongside a video.<ref>{{cite tweet|number=1358860160106520580|title=Do Not Watch ...|user=brentfaiyaz|date=February 8, 2021|accessdate=February 8, 2021}}</ref> He is currently working on a new album, titled ''Make It Out Alive'', which is in the early recording process.<ref name="Vogue" /> On July 1, 2021, Faiyaz released a new single, "[[Wasting Time (Brent Faiyaz song)|Wasting Time]]" featuring [[Drake (musician)|Drake]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Minsker|first=Evan|title=Brent Faiyaz and Drake Share New Neptunes-Produced Song "Wasting Time": Listen|url=https://pitchfork.com/news/brent-faiyaz-and-drake-share-new-neptunes-produced-song-wasting-time-listen/|access-date=July 1, 2021|website=Pitchfork|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Brent Faiyaz Taps Drake for New Song "Wasting Time"|url=https://www.complex.com/music/brent-faiyaz-drake-wasting-time-song-stream/|access-date=July 1, 2021|website=Complex|language=en}}</ref> Faiyaz has now worked on a number of features such as artists like [[Tems (singer)|Tems]], [[Baby Keem]] and [[Meek Mill]].
