'''Luis Abner Mojica Sierra''' (born 17 August 1988), better known as '''Ele A el Dominio''', is a Puerto Rican [[Latin trap]] and [[reggaeton]] singer. He is known for being a key member of the Maliantiando movement in Trap Latino.<ref name="auto2"/>

==Career==

Ele A El Dominio is cousins with Jon Z, with whom he made the ''Super saiyan flow'' mixtape.<ref name="auto">{{Cite web|url=https://www.lacuarta.com/espectaculos/noticia/ele-a-el-dominio-villano-genero-urbano-mejor-ano/321185/|title="Ele A El Dominio Nigga": "Villano" del género urbano repasa su gran año|date=December 25, 2018|website=La Cuarta}}</ref><ref name="auto1">{{Cite web|url=https://remezcla.com/releases/music/ozuna-balenciaga-video/|title=Ozuna Teams Up With Ele a El Dominio For Jeva-Filled "Balenciaga" Video|date=April 3, 2018}}</ref> His mixtapes and singles have garnered over a million views on [[YouTube]].{{citation needed|date=December 2019}} He has risen to fame with songs such as "No podemos" which had a remix,<ref name="auto"/> "De matar tengo hambre", "Yo me la robé",<ref name="auto"/> "Maliantiando" and "Pal bote", which captured the eye of fans of underground [[hip hop music|hip hop]]. He is controversial for his social media conflicts, including public problems with artists such as [[Bad Bunny]] addressed by his track "Me tragué al Pacino" that discusses shooting the rapper and [[DJ Luian]]. He also has problems with [[Anuel AA]].<ref name="auto"/><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://larepublica.pe/espectaculos/1489092-instagram-anuel-aa-ozuna-copian-jon-z-ele-dominio-goku-vegeta-reggaeton-fotos/|title=Fans acusan a Anuel AA y Ozuna de copiar a Jon Z y Ele A El Dominio [FOTOS]|first=Redacción|last=LR|date=June 18, 2019|website=larepublica.pe}}</ref> El Dominio deliberately resists more commercial topics and music-making methods. His 2017 ''Mi Perico Remix'' mixtape with [[Arcángel (singer)|Arcángel]], [[Ñejo & Dalmata|Ñejo]] and [[Ñengo Flow]] proved influential in raising his profile.<ref name="auto2"/> He also worked with [[Ozuna (singer)|Ozuna]] in the song "Balenciaga"<ref name="auto1"/><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.oxigeno.fm/2018/ozuna-lanza-video-oficial-balenciaga-junto-ele-dominio-197428.html|title=Ozuna lanza el video oficial de "Balenciaga" junto a Ele A El Dominio|date=April 3, 2018}}</ref> and with [[Faraón Love Shady]] in "Panocha Remix".<ref>{{Cite web|last=Kid|first=Teki|title=Jon Z y Ele A El Dominio se unen a Faraón Love Shady para el remix de ”Panocha”|url=https://thenwordhh.com/jon-z-y-ele-a-el-dominio-se-unen-a-faraon-love-shady-para-el-remix-de-panocha/|access-date=2021-09-12|website=The N Word|language=es-MX}}</ref>
