'''Model''' was a Turkish rock band based in [[İzmir]], [[Turkey]].

== History ==
The band was founded with name A due Carmen in 2005 by Okan Işık, Aşkın Çolak and Can Temiz. [[Fatma Turgut]] and Serkan Gürüzümcü joined in 2007. The band members found each other on the internet. The group's name was changed to Model in 2008. Their first album, ''Perili Sirk'' was released in 2009.<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.modelband.com/MODEL/Biyografi.html |title= Model Band Biyografi|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111220094805/http://www.modelband.com/MODEL/Biyografi.html|archivedate=2011-12-20|language=tr}}</ref><ref name="milliyet" />

In 2011, Model released its second album, called ''Diğer Masallar'', which was produced by Demir Demirkan.<ref name="milliyet">{{cite web|url=https://www.milliyet.com.tr/cumartesi/birbirimizi-internetten-bulduk-1409200|title=“Birbirimizi internetten bulduk”|publisher=[[Milliyet]]|accessdate=2020-06-27|language=tr|date=2011-07-02}}</ref> Three songs, "Buzdan Şato", "Değmesin Ellerimiz", "Bir Melek Vardı", became very popular.{{citation needed|date=June 2020}} 

In 2012, Aşkın Çolak left the band and was replaced by Kerem Sedef on drums.<ref name="hürriyet">{{cite web|url=https://www.hurriyet.com.tr/kelebek/hayat/model-dagildi-bitti-mi-hikayeniz-bu-ne-bicim-son-boyle-40335864|title=Model dağıldı: Bitti mi hikâyeniz, bu ne biçim son böyle?|publisher=[[Hürriyet]]|accessdate=2020-06-27|date=2017-01-17|language=tr}}</ref>

Their third album, ''Levlâ'nın Hikayesi'', was put on the market by the label of GNL Entertainment in the last week of the November 2013.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sabah.com.tr/cumartesi/2013/11/16/levlnin-asiri-acikli-siradan-hikayesi|title=Levlâ'nın aşırı acıklı sıradan hikayesi|publisher=Sabah|accessdate=2020-06-27|language=tr|date=2013-11-16}}</ref> Model wanted to create a concept in this album: the five stages of mourning after separation, and then a human's own internal reckoning and relations are told with the help of the lyrics.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dr.com.tr/muzik/levlanin-hikayesi/model/yerli-albumler/turkce-rock/urunno=0000000569656|title=Levla'nın Hikayesi|publisher=DR.com|accessdate=2020-06-27|language=tr}}</ref>

The band disbanded in 2016 due to differences between the members.<ref name="hürriyet" />
