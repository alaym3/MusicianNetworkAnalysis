'''Savaş Yurderi''' (born 10 February 1975), known by his stage name '''Kool Savas''', is a [[German hip hop|German rapper]] and [[hip hop]] artist.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://rap.de/features/115 |title=Archived copy |access-date=2011-03-22 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110625192321/http://rap.de/features/115 |archive-date=25 June 2011}}</ref> Along with [[Taktlo$$]], he formed the highly influential German rap duo Westberlin Maskulin (1997–2000). He was also a founding member of the German rap crew Masters of Rap in 1996. 

In 2012, he collaborated in "Xavas", a duo formation with [[Xavier Naidoo]], a German [[Soul music|soul]] and [[Contemporary R&B|R&B]] singer-songwriter and actor in the album ''Gespaltene Persönlichkeit''. The two had already worked together on a number of other releases.

Savas is considered one of the most successful, best and influential German rappers. He is often referred to as the King of [[German rap]]. His nickname is ''King Kool Savas'' (KKS) and ''Essah''.

== Early life ==
Born in [[Aachen]], Germany to a German mother and a Turkish father. Savas' family moved to Turkey when he was one year old. His father, who is from [[Çorum]], Turkey, was arrested because of his political posture against the then-military government in Turkey, and as a result his mother was forced to return to Aachen with Savas.<ref>{{cite web|url={{Allmusic|class=artist|id=p703174|pure_url=yes}}|title=Biography: Kool Savas|last=Ankeny|first=Jason|publisher=[[AllMusic]]|access-date=22 May 2010}}</ref>

== Career ==
=== Essah Entertainment (2010–present) ===
His 2011 album ''Aura'' included a single with the same name and a song featuring [[Xavier Naidoo]], titled ''LMS 2012''. In May 2012, Savas announced a collaborative album with Xavier Naidoo titled ''[[Gespaltene Persönlichkeit]]'' under the alias "Xavas" scheduled for release in Germany on 21 September 2012.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.hiphop.de/magazin/news/detail/2012/5/7/kool-savas-xavier-naidoo-bringen-xavas-im-september-2012-63260/ |title=Kool Savas & Xavier Naidoo bringen XAVAS im September 2012 |publisher=hiphop.de (German) |date=7 May 2012 |access-date=21 August 2012}}</ref> The track list was announced in August 2012, containing a total of 15 tracks<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.hiphop.de/magazin/releases/detail/gespaltene-persoenlichkeit/?tx_cvreleases_pi1%5Bsearch%5D=gespaltene |title=Kool Savas & Xavier Naidoo Gespaltene Persönlichkeit |publisher=hiphop.de (German) |access-date=21 August 2012}}</ref>

His fourth studio album, ''Märtyrer'', was released on 14 November 2014.
