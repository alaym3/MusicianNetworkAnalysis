'''United Pursuit''' (also known as, '''United Pursuit Band''') is an American [[contemporary worship music]] band founded in 2006 from [[Knoxville, Tennessee]]. They have released three [[live album]]s, ''EP'' in 2008, ''Radiance'' in 2009, and ''[[Simple Gospel]]'' in 2015. The 2015 album was their breakthrough release upon the [[Billboard (magazine)|''Billboard'']] charts.

== Background ==
The band has its origins in a 2006 house purchase in the 5th Avenue section of downtown [[Knoxville, Tennessee]], where they hoped to attract believers and people for Jesus Christ.<ref name="AM Bio">{{cite web|title=United Pursuit Band : Biography|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/united-pursuit-band-mn0001581631/biography|first=Timothy|last=Monger|publisher=[[AllMusic]]|access-date=November 4, 2015}}</ref><ref name="CR Story">{{cite web|first=Tony|last=Cummings|title=Michael Ketterer & United Pursuit: Reflecting on the miracle of adoption|publisher=Cross Rhythms |url=http://www.crossrhythms.co.uk/articles/music/Michael_Ketterer__United_Pursuit_Reflecting_on_the_miracle_of_adoption/53682/p1/|access-date=November 4, 2015 |date=May 11, 2014 }}</ref> They started with the [[indie folk]] [[indie rock|rock]]  [[contemporary worship music|worship music]] back in 2008.<ref name="CR Profile">{{cite web|author=[[Cross Rhythms]]|title=United Pursuit Artist Profile|publisher=[[Cross Rhythms]]|url=http://www.crossrhythms.co.uk/artists/United_Pursuit/23101/ |access-date=November 4, 2015}}</ref> Their members are Michael Ketterer, Will Reagan, Brock Human, Nathan Fray, Jake LeBoeuf, Brandon Hampton, Andrea Marie Reagan, John Romero, and Yoosung Lee.<ref name="Facebook About">{{cite web|author=United Pursuit|title=About|publisher=[[Facebook]]|access-date=November 4, 2015 |url=https://www.facebook.com/unitedpursuit/info/?tab=overview}}</ref>
