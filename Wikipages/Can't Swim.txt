'''Can't Swim''' is an American [[Rock music|rock]] band from [[Keansburg, New Jersey]].<ref name="asburyparkpress">{{cite web|last1=Jordan|first1=Chris|title=Keansburg band Can't Swim makes a big splash|url=http://www.app.com/story/entertainment/music/2016/02/25/keansburg-band-cant-swim-makes-big-slash/80929722/|publisher=[[Asbury Park Press]]|accessdate=21 March 2017}}</ref>

==Career==
Can't Swim signed to Pure Noise Records in December 2015.<ref name="noiseyvice">{{cite web|last1=Elliot|first1=Griffin|title=Despite Their Name, Can't Swim Are Pretty Optimistic About Life|url=https://noisey.vice.com/en_us/article/cant-swim-death-deserves-a-name-punk-interview-2015|publisher=[[Vice (magazine)|Vice]]|accessdate=21 March 2017}}</ref> Following the signing, Can't Swim released their first EP a few months later in 2016 titled ''Death Deserves a Name''.<ref name="altpress">{{cite web|last1=Nickoloff|first1=Anne|title=Hear Can’t Swim’s entire EP, ‘Death Deserves A Name’ (exclusive)|url=http://www.altpress.com/features/entry/get_the_first_listen_of_cant_swims_entire_new_ep_exclusive|publisher=[[Alternative Press (music magazine)|Alternative Press]]|accessdate=21 March 2017}}</ref><ref name="punknews">{{cite web|title=Can't Swim Death Deserves A Name (2016)|url=https://www.punknews.org/review/14383/cant-swim-death-deserves-a-name|publisher=PunkNews|accessdate=21 March 2017}}</ref> In February 2017, the band announced plans to release their debut studio album.<ref name="altpress1">{{cite web|last1=Dickman|first1=Maggie|title=Can’t Swim announce album release date, debut new music video for "We Won’t Sleep"—watch|url=http://www.altpress.com/news/entry/cant_swim_announce_album_release_date_debut_new_music_video_for_we_wont_sle|publisher=Alternative Press|accessdate=21 March 2017}}</ref> The album, ''[[Fail You Again]]'', was released on March 10, 2017 via Pure Noise Records.<ref name="altpress2">{{cite web|last1=Ralph|first1=Caitlyn|title=Can’t Swim stream hard-hitting debut album over a week early—listen|url=http://www.altpress.com/features/entry/cant_swim_premiere_hard_hitting_debut_album_over_a_week_earlylisten|publisher=Alternative Press|accessdate=21 March 2017}}</ref><ref name="rocksound">{{cite web|last1=Doyle|first1=Tomas|title=CAN’T SWIM - ‘FAIL YOU AGAIN’|url=https://www.rocksound.tv/reviews/read/cant-swim-fail-you-again|publisher=[[Rock Sound]]|accessdate=21 March 2017}}</ref>

On October 4, 2018, the band released a new single (with an accompanying music video) titled "My Queen." They also released an album titled ''This Too Won't Pass'', on November 16, 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.kerrang.com/the-news/cant-swim-have-announced-their-new-album-this-too-wont-pass/|title=Can't Swim Have Announced Their New Album, This Too Won't Pass|website=Kerrang!}}</ref>

On October 11, 2019, Can’t Swim released a new EP titled "Foreign Language". This EP featured a whole new artistic direction with a heavier sound than previous releases.

On May 6, 2020, Can’t Swim released a new 4 Song EP titled “When the Dust Settles” featuring newly arranged tracks taken from their previous releases. 

On December 4, 2020, Can't Swim released their 4th EP "Someone Who Isn't Me" featuring yet another new artistic direction featuring a more electronic sound than previous releases.

On October 22, 2021, Can't Swim released their 3rd LP "Change of Plans" marking a turning point in the bands career as it combines all the elements from previous releases into one unified sound.
