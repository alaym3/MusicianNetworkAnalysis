'''William Nzobazola''' better known by his stage name '''Ninho''' (born 2 April 1996)<ref>[http://nextplz.fr/actualite/content/2283425-ninho-balance-son-freestyle-binks-to-binks-part-2 NextPlz: Ninho balance son freestyle Binks to Binks Part 2 - Le nouveau talent du rap français] {{in lang|fr}}</ref> is a [[France|French]] rapper of [[DR Congo|Congolese]] descent who has released five successful albums—''M.I.L.S (Maintenant ils le savent)'' in 2016, ''Comme prévu'' in 2017, ''M.I.L.S. 2.0'' in 2018, ''Destin'' in 2019, and M.I.L.S 3.
