'''3BallMTY''' (pronounced "'''Tribal [[Monterrey]]'''") is a [[Mexican people|Mexican]] pop DJ group from the city of [[Monterrey, Nuevo León]], [[Mexico]]. The "DJ Collective" (the name its members have formally given themselves) was formed in 2009 by two teenage DJs – Sergio Zavala (DJ Sheeqo Beat), and Alberto Presenda (DJ Otto). The name "3Ball" comes from the Spanish word "tribal", which refers to the culture of [[Trival|Guaracha tribal music]] popular in many Spanish-speaking countries around the world.

==History==
===2009: Formation of 3Ball MTY===
In August 2009, teenage Monterrey disc jockey [[Erick Rincon]] joined up with fellow DJs Sergio Zavala and Alberto Presenda. Quite fitting to their synthy, techno-inspired sound, the three DJs met through the Internet. The trio had a vision of combining two fundamental facets of the music industry – production and performance. Rincon has cited Between 2009 and 2010, the group became known as 3BallMTY, and set out to lay a new foundation for "latino electronica", which 3Ball has introduced that year.<ref>{{cite web|author=Reed Johnson |url=http://articles.latimes.com/2013/mar/04/entertainment/la-et-ms-0305-3ballmty-20130305 |title=Monterrey's 3BALLMTY gives electronic dance some new rhythms |website=Articles.latimes.com |date=2013-03-04 |access-date=2016-03-04}}</ref>

In December 2010, they were invited to the Worldtronics festival being held in [[Germany]]. It was the first time the group traveled outside Mexico. In 2011, the group signed a record contract with label Latin Power Music, a division of [[Universal Music]]. On December 6, they released their debut album, ''Intentalo'', which features collaborations with artists such as Smoky, America Sierra, El Bebeto, Morenito, Favela, Milkman and Horacio Palencia.<ref>[https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/intentalo/id482038011 "Intentalo by tribal monterrey ."] iTunes. Apple, Inc. Retrieved on May 6, 2012.</ref>

=== 2010 - 2013: ''Intentalo'', Latin Music Awards,Touring ===
3Ball MTY rose to prominence in 2012 when their single "Inténtalo", taken from [[Inténtalo|their first studio album of the same name]], featuring El Bebeto and America Sierra reached No.1 on the [[Billboard Latin Songs|''Billboard'' Latin Songs]] chart for two non-consecutive weeks. The song was performed live by the group at the 2012 ceremony of the [[Billboard Latin Music Awards]].<ref>[http://www.latinpowermusic.com.mx/nota/73 "3BALLMTY Encenderá los Premios Billboard Latinos 2012"]. Latin Music Power. Retrieved on May 6, 2012.</ref>

On February 4, 2012, they began their <ref>[http://www.hispanicbusiness.com/2012/4/24/stars_continue_to_line_up_for.htm "Stars Continue To Line Up For The 2012 Billboard Latin Music Awards Presented By State Farm April 26 At 7ET/6CT On Telemundo From Miami, Florida"] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140729144321/http://www.hispanicbusiness.com/2012/4/24/stars_continue_to_line_up_for.htm |date=2014-07-29 }}. Hispanic Business. Retrieved on May 6, 2012.</ref> first tour of [[Mexico]].

On October 18, 2012, 3Ball MTY and Gerardo Ortiz dominated the Billboard Mexican Music Awards. At the second annual music awards 3Ball MTY received 9 awards with some of those including New Artist of the Year, Artist of the Year, Duo or Group; Song Artist of the Year and Digital Download Artist of the Year.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/474601/3ballmty-gerardo-ortiz-dominate-billboard-mexican-music-awards|title=3BallMTY, Gerardo Ortiz Dominate Billboard Mexican Music Awards|website=Billboard|access-date=2019-07-14}}</ref>

On November 15, 2012, 3Ball MTY won the [[Latin Grammy Awards of 2012|Latin Grammy Award]] for [[Latin Grammy Award for Best New Artist|Best New Artist]].

In 2013, they released a new single "[[Quiero Bailar (All Through the Night)]]" featuring [[Becky G]]. It premiered on September 10, 2013 on [[Univision]]. It is featured on the [[PlayStation 4]] and [[Xbox One]] versions of ''[[Need for Speed Rivals]]''.

=== 2014 - 2015: ''Globall'', and Touring ===
On March 24, 2014, 3Ball MTY released their second studio album "Globall" with various artist on the albums; such as [[Belinda Peregrín|Belinda]], [[Far East Movement]], [[Becky G]], [[Gerardo Ortiz]], [[Cowboy Troy]], Las Cumbia Girls, and [[Jotdog]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/going-out-guide/wp/2014/12/04/3ballmty-forges-new-paths-across-the-global-dance-floor/|title=3BallMTY forges new paths across the global dance floor|website=Washington Post|language=en|access-date=2019-07-14}}</ref>

=== 2016 - 2017: Erick Rincon's departure, "''Bailar Contigo''" - Single ===
On July 5, 2016, it has been announced by both members that Erick Rincon decided to leave the group to venture off on individual projects. It was also revealed that he was struggling in keeping up with both individual projects and group projects.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.aldiadallas.com/espanol/al-dia/espectaculos/2016/07/06/se-queda-3ballmty-sin-un-integrante/|title=Se queda 3BALLMTY sin un integrante|date=2019-07-08|website=Dallas News|language=en|access-date=2019-07-14}}</ref> The same year the group released the single "Bailar Contigo" featuring Chyno Miranda and El Jova, and performed at various concerts.

=== 2018 - 2019: Various singles and collaborations, and Touring ===
On November 2, 2018 3Ball MTY released there single "''Tequila''" featuring Chayin Rubio.

On March 14–15, 2019 3Ball MTY attended the SXSW 2019 at [[Austin, Texas]], alongside [[Voz de Mando]], [[Vanessa Zamora]], and Agrupacion Carino.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sxsw.com/music/2019/sxsw-presents-voz-de-mando-3ball-mty-at-austin-city-limits-live-at-the-moody-theater/|title=SXSW Presents Voz De Mando & 3ball MTY at Austin City Limits Live at the Moody Theater|date=2019-03-04|website=SXSW|language=en-US|access-date=2019-07-14}}</ref>

=== 2020 - present: "''Ferrari''" - Single, ''SOMOS'', Other Projects ===
During the month of January "Intentalo" was on Billboards top 100 placing at 38.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Rosas|first=Mike|title="Intentalo" una de las mejores canciones de la lista Hot 100 de Billboard.|url=http://www.3ballmty.com.mx/noticias/intentalo_una_de_las_mejores_canciones_de_la_lista_hot_100_de_billboard|access-date=2020-08-23|website=Latin Power Music|language=es}}</ref> On January 29, 2020 3Ball MTY released a new single "Ferrari" featuring Morenito de Fuego.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Rosas|first=Mike|title=3BALLMTY vuelve con "Ferrari", su nuevo sencillo|url=http://www.3ballmty.com.mx/noticias/3ballmty_vuelve_con_ferrari_su_nuevo_sencillo|access-date=2020-08-23|website=Latin Power Music|language=es}}</ref> The following month 3BALL MTY released there third studio album ''SOMOS'' with promotions after the release date.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Rosas|first=Mike|title=Todos Somos 3BALLMTY|url=http://www.3ballmty.com.mx/noticias/todos_somos_3ballmty|access-date=2020-08-23|website=Latin Power Music|language=es}}</ref> Following the release of the album, and promotions. 3BALL MTY released a statement on July 22 through social media "Nueva Musica en Camino"<ref>{{Cite web|title=3BALLMTY Statement|url=https://twitter.com/3ballmty/status/1285982394785370112|url-status=live|archive-date=2020-07-22|access-date=2020-08-23|website=Twitter|language=en}}</ref>
