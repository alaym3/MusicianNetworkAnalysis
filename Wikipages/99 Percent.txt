'''99 Percent''' is an American hip hop duo from the [[San Francisco Bay Area|Bay Area]] that consists of Cameron "Camo" Schauer and Johnnie "JayB" Jacob Jr. The duo gained their initial notoriety by from their 2013 single "Yike in It".<ref>{{cite web|last1=Garvey|first1=Meaghan|title=Video: 99%, "Yike In It"|url=http://www.thefader.com/2014/01/20/video-99-yike-in-it|website=The Fader|access-date=6 July 2016}}</ref> In 2014, they released "iTwerk (She Twerk)".<ref>{{cite web|last1=Nostro|first1=Lauren|title=Premiere: OG Pollard Stars in 99 Percent's "iTwerk" Video|url=http://www.complex.com/music/2015/02/99-percent-itwerk-video-premiere|website=Complex News|access-date=6 July 2016}}</ref> In July 2015, they released the single "Does Ya Mama Know (Dance Like That)", which has collected over 3 million views on YouTube.<ref>{{Citation|last=its99Percent|title=99 Percent - Does Ya Mama Know? (Dance Like That) #HEYNOW|date=2015-07-22|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Fvk1TXKRtRM|access-date=2016-09-25}}</ref>

In conjunction with [[Live Nation Entertainment|Live Nation]], 99 Percent was on the Let’s Dance 2016 U.S. Tour headlining with [[Silentó]], [[iLoveMemphis]], [[DLow|DLOW]], and [[We Are Toonz|We are Toonz]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://investors.livenationentertainment.com/news-center/news-center-details/2016/Lets-Dance-The-Tour-2016-Starring-Silento-iLOVEMemphis-fka-iHEART-DLOW-We-Are-Toonz-And-99-Percent-To-Kick-Off-On-March-4th/default.aspx|title=Live Nation Entertainment - "Let's Dance" The Tour 2016 Starring Silento, iLOVEMemphis (fka iHEART), DLOW, We Are Toonz And 99 Percent To Kick Off On March 4th|website=investors.livenationentertainment.com|access-date=2016-07-13|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160818161450/http://investors.livenationentertainment.com/news-center/news-center-details/2016/Lets-Dance-The-Tour-2016-Starring-Silento-iLOVEMemphis-fka-iHEART-DLOW-We-Are-Toonz-And-99-Percent-To-Kick-Off-On-March-4th/default.aspx|archive-date=2016-08-18|url-status=dead}}</ref>{{better source needed|reason=press release|date=May 2020}}
