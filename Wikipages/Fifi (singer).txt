'''Filloreta Raçi''' ({{IPA-sq|fiɫɔˈɾɛta ˈɾatʃi|lang}}; born 31 March 1994), known professionally as '''Fifi''', is a Kosovo-Albanian singer and songwriter.

== Life and career ==

Fifi was born as Filloreta Raçi on 31 March 1994 in [[Herten]], Germany into an Albanian family from [[Ferizaj]], Kosovo.<ref name="TeksteShqip">{{cite web |publisher=TeksteShqip |title=Filloreta Raçi (Fifi)–Biografia |url=https://www.teksteshqip.com/filloreta-ra-i/biografia |access-date=10 March 2021 |language=sq}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Top Channel|Top Music Awards]] |title=Fifi #Bio |url=https://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/artist/fifi/ |access-date=10 March 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201025173034/https://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/artist/fifi/ |archive-date=25 October 2020 |language=sq |url-status=dead}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Televizioni Klan]] (TV Klan) |title=Fifi: Nuk do doja të isha kthyer në Kosove, nuk do kisha vuajtur kaq shumë |url=https://tvklan.al/fifi-nuk-do-doja-te-isha-kthyer-ne-kosove-nuk-do-kisha-vuajtur-kaq-shume/ |access-date=11 March 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200417041231/https://tvklan.al/fifi-nuk-do-doja-te-isha-kthyer-ne-kosove-nuk-do-kisha-vuajtur-kaq-shume/ |archive-date=11 March 2021 |language=sq |date=5 April 2020 |quote=Sepse unë kam lindur në Gjermani edhe kam jetuar atje deri në 2000}}</ref> Raised in Germany, she became interested in literature and theatre as well as music and attended the music school of [[Pristina]].<ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Top Channel|MelodyTv]] |title=Fifi (Filloreta Raçi Biografia |url=https://melodytv.al/artists/fifi-filloreta-raci/ |access-date=11 March 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210311124212/https://melodytv.al/artists/fifi-filloreta-raci/ |archive-date=11 March 2021 |language=sq}}</ref> Forming her early forays into the music industry, Raçi participated in various music and talent competitions, including at [[Albanians Got Talent]], [[The Voice of Albania]] and [[Top Fest]].<ref name="TeksteShqip"/><ref>{{cite web |work=[[Telegrafi]] |title=Befason Fifi: Do kisha rrjedhur psigjikisht, nëse do vazhdoja të isha në 'The Voice of Albania' |url=https://telegrafi.com/befason-fifi-kisha-rrjedhur-psigjikisht-nese-vazhdoja-te-isha-ne-voice-albania-video/ |access-date=11 March 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210311125653/https://telegrafi.com/befason-fifi-kisha-rrjedhur-psigjikisht-nese-vazhdoja-te-isha-ne-voice-albania-video/ |archive-date=11 March 2021 |language=sq |date=22 October 2017}}</ref> Adopting the stage name Fifi, she debuted with the single "FrymomMu" in 2015.<ref name="TeksteShqip"/> In March 2021, [[Televizioni Klan]] (TV Klan) announced Raçi along Albanian singer Bruno as one of the contestants selected to compete in the [[Kënga Magjike 2021|22nd edition]] of [[Kënga Magjike]], marking her fourth consecutive participation at the competition.<ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Televizioni Klan]] (TV Klan) |title=Kush janë artistët BIG që do të jenë në festivalin 'Kënga Magjike 2021'? 2021 |url-status=live}}</ref> The singer also appeared on the first season of Albanian reality show [[Big Brother VIP (Albanian TV series)|Big Brother VIP]] in the span from October to November 2021.
