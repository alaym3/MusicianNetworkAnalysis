'''Rikhado Ribeca Makhado''' (born July 20, 1987), known professionally as '''Riky Rick''', is a [[South Africa]]n rapper, songwriter, singer, composer, actor, entrepreneur, fashion designer. Makhado is the founder and owner of record label Cotton Club Records.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://sahiphopmag.co.za/2018/02/riky-rick-launches-cotton-club-records/|title=Riky Rick Launches Cotton Club Record|website=SA Hip Hop Mag}}</ref> He was also a member of ''Boyz N Bucks''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.dailysun.co.za/News/Entertainment/boyz-n-bucks-are-back-with-a-new-song-20180710|title=BOYZ N BUCKS ARE BACK WITH A NEW SONG|website=Daily Sun}}</ref>

Rick's debut studio album ''Family Values'' (2015), which became certified platinum by the [[Recording Industry of South Africa]] (RiSA). Makhado rose to fame when he released his single "Nafukwa", in 2014.

== Life and career ==
===1987-2012:Early life ===
Rikhado Ribeca Makhado born July June 20, 1987 and was raised in [[KwaMashu]] township near [[Durban]], [[KwaZulu-Natal]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.afda.co.za/article/1259/ALUMNI-OF-THE-WEEK-131-Riky-Rick/|title=ALUMNI OF THE WEEK # 131 Riky Rick &#124; AFDA Film School|website=www.afda.co.za}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sahistory.org.za/dated-event/riky-rick-one-most-esteemed-south-african-hip-hop-artists-born|title=Riky Rick, one of the most esteemed South African hip hop artists is born &#124; South African History Online|website=www.sahistory.org.za}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.ecr.co.za/east-coast-breakfast-darren-maule/listen-rapper-riky-ricks-rise-to-stardom/|title=Listen: Rapper Riky Rick's rise to stardom|website=ECR}}</ref>

=== Career ===
Riky Rick's first side of music began at a recording studio session with Bongani Fassie. It was by Bongani's side that Rick was inspired to make his own music.<ref>{{Cite web|title = Introducing Ricky Rick|url = http://drum.co.za/celebs/getting-to-know-ricky-rick/|website = DRUM|access-date = 2016-02-10}}</ref> That is when he released his first single "Barbershop" featuring [[Da L.E.S]].<ref>{{Cite web|title = Barbershop|url = http://channelo.dstv.com/2011/05/riky-rick-ft-da-l-e-s-barbershop/|website = channel O|access-date = 2016-02-10}}</ref> He then rose to fame by the release of his hit single "Nafukwa" which was  a big success.<ref>{{Cite web|title = Nafukwa|url = http://www.okayafrica.com/news/riky-rick-fokjullenaaiers-nafukwa/|website = Okayafrica|access-date = 2016-02-10}}</ref> He then released "Amantombazane" which was followed by "Amantombazane (Remix)" featuring fellow South African recording artists OkMalumkoolKat, Maggz, Kwesta, Ginger Bread Man, Kid X, [[Nadia Nakai]] and DJ Dimplez.<ref>{{Cite web|title = Amantombozane|url = http://www.okayafrica.com/video/riky-rick-okmalumkoolkat-amantombazane-remix-video/|website = Okayafrica|access-date = 2016-02-10}}</ref>

===2015-2019: ''Family Values'', ''Scooby Snacks'' EP===
On April 3, 2015, his debut studio album ''Family Values'' was released and received postive review from music critics.<ref>{{cite web|title=Riky Rick Drops Family Values Debut Album - Online Youth Magazine {{!}} Zkhiphani |url=https://www.zkhiphani.co.za/riky-rick-drops-family-values-debut-album/|publisher=Zkhiphani|access-date=April 3, 2015}}</ref> The album was certified platinum by the [[Recording Industry of South Africa]] (RiSA) with sales of 50 000 units.<ref>{{cite web|title=Riky Rick's Family Values goes platinum! |url=https://www.timeslive.co.za/tshisa-live/tshisa-live/2016-06-14-riky-ricks-family-values-goes-platinum/|publisher=Times LIVE| access-date=2016-06-14}}</ref> In April 24, his single "Sondela"  featuring  Zano was released, which was dedicated to his real love.<ref>{{Cite web|title = Sondela|url = http://blacknation.co.za/music-video-riky-rick-sondela-ft-zano/|website = Blacknation|access-date = 2016-02-10}}</ref> In October 28, his major debut single "Boss Zonke" was released<ref>{{cite web|title=Boss Zonke goes viral!|url=https://www.zkhiphani.co.za/boss-zonke-goes-viral/| publisher=Zkhiphani}}</ref>  which peaked at number 3 on the SA Hip Hop Top Ten List.   In June 2016, his  single "Sidlukotini" was released.<ref>{{Cite web|title = Riky Rick – Sidlukotini Music Video|url = https://360musicnghq.co/video-riky-rick-sidlukotini/|website = 360musicnghq.co|access-date = 2016-06-26|language = en-US}}</ref>

Riky Rick released the ''Scooby Snacks'' EP that features only one artist, Frank Casino. It includes the tracks "Oh Lord", "Bandz Over Fans" and "Family" (featuring Frank Casino).<ref>{{Cite web|title = Riky Rick Releases Three New Songs Called Scooby Snacks|url = http://hiphopza.com/riky-rick-releases-three-new-songs-called-scooby-snacks/|website = Hiphopza.com|access-date = 2017-06-15|language = en-US}}</ref> He released "Buy It Out" on 15 September 2017.<ref>{{Cite web|title = Riky Rick – Buy It Out|url = https://360musicnghq.co/riky-rick-buy/|website = 360musicnghq.co|access-date = 2017-09-16|language = en-US}}</ref> In November 2018, Makhado announced his first concert called "Cotton Fest", which he headlined on February 2, 2019.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.joburg.co.za/riky-rick-cotton-fest-2019/|title=Riky Rick Presents Cotton Fest 2019|website=Joburg}}</ref>

On July 2, 2021, he released a single "Forever" with Frank Casino.<ref>{{cite web|title=Mzansi Rappers Frank Casino, Riky Rick Tag Team on New Drop ‘Forever’|url= https://briefly.co.za/103584-frank-casino-riky-rick-tag-team-rap-drop-forever.html|date=July 1, 2021|first= Tshepiso |last=Mametela|work=Briefly}}</ref>

==Personal life==
===Relationships===
In early 2010, Makhado first met Bianca Naidoo in Sandton and later gave a birth to Jordan Makhado. In 2013, the couple got married, and had their son Maik Daniel Makhado (born. 2014). Rick suffered from an alcohol and drug addiction for a long time.<ref>{{Cite web|title = Riky Rick gets personal|url = http://www.channel24.co.za/The-Juice/WATCH-Riky-Rick-opens-up-about-his-struggles-20150510|website = channel24|access-date = 2016-02-10}}</ref>
