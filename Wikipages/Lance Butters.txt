'''Lance Butters''' (born 1988) is a [[Germany|German]] rapper. Born and raised in [[Friedrichshafen]] in southern Germany, Butters started rapping in his youth.

==Biography==
Lance Butters was born in 1988 in the South German town [[Friedrichshafen]]. In his childhood, his parents divorced, which he often mentioned in his music. Between 2006 and 2010, he participated in the Reimliga Battle Arena, a German online platform for [[Battle rap|rap battles]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://r-b-a.de/index.php?ID=4100&sid=45a8dd62296e5b090a48af43cdcade65&direction=last&MEMBER=32153|title=Lance Butters|publisher=Reimliga Battle Arena|language=de|accessdate=15 September 2020|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20200915201742/https://r-b-a.de/index.php?ID=4100&sid=45a8dd62296e5b090a48af43cdcade65&direction=last&MEMBER=32153|archivedate=15 September 2020|url-status=live}}</ref> In 2010 and 2011, he participated in the Videobattleturnier (VBT), but lost against Tamo-Flage in 2016 and T-Jey in 2011.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rappers.in/de/vbt_2010-Tamo-Flage-vs-Lance+Butters-946.html|title=VBT Videos 2010 – Tamo-Flage vs. Lance Butters|publisher=rappers.in|accessdate=September 16, 2020|language=de|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20181111080018/http://www.rappers.in/de/vbt_2010-Tamo-Flage-vs-Lance+Butters-946.html|archivedate=November 11, 2018|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rappers.in/de/vbt_2011-Lance+Butters-vs-Ti+Jay-2267.html|title=VBT Videos 2011 – Lance Butters vs. Ti Jay|publisher=rappers.in|accessdate=16 September 2020|language=de|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20200915222700/https://www.rappers.in/de/vbt_2011-Lance+Butters-vs-Ti+Jay-2267.html|archivedate=15 September 2020|url-status=live}}</ref> In the years, he also released multiple freetracks with the producer Bennett One on [[YouTube]]. On 22 December 2011, he released the studio album ''cooking sum'' as a member of the Frank Castle Cooking Gang, through [[Bandcamp]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://frankcastlecookinggang.bandcamp.com/album/cooking-sum|title=cooking sum by Frank Castle Cooking Gang|date=22 December 2011|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20200915223511/https://frankcastlecookinggang.bandcamp.com/album/cooking-sum|archivedate=15 September 2020|via=Bandcamp|access-date=15 September 2020|url-status=dead}}</ref> On 27 July 2012, he independently released his first solo project, the ''selfish EP''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://rap.de/allgemein/10908-neues-von-lance-butters-ep/|title=Neues von Lance Butters‘ EP|trans-title=New information of Lance Butters EP|website=Rap.de|date=3 July 2020|accessdate=16 September 2020|language=de}}</ref> In May 2013, he signed with [[Four Music]], through he released the EP ''futureshit'' on 12 July 2013.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://hiphop.de/magazin/news/lance-butters-signt-bei-four-music-181397|title=Lance Butters signt bei Four Music|trans-title=Lance Butters signs with Four Music|website=Hiphop.de|first=Kristof|last=Jansen|date=17 May 2013|accessdate=16 September 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://hiphop.de/magazin/news/lance-butters-ver%C3%B6ffentlicht-futureshit-ep-im-juli-181636|title=Lance Butters veröffentlicht "Futureshit"-EP im Juli|trans-title=Lance Butter to release "Futureshit"-EP in July|website=Hiphop.de|first=Kristof|last=Jansen|date=21 June 2013|accessdate=16 September 2020}}</ref> The EP charted at no. 32 for one week on the German Albums Chart,<ref name="GER">{{cite web|url=https://www.offiziellecharts.de/suche?artist_search=Lance+Butters&do_search=do|title=Suchen nach "Lance Butters"|trans-title=Search after "Lance Butters"|publisher=GfK web|url=https://austriancharts.at/showinterpret.asp?interpret=Lance+Butters|title=Diskografie – Lance Butters|publisher=Ö3 Austria Top 40|accessdate=16 September 2020|language=ja}}</ref>

His debut studio album ''Blaow'' was released on 8 May 2015 and debuted in the top ten in Germany and Austria. His second album was released on 12 October 2018.

On 20 April 2020, Butters released the single "Therapie" to commemorate the [[420 (cannabis culture)|International day for cannabis]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.laut.de/News/420-Lance-Butters-Therapie-heisst-Weed-20-04-2020-16923|title=4/20: Lance Butters "Therapie" heißt Weed|trans-title=4/20: Lance Butters "Therapie" means Weed|website=Laut.de|date=20 April 2020|accessdate=16 September 2020|language=de}}</ref> The following day, he released his fifth extended play ''Loner'' through A Corn Dawg Records.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://rap.de/featured/180471-lance-butters-veroeffentlicht-die-loner-ep/|title=Lance Butters – Loner (prod. Kidney Paradise)|first=Simon|last=Forster|date=21 April 2020|website=Rap.de|accessdate=16 September 2020|language=de}}</ref> The same day, he announced a tour in Germany and Austria for October 2020, which was postponed due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic]] to May/June 2021.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.instagram.com/p/B_PTtSOhIqm/|title=Loner Tour im Oktober!|trans-title=Loner Tour in October!|publisher=Lance Butters|via=Instagram|date=21 April 2020|accessdate=16 September 2020|language=de}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.instagram.com/p/CE1agM1h-9V/|title=Update|publisher=Lance Butters|via=Instagram|date=7 September 2020|accessdate=16 September 2020|language=de}}</ref>
