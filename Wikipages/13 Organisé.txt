'''''13 Organisé''''' ({{IPA-fr|tʁɛzɔʁɡanize}}), stylized as '''''13'Organisé''''', is a French rap album through a collaboration of around 50 French rappers mainly from [[Marseille]]. The album was released on Rien 100 Rien label on 9 October 2020.

==13 Organisé collective==
The project was launched by rapper [[Jul (rapper)|Jul]] in mid-August 2020, when he announced it naming the initial participants as [[SCH (rapper)|SCH]], [[Naps (rapper)|Naps]], [[Soprano (rapper)|Soprano]], [[Alonzo (rapper)|Alonzo]], [[Soso Maness]], Kofs, [[L'Algérino]] and groups and collectives like [[IAM (band)|IAM]], [[Fonky Family]] and [[Ghetto Phénomène]], and mentioning the album launch on 9 October 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Jul annonce "Bande organisée" avec SCH, Naps, Kofs, Soso Maness...|url= https://www.mouv.fr/musique/rap-fr/jul-annonce-bande-organisee-avec-sch-naps-kofs-soso-maness-362524|website=Mouv'|accessdate=25 October 2020|language=fr}}</ref> Others added later included Elams, Solda, Houari, Stone Black, [[Le Rat Luciano]], Veazy, Many, Moubarak, Jhonson, As, Fahar, Friz, Vincenzo, Drime, Oussagaza, Don Choa, SAF, 2Bang, Youzi, Sysa, Thabiti, Zbig, AM La Scampia, [[Keny Arkana]], Graya, 100 Blaze, Sauzer, Sat L'Artificier, Banguiz, Kamikaz, Moh, 100 Blaze, Dadinho, A-Deal & Zak, Diego, Tonyno, Kara, Daz, Bylk, Djiha, [[Akhenaton (rapper)|Akhenaton]], [[Shurik'n]].

==Release==
On 15 August 2020, Jul released the single "Bande organisée" with SCH, Kofs, Naps, Soso Maness, Elams, Houari and Solda.<ref>{{Cite web|title=JuL, SCH, Kofs, Naps, Soso Maness, Elams, Houari et Solda évoluent en "Bande organisée" (Clip)|url= https://www.13or-du-hiphop.fr/2020/08/15/jul-sch-kofs-naps-soso-maness-elams-houari-et-solda-evoluent-en-bande-organisee-clip/|website=13or-du-hiphop.fr|accessdate=25 October 2020}}.</ref> The single, a prelude for the album was an immediate success and topped the French Singles Chart for several weeks. the single went platinum and then diamond becoming the fastest single being classified diamond in French music history. It also attracted around 148 million views on YouTube until 25 October 2020.

In three days, the album ''13 Organisé'' sold more than 20,000 copies<ref name="13or">{{Cite web|title=13'Organisé de Jul, les chiffres des ventes connus, c'est monstrueux !|url= https://www.13or-du-hiphop.fr/2020/10/12/13organise-de-jul-les-chiffres-des-ventes-connus-cest-monstreux/|website=13or-du-hiphop.fr|accessdate=13 October 2020|language=fr}}</ref> reaching 36,000 copies in a week being certified gold just 2 weeks after release.<ref name="mcetv-certif">{{Cite web|title=Jul est très fier du disque d'or de 13 Organisé atteint en 14 jours !|url=https://mcetv.fr/mon-mag-culture/mon-mag-musique/jennifer-lopez-ravie-davoir-gagne-un-prix-billboard-latin-music-23102020/|website=mcetv.fr|accessdate=25 October 2020|language=fr}}</ref> The album topped the French Albums Chart in its first week of release.
