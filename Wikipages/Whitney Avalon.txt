'''Whitney Avalon''' is an American actress, writer, [[Online producer|producer]], singer, and [[rapper]] best known for creating the ''Princess Rap Battle'' series and other musical comedy on YouTube, where her channel has over 600 million views.<ref>{{cite news |last=Sifferlin  |first=Alexandra  |date=March 14, 2015 |title=Watch This Princess Rap Battle Between Cinderella and Belle |url=http://time.com/3745057/cinderella-belle-rap-battle/ |newspaper=[[Time (magazine)|Time]] }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last=Berkey  |first=Rachael  |date= September 24, 2014 |title=Disney Princesses Throw Down in an Epic Rap Battle |url=http://nerdist.com/disney-princesses-throw-down-in-epic-rap-battle/ |newspaper=[[Nerdist News|Nerdist]] }}</ref><ref name="channel">{{cite web | title = YouTube: WhitneyAvalon | url = https://www.youtube.com/user/WhitneyAvalon | via = YouTube | accessdate = July 8, 2015}}</ref>

''[[Variety (magazine)|Variety]]'' listed her in their 2015 Comedy Impact Report,<ref>{{cite news |date=November 10, 2015 |title=Variety Comedy Impact Report 2015: Whitney Avalon Digital Princess |url=https://variety.com/gallery/comedy-impact-report-2015/#!2/whitney-avalon/ }}</ref> stating her work has "amassed over half a billion views" worldwide in a year. She was also named one of ''[[Cosmopolitan (magazine)|Cosmopolitan]]'s'' "Internet's Most Fascinating" people in 2015.<ref name="CosmoMostFascinating" />

== ''Princess Rap Battles'' ==
The ''Princess Rap Battles'' series was created by Whitney Avalon and features fictional female characters facing off against each other in the form of a [[Battle rap|rap battle]]. Whitney Avalon appears in every episode as a major rap opponent.

She posted the first Princess Rap Battle "[[Snow White (Disney character)|Snow White]] vs. [[Elsa (Disney)|Elsa]]" in September 2014.<ref name=CosmoMostFascinating>{{cite news |last=Kim |first=Heeseung |date=n.d.|title=Just When You Thought Disney Princesses Were Played Out, Whitney Avalon Created Viral *~MaGiC~* Again |url=http://www.cosmopolitan.com/entertainment/celebs/a40064/princess-rap-battle-whitney-avalon-internets-most-fascinating/ |newspaper=[[Cosmopolitan (magazine)|Cosmopolitan]] }}</ref> Since then, she has posted more rap battles on the channel, including "[[Galadriel]] vs. [[Leia]]", "[[Mrs. Claus]] vs. [[Mary Poppins (character)|Mary Poppins]]", and "[[Cinderella (Disney character)|Cinderella]] vs. [[Belle (Disney)|Belle]]" starring [[Sarah Michelle Gellar]], who is a fan of the series.<ref name="CosmoMostFascinating"/> Subsequent episodes include "[[Maleficent]] vs. [[Daenerys]]" starring [[Yvonne Strahovski]],<ref name="channel"/><ref>{{cite web|last1=Riley|first1=Jenelle|title='Astronaut Wives Club' Star Yvonne Strahovski Enlisted for Next 'Princess Rap Battle'|url=https://variety.com/2015/digital/news/yvonne-strahovski-princess-rap-battle-1201511118/|work=Variety|accessdate=July 8, 2015}}</ref> "[[Katniss Everdeen|Katniss]] vs. [[Hermione Granger|Hermione]]" starring [[Molly Quinn]],<ref name="channel"/> "[[The Snow Queen|Freya]] vs. [[Queen (Snow White)|Ravenna]]"<ref>[https://www.imdb.com/title/tt5578084/ "Freya vs. Ravenna". ''IMDB'' Retrieved May 27, 2016.]</ref> and "[[Rapunzel (Disney)|Rapunzel]] & [[Flynn Rider|Flynn]] vs. [[Anna (Disney)|Anna]] & [[Kristoff (Disney)|Kristoff]]". Avalon then posted two Wonderland vs. Oz themed battles: "[[Dorothy Gale|Dorothy]] vs. [[Alice (Alice's Adventures in Wonderland)|Alice]]" starring [[Emily Kinney]], [[Ryan McCartan]], [[Jason Rogel]], and Joey Richter<ref name="channel"/> followed by "[[Queen of Hearts (Alice's Adventures in Wonderland)|The Queen of Hearts]] vs. [[Wicked Witch of the West|The Wicked Witch of the West]]" with Alyssa Preston, [[Ben Giroux]] and (like all the battles) Avalon herself.<ref name="channel"/> The most recent episode of the series featured [[Harley Quinn]], [[Black Canary]], and [[Huntress (Helena Bertinelli)|Huntress]] teaming up.

== Appearance in other media ==
Avalon graduated [[summa cum laude]] from [[Brandeis University]] in 2003 with a [[Bachelor of Arts|B.A.]] in Theater Arts.<ref>{{cite news |last=Farrell |first=Kerri|date=March 11, 2014 |title=Alumna enjoying success in Hollywood |url=https://alumni.brandeis.edu/news-publications/news-archive/avalon.html |website=alumni.brandeis.edu
}}</ref><ref name=Resume>{{Cite web|title=Whitney Avalon: actor, singer, writer, producer, princess rap battler, sillyface|url=https://www.whitneyavalon.com/reacutesumeacute.html|access-date=2020-07-19|website=WHITNEY AVALON|language=en}}</ref> She is a working actress in television, theater, and commercials.<ref name="CosmoMostFascinating" /> In 2013, she played the mom in the controversial [[Cheerios]] commercial "Just Checking", featuring an interracial family.<ref>[http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2013/06/06/whitney-avalon-actress-in_n_3399959.html "Whitney Avalon, Actress In Cheerios Ad Featuring Mixed Race Family: 'I Thought It Was Adorable'". ''Huffington Post''. June 6, 2013].</ref> She has appeared in television series such as ''[[Speechless (TV series)|Speechless]]'', ''[[Outlaw (TV series)|Outlaw]]'', ''[[The Big Bang Theory]]'', ''[[Days of Our Lives]]'', ''[[Monday Mornings]]'', ''[[Girl Meets World]]'', ''[[Love That Girl!]]'', and ''[[Jane the Virgin]]''. She played [[Mary Shelley]] in ''Edgar Allan Poe's Murder Mystery Dinner Party'' and was a series regular in ''Hyperlinked'', a [[YouTube Premium]] series distributed by [[Disney]].
