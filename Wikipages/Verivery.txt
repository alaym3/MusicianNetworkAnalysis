'''Verivery''' ([[Hangul]]: 베리베리; stylized as '''VERIVERY''' and also abbreviated '''VRVR''') is a South Korean boy band formed by [[Jellyfish Entertainment]] in 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://people.search.naver.com/search.naver?where=nexearch&sm=tab_ppn&query=%EA%B7%B8%EB%A3%B9verivery&os=8782700&ie=utf8&key=PeopleService|script-title=ko:베리베리 프로필|publisher=Naver|language=ko}}</ref> They debuted on January 9, 2019 with ''Veri-Us''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ilyoseoul.co.kr/news/articleView.html?idxno=278286|title=베리베리, 데뷔 앨범 'VERI-US(베리어스)' 트랙리스트 공개|publisher=Ilyo Seoul|language=ko}}</ref>

They self-produced their DIY Albums that was planned and designed themselves and DIY Music Videos that was directed and edited by Minchan along with Verivery members.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/312/0000448251|title=[신인사용설명서] 베리베리, 못 하는 게 없는 DIY돌이 왔다|publisher=Naver News|language=ko}}</ref>

== History ==
===2018: Pre-debut===
Jellyfish confirmed on August 23, 2018 that they would be debuting a seven member boy group<ref>{{cite web|url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=109&aid=0003849090|title=[단독] '빅스 남동생 그룹', 하반기 출격한다..젤리피쉬 6년만에 男아이돌|website=entertain.naver.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=076&aid=0003304766|title=[공식]젤리피쉬, 7인조 신인 보이그룹 나온다…빅스·구구단 잇는 '기대주'|website=entertain.naver.com}}</ref> and later confirmed their name as Verivery, Jellyfish Entertainment's first boy group in six years since [[VIXX]]. Their name comes from the Latin word 'veri' meaning 'truth' and the English word 'very', Verivery is a combination of the words ‘various', 'energetic', 'real' and 'innovation'.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://mtvdaily.asiae.co.kr/article.php?aid=15470192891427844010#09Rm|title='데뷔' 베리베리 "남자 그룹치고 팀명 너무 예쁘단 반응 많아"|website=mtvdaily.asiae.co.kr}}</ref>

Prior to their debut, on September 3, 2018 [[Mnet (TV channel)|Mnet]] revealed that the upcoming group would star in the upcoming reality show named ''Now Verivery: Real Road Movie''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://gaonchart.co.kr/main/section/article/view.gaon?idx=14445&sgenre=kpop|title=젤리피쉬 신인 베리베리, 21일 음원 출시와 리얼리티 첫 방송 |language=ko|date=2018-09-21|website=gaonchart.co.kr|access-date=2019-01-10}}</ref> The OST for their reality show titled ''Super Special'' was released on September 21, 2018 along with the airing of the reality show.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Yoo |first1=Byeong-cheol |title=젤리피쉬 신인 베리베리, 데뷔전 OST 'Super Special' 발표 |url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=215&aid=0000683628 |website=[[Naver]] |access-date=1 May 2020 |language=ko}}</ref>

On November 9, the group released the DIY MV of ''Now Verivery'' OST "Super Special".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=j3bWnI8ag_Y&list=PLDlie7qJlOncBe2Dves1xcBE74t6wE3eQ&index=13|title=[지금부터 베리베리해 OST] VERIVERY - Super Special M/V (Produced by VERIVERY)|publisher=Youtube|language=ko}}</ref>

===2019: Debut with ''Veri'' series===
The group officially debuted on January 9 with their first extended play ''Veri-Us'' and its lead single "Ring Ring Ring".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=021&aid=0002378065|title=남다른 신인 베리베리, 9일 '데뷔쇼' 전세계 동시 중계|website=entertain.naver.com}}</ref> On February 24, the groups released the DIY music video of ''Veri-Us'' album for the title song "Ring Ring Ring".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=215&aid=0000738998|title=베리베리, 직접 제작 한 DIY M/V 상영회 개최|publisher=Naver|language=ko}}</ref>  

On April 24, Verivery released their second extended play ''Veri-Able'' and its lead single "From Now".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=382&aid=0000722224|title=베리베리 24일 전격 컴백, 2번째 앨범명 'VERI-ABLE' 확정 [공식입장]|language=ko|website=Naver|date=April 6, 2019}}</ref> On June 1, the group released their DIY music video of ''Veri-Able'' album for the title song "From Now".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=015&aid=0004146811|title=‘베리베리’ 타이틀곡 ‘딱 잘라서 말해’ DIY MV 상영회 개최|publisher=Naver|language=ko}}</ref>   

On July 31, Verivery released their first single album ''Veri-Chill'' and its lead single "Tag Tag Tag".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/108/0002797926|title=베리베리, 31일 컴백 확정..타이틀곡 'Tag Tag Tag'|language=ko|work=naver|date=July 18, 2019}}</ref> On September 11, the groups released the DIY music video of ''Veri-Chill'' album for the combined song "Tag Tag Tag & 반할 수밖에 (Mystery Light)".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/112/0003210922|title=베리베리, 'Tag Tag Tag'+'반할 수밖에' DIY 뮤직비디오 풀 버전 공개|publisher=Naver News|language=ko}}</ref>   

===2020: ''Face It'' series, ''Road to Kingdom''===
On January 7, Verivery released their third extended play ''Face Me'' and its lead single "Lay Back".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/109/0004135688|title=베리베리, 1월 7일 미니 3집 'FACE ME' 컴백 "한층 짙어진 성숙美"|language=ko|work=naver|date=December 15, 2019}}</ref> The EP marks as the first series of the ''FACE it'' trilogy. On February 17, the group released the DIY music video for the b-side songs of ''Face Me'' album titled "Paradise & Photo".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/215/0000848522|title=베리베리, ‘Paradise’+‘PHOTO’ DIY 뮤직비디오 풀버전 공개|publisher=Naver News|language=ko}}</ref>   

On March 20, the group was confirmed to join Mnet's reality television competition ''[[Road to Kingdom]]''.<ref name="RTK">{{Cite web|url=http://osen.mt.co.kr/article/G1111326771|title=펜타곤·온앤오프·골든차일드·더보이즈·베리베리·원어스·TOO, '로드 투 킹덤' 출연 확정 [공식]|trans-title=Pentagon, ONF, Golden Child, The Boys, Verivery, Oneus, TOO, confirmed 'Road to Kingdom' appearance [Official]|work=OSEN|date=March 20, 2020}}</ref> They reached the final round with released a new song "Beautiful-X" for the show’s finale and finished in fifth place overall.

On July 1, Verivery released their fourth extended play ''Face You'' and its lead single "Thunder".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=108&aid=0002872359|title=베리베리, '로드투 킹덤'→ 7월 1일 '페이스 유' 컴백[공식]|language=ko|work=naver|date=June 18, 2020}}</ref> On August 8, the group released the DIY music video for the combined title track and b-side song "Connect & Thunder" of ''Face You'' album. <ref>{{cite web|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/109/0004254110|title=베리베리, 'Thunder'·'Connect' DIY 뮤비 풀버전 공개..영상미 업그레이드|publisher=Naver News|language=ko}}</ref>   

On October 13, Verivery released their fifth extended play ''Face Us'' and its lead single "G.B.T.B. (Go Beyond The Barrier)".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/108/0002897253|title=베리베리, 10월 13일 미니앨범 '페이스 어스' 컴백'[공식]|language=ko|work=naver|date=September 28, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=VERIVERY discuss their new album and the future of K-pop – Exclusive interview |url=https://darwinbeats.com/2020/10/26/verivery-discuss-their-new-album-and-the-future-of-k-pop-exclusive-interview/ |access-date=24 December 2020 |work=darwinbeats}}</ref> They also released a DIY music video for the song "So Gravity" from ''Face Us'' album on December 28.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/311/0001245586|title=베리베리, ‘소중력’ DIY 뮤비 공개…역대급 완성도|publisher=Naver News|language=ko}}</ref>   

On October 26, it was announced that Minchan would be going on a hiatus due to poor health and anxiety issues.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/now/article/311/0001218286|title=베리베리 민찬, 건강 악화로 일시적 활동 중단 "휴식 및 치료 집중" [전문]|language=ko|work=naver|date=October 26, 2020}}</ref>

The group released their first holiday track "Love at First Sight" on December 23, 2020. The track is only available on YouTube and VERIVERY's official [https://soundcloud.com/verivery SoundCloud] as of September 2021.

=== 2021–present: ''Series 'O' Round 1: Hall'' and ''Series 'O' Round 2: Hole'' ===
On March 2, the group released their second single album ''Series 'O' Round 1: Hall'' and its lead single "Get Away".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/018/0004854531|title=베리베리, 3월 2일 컴백 확정|language=ko|work=naver|date=February 17, 2021}}</ref>

On June 1, it was announced that Minchan would be returning to group activities.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=108&aid=0002959829|title=베리베리 민찬, 8개월만 활동 복귀…완전체 활동 기대 [공식]|language=ko|work=naver|date=June 1, 2021}}</ref>

On August 23, the group released their sixth extended play, ''Series 'O' Round 2: Hole'' and its lead single "Trigger".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/468/0000781485|title=베리베리, 8월 23일 완전체 컴백[공식]|language=ko|work=naver|date=July 31, 2021}}</ref>

Verivery announced their first US tour, taking place from December 5-20, 2021.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=477&aid=0000322259|title=베리베리, 12월 美 콘서트 투어 시작…'LA→뉴욕' 9개 도시 개최[공식]|language=ko|work=naver|date=October 6, 2021}}</ref>
