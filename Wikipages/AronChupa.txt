'''Aron Michael Ekberg''' (born 30 March 1991<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|date=22 December 2015|title=The Official Athletics Website of The Academy of Art University - Aron Ekberg - 2013 Men's Soccer|url=http://www.artuathletics.com/roster.aspx?rp_id=2138&path=msoc|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151222161314/http://www.artuathletics.com/roster.aspx?rp_id=2138&path=msoc|archive-date=22 December 2015|access-date=23 August 2020}}</ref>), better known by his stage name '''AronChupa''', is a [[Sweden|Swedish]] singer, record producer, songwriter, artist and DJ. His 2014 song "[[I'm an Albatraoz]]" reached number 1 on the [[Sverigetopplistan|Swedish Singles Chart]] and in [[Denmark]], and top 10 in many charts across [[Europe]]. It also reached number 25 in the [[United Kingdom|UK]] and number 10 in the [[United States|US]] dance charts. His younger sister, Nora Ekberg, better known professionally as Little Sis Nora, provided the vocals for the track and starred in the music video, but never appeared as featured artist on the track. The music video has surpassed 1.2 billion views on YouTube.

==Career==
Ekberg attended San Francisco's [[Academy of Art University]] around 2011-2013 and played on the school's soccer team<ref name=":0" /> before moving back to Sweden.[[File:Albatraoz in 2014.png|150px|thumb|left|upright|AronChupa (below, right) among the members of [[Albatraoz]].]]
Aron started his professional music career in Swedish [[electro house]]-[[Hip hop music|hip hop]] band, [[Albatraoz]], founded in 2012 by himself along with four of his best friends whom he had met while growing up. On 2 August 2013, their debut single was released, the self-titled song "''Albatraoz''"<ref>{{cite web|date=19 April 2011|title=Albatraoz – Single by Albatraoz|url=https://itunes.apple.com/se/album/albatraoz-single/id676084092|work=iTunes}}</ref> and shortly after they signed with [[Sony Music Entertainment|Sony Music]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mynewsdesk.com/se/sonybmg/news/boraasbandet-albatraoz-skapade-viral-succe-som-ledde-till-samarbete-med-sony-music-67312|title=Boråsbandet "Albatraoz" skapade viral succé som ledde till samarbete med Sony Music|work=My News Desk|date=19 April 2011}}</ref> The song reached 36 on the [[Sverigetopplistan|Swedish Singles Chart]] and remained on the chart for 18 weeks.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://swedishcharts.com/showitem.asp?interpret=Albatraoz&titel=Albatraoz&cat=s |title=ALBATRAOZ – ALBATRAOZ (SONG) |work=Swedish Charts |date=19 April 2011 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141202184304/http://swedishcharts.com/showitem.asp?interpret=Albatraoz&titel=Albatraoz&cat=s |archive-date=2 December 2014  }}</ref> "''Albatraoz''" was certified double platinum in Sweden.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hitlistan.se/netdata/ghl002.mbr/lista?liid=41&dfom=0000000 |title=Veckolista Singlar - Vecka 47, 20 november 2014 |work=Hitlistan |date=19 April 2011 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141129052112/http://www.hitlistan.se/netdata/ghl002.mbr/lista?liid=41&dfom=0000000 |archive-date=29 November 2014 }}</ref> In January 2014 the song had reached 8.5&nbsp;million plays on Spotify and the group toured to promote the song.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/article18194230.ab|title="Albatraoz" gjorde hitlåt på bakfyllan|work=Afton Bladet|date=19 April 2011}}</ref> The band followed up with a few singles, including "''Arriba''" and "''Wunderbar''", while touring [[Scandinavia]] from 2013 to 2017 before dissolving in 2017.

On 20 March 2014 Ekberg released his debut solo single, ''I'm an Albatraoz'', with his younger sister, Nora Ekberg, better known as Little Sis Nora, on vocals. After some success independently Ekberg also signed his solo project to [[Sony Music]]. The song reached number 1 in Sweden and Denmark and top 10 in Germany, Austria, Norway, Finland, Australia and the Netherlands. It was certified double platinum in [[Sweden]] and [[Australia]] and platinum in [[New Zealand]], [[Italy]] and [[Denmark]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/CertificacionesAmprofon/photos/ms.c.eJwztjA3NDU1M7EwMDc1N7TUM4bwTSF8CxjfzNDEwNLA1BAA6FsJog~-~-.bps.a.352187651572519.1073741842.167176543406965/387155648075719/?type=1|title=AronChupa – I'm An Albatraoz – Gold certification|publisher=Amprofon|access-date=27 July 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.sverigetopplistan.se/netdata/ghl002.mbr/artdata?dfom=20140101&sart=4413374| title=AronChupa – I'm an Albatraoz| publisher=[[Swedish Recording Industry Association|Grammofon Leverantörernas Förening]] | access-date=21 October 2014 | language=sv}}</ref> The music video also has over 1.1 billion views on YouTube.

His second single dropped on 20 November 2015, titled "''Fired Cuz I Was Late''", and peaked at number 100 in Sweden.<ref name="SWE" />

On 26 February 2016 Ekberg released his third solo single, "''Little Swing''", which features Little Sis Nora.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Smith|first=Garrett|date=3 March 2016|title=AronChupa – Little Swing ft. Little Sis Nora|url=https://www.youredm.com/2016/03/03/aronchupa-little-swing-ft-little-sis-nora/|url-status=live|access-date=5 May 2021|website=youredm}}</ref> The song gained slight recognition in the UK when [[Vodafone UK]] started using it in their roaming advert campaign.

AronChupa is known for often having his sister, Little Sis Nora, sing on his songs and to star in his music videos.

On 17 July 2020 he released the song "''The Woodchuck Song''". It peaked at number 12 on [[Spotify]]'s "Sweden Viral 50".
