'''Eddy de Pretto''' (born 2 May 1993 in [[Créteil]] in the [[Val-de-Marne]])<ref name="liberfetecommence">{{cite news|last1=Renault|first1=Gilles|title=Eddy de Pretto. Que la fête commence|url=http://next.liberation.fr/musique/2018/02/07/eddy-de-pretto-que-la-fete-commence_1628143|accessdate=4 March 2018|work=[[Libération]]|date=7 February 2018}}</ref> is a French singer-songwriter and actor.

== Early life ==
Eddy de Pretto was born on 2 May 1993 and grew up in [[Créteil]]<ref name="liberfetecommence"/><ref name="inrocks"/> in the [[Val-de-Marne]].<ref name="Grazia">{{cite web|first=Pascaline|last=Potdevin|title=Eddy de Pretto, sois bon|url=https://www.grazia.fr/culture/musique/eddy-de-pretto-sois-bon-878092|website=[[Grazia]]|date=30 December 2017|accessdate=9 April 2018}}</ref> His father is a truck driver and football fan; his mother a laboratory technician and an art lover.<ref name="inrocks-2017-11">{{cite web|first=Maud|last=Darbois|title=Eddy de Pretto : " C'est le travail d'une vie pour un homme d'accepter la féminité et la masculinité en lui "|url=https://www.lesinrocks.com/2017/11/26/style/eddy-de-pretto-cest-le-travail-dune-vie-pour-un-homme-daccepter-la-feminite-et-la-masculinite-en-lui-111012629/|website=[[Les Inrockuptibles]]|date=26 November 2017|accessdate=11 April 2018}}</ref> He sings about his native city in ''Beaulieue''.<ref name="Parisien">{{cite web|url=http://www.leparisien.fr/val-de-marne-94/creteil-berceau-du-rappeur-eddy-de-pretto-27-10-2017-7358604.php|title=Créteil, berceau du rappeur Eddy de Pretto|first=Clawdia|last=Prolongeau|website=[[Le Parisien]]|date=27 October 2017|accessdate=11 April 2018}}</ref> In an interview, he described his hometown:{{quote|
...a certain amount of anger, a desire to better myself. There's a bit of a sort of straightjacket in the ''[[banlieue]]s'', like it or not: we're told we're on the margin, that we'll amount to nothing, that we'll come to a bad end. Me, my head was full of dreams and a little voice told me I'd get there. The desire to overcome [it all] helped me succeed...}}<ref name="laparisienne13mars2018">{{cite web|url=http://www.leparisien.fr/laparisienne/loisirs-detente/culture/eddy-de-pretto-je-ne-rentrais-pas-dans-le-moule-physiquement-13-03-2018-7606014.php|title=Eddy de Pretto: " Je ne rentrais pas dans le moule physiquement "|first=Anne-Charlotte|last=Dancourt|date=13 March 2018|website=[[Le Parisien|La Parisienne]]|accessdate=9 April 2018}}{{quote|''...une certaine rage, une envie de me dépasser. Il y a un peu ce truc de carcan en banlieue, qu'on le veuille ou non : on nous dit qu'on est en marge, qu'on va arriver à rien, qu'on va mal finir. Moi j'avais des rêves plein la tête et une petite voix me disait que j'allais y arriver. L'envie de m'en sortir m'a aidé à y parvenir...''}}{{quote|''Je prenais la télécommande pour faire un micro et je tournais l’halogène pour faire la lumière. Du coup ma mère m’a proposé de prendre des cours de théâtre vu que je n’excellais pas au sport.''}}</ref>

At the age of twelve, he started lessons in theatre, singing, vocal technique and piano:<ref name="Obs1">{{cite web|url=http://o.nouvelobs.com/lifestyle/20170918.OBS4784/eddy-de-pretto-intriguant-rappeur-de-la-generation-dolan.html|title=Eddy de Pretto, intriguant rappeur de la génération Dolan|first=Juliette|last=Thévenot|date=25 September 2017|website=[[L'Obs]]|accessdate=11 April 2018}}.</ref> "I took the TV remote for a microphone and operated a halogen lamp for lighting. So my mother suggested taking theatre lessons since I didn't excel at sport."<ref name="laparisienne13mars2018"/> He continued his education at the ''Institut supérieur des arts de la scène'' (ECM-ISAS, "Advanced Institute of Dramatic Arts") in [[Paris]].<ref name="inrocks"/>

== Acting ==
Eddy de Pretto's first acting experiences were in TV advertisements, such as the role of a young [[Julius Caesar]] for [[Canal (TV provider)|CanalSat]] in September 2010,<ref name="inrocks-2016-04">{{cite web|url=https://www.lesinrocks.com/lesinrockslab/news/2016/04/decouverte-lab-151-eddy-de-pretto/|title=Découverte du lab #151 : Eddy de Pretto|first=Abigaïl|last=Aïnouz|date=25 April 2016|website=[[Les Inrockuptibles]]|accessdate=20 January 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zelCX9f1BLs|title=Publicitié Canalsat - Canalsat à la demande - Comédie/Film historique|date=6 September 2010|website=[[YouTube]]|accessdate=20 January 2018}}.</ref> before entering into the theatrical and film worlds: the [[short film]]s ''Königsberg'' by Philipp Mayrhofer in 2012 and ''Vivre sa vie'' by Paul D. Meyer in 2013 as well as an appearance in the [[feature film]] ''[[Paulette (film)|Paulette]]'' by [[Jérôme Enrico]] in the role of "the young hippy buyer", also in 2013.

== Music career ==
Between the ages of eighteen and nineteen he began to write songs. In his first public performance he sang [[R. Kelly]]'s "[[I Believe I Can Fly]]" at his local youth centre (''Maison des jeunes et de la culture''): "I was totally weak, ... so stressed out because there was a lot of rap and I didn’t fit into that genre at all," he said in an interview.<ref name="su6déc17">{{cite web|url=http://www.shoes-up.com/eddy-de-pretto-beau-parleur/|title=Interview : Eddy de Pretto, Beau Parleur|first=Mélodie|last=Raymond|date=6 December 2017|website=Shoes Up|accessdate=11 April 2018}}{{quote|''J’étais tout frêle, […] tellement stressé parce qu’il y avait beaucoup de rap et je n’étais pas du tout dans ces codes là.''}}</ref>

He participated in the [[Printemps de Bourges]] music festival, where he won the ''Inouïs'' ("unheard of, amazing") prize,<ref name="inouis">{{cite web|url=http://www.reseau-printemps.com/les-prix|title=Les prix – Les Inouïs|website=Les Inouïs du Printemps de Bourges|accessdate=11 April 2018}}</ref> and the ''Bars en Trans'' festival, and is a graduate of ''inRocKs Lab 2016''.<ref name="inrocks" /> In October 2017, he also took part in the ''Festival Off Off Off'' (''Nuits de Champagne'' festival) in [[Troyes]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://nuitsdechampagne.com/la-programmation/|title=Nuits de Champagne : la programmation|date=2017|website=Nuits de Champagne|accessdate=11 April 2018}}</ref>

=== ''Kid'' and ''Cure'' ===
He released his first [[Extended play|EP]], entitled ''Kid'', on 6 October 2017. In the live performances of the EP he played sound clips from his [[iPhone]] connected with a cable. The only live musician at his concerts was a [[drummer]].<ref name="inrocks" />

In early January 2018, on the stage of the [[television program]] ''[[Taratata]]'', he sang "[[Comme un boomerang (song)|Comme un boomerang]]" in duet with [[Julien Doré]], a song written by Serge Gainsbourg and previously performed by [[Dani (singer)|Dani]] and [[Étienne Daho]] in 2001.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.rfm.fr/news/Julien-Dore-chante-Comme-un-boomerang-avec-Eddy-de-Pretto-dans-Taratata-12380|title=Julien Doré chante ''Comme un boomerang'' avec Eddy de Pretto dans ''Taratata'' !|date=4 January 2018|website=[[RFM (French radio station)|RFM]]|accessdate=11 April 2018}}</ref>

In mid-January 2018, he launched "Random", the first song from his debut album {{interlanguage link|Cure (Eddy de Preto album)|lt=''Cure''|fr|Cure (album)}}, describing his heartaches.<ref name="Random">{{cite web|title=Eddy de Pretto raconte ses peines de cœur dans ''Random'', son nouveau titre|url=http://people.bfmtv.com/musique/eddy-de-pretto-raconte-ses-peines-de-coeur-dans-random-son-nouveau-titre-1353433.html|date=|website=[[BFM TV]]|accessdate=11 April 2018}}</ref>

Along with [[Gaël Faye]] and [[Fishbach]], he was nominated for "Best Newcomer Stage Act" (''Groupe ou artiste révélation scène'') at the 33rd ''[[Victoires de la Musique]]'' in 2018.<ref name="Victoires1">{{cite web|url=https://www.franceinter.fr/culture/decouvrez-les-nominations-des-victoires-de-la-musique-2018|title=Les nominations des Victoires de la musique 2018|date=9 January 2018|website=[[France Inter]]|accessdate=11 April 2018}}</ref><ref name="Victoires2">{{cite news|last1=Morin|first1=Fabien|title=Louane, Orelsan, Depardieu... Découvrez toutes les nominations des Victoires de la Musique 2018|url=http://people.bfmtv.com/musique/louane-orelsan-depardieu-decouvrez-toutes-les-nominations-des-victoires-de-la-musique-2018-1345717.html|accessdate=4 March 2018|work=BFM TV|date=9 January 2018}}</ref> Gaël Faye won the award.

In mid-February 2018, he introduced his song "Ego" with "a stripped down, mechanical instrumental", which the singer explained thus: "I set down the possibility of becoming unhinged because of the speed at which the project is going. By dint of hearing praise, the 'excess of self' implodes, and that's the risk I’m exposing here. The simple fear of degenerating into a [[fanaticism]] of the self through [[social media]], endearments, and all other flattering signals that create this monster of ego."<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.konbini.com/fr/entertainment-2/en-ecoute-eddy-de-pretto-questionne-mecanismes-ego/|title=En écoute : Eddy de Pretto questionne les mécanismes du succès dans ''Ego''|date=16 February 2018|website=Konbini|accessdate=11 April 2018}}{{quote|Je pose l'éventualité de devenir dingue à cause de la vitesse à laquelle va le projet. À force d'entendre des éloges, le « trop de soi » implose, et c'est le risque que j'expose ici. La peur simple de tomber dans le fanatisme de soi-même par les réseaux sociaux, les mots d'amour, et tout autre signe qui flatte et qui crée ce monstre d'ego.}}</ref>

On 2 March 2018, he released his first album {{interlanguage link|Cure (Eddy de Preto album)|lt=''Cure''|fr|Cure (album)}}, on CD, vinyl and in digital format.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://rocknfool.net/2018/03/01/eddy-de-pretto-cure-album/|title=Notre Eddy, notre idole|date=1 March 2018|website=Rocknfool|accessdate=11 April 2018}}</ref> At the same time, the clip for "Normal" was unveiled, which took on [[stereotype]]s of [[sexual identity]], fear of [[Other (philosophy)|the other]] and [[homophobia]]:<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chartsinfrance.net/Eddy-de-Pretto/news-106261.html|title=Clip " Normal " : Eddy de Pretto monte sur le ring pour tordre le cou à l'homophobie|date=2 March 2018|website=Charts in France|accessdate=11 April 2018}}</ref> "An anger about people who were attacking me because they didn't like the person I embodied," he explained in the magazine ''[[Têtu]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tetu.com/2017/10/09/eddy-de-pretto-le-kid-gay-du-rap-francais/|title=Eddy de Pretto, le " Kid " gay du rap français (interview)|first=Adrien|last=Naselli|date=9 October 2017|website=[[Têtu]]|accessdate=11 April 2018}}</ref> A week after its release, the recording was ranked the number one best-selling album ahead of ''Vous et moi'' by [[Julien Doré]], with 13,500 copies,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chartsinfrance.net/Eddy-de-Pretto/news-106318.html|title="Cure" : Eddy de Pretto entre numéro un des ventes avec son premier album|first=Yohann|last=Ruelle|date=9 March 2018|website=Charts in France|accessdate=11 April 2018}}</ref> "driving out" [[Grand Corps Malade]] from [[Fnac]]'s Top French Albums listing.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ladepeche.fr/article/2018/03/12/2758155-top-albums-france-fnac-eddy-pretto-deloge-grand-corps-malade.html|title=Top albums France Fnac : Eddy de Pretto déloge Grand Corps Malade|date=12 March 2018|website=[[La Dépêche du Midi]]|accessdate=11 April 2018}}</ref>

=== ''À TOUS LES BÂTARDS'' ===

On 25 March 2021, he released his new album entitled [[À TOUS LES BÂTARDS]], featuring his two previously released singles, [[Bateaux-Mouches]] and [[Désolé Caroline]], alongside a COLORS SHOW performance of the song ‘[[Parfaitement]]’ which was released on [[YouTube]] ahead of the album’s release. The 16-track album featured an artwork given to de Pretto by a fan, the design of which shows the sketch of the artist’s head superimposed onto a yellow background, a trend continued from his previous album’s cover where he superimposed bodybuilder [[Arnold Schwarzenegger]]’s arm onto an image of himself.

==Personal life==
As alluded to in some of his songs, Eddy de Pretto does not hide his homosexuality: "I'm not [[LGBT social movements|militant]]. I don't want to be a [[standard-bearer]]. I just want to give an account of my life, my reality," he added in an interview.<ref name="su6déc17"/> He defines himself as a "non-genre" artist.<ref name="HuffPost">{{cite web|title=Qui est Eddy de Pretto, le chanteur qui fait du " non-genre " un style à part entière ?|url=https://www.huffingtonpost.fr/2017/10/21/qui-est-eddy-de-pretto-le-chanteur-qui-fait-du-non-genre-un-style-a-part-entiere_a_23247141/|website=[[Huffington Post]]|date=22 October 2017|accessdate=13 April 2018}}</ref> In the TV program ''[[Taratata]]'', broadcast on 15 December 2017, he explained that he was trying to examine masculinity and the [[hypermasculinity]] that made up part of his education. When [[Nagui]] asked him if he talked about homosexuality in his songs, he responded that that was in no way his goal.<ref>{{cite web|title=''Taratata'' #521|url=http://mytaratata.com/taratata/521/taratata-ndeg521-avec-julien-clerc-noel-gallagher-eddy-de-pretto-jacob-banks|website=mytaratata.com|date=15 December 2017|accessdate=13 April 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171222051041/http://mytaratata.com/taratata/521/taratata-ndeg521-avec-julien-clerc-noel-gallagher-eddy-de-pretto-jacob-banks|archive-date=22 December 2017|url-status=dead}}</ref> In this regard, speaking to ''[[Les Inrockuptibles]]'' the singer declared: "I try to speak about my personal history and to normalise it as much as possible. And not to highlight it saying I'm the first [[queer]] to link rap and [[Chanson#Nouvelle chanson|French chanson]]".<ref name="inrocks"/>
