'''Kulwinder Billa''' is an Indian singer and actor associated with [[Punjabi language|Punjabi]] language [[Music of Punjab|music]] and [[Punjabi cinema|films]]. He started his singing career with albums "Koi Khaas" and "Punjab" whereas his acting debut was in 2018 with ''[[Subedar Joginder Singh (film)|Subedar Joginder Singh]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/topic/Kulwinder-Billa|title=Kulwinder Billa: Movies, Photos, Videos, News & Biography {{!}} eTimes|website=The Times of India|access-date=29 September 2018}}</ref>
