'''Oluwatobiloba Daniel Anidugbe''', better known by his stage name '''Kizz Daniel''', is a [[Nigerians|Nigerian]] singer and songwriter. He is best known for his singles "[[Woju (song)|Woju]]" and "Yeba".<ref>{{cite web|last1=Bella|first1=Naija|url=https://www.bellanaija.com/2018/05/kiss-daniel-name-kizz-daniel/|title=Kiss Daniel changes name to Kizz Daniel|website=Bella Naija|date=22 May 2018|access-date=29 May 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite magazine |url=http://thenationonlineng.net/new/drop-album-yet-kiss-daniel/ | title=Why I can't drop an album yet – Kiss Daniel |magazine=The Nation | date=3 February 2015| access-date=19 March 2015 | author=Famuyiwa Damilare}}</ref> He went by the stage name '''Kiss Daniel''' prior to changing it in May 2018.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Ohunyon|first1=Ehis|title=Singer changes his name to Kizz Daniel|url=http://www.pulse.ng/entertainment/music/kiss-daniel-changes-his-name-to-kizz-daniel-id8404761.html|website=Pulse.NG|date=22 May 2018|access-date=29 May 2018}}</ref> He signed a record deal with G-Worldwide Entertainment in 2013, but left the label following a publicized contract dispute and court case.<ref>{{cite web |author1=Ehis Ohunyon |title=Singer changes his name to Kizz Daniel |url=https://www.pulse.ng/entertainment/music/kiss-daniel-singer-changes-his-name-to-kizz-daniel/1exr02v |publisher=Pulse Nigeria |date=May 22, 2018}}</ref> He founded the Fly Boy Inc record label in November 2017.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://thenet.ng/2017/11/confirmed-kiss-daniel-exits-g-worldwide-unveils-new-record-label/|title=CONFIRMED: Kiss Daniel Exits G-Worldwide and Unveils New Record Label|date=2017-11-14|work=Nigerian Entertainment Today|access-date=2017-11-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171117122041/http://thenet.ng/2017/11/confirmed-kiss-daniel-exits-g-worldwide-unveils-new-record-label/|archive-date=2017-11-17|url-status=dead}}</ref>

==Early life==
Kizz Daniel was born Oluwatobiloba Daniel Anidugbe in [[Abeokuta]], [[Ogun State|Ogun state]], Nigeria.{{Citation needed|date=November 2021}} He hails from Abeokuta North local government area.

== Education ==
He attended [[Abeokuta Grammar School]] and graduated from [[Federal University of Agriculture, Abeokuta]], in 2013, with a degree in Water Resources Management and [[Agrometeorology]] (Water Engineering).<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.pulse.ng/entertainment/celebrities/kiss-daniel-5-interesting-facts-about-the-singer/845wevk|title=5 interesting facts about the singer|date=2017-12-06|website=www.pulse.ng|access-date=2019-05-07}}</ref> While in university, he decided to pursue music as a career alongside his studies.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.pulse.ng/entertainment/music/kiss-daniel-10-things-you-dont-know-about-woju-singer-id3507573.html|title=Kiss Daniel: 10 things you don"t know about "Woju" singer|last=Akan|first=Joey|access-date=2018-07-03}}</ref>

== Personal life ==
On May 1, 2021, he welcomed a set of triplets, Jamal, Jalil and Jelani, with his mysterious girlfriend.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Omotayo|date=2021-05-01|title=Nigerian singer, Kizz Daniel welcomes a set of twins on his birthday|url=https://www.freshpopmusic.co/forum/entertainment/celebrities/149296-nigerian-singer-kizz-daniel-welcomes-a-set-of-twins-on-his-birthday|access-date=2021-07-24|website=FreshPopMusic|language=en}}</ref> Kizz later revealed how he lost one of his triplets, Jamal, just four days after birth as he acquired a two-bedroom penthouse each for Jalil and Jelani.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Omotayo|date=2021-07-15|title=Kizz Daniel reveals how he lost one of his triplets, buys penthouses for Jalil & Jelani|url=https://www.freshpopmusic.co/forum/entertainment/celebrities/149337-kizz-daniel-reveals-how-he-lost-one-of-his-triplets-buys-penthouses-for-jalil-jelani|access-date=2021-07-24|website=FreshPopMusic|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|last=Emmanuel|date=2021-07-16|title=One Of The Triplets Of Kizz Daniel Is Dead, He Confirms|url=https://candytv.ng/one-of-the-triplets-of-kizz-daniel-is-died-he-confirms/|access-date=2021-07-27|website=Candy TV|language=en}}</ref>

==Career==
In May 2015, Kizz Daniel released a third follow up single, “Laye,” on his birthday, along with a video two weeks later which was shot in different locations in the eastern part of Africa and was directed by AJE FILMS.{{Citation needed|date=November 2021}}

Daniel released his first studio album titled [[New Era (Kiss Daniel album)|New Era]] on May 14, 2016.
<ref>http://www.pulse.ng/buzz/kiss-daniel-a-dope-album-will-always-have-enough-to-last-an-artiste-for-a-year-id6232774.html {{Bare URL inline|date=November 2021}}</ref>
After his exit from his former label G-WorldWide, he went to float his own personal imprint FLYBOY I.N.C. which recently signed two new acts Demmie Vee and Philkeyz. After which Kizz Daniel was featured on Demmie Vee single entitled "You Go Wait?".<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.mp3bullet.ng/demmie-vee-ft-kizz-daniel-you-go-wait-mp3|title=Demmie Vee ft. Kizz Daniel – You Go Wait?|access-date=2018-10-05}}</ref> 2018 was sure a good year for the WOJU crooner, he features [[Wizkid (musician)|Wizkid]] on a Hit Song "FOR YOU"<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.mp3bullet.ng/kizz-daniel-x-wizkid-for-you-prod-philkeyz-music|title=Kizz Daniel ft Wizkid - For You|access-date=2018-06-14}}</ref> and the DMW boss, [[Davido]] on another single "One Ticket" which is Currently Trending within 24hrs of its release. On the 30th of December 2018, Kizz Daniel released his 2nd Studio album and his first under FlyBoy Inc titled "No Bad Songz". The album contained 20 Songs including the pre-released One Ticket which he featured DMW Boss, [[Davido]]. Other features on the Album includes Nasty C, [[Diamond Platnumz|Diamond Platiumz]], Philkeys, Demmie Vee, [[DJ Xclusive|Dj Xclusive]], [[Wretch 32]], [[Diplo]] and [[Sarkodie (rapper)|Sarkodie]]. The album received positive reviews from music lovers, it debuted at No 55 on the US iTunes Chart and became number 1 on the world Album Chart within 24 hours of its release. On 25 June 2020, he released his third studio album 'King of Love'<ref>{{cite web |last1=Alake |first1=Motolani |url=https://www.pulse.ng/entertainment/music/kizz-daniel-releases-new-album-king-of-love/ks11b7t |website=Pulse Ng |access-date=July 4, 2020|title=Kizz Daniel releases new album, 'King of Love'|date=June 26, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Omotayo|date=2020-06-04|title=Kizz Daniel's King of Love album is dropping June 25 - See Tracklist|url=https://www.freshpopmusic.co/forum/entertainment/celebrities/131403-kizz-daniels-king-of-love-album-is-dropping-june-25-see-tracklist|access-date=2021-07-24|website=FreshPopMusic|language=en}}</ref>

In 2017, many of his fans thought he would switch into comedy in the nearest future as he was uploading comedy skits. However he said he has no intention switching into comedy as music is his main talent.<ref>{{Cite web|title=My comedy skits are strategic –Kiss Daniel|url=https://punchng.com/my-comedy-skits-are-strategic-kiss-daniel/|access-date=2021-02-26|website=Punch Newspapers|date=15 October 2017|language=en-US}}</ref>

===Record label dispute and name change===
In November 2017, Kizz Daniel announced his split from his record label, G-Worldwide and created his own label called FLYBOY I.N.C.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.pmnewsnigeria.com/2017/11/13/kiss-daniel-leaves-g-worldwide-label/|title=Kiss Daniel Leaves G-Worldwide Label - PM NEWS Nigeria|date=2017-11-13|work=PM NEWS Nigeria|access-date=2018-07-03}}</ref> He was sued to court by the former record label but was not found guilty for any of the crimes he was accused of.<ref>http://www.pulse.ng
/entertainment/music/kiss-daniel-g-worldwide-full-story-id7707718.html {{Bare URL inline|date=November 2021}}</ref>
<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.punchng.com/g-worldwide-drags-kiss-daniel-to-court/|title=G-Worldwide drags Kiss Daniel to court|work=Punch Newspapers|access-date=2018-07-03}}</ref>

In May 2018, Kizz Daniel announced that he had changed his stage name from Kiss Daniel to Kizz Daniel, a name which immediately reflected on his social media accounts. New Spotify and Apple Music accounts were also created immediately with the names Kizz Daniel, with his new songs featured.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://dailypost.ng/2018/05/22/nigerian-musician-kiss-daniel-changes-stage-name/|title=Nigerian musician, Kiss Daniel changes stage name - Daily Post Nigeria|date=2018-05-22|work=Daily Post Nigeria|access-date=2018-07-03}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bellanaija.com/2018/05/kiss-daniel-name-kizz-daniel/|title=Kiss Daniel changes name to Kizz Daniel - BellaNaija|website=www.bellanaija.com|date=22 May 2018|access-date=2018-07-03}}</ref>

In June 2018, Kizz Daniel's former record label G-Worldwide made a move to own the rights to the name “Kizz Daniel” and further advised the singer to desist from using the name or face legal consequences in a press release sent out to media houses.{{Citation needed|date=November 2021}} In October 2018  Daniel responded to the suit and statement made by his former record label G-Worldwide through his own lawyer.<ref name="Luvmp">{{cite web|url=https://luvmp.com/2019/10/kizz-daniel-fires-back-at-g-worldwide-record-label.html|title=Kizz Daniel Fires Back At G-Worldwide Record Label|work=Luvmp|last=Uche|first=Prinx|date=October 27, 2019}}</ref>
