'''''To the Grave''''' is the fifth [[studio album]] of the Danish [[Heavy metal music|heavy metal]] band [[Iron Fire]]. It was released through [[Napalm Records]] on 9 January 2009.<ref>{{cite web|title=Iron Fire at Metal Archives|url=http://www.metal-archives.com/albums/Iron_Fire/To_the_Grave/216064|website=metal-archives.com|accessdate=25 March 2015}}</ref>
