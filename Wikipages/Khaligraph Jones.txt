'''Brian Ouko Robert''', (born 12 June 1990)<ref>{{Cite web|date=2019-11-24|title=Khaligraph Jones Biography, Age, Wife, Songs, History, Career|url=https://ralingo.com/khaligraph-jones-biography-age-wife-songs-history-career/|access-date=2021-04-05|website=RALINGO|language=en-US}}</ref> popularly known by his stage name '''Khaligraph Jones''' and often referred to as "Papa Jones" is a Kenyan rapper known for his hit singles "Mazishi" and "Yego".<ref>{{Cite web|last=Oduku|first=Richard|date=2020-03-20|title=RICHARD ODUKU - Khaligraph Jones and Emerging Hip Hop Futures in Kenya {{!}} The Elephant|url=https://www.theelephant.info/culture/2020/03/20/khaligraph-jones-and-emerging-hip-hop-futures-in-kenya/|access-date=2021-04-05|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|title=Khaligraph Jones: How I made it in hip hop|language=en-GB|work=BBC News|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/av/world-africa-45686245|access-date=2021-04-05}}</ref>He released his debut full-length studio album ''Testimony 1990'' in June 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2018-06-19|title=REVIEW: Khaligraph Jones Debut Album 'Testimony 1990'|url=https://www.kenyanvibe.com/review-khaligraph-jones-debut-album-testimony-1990/|access-date=2021-04-05|website=KenyanVibe|language=en-US}}</ref>

== Early life ==
Jones was born and raised in Kayole, a neighborhood in [[Nairobi, Kenya]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Khaligraph Jones opens up about making ends meet and his days as a bouncer – The Sauce|url=https://www.capitalfm.co.ke/thesauce/khaligraph-jones-opens-up-about-making-ends-meet-and-his-days-as-a-bouncer/|access-date=2021-04-05|website=www.capitalfm.co.ke}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Khaligraph Jones {{!}} Biography & History|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/khaligraph-jones-mn0003965985/biography|access-date=2021-04-05|website=AllMusic|language=en}}</ref>

His mother gave him the name Ouko Robert in honor of the late Kenyan politician [[Robert Ouko (politician)|Dr. Robert Ouko]], who was assassinated on 13 February 1990.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Biography: Khaligraph Jones; Family, Wife, Music and Awards|url=https://www.pulselive.co.ke/entertainment/celebrities/biography-khaligraph-jones-family-wife-music-and-awards/t9c523d.amp|access-date=2021-04-05|website=www.pulselive.co.ke}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Enock|first=Sir|date=2021-02-08|title=Khaligraph Jones biography, age, family, hip hop songs, wife Georgina Muteti, awards, net worth|url=https://kisiifinest.co.ke/khaligraph-jones-biography|access-date=2021-04-05|website=KISII FINEST|language=en-US}}</ref>

== Career ==
Khaligraph started his musical career while still in elementary school. He officially began his career in 2008 at a function called "Words And Pictures."<ref>{{Cite web|last=https://www.the-star.co.ke/authors/kalondu|title=The rise and rise of Khaligraph, Kenya's music genius|url=https://www.the-star.co.ke/sasa/word-is/2019-12-23-the-rise-and-rise-of-khaligraph-jones-kenyas-music-genius/|access-date=2021-04-05|website=The Star|language=en-KE}}</ref>

He won [[Channel O Music Video Awards]] Emcee Africa in 2009, a highly visible competition that catapulted him to international acclaim almost instantly.<ref>{{Cite web|last=noraolagbaju|date=2021-02-11|title=Khaligraph – Passion over FAME|url=https://hiphopafrican.com/2021/02/10/kaligraph-jones-passion-over-fame/|access-date=2021-04-05|website=The Hip Hop African|language=en}}</ref>

In June 2018, he released his debut full-length studio album dubbed "Testimony 1990".<ref>{{Cite web|date=2018-06-12|title=Khaligraph unveils "Testimony 1990" album tracklist {{!}} Notjustok East Africa|url=https://notjustok.com/eastafrica/music/khaligraph-jones-unveils-testimony-1990-album-tracklist/|access-date=2021-04-05|website=Latest East African & Bongo Flava Music, Songs & Video - Notjustok|language=en-US}}</ref>

He was crowned "Best Rap Act of the Year" at the fifth Annual African Muzik Magazine Awards 2018 (Afrimma) held at House of Blues in Dallas, Texas in 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Big win for Kenya as Khaligraph Jones grabs AFRIMMA Best Rap Act - The Sauce|url=https://www.capitalfm.co.ke/thesauce/big-win-for-kenya-as-khaligraph-jones-grabs-afrimma-best-rap-act/|access-date=2021-04-05|website=www.capitalfm.co.ke}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Bwire|first=Dancan|title=2018 AFRIMMA Awards: Khaligraph, Papa Dennis win big|url=https://www.standardmedia.co.ke/entertainment/local-news/2001298309/2018-afrimma-awards-khaligraph-papa-dennis-win-big?amp=1|access-date=2021-04-05|website=2018 AFRIMMA Awards: Khaligraph, Papa Dennis win big}}</ref>

He was named the best Hip Hop act at the [[Soundcity MVP Awards Festival|Soundcity MVP Awards]] in January 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Khaligraph Jones wins SoundCity MVP award for best hip hop act|url=https://kiss100.co.ke/entertainment/2020-01-12-khaligraph-jones-wins-soundcity-mvp-award-for-best-hip-hop-act/|access-date=2021-04-05|website=Kiss 100|language=en-KE}}</ref> <ref>{{Cite web|date=2020|title=|url=https://www.standardmedia.co.ke/entertainment/local-news/2001360049/i-dropped-out-of-school-in-form-four-khaligraph-on-tough-childhood-depression|url-status=live}}</ref>The ceremony took place at the Eko Convention Center in Lagos, Nigeria.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Muli|first=Davis|title=MVP 2020 awards: Khaligraph crowned best hip-hop artiste in Africa|url=https://www.standardmedia.co.ke/entertainment/local-news/2001356369/mvp-2020-awards-khaligraph-jones-crowned-best-hip-hop-artiste-in-africa?amp=1|access-date=2021-04-05|website=MVP 2020 awards: Khaligraph Jones crowned best hip-hop artiste in Africa}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-01-12|title=Khaligraph Jones wins big at Sound City MVP Awards in Nigeria [Full list of winners]|url=https://www.pulselive.co.ke/entertainment/khaligraph-jones-wins-big-at-sound-city-mvp-awards-in-nigeria-full-list-of-winners/rxynngf|access-date=2021-04-05|website=Pulse Live Kenya|language=en-US}}</ref>

In 2020 he was nominated for the [[BET Awards]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=https://www.the-star.co.ke/authors/nyambs|title=Rapper Khaligraph nominated for 2020 BET Awards|url=https://www.the-star.co.ke/sasa/word-is/2020-09-30-rapper-khaligraph-nominated-for-2020-bet-awards/|access-date=2021-04-05|website=The Star|language=en-KE}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Kenyan rapper Khaligraph Jones Scoops hiS first BET AWARD NOMINATION – Transafrica Radio|url=http://www.transafricaradio.net/kenyan-rapper-khaligraph-jones-scoops-his-first-bet-award-nomination/|access-date=2021-04-05|language=en-US}}</ref> His nomination was for the Best International Flow category.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-10-06|title=Khaligraph Jones 2020 BET AWARDS – KAMATA SCHOOL OF MUSIC|url=https://www.kamataschool.com/khaligraph-jones-nominated-for-2020-bet-awards-nominees-list/|access-date=2021-04-05|website=www.kamataschool.com|language=en-US}}</ref> He was up against South Africa's [[Nasty C]], Brazil's DJonga, Ivorian Kaaris and eventual winner, [[Grime (music genre)|grime]] rapper [[Stormzy]] from the [[United Kingdom|UK]].<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-10-28|title=Khaligraph Jones reaction after losing BET Award to UK's Stomzy|url=https://www.pulselive.co.ke/entertainment/celebrities/khaligraph-jones-reaction-after-losing-bet-award-to-uks-stomzy-and-full-list-of/4s1c19b|access-date=2021-04-05|website=Pulse Live Kenya|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=https://www.the-star.co.ke/authors/nyambs|title=Khaligraph loses out to Stormzy at BET Awards|url=https://www.the-star.co.ke/sasa/word-is/2020-10-29-khaligraph-loses-out-to-stormzy-at-bet-awards/|access-date=2021-04-05|website=The Star|language=en-KE}}</ref>

In May 2021 he was named the East African Rapper of the Year at  [[MTN Uganda]]
