'''Mohammad Noh bin Salleh''' (born 15 March 1985) is a Malaysian singer-songwriter, musician, and record producer. He is best known as the frontman and principal songwriter of the rock band [[Hujan]], with whom they have released six studio albums.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.malaymail.com/s/981351/hujan-frontman-noh-salleh-to-open-for-yunas-exclusive-concert-in-kl|title=Hujan frontman Noh Salleh to open for Yuna's exclusive concert in KL|work=[[Malay Mail]]|access-date=22 August 2018|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thestar.com.my/lifestyle/people/2013/10/29/hujan-takes-bullying-and-booing-in-its-stride/|title=Hujan takes bullying and booing in its stride|last=Tan|first=Kevin|date=29 October 2013|website=[[The Star (Malaysia)|The Star]]|access-date=22 August 2018}}</ref> He has also recorded with his side project [[Extended play|EP]], "Angin Kencang" and "Debu Bercahaya" as a solo artist.

Born and raised in [[Miri]], [[Sarawak]], Noh moved to [[Kuala Lumpur]] and work as an assistant in [[recording studio]] own by [[Ahmad Idham]]. In between music sessions, he self-taught [[Music|musical]] knowledge and ask the musicians about [[jazz]] [[Chord (music)|chords]] that interested him. He began his career with [[Hujan]] in 2005, along with guitarist [[AG Coco]] who became a dominant figure in [[independent music]] scene. Their [[self-titled]] debut album ''Hujan'' in 2008, became the fastest-selling debut album in Malaysia history with massive success from the single "[[Hujan#Discography|Pagi yang Gelap]]" and "[[Hujan#Discography|Bila Aku Sudah Tiada]]".

==Music career==

===Band===
Noh began his music career as a [[hip hop]] singer with the group MIX with three of his friends; Asyraf Hardy, MC Wayne and Ogie. The trouped signed a contract with the Cat Presents Productions recording label owned by Cat Farish and appeared with their only album ''Mula Dari Dulu'' in 2006. MIX was later disbanded as Noh was starting his own band. 

Hujan began forming in 2005 after Noh uploaded some of his [[Demo (music)|demo]]s to [[Myspace]], which later discovered by [[AG Coco]] whom introduce him to bigger audience in music scene.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.whiteboardjournal.com/interview/ideas/music-of-malaysia-with-noh-salleh/|title=Music of Malaysia with Noh Salleh|last=Jenie|first=Ken|date=26 November 2014|website=Whiteboard Journal|language=en-US|access-date=22 August 2018}}</ref> In earlier stage of Hujan, they were heavily influenced by [[jazz]] and [[Rock music|rock]] in songwriting. Noh Salleh and Hang Dimas played their first gig on 17 February 2006 at Malam Pesta Gagalis Ubu in Lost Generation Space as Hujan.

===Solo career===
Noh debuted his solo work ''Angin Kencang'' in July 2014.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://masses.com.my/music/noh-sallehs-angin-kencang-reach-1-itunes/|title=Noh Salleh's "Angin Kencang" reach #1 on iTunes|date=2017-08-07|website=MASSES|language=en-US|access-date=2019-03-04}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://warnermusic.com.my/noh-salleh-angin-kencang-album/|title=NOH SALLEH – ANGIN KENCANG ALBUM|date=August 29, 2017|website=warnermusic.com.my|language=en-US|access-date=2019-03-04}}</ref> The album was mainly inspired by his wife [[Mizz Nina]] with elements of 1960s and 1970s [[French popular music]]; and it was co-produced with Adink Permana, Ade Paloh and Mondo Gascaro from the Indonesian band [[Sore (band)|Sore]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.sinarharian.com.my/hiburan/elemen-pop-retro-inspirasi-noh-salleh-1.736336|title=Elemen pop retro inspirasi Noh Salleh|last=Rafiq Mazlan|date=28 September 2017|work=[[Sinar Harian]]|access-date=22 August 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://kumparan.com/@kumparanhits/apa-alasan-noh-salleh-bekerja-sama-dengan-sore-untuk-urusan-musik|title=Apa Alasan Noh Salleh Bekerja Sama dengan Sore untuk Urusan Musik?|website=kumparan|access-date=22 August 2018}}</ref>
