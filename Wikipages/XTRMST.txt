'''XTRMST''' is an American [[straight edge]] [[hardcore punk]] band<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://noisey.vice.com/en_us/article/the-punk-rock-roots-of-xtrmst-the-new-straight-edge-hardcore-project-from-afis-davey-havok-jade-puget|title=The Punk Rock Roots of XTRMST, the New Straight-Edge Hardcore Project from AFI's Davey Havok and Jade Puget|website=Noisey|language=en-us|access-date=2017-04-01}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.altpress.com/news/entry/xtrmst_davey_and_jade_of_afis_hardcore_band_announce_debut_album|title=XTRMST (Davey and Jade of AFI's hardcore band) announce debut album - News - Alternative Press|website=Alternative Press|language=en|access-date=2017-04-01}}</ref> featuring [[Davey Havok]] and [[Jade Puget]] of [[AFI (band)|AFI]]. XTRMST is Havok and Puget's second side project after their electronic project [[Blaqk Audio]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.ocregister.com/articles/puget-649174-hardcore-xtrmst.html|title=Members of AFI go hardcore with XTRMST|last=FADROSKI|first=KELLI SKYE|work=The Orange County Register|access-date=2017-04-01|language=en-US}}</ref> They released their debut album ''[[XTRMST (album)|XTRMST]]'' in 2014<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sputnikmusic.com/review/61491/XTRMST-XTRMST/|title=XTRMST - XTRMST (album review ) {{!}} Sputnikmusic|website=www.sputnikmusic.com|access-date=2017-04-01}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://m.axs.com/afi-straight-edge-hardcore-side-project-xtrmst-announces-november-albu-22116|title=AFI straight edge hardcore side project XTRMST announces November album release|work=AXS|access-date=2017-04-01|language=en-US}}</ref> on long-time friend [[Steve Aoki|Steve Aoki's]] [[Dim Mak Records]].<ref>{{Citation|last=ArtisanNewsService|title=DAVEY HAVOK, JADE PUGET, STEVE AOKI PUT XTRMST HARDCORE ON DIM MAK DANCE LABEL|date=2015-01-15|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eAt9yBdx9Ps|access-date=2017-04-01}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=supercerealpunx|title=XTRMST|url=http://www.punknews.org/review/13032/xtrmst-xtrmst|publisher=Punknews.org|access-date=January 31, 2015|date=November 20, 2014}}</ref> Havok and Puget later added Chris Sorenson ([[Saosin]]) on bass, Josh James ([[Stick to Your Guns (band)|Stick to Your Guns]], [[Evergreen Terrace]], [[Casey Jones (band)|Casey Jones]]) on guitar, and Val Saucedo ([[Loma Prieta (band)|Loma Prieta]], Punch) on drums.
