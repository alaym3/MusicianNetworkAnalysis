'''Fresh''' is a British [[pop punk]] band formed in London in 2015 by Kathryn Woods (vocals/guitar), with George Phillips (bass) and Daniel Goldberg (drums) joining in 2016.<ref name="AllMusic"/><ref name="Upset2">{{cite web|url=https://www.upsetmagazine.com/features/fresh-youre-not-punk-youre-just-lad-listens-hardcore/|last=Shutler|first=Ali|date=18 August 2017 |title=Fresh: "You're not punk if you're just some lad who listens to hardcore"|access-date=1 March 2018|publisher=Upset Magazine}}</ref><ref name="Punktastic">{{cite web|url=http://www.punktastic.com/album-reviews/fresh-fresh/|last=Bhandari|first=Ashwin|date=27 August 2017 |title=Fresh – 'Fresh'|access-date=1 March 2018|publisher=Punktastic}}</ref> The current lineup is completed by Myles McCabe (guitar), who joined in 2017. They have released two full-length albums and three EPs.

==History==
Fresh was started in London in 2015 by songwriter Kathryn Woods (vocals/guitar). George Phillips (bass) and Daniel Goldberg (drums) joined the live band in 2016, and would go on to play on its debut album.<ref name="Upset2"/><ref name="Punktastic"/><ref name="AllMusic"/> The current lineup is completed by Myles McCabe (guitar), who joined in 2017.

After self-releasing two EPs in 2015 the band signed to [[Specialist Subject Records]] and released their self-titled debut album on 18 August 2017.<ref name="Kerrang News"/><ref name="Upset">{{cite web|url=https://www.upsetmagazine.com/news/listen/fresh-announce-debut-album-brilliant-fuck-life/|last=Shutler|first=Ali|date=19 June 2017 |title=Fresh announce debut album with the brilliant 'Fuck My Life'|access-date=1 March 2018|publisher=Upset Magazine}}</ref><ref name="Upset2"/><ref name="AllMusic"/> The album was recorded at The Ranch in Southampton.<ref name="Upset"/>

In July 2017 they were featured in [[Kerrang!]] who compared them to [[Modern Baseball]], [[Joyce Manor]], and [[Diet Cig]]. The article quotes Kathryn as saying, "Fresh started because I was a teenage girl who was bored of only seeing men playing in bands".<ref name="Kerrang News">{{cite magazine|last=De Freitas |first=Ryan |date=22 July 2017 |title=Fresh For Fans Of: Joyce Manor, Modern Baseball, Diet Cig|url=https://www.pressreader.com/uk/kerrang-uk/20170722/281689729864961 |magazine=[[Kerrang!]] |location=[[London]] |publisher=Wasted Talent Ltd |access-date=1 March 2018}}</ref>

In December 2017 [[ABC News]] listed their debut album as one of their 50 best albums of that year.<ref name="ABC News 50 best"/>

In February 2019 Fresh announced their second album ''Withdraw'' would be released on 7 June, unveiling lead single "Willa" (named after [[Willa Cather]]).<ref name="Ladyfuzz Withdraw">{{cite web|url=https://www.ladyfuzz.co.uk/ladyfuzz-blog/freshwilla|title=Premiere: "Willa" the first single from London punx Fresh's new album "Withdraw"|website=Ladyfuzz Zine|date=25 February 2019|access-date=1 March 2019|last=Livingstone|first=Lucinda}}</ref>

On 9 March 2021 the band released the single "Girl Clout".<ref name="Stereogum">{{cite web|url=https://www.stereogum.com/2118888/fresh-girl-clout/music/|title=Fresh – “Girl Clout”|website=[[Stereogum]]|date=9 March 2021|accessdate=13 April 2021|last=Deville|first=Chris}}</ref> Then on 19 April they announced it was from a new EP that was to be released on 30 April, entitled ''The Summer I Got Good At Guitar'', as well as unveiling a second single - "My Redemption Arc".<ref name="Stereogum Guitar EP">{{cite web|url=https://www.stereogum.com/2144609/fresh-my-redemption-arc/music/|title=Fresh – “My Redemption Arc”|website=[[Stereogum]]|date=19 April 2021|accessdate=19 April 2021|last=Deville|first=Chris}}</ref>  

Since 2018's "Daytime" / "Nighttime" AA single everything has been recorded with Rich Mandell of the band [[Happy Accidents (band)|Happy Accidents]]. Mandell also plays in the band ME REX led by McCabe and of which Woods is also a member.
