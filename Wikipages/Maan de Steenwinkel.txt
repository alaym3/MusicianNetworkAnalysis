'''Maan de Steenwinkel''' (born 10 February 1997), also known as simply '''Maan''', is a Dutch singer and actress. De Steenwinkel first came to prominence after winning [[The Voice of Holland (season 6)|season six]] of ''[[The Voice of Holland]]''. She has subsequently taken part in the Dutch television series ''[[De beste zangers van Nederland]]'' and in the Dutch version of ''[[Dance Dance Dance (UK TV series)#Other versions|Dance Dance Dance]]''.
[[File:Night of the Koemarkt 2018 (41320587600) (cropped).jpg|thumb|Maan performing in 2018]]

==Life and career==
De Steenwinkel was born in [[Utrecht]]. The family moved to [[Bergen, North Holland]] a year later. Her father is a television director and her mother was a handicraft teacher.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.mediacourant.nl/2016/05/maan-ondanks-kritiek-weer-met-papa-mee-naar-pauw/|title = Maan ondanks kritiek wéér met papa mee naar Pauw|date = 12 May 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |author1=Peter Van Brummelen |title=Zangeres Maan de Steenwinkel: 'Dit is mijn vierde tussenjaar' |url=https://www.parool.nl/kunst-media/zangeres-maan-de-steenwinkel-dit-is-mijn-vierde-tussenjaar~be2156e8/ |website=[[Het Parool]] |accessdate=1 May 2020 |language=nl-NL |date=21 March 2020}}</ref> She attended {{ill|Murmellius Gymnasium|nl}} in [[Alkmaar]].{{citation needed|date=May 2020}}

In 2015, de Steenwinkel auditioned for [[The Voice of Holland (season 6)|season six]] of ''[[The Voice of Holland]]'', where she was a member of Team [[Marco Borsato|Marco]]. She performed "[[The Power of Love (Frankie Goes to Hollywood song)|The Power of Love]]" in her blind audition, where all four coaches turned their chairs.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.top40.nl/maan/perfect-world_26946|title=Maan - Perfect World|first=Stichting Nederlandse Top|last=40|publisher=top40.nl}}</ref> On 29 January 2016, de Steenwinkel went on to win the competition. Following her win, she released the single "Perfect World", which was certified gold and peaked within the Top 40 in the Netherlands. Her follow-up single, "Ride It", achieved similar levels of success.<ref name="wiwi"/>

In 2017, de Steenwinkel became involved with season ten of ''[[Beste Zangers|De beste zangers van Nederland]]'' and season three of ''[[Dance Dance Dance (UK TV series)#Other versions|Dance Dance Dance]]''.<ref name="wiwi">{{cite web|url=http://wiwibloggs.com/2017/07/11/netherlands-maan-woman-eurovision-2018/192749/|title=THE NETHERLANDS: IS MAAN THE WOMAN FOR EUROVISION 2018?|date=11 July 2017|publisher=Wiwibloggs|website=wiwibloggs.com|author=Mike}}</ref>

In January 2020, she released her debut album ''{{ill|Onverstaanbaar|nl}}''.<ref>{{cite web |title=Maan is dankbaar voor reacties op debuutalbum |url=https://www.telegraaf.nl/entertainment/2118064729/maan-is-dankbaar-voor-reacties-op-debuutalbum |website=[[De Telegraaf]] |accessdate=1 May 2020 |language=nl-NL |date=1 February 2020}}</ref>
