'''Marco Anthony Archer''' (born October 11, 1994),<ref>{{cite web |last=Chesler |first=Josh |title=Rapper Phora May Be on a Major Label Now, But He Won't Stop Repping Anaheim – OC Weekly |url=https://www.ocweekly.com/rapper-phora-may-be-on-a-major-label-now-but-he-wont-stop-repping-anaheim-8488866/}}</ref> better known by his stage name '''Phora''', is an American [[rapper]] from [[Anaheim, California]]. In 2011 he founded his own record label, Yours Truly.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://steryo.com/2014/06/05/exclusive-interview-phora/ |title=Exclusive Interview: Phora |website=STERYO |access-date=June 5, 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151026125556/http://steryo.com/2014/06/05/exclusive-interview-phora/ |archive-date=October 26, 2015 |url-status=dead }}</ref> On February 14, 2017, Phora announced signed a major label deal with [[Warner Bros. Records]].<ref name="Major Deal">{{Cite web|url=https://www.instagram.com/p/BQhEWYghDe3/|title=Phora Signed a major label deal with Warner Bros. Records|website=Instagram|access-date=February 15, 2017}}</ref> So far Phora has written and released eleven albums, majority of which relates to his past struggles with depression. These include ''Still a Kid'', ''One Life to Live'', ''Sincerely Yours, Nights Like These,'' ''Angels'' ''with Broken Wings'', ''With Love, Heartbreak Hotel'', and his debut album ''[[Yours Truly Forever]]''. In 2018, he released a follow-up to his debut album, titled ''Love Is Hell''. In July 2019, Phora left his record deal with Warner Bros. Records and subsequently released his new album with Emmanuel Mendoza Herrero entitled ''Bury Me with Dead Roses''. On November 6, 2020, he released his fourth studio album, ''With Love 2''.

==Early life==
Marco Archer was born in [[Anaheim, California]]. Before his career in music, Archer worked as a [[tattoo artist]] which proved to be relatively lucrative and aided in the funding of his rap career. In 2011, Marco was nearly stabbed to death in Anaheim, CA just outside of Trident Learning Center, now known as Gilbert High.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.mtv.com/artists/phora/|title=Rapper Phora biography|website=MTV|url-status=bot: unknown|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160505034252/http://www.mtv.com/artists/phora/|archivedate=May 5, 2016}}</ref>
He has survived a stabbing and two shootings.<ref name="My Story">{{cite web|url=http://www.worldstarhiphop.com/videos/video.php?v=wshh85prVH2bDV5zoZ5W |title=Inspirational Lyrics: Phora - My Story (Near Death Experience At Age 16) |publisher=Worldstarhiphop.com |date=November 26, 2013 }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://theboombox.com/phora-wounded-in-highway-shooting/|title=Rapper Phora Shot on California Highway |website=TheBoombox|access-date=August 27, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://hiphopdx.com/news/id.35248/title.california-rapper-phora-survives-shooting|title=California Rapper Phora Survives Shooting|website=HipHopDX|access-date=August 25, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://ktla.com/2015/08/25/underground-rapper-known-as-phora-wounded-in-shooting-on-210-freeway/|title=Underground Rapper Known as 'Phora' Wounded in Shooting on 210 Freeway |website=ktla|access-date=August 26, 2015}}</ref>
On August 25, 2015, While he was driving on the [[Interstate 210 and State Route 210 (California)|210 freeway]] in [[Pasadena, California|Pasadena]] around 2 a.m., a gray 
[[Infiniti G-series (Q40/Q60)|Infiniti sedan]] pulled up alongside Archer, who was driving home with his then girlfriend, Destiny. Three (3) bullets from a [[.45 ACP|.45 caliber pistol]] were fired into his back and neck, narrowly missing his [[vertebra]]. And the crime remains unsolved.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.laweekly.com/music/oc-rapper-phora-has-nearly-been-murdered-twice-but-his-music-stays-positive-6210703|title=O.C. Rapper Phora Has Nearly Been Murdered Twice, But His Music Stays the same but he has talked about very serious topics like drug use and abuse.|website=LA Weekly|access-date=October 27, 2015}}</ref>

==Musical style==
Growing up, Archer was strongly influenced by graffiti to make music and influenced by his father to pursue music.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://genius.com/artists/Phora|title=About Phora on Genius |website=Genius |access-date=October 11, 2013}}</ref>
In his song ''The World'' from his album ''Angels With Broken Wings'' (2015) he calls [[J. Cole]], [[Hopsin]] and [[Logic (musician)|Logic]] the only rappers he likes.
His musical style can also be defined by his [[Hip hop production|producer]]<nowiki> Anthro Beats.</nowiki><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://theproducersunited.com/tag/phora/|title=Phora Archives - Producers United|website=ProducersUnited|access-date=August 6, 2013}}</ref>
