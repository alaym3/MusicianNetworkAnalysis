'''Orhidea Latifi''' ({{IPA-sq|ɔɾhiˈdɛa laˈtifi|lang}}; born 21 December 1997), known professionally as '''Kida''', is a Kosovo-Albanian singer.

== Life and career ==

Kida was born as Orhidea Latifi on 21 December 1997 into an Albanian family in the city of [[Orahovac|Rahovec]], then part of the [[Serbia and Montenegro|FR Yugoslavia]], present-day [[Kosovo]].<ref>{{cite web |publisher=TeksteShqip |title=Kida{{spaced ndash}}Biografia |url=https://www.teksteshqip.com/kida/biografia |access-date=25 March 2021 |language=sq}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Top Channel|Top Music Awards]] |title=Kida #Bio |url=http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/artist/kida/ |access-date=8 February 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201025172405/http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/artist/kida/ |archive-date=25 October 2020 |language=sq}}</ref> In July 2020, Latifi's singles "Paranoia" and "Pishmon" featuring Albanian rapper [[Mozzik]] reached number one in Albania and peaked at number 56 and 98 in Switzerland, respectively.<ref>{{cite web |last=Velija |first=Kreizia |publisher=[[Top Channel|Top Albania Radio]] |title=Dy bashkëpunime në një, Kida dhe Mozzik na suprizuan të gjithëve |url=https://www.topalbaniaradio.com/v7/dy-bashkepunime-ne-nje-kida-dhe-mozzik-na-suprizuan-te-gjitheve/ |access-date=24 April 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210424152050/https://www.topalbaniaradio.com/v7/dy-bashkepunime-ne-nje-kida-dhe-mozzik-na-suprizuan-te-gjitheve/ |archive-date=24 April 2021 |language=sq |date=3 July 2020 |url-status=live}}</ref> Featuring [[Pop music|pop]] and [[Contemporary R&B|R&B]] music, her follow-up single "Drunk" peaked at number seven in her native country.<ref>{{cite web |publisher=Sounds European! |title=Kida with "Drunk" |url=https://www.soundseuropean.com/2021/02/kosovo-kida-drunk.html |access-date=24 April 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210424153104/https://www.soundseuropean.com/2021/02/kosovo-kida-drunk.html |archive-date=24 April 2021 |date=8 February 2021 |url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Top Channel]] |title=Kida, më e pjekur dhe e dehur! |url=http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/kida-me-e-pjekur-dhe-e-dehur/ |access-date=24 April 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210424153352/http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/kida-me-e-pjekur-dhe-e-dehur/ |archive-date=24 April 2021 |language=sq |date=23 December 2020 |url-status=live}}</ref>
