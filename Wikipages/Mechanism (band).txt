'''Mechanism''' is a metal band from [[Vancouver, British Columbia]]. Their debut album, ''Inspired Horrific'', combines [[death metal]] and [[progressive metal]].
==Personnel==
* [[Gene Hoglan]] - Drums
* [[Chris Valagao]] - Vocals
* Kris Schulz - Guitar
* Bryan Somerville - Guitar, Samples
* Markus McCallum - Bass

==Discography==
* 2009 - ''Inspired Horrific''
