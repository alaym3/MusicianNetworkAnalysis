'''Im Chang-kyun''' ({{Korean|임창균}}; born January 26, 1996), known by the stage name '''I.M''' or [[Mononymous person|mononymously]] as '''Changkyun''', is a South Korean rapper and singer. He debuted in the South Korean [[boy band|boy group]] [[Monsta X]] through [[Mnet (TV channel)|Mnet]]'s survival show ''No.Mercy'' in 2015. He made his solo debut with the [[Extended play|EP]] ''[[Duality (EP)|Duality]]'' in 2021.

== Career ==
=== Early life and debut ===
I.M was born on January 26, 1996 [[Suwon]], [[Gyeonggi-do]], South Korea, although he cites the [[Gwangsan District|Gwangsan-gu]] of [[Gwangju]] as his hometown.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=07lEgQRdbro&feature=youtu.be&ab_channel=VOGUEKOREA|script-title=ko:퀴즈에 진심인 몬스타엑스(MONSTA X)의 TMI 대방출 아이엠이 집 가면 바로 하는 일은? 민혁의 태어난 진짜 출생지는?! (광주 편)|url=http://www.marketnews.co.kr/news/articleView.html?idxno=44409|access-date=February 4, 2021|website=Market News|language=ko|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200507045340/http://www.marketnews.co.kr/news/articleView.html?idxno=44409|archive-date=May 7, 2020}}</ref> He spent most of his early years living abroad because of his father's work as a scientist, including living in [[Boston]], Massachusetts where he gained fluency in English while his father worked at [[Harvard University]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Morin|first=Natalie|date=September 7, 2019|title=Up Close & Personal With The Many Sides of Monsta X|url=https://www.refinery29.com/en-us/2019/09/240480/monstax-kpop-masculinity-confidence-duality-interview|access-date=August 15, 2020|website=[[Refinery29]]|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Kim|first=Jae-Ha|date=July 3, 2018|title=Go Away With ... In 2013 and 2014, I.M was a part of the boy band Nu'bility under Special K Entertainment. The group never officially debuted despite holding several events and performances.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|last=Benjamin|first=Jeff|date=April 3, 2020|title=Meet I.M from Monsta X: from unwanted to invaluable|url=https://www.scmp.com/lifestyle/entertainment/article/3078222/meet-im-monsta-x-unwanted-competitor-who-became-global-k|access-date=August 1, 2020|website=[[South China Morning Post]]|language=en}}</ref>

I.M was a late addition to [[Mnet (TV channel)|Mnet]]'s survival show ''No.Mercy'', becoming the thirteenth competitor.<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web|last=Oh|first=Hyojin|date=January 28, 2015|title='노머시' 새연습생 I.M투입, 기존 연습생 텃세|url=https://www.newsen.com/news_view.php?uid=201501282315505410|access-date=August 16, 2020|website=Newsen|language=ko}}</ref> Despite his late addition, he was one of the seven contestants chosen to debut in [[Starship Entertainment]]'s new hip-hop boy group [[Monsta X]].<ref name="nomercy">{{Cite web|title=New idol group to be born through Mnet's 'No.Mercy'|url=http://kpopherald.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=201411131802564766044_2|access-date=July 30, 2020|website=[[The Korea Herald|K-pop Herald]]|last=Park|first=Gyu-oh|date=November 13, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Sun|first=Mi-kyung|date=February 12, 2015|title='노머시' 주헌·셔누·민혁 등 7인 멤버 확정..스타쉽 新보이그룹|url=http://osen.mt.co.kr/article/G1110076836|access-date=August 16, 2020|website=OSEN|language=ko}}</ref>

=== 2015–present: Monsta X and solo work ===
In May 2015, I.M debuted with Monsta X with their first EP ''[[Trespass (EP)|Trespass]]'', on which he has five writing credits.<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{cite news|title=Monsta X goes for street style|work=K-pop Herald|url=http://kpopherald.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=201508161741467671200_2&ACE_SEARCH=1|last=Ahn|first=Sung-mi|date=August 6, 2015|access-date=July 11, 2017}}</ref>

In May 2016, I.M released his first mixtape through Starship Entertainment's YouTube channel, titled "Who Am I?" featuring Yeseo.<ref name="who">{{Cite web|last=Yoo|first=Byung-cheol|date=May 6, 2016|title=몬스타엑스 아이엠 컴백 앞두고 첫 번째 믹스테잎 Who am i 깜짝 공개|url=http://www.wowtv.co.kr/NewsCenter/News/Read?articleId=A201605060013|access-date=August 16, 2020|website=Korea Economic Daily TV|language=ko}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=몬스타엑스 아이엠, 넋업샨·지슬로우와 함께한 믹스테잎 공개|url=http://stoo.asiae.co.kr/article.php?aid=27747043803|access-date=August 16, 2020|website=Sports Today|last=Han|first=Sujin|date=May 6, 2016|language=ko}}</ref>

On October 20, I.M and [[Brother Su]] featuring J.Han released a mixtape titled "Madeleine".<ref name="mdln" />

On July 2017, I.M released a mixtape titled "Be My Friend", alongside group member [[Joohoney]].<ref name="bmf" />

In February 2018, I.M released another solo mixtape titled "Fly With Me".<ref name="fwm" />

In February 2019, I.M narrated an audiobook [[Oscar Wilde]]'s ''[[The Happy Prince and Other Tales|The Happy Prince]]'', released through [[Naver Corporation|Naver]]'s Audioclip.<ref name="the happy prince">{{Cite web|script-title=ko:"시각장애인 위해"..몬스타엑스 아이엠, 오디오북 참여에 담긴 깊은 속내|url=https://entertain.naver.com/topic/1076271/1076271/read?oid=109&aid=0003954183|access-date=August 1, 2020|website=OSEN|via=Naver|last=Lee|first=Sodam|date=February 18, 2019|language=ko}}</ref> Later that year, he narrated two audiobooks alongside group member [[Shownu]], also released through Naver's Audioclip.<ref name="audio" />

In April, I.M released his mixtape with the lead single "Horizon", which was a collaboration with the American artist [[ELHAE]].<ref name="horizon">{{Cite web|last=Cummings-Grady|first=Mackenzie|date=May 26, 2020|title=ELHAE Talks TikTok Love and How He Connected with a K-Pop Superstar|url=https://www.popdust.com/elhae-interview-need-to-know-2646085320.html?share_id=5565899|access-date=August 1, 2020|website=Popdust|language=en}}</ref> He released "Scent" as the B-side of the single.<ref name="scent">{{Cite web|last=Herman|first=Tamar|date=April 19, 2019|title=Monsta X's I.M Teams Up With Elhae on 'Horizon' Video: Watch|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/k-town/8507914/monsta-xs-im-teams-up-with-elhae-on-horizon-video-watch|access-date=August 5, 2020|website=Billboard}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|script-title=ko:몬스타엑스 아이엠, "'글로벌 리더' 담당. 위트있게 하려고 노력"|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/408/0000100000|access-date=August 18, 2018|website=iMBC|via=[[Naver]]|last=Kim|first=Mijeong|date=August 18, 2018|language=ko}}</ref>
