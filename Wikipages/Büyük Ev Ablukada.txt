'''Büyük Ev Ablukada''' ({{IPA-tr|byjyc ev ɑbɫukɑdɑ|lang}}) (Big House under Blockade) is a Turkish music group founded in [[Istanbul]] in 2008.<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.radikal.com.tr/Radikal.aspx?aType=RadikalDetayV3&ArticleID=1040842&Date=23.02.2011&CategoryID=141 | title = Büyük ev ablukada şiir değil miydi? | date = 23 February 2011 | publisher = [[Radikal]] | author = Elif Ekinci }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.dipnot.tv/8119/Gencler-bu-muzik-grubunu-konusuyor-Buyuk-Ev-Ablukada.aspx |title=Gençler bu müzik grubunu konuşuyor: Büyük Ev Ablukada |website=Dipnot |language=tr |publication-date=29 June 2011 |url-status=bot: unknown |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120422200400/http://www.dipnot.tv/8119/Gencler-bu-muzik-grubunu-konusuyor-Buyuk-Ev-Ablukada.aspx |archivedate=22 April 2012 }}</ref> The band was started by Cem Yılmazer (a.k.a. Afordisman Salihins) and Bartu Küçükçağlayan (a.k.a. Canavar Banavar.) After some time the band turned into an electrical version, which is described by "Full Faça". Büyük Ev Ablukada became well-known in a very short time with the help of their own songs and special concerts. The group members use aliases instead of their own names and some of the original names of the members are still unknown. They first released a concert record named "Ay Şuram Ağrıyo" and an album named "Olmadı Kaçarız", and then on 21 December 2012, they released their debut album "Full Faça," which was issued in both CD and vinyl by their own record company, Olmadı Kaçarız.
