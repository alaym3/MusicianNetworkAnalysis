'''Harrison Armstrong''' (born 9 December 1999), known professionally as '''Aitch''', is a British [[rapper]] and [[songwriter]] from [[Manchester]]. His solo breakthrough single "Straight Rhymez" has garnered over 24 million views and brought him to the attention of [[London]] rapper [[Stormzy]].<ref name="occ2019">{{cite news|url=https://www.officialcharts.com/chart-news/who-is-aitch-get-to-know-the-manchester-rapper-taking-over-the-charts__27066/|title=Who is Aitch? Get to know the Manchester rapper taking over the charts|last=Ainsley|first=Helen|date=14 August 2019|access-date=21 August 2019|publisher=Official Charts|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190814154947/https://www.officialcharts.com/chart-news/who-is-aitch-get-to-know-the-manchester-rapper-taking-over-the-charts__27066/|archive-date=14 August 2019|url-status=live}}</ref> His song "[[Rain (Aitch and AJ Tracey song)|Rain]]" has garnered over 47 million views on YouTube and over 120 million streams on [[Spotify]].

Aitch has released three singles that have charted within the top 40 in the UK, including "[[Taste (Make It Shake)]]", which peaked at number two on the [[UK Singles Chart]].<ref>{{cite ==Early and personal life==
Aitch was born in [[Moston, Manchester|Moston]], [[Manchester]]. He is an avid supporter of [[Manchester United F.C.|Manchester United]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.manutd.com/en/news/detail/new-adidas-manchester-united-away-kit-for-2019-20-revealed |title=Revealed: Our new away kit for 2019/20 |work=Manchester United F.C. |date=12 July 2019 |access-date=31 January 2021 |archive-date=6 February 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210206143730/https://www.manutd.com/en/news/detail/new-adidas-manchester-united-away-kit-for-2019-20-revealed |url-status=live }}</ref>
