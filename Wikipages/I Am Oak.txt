'''I Am Oak''' is the band of [[Utrecht]]-based musician Thijs Kuijken. The band started in 2008. In that year the band released their first EP ‘Sou Ka’. The music of I Am Oak is often described as folk, indie and acoustic.<ref>[http://3voor12.vpro.nl/lokaal/zeeland/nieuws/2014/maart/I-Am-Oak-in-De-Spot.html] I Am Oak by 3VOOR12</ref>

The first full length album by the band ‘On Claws’ was released by [[Snowstar Records]] in 2010. One year later, in 2011, I Am Oak won the Dutch based 3VOOR12 Award for best album for their 2011 release, [[Oasem album|Oasem]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.desmet.tv/3-voor-12-awards/ |title=Archived copy |access-date=2012-04-22 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120422094831/http://www.desmet.tv/3-voor-12-awards/ |archive-date=22 April 2012 |df=dmy-all }} I Am Oak wins 3VOOR12 best album 2011 Award</ref> The band also appeared at the prestigious 2011 [[Popkomm]] festival<ref>[http://popkomm2011.sched.org/artist/iamoaknetherlands] Popkomm – I Am Oak</ref>

In March 2012, the band showcased at the [[South by Southwest]] Festival in Austin, Texas being described as one of the top artists performing from The Netherlands.<ref>[http://www.dutchperformingarts.com/music/2012/03/07/top-artists-from-the-netherlands-to-perform-at-sxsw-2012/] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120728095743/http://www.dutchperformingarts.com/music/2012/03/07/top-artists-from-the-netherlands-to-perform-at-sxsw-2012/ |date=28 July 2012 }} Top Artists From The Netherlands To Perform At SXSW 2012</ref> In May 2012, I Am Oak performed three shows at [[The Great Escape Festival]] in Brighton and were selected by the festival as their Band Of The Day.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://greatescapefestival.tumblr.com/post/5134910898/band-of-the-day-44-i-am-oak |title=Archived copy |access-date=2011-12-04 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111204012752/http://greatescapefestival.tumblr.com/post/5134910898/band-of-the-day-44-i-am-oak |archive-date=4 December 2011 |df=dmy-all }} I Am Oak – The Great Escape Band Of The Day</ref>

2012 saw the release of the band's third full-length album entitled "Nowhere Or Tammensaari" on Snowstar Records in The Netherlands and on [[Heist or Hit Records|Heist Or Hit Records]] on the 2nd of July in the UK & Ireland. The album was recorded in a house in a small town in Finland, the name of which translates to “oak island.” However, although located in Finland, the majority language there is Swedish, thus the town is also known as Ekenäs, which translates to “oak peninsula."<ref>[http://berlinbeat.wordpress.com/2012/04/24/interview-i-am-oak/] Berlin Beat – Interview with I Am Oak</ref>

The first single 'Palpable' from the album “Nowhere Or Tammensaari" has been featured on several occasions on [[BBC Radio 6 Music|BBC 6 Music]] on the [[Lauren Laverne]] show, first played on 3 May 2012<ref>[http://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/b01gyh7v] 6Music track list – [[Lauren Laverne]] 3 May 2012</ref> On 25 June 2012, Lauren Laverne added the single 'Palpable' as her [[BBC Radio 6 Music|BBC 6 Music]] 'MPFree' track <ref>[http://www.hseeb44i./blogspot.com/laurenlaverne/2012/06/mpfree-25th-june-2012.shtml] 25 June 2012 Lauren Laverne BBC 6 Music MPFree track</ref>

31 January 2014 marked the release-date for I am Oak's fourth full-length album.<ref>[http://www.snowstar.nl/release/ols-songd/] Official Website Snowstar with releaseinformation about Ols Songd</ref> The album, titled 'Ols Songd', is a collection of previously unreleased songs. Most songs on the album have been written a long time ago, some even prior to the debut-album 'On Claws'. Critical reactions to the album have been mostly positive. Several platforms rewarded 'Ols Songd' with a four out of five star rating,<ref>[http://www.cuttingedge.nl/muziek/i-am-oak-ols-songd] Review by Cutting Edge</ref> praising I am Oak's ability to write touching music that excels in the details. First single 'Honeycomb' is widely claimed to be the strongest song on the record.<ref>[http://www.motherlovemusic.be/album/oak-ols-songd] Review by Mother Love Music</ref>

Early 2014 saw another release for I am Oak. Singer Thijs Kuijken recorded a split EP together with The Black Atlantic. The cooperation resulted in a 10" vinyl titled 'Black Oak'. The record contains a total of 6 songs, three on each side. Consisting of two songs written together, one new song of either artist and a cover of a song originally written by the other artist.<ref>[http://kickingthehabit.nl/2014-02-14/i-am-oak-the-black-atlantic-brengen-split-10-uit-als-black-oak-h] Announcement release + details Black Oak</ref> Apart from the 10" vinyl I am Oak & The Black Atlantic recorded a video for the song 'Rove' and did a 'Black Oak'  tour through Germany and Switzerland.<ref>[http://en.paperblog.com/i-am-oak-solo-tour-w-the-black-atlantic-new-album-old-songd-752136/] Announcement tour 'Black Oak'</ref>
