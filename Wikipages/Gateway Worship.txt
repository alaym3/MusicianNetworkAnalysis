'''Gateway Worship''' is an American Christian worship band from [[Dallas-Fort Worth]], [[Texas]].<ref>Poch, Cindy. "Review on Soul-Audio.com." "[http://www.soul-audio.com/album-reviews/05-06-2008/gateway-worship/ Article] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170909052927/http://www.soul-audio.com/album-reviews/05-06-2008/gateway-worship/ |date=September 9, 2017 }}". Retrieved August 1, 2008.</ref> The team leads worship at [[Gateway Church (Texas)|Gateway Church]], a 39,000 member congregation located in [[Southlake, Texas]], which has grown quickly since the church's beginnings in 2000.<ref>Unknown. "Gateway Biography" "[http://www.integritymusic.com/?option=com_artists&task=bio&aid=24&ltemid=72 Gateway Worship Bio]". Retrieved August 1, 2008.</ref> Gateway Worship is made of a whole team of worshippers who serve Gateway.  [[Thomas Miller (pastor)|Thomas Miller]] served as the original senior worship pastor until becoming executive ministry pastor in 2018. He was succeeded by [[Mark Harris (musician)|Mark Harris]], formerly of [[4Him]].{{citation needed|date=March 2017}} Harris leads a team of worship pastors including, David Moore, Matt Birkenfeld, Zach Neese, Aaron Crider, Jason Tam, Tim Sheppard, Jessica Sheppard, Levi Smith, Austin Benjamin.{{citation needed|date=March 2017}}

Gateway Worship is currently signed with Gateway Create Publishing, [[Fair Trade Services]], and [[Integrity Music]].<ref>Farias, Andree. "Gateway Worship: Wake Up The World" "[http://www.christianitytoday.com/music/reviews/2008/wakeuptheworld.html Review on Christianity Today]". Retrieved August 1, 2008.</ref>

The group's 2008 release ''Wake Up the World'' reached No.&nbsp;2 on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[List of number-one Billboard Christian Albums|Top Christian Albums]] chart.<ref>[{{AllMusic|class=artist|id=p909260/charts-awards|pure_url=yes}} Billboard], [[Allmusic]]</ref>

Gateway's live album, ''[[Walls (Gateway Worship album)|Walls]]'', was released on October 2, 2015<ref>[{{AllMusic|class=artist|id=p909260/charts-awards|pure_url=yes}} Billboard][[Allmusic]]</ref> and reached No.&nbsp;1 on ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]''{{'}}s Top Christian Albums Chart.<ref name="BillboardCCM">{{cite web|title=Gateway Worship Song & Chart History|url={{BillboardURLbyName|artist=Gateway Worship|chart=Christian Albums}}|publisher=Billboard Magazine|accessdate=May 6, 2016}}</ref>
