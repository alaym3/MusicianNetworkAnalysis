'''D'Kyla Paige Woolen''' (born January 8, 2001), known professionally as '''Kaash Paige''' (a [[backronym]] for Kill All Arrogance, Stop Hatred), is an American singer-songwriter from [[Dallas]], Texas. She is signed to Se Lavi Productions and [[Def Jam Recordings]] and managed by [[Roc Nation]].<ref name="Twitter">https://twitter.com/KAASHMYCHECKS/status/1287816345988431872 {{Bare URL inline|date=November 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Wallace |first1=Riley |title=Singer Kaash Paige Talks 'Love Songs' & Def Jam Debut |url=https://hiphopdx.com/news/id.53651/title.singer-kaash-paige-talks-love-songs-def-jam-debut |website=HipHopDX |date=December 2, 2019 |access-date=June 3, 2020}}</ref> She released her debut extended play ''Parked Car Convos'' on November 15, 2019.<ref>{{cite web |title=Kaash Paige is one to watch out for, with new project 'Parked Car Convos' |url=https://earmilk.com/2019/11/21/kaash-paige-is-one-to-watch-out-for-with-new-project-parked-car-convos/ |website=Earmilk |date=November 21, 2019 |access-date=June 3, 2020}}</ref>

==Early life==
D'Kyla Paige Woolen was born on January 8, 2001, in [[Dallas]], Texas. She started writing and recording in high school, uploading tracks to [[SoundCloud]], where she quickly gained a local following.<ref>{{cite web |title=Kaash Paige Is R&B's New Dream Girl |url=https://www.onestowatch.com/blog/kaash-paige-is-rbs-new-dream-girl |website=OnesToWatch |access-date=June 3, 2020}}</ref>

==Career==
By early 2019, Paige had attracted attention from several record labels, and eventually landed a deal with Se Lavi Productions and [[Def Jam Recordings]].<ref name="AllMusic">{{cite web |last1=Kellman |first1=Andy |title=Kaash Paige – Biography |url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/kaash-paige-mn0003889831/biography |website=AllMusic}}</ref> The single "[[Love Songs (Kaash Paige song)|Love Songs]]" went viral on various social media platforms and spent six weeks at the top of [[Spotify]]'s global viral chart, reaching [[Billboard (magazine)|''Billboard'']]'s [[Hot R&B Songs]] chart.<ref>{{cite web |title=10 things you need to know about Kaash Paige, the Texas heartbreaker making intimate R&B |url=https://i-d.vice.com/en_uk/article/bvgnbv/kaash-paige-tiktok-love-songs-interview |website=Vice |access-date=June 3, 2020}}</ref> On November 15, 2019, she released her debut EP, ''Parked Car Convos''. The remix of "Love Songs" was released in January 2020 featuring [[6lack]].<ref name="AllMusic"/> In March 2020, she appeared on the track "Euphoria" with [[Don Toliver]] and [[Travis Scott]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Don Toliver, Travis Scott, & Kaash Paige Ride A Cloud On "Euphoria" |url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/don-toliver-travis-scott-and-kaash-paige-ride-a-cloud-on-euphoria-new-song.1986524.html |website=HotNewHipHop |access-date=June 3, 2020}}</ref>
Her debut album, ''Teenage Fever'', was released on August 14, 2020.<ref name= TF>{{cite tweet|url=https://www.twitter.com/KAASHMYCHECKS/status/1292899252796944384|title=YOOOOOO , MY DEBUT ALBUM " TEENAGE FEVER " IS OUT THIS FRIDAY ( 8/14 )...|number=1292899252796944384|user=KAASHMYCHECKS|date=August 10, 2020|access-date=August 11, 2020}}</ref>

==Influences==
Woolen has cited [[Erykah Badu]], Drake, [[Mac Miller]], [[Isaiah Rashad]], [[SZA (singer)|SZA]], [[The Internet (band)|The Internet]], [[Flatbush Zombies]], and [[Frank Ocean]] as her musical influences.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Chesman |first1=Donna-Claire |title=Meet Kaash Paige, Dallas' Deeply Honest Rising R&B Star |url=https://djbooth.net/features/2019-11-22-kaash-paige-interview-parked-car-convos-discovery |website=DJBooth |access-date=June 3, 2020}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Paige identifies as bisexual.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.dallasobserver.com/music/def-jam-artist-kaash-paige-says-bi-sexuality-gives-her-an-advantage-11821309|title=Dallas' Kaash Paige, Newly Signed to Def Jam Label, Already Has a Fan in Kylie Jenner|work=[[Dallas Observer]]|last=Gonzalez|first=Alex|date=December 17, 2019|access-date=August 11, 2020}}</ref>
