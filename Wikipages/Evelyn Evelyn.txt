'''Evelyn Evelyn''' are a fictional musical duo created by [[Amanda Palmer]] (of [[The Dresden Dolls]]) and [[Jason Webley]]. According to the backstory provided by Palmer and Webley, the duo consists of [[conjoined twin]] sisters (aka "Eva" and "Lyn"), Evelyn and Evelyn Neville,<ref>{{cite web|date=January 2010|title=The Musical Chairs – Evelyn Evelyn|url=http://themusicalchairs.com/2010/01/20/evelyn-evelyn/|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110717014648/http://themusicalchairs.com/2010/01/20/evelyn-evelyn/|archive-date=July 17, 2011|work=The Musical Chairs}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://mvremix.com/rock_blogs/2010/02/02/ladies-and-gentlemen-critics-and-hipsters-we-are-proud-to-introduce-evelyn-evelyn-the-worlds-only-conjoined-twin-singer-songwriter-duo/|title=Ladies and Gentlemen! Critics and hipsters! We are proud to introduce “Evelyn Evelyn” – the world's only conjoined-twin singer-songwriter duo|website=MVRemix Rock|date=February 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://musicremedy.com/e/evelyn-evelyn/album/self-titled-7991.html|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120304064728/http://musicremedy.com/e/evelyn-evelyn/album/self-titled-7991.html|archive-date=March 4, 2012|url-status=dead|title=Evelyn Evelyn release self-titled album}}</ref><ref name="top40-charts.com">{{cite web|url=http://top40-charts.com/news/Pop-Rock/Critics-And-hipsters!-We-Are-Proud-To-Introduce-Evelyn-Evelyn/54224.html|title=Critics And hipsters! We Are Proud To Introduce 'Evelyn Evelyn'|work=Top40-Charts.com}}</ref> who were discovered in 2007 by Palmer and Webley. The twins are actually portrayed by Palmer and Webley,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.wired.com/underwire/2010/03/amanda-palmer/|title=Amanda Palmer Freaks Out With Evelyn Evelyn|work=Wired.com|date=2010}}</ref> dressed in connected garments.

==Discography==
In 2007, the band released a 3-song, colored 7" [[gramophone record|vinyl record]], along with a 6-track CD titled ''[[Evelyn Evelyn]]'', which was released in a limited edition of 1,111 copies on Jason's label (11 records); it quickly sold out. The package also contains Amanda and Jason's story of how they met the Evelyns, as well as the process of recording the vinyl, and a sticker of the two-headed elephant, Bimba & Kimba.

A full-length album, ''[[Evelyn Evelyn (album)|Evelyn Evelyn]]'' was released March 30, 2010, followed by a worldwide tour.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.jasonwebley.com/events.html|title=Jason Webley Tours|website=Jasonwebley.com}}</ref>
