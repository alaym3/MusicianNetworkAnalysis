'''Beast in Black''' is a Finnish-Greek-Hungarian [[Heavy metal music|heavy metal]] band that was founded in 2015 in [[Helsinki]] by guitarist and songwriter Anton Kabanen.

The musical influences of the band include [[Judas Priest]], [[Manowar]], [[W.A.S.P. (band)|W.A.S.P.]], [[Accept (band)|Accept]] and [[Black Sabbath]].<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.facebook.com/pg/beastinblackofficial/about/?ref=page_internal| title = Beast In Black Facebook info|website=Facebook.com| access-date = 11 November 2017}}</ref>

== History ==
Anton Kabanen founded the band in 2015 after he left his former band [[Battle Beast (band)|Battle Beast]] earlier in the same year.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://teamrock.com/feature/2017-11-20/new-band-of-the-week-beast-in-black|title=A New Band Of The Week: Beast In Black|website=Teamrock.com|access-date=2017-11-20}}</ref> Like Battle Beast, the band's name is a tribute to the Japanese [[manga]] and [[anime]] series ''[[Berserk (manga)|Berserk]]''. 

Beast in Black signed with the [[Nuclear Blast]] label and released the first album, ''Berserker'' in November 3, 2017, which received a very positive response worldwide.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.nuclearblast.com/en/music/band/discography/details/4882159.4882021.berserker.html|title=BERSERKER|website=Nuclearblast.com|access-date=2017-11-03}}</ref> The debut album ranked seventh on Finnish charts albums. In addition, ''Berserker'' also charted in Germany, United Kingdom, Sweden, Switzerland and France.<ref>{{cite web | url = http://kaaoszine.fi/beast-in-blackin-debyytti-menestyksekas-pelinavaus-levyn-listasijoitukset-julki/| title = Beast In Blackin debyytti menestyksekäs pelinavaus – levyn listasijoitukset julki| website=KaaosZine.fi| date = 17 November 2017| access-date = 17 November 2017}}</ref>

[[File:Yannis Papadopoulos.jpg|thumb|upright|Yannis Papadopoulos at [[Kuopio Rock]] 2019]]
On February 7, 2018 it was announced that Atte Palokangas will replace Sami Hänninen as the permanent drummer.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://bravewords.com/news/beast-in-black-introduce-new-drummer-atte-palokangas-video|title=BEAST IN BLACK INTRODUCE NEW DRUMMER ATTE PALOKANGAS|website=Bravewords.com|access-date=2018-02-20}}</ref> The band includes the Greek vocalist Yannis Papadopoulos (ex-Wardrum), the Hungarian bassist Mate Molnar ([[Wisdom (band)|Wisdom]]) and [[Kasperi Heikkinen]] (former guitarist of bands as [[U.D.O.]], [[Amberian Dawn]] etc.). 

On March 21, 2018, it was announced that Beast in Black would be opening for [[Nightwish]] on the European leg of their [[Decades: World Tour]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hardwiredmagazine.com/beast-in-black-supporting-nightwish-european-decades-tour-2018/?lang=en|title=BEAST IN BLACK Supporting Nightwish on European DECADES tour|website=Hardwiredmagazine.com|access-date=2018-03-21}}</ref>

The band's second album, ''From Hell with Love'', was released on February 8, 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.nuclearblast.de/en/label/music/band/discography/details/5660426.4882021.from-hell-with-love.html|title=From Hell With Love Beast in Black | website=Nuclearblast.de | access-date = 30 November 2018}}</ref> After the release of the album, the band did their first headline tour in Europe with the Finnish industrial metal band [[Turmion Kätilöt]] as support.<ref>[https://www.nordicmetal.net/beast-in-black-announce-european-headline-tour-2019-with-turmion-katilot/ BEAST IN BLACK announce European headline tour 2019 with TURMION KÄTILÖT] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190412051847/https://www.nordicmetal.net/beast-in-black-announce-european-headline-tour-2019-with-turmion-katilot/ |date=2019-04-12 }}, Nordicmetal.net</ref>
