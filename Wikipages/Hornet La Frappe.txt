'''Hornet La Frappe''' ({{IPA-fr|ɔʁnɛt la fʁap}}) is a French rapper of Algerian descent from [[Epinay-sur-Seine]], [[Seine-Saint-Denis]], in the northern suburbs of Paris. He started rapping in 2012 and released his first mixtape ''Réussir ou Mounir'' in 2014 and the follow-up mixtape ''Nous-mêmes'' on 29 September 2017. "Gramme 2 Peuf" was his first big success as a single
