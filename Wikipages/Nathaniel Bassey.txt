'''Nathaniel Bassey''' is a Nigerian singer, pastor, trumpeter and gospel songwriter popularly known for his songs "Imela", "Onise Iyanu" and "Olowogbogboro."<ref name="thenationonlineng.net">{{cite web|date=3 October 2018|title=Nathaniel Bassey: A blessing in our time|url=https://thenationonlineng.net/nathaniel-bassey-a-blessing-in-our-time/|publisher=The Nation}}</ref> He attends [[Redeemed Christian Church of God|The Redeemed Christian Church Of God]].<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-02-02|title=Churches Attended by Nigerian Gospel Ministers|url=https://www.churchlist.ng/churches-attended-by-nigerian-gospel-ministers/|access-date=2020-08-27|website=churchlist.ng|language=en-US}}</ref>

== Early life and education ==
Bassey was born in [[Lagos]], Nigeria in 1981. He studied international relations and politics at the [[University of Lagos]] before moving to [[London]] to study [[politics]] thereafter. Conversely, he studied music at [[Middlesex University|Middlesex University Summer School]].<ref name="thenationonlineng.net" />

== Music career ==
Bassey started his musical career in the church where he joined the Rhodes Orchestra and played the trumpet for two years. Bassey was just an ordinary trumpeter until he composed a song at the visit to [[Stella Obasanjo]], the late wife of former president [[Olusegun Obasanjo]].<ref name="thenationonlineng.net" /> In 2018, Bassey was one of the leading [[wikt:artiste|artistes]] in the Nigerian Christian gathering - The Experience.<ref>{{cite web|title=Kirk Franklin, Travis Greene, Tope Alabi, others to headline The Experience 2018|url=https://punchng.com/kirk-franklin-travis-greene-tope-alabi-others-to-headline-the-experience-2018/|publisher=Punch Nigeria}}</ref> His debut album Elohim was recorded and mixed in Cape Town, [[South Africa]] in the year 2008. It has been described as a spiritual and artistic masterpiece with the hit track, "someone's knocking at the door," a soft-rock tune currently generating so much interest locally and internationally.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Profile|url=https://nathanielbassey.net/profile/|access-date=2020-12-18|website=nathanielbassey.net|language=en}}</ref>
