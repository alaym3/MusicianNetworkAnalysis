'''Peg Parnevik''' (born 3 September 1995) is a Swedish-American singer, songwriter, and television personality, known for starring in the Swedish reality show ''[[Parneviks]]'' mainly broadcast from their home in [[Jupiter, Florida]].

==Life and career==

===Early life===
Parnevik was born in [[Stockholm]] to professional golfer [[Jesper Parnevik]] and his wife Mia. She has three younger siblings; Penny, Philippa, and Phoenix and was raised in [[Jupiter, Florida]], in the United States. Her paternal grandfather is Swedish impersonator [[Bosse Parnevik]]. She attended [[Pine School|The Pine School]] in [[Hobe Sound, Florida|Hobe Sound]]. She spent her freshman year of college at [[Washington and Lee University]] in [[Lexington, Virginia]]. She then  enrolled at [[Savannah College of Art and Design]] in [[Savannah, Georgia]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/peg.parnevik|title=Peg Parnevik|accessdate=17 March 2016}}</ref>

===2015–present: ''Parneviks'' and music career===
In 2015, Parnevik began starring in the reality show ''[[Parneviks]]'' broadcast on the Swedish television channel [[TV3 (Sweden)|TV3]].<ref name="aftonbladet.se">{{cite web|url=http://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/tv/article21330668.ab|title=Mia Parnevik: "Finns väl ingen finare än min svärfar"|work=[[Aftonbladet]]|accessdate=20 February 2016|language=sv|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160302213831/http://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/tv/article21330668.ab|archive-date=2 March 2016|url-status=live}}</ref> The show features Parnevik and the rest of her family welcoming Swedish celebrity guests to stay at their [[Florida]] mansion. It has won numerous awards in Sweden, including a [[Kristallen]] in 2015 for Best Reality Series.<ref name="aftonbladet.se1">{{cite web|url=http://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/tv/article21334416.ab|title=Mia Parnevik: "Det är helt galet"|work=[[Aftonbladet]]|accessdate=20 February 2016|language=sv|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160302210418/http://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/tv/article21334416.ab|archive-date=2 March 2016|url-status=live}}</ref> Parnevik later signed to [[Sony Music Entertainment|Sony Music Sweden]] as a solo artist and released her debut single "[[Ain't No Saint (song)|Ain't No Saint]]" in March 2016.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.expressen.se/noje/peg-parneviks-nya-karriar---gor-musik/|title=Peg Parneviks nya karriär - gör musik|newspaper=Expressen|date=14 March 2016|access-date=18 March 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160317071024/http://www.expressen.se/noje/peg-parneviks-nya-karriar---gor-musik/|archive-date=17 March 2016|url-status=live}}</ref> On 3 June 2016, she released the single "We Are (Ziggy & Carola)".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mynewsdesk.com/se/sonybmg/pressreleases/we-are-ziggy-carola-ny-laat-fraan-peg-1427107|title=We Are (Ziggy & Carola) - ny låt från Peg!|publisher=My News Desk|language=Swedish|date=3 June 2016|accessdate=4 June 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160606181948/http://www.mynewsdesk.com/se/sonybmg/pressreleases/we-are-ziggy-carola-ny-laat-fraan-peg-1427107|archive-date=6 June 2016|url-status=live}}</ref>

Her song Ain't No Saint charted in second place on the Swedish singleschart.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.sydsvenskan.se/2016-03-24/topplistorna-peg-parnevik-ny-tvaa|title=Topplistorna: Peg Parnevik ny tvåa|newspaper=Sydsvenskan|accessdate=9 August 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160920191733/http://www.sydsvenskan.se/2016-03-24/topplistorna-peg-parnevik-ny-tvaa|archive-date=20 September 2016|url-status=live}}</ref> In August 2016 Parnevik participated in [[Allsång på Skansen]] broadcast on [[Sveriges Television|SVT]].<ref name="svt.se">{{cite web|url=http://www.svt.se/allsang/peg-parnevik-klar-for-allsang-pa-skansen-2016-det-kanns-som-en-drom|title=Peg Parnevik till Allsången: "Det känns som en dröm!"|publisher=svt.se|accessdate=9 August 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160809151948/http://www.svt.se/allsang/peg-parnevik-klar-for-allsang-pa-skansen-2016-det-kanns-som-en-drom|archive-date=9 August 2016|url-status=live}}</ref> She also had her own reality show called Peg på turné to be broadcast on TV3, a show that will follow her way through Sweden when she promotes her new songs.<ref name="svt.se"/>
