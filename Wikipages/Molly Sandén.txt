'''Molly My Marianne Sandén'''<ref name="ISWC"/> (born 3 July 1992), also known by her middle name '''My Marianne''', is a [[Sweden|Swedish]] pop singer and voice actress.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.swedishcharts.com/showitem.asp?interpret=Molly+Sand%E9n&titel=Unchained&cat=a|title=Unchained|publisher=Swedishcharts|year=2012|access-date=21 November 2014}}</ref> As a teenager, she represented Sweden in the [[Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2006|Junior Eurovision Song Contest]] in 2006 and participated in [[Melodifestivalen]] in [[Melodifestivalen 2009|2009]], [[Melodifestivalen 2012|2012]], and [[Melodifestivalen 2016|2016]]. Her sisters [[Frida Sandén|Frida]] and [[Mimmi Sandén|Mimmi]] are also singers.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-08-07|title=The saga of Molly Sandén: From Junior Eurovision to vocals for a movie|url=https://junioreurovision.tv/story/the-saga-of-molly-sanden|access-date=2021-03-23|website=junioreurovision.tv|language=en}}</ref> In 2018, Sandén released her third studio album, ''Större''. Her fourth studio album was released in early 2019, called ''Det bästa kanske inte hänt än.''<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.mynewsdesk.com/se/sonybmg/pressreleases/molly-sanden-slaepper-albumet-det-baesta-kanske-inte-haent-aen-2880274|title=Molly Sandén släpper albumet "Det bästa kanske inte hänt än"!|date=29 May 2019|website=[[Mynewsdesk]]|language=sv|access-date=26 March 2020}}</ref>

==Career==

===2006–2007: ''Junior Eurovision Song Contest''===
Sandén represented Sweden at the [[Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2006|Junior Eurovision Song Contest]] in 2006, singing "Det finaste någon kan få".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.svt.se/melodifestivalen/premiar-nu-borjar-lilla-melodifestivalen|title=Premiär – nu börjar Lilla Melodifestivalen|publisher=Sveriges Television|access-date=24 February 2016|language=sv|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303201039/http://www.svt.se/melodifestivalen/premiar-nu-borjar-lilla-melodifestivalen|archive-date=3 March 2016|url-status=live}}</ref> She came third, which remains Sweden's best result.<ref name="aftonbladet.se">{{cite web|url=http://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/tv/article11008115.ab|title=Läsarna: Du var bäst, Molly|work=Aftonbladet|access-date=24 February 2016|language=sv|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304025728/http://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/tv/article11008115.ab|archive-date=4 March 2016|url-status=live}}</ref> She appeared in [[Sveriges Television|SVT]]'s New Years' celebrations on 31 December 2006, which was broadcast live from [[Skansen]]. She also won the talent competition, Stellar Shots.<ref name="bloggplatsen.se" /> She performed a [[duet]] with [[Magnus Carlsson]] in the song "Julens tid är här" ("Christmas time is here"), on his [[Christmas Music|Christmas album]]'' [[Spår i snön]]'' in 2006.<ref name="bloggplatsen.se" />

Sandén also met [[Markoolio]] and sang with him during this period, on his new album called ''Snow at Christmas rock''.<ref name="bloggplatsen.se" /> In summer 2007, Sandén participated in [[Diggiloo]] along with [[Lasse Holm]], [[Linda Bengtzing]], [[Lotta Engberg]], [[Tony Roche]], [[Charlotte Perrelli]], Thomas Pettersson, [[Jan Johansen (singer)|Jan Johansen]], [[Niklas Andersson]], [[Lasse Berghagen]], [[Benjamin Wahlgren]] and Magnus Johansson.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.gp.se/kulturnoje/1.71818-benjamin-pa-diggiloo-turnen?m=print|title=Benjamin på Diggiloo-turnén|first=Magnus|last=Vasell|work=[[Göteborgs-Posten]]|access-date=24 February 2016|language=sv|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304224721/http://www.gp.se/kulturnoje/1.71818-benjamin-pa-diggiloo-turnen?m=print|archive-date=4 March 2016|url-status=dead}}</ref>

She sang a duet with [[Ola Svensson]] on [[You Are the Music in Me#Molly Sandén and Ola Svensson version|a Swedish version]] of "[[You Are the Music in Me]]" from the [[Disney]] film ''[[High School Musical 2]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/se/album/you-are-music-in-me-du-ar/id716773476|title=You Are the Music in Me (Du Är Musiken I Mig) – Single|publisher=iTunes Store|access-date=24 February 2016|language=sv|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160305222232/https://itunes.apple.com/se/album/you-are-music-in-me-du-ar/id716773476|archive-date=5 March 2016|url-status=live}}</ref> They also sang in the [[Lilla Melodifestivalen]] final ahead of the [[Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2007|Junior Eurovision Song Contest]] in 2007. The [[Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2006|previous year]], Sandén was chosen to present the Swedish votes during the contest.<ref name="bloggplatsen.se" />

===2008–2016: ''Melodifestivalen'', and ''Like No One's Watching''===
[[File:Molly Sandén.jpg|thumb|right|Sandén in 2008]]

Sandén appeared on ''[[Så ska det låta]]'' on 11 April 2008 with [[Magnus Bäcklund (singer)|Magnus Bäcklund]].<ref name="bloggplatsen.se" /> In summer 2008, she participated again in Diggiloo with Lasse Holm, Linda Bengtzing, Lotta Engberg, Thomas Pettersson, [[Måns Zelmerlöw]], [[Nanne Grönvall]], Stefan and Kim and Magnus Johansson.<ref name="bloggplatsen.se" /> A compilation CD was released and included her performances of "[[Hallelujah (Leonard Cohen song)|Hallelujah]]" and "Gabriellas song".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://lyricstranslate.com/en/Molly-Sanden-Hallelujah-lyrics.html|title=Molly Sandén – Hallelujah lyrics|website=Lyrics Translate|access-date=24 February 2016|language=sv|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303195233/http://lyricstranslate.com/en/Molly-Sanden-Hallelujah-lyrics.html|archive-date=3 March 2016|url-status=live}}</ref> Her [[cover version|cover]] of [[Dea Norberg]]'s song "Den underbara dagen du kommer hem" was used in advertisements for the fast food chain [[Sibylla (fast food)|Sibylla]] in 2008.<ref name="bloggplatsen.se" />

Also in 2008, Sandén participated with [[Janne Schaffer]], Jan Johansen, Johan Boding and [[Sara Löfgren]] in ''Sun, Wind & Water'', a tribute to [[Ted Gärdestad]].<ref name="bloggplatsen.se" /> The same year, she and Brandur released a Swedish version of the song "[[Right Here, Right Now (High School Musical song)|Right Here, Right Now]]" from ''[[High School Musical 3]]''.<ref name="bloggplatsen.se" /> She participated in the celebrity dancing competition ''[[Let's Dance (Swedish TV series)|Let's Dance]]'' in 2010, which was broadcast on [[TV4 (Sweden)|TV4]]; she was placed fourth.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.expressen.se/noje/lets-dance/molly-sanden-klart-jag-ar-kar---han-ar-varldens-finaste/|title=Molly Sandén: "Klart jag är kär – han är världens finaste"|work=Expressen|access-date=25 February 2016|language=sv|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160305071505/http://www.expressen.se/noje/lets-dance/molly-sanden-klart-jag-ar-kar---han-ar-varldens-finaste/|archive-date=5 March 2016|url-status=live}}</ref>

In the [[Melodifestivalen|2009 Melodifestivalen]], Sandén qualified for the grand final with her song "Så vill stjärnorna" but finished last with only two points, which were awarded by the international jury.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.svt.se/melodifestivalen/artister/2016/molly-sanden/molly-sanden-vinner-garna-melodifestivalen-2016-inte-vagat-tanka-pa-det-tidigare|title=Molly Sandén vinner gärna Melodifestivalen 2016: 'Inte vågat tänka på det tidigare'|publisher=Sveriges Television|access-date=24 February 2016|language=sv|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303235715/http://www.svt.se/melodifestivalen/artister/2016/molly-sanden/molly-sanden-vinner-garna-melodifestivalen-2016-inte-vagat-tanka-pa-det-tidigare|archive-date=3 March 2016|url-status=live}}</ref><ref name="skd.se">{{cite web|url=http://www.skd.se/2012/05/22/storpublik-till-kalaset/|title=Storpublik till kalaset|work=Skånska Dagbladet|access-date=24 February 2016|language=sv|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304083036/http://www.skd.se/2012/05/22/storpublik-till-kalaset/|archive-date=4 March 2016|url-status=dead}}</ref>

Sandén entered the Melodifestivalen again in [[Melodifestivalen 2012|2012]], again qualifying for the final with her song "[[Why Am I Crying]]", for which she finished in fifth place.<ref name="skd.se" /> In August 2013, Sandén announced the premiere of her online clothing brand, 'My by Molly'.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://blogg.veckorevyn.com/mollysanden/2013/08/13/my-by-molly-premiar/ |title=My by Molly premiär |date=13 August 2013 |work=[[Veckorevyn]] |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140424035718/http://blogg.veckorevyn.com/mollysanden/2013/08/13/my-by-molly-premiar/ |archive-date=24 April 2014|language=sv}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://mybymolly.com/ |title=My by Molly |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130825185851/http://mybymolly.com/ |archive-date=25 August 2013|language=sv}}</ref>

She released the EP titled ''Like No One's Watching'', on 12 June 2015. It includes the hit singles "Freak", "Phoenix" and "Like No One’s Watching".

In November 2015, it was announced that Sandén was to participate in the Melodifestivalen for a third time in [[Melodifestivalen 2016]].<ref name="svt.se" /> She performed in the fourth heat in [[Gävle]] on 27 February with the song "Youniverse", which she co-wrote with [[Danny Saucedo]] and John Alexis.<ref name="svt.se" /> She qualified from the semi-final to the final on 12 March.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.svd.se/just-nu-pampigt-nummer-storfavorit-i-gavle/om/kultur|title=Favorit gick vidare vidare i Melodifestivalen|date=27 February 2016|work=[[Svenska Dagbladet]]|language=sv|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303223836/http://www.svd.se/just-nu-pampigt-nummer-storfavorit-i-gavle/om/kultur|archive-date=3 March 2016|access-date=27 February 2016|agency=[[Tidningarnas Telegrambyrå]]}}</ref> In the final, she placed sixth with the juries and televoting and also placed sixth overall.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.eurovision.tv/page/news?id=frans_wins_melodifestivalen_in_sweden|title=Frans wins Melodifestivalen in Sweden|publisher=EBU|last=Escudero|first=Victor M.|date=12 March 2016|access-date=12 March 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160315032335/http://www.eurovision.tv/page/news?id=frans_wins_melodifestivalen_in_sweden|archive-date=15 March 2016|url-status=live}}</ref>

===2017–2019: ''Större'', and ''Det bästa kanske inte hänt än''===
Sandén released the studio album ''Större'' through Sony on 27 April 2018.  It contains six singles. It peaked at number four in Sweden.

Sandén's fourth studio album, ''Det bästa kanske inte hänt än,'' was released on 29 May 2019 under the Sony label. The album contains nine tracks, including two singles and one promotional single. The album peaked at number two on the [[Sverigetopplistan|Swedish album chart]].<ref name="SWE" />

===2020–present: ''Eurovision Song Contest: The Story of Fire Saga'' and ''Dom ska veta''===
In the [[Netflix]] musical film ''[[Eurovision Song Contest: The Story of Fire Saga]]'', released in 2020, Sandén lent her voice to all songs performed by the character Sigrit Ericksdóttir, played by [[Rachel McAdams]] in the film. The film's [[soundtrack album]], on which Sandén is heavily featured, was nominated for [[Grammy Award for Best Compilation Soundtrack for Visual Media|Best Compilation Soundtrack For Visual Media]] at the [[63rd Annual Grammy Awards]]. The song "[[Husavik (song)|Husavik (My Hometown)]]" was nominated for [[Academy Award for Best Original Song|Best Original Song]] at the [[93rd Academy Awards]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://wiwibloggs.com/2021/04/17/molly-sanden-sing-husavik-oscars-pre-show/264362/|title=Molly Sandén will sing from Husavik during the Oscars pre-show|date=17 April 2021|website=wiwibloggs|accessdate=23 April 2021}}</ref> ''Dom ska veta'', Sandén's third studio album in Swedish, was released on 7 May 2021 and was preceded by three singles: "Jag mår bra nu" with [[Newkid]], "Kärlek slutar alltid med bråk", and "Nån annan nu".<ref>{{Cite press release|url=https://www.mynewsdesk.com/se/sonybmg/pressreleases/molly-sanden-slaepper-singeln-kaerlek-slutar-alltid-med-braak-fraan-kommande-albumet-dom-ska-veta-3064750|title=Molly Sandén släpper singeln 'Kärlek slutar alltid med bråk' från kommande albumet 'Dom ska veta'|date=22 January 2021|publisher=[[Sony Music]]|agency=[[Mynewsdesk]]|language=sv|trans-title=Molly Sandén releases the single "Kärlek slutar alltid med bråk" from the upcoming album ''Dom ska veta''|access-date=25 April 2021}}</ref><ref>{{Cite press release|url=https://www.mynewsdesk.com/se/sonybmg/pressreleases/molly-sanden-slaepper-singeln-naan-annan-nu-och-releasedatum-foer-kommande-albumet-dom-ska-veta-3083083|title=Molly Sandén släpper singeln ”Nån annan nu” och releasedatum för kommande albumet ”DOM SKA VETA”|date=26 March 2021|publisher=[[Sony Music]]|agency=[[Mynewsdesk]]|language=sv|trans-title=Molly Sandén releases the single "Nån annan nu" and the release date for the upcoming album ''Dom ska veta''|access-date=25 April 2021}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Sandén dated [[Eric Saade]] from 2007 to 2012. Sandén was in a relationship with [[Danny Saucedo]] from February 2013 to 2019, and they were formerly engaged.<ref name="expressen.se">{{cite web|url=http://www.expressen.se/noje/molly-danny-stottade-mig--efter-eric-saade/|title=Molly: Danny stöttade mig – efter Eric Saade|work=Expressen|access-date=24 February 2016|language=sv|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304152523/http://www.expressen.se/noje/molly-danny-stottade-mig--efter-eric-saade/|archive-date=4 March 2016|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.posh24.se/eric_saade/eric_saade_och_molly_har_forlovat_sig|title=Eric Saade och Molly har förlovat sig!|work=Posh24|access-date=24 February 2016|language=sv|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304200550/http://www.posh24.se/eric_saade/eric_saade_och_molly_har_forlovat_sig|archive-date=4 March 2016|url-status=live}}</ref>

On 28 September 2012, she announced that she had been diagnosed with [[type 1 diabetes]] after collapsing while on a trip to [[Barcelona]], Spain.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://veckorevyn.com/kandis/min-farmor-ringde-och-fragade-om-jag-var-med-barn/|title=Molly Sandén: 'Min farmor ringde och frågade om jag var med barn.'|work=[[Veckorevyn]]|access-date=24 February 2016|language=sv|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304071314/http://veckorevyn.com/kandis/min-farmor-ringde-och-fragade-om-jag-var-med-barn/|archive-date=4 March 2016|url-status=live}}</ref>
