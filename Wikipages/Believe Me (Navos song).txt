"'''Believe Me'''" is a song by British producer Navos. It was released on 22 January 2021, via [[Island Records]]. The song went viral on [[TikTok]]<ref>https://www.tiktok.com/music/Believe-Me-6925124562560485378</ref> and reached number 23 on the UK Singles Chart.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.officialcharts.com/artist/59335/navos/|title=Navos Chart History|publisher=[[Official Charts Company]]|access-date=17 November 2021}}</ref>

==Music video==
An accompanying music video was released on 19 March 2021.
