'''TWRP''' (pronounced ''twerp''), formerly known as '''Tupper Ware Remix Party''', is a Canadian [[Rock music|rock]] band originally from [[Halifax, Nova Scotia]]. Initially founded in 2007,<ref name=":0" /> the band moved to [[Toronto]] in 2011 with a change in members and musical direction. Their lineup consists of keyboardist and vocalist Doctor Sung, guitarist Lord Phobos, bassist Commander Meouch, and drummer Havve Hogan.

The band always performs in full costume, with their faces and real names unknown; they also have a fictional band history, involving Doctor Sung finding his bandmates through space and time.<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=https://noisey.vice.com/en_ca/article/6xzqg7/tupper-ware-remix-party-told-us-about-the-future-of-music|title=Tupper Ware Remix Party Told Us What the Future is Going to Be Like|date=2015-01-19|work=Noisey|access-date=2018-07-16|language=en-ca}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.scope-mag.com/2015/08/tupper-ware-remix-partys-epic-quest-to-annihilate-boredom/|title=Tupper Ware Remix Party's Epic Quest to Annihilate Boredom {{!}} SCOPE|website=www.scope-mag.com|language=en-US|access-date=2018-06-11}}</ref> TWRP released their first album, ''Poised to Dominate'', in 2010. It was followed by
''ladyworld'' (2017), ''Together Through Time'' (2018), ''Return to Wherever'' (2019), ''Over the Top'' (2020),<ref>{{cite tweet|number=1299014052606246912|user=TWRPband|title=OVER THE TOPA new album from TWRPReleasing September 25th, 2020<!-- full text of tweet that Twitter returned to the bot (excluding links) added by TweetCiteBot. This may be better truncated or may need expanding (TW limits responses to 140 characters) or case changes. --> |date=27 August 2020}}</ref> and ''New & Improved'' (2021). They have also released six [[Extended play|EP]]s.

They are also known for their collaborations with [[Ninja Sex Party]], having become their [[opening act]] and [[backup band|backing band]] in 2015 on both live performances and studio releases, starting with the album ''[[Under the Covers (Ninja Sex Party album)|Under the Covers]]''.<ref name="UnderCoversII">{{cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/tupperwareremixparty/posts/10154920680600805|title=Ninja Sex Party released the official track list to Under The Covers 2!|publisher=Tupper Ware Remix Party's official Facebook|accessdate=9 September 2017|date=2 July 2017}}</ref> They are also the backing band on ''[[The TryForce]]'', the third album of [[Starbomb]], another band featuring the members of Ninja Sex Party.<ref name="Starbomb">{{Cite news|url=https://twitter.com/ninjasexparty/status/993914342415495168|title=Ninja Sex Party on Twitter|work=Twitter|access-date=2018-05-18|language=en}}</ref>

On September 17, 2018, TWRP appeared on ''[[Conan (talk show)|Conan]]'' as the backing band for Ninja Sex Party, in a clean version of "[[Danny Don't You Know]]".<ref>{{Cite web|title=Ninja Sex Party Delivers Eccentric 'Danny Don't You Know' Performance On 'Conan': Watch|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/television/8475752/ninja-sex-party-conan-performance-video|access-date=2021-07-03|website=Billboard|language=en}}</ref>
