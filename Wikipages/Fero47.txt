'''Ferhat Tuncel''' (born 16 May 1998), known professionally as '''Fero47''', is a German rapper and songwriter of [[Kurds|Kurdish]] origin. Born in [[Turkey]] he moved to [[Germany]] at a young age, where he began posting musical videos on social media. In 2019, he signed to [[Epic Records]] and released his debut single "Jaja", which has reached at top 10 in the [[GfK Entertainment charts|German singles chart]]. Fero released his debut album, ''47'' in late 2019.

==Early life==
His family, of [[Kurds|Kurdish]] descent, emigrated from [[Turkey]] to Germany when he was a child.<ref name="Dewezet">{{cite web|url=https://www.dewezet.de/region/bad-pyrmont_artikel,-fero47-veroeffentlicht-album-und-widmet-pyrmont-einen-song-_arid,2592482.html|title=Fero47 veröffentlicht Album - und widmet Pyrmont einen Song|work=Dewezet|date=13 December 2019|accessdate=2 February 2021}}</ref> Tuncel grew up in [[Bad Pyrmont]] with his seven siblings.<ref name="Dewezet" /> In 2015 he moved to [[Frankfurt]] to do an apprenticeship as a banker, which he did not finish.<ref name="Dewezet" />

At the age of 15, Fero began recording videos of himself rapping, which he uploaded first on [[Facebook]] and later on [[Instagram]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.vice.com/de/article/9kpwyy/deutschrap-newcomer-2019-mero-fero47-sero-el-mero-analyse|title=Fero, Mero, Sero el Mero – Eine kurze Analyse der Instagram-Rapper, die gerade Deutschrap fluten|work=[[Vice Media]]|date=2 November 2018|accessdate=2 February 2021}}</ref> By 2018 he reached 150,000 followers on Instagram.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://raptastisch.net/2019/01/03/fast-gestorben-video-von-feros-autounfall-veroeffentlicht/|title=Fast gestorben – Video von Feros Autounfall veröffentlicht|work=raptastisch.net|date=3 January 2019|accessdate=2 February 2021}}</ref> The 47 in his stage name refers to the [[Vehicle registration plates of Turkey|vehicle registration plate of Mardin]], where he was born.<ref name="Dewezet" /> He has said that he is following his father's footsteps and it is important to him that the people who listen to his music also know who he is and where he come from.<ref name="praisemag" />

==Career==
In December 2018, Fero announced the release of his debut single, which however was postponed by a few weeks because of a car accident he was involved in together with his manager.<ref name="Dewezet" /> The debut single, "Jaja" was released in January 2019 and reached number eight in the [[GfK Entertainment charts|German singles chart]].<ref name="GER" /> The single [[Sampling (music)|samples]] [[Justin Timberlake]]'s "[[Cry Me a River (Justin Timberlake song)|Cry Me a River]]" and was produced by SiNCH and Typhoon.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://musikfreitag.com/2019/01/13/neue-musik-single-instant-grat-neuerscheinungen-11-01-2019/|title=Neue Musik: Single & Instant-Grat Neuerscheinungen 11.01.2019|work=|date=14 January 2019|accessdate=2 February 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://beatkings.de/2020/01/sinch-typhoon-making-of-fero47-jajavideo/|title=Sinch & Typhoon - Making of Fero47 “JaJa” (Video)|work=BeatKings.de|accessdate=2 February 2021}}</ref> After signing to [[Epic Records]], he released his second single, "Puerto Rico", which has peaked at number seven in the German chart.<ref name="Dewezet" /><ref name="GER" /> In December 2019, Fero's debut album, ''47'' was released.<ref name="Dewezet" />

==Artistry==
Fero, who has expressed pride in his Kurdish origin, often uses [[Kurdish music]] elements in his songs; "I just try to make sure that not too much of it is built into the music, otherwise it won't have so much to do with [[German hip hop|German rap]] in the end".<ref name="praisemag">{{cite web|url=https://www.praisemag.com/2019/11/08/fero47-interview/|title=Fero47 Interview|work=Praise Mag|date=8 November 2019|accessdate=6 February 2021}}</ref> The [[larynx]] has a special role in the voiced formation of sounds. [[laut.de]] described his voice as "distinctive", saying it is "somewhere between [[Bausa (rapper)|Bausa]] and [[Casper (rapper)|Casper]]".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.laut.de/News/Mero,-Loredana-Co-Zum-Rap-Ruhm-via-Instagram-12-04-2019-15828/Seite-3|title=Mero, Loredana & Co: Zum Rap-Ruhm via Instagram|work=[[laut.de]]|date=12 April 2019|accessdate=5 February 2021}}</ref>
