'''Nir Tibor''' ({{lang-he|ניר טיבור}}; born 18 June 1993), known professionally by his stage name '''Dennis Lloyd''', is an [[Israel]]i musician, [[Record producer|producer]], singer, songwriter, and multi-instrumentalist. He is best known for his 2016 hit single, "[[Nevermind (Dennis Lloyd song)|Nevermind]]", which charted in several countries.

==Early life and education==
Nir Tibor was born<ref name="AMBio">{{cite web |url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/dennis-lloyd-mn0003616306/biography |title=Dennis Lloyd - Artist Biography by Andy Kellman| work = AllMusic |access-date=30 June 2018}}</ref> in [[Tel Aviv]], Israel, to a family of [[Jewish]] descent. He grew up largely in [[Ramat Gan]]. Lloyd learned how to play the trumpet at age 8 and taught himself to play the guitar at 13. He attended the Thelma Yellin High School of the Arts in [[Givatayim]]. At 18, he was [[Conscription in Israel|conscripted]] into the [[Israeli Navy]] and served as a combat sailor for three years.<ref name="TO">{{cite news | last=Bonné| first=Noa| title =International musician, Dennis Lloyd, is actually an Israeli marketing genius| work = [[Time Out (magazine)|Time Out]]| date =9 November 2017 | url =https://www.timeout.com/israel/blog/international-musician-dennis-lloyd-is-actually-an-israeli-marketing-genius-110917| access-date =30 June 2018}}</ref><ref name="VFI">{{cite news | last=Cotugno| first=Ferdinando| title =Como Ho Scoperto di Essere un Fenomeno| work = Vanity Fair Italy| language=it| date =23 August 2017 | url =https://www.pressreader.com/italy/vanity-fair-italy/20170823/283811443764329| access-date =30 June 2018}}</ref>

==Career==
Lloyd released his first single, "Playa (Say That)", in mid-2015. Toward the end of 2015, he moved to [[Bangkok]], Thailand, where he stayed for a year. During his time in Bangkok, Lloyd focused exclusively on music and wrote 35 songs.<ref name="Ilsussi">{{cite news | last=Barraco| first=Morgan K.| title =Dennis Lloyd/ "Nevermind è dedicata alla mia ex" (Che tempo che fa)|language=it | work = Ilsussi Diario| date =5 November 2017 | url name="Officiel">{{cite news | last=Pisacane| first=Giovanna| title =talkingwith Dennis Lloyd|language=it | work = L'Officiel| date =20 November 2017 | url =https://www.lofficielitalia.com/musica/%23talkingwith-dennis-lloyd| access-date =30 June 2018}}</ref> After his year in Bangkok, Lloyd returned to Israel where he began releasing more music including the songs, "Snow White" and "Nevermind (Alright)".<ref name="Way">{{cite news | last=D'Antonio| first=Christian| title =Dennis Lloyd: "Adesso la mia musica va anche in Israele"|language=it | work=The Way | date =13 November 2017 | url =http://www.thewaymagazine.it/leisure/dennis-lloyd-adesso-la-mia-musica-va-anche-in-israele/?doing_wp_cron=1525336556.1321640014648437500000| access-date =30 June 2018}}</ref><ref name="RTL">{{cite news | title =Dennis Lloyd, fenomeno pop con "Nevermind" |language=it | publisher =RTL| date =13 July 2017 | url =https://www.rtl.it/notizie/articoli/dennis-lloyd-fenomeno-pop-con-nevermind/| access-date =30 June 2018}}</ref> Lloyd remixed the latter song and released it as "Nevermind" in the winter of 2016.<ref name="TO"/><ref name="VFI"/> The remixed version quickly garnered success on [[Spotify]], eventually peaking at number 4 on the Spotify Global Viral chart.<ref name="RTL"/><ref name="Aska">{{cite news | title =Dennis Lloyd, parte dall'Italia il MTFKR Tour: a maggio con tre live|language= it| publisher =Askanews| date =13 March 2018 | url more than 500 million streams on Spotify and over 1.5 million [[Shazam (application)|Shazams]].<ref name="Spot">{{cite web |url=https://open.spotify.com/artist/3EOEK57CV77D4ovYVcmiyt |title=Dennis Lloyd| publisher = Spotify |access-date=30 June 2018}}</ref><ref name="Shazam">{{cite web |url=https://www.shazam.com/artist/41239934/dennis-lloyd |title=Dennis Lloyd| publisher = Shazam |access-date=30 June 2018}}</ref> In June 2018, ''[[Time (magazine)|Time]]'' listed it as one of the "songs of summer".<ref name="Time">{{cite news | last=Bruner| first=Raisa| title =Here Are Our Predictions for the 2018 Song of Summer| work= [[Time (magazine)|Time]] | date =13 June 2018 | url =http://time.com/5303790/songs-of-summer-2018/| access-date =30 June 2018}}</ref>

In June 2017, Lloyd was featured in a video on the German "Colors" [[YouTube]] channel, performing an acoustic version of his song "Leftovers". The video has accrued 43 million views.<ref name="TO"/><ref name="COLORS">{{cite AV media | people=Dennis Lloyd (performer) | date=1 June 2017 | title=Dennis Lloyd - Leftovers {{!}} A COLORS SHOW|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WYeAUpvWeI8 | publisher=COLORS}}</ref> In March 2018, Lloyd announced the MTFKR Tour that began in May 2018 in [[Italy]] with dates across Europe and North America.<ref name="Optima01">{{cite news | last=Del Prete| first=Cinzia| title =Dennis Lloyd in Italia nel 2018, in concerto a Torino, Milano e Roma: biglietti in prevendita su TicketOne|language=it | work = Optima Italia| date =12 March 2018 | url =http://www.optimaitalia.com/blog/2018/03/12/dennis-lloyd-in-italia-nel-2018-in-concerto-a-torino-milano-e-roma-biglietti-in-prevendita-su-ticketone/1029929| access-date =30 June 2018}}</ref><ref name="Promo">{{cite news | last=Meier| first=Sam| title =Dennis Lloyd Makes Noise With Trending Track 'Nevermind'| work = Promotion Music News| date =6 April 2018 | url =http://promotionmusicnews.com/dennis-lloyd| access-date =30 June 2018}}</ref>
The song "Nevermind" was featured for in the commercial for the release of the new BMW 3 Series in 2019. Lloyd performed "Nevermind" on ''[[Jimmy Kimmel Live!]]'' on July 24, 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8466990/dennis-lloyd-performs-nevermind-jimmy-kimmel-video|title=Dennis Lloyd Delivers Pulsing Performance of 'Nevermind' On 'Jimmy Kimmel': Watch|website=Billboard|access-date=2019-10-04}}</ref>

In February 2019, Lloyd released his single "[[Never Go Back (Dennis Lloyd song)|Never Go Back]]".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.complex.com/music/2019/02/premiere-dennis-lloyd-never-go-back|title=Premiere: Israeli Singer-Songwriter And Producer Dennis Lloyd Vows To "Never Go Back"|website=Complex|language=en|access-date=2019-10-04}}</ref> "Never Go Back" received praise by various publications including ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'', ''Time'' and ''[[Forbes]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8503398/dennis-lloyd-never-go-back-music-video|title=Dennis Lloyd Releases Love Story Follow-Up Music Video for 'Never Go Back': Watch|website=Billboard|access-date=2019-10-04}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://time.com/5529907/5-songs-you-need-to-listen-to-this-week-74/|title=5 Songs You Need to Listen to This Week|website=Time|language=en|access-date=2019-10-04}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/monicamercuri/2019/02/15/dennis-lloyd-talks-massive-success-of-nevermind-and-new-single-never-go-back/|title=Dennis Lloyd Talks Massive Success Of 'Nevermind' And New Single 'Never Go Back'|last=Mercuri|first=Monica|website=Forbes|language=en|access-date=2019-10-04}}</ref> This single would lead into the release of Lloyd's debut EP ''Exident'' on April 5, 2019. Lloyd shared music videos for EP tracks "Never Go Back" and "GFY".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8505708/dennis-lloyd-exident-ep-breakdown|title=Dennis Lloyd Breaks Down His Debut EP 'Exident': Exclusive|website=Billboard|access-date=2019-10-04}}</ref> Following this, Lloyd embarked on his sold-out Never Go Back tour across the United States, [[Canada]] and Australia, also performing at various music festivals including [[Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival|Coachella]], [[Governors Ball Music Festival|Governor's Ball]] and [[Montreux Jazz Festival]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.governorsballmusicfestival.com/artist/dennis-lloyd/|title=Dennis Lloyd|website=The Governors Ball Music Festival|language=en-US|access-date=2019-10-04}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8507847/dennis-lloyd-interview-coachella|title=Dennis Lloyd on Coachella Debut, Creating Outside the Typical Album Format: 'I Want to Tell a Story'|website=Billboard|access-date=2019-10-04}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.montreuxjazzfestival.com/en/artist/dennis-lloyd|title=Dennis Lloyd {{!}} Montreux Jazz Festival|website=www.montreuxjazzfestival.com|language=en|access-date=2019-10-04}}</ref>

In July 2019, Dennis featured on a [[Spotify Singles]] session, collaborating with guitarist [[Tom Morello]] to cover [[Audioslave]]'s "[[Like a Stone]]".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-news/tom-morello-audioslave-like-a-stone-dennis-lloyd-868385/|title=Hear Tom Morello Update Audioslave's 'Like a Stone' With Dennis Lloyd|last=Reed|first=Ryan|date=2019-08-07|website=Rolling Stone|language=en-US|access-date=2019-10-04}}</ref> On September 14, 2019, Lloyd released his song "Wild West", which was included on EA Sports' ''[[FIFA 20]]'' soundtrack.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.ea.com/en-gb/games/fifa/fifa-20/news/fifa-20-soundtrack-volta-football|title=FIFA 20 Soundtrack, featuring Major Lazer, Diplo, Skepta|date=2019-09-13|website=Electronic Arts Inc.|language=en|access-date=2019-10-04}}</ref>
