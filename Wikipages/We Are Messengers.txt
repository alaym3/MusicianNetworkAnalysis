'''We Are Messengers''' are originally from [[Monaghan]], Ireland, but have been based in the US since 2015.<ref>{{Cite web|title=We Are Messengers on K-LOVE|url=http://www.klove.com/music/artists/we-are-messengers/|access-date=10 June 2020|website=K-LOVE}}</ref> Their first studio album, ''[[We Are Messengers (album)|We Are Messengers]]'', was released in 2016 through [[Word Records]] and [[Curb Records]]. The album hit the top 10 on ''Billboard'' album charts and top 15 on the ''Billboard'' Heatseeker charts. The follow-up EP, ''Honest'', was released in 2019 and reached the top 10 of the ''Billboard'' Christian Albums chart.

==Background==

The band formed in [[Monaghan]], Ireland, in 2015. Current members include Darren Mulligan (lead vocalist), Kyle Williams (vocalist and guitarist), Drew Kerxton (drummer), and Raul Aguilar (bassist).<ref name="about-us">{{Cite web|url=https://wearemessengersmusic.com/pages/about-us|title=About us|website=We Are Messengers|accessdate=30 June 2021}}</ref>

The group's first studio album, ''[[We Are Messengers (album)|We Are Messengers]]'', was released on 22 April 2016 through [[Word Records]] and [[Curb Records]].<ref name="TCB Interview">{{cite web|url=http://wearemessengersmusic.com/news/we-are-messengers-announce-debut-self-titled-album-release-21451|title=We Are Messengers Announce Debut, Self-Titled Album Release!|last=|first=|date=16 January 2016|website=|publisher=Word Entertainment LLC|accessdate=18 March 2018}}</ref><ref name="NRT Review">{{cite web|first=Jonathan J.|last=Francesco|title=Heed the Messengers|url=http://www.newreleasetoday.com/userprofile_reviewssinglepost.php?review_id=23326&user_id=7472|publisher=New Release Today|accessdate=1 March 2016|date=22 January 2016}}</ref><ref name="CR Review">{{cite web |first=Tony |last=Cummings |title=Review: We Are Messengers – We Are Messengers |url=http://www.crossrhythms.co.uk/products/We_Are_Messengers/We_Are_Messengers/160083/ |publisher=[[Cross Rhythms]] |accessdate=6 April 2016 |date=5 April 2016}}</ref> This album peaked at No. 10 on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Top Christian Albums]] chart and hit No. 16 on the [[Top Heatseekers Albums]]. Their second EP, ''Honest'', released on 29 March 2019, charting at No. 7 on the Top Christian Albums chart<ref>{{Cite web|title=We Are Messengers|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/we-are-messengers/chart-history/ILL/song/1129320|access-date=10 June 2020|website=Billboard}}</ref> and No. 44 on the [[Top Album Sales]] chart.

The band has had five ''Billboard'' [[Christian Airplay]] top tens, including "Maybe It's Ok"<ref>{{cite web |title=We Are Messengers New Single "Maybe It's Ok" |url=http://www.thechristianbeat.org/index.php/new-music/5467-we-are-messengers-new-single-maybe-it-s-ok |website=The Christian Beat |accessdate=18 November 2018}}</ref> and "Magnify."<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/8500481/lauren-daigle-look-up-child-hits-top-10-hot-christian-songs|title=Lauren Daigle Adds Another Record, as 'Look Up Child' Hits Top 10 On Hot Christian Songs|website=Billboard.com|accessdate=1 March 2019|date=28 February 2019}}</ref>
