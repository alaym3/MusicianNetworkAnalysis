'''Robin Banks''' <ref>http://www.robinbanks.com/about/ {{Bare URL inline|date=May 2021}}</ref> is a TV presenter, narrator and [[Disk jockey#Radio DJs|radio DJ]] originally from [[Kilkenny]], [[Republic of Ireland|Ireland]].

==Career==
He was the [[narrator]] of the British/European version of the popular [[Discovery Channel]] show ''[[MythBusters]]'', from season 2 to the present day. He has worked as a reporter for the [[Bravo (UK TV channel)|Bravo]] television show ''Bravado''. He has also presented shows for the [[BBC]], [[Channel 4]], [[Sky1]], [[Sky Witness|Living]], on London's [[Kiss 100 London|Kiss 100]] and has reportedly presented several guest shows on [[Galaxy Radio|Galaxy FM]]. He has previously had radio shows on [[Radio Nova (Ireland)|Radio Nova]], [[Atlantic 252]], [[Virgin Radio]], [[Galaxy Scotland|Beat 106]] and [[Xfm]].

On 16 June 2008, Robin Banks joined [[Leicester]] radio station [[Leicester Sound]] to host the weekday 6{{nbsp}}am{{snd}}10{{nbsp}}am breakfast show, but was let go due to financial constraints.{{Citation needed|date= January 2012}}

On 1 September 2008, Banks appeared on ''[[Dragons' Den (UK)|Dragons' Den]]'', under his real name, Christian Richardson, to pitch for investment in the eco-friendly packaging suppliers [https://www.tinyboxcompany.co.uk/ Tiny Box Company] that he helped set up. He and his business partner Rachel Watkyn secured an investment of Â£60,000 (more than they asked for) from Dragons [[Theo Paphitis]] and [[Peter Jones (entrepreneur)|Peter Jones]].

On 7 March 2009, Robin Banks made a post on Discovery Channel UK forums revealing that he will shortly return as the series narrator for ''MythBusters''.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://community.discoverychannel.co.uk/eve/forums/a/tpc/f/95010646/m/324103744/p/2 |title=Please leave Robin banks as narrator on EU disc. /UPDATE> HE's BACK! - Topic Powered by Eve Community |publisher=Community.discoverychannel.co.uk |access-date=2009-10-15 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090901091646/http://community.discoverychannel.co.uk/eve/forums/a/tpc/f/95010646/m/324103744/p/2 |archive-date=1 September 2009 |df=dmy-all }}</ref> He mentioned that it would not be possible without fans supporting him, thus wanting him back. He has narrated ''MythBusters'' for 6 series in the UK, Europe and Asia.

From March 2010 he also narrates ''Dirty Jobs'' for Discovery Channel.{{Citation needed|date=October 2010}} Banks presented the weekday evening show for [[Orion Media]]'s network of West Midlands stations ([[BRMB]], [[Beacon Radio|Beacon]], [[Mercia (radio station)|Mercia]] and [[Wyvern (radio)|Wyvern]]). He left the company in July 2011.

Banks offers Radio Coaching (coaches worldwide), runs Radio Workshops and has consulted for a number of businesses.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.robinbanks.com/services/personal-coaching/|publisher=Robin Banks|access-date=2015-12-27|title=Personal Coaching}}</ref>{{primary source inline|date=December 2015}}

Between November 2012 and October 2013 he was the Programme Director at [[Star Radio North East]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://radiotoday.co.uk/2012/11/robin-banks-joins-star-radio-north-east/|title = Robin Banks joins Star Radio North East|date = 12 November 2012}}</ref> He has moved onto new projects after fulfilling his brief to revamp the station<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://radiotoday.co.uk/2013/02/and-the-winning-format-for-star-radio-is/|title = And the winning format for Star Radio is|date = 3 February 2013}}</ref> and improve its audience figures.<ref>https://media.info/radio/stations/star-radio/listening-figures {{Bare URL inline|date=May 2021}}</ref>

At the beginning of March 2014 he joined [[Reading 107 FM|Jack FM Berkshire]], the relaunched Reading 107, to front the breakfast show.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://radiotoday.co.uk/2014/02/banks-for-breakfast-at-jack-fm-berkshire/|title=Banks for breakfast at JACK fm Berkshire|access-date=2015-12-27|date=2014-02-13|first1=Roy|last1=Martin|publisher=[[Radio Today (website)|Radio Today]]}}</ref>

From September 2016, Banks is working as content director with Hi FM in [[Oman]], including presenting the weekday breakfast show. He is also responsible for all output.{{Citation needed|date=October 2020}}
