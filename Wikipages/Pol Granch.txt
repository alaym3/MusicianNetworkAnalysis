'''Pablo Grandjean''', better known as '''Pol Granch''' (April 4, 1998), is a Spanish-French singer-songwriter and actor.

== Career ==
In 2018, Granch participated in the talent contest ''[[Factor X (Spanish TV series)|Factor X España]]'' being one of the favourites since castings. During the contest, he defended his permanence at [[Laura Pausini]]'s team, performing songs like ''El sitio de mi recreo'' by [[Antonio Vega (singer)|Antonio Vega]], ''Pausa'' by Izal or ''La quiero a morir'' by [[Francis Cabrel]].<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|date=July 5, 2018|title=Las mejores actuaciones de Pol Granch, el ganador de 'Factor X' 2018|url=https://www.telecinco.es/factorx/mejores-actuaciones-Pol-Granch-Factor_0_2589825154.html|access-date=April 13, 2021|website=Telecinco}}</ref>

In 2019, his first solo single «Late» was released, release which came along with his first music video.<ref>{{Citation|title=Pol Granch – Late|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pOFMP1Ub6ic|language=en|access-date=July 1, 2021}}</ref> It was at this moment when he begun balancing his music career with songwriting and recording projects. His first [[Extended play|EP]], self-titled, was released on April 26, 2019l.<ref>{{Cite web|date=June 23, 2020|title=Hablamos con Pol Granch de su nuevo single, su próximo disco #TQC y del balance que hace desde el lanzamiento de 'Late' hasta hoy, un año después|url=https://los40.com/los40/2020/06/23/musica/1592922734_570550.html|access-date=April 13, 2021|publisher=Los40|language=es}}</ref>

In 2020, he published his debut studio album's lead single «Tengo que calmarme». The full album «Tengo que calmarme» was published on June 26, 2020. 

That same year, it was announced that he was jumping into acting, by becoming a part [[Netflix]] Spanish series ''[[Elite (TV series)|Élite]],'' for its fourth season, where he plays the role of Phillipe, a French prince new to the series' high school, Las Encinas.<ref>{{Cite web|date=April 12, 2020|title=Todo lo que debes saber sobre la cuarta temporada de Élite|url=https://www.rtve.es/playz/20210412/todo-debes-saber-sobre-cuarta-temporada-elite/2085486.shtml|access-date=April 13, 2021|publisher=RTVE|language=es}}</ref> The season was released on June 2021.<ref>{{Cite web|date=June 14, 2021|title='Élite': Conoce a Patrick, Ari, Mencía y Phillipe, los nuevos alumnos de la cuarta temporada|url=https://www.formulatv.com/videos/elite-conoce-patrick-ari-mencia-phillipe-23526/|access-date=June 16, 2021|website=Netflix|publisher=Formula TV|language=es}}</ref>

In 2021 the artist publishes his new single «Tiroteo», featuring Marc Seguí. Due to its success in Spain, time after a remix of the song, featuring [[Rauw Alejandro]] is released, and is also a big success. This year, Granch also releases «No pegamos» and «Lüky Charm», two non-album singles.
