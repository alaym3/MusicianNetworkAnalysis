'''Gaia Gozzi''', known as '''Gaia''' (born 29 September 1997), is an Italian singer and songwriter.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.theitaliantimes.it/spettacolo/gaia-gozzi-x-factor-biografia-eta-altezza-fidanzata_040420/|title=Gaia Gozzi: vince Amici 2020, chi è Gaia età altezza peso biografia|website=TheItalianTimes.it|date=4 April 2020|language=it|access-date=24 April 2020}}</ref>

She became famous for winning the nineteenth edition of the talent show ''[[Amici di Maria De Filippi]]'' in 2020,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.corriere.it/spettacoli/20_aprile_04/amici-2020-vincitore-cantante-gaia-battuto-ballerino-javier-3b079da2-75e4-11ea-856e-f9aa62c97d7a.shtml|title=Amici 2020, vince la cantante Gaia Battuto il ballerino Javier|first=Renato|last=Franco|publisher=[[Corriere della Sera]]|date=4 April 2020|language=it|access-date=24 April 2020}}</ref> and for being the runner-up at the [[X Factor (Italian season 10)|tenth edition]] of the Italian version of ''[[X Factor (Italian TV series)|X Factor]]'' in 2016.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rockol.it/recensioni-musicali/album/6756/gaia-new-dawns|title=L'EP di Gaia, la simil-Florence Welch finalista di X Factor|website=rockol.it|access-date=24 April 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.allmusicitalia.it/news/gaia-gozzi-genesi.html|title=Gaia Gozzi Genesi tracklist e autori del nuovo album (audio)|website=All Music Italia|date=20 March 2020|language=it|access-date=24 April 2020}}</ref>

== Biography and career ==
Gaia was born in [[Guastalla]] ([[Reggio Emilia]]), from a Brazilian mother and an Italian father; she currently lives in [[Viadana, Lombardy|Viadana]] ([[Mantua]]). In her songs she mixes Italian and [[Brazilian Portuguese]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Gaia Gozzi: vince Amici 2020, chi è Gaia età altezza peso biografia|url=https://www.theitaliantimes.it/spettacolo/gaia-gozzi-x-factor-biografia-eta-altezza-fidanzata_040420/|date=2020-04-04|website=TheItalianTimes.it|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-04-30}}</ref>

In 2016 she participated as a contestant in the [[X Factor (Italian season 10)|tenth edition]] of the TV format ''[[X Factor (Italian TV series)|X Factor]]'', becoming part of [[Fedez]]'s team, finishing in second place.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Gaia - X Factor 2016 {{!}} Sky Uno|url=https://xfactor.sky.it/xf10/concorrenti/gaiagozzi/|website=X Factor|access-date=2020-04-30}}</ref> She released her EP ''New Dawns'', containing the same-titled single, certified gold by FIMI for selling over 25,000 copies.<ref>{{Cite web|title=√ L'EP di Gaia, la simil-Florence Welch finalista di X Factor|url=https://www.rockol.it/recensioni-musicali/album/6756/gaia-new-dawns|last=s.r.l|first=Rockol com|website=Rockol|language=it|access-date=2020-04-30}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Certificazioni Singoli Digitali (week 02 / 2017)|url=https://www.fimi.it/top-of-the-music/archivio-certificazioni/certificazioni-singoli-digitali-week-02-2017.kl|website=www.fimi.it|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-04-30}}</ref> During 2017 she opened the concerts of [[Giorgia (singer)|Giorgia]]'s Oronero Tour and released the single "Fotogramas".<ref>{{Cite web|title=Gaia Gozzi apre l’Oronero tour di Giorgia tra manichini e cover a sorpresa: scaletta e video del concerto|url=https://www.optimagazine.com/2017/03/20/gaia-gozzi-apre-loronero-tour-di-giorgia-tra-manichini-e-cover-a-sorpresa-scaletta-e-video-del-concerto/622593|last=Tomao|first=Chiara|date=2017-03-20|website=Optimagazine: ultime news, video e notizie italiane e dal mondo|access-date=2020-04-30}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Torna GAIA con il singolo "Fotogramas"|url=https://www.allmusicitalia.it/news/gaia-fotogramas.html|date=2017-05-08|website=All Music Italia|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-04-30}}</ref>

In 2019 she was cast in the nineteenth edition of the talent show ''[[Amici di Maria De Filippi]]''. The singer wrote and composed several songs during the program, included in the album ''Genesi'', released on March 20, 2020 by [[Sony Music]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Gaia Gozzi Genesi tracklist e autori del nuovo album (audio)|url=https://www.allmusicitalia.it/news/gaia-gozzi-genesi.html|date=2020-03-20|website=All Music Italia|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-04-30}}</ref> The album debuted at number 15 in the [[FIMI]] chart, peaking the fifth position following the talent show singer's victory.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Classifiche - FIMI|url=https://www.fimi.it/top-of-the-music/classifiche.kl|website=www.fimi.it|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-04-30}}</ref> The promotional single "Chega", reached number 11 in the Italian Singles charts.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Classifiche - FIMI|url=https://www.fimi.it/top-of-the-music/classifiche.kl|website=www.fimi.it|language=it-IT|access-date=2020-04-30}}</ref> On April 22, she announced the reissue of the album in physical format, entitled ''Nuova Genesi''.<ref>{{Cite web|title=√ Gaia, torna ‘Genesi’ con nuove canzoni (e in formato fisico)|url=https://www.rockol.it/news-713272/gaia-torna-genesi-con-nuove-canzoni-e-in-formato-fisico|last=s.r.l|first=Rockol com|website=Rockol|language=it|access-date=2020-04-30}}</ref>

She competed at the [[Sanremo Music Festival 2021]] with the song "[[Cuore amaro]]", whitch was placed 19th in the grand final and was lately certified gold in Italy.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Digital|first=Di Redazione|date=2021-03-03|title=Gaia Gozzi fa due chiacchiere con Cosmo e racconta la sua Cuore Amaro per Sanremo 2021|url=https://www.cosmopolitan.com/it/star/a35699528/gaia-gozzi-sanremo-2021/|access-date=2021-03-21|website=Cosmopolitan|language=it-IT}}</ref> On 14 May 2021 was published the collaboration in Portuguese "Boca" with [[Sean Paul]], later certified golden disc by FIMI.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-06-29|title=Gaia e Sean Paul, il video di "Boca"|url=https://www.sorrisi.com/musica/canzoni/gaia-e-sean-paul-insieme-per-il-nuovo-singolo-boca/|access-date=2021-10-18|website=TV Sorrisi e Canzoni|language=it-IT}}</ref> On 1 October 2021 released "Nuvole di zanzare", a single that anticipates the second studio album ''Alma'', out on 29 October.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-10-01|title=Gaia: il nuovo singolo è "Nuvole di zanzare"|url=https://www.sorrisi.com/musica/canzoni/gaia-il-nuovo-singolo-e-nuvole-di-zanzare/|access-date=2021-10-18|website=TV Sorrisi e Canzoni|language=it-IT}}</ref>
