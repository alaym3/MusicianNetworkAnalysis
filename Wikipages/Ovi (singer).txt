'''Ovidio Guzman''' (born in Cuba on October 10 1995), better known by his stage name as '''Ovi''', is a Cuban singer-songwriter of [[Corrido]], [[Reggaeton|Reggaetón]] and [[Latin trap]].<ref name=":3">{{Cite web|last=Mtz|first=Alejandro Cruz|title=▷ Biografía de Ovi ◁ Edad, estatura, novia, cantante, de dónde es|url=https://www.laletrade.com/biografias/regueton/4100-ovi|access-date=2021-04-14|website=www.laletrade.com|language=es-es}}</ref><ref name=":4">{{Cite web|title=Biografía de Ovi|url=https://www.buenamusica.com/ovi/biografia|access-date=2021-04-14|website=www.buenamusica.com|language=es}}</ref>

== Biography ==
He was born in [[Cuba]] on October 10, 1995. Since he was little, he was attracted to music, because when he was in school he formed a musical group with a group of friends, but they never had the opportunity to record any single. In his native Cuba he studied at the University of Medical Sciences of Havana, but he stopped studying due to financial problems and because his true passion was music, that's when he decided to leave [[Cuba]] for [[Ecuador]] and for that he had to sell his house and some personal belongings to in this way undertake a new trip to the United States with the desire to start his musical career, his destination was [[Miami]].<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|last=LOS40|date=2020-10-05|title=OVI, La sensación de la música regional urbana|url=https://los40.com/los40/2020/10/05/los40urban/1601920572_501751.html|access-date=2021-04-14|website=LOS40|language=es}}</ref><ref name=":1">{{Cite web|title=OVI, la promesa cubana de la música que cruzó fronteras para cumplir sueños|url=https://es-us.noticias.yahoo.com/ovi-promesa-cubana-m%C3%BAsica-cruz%C3%B3-222100673.html|access-date=2021-04-14|website=es-us.noticias.yahoo.com|language=es-US}}</ref><ref name=":2" /> However, at the beginning of his arrival it did not go well for him since he did not have a job and could only survive as a kitchen assistant, after those times he ended up in drug addiction but he managed to recover, then he was in [[Arizona]] until he arrived in [[Los Angeles]] and He met the record label "Rancho Humilde", which opened the doors for him to be part of it.<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|date=2021-03-01|title=Ovi, el joven que vendió su casa en Cuba y cruzó fronteras para cumplir sus sueños en la música|url=https://www.cibercuba.com/noticias/2021-03-01-u198484-e198484-s27065-ovi-joven-vendio-su-casa-cuba-cruzo-fronteras-cumplir-sus|access-date=2021-04-14|website=CiberCuba|language=es}}</ref><ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1" />

He became known in the musical environment with the singles "Se me dio" and "Bailen" under the record label "Rancho humilde". But it was in 2019 when he began to have greater notoriety when he released the single "Cuando me ve" with the Mexican singer Adriel Favela.<ref name=":3" /><ref name=":4" /><ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-10-27|title=OVI, la promesa cubana de la música que cruzó fronteras para cumplir sueños|url=https://holanews.com/ovi-la-promesa-cubana-de-la-musica-que-cruzo-fronteras-para-cumplir-suenos-2/|access-date=2021-04-14|website=Hola News|language=en-GB}}</ref> Then a few months later, the premiere of "Yo ya lo enrolé" with Natanael Cano, which was a success in several Latin American countries, especially in [[Mexico]]. In May of the same year, he released the single "Nadie como tú" with Carolina Ross, which reached more than 4 million views on [[YouTube]] just a few days after its premiere. On March 19, 2020, the Mexican singer Natanael Cano premiered in collaboration with '' '''Ovi''' '' the single "Pacas Verdes" that in just one month got 20 million views on YouTube. The song also caught the attention of the singer [[Bad Bunny]], who congratulated them for the initiative to include the genre corridos tumbados.<ref name=":4" /> Finally, on April 20, Ovi released their first studio album called "Amen" that includes collaborations with Gera MX, Junior H and Natanael Cano. 

A month later, he released the song “Vengo de nada”, with Natanael Cano, Alemán and Big Soto,<ref>{{Cite web|title=Natanael Cano, Alemán, Ovi y Big Soto estrenan nueva colaboración|url=https://www.kebuena.com.mx/2020/natanael-cano-aleman-ovi-y-big-soto-estrenan-nueva-colaboracion-167921.html|access-date=2021-04-14|website=www.kebuena.com.mx}}</ref> which made it to the Hot Latin Songs Billboard chart in the United States. In October he released the album "Buen viaje", which includes songs produced by Teejay, Young Hollywood, Krizous and Junior H.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-10-03|title=Ovi lanza su nuevo disco “Buen Viaje”|url=https://reggaetonsinlimite.com/ovi-lanza-su-nuevo-disco-buen-viaje/|access-date=2021-04-14|website=ReggaetonSinLimite|language=en-US}}</ref> The album makes references to the movie "Scarface", the program "Caso cerrado" and the song "Umbrella" by [[Rihanna]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Redacción|first=La|date=2020-10-03|title=Cantante cubano Ovi estrena su electrizante álbum “Buen Viaje”|url=https://www.ultimasnoticias.com.do/2020/10/02/cantante-cubano-ovi-estrena-su-electrizante-album-buen-viaje/|access-date=2021-04-14|website=Últimas Noticias de la República Dominicana|language=es}}</ref>

In December 2020, he published his third studio album, “Las 3 torres”, in which Natanael Cano and Junior H participates.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-12-18|title=Natanael Cano, Junior H y OVI, nos traen su nuevo álbum: «Las 3 Torres»|url=https://radiohoy.cl/natanael-cano-junior-h-y-ovi-nos-traen-su-nuevo-album-las-3-torres/|access-date=2021-04-14|website=Radio Hoy|language=es}}</ref>
