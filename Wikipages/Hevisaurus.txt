'''Hevisaurus''' are a Finnish [[heavy metal music|heavy metal]] band that formed in 2009 with music aimed at children. The band members are known for performing in [[dinosaur]] costumes. Hevisaurus's debut concert was at the [[Elämä Lapselle]] charity concert on September 9, 2009. Their first tour started at the [[Tavastia Club]] in Helsinki on November 22, 2009.<ref>{{citation|url=http://www.sonybmg.fi/sonybmg/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=231044&Itemid=50 |title=Hevisauruksen kiertueen avaus oli lastenjuhlaa |publisher=sonybmg.fi |work=Sony BMG |date=2009-11-26 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160131140143/http://www.sonybmg.fi/sonybmg/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=231044&Itemid=50 |archive-date=2016-01-31 }}</ref>

==Biography==
Hevisaurus has released seven albums to date and the albums have sold over 170,000 copies alone in Finland. Localised versions of Hevisaurus’ albums can also be found in Spanish, Hungarian, Swedish and German.<ref>{{citation | url = http://heavysaurios.com/ | title = Heavysaurios | publisher = Heavysaurios | language = es }}</ref><ref>{{citation | url = http://www.klaava.fi/Musiikki/Hevisaurus_kiinnostaa_jo_Australiassa_asti | title = Hevisaurus kiinnostaa jo Australiassa asti | publisher = Klaava | language = fi }}</ref><ref>{{citation | url = http://hevisaurus.se/ | title = Hevisaurus | work = hevisaurus.se | date = 2014-10-25 | language = sv | access-date = 2015-01-27 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20141025114310/http://hevisaurus.se/ | archive-date = 2014-10-25 | url-status = dead }}</ref>

The album ”Hirmuliskojen Yö” (Night of the Dinosaurs) was the 2nd most sold album in Finland in 2010, when the band was rewarded with the best children album Emma (Finnish equivalent to Grammy).<ref>{{citation|url=http://www.sonymusic.fi/2014/09/12/lasten-suosikkiyhtye-hevisaurus-julkaisee-kokoelmalevyn-jurahevin-ikivihreat-ilmestyy-24-10/ |title=Hevisaurus julkaisee kokoelmalevyn Jurahevin Ikivihreät, Ilmestuu 24.10 |work=sonymusic.fi |publisher=Sony Music |date=2015-01-20 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141214172621/http://www.sonymusic.fi/2014/09/12/lasten-suosikkiyhtye-hevisaurus-julkaisee-kokoelmalevyn-jurahevin-ikivihreat-ilmestyy-24-10/ |archive-date=2014-12-14 }}</ref>

[[The Dudesons]] and Hevisaurus opened up an indoor amusement park to [[Oulu]]’s Super Park in April 2013.<ref>{{citation|url=http://www.superparkoulu.fi/fi/Aktiviteettipuisto/Hevisaurus-seikkailut |title=Hevisaurus-seikkailut |work=superparkoulu.fi |publisher=Super Park Oulu |date=2015-01-20 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150120143800/http://www.superparkoulu.fi/fi/Aktiviteettipuisto/Hevisaurus-seikkailut |archive-date=2015-01-20 }}</ref>
The band also has their own Hevisaurus game for [[Android (operating system)|Android]] and [[iOS]] devices. In the game, players must finish the levels while listening to the band’s songs. The application also includes mini-games like puzzles. The application was released in 2013.<ref>{{citation|url=http://www.sonymusic.fi/2013/11/07/lasten-suosikkiyhtye-hevisaurus-julkaisi-oman-pelin/ |title=Lasten suosikkiyhtye Hevisaurus julkaisi oman pelin |work=sonymusic.fi |publisher=Sony Music |date=2015-01-20 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150120143605/http://www.sonymusic.fi/2013/11/07/lasten-suosikkiyhtye-hevisaurus-julkaisi-oman-pelin/ |archive-date=2015-01-20 }}</ref>

Hevisaurus have their photo on the walls of [[Hard Rock Café]] and have also been listed in the Hard Rock Café’s international database of artists/bands. They are the second Finnish band, besides [[Amorphis]], who have received the honor.<ref>{{citation|url=http://www.sonymusic.fi/2014/10/23/lasten-suosikkiyhtye-hevisaurus-saa-kunniataulun-hard-rock-cafen-kokoelmiin/ |title=Lasten suosikkiyhtye Hevisaurus saa kunniataulun Hard Rock Cafen kokoelmiin |publisher=Sony Music |date=2015-01-20 |language=fi |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150120133737/http://www.sonymusic.fi/2014/10/23/lasten-suosikkiyhtye-hevisaurus-saa-kunniataulun-hard-rock-cafen-kokoelmiin/ |archive-date=2015-01-20 }}</ref>

==Origin story and characters==
According to legend, five dinosaur eggs made from metal survived the mass extinction some 65 million years ago in the mountain of wizards. In the year 2009, witches gathered at the same place. A giant lightning bolt hit the ground and simultaneously created ash and revealed the eggs. From the power of the witches’ chants, the eggs exploded open and five Hevisauri hatched.<ref>{{citation|url=http://www.hevisaurus.com/yhtye/ |title=Yhtye |publisher=Hevisaurus |language=fi |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130919164627/http://www.hevisaurus.com/yhtye/ |archive-date=September 19, 2013 }}</ref>

==Movie==
The Hevisaurus movie was produced by [[Solar Films]] and was filmed in the fall of 2014 and was released in the end of 2015. The movie stars the beloved Hevisaurus characters, as well as famous and rising Finnish actors.<ref>{{citation|url=http://www.sonymusic.fi/2014/09/16/hevisaurus-elokuvaa-tahdittavat-mm-taneli-makela-kari-hietalahti-timo-kahilainen-heikki-hela-sanna-stellan-ja-eija-vilpas/ |title=Hevisauruks elokuva |work=sonymusic.fi |publisher=Sony Music |date=2015-01-20 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150120134732/http://www.sonymusic.fi/2014/09/16/hevisaurus-elokuvaa-tahdittavat-mm-taneli-makela-kari-hietalahti-timo-kahilainen-heikki-hela-sanna-stellan-ja-eija-vilpas/ |archive-date=2015-01-20 }}</ref>

==Disagreement with Sony Music==
In the beginning of 2011, [[Mirka Rantanen]], the drummer and one of the founding members, fell into a dispute between their record label [[Sony Music Entertainment]] about the copyrights and trademarks of the characters. As a result, Rantanen formed a competing band called “Sauruxet”, with the people who played in the Hevisaurus live set-up. The court ordered “Sauruxet” to pay the record label €100,000 and legal fees. Legal documents stated that the rights for the characters had always belonged to Sony Music Entertainment.<ref>{{citation | title = Hevisaurukset löi Sauruxet käräjäoikeudessa | date = February 2012 | url = http://www.mtv.fi/uutiset/rikos/artikkeli/hevisaurukset-loi-sauruxet-karajaoikeudessa/2046692 | publisher = MTV }}</ref><ref>{{citation | title = Lastenbändi Hevisauruksen oikeuksista kiistaa | url = http://www.hs.fi/kulttuuri/artikkeli/Lastenb%C3%A4ndi+Hevisauruksen+oikeuksista+kiistaa/1135264014017 | publisher = Helsingin Sanomat}}</ref>
