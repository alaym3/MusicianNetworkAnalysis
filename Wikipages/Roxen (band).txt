'''Roxen''' ({{Lang-ur|روزن}} {{Translation|Hope}}) is a Pakistani [[Rock music|rock]] band from [[Lahore]], [[Punjab, Pakistan]]. The band had a debut studio album ''[[Rozen-e-Deewar]]'', released in 2006. Following this, the band had singles "Toh Phir Aao" and "Tera Mera Rishta" which featured as soundtracks for the [[Bollywood]] movie ''[[Awarapan]]'' in 2007.

== Music ==
Roxen was formed back in September 2002 with initial line up of [[Mustafa Zahid|Mustafa "Musti" Zahid]], Jawad, known onstage as Jodi, and Omar. Their debut song was "Yaadein". After a couple of months they came out with web release of the demo song, "Tau Phir Aoo". The song registered over 65000 downloads in one month.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.gulf-daily-news.com/arc_Articles.asp?Article=187467&Sn=BNEW&IssueID=30113|title=Gulf Daily News: Tau phir aao-65,000 downloads in one month|website=Gulf-daily-news.com}}</ref>

Roxen's debut album, ''Rozen-E-Deewar'', was released on 31 August 2006, on [[Fire Records (Pakistan)|Fire Records]] in [[Pakistan]], Hom Records in India and on Sangeet Records in the United States, Canada, and the Middle East.

The release date for Roxen's second album ''Bhoola Samundar'', has not been announced. The album is scheduled to be released by [[Universal Music]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://pakium.pk/2013/08/07/universal-music-signs-mustafa-zahid-roxen-band|title=Universal Music Signs Mustafa Zahid and his Band ROXEN »|first=Danish|last=Mughal|website=Pakium.pk|date=August 7, 2013}}</ref>

In 2015, Roxen released their single "Anjanay Raaston" for the drama series ''Mere Khwaab Lauta Do''.
