'''Happoradio''' (Finnish for ''Acid Radio'') is a [[Finland|Finnish]] [[Rock music|rock]] band formed in 2001. They achieved their first success with the single "Pois Kalliosta" (2003). "Tavikset", "Che Guevara", "Ahmat tulevat" and "Pelastaja" are some of their other hits in the [[Scandinavia]]n market.

== Members ==
* Aki Tykki ([[voice]], [[guitar]])
* Mika "AH" Haapasalo ([[guitar]])
* Jatu Motti ([[Bass guitar|bass]])
* Markku DeFrost ([[Drum kit|drums]])
