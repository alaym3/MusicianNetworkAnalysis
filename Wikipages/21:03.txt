'''21:03''' is an American contemporary [[Gospel music|gospel]], [[Christian R&B]] and [[Christian hip hop]] group from  [[Detroit|Detroit, Michigan]]. Its members include Evin Martin and Torrence Greene.<ref>{{cite web | first1=Mike | last1=Rimmer | url=http://www.crossrhythms.co.uk/articles/music/2103_The_RB_Gospel_Harmonizers_With_PAJAM_Produced_Debut/25309/p1/ | title=21:03: The R&B Gospel Harmonizers With PAJAM Produced Debut | publisher=Cross Rhythms | accessdate=13 March 2015 | website=crossrhythms.co.uk/}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.christianmusic.com/21_03/21_03.htm | title=21:03 - ChristianMusic.com | publisher=Christian Music | accessdate=13 March 2015 | website=christianmusic.com}}</ref> Former members were Jo'rel Quinn and Sean Grant. The group is signed to [[PAJAM]] and some of their albums include ''PAJAM Presents Twenty One O’ Three'' (2006) and ''Total Attention'' (2008).<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.gospelflava.com/musicdownload/2103attention.html | title=Behind the Scenes with 21:03 (Feb, 2010) | publisher=Gospel Flava | accessdate=13 March 2015 | website=gospelflava.com/}}</ref> The group has also experienced ''Billboard'' success with their hit singles, "Still Here", "Incredible" and "Cover Me".<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/431871/2103/chart | title=Hot Gospel Songs Charts | publisher=Billboard | accessdate=17 March 2015 | website=billboard.com/}}</ref>

== History ==

The members of 21:03 were hand picked from hundreds of other singers in the Detroit area to be a part of the [[PAJAM]] record label.<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.myplay.com/artists/21-03/bio | title=21:03 Bio | accessdate=20 March 2015 | website=myplay.com/ | archive-url=https://archive.is/20150320052733/http://www.myplay.com/artists/21-03/bio | archive-date=20 March 2015 | url-status=dead | df=dmy-all }}</ref> Critical acclaim for 21:03 includes a [[Stellar Awards|Stellar Award]] win and [[Grammy Award]] nomination.<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.newreleasetuesday.com/artistdetail.php?artist_id=1215 | title=newreleasetuesday | publisher=New Release Tuesday | accessdate=20 March 2015 | website=newreleasetuesday.com/}}</ref>
