'''Nicola Teresa DeMartino''' and '''Gabriella Nelida DeMartino''' (born May 5, 1995),<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-09-04|title=Gabi Demartino Net Worth – Fashion and Beauty YouTube Blogger|url=https://www.explosion.com/137360/gabi-demartino/|access-date=2021-03-24|website=Explosion|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Bain|first=Ellissa|date=2020-12-04|title=What happened with Niki and Gabi? In 2018, the duo released their debut [[extended play]], [[Individual (EP)|''Individual'']], and won a [[Streamy Award]] in the category of fashion.

The duo went solo in 2020, however they've released solo singles previously, Gabi's debut single being "Ever After" released in 2016, and Niki's debut single being "Let It Roar" released in 2018. Gabi's debut extended play, ''Gabroadway'', was released in 2020, and her debut [[Album|studio album]], ''Beautiful Mess'', is set for release in 2021

==Career==
Niki and Gabi are twin sisters. They studied at [[Notre Dame High School (Easton, Pennsylvania)|Notre Dame High School]] and [[DeSales University]] where Niki majored in television and film and Gabi majored in musical theatre. They dropped out of college after their junior year.<ref name="CareerStart">{{Cite news |url=https://www.lehighvalleylive.com/entertainment-general/2014/06/bethlehem_teens_hit_it_big_on.html|title=Bethlehem teens hit it big on YouTube with fashion channel |date=June 15, 2014|work=Lehigh Valley Live |access-date=July 15, 2020}}</ref>

==Controversies==
In September 2019, Niki and Gabi released a video on their channel titled "Going to College Dressed as Celebrities Challenge" in which American singer [[Normani]] was portrayed by a man. The video was criticized for "furthering [[Angry black woman|stereotypes about black women having masculine features]]".<ref name="TikTok">{{Cite news |url=https://www.vulture.com/2020/03/niki-demar-coronavirus-tiktok-apology-video-watch.html|title=Influencer Sorry About That Racist Coronavirus TikTok |date=March 18, 2020|work=Vulture |access-date=July 15, 2020}}</ref>

In March 2020, Niki posted a [[TikTok]] in wake of the [[COVID-19 pandemic]] which showed her with an Asian [[manicurist]], both masked, while the song "It's Corona Time" plays over the video. The TikTok was criticized as racist and insensitive due to the implication that the manicurist had [[COVID-19]] amid the [[Xenophobia and racism related to the COVID-19 pandemic#United States|rising xenophobia and racism experienced by Asians in the United States]] during the pandemic.<ref name="TikTok"/> The video was deleted and DeMar issued an apology.

In September 2020, Gabi was criticized for a video titled "BFF Income Challenge" in which she was accused of flaunting her income. In the video, she compared her spending income with a friend revealing a "spending disparity of thousands of dollars per week". She later issued an apology.<ref name="OnlyFansVulture">{{Cite news |url=https://www.vulture.com/2020/12/youtuber-gabi-demartino-onlyfans-child-pornography.html|title=The Latest OnlyFans Drama Involves a YouTuber Posting a Video of Herself As a Toddler |date=December 3, 2020|work=Vulture |access-date=December 5, 2020}}</ref>
