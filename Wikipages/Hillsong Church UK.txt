'''Hillsong Church UK''' is a [[Evangelical charismatic movement|charismatic Christian]] [[megachurch]] in the United Kingdom which is a part of [[Hillsong Church]] global.

==History==
The church was planted out of Sydney by Gerard and Sue Keehan in 1992 as the London Christian Life Centre,<ref name="Tomkins">{{cite news|last1=Tomkins|first1=Steve|title=Theatre of God|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/uk_news/magazine/4677440.stm|access-date=1 June 2015|publisher=BBC|date=3 February 2006}}</ref> becoming Hillsong Church London in 1999, and is currently pastored by Gary and Cathy Clarke.<ref name="Pastors & Leaders - Hillsong London">{{cite web|url=http://www.hillsong.co.uk/leaders/|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090511000000/http://www.hillsong.co.uk/leaders/ |archive-date=11 May 2009 |title=Pastors & Leaders - Hillsong London |publisher=Hillsong Church |access-date=15 April 2013}} [http://www.smh.com.au/national/hillsong-on-a-mission-to-spread-the-word-north-20090510-az7o.html Alt URL]</ref>

From 2000 to 2002 Hillsong London met in various West End theatres and university halls around London.<ref name="Tomkins"/> Towards the end of 2004, it ended a two-year residency in the [[Mermaid Theatre|Mermaid Conference and Events Centre]], holding seven services a weekend. {{As of|2005|alt=As of January 2005}}, the church holds four services on a Sunday at the [[Dominion Theatre]], which seats around 2200 people.

In August 2005, Hillsong London began holding services in [[Leatherhead]], [[Surrey]] (southwest of London), and in 2011 services commenced in [[Tonbridge]], [[Kent]] (southeast of London).

In 2006 Hillsong London drew 3,000 people to the Excel Center for the first Hillsong Conference Europe.<ref name=Zinchini>{{cite news|last1=Zinchini|first1=Cassandra|title=Taking Revival to the World|url=http://www.christianitytoday.com/ct/2007/october/44.34.html|access-date=1 June 2015|publisher=Christianity Today|date=26 October 2007}}</ref>

On 13 September 2009 Hillsong London held a service at the London [[The O2 arena (London)|O2 Arena]], to celebrate ten years of the church in London.  In 2013, Hillsong London formed the Warehouse Project just outside Millwall's Stadium, The Den.

In 2016, the attendance in Dominion Theatre is 2,000 people.<ref>Joe Shute, [https://www.telegraph.co.uk/family/life/inside-hillsong-hollywoods-evangelical-megachurch-taking-london/ Inside Hillsong: Hollywood's evangelical megachurch that is taking London by storm], telegraph.co.uk, UK, 26 July 2019</ref>

In 2019, the Church had 15 locations in the UK.<ref>Joshua Statter, [https://www.eastlondonlines.co.uk/2019/10/megachurchcroydon/ 600 worshippers flock to megachurch Hillsong's new weekly service in Croydon], eastlondonlines.co.uk, UK, 7 October 2019</ref>

Hillsong London has hosted their special Christmas event called 'Carols at Wembley Arena' at the [[Wembley Arena|SSE Arena, Wembley]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hillsong.co.uk/calendar/2012/12/21/christmas-carols/ |title=Christmas Carols &#124; Hillsong London |publisher=Hillsong.co.uk |access-date=20 August 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://christiannews.net/2015/12/18/about-jesus-hillsong-sells-out-advertised-xmas-services-with-dancing-elves-santa-mini-skirt-flappers/ |title=About Jesus'? Hillsong 'Sells Out' Advertised Xmas Services With Dancing Elves, Santa, Mini-Skirt Flappers |publisher=Christian News Network |date= 18 December 2016|access-date=26 June 2020}}</ref>
