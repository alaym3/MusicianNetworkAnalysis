'''Wiktoria Vendela Johansson''' (born 8 November 1996), known professionally as simply '''Wiktoria''', is a Swedish singer and songwriter. She first achieved public attention during [[Melodifestivalen 2016]], where she placed fourth.

==Life and career==
Johansson was born on 8 November 1996 in [[Brämhult]], a suburb of [[Borås]]. In 2011, she participated in [[Lilla Melodifestivalen]] and placed fourth with the self-penned song "Jag behöver dig".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.allas.se/wiktorias-drom-om-skivkontrakt-slog-in-nu-gor-hon-debut-i-melodifestivalen/|title=Melodifestivalaktuella Wiktoria: Jag blev retad i skolan|publisher=|accessdate=13 February 2016}}</ref> On 30 November 2015, Johansson was announced as one of the 28 participants in [[Melodifestivalen 2016]] with the song "[[Save Me (Wiktoria song)|Save Me]]".<ref>{{cite web|last1=Escudero|first1=Victor M.|title=Sweden: Who will compete in Melodifestivalen 2016?|url=http://www.eurovision.tv/page/news?id=sweden_who_will_compete_in_melodifestivalen_2016|website=eurovision.tv|publisher=[[European Broadcasting Union]]|accessdate=17 January 2016|date=30 November 2015}}</ref>

She performed last in the second semi-final held on 13 February 2016, and qualified directly to the final.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.eurovision.tv/page/news?id=sweden_two_more_finalists_in_melodifestivalen|title=Sweden: two more finalists in Melodifestivalen|date=13 Feb 2016|last=Escudero|first=Victor M.|publisher=EBU|website=eurovision.tv}}</ref> In the final, Johansson placed fourth with the juries, second with the Swedish public, and fourth overall.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.eurovision.tv/page/news?id=frans_wins_melodifestivalen_in_sweden|title=Frans wins Melodifestivalen in Sweden|publisher=EBU|last=Escudero|first=Victor M.|date=12 March 2016}}</ref> Johansson also voices Moana in the Swedish dub of the 2016 film ''[[Moana (2016 film)|Moana]]''. She competed in [[Melodifestivalen 2017]] with the song "[[As I Lay Me Down (Wiktoria song)|As I Lay Me Down]]", qualifying once again directly to the final, where she finished sixth overall; eight with the juries, and second with the public.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.eurovision.tv/page/news?id=sweden_robin_bengtsson_wins_melodifestivalen|title=Sweden:Robin Bengtsson wins Melodifestivalen!|publisher=eurovision.tv|date=11 March 2017}}</ref> She was the Swedish spokesperson in the [[Eurovision Song Contest 2017]],<ref>{{cite web|url=http://wiwibloggs.com/2017/04/12/sweden-mans-zelmerlow-commentary/185859/|title=SWEDEN: MÅNS ZELMERLÖW TO CO-PRESENT SWEDISH COMMENTARY IN KYIV, WIKTORIA TO DELIVER POINTS|date=12 April 2017|publisher=Wiwibloggs|website=wiwibloggs.com|last=Gallagher|first=Robyn}}</ref> and toured with the ''Diggiloo Tour'' (in Swedish: ''Diggilooturnén''), during the summer. She participated in [[Stjärnornas stjärna]] broadcast on [[TV4 (Sweden)|TV4]] in 2018, where she finished in fifth place out of eight contestants. She participated in [[Melodifestivalen 2019]] with the song "Not with Me", qualifying directly to the final for the third time, where she finished sixth overall; sixth with the juries and eighth with the public.<ref>{{cite web|last=Dahlander|first=Gustav|url=https://blogg.svt.se/melodifestivalen-expertbloggen/artisterna-melodifestivalen-2019-hela-listan-s%C3%A5-blir-delt%C3%A4vlingarna/|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181127092702/https://blogg.svt.se/melodifestivalen-expertbloggen/artisterna-melodifestivalen-2019-hela-listan-s%C3%A5-blir-delt%C3%A4vlingarna/|url-status=dead|archive-date=27 November 2018|title=Artisterna i Melodifestivalen 2019: Hela listan – så blir deltävlingarna|work=Gustavs expertblogg|publisher=[[Sveriges Television|SVT]]|language=Swedish|date=27 November 2018|accessdate=27 November 2018}}</ref>

On 30 April 2021, Wiktoria released "Need You to Know" and announced the forthcoming released of her debut album, which was released in June 2021.<ref>{{Cite press release|url=https://www.mynewsdesk.com/se/sonybmg/pressreleases/wiktoria-slaepper-powerballaden-need-you-to-know-och-presenterar-kommande-debutalbum-3093842|title=Wiktoria släpper powerballaden ”Need you to know” och presenterar kommande debutalbum|date=30 April 2021|publisher=[[Sony Music]]|language=sv|access-date=1 May 2021}}</ref>
