'''Mathilde Gerner''' ({{IPA-fr|matild ʒɛʁnɛʁ}}; born 14 September 1996), better known by her stage [[mononym]] '''Hoshi''', is a French singer-songwriter.

== Biography ==

=== Early life ===

Mathilde Gerner was born on 14 September 1996 in [[Versailles, Yvelines|Versailles]] and grew up in the [[communes of France|commune]] [[Montigny-le-Bretonneux]] of the [[communauté d'agglomération]] [[Saint-Quentin-en-Yvelines]].<ref name="Ignymontain142">{{cite magazine|title=Hoshi, étoile montante à Montigny |magazine=L'Ignymontain |issue=142 |date=November 2014 |url=https://www.montigny78.fr/fileadmin/montigny/MEDIA/PDF/Journal/2014/Ignymontain_2014-11.pdf|pages=3}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Virgin Radio Officiel|title=L'interview de HOSHI – Le Lab Virgin Radio|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FW2xK3EgV3A|date=19 February 2018|website=YouTube|access-date=22 May 2018}}</ref> She started playing the piano at the age of 6 and the guitar at the age of 15,<ref name="itw-just"/> which is when she wrote her first songs.<ref name="itw-just"/>

=== Musical career ===

Gerner began her musical career with the amateur music group TransyStory, which was formed in September 2011. She provided vocals, keyboard, and guitar<ref>{{cite web|title=Biographie de l'artiste Hoshi de la communauté ZicMeUp |url=http://www.zicmeup.com/artiste/hoshi/bio |website=www.zicmeup.com |access-date=30 August 2019 }}</ref> to this four-person group. The band occasionally put up municipal performances in Montigny-le-Bretonneux.<ref>{{cite web|author=Philippe Chauveau-Beaubaton|title=Hoshi Hideko, la chanteuse tout droit sortie d'un manga !|url=https://philippeldl.wordpress.com/2012/07/16/hoshi-hideko-la-chanteuse-tout-droit-sortie-dun-manga/|website=LA LIBRE GAZETTE|date=16 July 2012|access-date=30 August 2019}}</ref> The group played "Lou", their first recorded piece of music.<ref>{{cite AV media|author=Hoshi|title=Lou (ma compo) en live|url=https://hoshi-official.skyrock.com/3048520261-Lou-ma-compo-en-live-2011.html|date=27 November 2011|access-date=30 August 2019}}</ref> Around the same time, in December 2011, Gerner alone competed in and won the "Jeunes Talents, c’est maintenant!" competition, organized by the city.<ref>{{cite magazine|title=Hoshi, étoile montante à Montigny |magazine=L'Ignymontain |issue=111|date=January 2012|url=https://www.montigny78.fr/fileadmin/montigny/MEDIA/PDF/Journal/2012/Ignymontain_2012-01.pdf|pages=7}}</ref> During this competition, she was noticed by Marc Isacco, who was in charge of a musical section in Montigny. Through Isacco, she was able to meet established artists and perform in larger venues.<ref name="Ignymontain142"/><ref>{{cite magazine|author=Nicolas Gervais|title=Hoshi la voix du succès|magazine=Sqy Mag|issue=12|date=January 2016|url=https://www.calameo.com/read/000003871a11c98953edb|pages=23|access-date=31 August 2019 }}</ref> TransyStory separated at the end of 2012 and Gerner continued her solo career.

Being passionate about Japanese culture, she chose Hoshi Hideko<ref name="figaro">{{cite web|author=Damien Mercereau |title=The Voice : un talent tourne le dos aux auditions à l'aveugle|url=http://tvmag.lefigaro.fr/programme-tv/article/television/79849/the-voice-un-talent-tourne-le-dos-aux-auditions-a-l-aveugle.html |website=tvmag.lefigaro.fr|date=18 February 2014 |access-date=30 August 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=Reizak|title=Les Découvertes Du Mouton -Hoshi Hideko |url=https://error404.fr/les-decouvertes-du-moutons-hoshi-hideko/ |website=Error404 |date=11 June 2014 |access-date=30 August 2019}}</ref> as her stage name, later shortened to simply Hoshi (星), which means "star" in [[Japanese language|Japanese]].<ref name="itw-just" /> She became known by publishing [[Cover version|covers]] on the Internet and she received the support of the [[Record label|label]] Styiens. In 2013, at the age of 16, she was invited by the casting director of the French ''[[The Voice (French TV series)|The Voice]]'' to compete. She successfully passed auditions but decided not to continue when the show's production wanted to control her song choice.<ref name="figaro" />

Gerner left high school and devoted herself to music by performing in the streets.<ref>{{cite web |author=Clémence Duranton|title=Hoshi : "La musique m'a aidée à me créer des repères" |url=https://www.parismatch.com/Culture/Musique/Hoshi-La-musique-m-a-aidee-a-me-creer-des-reperes-1594216 |website=parismatch.com |access-date=30 August 2019 }}</ref><ref>{{cite magazine|author=Tanneguy de Kerpoisson|title=Retenez bien son nom : Hoshi, un soleil levant |url=http://www.leparisien.fr/culture-loisirs/musique/retenez-bien-son-nom-hoshi-un-soleil-levant-06-04-2018-7949430.php |magazine=Le Parisien|date=22 November 2018 |access-date=31 August 2019 }}</ref> In 2014, she participated in the show ''[[Rising Star (France)|Rising Star]]'' on the channel [[M6 (TV channel)|M6]], but her cover of "[[Mistral gagnant (song)|Mistral gagnant]]"<ref>{{cite web
|url=http://www.leparisien.fr/espace-premium/yvelines-78/hoshi-tente-sa-chance-face-au-mur-de-rising-star-02-10-2014-4180285.php
|title=Hoshi tente sa chance face au mur de ''Rising Star''
|website=[[La Parisien|leparisien.fr]]
|access-date=1 May 2018
}}.</ref> did not qualify her. She returned to singing in the streets,<ref name="telepro"/> where she was able to try out compositions.<ref>{{cite magazine|title=La voix de la jeunesse – Hoshi, étoile montante|magazine=Le Petit Versaillais|issue=55|date=November 2018|pages=5|url=https://www.calameo.com/read/0030084152d640129b9ba|access-date=2 September 2019}}.</ref> She also performed solo for small concerts.<ref name="nrj_bio"/> She was contacted almost two years later by a manager, through whom she was signed by the label Jo & Co.<ref name="telepro">{{cite web |title=[Solidarités 2018] Hoshi : " Quand Gaetan Roussel m'a contactée, j'ai halluciné ! " {{!}} Infos People |url=https://www.telepro.be/011-45485-Solidarites-2018-Hoshi-Quand-Gaetan-Roussel-ma-contactee-jai-hallucine.html |website=Télépro |access-date=30 August 2019 }}</ref><ref name="nrj_bio">{{cite web |title=Biographie de Hoshi |url=http://www.nrj.fr/artistes/hoshi/biographie |website=Nrj.fr|access-date=1 May 2018}}</ref>

==== Gaining recognition ====

On 3 March 2017, she released her first [[Single (music)|single]], "Comment je vais faire". The song and its music video achieved some success.<ref name="itw-just"/>

On 19 May 2017, she released her first [[Extended play|EP]], "Comment je vais faire",<ref name="itw-just"/> through the label Jo & Co. It contained the eponymous track along with an acoustic version, as well as three original tracks, entitled "Manège à trois", "En gros tout est gris", and "Parking sonne".

On 23 March 2018, she released her first album, "{{interlanguage link|Il suffit d'y croire|fr}}".<ref name="nrj_bio" /> Her breakthrough success came with the title "Ta marinière".<ref>{{cite web |title=Hoshi : La chanteuse sort enfin son premier album ! |url=https://www.aufeminin.com/actualite-musicale/hoshi-la-chanteuse-sort-enfin-son-premier-album-s2511160.html |website=Aufeminin.fr |access-date=1 May 2018 }}</ref>

On 5 October 2018, she sang a [[duet]] in [[Ycare]]'s song "À qui la faute".

In 2020, she was nominated in the [[Victoires de la musique]] in the category "Stage Révélation of the Year".<ref>{{cite web|language=fr|title=Victoires de la Musique 2020 : la liste complète des nominations|url=https://www.programme-television.org/news-tv/Victoires-de-la-Musique-2020-la-liste-complete-des-nominations-4653793|website=Télé 7 jours|date=14 January 2020}}</ref>

=== Personal life ===

Gerner appeared with Gia Martinelli in November 2018 on the [[red carpet]] at the [[NRJ Music Awards]] when she was nominated for the category of Francophone Breakthrough of the Year (''Révélation francophone de l'année'').<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.purepeople.com/article/hoshi-avec-sa-compagne-aux-nrj-music-awards-sa-belle-declaration-d-amour-a-mia_a312485/1|title=Hoshi avec sa compagne aux NRJ Music Awards : Sa belle déclaration d'amour à Mia|website=purepeople.com|access-date= 12 November 2018
}}</ref> In December the same year, she said she was the victim of a "forced [[coming out]]" due to the publication of an article in ''[[Paris Match]]'' that wrote about her homosexuality based on comments she denies ever making.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chartsinfrance.net/Hoshi/news-108901.html|title=Hoshi victime d'un "coming out forcé" : la chanteuse pousse un coup de gueule|website=chartsinfrance.net|access-date=18 December 2018}}</ref>
