==Biography==
===Before Zardonic (2001-2003)===
Federico's earlier projects include Venezuelan black metal project Gorepriest. With the release of ''War Against Humanity: The Armageddon Chronicles'', he was awarded the Best Keyboardist category in the Premios Metal Hecho en Venezuela 2002–2003.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.oidossucios.com/noticias/917/gillman-metempsicosis-arrasan-en-los-premios-metal/ |title=Gillman y Metempsicosis arrasan en los Premios Metal Hecho en Venezuela |accessdate=12 Jan 2017}}</ref>

===Early Zardonic career (2004–2005)===
Federico's first tracks as Zardonic were composed in 2004 and 2005, respectively in the albums ''Subsonic Stigmata'' and ''Red Phasing'', made available through '''VeNet Music Development''', a netlabel which released most of his early works.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.dargothar.com/gorepriest/discography.html |title=Gore Priest Discography |publisher=Dargothar.com |accessdate=10 March 2011}}</ref> His remix of [[Impaled (band)|Impaled]]'s track ''Gutless'' was also freely released this year on the band's website.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.myspace.com/impaled/music/songs/gutless-ala-dj-zardonic-34575225 |title=Gutless a la DJ Zardonic |publisher=Impaled on MySpace |accessdate=10 March 2011}}</ref>

===First international events and worldwide releases (2006–2008)===
In 2006 Zardonic's first vinyl was released through '''Death Brigade Records''', becoming the first Venezuelan drum and bass producer to release a single in an international imprint.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.subsoniksoundrecordings.com/zardonic.html |title=Subsonik Sound Recordings |access-date=27 February 2011 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100913062735/http://www.subsoniksoundrecordings.com/zardonic.html |archive-date=13 September 2010 |df= }}</ref> Later this year, he also founded '''Zardonic Recordings''', an independent drum and bass [[netlabel]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.kmag.co.uk/editorial/news/zardonic-releases-rare-grooves-3 |title=Zardonic Releases Rare Grooves 3 |date=February 15, 2011 |publisher=Knowledge Magazine |access-date=25 February 2011 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110218035331/http://www.kmag.co.uk/editorial/news/zardonic-releases-rare-grooves-3 |archive-date=18 February 2011 |df= }}</ref> His remix of [[Avulsed]]'s track ''Stabwound Orgasm'' was included on their compilation album entitled ''Reanimations''<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.discogs.com/Avulsed-Reanimations/release/1976139 |title=Avulsed – Reanimations (CD) at Discogs |accessdate=10 March 2012}}</ref> and a remix of [[Gorgoroth]]'s track ''Procreating Satan'' was freely released through the band's website.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://legacy.roadrunnerrecords.com/blabbermouth.net/news.aspx?mode=Article&newsitemID=47556 |archive-url=https://archive.today/20120630043410/http://legacy.roadrunnerrecords.com/blabbermouth.net/news.aspx?mode=Article&newsitemID=47556 |url-status=dead |archive-date=30 June 2012 |title=BLABBERMOUTH.NET – GORGOROTH Begin Recording Vocals For New Album |access-date=10 March 2012 }}</ref>

In 2007 he was booked for his first international event in Mexico on 19 May.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.drumandbass.com.mx/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=32&Itemid=50 |title=Zardonic (Venezuela) 19 May 2007 |date=May 23, 2008 |publisher=Drumandbass.com.mx |access-date=27 February 2011 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110722223201/http://www.drumandbass.com.mx/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=32&Itemid=50 |archive-date=22 July 2011 |df= }}</ref> Months later [[AK1200]], pioneer of the American Drum & Bass scene, selected Zardonic's ''Moonlight Ceremony'' for his ''Weapons of Tomorrow'' mixed compilation CD.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.discogs.com/AK1200-Weapons-Of-Tomorrow/release/1211933 |title=AK1200 – Weapons Of Tomorrow (CD) at Discogs |date=October 23, 2007 |publisher=Discogs |accessdate=25 February 2011}}</ref> Soon after, his tunes earned the support of international performers such as [[Dieselboy]], [[Pendulum]] and [[John B]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.orderinkaos.co.uk/zardonic.html |title=ORDER IN KAOS DIGITAL |date=November 29, 2010 |publisher=Order In Kaos |access-date=26 February 2011 |archive-url=https://www.webcitation.org/69RqWUR75?url=http://www.orderinkaos.co.uk/zardonic.html |archive-date=2012-07-26 |url-status=dead |df= }}</ref>

In 2008, his remix of [[The Berzerker]]'s ''Caught In The Crossfire'' was featured on the Digipak edition of the band's album ''The Reawakening''.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.discogs.com/Berzerker-The-Reawakening/release/1463919 |title=Berzerker, The – The Reawakening (CD, Album) at Discogs |accessdate=10 March 2012}}</ref> In December, he was featured in the ''Venezuela Electrónica Vol. 3'' compilation, sponsored by Venezuelan wireless telecommunications company [[Digitel GSM]] and [[Motorola]], by selling the new [[MOTOROKR]] EM30 mobile phone model together with a [[microSD]] card including all the tracks.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.diversomagazine.com/2008/12/venezuela-electrnica-vol3.html |title=Venezuela Electrónica Vol. 3 |date=December 3, 2008 |publisher=Diverso Magazine |accessdate=26 February 2011}}</ref>

===''Vulgar Display Of Bass'', ''Revolution'' and stage show costume (2012)===
In 2012, Zardonic announced the release of a Full Length collaboration album entitled ''Vulgar Display Of Bass'' on March 26 through Big Riddim Recordings, paired with a European tour for April and May. Highly acclaimed upon release, the album reached #1 on the Top Drum & Bass releases on leading online music store [[Beatport]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://natalie-nataliesworld.blogspot.pt/2012/04/zardonic-releases-revolution-official.html |title=Zardonic Releases "Revolution" Official Video" |accessdate=13 April 2012}}</ref> An official video for the opening track ''Revolution'' was produced and made public through video hosting network [[YouTube]] on April 10.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xj7I7dnCss4 |title=Revolution (OFFICIAL VIDEO) by Zardonic, Counterstrike, Gein & Robyn Chaos |accessdate=10 April 2012}}</ref> The video consisted of a collage of crowd-based footage showing their country flags, with a total of 46 countries participating.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=55JUIQEvdi8 |title=UNETE A LA REVOLUTION! |accessdate=10 April 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://revolution.zardonic.net/ |title=JOIN THE REVOLUTION |access-date=10 April 2012 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120803093915/http://revolution.zardonic.net/ |archive-date=2012-08-03 |url-status=dead |df= }}</ref>

The artist also revealed a new set of pictures wearing a mask based on the Zardonic logo, as a part of his stage show to be seen on [[Dimmu Borgir]]'s concert in [[Caracas]] as the opening act for the band.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.conciertosmetal.com.ve/zardonic-one-man-project-designado-para-aperturar-el-concierto-de-dimmu-borgir-en-venezuela/ |title=Conciertos Metal – Zardonic (One Man Project) designado para aperturar el concierto de Dimmu Borgir en Venezuela |access-date=17 February 2012 |archive-url=https://archive.is/20120630043418/http://www.conciertosmetal.com.ve/zardonic-one-man-project-designado-para-aperturar-el-concierto-de-dimmu-borgir-en-venezuela/ |archive-date=2012-06-30 |url-status=dead |df= }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.conciertosmetal.com.ve/entrevista-exclusiva-a-federico-agreda-zardonic/ |title=Conciertos Metal – Entrevista Exclusiva a Federico Agreda |access-date=17 February 2012 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120826010341/http://www.conciertosmetal.com.ve/entrevista-exclusiva-a-federico-agreda-zardonic/ |archive-date=2012-08-26 |url-status=dead |df= }}</ref> Four of his new tracks were included again on Dieselboy's new mixtape for the year entitled ''Wake The Dead''<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.rockthedub.com/2012/02/dieselboy-wake-dead.html |title=rockthedub: Dieselboy – Wake The Dead |accessdate=17 February 2012}}</ref> and a new collaboration track with UK dubstep duo '''Bare Noize''' was included on a compilation album to be released on March 13 by [[OWSLA]], a label helmed by the 3-time Grammy winner [[Skrillex]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.musicemissions.com/blog/index.php/2012/03/08/owsla-presents-free-treats-vol-2/ |title=OWSLA Presents – Free Treats Vol. 2 |accessdate=9 March 2012}}</ref>

Later on, an exclusive record deal was signed with [[eOne Music]] for the new single "For Justice", released in December.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://us.eonemusic.com/news-and-events/entertainment-one-signs-zardonic |title=ENTERTAINMENT ONE SIGNS ZARDONIC |access-date=26 May 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150913051723/http://us.eonemusic.com/news-and-events/entertainment-one-signs-zardonic |archive-date=2015-09-13 |url-status=dead |df= }}</ref> The single was reviewed as "a single that not only blends the best of both worlds, but does it in a highly polished and flawlessly produced way" by Intravenous Magazine<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.intravenousmag.co.uk/2015/01/review-zardonic-for-justice.html |title=Review: Zardonic - "For Justice" |accessdate=3 Jun 2015}}</ref> and "a track that deserves the repeat button for a never-ending roller coaster night ride" by 24OurMusic.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://24ourmusic.net/2014/11/29/zardonics-latest-track-justifyingly-rocking/ |title=Zardonic's Latest Track Fights For Justice |accessdate=3 Jun 2015}}</ref> "For Justice" premiered on National U.S. radio on [[Amber Lynn]]'s Talk Show ''Rock N Sexxxy Uncensored'' during a feature show together with [[Ron Jeremy]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.latalkradio.com/Amberlynn.php |title=LA Talk Radio Amber Lynn Rock N Sexxxy Uncensored |accessdate=3 Jun 2015}}</ref>

=== ''Bring It On'', Europe Tour, ''Warlocks vs Shadows'', ''Antihero'', Best DJ Award (2015) ===
The ''Bring It On'' single is chosen as the main track in World Series Of Fighting on [[NBC Sports Network]] and is later released by eOne Music, which led to a guest performance at [[World Series of Fighting 22: Palhares vs. Shields]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://rockrevoltmagazine.com/venezuelan-electronic-metal-mastermind-zardonic-challenges-nbcs-mma-world-series-of-fighting-to-bring-it-on/ |title=Venezuelan Electronic Metal Mastermind ZARDONIC Challenges NBC's MMA World Series of Fighting To "Bring It On" |accessdate=28 Jul 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bjpenn.com/mmanews/wsof-22-will-feature-a-live-music-act/|title=WSOF 22 Will Feature A Live Music Act|publisher=BJPENN.COM News|accessdate=2017-01-28}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://livestream.com/accounts/1719228/events/4235160/videos/94846061|title=WSOF 22 - World Series Of Fighting|publisher=Livestream|accessdate=2017-01-28}}</ref>

Between April and July, Zardonic tours Europe with 20 stops in Portugal, Netherlands, France, Spain, Italy, Slovakia, Hungary, Czech Republic, Estonia, Poland and Romania.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://edmranks.com/zardonic-2015-europe-tour-dates/ |title=Zardonic 2015 Europe Tour Dates |accessdate=28 Jul 2015}}</ref> As a result of a collaboration with polish company One More Level, Zardonic appears as a playable character in the ''Warlocks vs Shadows'' game for which he created his own sound effects and contributed with the tracks ''Kickass'' and ''Sideshow Symphony'', taken from the album Vulgar Display Of Bass.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.gamasutra.com/view/pressreleases/249177/Heavy_electronic_dance_music_DJ_Zardonic_to_become_a_new_heroin_Warlocks_vs_Shadows.php |title=Heavy electronic dance music DJ Zardonic to become a new hero in Warlocks vs. Shadows |accessdate=28 Jul 2015}}</ref>

The new album ''Antihero'' is released on [[Entertainment One Music]] on September 18, 2015, totaling a compilation of 26 tracks including 11 original mixes, 6 instrumental mixes and 9 remixes spanning all styles and different [[Electronic dance music|EDM]] genres.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.youredm.com/2015/08/14/your-edm-interview-zardonic-reveals-new-album-single/ |title=Your EDM Interview: Zardonic Reveals New Album + Single |accessdate=17 Aug 2015}}</ref> Antihero peaked at #4 in [[Tower Records]] Japan Hard & Heavy chart and #5 in the Western chart.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.hellhoundmusic.com/zardonic-joins-dream-theater-megadeth-conquer-divide-fleshgod-apocalypse-tower-records-japan-top-5-hard-heavy-chart/ |title=Zardonic Joins Dream Theater, Megadeth, Conquer Divide, and Fleshgod Apocalypse on Tower Records Japan Top 5 Hard & Heavy Chart |accessdate=15 Mar 2016}}</ref>

In the Fifth Edition of the Unión Rock Show Awards held in Venezuela, Zardonic was nominated as Best DJ and Personality Of The Year, being awarded with the Best DJ category.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.unionrockshow.com/?page_id=1144 |title=Ganadores Premios URS 2015 |accessdate=12 Jan 2017}}</ref>

=== World Tour, Huffington Post premiere, Artist Of The Year Award (2016) ===
Following the success of the Antihero release, Zardonic toured a total of 28 dates in 18 countries for his 2016 World Tour.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://eventful.com/events/hardbeat-meet-voodoo-zardonic-hallucinator-radium-/E0-001-095559484-8 |title=Zardonic in La Villette |accessdate=12 Jan 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://mannheim.nachtkultur.info/events/zardonic-world-tour-ms-connexion_295690/ |title=Zardonic World tour @ MS Connexion Mannheim |accessdate=12 Jan 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://truemetal.lv/forum/124506-zardonic-%7C-22-10-2016-nabaklab |title=ZARDONIC 22.10.2016 @ Nabaklab |accessdate=12 Jan 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.ticketea.com/entradas-concierto-kripton-industries-presenta-a-zardonic/ |title=KRIPTON INDUSTRIES PRESENTA A: ZARDONIC |accessdate=12 Jan 2017}}</ref> Highlights of the tour include the [[Loud Park Festival]] held annually in Japan at the [[Saitama Super Arena]];<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.loudpark.com/16/lineup/zardonic.php |title=ZARDONIC - LOUD PARK 16 |accessdate=12 Jan 2017}}</ref> and Baroeg Open Air in the Netherlands with [[Caliban]], [[Discharge (band)|Discharge]], [[Arkona (band)|Arkona]], [[Counterstrike (drum and bass group)|Counterstrike]] and many more.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.baroegopenair.nl/2016/08/09/zardonic/ |title=Zardonic - Baroeg Open Air |accessdate=12 Jan 2017}}</ref> Several video captures of his tour and previous events can be seen in the official video of the single ''Pure Power'', premiered on [[The Huffington Post]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/entry/zardonic-pure-power-lyric-video-premiere-qa_us_57e93da0e4b00267764fc90c |title=Zardonic Pure Power Video Premiere & Q&A |accessdate=12 Jan 2017}}</ref>

For the Sixth Edition of the Unión Rock Show Awards, Zardonic was nominated as Best DJ and Artist Of The Year, with his 2015 album Antihero nominated for Best Album Cover and Album Of The Year. He was awarded with the Artist Of The Year category.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.unionrockshow.com/?page_id=1323 |title=Ganadores Premios URS 2016 |accessdate=12 Jan 2017}}</ref>

His 2016 releases include several remixes for artists like [[Sonic Syndicate]], [[The Qemists]], [[Celldweller]], Richy Nix, Xmas Eileen, [[Damage Inc (band)|Damage Inc]], [[Heavygrinder]] and Earbutter.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://hardrockhaven.net/online/2016/11/sonic-syndicate-new-single-start-a-war-zardonic-remix/ |title=Sonic Syndicate - New Single |accessdate=12 Jan 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://celldweller.bandcamp.com/track/metropolitan-zardonic-remix |title=Metropolitan (Zardonic Remix) - Celldweller |accessdate=12 Jan 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://gekirock.com/news/2016/10/the-qemists_new-ep_release.php |title=THE QEMISTS、12/2にニューEP『Warrior Soundsystem』リリース決定！ KORNのカバー＆ZARDONICによるリミックス曲を収録！ |accessdate=12 Jan 2017}}</ref>
