'''Stig Joar Haugen'''<ref name="snl">{{Cite web |url=https://snl.no/Unge_Ferrari |title=Unge Ferrari - Store norske leksikon|date=January 15, 2018 |publisher=[[Store norske leksikon]]|language=nb-NO}}</ref> (born August 2, 1990 in [[Hamar]], Norway),<ref name="birthday"/> known professionally as '''Unge Ferrari''' and '''Stig Brenner''', is a Norwegian rapper, singer, and songwriter.<ref name="snl"/> Haugen debuted in 2015 with the EP ''Til mine venner''. It featured the breakthrough singles "Lianer" and "Hvis du vil" with Tomine Harket, and was nominated for [[Spellemannprisen]] in the "Urban" category<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.nrk.no/kultur/kan-bli-tidenes-yngste-spellemann-vinnere-1.12733439|title=Kan bli tidenes yngste Spellemann-vinnere|publisher=nrk.no|date=2016-01-05|access-date=2019-12-29|language=nb-NO}}</ref><ref name="snl"/> as well as for [[P3 Gull]] 2015 in the Newcomer of the Year category.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://p3.no/musikk/her-er-de-nominerte-til-p3-gull-2015/|title=Her er de nominerte til P3 Gull 2015|publisher=p3.no|date=2015-10-28|access-date=2019-12-29|language=nb-NO}}</ref>

In 2018 he released the album ''Midt imellom magisk og manisk'', which debuted with atop the Norwegian [[VG-lista]] albums chart.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.h-a.no/nyhet/naa-topper-han-lista- |title=Topper VG-lista Ã©n uke etter albumslipp |last=BrendlÃ¸kken |first=Ã˜rjan |date=2018-12-08 |website=www.h-a.no |access-date=2020-01-09|language=nb-NO}}</ref> For the album, he was nominated for two awards ahead of Spellemannprisen 2018: Songwriter of the Year and Album of the Year.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.vg.no/rampelys/musikk/i/WLp2Ma/spellemann-glipp-nominert-artist-falt-ut-av-listen|title=Spellemann-glipp â€“ nominert artist falt ut av listen|website=vg.no|date=2019-02-08|access-date=2019-02-11|language=nb-NO}}</ref> He was also nominated for [[P3 Gull]] 2018 in the Live Artist of the Year category.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://p3.no/musikk/her-er-de-nominerte-til-p3-gull-2018/ |title=Her er de nominerte til P3 Gull 2018 |date=2018-11-15 |website=NRK P3 |language=nb-NO |access-date=2020-01-09}}</ref>
