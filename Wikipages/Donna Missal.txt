'''Donna Joy Missal''' is an American singer-songwriter from [[New Jersey]].<ref name="Billboard1">{{Cite news |last=Bote, Joshua |date=September 26, 2018 |title=Singer-Songwriter Donna Missal Opens Up About Her Sexuality: 'It's Not a Phase' |work=Billboard |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/pride/8477115/donna-missal-sexuality |access-date=February 19, 2021}}</ref>

== Early life and career ==
Missal's grandmother was a songwriter in the 1940s; her father was a musician in the 1980s. She recorded her own song "Keep Lying" in 2015, hoping other performers would cover it. Her version went viral and got 2.7 million plays on streaming services.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/donna-missal-mn0003320344/biography|title = Donna Missal &#124; Biography & History}}</ref>  She released a number of other singles, and collaborated on a track with [[Macklemore]] in 2017.<ref name="Billboard1" /> She released her debut album ''This Time'', on September 7, 2018 through Harvest Records.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.discogs.com/Donna-Missal-This-Time/master/1442191|title=Donna Missal - This Time|website=Discogs}}</ref>

== Personal life ==
Missal is [[bisexual]].<ref>{{Cite news |last=Shouneyia, Alexa |date=November 7, 2018 |title=Donna Missal Talks Touring With King Princess, Songwriting & Opening Up About Her Bisexuality |work=Billboard |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/pride/8483633/donna-missal-bisexual-tour-king-princess/ |access-date=February 19, 2021}}</ref>

She has one brother, Steve Missal, who’s currently playing on tour with her. She also has 4 other sisters: Linda Missal, [[Kelley Missal]], Becky  Missal, and Catherine Missal.<ref name=Billboard1/>
