'''Blaq Diamond''' (stylized '''BlaQ Diamond''') is a [[South Africa]]n Afro pop duo from [[Ladysmith, KwaZulu-Natal|Ladysmith KwaZulu-Natal]]. 
They met in 2010 on a school trip where both participated in a music cypher in the school bus. They signed to record label [[Ambitiouz Entertainment]], releasing their debut album ''Inqola'' (2017), which became their first [[iTunes]] number one.

The band first  gained  prominence  for their single "Ibhanoyi" released 2019, which received "[[25th South African Music Awards|SAMA Record of the year]]" .<ref>{{Cite web|title=2020 Record of the Year Winner : blaq Diamond – Ibhanoyi|url=https://samusicawards.co.za/ak_sama_award/auto-draft-677/|access-date=2020-11-07|language=en}}</ref> 

Their second  studio album  ''Umuthi'' (2020), spawned  two platinum selling singles "Ibhanoyi" and "Love Letter".<ref>
{{Cite web|url=https://samusicmag.co.za/2021/03/blaq-diamond-goes-multi-platinum-on-umuthi-album-and-three-singles/amp/|title = Blaq Diamond Goes Multi-Platinum on Umuthi Album and Three Singles|date = 16 March 2021}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.dailysun.co.za/Celebs/blaq-diamond-receives-four-platinum-plaques-20210316|title = Blaq Diamond Receives Four Platinum Plaques!}}</ref>

==History==
===2010-2017===
In 2010, they met while on a school trip where they participated in a rap cypher in the school bus and began spending more time together, participating in school functions which earned them recognition in the community and local surroundings. The two decided to move to [[Johannesburg]] for better musical prospects which proved difficult at first as, not knowing anyone, forced them to get jobs to earn an income which was used to sustain their musical ambitions.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Kandie|first=Lynn|date=2020-03-03|title=Interesting facts about Blaq Diamond and why we all love them|url=https://briefly.co.za/55459-blaq-diamond-bio-real-names-songs-albums-sjava-profile.html|access-date=2020-11-07|website=Briefly|language=en}}</ref>

===2017-2019:''Inqola''===
In early  2017, the duo signed a record deal with [[Ambitiouz Entertainment]] and began to work on the debut album. In June 19, their album's  lead  single "Sthandwa" was released followed by second single "Emzini kaBaba" which was accompanied by music video.<ref>{{cite web|title=Blaq Diamond Releases Visuals for “Emzini Kababa” - Online Youth Magazine {{!}} Zkhiphani.com |url=https://www.zkhiphani.co.za/blaq-diamond-releases-visuals-for-emzini-kababa/|publisher=Zkhiphani}}</ref>  In the winter of 2017, they released their debut album ''Inqola'' to critical acclaim.<ref name="on"></ref> The album is a fusion of [[Afro-soul]] with [[Maskandi]] and [[Mbaqanga|umBhaqanga]] fusion which they made sound modern and youthful.<ref name="on">{{Cite web|date=2017-11-22|title=Afro Pop Duo, Blaq Diamond Release Debut Album Titled Inqola|url=https://www.zkhiphani.co.za/afro-pop-duo-blaq-diamond-release-debut-album-titled-inqola/|access-date=2020-11-07|first=Mandisa|last=Ntsinde|website=Online Youth Magazine {{!}} Zkhiphani.com|language=en-US}}</ref> At the [[24th South African Music Awards]], they received two SAMA Awards nominations. In 2018, they performed for the first time in [[Pietermaritzburg|Piertermaritzburg]] invited by a local event group to perform in a fashion show <ref>{{Cite web|date=2019-03-14|title=Blaq Diamond embrace being in love in the latest video|url=https://sowetourban.co.za/57728/blaq-diamond-embrace-love-latest-video/|access-date=2020-11-07|website=Soweto Urban|language=en-US}}</ref>

===2019-2020: ''Umuthi''===
On May 2, 2019, their  single  "Memeza" featuring [[Sjava]] was released, <ref>{{cite web|title=Blaq Diamond and Sjava scream for help on memeza|url=https://www.zkhiphani.co.za/blaq-diamond-sjava-memeza/|work=Zkhiphani|last=The Journalist}}</ref> as album's  lead single. The song  peaked  number 9 on Radio Airplay Charts. In August 2, their second single "Ibhanoyi" was released.<ref>{{cite web|title=WATCH: Blaq Diamond’s Ibhanoyi has finally landed - Soweto Urban|url=https://sowetourban.co.za/61846/watch-blaq-diamonds-ibhanoyi-finally-landed/?pwa-amp&|access-date=23 August 2019|work=Soweto Urban}}</ref> The song   peaked number one [[iTunes]] charts.<ref>{{cite web|title=Ibhanoyi - Blaq Diamond - Chart History {{!}} Top Charts|url= https://www.top-charts.com/s/ibhanoyi-blaq-diamond|work=Top Charts}}</ref>
At the [[26th South African Music Awards|26th ceremony]] of [[South African Music Awards]], "Ibhanoyi" won Record of the Year.<ref>{{cite web|title=Blaq Diamond bags Record of The Year Award at SAMA26 grand finale|url=https://www.iol.co.za/amp/entertainment/music/local/blaq-diamond-bags-record-of-the-year-award-at-sama26-grand-finale-a3203c19-4891-4e7f-b48c-e39f80087039|first=Entertainment |last=Reporter |access-date =August 7, 2020|work=Independent Online}}</ref>

On January 23, 2020, their  third single "Love Letter" was released.<ref>{{cite web|title=WATCH: Blaq Diamond Drop Compelling Visuals For Latest Single, “Love Letter”|url=https://www.zkhiphani.co.za/watch-blaq-diamond-drop-compelling-visuals-for-latest-single-love-letter/ |first=Mandisa|last=Ntsinde|work=Zkhiphani}}</ref> The song peaked number 3 on iTunes charts and number 11 on Radio Airplay Charts.<ref>{{cite web|title=Love Letter Charts|url=https://www.top-charts.com/s/love-letter-blaq-diamond|work=Top Charts}}</ref>

On 31 January 2020, their second studio  album ''Umuthi'' was released.<ref>{{cite web|title=Listen to Blaq Diamond’s New Album ‘Umuthi’ - OkayAfrica|url=https://www.okayafrica.com/amp/blaq-diamond-umuthi-album-2644988067|first=Sabelo|last=Mkhabela|work= OkayAfrica|access-date=31 January 2021}}</ref> The album  features  [[Sjava]], Ci Ci, [[Thee Legacy|Thee legacy]] and Igcokama Elisha.  The album produced  three  singles "Memeza", "Love Letter" and "Ibhanoyi. The album  debuted at number 1 on [[iTunes]] Albums Charts.<ref>{{cite web|title=Blaq Diamond's Umuthi Album Rolls In At Number 1 |url=https://www.ubetoo.com/news/blaq-diamonds-umuthi-album-rolls-in-at-number-1/|first=John |last=Jabulani|date=3 February 2020|work=Ubetoo}}</ref>

On June 2020, they established Umuthi Records joint-venture  with [[Ambitiouz Entertainment]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Blaq Diamond In Partnership With Ambitiouz Entertainment Unveils "Umuthi Records|url=https://www.ubetoo.com/news/blaq-diamond-in-partnership-with-ambitiouz-entertainment-unveils-umuthi-records/|first=John|last= Jabulani |access-date=20 June 2020|work=Ubetoo}}</ref> In mid of 2020, the duo made collaboration on  "Price to pay" and "Uhulumeni" by DJ Miss Pru. In November 20, their single "Summer YoMuthi"  was released <ref>{{cite web|title=Blaq Diamond Release Their New Single "SummerYoMuthi" {{!}} Slikouronlife |url=https://slikouronlife.co.za/blog/18891/at-blaqdiamond150-release-their-new-single-summeryomuthi|first=Theo|last=Manjo|access-date=November 20, 2020|publisher=Slikouronlife}}</ref> and debuted number one on ''Channel 0''  Top 30 music chart.<ref>{{cite web|title=Channel O - Blaq Diamond, Mduduzi, Big Zulu Dominate — Channel O Top 30 |url=https://channelo.dstv.com/news/blaq-diamond-mduduzi-big-zulu-dominate-channel-o-top-30|publisher=Channel O Top 30  |access-date= March 3, 2021}}</ref>

=== 2021-present: new projects ===
In June 4, they released a single "Messiah" featuring Dumi Mkokstad. The song garnned 3 millions views on YouTube.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://fakazanews.com/2021/08/11/blaq-diamonds-messiah-hits-over-3million-views/|title=Blaq Diamond's 'Messiah' hits over 3million views|last=Bukola|work=Fakaza News|location=South Africa |language=en |date=August 11, 2021}}</ref> 

At the 6th ceremony of  [[All Africa Music Awards]]  their song "Summer YoMuthi" received eight nominations  includes; Best Male Artiste in Southern Africa, Artiste of the Year in Africa; Song of the Year in Africa; Producer of the Year in Africa; Best Male Artiste in African Inspirational Music; Breakout Artiste of the Year; Best Artiste, Duo or Group in African Pop; Best African Duo, Group or Band.<ref>{{cite web|title=AFRIMA 2021: Blaq Diamond, Focalistic, Wizkid lead nominations - Daily Post Nigeria|url=https://dailypost.ng/2021/09/23/afrima-2021-blaq-diamond-focalistic-wizkid-lead-nominations/|date=September 23, 2021|first=Wale |last=Odunsi|work=Daily Post Nigeria}}</ref> In October 29, their single "Italy" was released.<ref>{{cite web|title=Blaq Diamond Drop Their New Song & Visuals Titled "Italy" {{!}} Slikouronlife |url=https://slikouronlife.co.za/blog/20713/at-blaqdiamond150-drop-their-new-song-visuals-titled-italy|publisher=Slikouronlife|first=Theo|last=Manjo|access-date= October 29, 2021}}</ref>
