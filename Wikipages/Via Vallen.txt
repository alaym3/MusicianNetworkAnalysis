'''Maulidia Octavia''' (born 1 October 1991), known professionally as '''Via Vallen''', is an Indonesian [[dangdut]] singer.<ref name="JPost">{{cite news|url=http://www.thejakartapost.com/news/2018/01/06/guess-what-via-vallen-finds-her-career-journey-crazily-amazing.html|title=Guess what?: Via Vallen finds her career journey as crazily amazing|newspaper=[[The Jakarta Post]]|date=6 January 2018|access-date=3 February 2018}}</ref><ref name="Antara Via-Dad">{{cite news|url=https://www.antaranews.com/berita/679396/via-vallen-terharu-saat-duet-dengan-sang-ayah|title=Via Vallen terharu saat duet dengan sang ayah|trans-title=Via Vallen was touched in a duet with her father|website=[[Antara (news agency)|Antara News]]|language=id|first=Slamet Agus|last=Sudarmojo|date=22 January 2018|access-date=3 February 2018}}</ref> Her stage name, "Vallen", is taken from [[Evanescence]]'s first album ''[[Fallen (Evanescence album)|Fallen]]''.<ref name="Via Dangdut Queen">{{cite news|url=https://www.jawapos.com/read/2017/05/13/129604/mengenal-lebih-dekat-via-vallen-penguasa-panggung-dangdut-saat-ini|title=Mengenal Lebih Dekat Via Vallen, "Penguasa" Panggung Dangdut Saat Ini|last=Afrilene|first=Resvia|date=13 May 2017|access-date=3 February 2018|language=id|trans-title=Getting closer to Via Vallen, the current "Queen" of Dangdut|website=[[Jawa Pos Group|Jawa Pos]]}}</ref>

Via started a career in singing at the age of 15.<ref name="JPost"/><ref name="Via-Past Okezone">{{cite news|url=https://celebrity.okezone.com/read/2018/01/06/205/1840997/sebelum-tenar-via-vallen-ungkap-pernah-jadi-pengamen|title=Sebelum Tenar, Via Vallen Ungkap Pernah Jadi Pengamen|trans-title=Before fame, Via Vallen said once been a street busker|website=[[Media Nusantara Citra|Okezone]]|language=id|first=Revi C.|last=Rantung|date=6 January 2018|access-date=3 February 2018}}</ref> Her presence in the Indonesian show-business is often considered similar to [[Isyana Sarasvati]] because of the resemblance of her face.<ref name="Via-Isyana">{{cite news|url=http://entertainment.kompas.com/read/2018/01/18/205203510/disebut-mirip-isyana-sarasvati-via-vallen-pasrah|title=Disebut Mirip Isyana Sarasvati, Via Vallen Pasrah|trans-title=Considered looks like Isyana Sarasvati, Via Vallen surrendered|newspaper=[[Kompas]]|language=id|first=Dian Reinis|last=Kumampung|date=18 January 2018|access-date=3 February 2018}}</ref> Via has a support group of fans from different cities named Vyanisty, inaugurated on 24 September 2010. Her solo career began with the release of her first single "Selingkuh" in early 2015.<ref name="Via-WI">{{cite web|url=http://wanitaindonesia.co.id/index.php?view=viewarticle&id=17110061|title=Via Vallen, Warna Baru Dangdut Koplo|trans-title=Via Vallen, the new color of Dangdut Koplo|website=[[Wanita Indonesia]]|language=id|author=Hanik|date=14 November 2017|access-date=3 February 2018}}</ref> Her single "Sayang" is her most popular song, having reach more than 100 million views on YouTube, making it as the second most viewed Indonesian music video of 2017.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.thejakartapost.com/life/2017/12/07/youtube-reveals-most-popular-indonesian-videos-of-2017.html|title=YouTube reveals most popular Indonesian videos of 2017|newspaper=[[The Jakarta Post]]|first=Jessicha|last=Valentina|date=7 December 2017|access-date=1 May 2018}}</ref> Via is also a dangdut singer who sang the theme song for the [[2018 Asian Games]], "Meraih Bintang" (lit. "[[Energy of Asia: Official Album of Asian Games 2018#Track listing|Reach for The Stars]]").<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.thejakartapost.com/life/2018/07/01/via-vallen-sings-asian-games-official-theme-song.html|title=Via Vallen sings Asian Games official theme song|newspaper=The Jakarta Post|date=1 July 2018|access-date=14 October 2018}}</ref>

==Background==
Via Vallen is a dangdut singer from [[East Java]] who has been known as a national artist.<ref name="JPost"/> Via is also successful in bringing the genre of [[koplo]] and earned the nickname "Pop Queen Koplo" because the pop songs are sung in the rhythm of dangdut koplo along with a number of famous dangdut music groups such as New Pallapa, SERA, Monata and others. Her work became more widely known when a number of national television stations invited her to become a guest star on the Inbox [[SCTV (Indonesia)|SCTV]] Carnival.<ref name="Via Dangdut Queen"/>

Via has been invited to the 2016 Anugerah Dangdut Indonesia [[MNCTV]] award ceremony, and has performed with a number of famous artists such as [[Dewi Persik]], Zaskia Gotik, [[Raffi Ahmad]], [[Soimah Pancawati]] and others.<ref name="Antara Via-Dad"/>

In addition to having fans named Vyanisty, Via Vallen is herself a fan of [[Beyonce]] and [[Mariah Carey]]. Via claimed not to love dangdut as a teenager, preferring [[pop rock]] music including that by the American rock band [[Evanescence]], and singer [[Avril Lavigne]].<ref name="Via Dangdut Queen"/>

Her stage name Vallen is taken from the first album [[Evanescence]] titled ''[[Fallen (Evanescence album)|Fallen]]''. Via Vallen joined Sera, an Indonesian dangdut band, in 2008. She appeared in televised concerts in Hong Kong and China in 2016.<ref name="Antara Via-Dad"/><ref name="Via Dangdut Queen"/>

Via's maternal family are from [[Bandung]] and [[Aceh]]. Via started her career in entertainment in second grade of junior high school. Since 5th grade she attended gigs as a trainee singer, with her father who is himself a dangdut musician.<ref name="Via Dangdut Queen"/><ref name="Via-WI"/><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.viva.co.id/showbiz/musik/987255-dari-panggung-hajatan-via-vallen-kini-jadi-artis|title=Dari Panggung Hajatan, Via Vallen Kini Jadi Artis|trans-title=From the stage invitation, Via Vallen is now an artist|website=[[Visi Media Asia|Viva]]|language=id|date=14 December 2017|access-date=3 February 2018}}</ref>

Via released her first single entitled "Selingkuh" in 2015. Next she issued singles such as "Secawan Madu", "Sayang", "Sakit Sakit Hatiku", "Baper", "Peternak Luka", and many more, and her first album titled ''The Names'' was released in 2016.<ref name="Via-Album">{{cite web|url=https://celebrity.okezone.com/read/2017/08/07/205/1750989/via-vallen-siap-comeback-dengan-album-baru-bulan-depan|title=Via Vallen Siap Comeback dengan Album Baru Bulan Depan|trans-title=Via Vallen is ready to comeback with a new album next month|website=[[Media Nusantara Citra|Okezone]]|language=id|author=Sumarni|date=7 August 2017|access-date=3 February 2018}}</ref>

Via Vallen won the 2017 SCTV Music Awards Most Popular Dangdut Singer category, winning this award against competition from a number of famous dangdut singers.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.en.netralnews.com/news/lifestyle/read/13087/via.vallen.edges.ayu.ting.ting.to.win.2017.indonesian.dangdut.awards|title=Via Vallen edges Ayu Ting Ting to win 2017 Indonesian Dangdut Awards|website=Netral News|first=Christophorus Aji|last=Saputro|date=14 October 2017|access-date=10 January 2018}}.</ref> Via also received the Most Popular Female Dangdut Singer in Indonesian Dangdut Award (IDA) 2017 and also get award from DWP (Dangdut Wilayah Pantura) for Best Singer ter-WP.

In 2018, she performed the [[2018 Asian Games]] official theme song "Meraih Bintang", a pop dangdut song written by Parlin Burman "Pay" Siburian.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.cnnindonesia.com/hiburan/20180607101534-227-304195/via-vallen-bakar-semangat-asian-games-lewat-meraih-bintang#|title=Via Vallen Bakar Semangat Asian Games lewat "Meraih Bintang"|trans-title=Via Vallen keep Asian Games spirit through "Meraih Bintang"|website=[[CNN Indonesia]]|language=id|first= Muhammad Andika|last=Putra|date=7 June 2018|access-date=18 June 2018}}</ref> On 6 July 2018, it was announced that she will perform alongside 107 bands from 30 countries at the International Indie Music Festival at [[Indonesia Convention Exhibition]]. The event will be held on 27 September – 7 October 2018.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.bintang.com/celeb/read/3582879/erk-hingga-via-vallen-juga-ramaikan-international-indie-music-festival/page-2|title=Efek Rumah Kaca hingga Via Vallen juga Ramaikan International Indie Music Festival|trans-title=Efek Rumah Kaca to Via Vallen will enliven the International Indie Music Festival|website=Bintang.com|language=id|first=Anto|last=Karibo|date=9 July 2018|access-date=9 July 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.republika.co.id/berita/senggang/musik/18/07/09/pbl7in313-antusiasme-menyambut-iimf-2018|title=Antusiasme Menyambut IIMF 2018|trans-title=Enthusiasm welcomes the 2018 IIMF|newspaper=[[Republika (Indonesian newspaper)|Republika]]|language=id|first=Agung|last=Sasongko|date=9 July 2018|access-date=9 July 2018}}</ref>

==Style==
Via does not seek to imitate the style of traditional dangdut singers, instead aims at a more fresh, young, and modern style, a shared characteristic of Korean women or the Japanese [[Japanese street fashion|Harajuku]] style.<ref name="Via Dangdut Queen"/><ref name="Via-WI"/>

==Personal life==
Via Vallen has been harassed by a famous footballer based in Indonesia via Instagram direct message. She shared her harassment conversation with the footballer on Instagram and told him to stay away from her. Via Vallen did not disclose the account that harassed her but some reports and evidences said that the account belonged to [[Persija]] player [[Marko Šimić (footballer, born 1988)|Marko Šimić]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.liputan6.com/showbiz/read/3549716/via-vallen-mengaku-dilecehkan-oknum-pesepak-bola-terkenal|title=Via Vallen Mengaku Dilecehkan Oknum Pesepak Bola Terkenal|publisher=Liputan 6|language=id|date=5 June 2018|access-date=5 June 2018}}</ref>
