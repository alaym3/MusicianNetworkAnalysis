'''Aly Spaltro''', better known by her stage name '''Lady Lamb''' (formerly called '''Lady Lamb the Beekeeper''') is an [[United States|American]] songwriter and musician.<ref name="AMG"/>  Spaltro first began writing music in 2007 while working at Bart & Greg's DVD Explosion, the local video rental store in her hometown of [[Brunswick, Maine]]. She is a [[Brunswick High School (Maine)|Brunswick High School]] graduate.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Laclaire |first1=Hannah |title=Lady Lamb to perform at Brunswick High School as part of new ‘Generation Text’ series |url=https://www.pressherald.com/2019/11/22/lady-lamb-to-perform-at-brunswick-high-school-as-part-of-new-generation-text-series/ |work=Times Record |date=November 25, 2019}}</ref> Spaltro worked the closing shift, and would experiment with and record music all night into the morning.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://thewoundedjukebox.com/2013/07/18/twj-interviews-lady-lamb-the-beekeeper/ |title=TWJ Interviews: Lady Lamb The Beekeeper |publisher=Thewoundedjukebox.com |date=2013-07-18 |access-date=2015-04-05}}</ref> The first Lady Lamb recordings were [[home recordings]] distributed in handmade packages to the local Bullmoose<ref>{{Cite web|title=Bull Moose|url=https://www.bullmoose.com/|access-date=2021-11-16|website=www.bullmoose.com}}</ref> Record Store in [[Brunswick, Maine]].

In 2010, Spaltro moved to Brooklyn, New York. In 2012, Spaltro met producer [http://www.nadimissa.com/ Nadim Issa] and recorded her debut studio record, ''[[Ripely Pine]]'' at Let Em In Music in Gowanus, Brooklyn. It was released February 19, 2013 on Brooklyn's Ba Da Bing Records.

In 2014, Spaltro recorded and co-produced her sophomore studio album, ''[[After (Lady Lamb album)|After]]'', with Nadim Issa at Let Em In Music. Late that same year, Spaltro signed with Mom + Pop Music and released the album ''After'' on March 3, 2015.<ref name="AMG">{{cite web|author=Mark Deming |url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/after-mw0002799965 |title=After - Lady Lamb the Beekeeper &#124; Songs, Reviews, Credits, Awards |publisher=[[AllMusic]] |date=2015-03-03 |access-date=2015-04-25}}</ref> In December 2016 she released the seven track EP "Tender Warriors Club".<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://consequenceofsound.net/2016/12/stream-lady-lambs-new-ep-tender-warriors-club/|title=Stream: Lady Lamb’s new EP Tender Warriors Club|last=Brennan|first=Collin|date=December 1, 2016|newspaper=Consequence of Sound|language=en-US|access-date=2017-02-02}}</ref>

In 2019, Spaltro released [[Even in the Tremor|''Even in the Tremor'']], her third studio album.
