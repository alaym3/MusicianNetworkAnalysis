'''Alexander "Alex P" Papaconstantinou''' ([[Greek language|Greek]]: Αλέξανδρος "Άλεξ" Παπακωνσταντίνου; born 29 December 1979 in [[Brännkyrka]], [[Sweden]]) is a Greek-Swedish songwriter and music producer. He has worked with a number of artists including [[Jennifer Lopez|Jennifer López]], [[Paulina Rubio]], [[Enrique Iglesias]], [[Antique (duo)|Antique]], [[Elena Paparizou]], [[Arash (singer)|Arash]], [[Anna Vissi]], [[Cameron Cartio]], [[Eleni Foureira]], [[Sarbel]] and [[Tamta]]. In 2011, Papaconstantinou adopted the production [[pseudonym]] '''Alex P''' and started collaborating with record producer [[RedOne]], joined RedOne's production and [[RedOne#2101 Songs|2101 songs]] writing team. His collaboration continued until 2014. Along with Adam Baptiste, Papaconstantinou has also been credited as a featured artist under the name "The WAV.s".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.discogs.com/artist/2343316-WAVs-The|title=WAV.s, The|work=[[Discogs]]|access-date=30 July 2014}}</ref> Since 2017, he has been a member of the pop band VAX. Papaconstantinou has also written and co-written a number of notable songs for [[Eurovision Song Contest]] for [[Sarbel]] (Greece), [[Aysel Teymurzadeh|Aysel]] and [[Arash (entertainer)|Arash]] (Azerbaijan), [[Ivi Adamou]], [[Eleni Foureira]] and [[Tamta]] (Cyprus).

==Collaboration with RedOne==
In 2011, Papaconstantinou adopted the production [[pseudonym]] '''Alex P''' and started collaborating with record producer [[RedOne]], joined RedOne's production and [[RedOne#2101 Songs|2101 songs]] writing team. This collaboration lasted three years until 2014, during which time he co-wrote and co-produced the [[Enrique Iglesias]] hit song "[[I Like How It Feels]]" and co-wrote and co-produced the [[Khaled (musician)|Khaled]] album ''[[C'est la vie (Khaled album)|C'est la vie]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.redoneprod.com/team-red/|title=Team Red|publisher=RedOne|access-date=28 May In 2017, Papaconstantinou co-founded and became a member of the music trio '''VAX''', along with singer Teddy Sky and guitarist Viktor Svensson.<ref name="ESC1">{{cite web|url=https://eurovision.tv/story/cyprus-eurovision-entry-2018|title=Cyprus enlists award-winning composer Alex Papaconstantinou for 2018 - Eurovision Song Contest Lisbon 2018|last=Jordan|first=Paul|date=15 September 2017|publisher=Eurovision.TV|access-date=27 January 2018}}</ref> VAX released the song "6 in the Morning" featuring the Polish singer-songwriter [[Margaret (singer)|Margaret]] followed  by "Fireproof" featuring band lead singer Teddy Sky.
