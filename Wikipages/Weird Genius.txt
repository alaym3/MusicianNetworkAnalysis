'''Weird Genius''' is an [[Indonesia]]n [[Disc jockey|DJ]] and record production group based [[Pop music]], [[Electronic dance music|EDM]], and [[synth-pop]] consisting of three members are [[Reza Oktovian]], [[Eka Gustiwana]], and [[Gerald Liu]]. This group was formed in 2016 and released their first song in 2017 with the title ''DPS''.

== History ==
Weird Genius started composing music together in 2016. Previously, they already had their respective projects. As soon as they decided to join, all their abilities poured fully into this collaborative endeavor. They take inspiration from anywhere about love to life, social to gaming. When asked about the description of Weird Genius' music, they described it as ''hard, upbeat, pumping, crazy-drop''. This style of music that they deliberately built from the beginning.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Weird Genius - Profil Artis Indonesia|url=http://bicaramusik.id/profil/w/weird-genius/|access-date=2020-12-09|website=Bicara Musik|language=id}}</ref>

However. on 25 September 2019, Billy Taner decided to leave the group and was eventually replaced by Gerald Liu.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Adjie|date=2019-09-26|title=Dua Tahun Berkarya, Weird Genius Umumkan Hengkangnya Satu Personilnya|url=https://www.velvetmedia.id/post/dua-tahun-berkarya-weird-genius-umumkan-hengkangnya-satu-personilnya|access-date=2020-12-09|website=velvetmedia|language=en}}</ref>

On 28 February 2020, Weird Genius release the song "Lathi" featuring Sara Fajira on YouTube and viral at social media specially on TikTok.<ref name=":5">{{Cite web|last=Rura|first=Cecylia|date=2020-02-27|title=Weird Genius Gandeng Rapper asal Surabaya di Lagu Lathi|url=https://www.medcom.id/hiburan/musik/8koBadRb-weird-genius-gandeng-rapper-asal-surabaya-di-lagu-lathi|url-status=live|access-date=2021-06-06|website=medcom.id|language=id}}</ref> Some of Weird Genius's achievements with their song "Lathi" in 2020 include: #1 on [[Spotify]] Indonesia Top 50, #2 on [[Spotify]] Viral Top 50 Global, #1 on [[Spotify]] Viral Top 50 Indonesia, #1 in [[iTunes]] Indonesia Top 200, #1 in [[Deezer]] Indonesia Top 300, #1 in [[JOOX]] Indonesia Top 100, #1 on Resso Top 30 Global, #1 on Resso Indonesia Top 20, #1 on [[TikTok]] Global 20, on #1 [[Shazam (application)|Shazam]] Chart Indonesia and topped song dates on some of the top radio stations in major cities in Indonesia.<ref>{{Cite web|title=EDM meets traditional Javanese elements in a chart topper called 'Lathi' by Weird Genius|url=https://mixmag.asia/read/edm-traditional-javanese-elements-chart-topper-by-weird-genius-local|access-date=2020-12-09|website=Mixmag Asia}}</ref>

On 30 June 2020, [[Spotify]] released a press release stating that the song "Lathi" has broken a new record as a local song that has topped the # 1 longest chart for 6 consecutive weeks on Spotify Indonesia's Top 50 Chart.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cnnindonesia.com/hibur/20200630183840-227-519190/lagu-lathi-geser-rekor-hindia-di-spotify|title = Song Lathi Shifts Indies Record on Spotify|date =30 June 2020|website = CNN Indonesia|language = id-ID}}</ref>

On 31 July 2020, Weird Genius have collaboration single with several international DJs to collaborate on music, including [[Yellow Claw (DJs)|Yellow Claw]] featuring Reikko with the title track "Hush".<ref name=":4">{{Cite web|date=2020-08-04|title=Yellow Claw & Weird Genius Release Sentimental "Hush"|url=https://oneedm.com/edm-releases/yellow-claw-weird-genius-release-sentimental-hush/|access-date=2021-06-06|website=One EDM|language=en-US}}</ref>

On 18 August 2020, Weird Genius officially signed with American record label [[Astralwerks]] for international music activities.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.liputan6.com/showbiz/read/4333485/weird-genius-resmi-direkrut-astralwerks-satu-label-dengan-marshmello|title=Weird Genius Resmi Direkrut Astralwerks|last=Genius|first=Weird|date=18 August 2020|website=Liputan6|language=id-ID}}</ref>

Weird Genius won Best of the Best Production Work, Best Sound Production Team, and Best Male/Female Solo Artist/Group/Dance Collaboration for the song "Lathi" at the 23rd Anugerah Musik Indonesia on 26 November 2020.<ref name=":0" /> The day after that, they released a collaborative song with [[League of Legends: Wild Rift]], called "All In" and for vocals they collaborated with Singaporean singer, [[Tabitha Nauser]].<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|date=2020-11-27|title=Weird Genius Drop 'All In' featuring Tabitha Nauser To Mark The Release Of RIOT's League Of Legends 'Wild Rift'|url=https://www.cultr.com/news/weird-genius-drop-all-in-featuring-tabitha-nauser-to-mark-the-release-of-riots-league-of-legends-wild-rift/|access-date=2021-06-06|website=CULTR|language=en-US}}</ref>

On 04 December 2020, Weird Genius released a remixed version of "Lathi" consisting of three remixes by [[R3hab]], Sihk, and RayRay.<ref name=":2" />

On 05 February 2021, they released "Last Summer", a collaboration with Tokyo Machine and Canadian singer [[Lights (musician)|Lights]] via Monstercat.<ref name=":3">{{Cite web|last=Scilippa|first=Phil|title=Tokyo Machine, Weird Genius, and Lights Release Emotive Single "Last Summer"|url=https://edm.com/music-releases/tokyo-machine-weird-genius-lights-last-summer|access-date=2021-06-06|website=EDM.com - The Latest Electronic Dance Music News, Reviews & Artists|language=en}}</ref> The accompanying animated music video for the track was later released on 24 May 2021.<ref>{{Citation|title=Tokyo Machine & Weird Genius - Last Summer feat. Lights (Official Music Video)|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vx3WIVcsTFU|language=en|access-date=2021-06-06}}</ref>

On 10 September 2021, Weird Genius released "Future Ghost" featuring Thai-Belgian singer [[Violette Wautier]].<ref name=":6" />
