{{Nihongo|'''Masatoshi Ono'''|小野正利|Ono Masatoshi|born January 29, 1967 in [[Adachi, Tokyo]], Japan}}, also known as '''Sho''', is a Japanese rock/heavy metal singer-songwriter and vocal coach. Ono got his start in the 1980s as vocalist of the heavy metal band Fort Bragg. In 1992, he released his first solo single,  "Pure ni Nare", on [[Sony Music Entertainment|Sony Records]].<ref name="profile">{{cite web| publisher=Shibuya Television| title=Profile| language=Japanese| accessdate=November 4, 2011| url=http://www.onomasatoshi.com/html/profile.html| url-status=dead| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090908153158/http://www.onomasatoshi.com/html/profile.html| archivedate=September 8, 2009}}</ref> It was not until his third single, "You're the Only...", that he began to receive notable attention. The single helped Ono win the "Rookie of the Year" award at the [[34th Japan Record Awards]] and an invitation to that year's ''[[Kōhaku Uta Gassen]]''.

In 2009, Ono became the vocalist of the [[power metal]] band [[Galneryus]].<ref>{{cite web| title=Japan is winning this heavy metal game: Galneryus| publisher=Heavy Metal Infinity| date=November 2, 2009| accessdate=November 4, 2011| url=http://heavymetalinfinity.blogspot.com/2009/09/japan-is-winning-this-heavy-metal-game.html| archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120425135351/http://heavymetalinfinity.blogspot.com/2009/09/japan-is-winning-this-heavy-metal-game.html| archive-date=April 25, 2012| url-status=dead}}</ref> His first single in eight years, "Departure!", was used as the opening theme song for the 2011 ''[[Hunter × Hunter]]'' anime.<ref name="hunter">{{cite web| date=March 14, 2012 | title=2011 Hunter x Hunter Anime's New Theme Songs Listed | url=http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/interest/2012-03-14/2011-hunter-x-hunter-anime-new-theme-songs-listed | publisher=[[Anime News Network]] | accessdate=March 14, 2012}}</ref> "Departure! -Second Version-" was used as the series' second opening theme, with Galneryus' "Hunting for Your Dream" as the second ending theme.<ref name="hunter"/> Ono sings "Fight It Out!!", the opening theme song for the international versions of the final arc of ''[[Dragon Ball Kai]]''.

In 2010 he established his own vocal school "Bese".
