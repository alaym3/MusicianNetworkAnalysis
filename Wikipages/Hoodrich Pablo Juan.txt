'''Sterling Leroy Pennix Jr.''' (born October 28, 1989), better known by his stage name '''Hoodrich Pablo Juan''', is an American rapper. He is best known for his track "We Don't Luv Em".<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=|title=Hoodrich Pablo Juan’s New Song “We Don’t Luv Em” Goes Crazy|url=https://www.thefader.com/2017/09/14/hoodrich-pablo-juan-we-dont-luv-em-premiere-fader-doc|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=July 12, 2020|website=The Fader}}</ref>

== Career ==
Pennix started pursuing a rap career in 2014 after he released a song titled "Go Get Money" and featured on a [[Peewee Longway]] track titled "African Diamonds".<ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=|title=Hoodrich Pablo Juan Is Developing His Sound and Learning From Gucci Mane|url=https://www.xxlmag.com/hoodrich-pablo-juan-deisgner-drugz-interview/|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=July 12, 2020|website=XXL}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=|title=The Break Presents: Hoodrich Pablo Juan|url=https://www.xxlmag.com/hoodrich-pablo-juan-interview-the-break/|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=July 12, 2020|website=XXL}}</ref> He released a string of mixtapes during 2015 to 2017, and was later signed to [[Gucci Mane]]'s label [[1017 Records]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=|title=Gucci Mane Signs Hoodrich Pablo Juan, Lil Wop to 1017 Eskimo|url=https://www.xxlmag.com/gucci-mane-signs-hoodrich-pablo-juan-lil-wop/|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=July 12, 2020|website=XXL}}</ref> He gained attention with his track "We Don't Luv Em".<ref name=":0" />
