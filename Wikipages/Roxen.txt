'''Roxen''' ({{IPA-sv|ˈrɔ̌ksɛn}})<ref>{{cite book|url=http://runeberg.org/ortnamn/0024.html|first1=Jöran |last1=Sahlgren|first2=Gösta |last2=Bergman|title=Svenska ortnamn med uttalsuppgifter|language=sv|year=1979|page=20}}</ref> is a medium-sized [[lake]] in south-central [[Sweden]], east of Lake [[Vättern]], part of the waterpath [[Motala ström]] and the [[Göta Canal]]. South of Lake Roxen is the city [[Linköping]].

Roxen, especially the western parts, is very good for [[birdwatching]]. There are natural reserves at Kungsbro<ref>{{cite web |title=Kungsbro|url=http://www5.e.lst.se/reservat/lst/reservat.asp?id=32&sprak=eng |website=www5.e.lst.se |access-date=7 November 2017 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070902155110/http://www5.e.lst.se/reservat/lst/reservat.asp?id=32&sprak=eng |archive-date=2 September 2007 |url-status=dead}}</ref> and Svartåmynningen.<ref>{{cite web |title=Svartåmynningen |url=http://www5.e.lst.se/reservat/lst/reservat.asp?id=26&sprak=eng |website=www5.e.lst.se |access-date=7 November 2017 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070902155110/http://www5.e.lst.se/reservat/lst/reservat.asp?id=26&sprak=eng |archive-date=2 September 2007 |url-status=dead}}</ref>

The lake develops in a [[graben]] depression.<ref>{{cite book |editor1-last=Mattsson |editor1-first=Jan O. |title=Terrängformer i Norden. |date=1984 |publisher=Nordiska ministerrådet |isbn=91-38-08241-1 |page=133 |url=https://www.researchgate.net/publication/263926981 |language=sv}}</ref>
