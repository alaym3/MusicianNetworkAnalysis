'''Abdurrahim Akça''', better known by his stage name '''Eypio''' (born 2 February 1983), is a [[Turkish people|Turkish]] [[rapper]], [[songwriter]] and musician.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/tr/artist/eypio/id921532626?l=tr|title=Eypio, Apple Music'te|website=Apple Music}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hurriyet.com.tr/eypio-liseden-atildim-20-ulke-gezdim-40112362|title=Eypio: Liseden atıldım 20 ülke gezdim|publisher=}}</ref>

== Early life and career ==
Eypio was born to an [[Turkmens#Turkmen in Afghanistan|Afghan Turkmen]] migrant family.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://biletinial.com/muzik/eypio|title=Eypio}}</ref> He is active in music industry since 2002, founded his own studio in June 2009. Eypio records some of his songs in his own studio but some of his works have been produced by other production companies. His studio provides information on rap music and rap for new rappers.<ref name="webkurnaz.net"/><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.raptun.com/sanatcilar/a-p-o/ |title=Raptun.com |publisher=Raptun.com |date=2018-08-18 |accessdate=2018-08-28}}</ref> In 2013, he changed his stage name from A.P.O. to Eypio. In 2013, his song "Ayrım Yok" was made into a music video. In 2014, new clips were mafe for his songs "Kral Çıplak" and "Ay Kızım" form the album ''Beton Duvar''.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://itunes.apple.com/tr/album/ay-k-z-m-single/id1076675961?l=tr# |title=Archived copy |access-date=2018-08-28 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160923230736/https://itunes.apple.com/tr/album/ay-k-z-m-single/id1076675961?l=tr# |archive-date=2016-09-23 |url-status=dead }}</ref> On 24 December 2015, the music video of his song with [[Burak King]] titled "Günah Benim" was released on YouTube which has more than 230 million views.<ref name="youtube1">{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ul0qEpswXj8|title=Eypio & Burak King - #Günah Benim ( Official Video )|last=3 Adım Müzik|date=28 December 2015|publisher=|via=YouTube}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.sivashabermerkezi.com/kultur-sanat/eypio-burak-king-gunah-benim-dinle-sozleri-h4297.html# |title=Archived copy |access-date=2018-08-28 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180828111338/http://www.sivashabermerkezi.com/kultur-sanat/eypio-burak-king-gunah-benim-dinle-sozleri-h4297.html# |archive-date=2018-08-28 |url-status=dead }}</ref> He later began working on his new album.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sozcu.com.tr/hayatim/kultur-sanat-haberleri/eypio-album-hazirliklarina-basladi/|title=Eypio albüm hazırlıklarına başladı|publisher=}}</ref> On 17 June 2016, together with Burak King, they published a piece for the national team titled "Ay Bizim Yıldız Bizim".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wVW6wX0Lo2g|title=Eypio ft. Burak King - Ay Bizim Yıldız Bizim|last=EyPiO Official|date=17 June 2016|publisher=|via=YouTube}}</ref> On 1 July 2016, his album ''Günah Benim'' was released.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/tr/album/gunah-benim/id1123096015?l=tr|title=Eypio adlı sanatçının Günah Benim albümü|date=29 September 2017|publisher=}}</ref>
