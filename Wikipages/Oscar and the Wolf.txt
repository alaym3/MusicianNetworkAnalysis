'''Oscar and the Wolf''' is the moniker for Belgian solo artist Max Colombie.

== Life and career ==

Oscar and the Wolf first gained international attention with the single "Orange Sky" in 2013, which got them support slots for the likes of [[Lou Reed]], but gained critical acclaim and success with debut album ''Entity'', released in April 2014. This album departed from the sound of Colombie's indie folk EPs and ventured into a new electronic sound. It was the first time Max Colombie took control, and the album was co-produced and mixed by [[Leo Abrahams]].

'Entity' includes the tracks "Princes", "Undress" and "Strange Entity". It was received reviews in Belgium and abroad, including praise from [[Drowned in Sound]] and [[Les Inrockuptibles|Les Inrocks]], and went double platinum in Belgium.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cuttingedge.be/muziek/oscar-and-wolf-entity|title=Oscar and The Wolf, 'Entity'|language=nl|publisher=cuttingedge.be|date=3 May 2014|access-date=19 October 2014}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://drownedinsound.com/releases/18480/reviews/4148371|title=Drowned In Sound|access-date=August 31, 2016|archive-date=September 24, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160924073032/http://drownedinsound.com/releases/18480/reviews/4148371|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.lesinrocks.com/musique/critique-album/oscar-and-the-wolf-entity/|title=Les Inrocks}}</ref>

'The Game', the first single off his second album, was released in July 2016. It peaked at n° 14 on the [[Hype Machine]] chart.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://hypem.com/track/2hna8/Oscar+&+the+Wolf+-+The+Game|title=Hypem}}</ref>

In 2015, after extensive touring across Europe, including a support tour for [[Róisín Murphy discography|Roisin Murphy]], Oscar and the Wolf sold out [[Sportpaleis]] (Antwerp, Belgium) and [[Heineken Music Hall]] (Amsterdam, Netherlands), with a total of 27000 tickets. In 2016, after headlining [[Pukkelpop]] festival, he sold out both [[Ancienne Belgique]] (Brussels, Belgium) and [[Paradiso (Amsterdam)|Paradiso]] (Amsterdam) in just 4 minutes. He announced new shows at Sportpaleis and Heineken Music Hall immediately after.

In an interview with Pi-Pôle, Max revealed the meaning behind his band's name: "Oscar is a name I really like – it's poetic and light. And wolf is the dark animal that comes out at night and howls to the full moon, and moon stands for solitude and coldness. It's bright and dark together, because that's how I see my music – as a balance between light and dark."<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.pi-pole.com/artiste.php?id_artiste=309|title=Pi-Pôle: Oscar and The Wolf|language=en|date=August 2014|access-date=8 October 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304042302/http://www.pi-pole.com/artiste.php?id_artiste=309|archive-date=March 4, 2016|url-status=dead}}</ref>
