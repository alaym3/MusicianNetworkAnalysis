'''Bravo''' ({{lang-ru|Бра́во}}) is a [[rock and roll]] band founded in 1983 in Moscow, Russia<ref>[http://www.po4emu.ru/drugoe/history/index/music/stat_music/7.htm История группы Браво]</ref> by guitarist Evgeny Havtan.

== Biography ==
[[File:Evgeniy Havtan 2008.jpg|thumb|200px|Evgeny Havtan in 2008]]
[[File:Zhanna Aguzarova, DSC 4142.jpg|thumb|200px|Zhanna Aguzarova in 2007]]
[[File:Robert Lenz.jpg|thumb|200px|Robert Lenz in 2008]]
Drawing heavy inspiration from 1950s western music, Bravo was a part of the Soviet rock and roll revival of the 1980s, along with [[Secret (Russian band)|Secret]]. Their first album was made in 1983.

Despite the fact that at that time rock and roll and beat music (except for [[The Beatles]]) were less popular among Soviet citizens than classic rock, the band was one of the most popular underground acts in Russia in the 1980s, until the departure of original lead singer Zhanna Aguzarova ([[:ru:Жанна Агузарова]])<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://pesnyagoda.nm.ru/molod.html |title=Фестиваль молодых исполнителей Юрмала-Ялта-Москва |access-date=2016-07-09 |archive-url=https://archive.today/20120713222122/http://pesnyagoda.nm.ru/molod.html |archive-date=2012-07-13 |url-status=dead }}</ref> in 1988. Since then Bravo has achieved success with several different singers, [[Valeriy Syutkin]]<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qleHGo_6uCA Утренняя почта, клип «Вася»]</ref> (1990-1994) and Robert Lenz (since 1996).

In 2011, after a ten-year break from studio recordings, Bravo released an album ''Fashion'' (''{{lang-ru|Мода}}''), which received highly positive reviews from critics<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.intermedia.ru/news/230328?page=1|title="Браво" — "Мода"|author=Алексей Мажаев.|date=2011-09-06|publisher=InterMedia|accessdate=2011-09-10|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111123121131/http://www.intermedia.ru/news/230328?page=1|archivedate=2011-11-23|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.afisha.ru/cd/1981|title=Возвращение главной гитарной поп-группы Тынды и Еревана|author=Иван Сорокин.|date=2011-10-11|work=[[Афиша (журнал)|Афиша]]|accessdate=2011-10-11|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120207210912/http://www.afisha.ru/cd/1981/|archivedate=2012-02-07|url-status=live}}</ref> and good attention from younger audiences. The band recorded the album using vintage instruments from the 50s and 60s.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.soyuz.ru/afisha/evgeniy_havtan|title=Евгений Хавтан ("Браво"): Винил и пластиковые кружочки звучат по-разному!|author=Александр В. Волков.|publisher=[[Союз (концерн)|Soyuz Music]]|accessdate=2011-11-14|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304135544/http://www.soyuz.ru/afisha/evgeniy_havtan/|archivedate=2016-03-04|url-status=dead}}</ref> The album was produced by [[Ghian Wright]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.openspace.ru/music_modern/projects/20749/details/30040|title=Браво. "Между Западом и Востоком"|author=Александр Великанов-младший.|date=2011-09-10|publisher=[[OpenSpace.ru]]|accessdate=2011-09-11|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111123184314/http://www.openspace.ru/music_modern/projects/20749/details/30040/|archivedate=2011-11-23|url-status=dead}}</ref> The album cover includes a photography of [[Audrey Hepburn]] from the US-American romantic comedy ''[[Roman Holiday]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.best-woman.ru/showbiz/108|title="Мода" группы "Браво"|author=Екатерина Архипова, Артем Костенко.|publisher=Best-Woman.ru|accessdate=2011-11-17|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111025163218/http://www.best-woman.ru/showbiz/108/|archivedate=2011-10-25|url-status=dead}}</ref>
