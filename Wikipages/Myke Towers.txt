'''Michael Anthony Torres Monge''' <ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.vibe.com/2015/10/premiere-mike-towers-dinero-en-mano-video|title=Premiere: Mike Towers Plays The Culprit In "Cash On Hand" Video|work=[[Vibe (magazine)|Vibe]]|last=Estevez|first=Marjua|date=October 9, 2015|accessdate=August 20, 2020}}</ref> (born January 15, 1994), known by his stage name '''Myke Towers''', is a Puerto Rican rapper, singer and songwriter.<ref>{{cite magazine |last=Fernandez |first=Suzette |title=9 New Latin Urban Acts Who Scored Their First Hot Latin Songs Hit in 2019 |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/latin/8510559/latin-urban-acts-first-hot-latin-song-hit |magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] |date=May 9, 2019 |accessdate=July 19, 2019}}</ref>

== Career ==
His mixtape ''El Final del Principio'' (2016) peaked at 12 on [[Latin Rhythm Albums]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/charts/latin-rhythm-albums/2016-04-02|title=Latin Rhythm Albums : Page 1|last=|first=|date=|website=Billboard|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2019-07-20}}</ref> Towers collaborated with [[Bad Bunny]] with the song "Estamos Arriba" released in June 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-latin/bad-bunny-new-song-estamos-arriba-video-myke-towers-848409/|title=Watch Bad Bunny’s ‘Estamos Arriba’ Video With Myke Towers|last=Kreps|first=Daniel|date=2019-06-14|website=Rolling Stone|access-date=2019-07-13}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thefader.com/2019/06/18/bad-bunny-estamos-arriba-lil-keed-sleater-kinney-best-songs|title=10 songs you need in your life this week|last=Trilling|first=Ava|date=2019-06-18|website=The FADER|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2019-07-13}}</ref> On July 5, 2019, [[Piso 21]] released the single "Una Vida Para Recordar" featuring Towers.<ref name="Fernandez">{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/latin/8518696/piso-21-myke-towers-una-vida-para-recordar|title=Piso 21 Returns With Romantic Single 'Una Vida Para Recordar' Featuring Myke Towers: Watch|last=Fernandez|first=Suzette|date=2019-07-05|website=Billboard|access-date=2019-07-13}}</ref> He collaborated with [[Becky G]] with the song "Dollar"; a music video was released July 12, 2019.<ref name="billboard.com">{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/latin/8519685/viva-friday-becky-g-myke-towers-raquel-sofia|title=Viva Friday: New Latin Jams by Becky G with Myke Towers, Raquel Sofía and More|last=Fernandez|first=Suzette|date=2019-07-12|website=Billboard|access-date=2019-07-13}}</ref> His first studio album ''[[Easy Money Baby]]'' was released on January 23, 2020. His EP ''PARA MI EX'' was released on December 16, 2020. His second album, ''LYKE MIKE'', was released on April 23, 2021.<ref>{{Cite web|last=abril 23|first=Por Equipo MHNoticiasen|last2=2021|title=Myke Towers lanzó "Lyke Mike", su segundo álbum de estudio|url=https://musica-hoy.com/noticias/myke-towers-lanzo-lyke-mike/|access-date=2021-05-25|language=es}}</ref>
