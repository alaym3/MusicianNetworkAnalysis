'''Marcin Przybyłowicz''' (born {{Birth date|1985|5|4|df=y}})<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.eurogamer.pl/articles/2016-06-08-marcin-przybylowicz-i-wiedzmin-3-o-pasji-do-muzyki-i-pracy-nad-grami|title=Marcin Przybyłowicz i Wiedźmin 3 - o pasji do muzyki i pracy nad grami|work=Eurogamer.pl|access-date=2018-09-03|language=pl}}</ref> is a Polish [[composer]] and [[sound design]]er. He is mostly known for his contributions as music director and lead composer for ''[[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt]]'',<ref>{{Cite web |title="We don't define folk" Says The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt Composer |url=http://www.gamepressure.com/e.asp?ID=8 |website=Gamepressure.com |accessdate=2015-09-16 |date=2014-08-21}}</ref> and as the sound designer for ''[[The Vanishing of Ethan Carter]]''.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.igf.com/2015finalists.html#game_8022 |title=The 17th Annual Independent Games Festival Finalists |accessdate=2016-03-12 |publisher=[[Independent Games Festival]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.vgmonline.net/awards2014nominations/ |title=Annual Game Music Awards 2014 Nominations |date=30 December 2014 |accessdate=2016-03-12 |publisher=Video Game Music Online}}</ref> He also composed the score to a Polish historical television drama series ''[[Korona królów]]'' produced by [[Telewizja Polska]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.filmpolski.pl/fp/index.php?osoba=11107223|title=FilmPolski.pl|work=FilmPolski|access-date=2018-06-15|language=pl}}</ref>

In an interview with ''Headliner Magazine'' about how the soundtrack and score for ''[[Cyberpunk 2077]]'' was created, he said: “We quickly found out that the key word for our work on Cyberpunk was attitude. The music is written for a dangerous, dirty world that doesn’t play fair.”<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://headlinermagazine.net/gaming/cyberpunk-2077-inside-the-score.html|title = Cyberpunk 2077: Inside the Score}}</ref>
