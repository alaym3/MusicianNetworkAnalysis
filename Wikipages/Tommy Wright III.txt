== Musical career ==
Growing up in Memphis, Tommy Wright began producing music using the four-track recording equipment. Between 1992 and 2001, he released five self-produced solo albums, one with N.O.D., and two more with group Ten Wanted Men, owned, operated and produced the entirety of Street Smart Record's discography and previewed – if possibly never distributed – a street documentary, Behind Closed Doors, for which there's also a soundtrack.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://daily.redbullmusicacademy.com/2017/08/memphis-rappers|title=The Unsung Heroes of Early Memphis Rap}}</ref>

=== Resurgence ===
Despite stepping back from the music industry after 2001's ''Behind Closed Doors (Da Soundtrack)'', Tommy Wright III maintained a cult following through the 2000s and 2010s.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://daily.redbullmusicacademy.com/2018/11/tommy-wright-iii|title = Still Runnin-N-Gunnin: The Rebirth of Memphis Rap Legend Tommy Wright III}}</ref>

Tommy Wright III collaborated with [[Bay Area]] rapper [[Lil B]] on Secret, a song on the latter's 2019 mixtape, ''Loyalty Casket''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.datpiff.com/profile/LilBtheBasedGod|title = DatPiff :: The Authority in Free Mixtapes}}</ref>
