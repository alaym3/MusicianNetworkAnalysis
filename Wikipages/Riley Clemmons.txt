'''Riley Elizabeth Clemmons'''<ref name="ASCAP">{{cite web|title=BETTER FOR IT Work ID No.894268039 IPI No.779748265 (CLEMMONS RILEY ELIZABETH) |url=https://www.ascap.com/Home/ace-title-search/index.aspx|publisher=[[American Society of Composers, Authors and Publishers]]|author=[[American Society of Composers, Authors and Publishers]]|accessdate=January 6, 2015}} BETTER FOR IT Work ID No.894268039 IPI No.779748265 (CLEMMONS RILEY ELIZABETH)</ref> is an American [[Christian music]]ian, who plays [[contemporary Christian music|Christian pop]] style [[contemporary worship music]]. She is known for her Christian radio hits, "[[Broken Prayers]]" and "Better for It".<ref>{{cite web|title="Broken Prayers" Proves To Be Breakthrough Debut For Riley Clemmons Single Resonates With Radio Alongside Over Two Million Streams And Views |url=https://www.jesusfreakhideout.com/news/2018/03/09.BrokenPrayersProvesToBeBreakthroughDebutForRileyClemmons.asp|publisher=JFHNews|author=JFHNews|accessdate=August 7, 2018}}</ref> She released her debut self-titled album via American music distributor [[Capitol CMG]] on August 3, 2018.<ref name="Debut">{{cite web|title=Riley Clemmons Releases Debut Album Today
 |url=http://www.hallels.com/articles/19814/20180804/riley-clemmons-releases-debut-album-today.htm|publisher=Hallels|author=Tmothy Yap|accessdate=August 7, 2018}}</ref>

==Early and personal life==
Riley Clemmons was "discovered" at a school pageant in Nashville by manager, Mitchell Solarek.<ref>{{cite web|title=ARTIST PROFILE Riley Clemmons|url=https://www.newreleasetoday.com/artistdetail.php?artist_id=5752|publisher=NewReleaseToday|author=NewReleaseToday|accessdate=August 7, 2018}}</ref> She would begin writing songs at the age of 13 and played shows around her hometown.<ref>{{cite web|title=Riley Clemmons – MAXIMUM ARTIST|url=http://www.maximumartist.com/rileyclemmons|publisher=Maximum Artist Group|accessdate=August 7, 2018}}</ref> She has worked on her musical craft for several years, including eight years of vocal lessons and a decade of dance lessons.<ref>{{cite web|title=Riley Clemmons – BMG|url=https://www.bmg.com/de/artist/Riley-Clemmons|publisher=BMG|author=BMG|accessdate=August 7, 2018}}</ref> She is currently signed to Capitol CMG.

==Music career==
In 2015, Clemmons signed to the independent label, Maxx Recordings. Her lyrics are inspired by the struggles of her relationships and faith. She wants to use the power of music and words to wake up and encourage the generation she is a part of.<ref name="MaxxRecordings">{{cite web|url=https://btscelebs.com/2015/11/06/interview-maxx-recordings-singer-riley-clemmons-timing-is-everything/|title=[Interview] Maxx Recordings’ Singer Riley Clemmons: "Timing Is Everything!"|work=BTSCELEBS|accessdate=August 7, 2018}}</ref>

===2017–present: Breakthrough and self-titled debut studio album===
Clemmons released her debut single on Capitol CMG, "Broken Prayers", on December 8, 2017.<ref name=BrokenPrayers>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/riley-clemmons/1399660616|title=Riley Clemmons by Riley Clemmons
|work=Billboard|accessdate=August 7, 2018}}</ref> A music video was released on the same day.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cBDt_-tIfLI|title=Riley Clemmons – Broken Prayers (Official Video)|via=YouTube|accessdate=August 7, 2018}}</ref> She discussed her story behind the song in a video uploaded to her YouTube channel, "The song "Broken Prayers" came truly from a place of brokenness and that feeling of having to get yourself together and bring the best most picture-perfect version of yourself to God, basically feeling like the broken pieces aren't good enough for God. The song came from a place of truly finding peace in the fact that God takes you at your most broken, at your lowest place and at your roughest. And not only does He take you there but He delights in it. And He genuinely loves you in that place."<ref>{{cite web|url=http://freeccm.com/2018/02/20/behind-the-song-riley-clemmons-shares-the-heart-behind-her-single-broken-prayers/|title=Behind The Song: Riley Clemmons Shares The Heart Behind Her Single "Broken Prayers" |work=FreeCCM|accessdate=August 7, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bSbllLLrbUM|title=Riley Clemmons – Broken Prayers (Song Story) |via=YouTube|accessdate=August 7, 2018}}</ref> The song was released to Christian radio and peaked at No. 17 on the [[Hot Christian Songs]] chart.

She released her second single, "Better For It," on June 8, 2018.<ref name=Better>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/better-for-it/1392787021?i=1392787226|title=Better For It – Single
|publisher=iTunes Store|accessdate=August 7, 2018}}</ref> A music video was also released.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=c5cgC8H0uXc|title=Riley Clemmons – Better For It |via=YouTube|accessdate=August 7, 2018}}</ref> Clemmons announced she will join [[Danny Gokey]] and [[Tauren Wells]] for "The Hope Encounter" Tour in September until November.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.jesusfreakhideout.com/news/2018/05/25.RileyClemmonsDropsBetterForItOnHeelsOfBreakthroughHit.asp|title=Riley Clemmons Drops 'Better For It' On Heels of Breakthrough Hit |work=JFHNews|accessdate=August 7, 2018}}</ref> The song peaked at No. 37 on the Hot Christian Songs chart. A music video for "Hold On" was released on July 19, 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I9F8R9R8XVs|title=Riley Clemmons – Hold On |via=YouTube|accessdate=August 7, 2018}}</ref>

Clemmons' self-titled debut studio album was released on August 4, 2018.<ref name="Debut"/> The album debuted at No. 28 on the Top Christian Albums chart and No. 13 on the US Heatseekers Albums chart.
