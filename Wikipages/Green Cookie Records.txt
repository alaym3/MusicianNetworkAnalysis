'''Green Cookie Records''' is an [[independent record label]] based in [[Thessaloniki]], [[Greece]].<ref>[http://www.musicportal.gr/alpha?lang=el&tb=diskografikes&alpha=name&r=record_companies&i=GREEN+COOKIE+RECORDS Musicportal.gr]</ref> The label releases [[instrumental]] [[Surf music|surf rock]], [[garage rock]], [[rock and roll]] and everything in between and has an international roster of artists.

==Surf bands==
One band that had its first release on the label was French surf band, Les Agamemnonz.<ref>''Soul Kitchen'', 04/01/2016 - [https://www.soul-kitchen.fr/62026-les-mythiques-agamemnonz Interviews › Les mythiques Agamemnonz - Louis]</ref> Another was Los Protones (The Protons) which are a Peruvian group whose music is psychedelia, surf and rock. Their album was ''20 Monstruos! 2007-2015''.<ref>''[[Perú.21]], 23/11/2015 - [https://peru21.pe/espectaculos/protones-debutan-noche-brasil-204869 Los Protones debutan esta noche en Brasil<br>Herederos del sonido The Ventures ofrecen uno de los actos de música instrumental —psicodelia, surf y rock— más interesantes de Latinoamérica.]</ref> Japanese surf group, [[The El Caminos]] had their ''Behind the Surf'' album released in 2016.<ref>''Boppin' Around'', 29 December 2016 - [http://www.boppinaround.nl/RECENSIES-2016-2/RECENSIES-2016-2.htm OUT NOW! ''CD Recensies'' BEHIND THE SURF/ THE EL CAMINOS]</ref>
