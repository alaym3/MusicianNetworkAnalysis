'''Keron Joel Foriest''' (born August 3, 2000), known by his stage name '''Ron Suno''', is an American [[Drill music|drill rapper]],<ref>{{Cite web|title=Comedian Turned Rapper Ron Suno Taps Blueface for "Pinocchio" Remix|url=https://www.newsbreak.com/news/1558222039994/comedian-turned-rapper-ron-suno-taps-blueface-for-pinocchio-remix|access-date=2020-09-12|website=News Break|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-02-27|title=New Age Hip-Hop Emerges From The Bronx On Episode 1 of Who Is: Ron Suno|url=https://www.hot97.com/hip-hop-news/new-age-hip-hop-emerges-from-the-bronx-on-episode-1-of-who-is-ron-suno/|access-date=2020-09-12|website=Hot97|language=en-US}}</ref> songwriter, comedian and youtuber. In June 2020, Suno released his debut studio album, ''Swag Like Mike'',<ref>{{Cite web|last=Schneider|first=Calvin|date=2020-06-20|title=New Music: Ron Suno – 'Swag Like Mike'|url=https://musicxclusives.com/index.php/2020/06/20/new-music-ron-suno-swag-like-mike/|access-date=2020-09-12|website=MusicXclusives {{!}} We Play It First|language=en-US}}</ref> dedicated to entertainment legends such as Michael Jordan, Mike Tyson and [[Michael Jackson]] who inspired his art.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Bijur|first=Ben|date=2020-06-19|title=Get To Know Ron Suno and his latest project 'Swag Like Mike'|url=https://kazimagazine.com/editorials/get-to-know/meet-ron-suno-the-worlds-first-comedian-drill-rapper/|access-date=2020-09-12|website=KAZI. MAGAZINE|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Ron Suno: The First Comedian Drill Rapper|url=https://flaunt.com/content/ron-suno-the-first-comedian-drill-rapper|access-date=2020-09-12|website=Flaunt Magazine|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-06-19|title=Ron Suno - Swag Like Mike|url=https://rapsource.net/digital/ron-suno-swag-like-mike/|access-date=2020-09-12|website=RAPSOURCE.NET|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Rah Swish & Ron Suno Connect On DJ Drewski's "MOP"|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/rah-swish-and-ron-suno-connect-on-dj-drewskis-mop-new-song.1988526.html|access-date=2020-09-12|website=HotNewHipHop|language=en}}</ref>

== Early life ==
Keron Foriest was born in Co-op City, a cooperative housing development in the northeast section of Bronx. Suno started entertaining at the age of 13 when he began recording drill rap songs in a tiny home studio in Bronx  but only gained online audience attention with his comedy on social media platforms such as YouTube, Instagram and TikTok.<ref>{{Cite web|last1=July 29|first1=Robby Seabrook IIIPublished|last2=2020|title=The Break Presents: Ron Suno|url=https://www.xxlmag.com/ron-suno-interview-the-break/|access-date=2020-09-12|website=XXL Mag|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Rodriguez|first=Tommy|date=2020-05-29|title=Ron Suno Makes a Movie for "Nunchucks" Music Video|url=https://www.bleumag.com/2020/05/29/ron-suno-makes-a-movie-for-nunchucks-music-video/|access-date=2020-09-12|website=BLEU|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-05-20|title='Hip Hop Harry' Comeback: Folks Parody The 2000s Kids Show With Hilarious Dancing|url=https://globalgrind.com/playlist/hip-hop-harry-parody-2000s-kids-show-hilarious-dancing/|access-date=2020-09-12|website=Global Grind|language=en}}</ref> In 2017, he started a social media trend #WeaveChallenge, which helped him gain over half a million Instagram followers within months.<ref>{{Cite web|title=EXCLUSIVE: Ron Suno on Going Viral with the "Weave Challenge"|url=https://www.vladtv.com/article/261919/ron-suno-on-going-viral-with-the-weave-challenge|access-date=2020-09-12|website=www.vladtv.com}}</ref>

== Music career ==
In early 2019, Ron began building fans base as a drill rapper by releasing music videos such as ''With My Crew'', ''Party on Jump'' and his seven-song EP on YouTube and promoting them on Instagram. His musical breakthrough came in October 2019 with the release of a hit song and video ''Pinnochio,'' an upbeat with a danceable flow receiving 15 million views on TikTok, over 9 million on YouTube with about 6 million plays on Spotify.<ref>{{Cite web|last=https://hiphopdx.com|first=HipHopDX-|date=2020-09-04|title=DJ Drewski, Rah Swish & Ron Suno Recreate Famous 'Belly' Scene In 'MOP' Video|url=https://hiphopdx.com/news/id.57711/title.dj-drewski-rah-swish-ron-suno-recreate-famous-belly-scene-in-mop-video|access-date=2020-09-12|website=HipHopDX}}</ref> Later he did a remix of ''Pinnochio'' featuring Los Angeles musician [[Blueface]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=New Music Alert! Ron Suno Drops The Visual To 'Pinocchio' Remix Featuring Blueface {{!}} News Break|url=https://www.newsbreak.com/new-york/bronx/news/1558053243292/new-music-alert-ron-suno-drops-the-visual-to-pinocchio-remix-featuring-blueface|access-date=2020-09-12|website=News Break Bronx, NY|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Comedian Turned Rapper Ron Suno Taps Blueface for "Pinocchio" Remix|url=https://www.newsbreak.com/news/1558222039994/comedian-turned-rapper-ron-suno-taps-blueface-for-pinocchio-remix|access-date=2020-09-12|website=News Break|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=You are being redirected...|url=https://theknockturnal.com/premiere-ron-suno-drops-pinocchio-remix-ft-blueface-talks-upcoming-ep-youtube-series-and-more/|access-date=2020-09-12|website=theknockturnal.com}}</ref> In February 2020, Suno released a single and music video, ''Spider-Man'' featuring fellow New York rapper [[Fivio Foreign]].

In June 2020, amid COVID-19 pandemic Suno released his debut studio album with 9 tracks featuring 5 artists under SBL Music / UnitedMasters record label. Later he released a single, ''Wraith'' featuring [[DDG (entertainer)|DDG]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=You are being redirected...|url=https://theknockturnal.com/ron-suno-premieres-new-single-wraith-featuring-ddg/|access-date=2020-09-12|website=theknockturnal.com}}</ref> In October 2020, Suno dropped a single ''Netflix'' in audio and visual.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Jones|first=Jiggy|date=2020-10-30|title=The Source {{!}}Ron Suno Drops Visuals For Latest Track, "Netflix"|url=https://thesource.com/2020/10/30/ron-suno-drops-visuals-for-latest-track-netflix/|access-date=2020-11-02|website=The Source|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-10-31|title=RON SUNO RELEASES AUDIO AND VISUAL FOR NEW SONG "NETFLIX"|url=https://hiphopsince1987.com/2020/music/ron-suno-releases-audio-and-visual-for-new-song-netflix/|access-date=2020-11-02|website=Home of Hip Hop Videos & Rap Music, News, Video, Mixtapes & more|language=en-US}}</ref> NBA 2K League affiliate of the Atlanta Hawks known as The Hawks Talon Gaming Club in November 2020, included Suno as one of the celebrity guests on its new virtual game show where fans could play against their favorite celebrities.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-11-17|title=The Launch Of Hawks Talon Takeover on Twitch Featuring Ron Suno & More|url=https://ukhiphoptalk.com/the-launch-of-hawks-talon-takeover-on-twitch-featuring-ron-suno-more/|access-date=2020-11-24|website=UK Hiphop Talk|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=November 17|first=Itoro Umontuen {{!}} on|last2=2020|date=2020-11-17|title=Atlanta Hawks' E Sports team announces "Talon Takeover" {{!}} The Atlanta Voice|url=https://www.theatlantavoice.com/articles/atlanta-hawks-e-sports-team-announces-talon-takeover/|access-date=2020-11-24|website=The Atlanta Voice {{!}} Atlanta GA News}}</ref>
