'''The Island''' (also known as '''Newgate Tower''',<ref name="cad">{{cite news|url=http://www.croydonadvertiser.co.uk/firefighters-and-helicopter-called-out-after-man-has-bbq-on-balcony/story-30496366-detail/story.html|title=Croydon tower block evacuated after man has barbecue on balcony|date=27 August 2017|publisher=Local World}}{{deadlink|date=December 2019}}</ref> and previously '''ISLAND'''<ref name="developcroydon.com">{{cite web |url=http://www.developcroydon.com/news/4786/IYLO-development-restarts-as-039Island039-apartments |title=IYLO development restarts as 'Island' apartments |accessdate=20 June 2013|author=Jo Gumb|date=18 June 2013|url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131203023402/http://www.developcroydon.com/news/4786/IYLO-development-restarts-as-039Island039-apartments |archivedate=3 December 2013}}</ref> and '''IYLO''') is a residential skyscraper in the [[London Borough of Croydon]], [[London]]. In 2018, the building's 17th-floor penthouse apartment was described as the "most expensive flat in Croydon".<ref name="ca18"/>

The building was designed to have 20 floors of apartments and a roof height of {{convert|61|m|ft}}.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.skyscrapernews.com/buildings.php?id=2325|title=Island, London - Building #2325|work=SkyScraperNews.com}}</ref> The tower is elliptical in plan comprising two equal halves which appear to "slide" past one another.<ref name="wan">[http://www.emporis.com/en/wm/bu/?id=streetstreetjamesssite-london-unitedkingdom Island, Croydon] Emporis, 2006</ref> The architects of the building are Darling Associates Architecture,{{fact|date=December 2019}} with a number of other firms working on the building including Phoenix Logistics, E3 Property International, Lancsville Construction, Jones Lang LaSalle, Matthew Consultants, Macfarlane Wilder, Scott Wilson, Scott Wilson, Cole Jarman Associates and DP9.{{fact|date=December 2019}}

The tower is part of the [[Croydon Vision 2020]] regeneration plan for [[Croydon]]{{fact|date=December 2019}} to add to its goal of being "London's Third City".{{fact|date=December 2019}}

== Transport links ==
The building is located at the northern end of [[Croydon]] town centre and is close to transport links in the town. The nearest railway station is [[West Croydon railway station|West Croydon]]. There is a bus station at West Croydon. [[Tramlink]] services from West Croydon are on the Croydon Loop and go eastbound only towards East Croydon and beyond. The closest tram stop for westbound services is [[East Croydon station]].
