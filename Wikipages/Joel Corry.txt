'''Joel Corry''' (born 10 June 1989) is an English DJ, producer and television personality. He came to prominence in 2019 with the release of his single "[[Sorry (I Didn't Know)#Joel Corry version|Sorry]]", featuring vocals from Hayley May (who was not credited in the official music video), which reached number six in the [[UK Singles Chart]]. In 2020, Corry released the singles "[[Lonely (Joel Corry song)|Lonely]]" and "[[Head & Heart]]". The latter, which features [[MNEK]] on vocals, spent six weeks at number one in the UK Singles Chart and also became Corry's first entry on the US [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]]. Corry stated that he was influenced by [[UK garage]] growing up, and that it played a "huge role in his development as a DJ and a producer".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.perfecthavoc.com/news/joel-corry-sorry-enters-the-top-40-charts-at-38/|title=Joel Corry 'Sorry' Enters The Top 40 Charts At #38|publisher=Perfect Havoc|website=Perfecthavoc.com|date=26 July 2019|access-date=5 September 2019}}</ref>

==Career==
Corry featured on [[MTV (UK & Ireland)|MTV]] reality television series ''[[Geordie Shore]]'', alongside then-girlfriend [[Sophie Kasaei (television personality)|Sophie Kasaei]] until 2013. He also has a personal training business, an app called Joel Corry PT and a gym and leisurewear company Most Rated, which is also the name of his own record label.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.officialcharts.com/chart-news/who-is-joel-corry-the-man-behind-the-record-breaking-love-island-anthem-sorry__26964/|title=Who is Joel Corry? The man behind the record-breaking Love Island anthem Sorry|publisher=[[Official Charts Company]]|website=officialcharts.com|access-date=5 September 2019}}</ref> In 2015 he released his debut singles "Back Again" and "Light It Up". In 2017 he released the singles "Just Wanna", "All the Things", "All Night", "Sunlight" and "Feel This Way". He released the singles "Hurt", "All I Need", "Only You", "Good As Gold" and "Fallen" in 2018 which premiered on ''[[Our Culture Mag]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ourculturemag.com/2018/12/14/premiere-joel-corry-fallen-feat-hayley-may/|title=Premiere: Joel Corry 'Fallen' feat. Hayley May|last=Modestas|first=Mankus|date=December 14, 2018}}</ref> In April 2019, he released the single "[[Sorry (Joel Corry song)|Sorry]]"; the song features uncredited vocals from [[Hayley May]]. In July 2019, the song broke the record for the most Shazamed track in one day, with 41,000 [[Shazam (application)|Shazams]]. The song was featured on the [[ITV2]] reality television series ''[[Love Island (2015 TV series)|Love Island]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-03-11|title='I'm isolated': Joel Corry on bodybuilding, reality TV and his sacrifices to reach No 1|url=http://www.theguardian.com/music/2021/mar/11/chart-star-joel-corry-im-pretty-isolated-i-have-one-good-friend|access-date=2021-08-06|website=the Guardian|language=en}}</ref> The song peaked at number six on the [[UK Singles Chart]].

In July 2020, Corry achieved his first UK number-one single with the [[MNEK]] collaboration "[[Head & Heart]]". It stayed at number 1 for six consecutive weeks and became an international hit for Corry.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.officialcharts.com/charts/singles-chart/20200904/7501/|title=Official Singles Chart Top 100 &#124; Official Charts Company|website=www.officialcharts.com}}</ref> It was certified [[British Phonographic Industry|platinum]] for exceeding 600,000 units on 11 September 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bpi.co.uk/award/16587-5331-1|title = Award}}</ref>
