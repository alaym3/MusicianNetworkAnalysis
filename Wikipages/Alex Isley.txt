'''Alexandra Isley''' is an American singer-songwriter and producer. She released her debut EP ''The Love/Art Memoirs'' in 2012. Isley has worked with artists including [[Lucky Daye]], [[Tank and the Bangas]], [[Terrace Martin]], [[Masego]], and [[9th Wonder]].

== Early life and education ==
Born Alexandra Isley, she is the daughter of [[Ernie Isley]] of the prolific soul group [[the Isley Brothers]].<ref name="npr2020">{{Cite web|last=Carter|first=Bobby|date=2020-05-15|title=Alex Isley: Tiny Desk (Home) Concert|url=https://www.npr.org/2020/05/15/855404539/alex-isley-tiny-desk-home-concert|url-status=live|access-date=2021-04-01|website=NPR.org|language=en}}</ref> Isley spent most of her childhood in New Jersey before moving to Los Angeles.<ref name="essence" /> She named [[Stevie Wonder]], [[Prince (musician)|Prince]], [[Ella Fitzgerald]], [[Toni Braxton]], [[Aaliyah]], and [[Mariah Carey]] as early favorite musicians.<ref name="essence">{{Cite web|last=Callender|first=Samantha|date=2021-02-22|title=Alex Isley Is R&B’s Breath Of Fresh Air|url=https://www.essence.com/entertainment/alex-isley/|url-status=live|access-date=2021-04-01|website=Essence|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Sunnucks|first=Jack|date=2018-06-13|title=young nyc creatives on what aaliyah means to them|url=https://i-d.vice.com/en_uk/article/mbkk9a/young-nyc-creatives-on-what-aaliyah-means-to-them|url-status=live|access-date=2021-04-06|website=i-D Vice|language=en}}</ref> She loved singing from childhood and her family supported her pursuit of music as a professional career.<ref name="essence" />

She began classical vocal training at age 12 and later graduated from [[Los Angeles County High School for the Arts|LA County High School for the Arts]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Carter|first=Corein|date=2020-10-13|title=Alex Isley is Expanding On the Isley Legacy and Bringing Us Her Unique Jazz-Fusion Style|url=https://www.sheenmagazine.com/alex-isley-is-expanding-on-the-isley-legacy-and-bringing-us-her-unique-jazz-fusion-style/|access-date=2021-04-01|website=Sheen Magazine|language=en-US}}</ref> Isley received her bachelor's degree from [[University of California, Los Angeles|UCLA]], where she studied in the jazz department.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|last=Sumney|first=Moses|date=2012-11-02|title=Alex Isley Has Her Own Thing|url=https://www.laweekly.com/alex-isley-has-her-own-thing/|url-status=live|access-date=2021-04-01|website=LA Weekly|language=en-US}}</ref>

Isley has [[synesthesia]], and described the phenomenon to ''[[LA Weekly]]'': "There's a canvas in my mind, and the whole thing is painted, or it's a continuous wave of color...Sometimes I'll start composing something and I'll have to change the key because the color won't sit right with me."<ref name=":0" />

== Career ==
Isley released her self-produced debut EP ''The'' ''Love Art/Memoirs'' in 2012.<ref name=":0" /> [[Okayplayer]] reviewed the EP positively: "please believe Alex’s lush vocals over her own calming production is capable of spawning a generation of love babies much like “Between The Sheets” did."<ref>{{Cite web|author=Shamz|date=2012-07-10|title=Alex Isley - The Love/Art Memoirs [EP]|url=https://www.okayplayer.com/news/alex-isley-the-loveart-memoirs-ep.html|url-status=live|access-date=2021-04-06|website=Okayplayer|language=en-US}}</ref>

Isley described her music as r&b with hip hop and jazz influences.<ref name="essence" /> Samantha Callender of ''[[Essence (magazine)|Essence]]'' referred to her voice as "silky smooth vocals that ride neo-soul melodies."<ref name="essence" /> She has collaborated with artists such as [[9th Wonder]] on the track "Rain Clouds"; [[Lucky Daye]] and [[Masego (musician)|Masego]] on "Good & Plenty (Remix)"; Chromonicci on "Interstellar"; and Christian Kuria on "Toroka."<ref name="essence" /><ref>{{Cite web|last=Duong|first=Paul|date=2021-03-17|title=Alex Isley, Masego, Lucky Daye "Good & Plenty (Remix)"|url=http://rapradar.com/2021/03/17/alex-isley-masego-lucky-daye-good-plenty-remix/|url-status=live|access-date=2021-04-01|website=Rap Radar|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Robinson|first=Chloe|date=2021-03-18|title=Christian Kuria unleashes a stunning remix of "Toroka" featuring Alex Isley|url=https://earmilk.com/2021/03/18/christian-kuria-unleashes-a-stunning-remix-of-toroka-featuring-alex-isley/|access-date=2021-04-01|website=EARMILK|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-12-28|title=Chromonicci & Alex Isley Soar On 'Interstellar.'|url=http://www.soulbounce.com/2020/12/chromonicci-alex-isley-soar-on-interstellar/|url-status=live|access-date=2021-04-06|website=SoulBounce.com|language=en-US}}</ref>

In November 2019 she released the joint EP ''Wilton'' with producer Jack Dine.<ref name="soulbounce">{{Cite web|author=Ivory|date=2019-11-15|title=Alex Isley & Jack Dine Explore Love On 'Wilton' EP|url=https://www.soulbounce.com/2019/11/alex-isley-jack-dine-explore-love-on-wilton-ep/|url-status=live|access-date=2021-04-01|website=SoulBounce.com|language=en-US}}</ref> In May 2020 Isley performed a concert for ''[[NPR]]''<nowiki/>'s [[Tiny Desk Concerts|Tiny Desk]] series.<ref name="npr2020" /> That year she was also a featured vocalist for the [[Cautious Clay]] track "Reaching", on the soundtrack for ''[[Insecure (TV series)|Insecure]]'''s fourth season.<ref name="mtv">{{Cite web|last=Alston|first=Trey|date=2020-04-10|title=Cautious Clay And Alex Isley Argue About Love On Vibey 'Reaching'|url=http://www.mtv.com/news/3162718/cautious-clay-reaching-insecure-season-4-soundtrack-alex-isley/|url-status=live|access-date=2021-04-01|website=MTV News|language=en}}</ref>

== Personal life ==
Isley resides in Los Angeles.<ref name="npr2020" /> She has a daughter (b. 2017).<ref name="essence" />
