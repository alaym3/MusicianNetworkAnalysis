'''Phony Ppl''' is a musical group based in [[Brooklyn, New York]]. Originally founded in 2010<ref name="blue note jazz">{{cite web |title=Blue Note PHONY PPL feat. SPECIAL GUESTS MAX & CHRIS TURNER - 2016-04-13 |url=http://www.bluenotejazz.com/newyork/schedule/moreinfo.cgi?id=14171 |website=www.bluenotejazz.com |accessdate=6 June 2018}}</ref> with five members; the current members are Elbee Thrie (vocals), Elijah Rawk (lead guitar), Matt "Maffyuu" Byas (drums), Aja Grant (keyboard), and Bari Bass (bass guitar).<ref name="not mad">{{cite web |last1=Shaw-Faber |first1=Levi |title=Phony Ppl: The Most Famous Band You've Never Heard Of |url=http://notmad.us/2015/01/phony-ppl-the-most-famous-band-youve-never-heard-of/# |website=Not Mad |accessdate=6 June 2018 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180812200639/http://notmad.us/2015/01/phony-ppl-the-most-famous-band-youve-never-heard-of/ |archive-date=12 August 2018 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

==Background==
Members of Phony Ppl met in high school. Elbee (Robert Booker) and Aja first formed the group, with Dyme-A-Duzin (Donnovan Blocker), Bari Bass (Omar Grant), Elijah Rawk, Ian Bakerman, Temi O, Maffyuu, and Sheriff PJ joining later.<ref name="Afropunk">{{cite web |title=Phony Ppl, Real Talk: An Interview with the New York Collective + Video & Free Download {{!}} AFROPUNK |url=http://afropunk.com/2011/11/phony-ppl-real-talk-an-interview-with-the-new-york-collective-video-free-download/ |website=AFROPUNK |accessdate=6 June 2018 |date=11 November 2011}}</ref> In 2012, they released the album ''Phonyland''.<ref name="Mass Appeal" />

In January 2015, they released the album ''Yesterday's Tomorrow''. The album peaked at number 24 on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Top Heatseekers#Albums chart|Heatseekers Album chart]].<ref name="billboard chart">{{cite magazine |title=Phony Ppl Yesterday's Tomorrow Chart History |url=https://www.billboard.com/music/phony-ppl/chart-history/heatseekers-albums/song/875309 |magazine=Billboard |accessdate=6 June 2018}}</ref> It also peaked at number 15 on the Trending 140 chart and at number 7 on the Emerging Artists chart.<ref name="LA times">{{cite web |last1=Roberts |first1=Randall |title=Social media buzz the new barometer of a hit |url=https://www.latimes.com/entertainment/music/la-et-ms-ca-billboard-twitter-20150118-column.html |website=[[Los Angeles Times]] |accessdate=6 June 2018}}</ref> They released the single "This Must Be Heaven" in November 2016.<ref name="Fader">{{cite web |last1=Bundy |first1=Will |title=Hear Phony Ppl's Sunny New Single "This Must Be Heaven" |url=http://www.thefader.com/2016/11/07/phony-ppl-this-must-be-heaven |website=The FADER |accessdate=6 June 2018 |language=en}}</ref> They made their first television appearance on June 9, 2015 on ''[[Jimmy Kimmel Live!]]'' performing "[[Trap Queen]]" with [[Fetty Wap]].<ref name="illroots">{{cite web |title=Fetty Wap & Phony Ppl - Trap Queen + My Way (Live On Jimmy Kimmel) |url=http://illroots.com/watch/fetty-wap-phony-ppl-trap-queen-my-way-live-on-jimmy-kimmel |website=ILLROOTS |accessdate=6 June 2018}}</ref>

In October 2018, they released the single "Something About Your Love" (stylized as "somethinG about your love."). Their album ''mō’zā-ik.'' was released on October 19, 2018.<ref>http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2018/10/phony-ppl-mozaik-album-release/</ref> In January 2020, the group released "Fkn Around" with [[Megan Thee Stallion]];<ref>{{Cite web|title=Phony Ppl Enlists Megan Thee Stallion For Raunchy And Hilarious 'Fkn Around'|url=https://www.npr.org/2020/01/31/801444530/phony-ppl-enlists-megan-thee-stallion-for-raunchy-and-hilarious-fkn-around|website=NPR.org|language=en|access-date=2020-05-11}}</ref> their solo version was released in May.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Fkn Around - Single by Phony Ppl|url=https://music.apple.com/us/album/fkn-around-single/1511725076|website=Apple Music|language=en-us|access-date=2020-05-11}}</ref> In November, they released the single "On My Shit" with [[Joey Badass]].<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-11-13|title=Phony Ppl & Joey Badass Join Forces For "On My Shit" [New Single + Music Video]|url=https://respect-mag.com/2020/11/phony-ppl-joey-badass-join-forces-for-on-my-shit-new-single-music-video%e2%80%af%e2%80%80/|access-date=2020-11-16|website=RESPECT.|language=en-US}}</ref>
