'''Zelle''' is a Filipino alternative pop/rock band.<ref name="AgimatBio">{{Cite web |title=» #Agimat : Music » Zelle » ... |url=http://agimat.net/music/2002006.htm |access-date=2021-02-23 |website=agimat.net}}</ref>

==Career==
Formed by and named after the group's female vocalist, Jeazell Grutas, the band released their debut album ''Search for Warmth'' in 2007 on Sony BMG, which consists of 11 original songs.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/album/search-for-warmth-mw0001693630|title=Search for Warmth - Zelle - Songs, Reviews, Credits - AllMusic|website=AllMusic}}</ref>

At the [[Myx Music Awards 2008]], the band won the award for Favorite Bandarito Performance.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.pep.ph/guide/music/1759/zelle-talks-about-winning-in-the-myx-music-awards|title=Zelle talks about winning in the MYX Music Awards|first=Rommel|last=Gonzales|publisher=}}</ref>

In 2010, Grutas' collaboration with rap artist [[Gloc-9]], titled "Upuan", won five awards including Song of The Year at the 23rd [[Awit Awards]].<ref>{{Cite news |title=Gloc-9 bags 5 Awit awards |url=https://www.philstar.com/entertainment/2010/10/12/619761/gloc-9-bags-5-awit-awards |access-date=2021-02-23}}</ref>
