'''Astrid Melissa Edwarda Horn Weitzberg''' (born 8 April 1987), known as '''Melissa Horn''', is a [[Sweden|Swedish]] pop artist. She is the daughter of singer Maritza Horn.

==Biography==
She was born to singer [[Maritza Horn]] (born 1951), and Edvard Terrence Weitzberg (born 1955) in an area in [[Södermalm]], near Stockholm.  She has five siblings, all boys. Her parents got her a guitar when she turned 16 and not long afterwards she wrote her first song.  She took vocal courses and then got hold of a producer and eventually got a record contract before she finished high school.  In 2010, she started a company called Edward AB.{{Citation needed|date = April 2016}}

==Career==
Melissa Horn's debut album ''[[Långa nätter]]'' was released on 30 April 2008 followed by ''[[Säg ingenting till mig]]'' on 14 October 2009. This record eventually went gold, exposing her to a larger audience; she was nominated for Gramiss (Swedish equivalent of the Grammy Awards) in the categories Artist of the Year, Composer of the Year and Lyricist of the Year.<ref>[http://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/article14373484.ab]</ref> A sequel to that album ''[[Innan jag kände dig]]'' was launched on 16 September 2011 and topped the Swedish Album Charts within its first week of release. She promoted it with a tour in Norway, Denmark and Sweden. Her fourth album ''[[Om du vill vara med mig]]'' was released on 2 October 2013 and her fifth album entitled ''[[Jag går nu]]'' came out on 27 November 2015.  All her releases are in [[Swedish language|Swedish]].

Horn also sang "Kungsholmens hamn" in a memorial ceremony for the victims of the [[2011 Norway attacks]] in [[Utøya]] and [[Oslo]].<ref>[http://svenskdam.se/2011/08/victoria-i-svart-vid-minnesceremoni-i-oslo/ Victoria i svart vid minnesceremoni i Oslo], ''Svensk Damtidning'', 21 August  2011</ref><ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5p44lGaiDrw Melissa Horn - Kungsholmens hamn (Live Minnesceremoni Oslo 2011)]</ref>

After 4 years of hiatus since her last release, Melissa Horn released a new album ''[[:sv: Konstgjord andning (musikalbum av Melissa Horn)  | Konstgjord andning
]] '' on 13 September 2019. She is collaborating with singer [[Kaah]] and singer White Collar.
