'''TOPS''' is a Canadian [[indie rock]] band from [[Montreal]], [[Quebec]], which currently consists of Jane Penny, David Carriere, Riley Fleck, and Marta Cikojevic. They were formed in 2011 following the disbandment of [[synthpop]] group Silly Kissers, of which Carriere, Penny, and Gillies were members, along with notable [[Arbutus Records]] recording artist [[Sean Nicholas Savage]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://exclaim.ca/News/tops_announce_debut_full-length_tender_opposites |title=TOPS Announce Debut Full-length 'Tender Opposites'  |publisher=Exclaim! |date=2012-01-16 |accessdate=2013-08-14}}</ref> TOPS are prominent within the [[Arbutus Records]] and Canadian [[indie music scene]]s.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dazeddigital.com/music/article/16593/1/arbutus |title=The Warehouse Pop Of Arbutus Records  |publisher=Dazed Digital |date=2013-07-12 |accessdate=2013-08-14}}</ref> Their debut album, ''Tender Opposites,'' was released on February 28, 2012, in Canada and the United States, and October 1, 2012, in the rest of the world, to generally positive reviews. The album was named the eighth best album of 2012 by [[Gorilla vs. Bear]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.gorillavsbear.net/2012/12/02/gorilla-vs-bears-albums-of-2012/ |title=gorilla vs. bear's albums of 2012  |publisher=Gorilla Vs. Bear |date=2012-12-02 |accessdate=2013-08-14}}</ref> Their second studio album, ''Picture You Staring,'' was released in 2014 on [[Arbutus Records]] and featured artwork from LA-based artist Jessica Dean Harrison.
