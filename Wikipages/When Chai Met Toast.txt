'''When Chai Met Toast''' is a multilingual indie-folk alternative band<ref>{{Cite web|last=Prabhan|first=Pooja|date=2017-09-05|title=‘Chai’vinists and proud!|url=https://www.deccanchronicle.com/entertainment/music/050917/chaivinists-and-proud.html|access-date=2021-10-20|website=Deccan Chronicle|language=en}}</ref> formed in [[Kochi]] in 2016. The band’s lineup comprises lead vocalist Ashwin Gopakumar, guitarist Achyuth Jaigopal, keyboardist Palee Francis, and drummer Pai Sailesh. The band is known for their happy and lighthearted music which they create in [[English language|English]] and [[Hindi]], sometimes featuring [[Tamil language|Tamil]] and [[Malayalam]] lyrics.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-09-15|title=Asia Spotlight: India's When Chai Met Toast on sparking joy with their indie folk tunes|url=https://www.bandwagon.asia/articles/asia-spotlight-when-chai-met-toast-interview-India-Kochi-When-We-Feel-Young-indie-folk-All-That-Matters-2020|access-date=2021-10-20|website=Asia Spotlight: India&#39;s When Chai Met Toast on sparking joy with their indie folk tunes {{!}} Bandwagon {{!}} Music media championing and spotlighting music in Asia.|language=en}}</ref>

Having released two EPs and several singles, the band has been recognized as a rising artist in [[Spotify]]’s RADAR program.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Anna Ben appears in When Chai Met Toast’s new video|url=https://www.newindianexpress.com/entertainment/malayalam/2020/oct/08/anna-ben-appears-in-when-chai-met-toasts-new-video-2207211.html|access-date=2021-10-20|website=The New Indian Express}}</ref> The video of their single Break Free was chosen as one of [[VH1]] India’s top 50 hit videos. They have also developed the tracks for comedian [[Kenny Sebastian]]’s 2018 web series Die Trying.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2018-02-28|title=Life of a struggling musician|url=https://www.deccanherald.com/content/662029/life-struggling-musician.html|access-date=2021-10-20|website=Deccan Herald|language=en}}</ref>

When Chai Met Toast frequently performs at live events and music festivals in India, including Bacardi [[NH7 Weekender]], SulaFest, [[OnePlus]] Music festival and Red Bull Tour Bus. In August 2021, their music was featured in the trailer of [[Farhan Akhtar]]’s upcoming [[Bollywood]] film Jee Le Zaraa.<ref>{{Cite web|title=From Shirley Setia to Ritviz... Independent music is scoring the charts amid the pandemic|url=https://www.freepressjournal.in/entertainment/from-shirley-setia-to-ritviz-independent-music-is-scoring-the-charts-amid-the-pandemic|access-date=2021-10-20|website=Free Press Journal|language=en}}</ref>
