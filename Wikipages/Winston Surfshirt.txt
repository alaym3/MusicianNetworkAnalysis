'''Winston Surfshirt''' is an Australian funk, hip hop band formed in 2015 by lead vocals and multi-instrumentalist Winston, Bustlip on beats, The Bone on trombone, Bik Julio on bass guitar, Dool on keyboards and Mi-K on guitar.

== History ==

The group started when solo musician, Winston Surfshirt, formed the [[eponym]]ous six-piece funk, hip hop band in 2015 with Surfshirt on lead vocals and multi-instrumentals, Bustlip on beats, The Bone  on trombone, Bik Julio on bass guitar, Dool on keyboards and Mi-K on guitar and keys.<ref name="Schwartz">{{cite web | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20170622032645/https://rollingstoneaus.com/music/post/future-is-now-winston-surfshirt/6437 | url = https://rollingstoneaus.com/music/post/future-is-now-winston-surfshirt/6437 | work = [[Rolling Stone Australia]] | title = Future Is Now: Winston Surfshirt | last = Schwartz | first = Jacquelyn | archivedate = 22 June 2017 | date = 22 June 2017 | accessdate = 16 March 2018 }}</ref><ref name="Cameron"/> Their stylistic influences include both [[surf rock]] and [[West Coast hip hop]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://musicfeeds.com.au/news/winston-surfshirt-shares-new-single-album-announcement-national-tour-dates/|title=Winston Surfshirt Shares New Single, Album Announcement & National Tour Dates - Music Feeds|date=23 August 2017|publisher=}}</ref><ref name="Cameron">{{cite web|url=https://hhhhappy.com/old-tracks-new-cuts-and-future-tunes-with-winston-surfshirt/|title=Where did Winston Surfshirt get their jazzy names? Dive deep on the past of Sydney's hip hop hooligans|date=5 May 2017|publisher=}}</ref> Winston Surfshirt have performed at [[Falls Festival]] and [[Splendour in the Grass]], and have supported [[Rüfüs]] on tour.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://themusic.com.au/news/all/2017/01/24/exclusive-winston-surfshirt-signs-label-and-booking-agency-deals/|title=EXCLUSIVE: Winston Surfshirt Signs Label And Booking Agency Deals|website=theMusic}}</ref> They released their first album, ''Sponge Cake'', on 29 September 2017, and released their second LP, ''Apple Crumble'', on 15 November 2019.
