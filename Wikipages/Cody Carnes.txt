'''Cody Jay Carnes''' is an American [[Contemporary Christian music|Christian]] singer, songwriter and [[worship pastor]].

Carnes launched his solo music career in 2017 after signing with [[Capitol Christian Music Group]], with the release of his critically acclaimed debut studio album, ''[[The Darker the Night / The Brighter the Morning]]''. His second studio album, ''[[Run to the Father]]'', was released in 2020. ''Run to the Father'' was nominated for a [[Grammy Award]] for [[Grammy Award for Best Contemporary Christian Music Album|Best Contemporary Christian Music Album]] at the [[63rd Annual Grammy Awards]]. Carnes also featured alongside [[Kari Jobe]] and [[Elevation Worship]] on the hit single "[[The Blessing (song)|The Blessing]]", which won the [[GMA Dove Award]] for Worship Recorded Song of the Year at the [[51st GMA Dove Awards]] and garnered a nomination for [[Grammy Award for Best Contemporary Christian Music Performance/Song|Best Contemporary Christian Music Performance/Song]] at the 63rd Annual Grammy Awards.

==Career==
In the early 2010s, Cody Carnes began making music as part of [[Gateway Worship]],<ref name="AllMusic Bio">{{cite web |url= https://www.allmusic.com/artist/cody-carnes-mn0002669057/biography |title= Cody Carnes {{!}} Biography & History {{!}} AllMusic  |last= Monger |first= Timothy |website= [[AllMusic]] |publisher= [[All Media Network]] |access-date= June 1, 2021}}</ref> while serving as a worship pastor at [[Gateway Church (Texas)|Gateway Church]], in [[Dallas, Texas]]. In 2014, Carnes released the extended play, ''All He Says I Am''.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.nashvillechristiansongwriters.com/cody-carnes-songwriting-worship-wife-kari-jobe-new-album-darker-night-brighter-morning-2/ |title= Cody Carnes on Songwriting, Worship, wife Kari Jobe, and His New Album "The Darker the Night/The Brighter the Morning" - Nashville Christian Songwriters |last= Chisum |first= John |date= February 23, 2018 |website= Nashville Christian Songwriters |access-date= June 1, 2021}}</ref>

On February 24, 2017, [[Capitol Christian Music Group]] announced that Cody Carnes had signed with the label, while releasing his first single called "[[The Cross Has the Final Word]]",<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/worship-leader-cody-carnes-releases-debut-single-today/ |title= Cody Carnes Releases Capitol CMG Debut Single - TCB |last= Clarks |first= Jessie |date= February 24, 2017 |website= The Christian Beat |access-date= June 1, 2021}}</ref> in the lead-up to his debut album slated for release in 2017.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://jubileecast.com/articles/16982/20170224/cody-carnes-releases-debut-single-the-cross-has-the-final-word.htm |title= Cody Carnes Releases Debut Single "The Cross Has the Final Word" : News : JubileeCast |last= Yap |first= Timothy |date= February 24, 2017 |website= JubileeCast |access-date= June 1, 2021}}</ref> "The Cross Has the Final Word" debuted at No. 45 on the US [[Hot Christian Songs]] chart.<ref name="TCHTFW Billboard Christian" /> On April 7, 2017, Carnes released "Hold It All" as the second single from his debut album.<ref name="365 Singles Review">{{cite web |url= http://www.365daysofinspiringmedia.com/reviews/cody-carnes-the-cross-has-the-final-wordhold-it-alltil-the-end-of-timewhat-freedom-feels-like/ |title= Cody Carnes – The Cross Has the Final Word/Hold It All/Til the End of Time/What Freedom Feels Like |last= Andre |first= Jonathan |date= July 24, 2017 |website= 365 Days Of Inspiring Media |access-date= June 1, 2021}}</ref> Cody Carnes released "[[Til the End of Time]]" featuring Kari Jobe as the third single from his debut album on June 16, 2017.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/cody-carnes-teams-up-with-kari-jobe-in-new-single-till-the-end-of-time/ |title= Cody Carnes Teams Up With Kari Jobe In New Single "Till The End Of Time" - TCB |last= Longs |first= Herb |date= June 16, 2017 |website= The Christian Beat |access-date= June 1, 2021}}</ref> "Til the End of Time" debuted at No. 50 on the US Hot Christian Songs chart.<ref name="TTEOT Billboard Christian" /> On July 14, 2017, Carnes released "[[What Freedom Feels Like]]" as the fourth single from his debut album,<ref name="365 Singles Review" /> revealed to be titled ''[[The Darker the Night / The Brighter the Morning]]'', and slated for release on September 15, 2017.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://jubileecast.com/articles/17789/20170721/details-of-cody-carnes-debut-studio-album-revealed.htm |title= Details of Cody Carnes' Debut Studio Album Revealed : News : JubileeCast |last= Yap |first= Timothy |date= July 21, 2017 |website= JubileeCast |access-date= June 1, 2021}}</ref> "What Freedom Feels Like" peaked at No. 44 on the US Hot Christian Songs chart.<ref name="WFFL Billboard Christian" /> ''The Darker the Night / The Brighter the Morning'' was released on September 15, 2017.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.multitracks.com/blog/multitracks-now-available-for-the-darker-the-night-the-brighter-the-morning-by-cody-carnes |title= MultiTracks Now Available for The Darker the Night / The Brighter the Morning by Cody Carnes |date= September 15, 2017 |website= MultiTracks.com |publisher= MultiTracks LLC |access-date= June 1, 2021}}</ref> The album debuted at No. 17, on the US [[Top Christian Albums Chart]].<ref name="TDTNTBTM Christian Albums" /> On September 14, 2018, Kari Jobe and Cody Carnes released "[[Cover the Earth]]" as a standalone single.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/kari-jobe-and-cody-carnes-release-new-single/ |title= Kari Jobe And Cody Carnes Release New Single - TCB |last= Clarks |first= Jessie |date= September 14, 2018 |website= The Christian Beat |access-date= June 1, 2021}}</ref> The song peaked at No. 29 on the US Hot Christian Songs chart.<ref name="CTE Billboard Christian" />

On January 4, 2019, Cody Carnes released "[[Nothing Else (song)|Nothing Else]]" as a single.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.louderthanthemusic.com/product.php?id=3821 |title= Louder Than The Music - Cody Carnes - Nothing Else |date= January 4, 2019 |website= Louder Than The Music |access-date= June 1, 2021}}</ref> "Nothing Else" peaked at No. 31 on the US Hot Christian Songs chart.<ref name="NE Billboard Christian" /> Carnes released his second single of the year, "Heaven Fall", on March 8, 2019.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.ccmmagazine.com/news/worship-leader-cody-carnes-releases-heaven-fall/ |title= Worship Leader Cody Carnes Releases 'Heaven Fall' {{!}} CCM Magazine |last= Cluver |first= Ross |date= March 8, 2019 |website= [[CCM Magazine]] |access-date= June 1, 2021}}</ref> On July 26, 2019, Carnes released "[[Run to the Father (song)|Run to the Father]]" as a single.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://todayschristianent.com/music-news-cody-carnes-releases-new-song-run-to-the-father/ |title= Music News: Cody Carnes Releases New Song "Run To The Father" – Today's Christian Entertainment |last= Meade |first= Kelly |date= July 26, 2019 |website= Today's Christian Entertainment |access-date= June 1, 2021}}</ref> "Run to the Father" peaked at No. 23 on the US Hot Christian Songs chart.<ref name="RTTF Billboard Christian" /> Carnes released "[[Christ Be Magnified]]" as a single on January 1, 2020.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.louderthanthemusic.com/product.php?id=4760 |title= Louder Than The Music - Cody Carnes - Christ Be Magnified - Single |date= January 1, 2020 |website= Louder Than The Music |access-date= June 1, 2021}}</ref> "Christ Be Magnified" debuted at No. 45 on the US Hot Christian Songs chart.<ref name="CBM Billboard Christian" /> Carnes released his second studio album, ''[[Run to the Father]]'' on March 13, 2020.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.ccmmagazine.com/news/cody-carnes-released-new-album-run-to-the-father/ |title= Cody Carnes Released New Album 'Run To The Father' {{!}} CCM Magazine |last= Cluver |first= Ross |date= March 13, 2020 |website= [[CCM Magazine]] |access-date= June 1, 2021}}</ref> ''Run to the Father'' debuted at No. 12 on the Top Christian Albums Chart.<ref name="RTTF Christian Albums" /> The album was nominated for the [[Grammy Award for Best Contemporary Christian Music Album]] at the [[63rd Annual Grammy Awards]].<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/cody-carnes-receives-double-grammy-nominations/ |title= Cody Carnes Reacts To Double GRAMMY Nominations - On March 20, 2020, Cody Carnes alongside Kari Jobe and [[Elevation Worship]] released "[[The Blessing (song)|The Blessing]]" as a single.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/index.php/new-music/7345-kari-jobe-cody-carnes-and-elevation-worship-release-the-blessing-live |title= Kari Jobe, Cody Carnes & Elevation Worship Release "The Blessing (Live)" |last= Clarks |first= Jessie |date= March 21, 2020 |website= The Christian Beat |access-date= June 1, 2021}}</ref> The song peaked at No. 2 on the Hot Christian Songs chart,<ref name="TB Billboard Christian" /> and No. 15 on the [[Bubbling Under Hot 100]] chart.<ref name="TB Billboard Christian" /> "The Blessing" won the [[GMA Dove Award]] for Worship Recorded Song of the Year at the [[51st GMA Dove Awards|2020 GMA Dove Awards]]. The song was also nominated for [[Grammy Award for Best Contemporary Christian Music Performance/Song|Best Contemporary Christian Music Performance/Song]] at the 63rd Annual Grammy Awards,<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.ccmmagazine.com/news/cody-carnes-earns-2-grammy-nominations/ |title= Cody Carnes Earns 2 GRAMMY Nominations {{!}} CCM Magazine |last= Cluver |first= Ross |date= November 24, 2020 |website= [[CCM Magazine]] |access-date= June 1, 2021}}</ref> and the [[Billboard Music Award for Top Christian Song|''Billboard'' Music Award for Top Christian Song]] at the [[2021 Billboard Music Awards]].<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/billboard-music-awards-announce-2021-nominees-for-top-christian-gospel-artist-song-album/ |title= Billboard Music Awards Announce 2021 Nominees For Top Christian/Gospel Artist, Song & Album - TCB |last= Longs |first= Herb |date= April 29, 2021 |website= The Christian Beat |access-date= June 1, 2021}}</ref>

On May 28, 2021, Cody Carnes and [[Brandon Lake (musician)|Brandon Lake]] released "[[Too Good to Not Believe]]" as a single.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/cody-carnes-brandon-lake-release-too-good-to-not-believe/ |title= Cody Carnes & Brandon Lake Release "Too Good To Not Believe" - TCB |last= Longs |first= Herb |date= May 28, 2021 |website= The Christian Beat |access-date= June 1, 2021}}</ref> On August 20, 2021, Carnes alongside [[Hillsong Worship]] and [[Reuben Morgan]] released "[[Hope of the Ages]]" as a single.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/hillsong-worship-releases-hope-of-the-ages/ |title= Hillsong Worship Releases "Hope Of The Ages" - TCB |last= Longs |first= Herb |date= August 20, 2021 |website= The Christian Beat |access-date= September 1, 2021}}</ref> "Hope of the Ages" debuted at No. 49 on the US Hot Christian Songs chart.<ref name="HOTA Billboard Christian" />

==Personal life==
Cody Carnes married [[Kari Jobe]] on November 21, 2014.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.jubileecast.com/articles/12082/20141122/kari-jobe-and-cody-carnes-get-married.htm |title= Kari Jobe and Cody Carnes Are Married : News : JubileeCast |last= Yap |first= Timothy |date= November 22, 2014 |website= JubileeCast |access-date= June 1, 2021}}</ref> They have two sons.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.jubileecast.com/articles/14815/20160220/kari-jobe-and-cody-carnes-welcome-their-first-born-son.htm |title= Kari Jobe and Cody Carnes Welcome Their First Born Son : News : JubileeCast |last= Yap |first= Timothy |date= February 20, 2016 |website= JubileeCast |access-date= June 1, 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url= https://gospelmusic.org/kari-jobe-cody-carnes-welcome-baby-2/ |title= Kari Jobe & Cody Carnes Welcome Baby #2 - The Gospel Music Association |date= February 7, 2019 |website= Gospel Music Association |access-date= June 1, 2021}}</ref>
