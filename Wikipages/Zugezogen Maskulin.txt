'''Zugezogen Maskulin''' are a German rap duo, formed by [[grim104]] and [[Testo (Rapper)|Testo]] in Berlin.<ref name="Musikmarkt">{{cite web |author=Musikmarkt.de |url=http://www.musikmarkt.de/Aktuell/News/Buback-nimmt-Zugezogen-Maskulin-unter-Vertrag |title=Buback nimmt Zugezogen Maskulin unter Vertrag |access-date=2014-03-12}}</ref> They are signed to the Hamburg label Buback.<ref name="Musikmarkt"/>

Zugezogen means 'newcomers' and the name is a reference to the fact that both members of the group are newcomers to Berlin, as well as an allusion to previous German rap duos 'Westberlin Maskulin' and '[[Südberlin Maskulin]]'.<ref name="Interview">{{cite web |author=Toni Lukic|url=http://noisey.vice.com/de/blog/interview-grim104-testo-zugezogen-maskulin-hassen-ihren-namen |title=Zugezogen Maskulin hassen ihren namen |access-date=2014-03-12}}</ref>

== History ==
Grim104 and Testo founded the duo on 1 July 2010. In 2011 the free album ''Kauft nicht bei Zugezogenen!'' (Do not buy from newcomers) was released. The duo presents themselves, like [[K.I.Z]], as a mockery of [[gangster rap]] and machoism, where they exaggerate the stereotypes. In addition, there are many [[sarcasm|sarcastic]] and [[irony|ironic]] texts.
In the fall of 2013, the duo were signed by the leftwing-alternative [[Independent label]] Buback located in Hamburg. On there grim104's self titled solo [[Extended Play|EP]] appeared in November 2013.
On 13 February 2015, the second album of the group followed, ''Alles brennt''. (Everything Burns). <ref name="Laut">{{cite web |author=Laut.de |url=http://www.laut.de/Zugezogen-Maskulin |title=Zugezogen Maskulin on Laut.de |access-date=2014-03-12}}</ref>
