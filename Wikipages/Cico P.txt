'''Christopher Simms Jr.''' (born February 6, 1998), known by his stage name '''Cico P''' (formerly "Cicoeee P"), is an American rapper, singer, and [[songwriter]].<ref name="WY">Xavier Hunter,{{cite web|title=Cico P Releases Video for Viral Hit "Tampa"|url=https://www.wyexpect.com/stories/cico-p-releases-video-for-viral-hit-tampa|publisher=wyexpect.com|accessdate=May 7, 2021|date=}}</ref><ref>Emily Blake,{{cite web|title=Big Scarr, Cico P and Lakeyah Lead Breakthrough 25 Chart|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-news/big-scarr-cico-p-lakeyah-breakthrough-1165280/|publisher=rollingstone.com|accessdate=May 7, 2021|date=May 5, 2021}}</ref>

== Life and career ==
Cico P hails from [[Jacksonville, Texas]].<ref name="WY"/> His recent single, "Tampa" has achieved viral popularity on [[TikTok]] in early 2021.<ref>{{cite web|title=Tampa - Cico P|url=https://www.tiktok.com/music/Tampa-6913631288701814786?lang=en|publisher=tiktok.com|accessdate=May 7, 2021|date=}}</ref> "Tampa" catapulted Cico P to the top of the [[Spotify|US Spotify]] Viral 50 chart<ref>{{cite web|title= Viral 50 chart|url=https://spotifycharts.com/viral/|publisher=spotifycharts.com|accessdate=May 7, 2021|date=}}</ref> as well as the #3 spot on [[Rolling Stone]]’s ''Breakthrough 25''<ref>{{cite web|title=Breakthrough 25 - The fastest-rising artists of the month|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/charts/breakthrough/|publisher=rollingstone.com|accessdate=May 7, 2021|date=}}</ref><ref>Emily Blake,{{cite web|title=RS Charts: Burna Boy, Money Man and Victoria Monet Lead the Breakthrough 25 Chart|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-news/charts-burna-boy-money-man-breakthrough-1054322/|publisher=rollingstone.com|accessdate=May 7, 2021|date= September 2, 2020}}</ref> and #41 on [[Billboard charts|Billboard]]’s ''Emerging Artists''.<ref>{{cite web|title=Emerging Artists|url=https://www.billboard.com/charts/emerging-artists/2021-04-24|publisher=billboard.com|accessdate=May 7, 2021|date=}}</ref>
