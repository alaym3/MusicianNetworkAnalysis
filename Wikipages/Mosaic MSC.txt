'''Mosaic MSC'''  is a [[contemporary worship music]] band from [[Mosaic (church)|Mosaic Church]] in [[Los Angeles]], California. The band is led by Worship Pastor Mariah McManus.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.foxnews.com/entertainment/hollywood-worship-band-that-started-in-a-nightclub-worship-can-be-fun-too |title= Hollywood worship band that started in a nightclub: 'Worship can be fun, too' |last= Parke |first= Caleb |date= February 27, 2019 |website= Fox News |access-date= May 15, 2019}}</ref> The band have released three live albums and three [[extended plays]].

==History==
Mosaic MSC recorded its first worship album, ''MSC (Live from LA)'' on May 24, 2014 at the historic [[Pellissier Building and Wiltern Theatre|Wiltern Theater]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.christianpost.com/news/erwin-mcmanus-ushers-in-new-chapter-with-msc-live-worship-recording-at-historic-wiltern-theater.html|title=Erwin McManus Ushers in New Chapter With MSC, Live Worship Recording at Historic Wiltern Theater|website=The Christian Post|access-date=May 15, 2019}}</ref> After it was released in 2015,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/msc-live-in-la/id981478527|title=MSC (Live in LA) by Mosaic MSC|publisher=iTunes Store|accessdate=April 21, 2015}}</ref> it peaked at number 21 on the Christian Albums chart.<ref name="Billboard Christian Albums" /> Mosaic MSC has several charting singles. The song "Nvr Stp" peaked at number 49 on the Billboard Hot Christian Songs in 2015 and their song "Tremble" peaked at number 19 on the chart in March 2018.<ref name="BillboardHotChristian">{{cite web |title=Hot Christian Songs |url=https://www.billboard.com/music/mosaic-msc |work=Billboard |accessdate=July 21, 2018}}</ref>

Their second album ''Glory & Wonder'' peaked at number 16 in 2016.<ref name="Billboard Christian Albums" />
