'''Oceans of Slumber''' is an American [[progressive metal]] band from [[Houston]], [[Texas]], formed in 2011.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/oceans-of-slumber-mn0003427990/biography |title=Oceans of Slumber Biography |last=Monger |first=James Christopher  |website=[[Allmusic]]}}</ref>

==History==
Their self-released debut album ''Aetherial'' was released in February 2013.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.blabbermouth.net/cdreviews/aetherial/|title=Aetherial - OCEANS OF SLUMBER|date=May 15, 2013|publisher=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.themonolith.com/music/oceans-of-slumber-unleash-their-debt-album-aetherial/|title=OCEANS OF SLUMBER UNLEASH THEIR DEBT ALBUM - AETHERIAL - The Monolith|website=www.themonolith.com}}</ref>

Lead vocalist Cammie Gilbert joined the band in 2014, after previous vocalist Ronnie Gates left.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.invisibleoranges.com/a-style-all-her-own-the-undefinable-cammie-gilbert/|title=A Style All Her Own: The Undefinable Cammie Gilbert|website=[[Invisible Oranges]] - The Metal Blog}}</ref> Gilbert became the primary songwriter for the group after joining.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://distortedsoundmag.com/interview-cammie-gilbert-oceans-of-slumber/|title=INTERVIEW: Cammie Gilbert - Oceans of Slumber - Distorted Sound Magazine|date=February 26, 2018|publisher=}}</ref>

In 2015, the band signed with [[Century Media Records]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.centurymedia.com/newsdetailed.aspx?IdNews=16040&IdCompany=3|title=Century Media Records - : OCEANS OF SLUMBER sign worldwide deal with Century Media Records; Blue EP to be released on limited edition vinyl|website=www.centurymedia.com}}</ref>
In 2016, they toured with [[Ne Obliviscaris (band)|Ne Obliviscaris]] and [[Enslaved (band)|Enslaved]] on a 34 date European tour.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.houstoniamag.com/articles/2017/2/9/oceans-of-slumber-houston-concert|title=Oceans of Slumber Returns to Houston After European Tour|publisher=}}</ref>

In March 2016, they released their second album, ''Winter'', which received positive reviews from [[The Houston Press]] and Metal Injection.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.houstonpress.com/music/winter-is-coming-oceans-of-slumbers-latest-is-a-game-changer-8150976|title='Winter' Is Coming: Oceans of Slumber's Latest Is a Game-Changer|first=Kristy|last=Loye|date=March 1, 2016|publisher=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.metalinjection.net/reviews/oceans-of-slumber-winter|title=Album Review: OCEANS OF SLUMBER Winter|date=March 9, 2016|publisher=}}</ref>

On December 17, 2017, they released the lead single "The Decay of Disregard" off of their upcoming album ''The Banished Heart''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://bravewords.com/news/oceans-of-slumber-launch-music-video-for-new-single-the-decay-of-disregard|title=OCEANS OF SLUMBER Launch Music Video For New Single "The Decay Of Disregard"|last=bravewords.com|publisher=}}</ref> The second single was "The Banished Heart". The third single "No Color, No Light" in February 2018, featuring [[Tom S. Englund]] from [[Evergrey]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.metalforcesmagazine.com/site/news-oceans-of-slumber-release-third-studio-album-the-banished-heart/|title=OCEANS OF SLUMBER Issue 'No Color, No Light' Animated Music Video Feat. EVERGREY's Thomas Englund (February 16th, 2018) - News @ Metal Forces Magazine|website=www.metalforcesmagazine.com}}</ref> The album was released on March 3, 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.metalinjection.net/upcoming-releases/weekly-injection/the-weekly-injection-new-releases-from-oceans-of-slumber-iron-reagan-gatecreeper-and-more-out-today-3-2|title=THE WEEKLY INJECTION: New Releases From OCEANS OF SLUMBER, IRON REAGAN & GATECREEPER, and More Out Today - 3/2|date=March 2, 2018|publisher=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.houstonpress.com/music/the-best-music-of-the-week-in-houston-starting-february-28-2018-10229156|title=Neon Baby: The Best Music of The Week in Houston|first=David|last=Garrick|date=February 28, 2018|publisher=}}</ref> The album was included on the list of "50 Best albums of 2018 So Far" by [[Metal Hammer]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ultimate-guitar.com/news/general_music_news/metal_hammer_says_these_are_50_best_albums_of_2018_so_far.html|title=Metal Hammer Says These Are 50 Best Albums of 2018 So Far|publisher=}}</ref>
The band has been called a "female fronted [[Opeth (band)|Opeth]]" by [[Noisey]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://noisey.vice.com/en_us/article/65z8gd/cammie-gilbert-oceans-of-slumber-interview|title=Oceans of Slumber's Cammie Gilbert's Voice Proves Everything Really Is Bigger in Texas|date=April 21, 2016|publisher=}}</ref>
In 2018, the band toured with [[Insomnium]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://metalriot.com/news/oceans-of-slumber-to-tour-with-insomnium/|title=Oceans Of Slumber to tour with Insomnium – Metal Riot|website=metalriot.com}}</ref> In 2019, they joined [[Swallow the Sun]] as support for their European tour.

*Dobber Beverly – drums, piano (2011–present)
*Cammie Gilbert – lead vocals (2014–present)
*Mat V. Aleman – keyboards (2014–present)
*Semir Ozerkan – bass, backing vocals (2019–present)
*Jessie Santos – guitars (2019–present)
*Alexander Lucian – guitars, backing vocals (2019–present)

*Beau Beasley – keyboards (2015)
*Sean Gary – guitars, harsh vocals (2011–2018)
*Anthony Contreras – guitars, backing vocals (2011–2018)
*Keegan Kelly – bass, backing vocals (2011–2019)
