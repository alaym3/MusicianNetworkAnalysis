'''Rupinder Singh "Gippy" Grewal''' (born 2 January 1983), is an Indian actor, singer, film director and producer whose works span over [[Punjabi cinema|Punjabi]] and [[Bollywood|Hindi]] film industry. He is a Prominent figure in [[Music of Punjab|Punjabi Music]] and [[Punjabi cinema|Film]] Industry.

His single "Phulkari" was very successful in the Punjabi music industry.<ref>It received nearly 2 million hits hours after its release. (10 Oct. 2019): https://lifeandtrendz.com/1-4-million-hits-for-phulkari-feat-gippy-grewal-and-zareen-khan/ {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201011141333/https://lifeandtrendz.com/1-4-million-hits-for-phulkari-feat-gippy-grewal-and-zareen-khan/ |date=11 October 2020 }}</ref> He made his acting debut in the 2010 movie, ''[[Mel Karade Rabba]]'', and which he followed with ''[[Carry On Jatta]]'', ''[[Lucky Di Unlucky Story]]'', ''[[Bhaji in Problem]]'' and ''[[Jatt James Bond]]''. He revived "PTC Best Actor Award" in 2011 for his performance in the 2011 film '' [[Jihne Mera Dil Luteya]]''. He received the "PIFAA Best Actor Award" in 2012 along with [[Diljit Dosanjh]] and received "PTC Best Actor Award" in 2015 for ''Jatt James Bond'' along with Diljit Dosanjh.

==Personal life==
Grewal was born in [[Ludhiana]] and his hometown is Koom Kalan, [[Ludhiana district|Ludhiana]]. He did his schooling from Nankana Sahib Public School, Kot Gangu Rai and studied at North India Institute of Hotel Management, [[Panchkula]].<ref name="birthplace">{{cite news |title=Personal Agenda: Gippy Grewal |url=https://www.hindustantimes.com/chandigarh/personal-agenda-gippy-grewal/story-Qrby59fUpnG1pgDpNIBIeO.html |access-date=24 May 2019 |work=[[Hindustan Times]] |date=18 January 2014 |author=Navleen Kaur Lakhi |archive-date=11 October 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201011141336/https://www.hindustantimes.com/chandigarh/personal-agenda-gippy-grewal/story-Qrby59fUpnG1pgDpNIBIeO.html |url-status=live }}</ref> His brother [[Sippy Grewal]] is a distributor based in Australia.<ref>{{cite web |title="I Have Been Approached To Do Bollywood Films", Says Gippy Grewal |url=http://thelinkpaper.ca/?p=27963 |website=The Link |access-date=1 January 2019 |archive-date=2 January 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190102050442/http://thelinkpaper.ca/?p=27963 |url-status=live }}</ref>

Grewal is married to Ravneet Kaur and has three sons.<ref name="HT"/><ref>{{cite news |title=Have you seen these personal photos of Gippy Grewal? |url=https://www.news18.com/photogallery/movies/have-you-seen-these-personal-photos-of-gippy-grewal-1013854.html |work=[[CNN-News18]] |date=20 June 2015 |access-date=1 January 2019 |archive-date=2 January 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190102050545/https://www.news18.com/photogallery/movies/have-you-seen-these-personal-photos-of-gippy-grewal-1013854.html |url-status=live }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Gippy Grewal blessed with third son; shares adorable photos of his three children |url=https://www.timesnownews.com/entertainment/news/people/article/gippy-grewal-blessed-with-third-son-shares-adorable-photos-of-his-three-children/525624 |access-date=11 December 2019 |work=www.timesnownews.com |date=10 December 2019 |language=en |archive-date=10 December 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191210222114/https://www.timesnownews.com/entertainment/news/people/article/gippy-grewal-blessed-with-third-son-shares-adorable-photos-of-his-three-children/525624 |url-status=live }}</ref>

==Music career==
[[File:Gippy Grewal performing live in Chandigarh.jpg|thumb|Grewal performing live in [[Chandigarh]]]]
Grewal made his debut with the album ''Chakkh Lai'' was produced by Atul Sharma.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://gippygrewal.com/biography/|title=Biography|website=Gippy Grewal|access-date=21 May 2019|archive-date=19 May 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190519060059/http://gippygrewal.com/biography/|url-status=live}}</ref> He followed with the albums Aaja Ve Mitraa,Mele Mitraan De, Phulkari 2 and many more. He become a famous name in Punjabi Music among the audience. In 2021, his eighth album "Desi Rockstar" produced by [[Aman Hayer]] was a huge hit and was very much loved by Audience. After the album he released song " Angreji Beat" with [[Yo Yo Honey Singh]] which proved to be the biggest song of his career. He enjoyed his prime time of stardom during 2010-2014 making him leading artist in Punjabi Music Industry . In  2012 his song "Angreji Beat", featured in the [[Bollywood]] film ''[[Cocktail (2012 film)|Cocktail]]''. 

The video for his 2013 single "Hello Hello" was shot in [[Las Vegas]], Nevada. He performed at the Sandwell and Birmingham Mela in 2014.<ref>{{cite web |title=Sandwell & Birmingham Mela |url=https://www.expressandstar.com/news/2014/07/07/asian-music-event-sees-10000-flock-to-sandwell-park |publisher=Express & Star |access-date=21 March 2018 |archive-date=25 July 2018 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180725214333/https://www.expressandstar.com/news/2014/07/07/asian-music-event-sees-10000-flock-to-sandwell-park/ |url-status=live }}</ref>
Before his fame though he was a wedding singer. He frequently performed at weddings in Punjab, India.

==Film career==
[[File:Gippysargun.jpg|thumb|Gippy Grewal with [[Sargun Mehta]] during the promotions of [[Chandigarh Amritsar Chandigarh]]]]
Grewal made his film debut in a supporting role in 2010 Punjabi-language film ''[[Mel Karade Rabba]]''. He followed that up with a lead role in ''[[Jihne Mera Dil Luteya]]'' which became the biggest hit in Punjabi cinema when it released.<ref>{{cite news |title=Punjab Takes Center Stage With Its Own Hits |url=http://businesstoday.intoday.in/story/punjabi-film-industry-and-bollywood/1/19574.html |date=13 November 2011 |access-date=16 November 2018 |work=[[Business Today (India)|Business Today]] |author1=Anand J |author2=Anusha Subramanian |archive-date=7 January 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190107054646/https://www.businesstoday.in/magazine/reporters-diary/punjabi-film-industry-and-bollywood/story/19574.html |url-status=dead }}</ref> In April 2012 his film ''[[Mirza – The Untold Story]]'' released with the highest opening for a Punjabi film at the time.<ref>{{cite web |work=Box Office India Trade Network |title=Mirza Is The Highest Opening For Punjabi Film |url=http://www.boxofficeindia.com/boxnewsdetail.php?page=shownews&articleid=4420&nCat= |access-date=12 May 2012 |archive-date=15 May 2012 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120515115457/http://www.boxofficeindia.com/boxnewsdetail.php?page=shownews&articleid=4420&nCat= |url-status=live }}</ref>

His next movie ''[[Carry On Jatta]]'' released in July 2012 and had the second highest opening and total collections for Punjabi film.<ref>{{cite web |work=Box Office India Trade Network |title=Carry On Jatta Is A Blockbuster |url=http://boxofficeindia.com/boxnewsdetail.php?page=shownews&articleid=4745&nCat= |access-date=2 August 2012 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120805192209/http://www.boxofficeindia.com/boxnewsdetail.php?page=shownews&articleid=4745&nCat= |archive-date=5 August 2012}}</ref> In 2013, Grewal released the action film ''[[Singh vs Kaur]]'', comedy film ''[[Lucky Di Unlucky Story]]'' and ''[[Best of Luck (2013 film)|Best of Luck]]'' and the comedy movie ''[[Bhaji in Problem]]''.

In 2014, he appeared in the dramatic thriller ''[[Jatt James Bond]]''. His second film of 2014 was the comedy film ''[[Double Di Trouble]]''.

He [[Dubbing (filmmaking)|dubbed]] a voice in the Punjabi version of ''[[A Good Day to Die Hard]]'' which was the first Hollywood movie to be dubbed in Punjabi.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2013-01-28/news-interviews/36595279_1_punjabi-hollywood-film-gippy |title=Die Hard 5 goes Punjabi |date=28 January 2013 |access-date=20 February 2013 |archive-date=17 September 2013 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130917034527/http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2013-01-28/news-interviews/36595279_1_punjabi-hollywood-film-gippy |work=[[The Times of India]] |url-status=dead }}</ref>

In 2015, Grewal entered Bollywood with a guest appearance in the comedy-drama film ''[[Dharam Sankat Mein]]''. He then made his full acting debut in Bollywood with the romantic-comedy film ''[[Second Hand Husband]]''.

In September 2017 Gippy Grewal starred in his most recent Hindi movie, ''[[Lucknow Central]]'', along with Farhan Akhtar.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.huffingtonpost.in/murtaza-ali-khan/lucknow-central-a-prison-musical-that-s-beyond-redemption_a_23210402/ |title='Lucknow Central': A Prison Musical That's Beyond Redemption |publisher=Huffingtonpost.in |date=20 September 2017 |access-date=10 May 2018 |archive-date=29 April 2018 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180429092436/https://www.huffingtonpost.in/murtaza-ali-khan/lucknow-central-a-prison-musical-that-s-beyond-redemption_a_23210402/ |url-status=live }}</ref> ''[[Carry on Jatta 2]]'' released on 1 June 2018.
