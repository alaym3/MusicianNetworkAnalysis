'''Ronell Langston Plasschaert''' (born 16 April 1992),<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.telegraaf.nl/entertainment/261066/ronnie-flex-plant-volgende-album-in-april|title=Ronnie Flex plant volgende album in april|date=17 July 2017|website=Telegraaf|language=nl|access-date=22 March 2019}}</ref> known professionally as '''Ronnie Flex''', is a Dutch [[Hip hop music|hip hop]] performer and [[rapper]].

== Career ==
Plasschaert started making music at a young age. In 2012, he got his first hit in the Netherlands with his song "Soldaatje", which he made with fellow rapper Mr. Polska. On 20 March 2013, Plasschaert released his first solo single, "Tankstation", which reached the 100th place in the Dutch [[Single Top 100]]. He released his debut album ''De nacht is nog jong, net als wij voor altijd'' on 14 November 2014.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://hitparade.ch/showitem.asp?interpret=Ronnie+Flex&titel=De+nacht+is+nog+jong,+net+als+wij+voor+altijd&cat=a|title=Ronnie Flex – De nacht is nog jong, net als wij voor altijd|last=Hung|first=Steffen|website=hitparade.ch|access-date=22 March 2019}}</ref> Plasschaert then released several singles, including "[[Drank & Drugs]]", a song made in collaboration with fellow rapper [[Lil' Kleine]], which achieved first place in the Single Top 100 and remained in the chart for a total of 35 weeks.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://dutchcharts.nl/search.asp?todo=notfound|title=Dutch Charts – Drank & drugs (single)|website=Dutchcharts.nl|language=Dutch|access-date=22 March 2019}}</ref>

Plasschaert took part on the [[Expeditie Robinson 2015|16th season]] of the television series ''[[Expeditie Robinson]]'' in September 2015.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.mediacourant.nl/2015/06/ronnie-flex-doet-mee-aan-expeditie-robinson-2015/|title=Ronnie Flex doet mee aan Expeditie Robinson 2015|publisher=Mediacourant.nl|language=Dutch|access-date=22 March 2019}}</ref> Since 2018, he has been a member of the judging panel on the television program ''[[It Takes 2]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.televizier.nl/amusement/ronnie-flex-nieuw-jurylid-it-takes-2|title=Ronnie Flex nieuw jurylid It Takes 2|website=televizier.nl|language=nl|access-date=22 March 2019}}</ref> In June of that year, in collaboration with [[BLØF]], Plasschaert released the single "Omarm me".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.100p.nl/nieuws/bl-f-en-ronnie-flex-steken-omarm-in-nieuw-jasje|title=100% NL – Bløf en Ronnie Flex steken 'Omarm' in nieuw jasje!|website=www.100p.nl|language=Dutch|access-date=22 March 2019}}</ref> On 12 July, he released a remix of the song "Wine Slow" in collaboration with Idaly, [[Famke Louise]] and [[Bizzey]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rtlboulevard.nl/entertainment/music/artikel/4284866/ronnie-flex-famke-louise-en-bizzey-knallen-dikke-remix-online|title=Ronnie Flex, Famke Louise én Bizzey knallen dikke remix online|date=12 July 2018|website=RTL Boulevard|language=nl|access-date=22 March 2019}}</ref>

In October 2018, Plasschaert launched his own clothing line in collaboration with clothing company CoolCat.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.ad.nl/show/hiphopper-ronnie-flex-komt-met-eigen-kledinglijn~ac4ffc06/|title=Hiphopper Ronnie Flex komt met eigen kledinglijn|newspaper=Algemeen Dagblad|language=Dutch|access-date=22 March 2019}}</ref>

== Personal life ==
Plasschaert had a relationship with [[Famke Louise]] from mid-2018, until early-2019. They announced their relationship in their Christmas song "Alleen door jou".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.metronieuws.nl/musiccity/2018/12/nieuwe-kersthit-famke-en-ronnie-bevestigt-relatie|title=Nieuwe kersthit Famke en Ronnie bevestigt relatie|website=Metronieuws.nl|language=nl|access-date=22 March 2019}}</ref> Plasschaert has a daughter with his ex-girlfriend DJ Wef.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.telegraaf.nl/entertainment/2938430/ronnie-flex-vader-geworden|title=Ronnie Flex vader geworden|date=19 December 2018|website=Telegraaf|language=nl|access-date=22 March 2019}}</ref>
