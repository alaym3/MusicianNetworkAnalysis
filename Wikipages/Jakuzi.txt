'''Jakuzi''' is a Turkish [[synthpop]] band, formed in [[Istanbul]] in 2015. The band consists of Kutay Soyocak, Can Kalyoncu, Meriç Erseçgen and Ahmetcan Gökçeer. Kutay Soyocak and Taner Yücel formed the project to move away from their previous [[punk rock|punk]]-oriented music projects. The duo rose to prominence in Istanbul's [[underground music]] scene<ref name="quietus"/> after the release of their debut album ''Fantezi Müzik''. Originally released in 2016 through Domuz Records,<ref name="to">[https://www.timeout.com/istanbul/tr/muezik/jakuzi Jakuzi]. ''[[Time Out (magazine)|Time Out İstanbul]]''</ref> it was reissued by [[City Slang]] on 24 March 2017.<ref name="quietus"/>

The band's sound has been described as synthpop, [[dark wave]]<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.dailysabah.com/music/2016/09/03/countdown-starts-for-salon-iksvs-concerts |title=Countdown starts for Salon İKSV's concerts |last=Koca |first=Zeynep Esra |date=2 September 2016 |publisher=[[Sabah (newspaper)|The Daily Sabah]] |access-date=18 February 2017}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.hurriyetdailynews.com/salon-iksv-opens-new-season-.aspx?pageID=238&nID=104064&NewsCatID=385 |title=Salon İKSV opens new season |publisher=[[Hürriyet|Hürriyet Daily News]] |access-date=18 January 2017}}</ref> and [[New wave music|new wave]].<ref name="to"/> Jakuzi is influenced by [[Turkish pop music]], as well as genres ranging from [[krautrock]], [[disco]] and [[post-punk]].<ref name="quietus">{{Cite web |url=http://thequietus.com/articles/21817-new-jakuzi-track |title=Listen: New Jakuzi Track  |last=Smith |first=Matthew |date=17 February 2017 |website=[[The Quietus]] |access-date=18 February 2017}}</ref>
