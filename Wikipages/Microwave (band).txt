'''Microwave''' is an American rock band from [[Atlanta, Georgia]]. They have supported bands such as [[The Wonder Years (band)|The Wonder Years]], [[letlive.]], [[Have Mercy (band)|Have Mercy]], [[Man Overboard (band)|Man Overboard]], [[Tiny Moving Parts]], [[Motion City Soundtrack]], [[Jimmy Eat World]], [[Can't Swim]], [[The Dangerous Summer (band)|The Dangerous Summer]], and [[Boston Manor (band)|Boston Manor]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.altpress.com/news/entry/motion_city_soundtrack_announce_farewell_tour|title=Motion City Soundtrack announce farewell tour - News - Alternative Press|website=Alternative Press|last1=Ralph|first1=Caitlyn}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.altpress.com/news/entry/love_your_friends_ die_laughing_man_overboard_to_go_on_hiatus|title=Love your friends, die laughing: Man Overboard to go on hiatus - News - Alternative Press|website=Alternative Press|last1=Sharp|first1=Tyler}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.altpress.com/news/entry/have_mercy_announce_headlining_tour_upcoming_new_single|title=Have Mercy announce headlining tour, upcoming new single - News - Alternative Press|website=Alternative Press|last1=Leak|first1=Brian}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.altpress.com/news/entry/the_wonder_years_announce_spring_tour_with_letlive._others|title=The Wonder Years announce spring tour with letlive., others - News - Alternative Press|website=Alternative Press|last1=Ryken|first1=Atreyue|accessdate=22 April 2016}}</ref> They are currently signed to [[Pure Noise Records]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bringthenoise.com/blog/microwave-sign-to-pure-noise-records-announce-new|title=Microwave sign to Pure Noise Records, announce new LP 'Death Is A Warm Blanket'|website=Bring The Noise}}</ref>

== History ==
Microwave formed in Woodstock, Georgia in 2012.<ref>{{cite web|title=Microwave Release "When The Fever Breaks" |website=Lost In The Sound |url=http://lostinthesound.com/2013/09/microwave-released-when-the-fever-breaks/}}</ref> The group released their first and second EPs, ''Nowhere Feels Like Home'' and ''Swine Driver'', on January 10 and March 30, 2013.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://microwavetheband.bandcamp.com/album/nowhere-feels-like-home|title=Nowhere Feels Like Home, by Microwave|website=Microwave|access-date=2016-04-22}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://microwavetheband.bandcamp.com/track/swine-driver|title=Swine Driver, by Microwave|website=Microwave|access-date=2016-04-22}}</ref> Their third EP ''When the Fever Breaks'' was released on September 17, 2013.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://microwavetheband.bandcamp.com/album/when-the-fever-breaks|title=When The Fever Breaks, by Microwave|website=Microwave|access-date=2016-04-22}}</ref>

Microwave released their first full-length album ''Stovall'' on August 23, 2014.<ref>{{cite web|title=Stovall, by Microwave|url=https://microwavetheband.bandcamp.com/|website=Microwave}}</ref> They released their second full-length album ''Much Love'' on September 30, 2016.<ref>{{cite web|title=Much Love, by Microwave|url=https://microwavetheband.bandcamp.com/|website=Microwave}}</ref>
Their third full-length-album '' Death Is a Warm Blanket '' was released September 13th 2019, via Pure Noise Records.{{cn|date=December 2019}} ''[[Loudwire]]'' named it one of the 50 best rock albums of 2019.<ref name="Ltop5019">{{cite web |title=The 50 Best Rock Albums of 2019 |url=https://loudwire.com/best-rock-albums-of-2019/ |website=[[Loudwire]] |publisher=[[Townsquare Media]] |access-date=March 7, 2021 |date=December 5, 2019}}</ref>
