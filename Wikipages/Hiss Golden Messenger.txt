'''Hiss Golden Messenger''' is an American [[folk music]] band, originating from [[Durham, North Carolina|Durham]], [[North Carolina]], led by MC Taylor (born September 9, 1975). <ref>[https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/style/wp/2014/09/08/complicated-faith-searching-for-god-in-the-songs-of-hiss-golden-messenger/] {{dead link|date=July 2021}}</ref><ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/style/wp/2014/09/08/complicated-faith-searching-for-god-in-the-songs-of-hiss-golden-messenger/|title=Complicated faith: Searching for God in the songs of Hiss Golden Messenger|last=Richards|first=Chris|date=2014-09-08|newspaper=The Washington Post|language=en-US|issn=0190-8286|access-date=2016-11-08}}</ref>

==History==
The band was formed by Mike (MC) Taylor and Scott Hirsch in 2007.{{cn|date=April 2020}} The band's early recordings were released through Taylor's own recording label, "Heaven & Earth Magic Recording Company."<ref name="american">{{cite web | url=http://www.americansongwriter.com/2013/03/dead-reckoning-a-qa-with-hiss-golden-messenger/ | title=Dead Reckoning: A Q&A with Hiss Golden Messenger | publisher=[[American Songwriter]] | date=March 20, 2013 | accessdate=April 2, 2013 | author=Inman, Davis}}</ref>

The band's debut album, ''[[Country Hai East Cotton]]'', was released in 2009 via Taylor's label in a limited edition of 500.<ref name="debut">{{cite web | url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/country-hai-east-cotton-mw0001504282 | title=Hiss Golden Messenger - Country Hai East Cotton | publisher=[[Allmusic]] | accessdate=April 2, 2013 | author=Poet, J.}}</ref> This was followed by their international debut album, ''Bad Debt'', in 2010, released via Black Maps.<ref name="allmusic">{{cite web |url = http://www.allmusic.com/artist/hiss-golden-messenger-mn0002049671|title = Hiss Golden Messenger|publisher = [[Allmusic]]|accessdate = April 2, 2013|author = O'Brien, Jon}}</ref><ref name="DiS">{{cite web | url=http://drownedinsound.com/releases/15856/reviews/4141687 | title=Hiss Golden Messenger - Bad Debt | publisher=[[Drowned in Sound]] | date=December 7, 2010 | accessdate=April 2, 2013 | author=Monroe, Jazz | archive-date=November 18, 2013 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131118073854/http://drownedinsound.com/releases/15856/reviews/4141687 | url-status=dead }}</ref> The band's third album, ''Poor Moon'', was released in 2012 via North Carolina-based "Paradise of Bachelors", and received positive reviews.<ref name="p4k">{{cite web | url=http://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/16546-poor-moon/ | title=Hiss Golden Messenger - Poor Moon | publisher=[[Pitchfork Media|Pitchfork]] | date=April 26, 2012 | accessdate=April 2, 2013 | author=Currin, Grayson}}</ref><ref name="CoS">{{cite web | url=http://consequenceofsound.net/2012/05/album-review-hiss-golden-messenger-poor-moon/ | title=Album Review: Hiss Golden Messenger – Poor Moon | publisher=[[Consequence of Sound]] | date=May 18, 2012 | accessdate=April 2, 2013 | author=Litowitz}}</ref><ref name="huff">{{cite web | url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/bob-moses/a-revelation-hiss-golden-_1_b_1437518.html | title=A Revelation: Hiss Golden Messenger's Poor Moon -- Interview and Tracks | publisher=[[The Huffington Post]] | date=April 19, 2012 | accessdate=April 2, 2013 | author=Moses, Bob}}</ref> The band's fourth album, ''Haw'', was released on April 2, 2013.<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.pastemagazine.com/blogs/av/2013/01/song-premiere-hiss-golden-messenger---sufferer-lov.html | title=Song Premiere: Hiss Golden Messenger - Sufferer (Love My Conqueror) | publisher=[[Paste (magazine)|Paste]] | date=January 18, 2013 | accessdate=April 2, 2013 | author=Orr, Dacey}}</ref><ref name="haw">{{cite web | url=http://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/17819-hiss-golden-messenger-haw/ | title=Hiss Golden Messenger - Haw | publisher=[[Pitchfork Media|Pitchfork]] | date=April 1, 2013 | accessdate=April 2, 2013 | author=Deusner, Stephen M.}}</ref><ref name="swampy">{{cite web | url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/haw-mw0002490480 | title=Hiss Golden Messenger - Haw | publisher=[[Allmusic]] | accessdate=April 2, 2013 | author=Jurek, Thom}}</ref>

[[File:Hiss_Golden_Messenger_2018.jpg | thumb | right | Hiss Golden Messenger performing live in December, 2018.]]
 
Hiss Golden Messenger were signed to [[Merge Records|Merge]] in April 2014 and released their fifth album and Merge label debut, ''Lateness of Dancers'' on September 9, 2014.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mergerecords.com/merge-welcomes-hiss-golden-messenger|title=Merge welcomes Hiss Golden Messenger|publisher=Mergerecords.com|accessdate=15 October 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mergerecords.com/hiss-golden-messenger-announces-lateness-of-dancers-lp-out-september-9|title=Hiss Golden Messenger announces Lateness of Dancers LP, out September 9|publisher=Mergerecords.com|accessdate=15 October 2014}}</ref> They have gone on to release four additional full-length records via Merge, as well as remastered versions of ''[[Bad Debt (album)|Bad Debt]],'' [[Poor Moon (Hiss Golden Messenger album)|''Poor Moon'']], and ''Haw''.

Their 2019 album ''[[Terms of Surrender]]'' received a nomination for [[Grammy Award for Best Americana Album|Best Americana Album]] at the [[63rd Annual Grammy Awards]].<ref name="grammy">{{cite web |url=https://www.grammy.com/grammys/awards/winners-nominees/273 |title=Grammy Awards Winners & Nominees for Best Americana Album |author=<!--Not stated--> |date=2021 |website=grammy.com |publisher=Recording Academy |access-date=March 15, 2021}}</ref>

In 2021 the band was inter alia part of the [[Newport Folk Festival]] in July.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Newport Folk Festival stage schedule 2021 |url=https://newportfolk.org/2021-schedule/ |access-date=2021-09-21 |website=newportfolk.org}}</ref>

==Musical style==
The band's music contains elements from various musical genres, such as folk, [[country music|country]], [[Dub music|dub]], [[Southern soul|country soul]], [[rhythm and blues]], [[bluegrass music|bluegrass]], [[jazz]], [[funk]], [[swamp pop]], [[gospel music|gospel]], [[blues]], and [[rock music|rock]].<ref name="p4k"/><ref name="debut"/><ref name="haw"/><ref name="swampy"/> The band's style was also described as "[[alternative country]]" and "[[country rock]]."<ref name="debut"/><ref name="swampy"/><ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.dustedmagazine.com/reviews/7632 | title=Hiss Golden Messenger - Haw | publisher=Dusted | date=March 26, 2013 | accessdate=April 2, 2013 | author=Kelly, Jennifer}}</ref> The band's main influences include [[the Beatles]], [[The Byrds]], and [[Buffalo Springfield]].<ref name="allmusic" /> The band has been compared to [[Will Oldham]] and [[Bill Callahan (musician)|Bill Callahan]].<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2011/jan/13/new-band-hiss-golden-messenger | title=Hiss Golden Messenger (No 945) | publisher=[[The Guardian]] | date=January 13, 2011 | accessdate=April 2, 2013 | author=Lester, Paul}}</ref>
