== History ==

=== Formation: 2012 ===
It Looks Sad. was originally formed as "It Looks Sad, That's Why I Said It's You", by Brett Green, Jimmy Turner, Lamont Brown, and Vince D’Ambrosio in early 2012. They released their first EP ''Welcome to Good Woods'' in May 2012 and their second EP ''Daughters Cottage'' in October 2012.

=== ''Self-Titled'' and ''Kaiju'': 2013-2015 ===
In late 2013, the band emerged under the new and shortened name, "It Looks Sad." with a new and also signing with local label [[Tiny Engines]]. Seasons was the first single they released under their new shortened name<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://itlookssad.bandcamp.com/track/seasons|title=Seasons, by It Looks Sad.|website=It Looks Sad.|access-date=2019-02-15}}</ref> and in August 2014 they released their third EP with [[Tiny Engines]] called ''It Looks Sad''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.tinyengines.net/products/527653-it-looks-sad-self-titled-7|title=It Looks Sad.|website=Tiny Engines|language=en|access-date=2019-02-15}}</ref> They continued touring into 2015 with them announcing they are working on new material. In June 2015 they released their fourth EP ''Kaiju'' with the songs "Creature" and "Nagoya" and went on another East Coast tour.

=== ''Lost Songs'' and ''Sky Lake'': 2017-2018 ===
In late 2017 they released Lost Songs,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://itlookssad.bandcamp.com/album/lost-songs|title=Lost Songs, by It Looks Sad.|website=It Looks Sad.|access-date=2019-02-15}}</ref> it was released in order to help pay for one of the band members car, which broke down. On Twitter It Looks Sad. stated that it would cost $750 to repair his car and that he would release demos of unused songs or make new ones in-order to help pay for the repair costs.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://twitter.com/itlookssad/status/944331420482318336|title=My car is going to cost me $750 that I don't have right now, if I put out a single or something on bandcamp would anyone attempt to help me out? I'd love you forever|last=Sad|first=It Looks|date=2017-12-22|website=@itlookssad|language=en|access-date=2019-02-15}}</ref>

In 2018 the first single "Light" from It Looks Sad's debut album Sky Lake<ref name="Sky Lake">{{Cite web|url=http://www.tinyengines.net/products/624644-it-looks-sad-sky-lake|title=It Looks Sad. - Sky Lake|website=Tiny Engines|language=en|access-date=2019-02-15}}</ref> was released on the [[Tiny Engines]] YouTube channel<ref>{{Citation|last=Tiny Engines|title=It Looks Sad. - Light (Sky Lake Album Teaser)|date=2018-08-15|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=a-MRQ8SDnwI|access-date=2019-02-15}}</ref> followed by the singles "Drool", "Bike" and "Visor". Sky Lake was released November 2018.
