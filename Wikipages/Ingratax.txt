'''Ingrid Fernanda Enríquez Guillén''' (born {{Circa|2000}}) is a Mexican singer and social media personality known professionally as '''Ingratax'''. She released her debut single, "Paris," in July 2021.

== Life ==
Ingratax is from [[Mazatlán]] and [[Chihuahua (state)|Chihuahua]], Mexico.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Espinoza|first=Fernando|date=September 25, 2021|title=Son de Sinaloa y están dominando las redes sociales: los nuevos rostros que debes conocer|url=https://www.noroeste.com.mx/entretenimiento/espectaculos/son-de-sinaloa-y-estan-dominando-las-redes-sociales-los-nuevos-rostros-que-debes-conocer-DF1430713|url-status=live|access-date=2021-09-29|website=Noroeste|language=es-MX}}</ref><ref name=":0" /> When she was five years old, her grandmother, Yolanda, began teaching her guitar and piano.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|last=Flores|first=Griselda|date=2021-09-02|title=5 Things to Know About Mexican Artist Ingratax & Her Viral Debut Single 'Paris'|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/latin/9623870/ingatrax-paris-interview/|url-status=live|access-date=2021-09-29|website=Billboard|language=en}}</ref> 

Ingratax joined [[TikTok]] in 2019 and often posts duets. She has 13 million followers.<ref name=":0" /> She is a member of the Team Cheli house.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|last=Loaeza|first=Dania|date=September 3, 2021|title=Tiktoker Ingratax en la cima con 'Paris'; ya tiene 50 millones de views y es elogiada por Billboard|url=https://www.random.news/instagram/Tiktoker-Ingratax-en-la-cima-con-Paris-ya-tiene-50-millones-de-views-y-es-elogiada-por-Billboard-20210903-0014.html|url-status=live|access-date=2021-09-30|website=Random|language=es-MX}}</ref> Ingratax released her debut single, "Paris," in mid-July 2021. ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' described the [[sad pop]] track as melancholic.<ref name=":0" /> The melody was written by Abbel.<ref name=":1" /> Ingratax was nominated at the [[2021 MTV Millennial Awards]] in the favorite new school category.<ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=2021-06-10|title=MTV Miaw 2021: Lista de nominados|url=https://los40.com.co/los40/2021/06/10/tendencias/1623337078_002185.html|url-status=live|access-date=2021-09-29|website=LOS40|language=es}}</ref> Ingratax is signed to [[Sergio Lizárraga]]'s indie label, Room 28. In mid-July 2021, her debut single, "Paris" went viral on TikTok and charted 36 on [[Hot Latin Songs]], 37 on [[Billboard Global Excl. U.S.]] and 63 on Billboard Global 200.<ref name=":0" /> The music video has over 50 million views on [[YouTube]].<ref>{{Cite web|title="Paris", la canción debut de Ingratax que es tendencia en Instagram|url=https://www.filo.news/viral/Paris-la-cancion-debut-de-Ingratax-que-es-tendencia-en-Instagram-20210823-0032.html|access-date=2021-09-29|website=Filo News|language=es}}</ref><ref name=":1" />
