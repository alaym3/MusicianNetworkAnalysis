'''Hillsong Young & Free''' (also known as '''Hillsong Y&F''' or simply '''Young & Free''' or '''Y&F''') is an Australian [[contemporary worship music]] group from [[Sydney]], Australia, where they started making Christian music in 2012 at [[Hillsong Church]]. They have released four [[live album]]s: ''[[We Are Young & Free]]'' (2013), ''[[Youth Revival]]'' (2016), ''[[III Live at Hillsong Conference|III Live]]'' (2018), and ''All of My Best Friends'' (2020); they have also released four [[studio album]]s: ''[[Youth Revival Acoustic]]'' (2017), ''[[III (Hillsong Young & Free album)|III]]'' (2018), ''[[III (Studio Sessions)]]'' (2019), and ''[[III (Reimagined)]]'' (2019). Additionally, the group have released three [[extended play]]s: ''[[This Is Living EP|This Is Living]]'' (2015), ''We Are Young & Free (The Remixes) - EP'' (2015), and ''Wish We Were All Together...but until then'' (2020).

==Background==
The group formed in 2012 out of [[Sydney]], Australia, where they were located at Hillsong Church. Their members are worship leaders Aodhan King, Alexander Pappas, Tyler Douglass, Renee Sieff, Ben Tan, and Melodie Wagner. [[Hillsong United]] (launched in 1998), the original group, began when the members were all relatively young, since it was started as part of the youth ministry at Hillsong Church. As they started to mature, have families of their own, and take on adulthood, many people at the church felt there was a need for a new group that would aim its message at younger people with a stimulating new sound.<ref name="CR Story">{{cite web|first=Chris|last=Mountford|title=Hillsong Young & Free: The latest offshoot of the Australian movement |url=http://www.crossrhythms.co.uk/articles/music/Hillsong_Young__Free_The_latest_offshoot_of_the_Australian_movement/52762/p1/|publisher=[[Cross Rhythms]]|access-date=5 March 2016|date=11 December 2013}}</ref><ref name="AM Bio">{{cite web|first=David|last=Jeffries|title=Hillsong Young & Free : Biography|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/hillsong-young-free-mn0003139286/biography|publisher=[[AllMusic]]|access-date=5 March 2016}}</ref><ref name="BB Interview">{{cite web|first=Jim|last=Askar|title=Hillsong Young & Free on New Live Album 'Youth Revival': 'The Focus Is on the Message' |url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/6890367/hillsong-young-free-interview-youth-revival-album-interview|magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|access-date=5 March 2016|date=28 February 2016}}</ref><ref name="CCM Interview">{{cite web |first=Daniel |last=Ritchie |title=Hillsong Young & Free Q&A |url=https://www.ccmmagazine.com/features/hillsong-young-free-qa/ |magazine=[[CCM Magazine]] |access-date=31 March 2016 |date=31 March 2016}}</ref>
