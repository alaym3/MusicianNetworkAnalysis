'''Hykeem Jamaal Carter Jr.''' (born October 22, 2000), professionally known as '''Baby Keem''', is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer from [[Carson, California]]. He initially gained major recognition following the release of his single "[[Orange Soda (song)|Orange Soda]]", from his second mixtape ''Die for My Bitch'' (2019), which peaked at number 98 on the US ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Billboard Hot 100|Hot 100]] chart.<ref>{{cite web |last=Zidel |first=Alex |title=Hykeem Carter Makes His Formal Introduction As "Baby Keem" |url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/hykeem-carter-makes-his-formal-introduction-as-baby-keem-new-song.1979498.html |website=HotNewHipHop|date=July 16, 2018 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Setaro |first1=Shawn |title=Baby Keem Has Arrived |url=https://www.complex.com/music/2019/07/baby-keem-interview-die-for-my-bitch |website=Complex |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Baby Keem Looks to Make a Leap With New 'Die for My B*tch' Project |url=https://hypebeast.com/2019/7/baby-keem-die-for-my-bitch-stream |website=Hypebeast|date=July 19, 2019 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Baby Keem - the Sound of Bad Habit |url=http://www.intheroughstyle.com/audio/baby-keem-the-sound-of-bad-habit}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Phillips |first1=Yoh |title=Baby Keem 'Die for My Bitch' 1 Listen Album Review |url=https://djbooth.net/features/2019-07-19-baby-keem-die-for-my-bitch-album-review |website=DJBooth}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Baby Keem Drops "Orange Soda" Off His Upcoming 'Die for My B*tch' Project |url=https://www.complex.com/music/2019/07/baby-keem-orange-soda-single |website=Complex}}</ref><ref name="Hot 100" /> He would later go on to collaborate with prominent artists such as [[Travis Scott]], [[Kanye West]], and his cousin [[Kendrick Lamar]], on the songs "[[Durag Activity]]", [[Praise God]]", and "[[Family Ties (song)|Family Ties]]" respectively, the latter two of which reached the [[Top 40|top 20]] on the Hot 100. His debut studio album ''[[The Melodic Blue]]'', which was preceded by the aforementioned singles, was issued in September 2021 by [[Columbia Records]] and [[PGLang|pgLang]].

==Musical career==
Keem has both songwriting and production credits on his cousin [[Kendrick Lamar]]'s project, ''[[Black Panther (soundtrack)|Black Panther: The Album]]'', [[Top Dawg Entertainment|TDE]] member [[Jay Rock]]'s ''[[Redemption (Jay Rock album)|Redemption]]'', [[Schoolboy Q]]'s ''[[Crash Talk]]'', and the song "Nile" from Disney's ''[[The Lion King: The Gift]]''.<ref>{{cite web |title=Meet Hykeem Carter, The Mysterious Artist Who Has Credits on Multiple TDE Projects |url=https://www.complex.com/pigeons-and-planes/2018/07/hykeem-carter |website=[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]]}}</ref> Keem worked with record producer [[Cardo (record producer)|Cardo]], on his two mixtapes, ''The Sound of a Bad Habit'' (2018) and ''Die for My Bitch'' (2019).

In March 2020, Keem was featured in the visual mission statement for Kendrick Lamar and [[Dave Free]]'s company, [[PGLang|pgLang]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://pitchfork.com/news/kendrick-lamar-launches-new-company-pglang/|title=Kendrick Lamar and Dave Free Launch New Company pgLang|website=Pitchfork|date=March 5, 2020|accessdate=March 6, 2020}}</ref> In August 2020, it was revealed Baby Keem was included on ''[[XXL (magazine)|XXL]]''{{'}}s 2020 [[XXL (magazine)#Annual Freshman Class List|Freshman Class]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.xxlmag.com/xxl-2020-freshman-class-revealed/|title=XXL 2020 FRESHMAN CLASS REVEALED|work=[[XXL (magazine)|XXL]]|last=Cline|first=Georgette|date=August 11, 2020|accessdate=August 11, 2020}}</ref>

==Artistry==
===Influences===
In a 2019 interview, Keem cited American musician [[Kid Cudi]], as a major influence: "I was inspired by Cudi's cadences and shit like that. Kid Cudi's one of my favorite artists."<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.complex.com/music/2019/07/baby-keem-interview-die-for-my-bitch |title=Baby Keem Has Arrived |first=Shawn |last=Setaro |date=July 31, 2019 |website=Complex}}</ref>
