'''Diana De Brito''',<ref name="interview 26.10.2018"/> (born 5 March 1996) known by her stage name '''IAMDDB''', is an Angolan [[Rapping|rapper]] and [[Singing|singer]] based in [[Manchester]], United Kingdom. IAMDDB has released three EPs and three mixtapes as of 2021 and was listed number three on [[BBC]] [[Sound of...|Sound of 2018]].<ref name="theguardian 31 March 2018"/>

== Life and career ==
De Brito was born in [[Lisbon]], Portugal and moved to [[Manchester]], United Kingdom at the age of five or six.<ref name="interview 26.10.2018"/> Her father is [[Angola]]n with [[Portuguese people|Portuguese]] ancestry, her mother is [[Demographics of Saudi Arabia| Saudi Arabian]]. Both of her parents were musicians.

<ref name="djmag 29 April 2019"/> She released the ''Hoodrich, Vol. 3'' mixtape in 2017<ref name="theguardian 31 March 2018"/> and ''Flightmode, Vol. 4'' in 2018.

IAMDDB was a supporting musician for [[Lauryn Hill]]'s UK tour in late 2018.<ref>[https://www.bustle.com/p/whos-supporting-lauryn-hill-on-her-uk-tour-the-iconic-singer-called-on-some-legends-for-her-us-tour-13131457 Who's Supporting Lauryn Hill On Her UK Tour? The Iconic Singer Called On Some Legends For Her U.S. Tour]. REBECCA FEARN. Nov 14 2018.</ref> Her latest album is 2019's ''Swervvvvv.5''.<ref name="djmag 29 April 2019"/>
