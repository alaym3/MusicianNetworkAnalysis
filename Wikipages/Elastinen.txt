'''Kimmo Ilpo Juhani Laiho''' (born 1981), better known by his stage name '''Elastinen''' (meaning ''elastic'' in Finnish), is a Finnish [[Hip hop music|rap musician]] and also known as one of the founders and is the CEO of the Finnish independent rap label [[Rähinä Records]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.kauppalehti.fi/uutiset/rappari-elastisen-oma-yritys-on-tuloskone-rahina-records-sen-sijaan-teki-mojovat-tappiot/08485992-3dd0-3de4-ad08-80b2486f6e18|title=Räppäri Elastisen oma yritys on tuloskone – Rähinä Records sen sijaan teki mojovat tappiot|last=Bhose|first=Cilla|website=Tärkeimmät talousuutiset {{!}} Kauppalehti|language=fi|access-date=2019-02-09}}</ref> Together with his colleague [[Iso H|Iso H (Big H)]], he founded the Finnish rap duo [[Fintelligens]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.iltalehti.fi/musiikki/a/201807112201068582|title=Elastinen ja Iso H 21-vuotisesta urastaan: "Fintelligensin vaikutusta ei voi kukaan kiistää" - lue erikoishaastattelu|website=www.iltalehti.fi|language=fi|access-date=2019-02-09}}</ref><ref name=":0" /> He has also released five solo albums.

==Biography==
===Beginnings===
Born and raised in [[Helsinki]], Elastinen began taking guitar lessons when he was seven years old.<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.iltalehti.fi/viihde/a/2015041219494794|title=Elastisen lapsuuskuvat - tältä räppäri näytti taaperona!|website=www.iltalehti.fi|language=fi|access-date=2019-02-09}}</ref> At the age of 11 he started dancing and has been a member of famous break-dance groups as [[IMC (dance group)|IMC]] and [[Savage Feet]]. Inspired by a hiphop-cassette, given by his elder-brother, "Ela" wrote his first rap-lyrics when he was 15.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.iltalehti.fi/viihde/a/2016041821435789|title=Elastinen täyttää tänään 35 vuotta: katso kuvakooste vuosien varrelta|website=www.iltalehti.fi|language=fi|access-date=2019-02-09}}</ref><ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=https://www.mtvuutiset.fi/artikkeli/kuvat-15-vuoden-takaa-elastisen-huima-muutos-siloposkesta-stadiontahdeksi/5104756|title=Kuvat 15 vuoden takaa: Elastisen huima muutos – siloposkesta stadiontähdeksi|last=VIIHDE|first=MTV|date=2015-05-20|website=mtvuutiset.fi|language=fi|access-date=2019-02-09}}</ref> He joined an anti-racist musical "Colorblind" and met [[Iso H]].<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1" />

===In Fintelligens===
{{Main|Fintelligens}}
In summer 1997, Elastinen and [[Iso H]] founded [[Fintelligens]]. Elastinen was only 18 when they got their first record-deal.<ref name=":1" />

Ela graduated from [[Sibelius Academy]], a music school in 2000.<ref name=":0" /> Same year [[Fintelligens]] released debut album "[[Renesanssi]]" with one-third produced by Elastinen. Renesanssi certified gold.<ref name=":0" /> In autumn 2001, Elastinen and [[Iso H]] released their second album, ''Tän Tahtiin''. Also partly produced by him, it was in its turned certified gold.<ref name=":0" />

Elastinen was then called in 2002 to do his military service at [[Santahamina]]. But he used his freetime performing shows with [[Iso H]]. Fintelligens released third studio album in November. ''Kokemusten Summa'' went gold.<ref name=":0" /> The album was entirely produced by Elastinen.<ref name=":0" />

===Solo career===
In 2004 Elastinen released his solo debut "Elaksis Kivi".<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rumba.fi/uutiset/elaksis-kivi-laittaa-7-veljesta-uusiksi-cheek-kirjailijan-kanssa/|title=Elaksis Kivi laittaa 7 veljestä uusiksi Cheek-kirjailijan kanssa|last=Hätinen|first=Jukka|website=Rumba.fi|language=fi|access-date=2019-02-09}}</ref> In 2006 he finished his second album ''Anna Soida''.<ref name=":0" /> The first single, the album's title track, was a hit. "Anna a" was released in 2006. His third album, ''[[E.L.A. (album)|E.L.A.]]'', was released in November 2007.<ref name=":0" /> First single from the album, "Ovet Paukkuu", was No. 1 on [[NRJ]]-Finland and had heavy rotation also on Finnish [[MTV]] and [[Kutonen (TV channel)|The Voice]]. He followed this with ''Joka päivä koko päivä'' in 2013, which peaked at number 4, and ''[[Elastinen feat.]]'' in 2016, which debuted at number 1. ''Elastinen feat.'' was preceded by a TV series featuring Elastinen collaborating with a different musician each episode.
