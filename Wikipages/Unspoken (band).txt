'''Unspoken''' is an American Christian band.<ref name=JFH/> The band is composed of Chad Mattson, Jon Lowry, and Ariel Munoz.<ref name=JFH/><ref name=KLOVEBIO>{{cite web |url=https://unspokenmusic.com/about/ |title=Biography: Unspoken |author=K-LOVE |author-link=K-LOVE |publisher=K-LOVE |access-date=November 10, 2012}}</ref> The band released their debut single, "Who You Are", on the [[Centricity Music]] label. Released on June 12, 2012,<ref name=AMAZON>{{cite web|url=https://www.amazon.com/Who-You-Are/dp/B0084P9DRO/ref=sr_1_2?ie=UTF8&qid=1352523097&sr=8-2&keywords=Unspoken|title=Who You Are|date=June 12, 2012|publisher=[[Amazon.com]]|access-date=November 10, 2012}}</ref> the song has charted on numerous Christian song charts, and was the only song from the band's debut EP ''Get to Me'' to be featured on their first full-length album. The self-titled debut LP ''[[Unspoken (Unspoken album)|Unspoken]]'' was released April 1, 2014. A five-song preview EP, ''[[The World Is Waking]]'', was released July 24, 2013. They have had four straight top-five AC singles in their career including "Who You Are", "Lift My Life Up", "Start a Fire", and "Good Fight", with "Start a Fire" reaching No. 1; the song "Lift My Life Up" was also included on the 2015 ''WOW Hits'' release.<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://www.musictimes.com/articles/6868/20140617/unspoken-inches-closer-top-charts.htm |title=Unspoken Inches Closer to the Top of the Charts : Genres |journal=Music Times |access-date=October 7, 2015}}</ref> The band also toured alongside [[Big Daddy Weave]] and [[Sanctus Real]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://christianconcertalerts.com/events/show/big-daddy-weave-with-chris-august-unspoken/ |title=Big Daddy Weave with Chris August & Unspoken - Charleston - Oct 31 |publisher=Christianconcertalerts.com |access-date=October 7, 2015}}</ref>

==Background==
Unspoken is a band made up of Chad Mattson on vocals, Jon Lowry on guitars, keys, bass guitar, and vocals, and Ariel Munoz on drums.<ref name=JFH/> Former members of the band are Mike Gomez on guitars, Ryan Babin and Don Eanes on keys, Yamil Jimenez on percussion and George Williams on bass guitar. In 2018, Gomez announced his departure from the band.<ref name="New Release Today">{{cite web|last1=AD|first1=Staff Reports|url=http://www.newreleasetoday.com/news_detail.php?newsid=2929|title=Unspoken Announces Departure of Guitarist Michael Gomez|website=New Release Today|publisher=New Release Today|access-date=January 16, 2018}}</ref> After his departure, the band was joined by guitarist Wiso Aponte and keyboardist Alan Pelno until February 16, 2020, when both announced their departures as well.
