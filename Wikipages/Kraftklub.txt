'''Kraftklub''' is a five-person band from [[Chemnitz]], [[Germany]]. Their music combines [[rock music|rock]] / [[indie rock|indie]] and [[Sprechgesang]] with German lyrics and is generally considered to be a mixture of [[rap]] and [[indie rock|indie]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.visions.de/live/tournee/16818/Kraftklub |title=VISIONS empfiehlt: Kraftklub |language=de |accessdate=13 May 2012 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120512223812/http://www.visions.de/live/tournee/16818/kraftklub |archivedate=12 May 2012 }}</ref>

== History ==

===Founding and early years (2009–2011)===
The band members first met at school <ref name="reflect">{{cite web |url=http://www.reflect.de/2011/12/kraftklub-interview-%E2%80%9Emicky-maus-ist-ein-bloder-kleiner-klugscheiser%E2%80%9C/ |title=Kraftklub-Interview: "Micky Maus ist ein blöder, kleiner Klugscheißer!" |language=de |accessdate=13 May 2012 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120520203538/http://www.reflect.de/2011/12/kraftklub-interview-%E2%80%9Emicky-maus-ist-ein-bloder-kleiner-klugscheiser%E2%80%9C/ |archivedate=20 May 2012 }}</ref> and founded the band at the end of 2009.<ref name="reflect"/> At the [[Splash! (festival)|Splash Festival]] 2009, rapper Bernd Bass (Felix Brummer) performed together with the rock band ''Neon Blocks'' for the first time. At the end of 2009, they founded the band Kraftklub and started recording their first studio album. On 13 February 2010, they released their first EP, titled ''Adonis Maximus'' at a record release party in the Chemnitz club Atomino.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.atomino-club.de/contrexx/index.php?section=calendar&catid=0&yearID=2010&monthID=2 |title=RAMBA ZAMBA pres. KRAFTKLUB RECORD RELEASE |language=de |accessdate=13 May 2012 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131109054250/http://www.atomino-club.de/contrexx/index.php?section=calendar&catid=0&yearID=2010&monthID=2 |archivedate=9 November 2013 }}</ref>

In September 2010, the band won the ''New Music Award'', a music prize awarded by various [[ARD (broadcaster)|ARD]] radio stations.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://on3.de/element/6800/new-music-award-2010-kraftklub-raeumen-ab#/element/6800/new-music-award-2010-kraftklub-raeumen-ab |title=New Music Award 2010 Kraftklub räumen ab |language=de |accessdate=13 May 2012 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110829051244/http://on3.de/element/6800/new-music-award-2010-kraftklub-raeumen-ab |archivedate=29 August 2011 }}</ref> As a result, several major labels became aware of them. As of January 2011, they are signed to Universal Domestic Rock/Vertigo Berlin.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.universal-music.de/kraftklub/news/detail/article:164867/neu-kraftklub-bei-vertigo-berlin |title=Neu! Kraftklub bei Vertigo Berlin! |publisher=[[Universal Music]] |language=de |accessdate=2014-12-01 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150617033304/http://www.universal-music.de/kraftklub/news/detail/article:164867/neu-kraftklub-bei-vertigo-berlin |archive-date=17 June 2015 |url-status=dead }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.musikmarkt.de/Aktuell/News/Universal-Domestic-Rock-angelt-sich-Newcomer-Band-Kraftklub |archive-url=https://archive.today/20120804224237/http://www.musikmarkt.de/Aktuell/News/Universal-Domestic-Rock-angelt-sich-Newcomer-Band-Kraftklub |url-status=dead |archive-date=2012-08-04 |title=Universal Domestic Rock angelt sich Newcomer-Band Kraftklub |website=musikmarkt.de |language=de |accessdate=2014-12-01 }}</ref>

Kraftklub have performed as the support act for bands including [[Beatsteaks]], [[Fettes Brot]], [[Casper (rapper)|Casper]], [[Die Toten Hosen]], and [[Rammstein]].<ref name="reflect" /> On 5 August 2011, they released their first single, ''Zu Jung''.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.universal-music.de/kraftklub/diskografie/detail/product:179460/zu-jung |title=KRAFTKLUB - Zu Jung - Diskografie |publisher=[[Universal Music]] |language=de |accessdate=2014-12-01 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120124143856/http://www.universal-music.de/kraftklub/diskografie/detail/product:179460/zu-jung |archive-date=24 January 2012 |url-status=dead |df=dmy-all }}</ref> 

=== Breakthrough with ''Mit K'' (2011–2013) ===
[[File:Kraftklub-2.jpg|thumb|Kraftklub at Melt! Festival 2013]]
On 29 September 2011, the band represented [[Saxony]] in the [[Bundesvision Song Contest]] with their song ''Ich will nicht nach Berlin'', coming fifth with a total of 89&nbsp;points.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tvtotal.prosieben.de/tvtotal/specials/bundesvision2011/index.html |title=Bundesvision2011 |language=de |accessdate=13 May 2012 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120512175713/http://tvtotal.prosieben.de/tvtotal/specials/bundesvision2011/index.html |archivedate=12 May 2012 }}</ref> The single was released the next day where it reached number 45 on the German [[Media Control Charts]].<ref name="acharts">{{cite web |url=http://acharts.us/album/68262 |title=Kraftklub - Mit K - Music Charts |website=acharts.us |accessdate=2014-12-01}}</ref> Their debut album ''Mit K'' was released on 20 January 2012 and shot straight to number one in Germany.<ref name="acharts" /><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.freiepresse.de/NACHRICHTEN/TOP-THEMA/Kraftklub-startet-durch-Album-Mit-K-von-0-auf-1-in-den-Charts-artikel7891041.php |title=Kraftklub startet durch: Album "Mit K" von 0 auf 1 in den Charts |accessdate=13 May 2012 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120416100710/http://www.freiepresse.de/NACHRICHTEN/TOP-THEMA/Kraftklub-startet-durch-Album-Mit-K-von-0-auf-1-in-den-Charts-artikel7891041.php |archivedate=16 April 2012 }}</ref> In April 2012, ''Songs für Liam'', the next single from their album, was released. It debuted at number 40 and peaked at number 14 in the German Media Control Charts.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://acharts.us/song/69538 |title=Kraftclub - Songs Für Liam - Music Charts |website=acharts.us |accessdate=2014-12-01}}</ref>

On 6 April 2012,  [[K.I.Z]] and Kraftklub gave an unannounced concert on the roof of the Chemnitz commercial complex ''Terminal 3'' as part of the documentary film series ''Durch die Nacht mit...'' commissioned by [[Arte|ARTE]]. The performance was announced only a few hours before via social networks, despite the ban on dancing on Good Friday, and as a result of the crowds of spectators the police closed off the Chemnitzer Brückenstraße. <ref>Florian Harlass: [http://www.371stadtmagazin.de/371magazin/items/Kraftklub-Jahresrueckblick-felixkummer-chemnitz-2012-Zuhause-371.html ''Jahresrückblick mit K – Kraftklub 2012'']. 371stadtmagazin.de</ref>

Kraftklub refer to their hometown of [[Chemnitz]] as "Karl-Marx-Stadt" <ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.laut.de/Kraftklub |title=laut.de-Biographie - Kraftklub |website=laut.de |language=de |accessdate=2014-12-01}}</ref> (this was the name the city was known as from 1953 to 1990) - one of their songs on ''Mit K'' is entitled ''Karl-Marx-Stadt'' - and they are known for their trademark poloshirts, varsity jackets and [[suspenders]].<ref name="reflect" />

For [[Record Store Day]] 2012, Kraftklub released the song ''Songs for Liam'' in a version with [[Casper (rapper)|Casper]] as vinyl single, which is limited to 700 pieces. <ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.discogs.com/KraftKlub-Feat-Casper-Songs-F%C3%BCr-Liam/release/3555694|title=Kraftklub feat. Casper - Songs für Liam (2012)|last=|first=|date=|website=discogs.com|language=de|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=21 April 2020}}</ref>

Its first concert outside of Germany was at 19 September 2012, in "La Puerta Grande" Bar in [[Bogotá]], thanks to the "Kolumbien mit K" tour promoted by the Goethe Institut - Colombia, which took them by Bogotá, [[Medellín]] and [[Cali]], each one with two concerts respectively, and one in Cartagena.{{citation needed |date=December 2014}}

In 2013, Kraftklub was nominated (among others) together with the group [[Frei.Wild]] for the ''music award Echo'' in the category ''Rock/Alternative National''. On 6 March 2013, Kraftklub announced that they want to be removed from the list of nominees, as Kraftklub does not want to be named in the same line as FreiWild. After [[MIA.|Mia.]] and [[Die Ärzte]] also withdrew their participation, Frei.Wild was removed from the list of nominees and Kraftklub received the "Kritikerpreis National".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.ok-magazin.de/entertainment/musik/16855/kraftklub-freuen-sich-nach-nominierten-eklat-ueber-echo-|title=Kraftklub wins 'Echo' after nomination scandel|last=|first=|date=20 March 2013|website=ok-magazin.de|language=de|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=21 April 2020}}</ref>

The song ''Eure Mädchen'' can be heard on the soundtrack of the PC and console game [[FIFA 13]]. It is the only German title in the game. Kraftklub has a guest appearance in the song ''Ganz schön okay'' on the album ''Hinterland'' by German rapper Casper.

=== In Schwarz (2014–2016) ===
[[File:Kraftklub (172).JPG|thumb|Kraftklub at Openin Festival 2014]]
On 20 May 2014, the music video ''Hand in Hand'' (in a version with bassist Till Brummer on vocals) of an allegedly new band called ''In Schwarz'' appeared on [[YouTube]]. The video was uploaded by the music label ''[[Audiolith Records]]''. <ref>https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6FzA3o9aXrQ</ref> On 2 June 2014, ''In Schwarz'' appeared on the German television show ''Circus HalliGalli'' and revealed themselves as Kraftklub.

On 20 November 2015, the first live album ''Randale'' was released. Part of the DVD is a recording of the concert in the [[Max-Schmeling-Halle]] in Berlin from 6. March 2015. In January 2016 followed the ''Randale''-tour, in which the band gave concerts in several German cities. <ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.musikexpress.de/kraftklub-hauen-live-dvd-raus-und-gehen-auf-randale-tour-409669/|title=Kraftklub releases new live DVD and goes on tour|last=|first=|date=23 October 2015|website=musikexpress.de|language=de|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=21 April 2020}}</ref>

=== Keine Nacht für Niemand (2017–present) ===
On 16 March 2017, the band they released the first single ''Dein Lied'' from the subsequently announced album ''Keine Nacht für Niemand'' during a 10-hour Facebook livestream, which was released on 2 June 2017. The title of the album is an allusion to the album ''Keine Macht für Niemand'' by the band [[Ton Steine Scherben]]. <ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.de/kraftklub-album-keine-nacht-fuer-niemand-im-juni-2017-1216323/|title=Kraftklub's new album "Keine Nacht für Niemand" to be published in June 2017|last=|first=|date=20 March 2017|website=rollingstone.de|language=de|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=21 April 2020}}</ref>
