'''The Gray Havens''' are an American [[Christian music|Christian]] [[folk pop]] husband and wife duo, David and Licia Radford, from [[Crystal Lake, Illinois]]. They started their music recording careers in 2012. Their first release, an [[extended play]], ''Where Eyes Don't Go'', was released by Zodlounge Music, in 2013. Their first full album, ''[[Fire and Stone (album)|Fire and Stone]]'', was released in 2015 with funds raised on [[Kickstarter]]<ref>{{Cite web|title = The Gray Havens Full Length Album!|url = https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/daveradford/the-gray-havens-full-length-album/updates|website = Kickstarter|access-date = January 2, 2016|language = en-US}}</ref>

== Music history ==
