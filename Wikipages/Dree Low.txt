'''Salah Abdi Abdulle''' (born 17 September 1994), known professionally as '''Dree Low''', is a Swedish rapper and songwriter of Somali origin.<ref>[https://www.stockholmdirekt.se/nyheter/forsoker-alltid-vara-en-bra-forebild/repseb!mrVe38Gi6qalwZTTrJRmNw/ StockholmDirekt.se: "Försöker alltid vara en bra förebild"] {{in lang|sv}}</ref> He lives in [[Husby, Stockholm|Husby]] in western [[Stockholm]]. He broke through in 2019 with his albums ''No hasta mañana'' and ''Flawless''. In September 2019, ''Flawless'' topped the Swedish Albums Chart and his single "Pippi" topped the Swedish Singles Chart. He has collaborated with a number of Swedish rappers; Adel, [[1.Cuz]], [[Einár (rapper)|Einár]] amongst many. He is childhood friends with footballer [[Robin Quaison]].

== Awards ==
In 2020, Abdulle received two awards from public service broadcaster [[Sveriges Radio]] award ceremony {{ILL|P3 Guld|sv}}.<ref>{{Cite news|date=2020-02-07|title=Dree Low: "Jag är långt ifrån klar"|language=sv|work=Sveriges Radio|url=https://sverigesradio.se/artikel/7403627|access-date=2021-11-01}}</ref>

== Crime ==
Abdulle has been convicted for driving without a license and for drug crime.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Dree Low: ”De säger att jag glamouriserar gatulivet”|url=https://www.expressen.se/noje/dree-low-de-sager-att-jag-glamouriserar-gatulivet/|access-date=2021-11-01|website=www.expressen.se|language=sv}}</ref>

According to police, Abdulle has connections of the ''Husbys Hyenor''  (English: hyenas of [[Husby, Stockholm|Husby]]) crime gang<ref name=":0" /> where it is said to be a driving force.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Holm |first1=Gusten |title=Dree Low dömd för rån – kopplas till kriminellt gäng |url=https://www.expressen.se/nyheter/dree-low-domd-for-ran-kopplas-till-kriminellt-gang/ |access-date=3 November 2021 |publisher=Expressen |date=1 November 2021}}</ref>

On September 22 2021, Abdulle was arrested at [[Stockholm Arlanda Airport]], as a suspect in a case where three men robbed a convenience store. He was found guilty on November 1 2021 and sentenced to one year in prison. Two other men were also found guilty.<ref name=":0">{{cite web |title=Prisbelönte rapparen Dree Low döms till fängelse för butiksrån |url=https://www.tv4.se/artikel/593nT9IeynS8FpQJWSLfH9/rapparen-dree-low-doems-till-faengelse |website=TV4 |access-date=1 November 2021}}</ref>
