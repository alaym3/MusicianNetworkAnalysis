'''Zayo Group Holdings, Inc.''', or '''Zayo Group''', is a privately held company headquartered in [[Boulder, Colorado]], U.S. with European headquarters in [[London]], England. The company provides communications infrastructure services, including fiber and bandwidth connectivity, colocation and cloud infrastructure. Zayo's primary customer segments include wireless carriers, national carriers, [[Internet service provider|ISPs]], enterprises and government agencies. Zayo Group was built largely through acquisitions; it took over thirty companies from 2007 to 2014, including [[AboveNet]] and [[360networks]].<ref name=invcom>{{cite web|url=http://news.investors.com/technology/101714-722470-zayo-group-up-16-in-tough-ipo-market.htm|title=IPO Stock Watch: Zayo Group Up 16% In Tough IPO Market ZAYO XENE - Investors.com|author=BRIAN DEAGON|work=Investor's Business Daily}}</ref> An [[initial public offering]] of stock raised $600 million in 2014.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://seekingalpha.com/article/2568965-ipo-preview-zayo-group-holdings|title=IPO Preview: Zayo Group Holdings|author=IPOdesktop|date=17 October 2014}}</ref> In May 2019, Zayo Group agreed to be acquired by global investment firms [[EQT Partners]] and Digital Colony Partners in a deal valued at $14.3 billion.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Narcisi |first1=Gina |title=Fiber Provider Zayo Will Go Private In $14.3 Billion Deal |url=https://www.crn.com/news/networking/fiber-provider-zayo-will-go-private-in-14-3-billion-deal |website=CRN |date=8 May 2019}}</ref>

==Network and services==
Zayo owns a fiber network that spans more than 130,000 route miles in North America and Europe. Zayo operates a [[Tier 1 network|Tier 1 IP network]], with settlement-free peering with all other Tier 1 providers. Additionally, Zayo operates 51 colocation facilities<ref>{{cite news|title=Zayo Acquires Dallas Based Provider, CoreXchange|url=http://www.thehostingnews.com/zayo-acquires-dallas-based-provider-corexchange-31066.html|access-date=June 2, 2014|publisher=The Hosting News|date=March 7, 2014}}</ref> with more than 1,000,000 sq. ft. of sellable space. Zayo's services include [[Dark fibre|dark fiber]], [[Wavelength-division multiplexing|wavelengths]], [[Synchronous optical networking|SONET]], [[Ethernet]], IP services, [[Colocation centre|colocation]], [[Backhaul (telecommunications)|wireless backhaul]] and [[small cell]]s.{{citation needed|date=April 2017}}
