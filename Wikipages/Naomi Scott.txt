'''Naomi Grace Scott''' (born 6 May 1993) is an English<ref name="English"/> actress and singer. Born in [[Hounslow]], she rose to prominence for her performances in the television film ''[[Lemonade Mouth (film)|Lemonade Mouth]]'' (2011) and the science fiction series ''[[Terra Nova (TV series)|Terra Nova]]'' (2011). Scott achieved further recognition for starring as [[Princess Jasmine]] in [[Disney]]'s musical live-action fantasy film ''[[Aladdin (2019 film)|Aladdin]]'' (2019). She has also starred in the superhero film ''[[Power Rangers (film)|Power Rangers]]'' (2017) and the action comedy film ''[[Charlie's Angels (2019 film)|Charlie's Angels]]'' (2019).

==Career==
Scott was discovered by British pop singer [[Kéllé Bryan]] from the girl group [[Eternal (band)|Eternal]], who signed her as a client.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.standard.co.uk/showbiz/naomi-scott-the-teen-star-with-a-mission-6436921.html|title=Naomi Scott: the teen star with a mission|newspaper=[[Evening Standard]]|date=25 August 2011|access-date=25 July 2020}}</ref> She went to work with British songwriters and producers [[Xenomania]].<ref name="DisneyChannelMedianet">{{cite web |url=http://www.disneychannelmedianet.com/web/showpage/showpage.aspx?program_id=3134438&type=scott |title=NAOMI SCOTT 'Mohini (Mo) Banarjee' |publisher=Disney Channel Medianet |access-date=17 April 2011 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120616174018/http://www.disneychannelmedianet.com/web/showpage/showpage.aspx?program_id=3134438&type=scott |archive-date=16 June 2012 }}</ref> In 2014, the YouTube channel "Reload" published two videos featuring her, as part of their "Reload Sessions" series.<ref>{{Citation|last=Reload|title=Hozier: Take Me To Church (Medley) - Naomi Scott|date=7 November 2014|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=snSaktPJeig|access-date=27 July 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last=Reload|title=Naomi Scott: Hear The Bells|date=19 November 2014|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=N5bwStdo3TE|access-date=27 July 2019}}</ref>

Scott is a [[Christianity|Christian]].<ref>{{cite web|last1=Heawood|first1=Sophie|url=https://www.vogue.co.uk/article/naomi-scott-aladdin-vogue-interview-2019|title=Naomi Scott, Disney's New Princess Jasmine, Is Redefining The Fairy Tale For The Modern Age|quote=It might be common in America, but being a committed Christian is unusual in a young British celebrity.
