'''Only The Family''', often abbreviated as '''OTF''', is a rap collective from [[Chicago]], [[Illinois]]. The group was formed by rapper [[Lil Durk]] in 2010.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.chiraqdrill.com/lil-durk-otf/|title=OTF (Only The Family) - OTF Records &#124; Founded by Lil Durk|website=Chiraq Drill - Chicago Rap, Hip Hop, Music Reviews, & Breaking News}}</ref> The majority of the OTF members are also members of the [[Black Disciples]] street gang, in the branch known as Only Trey Folks. The group is home to recording artists such as rapper [[King Von]] (deceased<ref>{{Cite news|last=Coscarelli|first=Joe|date=2020-11-06|title=King Von, Up-and-Coming Chicago Rapper, Shot and Killed in Atlanta|language=en-US|work=The New York Times|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2020/11/06/arts/music/king-von-dead.html|access-date=2021-02-28|issn=0362-4331}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Rapper King Von among three shot dead outside Atlanta nightclub|url=https://news.sky.com/story/rapper-king-von-among-three-shot-dead-outside-atlanta-nightclub-12126111|access-date=2021-02-28|website=Sky News|language=en}}</ref>), Booka600, Boss Top, Doodie Lo, C3, THF Zoo, JusBlow600, [[Hypno Carlito]], OTF Ikey, Boona, Boonie Moe, Chief Wuk,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://kollegekidd.com/news/otf-chief-wuk-father-bd-twin-varney-responds-to-game/|access-date=2021-02-28|website=kollegekidd.com|title=OTF Chief Wuk's Father BD Twin Varney Responds to Game|date=21 February 2015}}</ref> Slimelife Shawty, and  Lil Mexico<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.tvovermind.com/lil-durk/|title=10 Things You Didn't Know About Lil Durk|first=Jennifer|last=Borama|date=November 27, 2019|website=TVOvermind}}</ref>

==Record label==
The OTF label was part of [[French Montana]]'s Coke Boys, and as a result, adopted the nickname 'OTF Coke Boys'. This saw OTF members feature on the subsequent [[Coke Boys 3|Coke Boys]] album.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/lil-durk-lends-his-otf-certification-to-king-vons-crazy-story-20-new-song.1982833.html|title=Lil Durk Lends His OTF Certification To King Von's "Crazy Story 2.0"|website=HotNewHipHop}}</ref> The label has since grown to be independent from the Coke Boys imprint and are now signed to [[Todd Moscowitz|Alamo Records]] and [[Interscope Records]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.musicconnection.com/lil-durk-alamo-interscope/|title=Chicago Rapper Lil Durk Signs To Alamo/Interscope|website=musicconnection|date=27 July 2018}}</ref>
New OTF Signee Rich Loner Released “First Day Out” under moniker Big Gotti in 2021.
