'''Ekrem Bora''' (born 3 September 1983), better known by his stage name '''Eko Fresh''', is a German [[rapper]] of Turkish and [[Kurds|Kurdish]] descent.<ref name="alem">ALEM, [http://www.alem.de/index.php?id=803 EKO FRESH-EK IS BACK] Retrieved 5 September 2010 {{in lang|de}}</ref><ref name="Twitter">Eko Fresh auf Twitter: "@xRay_RU halber Kurde halber Türke aber eigentlich ja deutscher" / Twitter, [https://mobile.twitter.com/ekofreezy/status/734861035841159168?lang=de] Retrieved 20 May 2019 {{in lang|de}}</ref>

==Biography==
Bora was born in [[Cologne]] and grew up in [[Mönchengladbach]], raised by his single mother, a postal worker.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.myspace.com/eko-fresh |title=Official biography on his MySpace page|work=MySpace |access-date=12 November 2010}}{{unreliable source?|date=April 2014}}</ref>  He began rapping at age 14 and eventually dropped out of school in tenth grade. Afterwards, he started working in a shoe store to make ends meet. While working his retail gig, Bora met [[Kool Savas]], who agreed to produce his 2001 Royal Bunker debut EP, "Jetzt kommen wir auf die Sachen".<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|title=Eko Fresh {{!}} Biography & History|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/eko-fresh-mn0001925342/biography|website=AllMusic|language=en-us|access-date=5 May 2020}}</ref>

The German Stampede Wrestling, a [[professional wrestling promotion]] based in [[Cologne]], featured Bora.<ref>HAMBURG WEB, [http://www.hamburg-web.de/events/veranstaltung/German-Stampede-Wrestling-GSW-Northern-Night.htmGerman Stampede Wrestling GSW Northern Night]{{dead link|date=December 2017 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} Retrieved 5 September 2010 {{in lang|de}}</ref> He had wrestling matches in promotional events including ''Night in Motion X'', ''Southern Conflict II'', ''X-Limits'', ''Battlefield 2008'', and ''Northern Night'' since 2007.<ref>Online World of Wrestling, [https://archive.today/20131110231859/http://www.onlineworldofwrestling.com/results/gsw/German Stampede Wrestling] Retrieved 5 September 2010</ref>

Beginning in 2010, Eko Fresh began an extremely prolific run, releasing four new albums over a three year span. In 2013, he delivered the wildly successful Eksodus which topped the charts in Germany and reached the top ten in Austria and Switzerland as well.<ref name=":0" />

===Feuds with other rappers===
{{Main|Das Urteil (song)}}
In 2004, Eko Fresh started a feud with his song "Die Abrechnung", where he criticized his former mentors [[Kool Savas]], [[Optik Records]], [[Bushido (rapper)|Bushido]] and [[Aggro Berlin]].<ref name="markindahouse.myblog.de">http://markindahouse.myblog.de/markindahouse/page/1089116/Der-Beef-zwischen-KOOL-SAVAS-un-eko{{unreliable source?|date=April 2014}}</ref><ref>{{youtube|id=mlGK-aK9CRM|Die Abrechnung}}{{unreliable source?|date=April 2014}}</ref> The song was released on December 23, 2004 on ''Juice CD # 49'' and is a [[Diss Track|diss track]] against various other rappers, including his former mentor Kool Savas.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Google Translate|url=https://translate.google.com/translate?hl=en&sl=de&u=https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Die_Abrechnung_(Lied)&prev=search|website=translate.google.com|access-date=2020-05-05}}</ref> With the track billing in which he attacked Savas and Optik Records, he laid the foundation for the battle. Savas is suppressing his labelmates and MOR, Sava's former crew, would also have disintegrated for this reason, the statement said. The Berlin label Aggro and Bushido are also dissected in the track. However, he overdid it a little, which Sava's answer track quickly clarified the verdict.<ref>{{Cite web|title=HipHop Battle - Die besten Battles in Deutschland - World of Hip Hop & Rap - Mehr Music, Dance, Download, Videos und Charts|url=http://www.hip-hop.de/musik/hiphoprap/artikel/article/hiphop-battle-die-besten-battles-in-deutschland.html|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120924072157/http://www.hip-hop.de/musik/hiphoprap/artikel/article/hiphop-battle-die-besten-battles-in-deutschland.html|url-status=dead|archive-date=2012-09-24|date=2012-09-24|access-date=2020-05-05}}</ref> Many rappers replied to "Die Abrechnung", including Illmatic with "4Eko" and [[Fler]] with "Hollywoodtürke". Rappers from Optik Records also reacted: Caput with "Ich geb n fuck" and Ercandize with "Verdanken".<ref name="markindahouse.myblog.de"/>
