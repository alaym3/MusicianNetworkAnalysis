'''Genesis Yasmine Mohanraj''',<ref>[https://www.canadacompany.info/canada] {{dead link|date=March 2020}}</ref> known by her [[stage name]]  '''Tommy Genesis''', is a Canadian rapper, model and visual artist. In 2016, ''[[Dazed]]'' magazine described her as "the internet's most rebellious underground rap queen". Her music incorporates experimental style and often sexual verses.<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/lifestyle/7662384/tommy-genesis-rapper-downtown-records-style-interview|title=New Face, Fresh Style: 'Fetish Rapper' & New Downtown Records Signee Tommy Genesis|website=Billboard.com|access-date=2017-03-25}}</ref> Her debut album titled ''[[Tommy Genesis (album)|Tommy Genesis]]'' was released in November 2018. Her second album ''[[Goldilocks X]]'' was released in September 2021.

== Early life and education ==
Genesis was born in Vancouver, Canada. She is of [[Tamils|Tamil]] and [[Swedes|Swedish]] descent.<ref name=":1" /> She graduated from the [[Emily Carr University of Art and Design]]<ref>{{Cite web | url=https://ecuad.arcabc.ca/islandora/object/ecuad:6756?solr_nav%5Bid%5D=515201d4845b07265a48&solr_nav%5Bpage%5D=36&solr_nav%5Boffset%5D=17|title=2012 Graduation Exhibition|last=Mohanraj|first=Genesis Yasmine|date=2012|website=E. Collection at Emily Carr|access-date=June 28, 2019}}</ref> where she studied film and sculpture.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://beatroute.ca/2015/07/27/getting-to-the-roots-of-tommy-genesis/|title=Getting to the Roots of Tommy Genesis|last=Stopa|first=Thalia|date=July 27, 2015|website=BeatRoute Magazine|access-date=March 25, 2017}}</ref> Genesis is her birth name.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://i-d.vice.com/en_us/article/8x54xa/tommy-genesis-essay-exclusive|title=and then god created tommy|date=25 October 2017|website=I-d.vice.com}}</ref>

== Career ==
=== Music ===
Genesis started recording rap in 2013 as part of a project called G3NESIS.<ref name="allmusic bio"/> In 2015 Genesis signed with [[Awful Records]] and released her debut mixtape ''World'' ''Vision''.<ref name=":1" /> In 2017 she signed with [[Downtown Records]]/[[Universal Music Group|Universal]]. She collaborated with [[Abra (singer)|Abra]], another artist from Awful Records, on the track "Hair Like Water Wavy Like the Sea" from Genesis's debut mixtape ''World Vision''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.elleuk.com/life-and-culture/culture/news/a36805/tommy-genesis-instagram-miu-miu/|title=Tommy Genesis: 12 Things You Need To Know About The Bisexual Canadian 'Fetish Rapper'|date=3 July 2017|website=Elleuk.com}}</ref>

On 28 July 2018 she released the single "100 Bad". This song is the second collaboration that Tommy made with the music producer [[Charlie Heat]], with whom she previously collaborated on the 2017 song "Tommy". The song is featured on her debut album, ''[[Tommy Genesis (album)|Tommy Genesis]]'' through Downtown/The Orchard.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.clashmusic.com/news/listen-tommy-genesis-100-bad|title=Listen: Tommy Genesis - '100 Bad'|website=Clashmusic.com|accessdate= 1 July 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.highsnobiety.com/p/tommy-genesis-100-bad/|title=Tommy Genesis Talks a Big Game on '100 Bad' |website=HighSnobiety.com|accessdate= 1 July 2018}}</ref> Later, British singer-songwriter [[Charli XCX]] released a remix of "100 Bad" on 7 September. Her debut album, ''Tommy Genesis'' was released on 9 November 2018.

In 2020, she collaborated with American experimental hip-hop artist [[JPEGMAFIA]] on his ep called [[EP!]], on the track [[ROUGH 7]].

== Lyricism ==
Gender and sexuality are common themes in Genesis' music and she has described herself as a "fetish rapper".<ref name=":0" /> She is also an advocate against girl-on-girl cyberbullying.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://blog.urbanoutfitters.com/blog/class_of_2017_tommy_genesis|title=Class of 2017: Tommy Genesis - Urban Outfitters - Blog|website=Blog.urbanoutfitters.com|access-date=2017-03-26}}</ref>
