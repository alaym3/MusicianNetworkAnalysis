'''Joey Batey''' is an English actor, musician, singer and songwriter. He is known for portraying the bard Jaskier in the [[Netflix]] fantasy series ''[[The Witcher (TV series)|The Witcher]]''. He is also known for singing the song "[[Toss a Coin to Your Witcher]]", which was used in the series.

== Life and career ==
Batey was born in [[Newcastle upon Tyne]] in 1989. He has said he grew up in a "musical family" and has always been around music.<ref>{{cite podcast|url=https://im-obsessed-with-this.simplecast.com/episodes/the-witcher-ft-joey-batey-5wnSyzzg|title=The Witcher (ft. Joey Batey)|last=Finger|first=Bobby (Host)|publisher=I'm Obsessed With This ([[Netflix]])|date=25 February 2020|accessdate=12 March 2020}}</ref> He studied at [[Robinson College, Cambridge]]. Whilst at Cambridge, he co-wrote and performed the sketch show ‘Good Clean Men’ with Alex Owen and [[Ben Ashenden]] ([[The Pin (comedy act)|The Pin]]).<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.camdram.net/shows/2009-good-clean-men|title=Good Clean Men|access-date=2020-06-16}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://issuu.com/varsity/docs/issue705|title=Good. Clean. Men.|last=Reynolds|first=George|date=6 November 2009|newspaper=Varsity|language=en-GB|access-date=2020-06-16}}</ref>He began studies at L’École Internationale de Théâtre Jacques Lecoq, but left after a few weeks. He has never trained.

In 2013, Batey debuted in a British thriller ''Murder on the Home Front'' directed by [[Geoffrey Sax]]. In 2014, he appeared in ''[[The Riot Club]]'' directed by [[Lone Scherfig]]. In 2017, [[Paul McGuigan (filmmaker)|Paul McGuigan]] cast him as Eddie in ''[[Film Stars Don't Die in Liverpool]]''. Batey also played in the British TV series ''C.B. Strike'' (2017), ''In The Dark'' (2017), and ''Knightfall'' (2017).

He played the bard [[List of characters in The Witcher series|Jaskier]] in [[Netflix]]'s [[The Witcher (TV series)|adaptation of ''The Witcher'']], based on the Polish [[The Witcher|novel series of the same name]]. Batey's performance is notable due to his vocal prowess, which spawned the hit songs "[[Toss a Coin to Your Witcher]]" and "Her Sweet Kiss."<ref>{{Cite web| url =https://www.unitedagents.co.uk/joey-batey | title = Joey Batey|website=Unitedagents.co.uk}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web| url =https://www.imdb.com/name/nm5438541/bio?ref_=nm_ov_bio_sm | title =Biography|website=IMDb.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://deadline.com/2018/10/the-witcher-first-look-henry-cavill-as-geralt-of-rivia-netflix-cast-production-begins-1202492991/|title='The Witcher': First Look At Henry Cavill As Geralt Of Rivia; Netflix Rounds Out Cast As Production Begins|last=Petski|first=Denise|website=[[Deadline Hollywood]]|date=31 October 2018|accessdate=13 October 2018|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20181101015448/https://deadline.com/2018/10/the-witcher-first-look-henry-cavill-as-geralt-of-rivia-netflix-cast-production-begins-1202492991/|archive-date=1 November 2018|url-status=live}}</ref>

He is a vocalist and musician for the [[indie folk]] band The Amazing Devil, as well as the primary writer and composer.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.folkradio.co.uk/2016/07/amazing-devil-love-run/|title=The Amazing Devil: Love Run – Album Review|last=Davies|first=Mike|website=Folk Radio|date=19 July 2016|accessdate=5 January 2020|url-status=live}}</ref> Their most recent album, ''Ruin'', was released on 31 October 2021.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://theamazingdevil.bandcamp.com/album/ruin|title=ruin, by The Amazing Devil|website=Theamazingdevil.bandcamp.com|accessdate=31 October 2021}}</ref>
