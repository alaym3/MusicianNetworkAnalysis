'''Royal Republic''' is a Swedish [[Rock music|rock]] band from [[Malmö]]. The band is currently signed to [[Universal Music Group|Universal Music]] and managed by Odyssey Music Network.

==History==
[[File:RR Black.jpg|thumb|The band's logo]]
===2007–2012: Foundation and ''We Are the Royal''===

Royal Republic was founded in 2007. Their first album, ''We Are the Royal'', was recorded under the supervision of Swedish producer Anders Hallbäck in Malmö.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.beachhousemusic.com |title=Beach House Music |publisher=Beach House Music |date= |access-date=27 September 2015}}</ref> The album was mixed at ToyTown Studios and completed with the help of Stefan Glaumann.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.toytown.se |title=Presenter till barn och vuxna |publisher=Toytown.se |date= |access-date=27 September 2015}}</ref> On 6 August 2009, the record was released in Sweden and received wide acclaim from critics, fans and radio stations.

All three singles reached number 1 on [[Bandit Rock|Bandit Rock's]] Most-Wanted-List.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/royal-republic-mn0002117998/biography|title=Royal Republic - Biography & History - AllMusic|website=AllMusic|access-date=6 October 2017}}</ref> ''Tommy-Gun'' also reached first place on the [[MTV Rocks (British and Irish TV channel)|MTV Rocks]] charts.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.stubhub.com/royal-republic-tickets/performer/722546/|title=StubHub!|website=StubHub|access-date=6 October 2017}}</ref> Royal Republic gained success in Germany through a collective tour with the [[Donots]].<ref name ="ntv">{{cite web|url=http://www.n-tv.de/leute/film/Save-The-Nation-Neues-Album-der-Band-Royal-Republic-article7033991.html|title=Royal Republic retten die Nation|first=n-tv|last=Nachrichtenfernsehen|website=n-tv.de|access-date=6 October 2017}}</ref>

===2012–2014: ''Save the Nation''===

On 24 August 2012 the band released their second album titled ''Save the Nation''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.discogs.com/de/artist/1930046-Royal-Republic|title=Royal Republic|website=Discogs|access-date=6 October 2017}}</ref> It was recorded by Michael Ilbert at Hansa Studio 1 and Hansa Mix Room.<ref name ="ntv"/> [[Tom Coyne (music engineer)|Tom Coyne]] mastered the record at Sterling Sound in New York City.<ref name ="tom">{{cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/tom-coyne-mn0001032225|title=Tom Coyne - Credits - AllMusic|website=AllMusic|access-date=6 October 2017}}</ref>
Save the Nation was widely critically acclaimed and resulted in a better chart position than its predecessor ''We are the Royal''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.metal-temple.com/site/catalogues/entry/reviews/cd_3/r_2/royal-republic-save.htm|title=Royal Republic - Save The Nation (Review by Lewis McBlane)|last=Steinmetal|website=metal-temple.com|access-date=6 October 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sputnikmusic.com/review/53101/Royal-Republic-Save-The-Nation/|title=Royal Republic - Save The Nation (album review ) - Sputnikmusic|website=sputnikmusic.com|access-date=6 October 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://teamrock.com/review/2012-10-16/royal-republic-save-the-nation|title=Royal Republic: Save The Nation|date=16 October 2012|website=teamrock.com|access-date=6 October 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.rocksins.com/reviews/royal-republic-save-the-nation-3/|title=Royal Republic Save The Nation Album Review|website=rocksins.com|date=17 October 2012|access-date=6 October 2017}}</ref>
The release of the album was followed by concerts in Australia, Great Britain, Germany, France and the [[Benelux]] countries.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.setlist.fm/setlists/royal-republic-53d58f29.html?page=6|title=Royal Republic Concert Setlists (page 6)|website=setlist.fm|access-date=6 October 2017}}</ref> Through the support of bands like [[Social Distortion]], [[The Subways]], [[Die Toten Hosen]] and [[blink-182]] the band earned major recognition.<ref name ="ntv"/><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.rock-regeneration.co.uk/wordpress/2012/05/the-subwaysroyal-republicturbowolf/|title=Rock Regeneration - The Subways/Royal Republic/Turbowolf|website=rock-regeneration.co.uk|access-date=6 October 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.dietotenhosen.de/band/empfehlungen/cd/save-nation-das-neue-album-unserer-supportband-royal-republic |title=Archived copy |access-date=24 August 2016 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160824100519/http://www.dietotenhosen.de/band/empfehlungen/cd/save-nation-das-neue-album-unserer-supportband-royal-republic |archive-date=24 August 2016  }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.abendblatt.de/kultur-live/article108724922/Social-Distortion-der-schoenste-Haertefall-der-Stadtpark-Saison.html|title=Social Distortion, der schönste Härtefall der Stadtpark-Saison|first=Hamburger Abendblatt -|last=Hamburg|website=abendblatt.de|date=14 August 2012|access-date=6 October 2017}}</ref>

In addition, Royal Republic played in 2013 at the tradition-rich German festivals [[Rock am Ring and Rock im Park]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.rock-am-ring.com/history/2013|title=History - Rock am Ring 2017|website=Rock am Ring|access-date=6 October 2017}}</ref>

===2014–2016: ''Royal Republic and The Nosebreakers''===

In 2014 Royal Republic turned towards [[acoustic music]]. On 28 March the Swedes released Royal Republic and the Nosebreakers, on which songs from the previous albums ''We Are the Royal'' and ''Save the Nation'' were re-interpreted in an acoustic and [[Country music|country-like]] way.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.royalrepublic.net/video/royal-republic-and-the-nosebreakers--6--45-.html|title=Royal Republic - Royal Republic And The Nosebreakers - Acoustic EP|first=Royal|last=Republic|website=Royal Republic|access-date=6 October 2017}}</ref> For the realization of the project, Adam Grahn, Hannes Irengard, Jonas Almén and Per Andreasson were assisted by studio musicians Daniel Olsson, Anders Svensson and Oskar Appelqvist.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://paraplyproduktion.blogspot.de/2014/01/kunglig-turnepremiar.html|title=Kunglig turnépremiär|website=paraplyproduktion.blogspot.de|access-date=6 October 2017}}</ref> The latter even joined the band on the same tour.

All songs except ''This Means War'' were recorded at the Sunnana Studio by Markus Nilsson.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://metunes.ru/release-idfedgjdg/Royal-Republic-And-The-Nosebreakers|title=Royal Republic - Royal Republic And The Nosebreakers listen to all release completely in mp3, download release album mp3|website=metunes.ru|access-date=6 October 2017}}</ref> They were largely mixed by Michael Illbert, who already mixed the previous record, at the Hansa Mix Room. This time the mastering was carried out at Chartmakers by Svante Forsbäck.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.discogs.com/de/Royal-Republic-Royal-Republic-And-The-Nosebreakers/release/5387311|title=Royal Republic - Royal Republic And The Nosebreakers|website=Discogs|access-date=6 October 2017}}</ref>
The accompanying tour for this acoustic album consisted of ten dates, which led the band to Germany, France, Switzerland, Czech Republic, Austria, Poland, and Sweden.

===2016: ''Weekend Man''===

On 26 February 2016 Royal Republic released their fourth album, ''Weekend Man''. Christian Neander and Michael Tibes were brought in as producers for this project.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.universal-music.de/royal-republic/diskografie/detail/product:328927/weekend-man|title=Royal Republic - Musik|website=universal-music.de|access-date=6 October 2017}}</ref> The latter was also responsible for the recordings that took place at Fuzzfactory in [[Berlin]]. Like their predecessors, ''Weekend Man'' was mixed by Michael Illbert at the Hansa Mix Room, and mastered by [[Tom Coyne (music engineer)|Tom Coyne]] of Sterling Sound.<ref name ="tom"/><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.discogs.com/de/Royal-Republic-Weekend-Man/release/8677301|title=Royal Republic - Weekend Man|website=Discogs|access-date=6 October 2017}}</ref>

===2019: ''Club Majesty''===

On 31 May 2019 Royal Republic released 'Club Majesty'.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.nuclearblast.de/en/label/music/band/news/details/5981773.5844232.royal-republic-raquo-club-majesty-laquo-out-now.html|title=ROYAL REPUBLIC - "Club Majesty" out now!|date=31 May 2019}}</ref> It was produced by largely the same team as ''Weekend Man'';  Christian Neander and Michael Tibes with the addition of Adam Grahn. Like its predecessor it was mainly recorded at FuzzFactory Berlin but also Vicious D. Licious Production Malmo. The album was taken out on the road commencing summer 2019 with the festival circuit (appearing on the main stage at Download) before a European cities tour in the autumn and winter.
