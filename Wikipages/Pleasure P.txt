'''Marcus Ramone Cooper Sr''', better known by his stage name '''Pleasure P''', is an American [[Contemporary R&B|R&B]] singer-songwriter, who experienced success as a member of the group [[Pretty Ricky]] before embarking on a solo career in 2007. His debut album ''[[The Introduction of Marcus Cooper]]'' was released in June 2009. In October 2014, Pleasure P and the rest of Pretty Ricky officialized on Instagram a reunion tour with a Bluestars 2 album for 2015.<ref>[http://ratedrnb.com/2014/09/theyre-back-pretty-ricky-teases-reunion-tour/ THEY'RE BACK: Pretty Ricky Teases Reunion Tour]. Ratedrnb.com (September 18, 2014). Retrieved on December 25, 2016.</ref> His first reunion single with Pretty Ricky is called "Puddles" and has been released during Valentine's Day 2015.  It was the most requested song on South Florida's 99 Jamz radio station in the days following its release.<ref>[https://instagram.com/p/zlh8o4mwL7/?modal=true Instagram]. Instagram (February 27, 2015). Retrieved on December 25, 2016.</ref>

==Career==

===2009–2012: ''The Introduction of Marcus Cooper''===
After leaving the group [[Pretty Ricky]], Pleasure P began working on his solo career, he released his solo debut single "[[Did You Wrong]]" on July 22, 2008.<ref>[http://www.last.fm/music/Pleasure+P/Did+You+Wrong Did You Wrong – Pleasure P — Listen and discover music at]. Last.fm (July 22, 2008). Retrieved on December 25, 2016.</ref> The single debuted at No. 90 on the Billboard 100 Hot charts, and debuted at No. 20 on the top R&B/Hip-Hop charts as well. Pleasure P also released a 2008 EP entitled "[[Boyfriend No. 2|Boyfriend #2]]", including "[[Did You Wrong]]" and a promo single "Boyfriend #2", released digitally and as an LP disc. Videos for "[[Did You Wrong]]" and "[[Boyfriend No. 2|Boyfriend #2]]" were released digitally and on MySpace. Later on in 2009 he also released the song "[[Under (Pleasure P song)|Under]]" from the album. His solo debut album, ''[[The Introduction of Marcus Cooper]]'' was released on June 9, 2009. The album was originally to be released in January, but was finally released after several delays. Pleasure P's career received a boost by being featured in the second single from [[Flo Rida]]'s album ''[[R.O.O.T.S.]]'', gaining publicity from his presence on its guest list. In 2013, he appeared in [[Bad Girls Club (season 8)|Bad Girls Club 8: Las Vegas]].

===2012: New label and ''King of Romance''===
On June 21, 2012, [[Pleasure P.]] released the first single off his upcoming second album titled "I Love Girls" featuring Young Money artist [[Tyga]]. His upcoming album is entitled ''King of Romance'' and is due out later this year. This will be the first album off of his new record deal with [[E1 Music]]. Pleasure P is working on his second single called "I Really Need to Know". On September 16, 2013, Pleasure P came out with a new single called " Kiss Me".<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.blakmusicfirst.fr/14-categorie-11377933.html |title=Archived copy |access-date=November 5, 2012 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130123010221/http://www.blakmusicfirst.fr/14-categorie-11377933.html |archive-date=January 23, 2013 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

===2014-present: Pleasure P New Mixtape Pretty Ricky Reunion, and Present===

On February 6, 2014, Pleasure P announced on his official Instagram that he will be releasing his first single titled " Letter to My Ex." from his mixtape "Break Up to Make Up", in which was released on February 14, 2014.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://orangemixtapes.com/mixtape/P/643/1062-pleasure-p-break-up-to-make-up.html |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140222154701/http://orangemixtapes.com/mixtape/P/643/1062-pleasure-p-break-up-to-make-up.html |url-status=dead |archive-date=February 22, 2014 |title=Pleasure P – Break Up To Make Up – Pleasure P |access-date=October 12, 2019}}</ref>

In 2014, Pleasure P and the original members of his former group Pretty Ricky to announce an official reunion.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://keepittrill.com/online/2014/09/pretty-ricky-exclusive/ |title=HugeDomains.com – KeepItTrill.com is for sale |publisher=Keep It Trill |access-date=October 12, 2019}}</ref> The reunion aims an international reunion tour and a new album (Bluestars 2) with all the original members. The first commercial single of the group is called "Puddles" has been released during Valentine's Day 2015.<ref>[http://www.prettyricky.com/ www.RunwayInParis.com]. Prettyricky.com. Retrieved on December 25, 2016.</ref> In 2018, Pleasure P is featured in "Love & HipHop: Miami” with his girlfriend for 2 years Shay Johnson and the rest of the Pretty Ricky crew such as [[Spectacular Smith|Spectacular]] talking about the Pretty Ricky reunion, new album, New tours and more.

==Personal life==
Pleasure dated comedian [[Eddie Murphy]]'s daughter Bria in 2008, but the two broke up after two years.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://singersroom.com/content/2012-10-17/marcus-cooper-on-break-up-with-bria-murphy-it-hurt-me/|title=Marcus Cooper on Break Up with Bria Murphy: "It Hurt Me"}}</ref> He started dating ''[[Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta|Love & Hip Hop Atlanta]]'' star Shay Johnson in 2018. In 2019 Pleasure stated he ended his relationship with her.

In March 2020, Pleasure was arrested outside of a [[Checkers and Rally's|Checkers]] in Miami Gardens, FL. He got into an altercation with a female employee of the fast food chain after there was mistake with the $60 of food that he ordered. Pleasure was taken into custody at 5:15&nbsp;a.m. and was released a few hours later after posting bail.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.tmz.com/2020/03/05/pleasure-p-arrested-battery-checkers-drive-thru-food-attack/|title=Pleasure P Arrested for Battery After Checkers Drive-Thru Altercation|website=TMZ|language=en|access-date=March 11, 2020}}</ref>

He has a son named after him.
