'''Pegboard Nerds''' is a Danish-Norwegian electronic music group, consisting of Norwegian DJ Alexander Odden and Danish DJ [[Michael Parsberg]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.youredm.com/2015/11/10/aspire-to-inspire-095-pegboard-nerds/|title=Aspire to Inspire 095: Pegboard Nerds|last=Morrow|first=Stacy|date=10 November 2015|website=Your EDM|access-date=28 June 2016}}</ref> The name Pegboard Nerds is an anagram of the duo's surnames, Odden and Parsberg. Odden and Parsberg first met in 2005 and formed the group in 2011. Previously, their records were released under separate projects and both have been producing since the 1990s.

On 21 October 2015, the duo released ''[[Pink Cloud (EP)|Pink Cloud]]'' to fund [[breast cancer]] research which went to number 2 on iTunes Dance Albums, and into the Top 10 on ''Billboard'' Dance albums.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.techtimes.com/articles/114927/20151211/pegboard-nerds-emoji-video-explores-intersection-between-humans-and-emojis-for-a-good-cause-video.htm|title=Pegboard Nerds 'Emoji' Video Explores Intersection Between Humans And Emojis For A Good Cause (Video)|date=11 December 2015|website=Tech Times|access-date=24 May 2016|author=James Geddes}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.dancingastronaut.com/2015/10/pegboard-nerds-take-arms-breast-cancer-pink-clouds/|title=Pegboard Nerds take up arms against breast cancer with 'Pink Clouds'|date=6 October 2015|website=Dancing Astronaut|language=en-US|access-date=24 May 2016}}</ref>

In 2015, their collaboration with [[Jauz]], a single titled "Get On Up", was one of the top bass house songs of the year. "Superstar" with [[Nghtmre]] and [[Krewella]] was the number 1 dubstep song on the Beatport Dubstep Charts of May 2016 and peaked at 28 on ''Billboard''{{'}}s Dance/Mix Show Airplay chart in October 2016. Their collaboration "Deep In The Night" with Montreal-based artist Snails was released in August 2016.

In 2017, the duo released ''[[Nerds by Nature]]'' with a remix EP being released later that year.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://dubstepsmash.com/pegboard-nerds-release-nerds-by-nature-the-remixes/|title=Pegboard Nerds Release ‘Nerds By Nature’ (The Remixes) - Dubstep Smash|date=17 May 2017|work=Dubstep Smash|access-date=9 August 2017|language=en-US|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170809043219/http://dubstepsmash.com/pegboard-nerds-release-nerds-by-nature-the-remixes/|archive-date=9 August 2017|url-status=dead}}</ref> The EP reached number 12 on the [[Top Dance/Electronic Albums]] [[Billboard (magazine)|''Billboard'']] charts<ref name=":0" /> and received generally positive reviews.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://edm.com/articles/2017/1/13/pegboard-nerds-by-nature|title=Pegboard Nerds Show their Boundless Nature in New EP [LISTEN]|date=13 January 2017|work=EDM.com|access-date=9 August 2017|language=en-US|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170520002423/http://edm.com/articles/2017/1/13/pegboard-nerds-by-nature|archivedate=20 May 2017|df=dmy-all}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.youredm.com/2017/01/14/nerds-by-nature-ep/|title=Pegboard Nerds - Nerds by Nature EP [Monstercat]|last=Landon Fleury|date=14 January 2017|website=Your EDM|access-date=9 August 2017}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://thebanginbeats.com/2017/01/pegboard-nerds-nerds-nature-ep/|title=Pegboard Nerds - Nerds by Nature [EP] - The Bangin Beats|date=18 January 2017|work=The Bangin Beats|access-date=9 August 2017|language=en-US|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170809085918/http://thebanginbeats.com/2017/01/pegboard-nerds-nerds-nature-ep/|archive-date=9 August 2017|url-status=dead}}</ref>
