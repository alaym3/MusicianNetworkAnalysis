'''Francesca Calearo''' (born 16 January 2002), known professionally as '''Madame''', is an Italian rapper and singer-songwriter.
After signing a recording contract with [[Sugar Music]] at the age of 16, she gained popularity in August 2019, when her single "[[Sciccherie]]" was shared by [[Cristiano Ronaldo]] on his [[Instagram]] profile. Between 2019 and 2020, she released several singles as a lead artist and collaborations with, among others, [[Marracash]], [[Negramaro]], [[Dario Faini|DRD]], [[Ghali (rapper)|Ghali]], [[Ensi (rapper)|Ensi]] and producer Chris Nolan, and she competed in the [[Sanremo Music Festival 2021|71st Sanremo Music Festival]] in March 2021. Her entry "[[Voce (song)|Voce]]", as well as the single "[[Il mio amico (Madame song)|Il mio amico]]" recorded with [[Fabri Fibra]], were included in her debut album ''[[Madame (album)|Madame]]'', released on 19 March 2021.

==Biography==
Francesca Calearo was born in [[Vicenza]] on 16 January 2002, and raised in [[Creazzo]], also in the [[province of Vicenza]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://metropolitanmagazine.it/madame-voce-sanremo-2021/|work=Metropolitan Magazine|language=it|title=Madame, per la prima volta al Festival di Sanremo con il brano Voce|author=Nicky Abrami|date=18 December 2020|accessdate=16 January 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://corrieredelveneto.corriere.it/vicenza/cultura-tempo-libero/20_luglio_14/madame-rap-queen-vicenza-28d5b33e-c5a4-11ea-8bab-a348741614de.shtml|work=Corriere del Veneto|language=it|title=Madame, la rap queen di Vicenza|author=Francesco Verni|accessdate=16 January 2021}}</ref> As a child, she developed an interest in music thanks to her father, an amateur musician with an interest in Italian ''[[cantautori]]''.<ref name="Blitz Quotidiano"/><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bohmagazine.it/madame/|work=Boh Magazine|title=Rapper e Beatmaker}}</ref> During the [[scuola media|lower secondary school]] years, Calearo started taking piano lessons, and she later continued to study music as an autodidact, while listening to artists like [[Ludovico Einaudi]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ilgiornale.it/news/spettacoli/arriva-madame-pop-liceale-vicenza-che-ronaldo-ha-lanciato-1736676.html|work=[[Il Giornale]]|language=it|accessdate=16 January 2021|title=Arriva la Madame pop. La liceale di Vicenza che Ronaldo ha lanciato|date=6 August 2019|author=Paolo Giordano}}</ref> She studied [[Liceo delle scienze umane|Human Sciences at the ''liceo'']] Don Giuseppe Fogazzaro in Vicenza.<ref name="la Repubblica 2019"/> She chose the pseudonym "Madame" after trying a random generator for drag queen names, which produced "Madame Wild" for Calearo.<ref name="RadioZeta">{{cite web|url=https://www.radiozeta.it/notizie/articoli/chi-e-madame-la-prima-rapper-donna-in-gara-tra-i-campioni-del-festival-di-sanremo/|publisher=Radio Zeta. [[RTL 102.5]] Hit Radio S.r.l.|language=it|accessdate=16 January 2021|title=Chi è Madame, la prima rapper donna in gara tra i campioni del Festival di Sanremo|author=Ilaria Arpino|date=4 January 2021}}</ref> She found it represented the idea of "elegance and sophistication" she wanted to pursue, as well as her will to be "a Woman with capital W".<ref name="Blitz Quotidiano"/>

Madame's debut single, "Anna", was produced by rapper Eiemgei and released in September 2018 by Arcade Army Records.<ref name="Klamour">{{cite web|url=https://www.klamour.it/madame-chi-e-la-cantante-rap-di-anna-sciccherie/|publisher=Klamour.it|language=it|accessdate=16 January 2021|title=Madame: chi è la cantante rap di Anna e Sciccherie|author=Gabriele Zangarini|date=9 August 2019}}</ref><ref name="Rebel Mag">{{cite web|url=https://www.rebelmag.it/2018/09/15/chi-e-madame-la-ragazza-di-anna/|work=Rebel Mag|date=15 September 2019|accessdate=16 January 2021|title=Chi è Madame, la ragazza di Anna}}</ref> At the age of 16, she signed a recording contract with [[Sugar Music]], which released her second single, "[[Sciccherie]]", on 14 December 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.insidemusic.it/madame-trap-intervista/|publisher=InsideMusic.it|language=it|title=Intervista a Madame, la nuova stella della musica urban italiana|author=Antonio Sartori|date=2 February 2019|accessdate=16 January 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.radiobicocca.it/madame-17-anni-di-puro-stile/|title=Madame: 17 anni di puro stile|publisher=radiobicocca.it|language=it|date=5 July 2019|accessdate=17 November 2020}}</ref><ref name="Blitz Quotidiano">{{cite web|url=https://www.blitzquotidiano.it/musica-showblitz/madame-sciccherie-cliche-intervista-2993397/|publisher=BlitzQuotidiano.it|language=it|accessdate=16 January 2021|title=Madame, Sciccherie oltre i cliché. L'intervista|author=Gianluca Pace|date=7 February 2019}}</ref> The song received airplay from Italian radio networks including [[Radio DeeJay]].<ref name="Radio Deejay">{{cite web|url=https://www.deejay.it/articoli/cristiano-ronaldo-ascolta-sciccherie-di-madame-la-reazione-della-giovane-cantante/|title=Cristiano Ronaldo ascolta Sciccherie di Madame: la reazione della giovane cantante|publisher=[[Radio DeeJay]]|language=it|date=6 August 2019|accessdate=17 November 2020}}</ref> In August 2019, [[Cristiano Ronaldo]] posted an [[Instagram]] story on his account, with a video of himself listeing to "Sciccherie" while on his home's terrace. Ronaldo's story attracted media attention on the song, leading several media to talk about Madame's music for the first time.<ref name="la Repubblica 2019">{{cite web|url=https://www.repubblica.it/spettacoli/people/2019/08/06/news/madame-232988737/|work=[[la Repubblica]]|language=it|title=Madame, la rapper italiana che piace a Cristiano Ronaldo|date=6 August 2019|accessdate=16 January 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rebelmag.it/2019/08/07/ci-voleva-cristiano-ronaldo-per-far-parlare-di-madame/|work=Rebel Mag|title=Ci voleva Cristiano Ronaldo per far parlare di Madame?|date=7 August 2019|accessdate=16 January 2021|language=it}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://video.lastampa.it/spettacoli/musica/ronaldo-ascolta-il-brano-di-madame-giovane-talento-italiano/102180/102190|work=[[La Stampa]]|language=it|title=Ronaldo ascolta il brano di Madame, giovane talento italiano|date=4 August 2019|accessdate=16 January 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ilgiornaledivicenza.it/argomenti/spettacoli/cr7-stregato-dalla-vicentina-madame-1.7536449|work=Il Giornale di Vicenza|language=it|accessdate=16 January 2021|title=CR7 stregato dalla vicentina Madame|author=Filippo Bordignon|date=6 August 2019}}</ref>

The singles "[[17 (Madame song)|17]]", "La promessa dell'anno", "[[Baby (Madame song)|Baby]]", "[[Sentimi]]" and "[[Clito (song)|Clito]]" were also released between 2019 and 2020.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.insidemusic.it/madame-17-singolo/|publisher=InsideMusic.it|language=it|title=17 di Madame: nuovo singolo|author=Giuseppe Falbo|date=11 July 2019|accessdate=16 January 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.newsic.it/madame-ascolta-la-promessa-dellanno-il-suo-nuovo-singolo/|publisher=Newsic.it|language=it|title=Madame, ascolta La Promessa dell'anno il suo nuovo singolo|date=8 October 2019|accessdate=16 January 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.radioitalia.it/news/madame/nuova_uscita/20778_madame_baby_e_il_nuovo_singolo_della_cantante_e_rapper_classe_2002.php|publisher=[[Radio Italia Solo Musica Italiana]]|language=it|accessdate=16 January 2021|date=28 February 2020|title=Madame: Baby è il nuovo singolo della cantante e rapper, classe 2002}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.allmusicitalia.it/news/madame-sentimi-e-il-nuovo-singolo-prodotto-dai-crookers.html|title=Madame, Sentimi è il nuovo singolo prodotto dai Crookers|publisher=AllMusicItalia.it|language=it|author=Edoardo Bettoja|date=24 April 2020|accessdate=16 January 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rockol.it/news-717878/madame-la-nuova-canzone-clito-ha-un-testo-esplicito|author=Claudio Cabona|title=Madame, un click sul Clito per fare i conti con la vita|date=6 November 2020|accessdate=16 January 2021}}</ref>
In late 2019, Madame appeared on the album ''[[Persona (Marracash album)|Persona]]'' by Italian rapper [[Marracash]], performing as a featured artist in the track named after her pseudonym.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ilmattino.it/spettacoli/musica/marracash_album_persona_successo_disco_platino_ultime_notizie_oggi_29_novembre_2019-4894612.html|work=[[Il Mattino]]|language=it|title=Marracash, il suo nuovo album Persona è già disco di platino|date=29 November 2019}}</ref> 
In the summer of 2020, producer [[Dario Faini|DRD]] released the single "[[Defuera]]", with vocals by [[Ghali (rapper)|Ghali]], Marracash and Madame.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.it/musica/news-musica/guarda-il-video-di-defuera-il-primo-singolo-di-drd-con-ghali-marracash-e-madame/525187/|work=[[Rolling Stone (magazine)|Rolling Stone]]|language=it|title=Guarda il video di Defuera, il primo singolo di DRD con Ghali, Marracash e Madame|date=18 July 2020|accessdate=16 January 2021}}</ref>
She also recorded the song "Non è vero niente" with Italian rock band [[Negramaro]], included in their album ''Contatto'' in late 2020.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://tg24.sky.it/spettacolo/musica/2020/10/22/negramaro-madame-non-e-vero-niente|title=Negramaro, tra i brani del prossimo album Non è vero niente con Madame|publisher=tg24.sky.it|language=it|date=22 October 2020|accessdate=17 November 2020}}</ref>

In 2020 Madame was among the fifty candidates for the title of "Italian Woman of the Year" for ''D: La repubblica delle donne'' ({{lit|D: The republic of women}}), a magazine attached to the newspaper [[la Repubblica]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rapologia.it/2020/10/21/d-repubblica-madame/|title=Madame è tra le candidate di D la Repubblica per l'iniziativa volta a proclamare la donna dell'anno 2020.|publisher=rapologia.it|language=it|date=21 October 2020|accessdate=25 November 2020}}</ref> 
Madame made her first appearance on Italian television on 3 December 2020, when she opened the semi-final of the [[X Factor (Italian season 14)|fourteenth season]] of ''[[X Factor (Italian TV series)|X Factor]]'' on [[Sky Italia|Sky TV]], singing her song "Baby" in a duet with contestant [[Blind (rapper)|Blind]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.donnaglamour.it/chi-e-francesca-calearo/curiosita/|title=Madame: scopri chi è davvero la super ospite di X Factor 2020|publisher=donnaglamour.it|language=it|date=3 December 2020|accessdate=5 December 2020}}</ref> The following week, on 10 December, she was again a guest of the show, performing "Non è vero niente" with [[Negramaro]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://xfactor.sky.it/video/negramaro-madame-ospiti-finale-636169|title=I Negramaro ospiti della Finale accompagnati da Madame|publisher=xfactor.sky.it|language=it|date=11 December 2020|accessdate=11 December 2020}}</ref>

In March 2021, Madame competed in the [[Sanremo Music Festival 2021|71st Sanremo Music Festival]], performing the song "[[Voce (song)|Voce]]".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ansa.it/sito/notizie/topnews/2020/12/17/sanremo-irama-fulminacci-madame-tra-i-big_5ac1c857-fb98-44a5-bba5-0f1f79f48d8c.html|title=Sanremo: Irama, Fulminacci, Madame tra i Big|publisher=ansa.it|language=it|date=17 December 2020|accessdate=18 December 2020}}</ref> She became the first female rapper to compete in the contest's main section, and the youngest singer among the established artists competing in the 2021 contest.<ref name="RadioZeta"/><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.msn.com/it-it/intrattenimento/musica/madame-%C3%A8-lei-la-pi%C3%B9-giovane-sul-palco-di-sanremo-2021/ar-BB1c2oLa|publisher=[[MSN News]]|language=it|title=Madame: è lei la più giovane sul palco di Sanremo 2021|date=18 December 2020|accessdate=16 January 2021}}</ref> 
During the festival, she came out as bisexual.<ref>{{cite news|author=Silvia Fumarola|url=https://www.repubblica.it/dossier/spettacoli/sanremo-2021/2021/03/04/news/sanremo_2021_madame-290364346/|title=Sanremo 2021, Madame: "Il pudore è solo chiusura mentale I piedi nudi? Per comodità"|trans-title=Sanremo 2021, Madame: "Modesty is only narrow-mindedness. Barefoot? Only for comfort"|work=la Repubblica|language=it|date=4 March 2021|access-date=7 March 2021}}</ref>
Her entry "Voce" was included in her [[Madame (album)|self-titled debut album]], released on 19 March 2021.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://tg24.sky.it/spettacolo/musica/2021/03/17/madame-il-dico-desordio|title=L'album d'esordio, 'Madame': bisogna sentirsi liberi di essere sé stessi senza vergogna|date=18 March 2021|author=Federica Pirchio|work=[[Sky TG24]]|access-date=19 March 2021|language=it}}</ref>
The album was also preceded by the single "[[Il mio amico (Madame song)|Il mio amico]]", featuring Italian rapper [[Fabri Fibra]] and released in January 2021.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://tg24.sky.it/spettacolo/musica/2021/01/13/madame-fabri-fibra-il-mio-amico|publisher=[[Sky Italia]]|language=it|title=Madame, il nuovo singolo è Il mio amico con Fabri Fibra|date=13 January 2021}}</ref> 
Both the album and the song "Voce" were awarded a Targa Tenco, for Best Debut Album and Best Song, respectively.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rockol.it/news-723683/targhe-tenco-2021-premi-a-madame-e-samuele-bersani-i-vincitori|publisher=Rockol.it|language=it|title=Targhe Tenco 2021, premi a Madame e Samuele Bersani: i vincitori|date=13 July 2021|access-date=13 July 2021}}</ref>
Two new songs, entitled "[[Marea (song)|Marea]]" and "[[Tu mi hai capito]]", were released as singles on 4 July and 3 September 2021 respectively, and later added to the digital version of her debut album.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sorrisi.com/musica/canzoni/madame-torna-con-il-nuovo-singolo-marea/|work=[[TV Sorrisi e Canzoni]]|language=it|title=Madame torna con il nuovo singolo Marea|author=Giulia Ciavarelli|date=4 June 2021|access-date=13 July 2021}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.allmusicitalia.it/news/madame-tu-lhai-capito-testo-sfera-ebbasta.html|title=Madame unisce la sua musica a quella di Sfera Ebbasta per il nuovo singolo, 'Tu l'hai capito'|date=31 August 2021|work=All Music Italia|access-date=10 September 2021}}</ref>

==Musical style==
Among her influences, Madame cites singer-songwriter [[Fabrizio De André]], as well as [[trap music|trap]] and, despite not having southern origin, Sicilian [[neomelodic music]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.mtv.it/musica/madame-chi-e-la-cantante-di-sciccherie/|publisher=[[MTV Italy]]|language=it|title=Madame: chi è la cantante di Sciccherie, Baby e molte altre|date=20 July 2020}}</ref><ref name="Rebel Mag"/> Instrumental music also played an important role in her artistic development, with artists such as [[Ludovico Einaudi]] and [[Van Halen]].<ref name="Rebel Mag"/>

In 2019, her musical style was described by ''[[la Repubblica]]'' as "[[Urban contemporary|urban]], a very wide container in which [[trap music|trap]] and [[rap (music)|rap]] can merge, together with several other contemporary sounds".<ref name="la Repubblica 2019"/> Claudio Cabona wrote for Rockol.it that Madame's lyrical style is sometimes "visceral", and sometimes very explicit, without being banal.<ref name="Rockol Il Mio Amico">{{cite web|url=https://www.rockol.it/news-719410/madame-esce-il-mio-amico-con-fabri-fibra-la-recensione|publisher=Rockol.it|language=it|title=Madame, Il mio amico con Fabri Fibra: l'ascolto di Rockol|author=Claudio Cabona|date=14 January 2021|accessdate=16 January 2021}}</ref> He added that all her songs are characterized by very particular voice sounds.<ref name="Rockol Il Mio Amico"/> On ''Klamour'', Gabriele Zangarini compared her voice in "Sciccherie" to [[La Rappresentante di Lista]], describing it as "sometimes enraged, then whispered, often theatrical".<ref name="Klamour"/>
