'''Riccardo Fabbriconi''', known professionally as '''Blanco''' (born 10 February 2003) is an Italian singer and rapper.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.redbull.com/it-it/chi-e-blanco|title= Chi è Blanco: la vita del cantante de La Canzone Nostra|first=Claudio|last=Biazzetti|work=[[Red Bull]]|date=19 May 2021|access-date=29 September 2021}}</ref>

He debuted with the [[studio album]] ''Blu celeste'' in Septembre 2021, which peaked at number one of [[Federazione Industria Musicale Italiana|FIMI]]'s album chart.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.it/musica/interviste-musica/ma-sei-normale-blanco/581916/|title=Ma sei normale, Blanco?|first=Claudio|last=Todesco|work=[[Rolling Stone]]|date=10 September 2021|access-date=29 September 2021}}</ref> His most successful song, "[[Mi fai impazzire]]", topped the Italian single chart for eight weeks and was certified quintuple platinum in Italy.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://tg24.sky.it/spettacolo/musica/2021/06/18/blanco-sfera-ebbasta-mi-fai-impazzire-testo|title=Blanco e Sfera Ebbasta, il testo di Mi fai impazzire il nuovo singolo|work=[[Sky TG24]]|date=18 June 2021|access-date=29 September 2021}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://4quartimagazine.it/classifiche-mi-fai-impazzire-singolo-piu-venduto/|title=Classifiche: "Mi fai impazzire" è il singolo più venduto in Italia|first=Andrea|last=Picariello|work=4Quarti Magazine|date=26 July 2021|access-date=29 September 2021}}</ref>
