'''Vundabar''' is an American [[indie rock]] band from [[Boston]], [[Massachusetts]].

2018}}</ref> Vundabar released their first full-length album in 2013 titled ''Antics''.<ref name="consequenceofsound">{{cite web|last1=Kaye|first1=Ben|title=Stream: Vundabar’s sophomore album ==Gawk Records==
In an interview with [https://soundofboston.com/ Sound of Boston], McDonald explains that the band started a cassette label named Gawk Records in order avoid financial pressures of signing to a label and to foster a community of artists.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Marketti |first1=Anna |title=Local Spotlight: Vundabar |url=https://soundofboston.com/local-spotlight-vundabar-2/ |website=Sound of Boston |accessdate=8 July 2020}}</ref>
