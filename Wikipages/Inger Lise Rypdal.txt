'''Inger Lise Rypdal''' (born 14 December 1949 in [[Lena, Norway]]) is a [[Norwegians|Norwegian]] singer and actress in many different genres: [[pop (music)|pop]], rock, theater, film, and musical. She is the sister of singer [[Maj Britt Andersen]] and was married (1969–1985) to guitarist and composer [[Terje Rypdal]].<ref name=nbl>{{cite web | url=https://nbl.snl.no/Inger_Lise_Rypdal | title=Inger Lise Rypdal – Sanger og Skuespiller | last=Larsen | first=Svend Erik Løken | language=no| publisher=[[Norsk Biografisk Leksikon]] | date=13 February 2009 | access-date=9 November 2015}}</ref>

== Biography ==
Inger Lise Rypdal (originally Inger Lise Andersen) has been an artist since 1968. She has made 16 albums in Norway and some in Sweden, acted in several theatre performances, nine movies and attended the Norwegian heats of the [[Eurovision Song Contest]], the [[Melodi Grand Prix]] 10 times: 1969 ( as ''Inger Lise Andersen'' with ''Eventyr''), 1972 (''Lillebror''), 1973 (with two songs: ''Alternativ'', with Ola Neegaard, Gro Anita Schønn & Stein Ingebrigtsen ''Å for et spill''), 1976 (with Jahn Teigen ''Voodoo''), 1979 (''Så lenge du er hos meg''), 1980 (''Svart fortid''), 1981 (''Tankar''), 1982 (''Lady Di''), 1983 (with Freddy Berg ''Elegi''), 1984 (''Vindar''). Together with Øystein Wiik she has done several musical concerts all around Norway.<ref name=nbl/>

She started with soul-music together with Geir Wentzel, and she has often returned to soul, for instance in the band Chipahua with some of the best Norwegian artists. In `La Mome Piaf` audience experienced Inger Lise Rypdal acting parts of [[Edith Piaf]]'s life. All the well known songs were tied together with short stories from Piaf's life.<ref>{{cite web | url=http://visitdrammen.no/visitDRAMMEN/vDDetalj/tabid/561/ArticleID/1364/INGER-LISE-RYPDAL-T%C3%A5rene-rant-og-jeg-fant-svaret-p%C3%A5-det-jeg-s%C3%B8kte-etter.aspx | title=INGER LISE RYPDAL: Tårene rant og jeg fant svaret på det jeg søkte etter | last=Risvik | first=Øyvind | language=no| publisher=VisitDrammen.no | access-date=9 November 2015}}</ref>
