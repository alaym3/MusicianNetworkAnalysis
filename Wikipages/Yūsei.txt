'''Yūsei''', '''Yusei''' or '''Yuusei''' (written: 優成, 優征, 優生, 遊星, 雄星, 雄勢, 佑成, 祐生 or 侑生) is a masculine Japanese given name. Notable people with the name include:

*{{Nihongo|[[Yusei Kikuchi]]|菊池 雄星||born 1991}}, Japanese baseball player
*{{Nihongo|[[Yusei Kudo]]|工藤 祐生||born 1986}}, Japanese footballer
*{{Nihongo|[[Yūsei Matsui]]|松井 優征||born 1981}}, Japanese manga artist
*{{Nihongo|[[Yusei Nakahara]]|中原 優生||born 1993}}, Japanese footballer
*{{Nihongo|[[Yūsei Oda]]|織田 優成||born 1969}}, Japanese voice actor
*{{Nihongo|[[Yusei Ogasawara]]|小笠原 侑生||born 1988}}, Japanese footballer
*{{Nihongo|[[Yusei Ogawa]]|小川 雄勢||born 1996}}, Japanese judoka
*{{Nihongo|[[Yusei Sugiura]]|杉浦 佑成||born 1995}}, Japanese basketball player
