'''Dounia Tazi''' (born March 31, 1997), known mononymously as '''Dounia''', is a [[Moroccan Americans|Moroccan-American]] singer, songwriter, model, and activist. She rose to prominence as an [[Instagram]] model, known for her body-positivity and activism. She began releasing music commercially in 2017. Her first commercial [[Extended play|EP]], ''Intro To'', was independently released on October 20, 2017. This was followed by her second EP, ''The Avant-Garden'', released on November 30, 2018 through [[Empire Distribution]] and her third EP, ''NOT GOOD FOR THE EGO'', released on May 24, 2019. Her debut album, ''The Scandal'', was released July 17, 2019, preceded by the single "Delightful".

upbringing in a "strict Muslim household", which inspired her to seek out music that she enjoyed during her time in high school.<ref name="Time"/><ref name="Galore">{{cite Tazi began [[Charity shop|thrifting]] in high school and started a body-positive [[Instagram]] account to model her outfits.<ref name="Vogue"/> After flunking out of high school, she worked for a short time at an [[American Apparel]] store.<ref name="Time"/>

==Career==
Tazi began modeling when she received an offer to be part of a [[Forever 21]] campaign.<ref name="Vogue"/> She gained wider notice after modeling for other brands, such as [[Sephora]] and [[Refinery29]], and built an Instagram following of over 200,000 for her unique focus on body-positivity.<ref name="PaperMag">{{cite web|last=Valentine|first=Claire|url=http://www.papermag.com/premiere-dounia-tazi-shyne-video-2480003221.html|title=Premiere: Watch a Teaser Video for Dounia's Electric New Single "Shyne"|work=[[Paper (magazine)|Paper Mag]]|date=August 31, 2017|accessdate=January 2, 2019}}</ref> She subsequently used her modeling gigs to fund a career in music, taking advantage of her online connections to find a manager and begin producing music. During her time as a model, she had always planned to transition into music.<ref name="Vogue"/> Tazi's first commercial single, "East Coast Hiding" was released independently in January 2017,<ref Fader]]|date=March 17, 2017|accessdate=January 2, 2019}}</ref> Her debut, 8-track [[Extended play|EP]], ''Intro To'', was released in partnership with [[Empire Distribution]] on October 20, 2017.<ref which ''Prelude Press'' noted for its fusion of [[Pop music|Pop]], [[Soul music|Soul]], and R&B, with "sharp, sassy, and unapologetic lyrical content".<ref name="Elevator">{{cite web|last=Squid|first=Mike|url=https://www.elevatormag.com/dounia-delivers-dynamic-new-release/|title=Dounia delivers dynamic new release|work=Elevator Mag|date=May 24, 2019|accessdate=May 25, 2019}}</ref><ref name="Prelude"/> In spring of 2019, Dounia was a featured performer at numerous music festivals across the United States, including [[BUKU Music + Art Project]], [[SunFest]], and [[Soundset Music Festival]].<ref name="Prelude"/> Her debut album, ''The Scandal'', was released on July 17, 2019 and features new tracks as well as those featured on ''Not Good for the Ego''. Dounia stated that the album's themes will move away from glamorizing a party lifestyle and move towards promoting spirituality and positive ways of coping.<ref name="TeenVogue"/> The album was preceded by the single "Delightful", which became available for streaming and download alongside the album's pre-order. Previous singles "Royal", "Lowkey Grl", and "Toxic" were included on the album, in addition to the track "Up 4 Air" from ''Not Good for the Ego''.<ref name="album">{{cite web|last=Frometa|first=RJ|url=https://ventsmagazine.com/2019/07/02/dounia-releases-new-single-delightful/|title=DOUNIA RELEASES NEW SINGLE "DELIGHTFUL"|work=Vents|date=July 2, 2019|accessdate=July 17, 2019}}</ref> In continuation of her festival circuit, Tazi performed at Diplo's Mad City Festival on July 20 and is scheduled to perform at [[Rolling Loud]] Bay Area on September 28.<ref name="album"/>

Dounia was a featured performer on [[Marian Hill]]'s single "Take A Number", released with its music video on August 23, 2019.<ref>{{cite web|last=Spruch|first=Kirsten|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8528097/marian-hill-take-a-number-video-dounia|title=Marian Hill Return With Spooky 'Take A Number' Video Featuring Dounia: Exclusive|work=Billboard|date=August 23, 2019|accessdate=August 23, 2019}}</ref>
