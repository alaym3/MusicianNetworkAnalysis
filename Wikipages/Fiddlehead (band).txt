'''Fiddlehead''' is an American [[post-hardcore]]<ref name="kerrang"/> [[Supergroup (music)|supergroup]] consisting of Patrick Flynn and Shawn Costa of [[Have Heart (band)|Have Heart]], Alex Henery of [[Basement (band)|Basement]] and members of the bands Big Contest and Intent.

==History==
Fiddlehead released their first EP in 2014, titled ''Out of the Bloom''.<ref name="stereogum">{{cite web |last1=Rettig |first1=James |title=Fiddlehead – "Lay Low" Video |url=https://www.stereogum.com/1986284/fiddlehead-lay-low-video/video/ |publisher=[[Stereogum]] |accessdate=19 February 2019}}</ref> In 2018, Fiddlehead signed to the Boston record label [[Run for Cover Records]] and released their debut full-length album titled ''Springtime and Blind''. In 2021, they released their second full-length on Run for Cover, “Between the Richness.” <ref name="musicandriots">{{cite web |title=Fiddlehead Signs To Run For Cover & Share New Track + Video "Lay Low" |url=http://musicandriots.com/fiddlehead-signs-to-run-for-cover-share-new-track-video-lay-low/ |publisher=Music&Riots |accessdate=19 February 2019}}</ref><ref name="brooklynvegan">{{cite web |last1=Sacher |first1=Andrew |title=Fiddlehead (mem Have Heart, Basement) streaming LP, playing shows |url=http://www.brooklynvegan.com/fiddlehead-mem-have-heart-basement-streaming-lp-playing-shows/ |publisher=[[BrooklynVegan]] |accessdate=19 February 2019}}</ref><ref name="stereogum1">{{cite web |last1=Rettig |first1=James |title=Fiddlehead – "USMA" |url=https://www.stereogum.com/1989337/fiddlehead-usma/music/ |publisher=Stereogum |accessdate=19 February 2019}}</ref><ref name="kerrang">{{cite web |last1=Lewczyk |first1=Maria |title=6 Bands Pioneering a Dreamy Wave of Post-Hardcore Right Now |url=https://www.kerrang.com/features/6-bands-pioneering-a-dreamy-wave-of-post-hardcore/ |publisher=[[Kerrang!]] |accessdate=19 February 2019}}</ref><ref name="thefader">{{cite web |last1=Wang |first1=Steffanee |title=Listen to this Fiddlehead song, then call your parents |url=http://www.thefader.com/2018/04/10/fiddlehead-poem-you-premiere |publisher=[[The Fader]] |accessdate=22 February 2019}}</ref><ref name="stereogum2">{{cite web |last1=Rettig |first1=James |title=Fiddlehead – "Poem You" |url=https://www.stereogum.com/1990814/fiddlehead-poem-you/music/ |publisher=Stereogum |accessdate=22 February 2019}}</ref>
