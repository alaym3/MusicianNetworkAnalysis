'''Vanessa Marija Else Ferber'''<ref>{{Cite web|date=2018-09-12|title=Schlagerstar Vanessa Mai klärt auf: So heißt sie wirklich!|url=https://www.promiflash.de/news/2018/09/12/schlagerstar-vanessa-mai-klaert-auf-so-heisst-sie-wirklich.html|access-date=2021-02-23|website=Promiflash.de|language=de}}</ref> (''{{née}}'' '''Mandekić''', born 2 May 1992), known professionally as '''Vanessa Mai''', is a German singer. She was first known as a member of [[Schlager]] band [[Wolkenfrei]] and later began working as a solo artist following the band's split. She chose her stage name as a reference to her birth month,<ref name="Augsburg">{{cite web|date=11 August 2017|title=Was Vanessa Mai mit ihrem neuen Album verbindet (English: What connects Vanessa Mai to her new album)|url=http://www.augsburger-allgemeine.de/panorama/Was-Vanessa-Mai-mit-ihrem-neuen-Album-verbindet-id42358316.html|access-date=11 August 2017|website=[[Augsburger Allgemeine|augsburger-allgemeine.de]]}} {{in lang|de}}</ref><ref name="Welt"/> which is spelled Mai ({{IPA-de|maɪ|pron}}) in German.

==Early life==
Mai completed a [[:de:Mediengestalter Digital und Print|media designer]] apprenticeship.<ref name="Welt">{{cite web|title=Vanessa Mai: "Ich bin die mit dem Herz. Ich sage auch mal Ja"|url=https://www.welt.de/vermischtes/article150417962/Ich-bin-die-mit-dem-Herz-Ich-sage-auch-mal-Ja.html|website=[[Die Welt|welt.de]] (by Kerstin Rottmann)|access-date=13 June 2017|date=29 December 2015}} {{in lang|de}}</ref> She had one of her first performances as a singer in the school musical ''[[The Wonderful Wizard of Oz]]''.<ref>RTL Punkt 12 (13 July 2015, 12:00)</ref> Before that, she had occasionally accompanied her father, Croatian-born musician,<ref name="Welt"/> with the [[tambourine]]. In 2008, aged 15, she took part in the [[hip-hop]] world championship in Las Vegas, placing last, as part of the dance formation Getting Craz'd, who were German champions.<ref>{{cite web|title=Ein Treffen mit: Vanessa Mai (English: A meeting with: Vanessa Mai)|url=http://www.sat1.de/tv/fruehstuecksfernsehen/video/2-ein-treffen-mit-vanessa-mai-clip|website=[[Sat.1|sat1.de]]|access-date=7 August 2015|date=4 August 2015}} {{in lang|de}}</ref>

==Career==
===2012–2015: Wolkenfrei===
{{Main|Wolkenfrei}}
In the summer of 2012, then-singer of [[Wolkenfrei]], Heike Wanner, left the band for health reasons. While attending a concert of her father's band Musik-Express at the Sonnenhof in [[Aspach, Baden-Württemberg|Aspach]], Mai was enlisted as a singer by Wolkenfrei member Marc Fischer.<ref name="Wolkenfrei">{{cite web|url=http://www.wolken-frei.de/biographie.html?|title=Wolkenfrei biography|access-date=27 April 2018|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131126015816/http://www.wolken-frei.de/biographie.html|archive-date=26 November 2013}} {{in lang|de}}</ref> On 19 July 2013, their début single "Jeans, T-Shirts und Freiheit" ("Jeans, T-Shirts, and Freedom") was released.<ref>{{cite web|title="Jeans, T-Shirt und Freiheit" – Single|url=https://itunes.apple.com/de/album/ein-engel-in-der-weihnachtszeit/id1064132160|publisher=[[iTunes Store]]|access-date=11 June 2014}} {{in lang|de}}</ref> It was followed by their début studio album ''Endlos verliebt'' ("Endlessly in Love") in February 2014, which charted in Germany, Austria, and Switzerland. In January 2015, it was announced that Mai was the new face of the campaign Mein Herz schlägt Schlager. She promoted it through an ad as well as with the song "Mein Herz schlägt Schlager", which was released on a [[sampler album]] belonging to the project.<ref>{{cite web|title=Mein Herz schlägt Schlager|url=https://www.amazon.de/Mein-Herz-Schl%C3%A4gt-Schlager-Various/dp/B00TENWV7O/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&qid=1428340127&sr=8-1&keywords=mein+herz+schl%C3%A4gt+schlager|access-date=9 July 2015}} {{in lang|de}}</ref>

In April 2015, Marc Fischer and Stefan Kinski announced that they were leaving Wolkenfrei,<ref>{{cite web|title=smago! top-exklusiv vorab: Vanessa allein im Haus!|url=http://www.smago.de/d/artikel/70268/|website=smago.de|access-date=9 July 2015|date=7 April 2015}} {{in lang|de}}</ref> leading to the project being represented only by Mai (mostly as "Wolkenfrei-Star Vanessa Mai"). "Wolke 7", the first single from Wolkenfrei's second studio album, was released on 22 May. The album, titled ''Wachgeküsst'', was released on 10 July. A day before, Deutsches Musik Fernsehen had dedicated a 65-minute television special to Mai and to the album. Due to Mai's extensive television presence, including an appearance on [[Florian Silbereisen]]'s [[Das Erste|ARD]] show ''Die Besten im Sommer'' with the song "Wolke 7", the album reached place 7 of the [[GfK Entertainment charts|German albums chart]]<ref name="Schlagerplanet">{{cite web|title=Vanessa Mai ist nicht mehr Wolkenfrei (English: Vanessa Mai is not Wolkenfrei anymore)|url=http://www.schlagerplanet.com/news/promi-news/vanessa-mai-neues-album-nicht-mehr-als-wolkenfrei_n8042.html|website=schlagerplanet.com|access-date=21 February 2016|date=20 February 2016}} {{in lang|de}}</ref> and also charted in Austria and Switzerland. The single "Wolke 7" reached place 54 in Germany and 71 in Austria. On 18 September, "Wachgeküsst", the album's title track, was released as its second single. On 9 October, Mai's first live album (still as Wolkenfrei), ''Wachgeküsst (Live)'', was released. The last single "In all deinen Farben" ("In All Your Colours") was released on 11 December, followed a week later by a non-album Christmas single titled "Ein Engel in der Weihnachtszeit" ("An Angel at Christmas Time").<ref>{{cite web|title="Ein Engel in der Weihnachtszeit" – Single|url=https://itunes.apple.com/de/album/ein-engel-in-der-weihnachtszeit/id1064132160|publisher=[[iTunes Store]]|access-date=21 February 2016}} {{in lang|de}}</ref>

===2016–present: Abandonment of the Wolkenfrei name and solo career===
[[File:2018-05-17 Vanessa Mai in Mannheim (Regenbogen Tour) 016.jpg|thumb|Mai performing in 2018]]
From January to May 2016, Mai was a judge on the [[Deutschland sucht den Superstar (season 13)|thirteenth season]] of ''[[Deutschland sucht den Superstar]]'', the German version of ''[[Pop Idol]]'' and ''[[American Idol]]''. In February, she announced her decision to not use the stage name Wolkenfrei anymore.<ref name="Schlagerplanet"/> Her first single under the name Vanessa Mai, titled "Ich sterb für dich" ("I Die For You"), was released on 19 February.<ref>{{cite web|title=Vanessa Mai – "Ich sterb für dich"|url=http://www.mix1.de/music/vanessa-mai/ich-sterb-fuer-dich/|website=mix1.de|access-date=21 February 2016}} {{in lang|de}}</ref> It preceded her first studio album under the name Vanessa Mai and third studio album overall, ''[[Für Dich (Vanessa Mai album)|Für Dich]]'' ("For You"), which was released on 15 April. The single as well as the album were produced by her ''Deutschland sucht den Superstar'' colleague [[Dieter Bohlen]]. From September to November, Mai went on [[:de:Für Dich – Live aus Berlin#Für Dich Tournee|her first solo tour]], which visited Germany, Denmark, and Switzerland for 26 dates. A live album titled ''Für Dich – Live aus Berlin'', recorded at the [[Tempodrom]] in Berlin, was released on 6 January 2017. From March to May, she took part in the [[Let's Dance (German season 10)|tenth season]] of ''[[Let's Dance (German TV series)|Let's Dance]]'', the German version of ''[[Strictly Come Dancing]]'', and placed second. On 3 April 2017, Mai announced her second solo studio album and fourth overall with the single "Und wenn ich träum" (And When I Dream").

On 18 July 2017, she released a special acoustic version of her first studio album, titled ''Für Dich – Akustik Best Of'', available only as a download through [[Tchibo]]'s homepage as well as in all their shops in Germany. On 4 August 2017, she released the second single from her then-upcoming second solo album, "Nie wieder" ("Never Again"). The album was released a week later under the title ''[[:de:Regenbogen (Album)|Regenbogen]]'' ("Rainbow"), which, like its predecessor, was produced by Bohlen. In November 2017, Mai and Bohlen announced the end of their professional relationship <ref>{{cite web|title=Dieter Bohlen und Vanessa Mai arbeiten nicht mehr zusammen (English: Dieter Bohlen and Vanessa Mai will not work together anymore)|url=https://web.de/magazine/unterhaltung/stars/dieter-bohlen-vanessa-mai-32617928|website=[[web.de]]|access-date=28 March 2018|date=8 November 2017}} {{in lang|de}}</ref> and she started working on her third studio album in her solo career, ''Schlager''. In July 2018, Mai released her first single "WIR 2 IMMER 1 (feat. [[Olexesh]])" of this album which became the most successful pop song of her by now. The album "Schlager" was released on 3 August 2018 and topped the German Charts.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.offiziellecharts.de/news/item/537-vanessa-mai-toppt-die-offiziellen-deutschen-charts|title=Vanessa Mai toppt die Offiziellen Deutschen Charts – Offizielle Deutsche Charts|website=offiziellecharts.de|language=de-de|access-date=7 October 2018}}</ref> Thus she is the youngest [[Schlager music|Schlager]] music act of all time who topped the German Charts twice in a row. In 20 August 2020, Mai, together with [[Essen]]-based DJ Picco, took part in the [[ICSYVDE1|second episode]] in the [[I Can See Your Voice (German game show)|German version of ''I Can See Your Voice'']] as a guest artist.<ref>{{cite web|title=I can see your voice: Vanessa Mai und Superfan Picco rocken die Hütte
|url=https://www.rtl.de/cms/i-can-see-your-voice-vanessa-mai-und-superfan-picco-rocken-die-huette-4598536.html?|language=de|publisher=[[RTL (Germany)]]|date=19 August 2020|access-date=20 August 2020}}</ref>

==Personal life==
In 2012, Mai met [[Andrea Berg]]'s stepson Andreas Ferber, who became her manager, in a [[Stuttgart]] club, and began dating him in 2013.<ref name="VIP">{{cite web|title=''DSDS''-Jurorin Vanessa Mai und Andreas Ferber haben sich auf Mallorca verlobt (English: ''DSDS'' judge Vanessa Mai and Andreas Ferber got engaged in Mallorca)|url=http://www.vip.de/cms/dsds-jurorin-vanessa-mai-und-andreas-ferber-haben-sich-auf-mallorca-verlobt-2610351.html|website=vip.de|access-date=21 February 2016|date=5 January 2016}} {{in lang|de}}</ref> After getting engaged in early 2016,<ref name="VIP"/> they got married in June 2017. The civil marriage took place on 6 June, while the church wedding took place in [[Mallorca]] on 12 June.<ref>{{cite web|title=Geheime Hochzeit auf Mallorca: Vanessa Mai sagt "ja" zu ihrem Andreas (English: Secret wedding in Mallorca: Vanessa Mai says "yes" to her Andreas)|url=http://www.t-online.de/unterhaltung/stars/id_81418104/geheime-hochzeit-auf-mallorca-vanessa-mai-sagt-ja-zu-ihrem-andreas.html|website=[[T-Online]].de|access-date=13 June 2017|date=13 June 2017}} {{in lang|de}}</ref>
