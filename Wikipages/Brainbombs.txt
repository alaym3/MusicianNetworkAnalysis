'''Brainbombs''' is a [[Sweden|Swedish]] [[noise rock]] band formed 1985 in [[Hudiksvall]]. The members are Dan, Peter, Jonas, Drajan, and Lanchy.<ref name="Alzheimer #2">{{cite web |url=http://anka.dyndns.org/brainbombs/ |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20020912215855/http://anka.dyndns.org/brainbombs/ |url-status=dead |archive-date=September 12, 2002 |title=Alzheimer #2 |publisher=anka.dynds.org/brainbombs/ |accessdate=August 14, 2009 }}</ref> The latter was also a member of [[Totalitär]].<ref name="Alzheimer #2"/> They are notable for their very repetitive, noisy, untuned, raucous sound. Their lyrical themes and concept have been very controversial, as some of the songs depict acts of rape, torture, and murder, similar to the writings of author [[Peter Sotos]], who has been named as an influence for the group.<ref name="GAB">{{cite web|last1=Buckles|first1=Monty|title=ALL HAIL DEATH, RAPE, AND MURDER: AN INTERVIEW WITH THE BRAINBOMBS By MONTY BUCKLES|url=http://geniusandbrutality.com/brainbombs/interv/z-gun2.html|website=geniusandbrutality.com|publisher=Genius and Brutality|accessdate=2018-01-16}}</ref> Other artists named as influences for the band include [[Chrome (band)|Chrome]], [[James Chance]], and [[Whitehouse (band)|Whitehouse]],<ref name="Alzheimer #2"/> and the group's name comes from a [[Punishment of Luxury]] song.<ref name="GAB"/>
