'''Amy Lee''' (born May 30, 1989), known professionally as '''Ailee''', is a Korean-American singer and songwriter based in South Korea. Amassing digital sales success in [[South Korea]], she has released two [[studio album]]s, five [[extended play]]s, twenty one singles, six of which charted within the top five of the [[Gaon Digital Chart]].

Following a short stint at Muzo Entertainment in New York City, she moved to South Korea in 2010, signing to [[YMC Entertainment]]. Ailee debuted in 2012 with her first single "[[Heaven (Ailee song)|Heaven]]", peaking at number three on [[Gaon Digital Chart|Gaon]], earning her Best New Artist Awards at [[Melon Music Awards]], [[Golden Disc Awards]], [[Gaon Chart K-Pop Awards]] and the [[Seoul Music Awards]]. Through the years, she has won four [[Mnet Asian Music Award for Best Vocal Performance|Mnet Asian Music Award for Best Female Vocal Performance]] titles from "[[A's Doll House|U&I]]" in 2013, "Singing Got Better" in 2014, "[[Vivid (Ailee album)|Mind Your Own Business]]" in 2015, to "[[A New Empire|If You]]" in 2016. Her OST, "[[I Will Go to You Like the First Snow]]" released in 2017 won various awards and was the most digitally successful song of that year, becoming the best selling record in movies and dramas in the Korean sound record market.<ref>{{Cite web|title=첫눈 예보…'첫눈처럼 너에게 가겠다' 또 인기?|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/410/0000419782|website=MK Sports|language=ko|date=November 20, 2017|access-date=November 16, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210224091306/https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/410/0000419782|archive-date=February 24, 2021|url-status=live}}</ref>

==Life and career==

===1989–2011: Early life, career beginnings===
Ailee was born in [[Denver]], [[Colorado]], on May 30, 1989, and grew up in [[New Jersey]].<ref name="yonhapnews">{{cite web|title=에일리의 음악 세계로 초대합니다|url=https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=001&aid=0005879638|language=ko|date=October 18, 2012|access-date=November 10, 2020|archive-date=December 25, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201225144926/https://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=001&aid=0005879638|url-status=live}}</ref> She attended [[Palisades Park High School]] before moving to nearby [[Leonia, New Jersey|Leonia]]. After her [[Scotch Plains-Fanwood High School]] graduation, she studied communications at [[Pace University]]<ref name="koreajoongangdaily">{{Cite web|title=Ailee: The K-pop and YouTube sensation|url=http://koreajoongangdaily.joins.com/news/article/article.aspx?aid=2956602|website=Korea Joongang Daily|date=June 9, 2016|archive-date=April 6, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170406083240/http://koreajoongangdaily.joins.com/news/article/article.aspx?aid=2956602|url-status=live}}</ref> before dropping out to pursue a career in music.<ref>{{Cite web|url = http://www.ymcent.com/|title = YMC Entertainment Official Website|date = October 7, 2015|archive-date = January 11, 2016|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20160111042632/http://ymcent.com/|url-status = live}}</ref> Before her K-pop debut, Ailee was signed under Muzo Entertainment, an independent agency in [[New York City]] and New Jersey. Under that label she collaborated with several artists, including [[Johnnyphlo]] and [[Philadelphia]]-based rapper Decipher.{{citation needed|date=November 2020}} Before moving to South Korea, Ailee created the [[YouTube]] channels "mzamyx3" and "aileemusic" to bring attention to her singing on the web.{{citation needed|date=November 2020}}

Ailee moved to South Korea in 2010 after she landed a music label audition through her uncle's connections, she sang "Resignation" by [[Big Mama (band)|Big Mama]] during the audition, and was recruited for [[YMC Entertainment]] on the spot.<ref name="koreajoongangdaily" /> During her training, YMC featured her in the label's [[Wheesung]] song, "They Are Coming", which it released in October 2011.<ref>{{cite web|title=휘성, 콘서트서 듀엣무대 "미모에 가창력…에일리는 사기캐릭터"|url=https://sports.donga.com/3/all/20111107/41692826/2|website=[[Donga.com|Sports Donga]]|language=ko|author=Won Soo-yeon|date=November 7, 2011|access-date=February 21, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210221120811/https://sports.donga.com/3/all/20111107/41692826/2|archive-date=February 21, 2021|url-status=live}}</ref> She also sang in Decipher and [[Jay Park]]'s song "Catch Me If You Can".{{citation needed|date=November 2020}} In September 2011, Ailee and Wheesung were featured on the [[Munhwa Broadcasting Corporation|MBC]]'s [[Chuseok]] special episode of ''Singer and Trainee''. Ailee performed "[[Halo (Beyoncé song)|Halo]]" by [[Beyoncé]] to a positive reaction from the audience. Following her performance of "Halo", judge [[Big Mama King|BMK]] said, "Wherever she goes, she has the potential to be a big star. She definitely has the voice." After the judges had graded all the participants, Ailee was the first-place winner.<ref name="edaily1">{{cite web|title=휘성의 연습생 에일리, '가수와 연습생' 1위 후 관심 폭발|url=https://www.edaily.co.kr/news/read?newsId=01246406596380120&mediaCodeNo=258|language=ko|date=September 14, 2011|access-date=November 10, 2020|archive-date=December 25, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201225145032/https://www.edaily.co.kr/news/read?newsId=01246406596380120&mediaCodeNo=258|url-status=live}}</ref>

===2015–2016: Breakthrough with ''Vivid''===
After wrapping up her joint ''Unite the Mic Tour'' with Jay Park and [[San E]] in [[Toronto]] in March 2015, plans were revealed for Ailee to hold a solo concert three years after her debut. Her first solo concert, titled ''Fatal Attraction'', was held on July 4, 2015 at the [[Olympic Hall]].<ref>{{cite web|title=에일리, 7월 4일 생애 첫 단독 콘서트…'폭발적 가창력' 기대↑|url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=112&aid=0002701413|website=Herald Pop|language=ko|author=Kim Nam-eun|date=May 19, 2015|access-date=February 21, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210224092125/https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=112&aid=0002701413|archive-date=February 24, 2021|url-status=live}}</ref> Ailee was joined on stage several times by different artists, who helped her sing her many duets and collaborations.
