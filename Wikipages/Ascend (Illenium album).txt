'''''Ascend''''' (stylised as ''ASCEND'') is the third studio album by American [[Disc jockey|DJ]] and producer [[Illenium]], released on August 16, 2019, through [[Astralwerks]], making it his major-label debut and his only album on the label.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://music.apple.com/us/album/ascend/1468053314|title=ASCEND by Illenium on Apple Music|website=[[Apple Music]]|access-date=2019-06-18}}</ref> The album features six singles.

==Background==
Consisting of 17 songs, the album includes six previously released singles, "Take You Down", "[[Crashing (Illenium song)|Crashing]]", "Pray", "[[Good Things Fall Apart]]", "[[Takeaway (song)|Takeaway]]", and "Blood".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/8515881/illenium-ascend-album-north-american-tour-2019|title=Illenium Announces 'Ascend Album & North American Tour|last=Bein|first=Kat|date=2019-06-13|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|access-date=2019-06-18}}</ref> On his social media accounts, he announced the release date of the album and the following tour on June 12.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://illenium.com/illenium-announces-new-album-phase-1-of-ascend-tour/|title=Illenium announced new album + Phase 1 of Ascend Tour|website=illenium.com|date=2019-06-13|access-date=2019-06-18}}</ref>

==Promotion==
*The first single "[[Take You Down (Illenium song)|Take You Down]]" was released on August 3, 2018. The song brought forth the [[Substance abuse|drug abuse]] problem [[Illenium]] previously had.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/8468544/illenium-take-you-down|title=Illenium Opens Up About Addiction On 'Take You Down': Listen|date=August 3, 2018|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|accessdate=6 May 2020}}</ref>
*The second single "[[Crashing (Illenium song)|Crashing]]", featuring [[Bahari (band)|Bahari]], was released on January 25, 2019.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/8495022/illenium-bahari-crashing-lyric-video|title=Illenium & Bahari Are Drunk on Love in 'Crashing': Watch|date=January 25, 2019|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|accessdate=6 May 2020}}</ref>
*The third single "Pray", featuring [[Kameron Alexander]], was released on March 22, 2019.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://edm.com/music-releases/illenium-pray-kameron-alexander|title=Illenium is Back with a New Single, "Pray" ft. Kameron Alexander|date=March 22, 2019| last=Stone | first=Katie | work=EDM.com|accessdate=6 May 2020}}</ref>
*The fourth single "[[Good Things Fall Apart]]", with [[Jon Bellion]], was released on May 13, 2019.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/8511107/illenium-jon-bellion-good-things-fall-apart-lyric-video|title=Illenium & Jon Bellion Find Love Burns Out Bright On 'Good Things Fall Apart': Watch|date=May 13, 2019|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|accessdate=6 May 2020}}</ref>
*The fifth single "[[Takeaway (song)|Takeaway]]", with [[The Chainsmokers]] and featuring [[Lennon & Maisy|Lennon Stella]], was released on July 24, 2019.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/8523210/the-chainsmokers-illenium-lennon-stella-takeaway-video|title=The Chainsmokers, Illenium and Lennon Stella Unite For 'Takeaway': Watch|date=July 24, 2019|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|accessdate=6 May 2020}}</ref>
*The sixth and last single before the album release "Blood", featuring [[Foy Vance]], was released on August 2, 2019.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://edm.com/music-releases/illenium-blood-foy-vance|title=Illenium Goes Heavier in "Blood" ft. Foy Vance|date=August 2, 2019| last=Cameron | first=Jonh | work=EDM.com|accessdate=6 May 2020}}</ref>
