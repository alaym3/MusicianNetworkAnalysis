'''Lena''' is a [[female]] [[given name]]. The name has evolved in different languages as a variation of several earlier names. In some derivations, Lena comes from the endings of Alina and [[Helen (given name)|Helena]]. Leana is popular in [[Persian language|Persian]], Hindi, Swedish, French, Finnish, and some other cultures. Lena was the most popular name for girls born in Poland in 2013.<ref>[http://diminutive-names.com/9-most-popular-baby-names-2013-poland-girls-boys.article Diminutive-Names.com] {{webarchive|url=https://archive.is/20140119101845/http://diminutive-names.com/9-most-popular-baby-names-2013-poland-girls-boys.article |date=2014-01-19 }}</ref>
