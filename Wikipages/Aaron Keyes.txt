'''Aaron Robert Keyes''' (born September 8, 1978) is an American [[Christian music]]ian. He released ''Not Guilty Anymore'' through [[Kingsway Communications|Kingsway Records]] in 2007. His subsequent album, ''Dwell'', was released by Kingsway Records in 2011. This album was his breakthrough release on the [[Billboard (magazine)|''Billboard'' magazine]] [[Christian Albums]] chart.

==Early life==
Keyes was born on September 8, 1978,<ref name="Public Records">{{cite news|title=U.S. Public Records Index|year=2009|publisher=Family Search|id=312938595}}</ref> as Aaron Robert Keyes,<ref name="Intelius">{{cite web|title=Aaron Keyes in Greenville, SC|url=http://www.intelius.com/results.php?ReportType=1&formname=name&qf=Aaron&qmi=&qn=Keyes&qcs=Greenville%2C+SC&focusfirst=1|author=Intelius|author-link=Intelius|publisher=[[Intelius]]|accessdate=June 1, 2015}}</ref> in [[Greenville, South Carolina]], at Bob Jones Hospital. His father is a preacher.<ref name="CR Story">{{cite web|title=Aaron Keyes: A worship pastor who's written the "Not Guilty Anymore" classic|first=Tony |last=Cummings|publisher=[[Cross Rhythms]]|date=May 29, 2008 |accessdate=June 1, 2015 |url=http://www.crossrhythms.co.uk/articles/music/Aaron_Keyes_A_worship_pastor_whos_written_the_Not_Guilty_Anymore_classic/31841/p1/}}</ref><ref name="JFH Profile">{{cite web|title=Aaron Keyes |url=http://jesusfreakhideout.com/artists/AaronKeyes.asp|author=Jesus Freak Hideout|publisher=Jesus Freak Hideout|accessdate=June 1, 2015}}</ref>

==Music career==

Not Guilty Anymore |url=http://www.louderthanthemusic.com/document.php?id=1473|first=Jono|last=Davies|publisher=Jesus Freak Hideout|accessdate=June 1, 2015|date=July 5, 2010}}</ref> He released ''Dwell'' with Kingsway Communications on June 21, 2011.<ref name="JFH Album Three">{{cite web|title=Aaron Keyes, "Dwell" Review|url=http://jesusfreakhideout.com/cdreviews/Dwell.asp|first=Roger|last=Gelwicks|publisher=Jesus Freak Hideout|accessdate=June 1, 2015|date=June 30, 2011}}</ref> The album was his breakthrough release on the [[Billboard (magazine)|''Billboard'' magazine]] charts, where it peaked at No. 43 on the [[Christian Albums]] chart.<ref name="AM Awards">{{cite web|title=Aaron Keyes : Awards |url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/aaron-keyes-mn0000881647/awards|author=AllMusic|author-link=AllMusic|publisher=[[AllMusic]]|accessdate=June 1, 2015}}</ref> This album was reviewed by [[AllMusic]],<ref name="AM">{{cite web|title=Dwell – Aaron Keyes |url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/dwell-mw0002141827 |first=Jared |last=Johnson |publisher=[[AllMusic]] |accessdate=June 1, 2015}}</ref> ''Christianity Today'',<ref name="CT Review Two">{{cite web|title=Dwell |url=http://www.christianitytoday.com/ct/2011/juneweb-only/dwell-mini.html|first=Andrew|last=Greer|journal=[[Christianity Today]]|accessdate=June 1, 2015|date=June 21, 2011}}</ref> Cross Rhythms,<ref name="CR Review Two">{{cite web|title=Review: Dwell – Aaron Keyes |url=http://www.crossrhythms.co.uk/products/Aaron_Keyes/Dwell/108483/ |first=Jonathan |last=Sinclair |publisher=[[Cross Rhythms]] |accessdate=June 1, 2015 |date=October 27, 2011}}</ref> Jesus Freak Hideout,<ref name="JFH Album Three"/> and Louder Than the Music.<ref name="LTtM Review Two">{{cite web|title=Aaron Keyes -
