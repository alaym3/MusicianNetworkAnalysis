'''Avery Marcel Joshua Jones''' (born October 16, 1992), known by his stage name '''Kota the Friend''', is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer, born and raised in [[Brooklyn]], [[New York (state)|New York]].<ref name="Billboard">{{cite web |last1=Healy |first1=Sean |title=Kota the Friend Is Perfectly Happy With Living the Indie Life: 'I Know How the Game Works' |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/8526897/kota-the-friend-is-foto-interview |website=Billboard |accessdate=May 29, 2020 }}</ref>

Kota the Friend released his debut mixtape, ''Palm Tree Liquor'' in 2016, followed by his second mixtape ''Paloma Beach'' in 2017. In 2018, he released his first independent studio album ''Anything''. In 2019, he released ''Foto'', followed by ''Lyrics to GO, Vol. 1'' in January 2020 and '' Everything'' in late May. Kota then went on to release ''Lyrics to GO, Vol.2'' in January 2021 and ''To Kill A Sunrise'' with [[Statik Selektah]] in March 2021.

== Early life ==
Avery Jones was born on October 16, 1992 in [[Brooklyn]], [[New York City]]. Growing up around Brooklyn, he developed an affinity for music, learning keyboard, guitar, and bass. He attended the [[Brooklyn High School of the Arts]] and [[Five Towns College]] for trumpet. During college, he created the rap trio ''Nappy Hair'', releasing two mixtapes, "Autumn", and "Nappy Hair".<ref>{{cite web |last1=Chesman |first1=Donna |title=Kota the Friend Has a Heart of Gold: Interview |url=https://djbooth.net/features/2019-05-15-kota-the-friend-foto-interview-growth-success-depression |website=DJBooth |accessdate=June 6, 2020}}</ref>

== Career ==
Kota the Friend has remained an independent act throughout his career—turning down 3 major record labels; instead, creating his own label and apparel store FLTBYS.<ref name="Billboard" /><ref>{{cite web |title=Brooklyn Hip Hop Artist Kota The Friend Launches FLTBYS Apparel Website With Pop Up Showcase Event Friday May 18, 2018 |url=https://brooklynbuzz.com/brooklyn-hip-hop-artist-kota-the-friend-launches-fltbys-apparel-website-with-pop-up-showcase-event-friday-may-18-2018/ |website=Brooklyn Buzz}}</ref> He frequently cites a do-it-yourself mentality, such as filming his own music videos, recording within a home setup, and producing most of his discography.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Bhanot |first1=Ankita |title=To Kota The Friend, Home In Brooklyn Is 'Everything' |url=https://theknockturnal.com/to-kota-the-friend-home-in-brooklyn-is-everything/ |website=TheKnockTurnal}}</ref> His mentality was fueled by a desire for freedom and to avoid the sense of musical confinement.

In his third studio album ''Foto'', Kota featured appearances from artists [[Saba (rapper)|Saba]] and Hello Oshay. Focusing on mellow and jazzy beats, it was praised by many for its quality and attention to detail. ''Foto'' was ranked the 17th best hip-hop album of 2019 by ''[[Rolling Stone]]''.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Klinkenberg |first1=Brendan |title=20 Best Hip-Hop Albums of 2019 |url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-lists/20-best-hip-hop-albums-of-2019-931888/kota-the-friend-foto-931903/ |website=Billboard |accessdate=June 2, 2020}}</ref> He subsequently embarked on his first headlining tour, called the Foto Tour.{{citation needed|date=January 2021}}

His fourth studio album, ''Everything'', featured appearances from artists such as [[Joey Badass]], [[Bas (rapper)|Bas]], and [[Kyle (musician)|KYLE]], among others. In addition, he featured actress [[Lupita Nyong'o]] and actor [[Lakeith Stanfield]] in interludes. The album went on to peak at number 162 on the [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]].<ref name="billboard 200" />

== Personal life ==
In a [[Genius (website)|Genius]] interview, Kota the Friend cited [[Jay-Z]], [[Nas]], [[Kid Cudi]], [[Bob Dylan]], [[The Beatles]] and others as musical influences.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Jones |first1=Avery |title=Kota the Friend |url=https://genius.com/artists/Kota-the-friend |website=Genius |accessdate=May 29, 2020}}</ref>

Kota the Friend has one son.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Scott |first1=Ruby |title=Kota the Friend Continues to Pave His Own Path in 'Everything' [Q&A] |url=https://www.onestowatch.com/blog/kota-the-friend-everything |website=Onestowatch |accessdate=September 5, 2020}}</ref>
