'''Lovejoy''' is an English [[indie rock]] band based in [[Brighton]]. They debuted on 9 May 2021 with their [[Extended play|EP]] ''Are You Alright?'', along with music videos for their songs ''One Day'' and ''Taunt''.<ref>{{Cite tweet|number=1391549652307218435|user=lvjyonline|title=“Oh, baby isn’t life so fucking inconsistent?” We are Lovejoy.  Dropping our Spotify as soon as it’s available. New music video tomorrow with special guest|author=Lovejoy|date=May 9, 2021}}</ref> The band consists of [[Wilbur Soot|Will Gold]] (lead vocals, guitar, songwriting), Joe Goldsmith (guitar, songwriting), Ash Kabosu (bass guitar) and Mark Boardman (drums, percussion).

== History ==
The band was originally named Hang the DJ''.''{{Citation needed|date=November 2021}} The band was founded by Goldsmith and Gold at some point in 2019-2020.{{Citation needed|date=November 2021}} The band's name was later changed to Lovejoy, after Benedict Lovejoy, friend of the band.<ref>{{Cite web|title=lovejoy name origins|url=https://clips.twitch.tv/SolidImpartialGrassEagleEye-aWsViIkxYS66DyQm|url-status=live|access-date=2021-11-14|website=Twitch}}</ref>{{Unreliable source?|date=November 2021|reason=Source is a Twitch.tv clip}}

Gold met Kabosu in a burger shop, noticed the bass guitar on his back, and asked if he was in a band. Kabosu stated that he was not, so Gold invited him to join Lovejoy.<ref>{{Citation|title=WilburSoot - how will met ash|date=2021-06-13|url=https://clips.twitch.tv/AltruisticBitterPlumDancingBanana-rmi28rUfHF_t0Y1k|access-date=2021-11-14}}</ref>{{Unreliable source?|date=November 2021|reason=Source is Twitch.tv video clip}}

In May 2021, they debuted on the Billboard Emerging Artists chart at #10,<ref>{{Cite tweet|number=1395029371828776966|user=billboardcharts|title=This week's top 10 on the #EmergingArtists chart|author=billboard charts}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Lovejoy {{!}} full Official Chart History {{!}} Official Charts Company|url=https://www.officialcharts.com/artist/60174/lovejoy/|access-date=2021-11-14|website=www.officialcharts.com}}</ref> following the success of their debut EP ''Are You Alright?''.

On 14 October 2021, the band released their second EP, ''Pebble Brain''. The EP consists of 7 songs.<ref>{{Cite tweet|number=1447967764199657472|user=LVJYonline|title=Pebble Brain is the second EP from Lovejoy which (barring the disaster of our online distributors) should be available to stream 14th of October 2021!|author=Lovejoy}}</ref> Music videos for ''Model Buses'' and ''Concrete'' were released shortly after the release of ''Pebble Brain''.<ref>{{Citation|title=Lovejoy - Model Buses (OFFICIAL VIDEO)|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3uJd1sbEvow|language=en|access-date=2021-11-13}}</ref><ref>{{Cite tweet|number=1450502731584659461|user=LVJYonline|title="If I had it my way you'd sleep on the concrete floor" Lovejoy's Concrete Music Video!|author=Lovejoy}}</ref>
