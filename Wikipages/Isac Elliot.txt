'''Isac Elliot Lundén''' (born 26 December 2000), professionally known as '''Isac Elliot''', is a Finland-Swedish pop singer, songwriter, dancer and actor. He is best known for his song "[[New Way Home (Isac Elliot song)|New Way Home]]" and his debut album ''[[Wake Up World (album)|Wake Up World]]'' in 2013, both of which gained success in Finland and Norway. The follow-up album ''[[Follow Me (Isac Elliot album)|Follow Me]]'' was released in 2014 charting in Finland and Norway.

==Music career==
===2012: Career beginnings===
Elliot was a member of the boy choir [[Cantores minores|Cantores Minores]] and appeared in the [[Swedish Theatre|Svenska Teatern]] musicals ''[[Cabaret (musical)|Cabaret]]'' and ''[[Kristina från Duvemåla]]''.<ref>{{cite web|title=Uusi tähti on syttynyt|url=http://www.sonymusic.fi/2013/02/14/uusi-tahti-on-syttynyt-isac-elliot-on-taman-vuoden-kovin-musiikkikomeetta-josta-povataan-suomen-justin-bieberia/|publisher=Sony Music Entertainment Finland Oy|access-date=23 March 2013|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130324232125/http://www.sonymusic.fi/2013/02/14/uusi-tahti-on-syttynyt-isac-elliot-on-taman-vuoden-kovin-musiikkikomeetta-josta-povataan-suomen-justin-bieberia/|archive-date=24 March 2013|url-status=dead}}</ref> His song "Pop Goes My Heart" was heard in the film ''Ella ja kaverit'' (2012).<ref>{{cite web|title=Uusi Robin? 12-vuotias espoolaispoika tähtää tähtiin|url=http://www.iltasanomat.fi/musiikki/art-1288532547585.html|publisher=Ilta-Sanomat|access-date=23 March 2013|date=16 January 2013}}</ref>

===2013–present: ''Wake Up World'' and ''Follow Me''===
Isac Elliot's debut single “New Way Home” was released on 14 February 2013.<ref>{{cite web|title=New Star Has Born; Finnish Justin Bieber|url=http://www.sonymusic.fi/2013/02/14/uusi-tahti-on-syttynyt-isac-elliot-on-taman-vuoden-kovin-musiikkikomeetta-josta-povataan-suomen-justin-bieberia/|publisher=Sony Music Finland|access-date=20 October 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130324232125/http://www.sonymusic.fi/2013/02/14/uusi-tahti-on-syttynyt-isac-elliot-on-taman-vuoden-kovin-musiikkikomeetta-josta-povataan-suomen-justin-bieberia/|archive-date=24 March 2013|url-status=dead}}</ref>

In March 2013, he was included in the future stars section of ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'''s "Next Big Sound" list in the 11th place.<ref>{{cite web|title=Finnish artist to the Billboard list |url=http://ylex.yle.fi/uutiset/popuutiset/suomalaisartisti-billboardin-next-big-sound-listalle |publisher=YleX|access-date=20 October 2014}}</ref> The documentary ''Dream Big – The Movie'' about Elliot's career was released in cinemas on 14 February 2014.<ref>{{cite web|title= Dream Big – The Movie release|url=http://www.finnkino.fi/News/Elokuvauutisia/2013-12-17/3099/Isac-Elliot-Dream-Big-The-Movie-ensi-ilta-on-14-helmikuuta/?TheatreArea=1016|publisher= Finnkino|access-date=20 October 2014}}</ref>

Elliot's second studio album ''[[Follow Me (Isac Elliot album)|Follow Me]]'' was released on 7 November 2014.<ref>{{cite web|title= Release of Isac Elliot's second album|url=http://www.sonymusic.fi/2014/10/03/isac-elliot-julkaisee-odotetun-kakkosalbuminsa-follow-me-7-marraskuuta/|publisher= Sony Music Finland|access-date=20 October 2014}}</ref> The album was recorded in Helsinki, London, Stockholm and Los Angeles with producers [[Jonas W. Karlsson]], [[Axel Ehnström]], Victor Thell and Kevin Högdahl.<ref>{{cite web|title= Isac Elliot heads to studio with world class producers|url=https://www.sonymusic.fi/2014/05/16/isac-elliot-studioon-maailmankuulun-huipputuottajan-kanssa/|publisher= Sony Music Finland|access-date=20 October 2014}}</ref> Following the release of ''Follow Me'', Elliot also embarked on a "Save a Girl" tour in the end of November 2014.<ref>{{cite web|title= Isac Elliot Tours around scandinavia|url=http://ylex.yle.fi/uutiset/popuutiset/isac-elliot-pohjoismaiden-kiertueelle|publisher= YleX |access-date=20 October 2014}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Isac Elliot is [[Swedish-speaking population of Finland|Finland-Swedish]].<ref>{{cite news|last1=Kytölä|first1=Laura|title=Isac Elliot: "En tiedä, mitä tytöt ajattelevat"|url=http://www.hs.fi/hyvinvointi/a1424838965190|access-date=28 July 2015|work=Helsingin Sanomat|publisher=Sanoma Media Finland|date=26 February 2015|location=Helsinki|language=fi}}</ref> He is the son of Fredrik "Fredi" Lundén, the lead singer for the band The Capital Beat.<ref>{{cite web|last=Kenttämaa|first=Juhani|title=Espoon ihmepoika haastaa Robinin ja Bieberin|url=http://ylex.yle.fi/musiikki/viikon-tehot/espoon-ihmepoika-haastaa-robinin-ja-bieberin|publisher=Yle|access-date=8 April 2013}}</ref>
