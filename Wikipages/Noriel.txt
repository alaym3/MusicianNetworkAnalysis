'''Noel Santos Román''', known by his stage name '''Noriel''', is a Puerto Rican rapper and songwriter, signed to [[Sony Music Latin]]. Noriel became known after singing with [[Maluma (singer)|Maluma]] on his track "[[Cuatro Babys]]" and releasing a popular album called ''Trap Capos: Season 1''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/noriel-mn0003524382/biography|title=Noriel|website=AllMusic|accessdate=20 June 2018|url-status=live|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180620232733/https://www.allmusic.com/artist/noriel-mn0003524382/biography|archivedate=20 June 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.buenamusica.com/juhn-el-all-star/biografia|title=Biografía de Juhn El All Star|work=www.buenamusica.com|accessdate=23 June 2018|url-status=live|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180623171527/https://www.buenamusica.com/juhn-el-all-star/biografia|archivedate=23 June 2018}}</ref>
