'''Subutay Kesgin''', better known by his stage name '''Azer Bülbül''' (1 February 1967 – 6 January 2012), was a prominent Turkish<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.biyografi.net/kisiayrinti.asp?kisiid=4844|title=biyografi.net: Azer Bülbül biyografisi burada  ünlülerin biyografileri burada|website=Biyografi.net|accessdate=25 August 2018}}</ref> folk singer and actor.

==Biography==
Azer Bülbül was born Subutay Kesgin in [[Arpaçay]], [[Kars Province|Kars]] in [[Azerbaijanis|Azerbaijani]] [[Terekeme people|Terekeme]] family. After moving to [[Germany]] with his family, Bülbül started his music career, releasing his debut album ''Garip Yolcu'' in 1984. After releasing a string of folk music albums, he achieved commercial success with his 1994 album, ''Ben Babayım''. On 15 February 2001, he was convicted for drug-related charges and released on [[bail]]. In 2011, he stated that he was rehabilitated and had been clean for 18 months.<ref name="hurriyet">{{cite web | url=http://www.hurriyet.com.tr/magazin/magazinhatti/19622852.asp | title=İşte son görüntüsü | work=[[Hürriyet]]| language=Turkish | date=7 January 2012 | accessdate=1 July 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.milliyet.com.tr/azer-bulbul-hayatini-kaybetti/magazin/magazindetay/06.01.2012/1485489/default.htm | title=Azer Bülbül hayatını kaybetti | work=[[Milliyet]] | date=7 January 2012 | accessdate=1 July 2015 | language=Turkish}}</ref>

Bülbül died of a [[heart attack]] on 6 January 2012 in [[Antalya]]. He was found dead in his hotel room and was buried in [[Esenyurt]] Cemetery in [[Istanbul]].<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.sabah.com.tr/magazin/2014/12/28/yildiz-tilbeden-sasirtan-azer-bulbul-anisi | title=Yıldız Tilbe'den şaşırtan Azer Bülbül anısı | work=[[Sabah (newspaper)|Sabah]] | date=28 December 2014 | accessdate=1 July 2015 | language=Turkish}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.sabah.com.tr/yasam/2012/01/06/azer-bulbul-otelde-olu-bulundu | title=Azer Bülbül otelde ölü bulundu | publisher=[[Sabah (newspaper)|Sabah]]| language=Turkish | date=6 January 2012 | accessdate=1 July 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.ntv.com.tr/arsiv/id/25312122 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150702065538/http://www.ntv.com.tr/arsiv/id/25312122 | url-status=dead | archive-date=2 July 2015 | title=Azer Bülbül otel odasında ölü bulundu | work=[[NTV (Turkey)]] | date=6 January 2012 | accessdate=1 July 2015 }}</ref>

== Personal life ==
Azer Bülbül was married once and later divorced. He did not have any children.<ref name=Haber7com>{{cite web|url=http://www.haber7.com/yasam/haber/390951-azer-bulbul-muslum-dede-oldu-baba-benim|title=Azer Bülbül: Müslüm dede oldu, baba benim!|website=Haber7.com|accessdate=25 August 2018}}</ref> Bülbül also proposed to [[Yıldız Tilbe]] but she refused his marriage offer.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://m.milliyet.com.tr/yildiz-tilbe-den-bir-azer-bulbul-magazin-1991305|title=Yıldız Tilbe'den bir Azer Bülbül anısı! - Magazin Haberleri|website=M.milliyet.com.tr|accessdate=25 August 2018}}</ref>
