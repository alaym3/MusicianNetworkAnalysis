'''Loud Luxury''' are a Canadian<ref>{{cite web|url=https://edmidentity.com/2017/12/31/lowdown-loud-luxury/|title=Get The Lowdown on Canadian Duo Loud Luxury and Listen to Their 2017 Yearmix!|date=31 December 2017|website=Edmidentity.com|accessdate=2 December 2018}}</ref> music production and DJ duo consisting of Andrew Fedyk and Joe Depace and currently based in [[Los Angeles]].<ref name="BB">{{cite web |author=Murray, Gordon |url= https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/8062562/dance-chart-upstarts-lost-frequencies-loud-luxury-porter-robinson |title=Lost Frequencies & Zonderling, Loud Luxury and Virtual Self: Billboard Dance Chart Upstarts |work=Billboard |date=2017-12-07 |accessdate=2018-01-15}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title = Loud Luxury Lyrics|url = https://www.lyricsgetit.com/loud-luxury-lyrics-29vnln.html|website = LyricsGetit|accessdate = 5 August 2016}}</ref><ref name="EI">{{cite web |url= https://edmidentity.com/2017/12/31/lowdown-loud-luxury/ |title=Get The Lowdown on Canadian Duo Loud Luxury and Listen to Their 2017 Yearmix! |website=Edmidentity.com |date=2017-12-31 |author=Gilmore, Grant |accessdate=2018-01-15}}</ref><ref name="BBB">{{cite web |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/8275944/loud-luxury-body-interview |title= The Full 'Body' Experience: How Loud Luxury's Biggest Record is Taking the Duo to New Heights |work=Billboard |date=2018-03-30 |author= Rishty, David |accessdate=2018-10-15}}</ref> In 2017, they released a remix of the [[Martin Garrix]] single "[[Scared to Be Lonely]]", which gained them recognition.<ref name="EI"/>

==History==
The duo was formed when Fedyk and Depace, who met at school, collaborated.<ref name="EI"/> Going to a [[Porter Robinson]] concert, they became inspired with the way Robinson had connected with the crowd.<ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.youredm.com/2016/02/08/loud-luxury-old-man-interview-free-dl/ |title=Loud Luxury - Old Man |publisher=Your EDM |date=2018-02-08 |author=Kusnierek, Timmy |accessdate=2018-01-15}}</ref> In December 2016, they released the song "See It Again".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youredm.com/2016/12/15/loud-luxury-see-free-download/ |title=Loud Luxury - see it again |publisher=Your EDM |date=2016-12-15 |author=Yau, Jeffrey |accessdate=2018-01-15}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=Reaney, James |url=https://lfpress.com/2016/09/14/former-londoners-loud-luxury-topped-charts-with-remix-of-cant-stop-the-feeling |title=Loud and proud &#124; The London Free Press |website=Lfpress.com |date=2016-09-20 |accessdate=2018-01-15}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://thump.vice.com/en_ca/article/9aved5/northmix-loud-luxury |title=NORTHMIX: Loud Luxury - Thump |website=Thump.vice.com |date=2015-07-28 |accessdate=2018-01-15}}</ref>

In 2017, they remixed [[Martin Garrix]]'s song "[[Scared to Be Lonely]]", which was included on the remix EP. They released an original single titled "[[Body (Loud Luxury song)|Body]]" through [[Armin van Buuren]]'s record label [[Armada Music]]. <ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dancingastronaut.com/2017/10/loud-luxury-body-feat-brando-official-lyric-video/ |title=Exclusive: Loud Luxury - Body ft brando |publisher=Dancing Astronaut |date=2017-10-26 |author=Kohli, Kanvar |accessdate=2018-01-15}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.ravejungle.com/2017/10/30/loud-luxury-body-brando-armada/ |title=Loud Luxury debut on Armada Music with new dope track 'Body' ft. Brando |publisher=Rave Jungle |date=2017-10-30 |author=Bosk, Andry |accessdate=2018-01-15 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180116005837/http://www.ravejungle.com/2017/10/30/loud-luxury-body-brando-armada/ |archive-date=January 16, 2018 |url-status=dead }}</ref> The song, which features vocalist [[Brando (singer)|Brando]], debuted on the ''Billboard'' [[Hot Dance/Electronic Songs]] chart at number 49, selling 20,000 downloads and gaining over 40,000,000 streams.<ref name="BB"/><ref name="EI"/> On 17 February 2017, Loud Luxury collaborated with [[deep house]] musician Ryan Shephard to release the song "Fill Me In" (initial title: "Something to Say") on [[Tiesto]]'s record label AFTR:HRS.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.youredm.com/2017/02/07/loud-luxury-ryan-shepherd-release-something-say-tiestos-aftrhrs-records/ |title=Loud Luxury & Ryan Shepherd Release "Something To Say" On Tiësto's AFTR:HRS Records. |publisher=Your EDM |date=2017-02-07 |author=Claps, Andrew |accessdate=2018-01-15}}</ref>

In 2018, Loud Luxury received a nomination in the Independent Music Video category at the [[9th Hollywood Music in Media Awards]] for "Show Me" featuring Nikki's Wives.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hmmawards.com/2018-music-in-visual-media-nominations/|title=Complete list of 2018 HMMA Music In Visual Media Nominations|author=Costa Communications Inc.|publisher=[[Hollywood Music in Media Awards]]|year=2018|access-date=November 25, 2018}}</ref>

At the [[Juno Awards of 2019]], they won the [[Juno Award for Dance Recording of the Year]] for "Body".<ref>[https://www.cbc.ca/music/junos/news/junos-2019-the-complete-list-of-winners-1.5056461 "Junos 2019: the complete list of winners"]. [[CBC Music]], March 16, 2019.</ref>

In 2019, Loud Luxury performed at [[Electric Daisy Carnival|EDC]] Las Vegas and [[Lollapalooza]] in [[Chicago]].<ref>{{cite web |title=2019 Lineup – EDC Las Vegas 2019 |url=https://lasvegas.electricdaisycarnival.com/past-highlights/2019-lineup/ |website=lasvegas.electricdaisycarnival.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Watch Sets From Twenty One Pilots, The Chainsmokers & More With the 2019 Lollapalooza Live Stream. In addition to headlining the “Nights Like This” tour, with over 50 dates across North America. This year in addition to their residency at Hakkasan, Omnia and Wet Republic in Las Vegas.|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8525750/lollapalooza-2019-live-stream-watch |website=Billboard |accessdate=3 August 2019}}</ref>
