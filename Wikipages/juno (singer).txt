'''Kim Jun-ho''' ({{ko-hhrm|김준호}}) (born on December 15, 1986<ref>{{cite video |year=2005 |title=DBSK We Want To Know - JunSu (Part 1, Eng Sub) |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jOxcYIu4TWY |medium=TV program |language=Korean, English |publisher=KM News. KM |location= |accessdate=2012-10-20 |time=1:32 }}</ref><ref name="focus">{{cite web |url=http://www.focus.co.kr/content.asp?aid=fc21845cfa9141fca98eb4b9385c6488 |script-title=ko:동방신기와 결별…뮤지컬배우 도전…아픔에서 삶을 배웠죠 |author=Park Young-sun |date=11 April 2011 |work=The Daily Focus |publisher=Focus |language=Korean |accessdate=2012-10-23 |title=Archived copy |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141214230346/http://www.focus.co.kr/content.asp?aid=fc21845cfa9141fca98eb4b9385c6488 |archive-date=14 December 2014 |url-status=dead }}</ref>) is a [[South Korea]]n singer, who is mainly active in [[Japan]] and [[China]], where he is more well known by his stage names JUNO and ZUNO. His younger fraternal twin brother is [[JYJ]]-member [[Junsu]].<ref name="kbs">{{cite web |url=http://world.kbs.co.kr/english/program/program_artist_detail.htm?No=107138 |title=XIA |author= |date= |work= |publisher=[[Korean Broadcasting System|KBS]] World |accessdate=2012-10-01}}</ref> In 2014 he changed his name to '''Kim Moo-young'''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/55292/jyj-kimjunsu-twin-brother-to-pursue-acting-career|title=JYJ Kim Junsu’s Twin Brother to Change His Name and Pursue Acting|publisher=MWAVE|accessdate=2014-01-07|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140107193655/http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/55292/jyj-kimjunsu-twin-brother-to-pursue-acting-career|archive-date=2014-01-07|url-status=dead}}</ref>

==Career==
===Pre-debut===
Kim Jun-ho started playing [[baseball]] in elementary school and was scouted by the professional [[South Korea]]n team [[SK Wyverns]] during high school. He attended [[Dong-eui University]] in [[Busan]] but dropped out during his freshman year due to a sports injury, which also ended his career as a professional baseball player.<ref name="chosun">{{cite web |url=http://www.chosunonline.com/entame/20100316000011 |script-title=ja:インタビュー：ジュンス実兄、意気込みを語る |author=Lee He-wan |date=16 March 2010 |work=Chosun Online |publisher=The Chosun Ilbo & Chosunonline.com |language=Japanese |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100322193001/http://www.chosunonline.com/entame/20100316000011 |archivedate=22 March 2010 |accessdate=2012-11-23}}</ref>

===2010-12: Debut in China and Japan, acting debut===
In 2010, after having studied acting for two years and having spent a year at [[Beijing Normal University]],<ref name="chosun"/> Kim made his singing debut in China as ZUNO.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.allkpop.com/2010/03/xiah-junsu%E2%80%99s-brother-zuno-releases-debut-mv |title=Xiah Junsu’s brother, ZUNO releases debut MV |author=GhostWriter |date=29 March 2010 |work=allkpop |publisher=6Theory Media |accessdate=2012-11-23 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130224000006/http://www.allkpop.com/2010/03/xiah-junsu%E2%80%99s-brother-zuno-releases-debut-mv |archivedate=24 February 2013 }}</ref>

A year later, the singer debuted in Japan with his single album ''Fate'' as JUNO.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.mtviggy.com/blog-posts/juno-junsus-twin-brother-set-for-his-japanese-debut/ |title=JUNO, Junsu’s Twin Brother, Set for His Japanese Debut! |author=MTV K |date=12 July 2011 |work= [[Music Television|MTV Iggy]]|publisher=MTV |accessdate=2012-11-23}}</ref> The second single album ''Believe'' placed third on the [[Oricon]] daily chart and became the theme song for a Japanese Bee TV drama.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.allkpop.com/2011/12/junos-believe-ranks-in-3rd-place-on-the-oricon-chart |title=JUNO's "Believe~" ranks in 3rd place on the Oricon chart |author=VITALSIGN |date=28 December 2011 |work=allkpop |publisher=6Theory Media |accessdate=2012-11-23}}</ref> Kim's third single ''Everything'' entered the [[Oricon]] Charts in second place and reached number ten on the weekly charts.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.barks.jp/news/?id=1000079872&ref=rss |title=JUNO、3rdシングル「Everything」がデイリー2位、ウィークリー10位 |author= |date=23 May 2012 |work=Barks |publisher= |language=Japanese |accessdate=2012-11-23}}</ref>

In 2012, Kim made his acting debut in the [[Korean drama]] ''[[Ji Woon-Soo's Stroke of Luck|Stroke of Luck]]''.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.soompi.com/2012/04/19/jyjs-junsu-twin-brother-juno-acting-debut-through-stroke-of-luck/ |title=JYJ’s Junsu Twin Brother Juno Acting Debut Through "Stroke of Luck" |author=jbarky |date=19 April 2012 |work= |publisher=[[Soompi]] |accessdate=2012-11-23}}</ref>

Kim Jun-ho is currently a member of the [[South Korea]]n all star football team [[FC MEN]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bluewings.kr/player/player05.asp |title=FC MEN squad |work=Blue Wings Suwon football club official website |publisher=Samsung Sports |language=Korean |accessdate=2012-11-23 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130301011250/http://www.bluewings.kr/player/player05.asp |archivedate=2013-03-01 }}</ref>
