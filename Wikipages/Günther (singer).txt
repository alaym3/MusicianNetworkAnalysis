'''Mats Söderlund'''<ref>{{Cite web|title=Günther: "Melodifestivalen hade varit kul att göra igen"|url=https://www.sydsvenskan.se/2019-02-07/guenther-melodifestivalen-hade-varit-kul-att-gora-igen|access-date=9 March 2021|website=Sydsvenskan|language=sv}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Anoushiravani|first=Mitra|date=25 April 2008|title=Q&A with Gunther and the Sunshine Girls|url=https://www.browndailyherald.com/2008/04/25/qa-with-gunther-and-the-sunshine-girls/|access-date=9 March 2021|website=Brown Daily Herald|language=en-US}}</ref> (born 25 July 1967), better known by his stage name '''Günther''', is a Swedish singer.

==Career==
Söderlund was a model in his youth, and then later he was the owner of two nightclubs in [[Malmö|Malmo]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Tuna|first=Ieva|date=28 July 2004|title=Gunther - the man behind the moustache|url=https://www.baltictimes.com/news/articles/10567/|url-status=live|access-date=9 March 2021|website=BalticTimes.com}}</ref>

Günther's 2004 song "[[Ding Dong Song]]" became popular in Sweden. The song (which featured Günther's backup singers, the Sunshine Girls) topped the Swedish charts in 2004.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.answers.com/topic/mats-s-derlund |title=The Most Trusted Place for Answering Life's Questions |publisher=Answers |date=3 August 2016 |access-date=4 September 2016}}</ref>

In December 2013, Günther released the single "I'm Not Justin Bieber, Bitch". The song features strong [[electro house]] influences in its sound.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://rateyourmusic.com/release/single/gunther/im_not_justin_bieber__b_tch/ |title=I'm Not Justin Bieber, B*tch by Günther (Single, Electropop): Reviews, Ratings, Credits, Song list |publisher=[[Rate Your Music]] |access-date=4 September 2016}}</ref> The track's music video has gone viral, gaining momentum among fans and having 5 million views as of mid-2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hcKPAGoDz8A|access-date=14 October 2016|publisher=[[YouTube.com]]|date=13 December 2013|title=I'm Not Justin Bieber B**ch - Official Video}}</ref>
