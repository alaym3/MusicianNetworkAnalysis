'''The Uncluded''' was an American indie folk rap group, formed by rapper [[Aesop Rock]] and singer-songwriter [[Kimya Dawson]]. In 2013, they released an album titled ''Hokey Fright'', through [[Rhymesayers Entertainment]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/gb/album/hokey-fright-deluxe-version/id624756651 |title=iTunes - Music - Hokey Fright (Deluxe Version) by The Uncluded |website=Itunes.apple.com |date=2013-05-07 |accessdate=2015-04-16}}</ref> The band name is a reference to the Michael Bernard Loggins book ''Imaginationally''.<ref>{{cite web |title=The Uncluded - Biography & History |url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/the-uncluded-mn0002910444/biography |website=[[AllMusic]] |accessdate=January 12, 2019}}</ref>

On February 11, 2013, the Uncluded released their first music video on [[YouTube]]. The single is titled "Earthquake". Their debut album ''[[Hokey Fright]]'' was released on May 7, 2013.<ref>{{cite web|title=Aesop Rock and Kimya Dawson Form Band the Uncluded, Announce Album, Share Video|url=http://pitchfork.com/news/49528-aesop-rock-and-kimya-dawson-form-band-the-uncluded-announce-album-share-video/|website=Pitchfork.com|accessdate= February 11, 2013}}</ref> The video for their third single "Delicate Cycle" has a [[Cameo appearance|cameo]] of [[Lil Bub]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uHYhzg8QWbI |title=The Uncluded - Delicate Cycle |publisher=[[YouTube]] |date= |accessdate=2013-05-20}}</ref>

The band toured together in early 2014<ref>[https://www.brooklynbowl.com/event/521769-uncluded ]{{dead link|date=March 2020}}</ref> and performed a piece as part of Bedstock in 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vAnuBmdL8E4 |title=The Uncluded (Aesop Rock and Kimya Dawson) performs "Jambi Cafe" in bed &#124; MyMusicRx #Bedstock 2016 |publisher=[[YouTube]] |date=2016-11-28 |accessdate=2020-03-10}}</ref> They have not released any material since ''Hokey Fright'' and, in May 2018, Dawson tweeted "Aesop Rock is not my friend."<ref>{{cite web|author=Kimya Dawson |url=https://twitter.com/mrskimyadawson/status/1001135666720161792 |title=Kimya Dawson on Twitter: "Aesop Rock is not my friend." |website=Twitter.com |date= |accessdate=2020-03-10}}</ref>
