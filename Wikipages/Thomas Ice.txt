'''Thomas Ice''' is an American theologian and author of books on biblical prophecy.

== Education and career ==
Ice received his BA from [[Howard Payne University]] in 1975, his masters in theology from [[Dallas Theological Seminary]] in 1981, and a PhD from [[Tyndale Theological Seminary]] in 1995.<ref name="calvaryprof">{{cite web|url=https://www.calvary.edu/dr-tommy-ice/|title=Dr. Tommy Ice (faculty profile)|publisher=Calvary University|access-date=February 27, 2021}}</ref>  He performed post-doctoral studies in church history at the [[University of Wales]].<ref name="pretribprof">{{cite web|url=https://www.pre-trib.org/dr-thomas-ice-about|title=Dr. Thomas Ice (about page)|publisher=Pre-Trib Research Center|access-date=February 27, 2021}}</ref>

Ice is the executive director of the Pre-Trib Research Center on the campus of [[Liberty University]] in [[Lynchburg, Virginia|Lynchburg]], [[Virginia]].<ref name="pretribprof"/>{{dubious|date=February 2021|reason=the orgs webpage currently lists it as based at Calvary}}  The research center was founded in 1994 by [[Tim LaHaye]] and Ice to research, teach, proclaim, and defend [[Rapture|pre-tribulationism]].<ref name="pretribprof" /> The center currently sponsors prophecy meetings and conferences and provides speakers for the purpose of discussion and lecture on the topic of pre-tribulationism.{{cn|date=February 2021}}

Ice has taught at Liberty University.{{when?|date=February 2021}} He moved to [[Calvary University]] in 2018.<ref name="calvaryprof"/> He lives in [[Lee's Summit, Missouri]], with his wife Janice.<ref name="pretribprof"/>
