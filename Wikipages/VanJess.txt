'''VanJess''' is a [[Nigerian-American]] [[Rhythm and blues|R&B]] duo composed of sisters Ivana and Jessica Nwokike. The duo began their career on [[YouTube]] performing cover songs. After one of their videos, a cover of [[Drake (musician)|Drake]]'s "[[Headlines (Drake song)|Headlines]]", had viral success, they decided to pursue a serious career in music.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://myspace.com/article/2016/5/6/vanjess-10-things|title=10 Things You Should Know About VanJess|work=Myspace|access-date=2018-08-18}}</ref> In 2011 the duo hired a manager and recorded in a studio for the first time.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://hypebae.com/2017/11/baewatch-vanjess-interview|title=BAEwatch: VanJess|work=HYPEBAE|access-date=2018-08-18}}</ref> On 12 November 2018, the group announced that they had signed to the [[RCA Records|RCA]] joint venture label [[Keep Cool Records|Keep Cool]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.instagram.com/p/BqX7BU4lVmE/|title=VanJess on Instagram: "anyone who's followed our journey knows how crazy this is. being signed with a label who so strongly believed in our potential even when…"|website=Instagram|language=en|access-date=2018-11-20}}</ref> Thus far in their career, VanJess have released one studio album, ''Silk Canvas'', and one EP, ''Homegrown''.  
 
== History ==
The sisters are first generation Nigerian immigrants of [[Igbo people|Igbo]] origin,<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.factmag.com/2018/08/22/fact-rated-vanjess-silk-canvas/|title=VanJess are the new architects of electronic-soul on their debut album Silk Canvas|date=2018-08-22|work=FACT Magazine: Music News, New Music.|access-date=2018-08-23|language=en-US}}</ref> although they were both born in the United States.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.thefourohfive.com/music/article/sister-collab-the-405-meets-vanjess-151|title=Sister Collab: The 405 meets VanJess|last=http://thefourohfive.com|work=The 405|access-date=2018-08-18|language=en}}</ref> According to Jessica, they spent 'about eight or nine years' of their childhood in Nigeria, before moving to [[La Palma, California|La Palma]], California with their mother, when they were 10 and 8 years old, respectively. They later moved to [[Cerritos, California|Cerritos]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.okayafrica.com/vanjess-silk-canvas-interview/|title=VanJess Are Set to Takeover the R&B World and Beyond|date=2018-07-12|work=OkayAfrica|access-date=2018-08-18|language=en}}</ref> Jessica and Ivana attended a performing arts elementary school. In middle school and high school, they performed as a duo in talent shows. <ref>{{Cite news|url=https://pigeonsandplanes.com/in-depth/2017/12/vanjess-who-is|title=Who Is VanJess?|work=PigeonsandPlanes|access-date=2018-08-18|language=en}}</ref>

They have supported causes like the "Louder than Words" campaign in 2012 and performed at 2013's "Lights Camera Cure" dance-a-thon. 
They have made appearances on shows like VH1's ''The Linda Perry Projec''t.<ref>{{Citation
|title = VanJess On Working With Linda Perry
|url = http://www.vh1.com/news/133867/vanjess-interview-make-or-break-linda-perry/
|website = VH1
|access-date = 2014-08-28}}</ref>  

The duo re-released their debut EP ''00 till Escape'' in 2015 which charted in the [[iTunes]] top ten and trended worldwide.{{citation needed|date=October 2016}} Their debut album ''[[Silk Canvas]]'', an album of original material, was self-released by the duo in 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Nast|first=Condé|date=2021-06-04|title=TOKiMONSTA Announces Tour, Shares New Song With VanJess|url=https://pitchfork.com/news/tokimonsta-announces-tour-shares-new-song-with-vanjess-listen/|access-date=2021-11-26|website=Pitchfork|language=en-US}}</ref> The duo released five singles from the album: "Adore", "Touch the Floor", "Through Enough", "Addicted", "Easy", and "Control Me". The album includes collaborations with [[Masego (musician)|Masego]], [[GoldLink]], [[Little Simz]], [[Leikeli47]], and [[Berhana]]. 

They released their EP, ''Homegrown'', on February 5th, 2021. The project includes collaborations with [[Kaytranada]], [[Phony Ppl]], Jimi Tents, Garren, and Devin Morrison.  

The duo have listed [[Michael Jackson]], [[Janet Jackson]], [[Xscape (group)|Xscape]], [[TLC (group)|TLC]], and [[SWV]] as their biggest musical influences.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.okayafrica.com/vanjess-silk-canvas-interview/|title=VanJess Are Set to Takeover the R&B World and Beyond|date=2018-07-12|work=OkayAfrica|access-date=2018-08-18|language=en}}</ref> In a 2021 interview with ''Buzzfeed'', they listed [[Brandy Norwood|Brandy]], [[Toni Braxton]], and [[Whitney Houston]] as additional influences to their music.<ref>{{Cite web|title=VanJess Talks Us Through Creating In Quarantine And The Making Of Their EP, "Homegrown"|url=https://www.buzzfeednews.com/article/hissasalwe/vanjess-homegrown-quarantine-interview|access-date=2021-08-18|website=BuzzFeed News|language=en}}</ref>
