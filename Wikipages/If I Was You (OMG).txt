"'''If I Was You (OMG)'''" is a song by Asian-American hip hop group [[Far East Movement]]. The song features rapper [[Snoop Dogg]] and was produced by the [[Stereotypes (producers)|Stereotypes]] and [[The Smeezingtons]]. The original recording appears on the album ''[[Free Wired]]'' with a total run time of 3:25. It peaked at number 18 on the [[Recording Industry Association of New Zealand|New Zealand Singles Chart]]. An official remix was made by [[Far East Movement]] and [[Snoop Dogg]] called "On Campus Remix".

==Music video==
The music video for the song premiered on [[Vevo]] and [[YouTube]] on March 16, 2011.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IT3TP1ifpcs|title=Far East Movement - If I Was You (OMG) ft Snoop Dogg|publisher=YouTube}}</ref>
You can also see Natalia Kills, and [[Dominican people (Dominican Republic)|Dominican]] model Rosa Acosta as Snoop Dogg's love interest.
