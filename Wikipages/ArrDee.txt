'''Riley Davies''' (born 17 September 2002), known professionally as '''ArrDee''', is a British rapper. He saw success being featured on the [[remix]] of [[Russ Millions]] and [[Tion Wayne]]'s song "[[Body (Russ Millions and Tion Wayne song)|Body]]", which topped the charts in various countries including the UK.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.officialcharts.com/chart-news/who-is-arrdee-the-brighton-rapper-climbing-the-chart-with-oliver-twist__33296/ |title=Who is ArrDee? The Brighton rapper climbing the chart with Oliver Twist |last=Ainsley |first=Helen |publisher=[[Official Charts Company]] |date=7 June 2021 |access-date=13 June 2021 |archive-date=13 June 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210613210236/https://www.officialcharts.com/chart-news/who-is-arrdee-the-brighton-rapper-climbing-the-chart-with-oliver-twist__33296/ |url-status=live }}</ref> He then released his debut [[single (music)|single]] "Oliver Twist", which peaked at number six on the [[UK Singles Chart]]<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.officialcharts.com/artist/60366/arrdee/ |title=ArrDee |publisher=[[Official Charts Company]] |access-date=19 June 2021 |archive-date=13 June 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210613211447/https://www.officialcharts.com/artist/60366/arrdee/ |url-status=live }}</ref> His stage name is derived from the phonetic spelling of the initials of his birth name.<ref name="TT-20210513">{{cite news |url=https://thetab.com/uk/2021/05/13/arrdee-body-205057 |title='Have you seen the state of her body': Meet ArrDee, the guy behind TikTok's viral hit |last=Ainsworth |first=Harry |work=[[The Tab]] |date=13 May 2021 |access-date=13 June 2021 |archive-date=13 June 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210613210545/https://thetab.com/uk/2021/05/13/arrdee-body-205057 |url-status=live }}</ref>
