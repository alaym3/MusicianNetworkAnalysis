'''Abu Othaina''' ({{Lang-ar|أبو أذينة}}), popularly known as '''Shabjdeed''' ({{Lang|ar|عدي عباس/شب جديد}}) is a rapper from [[Kafr 'Aqab]], [[East Jerusalem]].<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|date=2019-08-19|title='If Israeli soldiers start shooting, we won't stop the interview': Palestinian hip-hop crew BLTNM|url=http://www.theguardian.com/music/2019/aug/19/bltnm-hip-hop-palestine-west-bank|access-date=2021-04-06|website=the Guardian|language=en}}</ref><ref name=":1">{{Cite web|last=Tapponi|first=Roisin|date=2019-12-05|title=BLTNM are the Palestinian record label breaking down borders|url=https://i-d.vice.com/en_uk/article/ywaadj/bltnm-palestinian-record-label-interview|access-date=2021-04-06|website=i-D|language=en}}</ref> Based in [[Ramallah]], he has become an influential figure in underground [[Palestinian hip hop]].<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1" />

== Style ==
Shabjdeed's lyrics often deal with conflicts between [[Israel Defense Forces]] and the [[Palestinians]].<ref name=":0" /> His use of the Arabic language is characterized as playful, using alliteration an rhyming to create a unique style.<ref>{{Cite web|last=هيب|first=شادن|date=2020-01-01|title=موسيقى فلسطين 2019... انحياز للراب والهيب هوب|url=https://www.arab48.com/فسحة/صوت/2020/01/01/موسيقى-فلسطين-2019-انحياز-للراب-والهيب-هوب|access-date=2021-06-04|website=فسحة - ثقافية فلسطينية|language=en}}</ref> His delivery has been described as a "constant overflow of words that fills every corner of a track."<ref name=":0" /> His rapping has been characterized as having a thick accent.<ref>{{Cite web|last=جدلية|first=Jadaliyya-|title=Subversive Sinbads: The Music of Shabjdeed and Al Nather|url=https://www.jadaliyya.com/Details/40203|access-date=2021-04-06|website=Jadaliyya - جدلية|language=en}}</ref>

He cites the Ramallah rap collective [[Saleb Wahed]] as a major source of inspiration.<ref name=":0" />

== BLTNM ==
Along with Al-Nather and Shabmouri, he is a part of BLTNM, pronounced "blatinum", in reference to [[Platinum Records (Dubai)|Platinum Records]] in Dubai and the fact that there's no [[P]] phoneme in [[Arabic]].<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":2">{{Cite web|date=2020-08-18|title=Palestinian rap group BLTNM stars in Burberry’s new campaign|url=https://arab.news/jpcqn|access-date=2021-04-06|website=Arab News|language=en}}</ref> The group participated in a campaign for [[Burberry]] August 2020.<ref name=":2" />

The critic [[Maan Abu Taleb|Ma'an Abu Taleb]] named Shabjdeed and Al Nather's 2019 track "Mtaktak" for a list of the "greatest hip-hop songs of all time" published by the [[BBC]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=The greatest hip-hop songs of all time – who voted|url=https://www.bbc.com/culture/article/20191007-the-greatest-hip-hop-songs-of-all-time-who-voted|access-date=2021-04-06|website=www.bbc.com|language=en}}</ref>
