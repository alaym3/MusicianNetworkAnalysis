'''Winston Bergwijn''' (born April 20, 1978), known professionally as '''Winne''', is a Dutch rapper. 

== Career ==
Winne was born in [[Paramaribo]], [[Suriname]], and moved to [[Rotterdam]] at a young age. He started rapping in English, initially under the name Static, until his friends encouraged him to rap in Dutch. With his group D.C.O., he performed at various locations in Rotterdam. In 2005, Winne suddenly became known to a wider audience when his track "Top 3 MC", produced by Kapitein Mo, was released on the internet. A few months later, the track "Pomp Die Shit!" was released on the compilation album ''What's da Flavor?!'', which left its mark on the Dutch hip hop scene. Then he appeared on albums of [[Opgezwolle]] and [[U-Niq]]. In 2006, he was signed by the label [[TopNotch]].<ref name="topnotch">{{cite web|url=http://top-notch.nl/artiesten/winne|title=Winne|website=Top-notch.nl}}</ref><ref>[https://www.trouw.nl/cultuur/rapper-winne-wil-mensen-naar-het-licht-brengen-rappers-zijn-de-preachers-van-deze-tijd-~af13a085/ Rapper Winne wil mensen naar het licht brengen: ‘Rappers zijn de preachers van deze tijd’], Trouw.nl, 1 December 2018</ref>

In 2007, he released his debut EP ''Onoverwinnelijk'', which included the tracks "Top 3 MC", "Pomp die Shit!" and "Begrijp me niet verkeerd". For the latter he won a State Award in 2008 for the best music video of that year.<ref name="topnotch"/><ref>[https://tvvisie.be/inhoud/nederland/deze-week-in-familie_26563/ The Opposites en Kempi grote winnaars 'State Awards 2008'], TVvisie.be, 8 December 2008</ref>

In 2009, he followed up with his debut studio album ''Winne Zonder Strijd''. The album contained the live and internet hits "W.I.N.N.E.", "Alles Wat Ik Wil" featuring GMB and "Lotgenoot". The same year he released the complilation mixtape ''So So Lobi'',  in collaboration with Patta.<ref name="topnotch"/><ref>[https://www.nrc.nl/nieuws/2018/08/22/rapper-winne-als-ik-de-stilte-verbreek-ga-ik-waarde-spreken-a1613868 Rapper Winne: ‘Als ik de stilte verbreek, ga ik waarde spreken’], Nrc.nl, 22 August 2018</ref>

In 2011, he was seen with [[Henny Vrienten]] of [[Doe Maar]] in an episode of ''Ali B op volle toeren''.<ref name="topnotch"/>
