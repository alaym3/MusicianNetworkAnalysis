'''WorshipMob''' is an American [[contemporary worship music|worship music]] collective from [[Colorado Springs, Colorado]], and they were formed on March 15, 2011 by Sean Mulholland. They were signed to the [[Integrity Media|Integrity Music]] label, releasing their first [[studio album]], [[Carry the Fire (WorshipMob album)|''Carry the Fire'']], in 2015. This album was their breakthrough release upon the [[Billboard (magazine)|''Billboard'' magazine]] [[Christian Albums]] and [[Top Heatseekers|Heatseekers Albums]] charts.  

Capturing both scripted and unscripted moments of heart-felt worship, WorshipMob shares their video recordings on YouTube to a global audience of millions.  WorshipMob shares extended uncut spontaneous worship sessions, as well as covers of songs from Bethel, All Sons & Daughters, Hillsong and more.  The WorshipMob model is spreading with local groups gathering in cities throughout the US along with WorshipMob startups in Australia, Africa and the Philippines.

==Music history==
The collective's music recording career commenced in 2015, with the [[studio album]], [[Carry the Fire (WorshipMob album)|''Carry the Fire'']], and it was released on March 3, 2015 by [[Integrity Media|Integrity Music]].<ref name="WL Review">{{cite web|title=Carry the Fire|url=http://worshipleader.com/albums/carry-the-fire/|journal=[[Worship Leader (magazine)|Worship Leader]]|first=Kristen|last=Gilles|accessdate=May 17, 2015|date=March 3, 2015}}</ref> This album was their breakthrough release upon the [[Billboard (magazine)|''Billboard'' magazine]] charts, where it placed on the [[Christian Albums]] chart at No. 33 and on the [[Top Heatseekers|Heatseekers Albums]] chart at No. 24.<ref name="chart search">[http://www.billboard.com/biz/search/charts?f%5b0%5d=ts_chart_artistname%3AWorshipMob&f%5b1%5d=ss_bb_type%3Achart_item&type=2&artist=WorshipMob Chart search for WorshipMob] at billboard.com/biz</ref><ref name="AM Awards">{{cite web|title=WorshipMob : Awards|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/worshipmob-mn0003348911/awards|author=[[Billboard (magazine)|''Billboard'']]|publisher=[[AllMusic]]|accessdate=May 17, 2015}}</ref>
