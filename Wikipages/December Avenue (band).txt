'''December Avenue''' is a 5-piece [[indie pop]]/[[alternative rock]] band from [[Manila]], [[Philippines]] known for their viral compositions online. The band is one of the most streamed [[Original Pilipino Music|OPM]] bands of all-time.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bworldonline.com/december-ave-is-most-streamed-artist-in-phl/|title = December Ave. Is most streamed artist in PHL|date = 5 December 2019}}</ref> The band is composed of Zel Bautista on vocals and guitars, Jem Manuel on guitars, Don Gregorio on bass, Jet Danao on drums and backing vocals, and Gelo Cruz on keyboards and backing vocals.

Bautista, Manuel, Gregorio, and Danao started out in their alma mater [[University of Santo Tomas]] in 2007. Cruz, an alumnus of [[De La Salle-College of St. Benilde]], was later added in 2016. Bautista is the chief composer of the band. Originally known for their predominantly English songs, the band has released Tagalog songs since 2016.<ref name="decemberave">[http://towerofdoom.net/december-avenue/ December Avenue | Tower of Doom]</ref><ref>[https://www.bandwagon.asia/artists/december-avenue December Avenue profile on Bandwagon]</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.onemusic.ph/news/december-avenue-music-life-2438|title=December Avenue: Music of Life|last=Ortega|first=Karlos|date=January 13, 2017|website=One Music PH}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://businessmirror.com.ph/december-avenue-their-music-is-for-moving-on/|title=December Avenue: Their music is for moving on|last=Enriquez|first=JM|date=September 2, 2018|publisher=[[BusinessMirror]]}}</ref>
