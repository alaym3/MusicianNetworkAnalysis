'''Silk Sonic''' is an American [[Contemporary R&B|R&B]] [[Supergroup (music)|superduo]] composed of singer [[Bruno Mars]] and rapper and singer [[Anderson .Paak]]. The duo released their debut single, "[[Leave the Door Open (song)|Leave the Door Open]]", in March 2021, with their debut album, ''[[An Evening with Silk Sonic]]'', released in November 2021.

==History==
===2017â€“present: Beginnings, breakthrough, and ''An Evening with Silk Sonic''===
[[File:Silksoniclogo.png|200px|thumb|left|Silk Sonic logo]]
In 2017, [[Anderson .Paak]] was the opening act for Bruno Mars on the European leg of the [[24K Magic World Tour]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://consequenceofsound.net/2017/01/anderson-paak-to-open-bruno-mars-24k-magic-world-tour/|title=Anderson .Paak to open Bruno Mars' 24K Magic World Tou|last=Young|first=Alex|work=[[Consequence of Sound]]|date=January 8, 2017|access-date=January 26, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190716032222/https://consequenceofsound.net/2017/01/anderson-paak-to-open-bruno-mars-24k-magic-world-tour/|archive-date=July 16, 2019|url-status=live}}</ref> In April, Mars and .Paak worked together at [[Abbey Road Studios]] in London with [[Nile Rodgers]] and [[Guy Lawrence]] for [[Chic (band)|Chic]]'s album ''[[It's About Time (Chic album)|It's About Time]]'', and worked on music while on tour that appeared on Paak's album ''[[Ventura (Anderson .Paak album)|Ventura]]''.<ref>{{cite magazine |title=Watch Bruno Mars, Anderson .Paak, Nile Rodgers & Disclosure Work Together at Abbey Road Studios |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/7775288/anderson-paak-disclosure-nile-rogers-bruno-mars-abbey-road-studio |magazine=Billboard |access-date=7 March 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Renshaw |first1=David |title=Nile Rodgers says Bruno Mars and Anderson. Paak will appear on the new Chic album |url=https://www.thefader.com/2018/04/23/nile-rodgers-chic-bruno-mars-anderson-paak |website=The Fader |access-date=7 March 2021}}</ref> The duo was formed in 2017 by Mars and .Paak "as a joke the two friends hatched on the road".<ref>{{Cite magazine|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-features/bruno-mars-anderson-paak-silk-sonic-album-release-date-1212141/|title = The Soothing Sounds of Silk Sonic|magazine = [[Rolling Stone]]|date = August 18, 2021}}</ref>

On February 26, 2021, Mars announced that he and .Paak had formed a band, saying, "[Silk Sonic] locked in and made an album."<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nme.com/news/music/bruno-mars-anderson-paak-new-band-silk-sonic-announce-album-2889601|title=Bruno Mars and Anderson .Paak form new band Silk Sonic, announce album|work=[[NME]]|last=Gallagher|first=Alex|date=February 26, 2021|access-date=March 5, 2021}}</ref> In a press release, they said the band was named by [[Bootsy Collins]] after he heard the album.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Hussey |first1=Allison |title=Bruno Mars and Anderson .Paak Share Video for First Silk Sonic Song: Watch |url=https://pitchfork.com/news/bruno-mars-and-anderson-paak-share-video-for-first-silk-sonic-song-watch/ |website=Pitchfork |date=March 5, 2021 |access-date=7 March 2021}}</ref> "[[Leave the Door Open (song)|Leave the Door Open]]", the lead single for their upcoming album, was released on March 5, along with the intro song, hosted by Collins.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.latimes.com/entertainment-arts/music/story/2021-02-26/bruno-mars-anderson-paak-band-silk-sonic-album|title=Bruno Mars and Anderson .Paak tease fans with new band called Silk Sonic|date=February 26, 2021|website=[[Los Angeles Times]]|last=Carras|first=Christi|access-date=March 5, 2021}}</ref> The band made their television debut at the [[63rd Annual Grammy Awards]] performing the single, alongside a tribute to [[Little Richard]] consisting of "[[Long Tall Sally]]" and "[[Good Golly, Miss Molly]]".<ref>{{cite web |title=Watch Silk Sonic's Seductive, '60s-Inspired Grammys Performance |url=https://www.vulture.com/2021/03/anderson-paak-bruno-mars-silk-sonic-grammys-2021-performance.html |website=Vulture|date=March 14, 2021 }}</ref>

"Leave the Door Open" would once again be performed at the [[2021 iHeartRadio Music Awards]], with a similar aesthetic.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.iheart.com/content/2021-05-27-silk-sonic-lead-serenade-in-steamy-leave-the-door-open-performance/|title=Silk Sonic Lead Serenade In Steamy 'Leave The Door Open' Performance|website=iHeartRadio}}</ref> The duo won [[BET Award for Best Group|Best Group]] at the [[BET Awards 2021]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://variety.com/2021/music/news/megan-thee-stallion-dababy-bet-awards-nominations-1234982856/|title=Megan Thee Stallion, DaBaby Lead BET Awards Nominations: The Full List|first1=Michele Amabile|last1=Angermiller|date=May 27, 2021}}</ref> At the same ceremony, they performed "Leave the Door Open" again, alongside teasers of their forthcoming releases.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bet.com/bet-awards/video-clips/r2c91t/bet-awards-2021-silk-sonic-leave-the-door-open|title=Silk Sonic - "Leave the Door Open" - BET Awards 2021 &#124; BET AWARDS|website=www.bet.com}}</ref>

On July 28, Mars announced that Silk Sonic would be hosting a "Summertime Jam" two days later.<ref>{{cite tweet |last=Mars |first=Bruno |author-link=Bruno Mars |user=BrunoMars |number=1420460774699323394 |date=July 28, 2021 |title=ðŸ’¥#SilkSonic https://t.co/4bYUBeOBtX |language=en |access-date=October 17, 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211010174853/https://twitter.com/BrunoMars/status/1420460774699323394 |archive-date=October 10, 2021 |url-status=live}}</ref> This event turned out to mark the release of another single, "[[Skate (song)|Skate]]", which was released on July 30 alongside an accompanying music video.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.complex.com/music/bruno-mars-anderson-paak-silk-sonic-skate-stream|title=Bruno Mars and Anderson .Paak Share New Silk Sonic Track "Skate"|last=Espinoza|first=Joshua|website=[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]]|date=July 30, 2021|access-date=July 30, 2021}}</ref>

On October 8, coinciding with Mars' 36th birthday, the band announced that the album's release had been pushed ahead to November 12, 2021.<ref>{{cite tweet |author=Silk Sonic |author-link=Silk Sonic |user=silksonic |number=1446554026565423108 |date=October 8, 2021 |title=âœ¨âœ¨ #SilkSonic ALBUM OUT NOVEMBER 12!!!! ðŸš€ðŸš€ Pre-Save/Pre-Order: https://t.co/mrMvL9n5w2 @AndersonPaak @Bootsy_Collins + a big happy birthday to @BrunoMars ðŸŽ‰ðŸŽ‰ https://t.co/V29Vm5mm7l |language=en |access-date=October 17, 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211008192025/https://twitter.com/silksonic/status/1446554026565423108 |archive-date=October 8, 2021 |url-status=live}}</ref>

On November 1, Silk Sonic announced that their third single, "[[Smokin Out the Window]]", would release on the 5th, a week before the album's launch. On November 12, ''An Evening with Silk Sonic'' was released. On November 21, "Smokin' Out the Window" was performed as the opening act of the [[American Music Awards of 2021]], where the duo also won [[American Music Award for Favorite Soul/R&B Song|Favorite Song â€“ R&B]].<ref>https://www.popsugar.com/entertainment/silk-sonic-american-music-awards-performance-2021-video-48613030?utm_medium=twitter&utm_source=post&utm_campaign=frontdoor</ref>
