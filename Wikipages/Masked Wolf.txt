'''Harry Michael Avramidis''' (born 1991), known professionally as '''Masked Wolf''', is a [[Greek Australians|Greek-Australian]] rapper from [[Sydney]], [[New South Wales]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://greekherald.com.au/culture/meet-the-greek-australian-rapper-masked-wolf-topping-international-charts/ |title=Meet the Greek-Australian rapper "Masked Wolf", topping international charts |work=[[The Greek Herald]] |date=30 April 2021 |access-date=29 May 2021 |author=Oglos, Peter |archive-url= |archive-date= |url-status=live}}</ref> He is best known for his song "[[Astronaut in the Ocean]]", which became a "[[sleeper hit]]" in 2021, peaking at number 4 on the Australian ARIA Charts and number 6 on the US [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]], having been initially released in June 2019.

==Early life==
In an interview in 2019, Michael said, "I've always been interested in music and as a young kid, my musical outlet was actually playing instruments like the piano, keyboard, guitar and drums." He is a "huge fan" of American [[hip hop music|hip hop]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://pilerats.com/music/rap/introducing-masked-wolf-vibin/ |title=Meet Masked Wolf and his high-energy new single, Vibin |work=Pilerats |date=2019 |access-date=29 May 2021 |author=Davies, Hayden}}</ref> He began writing at the age of 13.<ref name="Evil">{{cite web |url=https://scenestr.com.au/music/premiere-stream-masked-wolf-iiiconic-s-new-single-evil-on-the-inside-20191025/ |title=Premiere: Stream Masked Wolf & iiiConic's new single "Evil on the Inside" |work=Scenestr |date=25 October 2019 |access-date=14 February 2021}}</ref>

==Career==
Michael signed to Teamwrk Records and released his debut single "Speed Racer" in December 2018.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://goodintent.com.au/blog/2019/2/18/sydneys-masked-wolf-stands-out-from-the-hip-hop-pack-on-new-single-vibin/ |title=Sydney's Masked Wolf stands out from the hip hop pack on new single "Vibin'" |work=Good Intent |date=13 March 2019 |access-date=29 May 2021 |author=Dally-Watkins, Charlie
|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190313114541/https://goodintent.com.au/blog/2019/2/18/sydneys-masked-wolf-stands-out-from-the-hip-hop-pack-on-new-single-vibin/ |archive-date=13 March 2019 |url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://otchiphop.com/2018/12/19/premiere-speed-racer/ |title=Video Premiere: Masked Wolf – Speed Racer |work=Off the Clef |date=19 December 2018 |access-date=29 May 2021 |author=van der Staay, Jarrod
|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200920194624/https://otchiphop.com/2018/12/19/premiere-speed-racer/ |archive-date=20 September 2020 |url-status=live}}</ref> All of his subsequent releases were on the label, up until 2021, when "[[Astronaut in the Ocean]]" was re-released.

On 28 January 2021, Michael signed to US-based label [[Elektra Records]], owned by [[Warner Music Group]], in a multi-album deal. Elektra represent Michael's music in all regions except Australia and New Zealand, where he continues to be represented by Teamwrk Records. In a statement, Michael said: "I'm so happy to be a part of the Elektra family, I am really excited to be able to have the opportunity for the world to now hear my music... not only do they develop artists, but they care about each song and that's why I wanted to join them."<ref>{{cite web |url=https://themusicnetwork.com/masked-wolf-elektra-signing/ |title=Masked Wolf inks multi-album deal with US label Elektra Records |work=[[The Music Network]] |date=28 January 2021 |access-date=29 May 2021 |author=Triscari, Caleb
