'''Jamie Miller''' (born September 9, 1997 in [[Cardiff]], [[Wales]]) is a Welsh musician currently based in [[Los Angeles]]. He featured on ''[[The Voice UK]]'' in 2017, where he came in third place.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Jamie Miller|url=https://www.itv.com/thevoice/articles/jamie-miller|access-date=2021-04-21|website=ITV|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Squires|first=Carly|date=2017-08-04|title=The Voice star Jamie Miller has signed to a massive US record label|url=http://www.walesonline.co.uk/whats-on/music-nightlife-news/voice-star-jamie-miller-signed-13428342|access-date=2021-04-21|website=WalesOnline|language=en}}</ref> Following ''The Voice'', he signed a deal with [[Atlantic Records]] and is currently working on his debut album.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Atlantic Records Jamie Miller|url=https://www.atlanticrecords.com/|access-date=2021-04-22|website=Atlantic Records Official Website|language=en}}</ref>

== Early life ==
Miller was born in Cardiff, Wales with his mother, father and two sisters and started singing in primary school.

== Career ==

=== ''The Voice UK'' ===
Miller applied to go on ''[[The X Factor (British TV series)|X Factor]]'' initially but failed and he then tried pursuing music again by applying to be on ''[[The Voice UK]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|title=The Voice UK's Jamie Miller: Everything you need to know about the Team J-Hud finalist|url=https://www.radiotimes.com/tv/entertainment/talent-shows/the-voice-uks-jamie-miller-everything-you-need-to-know-about-the-team-j-hud-finalist/|access-date=2021-04-24|website=Radio Times|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|title=The Voice's Jamie Miller Has Already Had Marriage Proposals on Twitter! {{!}} Lorraine|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LxueX49j2uU|language=en|access-date=2021-04-28}}</ref> On the show, he progressed and got onto [[Jennifer Hudson]]'s team. He got through the Blind auditions, the Battle Round, the Live Shows, the Semi Finals and the Final. Miller ultimately finished third.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Jamie Miller is BACK!|url=https://www.itv.com/thevoice/articles/jamie-miller-is-back|access-date=2021-05-02|website=ITV|language=en}}</ref>

=== Atlantic Records ===
Miller produced covers of songs from [[Adele]] and [[Lewis Capaldi]], which were promoted by [[Khloé Kardashian]] on her [[Instagram]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Khloe Kardashian Stans For 'The Voice UK' Finalist Jamie Miller's Voice|url=https://www.iheart.com/content/2017-06-14-khloe-kardashian-stans-for-the-voice-uk-finalist-jamie-millers-voice/|access-date=2021-04-21|website=iHeartRadio|language=en}}</ref> This prompted Atlantic Records to offer him a recording contract. Since then he has played in various concerts including The Globe<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|date=2017-05-21|title=Jamie Miller To Headline Living With Laura Event In Cardiff|url=https://celebmix.com/jamie-miller-living-laura-live-event/|access-date=2021-04-23|website=CelebMix|language=en-GB}}</ref> and performed in various venues<ref name=":0" /> before moving to Los Angeles.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-08-21|title=Jamie Miller Sends A Much-Needed Love Letter To New York With Dreamy Pop Debut|url=https://www.huffpost.com/entry/jamie-miller-city-that-never-sleeps-new-song_n_5f3df45cc5b6634615808265|access-date=2021-04-24|website=HuffPost UK|language=en}}</ref> He has since written and released original songs ("The City That Never Sleeps", "Onto Something", "Hold You 'Til We're Old" and "Here's Your Perfect") and co-wrote [[Paloma Faith]]'s song "Loyal".<ref>{{Cite web|title=uk-charts.com - Jamie Miller|url=http://uk-charts.com/showperson.asp?name=Jamie+Miller|access-date=2021-05-02|website=uk-charts.com}}</ref>
