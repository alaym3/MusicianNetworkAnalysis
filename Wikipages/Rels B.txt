'''Daniel Heredia Vidal'''<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.20minutos.es/noticia/4439486/0/rels-b-estar-multinacional-puta-mierda/ |title=Rels B: "Estar con una multinacional es una puta mierda" |author=<!--Not stated--> |date=1 November 2020 |website=[[20 minutos|20minutos.es]] |language=es |access-date=18 March 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.neo2.com/rels-b-numero-1/ |title=Rels B, el número 1 de las listas de streaming en España |author=Mongomeri |date=30 October 2019 |website=neo2.com |language=es |access-date=18 March 2021}}</ref> (born 18 October 1993), known professionally as '''Rels B''', is a [[Spaniards|Spanish]] rapper, songwriter and music producer. He is considered one of the great values of the new wave of [[Spanish hip hop]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.ultimahora.es/noticias/cultura/2020/11/02/1210675/rels-estoy-mejor-momento-cuarentena-ayudado-encontrarme.html |title=Rels B: "Estoy en mi mejor momento, la cuarentena me ha ayudado a encontrarme" |author=<!--Not stated--> |date=2 November 2020 |website=[[Última Hora (Spain)|ultimahora.es]] |language=es |access-date=18 March 2021}}</ref>

== Early life ==
Born in [[Palma de Mallorca]],<ref name="CMTV">{{cite web |url=https://www.cmtv.com.ar/biografia/show.php?bnid=2727 |title=Rels B: Biografía |author=<!--Not stated--> |date=<!--Not stated--> |website=cmtv.com.ar |language=es |access-date=18 March 2021}}</ref> the largest city of the [[Balearic Islands]] in Spain, Rels B left home to work as a waiter and bricklayer at the age of 15.<ref name="BM">{{cite web |url=https://www.buenamusica.com/rels-b/biografia |title=Biografía de Rels B |author=<!--Not stated--> |date=<!--Not stated--> |website=buenamusica.com |language=es |access-date=18 March 2021}}</ref>

== Career ==
Rels B began his musical career at a young age, but initially dedicated solely to being a producer, although he recorded a couple of songs during that time.<ref name="BM" /> After working for several years as a producer, in addition to releasing some singles, he made his official debut with his first EP ''Change or Die'', in 2014.<ref name="CMTV" />

In 2015 he released his second EP ''Player Hater'', which is consist of 8 songs produced by [[Itchy & Buco Sounds]], mixed and mastered by {{ill|Quiroga (musician)|lt=Quiroga|es|Quiroga (MC)}}. Other songs such as 'Don't Tell My Mama', 'Word Up', 'Big Plan $', 'Hood Girl' and 'Palm Tree' achieved massive success on networks and millions of views on YouTube. During the same time, the music video for 'Mary Jane', with which he gained wide public acceptance, was produced by Itchy & Buco Sounds and Nibiru Films.<ref name="CMTV" />

In 2016, he premiered the music videos for 'Skinny Flakkkkkkk' and 'Tienes El Don' on YouTube, with which he managed to capture the attention of other exponents of the genre. His first album ''Boys Don't Cry'' was released in the same year, consisting of a total of 12 tracks, again produced by Itchy & Buco Sounds and mastered by Quiroga.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.tiumag.com/news/rels-b-se-consolida-boys-dont-cry-primer-album/ |title=Rels B se consolida con "Boys Don't Cry", su primer álbum |last=Mateu |first=Aleix |date=28 September 2016 |website=tiumag.com |language=es |access-date=18 March 2021}}</ref>

In 2017 he returned with an audiovisual project consisting of various songs with [[Indigo Jams]], such as 'Nueva generación', 'A solas', 'Lord Forgive Me' and 'I Know'.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.mondosonoro.com/noticias-actualidad-musical/primer-adelanto-rels-b-indigo-jams/ |title=Escucha el primer adelanto del EP de Rels B e Indigo Jams |author=<!--Not stated--> |date=6 April 2017 |website=[[Mondo Sonoro|mondosonoro.com]] |language=es |access-date=18 March 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.abc.es/cultura/musica/abci-rels-musica-entra-mas-ojos-oidos-201704200214_noticia.html |title=Rels B: "Hoy en día la música entra más por los ojos que por los oídos" |last=Serrano |first=Nacho |date=20 April 2017 |website=[[ABC (newspaper)|abc.es]] |language=es |access-date=18 March 2021}}</ref> Then he would launch the song 'Libres' and the music video of 'Rock & Roll', with which he had accumulated more than two million views in less than a month.<ref name="CMTV" />

In 2019, the Majorcan 'spent his time celebrating' and on his birthday he released the album ''Happy Birthday Flakko''.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://vanidad.es/mixed-up/692360783/HAPPY-BIRTHDAY-FLAKKO-Rels-B-nos-regala-nuevo-album-el-dia-de-su-cumpleanos.html |title=¡Happy Birthday Flakko! Rels B nos regala nuevo álbum el día de su cumpleaños |last=Sanz |first=Raquel |date=18 October 2019 |website=vanidad.es |language=es |access-date=18 March 2021}}</ref>

At the end of 2020, an album titled {{lang|es|La Isla LP}} was released by Rels B, which has been described as the 'most musical work of his career', consisting of ten songs that deal with confinement, heartbreak and his hometown, [[Mallorca|Majorca]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://okdiario.com/happyfm/rels-b-lanza-isla-lp-colaboraciones-ensueno-6632657 |title=Rels B lanza ‘La Isla LP’ con colaboraciones de ensueño |author=<!--Not stated--> |date=29 December 2020 |website=[[Okdiario|okdiario.com]] |language=es |access-date=18 March 2021}}</ref>
