'''Yunus Özyavuz''' (born 17 August 1978), better known by his stage name '''Sagopa Kajmer''' ({{IPA-tr|sɑgopɑ kɑʒmæɾ|lang}}), is a Turkish rapper, [[songwriter]], record producer, and DJ. He was born in 1978 in [[Samsun]], and finished his primary and high school there. He then started working as a DJ in one of Samsun's local radio stations. In order to continue his education, he moved to [[Istanbul]] and studied [[Persian language]] and literature at [[Istanbul University]]. In 1998, he founded the hip hop band Kuvvetmira and started his career with his stage name "Silahsız Kuvvet". He featured in the compilation album ''Yeraltı Operasyonu'' as Silahsız Kuvvet in 1999. He later changed his stage name to Sagopa Kajmer and released an album with the same name ''Sagopa Kajmer'' in 2002. Following the release of the album, he released ''Bir Pesimistin Gözyaşları'' in 2004. Same year, he contributed to the soundtrack for the movie ''[[G.O.R.A.]]''. He released another studio album ''Romantizma'' in 2005 and on 11 August 2005 found his own production company Melankolia Müzik. In 2006, he released the compilation album ''Kafile'' under the label Melankolia Müzik. On 1 August 2006, he married fellow rapper Kolera (Esen Güler). In 2007, they released their first mutual album ''İkimizi Anlatan Bir Şey''. He released two more solo albums, ''Kötü İnsanları Tanıma Senesi'' (2008) and ''Şarkı Koleksiyoncusu'' (2009), before releasing another mutual album with Kolera, ''Bendeki Sen'', in 2010. Since then, he has released three studio albums: ''Saydam Odalar'' (2011), ''Kalp Hastası'' (2013) and ''Ahmak Islatan'' (2017).

== Career ==
===1978–2002: Early life and career beginnings===
Yunus Özyavuz was born on 17 August 1978 in [[Samsun]].<ref>{{cite news | url = https://www.sozcu.com.tr/hayatim/magazin-haberleri/sagopa-kajmer-kimdir-sagopa-kajmer-nereli-ve-kac-yasindaszcu3/ | title = Sagopa Kajmer kimdir? Sagopa Kajmer nereli ve kaç yaşında? | newspaper = [[Sözcü]] | accessdate = 10 August 2019}}</ref> He finished primary and high school in Samsun.<ref name="AZ">{{cite news | url = http://www.sabah.com.tr/Gunaydin/Magazin/2013/04/28/adan-zye-sagopa-kajmer | title = A'dan Z'ye Sagopa Kajmer | newspaper = [[Sabah (newspaper)|Sabah]] | accessdate = 28 September 2016 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160413114226/http://www.sabah.com.tr/gunaydin/magazin/2013/04/28/adan-zye-sagopa-kajmer | archivedate = 13 April 2016}}</ref> In his own words, his interest in music rose after listening to African music by his mother, and Italian music by his father.<ref name="Zirvedekiler">{{cite web | url = https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tFCjx098yfM | title = Sagopa Kajmer Zirvedekiler TRT 1 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160401191511/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tFCjx098yfM | archivedate = 1 April 2016}}</ref><ref name="Hurriyet">{{cite web | url = http://melankolia.com.tr/sagopa-kajmer-hurriyet-gazetesi-2005-2.html | title = Sagopa Kajmer Hürriyet Gazetesi 2005 | work = [[Hürriyet]] | publisher = [[Melankolia Müzik|melankolia.com.tr]] | accessdate = 3 July 2016 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131015085619/http://melankolia.com.tr/sagopa-kajmer-hurriyet-gazetesi-2005-2.html | archivedate = 15 October 2013}}</ref> He started his music career by working as a DJ in one of Samsun's local radio stations. During that period, he used the name Dj Rapper M.C. (Dj Rapper Mic Check).<ref name="TTNet">{{cite web|url=http://www.ttnetmuzik.com.tr/artist/Sagopa_Kajmer/51010/biyografi |title=Sagopa Kajmer / TTNET Müzik |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130405075650/http://www.ttnetmuzik.com.tr/artist/Sagopa_Kajmer/51010/biyografi |archivedate=5 April 2013}}</ref> In 1997, he moved to Istanbul to continue his education and studied [[Persian language]] and literature at [[Istanbul University]].<ref name="TTNet" /><ref name="melankolia">{{cite web | url = http://melankolia.com.tr/diskografi.html | title= Sagopa Kajmer Diskografi  | publisher = [[Melankolia Müzik|melankolia.com.tr]] | accessdate = 25 June 2016 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160415063244/http://melankolia.com.tr/diskografi.html | archivedate = 15 April 2016}}</ref> He graduated 4 years later.<ref name="Zirvedekiler"/>

In 1998, he founded the rap group Kuvvetmira.<ref name="TTNet"/> In 1999, he featured in the compilation album ''Yeraltı Operasyonu'' as Silahsız Kuvvet.<ref>{{cite news | author = Ali Mert Alan | date = 14 December 2013 | url = http://www.gazetevatan.com/rap-in-kalbi-onunla-atiyor-591909-kultur-sanat/ | title = Rap'in kalbi onunla atıyor | newspaper = Vatan | accessdate = 24 December 2017 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20170225173510/http://www.gazetevatan.com/rap-in-kalbi-onunla-atiyor-591909-kultur-sanat/ | archivedate = 25 February 2017}}</ref> At the same time, he released another album, ''Gerilim 99 (Promo)''. In the same month, he released his first [[Extended play|EP]] ''Pesimist EP 1''.<ref name="melankolia2">{{cite web | url = http://www.melankolia.com.tr/sanatcilar_sagopa_biyografi.html | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20070318061908/http://www.melankolia.com.tr/sanatcilar_sagopa_biyografi.html | url-status = dead | archive-date = 18 March 2007 | title = Sagopa Kajmer | publisher = melankolia.com.tr | accessdate = 5 October 2016 }}</ref> In 2000, together with [[Ceza]], he released the album ''Toplama Kampı''. One year later, he released another album ''Sözlerim Silahım''.<ref>{{cite web | url = http://melankolia.com.tr/sagopa-kajmer-go-girl-roportaji-2009.html | title = Sagopa Kajmer Go Girl Röportajı 2009 | work = Go Girl | publisher = melankolia.com.tr | accessdate = 5 October 2016 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160828042222/http://melankolia.com.tr/sagopa-kajmer-go-girl-roportaji-2009.html | archivedate = 28 August 2016}}</ref> At the same time period he also released ''One Second''. In 2002, he released the studio album ''İhtiyar Heyeti'',<ref name="melankolia"/> and produced [[Ceza]]'s album ''Med Cezir''.<ref name="TTNet"/> At the same, he released the album ''Sagopa Kajmer'', which was followed by ''On Kurşun''.<ref name="TTNet"/>

===2003–2009: ''Bir Pesimistin Gözyaşları'', ''Romantizma'', ''İkimizi Anlatan Bir Şey'' and ''Kötü İnsanları Tanıma Senesi'' ===
In 2004, he gave an interview to ''[[Radikal]]'' in which he said that after 7 years of using the stage name Silahsız Kuvvet, he would use the stage name Sagopa Kajmer from now on.<ref name="Radikal">{{cite web | url = http://www.radikal.com.tr/haber.php?haberno=138568 | title = Mutfakta Sagopa Kajmer... | publisher = [[Radikal]] | accessdate = 3 July 2016 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20041228094114/http://www.radikal.com.tr:80/haber.php?haberno=138568 | archivedate = 28 December 2004}}</ref> In the same year, he produced the album ''Rapstar'' and appeared alongside Ceza on the music video for "Neyim Var Ki".<ref>{{cite web | url = http://beyazgazete.com/video/webtv/muzik-32/neyim-var-ki-ceza-4710.html | title = Neyim Var Ki | publisher = Beyaz Gazete | accessdate = 4 June 2018 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20180614164027/http://beyazgazete.com/video/webtv/muzik-32/neyim-var-ki-ceza-4710.html | archivedate = 14 June 2018}}</ref> In June 2003, his new album, ''Bir Pesimistin Gözyaşları'', was released by Hammer Müzik<ref>{{cite web | title = Sagopa Kajmer / Türk Telekom Müzik | url = https://www.turktelekommuzik.com/artist/Sagopa_Kajmer/550250/albumler#/album-Bir_Pesimistin_Gozyaslari-358117 | accessdate = 29 June 2017 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20170709142754/https://www.turktelekommuzik.com/artist/Sagopa_Kajmer/550250/albumler | archivedate = 9 July 2017}}</ref> and turned the songs "Maskeli Balo" and "Karikatür Komedya" from the album into music videos. In 2004, he produced Dr. Fuchs's album ''Huzur N Darem''.<ref>{{cite web | title = Fuchs* – Huzur N Darem (CD) at Discogs | url = https://www.discogs.com/Fuchs-Huzur-N-Darem/release/4834128 | accessdate = 29 June 2017 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20161124190135/https://www.discogs.com/Fuchs-Huzur-N-Darem/release/4834128 | archivedate = 24 November 2016}}</ref> Later, he made the music for [[Cem Yılmaz]]'s comedy film ''[[G.O.R.A.]]''.<ref name="Radikal"/>

On 19 August 2005, he released the album ''Romantizma'' under the label İrem Records<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.discogs.com/Sagopa-Kajmer-Romantizma/release/1260364 | title = Sagopa Kajmer - Romantizma (CD, Album) at Discogs | publisher = [[Discogs]] | accessdate = 26 June 2016 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150227230429/http://www.discogs.com:80/Sagopa-Kajmer-Romantizma/release/1260364 | archivedate = 27 February 2015}}</ref> and later turned the song "Vasiyet" into a music video. The music video for "Vasiyet" won the Best Video Clip award at the 12th [[Turkey Music Awards|Kral TV Video Music Awards]] in 2005.<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VN8MQgPtYQU | title = 2005 Yılı En İyi Klip - Kral TV Video Müzik Ödülleri | accessdate = 12 March 2017 }}</ref> In the same year, he released ''Pesimist EP 3''. To produce his own albums and to open doors for new opportunities, on 11 August 2005 Kajmer opened Melankolia Müzik together with Kolera.<ref>{{cite web | url = http://melankolia.com.tr/melankolia-muzik-diskografi | title = Diskografi | publisher = [[Melankolia Müzik|melankolia.com.tr]] | accessdate = 26 June 2016 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160414130933/http://melankolia.com.tr/melankolia-muzik-diskografi | archivedate = 14 April 2016}}</ref>



Together with other members of Kuvvetmira, Sagopa Kajmer released the album ''Kafile'' on 1 January 2006 under the label Melankolia Müzik.<ref>{{cite web | title = Sagopa Kajmer - Kafile (CD) at Discogs }}</ref>

On 26 Apriln 2007, he released his first mutual album with Kolera, ''İkimizi Anlatan Bir Şey''. In 2008, his solo album ''Kötü İnsanları Tanıma Senesi'' was released alongside two music videos for the songs "Ben Hüsrana Komşuyum" and "Düşersem Yanarım". On 6 May 2008, he released the single "Bu Şarkıyı Zevk İçin Yaptık", which featured Kolera.<ref>{{cite web | url = http://melankolia.com.tr/selamu-aleykum.html | title = Selamu aleykum | publisher = melankolia.com.tr | accessdate = 9 October 2016 }}</ref> On 27 December 2008, he released ''Pesimist EP 5 - Kör Cerrah'' on his official website.<ref>{{cite web | url = http://melankolia.com.tr/bu-gece-ep-zamani.html | title = Bu Gece EP Zamanı! | publisher = Melankolia Müzik | accessdate = 2 October 2016 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160413175720/http://melankolia.com.tr/bu-gece-ep-zamani.html | archivedate = 13 April 2016}}</ref>

In 2009, he released the songs from his previous EPs in a compilation album, titled ''Şarkı Koleksiyoncusu'', on 19 February.<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.deezer.com/album/9827398 | title = Sagopa Kajmer: Şarkı Koleksiyoncusu - Music Streaming - Listen on Deezer | publisher = [[Deezer]] | accessdate = 1 February 2018 }}</ref> On 1 April 2009, he released the single "Beslenme Çantam" as a duet with Kolera.<ref>{{cite web | url = http://melankolia.com.tr/sagopa-kajmerkoleradan-yeni-sarki-beslenme-cantam.html | title = Sagopa Kajmer&Kolera’dan Yeni şarkı "BESLENME ÇANTAM" | publisher = melankolia.com.tr | accessdate = 9 October 2016 }}</ref> On 18 July 2009, he released another single with Kolera, "Hain".<ref>{{cite web | url = http://melankolia.com.tr/sagopa-kajmer-feat-kolera-hain.html | title = Sagopa Kajmer Feat Kolera "Hain" | publisher = melankolia.com.tr | accessdate = 9 October 2016 }}</ref>

== Artistry ==
===Music style===
Sagopa Kajmer is a rap music artist. He started his career under the name Silahsız Kuvvet and later used elements of Anatolian melodies and [[türkü]]s in his songs.<ref name="Radikal"/> Although he initially used curse words in the album ''Sagopa Kajmer'', he later refrained from using them later in his career.<ref name="Hurriyet"/> He also performed his songs in a tone different than his actual voice to not become famous.<ref name="Zirvedekiler"/><ref name="Zaman">{{cite web | url = http://melankolia.com.tr/sagopa-kajmer-zaman-gazetesi-roportaji-2.html | title = Sagopa Kajmer Zaman Gazetesi Röportajı | work = [[Zaman (newspaper)|Zaman]] | publisher = melankolia.com.tr | accessdate = 9 October 2016 }}</ref> His voice is thought to be of [[Arabesque (Turkish music)|Arabesque]] style.<ref name="Radikal"/><ref name="Fuat">{{cite web | url = https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5ho7aErvLYE | title = Fuat'dan Sagopa Kajmer hakkında açıklamalar @ Flash Tv(Velet DADA) [20.02.2010<nowiki>]</nowiki> | publisher = [[YouTube]] | accessdate = 2 October 2016 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20161008185708/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5ho7aErvLYE | archivedate = 8 October 2016}}</ref> In his 2003 album, ''Romantizma'', there are far more love themes than his previous works.<ref name="Hurriyet"/><ref name="Radikal"/>

===Influences===
[[Run-DMC]]'s music was influential in forming Kajmer's rap music.<ref name="Hurriyet"/> He said that he was influenced by the works of [[Omar Khayyam]], [[Ferdowsi]] and [[Rumi]].<ref name="Hurriyet"/> In an interview with ''Pembe Newspaper'', he said: "When I was little I thought I was Jam Master Jay from Run-DMC. [[LL Cool J]] and Fat Boys are also very important names. I got my influence from them."<ref>{{cite web | url = http://melankolia.com.tr/sagopa-kajmer-pembe-gazete-roportaji-2009.html | title = Sagopa Kajmer Pembe Gazete Röportajı 2009 | work = Pembe Newspaper | publisher = melankolia.com.tr | accessdate = 5 October 2016 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20161006062246/http://melankolia.com.tr/sagopa-kajmer-pembe-gazete-roportaji-2009.html | archivedate = 6 October 2016}}</ref> The artist's style change and beard gave rise to the rumors that he was influenced by the philosophy of [[Ahmet Mahmut Ünlü]].<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.haber7.com/yasam/haber/425752-cubbeli-hoca-sagopayi-nasil-etkiledi-font-colorff0000videofont | title = Cübbeli Hoca, Sagopa'yı nasıl etkiledi | publisher = [[haber7.com]] | accessdate = 5 October 2016 }}</ref> The artist, however, firmly denied these allegations and said that it was only after reading a [[hadith]] that he decided to let his beard grow.<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=abbBblHo08I | title = Sagopa Kajmer & Kolera - Aksama Dogru TVNET | publisher = YouTube | accessdate = 5 October 2016 }}</ref>

== Meaning of Sagopa Kajmer ==
Yunus Özyavuz explains the meaning of Sagopa Kajmer with these words:

{{quote|It's an ancient pyramid in Egypt... and the one who's looking for it is Kajmer. So, the one who's trying to look for something or solve it is trying to find his Sagopa's like Kajmer... one who solves his issues.<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0Y8OpR1-tUw | title = Sagopa Kajmer Canli Röportaj - TRT2 (Part 1) | publisher = [[YouTube]] | accessdate = 3 October 2016 }}</ref>}}

On [[Beyazıt Öztürk]] and [[Meral Okay]]'s program ''Nasıl Yani'' he said:

{{quote|A scientist doing research in pyramids in Egypt... all the coordination inside is written in hieroglyph, and then he loses his life during his search. Scientists who came after him discovered that the man who originally found the pyramid, as they read from the papyrus, was a man named Kajmer who discovered the Pyramid of Sagopa. I read this article in my college years.<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eysumMY4Zhk | title = Sagopa Kajmer Nasıl Yani | publisher = YouTube | accessdate = 4 October 2016 }}</ref>}}

On 17 November 2015, in response to Mesut Yar's question "What does it mean?" he said:

{{quote|Mystery ... There is also a tree named Sago in Thailand. A nice palm type. Kajmer means child. Troubled child... This is also of a bit of eastern origin in [[Avesta]].<ref>{{cite episode |title= Burada Laf Çok - 17.11.2015 |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J7PcnvOi0AU |accessdate= 3 October 2016 |series= Burada Laf Çok | publisher= [[CNN Türk]] |date= 17 November 2015 |time= 38:33}}</ref>}}

== Personal life ==
His mother is Serpil Özyavuz, and his father is Mehmet Özyavuz.<ref name="melankolia2"/> He also has a brother named Emre Özyavuz.<ref name="Esquire">{{cite web | url = http://melankolia.com.tr/esquire-dergisigorunce-mutlu-oldugunuz-50-insan.html | title = Esquire Dergisi/Görünce Mutlu Olduğunuz 50 İnsan | work = [[Esquire (magazine)|Esquire]] | publisher = melankolia.com.tr | accessdate = 3 October 2016 }}</ref> He is a graduate of Persian language and literature from [[Istanbul University]].<ref name="melankolia"/> On 1 August 2006, he married fellow rapper Esen Güler (known as Kolera).<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.facebook.com/KoleraRapResmi/photos/a.344957598933908.79525.126212584141745/1056490364447291/ | title = Kolera - Zaman Tüneli | publisher = Facebook | accessdate = 5 October 2016 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20161008185600/https://www.facebook.com/KoleraRapResmi/photos/a.344957598933908.79525.126212584141745/1056490364447291/ | archivedate = 8 October 2016}}</ref> On 20 November 2017, they announced on their Instagram accounts that they had divorced after 11 years of marriage.<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.ntv.com.tr/yasam/sagopa-kajmer-ve-kolera-bosandi,NK6SmQyMVkWWcNDSBysR_g | title = Sagopa Kajmer ve Kolera boşandı | accessdate = 3 December 2017 }}</ref> In May 2018, in response to one of his followers on social media who asked "what is your religious belief" he said "I have no religious beliefs".<ref>{{cite news|title=Sagopa Kajmer 'dini inancım yok' dedi!|url=http://milliyet.com.tr/sagopa-kajmer-dini-inancim-yok--magazin-2677827/|accessdate=11 June 2018|newspaper=[[Milliyet]]|date=28 May 2018}}</ref>

== Awards and nominations ==
In 2005, at [[Kral TV Video Music Awards]], the music video for his song "Vasiyet" was awarded for the best music video. He was also nominated as the Best Turkish Act at the [[2008 MTV Europe Music Awards]].<ref>{{cite news | url = http://www.hurriyet.com.tr/liverpoola-kim-gidecek-10073501 | title = Liverpool'a kim gidecek | newspaper = [[Hürriyet]] | accessdate = 26 June 2016 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160626130451/http://www.hurriyet.com.tr/liverpoola-kim-gidecek-10073501 | archivedate = 26 June 2016}}</ref> His mutual album with Kolera ''Bendeki Sen'', was nominated as the best album at TRT Music Awards with the votes of people.<ref name="BendekiSen"/>

== Reception ==
[[Fuat Ergin]] has said about Sagopa Kajmer: "He's not a rapper. I'm a rapper, [[Ceza]] is a rapper but he is not ... He's a branch of Arabesque."<ref name="Fuat"/> Erol Köse described Sagopa Kajmer as a "hidden star".<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DRTumw_JNGQ | title = Erol Köse ' Sagopa Kajmer gizli bir starmış; onu anladım..' | publisher = YouTube | accessdate = 4 October 2016 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20161008185715/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DRTumw_JNGQ | archivedate = 8 October 2016}}</ref> ''[[Akşam]]''{{'}}s columnist Çağla Gürsoy said about him: "Sagopa's name appeared as one of the rap geniuses in Turkey from the beginning. I like both his style and his music."<ref>{{cite news | url = http://www.aksam.com.tr/life/bahar-geliyor-sesi-biraz-acin/haber-503723 | title = Bahar geliyor sesi biraz açın! | newspaper = [[Akşam]] | accessdate = 2 October 2016 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20161003073056/http://www.aksam.com.tr/life/bahar-geliyor-sesi-biraz-acin/haber-503723 | archivedate = 3 October 2016}}</ref> For the list of "50 People That Make You Happy", ''[[Esquire (magazine)|Esquire]]'' magazine described Sagopa Kajmer as a person who "brought a new breath to the rap music culture in Turkey."<ref name="Esquire"/>
