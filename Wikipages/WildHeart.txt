'''WildHeart''' (stylized as '''•WildHeart•''') is an American [[rock (music)|rock]] band from [[Atlanta, Georgia]]. Formed in 2018, the band currently consists of lead vocalist Teddy Swims, lead guitarist Kevin Hanson, rhythm guitarist Addy Maxwell, bass guitarist Turner Wood, and drummer Michael Bohn. The group released their debut single, "Lonely", in March 2019, and later released two more singles, "Animal" and "Seeing It Through", an extended play (EP), was scheduled to be released in late 2019 however as of 2020 the EP hasn't been released and the band has been inactive.

==Career==
In October 2018, former [[Issues (band)|Issues]] vocalist Michael Bohn announced on [[Instagram]] that he had been working on a musical project "for the past 10 months" with three other musicians.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.instagram.com/p/BpDjz5ylps1/|title=Michael Bohn on Instagram: "For the past 10 months I have been working from the ground up on a new project! It's been insanely tough and at times I've wanted to just…"|author=|date=|website=Instagram|accessdate=10 June 2019}}</ref> On November 26, 2018, the band announced its name, line-up, and formation, which includes former [[Woe, Is Me]] guitarist Kevin Hanson, lead vocalist Teddy Swims, guitarist Addy Maxwell, and bass guitarist Turner Wood.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/WildheartUS/status/1067260760294924291|title=We are Wildheart. A band made up of black sheep. Young and naive on a train chasin dreams burnin nothing but plans. We burst from the seams with our minds fixed on things that we know we can't have.|last=Wildheart|date=26 November 2018|website=twitter.com|accessdate=10 June 2019}}</ref> In a February 2019 interview with ''[[Rock Sound]]'', Bohn described the group as "at times almost like if [[Fall Out Boy]] went a bit modern [[country music|country]]."<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rocksound.tv/news/read/michael-bohn-wildheart-issues|title=Former ISSUES Vocalist Michael Bohn Has A New Band, Introducing: WildHeart - News|author=|date=|website=Rock Sound Magazine|accessdate=10 June 2019}}</ref>

Their debut single, "Lonely", was released on March 1, 2019.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://music.apple.com/us/album/lonely-single/1453347546|title=Lonely - Single by WildHeart|author=|date=March 2019|publisher=|accessdate=10 June 2019|via=music.apple.com}}</ref> A second single, "Animal", was released on April 19.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://music.apple.com/us/album/animal-single/1459466659|title=Animal - Single by WildHeart|author=|date=19 April 2019|publisher=|accessdate=10 June 2019|via=music.apple.com}}</ref> The band released its third single, "Seeing It Through", on June 7.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://music.apple.com/us/album/seeing-it-through-single/1464624732|title=Seeing It Through - Single by WildHeart|author=|date=7 June 2019|publisher=|accessdate=10 June 2019|via=music.apple.com}}</ref>

===Teddy Swims===
[[File:Teddy Swims (VOA).png|thumb|Teddy Swims on [[Voice of America]]'s Border Crossings TV in June 2021.]]'''Jaten Dimsdale''', an American singer songwriter known by his moniker '''Teddy Swims''', was the lead singer of WildHeart and now a solo independent singer. In 2019, with the break-up of the band, Dimsdale began the SWIMS Initiative, SWIMS being an acronym for "Someone Who Isn't Me Sometimes", in reference to his struggle to integrate the different parts of himself.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hip-hopvibe.com/2020/08/29/hhv-exclusive-teddy-swims-talks-exposing-the-world-to-something-different-as-he-dominates-social-media-with-his-sultry-soulful-voice-talks-about-making-music-for-emotional-support-for-the-people/|title=HHV EXCLUSIVE: Teddy Swims talks exposing the world to something different as he dominates social media with his sultry soulful voice + talks about making music for emotional support for the people|date=20 July 2021}}</ref> 

TeddySwims began to share covers on [[YouTube]], covering artists such as [[George Strait]], [[Marvin Gaye]], [[Amy Winehouse]], [[Michael Jackson]], [[Lewis Capaldi]] and [[Shania Twain]] amongst many others. According to Swims, "selections are based off of people requesting what they want to hear," in addition to personal favorites, leading him to cover "Rivers" by [[Six 60]] from New Zealand, where Swims has a significant following.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://hypeofflife.com/teddy-swims-interview-2020/amp/|title=Teddy Swims on Breaking Barriers & Uniting People Through His Music » Hype off Life|date=28 August 2020}}</ref> Swims quickly gained a big following on YouTube as a result of the release of his cover of "[[Rock With You]]" by [[Michael Jackson]] in June 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://hypeofflife.com/teddy-swims-interview-2020/amp/|title = Teddy Swims on Breaking Barriers & Uniting People Through His Music » Hype off Life|date = 28 August 2020}}</ref> He was signed to Warner Records later that year. Swims reached one million YouTube subscribers in April 2020. The 2020 release of "[[You're Still the One]]" by [[Shania Twain]], created by Swims in collaboration with [[Dave Cobb]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hip-hopvibe.com/2020/08/29/hhv-exclusive-teddy-swims-talks-exposing-the-world-to-something-different-as-he-dominates-social-media-with-his-sultry-soulful-voice-talks-about-making-music-for-emotional-support-for-the-people/|title = HHV EXCLUSIVE: Teddy Swims talks exposing the world to something different as he dominates social media with his sultry soulful voice + talks about making music for emotional support for the people|date = 30 August 2020}}</ref> was described by Twain as "a beautiful version".<ref>[https://twitter.com/shaniatwain/status/1284223579341627394?lang=en Shania Twain Twitter account]</ref>

In February, 2020, Swims played in sixteen cities in a sold out nationwide tour. Swims was set to do a second sold out tour in the [[United Kingdom]] and [[Ireland]] in May, 2020 but the tour was postponed due to coronavirus. Swims has released several singles, including "Night Off", "Picky" and "Broke", released in August 2020.  He currently has 1.92 million YouTube subscribers [http://mmusicmag.com/m/2020/09/video-exclusive-interview-teddy-swims/]. Swims describes his music as an integration of [[Soul music|soul]], [[funk]], [[rhythm and blues]], [[Country music|country]], [[Gospel music|gospel]] and show-tunes.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://hypeofflife.com/teddy-swims-interview-2020/amp/|title=Teddy Swims on Breaking Barriers & Uniting People Through His Music » Hype off Life|date=28 August 2020}}</ref> For Swims, soul music is at the center of his style and cites his upbringing in the Pentecostal church as a major musical influence.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hip-hopvibe.com/2020/08/29/hhv-exclusive-teddy-swims-talks-exposing-the-world-to-something-different-as-he-dominates-social-media-with-his-sultry-soulful-voice-talks-about-making-music-for-emotional-support-for-the-people/|title = HHV EXCLUSIVE: Teddy Swims talks exposing the world to something different as he dominates social media with his sultry soulful voice + talks about making music for emotional support for the people|date = 30 August 2020}}</ref> According to Swims, the members of his new band, Elevtnts, come from a background playing in [[mega church]]es as musicians but also come from the metal scene.  Swims started singing musical theater in tenth grade. In an interview with [[Robin Young]], Swims said "I fell in love with theater and it changed my entire life".<ref>[https://www.wbur.org/hereandnow/2020/08/31/youtube-sensation-teddy-swims].</ref> Prior to beginning the SWIMS Initiative, Swims played in a variety of bands ranging from show-tunes to funk, metalcore, soul, and alt-country.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://americansongwriter.com/teddy-swims-featured-in-vevo-dscvr-series-with-night-off-and-picky/|title = Teddy Swims Featured in Vevo DSCVR Series with "Night Off" and "Picky"|date = 16 February 2020}}</ref> 

Swims lives outside of [[Atlanta]], [[Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia]] with his band along with his videographer, manager, producer and his brother. When he started the SWIMS Initiative, Swims was working at Chili's and living with his father. Swims was raised in a football family and is the grandson of a [[Pentecostalism|Pentecostalist]] preacher. Swims describes his grandfather as a core influence on his work ethic and sense of morality.<ref>[https://www.wbur.org/hereandnow/2020/08/31/youtube-sensation-teddy-swims].</ref>

His debut EP titled ''Unlearning'' was released on May 21, 2021 followed by ''A Very Teddy Christmas'' on October 15.
