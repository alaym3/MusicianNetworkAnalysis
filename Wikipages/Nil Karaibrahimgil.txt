'''Ferhan<ref>{{cite web  |url=http://sosyal.hurriyet.com.tr/yazar/nil-karaibrahimgil_113/kimsesi-yok-ama-sevgisi-cok_27855279 |title=Kimsesi yok ama sevgisi çok (No One But Love) |publisher=Hürriyet|accessdate=28 April 2016 |date=28 April 2016 |language=tr}}</ref> Nil Karaibrahimgil''' (born 17 October 1976) is a Turkish singer and songwriter, mostly noted for her distinct [[lyrics]]. Nil's passion and admiration for [[music]] stems from her father, Suavi Karaibrahimgil, who is also a musician although he is not active in the [[Music of Turkey|Turkish music]] industry.

==Biography==
In 2000, Karaibrahimgil graduated from [[Boğaziçi University]] with a degree in [[political science]] and [[international relations]]. She also further received the [[Crystal Apple]] prize award twice, which recognized her achievements in [[advertising]]. Besides academic success, Nil has found fortune in the [[Turkish music]] scene. Many of her songs deal with women, freedom, life, [[love]], [[marriage]] and [[Intimate relationship|relationships]]. Her articles have been published weekly in ''[[Hürriyet]]'', a daily Turkish newspaper, since 2004.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.hurriyet.com.tr/yazarlar/nil-karaibrahimgil/|title=Hürriyet Archive|work=HÜRRİYET - ARAMA|access-date=5 April 2018|language=tr}}</ref> On 21 January 2010, she married [[Sertab Erener]]'s brother Serdar Erener near the shore of the Nile River.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.cnnturk.com/2010/yasam/diger/01/21/nil.karaibrahimgil.misirda.evlendi/560308.0/index.html |title=Nil Karaibrahimgil Mısır'da evlendi (Nil Karaibrahimgil married in Egypt) |publisher=CNNTurk.com  |accessdate=21 January 2010 |date=21 January 2010 |language=tr}}</ref> She has a son named Aziz Arif, born in May 2014.<ref>{{cite web|title=Nil Karaibrahimgil annelik duygularını paylaştı (Nil Karaibrahimgil Discusses Motherhood)|url=http://www.ankarahaber.com/haber/Nil-Karaibrahimgil-annelik-duygular%C4%B1n%C4%B1-paylast%C4%B1/159103|publisher=ankarahaber.com|accessdate=27 May 2014|language=tr|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140527230048/http://www.ankarahaber.com/haber/Nil-Karaibrahimgil-annelik-duygular%C4%B1n%C4%B1-paylast%C4%B1/159103#|archive-date=27 May 2014|url-status=dead}}</ref>
