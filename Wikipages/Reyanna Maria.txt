Reyanna Maria Cuevas é uma cantora e compositora filipina que mora em Melbourne, Austrália.Nascida em 3 de junho de 2001.

== Career ==
After covering some of her favorite artists for some time and gaining a large following on [[TikTok]], Maria shared her first track ''So Pretty'' in 2021 for a TikTok duet challenge. The sound clip eventually caught major traction on the platform, garnering more than 20 million views with over a million users using the clip for the challenge, including the likes of [[James Charles (Internet personality)|James Charles]], [[Alicia Keys]], and [[Chrissy Teigen]]. Rapper [[Tyga]] sent the clip to [[Steven Victor]] who signed Reyanna to [[Victor Victor Worldwide]], whose parent company is [[Universal Music Group]]. Maria later released the remix of her track featuring Tyga.
