'''AnnenMayKantereit''' (occasionally shortened ''AMK'') is a [[Germany|German]] [[Rock music|rock]] band founded in [[Cologne]] in 2011. Their songs are predominantly performed in [[German language|German]], but the band also occasionally publish cover songs in English. A notable feature of the band's music is the distinctly rough voice<ref>{{cite web |url = http://www.mainpost.de/ueberregional/kulturwelt/kultur/In-Wuerzburg-erleben-AnnenMayKantereit-ihr-groesstes-Tour-Konzert;art3809,8938899 |title=In Würzburg erleben AnnenMayKantereit ihr größtes Tour-Konzert |author=Catharina Hettiger |work=MainPost.de |date=2015-10-04 |access-date=2015-10-06}}</ref> of the singer {{Interlanguage link|Henning May|de}}.

== History ==
[[File:Annenmaykantereit_salzburg_2015_1.jpg|thumb|Henning May (2015)]]
The three founding members, whose joint surnames make up the band name, formed a band while at school at the Schiller-Gymnasium in [[Sülz]] in 2011.<ref>{{cite web |url = http://www.lizzynet.de/wws/annenmaykantereit.php |title=LizzyNet.de – Draußen Musik machen |author=Alina |work=LizzyNet |date=2014-02-01 |access-date=2014-12-22}}</ref> Initially, these three, Annen, May and Kantereit, performed as street musicians in Cologne. At a jam session, they were joined by double bass player Lars Lötgering.<ref>Julia Lührs, Elif Senel: [https://web.archive.org/web/20141222131945/http://www.wdr5.de/sendungen/neugiergenuegt/redezeit/annenmaykantereit104.html ''Redezeit mit „Annen May Kantereit“ – WDR 5.'']</ref> In 2013, they released their first, self-recorded album, which had a release party in Gebäude 9 in Cologne.<ref>{{cite web |url = http://www.ksta.de/klangprobe/annenmaykantereit-freundschaft-auf-musikalisch,16375040,25954344.html |title=AnnenMayKantereit Freundschaft auf musikalisch |author=Kölner Stadt Anzeiger |work=LizzyNet |date=2014-01-22 |access-date=2014-12-22}}</ref> The album, which was partly recorded on the street, is no longer available.<ref name="welt.de2015">{{cite web |url = https://www.welt.de/regionales/nrw/article147144312/Junge-Band-aus-Koeln-ueberrollt-den-Musikmarkt.html |title=Junge Band aus Köln überrollt den Musikmarkt |author=Marcus Bäcker |work=Welt.de |date=2015-10-03 |access-date=2015-10-06}}</ref> They have their own YouTube channel on which they have published their songs.<ref>{{cite web |url = http://www.sueddeutsche.de/kultur/pop-band-annenmaykantereit-liebeskummer-fuer-die-generation-tinder-1.2620995 |title=Liebeskummer für die Generation Tinder |author=Josa Mania-Schlegel |work=Süddeutsche.de |date=2015-08-26 |access-date=2015-10-06}}</ref> Malte Huck has been the bassist of the band since August 2014 but has since left the group in 2020.<ref>{{cite web |url = http://www.br.de/puls/tv/startrampe/bands/annenmaykantereit-steckbrief-startrampe-staffel-18-100.html |title=Eine Band wie ein Zuhause – BR |author=Malte Bormann |work=BR |date=2015-01-09 |access-date=2015-02-12}}</ref>

After the release of the music video of their song ''Wohin du gehst'', the band toured Germany. In summer 2014, they played at several big festivals, such as the Appletree Garden Festival, the Open Flair, and the Reeperbahn Festival. In autumn, they played at several concerts as the supporting act for the [[Beatsteaks]]<ref>{{cite web |url = http://www.rollingstone.de/live/konzertreviews/article640956/annenmaykantereit-live-im-berliner-lido-mit-jeder-einzelnen-faser-der-halsschlagader.html |title=AnnenMayKantereit live im Berliner Lido: Mit jeder einzelnen Faser der Halsschlagader |author=Jan Felix Walther |work=Rolling Stone Magazin |date=2014-10-01 |access-date=2014-12-22}}</ref> and accompanied [[Clueso]] on the ''Stadtrandlichter Tour'' as a support act.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.fourartists.com/news/clueso-gibt-annenmaykantereit-als-special-guest-fuer-stadtrandlichter-tour-bekannt.html |title=Clueso gibt AnnenMayKantereit als Special Guest für Stadtrandlichter Tour bekannt |work=FourArtists |date=2014-10-31 |access-date=2014-12-22 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170801081126/http://www.fourartists.com/news/clueso-gibt-annenmaykantereit-als-special-guest-fuer-stadtrandlichter-tour-bekannt.html |archive-date=2017-08-01 |url-status=dead }}</ref> They were finalists in the New Music Award of the ARD-Jugendprogramme.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sputnik.de/new-music-award/finalisten/im-finale-annenmaykantereit |title=SPUTNIK: Im Finale 2014: AnnenMayKantereit |work=MDR Sputnik |date=2014-07-09 |access-date=2014-12-22 |url-status = dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141222135109/http://www.sputnik.de/new-music-award/finalisten/im-finale-annenmaykantereit |archivedate=2014-12-22 |df= }}</ref>

In December 2014, AnnenMayKantereit were featured in the television programme ''Circus HalliGalli'',<ref>{{cite web |url = http://www.prosieben.de/tv/circus-halligalli/videos/414-annenmaykantereit-oft-gefragt-clip |title=CIRCUS HALLIGALLI – Video – AnnenMayKantereit – Oft gefragt – ProSieben |work=ProSieben |date=2014-12-08 |access-date=2014-12-22}}</ref> and at the beginning of 2015, they went on tour again. In summer 2015 they performed at more festivals, including the [[Haldern Pop|Haldern Pop Festival]] and Rock am Ring. The [[Hip hop music|Hip-Hop]] group [[K.I.Z]] featured singer Henning May in their song ''Hurra die Welt geht unter''.

In autumn 2015, the band signed with [[Universal Music Group|Universal]]. Together with [[Moses Schneider]] they recorded the EP ''Wird schon irgendwie gehen'' at [[Hansa Tonstudio|Hansa-Studios]] in [[Berlin]]. It was released on 16 October, 2015, by the label ''Vertigo Berlin''.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.pledgemusic.com/projects/amk |title=AnnenMayKantereit: Unsere neue EP auf PledgeMusic |author=PledgeMusic |date=2014-12-16 |access-date=2014-12-22 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170323012934/http://www.pledgemusic.com/projects/amk |archive-date=2017-03-23 |url-status=dead }}</ref> Subsequently they had a new tour and sold out numerous venues.<ref name="welt.de2015"/>

In February 2016 they released the single Pocahontas from their upcoming album "Alles Nix Konkretes". The whole album was released in March 2016 and reached number one on the German album charts.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.officialcharts.de/charts.asp?cat=a&country=de&year=2016&date=20160325 |title=Top 100 Album Charts |language=German |publisher=charts.de |date=25 March 2016 |accessdate=30 March 2016}}</ref>

On December 7, 2018, the band released their new album "Schlagschatten", produced by Markus Ganter, recorded in a small village in Spain.<ref>[https://www.offiziellecharts.de/album-details-373549 Official german charts: Schlagschatten]</ref>

On January 8, 2019 [[BMG Rights Management]] announced that they have concluded a worldwide publishing contract with the band <ref>[https://www.bmg.com/de/news/annenmaykantereit-sign-publishing-deal-with-bmg.html Annenmaykantereit sign publishing-deal with BMG]</ref> While not touring in 2018 the band started a new tour at the end of January 2019 in Mannheim (Club Tour).<ref>[https://www.instagram.com/p/BuD8eTTnPjK/ Instagram photo from Malte Huck of the club tour]</ref> In March 2019 they started a big hall tour.<ref>[https://annenmaykantereit.com/#live Dates of the 2019 tour on the website of the band]</ref>

Due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic]], the band was forced to cancel all planned concerts starting from March 2020. <ref>{{Cite web|title=AnnenMayKantereit|url=https://landstreicher-booking.de/artists/annenmaykantereit|access-date=2021-02-16|website=Landstreicher Booking|language=de}}</ref> In September 2020, AnnenMayKantereit participated at the mass demonstration [[School strike for climate|''Fridays for Future'']] in Berlin<ref>{{Cite web|title=Fridays for Future kündigt Großdemo in Berlin an|url=https://www.tagesspiegel.de/berlin/10-000-aktivisten-vor-dem-brandenburger-tor-fridays-for-future-kuendigt-grossdemo-in-berlin-an/26212616.html|access-date=2021-02-16|website=www.tagesspiegel.de|language=de}}</ref> where they announced that Malte Huck was leaving the band.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Kölner Stadt-Anzeiger - Aktuelle Nachrichten aus Köln und der ganzen Welt|url=https://www.ksta.de/html/dumont-consent/index.html?param=eyJyZWRpcmVjdFVybCI6Ii9rdWx0dXIvZ3Jvc3NkZW1vLWluLWJlcmxpbi1kZXNoYWxiLXNwaWVsZW4tLWFubmVubWF5a2FudGVyZWl0LS1iZWkta2xpbWEtZGVtby1udXItenUtZHJpdHQtMzczOTM5Mzg/Y2I9MTYxMzQ2NzczNjMwOCIsInJlZmVycmVyIjoiaHR0cHM6Ly9kZS53aWtpcGVkaWEub3JnLyJ9%20https://www.instagram.com/p/CFhupC7F6WD/|access-date=2021-02-16|website=www.ksta.de}}</ref> In November 2020, the band surprise released their third album ''12'' without prior announcement. <ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-11-16|title=AnnenMayKantereit: Überraschungsalbum um Mitternacht|url=https://kulturnews.de/annenmaykantereit-ueberraschungsalbum-um-mitternacht/|access-date=2021-02-16|website=kulturnews.de|language=de-DE}}</ref>
