'''Carl Garsbo''' (born 12 October 1995), better known by his stage name '''Kasbo''', is an electronic [[record producer]] and [[Disc jockey|DJ]], originating from [[Gothenburg]], [[Sweden]]. Starting his music career in 2013, he released multiple songs independently and through labels such as [[Monstercat]], Foreign Family Collective and [[bitbird]].

In 2017, he released the singles "Lay It on Me", "Bleed It Out" and "Snow In Gothenburg". When the fourth single, "Aldrig Mer" was released, Kasbo announced his debut album, ''[[Places We Don't Know]]'', released on 23 March 2018 through Counter Records and Foreign Family Collective.

==Career==

=== 2013 — 2016: Early career ===
Kasbo started in November 2013, with a collaboration with Just a Gent on "Sovereign".<ref name=":0" /> His debut song, "Reaching", dropped in May 2014 through Slimkat Records and [[UKF Music|UKF]].<ref name=":1" /> The song, "Kaleidoscope", was released in September as his debut through [[Monstercat]], along with "Again" in August.<ref name="kaleidoscope">{{cite web|url=http://www.youredm.com/2014/09/10/kasbo-kaleidoscope-monstercat/|website=YourEDM|title= KASBO – KALEIDOSCOPE [MONSTERCAT]|accessdate=2017-12-27}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/6656718/kasbo-premieres-synth-driven-new-single-again-on-monstercat-exclusive|title=Kasbo Premieres Synth-Driven New Single, 'Again,' on Monstercat: Exclusive|last=Medved|first=Matt|date=5 August 2015|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|access-date=5 March 2018}}</ref> That same month, he collaborated with Father Dude for "Time".<ref name=":2" /> Yet, in October, "World Away" was released on [[Odesza|ODESZA]]'s label, Foreign Family Collective.<ref name="worldaway">{{cite web|url=https://www.stereogum.com/1837244/kasbo-world-away-stereogum-premiere/mp3s/|title=Kasbo – "World Away"|website=[[Stereogum]]|accessdate=27 December 2017}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.edmtunes.com/2015/10/kasbo-world-away-odesza/|title=Kasbo – World Away|last=Tran|first=Dennis|date=16 October 2015|work=EDMTunes|access-date=5 March 2018}}</ref>

His debut [[extended play]] (EP), ''Umbrella Club EP'', was self-released on October 7 as a free download.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://dancingastronaut.com/2015/10/kasbo-releases-debut-ep-umbrella-club-free/|title=Kasbo releases his debut EP, ‘Umbrella Club,’ for free|last=Spada|first=Andrew|date=7 October 2015|work=[[Dancing Astronaut]]|access-date=5 March 2018}}</ref>

"Call" was the only song released by him in 2016, within November, featuring vocals from Cheney.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.dancingastronaut.com/2016/11/kasbo-call-feat-cheney-original-mix/|title=Kasbo – Call feat. Cheney (Original Mix)|last=Cooper|first=Michael|date=3 November 2016|work=[[Dancing Astronaut]]|access-date=5 March 2018}}</ref> In December, he was featured on the [[DJ mix|mix]] ''SnowTape Vol. 5'', during the SnowGlobe Festival, releasing "ID" as part of the track listing.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.youredm.com/2016/12/15/kasbo-releases-new-id-first-ever-mix/|title=Kasbo Releases New ID in First Ever Mix|date=15 December 2016|work=Your EDM|access-date=5 March 2018}}</ref>

=== 2017 — present: Recognition and ''Places We Don't Know'' ===
As an artist, Kasbo started to gain recognition in 2017. In April, featuring guest vocals from [[Chelsea Cutler]], "Found You" was released on [[San Holo]]'s [[bitbird]] and later premiered on ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]''.<ref name="foundyou">{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/7751388/kasbo-chelsea-cutler-found-you|website=Billboard |title= Kasbo & Chelsea Cutler Are Adorably Radiant on 'Found You': Premiere|accessdate=2017-12-27}}</ref> In 28 June, the [[Keiynan Lonsdale]]-featured song "Lay It on Me" premiered on ''Billboard'' as the [[lead single]] from his then-unannounced debut album.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/7849285/kasbo-kid-flash-keiynan-lonsdale-lay-it-on-me|title=Kasbo & 'Kid Flash' Keiynan Lonsdale 'Lay It On Me' With Perfect Summer Romance: Premiere|last=Bein|first=Kat|date=28 May 2017|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|access-date=5 March 2018}}</ref> The second single, "Bleed It Out", featured Nea and premiered on 24 October on ''EDM Sauce''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.edmsauce.com/2017/10/24/kasbo-bleed-it-out-feat-nea-edm-sauce-premiere/|title=Kasbo - Bleed It Out (feat. Nea) [EDM Sauce Premiere]|date=24 October 2017|website=EDM Sauce|access-date=5 March 2018}}</ref>

From October to November 2017, he was one of the openings act for several shows on ODESZA's "A Moment Apart Tour", promoting their [[A Moment Apart|third studio album of the same name]], along with artists like [[Sofi Tukker]], [[Hayden James]], Chet Porter and Louis Futon.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dancingastronaut.com/2017/10/illenium-joins-odesza-sofi-tukker-kasbo-special-halloween-show/|website=Dancing Astronaut |title= Illenium joins Odesza, Sofi Tukker and Kasbo for special Halloween show|accessdate=2017-12-27}}</ref> His remix of "Indian Summer" by [[Jai Wolf]] was included in the ''Kindred Spirits Remixes'' [[extended play]] (EP), released simultaneously in November.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.dancingastronaut.com/2017/11/kasbo-delights-rework-jai-wolfs-indian-summer-apart-kindred-spirits-remixes-ep/|title=Kasbo delights on rework of Jai Wolf’s ‘Indian Summer,’ apart of the ‘Kindred Spirits Remixes’ EP|last=Fleisher|first=Grace|date=18 November 2017|work=[[Dancing Astronaut]]|access-date=5 March 2018}}</ref> "Snow In Gothenburg", the third single, released on 12 December.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.edmsauce.com/2017/12/12/cuddle-up-with-kasbo/|title=Cuddel Up for Warmth to 'Snow In Gothenburg' by Kasbo|date=12 December 2017|website=EDM Sauce|access-date=5 March 2018}}</ref>

On January 18, Kasbo released the fourth single "Aldrig Mer", featuring the British musical duo Tender, and announced his debut album ''[[Places We Don't Know]]'', which released on 23 March.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.thefader.com/2018/01/18/kasbo-aldrig-mer-places-we-dont-know|title=Listen to this Kasbo song if you don’t know whether to dance or cry|last=Myers|first=Owen|date=18 January 2018|work=[[The Fader]]|access-date=5 March 2018}}</ref> The fifth single, "Your Tempo", was released on 13 February and the sixth single, "Over You" was released on 13 March.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/8099513/kasbo-your-tempo|title=Kasbo's Single 'Your Tempo' Is a Dreamy Breath of Fresh Air|last=Hussein|first=Wandera|date=13 February 2018|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|access-date=5 March 2018}}</ref> In March, he collaborated with [[Shallou]] and Cody Lovaas for the song, "Find".<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.dancingastronaut.com/2018/03/kasbo-shallou-cody-lovaas-effect-midtempo-bliss-new-single-find/|title=Kasbo, Shallou, and Cody Lovaas effect midtempo bliss on new single, ‘Find’|last=Narozniak|first=Rachel|date=1 March 2018|work=[[Dancing Astronaut]]|access-date=5 March 2018}}</ref>

From May 2019 to October 2019, Kasbo had paused music production due to severe hearing loss. The diagnosis for such hearing loss is unclear. On 29 October, Kasbo released a new single, "I Get You", which was in collaboration with artist Lizzy Land.<ref name=":3" /> Kasbo had released a teaser on his official YouTube channel 6 days prior.<ref>{{Citation|title=see you soon|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Op3M-sMv9yQ|language=en|access-date=2019-11-19}}</ref>
