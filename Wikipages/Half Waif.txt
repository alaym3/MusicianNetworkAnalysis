'''Half Waif''' is the stage name of American musician '''Nandi Rose Plunkett'''.

==Early life==
Plunkett was born to an Indian mother from [[Uganda]]. Plunkett grew up in [[Williamstown, Massachusetts]].<ref name="thenewyorker">{{cite web |last1=Trammell |first1=Matthew |title=Nandi Rose Plunkett’s Freewheeling Art Pop |url=https://www.newyorker.com/magazine/2017/04/10/nandi-rose-plunketts-freewheeling-art-pop |publisher=[[The New Yorker]] |accessdate=4 January 2020}}</ref> Her parents divorced when she was a child. Plunkett began writing music when she was a child in Massachusetts. Plunkett attended [[Kenyon College]].<ref name="pitchfork1"/>

==History==
Plunkett is a member of the [[indie rock]] band [[Pinegrove (band)|Pinegrove]].<ref name="stereogum">{{cite web |last1=Helman |first1=Peter |title=Nandi Rose Plunkett Of Pinegrove & Half Waif Shares |publisher=[[Stereogum]] |accessdate=25 December 2019}}</ref> Plunkett released her first full-length album as Half Waif in 2014 titled ''Kotekan''.<ref name="Stereogum3">{{cite web |last1=Leas |first1=Ryan |title=Enough Apocalypse |url=https://www.stereogum.com/featured/enough-apocalypse/ |publisher=Stereogum |accessdate=4 January 2020}}</ref> Plunkett followed up that release in 2016 with her second full-length album titled ''Probable Depths''.<ref name="stereogum1">{{cite web |last1=Innis |first1=Charles |title=Half Waif – "Nest" (Stereogum Premiere) |url=https://www.stereogum.com/1849360/half-waif-nest-stereogum-premiere/mp3s/ |publisher=Stereogum |accessdate=25 December 2019}}</ref><ref name="pitchfork">{{cite web |last1=Pelly |first1=Jenn |title=Half Waif: "Turn Me Around" |url=https://pitchfork.com/reviews/tracks/18490-half-waif-turn-me-around/ |publisher=Pitchfork |accessdate=25 December 2019}}</ref><ref name="pitchfork1">{{cite web |last1=Pelly |first1=Jenn |title=Half Waif’s Restless Electro Pop |url=https://pitchfork.com/features/rising/10090-half-waifs-restless-electro-pop/ |publisher=Pitchfork}}</ref> In 2017, Plunkett released an EP titled ''form/a''.<ref name="thefader">{{cite web |last1=McDermott |first1=Patrick |title=Half Waif’s New EP Is A Mesmerizing Collection Of Avant-Pop Plunkett's music has been described as [[synth-pop]]<ref name="nprmusic"/> and [[electronic music|electronic]].<ref name="pastemagazine">{{cite web |last1=DiBlasi |first1=Loren |title=Watch Half Waif Perform Three Gorgeous Lavender Tracks Live From the Paste Studio |url=https://www.pastemagazine.com/articles/2018/04/half-waif-lavender-session.html |publisher=[[Paste (magazine)|Paste]] |accessdate=4 January 2020}}</ref>
