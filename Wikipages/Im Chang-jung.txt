'''Im Chang-jung''' ({{Korean|hangul=임창정|hanja=|rr=}}; born November 30, 1973) is a South Korean singer-songwriter and actor.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://100.daum.net/encyclopedia/view/33XXXXX19302|title=임창정|website=Daum Encyclopedia|language=ko|access-date=August 24, 2018}}</ref> He is often referred by Koreans as "the original multi-entertainer" for being active in all three fields: music, film and entertainment. He made his acting debut in 1990 and his singing debut in 1995. Im has since released 17 full-length albums<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.asiatoday.co.kr/view.php?key=20180927000911155|title=임창정, ‘하루도 그대를 사랑하지 않은 적이 없었다’ 퍼펙트 올킬 달성|last=Kim|first=Yeong-jin|date=September 27, 2018|work=Asia Today|access-date=September 27, 2018|language=ko}}</ref> and is known for his hit songs that are vocally challenging to sing.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20171024000607|title=Veteran balladeer Im Chang-jung returns with heart-wrenching EP|last=Hong|first=Dam-young|date=October 24, 2017|work=The Korea Herald|access-date=August 26, 2018}}</ref> He is the only artist in South Korea who have songs that reached number one on the local music charts in the 1990s, 2000s, and 2010s.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://koreajoongangdaily.joins.com/news/article/article.aspx?aid=3067631|title=Thirty years in, Im Chang-jung is still smiling: The veteran singer’s latest album ‘Never Ending’ has a song for every month|last=Yoon|first=So-yeon|date=September 5, 2019|work=Korea JoongAng Daily|access-date=October 12, 2020}}</ref> He was selected as [[Gallup Korea's Singer of the Year|Singer of the Year]] and his song "The Love I Committed" was selected as [[Gallup Korea's Song of the Year|Song of the Year]] in the surveys conducted by [[Gallup Korea]] in 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.donga.com/news/Entertainment/article/all/20161220/81956192/2|script-title=ko:올해를 빛낸 가수·노래 2위 ‘트와이스’· ‘치어 업’…1위는?|last=Park|first=Ye-seul|date=December 20, 2012|work=[[The Dong-a Ilbo]]|lang=ko|trans-title=2nd place for singers and songs that shined this year 'TWICE', 'Cheer Up'... 1st place?|access-date=June 13, 2021}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Im married professional golfer Kim Hyun-joo in 2006.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20101210000933|title=Celebrities say age-gap makes no difference in planning marriage|last=Ryu|first=Jeong-hyun|date=December 10, 2010|work=The Korea Herald|access-date=August 24, 2018}}</ref> The couple, who have three sons together, divorced in 2013.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://english.chosun.com/site/data/html_dir/2013/04/04/2013040401007.html|title=Singer Im Chang-jung Files for Divorce|date=April 4, 2013|work=The Chosun Ilbo|access-date=August 24, 2018}}</ref> Im married a woman named Seo Ha-yan in 2017. The couple have 2 children together.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://news.joins.com/article/22240308|title=베트남 푸꿕 찾은 임창정·장동건·고소영|date=December 28, 2017|work=JoongAng Ilbo|access-date=August 24, 2018|language=ko-KR}}</ref>

On November 9, 2021 it was confirmed that Im had tested positive for [[COVID-19]], and has since halted all promotions for his 17th album ''Nothing Special With The Day''.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://news.naver.com/main/read.naver?mode=LSD&mid=sec&sid1=103&oid=015&aid=0004626500|title=임창정, 코로나19 확진 판정…컴백 8일만에 활동 중단|date=November 9, 2021|work=Hankook Kyungje|access-date=November 14, 2021|language=ko-KR}}</ref>
