'''Twiddle''' is an American rock band.  They are a [[jam band]], and utilize instrumental improvisation in their live performances.  They incorporate influences from a variety of music genres, including [[rock music|rock]], [[jazz]], [[bluegrass music|bluegrass]], [[reggae]], and [[funk]].<ref name=GratefulWeb1/><ref name=GratefulWeb2/><ref name=HuffingtonPost/><ref name=NYSMusic/>

Twiddle formed at [[Castleton State College]] in [[Vermont]] in 2004. The members of the band are Mihali Savoulidis (guitar, vocals), Ryan Dempsey (keyboards, vocals), Zdenek Gubb (bass, vocals), and Brook Jordan (drums, percussion, vocals).<ref name=Relix/>

Twiddle hold an annual festival called Tumble Down at Burlington Waterfront Park.<ref name=JambandsDotCom/><ref name=LiveForLiveMusic/><ref name=JamBase0/><ref name=JamBase1/>

On April 20, 2018 Twiddle appeared on ''[[The Dan Patrick Show]]'' and was announced as the "house band".<ref>{{cite web |first=Scott |last=Bernstein |date=April 20, 2018 |url=https://www.jambase.com/article/twiddle-performs-dan-patrick-show |title=Twiddle Performs on ''The Dan Patrick Show'' |website=JamBase |access-date=April 20, 2018}}</ref>
