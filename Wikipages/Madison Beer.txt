'''Madison Elle Beer'''<ref name="Elite"/> (born March 5, 1999<ref>[https://mobile.twitter.com/madisonbeer/status/1235629217317924864 My precious baby turns 21 today - Twitter]</ref>) is an American singer and songwriter. Born in New York, she began posting covers to [[YouTube]] in early 2012. Beer gained substantial media coverage when [[Justin Bieber]] posted a link to one of her covers. She released her debut single, "Melodies", in 2013.

In 2018, Beer released her debut EP, ''[[As She Pleases]]''. The project was supported by the singles "[[Dead (Madison Beer song)|Dead]]" and "[[Home with You (Madison Beer song)|Home with You]]", both of which were certified Gold by the RIAA. The following year, Beer signed with [[Epic Records]], later releasing her debut studio album, ''[[Life Support (Madison Beer album)|Life Support]]'' in 2021. The album was supported by several singles, including "[[Selfish (Madison Beer song)|Selfish]]", which gained traction on the media sharing app [[TikTok]], leading to the single's [[Recording Industry Association of America|RIAA]] Gold certification.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Madison Beer|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/Madison-Beer/chart-history/HBU|access-date=2021-02-26|website=Billboard}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Gold & Platinum|url=https://www.riaa.com/gold-platinum/|access-date=2021-02-26|website=RIAA|language=en-US}}</ref>

Aside from her solo work, Beer has voiced the virtual character Evelynn in the ''[[League of Legends]]'' [[virtual band]] [[K/DA]]. As a member of the group she has released the internationally charting singles "[[Pop/Stars]]" (2018) and "[[More (K/DA song)|More]]" (2020). She has also appeared on television series such as ''[[Todrick (TV series)|Todrick]]'' (2015) and ''[[RuPaul's Drag Race]]'' (2020), and in the film ''Louder Than Words'' (2013).

Beer began posting videos on YouTube of her singing covers of popular songs in early 2012.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Beer|first1=Madison|title=Madison Beer - Bruno Mars Mashup|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=COhrE0aW_tk|website=Youtube|date=February 29, 2012}}</ref> They gained the attention of [[Justin Bieber]], who tweeted a link to her cover of [[Etta James]]'s "[[At Last]]" to his followers. === Influences ===
Beer has stated that her inspirations in her music and songwriting are [[Lana Del Rey]], [[Daft Punk]], [[Melanie Martinez]], and [[Ariana Grande]]. When questioned if she was inspired by Grande, Beer responded "I mean, you know Ariana's been one of my favorite people ever for like, so much of my career and like, even just prior to my career, so I'm sure she's definitely influenced me, a lot. ... But, I hate this narrative that's being pushed about me and her, you know, that it's kinda like this competition thing, like this is someone I idolize and someone I look up to."<ref name="youtube.com" />

== Personal life ==
In 2016, Beer discussed her sexuality in a [[YouNow]] chat, stating, "I'm not a [[lesbian]], but I definitely love girls," and that she has been in love with a woman in the past.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Willen|first=Claudia|title=20 things you probably didn't know about Madison Beer|url=https://www.insider.com/who-is-madison-beer-fun-facts-2020-7|access-date=2021-02-26|website=Insider}}</ref> She addressed her sexuality again in a March 2020 [[TikTok]] livestream, stating that she is [[Bisexuality|bisexual]]: "'Are you bi?' Yeah, I came out like, 4, 3 years ago I'd say. People always ask me that, and I'm like I've answered this question so many times. It's kinda weird. ... I am bi, always have been, it's nothing new."<ref>{{Citation|title=Madison Beer talking about her bisexuality|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O3RvuFwhQ4A|language=en|access-date=2021-02-26}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Madison Beer Updates on Instagram: "#MadisonBeer in her live at Tik Tok talking about her bisexuality, two days ago."|url=https://www.instagram.com/p/B-DqvIDAply/|access-date=2021-02-26|website=Instagram|language=en}}</ref>

Beer was in a relationship with Jack Gilinsky of [[Jack & Jack]] from 2015 to 2017.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Prahl|first=Amanda|date=2020-03-08|title=Jack Gilinsky|url=https://www.popsugar.com/node/47206780|access-date=2021-02-26|website=POPSUGAR Celebrity|language=en-US}}</ref> She briefly dated [[Brooklyn Beckham]] after her breakup with Gilinsky.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Prahl|first=Amanda|date=2020-03-08|title=Brooklyn Beckham|url=https://www.popsugar.com/node/47206778|access-date=2021-02-26|website=POPSUGAR Celebrity|language=en-US}}</ref> She was in an on-and-off relationship with Zack Bia from late 2017 to early 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Prahl|first=Amanda|date=2020-03-08|title=Zack Bia|url=https://www.popsugar.com/node/47206779|access-date=2021-02-26|website=POPSUGAR Celebrity|language=en-US}}</ref>

Beer has struggled with mental health issues, and has said that social media and the internet have contributed to them. She has engaged in self-harm, and was diagnosed with [[borderline personality disorder]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Jokic|first=Natasha|title=Madison Beer Opened Up About Her Debut Album, Mental Health, And Why She Mostly Stays Off TikTok|url=https://www.buzzfeed.com/natashajokic1/madison-beer-life-support-interview|access-date=2021-02-26|website=BuzzFeed|language=en}}</ref>

Beer currently resides in [[Los Angeles]].{{Citation needed|date=September 2021}}
