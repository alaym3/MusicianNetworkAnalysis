"'''Not in the Mood'''" is a song by American rapper [[Lil Tjay]] featuring American rappers [[Fivio Foreign]] and Kay Flock. It was released on October 22, 2021 as a single and was produced by BS Beats and AyoAA.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|last=Powell|first=Jon|date=2021-10-22|title=Lil Tjay unveils "Not In The Mood" with Fivio Foreign and Kay Flock|url=https://www.revolt.tv/new-music/2021/10/22/22738204/lil-tjay-fivio-foreign-kay-flock-not-in-the-mood-video|url-status=live|website=[[Revolt (TV network)|REVOLT]]|language=en}}</ref>

==Composition==
"Not in the Mood" is a [[Drill music|drill]] song that sees the artists rapping about their determination to work harder and "overcome life's obstacles", as well as their lifestyles and street life.<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web|last=Okon|first=Wongo|date=2021-10-22|title=Lil Tjay, Fivio Foreign, And Kay Flock Connect On ’Not In The Mood’|url=https://uproxx.com/music/lil-tjay-fivio-foreign-kay-flock-right-now/|url-status=live|website=[[UPROXX]]|language=en-US}}</ref>

==Music video==
The music video, released alongside the single, was filmed in New York. In it, the rappers and their respective crews appear in the studio, outside apartments and on the streets of [[Times Square]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Espinoza|first=Joshua|date=October 22, 2021|title=Lil Tjay Drops Video for “Not in the Mood” f/ Fivio Foreign and Kay Flock|url=https://www.complex.com/music/lil-tjay-fivio-foreign-kay-flock-not-in-the-mood-video|url-status=live|website=[[Complex Networks|Complex]]|language=en}}</ref>
