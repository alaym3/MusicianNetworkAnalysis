'''Dominic Richard Harrison''' (born 5 August 1997), known professionally as '''Yungblud''' (stylized '''YUNGBLUD'''), is an English singer, musician and songwriter. In 2018, he released his first EP, ''Yungblud'', followed shortly after by the album ''[[21st Century Liability]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.readdork.com/news/yungblud-has-released-his-debut-album-21st-century-liability-check-it-out|title=Yungblud has released his debut album, '21st Century Liability' – check it out|work=Read Dork|first=Sam|last=Taylor|date=6 July 2018}}</ref> In 2019, he released another EP, ''The Underrated Youth''. His second album, ''[[Weird!]]'', was released in late 2020 and peaked at the top of the [[UK Albums Chart]] and reached number 75 on the US [[Billboard 200|'' Billboard'' 200]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.officialcharts.com/chart-news/yungblud-takes-us-through-his-new-ep-the-underrated-youth-track-by-track-its-a-tribute-to-individualism-__27562/|title=Yungblud talks Underrated Youth EP: "A tribute to individualism"|last=Copsey|first=Rob|date=23 October 2019|website=Official Charts Company|access-date=5 January 2020}}<br />- {{Cite web|url=https://www.deadpress.co.uk/ep-review-yungblud-the-underrated-youth/|title=EP REVIEW: Yungblud – The Underrated Youth|last=Blackburn|first=Stevie|date=20 October 2019|website=Dead Press!|access-date=5 January 2020}}</ref>

== Early life ==
Harrison was born on 5 August 1997, in [[Doncaster]], [[South Yorkshire]], to Samantha Harrison<ref>{{cite web|title=Yungblud reveals his love for Travis Scott & P!nk in the Auto-Tune Interview |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_jYEdrGONBc&t=243| archive-url=https://ghostarchive.org/varchive/youtube/20211103/_jYEdrGONBc| archive-date=3 November 2021 | url-status=live|via=YouTube|access-date=8 February 2020}}{{cbignore}}</ref> and Justin Harrison.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Arnold|first=Chuck|date=10 October 2019|title=English punk-rocker Yungblud leads a youth movement|url=https://nypost.com/2019/10/10/english-punk-rocker-yungblud-leads-a-youth-movement/|url-status=live|access-date=20 February 2021|website=New York Post}}</ref> He studied at [[Arts Educational Schools, London]] and appeared in ''[[Emmerdale]]'' and ''[[The Lodge (TV series)|The Lodge]]'' before beginning his music career.{{citation needed|date=March 2021}}

== Career ==
=== 2017–2018: ''Yungblud'' ===
On 7 April 2017, Harrison released his first single, "King Charles".<ref>{{cite web |last1=Hawkes |first1=Jake |title=Yungblud makes an explosive entrance with debut offering "King Charles" |url=https://www.thelineofbestfit.com/new-music/discovery/yungblud-king-charles |website=[[The Line of Best Fit]] |date=10 April 2017}}<br />- {{Cite web|url=https://www.tenementtv.com/tracks/yungblud-king-charles/|title=Yungblud 'King Charles'|last=Woodger|first=Calum|date=15 June 2017|website=Tenement TV|access-date=9 February 2020}}</ref> On 15 September 2017, he released "[[I Love You Will U Marry Me|I Love You, Will You Marry Me]]", a song about a dark, modern-day love story.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.abc.net.au/triplej/programs/drive/yungblyd/9149724|title=Yungblud explains the dark love story behind 'I Love You, Will You Marry Me'|date=14 November 2017|website=triple j|access-date=9 February 2020}}</ref>

On 10 November 2017, he released "Tin Pan Boy", a song about the construction project on [[Denmark Street|Tin Pan Alley]], a musical hotspot place in London.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Powell |first1=Aaron |title=Yungblud decries social injustice on new track "Tin Pan Boy" |url=https://www.thelineofbestfit.com/new-music/discovery/yungblud-tin-pan-boy |website=[[The Line of Best Fit]] |date=9 November 2017}}</ref><ref name="Atwood Mag">{{cite web |last1=Almeida |first1=Nicole |title=Raising The Voice Of The Younger Generation: A Conversation With Yungblud |url=https://atwoodmagazine.com/yungblud-2018-interview/ |website=[[Atwood Magazine]] |date=1 February 2018}}</ref> On 19 January 2018, he released his an EP, ''Yungblud'' (stylised in all capitals) which includes the three singles from earlier in the year.<ref name="Atwood Mag" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.onestowatch.com/blog/qa-yungblud-releases-self-titled|title=Q&A: Yungblud Releases Self-Titled Genre-Explorative Debut EP|last=Piccirillo|first=Angie|date=23 January 2018|website=[[Ones to Watch]]|access-date=9 February 2020}}</ref>

== Activism ==

== Personal life ==

=== Health issues ===
Harrison was diagnosed with [[attention deficit hyperactivity disorder]] (ADHD) at a young age, which made him a troublesome student. He was suspended from school after a dare from his friend to "[[Mooning|moon]]" his mathematics teacher.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YL9do7sJpZE&t=626s| archive-url=https://ghostarchive.org/varchive/youtube/20211103/YL9do7sJpZE| archive-date=3 November 2021 | url-status=live|title=YUNGBLUD and Foo Fighters: Road to Reading and Leeds |website=BBC Radio 1|date=20 August 2019|via=YouTube|access-date=19 March 2020}}{{cbignore}}</ref> In August 2018, he said through a tweet that he suffers from insomnia.<ref>{{Cite web|date=10 August 2018|title=insomnia sucks.|url=https://twitter.com/yungblud/status/1027743858044686339|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=14 December 2020|website=Twitter}}</ref> In February 2020, he spoke to the ''[[Evening Standard]]'' and said that he had attempted suicide twice after going through a series of positive and negative events in his career.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Fishwick|first=Samuel|date=19 February 2020|title=Meet Yungblud: the poster boy of Gen Z|url=https://www.standard.co.uk/lifestyle/esmagazine/yungblud-interview-gen-z-punk-a4366241.html|access-date=21 February 2020|website=[[Evening Standard]]}}</ref>

=== Relationships and sexuality ===
He described himself as [[sexually fluid]] in an interview with ''[[Attitude (magazine)|Attitude]]'' in August 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Stichbury|first=Thomas|date=15 August 2019|title=YUNGBLUD SPEAKS UP ABOUT GOING WITH THE FLOW OF SEXUAL FLUIDITY|url=https://attitude.co.uk/article/yungblud-speaks-up-about-going-with-the-flow-of-sexual-fluidity/21586/|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=14 December 2020|website=Attitude}}</ref> In another interview with the magazine in December 2020, he described himself as [[pansexual]] and [[polyamorous]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Yungblud Now Considers Himself a Part of the LGBTQ Community|url=https://attitude.co.uk/article/yungblud-now-considers-himself-a-part-of-the-lgbtq-community/24103/|access-date=2 December 2020|website=Attitude|date=December 2020}}</ref>

He was in a relationship with the American singer [[Halsey (singer)|Halsey]] for several months; she confirmed their break up in October 2019.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Nesvig |first1=Kara |title=Halsey on Her Yungblud Breakup: "Sometimes It Just Happens" |url=https://www.teenvogue.com/story/halsey-yungblud-breakup |website=[[Teen Vogue]] |date=29 October 2019}}</ref> Almost a year later, on [[Jessie Ware]]'s ''Table Manners'' podcast, in November 2020, Yungblud said that they broke up because they worked better as friends.<ref>{{Cite web|date=18 November 2020|title=S10 Ep 10: Yungblud |work= Table Manners with Jessie Ware|url=https://play.acast.com/s/tablemanners/s10ep10-yungblud|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=14 December 2020|via=Acast}}</ref>

Since July 2021, he has been dating the American singer and fashion designer Jesse Jo Stark.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Mier|first1=Tomás|title=Yungblud Makes Relationship with 'Luv of My Lyf' Jesse Jo Stark Instagram Official: 'I Luv You Baby'|url= https://people.com/music/yungblud-is-dating-jesse-jo-stark-instagram-official/?amp=true|work=People|date=11 June 2021|access-date=13 June 2021}}</ref>
