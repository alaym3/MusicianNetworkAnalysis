'''Niclas Benjamin Peltonen''' (born 15 April 1997<ref>{{Cite web | url = https://www.facebook.com/BenjaminMusicOfficial/posts/1065109853504786 | title = Benjamin 15. huhtikuuta 2015 kello 14:48 | author = | work = Benjamin | date = 15 May 2015 | access-date = 24 April 2015 |language=fi}}</ref>), known mononymously as '''Benjamin''', is a Finnish singer. Peltonen originally became famous through the photo-sharing service [[Instagram]], where his profile, opened in 2012 and as of 2021 has more than 280,000 followers.<ref>{{Cite web | url = http://ylex.yle.fi/uutiset/popuutiset/benjamin-olisi-kiva-jos-tytto-tekisi-aloitteen | title = Benjamin: Olisi kiva, jos tyttö tekisi aloitteen | author = Suomalainen, Ninni | work = YleX | date = 5 September 2014 | access-date = 13 September 2014 |language=fi}}</ref><ref name="nyt">{{Cite web| url = http://nyt.fi/a1305753818336 | title = 16-vuotiaalla suomalaispojalla on yli 80 000 fanityttöä netissä – kui oot nui täydellinen? | author = Heikkilä, Melissa | author2 = Saikkonen, Merituuli | work = Nyt | date = 28 November 2013 | access-date = 8 September 2014 |language=fi}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web | url = http://instagram.com/benjaminpeltonen | title = benjaminpeltonen | author = | work = Instagram | date = | access-date = 8 September 2014 |language=fi}}</ref><ref name="warner">{{Cite web | url = http://www.warnermusic.fi/uutiset/10060/benjaminin-debyyttisingle-underdogs-julkaistaan-8-syyskuuta | title = Benjaminin debyyttisingle Underdogs julkaistaan 8. syyskuuta | work = Warner Music Finland | date = 1 September 2014 | access-date = 8 September 2014 | language = fi | url-status = dead | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140908025823/http://www.warnermusic.fi/uutiset/10060/benjaminin-debyyttisingle-underdogs-julkaistaan-8-syyskuuta | archivedate = 8 September 2014 | df = dmy-all }}</ref> In May 2014 Peltonen signed a record deal with the record company [[Warner Music Finland]].<ref>{{Cite web | url = http://www.iltasanomat.fi/viihde/art-1288694351831.html | title = Instagram-suosikki Benjamin Peltonen sai levytyssopimuksen | author =  | work = Ilta-Sanomat | date = 28 May 2014 | access-date = 8 September 2014 | language = fi | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20140907234557/http://www.iltasanomat.fi/viihde/art-1288694351831.html | archive-date = 7 September 2014 | url-status = dead }}</ref>

== Career ==

=== 2014–2017: ''Square One'' and ''Fingerprints'' ===
Peltonen's first single "Underdogs" was released on 8 September 2014.<ref name="warner" /> It peaked at number ten on the official singles chart in Finland.<ref name="musiikkituottajat">{{Cite web | url = http://www.ifpi.fi/tilastot/virallinen-lista/artistit/benjamin/underdogs | title = Musiikkituottajat – Tilastot – Suomen virallinen lista: Benjamin – ''Underdogs'' | author = | work = Musiikkituottajat | date = | access-date = 21 September 2014 |language=fi}}</ref> In March 2015 the song was added to the playlist of the Spanish radio channel [[Los 40 Principales]].<ref name="espanja">{{Cite web | url = http://www.iltalehti.fi/popstars/2015033119451377_ps.shtml | title = Benjamin valloittaa nyt Espanjaa | author = | work = Iltalehti | date = 31 March 2015 | access-date = 31 March 2015 |language=fi}}</ref> Peltonen released his second single "Unbreakable" on 19 December 2014.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/gb/album/unbreakable-single/id949479110|title=Unbreakable – Single by Benjamin on Apple Music|website=iTunes|language=en|access-date=7 March 2017}}</ref> His first [[EP record|extended play]] ''Square One'' was released in May 2015, including both prior singles and three new songs, including third single "Young and Restless".<ref name="espanja" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/gb/album/square-one-ep/id995343453|title=Square One – EP by Benjamin on Apple Music|website=iTunes|language=en|access-date=7 March 2017}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last=BenjaminOfficial|title=Benjamin – Young And Restless|date=16 July 2015|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DgejtcRYxjs|accessdate=7 March 2017}}</ref>

A 20-part documentary series about Peltonen entitled ''Naked Diary: Benjamin Peltonen'', was aired on Yle Areena in the spring of 2015.<ref name="dokumentti">{{Cite web | url = http://yle.fi/ylex/tapahtumat/benjaminpeltonen | title = Alaston päiväkirja: Benjamin Peltonen | author = | work = YleX | date = | access-date = 31 March 2015 |language=fi}}</ref> A longer documentary of the same name was aired on [[Yle TV2]] on 3 May 2015.<ref name="dokumentti" /><ref>{{Cite web | url = http://yle.fi/aihe/artikkeli/2015/04/09/ylen-viikolta-18-valittua-dokumentti-instagram-sensaatio-benjamin-peltosesta | title = Ylen viikolta 18 valittua: Dokumentti Instagram-sensaatio Benjamin Peltosesta | author = | work = Yle | date = 9 April 2015 | access-date = 3 May 2015 |language=fi}}</ref>

Peltonen announced a brand new single called "Body" for release on 4 March 2016,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/gb/album/body-single/id1087016722|title=Body – Single by Benjamin on Apple Music|website=iTunes|language=en|access-date=7 March 2017}}</ref> which will precede his debut album. Benjamin's debut album ''Fingerprints'' was released in April 2016, including all previous singles, and the entire ''Square One'' EP. It debuted on the Finnish album chart at number 17<ref>[http://www.ifpi.fi/tilastot/virallinen-lista/artistit/benjamin/fingerprints Suomen virallinen lista – Benjamin: Fingerprints]</ref> and the Spanish album chart at number 3.<ref>http://www.hs.fi/kulttuuri/a1461730464037</ref> "Man on the Moon", featuring Spanish girl group Sweet California, was the fifth and final single released from the album on 8 July 2016.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/gb/album/man-on-the-moon-feat-sweet-california-single/id1128413256|title=Man on the Moon (feat. Sweet California) – Single by Benjamin on Apple Music|website=iTunes|language=en|access-date=7 March 2017}}</ref> He released the single "Bad Luck Love" on 8 March 2017.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://los40.com/los40/2017/03/13/musica/1489427657_110176.html|title=Benjamin vuelve con 'Bad Luck Love', un tema que refleja su evolución|work=[[Los 40]]|author=Alejandro Gómez Lizarraga|date=March 13, 2017|language=es}}</ref>

=== 2018–present: Transition to music in Finnish and ''Someveteraani'' ===
In 2018, Benjamin started releasing music in [[Finnish language|Finnish]]. The first release "Naarmuja" featuring Finnish rapper [[:fi:IBE|IBE]] was released on August 10, 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://news.abounderrattelser.fi/st-karinsuppvuxne-popsangaren-benjamin-peltonen-sjunger-nu-fran-hjartat/|title=S:t Karinsuppvuxne popsångaren Benjamin Peltonen sjunger nu från hjärtat|work=[[Åbo Underrättelser]]|author=Annina Suominen|date=September 22, 2018|language=fi}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://music.apple.com/fi/album/naarmuja-feat-ibe-single/1417911772|title=J Naarmuja (feat. IBE) - Single by Benjamin|work=iTunes, Apple Music|date=August 10, 2018}}</ref> Another single "Juon sut pois" was unveiled on November 16, 2017. Its official music video, directed by Karim Saheb and shot in [[Germany]] premiered a day later.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.iltalehti.fi/musiikki/a/4ae79e21-69d4-47d0-8813-6988144318f3|title=Benjamin Peltonen ajoi hiuksensa pois - uusi tyyli ihastuttaa somessa: "Sun pää on kauniin mallinen"|work=[[Iltalehti]]|author=Mari Pudas|date=November 20, 2018|language=fi}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Wn7X64E1l0g|title=Juon sut pois.mp4|work=[[YouTube]]|date=November 17, 2018|author=BenjaminOfficial}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://music.apple.com/fi/album/juon-sut-pois-single/1440611745|title=Juon sut pois - Single by Benjamin|work=iTunes, Apple Music|date=November 16, 2018}}</ref> More singles followed through 2019: "Tanssin yksin" on February 22,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.iltalehti.fi/viihdeuutiset/a/6d3d0e1a-79b8-4dec-85fb-04d1efa09066|title=Laulaja Benjamin Peltonen, 21, ei ole koskaan seurustellut – ihastuu lomillaan ulkomailla: "Sitten sydän särkyy"|work=Iltalehti|author=Lue Myös|date=February 22, 2019|language=fi}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://music.apple.com/es/album/tanssin-yksin-single/1451592184|title= Tanssin yksin - Single by Benjamin|work=iTunes, Apple Music|date=February 22, 2019}}</ref> "Orgasmi" on June 7, 2020<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.voice.fi/musiikki/a-179636|title=Benjamin Peltosen tulevan biisin rohkea nimi sekoitti fanit - "Aikas rajuu"|work=Voice|author=Iiro Myllymäki|date=June 6, 2019|language=fi}}</ref> and "Näytä mulle ne" on August 23.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://music.apple.com/fi/album/n%C3%A4yt%C3%A4-mulle-ne-single/1475623433|title=Näytä mulle ne - Single by Benjamin|work=iTunes, Apple Music|date=August 23, 2019}}</ref>

In January 24, 2020 a song about his parents' divorce "Kaksi kotia" premiered along a short prologue video and an upcoming album was teased.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.iltalehti.fi/musiikki/a/0259bc3d-0f59-447f-9d32-67f03918bc78|title=Teininä läpimurtonsa tehnyt Benjamin Peltonen paljastaa uutuuskappaleessaan yksityiskohtia vanhempiensa avioerosta – katso musiikkivideo|work=Iltalehti|author=Julia Aalto-Setälä|date=January 24, 2020|language=fi}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://music.apple.com/fi/album/kaksi-kotia-single/1484505065|title= Kaksi kotia - Single by Benjamin|work=iTunes, Apple Music|date=January 24, 2020}}</ref> Preceded by singles "WWW" and "TikTok", Benjamin's first album in Finnish ''Someveteraani'' was released on September 25, 2020.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.hs.fi/kulttuuri/art-2000006644511.html|title=Bikinimallien välissä poseerannut Benjamin Peltonen on yksi niistä sometähdistä, joista levy-yhtiöt kävivät kisaa muutama vuosi sitten – Näin laulaville tubettajille ja sometähdille on käynyt|work=[[Helsingin Sanomat]]|author=Oskari Onninen|date=September 23, 2020|language=fi}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.is.fi/viihde/art-2000006660864.html|title=Benjamin Peltonen oli Suomen ensimmäinen Instagram-tähti, jolle sovellus toi jopa levytyssopimuksen - nyt hän täräyttää suorat sanat "somelaulajan" leimastaan|work=[[Ilta-Sanomat]]|author=Iida Lida|date=October 6, 2020|language=fi}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.iltalehti.fi/viihdeuutiset/a/b1a10d78-5c3e-46b4-a8c8-65397ffeef16|title=Benjamin Peltonen eli "Justin Bieber -unelmaa" – fanin esittämä kysymys muutti kaiken|work=Iltalehti|author=Mari Pudas|date=September 25, 2020|language=fi}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://music.apple.com/fi/album/www-single/15280267883|title=WWW - Single by Benjamin|work=iTunes, Apple Music|date=August 28, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://music.apple.com/fi/album/tiktok-single/1528335827|title=TikTok - Single by Benjamin|work=iTunes, Apple Music|date=September 4, 2020}}</ref>
