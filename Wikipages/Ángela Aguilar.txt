'''Ángela Aguilar Álvarez''' (born October 8, 2003) is a Mexican-American singer. She was born in Los Angeles while her mother was accompanying her father, [[Pepe Aguilar]], on tour. Aguilar gained notable recognition after performing "[[La Llorona (song)|La Llorona]]" at the [[19th Annual Latin Grammy Awards]] in 2018.

Her debut solo studio album, ''[[Primero Soy Mexicana]]'' (2018), was met with critical acclaim and success. She has been nominated for a [[Grammy Award]] and two [[Latin Grammy Awards]], becoming one of the youngest artists nominated for both awards.

==Early life==
Ángela Aguilar Álvarez was born to [[Pepe Aguilar]] and Aneliz Álvarez-Alcala in [[Los Angeles, California]] on October 8, 2003, while her father was on tour.<ref>{{cite web|title=Biografía de Ángela Aguilar|url=https://www.angelaaguilaroficial.com/biografia/|website=Ángela Aguilar|accessdate=September 25, 2018|language=Spanish}}</ref> She was named after her great-grandmother Ángela Márquez-Barraza Valle, her father's paternal grandmother. Aguilar has [[dual citizenship]] with Mexico and the United States.<ref name="Grammyinterview">{{Cite web|url=https://www.grammy.com/grammys/news/meet-first-time-grammy-nominee-angela-aguilar-her-culture-and-family-legacy|title=Meet First-Time GRAMMY Nominee Angela Aguilar|date=January 11, 2019|website=GRAMMY.com|language=en|access-date=January 11, 2019}}</ref> Aguilar was born into a musical family, known as "La Dinastía Aguilar" (The Aguilar Dynasty).<ref>{{cite web |last1=Jurek |first1=Thom |title=Leonardo Aguilar Artist Biography |url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/leonardo-aguilar-mn0003482106/biography |website=Allmusic |accessdate=September 26, 2018}}</ref> Aguilar's father is Mexican singer Pepe Aguilar, her paternal grandparents are Mexican singer-actors [[Antonio Aguilar]] and [[Flor Silvestre]].<ref name="Laopinion">{{cite news|title=Hijos de Pepe Aguilar debutan con 'Nueva tradición'|url=https://laopinion.com/2012/12/23/hijos-de-pepe-aguilar-debutan-con-nueva-tradicion/|accessdate=September 27, 2017|work=LaOpinion|date=December 23, 2012|language=Spanish}}</ref> Since a young age, Aguilar has frequently accompanied her father on tour throughout Latin America with her brother, [[Leonardo Aguilar]].<ref name="Laopinion" />

In July 2018, Aguilar partnered with [[Voto Latino]] to encourage more Latino Americans to vote in American elections.<ref>{{cite news |title=Pepe Aguilar, y su hija Ángela, se unen a Mundo Latino para promover el voto latino |url=https://www.telemundo.com/shows/2018/07/26/pepe-aguilar-y-su-hija-angela-se-unen-mundo-latino-para-promover-el-voto-latino |accessdate=September 25, 2018 |work=Telemundo |date=July 4, 2018 |language=Spanish}}</ref>

==Career==
In 2012, at just nine years old, Aguilar released ''Nueva Tradición'', alongside her brother, Leonardo. It featured four songs by Leonardo and four by Ángela.<ref name="Laopinion" /> In 2016, Aguilar participated in the [[100 Women (BBC)|BBC 100 Woman]] festival in Mexico City. At just 13 years old, she was the youngest performer, she told BBC News that the music industry was dominated by men and hoped it would change.<ref>{{cite news |title=100 Women 2016: Mexico festival draws thousands |url=https://www.bbc.com/news/world-latin-america-38101195 |accessdate=September 25, 2018 |work=BBC News |date=November 25, 2016}}</ref>

In January 2018, Aguilar and her family launched an [[Equestrianism|equestrian]]-musical style tour dubbed "Jaripeo Sin Fronteras". Aguilar accompanied her father, Pepe, her uncle Antonio, and her brother, Leonardo Aguilar.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Kiest |first1=Jim |title=Ranchera star Pepe Aguilar bringing family-style rodeo to San Antonio |url=https://www.mysanantonio.com/entertainment/article/Ranchera-star-Pepe-Aguilar-bringing-family-style-12508426.php |accessdate=September 25, 2018 |work=my San Antonio |date=January 18, 2018}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Aguilar was born into a musical family, known as "La Dinastía Aguilar" (The Aguilar Dynasty). Aguilar's father is Mexican singer [[Pepe Aguilar]], her paternal grandparents are Mexican singer-actors [[Antonio Aguilar]] and [[Flor Silvestre]], and her brother is [[Leonardo Aguilar]].
