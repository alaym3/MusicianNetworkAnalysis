'''We the Kingdom''' is an American [[contemporary Christian music]] band signed to the [[Capitol Christian Music Group]].<ref name="NRT Profile">{{cite web |url= https://www.newreleasetoday.com/artistdetail.php?artist_id=6380 |title= We The Kingdom Artist Profile {{!}} Biography And Discography {{!}} NewReleaseToday |last= McNeese |first= Kevin |date= August 3, 2020 |website= NewReleaseToday |access-date= February 21, 2020}}</ref> The band consists of multiple generations of relatives: Andrew Bergthold, [[Ed Cash]], Franni Rae Cash, Martin Cash, and Scott Cash.<ref name="KLOVE">{{cite web |url= http://www.klove.com/music/artists/we-the-kingdom/ |title= We The Kingdom on K-LOVE |website= [[K-Love]] |access-date= February 21, 2020}}</ref> The band's name comes after the idea that God is here among us.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.ccmmagazine.com/news/we-the-kingdom-releases-live-video-for-holy-water/ |title= We The Kingdom Releases Live Video for ‘Holy Water’ |last= Cluver |first= Ross |website= [[CCM Magazine]] |access-date=February 21, 2020}}</ref> The band released its first singles, "[[Dancing on the Waves]]" and the breakout hit single "[[Holy Water (We the Kingdom song)|Holy Water]]", followed by their debut [[extended play|EP]], ''[[Live at the Wheelhouse]]'', in 2019. This garnered the band four nominations at the [[51st GMA Dove Awards]], ultimately winning the Dove Award for New Artist of the Year. In 2020, the band released their debut album, ''[[Holy Water (We the Kingdom album)|Holy Water]]'' which contained the previously released singles "Dancing on the Waves" and "Holy Water" as well as "[[Don't Tread on Me (We the Kingdom song)|Don't Tread on Me]]", "[[God So Loved]]" and "[[Child of Love]]". The band earned two [[Grammy Award]] nominations at the [[63rd Annual Grammy Awards]], with ''Holy Water'' for [[Grammy Award for Best Contemporary Christian Music Album|Best Contemporary Christian Music Album]], and the title track being nominated for the [[Grammy Award for Best Contemporary Christian Music Performance/Song|Best Contemporary Christian Music Performance/Song]]. In 2021, the band won the GMA Dove Award for Contemporary Christian Artist of the Year and Pop/Contemporary Album of the Year for ''Holy Water'' (2020), and earnt a nomination for the Worship Recorded Song of the Year for "God So Loved" at the [[52nd GMA Dove Awards]].

==History==
On August 16, 2019, We the Kingdom released their debut single, "[[Dancing on the Waves]]".<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.premierproductions.com/blog/we-kingdom-releases-new-single-dancing-waves |title= We The Kingdom Releases New Single, 'Dancing On The Waves' |date= August 16, 2019 |website= Premier Productions |access-date= June 24, 2021}}</ref> In September 2019, the band released their second single, "[[Holy Water (We the Kingdom song)|Holy Water]]".<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.multitracks.com/blog/we-the-kingdom-holy-water |title= We The Kingdom - "Holy Water" |date= September 19, 2019 |website= MultiTracks.com |publisher= MultiTracks LLC |access-date= June 24, 2021}}</ref> "Holy Water" went on to become the band breakthrough hit single, peaking at number two on the [[Hot Christian Songs]] chart. The band released their debut [[extended play|EP]], ''[[Live at the Wheelhouse]]'', in October 2019.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.multitracks.com/blog/we-the-kingdom-live-at-the-wheelhouse/ |title= We the Kingdom - Live at the Wheelhouse |date= October 28, 2019 |website= MultiTracks.com |publisher= MultiTracks LLC |access-date= June 24, 2021}}</ref> ''Live at the Wheelhouse'' reached number three on the [[Top Christian Albums Chart]]. 

On April 24, 2020, "[[Don't Tread on Me (We the Kingdom song)|Don't Tread on Me]]" was released as the third single from the album.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/we-the-kingdom-releases-sophomore-single-don-t-tread-on-me/ |title= We The Kingdom Releases Sophomore Single "Don't Tread On Me" - TCB" |last= Clarks |first= Jessie |date= April 24, 2020 |website= The Christian Beat |access-date= June 24, 2021}}</ref> "Don't Tread on Me" peaked at number 40 on the Hot Christian Songs chart. On May 29, 2020, We the Kingdom was released "[[God So Loved]]". "God So Loved" peaked at number four on the Hot Christian Songs chart. On July 3, 2020, We the Kingdom announced that their debut studio album, ''[[Holy Water (We the Kingdom album)|Holy Water]]'', will be released on August 7,<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.ccmmagazine.com/news/we-the-kingdom-releases-child-of-love/ |title= We The Kingdom Releases ‘Child of Love’ – CCM Magazine |last= Cluver |first= Ross |date= July 4, 2020 |website= [[CCM Magazine]] |access-date= June 24, 2021}}</ref> while releasing "[[Child of Love]]" as the first promotional single from the album.<ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.jubileecast.com/articles/24231/20200704/we-the-kingdom-drops-new-single-child-of-love-ahead-of-debut-album.htm |title= We The Kingdom Drops New Single "Child of Love" Ahead of Debut Album : News : JubileeCast |date= July 4, 2020 |website= Hallels |access-date= June 24, 2021}}</ref> "No Doubt About It" was released as the second promotional single from ''Holy Water'' on July 24, 2020.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.ccmmagazine.com/news/we-the-kingdom-releases-no-doubt-about-it/ |title= We The Kingdom Releases ‘No Doubt About It’ – CCM Magazine |last= Cluver |first= Ross |date= July 24, 2020 |website= [[CCM Magazine]] |access-date= June 24, 2021}}</ref> We the Kingdom released  ''Holy Water'' on August 7, 2020.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/we-the-kingdom-releases-debut-album-holy-water/ |title= We The Kingdom Releases Debut Album 'Holy Water' - TCB |last= Clarks |first= Jessie |date= August 7, 2020 |website= The Christian Beat |access-date= June 24, 2021}}</ref> The album debuted at number four on the Top Christian Albums Chart.

At the [[51st GMA Dove Awards]] in October 2020, We the Kingdom was nominated for four awards, these being New Artist of the Year, Song of the Year and Pop/Contemporary Recorded Song of the Year for "Holy Water", and Pop/Contemporary Album of the Year for ''Live at the Wheelhouse'',<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/we-the-kingdom-celebrates-chart-topping-album-dove-award-nominations/ |title= We The Kingdom Celebrates Chart-Topping Album & Dove Award Nominations - TCB |last= Longs |first= Herb |date= August 21, 2020 |website= The Christian Beat |access-date= June 24, 2021}}</ref> with the band only winning the New Artist of the Year award.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.ccmmagazine.com/news/we-the-kingdom-wins-new-artist-of-the-year/ |title= We The Kingdom Wins Dove Award for New Artist of the Year {{!}} CCM Magazine |last= Cluver |first= Ross |date= November 3, 2020 |website= [[CCM Magazine]] |access-date= June 24, 2021}}</ref> On October 30, 2020, the band released "[[Light of the World (Sing Hallelujah)]]" as a standalone single.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/we-the-kingdom-release-light-of-the-world-sing-hallelujah/ |title=We The Kingdom Release "Light Of The World (Sing Hallelujah)" - TCB |last= Clarks |first= Jessie |date= October 31, 2020 |website= The Christian Beat |access-date= June 24, 2021}}</ref> "Light Of The World (Sing Hallelujah)" peaked at number 29 on the Hot Christian Songs chart.

On January 29, 2021, the band released "Child of Love" featuring [[Bear Rinehart]] of [[Needtobreathe]] as the fifth single from ''Holy Water'' (2020).<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/we-the-kingdom-releases-new-version-of-child-of-love-feat-needtobreathe-s-bear-rinehart/ |title= We The Kingdom Debuts New Version Of "Child Of Love" Feat. NEEDTOBREATHE's Bear Rinehart - TCB |last= Clarks |first= Jessie |date= January 29, 2021 |website= The Christian Beat |access-date= June 24, 2021}}</ref> "Child of Love" peaked at number five on the Hot Christian Songs chart. At the [[63rd Annual Grammy Awards]] in March 2021, We the Kingdom received two nominations, with "Holy Water" being nominated for the [[Grammy Award for Best Contemporary Christian Music Performance/Song|Best Contemporary Christian Music Performance/Song]], and ''Holy Water'' for [[Grammy Award for Best Contemporary Christian Music Album|Best Contemporary Christian Music Album]].<ref name="2021 Grammy Nominations">{{cite web |url= https://www.ccmmagazine.com/news/we-the-kingdom-earns-2-grammy-nominations/ |title= We The Kingdom Earns 2 GRAMMY Nominations {{!}} CCM Magazine |last= Cluver |first= Ross |date= November 25, 2020 |website= [[CCM Magazine]] |access-date= June 24, 2021}}</ref>

We the Kingdom received three nominations for the [[52nd GMA Dove Awards|2021 GMA Dove Awards]] for Contemporary Christian Artist of the Year, Pop/Contemporary Album of the Year for ''Holy Water'', and Worship Recorded Song of the Year for "God So Loved",<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/awards/9613134/2021-gma-dove-awards-nominations |title= 2021 GMA Dove Award Nominees: Full List {{!}} Billboard |last= Grein |first= Paul |date= August 11, 2021 |website= [[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] |publisher= Billboard-Hollywood Reporter Media Group |access-date= August 13, 2021}}</ref> the band winning the GMA Dove Awards for Contemporary Christian Artist of the Year and Pop/Contemporary Album of the Year for ''Holy Water''.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/awards/9649154/2021-gma-dove-awards-winners-list |title= GMA Dove Awards Winners 202: Full List {{!}} Billboard |last= Nicholson |first= Jessica |date= October 22, 2021 |website= [[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] |publisher= Billboard-Hollywood Reporter Media Group |access-date= October 26, 2021}}</ref>
