'''''Intense''''' is the fifth studio album by Dutch DJ and record producer [[Armin van Buuren]]. It was released on 3 May 2013 by [[Armada Music]].<ref>[http://www.arminvanbuuren.com/news/2013/02/armin-announces-5th-artist-album-intense/ Armin announces 5th artist album 'Intense' | Armin van Buuren<!-- Bot generated title -->] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130720053213/http://www.arminvanbuuren.com/news/2013/02/armin-announces-5th-artist-album-intense/ |date=2013-07-20 }}</ref>

The first song announced to be on the album is "Waiting for the Night", featuring the singer [[Fiora (musician)|Fiora]], which was released on 21 January 2013<ref>[http://www.beatport.com/release/waiting-for-the-night/1025773 Waiting For The Night [Armind (Armada)&#93; :: Beatport<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref> as the theme song to the Dutch film ''Loving Ibiza (Verliefd op Ibiza)''.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.ibizathefilm.com/ |title=Loving Ibiza - de officiële filmsite<!-- Bot generated title --> |access-date=2013-04-05 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120429035416/http://www.ibizathefilm.com/ |archive-date=2012-04-29 |url-status=dead }}</ref> The second song announced to be on the album is "Forever is Ours", featuring the singer [[Emma Hewitt]].<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=c29bgvu4egI&list=PL0cWlOyqP6_MbDgLpyOFGwZE7rXvBZQCN&index=4 Armin van Buuren feat. Emma Hewitt - Forever Is Ours (Original Mix) (Preview) - YouTube<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref> The third song and first official single to be released is "[[This Is What It Feels Like]]", featuring the Canadian singer and songwriter [[Trevor Guthrie]], was released on 5 April 2013. The accompanying music video, featuring [[Ron Jeremy]], was released on 17 March 2013.<ref>[http://www.arminvanbuuren.com/news/2013/03/video-teaser-new-armin-van-buuren-single/ Video teaser new Armin van Buuren single! | Armin van Buuren<!-- Bot generated title -->] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130314075715/http://www.arminvanbuuren.com/news/2013/03/video-teaser-new-armin-van-buuren-single/ |date=2013-03-14 }}</ref>

The album was first released exclusively on [[Spotify]] on 29 April 2013, followed by its official release on digital and physical media on 3 May 2013. An extended version of the album, called ''Intense (The More Intense Edition)'', was released on 12 November 2013. This album contains remixes from musicians such as [[John Ewbank (composer)|John Ewbank]], [[Andrew Rayel]], [[W&W]], [[Cosmic Gate]], [[Tritonal (band)|Tritonal]], [[Ummet Ozcan]] and [[Ørjan Nilsen]], along with radio edits, music videos, as well as two new songs from Armin van Buuren: "[[Save My Night]]" and "Don't Want to Fight Love Away" featuring [[Cyndi Almouzni|Cindy Alma]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Armin van Buuren - Intense (The More Intense Edition) out now!|url=http://www.astateoftrance.com/news/armin-van-buuren-intense-the-more-intense-edition-out-now/|access-date=December 6, 2013}}</ref>

==Composition==
Armin van Buuren described genre composition of ''Intense'' as "more [[House music|house]]-y [[Electro (music)|electro]] stuff and of course trance, some [[Rock music|rock]] influence, even a little bit of [[dubstep]] and [[classical music]] in there" during an interview with British DJ [[Pete Tong]].<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mWZyYL7UcDk World Premiere BBC Radio 1: Armin van Buuren feat. Trevor Guthrie - This Is What It Feels Like - YouTube<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>
