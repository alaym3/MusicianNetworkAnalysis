'''Casey Frey''' ({{IPAc-en|f|ɹ|aɪ}}) is an American dancer and comedian notable for his videos on social media, particularly [[Vine (service)|Vine]].

==Early life and social media==
Frey grew up in [[Mendocino County]], California, where he has stated he made videos with his brother for family and friends growing up before turning to Vine, where he initially made videos of himself dancing.<ref name = interview>{{cite interview|last=Frey |first=Casey |interviewer=Ava Trilling |title=A rare interview with Casey Frey |url=https://www.thefader.com/2019/09/09/casey-frey-interview-2019 |date=2019-09-09 |work=The Fader |access-date=2020-12-24}}</ref> Frey had over 250,000 followers on Vine before the app's shutdown in January 2017.<ref name = Martinez>{{Cite web|last = Martinez |first = Ignacio |date=2019-06-06|title=Why Casey Frey's 'Get tf out my way' memes are taking over your timeline|url=https://www.dailydot.com/unclick/get-tf-out-my-way-memes-casey-frey/|access-date=2020-12-24|website=The Daily Dot|language=en-US}}</ref> In May 2019,<ref name = Locker>{{Cite web|last = Locker |first=Melissa |date = 2020-04-29|title=This Surreal Viral Dance Trend Is Back to Get Everybody Chopping Wood to the Same Song|url=https://time.com/5829144/casey-frey-get-out-my-way/|access-date=2020-12-24|website=Time}}</ref> a video set to the song "GOMF" by [[Dvbbs]] in which Frey as "a version of himself wearing sunglasses mocks [a clone of himself] for chopping wood and dancing, [and] a third Frey donning an open button-up and a bucket hat takes notice and intervenes" went viral.<ref name = Martinez/> The video inspired numerous subsequent remakes and parodies on [[TikTok]] in 2020.<ref name = Locker/>

==Dancing==
In response to his viral video featuring the song, Dvbbs enlisted Frey to star in the music video for "GOMF", where he played a "leather-jacketed man who runs the mechanical bull ride and speaks in the goofy German accent".<ref name = Bein>{{Cite web|last=Bein|first=Kate|date=2019-05-17 |title=Dvbbs Enlists Viral Sensation Casey Frey For 'GOMF' Video |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/8511997/dvbbs-gomf-video-casey-frey-bridge |access-date=2020-12-24|website=Billboard|language=en}}</ref> Frey has also appeared in the film [[Mainstream (film)|Mainstream]] by [[Gia Coppola]],<ref name = Kiang>{{Cite web|last=Kiang|first=Jessica|date=2020-09-05|title=‘Mainstream’ Review: Internet Fame Eats Itself in Gia Coppola’s Satire|url=https://variety.com/2020/film/reviews/mainstream-review-gia-coppola-1234759713/|access-date=2020-12-24|website=Variety|language=en-US}}</ref> and as the main performer in the music video for "[[The Business (Tiësto song)|The Business]]" by [[Tiësto]].<ref name = Gable>{{Cite web|last=Gable|first=Jake|date=2020-09-26|title=Tiësto marks Atlantic Records signing with the release of his anticipated anthem ‘The Business’|url=https://weraveyou.com/2020/09/tiesto-the-business/|access-date=2020-12-24|website=We Rave You|language=en-US}}</ref><ref name = Heffler>{{Cite web|last=Heffler|first=Jason|date=2020-09-25|title=Watch Viral Comedian Casey Frey as a Dancing Zombie in Tiësto's "The Business" Music Video|url=https://edm.com/music-releases/tiesto-casey-frey-the-business-music-video|access-date=2020-12-24|website=EDM.com - The Latest Electronic Dance Music News, Reviews & Artists|language=en}}</ref>
