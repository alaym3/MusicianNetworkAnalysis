'''Deedee Lynn Magno Hall''' (born April 2, 1975)  is an American actress and singer. She is best known for her work in the pop group [[The Party (band)|The Party]], and as the voice of [[Pearl (Steven Universe)|Pearl]] in the animated series ''[[Steven Universe]]'' and its epilogue series ''[[Steven Universe Future]]''.

==Early life==
Born in [[Portsmouth, Virginia]], Magno-Hall is the child of a sailor in the [[United States Navy]] and a [[nurse]], both [[Philippines|Filipino-American]] immigrants. She grew up in [[San Diego|San Diego, California]], and [[Orlando, Florida]].<ref>{{cite news |last=Calindas |first=Marconi |date=August 18, 2009 |title=The proud Fil-Am from Miss Saigon to Wicked |url=http://www.gmanetwork.com/news/story/170075/pinoyabroad/the-proud-fil-am-from-miss-saigon-to-wicked |newspaper=GMA News |location=Philippines |access-date=March 28, 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150402145713/http://www.gmanetwork.com/news/story/170075/pinoyabroad/the-proud-fil-am-from-miss-saigon-to-wicked |archive-date=April 2, 2015 |url-status=live }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Farris |first1=Christopher |title=These Filipinos Are the Voices of Modern Cartoon and Anime Characters |url=https://www.esquiremag.ph/culture/movies-and-tv/filipino-voice-actors-a00288-20190211-lfrm2 |website=[[Esquire (magazine)]] |publisher=[[Hearst Communications]] |access-date=June 30, 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190708091145/https://www.esquiremag.ph/culture/movies-and-tv/filipino-voice-actors-a00288-20190211-lfrm2 |archive-date=July 8, 2019 |date=February 11, 2019 |url-status=live |df=mdy-all }}</ref>

==Career==
She began acting  at a young age, lending her voice to the Disney show ''[[Mickey Mouse Club|The All New Mickey Mouse Club]]'' (first recorded in 1988) from 1988-1991, and occasionally in 1992.<ref>{{cite news |last=Hinman |first=Catherine |date=April 24, 1989 |title=They're Hip, They're Hot, They're The Mouseketeers |url=http://articles.orlandosentinel.com/1989-04-24/lifestyle/8904245219_1_mickey-mouse-club-show-kids-club-kids/2 |newspaper=Orlando Sentinel |access-date=March 28, 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150402151047/http://articles.orlandosentinel.com/1989-04-24/lifestyle/8904245219_1_mickey-mouse-club-show-kids-club-kids/2 |archive-date=April 2, 2015 |url-status=live }}</ref> She also appears as Jasmine on the ''[[Disney's Aladdin: A Musical Spectacular]]'' soundtrack. She is credited as one of the students in the 1993 film, ''Sister Act 2''.

After ''The Party'', Magno went on to perform in musical theatre, most notable was her role of Kim in the Broadway production of ''[[Miss Saigon]]'', a role she later reprised on the national tour.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.ibdb.com/broadway-cast-staff/deedee-magno-hall-500016/#touring|title=Deedee Magno-Hall – Broadway Cast & Staff {{!}} IBDB|website=www.ibdb.com|access-date=April 25, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191110003110/https://www.ibdb.com/broadway-cast-staff/deedee-magno-hall-500016#touring|archive-date=November 10, 2019|url-status=live}}</ref> She later played the role of [[Nessarose]] on the first national tour of ''[[Wicked (musical)|Wicked]]'', from December 12, 2006 until November 16, 2008.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.ibdb.com/tour-production/wicked-emerald-city-500327|title=Wicked – Broadway Musical – Emerald City Tour {{!}} IBDB|website=www.ibdb.com|access-date=April 25, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180503235541/http://www.ibdb.com/tour-production/wicked-emerald-city-500327|archive-date=May 3, 2018|url-status=live}}</ref> She reprised the role in the [[San Francisco]] sit-down production at the [[Orpheum Theatre (San Francisco)|Orpheum Theatre]]. Performances began January 27, 2009 with an opening night of February 6, 2009.<ref>Gans, Andrew. [http://www.playbill.com/article/dromard-flynn-hall-and-rioseco-will-join-kassebaum-and-wicks-in-san-fran-wicked-com-156912# "Dromard, Flynn, Hall and Rioseco Will Join Kassebaum and Wicks in San Fran 'Wicked'] Playbill, January 13, 2009</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.wickedthemusical.com/page.php#WickedCitiesSanFranciscoCompany|title=Wicked the New Broadway Musical: San Francisco Company|access-date=January 4, 2010|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100117210159/http://www.wickedthemusical.com/page.php#WickedCitiesSanFranciscoCompany|archive-date=January 17, 2010|url-status=dead}}</ref> She played her final performance on September 5, 2010, when the production closed.<ref>{{Cite news|first=Andrew |last=Gans |title=Wicked Flies Out of San Francisco Sept. 5 |date=September 5, 2010 |url=https://www.playbill.com/article/wicked-flies-out-of-san-francisco-sept-5-com-171457 |work=[[Playbill]] |access-date=August 3, 2021}}</ref> She joined the ensemble for the US tour of ''[[If/Then]]'' in October 2015, listed as "Cathy and others" and was the understudy for Elizabeth and Kate.<ref name=":0" /> Hall starred in the East West Players production of ''[[Next to Normal]]'' as Diana Goodman from May 12 to June 18, 2017.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.broadwayworld.com/los-angeles/article/Deedee-Magno-Hall-and-Cliffton-Hall-to-Lead-NEXT-TO-NORMAL-at-East-West-Players-Cast-Creatives-Set-20170317|title=Deedee Magno-Hall and Cliffton Hall to Lead NEXT TO NORMAL at East West Players; Cast, Creatives Set!|author=BWW News Desk|website=BroadwayWorld.com|language=en|access-date=April 25, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190425024520/https://www.broadwayworld.com/los-angeles/article/Deedee-Magno-Hall-and-Cliffton-Hall-to-Lead-NEXT-TO-NORMAL-at-East-West-Players-Cast-Creatives-Set-20170317|archive-date=April 25, 2019|url-status=live}}</ref>

Magno Hall came to wider attention for providing the voice of the character of Pearl on the [[Cartoon Network]] program ''[[Steven Universe]]'' <ref>{{Citation|title=Steven Universe|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt3061046/|access-date=April 25, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190315032501/https://www.imdb.com/title/tt3061046/|archive-date=March 15, 2019|url-status=live}}</ref> in 2013, and the follow-up series ''[[Steven Universe Future]]''<ref>{{Citation|title=Steven Universe Future|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt3061046/|access-date=April 25, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190315032501/https://www.imdb.com/title/tt3061046/|archive-date=March 15, 2019|url-status=live}}</ref> in 2019. Magno Hall provided both the spoken and singing voice for the character.

==Personal life==
Magno Hall is married to Cliffton Hall,<ref>{{cite web |url=http://asianjournal.com/aj-magazines/deedee-magno-hall-on-a-passionate-career-family-favorite-roles%E2%80%AF/ |title=Deedee Magno Hall: On a passionate career, family & favorite roles - AJ Magazines |access-date=May 10, 2017 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180330202158/http://asianjournal.com/aj-magazines/deedee-magno-hall-on-a-passionate-career-family-favorite-roles%E2%80%AF/ |archive-date=March 30, 2018 |url-status=dead }}</ref> who has starred opposite her in both ''Miss Saigon'' and ''Wicked'' national tours, and as Fiyero in the San Francisco production of ''Wicked''. He also played the role of Dan Goodman alongside her in East West Player's production of ''Next to Normal''. Most recently, they played opposite each other as Donna Sheridan and Sam Carmichael in a Guam production of "Mamma Mia!" They have two sons named Kaeden and Brycen.<ref name="Honolulu Advertiser">{{cite web|url=http://the.honoluluadvertiser.com/article/2005/Sep/23/en/FP509230308.html|title=Castle alums stage own revue|first=Wayne|last=Harada|date=September 23, 2005|access-date=May 20, 2008|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180821223128/http://the.honoluluadvertiser.com/article/2005/Sep/23/en/FP509230308.html|archive-date=August 21, 2018|url-status=live}}</ref>
