'''Lennon Ray Louise Stella''' (born August 13, 1999) is a Canadian singer and actress. She portrayed Maddie Conrad on the musical-drama series ''[[Nashville (2012 TV series)|Nashville]]'' (2012–2018). Before going solo in 2018, Stella performed with her sister as the duo [[Lennon & Maisy]]. Stella released her debut EP ''[[Love, Me (EP)|Love, Me]]'' in 2018. Her debut studio album ''[[Three. Two. One.]]'' was released on April 24, 2020.

== Career ==
In January 2018, it was announced that Lennon had signed a record deal with [[Barry Weiss|Records]] and [[Columbia Records]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tasteofcountry.com/nashville-lennon-stella-record-deal-columbia-records/|title='NASHVILLE' STAR LENNON STELLA SIGNS RECORD DEAL|publisher=tasteofcountry.com|date=January 18, 2018|access-date=July 30, 2018}}</ref> In 2018, Lennon featured on the track "[[Polaroid (Jonas Blue, Liam Payne and Lennon Stella song)|Polaroid]]" with [[Jonas Blue]] and [[Liam Payne]]. On November 16, 2018, Lennon released her debut [[extended play|EP]] titled ''[[Love, Me (EP)|Love, Me]]'' featuring previously released singles "Bad", "Breakaway", and "Fortress". This EP kickstarted her solo music career.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Lennon Stella|url=https://open.spotify.com/artist/1cZQSpDsxgKIX2yW5OR9Ot|access-date=2020-12-04|website=Spotify|language=en}}</ref>

In May and June 2019, Lennon opened for [[Anne-Marie (singer)|Anne-Marie]] on her [[Speak Your Mind]] tour.{{citation needed|date=September 2019}} She collaborated with [[The Chainsmokers]] and [[Illenium]] for a single titled "[[Takeaway (song)|Takeaway]]" which was released on July 24, 2019.{{citation needed|date=September 2019}}

In 2019, Lennon toured as an opening act with [[5 Seconds of Summer]] for the Chainsmokers' ''World War Joy'' Tour.<ref>{{Cite magazine|date=2019-02-12|title=5SOS & The Chainsmokers' Tour Dates For 2019: See Them Here|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8498031/the-chainsmokers-tour-dates|access-date=2020-07-11|magazine=Billboard}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Bellete|first=Sarah|title=5SOS & The Chainsmokers headline the 'World War Joy' concert|url=https://vanderbilthustler.com/27499/featured/5sos-the-chainsmokers-headline-the-world-war-joy-concert/|access-date=2020-07-11|website=The Vanderbilt Hustler}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Aneed|first=Samantha|date=2019-02-12|title=5 Seconds of Summer, The Chainsmokers Announce North American Tour|url=https://www.beyondthestagemagazine.com/the-chainsmokers-annouces-north-american-tour-with-5-seconds-of-summer/|access-date=2020-07-11|website=Beyond The Stage Magazine|language=en-US}}</ref>

In February 2020, Lennon won a Juno Award<ref>{{Citation|title=Juno Award|date=2020-07-28|url=https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Juno_Award&oldid=970013380|work=Wikipedia|language=en|access-date=2020-08-05}}</ref> for Breakthrough Artist of the Year,<ref>{{Citation|title=Juno Award for Breakthrough Artist of the Year|date=2020-06-30|url=https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Juno_Award_for_Breakthrough_Artist_of_the_Year&oldid=965207371|work=Wikipedia|language=en|access-date=2020-08-05}}</ref> making this the first award she has received.

== Artistry ==
Lennon Stella is mainly described as an [[indie pop]] and [[Pop music|pop]] musician whose music is "[[Rhythm and blues|R&B]] tinged".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.pollstar.com/article/three-two-one-lennon-stellas-music-career-takes-off-143825|title=Three. Two. One. Lennon Stella's Music Career Takes Off|website=www.pollstar.com|access-date=2020-03-17}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thelineofbestfit.com/features/interviews/lennon-stella-nine-favourite-songs|title=Nine Songs: Lennon Stella|website=The Line of Best Fit|language=en|access-date=2020-03-17}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.onestowatch.com/blog/lennon-stellas-whirlwind-of-success-first|title=Lennon Stella's Whirlwind of Success: First Headline Tour, Debut Album, & Opening for The Chainsmokers/5SOS [Q&A]|website=[[Ones to Watch]]|access-date=2020-03-17}}</ref> Her debut EP is described as a dance-club record.<ref>{{Citation|title=Love, me - EP by Lennon Stella|date=November 16, 2018|url=https://music.apple.com/us/album/love-me-ep/1441469193|language=en-us|access-date=2020-03-17}}</ref>
