'''Josh Bruce''' (born June 28, 1991), professionally known as '''Bru-C''', is a British [[rapper|MC]] and rapper from Long Eaton, Derbyshire, England. But resides in Nottingham. <ref>https://www.rock-city.co.uk/gigs/bru-c/</ref> He is known for collaborating with other UK producers, including the [[Bad Boy Chiller Crew]], to create [[drum and bass]] and [[Bassline (music genre)|bassline]] music. He’s currently signed to CruCast.<ref>https://earth-agency.com/artists/bru-c/</ref>

Bru-C has been to various places, performing his music in countries such as Estonia, New Zealand and Australia. His new single ‘Mission’ is out on the 20th August 2021.
