'''Kolohe Kai''' is a Hawaiian [[reggae pop]] musical group, formed in 2009 by lead singer and songwriter Roman De Peralta. 

Kolohe Kai consists of De Peralta, Jasmine Moikeha (vocals), Kolomona Wilson-Ku (keyboard and [[saxophone]]), Luke Daddario (drummer), Imua Garza ([[guitar]]), and Kahale Morales [[Bass guitar|(bass guitar]]).

==History==
The group's 2009 debut album was ''This is the Life'', and their 2011 follow-up album was ''Love Town''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/album/love-town-mw0002115720|title=Love Town - Kolohe Kai &#124; Songs, Reviews, Credits|website=AllMusic}}</ref> Successful singles included "Cool Down" and "Ehu Girl". Their 2014 album ''Paradise'' reached third place on ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' magazine's Reggae Album chart.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/kolohe-kai-mn0002045482/biography|title=Kolohe Kai {{!}} Biography & History|website=AllMusic|language=en-us|access-date=2020-03-08}}</ref>

In 2019, Kolohe Kai released their fourth album, ''Summer To Winter'',<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/album/summer-to-winter-mw0003262488|title=Summer to Winter - Kolohe Kai &#124; Songs, Reviews, Credits|website=AllMusic}}</ref> which reached number one on ''Billboard''<nowiki/>'s Reggae Album chart.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/charts/reggae-albums/2019-03-02|title=Reggae Albums Chart|website=Billboard|access-date=2020-03-08}}</ref>
