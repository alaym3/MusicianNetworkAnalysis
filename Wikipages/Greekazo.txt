'''Alexander Cielma Miliarakis''' (born 30 March 2001), known professionally as '''Greekazo''', is a Swedish rapper.
<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.expressen.se/kronikorer/alex-schulman/samtalet-hos-malou-i-tv4-sammanfattar-var-samtid/|title=ALEX SCHULMAN: Samtalet hos Malou i TV4 sammanfattar vår samtid|website=Expressen|accessdate=12 May 2020}}</ref> Greekazo grew up in [[Hässelby]] in Stockholm and was born to parents of [[Poles in Sweden|Polish]] and [[Greeks in Sweden|Greek]] background.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://flamman.se/a/sist-vi-stallde-upp-pa-en-intervju-borjade-reportern-stalla-fragor-om-husbykravallerna|title = "Sist vi ställde upp på en intervju började reportern ställa frågor om Husbykravallerna"}}</ref> 

Greekazo started his career on 31 July 2019, with the music single "HotSpot", which has received over 23 million streams on Spotify.<ref name="auto">{{Cite web|url=https://www.kingsizemag.se/noje/programledare-pa-svt-hanar-malou-von-sivers-efter-greekazo-intervju/|title=Programledare på SVT hånar Malou Von Sivers efter Greekazo-intervju|accessdate=12 May 2020|website=kingsizemag.se}}</ref>

His other songs have been streamed over 60 million times on Spotify.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.expressen.se/noje/greekazo-om-miljonsuccen-varfor-ska-jag-ga-i-skolan/|title=Greekazo om miljonsuccén: 'Varför ska jag gå i skolan?'|website=Expressen|accessdate=12 May 2020}}</ref> He released his debut studio album ''Gör nu, tänk sen'' on 30 March 2020, which peaked at number three on the [[Swedish Albums Chart]].

In December 2019, Greekazo appeared in a much publicized interview with [[Malou von Sivers]] on her talkshow Malou efter tio broadcast on [[TV4 (Sweden)|TV4]]. He had been invited to talk about his career, but the interview soon turned into questions about his views on criminality and drugs. The interview received a lot of criticism and a debate about if one person can be held accountable for crime sprees in the suburbs.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://sverigesradio.se/sida/artikel.aspx?programid=103&artikel=7363456|title=Greekazo om Malou-intervjun: Tänkte, är det här ett polisförhör eller?|accessdate=12 May 2020|publisher=Sveriges Radio}}</ref><ref name="auto"/> Greekazo later received an invitation to come back to the show for another interview, but denied the request, since he believed he didn't have the need to "prove anything".<ref>{{Cite news|last=Emanuelsson|first=Felicia|date=29 April 2020|title=Efter omtalade intervjun – Greekazo ratar Malou|language=sv|work=[[Expressen]]|url=https://www.expressen.se/noje/efter-omtalade-intervjun-greekazo-ratar-malou/|url-status=live|access-date=16 May 2020}}</ref>
