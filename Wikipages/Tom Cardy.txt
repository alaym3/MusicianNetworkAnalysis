'''Tom Cardy'''  is an Australian comedian, singer, songwriter, musician, and actor from [[Sydney]], [[New South Wales]]. Best known for his Song Sequels segment on [[Triple J]],<ref name="Billie1">{{cite web |url=https://www.aria.com.au/charts/news/billie-eilish-holds-aria-charts-1-album-for-second-week-with-happier-than-ever/ |title=Billie Eilish holds ARIA Charts #1 album for second week with Happier Than Ever |publisher=[[Australian Recording Industry Association]] |date=13 August 2021 |access-date=2 December 2021 |archive-url=http://web.archive.org/web/20210813071621/https://www.aria.com.au/charts/news/billie-eilish-holds-aria-charts-1-album-for-second-week-with-happier-than-ever/ |archive-date=13 August 2021 |url-status=live}}</ref> Cardy has composed music for SBS ''[[The Feed (Australian TV series)|The Feed]]'' and ''[[The Moth Effect]]''. His debut [[extended play]], ''[[Artificial Intelligence (EP)|Artificial Intelligence]]'' (2021), debuted and peaked at number 40 on the [[ARIA Charts|ARIA Albums Chart]].

==Career==
===2013–2020: Early career===
Tom Cardy is an improviser and musician. Whilst studying psychology and music, Cardy wrote and performed for several [[Sydney University]] arts revues as well as his own sell out comedy festival shows.<ref name="improv">{{cite web |url=https://improvtheatresydney.com.au/speaker/tom-cardy/ |title=Tom Cardy |publisher=Improv Theatre Sydney |access-date=2 December 2021 |archive-url=http://web.archive.org/web/20201124224833/https://improvtheatresydney.com.au/speaker/tom-cardy/ |archive-date=24 November 2020 |url-status=live}}</ref>
