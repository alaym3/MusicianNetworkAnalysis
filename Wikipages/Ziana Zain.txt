'''Siti Roziana binti Zain''' (born 2 May 1968) is a Malaysian pop singer-songwriter, model, entrepreneur and actress. Her music career began in the early '90s with her signature single, "Madah Berhelah" followed by hits like "Terlerai Kasih", "Setia Ku Di Sini" and "Puncak Kasih". In 1995, she was crowned [[Voice of Asia]] in Kazakhstan.<ref>{{cite news |title=Ziana tak hilang trademark |url=http://www.nib.com.my/archives/text/view/46499022?pos=3&hide_header=1&resultset=nstpec%3Awww/archives/text/search.php%3A_1291986348%3Aresultset |date=5 April 2008 |work=[[Harian Metro]] |access-date=10 December 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Return of the Voice |url=http://www.nib.com.my/archives/text/view/43906087?pos=6&hide_header=1&resultset=nstpec%3Awww/archives/text/search.php%3A_1291986348%3Aresultset |date=12 March 2007 |work=[[Malay Mail]] | access-date=10 December 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Amazed by Anis |url=http://www.nib.com.my/archives/text/view/10225203?pos=11&hide_header=1&resultset=nstpec%3Awww/archives/text/search.php%3A_1291986348%3Aresultset |date=20 August 2005 |work=Malay Mail |access-date=10 December 2010}}</ref>

==Career==

===1990–1992===
Ziana Zain signed a contract to record an album under [[Bertelsmann Music Group|BMG]] Asia Pacific (which has now become [[BMG Music]]). In 1991, she released her debut album ''[[Madah Berhelah]]'' and the hits single is "Madah Berhelah". It is a song about a jilted lover who was deceived by empty promises delivered through poetry and sweet words. Her subsequent singles from the same album, Riwayat Cinta, Dalam Kesakitan ini and Rentak Hidupku totally sealed her place as a singer who has powerful voice with wide octave range.

===1993–1997===
Ziana's second album, ''[[Ziana Zain 1993|Ziana Zain]]'' was released in 1993 and sold more than 85,000 copies. The album featured one foreign language track, "Chitose Bashi". The singles of the album included "Anggapanmu" and "Putus Terpaksa".

Her third album, ''[[Setia Ku Di Sini]]'' was released later in 1995 and she worked very closely with local composers. The album was certified 3x Platinum by RIM with 175,000 copies being sold. The album garnered her the "Best Female Vocalist in an Album" award at 1996's [[Anugerah Industri Muzik]] award. Ziana released five singles from the album and the lead single, "Kemelut Di Muara Kasih", was another commercial success. Four other singles, "Bersama Akhirnya", "Setia Ku Di Sini", "Sangkar Cinta" and "Tiada Kepastian" and the famous track on the album is Tuduhan and Antara Ikhlas dan Paksa went to top significant charts across Malaysia, Indonesia, Singapore and Brunei. The song "Bersama Akhirnya" garnered her the Best Solo Performance award in Pujaan 10 Nescafe.

Ziana released her fourth album, ''[[Puncak Kasih]]'', on 28 May 1997. It won Best Pop Album in 1998's Anugerah Industri Muzik. The lead single of the album, "Puncak Kasih", was a success. It managed to stay at No. 1 in Carta Muzik Muzik for 15 consecutive weeks. The song also won Best Ballad Song in 1998's [[Anugerah Juara Lagu]].
The other single is Satu Detik, Kalau Mencari Teman and Ada Cinta. The Music Video Ada Cinta is shotting at car park in K.L.

===1998–2004===
Her comeback album was released in 2001 right after her pregnancy. The album, ''[[Aku Cintakan Mu]]'', sold more than 20,000 copies. The album won Best Album category in 2002's Anugerah Planet Muzik. The album received a nomination for "Best Female Vocalist in an Album" in 2002's Anugerah Industri Muzik. The album's lead single, "Menadah Gerimis", won Best Ballad Song category in 2002's Anugerah Juara Lagu award. It also won Best Song in Anugerah Planet Muzik that year, apart from being voted as the Best Pop Song Choice award in Anugerah Era. Two other singles from the album were released, namely "Bagai Gahara" and "Terkenang Jua". Both of the singles did not make it to the final round in Anugerah Juara Lagu. This album was Ziana's last studio album with BMG label.

===Career break: 2005–2008===
After the release of the album, Ziana took a career break for four years without releasing any studio albums.

===2008–2011===

She released a mini album in 2008 entitled ''Dingin''. The album featured four tracks and was nominated for Best Pop Song category in 2008's Anugerah Industri Muzik award.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.manggaonline.com.my/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=1586:aim-16-cermin-masalah-industri-muzik&catid=34:berita-selebriti&Itemid=53 |title=AIM 16 reflects the problem in music industry <!-- translated --> |last=Rosli |first=Manah |date=27 March 2009 |work=Mangga Online |access-date=8 June 2009}}</ref> The song also managed to get into the final round of 2008's Anugerah Juara Lagu in Best Ballad Song category. However, it was lost to [[Faizal Tahir]]'s ''"Sampai Syurga"''.{{Verify source |date=November 2010}}

In February 2009, it was confirmed that Ziana was pregnant with twins.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.murai.com.my/article/default.asp?article_id=4247&c=2&s=3&whichpage=4&pagesize=10 |title=Hamil Kembar Lelaki Dan Perempuan? |date=4 June 2009 |website=Murai |access-date=29 January 2010}}</ref> She gave birth to twins, a boy and a girl on 23 July 2009 in [[Shah Alam]].<ref>{{cite news |url=http://mstar.com.my/hiburan/cerita.asp?file=/2009/7/30/mstar_hiburan/20090729161819&sec=mstar_hiburan |title=Bayi kembar Ziana belum diberi nama, masih ditahan di hospital |date=30 July 2009 |work=Mstar Online |access-date=29 January 2010}}</ref>{{Verify source |date=November 2010}}

In January 2010, after six months of absence, finally Ziana returned to public as a surprise guest in the second annual gathering of Murai.com to celebrate the launching of a new fashion segment, ''Stailista'', in the web portal.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.murai.com.my/article/default.asp?article_id=5393&c=1&s=1 |title=Ziana Meriahkan Pelancaran Stailista |last=Thani |first=Hasbullah |date=15 January 2010 |work=Murai |access-date=29 January 2010}}</ref> In that event, she stated that she would release two new singles from her upcoming untitled album. The singles, ''Pawana'' and ''Dirimu Satu'', were expected to be released in June 2010.<ref name="Untitled album">{{cite news |url=http://www.kosmo.com.my/kosmo/content.asp?y=2010&dt=0118&pub=Kosmo&sec=Hiburan&pg=hi_01.htm |title=Ariel dan Kaseh si kembar Ziana |last=Faizal |first=Saharuni |date=29 January 2010 |work=Mstar online |access-date=29 January 2010}}</ref>

Furthermore, Ziana was one of the mentors in the fourth season of a singing competition called, ''Mentor''. As the singing competition ended in July 2010, Ziana and her protégé, Mohd were announced as the winner for that season. With that success, they managed to take home RM 100 000 and a trophy.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.budiey.com/mohd-ziana-zain-juara-mentor-musim-4/ |title=Ziana Zain and Mohd Champion for Mentor 4 <!-- translated --> |last=Budiey |first=Abg |date=5 July 2010 |work=Sensasi Selebriti |access-date=5 July 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.hmetro.com.my/myMetro/articles/Tugasangilagilamentor-protege/Article |title=Tugasan 'gila-gila' mentor-protege! |last=Rahman |first=Ridzuan |date=12 August 2010 |work=Harian Metro |access-date=12 August 2010 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100814125206/http://www.hmetro.com.my/myMetro/articles/Tugasangilagilamentor-protege/Article |archive-date=14 August 2010}}</ref>

For her acting career, Ziana appeared in her new film, ''Magika'', which was directed by Edry KRU. During the preview of the film, Ziana received positive reviews on her excellent acting.<ref name="Untitled album" /><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.gua.com.my/Entertainment/Feature-Detail.aspx?ArticleID=4455 |title=Ziana has returned <!-- translated --> |last=Nor Aida |first=Ismail |date=17 January 2009 |work=Gua.com.my |access-date=29 January 2010 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100123011931/http://www.gua.com.my/Entertainment/Feature-Detail.aspx?ArticleID=4455 |archive-date=23 January 2010 |url-status=dead |df=dmy-all }}</ref>

In addition, taking place in Cititel Hotel, [[Kuala Lumpur]], Ziana Zain had been announced as a spokesperson for Slimworld Beauty House. This was the seventh years she was appointed as the ambassador of the beauty house.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.utusan.com.my/utusan/info.asp?y=2010&dt=0312&pub=Utusan_Malaysia&sec=Hiburan&pg=hi_01.htm |title=Ziana hampir hilang nyawa |last=Shazryn |first=Mohd Faizal |date=12 March 2010 |work=Utusan Malaysia |access-date=12 March 2010}}</ref>

Apart from singing, acting and being product ambassador, Ziana is also involved in theatrical performance. Together with fellow singer [[Misha Omar]], she was cast in the second season of a musical theatre production entitled ''Teater Muzikal Gamat 2''. The performance, which commenced on 12 March 2010, was held in the Auditorium of Malaysian Tourism Centre (MaTiC), Kuala Lumpur, for 11 consecutive days.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.murai.com.my/article/default.asp?article_id=5576&c=1&s=1 |title=Geli Gamat Tarik Minat Misha, Ziana |last=Thani |first=Hasbullah |date=17 February 2010 |work=Murai |access-date=18 February 2010}}</ref> This was Ziana's second participation in theatre after ''Musical Theater Antara''.

In 2011, Ziana Zain made some controversial remarks on Malaysian artist associations where she said the participation of Malaysian celebrities in these associations is just a waste of time. According to her, associations such as the Artists Association of Malaysia (Seniman), Karyawan Association (Karyawan) and Papita only serve the interests of a selected few and therefore should be disbanded.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.mohdrohaizad.com/2011/07/ziana-zain-kutuk-persatuan-seni-hiburan.html |title=Ziana Zain Kutuk Persatuan Seni Hiburan Malaysia |work=mohdrohaizad.com |access-date=8 July 2011}}</ref>

===2012-Present===
In April 2014, Zain announced her solo concert, ''I Am Ziana Zain'' will be held in September 2014. The concert was scheduled to be held at her home state, [[Malacca International Trade Centre]]. This will be her first solo concert after 15 years.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.rotikaya.com/lebih-sedekad-tanpa-konsert-solo-ziana-zain-janji-yang-terbaik/ |title=Lebih Sedekad Tanpa Konsert Solo, Ziana Zain Janji Yang Terbaik |work=RotiKaya.com |access-date=6 March 2015}}</ref> Unfortunately, due to "organizer's internal problems" the concert was cancelled.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.murai.com.my/berita/tempatan/article/articleid/559512/konsert-i-am-ziana-zain-batal-kerana-masalah-dalaman-penaja/ |title=Konsert I Am Ziana Zain Batal Kerana Masalah Dalaman Penaja |work=Murai.com |access-date=6 March 2015}}</ref>

On 15 July 2014, Zain stepped out in a new look, wearing full hijab<ref name="New chapter for Ziana Zain">{{cite news |url=http://www.therakyatpost.com/life/entertainment-life/2014/10/25/new-chapter-ziana-zain/ |title=New chapter for Ziana Zain |work=The Rakyat Post |access-date=6 March 2015}}</ref> with the approval of media outlets and fans. Zain also revealed in an interview that her upcoming album is in the works. She is working with [[Faizal Tahir]] and [[Awi Rafael]] for the new album.<ref name="New chapter for Ziana Zain"/>

==Acting career==
[[File:Erra Fazira, Amy Mastura and Ziana Zain on MeleTOP.jpg|thumb|ltr:Malaysian singers and actresses [[Erra Fazira]], [[Amy Mastura]] and Ziana Zain on ''[[MeleTOP]]'' in 2015]]
In addition to her music career, Ziana also diverted her focus into acting. Her acting career transpired when she was approached by Dato' Yusof Haslam. She agreed to appear in his film ''[[Sembilu]]'',<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.filemkita.com/filem/s/sembilu_01.html |title=Sembilu |author=Kamal |date=12 August 1994 |work=www.filemkita.com |access-date=12 August 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.sinemamalaysia.com.my/film/info/?id=Sembilu_159 |title=Sembilu 1994 |author=Kamal |date=12 August 1994 |work=www.sinemamalaysia.com |access-date=12 August 2010 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110623005835/http://www.sinemamalaysia.com.my/film/info/?id=Sembilu_159 |archive-date=23 June 2011 |url-status=dead |df=dmy-all }}</ref> as a supporting actress with the role "Ziana". The film marked her debut as an actress. It was a successful box-office, grossing RM4.2 million.<ref name="Ziana Zain Achievements">{{cite news |url=http://www.fortunecity.com/campus/newlandavenue/768/interest2.htm |title=Ziana Zain Achievements |date=19 February 2008 |work=Celebrity Pujaanku |access-date=23 September 2009 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120319042501/http://www.fortunecity.com/campus/newlandavenue/768/interest2.htm |archive-date=19 March 2012 |url-status=dead |df=dmy-all }}</ref> The film also helped Ziana establish herself as a successful actress, especially after winning the Most Popular Supporting Actress category in 1994's ''Anugerah Bintang Popular'' Awards, alongside her co-star [[Erra Fazira]] who was catapulted into fame after her debut appearance through this film. The film's soundtrack also featured a number of Ziana's songs including "Ku Cinta Padamu", "Anggapanmu" and "Mimpimu Bukan Mimpiku". In the 1996 ''Sembilu'' follow-up, ''Sembilu II'',<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.filemkita.com/filem/s/sembilu2_01.html |title=Sembilu II |author=Kamal |date=12 August 1995 |work=www.filemkita.com |access-date=12 August 2010}}</ref> Ziana reprised her role to critical claim.{{Verify source |date=November 2010}}

Other box-office films that Ziana has performed include ''[[Maria Mariana]]'' (1996),<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.filemkita.com/filem/m/maria_mariana_01.html |title=Maria Mariana |author=Kamal |date=12 August 1996 |work=www.filemkita.com |access-date=12 August 2010}}</ref> ''Merah'' (1997),<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.filemkita.com/filem/m/merah_01.html |title=Merah |author=Kamal |date=12 August 1997 |work=www.filemkita.com |access-date=12 August 2010}}</ref> ''[[Maria Mariana II]]'' (1998),<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.filemkita.com/filem/m/maria_mariana2_01.html |title=Maria Mariana II |author=Kamal |date=12 August 1998 |work=www.filemkita.com |access-date=12 August 2010}}</ref> ''Sembilu 2005'' (2005),<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.filemkita.com/filem/s/sembilu2005_01.html |title=Sembilu 2005 |author=Kamal |date=24 March 2005 |work=www.filemkita.com |access-date=12 August 2010}}</ref> ''Pontianak Menjerit'' (2005),<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.filemkita.com/filem/p/pontianak_menjerit_01.html |title=Pontianak Menjerit |author=Kamal |date=16 June 2005 |work=www.filemkita.com |access-date=12 August 2010}}</ref> and ''Qabil Khusry Qabil Igam'' (2007).<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.filemkita.com/filem/q/qabil_khushry_qabil_igam_01.html |title=Qabil Kushry Qabil Igam |author=Kamal |date=16 June 2007 |work=www.filemkita.com |access-date=12 August 2010}}</ref> Besides acting in film, Ziana also appeared in television dramas. Her first drama acting was directed by Erma Fatima entitled ''Cermin Pada Bulan''.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.nib.com.my/archives/text/view/46194415?pos=386&hide_header=1&resultset=nstpec%3Awww/cross-search/search.php%3A_1281836681%3Aresultset |title=Bosan Cara Lama |date=28 August 2007 |work=Harian Metro |access-date=12 August 2010}}</ref> Ziana was the leading actress in the drama and her performance in the drama generally received notable compliments. In 2007, Ziana varied her acting skill by accepting a role in a comedy drama entitled ''4 Diva Sekampung'',<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.nib.com.my/archives/text/view/46115174?pos=398&hide_header=1&resultset=nstpec%3Awww/cross-search/search.php%3A_1281836681%3Aresultset |title=Yang Kasim Berlakon Filem Tamil |date=17 September 2007 |work=Harian Metro |access-date=12 August 2010}}</ref> which is about four divas who get stuck in their way back to their hometown because of their van's breakdown. The drama was screened during Hari Raya. Ziana also appeared as a leading actress in ''Tetamu Allah'' as well as her latest drama which is ''Nur Kasih di Jabal Rahmah''. Both dramas were directed by Erma Fatima.
