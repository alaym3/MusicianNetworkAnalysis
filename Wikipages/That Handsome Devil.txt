'''That Handsome Devil''', often shortened to THD, is an American [[rock music|rock]] band from [[Brooklyn]], [[New York (state)|New York]], by way of [[Boston]], [[Massachusetts]].

The band mixes genres such as [[Rock music|rock]], [[funk]], [[jazz]], [[Swing music|jive]],<ref name=":1">{{Cite web |title=That Handsome Devil (CD) |url=http://www.amplifiermagazine.com/reviews/cds/that_handsome_devil_cd.php |last=Zimmerman |first=Lee |date=October 24, 2006 |website=Amplifier Magazine |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070127094555/http://www.amplifiermagazine.com/reviews/cds/that_handsome_devil_cd.php |archive-date=January 27, 2007 |access-date=May 31, 2020}}</ref> [[blues]], [[Surf music|surf]], [[rhythm and blues]], [[reggae]],<ref>{{Cite web |title=That Handsome Devil - That Handsome Devil |url=https://www.allmusic.com/album/that-handsome-devil-mw0001479138 |last=Greene |first=Jo-Ann |website=AllMusic |language=en-us |access-date=May 31, 2020}}</ref> [[rockabilly]], [[rapping|rap]], and [[psychedelic music|psychedelic]]. Their sound has also been described as "equal parts [[Screamin' Jay Hawkins|Screamin’ Jay Hawkins]], bizarre [[electronica]], creepy [[hip hop]] and in-your-face gonzo rock".<ref>{{Cite web |title=Le Diamant Brut: That Handsome Devil & WinoVino |url=http://austinist.com/2008/09/17/le_diamant_brut_that_handsome_devil.php |last=Mills |first=William |date=September 17, 2008 |website=Austinist |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090312065929/http://austinist.com/2008/09/17/le_diamant_brut_that_handsome_devil.php |archive-date=March 12, 2009 |access-date=May 31, 2020}}</ref> [[Gypsy jazz]] has been used in description of the band while frontman Godforbid describes the band's music as "fringe pop".<ref>{{Cite web |title=That Handsome Devil - A City Dressed in Dynamite |url=http://www.staticmultimedia.com/music/reviews/that_handsome_devil_-_a_city_dressed_in_dynamite |last=Porter |first=Bill |date=August 18, 2008 |website=Static Multimedia |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090203212626/http://www.staticmultimedia.com/music/reviews/that_handsome_devil_-_a_city_dressed_in_dynamite |archive-date=February 3, 2009 |access-date=May 31, 2020}}</ref>

==History==
The band was founded by Godforbid and Jeremy Page. Predating the band's existence, they were both members of the hip hop group Alaskan Fishermen, along with Thirstin' Howl III and Father Time.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Alaskan Fishermen |url=https://myspace.com/alaskanfishermen |website=Myspace |access-date=January 31, 2020}}</ref> That Handsome Devil released their first single "Dating Tips" in 2004, which featured the songs "Dating Tips", "How to Get Money", and "Don't Go".<ref>{{Cite web |title=That Handsome Devil - 'Dating Tips/How To Get Money/Don't Go' |url=http://www.undergroundhiphop.com/store/detail.asp?=That-Handsome-Devil-Dating-Tips-How-To-Get-Money-Dont-Go-Art-And-Craft-Music&UPC=AAC120112 |website=UndergroundHipHop |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090312030217/http://www.undergroundhiphop.com/store/detail.asp?=That-Handsome-Devil-Dating-Tips-How-To-Get-Money-Dont-Go-Art-And-Craft-Music&UPC=AAC120112 |archive-date=March 12, 2009 |access-date=January 31, 2020}}</ref> In 2006, the band released their eponymous debut EP ''That Handsome Devil'', which included the single "Dating Tips". "Elephant Bones", the fourth track on the album, was featured as a bonus song in ''[[Guitar Hero II]]''.

Their first studio album, ''[[A City Dressed in Dynamite]]'', was released in 2008 to several positive reviews, being praised by Amplifier Magazine as "a madcap musical frenzy as dizzying as it is dazzling".<ref name=":1" /><ref>{{Cite web |title=That Handsome Devil {{!}} Listen and Stream Free Music, Albums, New Releases, Photos, Videos |url=https://myspace.com/thathandsomedevil |website=That Handsome Devil |access-date=January 31, 2020}}</ref> With music as stylistically varied as their last release, the album expanded on the band's themes of [[emptiness]], [[Addiction|drug addiction]], [[materialism]], and [[death]], and featured their trademark of dated [[Sound bite|sound bites]] from the 1950s.

A portion of "Mexico" was featured in the seventh episode of season four of [[Weeds (TV series)|''Weeds'']], "Yes I Can", before ''A City Dressed in Dynamite'' was released.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Showtime |url=http://www.sho.com/site/video/brightcove/audio/home.do?bcpid=1329229918 |website=Showtime |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090309210737/http://www.sho.com/site/video/brightcove/audio/home.do?bcpid=1329229918 |archive-date=March 9, 2009 |access-date=May 31, 2020}}</ref>

"Rob the Prez-O-Dent" was featured as a playable track in ''[[Rock Band 2]]''.<ref name=":0" /> This, along with the appearance of "Elephant Bones" in ''Guitar Hero II'', greatly increased their visibility.{{To whom?|date=January 2020}}

In 2009, their second EP, ''Enlightenment's For Suckers,'' was released.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Enlightenment's For Suckers |url=https://www.amazon.com/dp/B002JDM374 |date=August 25, 2009 |website=Amazon |access-date=January 31, 2020}}</ref>

That Handsome Devil went on a national tour named "Hating New People" in the summer of 2010, where they sold small-print discs of previously unreleased singles and covers.{{Citation needed|date=January 2020}}

In 2011, That Handsome Devil released their third album, ''[[The Heart Goes to Heaven, The Head Goes to Hell]]'', to favorable reviews from fans and critics. The band supported the album with a tour and the launch of an independent website.<ref>{{Cite web |title=That Handsome Devil |url=http://www.thathandsomedevil.com:80/thdhome.cfm |website=That Handsome Devil |language=en-US |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130306051948/http://www.thathandsomedevil.com:80/thdhome.cfm |archive-date=March 6, 2013 |access-date=January 31, 2020}}</ref> The album was financed in part by a fundraising effort on project startup service [[Kickstarter]], raising $6,013 after a month.<ref>{{Cite web|title=That Handsome Devil|url=https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/969314148/that-handsome-devil|last=|first=|date=|website=Kickstarter|language=en|access-date=June 1, 2020}}</ref>

That Handsome Devil announced their fourth EP, ''The Jungle Book'', in 2012, available for free on their website. The album consists of covers of several songs from the original Disney movie.

In September 2014, That Handsome Devil released the first single, "$=❤", from their forthcoming album. In November, The band released the album ''Drugs & Guns For Everyone'' on their YouTube channel.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Drugs & Guns For Everyone |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=akV5cWQHMko |last=That Handsome Devil |date=November 24, 2014 |website=YouTube |language=en |access-date=January 31, 2020}}</ref>
