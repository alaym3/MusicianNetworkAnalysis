}}'''No Clear Mind''' is a Greek music trio that formed in 2006 in Chania, Greece by multi-instrumentalists Vasilis Dokakis, Lefteris Volanis and Kostas Rovlias. No Clear Mind have released three studio albums,<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://inner-ear.gr/artists/no-clear-mind/|title=Inner Ear Records - No Clear Mind|last=|first=|date=|website=Inner Ear Records|publisher=Periklis Pilavas|access-date=}}</ref> ''Dream is Destiny'' in 2012, ''Mets'' in 2013, and ''Makena'' in 2016.

==History==
The band played its debut shows in Greece and Italy in 2008 and are usually joined by a number of fellow musicians for their gigs. They have toured in Europe, played a number of festivals and opened for popular artists such as [[Ulrich Schnauss]] and [[A Whisper in the Noise]]. Their music received enthusiastic reviews and coverage,<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://thepostergirl.co/no-clear-mind/|title=No Clear Mind Mets|last=Suaza|first=Alejandra|date=|website=|publisher=|access-date=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://notedmusic.co.uk/2013/06/26/spotlight-no-clear-mind-mets/|title=Spotlight: No Clear Mind - Mets|last=Powell|first=Isaac|date=|website=Noted|publisher=Noted|access-date=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.sputnikmusic.com/bands/No-Clear-Mind/48108/|title=Sputnik Music - No Clear Mind|last=|first=|date=|website=|publisher=|access-date=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.sonofmarketing.it/mets-no-clear-mind/|title=Son of Marketing - Mets Review|last=Esposito|first=Mario|date=|website=Son of Marketing|publisher=|access-date=}}</ref> while [[The xx|The XX]] have previously featured them in one of their music blog post series in 2010.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://xx-xx.co.uk/post/13303470305/no-clear-mind-dream-is-destiny|title=The XX Official Website|website=thexx|access-date=2016-06-11}}</ref>
