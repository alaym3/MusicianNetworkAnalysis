'''Charlie Burg''' is an American singer-songwriter and producer. He has released a mixtape, ''Blue Wave Mosaic'', and a series of extended plays.

== Early life and education ==
Burg was born in [[Detroit]], Michigan.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.altrevue.com/single-post/2018/11/23/Artist-Spotlight-Charlie-Burg|title=Artist Spotlight - Charlie Burg|last=La Torre|first=Michael|date=November 23, 2018|website=Alt Revue|language=en|access-date=2019-06-18}}</ref> He attended [[Denison University]] for his freshman year of college, before transferring to [[Michigan State University]]'s [[Residential College in Arts and Humanities]], where he studied humanities and English. After his sophomore year, he transferred again to the [[Setnor School of Music]] at [[Syracuse University]] to major in music industry.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://studybreaks.com/students/syracuse-university-charlie-burg/|title=Syracuse University's Charlie Burg Produces an Eclectic Style of Music|last=Hynes Brody|first=Nola|date=2018-04-26|website=Study Breaks|language=en-US|access-date=2019-06-18}}</ref>

== Career ==
Burg describes his music as R&B/indie rock.<ref name=":0" /> He cites early influences to his music as [[Al Green]], [[Marvin Gaye]], and [[The Temptations]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://dailyorange.com/2018/03/charlie-burg-gears-release-2nd-3-part-ep-series/|title=Charlie Burg gears up to release 2nd of 3-part EP series|last=Niles|first=Lydia|date=2018-03-05|website=[[The Daily Orange]]|language=en-US|access-date=2019-06-18}}</ref> Burg was inspired by [[Ralph Waldo Emerson]]'s poems in his newest extended plays.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=http://www.onestowatch.com/blog/charlie-burg-is-transforming-literary-inspiration|title=Charlie Burg Is Transforming Literary Inspiration Into Stunning Music|last=Polo|first=Maxamillion|date=January 24, 2019|website=Ones To Watch|access-date=2019-06-18}}</ref>
