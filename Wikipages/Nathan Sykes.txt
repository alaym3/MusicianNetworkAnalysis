'''Nathan James Sykes''' (born 18 April 1993)<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.biography.com/people/nathan-sykes-21174341|publisher=biography.com|title=Nathan Sykes Biography|date=12 March 2015}}</ref> is an English singer, songwriter and record producer. He is best known for being the youngest member of the British [[boy band]] [[The Wanted]]. In 2013, Sykes appeared with his bandmates in the [[E!]] channel reality television series ''[[The Wanted Life]]''.<ref>{{cite web|last=Siegel|first=Melissa|url=http://www.hollywoodtake.com/nathan-sykes-nearly-quit-wanted-life-why-did-wanted-singer-want-out-e-reality-show-video-8853|title=NATHAN SYKES NEARLY QUIT 'A WANTED LIFE'! WHY DID WANTED SINGER WANT OUT OF E! REALITY SHOW? [VIDEO]|publisher=hollywoodtake.com|date=24 June 2013}}</ref> After the band's hiatus in 2014, Sykes embarked on a solo career. His debut single, "[[Kiss Me Quick (Nathan Sykes song)|Kiss Me Quick]]" was released in July 2015. It reached number-one on the US [[Dance Club Songs]] chart.<ref name="Nathan Sykes">{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/charts/dance-club-play-songs|title=Dance Club Songs. The Week of August 22, 2015|work=Billboard|date=11 August 2015|access-date=11 August 2015}}</ref>

==Early life==
Sykes was born on 18 April 1993 to Harry and Karen Sykes. He has a younger sister, Jessica Sykes, who is also a singer.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://m.gloucestercitizen.co.uk/VIDEO-Nathan-Sykes-sister-Jess-proves-singing/story-19609772-detail/story.html|archive-url=https://archive.today/20150315030420/http://m.gloucestercitizen.co.uk/VIDEO-Nathan-Sykes-sister-Jess-proves-singing/story-19609772-detail/story.html|url-status=dead|archive-date=15 March 2015|title=VIDEO: Move over Nathan Sykes, sister Jess proves singing talent|publisher=[[Gloucester Citizen]]|date=8 March 2013}}</ref> Sykes attended [[Sylvia Young Theatre School]] and [[Ribston Hall High School]] sixth form.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.glamourmagazine.co.uk/nathan-sykes|title=Nathan Sykes|work=[[Glamour (magazine)|Glamour]]|date=12 March 2015|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141202184020/http://www.glamourmagazine.co.uk/nathan-sykes|archive-date=2 December 2014|df=dmy-all}}</ref>

Sykes started singing when he was six years old. In 2003, he won various competitions, including [[Britney Spears]]' Karaoke Kriminals and one of the categories at the Cheltenham Competitive Festival of Dramatic Art. In 2004, he appeared on [[ITV (TV network)|ITV]]'s ''[[Holly & Stephen's Saturday Showdown|Ministry of Mayhem]]'', and won the Door Youth Project's "Undiscovered Youth Talent Contest" where he performed "[[Mack the Knife]]" in Derker, and he attempted to represent [[United Kingdom in the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2004|United Kingdom]] in the [[Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2004]] that was held in [[Lillehammer]]. In the finals, he finished in third place with the song, "Born to Dance", behind Cory Spedding and runner-up Andrew Merry. In 2008, he performed for the music competition "[[Live and Unsigned]]".

==Career==
===2009–2014: The Wanted===
{{main|The Wanted}}
[[File:Nathan sykes.jpg|thumb|Sykes at Changi Airport, Singapore (2010)]]
In 2009, at the age of 16, Sykes auditioned for the boy band The Wanted. The auditions were held by Jayne Collins. Sykes was chosen to be one of those five members for a group. He joined bandmates [[Max George (singer)|Max George]], [[List of The Wanted members#Siva Kaneswaran|Siva Kaneswaran]], [[Tom Parker (singer)|Tom Parker]] and [[Jay McGuiness]]. The band achieved 10 UK top 10 singles as well as 3 UK top 10 albums. The group has sold more than 12 million records worldwide. After enjoying much mainstream international success, in 2014, the band decided to take a break and pursue their personal endeavours after a tour of the United Kingdom and the United States.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mtv.co.uk/the-wanted/news/nathan-sykes-the-wanted-split-will-be-lengthy|title=Nathan Sykes: 'The Wanted Split Will Be Lengthy'|publisher=[[MTV]]|date=1 March 2014}}</ref>

At the same time, Sykes and American singer [[Ariana Grande]] released their collaborative single, "[[Almost Is Never Enough]]".<ref>{{cite web|last=Vena|first=Joselyn|url=http://www.mtv.com/news/1711236/ariana-grande-nathan-sykes-almost-never-enough-mortal-instruments/|title=Ariana Grande Admits The Wanted's Nathan Sykes 'Sang The Hell Out Of' Duet|publisher=[[MTV]]|date=25 August 2013}}</ref> In 2014, Sykes signed a record deal with Global Entertainment (a part of the Global Group, along with [[Capital (radio network)|Capital]]'s owner [[Global Radio]]).<ref>{{cite web| url =http://www.thisisglobal.com/entertainment/music-and-management/#nathan-sykes |title=Nathan Sykes|access-date=2 July 2015}}</ref> In June 2014, he joined singer [[Jessie J]] onstage for Capital's [[Summertime Ball]] to perform "[[Calling All Hearts (song)|Calling All Hearts]]".<ref>{{cite web|last=Davidson|first=Amy|url=http://www.digitalspy.co.uk/music/news/a580098/watch-nathan-sykes-of-the-wanted-join-jessie-j-and-dj-cassidy-on-stage.html|title=Watch Nathan Sykes of The Wanted join Jessie J and DJ Cassidy on stage|work=[[Digital Spy]]|date=24 June 2014}}</ref> 

Sykes reunited with his band mates on 20th of September 2021, seven years since their hiatus in support of band mate [[Tom Parker (singer)|Tom Parker]] for his charity concert 'Tom Parker: Inside My Head' in aid of raising money for [[Stand Up To Cancer]] and The National Brain Appeal at the [[Royal Albert Hall]].

The Wanted's first record since 2014; Rule The World will be released 13th September 2021 to accompany their greatest hits record 'Most Wanted - The Greatest Hits' out 12th November 2021.

===2015–present: ''Unfinished Business''===
In March 2015, Sykes debuted his first solo music video for "More Than You'll Ever Know" on [[Vevo]].<ref>{{cite web|last=Roth|first=Madeline|url=http://www.mtv.com/news/2104532/nathan-sykes-more-than-youll-ever-know-video/|title=Nathan Sykes Oozes Sex Appeal in New Video: Watch|publisher=[[MTV]]|date=12 March 2015}}</ref> Sykes went on his first solo tour on 8 April 2015, which included six sold-out shows in the United Kingdom. Sykes announced via the app Periscope that his debut single would be called "[[Kiss Me Quick (Nathan Sykes song)|Kiss Me Quick]]".<ref>{{cite web|last=Sykes|first=Nathan|url=https://instagram.com/p/1Oeey1GI9x/|title=Nathan Sykes Announces Debut Single 'Kiss Me Quick released in June'|date=8 April 2015}}</ref> The single was released on 5 July 2015.{{citation needed|date=October 2017}}<!-- WP:RS needed; removed link to WP:SPS (Twitter, FB, WP)--> On 21 May 2015, Sykes released his music video for his debut single, "Kiss Me Quick" on Vevo.<ref>{{cite web|last=Sykes|first=Nathan|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0tj4ye_3fAQ|title=Nathan Sykes releases music video for debut single 'Kiss Me Quick'|date=21 May 2015}}</ref> The song peaked at number 14 on the [[UK Singles Chart]].

In October 2015, Sykes announced his second single, called "[[Over and Over Again]]". The music video for the song was released in the UK on 28 October 2015. It was released worldwide on 20 November 2015. It was also announced that Sykes would support [[Little Mix]] on their [[The Get Weird Tour]] in March 2016. Sykes announced that his third single, called "[[Give It Up (Nathan Sykes song)|Give It Up]]", would be released on 13 May 2016.{{citation needed|date=October 2017}}<!-- WP:RS needed; removed link to WP:SPS (Twitter, FB, WP)--> The music video for "Give It Up" was released on 27 April 2016, and was later banned from television after featuring numerous scenes which came across as unsuitable for most audiences.

On 11 June 2016, Sykes performed at Capital's [[Summertime Ball]] for the seventh consecutive time (2010, 2011, 2012, 2013 with The Wanted, 2014 as Jessie J's guest, 2015 and 2016 as a solo performer).<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.capitalfm.com/artists/nathan-sykes/news/summertime-ball-lineup-2015/|title=Kiss Us Quick Nathan! The UK Singing Sensation Joins Our Summertime Ball Line Up|date=22 May 2015|work=Capital}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.capitalfm.com/artists/nathan-sykes/news/summertime-ball-2016-set-list/|title=Nathan Sykes Gives The #CapitalSTB Crowd A Surprise Duet… With X Factor’s Louisa Johnson!|website=Capitalfm.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://nathansykesofficial.com/news/2016/nathan-sykes-performs-to-an-over-80-000-people-crowd-at-the-capital-fm-summertime-ball|title=Nathan Sykes – Nathan Sykes performs to over 80,000 people at the Capital FM Summertime Ball!!|website=Nathansykesofficial.com}}</ref>

On 11 July 2016, Sykes announced via Twitter that he would be joining [[Alessia Cara]] on her ''[[Know-It-All]]'' headling tour in the United States.{{citation needed|date=October 2017}}<!-- WP:RS needed; removed link to WP:SPS (Twitter, FB, WP)--> On 12 October 2016, Sykes announced via Twitter that his debut studio album, ''[[Unfinished Business (Nathan Sykes album)|Unfinished Business]]'', would be out worldwide on 11 November 2016.{{citation needed|date=October 2017}}<!-- WP:RS needed; removed link to WP:SPS (Twitter, FB, WP)-->

==Personal life==
In the summer of 2012 he dated [[Dionne Bromfield]],<ref>{{cite web|last=Sperling|first=Daniel|url=http://m.digitalspy.co.uk/showbiz/news/a473670/dionne-bromfield-confirms-nathan-sykes-split-no-hard-feelings.html#~p6OLtLxKXRjfl4|title=Dionne Bromfield confirms Nathan Sykes split: 'No hard feelings'|work=[[Digital Spy]]|date=16 April 2013|access-date=13 March 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150725134032/http://m.digitalspy.co.uk/showbiz/news/a473670/dionne-bromfield-confirms-nathan-sykes-split-no-hard-feelings.html#~p6OLtLxKXRjfl4|archive-date=25 July 2015|url-status=dead}}</ref> he then dated [[Ariana Grande]] from August to December 2013.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.mirror.co.uk/3am/celebrity-news/ariana-grande-opens-up-split-3099819|title=Ariana Grande opens up about split from Nathan Sykes and says it happened 'a while ago'|last=Pocklington|first=Rebecca|date=31 January 2014|work=mirror|access-date=6 May 2017}}</ref> On his twentieth birthday, 18 April 2013, Nathan went in for surgery to remove a nodule in his throat. During episodes of ''The Wanted Life'', he talked about his throat hurting and burning. The day manager, Nano, had him see a specialist, who concluded he had a nodule on his throat and suggested that it be removed. When he recovered, he sang his solo in "I Found You" at the Summertime Ball in 2013, surprising fans when he appeared.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Sampson |first1=Izzy |title=The Wanted's Nathan Sykes talks about his throat surgery: "I was pretty scared" |url=https://www.mirror.co.uk/3am/celebrity-news/wanteds-nathan-sykes-talks-throat-1949942 |website=Mirror.co.uk |access-date=13 September 2018}}</ref>
