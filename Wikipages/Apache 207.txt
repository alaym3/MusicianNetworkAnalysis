'''Volkan Yaman''' ({{IPA-tr|voɫkan jaˈman}}; born 23 October 1997), known professionally as '''Apache 207''', is a German rapper of Turkish descent<ref>{{cite web|url=https://rap.de/soundandvideo/148030-apache-207-kein-problem-video/|title=Apache 207 – Kein Problem (prod. Worstbeatz)|last=S.|first=Yannik|website=rap.de|date=24 August 2019|accessdate=27 October 2019}}</ref> from [[Ludwigshafen]]. He became best known for his breakthrough hit "[[Roller (Apache 207 song)|Roller]]" in August 2019, which would be the first of several more Top 10 hits.

==Life and career==
Little about Apache 207's private life is known to the public. He finished high school in 2017 with a high-school diploma in Ludwigshafen.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mannheim24.de/region/apache-207-deutschraps-naechstes-grosses-ding-dieser-rapper-geht-durch-decke-12277600.html|title=Apache 207: Neues Musikvideo „Roller“ nach einem Tag schon 1 Millionen mal aufgerufen!|website=mannheim24.de|language=de|date=5 April 2019|accessdate=27 October 2019}}</ref>
The rapper started his mainstream career in June 2018 when he released his debut single "Kleine Hure" still without label support.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://hiphop.de/magazin/hintergrund/apache-207-deutschraps-miroslav-klose-geht-in-offensive-319803|title=Apache 207: Deutschraps Miroslav Klose fängt gerade erst an|last=Senger|first=Clark|website=hiphop.de|language=de|date=24 June 2019|accessdate=27 October 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://eventseeker.com/artist/90475-apache-207|title=Apache 207 - Artist Profile|last=|first=|date=|website=eventseeker.com|language=en|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2020-03-24}}</ref> The song was followed up by seven other singles, released over the course of six months.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://diffusmag.de/p/apache-207-mehr-als-ein-phaenomen|title=Apache 207: Mehr als ein Phänomen|author=Jxnosch|website=diffusmag.de|language=de|date=24 April 2019|accessdate=27 October 2019}}</ref> In early 2019, he was signed to musician [[Bausa (rapper)|Bausa]]'s label TwoSides.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://hiphop.de/video/apache-207-reezy-bausa-ueber-sein-label-twosides|title=Apache 207 & reezy: Bausa über sein Label TwoSides|last=Senger|first=Clark|website=hiphop.de|language=de|date=18 June 2019|accessdate=27 October 2019}}</ref> The first single to be released under the label was "Kein Problem" in April 2019. Initially, the song garnered little attention and did not peak in Germany until late October 2019, at 17. However, the song's music video reached over eight million clicks on [[YouTube]] in the following months.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mannheim24.de/region/apache-207-deutschraps-naechstes-grosses-ding-dieser-rapper-geht-durch-decke-12277600.html|title=Apache 207: Neues Musikvideo „Roller“ nach einem Tag schon 1 Millionen mal aufgerufen!|last=Buch|first=Jana|website=mannheim24.de|language=de|date=24 August 2019|accessdate=27 October 2019}}</ref> In May 2019, he earned his first chart entry in Germany with the release "Brot nach Hause". Even though the single "2 Minuten" climbed up to 35 in Germany, his chart success remained moderate. On 23 August 2019, he released "[[Roller (Apache 207 song)|Roller]]" which would debut at number two. In the following week, it reached number one on the chart.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://hiphop.de/magazin/news/charts-apache-207-nummer-1|title=Charts: Apache 207 sichert sich seine erste Nummer 1|last=Molke|first=David|website=hiphop.de|language=de|date=6 September 2019|accessdate=27 October 2019}}</ref> In Austria and Switzerland, the song peaked within the Top 10.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://acharts.co/song/124136|title=Roller by Apache 207 - Music Charts|website=acharts.co|access-date=2020-03-24}}</ref> His follow-up releases "200 km/h" and "2002" with [[Sido (rapper)|Sido]] became Top 10 hits as well. He achieved his second number one in Germany with the single "Wieso tust du dir das an?" in October 2019.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.deinupdate.de/?p=92799|title=4x Apache|website=deinupdate.de|language=de|date=25 October 2019|accessdate=27 October 2019}}</ref> His first EP ''Platte'' was released on 25 October 2019.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://music.apple.com/de/album/platte/1476253859|title=„Platte“ von Apache 207 bei Apple Music|website=[[iTunes]]|language=de|accessdate=27 October 2019}}</ref>
