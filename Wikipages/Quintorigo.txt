'''Quintorigo''' is an Italian band founded in 1996 by John de Leo, who left the band in 2005. Their genre vary from [[experimental music|experimental]], [[jazz]], [[reggae]], [[Pop music|pop]] and [[rock music]]. Although the musical ensemble is composed only by acoustic instruments, the band often makes heavy use of sound effects like [[Distortion (music)|distortion]], [[flanging]] and [[Wah-wah (music)|wah-wah]].

==Band members==
*Andrea Costa - violin
*Gionata Costa - cello
*Stefano Ricci - double bass
*Valentino Bianchi - saxophone

===Former members===
*John De Leo - vocals, left the group in 2005
*Luisa Cottifogli - vocals, left the group in 2009
*Luca Sapio - vocals, left the group in 2012
