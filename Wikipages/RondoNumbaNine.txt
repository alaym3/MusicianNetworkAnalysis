'''Clint Massey''' (born February 18, 1997), known professionally as '''RondoNumbaNine''', is an American [[rapper]] and convicted murderer. He is best known for the songs "Hang Wit Me" and "Taliban". Massey was also a member of the [[Black Disciples]] set "600"

== Music career ==
He released his debut single "Shooters" with rapper L'A Capone, with both music video, Rondo and L'A released both single "Play for Keeps" which has received over 50 million views on [[YouTube]]. His single "Hang wit Me"  was released as a music video on September 5, 2013, and he released his first [[mixtape]] "Real Ni**a 4 Life" in November 2013. He released his second mixtape "Real Ni**a 4 Life 2 (RN4L2)".

== Legal issues ==
In December 2013, Massey posted a picture of himself holding a [[rocket launcher]] on [[Instagram]] with the caption "coolin id with my new baby!!".

On February 22, 2014, Massey shot and killed 28-year-old Javan Boyd with his friend Courtney Ealy known as "Cdai 600". The taxi driver was shot seven times. Massey was arrested on charges of first-degree murder in Boyd's death in March 2014.<ref>{{cite news|author = Emily Morris|title = Teen Rapper RondoNumbaNine Charged with Murder of Livery Driver|url = https://www.dnainfo.com/chicago/20140309/armour-square/teen-charged-with-murder-of-livery-driver}}</ref> In 2016, he was convicted and sentenced to 39 years in prison.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thefader.com/2016/07/06/rondonumbanine-sentenced-jail-murder|title=RondoNumbaNine Sentenced to 39 Years in Jail for Murder}}</ref>
