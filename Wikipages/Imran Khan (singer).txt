'''Imran Khan''' (born 28 May 1984) is a Dutch<ref>{{Cite web|title=Home|url=http://imrankhanworld.com/|access-date=2021-04-05|website=ImranKhanWorld|language=en-US}}</ref> singer and songwriter who sings Pakistani Punjabi songs. He became known in 2007 after his first single "Ni Nachleh", produced by Eren E.<ref name="bbc">{{Cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/entertainment/8183434.stm|title=Talking Shop: Singer Imran Khan |access-date=18 August 2009 |publisher=[[BBC News]] | date=29 August 2009}}.</ref> Khan was signed to Prestige Records in late 2007, and released the single "Ni Nachleh" on the label.<ref name="bbc" /> His second single, "Amplifier", produced by Eren E, was released on 13 July 2009, and later several others. His song "Satisfya" is his most viewed video to date,{{when|date=November 2021}} with over 600 million+ views. His debut 15-track album ''Unforgettable'' was released on 27 July 2009 via Prestige Records,<ref name=BBC/> produced by Eren E and Hakan Ozan.

==Personal life==
Khan has two brothers and one sister.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/Imran-Khan-Salman-has-something-strong-coming-out-of-him/articleshow/45453545.cms|work=The Times of India|title=Imran Khan: Salman has something strong coming out of him|access-date=2 August 2017}}</ref>

==Career==
Khan started his music career in his late teens.<ref name=BIO>{{cite web |url= http://www.imrankhanworld.com/site.php#2 |title= About Imrankhanworld B.V. |publisher= imrankhanworld.com |access-date= 11 September 2012 |url-status= dead |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20120908043308/http://www.imrankhanworld.com/site.php#2 |archive-date= 8 September 2012 |df= dmy-all }}</ref> After Prestige Records released his debut single ''Ni Nachleh'',<ref name="bbc"/><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.prestigerecord.com/about.asp |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090716022429/http://prestigerecord.com/about.asp |url-status=dead |archive-date=16 July 2009 |title=About Us |work=prestigerecord.com |access-date=26 August 2010  }}</ref> he continued his career by performing in several countries.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.desi-box.com/media/interviews/interview-with-imran-khan.html |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100302150507/http://www.desi-box.com/media/interviews/interview-with-imran-khan.html |url-status=dead |archive-date=2 March 2010 |title=Interview with Imran Khan |work=desi-box.com |access-date=11 September 2012  }}</ref>

Khan's first released single, "Amplifier", was produced by Eren E, was released on 12 July 2009. It received 2 million views on YouTube on it first week of release and currently has 400&nbsp;million and counting views on YouTube.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.desi-box.com/media/bhangra/worldwide-premiere-imran-khan-amplifier-listen-full-now.html |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100803175931/http://desi-box.com/media/bhangra/worldwide-premiere-imran-khan-amplifier-listen-full-now.html |url-status=dead |archive-date=3 August 2010 |title=Worldwide premiere of Imran Khan's Amplifier |work=desi-box.com |access-date=11 September 2012  }}</ref> His second single, "Bewafa," was produced by Hakan Ozan and was released on 30 August 2009; as of January 2020, its music video had been viewed more than 100 million times on YouTube.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.desihits.com/news/view/imran-khan-releases-new-single-bewafa-20091130 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20091204143402/http://www.desihits.com/news/view/imran-khan-releases-new-single-bewafa-20091130 |url-status=dead |archive-date=4 December 2009 |title=Imran Khan releases new single Bewafa |publisher=[[DesiHits]] |access-date=11 September 2012  }}</ref>

In July 2009, Khan released his debut album, ''Unforgettable''. It includes the singles "Amplifier", "Bewafa", and a remix of his first single, "Ni Nachleh".<ref name=BBC>{{Cite web |url= https://www.bbc.co.uk/music/reviews/84rj |title=Review of Imran Khan's Unforgettable |work = [[BBC Music]] |access-date=11 September 2012}}</ref> It was nominated for Best Album at the 2010 [[UK Asian Music Awards]] but did not win.<ref name=AMA>{{cite web |url= http://www.bbc.co.uk/asiannetwork/events/ama2010/winners/ |title= AMA 2010 – Winners |publisher= [[BBC Radio Asian Network]] |access-date= 29 May 2011 |url-status= dead |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20100807171609/http://www.bbc.co.uk/asiannetwork/events/ama2010/winners/ |archive-date= 7 August 2010 |df= dmy-all }}</ref>

In 2011, Khan departed from Prestige Records to start his own label, later known as IK Records.<ref name=AMA/>

Khan released the single "Satisfya" on 9 May 2013.<ref>{{Citation|last=imrankhanworld|title=Imran Khan – Satisfya (Official Music Video)|date=10 May 2013|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pfVODjDBFxU|access-date=24 October 2016}}</ref><ref>[http://imrankhanworld.com/site.php#Bio Biography], Khan's official website {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110821002317/http://www.imrankhanworld.com/site.php |date=21 August 2011 }}</ref> Unlike his previous singles, it was released by IK Records. It was produced by his "Amplifier" music producer, Eren E. The music video shows Khan driving a yellow [[Lamborghini Aventador]] and sitting with a cheetah. The video was directed by [[David Zennie]], and as of 26 October 2020, has 500 million+ views on YouTube.

Khan made his Bollywood debut as a singer for the Hindi film ''[[Tevar]]'', which was released on 9 January 2015. As of 2016 the song has over more than 2 million hits.<ref>{{Citation|last=Eros Now|title=Let's Celebrate (Official Song Video) {{!}} Tevar {{!}} Arjun Kapoor, Sonakshi Sinha, Imran Khan|date=3 December 2014|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=L6C5wv5XbZQ|access-date=24 October 2016}}</ref>

Khan released a single titled "Imaginary" on 8 June 2015, produced by Eren E. The single was released on his own record label, IK Records. Khan shot the video with David Zennie in downtown Los Angeles. "Imaginary"'s official music video received 6 million views within a month of its release and currently has 70 million+ views on YouTube. Khan came out with another song, "Hattrick", which received 2 million views in a month and currently has 15 million+ views.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eqOewr6fzC8|title=Hattrick music video}}</ref>

In 2017, Khan released a music video called "Morocco" by Twin N Twice, who signed up with Khan's label record, IK Records.<ref>{{Citation|last=imrankhanworld|title=Twin N Twice – Morocco (Official Music Video)|date=4 January 2017|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uc3Oma0X-YQ|access-date=17 March 2017}}</ref>

On 28 September 2018, Khan released his new single, "Knightridah".<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thetealmango.com/entertainment/imran-khan-releases-knightridah-after-one-year-hiatus/amp|title=Imran Khan Releases 'KnightRidah' After One Year Hiatus|access-date= 26 February 2019}}</ref>

In 2021, he released two singles, "M.O.B." on 14 May, featuring J.J. Esko; and "They Don't Like It" on 28 September.

====''Unforgettable'' (2009)====
''Unforgettable'' is Khan's debut album, released on 27 July 2009 via Prestige Records.<ref name=BBC/> It was produced by Eren E and Hakan Ozan. It contains 15 tracks.

The album was nominated for the "Best Album" award at the [[UK Asian Music Awards]] 2010<ref name=NOMINATED /> and won "Best Album" at the 2010 Brit Asia TV Music Awards.<ref name=WON />
