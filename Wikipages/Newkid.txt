'''Jhun Alexander Ferrer''', also known by his stage name '''Newkid''' (born December 2, 1990), is a Swedish–Filipino rapper<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.expressen.se/noje/1.2413331/newkid-ar-rykande-het |title=Newkid är rykande het – Nöje – Expressen Nöje {{!}} Nöjesnyheter Musiknyheter Filmnyheter |access-date=May 2, 2011 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110427205419/http://www.expressen.se/noje/1.2413331/newkid-ar-rykande-het |archive-date=April 27, 2011 |url-status=dead }}</ref> signed to [[Sony Music Entertainment]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.metro.se/noje/newkid-alexander-jr-ferrer/EVHkdl!0dNtf12nUoWQ/ |title=Newkid – Alexander Jr Ferrer – Metro |access-date=May 2, 2011 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120314054605/http://www.metro.se/noje/newkid-alexander-jr-ferrer/EVHkdl!0dNtf12nUoWQ/ |archive-date=March 14, 2012 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

== Career ==
Jhun Alexander Ferrer was born in [[Uddevalla]], Sweden in 1990 to a Swedish mother and a Spanish–Filipino father.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.nojesguiden.se/recensioner/musik/newkid-alexander-jr-ferrer |title=Newkid – Alexander Jr Ferrer {{!}} Nöjesguiden |access-date=May 2, 2011 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110721061135/http://www.nojesguiden.se/recensioner/musik/newkid-alexander-jr-ferrer |archive-date=July 21, 2011 |url-status=live }}</ref>

Adopting the stage name Newkid, he released his debut studio album on April 15, 2011, named ''Alexander JR Ferrer'' and his debut single in Sweden was "Jag gråter bara i regnet". He is also featured on [[Lorentz & Sakarias]]{{'}} songs "Lever min dröm" and "Swischa förbi", as well as [[Ison & Fille]]'s song "Lever såhär". Ferrer released the EP ''SS18'' in the summer of 2018, which peaked at number 21 on the [[Swedish Albums Chart]]. He released his second studio album, ''SS/AW18'', later that year.<ref name=":0" /> He also featured on [[Tjuvjakt]]{{'}}s single "Apelsinskal", which peaked at number eleven on the [[Swedish Singles Chart]].<ref name="Tjuvjakt"/> On April 24, 2020, he released the single "Kanske var vi rätt bra ändå".<ref name="AM"/> It peaked at number six on the Swedish Singles Chart.<ref name="SWEV18"/> A music video for the song was released on Newkid{{'}}s YouTube channel on April 29, 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Newkid - Kanske var vi rätt bra ändå (Official Music Video)|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OzKGuKPzdHA|date=April 29, 2020|website=[[YouTube]]|access-date=May 7, 2020}}</ref> Swedish newspaper ''[[Aftonbladet]]'' stated that Newkid will participate in the upcoming eleventh season of [[TV4 (Sweden)|TV4]]{{'}}s TV-show ''[[Så mycket bättre]]'', which will be recorded in June 2020''.''<ref>{{Cite news|last=Ek|first=Torbjörn|date=April 30, 2020|title=Loreen klar för "Så mycket bättre"|language=sv|work=[[Aftonbladet]]|url=https://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/a/70v61o/loreen-klar-for-sa-mycket-battre|url-status=live|access-date=May 1, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Tommy Körberg uppges klar för "Så mycket bättre"|url=https://www.expressen.se/noje/tommy-korberg-uppges-klar-for-sa-mycket-battre/|last=Hedlund|first=Viktor|date=May 4, 2020|website=[[Expressen]]|language=sv|url-status=live|access-date=May 6, 2020}}</ref>

Reportedly, Newkid will release his third studio album in 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Newkid bjuder på ny singer från kommande album|url=https://www.kingsizemag.se/musik/newkid-bjuder-pa-ny-singel-fran-kommande-album/|date=February 28, 2020|website=Kingsizemag|language=sv|access-date=May 7, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Newkid släpper romantiska "Kanske var vi rätt bra ändå"|url=https://dopest.se/newkid-slapper-romantiska-kanske-var-vi-ratt-bra-anda/|website=Dopest|language=sv|url-status=live|access-date=May 7, 2020}}</ref> He released the single "Chambea" on May 22, 2020.<ref name="Chambea"/> On May 29, Newkid released his previously announced third studio album, titled ''Mount Jhun''.<ref name="AM3">{{Cite web|title=Mount Jhun by Newkid|url=https://music.apple.com/us/album/mount-jhun/1514145300|website=[[Apple Music]]|access-date=May 29, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Newkid släpper albumet 'MOUNT JHUN' idag|url=http://www.mynewsdesk.com/se/sonybmg/pressreleases/newkid-slaepper-albumet-mount-jhun-idag-3002670|date=May 29, 2020|website=MyNewsDesk|language=sv|access-date=May 29, 2020}}</ref> It peaked at number three on the Swedish Albums Chart, making it Newkid's first album to make the top 10 on the chart. The album contains the previously released singles "Kanske var vi rätt bra ändå", "Mi Amor", "Chambea" and "Över dig".
