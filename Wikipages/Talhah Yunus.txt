'''Muhammad Talhah Yunus''' (born: October 21st, 1996), professionally known as '''Talhah Yunus''', is a [[Pakistanis|Pakistani]] [[songwriter]], [[rapper]], [[filmmaker]] and [[musician]]. He is a part of the hip hop duo [[Young Stunners (music duo)|Young Stunners]] along with Talha Anjum<ref>{{Cite web |last=Qayyum |first=Mohammad |date=April 21, 2014 |title=Beat that! Pakistani rap rises: a tale of two Talhas |url=http://tribune.com.pk/story/698484/beat-that-pakistani-rap-rises-a-tale-of-two-talhas |access-date=2021-10-07 |website=[[The Express Tribune]] |language=en}}</ref>  and music producer Jokhay. He was born and raised in [[Karachi]]. He appeared in the PSL Anthem of 2021 "''Groove Mera''".<ref>{{Cite web |title=Atif Aslam, Humaima Malick to perform at PSL6 opening ceremony {{!}} SAMAA |url=https://www.samaa.tv/lifeandstyle/2021/02/atif-aslam-humaima-malick-to-perform-at-psl6-opening-ceremony/ |access-date=2021-10-07 |website=Samaa TV |language=en-US}}</ref>
