'''Jenni Mari Vartiainen''' (born March 20, 1983) is a Finnish pop singer. Before her professional musical endeavours, she was a [[figure skating|figure skater]] in her teen-age years and attended the [[Kuopio Senior High of Music and Dance]]. Vartiainen rose to publicity by winning the Finnish talent show ''[[Popstars#Europe|Popstars]]'' in October 2002 with Susanna Korvala, Ushma Karnani (currently Olava) and [[Jonna Pirinen]]. The four formed the band [[Gimmel (band)|Gimmel]] that released three studio albums, sold over 160,000&nbsp;records and received three [[Emma-gaala|Emma Awards]], accolades for outstanding achievements in music, awarded by the Finnish music industry federation, [[Musiikkituottajat – IFPI Finland|Musiikkituottajat]]. The band broke up in October 2004.

After Gimmel, Vartiainen launched her solo career, releasing her debut single "[[Tunnoton]]" in April 2007. In September of the same year, the single was followed by her debut album ''[[Ihmisten edessä]]'', which peaked at number six on the [[Finnish Albums Chart]], and its [[Ihmisten edessä (song)|eponymous second single]], both of which sold [[List of music recording certifications|gold]] in Finland with over 65,000 and 16,000&nbsp;copies, respectively. In late March 2010, Jenni Vartiainen released her second studio album ''[[Seili (album)|Seili]]'', after the arrival of its gold-selling debut single "[[En haluu kuolla tänä yönä]]". The second album debuted at number one on the Albums Chart, while charting non-consecutively for over a year, and went on to sell over 150,000&nbsp;copies (septuple platinum), becoming the best-selling album of 2010 in Finland; and ranking 14th in the list of all-time best-sellers in Finland. A&nbsp;combination of pop and ballads, ''Seili'' spawned another gold-selling single in August 2010, "[[Missä muruseni on]]", which dominated the top-three spots of the [[Finnish Singles Chart]] all the way to early&nbsp;2011, and the gold-selling promotional single "[[Duran Duran (song)|Duran Duran]]". Vartiainen's third studio album, ''[[Terra (Jenni Vartiainen album)|Terra]]'', released in October 2013, debuted at number one and sold triple platinum with over 62,000&nbsp;copies.

Jenni Vartiainen has sold over 330,000&nbsp;[[List of music recording certifications|certified]] records in Finland during her solo career, which places her among the&nbsp;50&nbsp;best-selling recording artists of all time in the country.{{r|sales}}{{r|records all-time}} She has also received nine [[Emma-gaala|Emma Awards]].

==Early life==
Jenni Mari Vartiainen was born on March 20, 1983, in [[Kuopio]].{{r|birth date and place}} She started to play classical piano at the age of six and she played it until she was 16,<ref name="mtv3jennivartiainen">{{Cite web|url=http://www.mtv3.fi/viihde/uutiset/musiikki.shtml/1064502?vartiainen_jenni|title=Jenni Vartiainen lehdessä: Ulkonäköpaineita on ollut|date=February 24, 2010|publisher=MTV3|language=fi|access-date=August 25, 2013}}</ref> building her musical skills later on by attending [[Kuopio Senior High of Music and Dance]].<ref name="vartiainen-city-magazine-2007">{{Cite journal|url=http://www.city.fi/artikkeli/Jenni+Vartiainen/2445/|title=Jenni Vartiainen|first=Jaana|last=Rinne|journal=[[City (magazine)|City]]|volume=2007|issue=19|publisher=Janton|language=fi|access-date=August 25, 2013}}</ref> In her teen-age years, Vartiainen was also a [[figure skater]], competing ultimately at Finland's senior level alongside future top Finnish skaters such as [[Susanna Pöykiö]]. In the summer of 1999, when she was 16, she was injured during a skating lesson and she developed a [[spinal disc herniation]] due to which she had to quit skating.<ref name="vartiainen-me-naiset-august-2010">{{Cite web|url=http://www.menaiset.fi/artikkelit/ihmiset/art1601-Jenni-Vartiainen---Olen-huono-luottamaan-.html|title=Jenni Vartiainen: "Olen huono luottamaan"|first=Taina|last=Latvala|date=August 12, 2010|work=Me Naiset|publisher=[[Sanoma|Sanoma Magazines]]|language=fi|access-date=August 25, 2013|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120317002525/http://www.menaiset.fi/artikkelit/ihmiset/art1601-Jenni-Vartiainen---Olen-huono-luottamaan-.html|archive-date=March 17, 2012}}</ref> In the spring of 2002, Vartiainen completed her [[Lukio|matriculation examination]] at the Senior High and moved to Helsinki. She was about to travel abroad, but her plans changed as she saw an advertisement of ''[[Popstars#Europe|Popstars]]'', the first Finnish season of the New Zealand-based casting show format.<ref name="vartiainen-me-naiset-august-2010"/>

==Career==

===2002–2004: Gimmel===
{{Main|Gimmel (band)}}
In the autumn of 2002, Vartiainen entered ''Popstars''. "finding herself".<ref name="vartiainen-city-magazine-2007"/> Along with Ushma Karnani (currently Olava), Susanna Korvala and [[Jonna Pirinen]], she won the contest in October and the quartet formed the band [[Gimmel (band)|Gimmel]].<ref name="finland-got-popstars-band-is">{{Cite web|url=http://www.iltasanomat.fi/viihde/suomi-sai-oman-popstars-bandin/art-1288338175543.html|title=Suomi sai oman Company]]|access-date=August 25, 2013}}</ref> Gimmel's debut single "Etsit muijaa seuraavaa" sold gold with over 17,000&nbsp;copies in 2002, placing in the top 70 of the best-selling singles of all two on the Albums Chart and managed to sell platinum with over 50,000&nbsp;copies,{{r|Gimmel sales}}<ref name="kaksi-kertaa-chart">{{Cite web|url=http://www.ifpi.fi/tilastot/virallinen-lista/artistit/gimmel/kaksi+kertaa+enemmän|title=Gimmel: Kaksi kertaa enemmän|publisher=Musiikkituottajat – IFPI Finland|language=fi|access-date=August ===2005–2008: ''Ihmisten edessä''===
[[File:JenniVartiainen.jpg|thumb|right|180px|Jenni Vartiainen in 2008.]]
In 2005, Vartiainen signed a record deal with [[Warner Music Group|Warner Music Finland]]. The production of her debut album together with producer [[Jukka Immonen]] took so long that Vartiainen ran out of money, which led her to ask for cleaning jobs at Warner studios and ultimately she worked at a company, processing consumer complaints.<ref name="vartiainen-me-naiset-august-2010"/> Vartiainen's first solo single "[[Tunnoton]]" was released in April 2007 and the debut album ''[[Ihmisten edessä]]'' in September 2007. The album spawned two later singles, "[[Ihmisten edessä (song)|Ihmisten edessä]]" and "[[Mustaa kahvia]]", of which "Ihmisten edessä", dealing with difficulties faced by [[LGBT]] people, was the biggest hit, peaking at number two on the Singles Chart and selling gold with over 16,000&nbsp;copies.{{r|sales}} Selling over 61,000&nbsp;copies and peaking at number six on the Albums Chart, her debut album went on to reach the Finnish [[List of music recording certifications#Albums|double-platinum certification]].{{r|sales}} Vartiainen was on the magazine cover for the December 2007 edition of the Finnish ''[[Cosmopolitan (magazine)|Cosmopolitan]]''<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.hs.fi/viihde/artikkeli/Jenni+Vartiainen+Cosmopolitanin+kanteen/1135232241307|title=Jenni Vartiainen Cosmopolitanin kanteen|date=November 30, 2007|work=[[Helsingin Sanomat]]|publisher=Sanoma News|language=fi|access-date=August 25, 2013}}</ref> and the March 2008 edition of the Mexican ''Cosmopolitan''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://dome.fi/musiikki/uutiset/jenni-vartiainen-meksikon-cosmopolitanin-kanteen|title=Jenni Vartiainen Meksikon Cospolitanin kanteen|publisher=Otavamedia|date=February 29, 2008|language=fi|access-date=August 25, 2013|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110720175559/http://dome.fi/musiikki/uutiset/jenni-vartiainen-meksikon-cosmopolitanin-kanteen|archive-date=July 20, 2011|url-status=dead}}</ref> With ''Ihmisten edessä'' and its title track, Vartiainen won the Award for Debut Album of the Year and Song of the Year at the 2008 Emma Awards and the album was nominated for Pop Album of the Year.<ref name="emma2008winners">{{cite web |url=http://www.emmagaala.fi/2008/voittajat.html |archive-url=https://www.webcitation.org/60rfjmOEf?url=http://www.emmagaala.fi/2008/voittajat.html |archive-date=August 11, 2011 |title=EMMA GAALA 2008 – Vuoden 2007 Emma-voittajat! |publisher=Musiikkituottajat – IFPI Finland |language=fi |access-date=August 25, 2013 |url-status=dead }}</ref><ref name="emma2008nominees">{{Cite web |url=http://www.emmagaala.fi/2008/artistit/ehdokkaat.html%3Fpop-albumi.html |archive-url=https://www.webcitation.org/60rfznUR3?url=http://www.emmagaala.fi/2008/artistit/ehdokkaat.html?pop-albumi.html |archive-date=August 11, 2011 |title=Ehdokkaat – Vuoden 2007 pop-albumi |publisher=Musiikkituottajat – IFPI Finland |language=fi |access-date=August 25, 2013 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

===2013–present: ''Terra''===
After a two-year break, Warner Music Finland announced in August 2013 that Vartiainen would release her third studio album, ''[[Terra (Jenni Vartiainen album)|Terra]]'', on October 4, 2013, preceded by its lead single "[[Junat ja naiset]]" on August 16.{{r|Warner Music Terra}} The lead single peaked at number 15 and the album debuted at number one on [[the Official Finnish Charts]].{{r|Junat chart}}{{r|Terra chart}} Vartiainen embarked on her new concert tour promoting the album on November 1.{{r|Warner Music Terra}}

==Personal life==
Jenni Vartiainen, who is known for keeping a very low profile on her private life,<ref name="vartiainen-me-naiset-august-2010"/> was reported on March 28, 2009, to have started a [[cohabitation]] with her producer [[Jukka Immonen]].<ref name="cohab-2009-savonsanomat">{{Cite web|url=http://www.savonsanomat.fi/viihde/viihdeuutiset/jenni-vartiainen-avoliittoon/399577|title=IS: Jenni Vartiainen avoliittoon|first=Jussi|last=Murtasaari|date=March 28, 2011|work=Savon Sanomat|publisher=Savon Sanomat Oy|language=fi|access-date=August 25, 2013|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120315201719/http://www.savonsanomat.fi/viihde/viihdeuutiset/jenni-vartiainen-avoliittoon/399577|archive-date=March 15, 2012|url-status=dead}}</ref> Since May 2008, She has had a [[Pumi (dog)|Pumi dog]] named Urho Kepponen.{{r|Stara 2008}} In August 2008, Vartiainen bought an apartment worth €400,000 in [[Laajasalo]] neighborhood in Helsinki.<ref name="vartiainens-income-august2008-is">{{Cite web|url=http://www.iltasanomat.fi/viihde/jenni-vartiaisella-hurjat-tulot/art-1288336149007.html|title=Jenni Vartiaisella hurjat tulot|date=August 22, 2008|work=Ilta-Sanomat|publisher=Sanoma News|language=fi|access-date=August 25, 2013}}</ref> During the summer of 2011, Vartiainen was a subject of pregnancy rumors in the media, due to her loose concert outfits, a slightly more rounded belly and the post-summer break the length of which was not specified. Vartiainen's spokesperson Carla Ahonius stated on the rumors to [[MTV3]] that "the record label has nothing to report on the issue" and that "[Vartiainen] will comment on the subject on Facebook if necessary".<ref name="raskaus-mtv3-july5-2011">{{Cite web|url=http://www.mtv3.fi/viihde/uutiset/musiikki.shtml/1356152/jenni-vartiainen-raskaushuhujen-pyorteissa---nain-vastaa-levy-yhtio|title=Jenni Vartiainen raskaushuhujen pyörteissä – näin vastaa levy-yhtiö|publisher=MTV3|date=July 5, 2011|language=fi|access-date=August 25, 2013}}</ref> In September 2014, news broke that Vartiainen and Immonen had separated.<ref>{{cite web|title=Seiska: Jenni Vartiainen on eronnut|url=http://www.iltalehti.fi/viihde/2014090418630860_vi.shtml|publisher=Iltalehti|access-date=September 13, 2014}}</ref> In 2019, Vartiainen moved to New York City.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://yle.fi/uutiset/3-11302194 |title=New Yorkissa asuva Jenni Vartiainen näkee ruumisrekan ikkunastaan – "Kaupungin koronatilanne pelottaa, mutta ihmisten auttamisenhalu ilahduttaa" |last=Vedenpää |first=Ville |date=April 9, 2020 |publisher=[[Yle]] |language=fi |access-date=August 6, 2020 |quote=...&nbsp;muutti pysyvästi New Yorkiin reilu vuosi sitten.}}</ref>

The relatively successful career has accrued Vartiainen's wealth—she earned almost €71,000 in 2008,{{r|income 2008}} €47,000 in 2009{{r|income 2009}} and €64,000 in 2010{{r|income 2010}} The success of ''Seili'' further increased her wealth, as she earned nearly €200,000 in 2011{{r|income 2011}} and €135,000 in 2012.{{r|income 2012}}
