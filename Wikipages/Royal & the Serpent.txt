'''Ryan Santiago''' (born May 25, 1994), known professionally as '''Royal & the Serpent''', is an American singer and songwriter.

==Early life==
A [[New Jersey]] native, Ryan Santiago was born on May 25, 1994.<ref name="Donelson">{{cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/royal-the-serpent-mn0003829264/biography|title=Royal & the Serpent|publisher=[[AllMusic]]|access-date=February 12, 2021|last=Donelson|first=Marcy}}</ref> Santiago took up [[competitive dance]] as a child, until she "shattered both [her] heel plates" after jumping off a [[Stage (theatre)|stage]] while performing when she was 14 years old.<ref name="Cingrana">{{cite web|url=https://www.radio.com/alt923/blogs/joe-cingrana/alt-923-pick-of-the-week-royal-the-serpent-overwhelmed|title=Ctrl+ALT+Del 'Pick of the Week': Royal & the Serpent – 'Overwhelmed'|publisher=[[WNYL (FM)|WNYL]]|date=August 25, 2020|access-date=February 12, 2021|last=Cingrana|first=Joe}}</ref><ref name="Jackson">{{cite web|url=http://www.ladygunn.com/music/interviews-music/royal-the-serpent-new-release-get-a-grip-virtual-tour/|title=Royal & The Serpent Wants You To 'Get A Grip'|website=Ladygunn|date=October 28, 2020|access-date=February 12, 2021|last=Jackson|first=Jada}}</ref> She subsequently delved into [[musical theatre|musical theater]].<ref name="Cingrana"/> In her teenage years, Santiago learned how to play the guitar and began writing songs.<ref name="Cingrana"/>

==Career==
At the age of 18, Santiago moved to [[Los Angeles]], where she worked as a bartender in a restaurant.<ref name="Cingrana"/><ref name="Limon">{{cite web|url=https://www.hertzmagazine.com/interviews/interview-with-royal-and-the-serpent|title=Interview With Royal & The Serpent|website=Hertz Magazine|date=November 4, 2020|access-date=February 12, 2021|last=Limon|first=Maria}}</ref><ref name="VT">{{cite web|url=https://www.vanityteen.com/royal-the-serpent-the-lonely-avocado-x-vteen/|title=Royal & The Serpent – The Lonely Avocado x VTEEN|website=[[Vanity Teen]]|date=January 2, 2021|access-date=February 12, 2021}}</ref> She has credited encouragement from a co-worker, who later became her first manager, for motivating her to pursue a professional career as a musician.<ref name="Cingrana"/><ref name="Limon"/><ref name="VT"/>

In 2017, Santiago independently released "Temperance", her debut single under the stage name Royal & the Serpent.<ref name="Cingrana"/> On her moniker, she has explained that "'Royal & the Serpent' translates to 'Me + My [[Ego (Freudian)|Ego]]'".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://preludepress.com/interviews/2020/08/11/royal-and-the-serpent-interview/|title=Royal & The Serpent Discusses 'Overwhelmed' and Upcoming EPs: 'Duality Is the Essence of Our Human Experience'|website=Prelude Press|date=August 11, 2020|access-date=February 12, 2021|last=Vigil|first=Dom}}</ref> In 2018, she released her second single, "Together",<ref name="Together"/> and was featured on the song "Wicked" by music producer [[Tommee Profitt]].<ref name="Donelson"/> By the end of 2019, she had signed with [[Atlantic Records]].<ref name="Cingrana"/><ref name="Jackson"/>

"Overwhelmed", Royal & the Serpent's first single on Atlantic, was released in June 2020,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.idolator.com/7912047/royal-the-serpent-makes-major-label-debut-with-overwhelmed|title=Royal & The Serpent Makes Major Label Debut With 'Overwhelmed'|website=[[Idolator (website)|Idolator]]|date=June 27, 2020|access-date=February 12, 2021|last=Wass|first=Mike}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.amazon.com/Overwhelmed-Royal-Serpent/dp/B088D15MPD|title=Overwhelmed by Royal & the Serpent|publisher=[[Amazon (company)|Amazon]]|location=United States|access-date=February 12, 2021}}</ref> and her debut [[extended play|EP]] ''Get a Grip'' was released in October.<ref name="Get a Grip"/> That year, she joined English singer [[Yungblud]]'s Weird Time of Life virtual tour as a supporting act.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.theaquarian.com/2020/11/25/royal-the-serpent-talks-silver-linings-and-stability-feature-watch-listen/|title=Royal & The Serpent Talks Silver Linings and Stability [Feature + Watch & Listen]|newspaper=[[The Aquarian Weekly]]|date=November 25, 2020|access-date=February 12, 2021|last=Schafer|first=Debra Kate}}</ref> Royal & the Serpent's second EP ''Searching for Nirvana'' was released in June 2021.<ref name="Searching for Nirvana"/>
