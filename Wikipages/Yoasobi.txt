'''Yoasobi''' (stylized in [[all caps]]) is a Japanese music duo that was formed and debuted in 2019 under [[Sony Music Entertainment Japan]], composing of [[Vocaloid]] ([[Speech-generating device|voice synthesizer software]]) producer and songwriter [[:ja:Ayase|Ayase]], and singer-songwriter [[:ja:幾田りら|Lilas Ikuta]]<ref name=lilas/> under the stage name Ikura. The duo has released songs based on [[short story|short stories]] posted on [[:ja:monogatary.com|Monogatary.com]], a website operated by their label, later also from various media like [[novels]], [[book]] tie-ups, and [[Letter (message)|letters]]. 

Yoasobi debuted with the single "[[Yoru ni Kakeru]]" in December 2019. It became successful on several music charts in Japan and gave them their breakthrough with charting atop for six weeks and 2020 year-end on the [[Billboard Japan Hot 100|''Billboard'' Japan Hot 100]]. It was followed by successful singles including "[[Gunjō (song)|Gunjō]]", and "[[Kaibutsu]]". Their debut extended play (EP) ''[[The Book (Yoasobi EP)|The Book]]'' was released in January 2021 and entered number two on both the [[Oricon Albums Chart]] and [[Billboard Japan#Albums|''Billboard Japan'' Hot Albums]].

== Name ==

The name "Yoasobi" originates from the Japanese word {{lang|ja|夜遊び}}, meaning "nightlife". Ayase, a producer of the group described he named it as a wish that he could take on various playful challenges by comparing their individual career as a daytime and their Yoasobi career as a nighttime.<ref>{{cite AV media |author=TBS News |date=August 16, 2020 |title=【ノーカット】YOASOBI をＮスタ井上キャスターがインタビュー |medium=video |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vqsK-bdAoqU |access-date=March 7, 2021 |publisher=Yoasobi |via=[[YouTube]]}}</ref> The duo's slogan is {{nihongo|"novel into music"|小説を音楽にする|shōsetsu o ongaku ni suru}}.<ref>{{cite web |title=YOASOBI / Profile |url=https://www.yoasobi-music.jp/profile |website=Yoasobi Official Website |publisher=Sony Music Entertainment Japan |access-date=March 7, 2021 |language=ja}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title="小説を音楽にする" YOASOBIが新曲の原作小説を募集開始 |url=https://www.moshimoshi-nippon.jp/ja/393144 |website=Moshimoshi Nippon |publisher=Moshimoshi Nippon |access-date=March 7, 2021 |language=ja |date=January 20, 2021}}</ref>
