'''Squid''' is an English [[post-punk]] band from [[Brighton]], England.<ref name="p4k1">{{cite web|url=https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/squid-town-centre/ |title=Squid: Town Centre Review|website=Pitchfork  |publisher=Pitchfork Media |date=9 September 2019 |access-date=17 June 2020}}</ref> They are currently based in London.<ref name="NME">{{cite web |title=Get tangled up in Squid – the weirdo-punk band making the avant-garde accessible |url=https://www.nme.com/blogs/nme-radar/squid-band-houseplants-speedy-wunderground-interview-2513463 |website=NME |access-date=17 June 2020}}</ref> Squid released their debut EP on [[Speedy Wunderground]], ''Town Centre,'' in 2019<ref name="p4k1"/><ref name="AOTY">{{cite web |title=Squid – Town Centre |url=https://www.albumoftheyear.org/album/165123-squid-town-centre.php |website=AOTY |access-date=17 June 2020}}</ref> and have since released four singles on [[Warp Records]].<ref name="p4k2">{{cite web |title=Squid Sign to Warp, Share New Song "Sludge": Listen |url=https://pitchfork.com/news/squid-sign-to-warp-share-new-song-sludge-listen/ |website=Pitchfork |publisher=Pitchfork Media |access-date=17 June 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Squid Unravel on New Single "Broadcaster" |url=https://consequenceofsound.net/2020/05/stream-squid-broadcaster-song/ |website=Consequence of Sound |access-date=17 June 2020}}</ref> Their debut album, ''[[Bright Green Field]]'', was released by Warp Records on 7 May 2021.<ref name="p4k3">{{cite web |title=Squid Announce Debut Album Bright Green Field, Share Video for New Song "Narrator": Watch |url=https://pitchfork.com/news/squid-announce-debut-album-bright-green-field-share-video-for-new-song-narrator-watch/ |website=Pitchfork Media |access-date=28 January 2021}}</ref>

== History ==
Squid formed in Brighton, England in 2017.<ref name="The Guardian">{{cite web |title=Ones to watch: Squid |url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2019/aug/03/one-to-watch-squid-south-london-band-town-centre |website=The Guardian |access-date=17 June 2020}}</ref> They have cited [[Neu!]] and [[This Heat]] as influences.<ref name="NME"/> In 2019, they released their second EP, titled ''Town Centre,'' to critical acclaim.<ref name="p4k1"/><ref name="AOTY"/> ''Town Centre'' was produced by [[Dan Carey (record producer)|Dan Carey]] and released on Speedy Wunderground. In March 2020, Squid signed to [[Warp Records]] and have released five singles with them as of April 2021.<ref>{{cite web |title=Squid Unravel on New Single "Broadcaster" |url=https://consequenceofsound.net/2020/05/stream-squid-broadcaster-song/ |website=Consequence of Sound |access-date=17 June 2020}}</ref> They were planning on touring Europe throughout 2020 before the [[COVID-19 pandemic]].<ref name="p4k2"/>

Taking advantage of lockdown, the band spend most of 2020 recording what would be their debut full length album, [[Bright Green Field]], again working with producer Dan Carey. The album was released on the 7th of May 2021 and was promoted by three singles, "Narrator", "Paddling" and "Pamphlets". It was well received by critics.
