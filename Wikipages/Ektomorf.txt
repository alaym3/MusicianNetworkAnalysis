'''Ektomorf''' is a [[heavy metal music|heavy metal]] band from Hungary.

== Biography ==
Ektomorf was founded in 1993 in [[Mezőkovácsháza]], Hungary, a small city near the Romanian border, by Zoltán "Zoli/Zotya" Farkas. In the actual line-up Zoli/Zotya – songwriter and leader of the band – is the only remaining founder member. The band is completed by Szebasztián Simon (lead guitar), Csaba Zahorán (bass) and Dániel Szabó (drums).

Due to his [[gypsy]] background, Farkas saw himself confronted with racism and prejudices, which is why the band had to put years of work into its international career. Their breakthrough came when Ektomorf started collaboration with Danish producer [[Tue Madsen]] in 2003.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/ektomorf-mn0000152266/biography|title=Ektomorf - Biography & History - AllMusic|website=AllMusic|access-date=29 March 2018}}</ref>

From 2002 to 2021, they have released fifteen studio albums and one live album.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/ektomorf-mn0000152266/discography|title=Ektomorf - Album Discography - AllMusic|website=AllMusic|access-date=29 March 2018}}</ref> On 7 September 2016, the band's single ''Az vagyok, aki voltam'' debuted on [[Rádió Rock 95.8]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mostszol.hu/radio/rock958/eloado/Ektomorf|title=Most szól! - Rádió Rock 95.8 - Ektomorf|date=24 November 2016|access-date=1 December 2016|publisher=Index.hu}}</ref>
