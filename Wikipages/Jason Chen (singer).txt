'''Jason Chen''' ({{zh|t=陳以桐|p=Chén Yǐtóng}}, born November 12, 1988{{Verify source|date=February 2017}}) is a [[Taiwanese-American]]<ref>{{cite web |last1=Chen |first1=Jason |title=Jason Chen |url=https://www.jasondchen.com |website=Jason Chen |accessdate=March 29, 2019}}</ref> pop singer. He began as a singer performing [[Cover version|covers]] on [[YouTube]], where he gained a sizable following. As of April 17, 2020, his YouTube channel, MusicNeverSleeps, has approximately 1.89 million [[subscribers]]. Since 2010, Chen has released dozens of singles and four full albums.

==Early life==
Chen was born in [[Boston|Boston, Massachusetts]] to Taiwanese parents but soon moved to [[Arcadia, California]], when he was a few months old.  He attended [[University of California, Los Angeles|UCLA]] in 2006 and graduated in 2010 with a BA in Economics. Although he grew up in the United States, he can also speak fluently in another language, Mandarin; thus, he is able to easily communicate to his fans in Taiwan through his secondary channel – JasonChenAsia.<ref>{{Cite web|title=JasonChenAsia|url=https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCgjnr7bWvrYtSBtvbm2Pk5Q|via=YouTube|accessdate=December 16, 2015}}</ref> Furthermore, Chen's mother is a piano teacher, so he had the advantage of growing up under the influence of music and was given opportunities to learn many different instruments, such as the piano, the guitar, and the violin.<ref>{{cite web|last=Hui-Ping Lee|date=August 12, 2011|title=年代新聞 Jason Chen 陳以桐 獨家專訪-2011-08-10|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wOiG3dxzaVo|via=YouTube|accessdate=February 15, 2019}}</ref> Chen discovered his vocal talent at the age of 18 when he was planning a proposal to his high school crush.<ref>{{cite web|date=n.d.|title=Jason Chen|url=http://www.jasondchen.com/|website=jasondchen.com|accessdate=February 15, 2019}}</ref> After graduating from UCLA, he became an accountant for about a year, but later decided to mainly focus on producing music.

==Career==
Chen started his musical career in 2007, during his second year as an undergraduate student at University of California, Los Angeles. His initial works included performing covers of songs by various renowned artists on YouTube, and as he became more experienced, he began producing and performing original music of his own.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://afspot.net/forum/topic/731671-rising-r-b-singer-jason-chen-amazing-netizens-in-america-and-china/|title=Rising R&B singer Jason Chen amazing netizens in America and China – Asian Entertainment News 2010 (Archive)|date=n.d.|website=AFspot Forum|accessdate=February 15, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180803074158/https://afspot.net/forum/topic/731671-rising-r-b-singer-jason-chen-amazing-netizens-in-america-and-china/|archive-date=August 3, 2018|url-status=dead}}</ref>

In 2011, he released his first album, ''Gravity'', and the single "Best Friend", which now has over twenty one million views.<ref>http://coversradio.com/jason-chen-singer-biography/</ref> He has also performed in various countries such as Australia, Canada, China, Indonesia, Italy, Malaysia, Singapore, and Taiwan, as well as throughout the United States. Although Jason did not receive positive response initially, he started to gain more popularity in 2010.

Encouraged by the rise of his popularity, Chen released his first original album, “Gravity”. Later, he released his second and third original album “Never for Nothing”<ref>{{Cite web|title = Never for Nothing by Jason Chen on iTunes|date = April 15, 2013|url = https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/never-for-nothing/id623924356|publisher = iTunes Store|accessdate = December 16, 2015}}</ref> and "What If Acoustic"<ref>{{Cite web|title = What If Acoustic by Jason Chen on iTunes|date = November 26, 2013|url = https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/what-if-acoustic/id736204302|publisher = iTunes Store|accessdate = December 16, 2015}}</ref> in 2013.

In 2014, Chen performed on the main stage at California food festival [[626 Night Market]].
