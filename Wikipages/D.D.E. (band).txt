'''D.D.E.''' is a [[Norway|Norwegian]] [[pop music|pop]]/[[rock music|rock]] group founded in [[Namsos]] in 1992. The members were previously in a band called After Dark.

==Current members==
*[[Bjarne Brøndbo]] - Accordion, vocals (1992&ndash;present)
*[[Daniel Viken]] - Guitar (2018-)
*[[Arnt Egil Rånes]] - Guitar (1992&ndash;present)
*[[Eivind Berre]] - Bass (1992&ndash;present)
*[[Bård Jørgen Iversen]] - Keyboards (1992&ndash;present)
*[[Eskil Brøndbo]] - Drums and percussion (1992&ndash;present)
*[[Terje Tranaas]] - Keyboards (1992&ndash;2002)
*[[Frode Viken]] - Guitar (1992&ndash; died 2018)
