'''Guglielmo Bruno''' (born 28 August 1985), known professionally as '''Willie Peyote''', is an Italian rapper and singer-songwriter.<ref name=times>{{Cite news|url=https://www.theitaliantimes.it/2021/02/18/willie-peyote-eta-carriera-biografia/|title=Chi è Willie Peyote: età, carriera e biografia, canzone Sanremo 2021|work=The Italian Times|date=18 February 2021|access-date=18 February 2021|language=it}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.it/musica/interviste-musica/willie-peyote-nessuno-vuole-essere-lo-stupido-di-qualcun-altro/385927/|title=Willie Peyote: "Nessuno vuole essere lo stupido di qualcun altro"|author=Marta Blumi Tripodi|date=6 October 2017|work=[[Rolling Stone]]|access-date=18 February 2021|language=it}}</ref>

His stage name is a pun on the cartoon character [[Wile E. Coyote]] and the [[peyote]].<ref name=today>{{Cite news|url=https://www.torinotoday.it/attualita/chi-e-willie-peyote.html|title=Chi è Willie Peyote, il cantante torinese (e del Toro) che parteciperà a Sanremo 2021|author=Claudio Pizzigallo|work=Torino Today|date=19 December 2020|access-date=18 February 2021|language=it}}</ref>

He participated at the [[Sanremo Music Festival 2021]] with the song "[[Mai dire mai (La locura)]]".<ref name=times/><ref name=today/>
