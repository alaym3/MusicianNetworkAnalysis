{{nihongo|'''Zutto Mayonaka de Iinoni'''|ずっと真夜中でいいのに。||{{lit}} "I wish it was midnight all the time."|lead=yes}}, abbreviated in English as '''Zutomayo''' (stylized in [[all caps]]) is a Japanese [[Rock music|rock]] group that debuted in 2018. Secretive by nature, the group has never released a full member list, crediting different people for music, arrangements, and music video production each time. The only reoccurring member is the vocalist, an unidentified woman named {{nihongo|"ACA-Ne"|ACAね}}, pronounced "Akane".<ref name="spotify">{{cite news |title=Spotifyのバイラルチャート首位に突如登場「秒針を噛む」 歌っている"zutomayo"とは何者？ |url=https://www.oricon.co.jp/confidence/special/51906/ |access-date=November 5, 2019 |agency=[[Oricon]] |date=October 8, 2018 |language=ja}}</ref>

Despite the little information released, the group is commercially successful. Zutomayo's three [[Extended play|EP]]'s have reached 8th, 1st, and 2nd on the [[Oricon Albums Chart]], respectively. The group was also invited to perform at the [[Fuji Rock Festival|2019 Fuji Rock Festival]] a year after their debut.<ref>{{cite web |title=『フジロック』第3弾出演アーティスト発表 TAKKYU ISHINO、HYUKOH、中村佳穂ら18組 |url=https://realsound.jp/2019/04/post-343684.html |website=Real Sound |access-date=November 6, 2019 |language=ja |date=April 5, 2019}}</ref>

==Style==
Aside from ACA-Ne, the vocalist, it is unclear how many people are in the band. The group has never revealed their faces, typically performing behind a translucent screen during concerts, including at the 2019 Fuji Rock Festival. Many have pointed to Zutomayo's secrecy regarding its members contributing towards its popularity.<ref>{{cite web |title=ずっと真夜中でいいのに。 |url=http://fujirockexpress.net/19/p_1757 |website=Fujirock Express |access-date=November 6, 2019 |language=ja |date=July 27, 2019}}</ref><ref name="natalie">{{cite news |title=ずっと真夜中でいいのに。満員のオーディエンス詰めかけたワンマンライブ追加公演 |url=https://natalie.mu/music/news/328279 |access-date=November 6, 2019 |agency=[[Natalie (website)|Natalie]] |date=April 17, 2019 |language=ja}}</ref>

Zutomayo typically produces rock music, often featuring intricate, funk-inspired basslines. ACA-Ne's voice has been described as "energetic", "expressive", and "delicate".<ref name="natalie"/><ref>{{cite web |author1=峯岸利恵 |title=ずとまよ、いよいよ本格始動！ |url=https://rockinon.com/disc/detail/181989 |website=[[Rockin'On Japan]] |access-date=November 6, 2019 |language=ja |date=December 3, 2018}}</ref>

==History==
The group debuted with the release of their first song, {{nihongo|"Byōshin o Kamu"|秒針を噛む}}, via [[YouTube]] on June 4, 2018. The song became an immediate hit, garnering 200,000 views within the first week.<ref name="spotify"/><ref name="spice">{{cite web |author1=山口哲生 |title=動画再生回数700万回超えの『ずっと真夜中でいいのに。』謎の多き超注目新人アーティストのシークレットライブレポートを掲載 |url=https://spice.eplus.jp/articles/207422 |website=Spice |publisher=eplus |access-date=November 5, 2019 |language=ja |date=September 21, 2019}}</ref> Additionally, after being released on streaming platforms on August 30, the song became the most streamed song in Japan that week.<ref>{{cite news |title=【知りたい】ずっと真夜中でいいのに。とは何者なのか？ |url=https://rockinon.com/news/detail/180776 |access-date=November 5, 2019 |agency=[[Rockin'On Japan]] |date=October 21, 2018 |language=ja}}</ref> Following the song's release, Zutomayo held their first concert at Daikanyama Loop in Tokyo. Attendees were handed opaque glasses and reported that the entire venue was dark for the concert's duration.<ref name="spice" /> The group would continue to conceal their identities in future concerts.

Zutomayo released two more singles digitally before releasing their first EP, {{nihongo|''[[Tadashii Itsuwari Kara no Kishō]]''|正しい偽りからの起床}}, on November 14 through [[EMI Records]]. The EP reached 8th on the [[Oricon Albums Chart]] and was nominated in the [[CD Shop Awards|19th CD Shop Awards]].<ref>{{cite web |title=ずっと真夜中でいいのに。 正しい偽りからの起床 |url=http://mikiki.tokyo.jp/articles/-/19780 |website=Mikiki |publisher=[[Tower Records Japan]] |access-date=November 6, 2019 |language=ja |date=November 13, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=CDショップ大賞 |url=http://www.cdshop-kumiai.jp/taisho/about/ |website=全日本CDショップ店員組合 |access-date=November 6, 2019 |language=ja}}</ref> On June 5, 2019, a second EP was released titled {{nihongo|''Ima wa Ima de Chikai wa Emi de''|今は今で誓いは笑みで}}, which topped the Oricon chart.<ref name="oricon" /> The group's first full-length album, {{nihongo|''Hisohiso Banashi''|潜潜話}}, was released on October 30 of the same year.<ref>{{cite news |title=ずっと真夜中でいいのに。1stフルアルバム「潜潜話」発売、新曲7曲含む13曲収録 |url=https://natalie.mu/music/news/342720 |access-date=November 6, 2019 |agency=[[Natalie (website)|Natalie]] |date=August 6, 2019 |language=ja}}</ref> The song "Darken" was used in the live-action film ''[[The Night Beyond the Tricornered Window]]'', and "Hypersomnia" is also featured in the film.<ref>{{cite news | url=https://natalie.mu/music/news/378076 | title=Lずっと真夜中でいいのに。、平手友梨奈出演「さんかく窓の外側は夜」主題歌担当 | language=ja | work=[[Natalie (website)|Natalie]] | date=2020-05-06 | accessdate=2020-07-11}}</ref> The group released {{nihongo|''Gusare''|ぐされ}} on February 10, 2021, featuring songs "One's Mind", "Can't Be Right", "STUDY ME", "Hunch Gray", "Have a", "Engine Oil", "Darken", "Milabo", "Loneliness", "Crop", "Hypersomnia", "Fastening", and "Inner Heart".

==Discography==
As of February 2021, Zutomayo has released three EPs and two full-length albums. Some songs contained in these albums were released digitally before the album's release.<ref>{{cite web |title=ずっと真夜中でいいのに。新曲「脳裏上のクラッカー」MV公開 |url=https://natalie.mu/music/news/302176 |website=[[Natalie (website)|Natalie]] |access-date=November 6, 2019 |language=ja |date=October 3, 2018}}</ref>
