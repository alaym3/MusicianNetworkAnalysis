'''Day6''' ({{ko-hhrm|데이식스|hanja=|mr=|rr=De-i-sik-seu}}; stylized in [[all caps]]) is a South Korean [[pop rock music|pop rock]] band under the label [[JYP Entertainment]]. The group debuted on September 7, 2015, with [[Extended play|EP]] ''[[The Day (EP)|The Day]],'' which peaked at No.2 on ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]''{{'}}s [[Billboard Charts|World Album Chart]] a week following its release.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://sg.celebrity.yahoo.com/day6-ranks-second-on-billboard-world-album-chart-084410505.html|title=Day6 Ranks Second on Billboard World Album Chart|website=sg.celebrity.yahoo.com|language=en-US|access-date=December 27, 2019}}</ref> The band consists of five members: Sungjin, [[Jae Park|Jae]], [[Young K]], Wonpil, and Dowoon. Originally a six-piece band, Day6 became a quintet after keyboardist Junhyeok left in February 2016.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.soompi.com/article/827167wpp/day6s-junhyeok-withdraws-from-group-and-contract-with-jype|title=Day6's Junhyeok Withdraws From Group and Contract With JYPE|date=February 27, 2016 |website=Soompi|language=en|access-date=December 27, 2019}}</ref> All of the Day6 members are heavily involved in writing, composing and producing the band's music.{{short description|South Korean band}}
In 2017, the band released two songs every month of the year for their ''Every Day6'' project. By the end of the project, 25 songs were added to their discography, with genres ranging from hard rock and upbeat sing-a-longs to mellow ballads. Their first full-length album, ''[[Sunrise (Day6 album)|Sunrise]],'' consisted of songs released in the first half of the ''Every Day6'' project.<ref>{{Cite news|last=Kim|first=Minji|date=2017-12-12|title=[딥:톡스①] "1년에 25곡"… 우리가 미처 몰랐던 데이식스의 음악 열정|language=Korean|work=News1|url=https://www.news1.kr/articles/?3177216|url-status=live|access-date=2021-03-28}}</ref> It was number 14 on ''Billboard''{{'}}s ''The 25 Greatest K-Pop Albums of the 2010s: Staff List,''<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/k-town/8546586/greatest-k-pop-albums-2010s-top-25|title=The 25 Greatest K-Pop Albums of the 2010s: Staff List|website=Billboard|access-date=December 27, 2019}}</ref> and the project kick-off song "I Wait" was placed No.81 on ''Billboard''{{'}}s ''The 100 Greatest K-Pop Songs of the 2010s: Staff List.''<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/list/8544710/best-k-pop-songs-2010s-top-100|title=The 100 Greatest K-Pop Songs of the 2010s: Staff List|website=Billboard|access-date=December 27, 2019}}</ref>

==History==
===2014–2016: Formation, debut and further releases===
{{See also|The Day (Day6 EP)|Daydream (Day6 EP)}}
JYP Entertainment initially announced the debut of a five-member band called 5LIVE, composed of Sungjin, [[Jae Park|Jae]], [[Young K]], Junhyeok, and Wonpil. In 2014, the band began promotions by appearing on the fourth episode of [[Mnet (TV channel)|Mnet]]'s reality-survival program ''Who is Next: Win'' and releasing a song titled "Lovely Girl" as part of the original soundtrack of 2013 TV series ''[[Bel Ami (South Korean TV series)|Bel Ami]]''.<ref name=predebut>{{cite web|last1=Yoon|first1=Sung-yul|title=[단독] JYP, 역대급미모 新걸그룹 출격..'내년 상반기·6인조'|url=http://star.mt.co.kr/stview.php?no=2013121908272999223&type=3|website=StarNews|access-date=September 4, 2015}}</ref>

In mid-2015, drummer Dowoon joined the line-up and the band was renamed Day6.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Kim|first1=Eun-ae|title=JYP "6인조 보이밴드 데이식스 준비 중…데뷔시기 미정"|url=http://stoo.asiae.co.kr/news/view.htm?sec=enter5&idxno=2015073113192796630|website=Sports Today|publisher=stoo.com|access-date=September 4, 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160724234301/http://stoo.asiae.co.kr/news/view.htm?sec=enter5&idxno=2015073113192796630|archive-date=July 24, 2016|url-status=dead}}</ref>

Day6 released their debut [[extended play|EP]], ''[[The Day (Day6 EP)|The Day]]'', with the lead single "Congratulations" on September 7, 2015, marking their debut.<ref name=debut1>{{cite web|last1=Jang|first1=So-jin|title=JYP, 첫 밴드 출격…데이식스(Day6) 오는 6일 데뷔|url=http://sports.khan.co.kr/news/sk_index.html?cat=view&art_id=201509021351193&sec_id=540301|website=Sports Kyunghyang|publisher=The Kyunghyang Shinmun|access-date=September 4, 2015}}</ref><ref name=debut2>{{cite web|last1=Lee|first1=Jae-hyun|title=JYP, 첫 남성 밴드 '데이식스'…데뷔 앨범 '더 데이'|url=http://www.newsis.com/ar_detail/view.html?ar_id=NISX20150902_0010262687&cID=10601&pID=10600|website=NEWSIS|publisher=NEWSIS|access-date=September 4, 2015}}</ref> The album charted at No.2 on ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]''{{'}}s World Album Chart a week following its release.

Day6 held their first live concert ''D-Day'' for 2 nights in November 21–22, 2015 at YES24 MUVhall. Aside from performing songs from their debut album, the band also did multiple covers and played some unreleased materials of theirs.

On October 18, 2015, Day6 held their first overseas showcase in [[Taiwan]], which was also their first overseas schedule, at ATT Show Box.

On December 5, 2015, Day6 held their first overseas fanmeeting in [[Singapore]] at the Marina Bay Sands Convention Centre.

On February 27, 2016, JYP Entertainment stated that Junhyeok had departed from the band due to personal reasons and terminated his contract with the company.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.instiz.net/pt/3619876|title=[인티포털] JYP밴드 데이식스 멤버 준혁 공식 탈퇴|website=인스티즈(instiz, 音思提资)}}</ref> Day6 remained as a five-member band.

On March 30, 2016, Day6 released their second EP, ''[[Daydream (Day6 EP)|Daydream]]'', with the lead single "Letting Go".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.soompi.com/2016/03/29/day6-is-letting-go-by-rocking-out-in-comeback-mv/|title=Day6 Is "Letting Go" and Rocking Out in Comeback MV - Soompi|website=www.soompi.com}}</ref> On March 31, Day6 made their official stage debut on the music program ''[[M Countdown]]'', six months after their initial debut. Following the release, the band held their second live concert ''Dream'' for 2 nights on May 28–29, 2016 at YES24 Live Hall, and one night overseas in [[Bangkok]], [[Thailand]] at the Thunder Dome.

===2017: ''Every Day6'' project===
{{See also|Sunrise (Day6 album)|Moonrise (Day6 album)}}
On December 29, 2016, Day6 announced their upcoming monthly project in 2017 titled ''Every Day6'', in which the band would release two songs every month on the sixth along with concerts prior to the song's release, music videos, and 4 fixed [[V Live]] broadcasts.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.kpopstarz.com/articles/277586/20170103/day6-release-teaser-images-for-every-day6-january.htm|title=Day6 release teaser images for 'Every Day6 January'|last=KpopStarz|date=January 3, 2017|work=KpopStarz|access-date=April 10, 2017}}</ref> 

Day6 released their first monthly single with the release of lead single "I Wait" and sidetrack "Goodbye Winter" on January 6, 2017.<ref>{{cite news|title=Day6, 2017 JYP 첫 컴백주자…오는 6일 신곡발표|url=http://joynews.inews24.com/php/news_view.php?g_menu=700300&g_serial=999539|access-date=January 2, 2017|agency=joy news|date=January 2, 2017|language=ko}}</ref> Their second monthly single was released with the lead single  "You Were Beautiful" and sidetrack "My Day" on February 6.<ref>{{cite news|title=Day6, 2월 프로젝트송 6일 발표…이별감성 '예뻤어'|url=http://news.jtbc.joins.com/article/article.aspx?news_id=NB11411691|access-date=February 2, 2017|agency=JTBC News|date=January 31, 2017|language=ko}}</ref>

Their third monthly single with the lead single "How Can I Say" and sidetrack "I Would" was released on March 6. They also promoted on the music program ''[[M Countdown]]'' on March 9.<ref>{{Citation|last=Mnet K-POP|title=[Day6 – How Can I Say] Comeback Stage {{!}} M COUNTDOWN 170309 EP.514|date=March 9, 2017|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JRle7GfLFAQ|access-date=April 10, 2017}}</ref> Their fourth monthly single consisting of the lead single  "I'm Serious" and sidetrack "Say Wow" was released on April 6. They also promoted on ''M Countdown'' the same day. Their fifth monthly single was released on May 8, with the lead single "Dance Dance" and sidetrack "Man in a Movie".<ref>{{cite news|title="신나게 놀아보자" 데이식스, 5월 신곡 '댄스 댄스' 공개
|url=http://m.entertain.naver.com/read?oid=112&aid=0002917946|access-date=May 20, 2017|work=naver|date=May 8, 2017|language=ko}}</ref>

In June 2017, Day6 released their first full-length album, ''[[Sunrise (Day6 album)|Sunrise]]'', with a total of 14 tracks. It consists of the releases from January to May, the rebooted version of "Letting Go", the final version of "Congratulations",<ref>{{Cite news |url=http://daily.hankooki.com/lpage/entv/201705/dh20170530132643139050.htm |title=Day6, 첫 정규앨범 발매 소식과 함께 티저 이미지 공개 '팬들 환호' |date=May 30, 2017|access-date=May 30, 2017|work=Daily Korea|language=ko}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|last=Hong|first=C.|date=June 6, 2017|title=Listen : Day6 Teases Upcoming Full Album "Sunrise" With Spoiler Video|work=Soompi|url=https://www.soompi.com/2017/06/06/day6-teases-upcoming-full-album-sunrise-spoiler-video/|access-date=June 19, 2017}}</ref> and two new tracks: "I Smile" (the lead single of the album) and "Lean On Me". Day6 also announced their official fanclub name, "My Day", the same day.<ref>{{cite web|title=Day6 팬클럽명 투표 안내|url=https://fans.jype.com/BoardView?BoardName=DAY6_Notice&Num=163|publisher=fans.jype|access-date=June 7, 2017|language=ko|date=June 1, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|last=K|first=J.|date=June 7, 2017|title=Day6 Announces Official Fan Club Name After Comeback|work=Soompi|url=https://www.soompi.com/2017/06/07/day6-announces-official-fan-club-name/|access-date=June 19, 2017}}</ref>

Their July single consisting of the lead single "Hi Hello" and sidetrack "Be Lazy" was released on July 6. Their August single consisting of the lead single "What Can I Do" and sidetrack "Whatever!" released on August 7.<ref>{{cite news|title=Day6, 오늘(7일) 8월 프로젝트 가동…해외 진출까지|url=http://news.jtbc.joins.com/article/article.aspx?news_id=NB11504437|access-date=August 8, 2017|agency=jtbc News|date=August 7, 2017|language=ko}}</ref> Their September single with the lead single "I Loved You" and sidetrack "I'll Remember" released on September 6.<ref>{{cite news|author1=최정민|title=데이식스 제이, 신곡 '아이 러브드 유' 티저 공개텐아시아|url=http://tenasia.hankyung.com/archives/1284826|access-date=September 2, 2017|work=텐아시아|date=September 1, 2017|language=ko}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|author1=정지원|title=[Oh!쎈 컷] 데이식스 Jae, 안경 벗고 미소년 매력…'풋풋 비주얼'|url=http://www.koreadaily.com/news/read.asp?art_id=5558409|access-date=September 2, 2017|agency=Korea Daily|date=August 31, 2017|language=ko}}</ref> Day6 originally would released their October single on October 7 and later installment to released on September 29, which consists of the lead single "When You Love Someone" and the sidetrack "I Need Somebody". The music videos of their August, September, and October singles were a three-part story about high school friendship and love.

Day6 held their North American meet and greet tour, Day6 Live & Meet in North America 2017, hosted by SubKulture Entertainment. The tour started in Los Angeles on October 20, Austin on October 22, New York on October 24, Detroit on October 27, and stopped in Toronto on October 29.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Oh|first=Christina|date=Aug 25, 2017|title=DAY6 Announces Dates And Locations For 2017 North American Meet & Greet Tour|url=https://www.soompi.com/article/1034041wpp/day6-announces-dates-locations-2017-north-american-meet-greet-tour|access-date=2020-12-22|website=Soompi|language=en}}</ref>

Their November single consisting of the lead single "All Alone" and sidetrack "Pouring" was released on November 6. On November 28, JYP Entertainment announced the band's first nationwide tour in 2018, Every Day6 Concert Nationwide Tour. The tour started in Busan on January 20–21, Daegu on January 27 and stopped in Daejeon on February 10.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Jeong|first=Jee-won|date=2017-11-28|title=[공식입장] 'JYP 밴드' DAY6, 데뷔 후 첫 전국투어 개최|url=http://mosen.mt.co.kr/article/G1110784965|access-date=2020-12-22|website=Osen|language=ko}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Kim|first=Seong-won|date=2017-12-11|title='믿듣데' DAY6, 2018년 첫 전국투어 11일 부산 공연 티켓 오픈|url=https://sports.chosun.com/news/news.htm?id=201712120100085290005985&ServiceDate=20171211|access-date=2020-12-22|website=Sports Chosun|language=kr}}</ref>

On December 6, Day6 official concluded their monthly project with the releases of their second full-length album, ''[[Moonrise (Day6 album)|Moonrise]]''. The album contained the previous releases from July to November, the lead single "I Like You", two sidetrack ("Better Better" and "I'll Try"), and the final versions of the B-sides from their debut EP ''[[The Day (EP)|The Day]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Choi|first=Jeong-min|date=2017-12-06|title=데이식스, 정규 2집 'MOONRISE' 오늘(6일) 정오 공개 {{!}} 텐아시아|url=https://tenasia.hankyung.com/topic/article/2017120646014|access-date=2020-12-22|website=Ten Asia|language=ko}}</ref>

===2018: ''Youth'' series, Japanese debut and first world tour===
Day6 official announced the recruitment of their first generation fan club from January 5 to 25.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Kim|first=D.|date=Jan 3, 2018|title=DAY6 Announces Details For First Official Fan Club Recruitment|url=https://www.soompi.com/article/1102543wpp/day6-announces-details-first-official-fan-club-recruitment|access-date=2020-12-22|website=Soompi|language=en}}</ref>

Day6 made their official debut in Japan on March 14 with their first Japanese single, "[[If (Mata Aetara)]]". The single also serves as theme song for the Japanese drama ''Repeat''.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Choi|first=Jeong-min|date=2018-01-11|title=데이식스, 3월 14일 日 데뷔 확정…2018년도 '열일' 예고 {{!}} 텐아시아|url=https://tenasia.hankyung.com/topic/article/2018011170124|access-date=2020-12-22|website=Ten Asia|language=ko}}</ref> The music video for the single was released on February 2.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Jeong|first=Jee-won|date=2018-02-07|title=데이식스, 日 데뷔싱글 MV공개…글로벌 밴드 활약예고|url=http://osen.mt.co.kr/article/G1110829753|access-date=2020-12-22|website=Osen|language=ko}}</ref>

Day6 held their Every Day6 Finale Concert - The Best Moments on March 3 and 4 in the Olympic Hall.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2018-03-05|title=[TEN 공연] DAY6, 쇼는 계속 돼야 해|url=https://www.hankyung.com/entertainment/article/2018030523944|access-date=2020-12-22|website=Korea Economic Daily|language=ko}}</ref>

In April 2018, Day6 announced their first Japanese concert, Day6 1st Live in Japan 'The Best Day' on June 13 at Tsutaya O-East in Tokyo.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Jeong|first=Jee-won|date=2018-04-06|title=[공식입장] '믿듣데' 데이식스, 日데뷔 한달만에 도쿄·오사카 콘서트 개최|url=https://www.chosun.com/site/data/html_dir/2018/04/06/2018040601715.html|access-date=2020-12-22|website=Chosun Ilbo|language=ko}}</ref>

In June 2018, Day6 released their first [[greatest hits album]] in Japan, ''[[The Best Day (Day6 album)|The Best Day]]'', which is composed of the band's previous Korean singles and three new-version tracks: "Congratulations" (English Ver.), "I Wait" (Japanese Ver.), and "You Were Beautiful" (English Ver.).

On June 26, Day6 released their third EP ''[[Shoot Me: Youth Part 1]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Ahn|first=Jin-yong|date=2018-06-26|title=데이식스, 26일 미니앨범 'Shoot Me : Youth Part 1' 발매|url=http://www.munhwa.com/news/view.html?no=20180626MW150118325793|access-date=2020-12-22|website=Munhwa}}</ref> The EP contains seven tracks, with the lead single titled "Shoot Me". The second part, ''[[Remember Us: Youth Part 2]]'', was released on December 10 with the lead single "days gone by".<ref>{{cite web|date=Nov 23, 2018|title=Day6 to drop new album in December|url=http://kpopherald.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=201811231024466862420_2&ACE_SEARCH=1|access-date=November 29, 2018|work=K-POP Herald|language=en}}</ref> On October 17, 2018, between the release of the two parts, their first Japanese studio album ''[[Unlock (album)|Unlock]]'' was released.

Day6 went on their first world tour, titled ''Youth'', subsequently performing in 24 cities in Asia, Australia, North America and Europe from June 22, 2018, to January 29, 2019.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://entertain.naver.com/topic/1076271/1076271/read?oid=109&aid=0003919450|title=[인터뷰③] 데이식스 "올해 '행복한 날들이었다'..월드투어 한국어 떼창 감동"|work=OSEN|publisher=Naver|language=ko|date=December 11, 2018|access-date=January 30, 2019}}</ref>

It was announced in February 26 that Day6 would make a full group comeback in April 2021.<ref>{{Cite web|title='믿듣데' 온다…JYP측 "데이식스, 새 앨범 준비 중"[공식입장]|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/now/article/311/0001268852|access-date=2021-02-26|website=n.news.naver.com|language=ko}}</ref> On March 8, 2021, Sungjin announced during his a V Live broadcast that he would be enlisting for his [[conscription in South Korea|mandatory military service]], having already recorded for the upcoming album.<ref>{{Cite web|title=JYP, 데이식스 성진 입대에 "앨범은 준비 중" [공식]|url=https://entertain.v.daum.net/v/20210308123507330|access-date=2021-03-07|website=entertain.v.daum.net|language=ko}}</ref>  Day6 released their seventh extended play ''[[The Book of Us: Negentropy – Chaos Swallowed Up in Love|The Book of Us: Negentropy]]'' and its lead single "You Make Me" on April 19; the album marked the final installment of ''The Book of Us'' series.<ref>{{Cite web|title=DAY6, 새 앨범명은 'The Book of Us : Negentropy'|url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=609&aid=0000420833|access-date=2021-03-31|website=entertain.naver.com|language=ko}}</ref> Due to Sungjin's military enlistment, the group decided that they would not be doing album promotions for ''The Book of Us: Negentropy''.<ref>{{Cite web|title=JYP Entertainment announces that DAY6 will not promote their new album|url=https://www.allkpop.com/article/2021/04/jyp-entertainment-announces-that-day6-will-not-promote-their-new-album|access-date=2021-04-30|website=allkpop|language=en}}</ref>

On July 5, 2021, Even of Day returned with their second EP, ''Right Through Me'' and its lead single of the same name.<ref>{{Cite web|title=DAY6 이븐 오브 데이, 7월 5일 'Right Through Me' 컴백[공식]|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/now/article/108/0002963211|access-date=2021-02-26|website=n.news.naver.com|language=ko}}</ref> Young K debuted as Day6's second soloist with his first extended play, ''Eternal,'' on September 6.  He will also enlist in the military on October 12, 2021, where he will be serving in [[Korean Augmentation To the United States Army|KATUSA]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=‘낮경영 밤밴드’ 데이식스 영케이, 아이돌 최초 카투사 합격[스타와치]|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/609/0000480028|access-date=2021-08-17|website=n.news.naver.com|language=ko}}</ref>  Dowoon debuted as Day6's third soloist with his first digital single, "Out of the Blue", on September 27, which is a duet with Song Heejin.<ref>{{Cite web|title=DAY6 도운, 팀내 2번째 솔로 데뷔..27일 '문득' 발표[공식]|url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=108&aid=0002989520|access-date=2021-09-17|website=entertain.naver.com|language=ko}}</ref>
