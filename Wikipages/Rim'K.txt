'''Abdelkrim Brahmi''' a.k.a. '''Rim'K''' (born 21 June 1978 in [[Paris]], [[France]]) is an Algerian–French rapper. Rim'K was raised in the [[Paris]]ian suburb of [[Vitry-sur-Seine]]. His family is originally from  [[Barbacha]] in [[Algeria]] and is mentioned many times in his lyrics.

He is a member of the group [[113 (band)|113]] alongside [[Mokobé]] and [[AP (rapper)|AP]] and the super-group music collective [[Mafia K-1 Fry]]. He also has a group called Maghreb United.
