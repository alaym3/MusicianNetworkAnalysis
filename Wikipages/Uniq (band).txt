'''Uniq''' ([[Hangul]]: 유니크; stylized '''UNIQ''') is a five-member Chinese–South Korean [[boy group]] formed by Chinese company [[Yuehua Entertainment]] in 2014.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.yuehuamusic.com/artist|title=Yuehua Artists List|date=May 14, 2015|access-date=May 14, 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170825142656/http://www.yuehuamusic.com/artist|archive-date=August 25, 2017|url-status=dead}}</ref> The group consists of [[Zhou Yixuan]], [[Kim Sung-joo (entertainer)|Kim Sung-joo]], [[Li Wenhan]], [[Cho Seung-youn]], and [[Wang Yibo]]. Uniq officially debuted on October 20, 2014 with their debut single "Falling In Love" in both China and South Korea.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2014/10/5-member-chinese-korean-boy-group-uniq-to-make-their-debut-on-the-20th-with-falling-in-love|title=5-member Chinese Korean boy group Uniq to make their debut on the 20th with 'Falling In Love'|publisher=[[Allkpop]]|date=October 6, 2014|access-date=October 7, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141009114424/http://www.allkpop.com/article/2014/10/5-member-chinese-korean-boy-group-uniq-to-make-their-debut-on-the-20th-with-falling-in-love|archive-date=October 9, 2014|url-status=live}}</ref>

==History==
===Pre-debut===
Uniq was initially planned as a [[YG Entertainment]] boy group, with the five members spending between two to five years at YG as trainees.<ref>{{Cite web|title=YG Entertainment has been secretly training a new boy group|url=https://hellokpop.com/news/yg-entertainment-has-been-secretly-training-a-new-boy-group/|date=17 September 2014|access-date=4 December 2018|work=Hellokpop|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171226190734/http://www.hellokpop.com/news/yg-entertainment-has-been-secretly-training-a-new-boy-group/|archive-date=26 December 2017|url-status=live}}</ref><ref name="bzkryg">{{Cite web|url=https://entertain.v.daum.net/v/20161101101418045?entertainSectionName=entertain&entertainLeafName=20161101101418045|title=유니크 승연 "과거 방송인이 꿈, SNL 합류했으면.."(TRANS 161101 MD Interview - UNIQ Seungyoun "In the past being a broadcaster was my dream, if I join SNL.." )|date=2016-11-01}}</ref> Uniq was to be a collaborative effort with the Chinese company Yuehua Entertainment. Ultimately, the group debuted solely under Yuehua.<ref>{{Cite web|title=(UPDATE) #UNIQ: YG's New Korean-Chinese Boy Group To Debut Under Yuehua Entertainment|url=https://hype.my/2014/26815/uniq-ygs-new-korean-chinese-boy-group-to-debut-under-yuehua-entertainment/|date=19 September 2014|access-date=4 December 2018|work=Hype Malaysia|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181006080737/https://hype.my/2014/26815/uniq-ygs-new-korean-chinese-boy-group-to-debut-under-yuehua-entertainment/|archive-date=6 October 2018|url-status=live}}</ref><ref name=yg>{{Cite web|url=https://www.allkpop.com/article/2016/11/uniqs-seungyoun-talks-about-auditioning-for-sm-jyp-yg-former-yg-trainee-days|title=UNIQ's Seungyoun talks about auditioning for SM, JYP & YG + former YG trainee days|date=2016-11-01|access-date=2019-06-03|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190525173820/https://www.allkpop.com/article/2016/11/uniqs-seungyoun-talks-about-auditioning-for-sm-jyp-yg-former-yg-trainee-days|archive-date=2019-05-25|url-status=live}}</ref>

===2014: Debut with ''Falling in Love''===
On October 16, 2014, Uniq had their first broadcast performance in South Korea, on ''[[M Countdown]]''. Early in their debut, Uniq was chosen to participate in the [[original soundtrack|OST]] for the movies [[TMNT (2014 film)|''TMNT'']] and ''[[Penguins of Madagascar]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2014/11/uniq-celebrate-in-ost-mv-for-animation-film-penguins-of-madagascar|title=UNIQ 'Celebrate' in OST MV for animation film 'Penguins of Madagascar'|work=Allkpop|date=November 10, 2014|access-date=December 17, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141217105404/http://www.allkpop.com/article/2014/11/uniq-celebrate-in-ost-mv-for-animation-film-penguins-of-madagascar|archive-date=December 17, 2014|url-status=live}}</ref> On October 23, they released the song "Born to Fight" for the Chinese release of TNMT.<ref>{{cite web|title=UNIQ are 'Born To Fight' in OST MV for 'Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles'|url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2014/10/uniq-are-born-to-fight-in-ost-mv-for-teenage-mutant-ninja-turtles|publisher=Allkpop|date=October 23, 2014|access-date=November 3, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141103195738/http://www.allkpop.com/article/2014/10/uniq-are-born-to-fight-in-ost-mv-for-teenage-mutant-ninja-turtles|archive-date=November 3, 2014|url-status=live}}</ref>  On November 10, they released "Celebrate" for the Chinese release of ''Penguins of Madagascar''.

=== 2015: ''EOEO'' and Japanese Debut ===
In January 2015, Uniq became endorsers for [[Xtep]], a Chinese athletic brand. In February 2015, Uniq began promoting a Chinese beauty shop, Mix-Box, replacing [[Ko Chen-tung]] as the official endorser.<ref>{{cite news|title=UNIQ顶替柯震东代言MIXBOX美爆妆扮形象代言人|url=http://www.hljtv.com/2015/0215/683820.shtml|access-date=June 7, 2015|work=HNBTV|date=February 15, 2015|language=zh|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150609165652/http://www.hljtv.com/2015/0215/683820.shtml|archive-date=June 9, 2015}}</ref>

Uniq held their first fanmeet in Thailand in Bangkok on March 7.<ref>{{cite news|title=UNIQ แจกไฮทัชทุกที่นั่ง UNIQ 1st Fan Meeting in Bangkok 2015|url=http://music.mthai.com/news/newsasia/206267.html|access-date=June 7, 2015|work=MThai|language=th|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150615131402/http://music.mthai.com/news/newsasia/206267.html|archive-date=June 15, 2015|url-status=live}}</ref>

In April, Yuehua Entertainment announced UNIQ's comeback with their first mini album titled "EOEO".<ref>{{cite news|script-title=ko:유니크, 스타쉽과 손잡고 4월 국내 컴백|url=http://joynews.inews24.com/php/news_view.php?g_serial=892180&g_menu=700300|access-date=June 7, 2015|work=News1|date=April 9, 2015|language=ko|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150616211525/http://joynews.inews24.com/php/news_view.php?g_serial=892180&g_menu=700300|archive-date=June 16, 2015|url-status=live}}</ref> On April 22 they held their first comeback broadcast performance on MBC's [[Show Champion]], performing their title track "EOEO" and "Listen to Me". On April 23, Uniq made their official comeback in China, performing "Listen to Me" at the [[KU Music Asian Music Awards]]. They were also awarded with KU Music Asian Music Awards' "Best New Artist" the same day.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://sg.entertainment.yahoo.com/news/uniq-garner-best-rookie-of-the-year-during-043425401.html|title=UNIQ garner 'Best rookie of the Year' during 'Ku Music Asia Awards'|access-date=June 6, 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150705232016/https://sg.entertainment.yahoo.com/news/uniq-garner-best-rookie-of-the-year-during-043425401.html|archive-date=July 5, 2015|url-status=live}}</ref> On April 24, Uniq released their mini album and the official music video for "EOEO". After the release of "EOEO", the music video reached number 3 on the [[YinYueTai]] Weekly MV Chart.<ref>{{cite web|title=2015年第18期|url=http://vchart.yinyuetai.com/vchart/v?area=ML&date=20150427|access-date=2015-05-06|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150518150316/http://vchart.yinyuetai.com/vchart/v?area=ML&date=20150427|archive-date=2015-05-18|url-status=live}}</ref>

On May 22, Uniq released the official music video for their follow up track "Luv Again".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2015/05/uniq-are-in-luv-again-letting-loose-with-their-youthful-charms-in-follow-up-mv|title=UNIQ are in 'Luv Again', letting loose with their youthful charms in follow-up MV!|access-date=June 6, 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150525023236/http://www.allkpop.com/article/2015/05/uniq-are-in-luv-again-letting-loose-with-their-youthful-charms-in-follow-up-mv|archive-date=May 25, 2015|url-status=live}}</ref> UNIQ achieved their first music show award on [[China Central Television|CCTV's]] Global Chinese Music chart on May 23.<ref name="tv.cntv.cn">http://tv.cntv.cn/video/VSET100188787553/a06b180f22d14d67939b58179ada67cf {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150524154318/http://tv.cntv.cn/video/VSET100188787553/a06b180f22d14d67939b58179ada67cf |date=2015-05-24 }}.</ref><ref name="weibo">{{cite web| work=weibo|url=http://weibo.com/3596732311/CjcwawAX5#_rnd1432466701451| title= CCTV music channel special program "Global Chinese music charts list" | date=2015-05-23 | access-date=2014-05-23}}</ref>  Uniq broke the record for most wins on Global Chinese Music Chart with three wins by non-live voting and three wins by live voting, beating the record previously held by [[EXO-M]] in 2014.<ref>http://www.wzaobao.com/p/g889OY.html{{Dead link|date=July 2018 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=no }}</ref>

Uniq held their first showcases in Japan on July 19 and July 20 in Osaka and Tokyo respectively marking their debut in Japan.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://risecom.jp/uniq/news01.html|title=UNIQ SHOWCASE in Japan|date=May 22, 2015|work=Risecom|language=ja|access-date=June 7, 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150615010718/http://risecom.jp/uniq/news01.html|archive-date=June 15, 2015|url-status=dead}}</ref>

In December, Uniq began to endorse Dr. Bear, a Chinese candy brand.

To commemorate their one year since debut, Uniq released their second digital album, "Best Friend", on October 16.<ref>{{cite news|title=UNIQ Release 'Best Friend' To Commemorate 1-Year Anniversary|url=http://www.kpopstarz.com/articles/249571/20151020/uniq-digital-album-released.htm|access-date=March 23, 2016|work=Kpopstarz|date=October 20, 2015|language=en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160405234317/http://www.kpopstarz.com/articles/249571/20151020/uniq-digital-album-released.htm|archive-date=April 5, 2016|url-status=live}}</ref> Uniq announced their 1st Anniversary Tour to be held in Beijing, Shanghai, and Japan. Uniq returned to Japan in November holding fanmeets across the country in Nagoya, Osaka, Fukuoka before ending the Japan tour in Tokyo. At this time, they released a special Japanese [[single album]], "Best Friend," which includes the Japanese, Korean and Instrumental versions of "Best Friend."<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://uniq-japan.com/2015/10/29/1822/|title=UNIQデビュー１周年記念New Single発売決定！！！ - UNIQ Japan Official Website ユニークジャパンオフィシャルウェブサイト|access-date=2017-03-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170330175259/http://uniq-japan.com/2015/10/29/1822/|archive-date=2017-03-30|url-status=live}}</ref> UNIQ concluded the tour in Beijing on November 28. The Shanghai stop was postponed, however a new date was never announced.{{Citation needed|date=September 2020}}

On December 7, Uniq released "Erase Your Little Sadness."  On December 28 UNIQ released "Happy New Year", with labelmate, [[Cosmic Girls]], making a guest appearance in the music video.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/xin-nian-kuai-le-single/id1077252308|title=Happy New Year by UNIQ on iTunes|publisher=iTunes|access-date=2016-04-01|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160418100721/https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/xin-nian-kuai-le-single/id1077252308|archive-date=2016-04-18|url-status=live}}</ref> The song debuted at #4 position in [[Billboard chart|China V Chart]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/charts/china-v-chart/2016-04-09|title=China V Chart Weekly Ranking (April 9, 2016)|publisher=Billboard chart|access-date=April 1, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161109121105/http://www.billboard.com/charts/china-v-chart/2016-04-09|archive-date=November 9, 2016|url-status=live}}</ref>

=== 2016: China and Japan Promotion ===
Uniq began Inke endorsements in February 2016, a Chinese live-streaming app.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://v.youku.com/v_show/id_XMTQ2MzQ3MjI2NA==.html|title=光库-60s_160131_h264—在线播放—优酷网，视频高清在线观看|website=v.youku.com|access-date=2017-03-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170330181321/http://v.youku.com/v_show/id_XMTQ2MzQ3MjI2NA==.html|archive-date=2017-03-30|url-status=live}}</ref> In March 24, the group released the song "My Dream" for the soundtrack of the movie ''[[MBA Partners]]'', debuting at #3 position in [[Billboard chart|China V Chart]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/charts/china-v-chart/2016-04-09|title=China V Chart Weekly Ranking (April 9, 2016)|publisher=Billboard chart|access-date=April 1, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161109121105/http://www.billboard.com/charts/china-v-chart/2016-04-09|archive-date=November 9, 2016|url-status=live}}</ref> Yixuan, Wenhan and Sungjoo went on a two-week-long roadshow in 14 cities to promote the movie.

On June 25, Uniq held their first fanmeet in Sao Paulo, Brazil.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sympla.com.br/uniq-fanmeeting-em-sao-paulo__54802|title=UNIQ Fanmeeting em São Paulo|website=www.sympla.com.br|language=pt-BR|access-date=2017-03-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160601094217/https://www.sympla.com.br/uniq-fanmeeting-em-sao-paulo__54802|archive-date=2016-06-01|url-status=live}}</ref>

Uniq released a Japanese single album in December 2016, "Falling in Love".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.funfantoday.com/cjve/shop/item.cgi?item_id=1196|title=Falling In Love (Japanese version) / UNIQ|publisher=Victor Entertainment Shop|language=ja|access-date=January 11, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170113140442/http://www.funfantoday.com/cjve/shop/item.cgi?item_id=1196|archive-date=January 13, 2017|url-status=live}}</ref> Japanese versions of "Falling in Love" and "Listen to Me" are included in the single.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://uniq-japan.com/2016/12/22/2155/|title=2016.12.22 来日記念シングル「Falling In Love」Japanese Ver.追加販売決定！|publisher=UNIQ Japanese Site|language=ja|date=December 22, 2016|access-date=January 11, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170113083302/http://uniq-japan.com/2016/12/22/2155/|archive-date=January 13, 2017|url-status=live}}</ref> UNIQ held a Live Meet and Greet in Japan on December 10, and a fan signing and photo event on December 11.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://uniq-japan.com/2016/12/09/2153/|title=UNIQ Live＆Meet 2016 in JAPAN 【お詫び】 - UNIQ Japan Official Website ユニークジャパンオフィシャルウェブサイト|access-date=2017-03-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170215123559/http://uniq-japan.com/2016/12/09/2153/|archive-date=2017-02-15|url-status=live}}</ref>

===2017–present===
On January 19, 2017, the group released the single "Happy New Year 2017". The song was written by Zhou Yixuan and brings the freshness of the year that begins. Yibo was unable to participate in the music video and the recording of the song due to scheduling conflicts. In the MV, Yixuan, Sungjoo, Wenhan, and Seungyoun assembled a snowman that represents Yibo.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2017/01/uniq-wish-fans-a-happy-new-year-in-chinese-in-merry-new-mv|title=UNIQ wish fans a 'Happy New Year' in Chinese in merry new MV!|publisher=Allkpop|access-date=2017-01-27|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170123153317/http://www.allkpop.com/article/2017/01/uniq-wish-fans-a-happy-new-year-in-chinese-in-merry-new-mv|archive-date=2017-01-23|url-status=live}}</ref>

Uniq had two singles "Never Left" and "Next Mistake" released on April 19, 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sohu.com/a/228784723_607140|title=UNIQ单曲《不曾离开过》惊喜上线 暌违两年合体献声|publisher=Sohu|language=zh|access-date=2018-12-08|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181209124501/http://www.sohu.com/a/228784723_607140|archive-date=2018-12-09|url-status=live}}</ref>

The group has been on indefinite hiatus since 2018, although they have not officially disbanded, while the members have pursued independent projects. Yibo has pursued acting projects,<ref>{{Cite web|last=Lusky|first=Bridget|date=2020-02-25|title='The Untamed' cracked seemingly permanent cultural barriers|url=https://filmdaily.co/news/the-untamed-cultural-barriers/|access-date=2020-09-06|website=Film Daily|language=en-US}}</ref> while Wenhan debuted in the group [[Unine (band)|UNINE]], Yixuan debuted in the group New Storm and Seungyoun in [[X1 (band)|X1]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/now/article/213/0001120297|title='프듀X' 엑스원, 8월 27일 고척돔서 데뷔 쇼콘 개최|work=TV Report|date=July 22, 2019|access-date=July 27, 2019|language=ko|last=Kim|first=Min-ji|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190722160159/https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/now/article/213/0001120297|archive-date=July 22, 2019|url-status=live}}</ref> Sungjoo enlisted for his military service.

On October 16th 2020, for their 6th anniversary of debuting, Yixuan released the song "Remember That Day When We" dedicated to the UNIQ members and the fans.
