'''77 Bombay Street''' is a [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[folk rock]] musical group that was formed in 2008 in [[Scharans]], canton [[Graubünden]].<ref name=rohrer>{{cite web|last=Rohrer|first=Andreas|title=Band|url=http://www.77bombaystreet.com/en-GB/web/band|publisher=77 Bombay Street|access-date=3 May 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131204172610/http://www.77bombaystreet.com/en-GB/web/band|archive-date=2013-12-04|url-status=dead}}</ref> It consists of four brothers Matt, Joe, Simri-Ramon and Esra Buchli. The band was named after their temporary home in [[Adelaide]], [[Australia]], where the whole Buchli family lived for two years. As of 2019, the band had released four studio albums, the first called ''[[Dead Bird (album)|Dead Bird]]'' self-released, the other three ''[[Up in the Sky (album)|Up in the Sky]]'', ''[[Oko Town (album)|Oko Town]]'' and ''[[Seven Mountains (album)|Seven Mountains]]'' in association with [[Gadget Records]].{{fact|date=October 2020}}

Both ''Up in the Sky'' (2011) and ''Oko Town'' (2012) went platinum in Switzerland, the former peaking at the third place, the latter hitting the top of [[Schweizer Hitparade]], the official Swiss Albums Chart.<ref name=hitparade1>{{cite web|title=77 BOMBAY STREET - UP IN THE SKY|url=http://hitparade.ch/song/77-Bombay-Street/Up-In-The-Sky-804259|publisher=Hitparade.ch|access-date=3 May 2014}}</ref> According to their official Facebook site, the band is currently working on their fourth studio album.<ref>{{cite web|title=77 Bombay Street Facebook status, 9th January|url=https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=10151956755408432&set=a.10151759011383432.1073741828.105300453431&type=1&permPage=1|publisher=77 Bombay Street|access-date=3 May 2014}}</ref> In an interview Matt Buchli said "Yes, we are busy writing new songs and we’ve got a feeling it will be fantastic! Every album is a new and great challenge."<ref name=blattmann>{{cite web|last=Blattmann|first=Tim|title=77 Bombay Street|url=http://anadeyo.com/77-bombay-street/|publisher=Anadeyo.com|access-date=3 May 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140223030348/http://anadeyo.com/77-bombay-street/|archive-date=2014-02-23|url-status=dead}}</ref> 

In 2012 the band won two [[Swiss Music Awards]] with album ''Up in the Sky'' and its title track "Up in the Sky" for Best Album Pop/Rock National and Best Hit National, respectively.<ref name=swma2012>{{cite web|title=Swiss Music Awards Winners 2012|url=http://www.swissmusicawards.ch/sites/all/themes/sma_2014/pdf/Winners-2012.pdf|publisher=Swiss Music Awards|access-date=3 May 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131216170207/http://www.swissmusicawards.ch/sites/all/themes/sma_2014/pdf/Winners-2012.pdf|archive-date=2013-12-16|url-status=dead}}</ref> In 2015, the band worked on their fourth studio album, ''Seven Mountains'', which has been released in fall 2015.<ref name="esseiva">{{cite web|last=Esseiva | first = Lucien | title =77 Bombay Street gehen down under |url =http://www.20min.ch/entertainment/musik/story/13132708 | date = February 3, 2015 | access-date = February 7, 2015 | publisher = 20 Minuten | language = de }}</ref>
