'''''IOWA''''' is a 2005 American independent [[neo-noir]] film directed by, written by, and starring Matt Farnsworth. The film follows two young Iowan lovers who decide to cook their own [[methamphetamine]]. Critics gave negative reviews.

==Plot==
After his father dies, Esper Harte learns that he may collect on his father's insurance.  However, his mother and a crooked cop want to get rid of him so that they can take the money.
Desperate to escape their problems, Esper and his girlfriend, Donna Huffman, decide to cook their own methamphetamine.

* [[Rosanna Arquette]] as Effie Harte
* [[John Savage (actor)|John Savage]] as Irv Huffman

==Release==
''IOWA'' premiered at the [[Tribeca Film Festival]] on April 22, 2005.<ref>{{cite web|title=Iowa|url=http://tribecafilm.com/filmguide/archive/512cdbe01c7d76e04600026b-iowa|work=[[Tribeca Film Festival]]|accessdate=15 July 2013}}</ref>

==Reception==
The film received negative reviews.  [[Rotten Tomatoes]] reports 15% of surveyed critics liked it, with a 3.9/10 rating average out of 13 reviews.<ref>{{cite web|title=Iowa|url=http://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/10006731-iowa/|work=[[Rotten Tomatoes]]|accessdate=15 July 2013}}</ref>  [[Metacritic]] gave it a score of 35/100, indicating "generally unfavorable reviews".<ref>{{cite web|title=Iowa reviews|url=http://www.metacritic.com/movie/iowa|work=[[Metacritic]]|accessdate=15 July 2013}}</ref>

''[[Slant Magazine]]'' called the film "stupendously slipshod" and "meandering, amateurish sleaze",<ref>{{cite web|last=Schager|first=Nick|title=Iowa|url=http://www.slantmagazine.com/film/review/iowa/2084|work=[[Slant Magazine]]|accessdate=15 July 2013|date=March 28, 2006}}</ref> while ''[[The New York Times]]'' criticized the lack of subtlety.<ref>{{cite news|last=Kern|first=Laura|title=Dreams, Drugs and Danger in 'Iowa'|url=https://movies.nytimes.com/2006/03/31/movies/31iowa.html?_r=0|accessdate=15 July 2013|newspaper=[[The New York Times]]|date=March 31, 2006}}</ref> In a more positive review, ''[[The Village Voice]]'' praised the film's energy and acting.<ref>{{cite news|last=Atkinson|first=Michael|title='Iowa'|url=http://www.villagevoice.com/2006-03-21/film/iowa/|accessdate=15 July 2013|newspaper=[[The Village Voice]]|date=Mar 21, 2006}}</ref>  [[Monsters and Critics]] said "This story and the characters in it are as real as the day is long and faithful to all that is good and bad about growing up with ever shrinking horizons."<ref>{{cite web|last=Wilkinson|first=Ron|title=Iowa|url=http://www.monstersandcritics.com/movies/reviews/article_1151477.php/Movie_Review_Iowa|work=[[Monsters and Critics]]|access-date=11 October 2013|date=Mar 31, 2006|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131012050652/http://www.monstersandcritics.com/movies/reviews/article_1151477.php/Movie_Review_Iowa|archive-date=12 October 2013|df=}}</ref>
