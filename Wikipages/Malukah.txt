'''Malukah''' is the artist name of '''Judith de los Santos'''. She is a Mexican composer and singer-songwriter known for her [[cover version]]s of music from video games or TV series and movies, which she publishes on YouTube. She became known to an international audience when a video of her cover of the song ''The Dragonborn Comes'' from the game ''[[The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim]]'' became a [[viral video]] in November 2011.<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=https://www.dailydot.com/parsec/gaming/gamer-covers-video-game-music/|title=From 'Dragon Age' to 'Skyrim,' singer gives new life to video game music|date=26 December 2013|work=The Daily Dot|access-date=10 January 2018|language=en-US}}</ref> Since then she has contributed to several [[video game soundtracks]], composed and produced original music, and performed live.

==Career==
=== Early career and education ===
Being a gamer since childhood, Judith de los Santos grew up with gaming related music. She started writing songs and singing at the age of 15,<ref name=":11">{{Cite web|url=https://www.broadjam.com/bio/summitsongs|title=Summit Songs Presents: Bio {{!}} Broadjam.com|website=Broadjam|language=en|access-date=12 February 2020}}</ref> took guitar, piano and percussion lessons<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.gamepressure.com/editorials/interview-with-malukah-gamers-favorite-musician-and-composer/za91|title=Interview with Malukah – gamers' favorite musician and composer|website=Gamepressure.com|language=en|access-date=10 February 2020}}</ref> to later study at the [[Berklee College of Music]]. She graduated in 2005 with a dual major in ''[[Music Production and Engineering Major at Berklee|Music Production and Engineering]]'' and ''[[Film score|Film Scoring]]'' with her debut album ''All of the Above''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.engadget.com/2012/09/20/youtube-skyrim-cover-phenom-malukah-on-epic-music-and-playing-wo/|title=YouTube Skyrim cover phenom Malukah on epic music and playing WoW|website=Engadget|language=en|access-date=28 October 2019}}</ref>

Malukah streamed as a partnered streamer on [[Twitch (service)|Twitch]] for almost 1,000 hours between 2016 and 2020, having reached 662,000 views and 38,800 followers in the music & performing arts and gaming category. Since 2020, she focused live streaming on [[YouTube]].

In 2019, she released her latest original album ''I Follow the Moon''.

== Style and genre ==
Her two original albums mostly cover the genres [[Pop rock]] or [[Folk rock]]. Collaborations with other musicians like [[Sascha Dikiciyan|Sonic Mayhem]] or [[Headhunterz]] are influenced by [[electronic music]]. Her contributions to the ''[[Call of Duty: Black Ops 4|Call of Duty: Black Ops]]'' games cover the genre of [[Heavy metal music|Heavy Metal]]. The video game contributions, movie related originals, covers and medleys can be classified as fantasy folk rock or fantasy music. Her covers are influenced by music set to games like ''[[Mass Effect]]'', ''[[The Elder Scrolls]]'', ''[[The Witcher (video game series)|The Witcher]],'' ''[[Halo (franchise)|Halo]]'' and ''[[Destiny (video game)|Destiny]]'', movies like ''[[The Hobbit (film series)|The Hobbit]]'' and ''[[The Lord of the Rings (film series)|The Lord of the Rings]]'' or TV series like ''[[Game of Thrones]]''.

Her [[vocal range]] can be considered as [[alto]].
