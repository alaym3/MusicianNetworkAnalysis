'''Victor Adere''' (born 2 August 1993), better known as '''Victor AD''', is a [[Nigerian]] singer and songwriter.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.legit.ng/1208393-how-i-felt-i-heard-song-encourages-rituals-fraud-victor-ad.html|title=How I felt when I heard my song encourages rituals and fraud – Victor AD|publisher=Legit Nigeria|date=13 August 2018 |access-date=11 January 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://punchng.com/my-music-not-the-cause-of-robbers-attack-victor-ad/|title=My music not the cause of robbers’ attack –Victor AD|publisher=Punch Nigeria|date=13 August 2018 |access-date=11 January 2019}}</ref> In 2019 He posted a picture of himself on the street of [[Dubai]] with the caption “God I still remember this time last year, I had no passport but now Dubai be like backyard" just to recall the journey to success so far. <ref>{{Cite web|date=2019-03-16|title=Victor AD shares grass to grace story|url=https://www.vanguardngr.com/2019/03/victor-ad-shares-grass-to-grace-story/|access-date=2021-02-27|website=Vanguard News|language=en-US}}</ref>

==Biography==
Victor Eugenie Adere was born in Kirikiri, [[Apapa]], [[Lagos State]], where he grew up with his family. He is the first of four children. He attended Heritage International School in Lagos and Ekpan Secondary School in [[Warri]].

Victor began his music career in early 2014. He has worked with producers such as Kulboybeat, Kizzybeatz, ID Clef, Mr Nolimitz. Victor AD's first music release was "Jowo"; the song became a theme song for Radio Continental.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.naijapals.com/music/Jowo_Victor_A_D_prod_bycoolboybeatz_-41953|title=Jowo – Victor.A.D|publisher=Naijapals|date=13 August 2018 |access-date=11 January 2019}}</ref>

In 2017, Victor AD released the single "No Idea".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tooxclusive.com/music/victor-ad-no-idea/|title=Victor AD – No Idea |publisher=TooXclusive|author=Tomiwa|date=20 November 2018|access-date=11 January 2019}}</ref> The music video for the song, which was shot and directed by Cinema House Images in late 2018, was endorsed by TV stations in Nigeria.<ref>{{cite web|last=Arunaye|first=Newton|date=11 August 2018|title=Victor AD – No Idea Video|url=https://legit9ja.com/2018/11/video-victor-ad-no-idea.html|url-status=live|access-date=11 January 2019|website=|publisher=Legit9ja}}</ref>

In 17 June 2018, Victor AD released the single "[[Wetin We Gain]]",<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.pulse.ng/entertainment/music/victor-ads-wetin-we-gain-is-the-theme-song-for-the-average-nigerian-youth/vzbrg2l|title=Victor AD's 'Wetin We Gain' is the theme song for the average Nigerian youth |publisher=Pulse Nigeria|date=13 August 2018 |access-date=11 January 2019}}</ref> and was discovered by the management company Longitude Promotions in 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://empirenaija.com/who-is-victor-ad-biography-history-songs/|title=Who is Victor Ad? (Biography, Net Worth, Songs)|last=Mvphenryeto|date=1 February 2019|access-date=3 February 2019}}</ref> The music video for "Wetin We Gain" has garnered more than 9 million views on YouTube.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://thenet.ng/watch-victor-ads-emotional-performance-day-2-felabration-2018/|title=Watch Victor AD's Emotional Performance at Day 2 Of Felabration 2018 |publisher=The Net Nigeria|date=13 August 2018 |access-date=11 January 2019}}</ref>
