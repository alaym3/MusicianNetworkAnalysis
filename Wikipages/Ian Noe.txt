'''Ian Noe''' (born May 9, 1990) is a singer-songwriter from [[Beattyville, Kentucky]] US.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-country/ian-noe-between-the-country-840836/ |title=Americana Artist You Need to Know: Ian Noe |last=Mooney |first=Thomas |date=May 28, 2019 |website=Rolling Stone  |access-date=June 2, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.codaagency.com/artist/ian-noe |title=Ian Noe – Coda Music Agency |website=codaagency.com |access-date=June 2, 2019}}</ref> Noe's debut album ''Between The Country''  was produced by [[Dave Cobb]] who plays guitar on the album,<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.capturekentucky.com/review-appalachian-gold-struck-again-ian-noe-releases-between-the-country-on-may-31st/ |title=REVIEW: Appalachian Gold Struck Again. Ian Noe Releases 'Between The Country' On May 31st |date=May 24, 2019 |website=Capture Kentucky |access-date=June 2, 2019}}</ref> while [[Adam Gardner]] plays bass and organ piano.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://americanahighways.org/2019/05/29/review-ian-noes-between-the-country-is-the-kind-of-album-nobody-makes-anymore/ |title=REVIEW: Ian Noe's "Between the Country" is the Kind of Album Nobody Makes Anymore • Americana Highways |date=May 29, 2019 |website=Americana Highways  |access-date=June 2, 2019}}</ref>

In 2007 Ian Noe won the grand prize in the Appalachian [[Starsearch]] in [[Hazard, Kentucky]] with his original song ''Don't Let The Morning Bring Ya Down''.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://ourstage.com/profile/iannoe |title=Ian Noe |website=OurStage  |access-date=June 2, 2019}}</ref>

His subject matter includes death, addiction, lost love, and poverty.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.raisedrowdy.com/2019/05/31/ian-noe-steps-out-of-appalachian-kentucky-and-into-somewhere-between-the-country/ |title=Ian Noe steps out of Appalachian Kentucky and into somewhere 'Between The Country' |date=May 31, 2019 |website=Raised Rowdy  |access-date=June 2, 2019}}</ref>

Noe is featured performing several of his songs on a [[Jason Momoa]] video and he supported [[John Prine]] on tour in Germany, Sweden and Norway in 2019. Noe has also supported [[Son Volt]], [[Jamestown Revival]] and [[Blackberry Smoke]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.broadwayworld.com/bwwmusic/article/Ian-Noes-BETWEEN-THE-COUNTRY-Now-Streaming-At-NPR-Music-20190523 |title=Ian Noe's BETWEEN THE COUNTRY Now Streaming at NPR Music |author=BWW News Desk |website=BroadwayWorld.com  |access-date=June 2, 2019}}</ref>

In June 2019 he was cover image of Spotify's Fresh Folk playlist.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://open.spotify.com/playlist/37i9dQZF1DXaUDcU6KDCj4 |title=Fresh Folk, a playlist by Spotify |website=Spotify  |access-date=June 2, 2019}}</ref>
