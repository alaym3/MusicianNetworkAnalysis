'''Cathrinus "Rinus" Dijkstra''' (born 19 October 1969<ref>[http://www.dekrantvantoen.nl/vw/article.do?code=LC&date=20110714&id=LC-20110714-ZU01001005 Filmpje zanger Rinus hit in Verenigde Staten], Leeuwarder Courant, 14 July 2007 (source for date of birth)</ref>), professionally known as '''Zanger Rinus''' ([[Dutch language|Dutch]] for "Singer Rinus"), is a Dutch musician.

== Biography ==
In 2009 Rinus released the song Hey Marlous, an adaptation of ''Schöne Maid, hast Du heut' für mich Zeit'' by the German singer [[Tony Marshall (singer)|Tony Marshall]] from 1972. ''Hey Marlous'' is about Dutch singer [[Marlous Oosting]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.waldnet.nl/wn/videonieuws/261/Zanger_Rinus_verrast_complete_interview.html|title=WâldNet – Het laatste nieuws uit Noordoost Friesland|work=WâldNet|access-date=2018-03-07|language=nl-NL}}</ref> He became well-known among the general public because the television program ''[[De Wereld Draait Door]]'' paid attention to it in one of their broadcast in the section ''De tv draait door''.<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QrTu2dBiBZw Zanger Rinus at "De Wereld Draait Door"]</ref>

After this followed more clips, such as ''Hé Kastelein'' (2010) and ''Met Romana op de Scooter''. The latter song was put on YouTube on 8 July 2011 and was viewed more than 300,000 times within five days.<ref>{{Citation|title=Zanger Rinus: 'Met Romana op de scooter'|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RXiJcFVds0E|language=en|access-date=2018-03-07}}</ref> The song also attracted international attention: on the American news blog ''[[HuffPost|The Huffington Post]]'' an article was dedicated to the video.<ref>[http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2011/07/13/rinus-and-romana-chill-on_n_896947.html Zanger Rinus and Romana Chill On A Scooter In Bizarre Dutch Music Video], Huffington Post, 13 juli 2011.</ref>

His life partner Debora (since 1998) can often be seen in his video clips. Both also make an appearance in the song "Make you pop" by [[Diplo]] & [[Don Diablo]].

Rinus has an [[intellectual disability]]. In his daily life, he is a woodworker in a social workshop.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.zangerrinus.nl/over-zanger-rinus/4553950755|title=Over Zanger Rinus – Zanger Rinus Dijkstra|website=www.zangerrinus.nl|language=en|access-date=2018-03-07}}</ref>
