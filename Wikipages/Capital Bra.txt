'''Vladislav Balovatsky'''<ref name="rbb24">{{cite web |url=https://www.rbb24.de/kultur/beitrag/2018/04/berliner-rapper-capital-bra.html |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190511191821/https://www.rbb24.de/kultur/beitrag/2018/04/berliner-rapper-capital-bra.html |url-status=dead |archive-date=11 May 2019 |title=Berliner Rapper in Spotify-Charts: Wer dieser Capital Bra ist |trans-title=Berlin rapper in the Spotify Charts: Who is Capital Bra |publisher=[[RBB Fernsehen|rbb24]] |first=Tom |last=Garus |date=22 April 2018 |access-date=11 November 2018 |language=de }}</ref> ({{lang-uk|Владислав Баловацький}}; born 23 November 1994), known professionally as '''Capital Bra''' (<small>German pronunciation:</small> [[Help:IPA/Standard German|[kɐpiˌtal ˈbɾa]]]) and informally as '''Capital''', '''Capi''' and '''Joker Bra''', is a German [[Rapping|rapper]] of Ukrainian and Russian descent.  ''Bra'' is the short form of ''Bratan'', the Russian word for brother.<ref>{{cite web|title=Hip-Hopper Capital Bra hat in Deutschland mehr Charterfolge als die Beatles - wie kann das sein?|periodical=stern.de|publisher=|url=https://www.stern.de/neon/feierabend/musik-literatur/capital-bra-hat-in-deutschland-riesenerfolg--wie-kann-das-sein--8848692.html|url-status=|format=|access-date=|archive-url=|archive-date=|last=Jurek Skrobala|date=2019-08-17|year=|language=de|pages=|quote=}}</ref>

Born in a small town in [[Siberia]], Russia and raised in [[Dnipro]]petrovsk in Ukraine, he and his mother moved to [[Berlin]], Germany in the early 2000s.<ref name="radiomonster">{{cite web |url=https://www.radiomonster.fm/interpret/tophits/capital-bra/ |title=Capital Bra – Steckbrief/Biografie |trans-title=Capital Bra – Characteristics/Biography |date=8 September 2018 |access-date=13 November 2018 |publisher=Radio Monster |language=de |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181113031614/https://www.radiomonster.fm/interpret/tophits/capital-bra/ |archive-date=13 November 2018 |url-status=live}}</ref> He started rapping at age 11 and participated at ''Rap am Mittwoch'' in 2014, a German [[battle rap]] tournament. Intent on pursuing a solo career as a rapper, Capital Bra released his first studio album ''Kuku Bra'' in January 2016 to minor commercial success in Germany. The follow-ups ''Makarov Complex'' and ''Blyat'' were released in February and September 2017 and peaked in the top 5 of German-speaking Europe. His fourth studio album, ''[[Berlin lebt]]'' (2018), peaked at number one in Germany, Austria and Switzerland and the follow-up ''[[Allein (album)|Allein]]'' (2018), debuted at number two on the German album charts. Balovatsky is the most successful charting German act, with 22 number-one songs in Germany.

==Biography==
===1994–2014: Early life and Rap am Mittwoch===
Vladislav Balovatsky was born on 23 November 1994 in [[Siberia]], Russia to Ukrainian<ref name="hiphopdeinterview">{{cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6iKstpz6zi0 |title=Capital Bra: LX, Bonez MC, Olexesh, Ufo361, Ukraine, russischer Rap & Fame (Interview) |trans-title=Capital Bra: LX, Bonez MC, Olexesh, Ufo361, Ukraine, russian rap & fame (Interview) |date=17 January 2017 |access-date=13 November 2018 |publisher=Hiphop.de |via=[[YouTube]] |language=de |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181209103242/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6iKstpz6zi0 |archive-date=9 December 2018 |url-status=live}}</ref> parents, who worked in the [[petroleum]] industry. At a young age Balovatsky and his parents moved to [[Dnipro]]petrovsk in Ukraine. Balovatsky's mother decided to move to Germany in 2001 to find a better life for her son.<ref name="hiphopdeinterview" /> They settled in [[Alt-Hohenschönhausen]], [[Berlin]], where Balovatsky spent his youth.<ref name="radiomonster" /> His father stayed in the Ukraine. He played [[Association football|football]] for [[Berliner FC Dynamo]].<ref name="juice">{{cite web |url=https://juice.de/capital-wenn-es-mit-musik-nicht-klappt-mache-ich-eben-wieder-sachen-auf-der-strasse-bra-interview/ |title=Capital: "Wenn es mit Musik nicht klappt, mache ich eben wieder Sachen auf der Straße, Bra" // HipHope |author=Johann Voigt |date=3 June 2016 |access-date=13 April 2018 |work=Juice |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180414010723/https://juice.de/capital-wenn-es-mit-musik-nicht-klappt-mache-ich-eben-wieder-sachen-auf-der-strasse-bra-interview/ |archive-date=14 April 2018 |url-status=live }}</ref> Under the pseudonym "Smia One", Balovatsky uploaded his first music video onto YouTube in 2009.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://raptastisch.net/2018/05/03/capital-bra-kleines-kind-song/ |title=Capital Bra hat schon als kleines Kind gerappt |date=3 May 2018 |publisher=Raptastisch |access-date=24 January 2019 |language=de |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190124102107/https://raptastisch.net/2018/05/03/capital-bra-kleines-kind-song/ |archive-date=24 January 2019 |url-status=live}}</ref> He rose to prominence in German underground rap through ''Rap am Mittwoch'', a German [[battle rap]] tournament where he participated in a number of battles. His last competition against Master Marv was the most-watched ''Rap am Mittwoch'' battle on [[YouTube]], with more than 7.8 million views as of January 2019.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZKkk8ARvIdQ |title=Rap am Mittwoch: Capital Bra vs Master Marv 04.02.15 BattleMania Finale (4/4) German Battle |date=12 February 2015 |access-date=19 January 2019 |via=[[YouTube]] |language=de |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180823120301/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZKkk8ARvIdQ |archive-date=23 August 2018 |url-status=live}}</ref><ref name="juice" /> Following his win, Balovatsky announced his departure from Rap am Mittwoch.<ref name="Brakarriere">{{cite web |url=https://hiphop.de/video/von-rap-am-mittwoch-in-charts-capital-bras-karriere-in-4-phasen?nopaging=1 |title=Von Rap Am Mittwoch in die Charts: Capital Bras Karriere in 4 Phasen |publisher=HipHop.de |date=27 September 2017 |first=Clark |last=Senger |language=de |access-date=19 January 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190119180203/https://hiphop.de/video/von-rap-am-mittwoch-in-charts-capital-bras-karriere-in-4-phasen?nopaging=1 |archive-date=19 January 2019 |url-status=live}}</ref> In March, Balovatsky released his first single "Bra".<ref name="Brakarriere" />

===2015–2017: First three albums===
[[File:Capital Bra bei GERMANIA (Video-Format von funk).png|thumb|Balovatsky in an interview in early 2017]]
Balovatsky, at this time known as Capital, announced his first release ''[[Kuku Bra]]'' on 1 December 2015, through the single "Bra macht die Uzi".<ref name="bra macht die uzi">{{cite web |url=https://www.def-shop.com/magazin/capital-bra-macht-die-uzi/ |title=Capital – "Bra macht die Uzi" |date=1 December 2015 |first=Christine |last=Leitzke |publisher=Defshop Magazin |access-date=12 December 2018 |language=de |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181215221931/https://www.def-shop.com/magazin/capital-bra-macht-die-uzi/ |archive-date=15 December 2018 |url-status=live}}</ref> Three more singles have been released, including "Kuku Habibi", "Fluchtwagen glänzen" and "Kreide", all three alongside German rapper King Khalil. None of them entered the charts however and the album was released on 29 January 2016 through Baba City, Chapter One and distributed through Universal.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://hiphop.de/release/capital-kuku-bra#.WDXXz7LhCUk |title=Capital (Bra) – Kuku Bra |date=12 February 2016 |access-date=2 November 2018 |publisher=Hiphop.de |language=de |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181104131412/https://hiphop.de/release/capital-kuku-bra#.WDXXz7LhCUk |archive-date=4 November 2018 |url-status=live }}</ref> The album debuted at No. 32 on the German album chart on issue dated 19 February 2016.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.offiziellecharts.de/charts/album/for-date-1455879600000 |title=Offizielle Deutsche Charts: Top 100 Album–Charts – 19. Februar 2016 |trans-title=Official German charts: Top 100 album charts – 19 February 2016 |publisher=[[GfK Entertainment Charts]] |date=19 February 2016 |access-date=12 December 2018 |language=de}}</ref> and No. 61 on the Austrian album charts.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://austriancharts.at/showitem.asp?interpret=Capital&titel=Kuku+bra&cat=a |title=Capital Bra – Kuku Bra |work=austriancharts.de |access-date=13 April 2018 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180613190052/http://austriancharts.at/showitem.asp?interpret=Capital&titel=Kuku+bra&cat=a |archive-date=13 June 2018 |url-status=live }}</ref> 

His second studio album was announced less than a year later in November 2016.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://hiphop.de/magazin/news/capital-kuendigt-ein-neues-album-an-release-datum-bekannt-296948 |title=Capital Bra kündigt neues Album an: Das Release-Datum steht fest |date=23 November 2018 |access-date=2 November 2018 |publisher=Hiphop.de |language=de |first=David |last=Molke |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181104132925/https://hiphop.de/magazin/news/capital-kuendigt-ein-neues-album-an-release-datum-bekannt-296948 |archive-date=4 November 2018 |url-status=live }}</ref> The first single of the album, "Ala Ba Ba" featuring [[Ufo361]] was released in mid December 2016.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://rap.de/soundandvideo/97674-capital-bra-feat-ufo361-ala-ba-ba-prod-saven-musiq/ |title=Capital Bra feat. Ufo361 – Ala Ba Ba (prod. Saven Musiq) |first=Oliver |last=Marquart |date=18 December 2016 |access-date=2 November 2018 |publisher=Rap.de |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181104125834/https://rap.de/soundandvideo/97674-capital-bra-feat-ufo361-ala-ba-ba-prod-saven-musiq/ |archive-date=4 November 2018 |url-status=live }}</ref> The second single "Es geht ums Geschäft", released in January 2017 and became his first song to enter the German single charts, reaching number 76.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.backspin.de/capital-bra-es-geht-ums-geschaeft/ |title=Capital Bra – "Es geht ums Geschäft" |date=16 January 2017 |publisher=Backspin |access-date=2 November 2018 |language=de |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180622060206/https://www.backspin.de/capital-bra-es-geht-ums-geschaeft/ |archive-date=22 June 2018 |url-status=live }}</ref> The following two singles "Geld machen" and "Die Echten" didn't enter the charts. ''Makarov Komplex'' was released on 3 February 2017, through Auf!Keinen!Fall!, Chapter One and Team Kuku. The album debuted within the top five in German-speaking Europe. It reached the top of the charts on the Austrian album charts and the German hip-hop album charts.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://austriancharts.at/showitem.asp?interpret=Capital+Bra&titel=Makarov+Komplex&cat=a |title=Capital Bra – Makarov Complex |date=17 February 2017 |access-date=2 November 2018 |language=de |publisher=[[Ö3 Austria Top 40]] |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181104131345/https://austriancharts.at/showitem.asp?interpret=Capital+Bra&titel=Makarov+Komplex&cat=a |archive-date=4 November 2018 |url-status=live }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.offiziellecharts.de/charts/hiphop/for-date-1486681200000 |title=Offizielle Deutsche Charts – Top 20 Hiphop Charts 10. Februar 2017 (''Makarov Complex'') |trans-title=Official german charts – top 20 hip hop charts (''Makarov Komplex'') |date=10 February 2017 |access-date=24 October 2018 |publisher=[[GfK Entertainment Charts]] |language=de |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181028052150/https://www.offiziellecharts.de/charts/hiphop/for-date-1486681200000 |archive-date=28 October 2018 |url-status=live }}</ref>

Three months later, he released his unannounced second EP ''[[Ibrakadabra]]''. It included five tracks, all produced by Saven Musiq and written by Balovatsky. Commercially, the EP charted at No. 77 in Switzerland. Balovatsky announced his third studio album ''[[Blyat (album)|Blyat]]'' on 16 June 2017, through [[Facebook]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.16bars.de/newsartikel/154529/capital-bra-kuendigt-neues-album-fuer-dieses-jahr-an/ |title=Capital Bra kündigt neues Album für dieses Jahr an |trans-title=Capital Bra announces new album forn this year |publisher=16Bars |date=16 June 2017 |access-date=4 November 2018 |language=de |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180618230140/http://www.16bars.de/newsartikel/154529/capital-bra-kuendigt-neues-album-fuer-dieses-jahr-an/ |archive-date=18 June 2018 |url-status=live }}</ref> The album was supported by six singles, including "Nur noch Gucci" and "Olé olé", which became his first singles to be certified gold by the [[Bundesverband Musikindustrie]] in early 2018.<ref name="BVMI">{{cite web |url=https://www.musikindustrie.de/markt-bestseller/gold-/platin-und-diamond-auszeichnung/datenbank/?action=suche&strTitel=Nur+Noch+Gucci&strInterpret=Capital+Bra&strTtArt=alle&strAwards=checked |title=Gold-/Platin Datenbank (Capital Bra; "Nur noch Gucci") |trans-title=Gold and platinum database (Capital Bra; "Nur noch Gucci") |publisher=[[Bundesverband Musikindustrie]] |access-date=13 April 2018 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181209103243/http://www.musikindustrie.de/nc/datenbank/?action=suche&strTitel=Nur+Noch+Gucci&strInterpret=Capital+Bra&strTtArt=alle&strAwards=checked |archive-date=9 December 2018 |url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://raptastisch.net/2018/03/24/capital-bra-feiert-seine-ersten-goldenen-schallplatten/ |publisher=Raptastisch |title=Capital Bra feiert seine ersten goldenen Schallplatten |trans-title=Capital Bra celebrates his first gold records |language=de |date=25 March 2018 |access-date=13 April 2018 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180414091334/https://raptastisch.net/2018/03/24/capital-bra-feiert-seine-ersten-goldenen-schallplatten/ |archive-date=14 April 2018 |url-status=live }}</ref> The album was released on 29 September 2017, through Team Kuku and Auf!Keinen!Fall!. The album debuted within the top five in German-speaking Europe. It spent 50 weeks on the German, 25 on the Austrian and five on the Swiss album charts. In February and March 2018, he went on a 16-date German-speaking Europe tour, alongside King Khalil.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.boutblank.com/2017/10/06/capital-bra-tour-tickets-2018-sichern/%20%2071293 |title=Capital Bra Tour Tickets 2018 sichern |date=6 October 2017 |publisher=Boutblank |first=Tyler |last=B. |access-date=26 March 2019 |language=de |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181210154615/https://www.boutblank.com/2017/10/06/capital-bra-tour-tickets-2018-sichern/%20%2071293 |archive-date=10 December 2018 |url-status=live}}</ref>
