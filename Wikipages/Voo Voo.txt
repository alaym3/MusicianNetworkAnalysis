'''Voo Voo''' is a Polish rock band formed in 1985.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.europopmusic.eu/Poland_pages/VooVoo.html|title=VooVoo|work=europopmusic.eu|access-date=15 May 2011}}</ref>

==Band members==
* [[Wojciech Waglewski]] &ndash; guitar, main vocals, and lyrics. Founder and leader of the band; its name comes from his initials (the English pronunciation of "Voo Voo" sounds like Polish pronunciation of "W.W."). Father of Bartosz Waglewski and Piotr Waglewski, better known as [[Fisz]] and [[Emade]]. He also plays with the bands "Osjan" and "Gaia".
* Mateusz Pospieszalski &ndash; saxophone, flute, bass clarinet, keyboard instruments, accordion, vocals. He also played with the band "[[Maanam]]". Currently playing with the bands "Tie Break" and "GRAAL", also [[Stanisław Sojka]].
* Piotr "Stopa" Żyżelewicz (died 12 May 2011)<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.poland.pl/news/article,Piotr_Zyzelewicz_Has_Died,id,457548.htm|title=Piotr Zyzelewicz Has Died|date=13 May 2011|work=poland.pl|access-date=15 May 2011}}</ref> &ndash; drums. He cooperated with the bands [[Armia]] and [[Izrael (band)|Izrael]] and 2Tm2,3.
* Karim Martusewicz &ndash; double bass, bass guitar, has played with Voo Voo since February 1998.
* [[Mamadou Diouf (musician)|Mamadou Diouf]] from Senegal &ndash; vocals. He has been cooperating with Voo Voo since 1994.
