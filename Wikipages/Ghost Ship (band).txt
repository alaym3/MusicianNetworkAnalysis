'''Ghost Ship''' is a [[contemporary worship music]] band from [[Seattle]], [[Washington (state)|Washington]], United States. They are on the [[BEC Recordings]] label and Mars Hill Music. In 2013, the band released the critically acclaimed and commercially successful album entitled ''[[The Good King (album)|The Good King]]''.

==Background==
In 2009, Ghost Ship formed at Mars Hill Church's Downtown Seattle campus in [[Seattle]], [[Washington (state)|Washington]].  They were one of the worship bands at the church, alongside [[Ivan & Alyosha]], Elysium, and Thunderoso.

==Music==
In 2013, the band was signed to [[BEC Recordings]], when the partnership with Mars Hill Music took place.<ref name=bec>{{cite web|url=http://www.becrecordings.com/news/4430/Ghost_Ship_To_Release_Debut_Album_June_11/|title=Ghost Ship To Release Debut Album June 11|author=BEC Recordings|date=May 9, 2013|publisher=BEC Recordings|accessdate=August 30, 2013}}</ref>

===''A River with No End'' EP===
On November 15, 2011, the EP with Mars Hill Music was released, which was the first EP by the band, but it never charted.<ref name=EP>{{cite web|url=http://www.jesusfreakhideout.com/cdreviews/ARiverWithNoEnd.asp|title=Ghost Ship, "A River With No End EP"|date=November 15, 2011|publisher=Jesus Freak Hideout|accessdate=August 30, 2013}}</ref><ref name=EP2>{{cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/a-river-with-no-end-mw0002423244|title=''A River With No End'' – Ghost Ship|author=Allmusic|publisher=Rovi Corporation|accessdate=August 30, 2013}}</ref>

===''Costly''===
