'''Steve Dang''' (born May 29, 1994) better known by his stage name '''Yung Swiss''', is a Cameroonian-born South African hip hop recording artist, singer, songwriter and performer from [[Rosettenville| Rosettenville, Johannesburg]].<ref name="Channel 24">{{citation |url=http://www.channel24.co.za/The-Juice/News/yung-swiss-is-dropping-a-new-track-20161025 |title=Yung Swiss is dropping a new track|website=Channel 24|date= 25 October 2016|access-date=2 February 2017}}</ref>

== Career ==

In early 2015, Swiss released his first single with South African rap sensation Reason, titled 'I Love It'.
The single made its debut on South Africa's leading youth radio station, Yfm, while the music video for said single debut on the infamous MTV Base.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Yung Swiss named #1 on MTV Base latest Top 5 list|url=https://secureservercdn.net/198.12.145.239/d0e.a1c.myftpupload.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/Yung-Swiss-named-1-on-MTV-Base-latest-Top-5-list.jpg?time=1590434205|website=SA Hip Hop Mag|language=en-US|access-date=2020-05-26}}</ref>

Swiss later released his second single 'David Genaro', from his EP titled ''The Desiree Project'',<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://sahiphopmag.co.za/2016/06/new-release-young-swiss-david-genaro-remix-video-ft-reason-ginger-trill/|title=New Release: Young Swiss – David Genaro Remix Video [ft Reason, Ginger Trill]|date=20 June 2016|work=SA Hip Hop Mag|access-date=18 February 2018|language=en-US}}</ref> which was a tribute to actor [[Jamie Bartlett]] for his role as puppet master and mogul-villain "David Genaro", which he plays in the popular local drama series, ''[[Rhythm City (TV series)|Rhythm City]].''<ref>{{cite web|title=David Genaro – Single |url=https://itunes.apple.com/za/album/david-genaro-single/id1123205013|website=iTunes|publisher=Yung Swiss|access-date=8 October 2016}}</ref>

Yung Swiss's "[[Mayo (song)|Mayo]]" chorus which was a freestyle on [[DJ Speedsta]]'s single is his highest charting hit single as it was the most played record on South African radio stations for 12 weeks in a row, as well as being #1 on the African Chart on Beat 99 FM Nigeria.  Mayo also went Gold 10 Months after its official release.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.sabreakingnews.co.za/2017/06/06/dj-speedstas-mayo-goes-gold/|title=DJ Speedsta's Mayo goes gold! – SA Breaking News|date=6 June 2017|work=SA Breaking News|access-date=18 February 2018|language=en-US}}</ref>

In 2015, Yung Swiss released his first single with South African rapper Reason, titled ''I Love It'', which made its debut on the radio station [[YFM]]. It also aired on other radio stations such as [[5FM]]. The ''I love it'' music debuted on [[MTV BASE]] Africa.

Yung Swiss released the remix for his second single ''#DavidGenaroRemix'' on 8 April 2016 which was the most downloaded track of the week on Slikour OnLife.

Yung Swiss released a promotional single "Locked Up" for his debut project Bottom Baby featuring Cameroon's powerhouse [[Stanley Enow]] The track received instant positive reviews, as he had crossed International borders so early in his career.

On 4 November 2016, Yung Swiss released his third single titled "The High" which is currently climbing on charts across radio stations and has peaked at #1 on YFM's Hot99HipHop and #1 on 5FM's The Stir Up.

On 25 November 2016, Swiss released his debut project an [[Extended play|EP]] titled Bottom Baby based on his personal life and the struggles and challenges he encountered during his rise in the industry. Bottom Baby's singles include "The High", "Jungle" ft. K.O, "Where The Work At" ft. Nadia Nakai & "Locked Up" ft Stanley Enow.

Yung Swiss has also collaborated with fellow labelmates Ricky Tyler & Astryd Brown.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.zkhiphani.co.za/new-video-astryd-brown-four-ft-yung-swiss/|title=NEW VIDEO: Astryd Brown- FOUR Ft. Yung Swiss|date=22 November 2017|work=Online Youth Magazine {{!}} Zkhiphani.com|access-date=18 February 2018|language=en-US}}</ref>
