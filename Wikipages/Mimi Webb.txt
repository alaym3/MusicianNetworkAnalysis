'''Amelia Webb''' (born 23 July 2000)<ref name="OCC">{{Cite web|title=Who is Mimi Webb? The 'Good Without' singer set for chart domination|url=https://www.officialcharts.com/chart-news/who-is-mimi-webb-the-good-without-singer-set-for-chart-domination__32816/|access-date=2021-04-23|website=www.officialcharts.com|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Webb|first=Amelia|date=2021-07-23|title=MIMI WEBB on Instagram: ‚Äú21 ladies & gentsssüçæüçæ thank u so much for all the bday wishes my angels üíãüíù‚Äù|url=https://www.instagram.com/p/CRrcTdzs8hE/|url-status=live|access-date=2021-07-23|website=[[Instagram]]}}</ref> is a British singer-songwriter. She is known for her singles "Before I Go" and "Good Without", the latter of which peaked at number eight on the [[UK Singles Chart]]. In October 2021, she released her debut [[extended play|EP]] ''[[Seven Shades of Heartbreak]]''.

== Early life ==
Webb was born and raised in [[Canterbury]] with her parents and brother. She attended the independent school [[Kent College]] in Canterbury. She learned guitar and piano, and began to write her own songs at age 13.<ref name=BringinItBackwards>{{cite web |url=https://americansongwriter.com/bringin-it-backwards-interview-with-mimi-webb/ |title=Bringin' It Backwards: Interview with Mimi Webb |website=American Songwriter |date=19 March 2021 |access-date=17 June 2021}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=interview {{!}} mimi webb|url=https://schonmagazine.com/interview-mimi-webb/|access-date=2021-04-22|website=Sch√∂n! Magazine|language=en-GB}}</ref><ref name=":0" /> She credits her piano teacher with encouraging a musical career when she was 15.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|title=Get To Know: Mimi Webb {{!}} MTV UK|url=http://www.mtv.co.uk/mtv-push/news/get-to-know-mimi-webb|access-date=2021-04-23|website=mtv.co.uk|language=en}}</ref> She cites [[Adele]], [[Amy Winehouse]], [[Emeli Sand√©]], and [[Sam Smith]] among her influences,<ref name="OCC"/> along with jazz/pop singers [[Louis Armstrong]], [[Etta James]], [[Ella Fitzgerald]]<ref name=BringinItBackwards/> and [[Nat King Cole]].<ref name=SRS>{{cite web|url=https://www.stagerightsecrets.com/mimi-webb-interview/ |title=Exclusive Interview: Mimi Webb |date=17 September 2020 |author=Jacklyn |website=Stage Right Secrets |access-date=17 June 2021}}</ref>

At 16, she moved to [[Brighton]] to attend Brighton Music College (part of the [[British and Irish Modern Music Institute]]). She participated with fellow students in musical performances and competitions.<ref name=BringinItBackwards/><ref>{{Cite web|title=Mimi Webb {{!}} Inspo Behind 'Reasons'|url=https://flaunt.com/content/mimi-webb|access-date=2021-04-22|website=Flaunt Magazine|language=en-US}}</ref> She visited [[Los Angeles]] in December 2018 with her artist manager to meet with record labels. In February 2019 at the age of 18, she signed with [[Epic Records]].<ref name=SRS/>
