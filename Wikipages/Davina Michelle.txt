'''Michelle Davina Hoogendoorn''' (born 12 November 1995),<ref name="about NL">{{cite web|url=https://davinamichelle.com/nl/biografie/|title=Biografie|website=davinamichelle.com|accessdate=13 December 2018}}</ref> known by her stage name '''Davina Michelle''', is a Dutch singer and [[YouTuber]]. She lives in [[Capelle aan den IJssel]]. Her cover of the song "Duurt Te Lang" reached the number 1 spot in the [[Dutch Top 40]], the [[Mega Top 50]] and the Dutch [[Single Top 100]].

== Career ==

=== Idols ===
In 2016, Hoogendoorn participated in the [[Idols 5|fifth season]] of ''[[Idols (Dutch TV series)|Idols]]''. During her audition, she sang [[Jessie J]]'s song "[[Nobody's Perfect (Jessie J song)|Nobody's Perfect]]", after which she went on to the theater round and the duet round, where she was eliminated.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.rtlboulevard.nl/entertainment/artikel/596556/kandidaten-idols-vechten-zich-naar-liveshow-op-bali |title=Kandidaten Idols vechten zich naar liveshow op Bali |date=4 May 2016 |website=RTL Boulevard |access-date=12 December 2018 |language=nl}}</ref>

=== YouTube ===
In February 2017, Hoogendoorn started her own YouTube-channel under her pseudonym Davina Michelle, where she would upload her [[cover version]] of an English-language song every week. On 21 August 2017, she posted her cover of "[[What About Us (Pink song)|What About Us]]" by the American singer [[Pink (singer)|Pink]]. In October, ''[[Glamour (magazine)|Glamour]]'' posted a video review by Pink where she called the cover amazing and said "That's better than I will ever sound".<ref>{{Cite AV In December 2017, Hoogendoorn published the single ''Ga'' in cooperation with the [[Efteling]] and Sterre (played by Lilo van den Bosch). It served as the official soundtrack for the musical ''Sprookjessprokkelaar,'' created by the Efteling.

=== Beste Zangers (eng. Best Singers) and debut single ===
In 2018, Hoogendoorn participated in the eleventh season of the [[AVROTROS]]-show ''[[Beste Zangers]]''. In the fifth episode, she performed a cover of the song '"Duurt Te Lang" by rapper Glen Faria (also known as ''MC Fit'').<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.televizier.nl/amusement/dit-zijn-de-beste-zangers-van-2018|title=Dit zijn de Beste Zangers van 2018|access-date=2 October 2018|date=1 June 2018|publisher=Televizier.nl|language=nl}}</ref> Two days after broadcasting, this song reached the first place of most downloaded songs on [[iTunes]]. Later in 2018 it reached the number 1 spot in the [[Dutch Top 40]], the [[Mega Top 50]] and the Dutch [[Single Top 100]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.ad.nl/show/wonderkind-davina-michelle-scoort-hit-na-bejubeld-optreden-beste-zangers~aeef453a/|title='Wonderkind' Davina Michelle scoort hit na optreden Beste Zangers|date=30 September 2018|website=|publisher=Het Algemeen Dagblad|language=nl|url-status=live|access-date=2 October 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rtlboulevard.nl/boulevard/artikel/4435031/davina-michella-scoort-haar-eerste-nederlandse-hit-na-beste-zangers|title=Davina Michelle verrast iedereen bij Beste Zangers|access-date=2 October 2018|date=1 October 2018|publisher=RTLboulevard.nl|language=nl}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.dutchcowboys.nl/video/davina-michelle-gaat-viral-met-nummer-duurt-te-lang|title=Davina Michelle gaat viral met nummer 'Duurt te lang'|access-date=2 October 2018|date=1 October 2018|publisher=DutchCowboys.nl|language=nl}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rtlboulevard.nl/entertainment/music/artikel/4480181/davina-michelle-voert-singles-top-100-aan|title=Davina Michelle voert Top 100 aan|date=9 November 2018|website=RTL Boulevard|accessdate=13 December 2018}}</ref> In November 2018, the single was [[Gold album|certified gold]].

=== Eurovision Song Contest ===
On 18 May 2021 Hoogendoorn performed her new single "Sweet Water" at the first semi-final of the [[Eurovision Song Contest 2021]], held in her birth city Rotterdam, as part of an interval act titled "The Power of Water" alongside Dutch actress [[Thekla Reuten]].<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-05-18|title=Davina Michelle crushes viewers with intermission performance at Eurovision {{!}} Show|url=https://netherlandsnewslive.com/davina-michelle-crushes-viewers-with-intermission-performance-at-eurovision-show/156781/|access-date=2021-05-25|website=Netherlands News Live|language=en-US}}</ref>

=== 2021 Dutch Grand Prix ===
On 5 September 2021, Hoogendoorn performed the national anthem of the Netherlands ahead of the [[Formula 1]] Heineken [[Dutch Grand Prix]] at [[Circuit Zandvoort]], the first Formula 1 race to be held in the country since 1985. She repeated her performance for the podium ceremony after Dutch driver [[Max Verstappen]] won the event.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.formula1.com/en/latest/article.verstappen-sends-home-fans-wild-with-victory-over-hamilton-in-the-dutch.5x4cEJF9B4DaIz2H6dVQrd.html|title=2021 Dutch Grand Prix report and highlights: Verstappen sends home fans wild with victory over Hamilton at Zandvoort &#124; Formula 1®}}</ref>
