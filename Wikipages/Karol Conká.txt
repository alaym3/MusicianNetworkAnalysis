'''Karoline dos Santos Oliveira''' (born January 1, 1986), known professionally as '''Karol Conká''', is a Brazilian singer-songwriter.<ref>{{citation |url=http://www.smh.com.au/entertainment/music/brazilian-hiphop-artist-karol-conka-set-to-rock-the-domain-for-free-concert-20150109-12jwpi.html |title=Brazilian hip-hop artist Karol Conka set to rock the Domain for free concert |work=[[The Sydney Morning Herald]] |date=January 9, 2015 }}</ref><ref>{{citation |url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2014/jun/19/sao-paulo-every-day-monday-every-night-friday |title=São Paulo's music scene: 'Every day is Monday, every night is Friday' |work=[[The Guardian]] |date=June 19, 2014 }}</ref> Her music is a blend of modern hip-hop and rap with sounds from Brazilian pop and traditional music.<ref>{{citation |url=http://www1.folha.uol.com.br/ilustrada/2013/07/1319023-critica-com-batuk-freak-karol-conka-avanca-e-deixa-de-ser-aposta.shtml |title=Crítica: Com 'Batuk Freak', Karol Conka avança e deixa de ser aposta |date=July 31, 2013 |work=[[Folha de S.Paulo]] }}</ref><ref>{{citation |url=http://www.refinery29.com/2015/07/91465/karol-conka-brazilian-rapper |date=July 28, 2015 |work=[[Refinery29]] |title=Brazilian Artist Karol Conka Opens Up About Discrimination, Self-Esteem & Personal Style }}</ref> In 2013, she won [[Multishow]]'s Best New Artist award and released her debut album, ''Batuk Freak''.<ref>{{citation |url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/pictures/10-new-artists-you-need-to-know-april-2014-20140410/karol-conka-0274380 |title=10 New Artists You Need to Know: April 2014 |work=[[Rolling Stone]] |date=10 April 2014 }}</ref> Her song, "Boa Noite", is featured in the [[EA Sports]] video game, ''[[FIFA 14]]''. Her album ''Ambulante'' was ranked as the 34th best Brazilian album of 2018 by the Brazilian edition of ''[[Rolling Stone]]'' magazine<ref name="rsbmelhores18">{{cite magazine |last1=Antunes |first1=Pedro |title=Rolling Stone Brasil: os 50 melhores discos nacionais de 2018 |url=https://rollingstone.uol.com.br/noticia/rolling-stone-brasil-os-50-melhores-discos-nacionais-de-2018/ |magazine=[[Rolling Stone]] Brasil |access-date=28 December 2020 |language=pt |date=21 December 2018}}</ref> and among the 25 best Brazilian albums of the second half of 2018 by the [[Associação Paulista de Críticos de Arte|São Paulo Association of Art Critics]].<ref name="apcamelhores18">{{cite magazine |last1=Antunes |first1=Pedro |title=Baco Exu do Blues, Gilberto Gil, Duda Beat: os 25 melhores discos brasileiros do segundo semestre de 2018, segundo a APCA |url=https://rollingstone.uol.com.br/noticia/baco-exu-do-blues-gilberto-gil-carne-doce-duda-beat-apca-escolhe-os-25-melhores-discos-brasileiros-do-segundo-semestre-2018/ |magazine=[[Rolling Stone]] Brasil |access-date=28 December 2020 |language=pt |date=30 November 2018}}</ref>

Karol Conká publicly came out as bisexual. She is an advocate of [[feminism]].<ref>{{cite web|url = http://portalpopline.com.br/karol-conka-estampa-capa-de-revista-e-diz-sempre-soube-que-era-bi-desde-pequena/|date = December 26, 2016|accessdate = September 13, 2017|title = Karol Conka estampa capa de revista e diz sempre soube que era bi desde pequena|website = POPLine|language = pt}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url = https://oglobo.globo.com/ela/moda/karol-conka-fala-sobre-feminismo-racismo-preconceito-machuca-20042189|date = September 3, 2016|accessdate = September 13, 2017|website = O Globo|title = Karol Conka fala sobre feminismo racismo preconceito machuca|language = pt}}</ref>

In January 2021, she was announced as a contestant of the reality show ''[[Big Brother Brasil 21]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url = https://odia.ig.com.br/diversao/bbb/2021/01/6067994-carol-conka-esta-no-bbb-21.html|date = 19 January 2021|accessdate = 20 January 2021|website = [[O Dia]]|title = Karol Conká estava no 'BBB 21'|language=pt}}</ref> Her sarcastic and non-empathic behavior towards her housemates has garnered a very negative reception from the participants and viewers alike. On February 23, she was evicted from the reality show with a record-breaking 99.17% of the public vote.<ref>{{cite web|url = https://screenrant.com/big-brother-brazil-karol-conka-eviction-record-public-vote/|date = 24 February 2021|accessdate = 24 February 2021|website = [[Screen Rant]]|title = Big Brother Brazil: Karol Conka Evicted With Record-Breaking 99.17% Of Public Vote}}</ref>

Her life is depicted in the 2021 [[Globoplay]] documentary series ''A Vida Depois do Tombo''.<ref>{{cite web|url = https://g1.globo.com/fantastico/noticia/2021/04/25/a-vida-depois-do-tombo-mostra-como-karol-conka-enfrentou-o-mundo-depois-da-rejeicao-recorde-no-bbb.ghtml|date = 25 April 2021|accessdate = 26 April 2021|website = [[G1 (website)|G1]]|title = 'A vida depois do tombo' mostra como Karol Conká enfrentou o mundo depois da rejeição recorde no BBB|language=pt}}</ref>
