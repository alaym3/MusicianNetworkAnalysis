'''Bizarre''' are a rock band from [[Blanes]], [[Catalonia]], [[Spain]]. They are best known in their country by their 2006 hit single "Sé", which is widely played not only in Catalonia and Spain but also in some Latin American countries.

Although their songs are mostly sung in [[Spanish language|Spanish]], several of them have been also recorded in [[Catalan language|Catalan]] ("Sé", "Difícil d'oblidar", "No és massa tard", "T'amagues"...), [[English language|English]] ("Alone in NY" and some unreleased demos) and [[Italian language|Italian]] ("So").

Bizarre have released up to date two albums: their debut ''Bizarre''<ref>{{cite web|title=Bizarre (2006)|url=http://www.bizarresound.com/index.php?language=en&menu=bizarre2006}}</ref> (Mass Records, 2006) and ''Zero''<ref>{{cite web|title=Zero (2010)|url=http://www.bizarresound.com/index.php?language=en&menu=zero}}</ref> (Mass Records, 2010).
