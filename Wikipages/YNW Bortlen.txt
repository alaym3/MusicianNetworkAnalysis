'''Cortlen Malik Henry''' (born January 24, 1999),<ref name=bail /> known professionally as '''YNW Bortlen''' (backronym for '''Young Nigga World Bortlen'''), is an American rapper most famous for being a member of the collective "YNW".

In February 2019, he was arrested and charged with two counts of [[first-degree murder]] and two counts of [[Accessory (legal term)#United States|accessory]] to a crime and faces life imprisonment if convicted. In March 2019, Henry pleaded "not guilty" to the charges and is currently awaiting trial.

== Early life and education ==
Cortlen Malik Henry was born on January 24, 1999, in [[Gifford, Florida]]. He was raised there and attended [[Vero Beach High School]].

== Legal issues ==
In February 2019, Henry was arrested in Texas, extradited to Florida, and charged with two counts of [[First-degree murder (United States law)|first-degree murder]] and two counts of being an [[Accessory (legal term)#United States|accessory after the fact]] in the shooting deaths of his childhood friends YNW SakChaser and [[YNW Juvy]] on October 26, 2018; [[YNW Melly]], another childhood friend, is accused of both murders as-well.<ref name=TCP>{{cite news |author=Corey Arwood |url=https://www.tcpalm.com/story/news/crime/indian-river-county/2020/02/28/bail-hearing-set-ynw-melly-associate-cortlen-henry/4890202002/ |title=Ruling expected next week in bail hearing for YNW Melly associate Cortlen Henry from Gifford |website=TCPalm |publisher=Treasure Coast Newspapers |date=March 2, 2020 |orig-year=February 28, 2020 |access-date=August 13, 2021 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |author1=Dacia Johnson |author2=Mary Helen Moore |url=https://www.tcpalm.com/story/news/crime/2019/03/05/ynw-melly-murder-charges-full-story/3056170002/ |title=Florida rapper YNW Melly murder charges: The full story |website=TCPalm |publisher=Treasure Coast Newspapers |date=February 12, 2020 |orig-year=March 5, 2019 |access-date=August 13, 2021 }}</ref> Both pleaded not guilty; after a series of hearings, Henry was granted bond in May 2020<ref name=bail>{{cite news |author=Corey Arwood |url=https://www.tcpalm.com/story/news/crime/indian-river-county/2020/05/27/cortlen-henry-bail-set-180-000-ynw-melly-double-murder-case-remains-jailed/5266901002/ |title=Cortlen Henry's bail set at $180,000 in YNW Melly double murder case; remains jailed |website=TCPalm |publisher=Treasure Coast Newspapers |date=May 27, 2020 |access-date=August 13, 2021 }}</ref> and was released while awaiting trial.<ref>{{cite news |author=Jose Martinez |url=https://www.complex.com/music/2020/05/ynw-bortlen-75000-bond |title=YNW Melly's Alleged Accomplice YNW Bortlen Released on $75,000 Bond |website=Complex |date=June 29, 2020 |orig-year=May 24, 2020 |access-date=August 13, 2021 }}</ref> He was re-arrested on April 8, 2021, after his [[ankle monitor]] revealed that he violated his pre-trial release agreement by going not only to work but also to a residence and a [[strip club]].<ref>{{cite news |author=Aleia Woods |url=https://www.xxlmag.com/ynw-melly-ynw-bortlen-arrested-violate-bond-strip-clubs/ |title=YNW Melly Co-Defendant YNW Bortlen Arrested for Violating Bond Release After Visiting Strip Clubs in Miami |website=XXL |date=April 12, 2021 |access-date=August 13, 2021 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |author=Kyle Eustice |url=https://hiphopdx.com/news/id.61561/title.ynw-bortlen-arrested-for-violating-bond-following-strip-club-spree# |title=YNW Bortlen Arrested For Violating Bond During Strip Club Spree |website=Hiphop DX |date=April 13, 2021 |access-date=August 13, 2021 }}</ref> On August 19, 2021, Henry was granted bond and was set for [[Electronic tagging|elmo release]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.complex.com/music/ynw-bortlen-house-arrest-victims-family-responds|title=YNW Bortlen Released on House Arrest Following Bond Hearing, Victim's Family Responds}}</ref>
