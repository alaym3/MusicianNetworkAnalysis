'''Missing Filemon''' is a Filipino rock band from [[Cebu]]. The band was founded in 2002.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://cebudailynews.inquirer.net/247334/three-cebuano-bands-to-perform-in-the-roadshow|title=Three Cebuano bands to perform in “The Roadshow”|website=cebudailynews.inquirer.net|language=en|access-date=2019-12-02}}</ref> They won a [[FAMAS Award]] for Best Theme Song for the 2007 film ''[[Confessional (film)|Confessional]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.pep.ph/guide/movies/3016/famas-2008-winners-bared|title=FAMAS 2008 winners bared|website=PEP.ph|language=en|access-date=2019-12-02}}</ref>
