'''Annisokay''' (stylized as '''annisokay''', pronounced ''Ann is okay'') is a German [[post-hardcore]] band from [[Halle (Saale)|Halle]], [[Saxony-Anhalt]]. Currently consists of scream vocalist Rudi Schwarzer, guitarist/clean vocalist Christoph Wieczorek, bassist Norbert Rose and drummer Nico Vaeen.

Up to date Annisokay released two demo-CDs, two EP and five studio albums: ''The Lucid Dream[er]'' was originally self-released in 2012 (2013 via [[Radtone Records]] in [[Japan]]) and was re-released in 2014 when the band signed a label deal with [[SPV GmbH]]. The follow-up record ''Enigmatic Smile'' was released in March 2015 and peaked at no. 68 in the [[Media Control Charts|German albums charts]].

Annisokay toured [[Germany]] several times and played alongside acts like [[Silverstein (band)|Silverstein]], [[Callejon (band)|Callejon]] and [[Eskimo Callboy]]. They played at 2014s edition of [[Summer Breeze Open Air]] and at [[Mair1 Festival]] in [[Montabaur]].

== Band history ==
=== 2007–2013: Beginnings and ''The Lucid Dream[er]'' ===
The band was founded in 2007 in [[Halle (Saale)|Halle]], [[Saxony-Anhalt]], [[Germany]]. In 2008 and 2009 the band released two demo CDs with ''My Ticket to Reno'' and ''The Point You Will Still Miss''. In 2010 the band produced and released their first official EP called ''You, Always'' which was available as CD and Download. The band covered the song "[[Telephone (Lady Gaga song)|Telephone]]" originally performed by [[Lady Gaga]] which was never released officially.

On September 2, 2012 the band released a music video for a song called "Sky" which was a single for the debut record which was released on October 1, 2012. This album is called ''The Lucid Dream[er]'' and was mastered by [[Joey Sturgis]] who had worked with acts like [[We Came as Romans]], [[Emmure]] and [[Asking Alexandria]] in the past.<ref name="Allschools">''Allschools Network'': [http://www.allschools.de/record/1309618 annisokay – ''The Lucid Dreamer Review''] (German)</ref> The album was produced by Christoph Wieczorek in his own recording studio Sawdust Recordings and by Aljosha Sieg at Pitchback Studios.<ref>''shop2.genesis-ec.com'': [https://archive.is/20130411002236/http://shop2.genesis-ec.com/search/item.asp?shopcd=17263&item=307697 Annisokay – The Lucid Dreamer]</ref> Since the band's inception the band toured together with acts like [[Upon This Dawning]], [[Intohimo]], [[Broadway (band)|Broadway]] and [[His Statue Falls]].<ref>''partyausfall.de'': [http://www.partyausfall.de/konzert-upon-this-dawning-annisokay-th-3814.html Konzert: UPON THIS DAWNING, ANNISOKAY, THROUGH DEVASTATION, WELCOME KAREN] (German)</ref><ref>''partyausfall.de'': [http://www.partyausfall.de/konzert-intohimo-annisokay-skywalker-4869.html Konzert: INTOHIMO, ANNISOKAY, SKYWALKER] (German)</ref>

In 2013, Annisokay got signed to [[Radtone Music]] to release the album in [[Japan]].<ref>''gekirock.com'': [http://gekirock.com/news/2013/03/annisokay.php ドイツ産ポスト・ハードコア/メタルコア、ANNISOKAYのインタビュー公開！日本デビュー作を3/6にリリースし日本上陸!] (Japanese)</ref> On June 28, 2013, the band played [[Mair1 Festival]] in [[Montabaur]] and played together with acts like [[Boysetsfire]], [[Deez Nuts (band)|Deez Nuts]], [[Silent Screams]] and [[Bury Your Dead]].<ref>mair1-festival.de: [http://mair1-festival.de/home/show/84 Here we go: Heaven Shall Burn, ... – Next 15 Bands] (German)</ref><ref>mai1-festival.de: [http://mair1-festival.de/lineup/days/ Tagesplan] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150410101313/http://mair1-festival.de/lineup/days/ |date=2015-04-10 }} (German)</ref> In the end of July the band toured Germany for the first time as headline act. The tour started in [[Venray]], [[Netherlands]] which was their first international gig.<ref>SOSGermany: ''Outspoken.de'': [http://outspoken.de/page.php?id=3749 ANNISOKAY: Headliner-Tour angekündigt!] (German)</ref>

=== 2014–2015: ''Enigmatic Smile'' ===
In January 2014, the band was part of the ''We Are the Mess Tour'' headlined by [[Eskimo Callboy]]. The tour went through cities such as [[Cologne]], [[Berlin]], [[Munich]] and [[Hamburg]].<ref>''Monster Artists'': [http://monster-artists.com/blog/annisokay-gehen-auf-tour/ Annisokay gehen auf Tour] (German)</ref> Just few days before tour started the band announced flying to the USA sometime in 2014 to start working with [[Joey Sturgis]] for their second record.

On April 20, 2014, the band got signed to [[SPV GmbH]] which re-released the debut record ''The Lucid Dream[er]'' in [[Central Europe]] and [[North America]] with bonus material.<ref name="SPV">''[[SPV (Unternehmen)|SPV]]'': [https://archive.is/20140423070455/http://www.spv.de/News/newsdetail.php?newsID=668 Annisokay unterschreiben bei Steamhamme/SPV] (German)</ref> In June 2014 the band toured as support act for [[Silverstein (band)|Silverstein]] in some cities in Germany.<ref name="SPV" /> In Summer the band played [[Summer Breeze Open Air]] and [[Vainstream Rockfest]] for the first time.<ref>Christina Wenig: ''[[Metal Hammer]]'': [http://www.metal-hammer.de/news/meldungen/article602931/so-war-das-vainstream-rockfest-2014-in-muenster.html So war das Vainstream Rockfest 2014 in Münster (Festival Review)] (German)</ref><ref>Christian Zanders: ''Metal Hammer'': [http://www.metal-hammer.de/news/meldungen/article567499/oster-sepcial-neue-bands-fuer-summer-breeze-open-air-bestaetigt.html Oster-Sepcial: Neue Bands für Summer Breeze Open Air bestätigt] (German)</ref> In February and March 2015, Annisokay and [[Vitja (band)|Vitja]] played in support for [[Callejon (band)|Callejon]] on their ''Wir sind Angst'' tour.<ref>''Rockszene.de'': [http://www.rockszene.de/news/1325-neues-album-video-und-tour-callejon-zusammen-mit-vitja-und-annisokay-in-hannover.html Neues Album, Video und Tour] (German)</ref><ref>''Allschools Network'': [http://www.allschools.de/news/entry/1327629 CALLEJON – geben Toursupports bekannt]</ref> On March 20, 2015 the band officially released their second studio album called ''Enigmatic Smile'' which ranked at no. 68 in the [[Media Control Charts|German albums charts]].<ref name="Charts">Chart review: [http://www.musicline.de/de/chartverfolgung_summary/artist/Annisokay/75295/longplay DE]</ref>

Between April 10, 2015 and April 25, 2015 the band will tour as support act for [[Emil Bulls]] in [[Germany]].<ref>Mitch: ''Allschools Network'': [http://www.allschools.de/news/entry/1329771 ANNISOKAY – als Support mit EMIL BULLS auf Tour] (German)</ref> In October the band is set to tour a headline tour in Germany, [[Luxembourg]], [[Austria]] and [[Switzerland]] with support from [[Fearless Vampire Killers (band)|Fearless Vampire Killers]] from the [[United Kingdom]] and Novelists from France.<ref>''Monster Artists Management'': [http://monster-artists.com/annisokay-auf-grosse-headliner-tour/ Annisokay auf große Headliner Tour!] (German)</ref> Fearless Vampire Killers announced Annisokay as their support for their UK tour in May 2015 before.<ref>''Alt Mu Magazine'': [http://altmu.co.uk/melodic-metalcore-band-annisokay-join-fearless-vampire-killers-on-may-tour/ Melodic Metalcore Band, Annisokay Join Fearless Vampire Killers on May Tour]</ref>

After an own album tour and several festivals the band announced in late 2015 that they played 72 shows in nine different countries making the year 2015 to their biggest year to date. 
On 2 December 2015, they announced via [[Facebook]] to take a break from live shows and touring to hit the studio for a third album and so-called “secret things”. One month later Annisokay made public, that they had break-up with their drummer Daniel Herrmann before starting the work for their upcoming album.

=== 2016–2017: ''Annie Are You Okay?'' and ''Devil May Care'' ===
Despite their message to not play live shows for a while Annisokay returned in February to support [[Parkway Drive]] for two shows in [[Russia]]. Also February 2016 it was announced that Annisokay are set to support [[The World Alive]] on their “The Dark Matter EU/UK Tour” in May 2016 including shows in [[United Kingdom]], [[France]], [[Italy]], [[Belgium]] and more.

On 28 April, annisokay released the music video of the song “What's Wrong” and announced the new album to be released on 11 November 2016. Six month after parting ways with their drummer the band introduced Nico Vaeen as new member in June 2016. A few weeks later they also revealed the “secret thing” – hinted in December 2015 – to be an EP called “Annie Are You Okay?” as tribute to their bandname giver [[Michael Jackson]].

The EP was released only digital at 19 June 2016 and includes hardcore covers of the songs "[[Beat It]]", "[[Thriller (song)|Thriller]]", "[[Scream (Michael Jackson and Janet Jackson song)|Scream]]" and "[[They Don't Care About Us]]".

On 7 September, they officially revealed cover and name of the new album, ''Devil May Care'', and released the music videos “Loud” (October 2016) and “Blind Lane” (November 2016) before the album was hitting the market. At the end of 2016 annisokay were special guest at [[Eskimo Callboy]] “Fandigo Tour” and announced their own headliner “Devil May Care Tour” for 2017. This tour contained shows in United Kingdom, [[Czech Republic]], Russia, [[Belarus]], [[Ukraine]] as well as 14 shows in Germany. Notwithstanding the above, they played at national and international festivals for example Woodstock ([[Poland]]), Rhein Rock Open, Faine Misto Festival (Ukraine).

On 16 November 2017, the band announced they signed with the German record label [[Arising Empire]]. Later in 2017, they expand their Devil May Care Tour for three shows and confirmed different festival gigs 2018 including Reload Festival, Rhein Riot and Summer Breeze.

=== 2018–2019: ''Arms'' ===
On 18 January 2018, Annisokay hinted via social media they have started work on their fourth studio album.  Two month later they announced to support [[I Set My Friends On Fire]] on their “10 Years Of Slaughter Tour” which contains 34 shows from 13 July till 19 August. With this Annisokay set the record for the longest [[United States]] tour ever by a German band. The band documented their days in the USA with a ten episodes long band diary released via [[YouTube]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLv-tWsWq6idfn_X4UIoRKHuNmkn8T6igt|title=annisokay USA Tour Documentary|last=|first=|date=|publisher=[[YouTube]]|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=}}</ref> and also documented their later tours through Japan, Russia and festivals.

In return of the USA tour it was revealed that I Set My Friends On Fire will support Annisokay on their headliner “Fully Automatic Tour” for two shows in UK and 16 shows in Germany till November 2018.

On 12 June, Annisokay made public that the album will be released on 17 August 2018 and is named ''Arms''. A few weeks later they released the songs “Unaware” plus “Coma Blue” and also announced they will play four shows in [[Japan]] for the first time while being supported by German band [[Walking Dead on Broadway]].

After the release of ''Arms'' the album reached #26 of the official German album charts which made it the most successful Annisokay album in their band history. Till then the band played a Russia tour in March 2019 and appeared on some German festivals including [[With Full Force]] and [[Deichbrand]]. Later in the year they announced to support [[Emil Bulls]] on their “X-Mas Bash Tour" in November/December 2019.

=== 2019–present: Departure of Dave Grunewald and ''Aurora'' ===
On 21 October 2019, the band surprisingly announced via Facebook that they have immediately parted ways with unclean vocalist Dave Grunewald. Even though Grunewald was not an active band member any more, Annisokay promised to play every announced show as planned. At the same time the band revealed a fifth studio album to be released in 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/102788356423156/posts/2486455234723111/|title=Statement of annisokay about parting ways with Dave Grunewald|last=|first=|date=21 October 2019|website=Facebook|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=23 October 2019}}</ref> Only moments after the band's message Dave Grunewald confirmed the break-up and assured that annisokay and himself have been parting ways on amicable terms, without disputes and no one made this decision reckless.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://m.facebook.com/story.php?story_fbid=2729320103786473&id=100001255304393|title=Statement of Dave Grunewald about break-up with annisokay|last=|first=|date=2019-10-21|website=Facebook|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2019-10-23}}</ref> On demand of morecore.de, a german online music magazine, band leader and clean vocalist Christoph Wieczorek said that shouts and screams will still be a part of the band's music in future.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.morecore.de/news/annisokay-und-frontmann-dave-grunewald-gehen-getrennte-wege/|title=Annisokay und Frontmann Dave Grunewald gehen getrennte Wege|date=2019-10-21|website=MoreCore.de|language=de-DE|access-date=2019-10-23}}</ref> On 26 October, annisokay played their first live show without Grunewald in [[Schüttorf|Schüttorf, Germany]] and presented Rudi Schwarzer – a member of German hardcore band Wither as well as former member of LEAVE. and Arctic Island – as their new Shouter.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.morecore.de/news/annisokay-spielen-das-erste-konzert-mit-ihrem-neuen-shouter/|title=Annisokay spielen das erste Konzert mit ihrem neuen Shouter|last=Lotz|first=Julia|date=2019-10-27|website=Morecore.de|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=}}</ref>

While supporting Emil Bulls on their yearly X-mas Bash tour Annisokay presented the first brand new song with their new shouter Rudi. However they did not reveal the title so it is not known what the name is, or if the song is part of the upcoming album. On April 17, 2020, the band released a new song called "STFU"<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZlZ_f1KIGGE|title=ANNISOKAY – STFU (OFFICIAL VIDEO)|publisher=[[YouTube]]|accessdate=2020-10-02}}</ref> which is the first song to feature new unclean vocalist Rudi Schwarzer. On September 28, 2020, the band released another song called "Bonfire of the Millennials" which will also appear on their new album "Aurora", which will be released in January 29, 2021.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QIpelqCXVLg|title=ANNISOKAY – Bonfire Of The Millennials (OFFICIAL VIDEO)|publisher=[[YouTube]]|accessdate=2021-01-10}}</ref><ref name="Aurora">{{cite news |last1=Slessor, Dan |title=Album review: Annisokay – Aurora |url=https://www.kerrang.com/reviews/album-review-annisokay-aurora/ |access-date=25 January 2021 |work=[[Kerrang!]] |date=January 25, 2021}}</ref>

On 1 June 2021, Dutch symphonic metal band [[Within Temptation]] announced that they have collaborated with Annisokay and recorded the single "[[Shed My Skin]]", which was released on 25 June 2021. The music video itself premiered on 8 and 9 July 2021 during Within Temptation's virtual performances.<ref>{{cite web |title=WITHIN TEMPTATION Announce The Aftermath - A Show In Virtual Reality; New Single "Shed My Skin" Due This Month |url=https://bravewords.com/news/within-temptation-announce-the-aftermath-a-show-in-virtual-reality-new-single-shed-my-skin-due-this-month |website=[[Brave Words & Bloody Knuckles]] |access-date=1 June 2021 |language=en}}</ref>
