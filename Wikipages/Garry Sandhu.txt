'''Gurmukh Singh "Garry" Sandhu''',<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/pages/category/Artist/OfficialGarrySandhuFanpage/about/|title=Garry Sandhu|website=facebook.com|access-date=21 June 2019}}{{Primary source inline|date=October 2019}}</ref> is an Indian singer, songwriter and actor known for his work in [[Music of Punjab|Punjabi music]].{{cn|date=September 2021}} He made his singing debut in 2010 with song "Main Ni Peenda" and acting debut with the film ''[[Romeo Ranjha]]'' (2014).<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.garrysandhumusic.com/about/|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150810223252/http://www.garrysandhumusic.com/about/|title=Garry Sandhu – Singer, actor, Lyricst|archive-date=10 August 2015|website=garrysandhumusic.com|access-date=21 June 2019}}</ref> He has released songs including Illegal Weapon, Yeah Baby, Banda Ban Ja, Excuses etc. He also owns his record label ''Fresh Media Records''. In 2019, his song "Yeah Baby" was released in a bollywood film [[De De Pyaar De]] as "[[Hauli Hauli]]".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/music/news/de-de-pyaar-de-new-song-hauli-hauli-gets-ajay-devgn-tabu-and-rakul-preet-grooving-on-the-dance-floor/articleshow/69052989.cms|title='De De Pyaar De' new song: 'Hauli Hauli' gets Ajay Devgn, Tabu and Rakul Preet grooving on the dance floor – Times of India ►|website=The Times of India|access-date=21 June 2019}}</ref> He has been nominated for [[#Awards and nominations|many awards]] at PTC Punjabi Music Awards.{{cn|date=September 2021}} On December 14, [[Business Today (India)|Business Today]] reported that Sandhu's song ''Illegal Weapon 2.0'' was one among top YouTube music videos in India.<ref>https://www.businesstoday.in/latest/trends/badshah-carry-minati-bb-ki-vines-youtube-reveals-top-10-creators-videos-of-2020/story/424866.html</ref>
