''' Gemini Syndrome '''is an American [[alternative metal]] band formed in 2010<ref name="allmusic2">{{cite web|author=Artist Biography by James Christopher Monger |url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/gemini-syndrome-mn0003064372/biography |title=Gemini Syndrome &#124; Biography |publisher=[[AllMusic]] |access-date=2014-07-31}}</ref> in [[Los Angeles]], [[California]].  The band currently consists of [[vocalist]] Aaron Nordstrom,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://noisecreep.com/gemini-syndrome-lux-exclusive-album-stream-premiere/ |title=Gemini Syndrome, 'Lux' - Exclusive Album Stream Premiere |website=Noisecreep.com |date=2013-09-03 |access-date=2014-07-19}}</ref> drummer Brian Steele Medina, bassist Alessandro "(AP)" Paveri and guitarists Miguel "Meegs" Rascon and Nicholas Paul Arnold.<ref name=audio360>{{cite web |last1=Borges |first1=Arly |last2=Venable |first2=Kevin |title=LUX, THE DEBUT LP FROM GEMINI SYNDROME |url=http://www.audio360.org/music_a0003_review_gemini_symdrome_lux.php |website=Audio360.org |access-date=June 11, 2015 |date=October 18, 2013 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150609042134/http://www.audio360.org/music_a0003_review_gemini_symdrome_lux.php |archive-date=June 9, 2015 }}</ref>  Their debut album, titled ''Lux'', was released in September 2013. Reviewers drew comparisons to bands like [[Mudvayne]], [[Five Finger Death Punch]], and [[Tool (band)|Tool]].<ref name="allmusic2"/> Four singles spawned from ''Lux:'' "Pleasure and Pain," "Left of Me," "Basement," and "Stardust," which peaked at no. 19 on the Mainstream Rock Tracks chart.<ref>{{cite web|title=Gemini Syndrome - complete achievements (Albums)|url=http://www.spirit-of-metal.com/discographie-groupe-Gemini_Syndrome-type-Albums-l-en.html|website=Spirit of Metal Webzine|access-date=February 20, 2015}}</ref>

Gemini Syndrome released their sophomore album, ''Memento Mori'', on August 16, 2016, which hit #1 on Billboard Heatseakers Charts <ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/gemini-syndrome/chart-history/heatseekers-albums/song/990601|title=Gemini Syndrome Memento Mori Chart History|website=Billboard.com}}</ref> Five singles spawned from ''Memento Mori:'' "Eternity,<ref name="loudwire.com">{{cite web|url=http://loudwire.com/gemini-syndrome-eternity/|title=Gemini Syndrome Unleash Crushing New Song 'Eternity'|website=Loudwire}}</ref>""Anonymous,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://loudwire.com/gemini-syndrome-memento-mori-album-details-anonymous-video/|title=Gemini Syndrome Reveal 'Memento Mori' Details, Issue Video|website=Loudwire}}</ref>" "Alive Inside,"<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/rock/7461690/gemini-syndrome-alive-inside-lyric-video-premiere|title=Gemini Syndrome's 'Alive Inside' Lyric Video: Exclusive Premiere|website=Billboard.com}}</ref>" "Sorry Not Sorry,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://loudwire.com/gemini-syndrome-sorry-not-sorry-exclusive-video-premiere |title=Gemini Syndrome, 'Sorry Not Sorry' – Exclusive Video Premiere |website=Loudwire.com}}</ref>" and "Remember We Die," which reached no. 24, and spent 20 weeks on the Mainstream Rock Tracks chart.<ref name="auto">{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/gemini-syndrome/chart-history/hot-mainstream-rock-tracks/song/987204|title=Gemini Syndrome Remember We Die Chart History|website=Billboard.com}}</ref>

In a press release on September 13, 2017, the band announced that Miguel "Meegs" Rascón (formerly of [[Coal Chamber]]) joined the band,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://loudwire.com/gemini-syndrome-fall-2017-tour-plan-new-album-welcome-guitarist-meegs-rascon/ |title=Gemini Syndrome Book Fall 2017 Tour, Plan New Album + Welcome Guitarist Meegs Rascon |website=Loudwire.com}}</ref> replacing both Daniel Sahagún and Charles Lee Salvaggio.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/GeminiSyndrome/photos/a.10150215778768525.316358.328663243524/10155232832298525/?type=3&theater|title=Gemini Syndrome|website=Facebook.com}}</ref>

In August 2018, the group performed throughout the United States with American rock band [[Messer (band)|Messer]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/messerband/photos/pb.227974646712.-2207520000.1532710777./10155882289586713/?type=3&theater|title=MESSER|website=Facebook.com|access-date=27 July 2018}}</ref>

On December 17, 2019, the band released a video announcing a new album and that the first single is expected to be released early in 2020.<ref>{{cite web |title=Gemini Syndrome [LET THERE BE LIGHT] |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I2InmPasEiA |website=www.youtube.com |publisher=YouTube |access-date=17 December 2019}}</ref>

On July 23, 2021, the band announced their third album ''[[3rd Degree - The Raising]]'' to be released on October 15, 2021. The album will feature the three previously released singles, "Die With Me," "IDK," and "Reintegration," along with a newly released single "Abandoned."<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://centurymedia.store/product/Y4CE435COMBO/gemini-syndrome-3rd-degree-the-raising-spring-cd-digipack-digital-download?cp=102832_103046_103773|title = Gemini Syndrome - 3rd Degree - the Raising CD Digipack + Digital Download &#124; Shop the Century Media Official Store}}</ref>
