'''Ryan James Hurd''' (born November 2, 1986) is an American [[country music]] singer and songwriter. In addition to writing No.&nbsp;1 singles for [[Blake Shelton]], [[Lady A]], and [[Luke Bryan]], Hurd has recorded for [[RCA Records Nashville]].

== Career ==
Hurd was born in [[Chicago, Illinois]] and raised in [[Kalamazoo, Michigan]] before moving to [[Nashville, Tennessee]].<ref>[http://www.us99.radio.com/blogs/erin-creedon/ryan-hurd-and-maren-morris-received-gift-cubs-team/ “Ryan Hurd and Maren Morris Received the Cutest Gift from the Cubs!”], WUSN, Chicago, October 30, 2019.</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.herald-dispatch.com/features_entertainment/ryan-hurd-going-from-songwriter-to-singer/article_d0ab7a88-ed39-5c37-b5c6-5d633a0851e0.html|title=Ryan Hurd going from songwriter to singer|author=Derek Halsey|website=Herald-dispatch.com}}</ref> Hurd met his future wife [[Maren Morris]] while writing the ballad “Last Turn Home” for [[Tim McGraw]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://people.com/country/maren-morris-met-boyfriend-ryan-hurd-writing-tim-mcgraw-song/|title=Maren Morris and Boyfriend Ryan Hurd Met While Penning a Song for Tim McGraw: 'There Was Always This Writing Chemistry'|website=People.com}}</ref>

In 2015, Hurd wrote "[[Lonely Tonight]]", a No&nbsp;1 [[Country Airplay]] single for [[Blake Shelton]] and [[Ashley Monroe]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cmt.com/news/1751577/blake-shelton-helps-ashley-monroe-score-her-first-no-1/|title=Blake Shelton Helps Ashley Monroe Score Her First No. 1|website=Cmt.com}}</ref>

Hurd signed with Sony Music Nashville in 2017 to release a forthcoming debut album.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://countrymusicrocks.net/2017/01/ryan-hurd-signs-with-sony-music-nashville-on-the-rca-nashville-roster.html/|title=Ryan Hurd Signs With Sony Music Nashville on the RCA Nashville Roster|date=5 January 2017|website=Countrymusicrocks.net}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/country/7858047/maren-morris-ryan-hurd-engaged|title=Maren Morris & Ryan Hurd Are Engaged|website=Billboard.com}}</ref>
His first single from his self-titled EP, “We Do Us” has received over 2 million streams on Spotify.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tasteofcountry.com/ryan-hurd-we-do-us-artist-spotify/|title=Ryan Hurd's 'We Do Us' Is Helping Him Do 'Him' as an Artist|website=Taste of Country}}</ref>

Hurd was featured on Rolling Stone Country's "Artists You Need to Know" list.<ref>{{cite magazine|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/lists/10-new-country-artists-you-need-to-know-february-2016-20160201/ryan-hurd-20160131|title=10 New Country Artists You Need to Know: February 2016|magazine=[[Rolling Stone]]}}</ref> and CMT named him one of their "Listen Up" Artists of 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cmt.com/news/1761844/cmt-reveals-16-artists-for-listen-up-in-2016/|title=CMT Reveals 16 Artists for Listen Up in 2016|website=Cmt.com}}</ref> Hurd has also toured with [[Chase Rice]], [[Thomas Rhett]], [[Florida Georgia Line]], and Morris.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://musicrow.com/2017/01/ryan-hurd-joins-rca-nashville-roster/|title=Ryan Hurd Joins RCA Nashville Roster|date=3 January 2017|website=Musicrow.com}}</ref> Hurd's debut single was "Love in a Bar".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://people.com/country/ryan-hurd-love-in-a-bar-maren-morris-first-single/|title=Ryan Hurd on Finding 'Love In a Bar' with Maren Morris and the Inspiration for His First Single|website=People.com}}</ref> In January 2020, he embarked on a tour in support of his ''Platonic'' EP with [[Adam Doleac]] and [[Niko Moon]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://musicrow.com/2019/11/ryan-hurd-preps-platonic-tour-with-adam-doleac-niko-moon/|title=Ryan Hurd Preps Platonic Tour With Adam Doleac, Niko Moon|website=Musicrow.com|date=November 19, 2019}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Hurd and [[Maren Morris]] were married on March 24, 2018, in [[Nashville, Tennessee]].<ref>{{cite web|last1=Pasquini|first1=Maria|title=They're Married! Maren Morris Weds Ryan Hurd in Nashville |url=http://people.com/music/maren-morris-marries-ryan-hurd/|website=People.com|accessdate=March 23, 2020|language=en}}</ref> In March 2020 they had a son.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.eonline.com/news/1126338/maren-morris-gives-birth-to-her-first-child|title=Maren Morris Gives Birth to Her First Child|last=Aiello|first=McKenna|date=March 23, 2020|website=Eonline.com|accessdate=March 23, 2020}}</ref>
