'''Jason Andrè Juami''' (born 27 January 1997), better known by his stage name '''Jay1''' (stylised as '''JAY1'''), is a British rapper and songwriter who is from [[Coventry]]. He is of [[Kongo people|Congolese]] ancestry.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.coventrytelegraph.net/news/coventry-news/jay1-your-mrs-charts-rap-16101283|title=London rapper is taking the charts by storm|first=Katy|last=Hallam|date=April 9, 2019|website=Coventry Live}}</ref> He has had <!--{{ }}-->two top 20 singles in the UK.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.officialcharts.com/artist/55341/jay1/|title=JAY1 &#124; full Official Chart History &#124; Official Charts Company|website=www.officialcharts.com}}</ref>

Jay1 is best known for his single "Your Mrs", which was released in March 2019. He released his debut [[Extended play|EP]] titled ''One Wave'' on 4 July 2019.

==Early life==
Before his keen interest in music, Jay1 undertook a PESS apprenticeship with local Coventry apprenticeship provider SCCU.

He started taking music seriously in 2016, when he moved to [[Coventry]] from [[Southgate, London|Southgate]], [[North London]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.coventrytelegraph.net/news/coventry-news/jay1-your-mrs-charts-rap-16101283|title=Coventry rapper is taking the charts by storm|first=Katy|last=Hallam|publisher=[[Coventry Telegraph]]|date=April 9, 2019|accessdate=July 15, 2019}}</ref>
