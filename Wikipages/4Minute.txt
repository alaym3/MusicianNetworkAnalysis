'''4Minute''' ({{ko-hhrm|hangul=포미닛}}, often stylized as '''4MINUTE''') was a South Korean [[girl group]] formed in 2009 by [[Cube Entertainment]].<ref name="yonhap" /> The members of the group were [[Son Ji-hyun|Nam Ji-hyun]], [[Heo Ga-yoon]], [[Jeon Ji-yoon]], [[Hyuna|Kim Hyun-a]], and [[Kwon So-hyun]].<ref name="KBS">{{cite web|url=http://rki.kbs.co.kr/service/contents_view.htm?lang=e&menu_cate=artist&id=&board_seq=234117&page=0|title=4minute|date=March 25, 2015|work=KBS World|access-date=November 15, 2018|archive-date=November 16, 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181116001058/http://rki.kbs.co.kr/service/contents_view.htm?lang=e&menu_cate=artist&id=&board_seq=234117&page=0|url-status=live}}</ref> The group was known for its edgy [[dance-pop]] style and "girl crush" image that attracted a large female fan base.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/k-town/7407988/4minute-disband-best-songs|title=4Minute Disbands: 7 Songs to Celebrate Their 7 Years Together|last=Herman|first=Tamar|date=June 15, 2016|work=Billboard|access-date=November 15, 2018|archive-date=April 27, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200427182033/https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/k-town/7407988/4minute-disband-best-songs|url-status=live}}</ref><ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=http://www.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20160201001158|title=4minute brings love story, EDM twist in 'Act. 7'|last=Won|first=Ho-jung|date=February 1, 2016|work=The Korea Herald|access-date=November 15, 2018|language=en|archive-date=November 16, 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181116000926/http://www.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20160201001158|url-status=live}}</ref>

The group debuted in June 2009 with the single, "Hot Issue."<ref name=":1" /> They ultimately released one full-length Japanese album, ''[[Diamond (4Minute album)|Diamond]]'' (2010), one full-length Korean album, ''[[4Minutes Left]]'' (2011), seven Korean [[extended play]]s, and multiple singles.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.asiae.co.kr/news/view.htm?idxno=2010121513442854016|script-title=ko:포미닛, 日 정규 앨범 '다이아몬드' 발매|last=Bak|first=Gyeon-uk|date=December 15, 2010|work=Asia Economy Daily|access-date=November 15, 2018|language=ko-KR|archive-date=November 16, 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181116001153/http://www.asiae.co.kr/news/view.htm?idxno=2010121513442854016|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://sports.donga.com/3//20110417/36485359/3|script-title=ko:포미닛 “쩍벌춤, 피멍 들며 3개월 연습했는데…”|last=I|first=Jeong-yeon|date=April 18, 2011|work=Sports DongA|access-date=November 15, 2018|language=ko|title=포미닛 "쩍벌춤, 피멍 들며 3개월 연습했는데…"|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181116001332/http://sports.donga.com/3//20110417/36485359/3|archive-date=November 16, 2018|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref name=":0" />

The group received several major awards, including the Rookie Award at the 2009 [[Golden Disc Awards]],<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://news.joins.com/article/3916951|script-title=ko:[9보] 이승기·이승철, 골든디스크 본상 수상|date=December 10, 2009|work=JoongAng Ilbo|access-date=November 15, 2018|language=ko-KR|archive-date=November 16, 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181116001103/https://news.joins.com/article/3916951|url-status=live}}</ref> the K-pop New Artist Award at the 2010 [[Billboard Japan Music Awards]],<ref name=":2" /> and the Bonsang Award at the 2011, 2012, and 2014 [[Seoul Music Awards]].

4Minute disbanded in June 2016 after unsuccessful contract renewal negotiations between Cube Entertainment and the majority of the group members.<ref name="yonhap" />

* {{note|Alpha|α|group="award_note"}} For Golden Disc Awards, Bonsang awards (Main Prize) are given to up to ten music groups. One of the groups is chosen for a Grand Prize (Daesang).  A Disk Bonsang/Daesang is an Album of the Year, while a Digital Bonsang/Daesang is a Song of the Year.
* {{note|Beta|β|group="award_note"}} For Seoul Music Awards Best Artists, Bonsang awards are given to up to 12 music groups. One of the groups is chosen for a Daesang.
* {{note|Gamma|γ|group="award_note"}} For Mnet Asian Music Awards Song of the Year, Initial nominees are a pool of about 15-20 artists, of which 5 are finalists and 1 is a winner.
* {{note|Delta|δ|group="award_note"}} Mnet Asian Music Awards occasionally hand out [[Mnet Asian Music Awards in the Special Awards Category|special awards]] that are uniquely named or have a limited run.
