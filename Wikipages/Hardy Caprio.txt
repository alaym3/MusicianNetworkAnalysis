'''Hardy Tayyib-Bah''' (born 23 May 1996), known professionally as '''Hardy Caprio''',<ref name="bandcamp">{{cite web |url=https://hardyuk.bandcamp.com/ |title=Archived copy |access-date=1 August 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190801185359/https://hardyuk.bandcamp.com/ |archive-date=1 August 2019 |url-status=live }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ByRYzux2QDg |title=Profitz ft J.A.Y - Be The Best |publisher=[[YouTube]] |date=2 August 2010 |accessdate=21 May 2020}}</ref> is a British rapper and singer.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/newsbeat-44938502|title=Hardy Caprio: The Croydon rapper with a first-class degree|date=28 July 2018|publisher=BBC News|access-date=15 September 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180802160513/https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/newsbeat-44938502|archive-date=2 August 2018|url-status=live}}</ref>

By 2020, Hardy has released a string of successful releases, broken into The Official UK Top 20, and amassed over 270 million streams online.<ref name=":0">{{cite web|url=https://nationofbillions.com/the-rise-rise-of-hardy-caprio|title=The Rise & Rise of Hardy Caprio|website=A Nation of Billions|access-date=9 August 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190809024944/https://nationofbillions.com/the-rise-rise-of-hardy-caprio|archive-date=9 August 2019|url-status=live}}</ref>

== Career ==
=== 2010–2016: Early career ===
Hardy began his music career in 2010, debuting his career under the rap name Profitz.<ref name="bandcamp">{{cite web |url=https://hardyuk.bandcamp.com/ |title=Archived copy |access-date=1 August 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190801185359/https://hardyuk.bandcamp.com/ |archive-date=1 August 2019 |url-status=live }}</ref> At the age of 14, Profitz introduced himself to the music scene with his '#KNWME freestyle'.<ref>{{Citation|title=Profitz #KNWME|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ejyz0fJsIF4|language=en|access-date=9 August 2019}}</ref> Hardy also featured on a few freestyles alongside South London rapper [[Novelist (MC)]].<ref>{{Citation|title=PD.TV {{!}} Novelist, Capa, Javen & Profitz [1/2] (@Prodeetv)|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SWmWDedhz60|language=en|access-date=9 August 2019}}</ref> In 2010 Profitz went on to release his first project titled 'A New Page'. He later went on to release his first mixtape 'Indecisive' in 2013 under a new rap name, Hardy.<ref name="bandcamp" />

=== 2017–2018: Rise to popularity, ''Hardy Season'' and ''The Hollywood EP'' ===
Under the new name Hardy Caprio, he was inspired by Croydon artists such as [[Krept and Konan]] and [[Stormzy]] receiving a widespread success across the UK, which motivated him to pursue music more seriously.<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{cite web|url=https://trappedmagazine.com/exclusive-interview-meet-hardy-caprio/|title=We Meet Hardy Caprio: Exclusive Interview|last=Magazine|first=Mr Trapped|date=10 August 2016|website=Trapped Magazine|language=en-US|access-date=9 August 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190809031719/https://trappedmagazine.com/exclusive-interview-meet-hardy-caprio/|archive-date=9 August 2019|url-status=live}}</ref> Hardy went on to release over 20 videos in 2017, going from 10,000 views to 100,000 in a year.<ref name=":0" /> One notable freestyle he dropped was on the [[SB.TV]] platform, which helped raise his profile. Hardy rapped over Flukes instrumental "Wifey", a [[Grime (music genre)#Rhythm & Grime|Rhythm & Grime]] song originally released and made famous by [[Tinie Tempah]] in 2006.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.dummymag.com/10-best/the-10-best-rhythm-and-grime-tracks-according-to-sian-anderson/|title=The 10 best rhythm and grime tracks, according to Sian Anderson|last=Dummy|website=DummyMag|language=en-US|access-date=9 August 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190809031718/https://www.dummymag.com/10-best/the-10-best-rhythm-and-grime-tracks-according-to-sian-anderson/|archive-date=9 August 2019|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|title=Hardy Caprio {{!}} Warm Up Sessions [S9.EP20]: SBTV|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dBoqEbV47_o|language=en|access-date=9 August 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190809035939/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dBoqEbV47_o|archive-date=9 August 2019|url-status=live}}</ref><ref name=":0" /> In 2017, Hardy released "Unsigned" and "Super Soaker" alongside One Acen. "Unsigned" became a [[Platinum Single|platinum single]], garnered over 14 million views on YouTube, over 30 million streams, and reached number 2 on the [[UK Independent Chart]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/newsbeat-44938502|title=The UK rapper with a first-class degree|last1=Mensah|first1=De'Graft|date=28 July 2018|access-date=9 August 2019|last2=Barrett|first2=Gena-mour|language=en-GB|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190809033217/https://www.bbc.com/news/newsbeat-44938502|archive-date=9 August 2019|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.musicweek.com/interviews/read/rising-star-meet-a2i-group-s-ashley-lauder/072585|title=Rising Star: Meet A2i Group's Ashley Lauder|website=www.musicweek.com|access-date=9 August 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190809031725/https://www.musicweek.com/interviews/read/rising-star-meet-a2i-group-s-ashley-lauder/072585|archive-date=9 August 2019|url-status=live}}</ref><ref name=":0" /><br />
