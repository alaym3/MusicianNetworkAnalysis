'''Llandel Veguilla Malavé''' (born; January 14, 1977), known as '''Yandel''', is a [[Puerto Rico|Puerto Rican]] [[singer]], [[songwriter]], and [[record producer]].<ref name=terra>{{cite web|url=http://entretenimiento.terra.com/famosos/yandel-legacy-de-lider-a-leyenda-tour-y-yandel-camino-al-concierto-un-nuevo-especial-de-conciertos-que-celebra-al-innovador-reggeatonero-yandel-se-est,e379228b0e50c410VgnCLD200000b1bf46d0RCRD.html |title=Yandel: Legacy-De Líder a Leyenda Tour... |work=[[Terra Networks|Terra]]|access-date=March 11, 2015}}</ref> He is best known as a member of the [[reggaeton]] duo [[Wisin & Yandel]]. He released his first solo album, ''[[Quien Contra Mí]]'', in September 2003. His second solo studio album, ''[[De Líder a Leyenda]]'', was released in November 2013, which was certified Gold (Latin) by the [[Recording Industry Association of America|RIAA]] in 2014.{{Certification Cite Ref|type=album|region=United States|artist=Yandel|title=De Lider a Leyenda|access-date=December 12, 2014}}

==Early life and family==
Llandel Veguilla Malavé was born on January 14, 1977, in [[Cayey, Puerto Rico]],<ref name=bio>{{cite web|url=http://www.cmtv.com.ar/biografia/show.php?bnid=2285|title=Biografía de Yandel|work=CMTV|access-date=February 27, 2014}}</ref> the son of Julio Veguilla and Lucy Malavé. Veguilla has two siblings, a younger brother named Gadiel, who is also a singer and a sister named Linnette.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://twitter.com/llandel_malave/status/212587835939749891 |title=Yandel status: MUCHO SOL!!!" DE PLAYA CON MI HERMANITA... |publisher=Twitter |access-date=February 11, 2015}}</ref> Before he became a singer, Yandel worked as a [[barber]] in his hometown.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.peopleenespanol.com/article/wisin-yandel-llegan-revolucionando|title=Wisin & Yandel llegan revolucionando|work=[[People en Español]]|access-date=February 27, 2014}}</ref>

In July 2004,<ref name=aniversario>{{cite web|url=http://ritmoson.tv/noticias/Yandel-celebra-su-aniversario-en-Las-Bahamas-267182491.html |title=Yandel celebra 10 años de matrimonio |work=[[Ritmoson Latino]]|access-date=July 16, 2014}}</ref> Veguilla married his long-time girlfriend<ref>{{cite news|last=Cruz Candelaria |first=José L. |url=http://reggaeton.mforos.com/168298/2103510-yandel-disfruta-a-su-segundo-hijo/ |title=Yandel disfruta a su segundo hijo |date=June 18, 2004 |access-date=May 27, 2014 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://www.webcitation.org/6PtYEwNjl?url=http://reggaeton.mforos.com/168298/2103510-yandel-disfruta-a-su-segundo-hijo/ |archive-date=May 28, 2014 }}</ref> Edneris Espada Figueroa.<ref name=aniversario /> The couple have two children: Adrián Yandel Veguilla, born on {{Birth date and age|2001|01|25}} and Dereck Adrián Veguilla, born on {{Birth date and age|2004|05|27}}.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.primerahora.com/entretenimiento/farandula/nota/yandelcelebrasuaniversarioenlasbahamas-1022467/ |title=Yandel celebra su aniversario en Las Bahamas |work=[[Primera Hora (Puerto Rico)|Primera Hora]]|access-date=July 16, 2014}}</ref>

==Music career==
Yandel met [[Wisin]] in school.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.milenio.com/hey/musica/adios-Lideres-Wisin-Yandel_0_229177124.html |title=El adiós de Los Líderes |work=Milenio |access-date=May 28, 2014}}</ref> They began performing as a duo in 1998<ref name=terra /> (Yandel then billing himself as "Llandel") and made their album debut in 2000 with ''[[Los Reyes del Nuevo Milenio]]''. They later made the jump to a major label in 2003 with ''[[Mi Vida... My Life]]'', their first for [[Universal Music Latin Entertainment|Universal]] subsidiary Fresh Productions. ''[[Pa'l Mundo]]'' then catapulted them to mainstream success in 2005. Subsequently, Wisin & Yandel established their own label, [[WY Records]] and presented an affiliate group, Los Vaqueros.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mtv.com/artists/yandel/biography/|title=Yandel Bio|work=[[MTV]]|access-date=March 1, 2014}}</ref> In November 2013, after the duo's Líderes tour, they went on hiatus.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.cosmopolitan.com/entertainment/news/a14423/yandel-denies-breakup-rumors/ |title=Yandel Denies Reggaeton Duo Will Be Breaking Up |publisher=Cosmopolitan |access-date=December 16, 2014}}</ref> Since then he has continued to tour.<ref>{{Cite web|title = Yandel Announces New Solo 'VIP Tour'|url = http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/latin-notas/5923155/yandel-announces-new-solo-vip-tour|website = Billboard|access-date = November 2, 2015}}</ref> A third album, ''Dangerous'', was released in November 2015.<ref>{{Cite web|title = Dangerous By Yandel|url = https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/dangerous/id1046026029|website = iTunes|access-date = February 17, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title = Yandel Is First Latin Artist to Live Stream Concert on Tidal; Boxing Champ Miguel Cotto to Produce|url = http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/latin/6708031/yandel-tidal-concert-livestream-miguel-cotto-fight|website = Billboard|access-date = November 2, 2015}}</ref>
