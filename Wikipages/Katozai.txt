'''Katozai''' is a town and [[union councils of Pakistan|union council]] of [[Charsadda District, Pakistan|Charsadda District]] in [[Khyber Pakhtunkhwa]] province of [[Pakistan]].<ref>[http://www.nrb.gov.pk/lg_election/union.asp?district=52&dn=Charsada Tehsils & Unions in the District of Charsadda - Government of Pakistan] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080322212739/http://www.nrb.gov.pk/lg_election/union.asp?district=52&dn=Charsada |date=2008-03-22 }}</ref> It is located at 34°16'54N 71°35'53E and has an altitude of 327 metres (1076 feet).<ref>[http://www.fallingrain.com/world/PK/3/Katozai.html Location of Katozai - Falling Rain Genomics]</ref>Here many tribes of Puktoons are settled like Yousafzai, Akhunzada, Mehmod Kheil, Sardar Kheil, Chawar Kheil, Pasand Kheil, Utman Kheil etc. Mehmod {{Reflist}}
