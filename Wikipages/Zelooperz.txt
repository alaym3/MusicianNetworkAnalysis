'''Walter Williams''' (born June 18, 1993), known as '''ZelooperZ''',<ref name="OC Weekly - 14 May 2014 - ZelooperZ is the Youngest Rapper on Danny Brown's OLD Tour">{{cite news|url=http://www.ocweekly.com/music/zelooperz-is-the-youngest-rapper-on-danny-browns-old-tour-6586629|title=ZelooperZ is the Youngest Rapper on Danny Brown's "OLD" Tour|last=Montez|first=Patrick|date=May 2, 2014|work=[[OC Weekly]]|access-date=7 September 2016}}</ref> is an American rapper.<ref name="Vice (magazine) - 1 March 2016 - Detroit Rapper ZelooperZ and Ratking's Wiki Crack Skulls with Heart">{{cite news|url=http://noisey.vice.com/blog/zelooperz-wiki-heart-bothic-interview|title=Detroit Rapper ZelooperZ and Ratking's Wiki Crack Skulls with "Heart"|last=Jenkins|first=Craig|date=March 1, 2016|work=[[Vice (magazine)|Vice]]|access-date=7 September 2016}}</ref> He is a member of [[Detroit]]-based [[hip hop music|hip hop]] group [[Bruiser Brigade]] and is signed to their record label Bruiser Brigade Records.<ref name="The Huffington Post - 7 April 2016 - Detroit Hip Hop Artist Zelooperz Talks About Linking With Danny Brown’s Bruiser Brigade and Dealing With Haters, New Album 'Bothic' Out Now">{{cite news|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/kelly-frazier/detroit-hip-hop-artist-ze_b_9630818.html|title=Detroit Hip Hop Artist Zelooperz Talks About Linking With Danny Brown's Bruiser Brigade and Dealing With Haters, New Album 'Bothic' Out Now|last=Frazier|first=Kelly|date=April 7, 2016|work=[[The Huffington Post]]|access-date=7 September 2016}}</ref>
