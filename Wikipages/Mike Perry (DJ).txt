'''Mikael Mats Robert Persson''' (born 24 June 1983), better known by his stage name '''Mike Perry''', is a Swedish DJ and music producer from [[Skövde]]. He is best known for his 2016 single "[[The Ocean (Mike Perry song)|The Ocean]]" featuring vocals from [[Shy Martin]], which peaked at number 1 on the [[Sverigetopplistan|Swedish Singles Chart]]. Also, some other songs of Perry's are "Inside the Lines", "Stay Young" and "Don't Hide".

==Career==
===2012–2015: Beginnings===
In 2007, Perry released "Psycadelic" with Stephan M.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/de/album/psycadelic/id297718297|title="Psycadelic" von Mike Perry auf Apple Music|website=iTunes}}</ref> In 2012, Perry released "Put Me Up", an electronic dance music production on the Mark Brown label, and released on CR2 Records.<ref>Danny Golpanian: [http://www.dancingastronaut.com/2012/06/mike-perry-put-me-up-original-mix/ "Mike Perry - Put Me Up"]. [[Dancing Astronaut]]</ref><ref>Dan Carter: [http://www.beatmyday.com/2012/06/19/mike-perry-put-me-up/ "Mike Perry - Put Me Up"] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121019040425/http://www.beatmyday.com/2012/06/19/mike-perry-put-me-up/ |date=2012-10-19 }}. Beat My Day</ref> This was followed by a collaboration with Cecilia Axeland called "We Don't Sleep in the Night"<ref>Danny Golpanian: [http://www.getliftedtonight.com/mike-perry-cecilia-axeland-we-dont-sleep-in-the-night-original-mix/ "Mike Perry & Cecilia Axeland – We Don’t Sleep In The Night (Original Mix)"]. Get Lifted Tonight</ref> as well as "Whompin" with Filip Jenven.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/events/492028507548588/|title=MIKE PERRY @ BÅNKEN|website=www.facebook.com}}</ref> In 2013, Perry began releasing music in a "big room" style.<ref>[https://www.housebloggen.no/musikk/release-icona-pop-night-remixes/ "Release: Icona Pop All Night Remixes"]. Housebloggen</ref> Also in 2013, [[Miriam Bryant]]'s "Push & Play" was remixed by Filip Jenven and Mike Perry.<ref>[https://themostunrealbeats.com/2013/04/26/house-push-play-filip-jenven-mike-perry-remix-by-miriam-bryant/ "PUSH PLAY (FILIP JENVEN & MIKE PERRY REMIX) BY MIRIAM BRYANT"]. The Most Unreal Beats</ref> Perry released several pieces of music from 2013 to 2016.<ref>[http://www.bptoptracker.com/artist/mike-perry/20866/tracks "Beatport Release Statistics"]. BP-Top-Tracker</ref>

===2016: Breakthrough with "The Ocean"===
After playing a track called "[[The Ocean (Mike Perry song)|The Ocean]]",<ref>[http://www.dasding.de/musik/Mike-Perry-Interview-mit-Basti/-/id=286/nid=286/did=1176124/dw9ngk/ "Mike Perry: Wer ist der Typ mit dem Sommerhit 'The Ocean'?"]. dasding.de (German)</ref> [[Sony Music Entertainment]] signed him up to their label.<ref>Matt Ogle: [https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2016-09-21/spotify-is-perfecting-the-art-of-the-playlist "Spotify Is Perfecting the Art of the Playlist"]. Bloomberg</ref> On 15 April 2016 the track was released, featuring vocals from Shy Martin. The song peaked at number 1 on the [[Sverigetopplistan|Swedish Singles Chart]], also reaching the Top 10 in [[Austria]], [[Finland]], [[Germany]], the [[Netherlands]], [[Norway]], [[Poland]] and [[Switzerland]] as well as the Top 20 in, [[Australia]], [[Belgium]], [[Denmark]], [[France]] and [[Ireland]] as well as the Top 40 in [[Italy]], [[New Zealand]], [[Portugal]] and [[United Kingdom]].<ref>[https://acharts.co/song/99721 "The Ocean"] acharts.us</ref>

The follow-up single "Inside the Lines" was released on 21 October 2016.<ref>Tim Tabens: [http://www.dance-charts.de/201610217831/mike-perry-feat-casso-inside-the-lines "Nachfolger vom Megahit "The Ocean": Mjusikvideo » Mike Perry feat. Casso - Inside The Lines"]. Dance-Charts (German)</ref> The track features vocals by Casso and reached number 19 in Finland and number 25 in Sweden.<ref>[https://acharts.co/song/102738 "Inside the Lines"] on acharts.us</ref> His third tropical house release in 2016 was the track "Touching You Again".<ref>bigFM Staff: [http://www.bigfm.de/news/17020/mike-perry-single-touching-you-again-startloechern "Mike Perry Steht Mit Der Single "Touching You Again" In Den Startlöchern"] (German)</ref> It was produced with newcomers Hot Shade and Jane XØ as well as singer [[Phoebe Ryan]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://hitparade.ch/song/Hot-Shade-_-Mike-Perry-_-Jane-XOe/Touching-You-Again-1618187|title=Hot Shade / Mike Perry / Jane XØ - Touching You Again - hitparade.ch|first=Steffen|last=Hung|website=hitparade.ch}}</ref> On 15 May 2017, Perry announced his upcoming single "[[Hands (Mike Perry song)|Hands]]" featuring American singer [[Sabrina Carpenter]] and British boy band [[The Vamps (British band)|The Vamps]], which was released later in the month.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://celebmix.com/mike-perry-hands-the-vamps-sabrina-carpenter/|title=Mike Perry Announces 'Hands' Featuring The Vamps and Sabrina Carpenter - CelebMix|last=Craig|first=Nicola|date=2017-05-15|work=CelebMix|access-date=2017-05-16|language=en-GB}}</ref>

Perry released more singles going into 2018 including "California" featuring Hot Shade and Karlyn and "Don't Hide" with Willemijn May, which number 35 and 31 on the Swedish charts respectively.

On 18 January 2019, Perry released "Runaway", peaking #1 position in  [[United States]], [[Chile]] and [[Hungary]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Runaway|url=https://www.top-charts.com/s/runaway-mike-perry|access-date=2020-10-04|website=www.top-charts.com|language=en}}</ref>

In 2019, Perry released the single "Changes" with fellow DJs Ten Times and Dmitri Vangelis and Wyman with vocals from The Companions which reached number 65 in Sweden.
