'''Rodney Lamont Wright Jr.'''<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|title=Memphis rapper 'Big 30' arrested in Alabama on felony drug charges|url=https://wreg.com/news/memphis-rapper-big-30-arrested-in-alabama-on-felony-drug-charges/|url-status=live|access-date=April 17, 2021|website=WREG}}</ref> (born December 28, 1999), better known by his stage name '''Big30''' (stylized as '''BIG30'''), is an American rapper from [[Memphis, Tennessee]]. He is best known for his feature on his childhood friend [[Pooh Shiesty]]'s 2021 single "[[Neighbors (Pooh Shiesty song)|Neighbors]]", which peaked at number 51 on the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]]. Big30 released his debut mixtape, ''King of Killbranch'', in September 2021. It includes appearances from [[Future (rapper)|Future]], [[Lil Durk]], Pooh Shiesty, and [[Moneybagg Yo]], among others.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://hiphopdx.com/news/id.64277/title.future-lil-durk-pooh-shiesty-more-join-big30-on-king-of-killbranch-album#|title=Big30 Releases 'King Of Killbranch' Album|work=[[HipHopDX]]|last=Walker|first=Joe|date=September 2, 2021|access-date=September 2, 2021}}</ref>

== Controversies ==

=== Feuds ===
In June 2020, rapper [[Gucci Mane]] called out Wright for allegedly posting him on his [[Instagram]] account multiple times, claiming that they only met once. Wright did not respond.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Gucci Mane Calls Out Moneybagg Yo's Artist For Posting Same Encounter For Clout|url=https://hiphopdx.com/news/id.56561/title.gucci-mane-calls-out-moneybagg-yos-artist-for-posting-same-encounter-for-clout|url-status=live|access-date=April 17, 2021|website=HipHopDX}}</ref> The beef was squashed shortly thereafter, with Wright appearing on Gucci Mane's 2021 single, "Shit Crazy".<ref>{{Cite web|title=Gucci Mane Makes 'Shit Crazy' With Big30 in His New Music Video|url=https://www.vulture.com/2021/04/gucci-mane-drops-new-song-shit-crazy-video-with-big30.html|url-status=live|access-date=May 29, 2021|website=Vulture}}</ref>

=== Legal issues ===
In October 2020, Wright was arrested in [[Birmingham, Alabama]], along with the driver, who was identified as Leaton Foster of [[Decatur, Georgia]]. The two were traveling from [[Memphis, Tennessee]] to [[Atlanta|Atlanta, Georgia]] with more than $17,000 in cash, a loaded handgun, [[promethazine]], [[codeine]], and packaged [[Cannabis (drug)|cannabis]]. Wright was charged with first-degree possession of marijuana, possession of drug paraphernalia, and being a felon in possession of firearm, along with other federal charges.<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web|title=Memphis rapper 'Big 30' jailed in Alabama on drug charges|url=https://www.al.com/news/birmingham/2020/10/memphis-rapper-big-30-jailed-in-alabama-on-drug-charges.html|url-status=live|access-date=April 17, 2021|website=AL.com}}</ref>
