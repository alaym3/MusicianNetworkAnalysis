'''Violetta Zironi''' (born 5 April 1995) is an Italian singer-songwriter and actress.

In 2013, she took part in [[X Factor (Italy series 7)|season 7]] of the [[X Factor (Italian TV series)|Italian version]] of ''[[The X Factor]]'' with her [[ukulele]] which soon became her signature instrument.<ref name=delusione>{{Cite news|url=https://www.ilfattoquotidiano.it/2013/12/13/finale-x-factor-la-delusione-di-violetta-la-storia-della-ragazza-con-lukulele/812387/|title=Finale X Factor, la delusione di Violetta. La storia della ragazza con l'ukulele|date=13 December 2013|publisher=[[Il Fatto Quotidiano]]|access-date=28 October 2020|language=it}}</ref> She reached the final and finished third.<ref name=delusione/> She debuted with her EP ''[[Dimmi che non passa (EP)|Dimmi che non passa]]'', containing her debut single "[[Dimmi che non passa (song)|Dimmi che non passa]]".<ref name=sonymusic>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sonymusic.it/news/e-disponibile-su-itunes-dimmi-che-non-passa-lep-di-violetta/|title=È disponibile su iTunes 'Dimmi che non passa', l'EP di Violetta|website=[[Sony Music]]|access-date=28 October 2020|language=it}}</ref>

In 2020, she starred in the [[Netflix]]'s original film ''[[Rose Island (film)|Rose Island]]'', directed by [[Sydney Sibilia]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.24emilia.com/ce-anche-violetta-zironi-nel-nuovo-film-originale-di-netflix-sulla-storia-dellisola-delle-rose/|title=Isola delle Rose, Violetta nel film|website=24emilia.com|date=2 October 2019|access-date=27 October 2020|language=it}}</ref>

==Biography==
===Early years===
Violetta Zironi is born in [[Correggio, Emilia Romagna]], to comic book artist and screenwriter Giuseppe Zironi, who worked for [[The Walt Disney Company Italy|Walt Disney Italia]], [[RAI]] and [[Mediaset]],<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://st.ilsole24ore.com/art/cultura/2013-10-18/violetta-casta-diva-x-factor-padre-e-sceneggiatore-tv-giuseppe-zironi-160924.shtml?uuid=AB87FgX&refresh_ce=1|title=Violetta 'casta diva' di X Factor: il padre è lo sceneggiatore Zironi|author=Francesco Prisco|date=18 October 2013|publisher=[[Il Sole 24 Ore]]|access-date=28 October 2020|language=it}}</ref> and municipal clerk Annalisa Sansone.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.ilrestodelcarlino.it/reggio_emilia/spettacoli/musica/2013/12/11/995157-x-factor-violetta-intervista-mamma.shtml|title=Mamma Annalisa: 'Violetta, segui i tuoi sogni'|publisher=[[Il Resto del Carlino]]|date=11 December 2013|access-date=28 October 2020|language=it}}</ref> She was first introduced to music at age of three, when she joined a local children's choir, before starting taking piano lessons.<ref name=sonymusic/>

Violetta started performing live in her early teens, singing and playing guitar, often accompanied by her father Giuseppe on harmonica and by Oscar Abelli on drums, touring in local festivals and clubs. In 2012, she met [[ukulele]] player Luca "Jontom" Tomassini, who introduced Violetta to this instrument. They performed together at the 2012 YOUkulele Festival in Rome,<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.dasapere.it/2012/09/12/youkulele-festival-conclude-festambiente-mondi-possibili/|title=YOUkulele Festival conclude Festambiente Mondi Possibili|website=Dasapere.it|date=12 September 2020|access-date=28 October 2020|language=it}}</ref> and Jontom wanted Violetta as a guest on his EP ''Likelike Highway'', released by the independent label Insomnia Records.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://gazzettadireggio.gelocal.it/reggio/cronaca/2014/02/12/news/il-ritorno-di-violetta-con-l-ukulele-di-jontom-1.8653511|title=Il ritorno di Violetta con l'ukulele di Jontom|publisher=Gazzetta di Reggio|date=12 February 2014|access-date=28 October 2020|language=it}}</ref>

===2013–2014: ''The X Factor'' and her first EP===
In 2013 Violetta showed up at the auditions for [[X Factor (Italy series 7)|season 7]] of the [[X Factor (Italian TV series)|Italian version]] of ''[[The X Factor]]'' with her [[ukulele]] which soon became her signature instrument, and after impressing the judges with her version of "[[Shortnin' Bread]]" she was admitted to the live show and included in the "Under 25 Girls" team mentored by [[Mika (singer)|Mika]].<ref name=delusione/> During the live shows, she performed songs such as [[Let Her Go (Passenger song)|"Let Her Go"]] by [[Passenger (singer)|Passenger]], "Le tasche piene di sassi" by Italian popstar [[Jovanotti]], "[[Friday I'm in Love]]" by [[The Cure]], "[[One Day / Reckoning Song]]" by [[Asaf Avidan]], "[[Royals (song)|Royals]]" by [[Lorde]], "[[9 to 5 (Dolly Parton song)|9 to 5]]" by [[Dolly Parton]], "[[Skinny Love]]" by [[Birdy (musician)|Birdy]] and "[[One (U2 song)|One]]" by [[U2]].<ref name=delusione/> She finished third place and released her debut EP entitled ''[[Dimmi che non passa (EP)|Dimmi che non passa]]'' with [[Sony Music]] on 13 December 2013.<ref name=sonymusic/>

In 2014, Violetta was chosen to perform [[Dorothy Gale]]'s songs in the Italian version of ''[[Legends of Oz: Dorothy's Return]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://it.cinema.yahoo.com/blog/multisala/il-magico-mondo-di-oz-trailer-italiano-in-esclusiva---075757508.html|title=''Violetta Zironi nella Colonna Sonora di Legends of Oz: Dorothy's Return|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140514212435/https://it.cinema.yahoo.com/blog/multisala/il-magico-mondo-di-oz-trailer-italiano-in-esclusiva---075757508.html|archive-date=14 May 2014}}</ref> On 21 June 2014, she released her second single "[[Il primo giorno d'estate]]", written by Luca Mattioni and Mario Cianchi, and produced by Alessandro Magnanini.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.sonymusic.it/it/news/domani-21-giugno-arriva-%E2%80%9Cil-primo-giorno-d%E2%80%99estate%E2%80%9D-il-nuovo-singolo-di-violetta |title=Archived copy |access-date=15 October 2014 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140715001204/http://www.sonymusic.it/it/news/domani-21-giugno-arriva-%E2%80%9Cil-primo-giorno-d%E2%80%99estate%E2%80%9D-il-nuovo-singolo-di-violetta |archive-date=15 July 2014 }}</ref> During the summer, her version of "[[Bring Me Sunshine]]" was used for the TV commercial of brand Pavesini.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.radiomusik.it/la-canzone-della-pubblicita-dei-pavesini-video/|title=La canzone della pubblicità dei Pavesini (video)|website=Radiomusik.it|date=18 June 2014|access-date=28 October 2020|language=it}}</ref>

On 2 August 2014 she performed at the [[rockabilly]] festival "Summer Jamboree" in [[Senigallia]], when she opened [[Ben E. King]]'s show and duet with him performing "[[Stand by Me (Ben E. King song)|Stand By Me]]".<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://video.repubblica.it/edizione/bologna/duetto-a-sorpresa-ben-e-king-e-violetta-di-x-factor-cantano-stand-by-me/174100/172684|title=Duetto a sorpresa: Ben E. King e Violetta di X Factor cantano Stand by me|date=3 August 2004|publisher=[[la Repubblica]]|access-date=28 October 2020|language=it}}</ref>

On 25 November 2014 her version of "[[Winter Wonderland]]" was included in the compilation ''X Factor Christmas 2014'', released by Sony Music.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.allmusicitalia.it/news/xfactor-christmas-2014-disco-natalizio-realizzato-dai-concorrenti-attuali-ed-ex-talent.html|title=X Factor Christmas 2014, un disco natalizio realizzato dai concorrenti (attuali ed ex) del talent|date=22 November 2014|publisher=All Music Italia|access-date=28 October 2020|language=it}}</ref>

===2015–2016: Post-''X Factor'' and Written in Scars Tour ===
In 2015 Violetta's song "Eppure mi va" was included in a shortlist of 60 tracks set to compete in the newcomers section of the [[Sanremo Music Festival 2015]], but didn't make it to the final competition.<ref name=semplice>{{Cite news|url=https://www.allmusicitalia.it/news/violetta-zironi-tour-singolo-semplice-zibba.html|title=Violetta Zironi: pronta per il tour, dopo il singolo 'Semplice'|date=10 June 2016|author=Jennifer Engelmann|website=All Music Italia|access-date=28 October 2020|language=it}}</ref> In April, Violetta guest-starred in [[Jack Savoretti]]'s Written in Scars Tour, being the opening act of almost all the Italian shows and also joining the singer on stage.<ref name=sorrisi>{{Cite news|url=https://www.sorrisi.com/musica/news/violetta-zironi-x-factor-con-il-broncio-non-e-stato-semplice/|title=Violetta Zironi: 'X Factor con il broncio? Non è stato semplice|date=20 July 2016|publisher=TV Sorrisi e Canzoni|access-date=28 October 2020|language=it}}</ref> She once again took part in the selections for the "Newcomers" section at the [[Sanremo Music Festival 2016]] and again her song "Piccola ferita" was included in the shortlist, but didn't make it to the stage.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.reggionline.com/antonella-lo-coco-violetta-zironi-e-valentina-tioli-in-corsa-per-sanremo-giovani/|title=Antonella Lo Coco, Violetta Zironi e Valentina Tioli in corsa per Sanremo Giovani|publisher=Reggio Online|date=3 November 2015|access-date=28 October 2020|language=it}}</ref>

On 3 June 2016, she released her third single "Semplice", written and performed together with Italian singer-songwriter Zibba.<ref name=semplice/><ref name=sorrisi/>

===2017-present: New projects===
In 2017, she moved to [[Berlin]] and signed with label Pon't Danic Music, releasing a new EP entitled ''Half Moon Lane'' on 23 February 2018.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.comingsoon.it/musica/speciali/intervista-a-violetta-zironi-cinque-anni-dopo-x-factor-torna-con-un-ep-in/n76120/|title=Intervista a Violetta Zironi: cinque anni dopo X Factor, torna con un EP in inglese|date=6 March 2018|website=Comingsoon.it|access-date=28 October 2020|language=it}}</ref>

On 14 September 2019, she released another EP entitled ''Scenes from My Lonely Window''.<ref name=tender>{{cite news|url=https://www.nylon.com/violetta-zironi-interview|title=Violetta Zironi Makes Tender Music For Chaotic Times|website=Nylon|date=13 September 2019|access-date=27 October 2020}}</ref>
