'''Vive la Fête''' ({{lang-en|Long live the party}}) are a Belgian music duo from [[Ghent]], [[East Flanders]], formed in 1997 by Danny Mommens (guitar, vocals) and Els Pynoo (vocals).

==History==
Vive la Fête was founded in 1997 when Mommens (then still playing in his former band [[Deus (band)|dEUS]]) met Els at a party hosted by or for her sister. He had previously been an acquaintance of Pynoo's sister who had photographed Els naked and had later showed the picture to Mommens. As Els stated in a 2005 interview; Mommens had approached her in a normal fashion and said randomly: "Hey I saw you naked!" Mommen's recorded a few demos with Pynoo on his [[8-track tape|8-track recorder]]. These demos were released on the [[extended play|EP]] ''Je ne veux pas'' (sometimes also referred to as ''Paris'') which gathered some attention for its resemblance to '80s [[new wave music]]. Their first success came with their debut ''Attaque Surprise'' (2000). Later records such as ''République Populaire'' (2001) and ''Nuit Blanche'' (2003) made them popular, especially in the fashion world, where [[Karl Lagerfeld]] is one of their fans, and has enlisted them to perform at a number of his big shows in [[New York City]] and [[Paris]].<ref>{{cite web |last=Gottehrer |first=Zoe |url=http://www.365mag.com/index.php?pg=evrw&recnum=704&Title=Vive+La+Fete+on+365MAG+International+Music+Magazine |title=Vive La Fete |publisher=[[365mag]] |date=4 June 2005 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20061020082938/http://www.365mag.com/index.php?pg=evrw&recnum=704&Title=Vive+La+Fete+on+365MAG+International+Music+Magazine |archive-date=20 October 2006 |access-date=10 May 2007}}</ref> In 2005 they released ''Grand Prix'' and toured all over Europe and played some shows in Brazil<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=L0l6KK7Z6kc YouTube - Vive la fete - Recife Rock 2004-04-18]</ref> and Mexico.
