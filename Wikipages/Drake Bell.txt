'''Jared Drake Bell'''<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/drake-bell-mn0000209019/biography|title=Drake Bell - Biography|last=Brown|first=Marisa|work=[[AllMusic]]|access-date=June 5, 2015}}</ref> (born June 27, 1986), also known as '''Drake Campana''',<ref>{{Cite web|last=Fowler|first=Kate|date=November 20, 2020|title=Drake Bell's Mexican name change explained: Singer changes Twitter to Drake Campana!|url=https://www.hitc.com/en-gb/2020/11/20/drake-bell-drake-campana/|access-date=November 20, 2020|website=HITC|language=en-GB}}</ref> is an American actor, singer, songwriter, and musician.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Flores|first1=Brandon|title=Blast Album Review: Drake Bell - Ready Steady Go!|url=http://blastoutyourstereo.com/music/music-reviews/album-reviews/drake-bell-ready-steady-go-album-review-9247|website=Blast Out Your Stereo|access-date=December 2, 2014 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141209031413/http://blastoutyourstereo.com/music/music-reviews/album-reviews/drake-bell-ready-steady-go-album-review-9247 |archive-date=December 9, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=High School Nation Tour Featuring Drake Bell, Dakota Bradley and Bean plays Kentwood High|url=http://www.northwestmusicscene.com/2014/09/high-school-nation-tour-drake-bell-plays-kentwood-high/|website=Northwest Music Scene|access-date=December 6, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141019221733/http://www.northwestmusicscene.com/2014/09/high-school-nation-tour-drake-bell-plays-kentwood-high/|archive-date=October 19, 2014|url-status=dead}}</ref> Born in [[Newport Beach, California]], he began his career as an actor in the early 1990s at the age of five with his first televised appearance on ''[[Home Improvement (TV series)|Home Improvement]]'', and also appeared in several commercials as a child. Bell is best known for his starring roles on [[Nickelodeon]]'s ''[[The Amanda Show]]'' and ''[[Drake & Josh]]''. He also starred in a trilogy of ''[[The Fairly OddParents]]'' movies on Nickelodeon. Bell was the voice of [[Spider-Man|Peter Parker / Spider-Man]] in the animated series ''[[Ultimate Spider-Man (TV series)|Ultimate Spider-Man]]'' on [[Disney XD]].

In addition to acting, Bell has a career in music starting in the early 2000s, at the time of his appearances on ''The Amanda Show'', with a band named Drake 24/7. He also co-wrote and performed the theme song to ''Drake & Josh'', entitled "[[Found a Way]]". In 2005, he independently released his debut album, ''[[Telegraph (album)|Telegraph]]'', released under Bell's own name. His second album, ''[[It's Only Time]]'', was released in 2006 after signing with [[Universal Motown Records]] and debuted at #81 on the ''Billboard'' 200, selling over 23,000 copies during its first week of release. ''It's Only Time'' has sold 178,000 copies in the United States as of 2012. His first video album, ''[[Drake Bell in Concert]]'', was released in 2008. It debuted at #81 on the Top 100 Mexican Albums Chart. Bell released an EP in 2011 called ''[[A Reminder]]'' independently. In 2014, Bell released his third album, ''[[Ready Steady Go! (album)|Ready Steady Go!]]'' under indie label [[Surfdog Records]]. ''Ready Steady Go!'' debuted at #182 on the [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]], and sold 2,000 copies in its first week of release.

==Personal life==
On December 29, 2005, Bell, then 19 years old, and a friend of his were driving from [[Malibu, California|Malibu]] to [[Los Angeles]] when, while stopped at a red light on [[California State Route 1]], the two were struck by an oncoming vehicle.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.cbsnews.com/news/nickelodeon-star-hurt-in-car-crash/|title=Nickelodeon Star Hurt In Car Crash|work=[[CBS News]]|access-date=April 17, 2018|language=en}}</ref> Though Bell's passenger suffered only bruises, Bell's injuries, while not life-threatening, were severe, including a fractured neck and [[vertebra]], a broken jaw, the loss of seven teeth, and deep lacerations to his face.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Keller|first1=June|title=Drake Bell Survives Car Crash|url=http://www.eonline.com/news/51371/drake-bell-survives-car-crash|publisher=E! News|access-date=January 15, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite magazine|last1=Silverman|first1=Stephen|title=Nickelodeon Star Drake Bell in Car Crash|url=http://www.people.com/people/article/0,,1145632,00.html|magazine=[[People (magazine)|People]]| publisher=Time Inc.|access-date=January 15, 2016}}</ref> As part of his recovery, Bell required more than 70 facial stitches, had to have his jaw wired shut for two months, and underwent surgery on his chin and mouth.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://people.com/archive/crash-learn-vol-65-no-14/|title=Crash & Learn|magazine=People|access-date=April 17, 2018|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.eonline.com/news/51371/drake-bell-survives-car-crash|title=Drake Bell Survives Car Crash|publisher=E! In 2007, Bell purchased a 2,640-square-foot (245 m<sup>2</sup>) house in [[Los Feliz, Los Angeles|Los Feliz]] for $2,050,000.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Beale|first1=Lauren|title=Drake Bell foreclosure house on the market in Los Feliz|url=http://www.latimes.com/business/realestate/hot-property/la-fi-hotprop-drake-bell-20140626-story.html|newspaper=Los Angeles Times|access-date=January 15, 2016}}</ref> After Bell filed for bankruptcy in [[California]] in early 2014, the house was taken in foreclosure.<ref>{{cite magazine|last1=David|first1=Mark |title=Drake Bell Lists at a Loss in Los Feliz|url= https://variety.com/2013/dirt/real-estalker/drake-bell-lists-at-a-loss-in-los-feliz-1201235544/|magazine=Variety|access-date=April 2, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Knight|first1=James|title=Skinny Josh Peck Snubs Bankrupt 'Ultimate Spider-man's' Drake Bell; 'Drake and Josh' Movie Plans|url=http://www.classicalite.com/articles/6132/20140219/skinny-josh-peck-snubs-bankrupt-ultimate-spider-man-drake-and-josh-movie-plans.htm|website=Classicalite|date=February 19, 2014|publisher=The Classicalite Company LLC|access-date=January 12, 2015}}</ref>

Bell is a cousin of former [[San Diego Padres]] [[relief pitcher]] [[Heath Bell]].<ref>{{cite web|last=Kurkjian |first=Tim |title=Fun-loving Bell finally has his dream job |url=http://sports.espn.go.com/mlb/columns/story?columnist=kurkjian_tim&id=4073444 |work=[[ESPN The Magazine]] |date=April 17, 2009 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100826073050/http://sports.espn.go.com/mlb/columns/story?columnist=kurkjian_tim&id=4073444 |archive-date=August 26, 2010 }}</ref>

==Legal issues==
===Driving under the influence===
Bell was arrested on December&nbsp;21, 2015, for [[driving under the influence]] in [[Glendale, California]] at 2:45&nbsp;AM after police officers witnessed him swerving and driving well over the speed limit.<ref>{{Cite news|url= http://www.nydailynews.com/entertainment/gossip/drake-bell-facing-jail-time-dui-arrest-article-1.2496322|title=Drake Bell facing jail time for DUI arrest|newspaper=[[New York Daily News|Daily News]]| location=New York City|access-date=April 17, 2018|language=en}}</ref> He was subsequently released on $20,000 bond and pleaded not guilty.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Rothman|first1=Michael|title='Drake & Josh' Star Drake Bell Arrested for Suspicion of DUI|url=https://abcnews.go.com/Entertainment/drake-josh-star-drake-bell-arrested-suspicion-dui/story?id=35912683|access-date=December 22, 2015|work=[[ABC News]]}}</ref> In September 2016, Bell was found guilty and sentenced to four days in jail and four years' probation and would be required to attend an alcohol education program; Bell served only one day for good behavior.<ref>{{Cite news|url= http://www.tmz.com/2016/09/03/drake-bell-dui-jail-plea/|title=Drake Bell -- Jail-Bound After DUI Plea|publisher=[[TMZ]]|access-date=April 17, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.eonline.com/news/792187/drake-bell-sentenced-to-96-hours-in-jail-for-dui|title=Drake Bell Serves One Day in Jail for DUI Conviction|date=September 6, 2016|publisher=[[E! ===Sexual assault accusation and attempted endangering of children===
On June&nbsp;4, 2021, Bell was arrested in [[Cleveland, Ohio]], on charges relating to "attempted endangering [of] children and disseminating matter harmful to juveniles."<ref>{{cite web |last1=Hlavaty |first1=Kaylyn |title=Jared Bell, Drake of Nickelodeon's 'Drake and Josh,' indicted in Cuyahoga County |url=https://www.news5cleveland.com/news/local-news/jared-bell-drake-of-nickelodeons-drake-and-josh-indicted-in-cuyahoga-county |website=[[WEWS-TV]] News 5 Cleveland |date=June 4, 2021 |publisher=Scripps Media, Inc |access-date=June 4, 2021}}</ref> A public information officer with the [[Cuyahoga County]] Prosecutor's Office, said "the 15-year-old victim, who had established a relationship with Bell several years prior, attended his concert in December 2017 [...] While there, Bell violated his [[duty of care]] and, in doing so, created a risk of harm to the victim." The [[Cleveland Division of Police|Cleveland Police]] also found that Bell had sent the minor "inappropriate social media messages" for "months leading up to the concert." Bell was 31 at the time of the alleged incident. Bell pleaded not guilty, and was freed on a $2,500 personal bond, and was ordered to provide a [[DNA sample]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Strapagiel|first=Lauren|date=June 5, 2021|title=Drake Bell Has Been Charged With Child Endangerment|url=https://www.buzzfeednews.com/article/laurenstrapagiel/drake-bell-child-endangerment-charge|url-status=live|access-date=June 5, 2021|website=[[BuzzFeed News]]|language=en}}</ref> He agreed to have no contact with the alleged victim in the case with a pretrial hearing scheduled for June&nbsp;23.<ref>{{cite news |title=Drake Bell, star of "Drake and Josh," charged for crimes against a child in Ohio |url=https://www.cbsnews.com/news/drake-bell-star-of-drake-and-josh-charged-for-crimes-against-a-child-in-ohio/ |work=CBS News}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Naquin |first1=Talia |last2=Shea |first2=Jack |title=Jared Bell of 'Drake and Josh' charged with crimes against a child |url=https://fox8.com/news/jared-bell-of-drake-and-josh-charged-with-crimes-against-a-child/ |website=Fox 8 Cleveland WJW |access-date=June 4, 2021 |date=June 4, 2021}}</ref> On June&nbsp;23, Bell pleaded guilty to both charges.<ref>{{cite news |title= Actor Drake Bell Pleads Guilty In Ohio Child Endangerment Case, Sentencing Set For July |url= https://www.deadline.com/2021/06/drake-bell-guilty-plea-child-endangerment-ohio-1234780213/amp/ |work=Deadline}}</ref>  On July&nbsp;12, 2021, Bell's victim, now 19, made a public appearance and statement accusing him of sexually assaulting her while she was underage, in addition to the endangerment charges. Bell was sentenced to two years of [[probation]] and 200 hours of community service (which he may complete in California). He also is not allowed to have any contact with his victim.<ref>{{Cite news|last=Napoli|first=Jessica|date=July 12, 2021|title=Drake Bell sentenced to two years probation, community service for charges involving a minor|work=[[Fox News]]|url=https://www.foxnews.com/entertainment/drake-bell-sentenced-two-years-probation|access-date=July 12, 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2021/06/23/arts/television/drake-bell-child-endangerment-plea.html?surface=most-popular&fellback=false&req_id=937670863&algo=bandit-all-surfaces-uh-lasttoday-alpha-01&variant=3_bandit-all-surfaces-uh-lasttoday-alpha-01&pool=pool/91fcf81c-4fb0-49ff-bd57-a24647c85ea1&imp_id=527481764&action=click&module=Popular%20in%20The%20Times&pgtype=Homepage|title=Drake Bell Pleads Guilty to Attempted Child Endangerment|last=Bahr|first=Sarah|work=[[The New York Times]]|date=June 23, 2021}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Drake Bell's Victim Accused Him of Grooming, Sexual Assault During Hearing — Read Her Statement|url=https://people.com/tv/drake-bell-child-endangerment-sentencing-victim-statement-full/|access-date=2021-07-20|website=PEOPLE.com|language=en}}</ref>  Several months later, Bell posted his own account of the incident on TikTok, claiming that he had no prior knowledge of the girl's true age during their conversations, for the most part, ending all ties with her upon learning the truth. ==Awards and nominations==

Bell was nominated for a [[21st Young Artist Awards|Young Artist Award]] twice, once in 2000 for his role in ''[[The Jack Bull]]'', in which he won,<ref>{{cite web|title=You see my spidey-sense? It's this thing in my brain that warns me of danger.|url=http://www.wizardworld.com/drbeuspdjoco.html|website=Wizard World|access-date=January 15, 2016|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150905110532/http://www.wizardworld.com/drbeuspdjoco.html|archive-date=September 5, 2015}}</ref> and another time in 2006 for ''[[Yours, Mine and Ours (2005 film)|Yours, Mine and Ours]]''. He has won 10 [[Kids Choice Awards]], 3 in the United States and 5 internationally for his role as Drake Parker on ''[[Drake & Josh]]''.<ref name="Awards">{{cite web|title=DRAKE & JOSH|url=http://www.buddytv.com/tvshow/page/drake-and-josh-info-1.aspx|website=Buddy TV|access-date=November 19, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141129092426/http://www.buddytv.com/tvshow/page/drake-and-josh-info-1.aspx|archive-date=November 29, 2014|url-status=dead}}</ref> He also won a Kids Choice Award in 2007 in the United Kingdom for Best Male Singer, winning over [[Justin Timberlake]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Winners Announced for the First Ever Nickelodeon Kids' Choice Awards UK|url=http://www.nickkcapress.com/2007KCA/|website=Nick CA Press|access-date=November 19, 2014}}</ref> He was also nominated for Best TV Actor, but lost to [[Nat Wolff]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Justin Timberlake Slimed at Nickelodeon Kids' Choice Awards|url=http://www.foxnews.com/story/2007/04/01/justin-timberlake-slimed-at-nickelodeon-kids-choice-awards/|publisher=Fox News Channel|access-date=November 19, 2014}}</ref> Bell has also won a [[Teen Choice Awards|Teen Choice Award]] in 2008 for his role as the Dragonfly in [[Superhero Movie]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Jonas Brothers, Gossip Girl win big at the Teen Choice Awards|url=http://www.canada.com/montrealgazette/news/arts/story.html?id=5caf987c-08d0-43a6-aa3c-5f99900c3cd9|website=Canada|publisher=Postmedia Network|access-date=January 15, 2016|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160214194715/http://www.canada.com/montrealgazette/news/arts/story.html?id=5caf987c-08d0-43a6-aa3c-5f99900c3cd9|archive-date=February 14, 2016}}</ref> He was also nominated for an award in 2014 in the Capital Twitter Awards for biggest Twitter feud with [[Justin Bieber]].<ref name="Bieber" />
