'''Celeste''' are a French band from [[Lyon]], founded in 2005. Combining [[black metal]], [[sludge metal]], and [[post-hardcore]], they have released five albums to date.

== History ==
The band was founded 2005 in the Lyon [[hardcore punk]] scene.<ref name="iv-vp">vampster.com: ''[http://vampster.com/artikel/show/35529_CELESTE__B%C3%B6se_Jungs_interview_.html CELESTE: Böse Jungs (Interview) @ vampster.com webzine]'', Zugriff am 13.</ref> Guitarist Guillaume Rieth, drummer Antoine Royer, and bassist Antoine Kerbrat had known each other since high school, and recruited vocalist Johan Girardeau. Rieth and Girardeau are former members of the [[screamo]] band Mihai Edrisch,<ref name="quiet">{{cite web|url=https://thequietus.com/articles/05376-celeste-interview-morte-s-nee-s|title=Why Black Metal Isn't Our Cup Of Tea: Celeste Interviewed|last=Cook|first=Toby|work=[[The Quietus]]|date=1 December 2010|accessdate=7 March 2019}}</ref> which had shared members with Daïtro.<ref name="iv-ox"/> Antoine has since left the band, with Girardeau taking up bass and Sébastien Ducotté joining as second guitarist.

In 2006, Celeste released their first EP, followed in 2008 by their debut album, released by German label [[Denovali Records]].<ref name="iv-vp"/> Since then, they released four more albums and toured through Europe repeatedly and as far as Russia, Asia, and the US.<ref>metal1.info: ''[https://www.metal1.info/interviews/celeste-2017-infideles/ Celeste | Interviews | Metal1.info]''</ref>

[[File:Droneberg Berlin Celeste Apr 16 2015 57.jpg|thumb|Guillaume performing at Droneberg Festival 2015 in [[Berlin]]]]

== Style ==
The band play a mix of [[Sludge metal|sludge]], [[black metal]] and [[post-hardcore]],<ref name="iv-bld">bloodchamber.de: ''[http://www.bloodchamber.de/interview/c/498/ Interview mit Celeste: Französische Aggression aus dem Dunkeln @ Bloodchamber.de]'', Zugriff am 14.</ref> with elements of [[doom metal]] and [[death metal]].<ref name="iv-ox">Christoph Schulz: ''[http://www.ox-fanzine.de/web/itv/3751/interviews.212.html Interview : CELESTE :: ox-fanzine.de]'', Zugriff am 13.</ref> They refuse other bands as influences.<ref name="iv-vp" />
