'''Lee Ru-ma''' ({{lang-ko|이루마}}; born 15 February 1978), better known by his stage name '''Yiruma''' ({{lang-ko|이루마|links=no}}), is a [[South Korea|South Korean]] pianist and composer.

==Biography==
Yiruma was born in Seoul to a pastor. At age five, he began learning the piano and moved to the [[United Kingdom]], where he attended the [[Purcell School for Young Musicians]]<ref>{{cite web|title=Alumni|url=https://www.purcell-school.org/about-us/alumni/|access-date=14 December 2020|publisher=[[Purcell School for Young Musicians]]}}</ref> and graduated from [[King's College London]].<ref name="ClassicFM">{{cite news|last=Shaw Roberts|first=Maddy|date=27 February 2019|title=Yiruma's River Flows in You: from its meaning to its starring role in the Twilight saga|publisher=[[Classic FM (UK)|Classic FM]]|url=https://www.classicfm.com/discover-music/instruments/piano/yiruma-river-flows-in-you-meaning-twilight/|access-date=19 February 2021}}</ref>

After graduation, Yiruma returned to South Korea and released his debut album, ''Love Scene'', in 2001. He gained wider public recognition for performing the piano versions of the soundtracks from the popular 2006 drama ''[[Spring Waltz]]''.<ref>{{cite news|date=13 March 2006|title=이루마·러브홀릭, 드라마 '봄의 왈츠' OST 참여|language=ko|work=[[The Hankyoreh]]|agency=[[Yonhap News Agency]]|url=http://www.hani.co.kr/arti/culture/music/108351.html|access-date=2 November 2021}}</ref> On 1 January 2009, he became a [[DJ]] for [[KBS Classic FM|KBS 1FM]] ''Yiruma's Music from All Around the World''.<ref>{{cite web|author=ay_link|date=2 January 2009|title=KBS1 93.1FM new radio DJ, Yiruma |url=http://www.jazzholic.com/2009/01/02/dj-yiruma/|access-date=16 March 2012|website=Jazzholic|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090122043536/http://www.jazzholic.com/2009/01/02/dj-yiruma/|archive-date=22 January 2009|url-status=dead}}</ref>

Yiruma's 2011 compilation album ''The Best: Reminiscent, 10th Anniversary'' debuted at number 3 in the [[Billboard charts|''Billboard'' classical charts]] in July 2020 and reached number 1 just two weeks later; ''Billboard''{{'s}} classical charts had done away with its rules of only counting albums sold in the United States and recognizing albums released in the last 18 months.<ref>{{cite magazine |last=Lee|first=Minhyeong |date=7 July 2020|title=How Yiruma Made It to the Top of the Classical Albums Chart For 17 Weeks|magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/9413829/how-yiruma-topped-classical-albums-chart-17-weeks|access-date=2 November 2021}}</ref> His music has seen a resurgence in popularity due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic lockdowns]] in 2020. Several videos of his compositions posted on [[YouTube]] and other social media platforms have garnered millions of views.<ref>{{cite news|date=22 May 2020|title=[e글중심] "K-뉴에이지의 시작?" … 이루마 빌보드 클래식 부문 1위|language=ko|work=[[JoongAng Ilbo]]|url=https://www.joongang.co.kr/article/23783390|access-date=2 November 2021}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Yiruma previously held British citizenship, which he voluntarily renounced in order to enlist for [[Conscription in South Korea|mandatory military service]].<ref>{{cite news|title=이루마, 英 시민권 포기하고 5월 입대|url=http://mn.kbs.co.kr/news/view.do?ncd=1128595|publisher=[[Korean Broadcasting System]]|agency=Yonhap News Agency|date=26 January 2006|language=ko|access-date=2 November 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=피아니스트 이루마 "영국 시민권 포기 … 입대"|url=https://www.joongang.co.kr/article/2126310|work=JoongAng Ilbo|agency=Yonhap News Agency|date=27 January 2006|language=ko|access-date=2 November 2021}}</ref> He enlisted in the [[Republic of Korea Navy]] in July 2006 and was assigned to public relations duties.<ref>{{cite news|title=이루마, 해군 군악대와 송년 공연|url=https://news.kbs.co.kr/news/view.do?ncd=1274843|publisher=Korean Broadcasting System|date=27 December 2006|language=ko|access-date=2 November 2021}}</ref><ref name="ChosunIlbo.May2007">{{cite news|last=Kang |first=Young-soo|title=군복무 이루마,손혜임과 결혼 "딸 하나만 낳고 싶어요"|url=https://www.chosun.com/site/data/html_dir/2007/05/27/2007052700264.html|work=[[The Chosun Ilbo]]|date=27 May 2007|language=ko|access-date=2 November 2021}}</ref> He was discharged in August 2008.<ref>{{cite news|last=Kang |first=Jeong-hoon|title=해군 이루마 제대 연주회|url=https://www.donga.com/news/Peoples/article/all/20080823/8621474/1|work=[[The Dong-a Ilbo]]|date=23 August 2008|language=ko|access-date=2 November 2021}}</ref>

In April 2007, while on leave, Yiruma married his girlfriend Son Hye-im, the older sister of actress and former [[Miss Korea]] contestant [[Son Tae-young]].<ref>{{cite news|title=이루마-손태영 언니 미스코리아 결혼|url=https://www.joongang.co.kr/article/2699327|work=JoongAng Ilbo|agency=[[Newsis]]|date=18 April 2007|language=ko|access-date=2 November 2021}}</ref><ref name="ChosunIlbo.May2007"/> They have a daughter (born in 2008).<ref>{{cite news|last=Kim|first=Jun-seok|title=손혜임 결혼 이루마 "처제 손태영 보다 아내가 더 예뻐"|url=https://www.chosun.com/site/data/html_dir/2013/12/12/2013121200965.html|work=The Chosun Ilbo|date=12 December 2013|language=ko|access-date=2 November 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=Lee |first=Seul-bi|title=이루마, 붕어빵 딸 공개 '깜찍해'|url=https://www.donga.com/news/Entertainment/article/all/20111126/42161015/4|work=The Dong-a Ilbo|date=26 November 2011|language=ko|access-date=2 November 2021}}</ref>
