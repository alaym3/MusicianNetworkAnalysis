"'''Rampampam'''" is a song by Romanian singer Minelli, released for [[Music download|digital download]] and [[Streaming media|streaming]] by [[Global Records]] on 18 March 2021 as a [[Single (music)|single]]. A [[House music|house]] track, it was written by Minelli and produced by Viky Red. Lyrically, it discusses a toxic relationship and the other partner's eventual revengeful feelings. Manuel Probst, writing for the German website Dance Charts, gave "Rampampam" a positive review, praising the song's catchiness and Minelli's vocal delivery.<ref name="dance"/> 

To promote "Rampampam", an accompanying music video was uploaded to the singer's [[YouTube]] channel simultaneously with the single's digital release. Directed by Kibzzon, it portrays Minelli and two other women pouring gasoline on a car which features a man trapped inside. The singer further performed the track on Romanian and Bulgarian radio stations throughout May and June 2021. Upon trending on several [[Shazam (service)|Shazam]] and [[Spotify]] charts worldwide,<ref name="avantaje"/><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.radardemedia.ro/hit-ul-rampampam-domina-radio-airplay-ul-din-rusia-si-topurile-shazam-din-lume/|title=Hit-ul "Rampampam" domină Radio Airplay-ul din Rusia și topurile Shazam din lume|trans-title=The hit 'Rampampam' dominated the Russia radio airplay and the worldwide Shazam charts|language=ro|publisher=Radar de Media|author=Nechifor, Petre|date=3 July 2021|accessdate=24 July 2021|archive-date=24 July 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210724180954/https://www.radardemedia.ro/hit-ul-rampampam-domina-radio-airplay-ul-din-rusia-si-topurile-shazam-din-lume/|url-status=live}}</ref> "Rampampam" topped the official rankings in Belarus, Bulgaria, Czech Republic, Hungary, the [[Commonwealth of Independent States]] (CIS), Lithuania and Russia, and peaked at number two in native Romania, Poland and Ukraine. The song has been certified gold by the [[Polish Society of the Phonographic Industry]] (ZPAV).

"Rampampam", a [[house music|house]] track with [[Brazilian bass|slap house]]-inspired basslines,<ref name="dance">{{cite news|url=https://www.dance-charts.de/2021032714209/musikvideo-minelli-rampampam|title=Musikvideo: 'Minelli - Rampampam'|author=Probst, Manuel|date=27 March 2021|accessdate=24 July 2021|publisher=Dance Charts|language=de|archive-date=24 July 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210724180925/https://www.dance-charts.de/2021032714209/musikvideo-minelli-rampampam|url-status=live}}</ref> was composed by its songwriter Minelli and producer Viky Red.<ref name="infomusic">{{cite news|url=https://www.infomusic.ro/videoclip/minelli-rampampam/|title=Videoclip: Minelli – Rampampam|language=ro|date=18 March 2021|author=Ghimus, Alina|accessdate=24 July 2021|publisher=InfoMusic|archive-date=24 July 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210724180932/https://www.infomusic.ro/videoclip/minelli-rampampam/|url-status=live}}</ref> Minelli came up with its [[Hook (music)|hook]] in around five minutes after Viky Red had presented her an instrumental. Seven of the several remixes created for "Rampampam" are featured on a remix [[extended play|EP]] distributed by [[Spinnin' Records]].<ref name="alternative"/>

==Music video and promotion==
An accompanying music video for "Rampampam" was uploaded to Minelli's [[YouTube]] channel on 18 March 2021.<ref name="video">{{cite news|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XqgYj8atJqo|title=Minelli - Rampampam {{!}} Official Video|publisher=[[YouTube]]|date=18 March 2021|accessdate=24 July 2021|archive-date=24 July 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210724181013/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XqgYj8atJqo|url-status=live}}</ref> It was directed by Kobzzon, while Paul Tatcu was hired as the [[Cinematographer|director of photography]].<ref name="infomusic"/><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.profm.ro/stiri/news/muzica/minelli-lanseaza-o-noua-piesa-de-pus-pe-repeat-rampampam-114462|title=Minelli lansează o nouă piesă de pus pe repeat, Rampampam|author=Cojocaru, Raluca|date=19 March 2021|language=ro|trans-title=Minelli releases a new song to be put on repeat, Rampampam|publisher=[[Pro FM]]|accessdate=24 July 2021|archive-date=21 March 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210321111840/https://www.profm.ro/stiri/news/muzica/minelli-lanseaza-o-noua-piesa-de-pus-pe-repeat-rampampam-114462|url-status=live}}</ref> The clip is centered around the revengeful lyrical content of the song,<ref name="infomusic"/> portraying Minelli and two other women in a dark room, pouring gasoline over a car which features a man locked inside. Interspersed scenes feature the singer surrounded by black balloons and sitting on a red chair watching footage of the aforementioned man on an oversized display.
