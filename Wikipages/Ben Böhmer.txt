'''Ben Böhmer''' is a [[Germany|German]] composer, producer and [[DJ]] from [[Göttingen]], Germany. He primarily produces and performs deep and progressive house music, and is currently signed on to the labels Anjunadeep, Keller Records, Cercle Music, and his own label - Ton Töpferei.

== Career ==

Ben Böhmer played piano and trumpet in his childhood before he began to compose music with the synthesizer.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://translate.googleusercontent.com/translate_c?depth=1&hl=en&prev=search&rurl=translate.google.co.in&sl=de&sp=nmt4&u=https://2017.wildemoehrefestival.de/dj/ben-boehmer/&xid=25657,15700021,15700124,15700149,15700186,15700190,15700201,15700214,15700230&usg=ALkJrhiw4Y5kItKN9_rx092CzOz8tHe11A|title=Wilde Möhre Festival 2017 {{!}} DJ: Ben Böhmer|website=translate.googleusercontent.com|access-date=2018-10-30}}</ref>

At the end of 2015 he produced his first single, Promise You, which appeared on the ''Mauerblümchen'' (Ger., [[Wallflower (people)|wallflower]]) compilation of his own label Ton Töpferei, based in his home town of Göttingen, Germany.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://translate.googleusercontent.com/translate_c?depth=1&hl=en&prev=search&rurl=translate.google.co.in&sl=de&sp=nmt4&u=http://trndmusik.de/2016/10/ben-boehmer-bringt-seine-faszinierende-ep-promise-you-bei-der-ton-toepferei-raus/&xid=25657,15700021,15700124,15700149,15700186,15700190,15700201,15700214,15700230&usg=ALkJrhgdPVv8Tw90WszS0ZK_HlDiFmARWA|title=Ben Böhmer bringt seine faszinierende EP "Promise You" bei der Ton Töpferei raus [German]|website=translate.googleusercontent.com|access-date=2018-10-30}}</ref> On November 1, 2016, the eponymous Promise You EP, also on Ton Töpferei, was released. This was followed by other releases on Keller Records, Ostfunk Records, Bade Records, Audiolith Records, Carton-Pate Records, The Soundgarden Records and Freundchen Records.{{Citation needed|date=October 2018}}

After witnessing some success, Ben's Submission EP was released in 2017 on the label Sacrebleu, headed by German DJ duo AKA AKA, which was founded in the same year.

On 22 September 2017 Böhmer published the single Flug & Fall on the fourth exploration compilation of the London label Anjunadeep. Shortly thereafter in early 2018, another release - Morning Falls EP, followed.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://dancingastronaut.com/2018/01/ben-bohmer-shares-dreamy-brimming-morning-falls-ep/|title=Ben Böhmer shares dreamy, brimming 'Morning Falls' EP - Dancing Astronaut|date=2018-01-07|work=Dancing Astronaut|access-date=2018-10-30|language=en-US}}</ref>

Böhmer has performed at the Distortion Festival 2017 in [[Copenhagen]], at the Habitat Festival, Anjunadeep Open Air in London and at the Tomorrowland Festival in Belgium, in 2018. Ben Böhmer has also performed in Cyprus, the [[Czech Republic]], [[France]], Italy, Spain, [[Greece]] and India.{{citation needed|date=October 2018}} He also performed at Group Therapy 300 in Hong Kong, an annual concert headlined by [[Above & Beyond (group)|Above & Beyond]]. This was his first performance at a Group Therapy event.{{citation needed|date=October 2018}}

In July 2018, he released another 3 track EP - Dive, on the Anjunadeep label.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://edmli.com/2018/07/07/ben-bohmer-releases-ecstatic-3-track-ep-on-anjunadeep-imprint/|title=Ben Böhmer Releases Ecstatic 3 Track EP On Anjunadeep Imprint|date=2018-07-07|work=EDMLI|access-date=2018-10-30|language=en-US}}</ref> In the same year, he collaborated with fellow Anjunadeep artist [[Lane 8]] to release a remix of  his track 'Hold On'.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://thegroovecartel.com/news/lane-8-hold-on-ben-bohmer-remix/|title=Lane 8 hires rising talent Ben Böhmer to remix ‘Hold On’|date=2018-09-24|work=The Groove Cartel|access-date=2018-10-30|language=en-US}}</ref>

In November 2019, he released his debut LP - Breathing, on the label Anjunadeep.
