'''Vanice Palmer''' (born August 5, 1993), known professionally as '''Cookiee Kawaii''', is an American singer. She is best known for her song "Vibe (If I Back It Up)", which became popular on [[TikTok]] in 2020. After the song went viral, she signed to [[Empire Distribution|Empire]], and released her debut studio album, ''Vanice'', in 2021.

==Life and career==
Vanice Palmer<ref name="vogue">{{cite web |last1=Collins |first1=Hattie |title=6 Musicians About to Blow Up in 2021 |url=https://www.vogue.com/article/6-musicians-to-watch-2021 |website=[[Vogue (magazine)|Vogue]] |access-date=October 8, 2021 |language=en |date=December 13, 2020}}</ref> was born on August 5, 1993,<ref>{{cite web |title=Cookie Kawaii |url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/cookiekawaii/profile/ |website=HotNewHipHop |access-date=October 8, 2021 |language=en}}</ref> and raised in [[Irvington, New Jersey]].<ref name="xxlinterview" /><ref name="wonderlandint" /> Her parents are both DJs, who mostly played [[Chicago house]] music.<ref name="intmag">{{cite web |last1=Ukiomogbe |first1=Juliana |title=Cookiee Kawaii Is Listening to Daft Punk, Missy Elliott, and Journey |url=https://www.interviewmagazine.com/music/cookiee-kawaii-is-listening-to-daft-punk-missy-elliott-and-journey |website=[[Interview (magazine)|Interview]] |access-date=October 8, 2021 |language=en |date=October 7, 2020}}</ref> She attended Catholic school, where she performed in choirs.<ref name="guardian" /> At age 12, she began writing poetry and performing her poems over beats and in 2011, she started making her own music. She worked various jobs, including as a [[Stripper|exotic dancer]] and a delivery driver for [[DoorDash]] and [[Uber Eats]].<ref name="xxlinterview" /> While she was homeless and living out of her car, she worked as a live-in caregiver.<ref>{{cite web |title=Cookiee Kawaii: In Short |url=https://www.londoninstereo.com/cookiee-kawaii-in-short/ |website=[[London in Stereo]] |access-date=October 8, 2021 |language=en |date=May 19, 2020}}</ref>

She began making music under the name Harajuku Cookiee, before changing it to Cookiee Kawa, and finally to Cookiee Kawaii, inspired by her love of cookies and anime.<ref>{{cite web |title=Zen, Prose and Pose with Cookiee Kawaii |url=https://www.sdent.net/zen-prose-and-pose-with-cookiee-kawaii/ |website=Soulcial Dreamin' Ent. |access-date=October 8, 2021 |language=en |date=September 26, 2016}}</ref>{{better source needed|date=October 2021}}<ref name="wonderlandint">{{cite web |last1=Morgan |first1=Maybelle |title=Cookiee Kawaii On How "Vibe" Became A TikTok Sensation |url=https://www.wonderlandmagazine.com/2020/05/11/cookiee-kawaii-vibe-tiktok/ |website=[[Wonderland (magazine)|Wonderland]] |access-date=October 8, 2021 |language=en |date=May 11, 2020}}</ref> She released her debut project, ''Zen'', in 2016. In July 2019, she released her debut mixtape ''Club Soda, Vol. 1''. ==Artistry==
Cookiee Kawaii's music has primarily been described as [[Jersey club]].<ref>{{cite web |last1=Sahai |first1=Fred |title=Cookiee Kawaii Wants to Serve You a Giant Glass of 'Club Soda' |url=https://www.papermag.com/cookiee-kawaii-club-soda-vibe-2647394740.html?rebelltitem=20#rebelltitem20 |website=[[Paper (magazine)|Paper]] |access-date=October 8, 2021 |language=en |date=August 28, 2020}}</ref><ref name="wonderlandint" /><ref name="nj.com">{{cite web |last1=Kuperinsky |first1=Amy |title=Cookiee Kawaii is leading Jersey Club music’s viral TikTok domination. It’s a 'Vibe.' |url=https://www.nj.com/entertainment/2020/08/cookiee-kawaii-is-leading-jersey-club-musics-viral-tiktok-domination-its-a-vibe.html |website=[[NJ.com]] |access-date=October 8, 2021 |language=en |date=August 15, 2020}}</ref> Early in her career, she made mostly [[Contemporary R&B|R&B]] music, but began making Jersey club music per a suggestion from DJ Jayhood, with whom she made her first Jersey club song.<ref name="xxlinterview">{{cite web |last1=III |first1=Robby Seabrook |title=The Break Presents: Cookiee Kawaii |url=https://www.xxlmag.com/cookiee-kawaii-interview-the-break/ |website=[[XXL (magazine)|XXL]] |access-date=October 8, 2021 |language=en |date=September 16, 2020}}</ref><ref name="nj.com" /> Kadish Morris described her music as feeling "fluorescent, high-energy and made for partying".<ref name="guardian">{{cite web |last1=Morris |first1=Kadish |title=One to watch: Cookiee Kawaii |url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2020/oct/10/one-to-watch-cookiee-kawaii |website=[[The Guardian]] |access-date=October 8, 2021 |language=en |date=October 10, 2020}}</ref> She has cited [[Missy Elliott]] as an influence.<ref name="nj.com" />

==Personal life==
Cookiee Kawaii is [[pansexual]].<ref name="flaunt" /> Before signing with Empire, she was studying business and marketing at [[Essex County College]]. As of 2020, she lives in [[East Orange, New Jersey]].<ref name="nj.com" />
