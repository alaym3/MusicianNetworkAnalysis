'''I'm Glad It's You''' is an American rock band from [[Redlands, California]].

==History==
The group's debut album, ''The Things I Never Say'', was released in 2016.<ref>[https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/im-glad-its-you-every-sun-every-moon/ Review], [[Pitchfork Media]]</ref> While touring in July 2017, the band's touring van overturned while driving near [[Barstow, California]], which resulted in the death of their videographer, Chris Davis.<ref>[https://www.stereogum.com/2079438/im-glad-its-you-interview/music/ Grief, Growth, & Britpop: I’m Glad It’s You Talk Every Sun, Every Moon]. [[Stereogum]], April 7, 2020.</ref> This event informed the songwriting of their next album, ''Every Sun, Every Moon'', which was produced by [[J. Robbins]] and released on [[6131 Records]] in May 2020.<ref>[https://exclaim.ca/music/article/im_glad_its_you_every_sun_every_moon_album_review Review], [[Exclaim!]]</ref> ''[[Slant (magazine)|Slant Magazine]]'' named the album one of its top 50 releases of 2020.<ref>[https://www.slantmagazine.com/features/the-50-best-albums-of-2020/3/ The 50 Best Albums of 2020]. ''[[Slant (magazine)|Slant]], December 9, 2020.</ref>
