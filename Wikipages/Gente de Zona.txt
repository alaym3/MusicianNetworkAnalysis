'''Gente de Zona''' is a [[Cuban music|Cuban]] [[reggaeton]] duo made up of musicians '''Alexander Delgado (Hernandez)''' and '''Randy Malcom'''. In 2014, the duo gained success with the collaboration of Mario Proenza, who helped them record with Enrique Iglesias the song "[[Bailando (Enrique Iglesias song)|Bailando]]". This hit gave the duo numerous prestigious awards like the Latin Grammy and Latin ''Billboard'' Awards.
