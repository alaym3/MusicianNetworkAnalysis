'''Young Oceans''' is a Christian [[art rock|art-rock]] recording project from [[Brooklyn]], [[New York City|New York]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.relevantmagazine.com/culture/music/relevants-top-10-albums-2012 |title=RELEVANT's Top 10 Albums of 2012 |publisher=Relevantmagazine.com |access-date=October 14, 2014}}</ref>  The project originated as a collective of artists affiliated with Trinity Grace Church in greater New York City recording songs by songwriter Eric Marshall. Songs originally written for church gatherings came were recorded by members of the band and their producer Mike Beck.
