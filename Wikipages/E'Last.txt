'''E'Last''' ([[Korean language|Korean]]: 엘라스트, pronounced "El-Last" and commonly stylized as E'LAST), previously known as '''EBoyz''' (이보이즈), is an eight-member [[Musical ensemble|group]] from South Korea. Formed by E Entertainment, they debuted on June 9, 2020 with their first mini album ''Day Dream'' and the single "Swear" (Korean : 기사의 맹세, literally "Knight's Oath").<ref>{{Cite web|last=한국경제티브이|date=2020-06-08|title=엘라스트, 8일 데뷔 하루 앞서 팬 쇼케이스 개최|url=http://www.wowtv.co.kr/NewsCenter/News/Read?articleId=A202006080046|access-date=2020-06-16|website=www.wowtv.co.kr|language=ko|archive-date=2020-06-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200616003511/http://www.wowtv.co.kr/NewsCenter/News/Read?articleId=A202006080046|url-status=live}}</ref>

== History ==
E'Last is the first group of E Entertainment. Their name is an abbreviation of "Everlasting", which either means "Eternity" and "Infinity.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|date=2020-05-19|title=E'LAST Reveals Debut Date and Logo Through Intriguing Video|url=https://www.hellokpop.com/kpop/elast-debut-date-logo/|access-date=2020-06-15|website=hellokpop|language=en-US|archive-date=2020-06-04|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200604013233/https://www.hellokpop.com/kpop/elast-debut-date-logo/|url-status=live}}</ref><ref name=":2">{{Cite web|last=Kim|first=Soo-jeong|date=June 9, 2020|title='오늘 데뷔' 엘라스트, 스스로 '뜰 수 있겠다'고 생각한 까닭|url=https://www.nocutnews.co.kr/news/5358156|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200616003858/https://www.nocutnews.co.kr/news/5358156|archive-date=June 16, 2020|access-date=June 16, 2020|website=Nocutnews}}</ref> Won Hyuk and Wonjun were contestants on ''[[Produce X 101]]''<ref>{{Cite web|title=이엔터테인먼트 on Instagram: "#Repost @produce_x_101 (@get_repost) ・・・ 원혁(WON HYUK) l E엔터 . 연습생을 소개합니다! . @ 1분 PR 영상 확인하기 / View the trainees' 1 Minute PR…"|url=https://www.instagram.com/p/BvaoKBQlTg3/|access-date=2020-06-15|website=Instagram|language=en}}</ref><ref name=":2" /> and were the first two to be introduced into the EBoyz group. They were joined during summer 2019 by Choi In, Rano, and Seungyeop.

The members have been releasing their own reality show, named ''Unlock'',<ref>{{Cite web|title=YouTube|url=https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLoszt8ta2_T8cLRBxkjkGJJYxSzuBQguL|access-date=2020-06-29|website=www.youtube.com|archive-date=2020-09-01|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200901022716/https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLoszt8ta2_T8cLRBxkjkGJJYxSzuBQguL|url-status=live}}</ref> prior to their debut. The official YouTube channel of the group uploaded an episode every Friday, showcasing the boys’ daily lives and debut preparations.<ref name=":1" />

The group debuted on June 9, 2020 with their first extended play ''Day Dream'', featuring the title track "Swear".  In their debut album, the members said in the interview that Rano, Baekgyeul, Won Hyuk, and Wonjun participated in writing and composing songs.<ref name=":2" />

The group returned with their second extended play ''Awake'' and its title track "Tears Of Chaos" on November 11, 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/382/0000863671|title=엘라스트, 11월 11일 컴백…커밍순 이미지 공개|website=n.news.naver.com|language=ko|access-date=2020-10-21|archive-date=2020-10-26|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201026104431/https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/382/0000863671|url-status=live}}</ref> Wonjun was not involved in the comeback due to schedule clashes, as he was the host of EBS show Boni Hani.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=079&aid=0003422716|title=엘라스트, '보니하니 MC' 원준 빠진 7인 체제로 활동|website=No Cut News via Naver|language=ko|access-date=2020-10-23|archive-date=2020-10-27|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201027004324/https://entertain.naver.com/now/read?oid=079&aid=0003422716|url-status=live}}</ref>

On April 2021, it was announced that members Choi In, Seungyeop, Romin, and Wonjun would form the new sub-unit E'Last U.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://n.news.naver.com/entertain/article/108/0002950677|title=엘라스트 최인·승엽·로민·원준, 데뷔 첫 유닛 결성[공식]|website=n.news.naver.com|language=ko|access-date=2020-04-28}}</ref>  The sub-unit released their debut digital single "Remember" on May 19, 2021.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://star.mt.co.kr/stview.php?no=2021050409221511669|title=엘라스트 U, 19일 싱글 'Remember' 발매…커밍순 이미지 공개 [공식]|website=Star News|language=ko|access-date=2021-05-04|url-status=live}}</ref>

On September 29, 2021, the group released their first single album ''Dark Dream'' and its title track of the same name.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.nocutnews.co.kr/news/5627155|title=엘라스트, 데뷔 후 첫 싱글 '다크 드림'으로 29일 컴백|website=No Cut News|language=ko|access-date=2021-09-17|url-status=live}}</ref>
