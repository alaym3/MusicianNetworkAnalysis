'''A1 x J1''' (real names '''Phineas Waweru''' and '''Joshua Somerkun''')  are a British rap duo. They released their debut single "[[Latest Trends]]" in February 2021, which turned out to be a huge hit, charting at number 2 in the UK and earning remixes with [[Aitch (rapper)|Aitch]], and [[A Boogie wit da Hoodie]] respectively.  They then released their second single "Baby", which features British singer and rapper Deno. They were also featured on the deluxe edition of [[KSI]]'s album ''[[All Over the Place (KSI album)|All Over the Place]]'', and on the debut single by British rapper SwitchOTR, as well as recording a song for the Plugged In series by producer Fumez The Engineer. Their ages are 16 and 17.
