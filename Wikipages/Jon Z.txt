'''Jonathan Resto Quiñones''' (born May 20, 1991), better known by his stage name '''Jon Z''', is a Puerto Rican rapper, singer and record producer.<ref>{{Cite web|last=ReggaetonColombia.Net|date=2018-02-11|title=Biografía de Jon Z ReggaetonColombia.Net Biografias|url=https://reggaetoncolombia.net/biografia-de-jon-z/|access-date=2020-06-23|website=ReggaetonColombia.Net|language=es}}</ref>

He was nominated for "Song of the Year" Urbano / Trap in [[Lo Nuestro Awards|Premios Lo Nuestro]] 2020 and in two categories in the Premios Tu Música Urbana.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Editorial|title=JON Z RECIBE SU PRIMERA NOMINACION A PREMIO LO NUESTRO {{!}} Fusion4Media|url=https://fusion4media.com/jon-z-recibe-su-primera-nominacion-a-premio-lo-nuestro/|access-date=2020-06-23|language=es}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Feijoo|first=Bolivar|title=JON Z nominado a 'Premios Tú Música Urbano' {{!}} Wow La Revista|url=http://wowlarevista.com/jon-z-nominado-a-premios-tu-musica-urbano/|access-date=2020-06-23|website=wowlarevista.com|language=en-US}}</ref>

Jon Z has collaborated with high-profile artists such as [[Enrique Iglesias]], [[Yellow Claw (DJs)|Yellow Claw]], [[Farruko]], [[Almighty (rapper)|Almighty]], [[Anuel AA]], [[Arcángel]], [[Ñengo Flow]], [[Baby Rasta & Gringo|Baby Rasta]],<ref>{{Cite magazine|date=2019-08-08|title=Baby Rasta & Jon Z Interview: Exclusive|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/latin/8526748/baby-rasta-jon-z-interview|access-date=2020-06-23|magazine=Billboard}}</ref> [[Wisin]], [[Don Chezina]], among others,<ref>{{Cite web|title=Jon Z – DeepDelay Management|url=https://www.deepdelaymanagement.com/artistas/jon-z/|access-date=2020-06-23|website=www.deepdelaymanagement.com|language=es}}</ref> who have been in high positions on the ''Billboard'' list and have received RIAA certifications.<ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=|title=Gold & Platinum {{!}} Jon Z {{!}} RIAA|url=https://www.riaa.com/gold-platinum/?tab_active=default-award&se=jon+z#search_section|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=|website=RIAA}}</ref>

==Career==
His career began in 2011 when he was part of the duo J-Tonez and Jon Z for almost 2 years, with which he decided to release his first single entitled «Nunca te veo» and some other singles that he later released as «El dinero me llama», «Háblame de dinero», among others.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=irUP156ga-Y|title=J-Tonez & Jon-Z Feat. Yani - El Dinero Me Llama|via=www.youtube.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DoBS68TLDow|title=J-Tonez & Jon-Z Ft Randy Glock--Hablame De Dinero(Prod.Ivan Lee)(Millones Records)|via=www.youtube.com}}</ref> In 2013 he started uploading [[Freestyle rap|freestyle]] videos on [[YouTube]].
Jon Z is cousins with [[Ele A El Dominio]], with whom he made the ''Super Saiyian Flow'' mixtape.<ref name="auto">{{Cite web|url=https://www.lacuarta.com/espectaculos/noticia/ele-a-el-dominio-villano-genero-urbano-mejor-ano/321185/|title="Ele A El Dominio Nigga": "Villano" del género urbano repasa su gran año|date=December 25, 2018|website=La Cuarta}}</ref><ref name="auto1">{{Cite web|url=https://remezcla.com/releases/music/ozuna-balenciaga-video/|title=Ozuna Teams Up With Ele a El Dominio For Jeva-Filled "Balenciaga" Video|date=April 3, 2018}}</ref>

His debut album, ''JonTrapVolta'', was released in 2017.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Staff|first=Rapetón|date=2017-12-05|title=Jon Z debuta #1 en Billboard con el EP "JonTrapVolta"|url=http://www.rapeton.com/jon-z-debuta-1-en-billboard-con-el-ep-jontrapvolta/|access-date=2020-06-23|website=Rapetón|language=en-US}}</ref>
