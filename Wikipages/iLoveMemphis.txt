'''Richard Maurice Colbert''' (born March 29, 1993), better known by his stage name '''iLoveMemphis''', or '''iHeartMemphis''', is an American rapper from [[Memphis, Tennessee]]. He is best known for his debut single "[[Hit the Quan]]" which peaked at number 15 on the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]].

==Career==

===2015: Breakthrough===
iLoveMemphis' debut single "[[Hit the Quan]]" was produced by Buck Nasty and released on July 4, 2015. Hit the Quan is a dance that was originally performed by [[Rich Homie Quan]] in his video for the song "[[Flex (Ooh, Ooh, Ooh)]]" which was released in April 2015. The dance Rich Homie Quan did in the video soon became a hit, with numerous Vines being produced portraying the dance. At that time, most people referred to the dance routine as simply the "Rich Homie Quan" dance. In July 2015, three months following the release of the song, iLoveMemphis released a song titled "Hit The Quan" which then became popular. He said that he spent $35 on making the song. The video for "Hit the Quan" debuted worldwide on November 12, 2015.<ref>{{Citation|last=iLoveMemphisVEVO|title=iLoveMemphis - Hit the Quan (Official Video)|date=2015-11-12|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JrCUEjSzLAk|accessdate=2016-03-04}}</ref> On December 18, 2015, iLoveMemphis' second single "Lean and Dabb" was released on iTunes.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/lean-and-dabb-single/id1068291031?app=itunes|title=Lean and Dabb - Single by iLoveMemphis on iTunes|website=iTunes|access-date=2016-03-04}}</ref>

===2016–present: Further ventures===
iLoveMemphis is currently working with an rising artist named Dkotic whose real name is Arthur Moore. He was founded by iLoveMemphis in the summer of 2017, and released a song with iLoveMemphis called "Lil Dance" which kicked off summer of 2017 on Instagram and Musical.ly. The song Lil Dance performed by Dkotic accumulated over 1 million streams.<ref>{{Citation|last=Dkotic |title= Dkotic and Iheartmemphis – Lil Dance|date=2017-09-20|url= https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IVFT1Il9NWg|accessdate=2017-06-14}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last=Beautiful People |title= Lil Dance Challenge Dance Compilation - Bjayx Quax Tay Lil Dance Challenge|date=2017-09-20|url= https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cAmFhJaRuWw |accessdate=2017-06-14}}</ref>

=== Television appearances ===
In November 2015, iLoveMemphis made two major television appearances. The first was on [[The Ellen DeGeneres Show]], and the second appearance was on the talk show [[The Real (TV series)|The Real]].<ref>{{Citation|last=TheEllenShow|title=iLoveMemphis Performs 'Hit the Quan'|date=2015-11-02|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GT9cjGAWzhg|accessdate=2016-03-04}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last=The Real Daytime|title=ILoveMemphis Hits the Quan|date=2015-11-03|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3DQGta9iPJk|accessdate=2016-03-04}}</ref>
In 2017, iLoveMemphis was featured on rapper Teflon Don‘s single "Stop & Go".

==Legal issues==
In July 2015, Colbert was criminally charged for allegedly participating in what Memphis Police call a "gang rape" of a woman who was under the influence at the time. Colbert, along with two other individuals, face charges that include aggravated rape.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://wreg.com/2015/07/21/hit-the-quan-rapper-charged-in-memphis-gang-rape/|title="Hit The Quan" rapper charged in Memphis gang rape|website=WREG.com|date=22 July 2015|access-date=2016-03-04}}</ref> Colbert denies the allegations against him. He pled not guilty, and the charges against him were eventually dropped based on DNA evidence.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.fox13memphis.com/top-stories/iheartmemphis-cleared-of-rape-charges/511665337|title=IHeartMemphis cleared of rape charges}}</ref>
