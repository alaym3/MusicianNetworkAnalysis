'''''The Beauty of Independence''''' is the first [[extended play]] (EP) by American hip hop group [[G-Unit]]. It was released for [[Music download|digital download]] on August 25, 2014 and includes six previously unreleased songs.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/6229240/g-unit-quietly-drops-six-track-the-beauty-of-independence |title=G-Unit Quietly Drops Six-Track 'The Beauty of Independence' |publisher=Billboard |date= |accessdate=2015-03-03}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=michael.trampe |url=http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/interviews/id.2541/title.many-men-a-q-a-with-producers-of-g-unit-s-the-beauty-of-independence- |title=Many Men: A Q&A With Producers Of G-Unit's "The Beauty Of Independence" &#124; Rappers Talk Hip Hop Beef & Old School Hip Hop |publisher=HipHopDX |date=2014-08-28 |accessdate=2015-03-03}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hotnewhiphop.com/g-unit-releases-a-surprise-new-ep-the-beauty-of-independence-news.11790.html |title=G-Unit Releases A Surprise New EP, "The Beauty Of Independence" |publisher=Hotnewhiphop.com |date=2014-08-24 |accessdate=2015-03-03}}</ref>

==Commercial performance==
The album debuted at number 17 on the [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]] chart, with first-week sales of 14,472 copies in the United States.<ref>{{cite web|last=Tardio |first=Andres |url=http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/news/id.30483/title.hip-hop-album-sales-g-unit-beyonce-wiz-khalifa |title=Hip Hop Album Sales: G-Unit, Beyonce, Wiz Khalifa |publisher=HipHop DX |date=2014-09-03 |accessdate=2014-09-03}}</ref>
