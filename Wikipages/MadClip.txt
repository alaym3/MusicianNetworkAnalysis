'''Mad Clip''' (born '''Panagiotis Peter Anastasopoulos''', {{Lang-el|Παναγιώτης Πίτερ Αναστασόπουλος}}; 25 May 1987 – 2 September 2021) was a [[Greek Americans|Greek American]] rapper, signed to the Capital Music label. He released many songs such as "Dealer","Kotera", "Sinthikes", which have grossed more than ten million pageviews in [[YouTube]]. He released four albums: ''Super Trapper'', ''O Amerikanos LP'', ''Super Trapper 2'' and ''Still Active'' featuring Strat. From the beginning of his music career he kept the artistic name Mad Clip which comes from the initials "Money and Drugs, Can't Live in Poverty".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.news247.gr/politismos/mousiki/mad-clip-ti-simainei-to-onoma-toy.9341298.html|title=Mad Clip: Τι σημαίνει το όνομά του|website=www.news247.gr|language=el|accessdate=2021-09-02}}</ref>

Rapper [[Taki Tsan]] helped MadClip to release the album ''Eimai Trelos'' at the age of 27. Shortly after he signed with Capital Music. He had been a member of Outcast Cast Excellence since 2004 with Constantine the G and N.O.E., where he performed in English.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.protothema.gr/greece/article/1157177/mad-clip-poios-itan-o-traper-pou-skotothike-se-trohaio-apo-ti-nea-uorki-sti-mousiki-kataxiosi-stin-ellada/|title=Mad Clip: Ποιος ήταν ο τράπερ που σκοτώθηκε σε τροχαίο - Από τη Νέα Υόρκη στη μουσική καταξίωση στην Ελλάδα|date=2021-09-02|website=ProtoThema|language=el|accessdate=2021-09-02}}</ref>

He had collaborated with [[Eleni Foureira]], [[Josephine (singer)|Josephine]], and other well-known artists in the field of rap and trap.

Mad Clip died at the age of 34 on 2 September 2021, after a car accident in [[Vouliagmeni]].<ref>{{cite web|date=2 September 2021|title=Mad Clip: Νεκρός ο διάσημος τράπερ – Σοκάρουν οι εικόνες από το σημείο του τροχαίου|url=https://www.enikos.gr/society/mad-clip-nekros-o-diasimos-traper/1664315/|publisher=enikos.gr|accessdate=2 September 2021}}</ref>

== Biography ==

He was born in [[New York City|New York]] on May 25, 1987. He came from a [[Greek American]] family, with his mother being from an immigrant family that has settled in the United States from the 1950s. From 1999 onwards, he divided his life between in Greece and America for many years, living in [[Paleo Faliro]], the place of birth of his father.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.in.gr/2021/09/02/greece/mad-clip-pos-egine-tragiko-dystyxima-etrexe-iliggiodi-taxytita/|title=Πώς έγινε το τραγικό δυστύχημα με τον Mad Clip: Πληροφορίες ότι έτρεχε με ιλιγγιώδη ταχύτητα - Άμορφη μάζα το αυτοκίνητό του|date=2021-09-02|website=in.gr|language=el|accessdate=2021-09-02}}</ref> He had lived in Greece permanently from 2016 until his death, where he became famous, despite his ambition to succeed abroad.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://newpost.gr/lifestyle/61306776336a289b4bf34b6d/mad-clip-ti-elege-gia-tin-foyreira-kai-gia-ton-koronoio|title=Mad Clip: Τι έλεγε για την Φουρέιρα και για τον κορωνοϊό|website=Newpost.gr|language=el|accessdate=2021-09-02}}</ref> Mad Clip expressed his preference for luxury living with expensive cars and clothes, both privately and in his songs. He was one of the most popular singers of recent years, with many of his songs high on the charts. It has been reported that due to his work, Mad Clip has significantly contributed to the rise in popularity of [[trap music]] in Greece.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.skai.gr/news/greece/mad-clip-i-endoksi-kariera-kai-i-agapi-tou-gia-tin-polyteli-zoi|title=Mad clip: Η ένδοξη καριέρα και η αγάπη του για την πολυτελή ζωή {{!}} ΣΚΑΪ|website=www.skai.gr|language=el|accessdate=2021-09-02}}</ref> From 2017, he had been in a relationship with Areti, for whom he had written a song that bears her name, she was also a source of inspiration for him. The couple had broken up, a couple of months prior to his death.

He had won eight times platinum certification for his records in Greece, equivalent to 160,000 copies. He appeared first five times in the local charts compiled by [[IFPI Greece]].
