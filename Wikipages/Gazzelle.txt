'''Flavio Bruno Pardini''' (born 7 December 1989), known professionally as '''Gazzelle''', is an Italian singer-songwriter.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.indielife.it/2019/03/18/i-10-artisti-indie-che-devi-assolutamente-conoscere/|publisher=Indielife.it|language=Italian|title=I 10 artisti Indie che devi assolutamente conoscere|date=18 March 2019|author=Fabio Tranchina}}</ref> His stage name was chosen without any connection with Pardini's real personality, mainly because of the word's sound, and after [[Adidas]] Gazelle sneakers.<ref name="XL">{{cite web|url=https://xl.repubblica.it/articoli/gazzelle-vi-presento-il-mio-sexy-pop/55581/|work=Xl. [[la Repubblica]]|language=Italian|title=Gazzelle: vi presento il mio sexy pop|author=Giulia Zanichelli|date=2 May 2017|access-date=5 October 2020}}</ref>

His debut single, "Quella te", was released by Maciste Dischi in late 2016. At the time, he refused to appear and to reveal any detail about himself.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.it/musica/news-musica/quella-te-il-nuovo-video-del-misterioso-gazzelle/342662/|work=[[Rolling Stone (magazine)|Rolling Stone]]|language=Italian|access-date=5 October 2020|date=8 December 2016|title=Quella Te, il nuovo video del misterioso Gazzelle}}</ref> He later explained he wanted to preserve his privacy and to be one step back from his songs, in order to let people focus on his music.<ref name="XL"/> His real identity and image were released in March 2017, when he released his debut album, ''Superbattito'', and embarked on his first concert tour.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nonsensemag.it/no-interview-gazzelle-superbattito-un-disco-about-love/|work=Non Sense Mag|author=Egle Taccia|access-date=5 October 2020|language=Italian|title=No Interview – Gazzelle: Superbattito è un disco about love}}</ref>
One year later, the album was re-released under the title ''Megasuperbattito'', also featuring the singles "Sayonara", "Stelle filanti" and "Martelli".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rockol.it/news-686474/gazzelle-edizione-deluxe-superbattito-megasuperbattito-esce-il-2-marzo|publisher=Rockol.it|language=Italian|title=Gazzelle: l'edizione deluxe Megasuperbattito esce il 2 marzo – Copertina / Tracklist|date=24 February 2018}}</ref>

His second studio album, ''Punk'' (2018), was preceded by the singles "Tutta la vita", "Sopra" and "Scintille".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rockol.it/news-697661/gazzelle-nuovo-singolo-scintille-ascolta?refresh_ce|publisher=Rockol.it|language=Italian|access-date=5 October 2020|title=Gazzelle, è uscito il nuovo singolo Scintille}}</ref> In March 2018, he performed for the first time in indoor sports arenas [[Mediolanum Forum]] in Milan and [[PalaLottomatica]] in Rome.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.it/musica/interviste-musica/gazzelle-vuole-diventare-un-classico/482892/|work=[[Rolling Stone (magazine)|Rolling Stone]]|language=Italian|access-date=5 October 2020|title=Gazzelle vuole diventare un classico|author=Raffaella Oliva|date=25 October 2019}}</ref> He also took part in the 2019 International Worker's Day concert in Piazza San Giovanni Laterano, [[Rome]], annually supported by Italian trade unions [[Italian General Confederation of Labour|CGIL]], [[Italian Confederation of Workers' Trade Unions|CISL]] and [[Italian Labour Union|UIL]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://tg24.sky.it/spettacolo/musica/2019/04/19/concerto-primo-maggio-2019-cantanti|publisher=[[Sky Italia]]|language=Italian|access-date=5 October 2020|title=Concerto Primo Maggio 2019, a Roma Anastasio, Gazzelle e Zen Circus tra i cantanti|date=19 April 2019}}</ref>
A new edition of the album, ''Post Punk'', was released in late 2019. It also included the singles "Polynesia", "Settembre" and "Una canzone che non so".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.gqitalia.it/show/article/gazzelle-post-punk-album-tour-testo-canzoni|publisher=[[GQ]]|language=Italian|access-date=5 October 2020|title=Gazzelle e il superbattito "Post Punk" di Flavio Pardini|author=Giuditta Avellina|date=13 November 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.allmusicitalia.it/news/gazzelle-post-punk-album.html|publisher=AllMusicItalia.it|language=Italian|title=Gazzelle: Fuori il 25 Ottobre Post Punk, repack in diverse versioni|author=Simone Zani|date=9 October 2019}}</ref>
In order to promote the album, he embarked on an indoor tour in January and February 2020.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://tg24.sky.it/spettacolo/musica/2020/01/14/gazzelle-concerti-tour-2020|publisher=[[Sky Italia]]|language=Italian|title=Gazzelle parte da Firenze con il "Post Punk tour": tutti i concerti|date=14 January 2020|access-date=5 October 2020}}</ref>
