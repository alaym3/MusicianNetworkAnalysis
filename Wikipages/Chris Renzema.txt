'''Chris Renzema''' is a singer/songwriter and performer based out of [[Nashville, Tennessee]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.ethoschurch.org/chrisrenzema/|title=Chris Renzema {{!}} Ethos Church|website=www.ethoschurch.org|access-date=25 March 2019}}</ref> Chris grew up in [[Grand Rapids]], [[Michigan]], where he learned to play guitar, write songs, and performed at local churches. Renzema blends aspects of indie rock, CCM, and folk amongst other musical influences.

His self-produced indie albums have been played in churches across the country and made a strong impact on digital listeners.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/chrisrenzema/photos/a.889568344484578/1420886414686099/?type=3|title=Chris Renzema|website=facebook.com|access-date=25 March 2019}}{{Primary source inline|date=July 2019}}</ref> His early work included ''Age To Age - EP'', 2014 (independent) (as Chris Renzema & Moriah Hazeltine) and a single, ''Son Of God - Single'', 2017 (independent).<ref>{{Cite web|title=Chris Renzema Discography, Chris Renzema Artist Database {{!}} Jesusfreakhideout.com|url=https://www.jesusfreakhideout.com/artists/ChrisRenzema.asp|access-date=2021-01-01|website=www.jesusfreakhideout.com}}</ref> His second album was released independently in 2018 - ''I'll Be The Branches.'' In December 2018, Renzema announced signing with ''[[Centricity Music]].''
