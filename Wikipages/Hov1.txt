'''Hov1''' (pronounced Hovet) is a Swedish hip hop group formed in 2015<ref>[http://www.kuriren.nu/noje/luleaspelningen-gav-hov1-en-succstart-8599016.aspx ''Norbottens Kuriren'': Luleåspelningen gav Hov1 en succéstart] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170424041117/http://www.kuriren.nu/noje/luleaspelningen-gav-hov1-en-succstart-8599016.aspx |date=2017-04-24 }}
 {{in lang|sv}}</ref> that is signed to [[EMI|EMI Sweden]] / [[Universal Music Group|Universal Music]] Sweden.<ref>[http://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/musik/rockbjornen/article23264246.ab ''Aftonbladet'': Hov1: ”Vi är riktigt jävla taggade för Rockbjörnen”] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170505085114/http://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/musik/rockbjornen/article23264246.ab |date=2017-05-05 }} {{in lang|sv}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Labels|url=https://universalmusic.se/labels/|access-date=2021-01-05|website=Universal Music Sweden|language=sv-SE}}</ref> The group has released three studio albums; ''[[Hov1 (album)|Hov1]]'', ''[[Gudarna på Västerbron]]'', ''[[Vindar på Mars]]'' and one mixtape ''[[Montague (album)|Montague]]''. They all charted atop the Swedish Albums Chart. The group have had 26 top 10 hits on the Swedish Singles Chart, with 12 of them reaching the top spot.

== History ==

=== 2015–2018: First releases and projects ===
Made up of Ludwig Kronstrand, Noel Flike, Dante Lindhe and Axel Liljefors Jansson,<ref>[http://gaffa.se/artikel/112246/intervju-hov1-om-haengivna-fans-och-ett-framtida-stjaernsamarbete/ Gaffa.se: Intervju - Hov1 om hängivna fans och ett framtida stjärnsamarbete] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170428194148/http://gaffa.se/artikel/112246/intervju-hov1-om-haengivna-fans-och-ett-framtida-stjaernsamarbete |date=2017-04-28 }} {{in lang|sv}}</ref> Hov1's debut hit single was "Hur kan du säga saker", which was released in 2015. They released their self-titled debut album ''Hov1'' in April 2017, promoting it with the "Abbey Road Tour". The follow-up album was ''Gudarna på Västerbron'', released in April 2018. Both albums have topped the Swedish Albums Chart.

== Awards ==
Hov1 has received several awards during its career. The group received three [[Rockbjörnen]] awards in 2018, as well as in 2019. The awards were, for instance, "Live-group of the year", "Best fans of the year" and "Concert of the year". The group also received three awards in the "P3 Guld gala" in 2019; "Group of the year", "Guldmicken" and "Best song of the year", the latter with the song "Hon dansar vidare i livet".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/a/qnBwOo/rockbjornen-2018-har-ar-alla-vinnare-fran-musikfesten|title=Rockbjörnen 2018: Här är alla vinnare från musikfesten|last=Pettersson|first=Leo|last2=Albinsson|first2=Mathilde|date=15 August 2018|website=Aftonbladet|access-date=22 April 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/a/OnK1L1/hov1-stora-vinnare-i-p3-guld|title=Hov1 stora vinnare i ”P3 guld”|last=Hartelius|first=Alex|date=19 January 2019|website=Aftonbladet|access-date=22 April 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.aftonbladet.se/nojesbladet/musik/rockbjornen/a/50O6RK/hov1-kungar-over-rockbjornen|title=Hov1 kungar över Rockbjörnen|last=Hartelius|first=Alex|date=13 August 2019|website=Aftonbladet|access-date=22 April 2020}}</ref>

==Members==
{{Expand section|mote details|date=March 2021}} 
===Dante Lindhe===
'''Dante Sebastian Lindhe''', born in [[Gothenburg]], is a Swedish musician and model. His family moved to Stockholm when he was 3 years old. Besides membership in Hov1, Lindhe collaborated with [[Yasin (rapper)|Yasin]] on the 2020 track "Canada Goose". Since 2019, Lindhe has modelled for Select Model Stockholm.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Dante Lindhe {{!}} Select Stockholm|url=https://selectmodel.com/stockholm/model/dante-lindhe|access-date=2021-01-28|website=Select Model Management|language=en}}</ref>

===Ludwig Kronstrand===
He is born on 29 June 1997. He previously dated singer [[Zara Larsson]], a high school friend. She wrote the song "Never Forget You" about him. The two parted when Larsson moved to the States in 2014.

===Axel Liljefors Jansson===
He is born on 29 August 1997. He uses the name "Jiggy" in the band. He is the band's producer.

===Noel Flike===
Rapper Noel Filke is born in Stockholm on 13 May 1997. Besides his membership in Hov1, he has worked with singer [[Zara Larsson]] on her [[podcast]]s in 2015 and 2016.
