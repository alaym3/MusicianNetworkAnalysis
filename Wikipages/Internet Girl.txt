==Early life==
McFadden was born in [[Winnipeg]], [[Manitoba]] on August 2, 1995.<ref>{{cite news |date=2015-12-06 |title=Meet the women at the forefront of Winnipeg's fashion scene |url=https://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/manitoba/meet-the-women-at-the-forefront-of-winnipeg-s-fashion-scene-1.3353093 |work=CBC |access-date=2020-10-27 |archive-date=2018-08-19 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180819195557/http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/manitoba/meet-the-women-at-the-forefront-of-winnipeg-s-fashion-scene-1.3353093 |url-status=live }}</ref> She attended [[Lawrence Park Collegiate Institute]] in [[Toronto]], and studied communication and film at [[Concordia University]] in [[Montreal]], before dropping out to move to [[Los Angeles]] to become a [[Depop]] seller.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.thestar.com/life/2020/10/01/a-love-of-vintage-clothing-led-a-toronto-goth-loving-fashionista-to-online-fame-and-now-shes-launching-her-own-website.html|title=A love of vintage clothing led a Toronto goth-loving fashionista to online fame.
