'''Raisa Andriana''', better known by her [[:wikt:mononym|mononym]] '''Raisa''' (born in Jakarta on 6 June 1990), is an Indonesian [[singer]] and [[songwriter]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thejakartapost.com/news/2011/11/13/raisa-andriana-a-new-crooner.html|title=Raisa Andriana: A new crooner|publisher=[[The Jakarta Post]]|first=Indah|last=Setiawati|date=13 November 2011|access-date=31 May 2018}}</ref> She became publicly known for her song titled "Serba Salah".<ref name="BBC"/> Prior to her solo career, she was one of the lead vocalist of Kevin Aprilio's band, Andante, which later renamed as Vierra (now Vierratale).<ref>{{cite web|url=https://musik.kapanlagi.com/berita/raisa-pernah-jadi-vokalis-vierra-benarkah-44e6c6.html|title=Raisa Pernah Jadi Vokalis Vierra, Benarkah?|trans-title=Raisa was a vocalist of Vierra, is it true?|publisher=KapanLagi.com|first=Nizar|last=Zulmi|language=id|date=18 July 2014|access-date=31 May 2018}}</ref>

Raisa has received a number of accolades, including [[Anugerah Musik Indonesia|Indonesian Music Awards]], [[Anugerah Planet Muzik|Music Planet Awards]], and [[Mnet Asian Music Awards]]. In 2014, she was charted at 45th place on [[Uncharted (chart)|''Billboard'' Uncharted]],<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/raisa/chart-history/uncharted/song/811235|title=Raisa {{!}} Uncharted Chart History|website=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|date=12 April 2014|access-date=2 June 2018}}</ref> marking her first time to enter the [[Billboard (magazine)|''Billboard'']] chart. Later in 2017, she reached 29th place on [[Social 50|''Billboard'' Social 50]],<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/raisa/chart-history/social-50/song/811235|title=Raisa {{!}} Social 50 Chart History|website=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|date=23 September 2017|access-date=2 June 2018}}</ref> a chart which ranks the most active musical artists on the world's leading [[social networking service]]s.

==Background==
Raisa Andriana was born on 6 June 1990 in [[Jakarta]], Indonesia to Allan Rachman and Ria Mariaty. She studied at [[Bina Nusantara University|Binus University]].<ref name="BBC"/> Raisa's singing talent was shown since her early age. When she was 3, Raisa often pretended to be a singer on stage.<ref name="BBC"/> Growing up with minimal music education, she picked up her unique singing style and voice through some of her favorite singers such as [[Brian Mcknight]], [[India Arie]], [[Stevie Wonder]], [[Joss Stone]] etc.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://prestigeonline.com/id/people/celebrities/raisa-making-impact/|title=Raisa: Making an Impact|publisher=Prestige|first=Saradeta|last=Sinaga|date=18 December 2017|access-date=31 May 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.femina.co.id/article/raisa--penyanyi-itu-membaca-situasi|title=Raisa: Penyanyi Itu Membaca Situasi|trans-title=Raisa: The singer reads the situation|publisher=[[Femina (Indonesia)|Femina]]|language=id|date=21 July 2012|access-date=3 February 2016}}</ref>

==Career==
===2008–2010: Career beginning and "Andante"===
Her career as a singer began when she collaborated with famous composer [[David Foster]] to perform in a concert in [[Jakarta]].<ref name="BBC"/> She was the vocalist of Andante, a band founded by Kevin Aprilio. In 2008 the band was reshaped and named Vierra, consisting of five members: Raisa, Widi Soediro, Raka Cyril, Satrianda Widjanarko, and Kevin Aprilio himself. She exited the band when the record's label decided it wanted a different concept.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.kapanlagi.com/andante/profil/|title=Andante Profile|publisher=KapanLagi.com|language=id|access-date=28 June 2014}}</ref>

===2010–2013: Early success with debut album===
Raisa started gaining recognition with her single "Serba Salah". The song led her to gain popularity in Indonesian music industry.<ref name="BBC"/> She then won an award in [[Anugerah Musik Indonesia|Indonesian Music Awards]] for Best of the Best Newcomer category.<ref name="Java Jazz Festival 2014">{{cite web|url=http://javajazzfestival.com/2014/artistdetail.php?action=detail&nid=2512#.U68qZETZEwh|title=Java Jazz Festival 2014|access-date=29 June 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140826115726/http://javajazzfestival.com/2014/artistdetail.php?action=detail&nid=2512#.U68qZETZEwh|archive-date=26 August 2014|url-status=dead}}</ref>

Her debut album, ''[[Raisa (album)|Raisa]]'', was produced and released in 2011 by Solid Records and [[Universal Music Group|Universal Music Indonesia]]. The album was supported by the young musicians who collaborated as producers: Asta Andoko ([[RAN (Indonesian group)|RAN]]), Ramadhan Handy (Soulvibe), and Adrianto Ario Seto (Soulvibe). Nanda Oka and Asta Andoko are Executive Producers of Solid Records.<ref name="BBC"/><ref>{{cite web|url=http://mysumber.com/senarai-keputusan-pemenang-anugerah-planet-muzik-2012-apm.html|title=Keputusan Pemenang Anugerah Planet Muzik 2012|trans-title=Winners of the 2012 Planet Muzik Awards|website=Mysumber.com|access-date=1 July 2014|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141020052407/http://www.mysumber.com/senarai-keputusan-pemenang-anugerah-planet-muzik-2012-apm.html|archive-date=20 October 2014}}</ref>

===2013–2015: ''Heart To Heart'' and ''Indonesian Idol''===
[[File:Raisa at Dental Project 2015 - 4.jpg|thumb|Raisa at Dental Project 2015.]]
Raisa launched her second album, titled ''Heart to Heart'' on 27 November 2013.<ref name="Raisa Album Launching Heart To Heart">{{cite web|url=https://flagig.com/lifestyle/raisa-album-launching-heart-to-heart/|title=Kerennya Raisa 'Heart to Heart' Album Launch Party!|trans-title=Raisa 'Heart to Heart' album launch party!|website=Flagig|language=id|first=Dimas|last=Andhana|date=29 November 2013|access-date=30 June 2014}}</ref> The album featured two singles "Bye-Bye" and "Pemeran Utama". She also performed a concert "Heart to Heart Showcase: Happy (Raisa's version)", targeted for the media and 300 of 'YourRaisa' fans that had been previously selected by pre-ordering the ''Raisa'' box set online.

On 25 August 2013, she opened [[Metallica]]'s concert in [[Jakarta]] with Indonesian rock/[[Heavy metal music|metal]] band [[Seringai]], performing Indonesia's national anthem "[[Indonesia Raya]]".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://hiburan.dreamers.id/article/22773/tampil-di-konser-metallica-raisa-bangga|title=Tampil di Konser Metallica, Raisa Bangga!|trans-title=Perform at Metallica's concert, Raisa is proud!|publisher=Dreamers.id|language=id|author=Syaifan|date=27 August 2013|access-date=3 June 2018}}</ref>

In 2014, Raisa was appointed as a guest judge for a television talent show: ''[[Indonesian Idol (season 8)|Indonesian Idol Season 8]]''. At first, she was a guest judge but later became permanently involved. In February 2015, [[Walt Disney Pictures]] selected Raisa to sing the soundtrack "[[A Dream Is a Wish Your Heart Makes]]" translated to the Indonesian version "Mimpi adalah Harapan Hati" for a Disney remake of ''[[Cinderella (1950 film)|Cinderella]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.cnnindonesia.com/hiburan/20150218194001-220-33248/raisa-cinderella-disney-versi-indonesia/|title=Raisa, 'Cinderella' Disney versi Indonesia|trans-title=Raisa, Indonesian version of Disney's Cinderella|publisher=[[CNN Indonesia]]|language=id|first=Rahmi|last=Suci|date=18 February 2015|access-date=8 January 2018}}</ref>

===2015–present: ''Handmade'' and ''The Voice Indonesia''===
In 2015, Raisa collaborated with singer [[Afgan Syahreza|Afgan]] for soundtrack film ''[[London Love Story]]'', titled "Percayalah". She released her third studio album ''Handmade'' in 2016 under label Juni Records and produced two singles "Jatuh Hati" and "Kali Kedua".

In April 2016, Raisa performed a concert "Handmade Showcase" which was held on Ciputra Artpreneur.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bintang.com/celeb/read/2475314/raisa-bocorkan-isi-boxset-handmade-lewat-video-unboxing|title=Raisa bocorkan isi boxset Handmade lewat video unboxing|trans-title=Raisa leaked the contents of the 'Handmade' boxset via unboxing video|language=id|publisher=Bintang.com|first=Dreses|last=Putranama|date=5 April 2016|access-date=26 June 2016}}</ref> She is currently preparing to perform in tours in major Indonesian cities: "Handmade Tour" which will begin on August until September.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://celebrity.okezone.com/read/2016/06/23/205/1423312/top-gossip-2-mulai-agustus-raisa-konser-tur-keliling-indonesia|title=Mulai Agustus Raisa Konser Tur Keliling Indonesia|trans-title=From August, Raisa will hold concert tour around Indonesia|language=id|publisher=[[Media Nusantara Citra|Okezone]]|first=Raynaldi|last=Wahyu|date=23 June 2016|access-date=26 June 2016}}</ref>

In June 2016, Raisa was a guest star for the Grand Final of ''[[The Voice Indonesia (season 2)|The Voice Indonesia Season 2]]'' where she performed a song "Cahaya Cantik Harimu".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://celebrity.okezone.com/read/2016/06/20/205/1420598/raisa-maniskan-grand-final-the-voice-indonesia-2016|title=Raisa 'Maniskan' Grand Final The Voice Indonesia 2016|trans-title=Raisa enliven for Grand Final of 2016 The Voice Indonesia|language=id|publisher=[[Media Nusantara Citra|Okezone]]|first=Raynaldi|last=Wahyu|date=20 June 2016|access-date=26 June 2016}}</ref> Raisa was appointed as a guest star at the [[Peabo Bryson]]' ''Live in Concert – Celebrating 40 Years of Magic'' in [[Senayan, Kebayoran Baru|The Hall, Senayan City]]. In that occasion, she performed a duet "[[Beauty and the Beast (Disney song)|Beauty and the Beast]]" and "[[A Whole New World]]" with him.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://life.viva.co.id/gaya-hidup/805957-raisa-tampil-memukau-bersama-peabo-bryson|title=Raisa Tampil Memukau Bersama Peabo Bryson|trans-title=Raisa look stunning with Peabo Bryson|author=Eko Priwilianto, Putri Firdaus|publisher=[[Visi Media Asia|Viva]]|language=id|date=7 August 2016|access-date=19 August 2016}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Raisa Andriana married an Australian-born Indonesian actor Hamish Daud on Sunday, 3 September 2017.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.cnnindonesia.com/hiburan/20170903085249-234-239094/raisa-hamish-menikah-dengan-konsep-pesta-kebun|title=Raisa-Hamish Menikah dengan Konsep Pesta Kebun|trans-title=Raisa-Hamish use garden party concept for their wedding|publisher=[[CNN Indonesia]]|language=id|first=Puput Tripeni|last=Juniman|date=3 September 2017|access-date=8 January 2018}}</ref>
