'''2nd II None''' is a [[hip hop music|rap]] group from [[Compton, California|Compton]], [[California]]. It consists of cousins '''KK''' (born Kelton L. McDonald) and '''Gangsta D''' (born Deon Barnett). They were members of the [[Pirus|Elm Street Piru]] [[Bloods]].<ref>[http://unitedgangs.com/2013/09/22/2nd-ii-none/ 2nd II None]{{Dead link|date=March 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} UnitedGangs.com Retrieved. 2015-10-03</ref>

== History ==

=== Early years ===
Their career started in 1990 after the release of "The Red Tape", they signed with [[Profile Records]]. They released their first album in 1991, the self-titled ''[[2nd II None (album)|2nd II None]]'' on [[Profile Records]]. Their second album, ''[[Classic 220]]'' on [[Arista Records]], released in 1999. Their albums were produced by 2nd II None and [[DJ Quik]]. In 2008, their 1994 unreleased album ''Tha Shit'' was leaked on the internet.
