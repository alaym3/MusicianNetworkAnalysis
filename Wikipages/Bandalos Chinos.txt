'''Bandalos Chinos''' is an [[Argentine]] [[indie pop]] band formed in 2009. The band consists of Gregorio Degano (vocals and synthesizers), Salvador Colombo (synthesizers), Tomás Verduga (guitar and backing vocals), Matías Verduga (drums), Iñaki Colombo (guitars and synthesizers) and Nicolás Rodríguez del Pozo (bass). The band has released four studio albums and has received several awards and nominations including two [[Gardel Awards]] and a [[Latin Grammy Award]] nomination.

==Career==
Gregorio "Goyo" Degano and Iñaki Colombo met while assisting to the same school in [[San Fernando de la Buena Vista|San Fernando]] and started playing music together at 14. After finishing high school in 2009, they formed the band in [[Béccar]], a town in the northern part of the [[Buenos Aires Province]] in [[Argentina]].<ref name="Clarin">{{Cite web|url=https://www.clarin.com/espectaculos/musica/bandalos-chinos-solo-queriamos-spinetta_0_39ePJwoG2.html|title=Bandalos Chinos: "Sólo queríamos ser como Spinetta"|work=[[Clarín (Argentine newspaper)|Clarín]]|first=Hernán|last=Firpo|date=January 17, 2019|accessdate=August 18, 2021|language=es}}</ref> The name of the band comes from a performance in 2009 where a soundman introduced them as "los chicos" ("the boys" in Spanish) but it was misundertood as "los chinos", the name would turn into "Banda Los Chinos", later transforming into "Bandalos Chinos".<ref name="Bio">{{Cite web|url=http://rock.com.ar/artistas/22908/biografia|title=Bandalos Chinos|website=Rock.com.ar|accessdate=August 18, 2021|language=es}}</ref> Their debut album ''Bandalos Chinos'', was released in 2012 and was premiered at the Auditorium San Isidro.<ref name="CMTV">{{Cite web|url=https://www.cmtv.com.ar/biografia/show.php?bnid=2365&banda=Bandalos_Chinos|title=Bandalos Chinos|website=CMTV.com.ar|accessdate=August 18, 2021|language=es}}</ref>

Following the release of their first album, they released the EP ''Nunca Estuve Acá'' in 2014, consisting of six songs. On January 4, 2017, they released their second EP ''En el Aire'', consisting of five songs.<ref name="CMTV"/> Their second album ''BACH'' was released on August 3, 2018, it was recorded at [[Sonic Ranch#Studio complex|Sonic Ranch Studios]] in [[Texas]], [[United States]] and was produced by [[Adán Jodorowsky]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://indiehoy.com/discos/bandalos-chinos-bach/|title=Bandalos chinos - BACH|work=Indiehoy|first=Marina|last=Cimerilli|date=August 8, 2018|accessdate=August 18, 2021|language=es}}</ref> At the [[20th Annual Latin Grammy Awards]], the album received nominations for [[Latin Grammy Award for Best Alternative Music Album|Best Alternative Music Album]] and [[Latin Grammy Award for Best Engineered Album|Best Engineered Album]] while at the [[21st Annual Gardel Awards]], they won Best Pop Group Album.

On October 9, 2020, the band released their third album ''Paranoia Pop'', similar to their previous effort, it was produced by [[Adán Jodorowsky]] and was recorded at [[Sonic Ranch#Studio complex|Sonic Ranch Studios]], it also features collaborations with David Aguilar, LOUTA and [[Tei Shi]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.indierocks.mx/musica/resenas/bandalos-chinos-paranoia-pop/|title=Bandalos Chinos - Paranoia Pop|work=Indierocks|first=Chentes|last=Carcaño|date=October 20, 2020|accessdate=August 18, 2021|language=es}}</ref> To promote the album, the band performed a series of concerts via streaming named ''Paranoia Pop: El Musical'' at the Movistar Arena in [[Buenos Aires]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://suenaspe.com/notas/coberturas/el-musical-de-bandalos-chinos-movistar-arena/|title=PARANOIA POP: EL MUSICAL DE BANDALOS CHINOS|work=Suenaspe|first=Lucas|last=Rodriguez Fierro|date=November 3, 2020|accessdate=August 18, 2021|language=es}}</ref> To celebrate the ten years of the band, they released ''Feliz Nabibach'' on December 24, 2020, an album consisting of new versions, demos and live performances of songs from their previous albums.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://indiehoy.com/noticias/feliz-navibach-bandalos-chinos-publica-nuevo-disco/|title=Feliz Navibach: Bandalos Chinos publica nuevo disco|work=Indiehoy|first=Maximiliano|last=Rivarola|date=December 24, 2020|accessdate=August 18, 2021|language=es}}</ref>

==Style and influences==
Among their influences, the band mentions artists such as [[Luis Alberto Spinetta]] and [[Tame Impala]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.lanacion.com.ar/espectaculos/bandalos-chinos-quiere-salir-del-under-si-nid2198725|title=Bandalos Chinos, la banda que sigue la mejor receta del rock nacional|work=[[La Nación]]|first=Alejandro|last=Lingenti|date= December 19, 2018|accessdate=August 18, 2021|language=es}}</ref>
