'''Stephanie Victoria Allen''' (born 14 December 1991), better known by her stage name '''Stefflon Don''', is a British rapper and singer. She rose to fame after her 2017 single "[[Hurtin' Me]]" featuring [[French Montana]] peaked at number 7 on [[UK Singles Chart]]. In 2016, Allen released her debut mixtape ''Real Ting'' followed by another mixtape, ''[[Secure (mixtape)|Secure]]'', in 2018.

== Early life ==
Stefflon Don was born in [[Birmingham]], England, and is of Jamaican descent.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://www.mtv.co.uk/brand-new-for-2017/pictures/9-things-you-need-to-know-about-stefflon-don|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190422142110/http://www.mtv.co.uk/brand-new-for-2017/pictures/9-things-you-need-to-know-about-stefflon-don|url-status=dead|archive-date=22 April 2019|title=9 Things You Need To Know About Stefflon Don {{!}} MTV UK|date=22 April 2019|access-date=12 November 2019}}</ref> She has seven siblings, one of them being rapper [[Dutchavelli]]. When she was five years old, her family moved to [[Rotterdam]], Netherlands. As a result, Allen is fluent in Dutch.<ref name=":0" /><ref name="hunger">{{cite web|title=Stefflon Don on bubbling, family and why her house is the place to stay fambe|publisher=HungerTV|url=http://www.hungertv.com/feature/stefflon-don-on-bubbling-family-and-why-her-house-is-the-place-to-be/|access-date=16 October 2017}}</ref> Allen has been singing and writing music since [[primary school]]. At the age of nine, she recorded a "Hard Knock Life" hook for rapper [[U-Niq]]. The song was never released, but she was surprised to hear her voice on the playback.<ref>{{Cite magazine|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/8099719/stefflon-don-interview-british-jamaican-mc-world-domination|title=Meet Stefflon Don, the British-Jamaican MC Ready for 'World Domination'|magazine=Billboard|access-date=25 April 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.umusic.ca/2018/08/17/meet-stefflon-don/|title=Meet: Stefflon Don, The British Artist Bridging Dancehall & Hip-Hop|date=17 August 2018|website=Umusic|language=en-US|access-date=25 April 2019}}</ref> At the age of 14, she returned to Britain to study at a London school.<ref name=":0" /><ref name="hunger" /> She worked as a [[cake decorating|cake decorator]] and hairdresser before her career in music.<ref name=":0" />

== Career ==

=== 2015–2017: Career beginnings and ''Real Ting'' ===
Don first surfaced in 2015 by releasing a cover of [[Wretch 32]]'s "6 Words" and went on to be featured on tracks such as [[Lethal Bizzle]]'s "Wobble", [[Sneakbo]]'s "Work" remix, [[Angel (British musician)|Angel]]'s Hop On and [[KSI]]'s 2016 song "Touch Down", which appears on the soundtrack of the 2017 film ''[[Baywatch (film)|Baywatch]]''.<ref name="54 London" /> "Fashion Killa (Papapapa)" by Mason, released 16 August 2016, was written by Stefflon Don and Mason (Iason Chronis). Don made her first appearance on an American song with the track "London" by singer [[Jeremih]],<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.factmag.com/2016/12/14/jeremih-london-video-stefflon-don-krept-konan/|title=Jeremih releases 'London' video featuring Stefflon Don and Krept & Konan|date=14 December 2016}}</ref> and was later featured on [[Lil Yachty]]'s "Better" from the album ''[[Teenage Emotions]]''. On 16 December 2016, Don released her first mixtape titled ''Real Ting''.

In the spring of 2016, Don was signed to 54 London a subsidiary of [[Universal Music Group]] that was created just for her.<ref name="54 London">{{cite web |last1=Kieran |first1=Yates |title=Stefflon Don: 'I'm not going to compromise or not say what I want' |url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2017/apr/02/stefflon-don-rapper-stephanie-allen-interview-not-going-to-compromise |website=The Guardian |date=2 April 2017 |access-date=19 August 2018}}</ref> A few months later, she was featured on the dancehall track "[[Instruction (song)|Instruction]]" with [[Jax Jones]] and [[Demi Lovato]]. The trio performed the song live for the first time in June 2018 at the [[Summertime Ball]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Jax Jones feat Demi Lovato & Stefflon Don – 'Instruction' (Live At The Summertime Ball 2018) |url=https://www.capitalfm.com/artists/jax-jones/instruction-stb-18/ |website=Capital FM |access-date=19 August 2018}}</ref>

On 15 August 2017, she released the lead single "[[Hurtin' Me]]" with [[French Montana]] from her debut EP titled ''Hurtin' Me – The EP'', which reached number 7 on the [[UK Singles Chart]]. The EP was later released on 12 January 2018.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Fresh |first1=Mikey |title=Stefflon Don Teams Up With French Montana On "Hurtin Me" |url=https://www.vibe.com/2017/08/stefflon-don-hurtin-me-french-montana/ |website=Vibe |date=15 August 2017 |access-date=17 August 2018}}</ref>

On 29 November, Don took home the award for Best Female at the 2017 [[MOBO Awards]].<ref>{{cite web |last1=Bruce-Jones |first1=Henry |title=Stefflon Don releases new mixtape Secure |url=http://www.factmag.com/2018/08/17/stefflon-don-releases-new-mixtape-secure/ |website=Fact Mag |date=17 August 2018 |access-date=19 August 2018}}</ref>

=== 2018: ''Secure'' ===
In March 2018, Don appeared on the front cover of the final print edition in the 66-year run of ''[[NME]]''.<ref>{{cite news |title=NME's most memorable front covers as it prints for the last time |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/newsbeat-43322793 |access-date=3 November 2018 |publisher=BBC News |date=9 March 2018}}</ref>

In March 2018, Don was featured on the remix for American singer [[Halsey (singer)|Halsey's]] "Alone" along with American rapper [[Big Sean]]. The song peaked at number 66 on the [[Billboard Hot 100]], becoming her first entry on the chart and also earned Don her first [[Dance Club Songs]] number one. The music video was released on 6 April 2018.

In March 2019, Don was featured on the [[Mariah Carey]] remix for the song "[[A No No]]" from her 2018 album ''[[Caution (Mariah Carey album)|Caution]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=df9zXWs36Ro&gl=US&hl=en|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20190322073545/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=df9zXWs36Ro&gl=US&hl=en|archive-date = 22 March 2019|title = Mariah Carey - A No No (Remix) ft. Stefflon Don|website = [[YouTube]]}}</ref>

In June 2018, Don was included in the ''[[XXL (magazine)|XXL]]'' [[XXL (magazine)#XXL Annual Freshman List|Freshman Class.]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2018/06/2018-xxl-freshman-cover/|title=XXL 2018 Freshman Class Revealed|publisher=XXL|date=12 June 2018|access-date=12 June 2018}}</ref>
On 17 August 2018, Don released her second mixtape ''[[Secure (mixtape)|Secure]]''.<ref name="secure">{{cite web|url=http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2018/08/stefflon-don-lil-kim-secure-cover/|title=Stefflon Don Channels Lil' Kim on Cover of 'Secure' Mixtape|work=XXL|last=Seabrook III|first=Robbie|date=8 August 2018|access-date=10 August 2018}}</ref>

In March 2019, she collaborated with [[Wiley (musician)|Wiley]] on the song "[[Boasty]]".

In July 2019, her vocals were featured on [[XXXTentacion]]'s posthumous track "Royalty".

== Personal life ==
She gave birth to her first child, a son named Jaylen, at 17 years old.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.capitalxtra.com/features/lists/stefflon-don-facts/son/|title=Does Stefflon Don have a son?|website=Capital Xtra|access-date=15 December 2020}}</ref>

Don is the sister of the well-known British rapper [[Dutchavelli]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.kentonline.co.uk/weald/news/i-didnt-need-to-rob-because-of-my-famous-sister-217222/|title=Rapper Stefflon Don's brother 'didn't need to' rob Yalding family 'because of famous sister'|website=KentOnline|date=27 November 2019|access-date=25 June 2020}}</ref>

In September 2019, she held a giveaway on Twitter after reaching a billion streams as an artist. British artist [[Shocka]] reached out to Stefflon Don stating that he was in Nigeria for cancer treatment for his mother. She decided to give away the money for Shocka's mother's treatment.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.legit.ng/1257667-burna-boys-bae-stefflon-don-send-n22m-treat-nigerian-mans-sick-mother.html|title=Burna Boy's boo Stefflon Don vows to send N2.2m to treat Nigerian man's sick mom|first=Simbiat|last=Ayoola|date=3 September 2019|website=Legit.ng – Nigeria news.}}</ref> She is currently in a high profile relationship with Afro-fusion star [[Burna Boy]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2020/dec/13/rapper-stefflon-don-interview-i-am-never-scared-to-try-things|title=Stefflon Don: 'I'm never scared to try things'|work=The Guardian|date=13 December 2020|accessdate=26 July 2021}}</ref>
