'''Zack Fox''' (born December 6, 1990) is an American stand-up comedian, rapper, writer, illustrator and [[Internet celebrity|internet personality]] from [[Atlanta|Atlanta, Georgia]].<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.complex.com/music/2018/10/square-up-zack-fox-interview|title=Zack Fox Wants to Make You Laugh, and He's Willing to Rap to Do It|website=Complex|access-date=5 August 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.gq.com/story/new-comedians-2019|title=The 7 Funny People Reshaping Comedy Right Now|website=GQ|access-date=6 August 2019}}</ref> He has collaborated with [[Kenny Beats]], [[Flying Lotus]], and [[Thundercat (musician)|Thundercat]].

==Career==
Fox got his start in 2013 on [[Twitter]], tweeting jokes and amassing tens of thousands of followers under the alias "Bootymath." In subsequent years, he dropped the alias and expanded into music, joining the [[Awful Records]] collective.<ref name=":0" /> In 2017, his publicity increased as he illustrated the album sleeve of [[Thundercat (musician)|Thundercat's]] album [[Drunk (Thundercat album)|''Drunk'']],<ref name=":0" /> while also acted in and writing for ''[[Kuso (film)|Kuso]],'' the 2017 horror comedy film directed by [[Flying Lotus]].<ref name=":1" />

In October 2018, Fox released his first song, "Square Up" in collaboration with [[Hip hop music|hip hop]] producer [[Kenny Beats]].<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=https://www.thefader.com/2018/12/11/zack-fox-rap-square-up-family-function|title=I'm officially excited for Zack Fox's rap career|website=The FADER|access-date=6 August 2019}}</ref>

In April 2019, Fox appeared on ''The Cave'', a [[YouTube]] series in which [[Kenny Beats]] invites different artists to freestyle in his studio. Kenny and Fox recorded "[[Jesus Is the One (I Got Depression)]]," in the episode, and the song reached the number one spot on [[Spotify]]'s U.S. viral chart on July 8, 2019.<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-features/zack-fox-kenny-beats-jesus-is-the-one-864720/|title=Comedian Zack Fox Trolled His Way to the Top of the Charts (and Hated It)|last=Leight|first=Elias|date=August 1, 2019|website=Rolling Stone|access-date=5 August 2019}}</ref> The two never intended to release the song commercially, but after its success they released an official version of it in June.<ref name=":2" />

On October 15, 2021 Zack Fox released his debut studio album "shut the fuck up talking to me."<ref name="Pitchfork">{{cite web |title=Zack Fox: shut the fuck up talking to me |url=https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/zack-fox-shut-the-fuck-up-talking-to-me/ |website=Pitchfork |access-date=4 November 2021 |language=en}}</ref>

Fox makes several appearances on the fictional radio station "Flylo Radio" in [[Grand Theft Auto V]].
