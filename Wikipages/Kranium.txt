'''Kemar Donaldson''' popularly known as '''Kranium''', is a [[Jamaican reggae]] and [[dancehall]] singer known for his 2013 hit single "Nobody Has To Know" which gave him an international recognition and led him to the [[Atlantic Records]] record label.<ref>{{cite news|title=Dancehall Star Kranium Talks to VIBE|url=https://www.vibe.com/2015/06/kranium-interview-dancehall-atlantic-records-drake-nick-jonas-collaboration/|accessdate=4 October 2017|work=Vibe|date=30 June 2015}}</ref>

== Personal life ==
The nephew of [[Screwdriver (musician)|Screwdriver]], he was in [[Montego Bay]] before moving to [[Miami, Florida]]  in 2005. He moved to [[New York City]] one year later.

==Career==
His career began with a series of appearances in New York City and the tri-state area. He opened shows for Gyptian, Serani, I-Octane and Tarrus Riley.<ref>{{cite news|title=The FADER - Kranium|url=http://www.thefader.com/artist/kranium|accessdate=4 October 2017|work=The FADER|language=en}}</ref>

His song, "Nobody Has to Know", produced by PLMR Productions, played on ethnic radio stations in New York City, including the influential [[Hot 97 FM]]. "Nobody Has To Know" sold more than 39,000 copies in 2015, peaking at #32 on the Reggae Digital Songs chart. Kranium has been working with producers including Tony Kelly, TJ Records and Cash Flow.<ref>{{cite news|title=Kranium|url=http://www.billboard.com/music/kranium|accessdate=4 October 2017|work=Billboard}}</ref> In 2021 he released and EP titled ''Toxic'' ''<ref>{{cite web|last=zadok|first=Zoreno|date=2021-02-10|title=ALBUM: Kranium – Toxic EP|url=https://tripplejam.com/album-kranium-toxic-ep/|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=|website=tripplejam.com|language=en}}</ref>''
