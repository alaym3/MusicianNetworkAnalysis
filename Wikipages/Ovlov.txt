'''Ovlov''' is an American [[rock music|rock]] band from [[Newtown, Connecticut]].

==Career==
Ovlov began in 2009 with the release of an EP titled ''Crazy Motorcycle Jump''. They followed that up with another EP in 2011 titled ''What's So Great About The City''.<ref name="posttrash">{{cite web |last1=Crisp |first1=Tim |title=Ovlov - "Greatest Hits Vol. II" - Album Review |url=http://post-trash.com/news/2017/12/3/ovlov-greatest-hits-vol-ii-album-review |publisher=Post Trash |access-date=1 October 2021}}</ref> In 2013, Ovlov released their first full-lenfgth album on [[Exploding in Sound]] titled ''Am''.<ref name="consequenceofsound">{{cite web |last1=Bray |first1=Ryan |title=Album Review: Ovlov – Am |url=https://consequence.net/2013/07/album-review-ovlov-am/ |publisher=[[Consequence of Sound]] |access-date=1 October 2021}}</ref><ref name="popmatters">{{cite web |last1=Stevens |first1=Nathan |title=Ovlov: am |url=https://www.popmatters.com/177311-ovlov-am-2495702287.html |publisher=[[PopMatters]] |access-date=1 October 2021}}</ref> In August 2014, Ovlov released a split with [[Little Big League (band)|Little Big League]]. Two months later, the band released a split with Krill, [[LVL UP]], and [[Radiator Hospital]].<ref name="brooklynvegan">{{cite web |title=Krill streaming “Peanut Butter,” touring, playing CMJ; |url=https://www.brooklynvegan.com/krill-streaming/ |publisher=[[BrooklynVegan]] |access-date=1 October 2021}}</ref> In 2018, Ovlov released their second full-length album on Exploding in Sound titled [[Tru (Ovlov album)|Tru]].<ref name="pitchfork">{{cite web |last1=Berman |first1=Stuart |title=Ovlov: Tru |url=https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/ovlov-tru/ |publisher=[[Pitchfork (website)|Pitchfork]] |access-date=1 October 2021}}</ref> Ovlov will release their third full-length album, ''Buds'', in 2021.<ref name="pastemagazine">{{cite web |last1=Russell |first1=Scott |title=Ovlov Announce New Album Buds, Share Lead Single "Land of Steve-O" |url=https://www.pastemagazine.com/music/ovlov/ovlov-new-album-buds-lead-single-land-of-steve-o/ |publisher=[[Paste (magazine)|Paste]] |access-date=1 October 2021}}</ref>
