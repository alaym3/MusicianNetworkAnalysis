'''Ramona Wulf''' (born 18 October 1954) is a German singer.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Ramona Wulf Alben Vinyl {{!}} Schallplatten {{!}} Recordsale|url=https://recordsale.de/de/kuenstler/ramona-wulf|access-date=2021-08-16|website=recordsale.de|language=de}}</ref> Her biggest success as a solo singer was in 1971 with the song ''Alles was wir woll'n auf Erden'' with an 8th place in the [[German single charts]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Ramona - Alles was wir woll'n auf Erden - hitparade.ch|url=https://hitparade.ch/song/Ramona/Alles-was-wir-woll%27n-auf-Erden-116865|access-date=2021-08-16|website=hitparade.ch}}</ref> She was the public face of [[Silver Convention]] from 1974 to 1979.<ref name="nor">{{cite web|url=http://norwegiancharts.com/showitem.asp?interpret=Silver%20Convention%2C%20Penny%20McLean%2C%20Ramona%20Wulf%2C%20Linda%20G.%20Thompson&titel=Success&cat=a |title=Norway Charts>>Silver Convention, Penny McLean, Ramona Wulf, Linda G. Thompson - SUCCESS (album) |access-date=2017-05-20 |url-status=bot: unknown |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160616170714/http://norwegiancharts.com/showitem.asp?interpret=Silver+Convention%2C+Penny+McLean%2C+Ramona+Wulf%2C+Linda+G.+Thompson&titel=Success&cat=a |archive-date=June 16, 2016 }}. norwegiancharts.com.</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=NDR|title=1977: Deutscher Vorentscheid|url=https://www.eurovision.de/geschichte/1977-Deutscher-Vorentscheid,vorentscheidhr105.html|access-date=2021-08-16|website=www.eurovision.de|language=de}}</ref> 

"[[Fly, Robin, Fly]]", released by Silver Convention as the third single from ''Save Me'' in September 1975, reached at number one on the United States [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=SPIEGEL|first=DER|title=Made in Germany: Deutsche Hits in den USA|url=https://www.spiegel.de/fotostrecke/made-in-germany-deutsche-hits-in-den-usa-fotostrecke-135900.html|access-date=2021-08-16|website=www.spiegel.de|language=de}}</ref>

After the dissolution of Silver Convention in 1979, she worked as a solo singer but the success was moderate.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2014-11-22|title=Silver Convention: Was ist aus dem Trio geworden?|url=https://www.bild.de/unterhaltung/musik/silverconvention/das-ist-aus-dem-trio-geworden-38681330.bild.html|access-date=2021-08-16|website=bild.de|language=de}}</ref>
