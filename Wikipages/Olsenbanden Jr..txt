'''''Olsenbanden Jr.''''' ({{lang-en|The Olsen Junior Gang}}) is a Norwegian comedy franchise based around a series of films and TV series of under-age criminals, based on the ''[[Olsenbanden]]'' films. Despite the original Olsen Gang being a Danish invention, the child version was originally a Swedish idea, ''[[Lilla Jönssonligan]]'', with Denmark and Norway later following suit.
