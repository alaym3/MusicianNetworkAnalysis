'''Ben Caplan''' is a Canadian [[folk musician]] from [[Halifax, Nova Scotia]]. He often performs with his band The Casual Smokers, and his first full-length studio album, ''In the Time of the Great Remembering'', was released on October 20, 2011.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.bringthenoiseuk.com/201211/music/reviews/album-review-ben-caplan-in-the-time-of-the-great-remembering |title=ALBUM REVIEW: Ben Caplan – In The Time Of The Great Remembering « Bring the Noise UK<!-- Bot generated title --> |access-date=2016-01-12 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160305073553/http://www.bringthenoiseuk.com/201211/music/reviews/album-review-ben-caplan-in-the-time-of-the-great-remembering |archive-date=2016-03-05 |url-status=dead }}</ref><ref>[http://www.musicomh.com/reviews/albums/ben-caplan-the-casual-smokers-in-the-time-of-the-great-remembering Ben Caplan & The Casual Smokers – In The Time Of The Great Remembering | Album Reviews | musicOMH<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref> His second record, ''Birds With Broken Wings'', was released September 18, 2015.<ref>[http://exclaim.ca/Music/article/ben_caplan-birds_with_broken_wings_album_stream Birds With Broken Wings]. Exclaim!.</ref>

==Career==

===Early years===
Ben Caplan was born and raised in [[Hamilton, Ontario]] and now resides in [[Halifax, Nova Scotia]].<ref>(January 23, 2013). [http://www.cbc.ca/news/entertainment/singer-songwriter-ben-caplan-s-old-soul-1.1345984 Singer-songwriter Ben Caplan’s old soul]. CBC News. Retrieved March 20, 2015.</ref> He began writing music at the age of 13 with friends, but it was at the age of 19 that he started his journey as an independent singer-songwriter.<ref>[http://www.takenbysound.com/#!an-interview-with-ben-caplan/cf7a An Interview With Ben Caplan] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150327153421/http://www.takenbysound.com/#!an-interview-with-ben-caplan/cf7a |date=2015-03-27 }} Taken by Soul. Retrieved March 26, 2015.</ref> Although he  studied at the [[University of King's College]], he decided that in order to have a career in music, it would have to become his priority.<ref>[http://www.sonicbids.com/band/bencaplanmusic/press/ BAND STRIKES ROOTSY CHORD - Ben Caplan & The Casual Smokers More Than Just a Party Band] The Dalhousie Gazette. Retrieved March 26, 2015.</ref> He participated in the Artist Entrepreneur program through [http://www.coalitionmusic.com/ Coalition Music (Canada’s Music Incubator)] which helps emerging artists build their careers, businesses, and brands.<ref>[http://www.music-ontario.ca/coalition-music-torontos-music-incubator-artist-entrepreneur-program/ COALITION MUSIC (Toronto’s Music Incubator) – Artist Entrepreneur Program] Music Ontario. Retrieved March 26, 2015.</ref> He has been a touring musician since 2006 and released his first full-length album in 2009.<ref>[http://ecma.com/members/ben-caplan Ben Caplan].East Coast Music Association. Retrieved March 20, 2015.</ref>

===Touring===
In 2011 Caplan toured across Canada, and performed some concerts in Europe as well.<ref>[http://www.thecoast.ca/halifax/ben-caplans-towering-success/Content?oid=3039324 Ben Caplan’s towering success | Music Feature | Halifax, Nova Scotia | THE COAST<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>
In 2012, Ben Caplan & The Casual Smokers toured the east coast of Canada, performed at [[South by Southwest]] in [[Austin, Texas]], and performed at the [[East Coast Music Awards]] (ECMA) Gala.<ref>[http://www.audioblood.com/2012/02/ben-caplan-the-casual-smokers-to-perform-at-sxsw-cmw-ecma-tge-canadian-and-european-tour-dates-announced/ Ben Caplan & the Casual Smokers to Perform at SXSW, CMW, ECMA, & TGE – Canadian and European Tour Dates Announced] Audio Blood. Retrieved March 26, 2015</ref>

Caplan performed live at the 2013 [[Bluesfest Byron bay|Byron Bay Bluesfest]] that took place from March 30 to April 1.<ref>[http://www.bluesfest.com.au/pages/?ParentPageID=2&PageID=55#2013 Archive 2013] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150321000533/http://www.bluesfest.com.au/pages/?ParentPageID=2&PageID=55#2013 |date=2015-03-21 }} Byron Bay Bluesfest. Retrieved March 20, 2015.</ref>

From April to the end of July in 2013, Caplan played a number of shows that spread across [[Australia]], the [[Netherlands]], [[Germany]], [[Switzerland]], [[Poland]], [[Norway]], the [[United Kingdom]], and in [[Canada]]. Among these shows, he performed at [[Glastonbury Festival]] ([[UK]]), [[The Hop Farm Festival]] (UK), and Roots In The Park Music Festival (Netherlands).<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.audioblood.com/2013/04/ben-caplan-announces-performance-at-glastonbury-festival-west-coast-canadian-tour-and-a-busy-summer-filled-with-festival-apperiances/ |title=Ben Caplan Announces Performance At Glastonbury Festival, West Coast Canadian Tour and A Busy Summer Filled With Festival Apperances [sic] |publisher=Audio Blood |date=April 2, 2013 |first=Erin |last=Carroll |access-date=March 30, 2015}}</ref>

In December 2013, Caplan returned to the [[Netherlands]] for a weekend tour that consisted of dates in Tilburg, Arnhem, and Haarlem.<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0jWp5S0GEek Netherlands Tour: December 2013] Youtube. Retrieved March 20, 2015.</ref>

From December 28, 2013 to January 10, 2014, Caplan played a series of shows in [[Australia]] and participated in the [[Woodford Folk Festival]] and [[Sydney Festival]].<ref>[http://www.theaureview.com/artist/ben-caplan The AU Interview: Ben Caplan (Canada) Talks 2014 Tour!] The AU Review. Retrieved March 26, 2015</ref>

From February 21 to April 1, 2015, Caplan toured the [[United States]] for the first time. He performed three times during [[South By Southwest]] in [[Austin, Texas]] in the middle of the tour.<ref>[http://www.axs.com/news/exclusive-ben-caplan-announces-upcoming-u-s-tour-in-new-video-41102 Exclusive: Ben Caplan announces upcoming U.S. tour in new video] AXS. Retrieved March 26, 2015.</ref>

Between 2015 and 2016 Ben embarked on back to back North American and European Tours <ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.thecoast.ca/halifax/ben-caplan-performer-of-the-year/Content?oid=5108564http://bencaplan.ca/2015/07/new-north-america-2015-tour/.|title = Ben Caplan, performer of the year}}</ref> While touring Europe Ben has received positive reviews major publications such as Independent UK and The Guardian UK who say "For all the fierceness of the Canadian's appearance, nothing quite prepares for his voice, both spoken and singing. An unmistakable presence. " <ref>://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/music/reviews/ben-caplan-the-casual-smokers-king-tuts-wah-wah-hut-glasgow-review-an-unmistakable-presence-a6800756.html</ref> and "From Satanic hoedowns to swaying country heartbreakers, this evening with the larger-than-life Caplan makes for rollicking fun." ranking him – 4/5 stars<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2016/jan/06/ben-caplan-review-king-tuts-glasgow-casual-smokers|title=Ben Caplan review – burlesque folk with a bellowing ringmaster|date=6 January 2016}}</ref>
