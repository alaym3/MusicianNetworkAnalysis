'''Naseeb Abdul Juma Issack''' (born 2 October 1989), popularly known by his stage name '''Diamond Platnumz''', is a [[Tanzanian]], [[bongo flava]] recording artist, dancer, philanthropist and a businessman of [[Ha people|Ha]] heritage. He was born and raised in [[Tandale]], [[Dar es Salaam]]. He is the founder and CEO of [[WCB Wasafi]] Record Label, Zoom Extra and Wasafi Media. Diamond has gained a cult following in East and Central Africa.<ref name="bongo5">{{cite web|title=Mastaa 20 wa burudani, michezo na urembo Tanzania wenye ushawishi mkubwa zaidi|url=http://www.bongo5.com/mastaa-20-wa-burudanimichezo-na-urembo-tanzania-wenye-ushawishi-mkubwa-zaidi-04-2013/|publisher=bongo5|date=26 April 2013}}</ref> He became the first Africa-based artist to reach a combined total of 900 million YouTube views.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thecitizen.co.tz/tanzania/news/diamond-platnumz-becomes-first-african-artiste-to-hit-900-million-views-on-youtube-2706234|title = Diamond Platnumz becomes first African artiste to hit 900 million views on Youtube}}</ref>

After signing a record deal with [[Universal Music]] in 2017, Platnumz released his third studio album, ''[[A Boy from Tandale]]'' (2018).
In 2021, Diamond together with his record label [[WCB Wasafi]]<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-05-18|title=Diamond & WCB-Wasafi Ink Another Lucrative Deal with International Company (Photos)|url=https://www.pulselive.co.ke/entertainment/celebrities/diamond-and-his-wcb-wasafi-ink-another-lucrative-deal-with-warner-music-south-africa/vpdwbq7|access-date=2021-05-18|website=Pulse Kenya|language=en}}</ref> entered into a 360 Partnership with Warner Music Group. <ref>{{Cite web|title=Warner Music Group Warner Music Announces Partnership with Diamond Platnumz and WCB-Wasafi|url=https://www.wmg.com/news/warner-music-announces-partnership-diamond-platnumz-and-wcb-wasafi-35466|access-date=2021-05-18|website=Warner Music Group Official Website|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Warner Music partners with Diamond Platnumz {{!}} Complete Music Update|url=https://completemusicupdate.com/article/warner-music-partners-with-diamond-platnumz/|access-date=2021-05-18}}</ref>

== Career ==
=== Career beginnings and breakthrough ===
His career began at the age of 17, in 2006 selling clothes and record songs with money he earns and recorded his first single "Toka Mwanzo" fused with [[R&B]].<ref name="Citizen">{{cite web|title=How Diamond Platnumz Became Tanzania's Biggest Star - The Citizen|url=https://www.thecitizen.co.tz/tanzania/news/entertainment/how-diamond-platnumz-became-tanzania-s-biggest-star-3503686|access-date =August 8, 2021|work=The Citizen}}</ref> The song was not successful commercially.

His breakthrough hit single "Kamwambie" was released in 2010.<ref name="Citizen"></ref> The song won three Tanzania Music Awards.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/9610830/diamond-platnumz-tanzania-bongo-flava-interview/|title=How Diamond Platnumz Became Tanzania's Biggest Star|first=Nadia |last=Neophytou
|access-date =8 June 2021|work=Billaboard}}</ref> He then released his debut studio album ''Kamwambie'' same year in 2010.

In 2014, Platnumz earned nomination at [[BET Awards 2014]] for Best International Act: [[Africa]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/the-juice/6084917/beyonce-jay-z-lead-2014-bet-awards |title=Beyonce & Jay Z Lead 2014 BET Awards |publisher=Billboard |date=2014-01-26 |access-date=2014-05-14}}</ref>

Platnumz has frequently collaborated with videographer [[Director Kenny]] for his music videos. The music video for "Waah", directed by Kenny, was nominated for "Best African Video" at the 2021 [[All Africa Music Awards]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Best African Video|url=https://afrima.org/AFRIMA2021voting/best-african-video|website=www.afrima.org|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=What Artists Are Nominated for All Africa Music Awards 2021|url=https://lifestyleug.com/nominees-africa-music-awards-2021/|access-date=12 October 2021|website=www.lifestyleug.com|language=en}}</ref>

== Personal life ==
Diamond is a Muslim.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9gNyERFAm14&t=30m15s |title=Diamond on the Sporah Show |publisher=Sporah Show |access-date=16 June 2014}}</ref> With his former partner, South African based Ugandan businesswoman [[Zari Hassan]],<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://nairobinews.nation.co.ke/chillax/zari-hassan-finally-divorces-diamond-platinumz-valentines-day/|title=Zari Hassan finally divorces Diamond Platnumz... on Valentine's Day|last=Musungu|first=Nahashon|date=2018-02-15|work=Nairobi News|access-date=2018-04-05|language=en-US}}</ref> he has two children.<ref name="Aswani 2018">{{cite web |last=Aswani |first=Nixon |title=Diamond Platnumz biography: facts you didn't know about the king of Wasafi |website=Tuko.co.ke - Kenya news. |date=2018-02-21 |url=https://www.tuko.co.ke/266426-diamond-platnumz-biography-facts-didnt-king-wasafi.html |access-date=2018-11-19}}</ref><ref name="Mbuthia 2018">{{cite web | last=Mbuthia | first=Geoffrey | title=Diamond denies rumours that Prince Nillan is not his son | website=Mpasho News | date=2018-05-11 | url=https://mpasho.co.ke/diamond-denies-rumours-nillan-son/ | access-date=2018-11-19}}</ref> He became a father for the third time with Tanzanian model Hamisa Mobeto.<ref name="TowerPostNews 2017">{{cite web | title=Diamond Platnumz Admits Cheating on Zari With Model Hamisa Mobeto | website=TowerPostNews | date=2017-09-19 | url=http://thetowerpost.com/2017/09/19/diamond-platnumz-admits-cheating-on-zari-with-model-hamisa-mobeto/ | access-date=2018-11-19}}</ref> As of 2019 Diamond was dating Kenyan model and musician Tanasha Donna, with whom he fathered one son, born in October 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sde.co.ke/article/2001344186/17-hours-of-labour-tanasha-donna-welcomes-first-child-with-diamond|title=17 hours of labour: Tanasha Donna welcomes first child with Diamond|last=Muli|first=Davis|website=Standard Digital News|access-date=2020-02-18}}</ref> The two have since separated with Tanasha Donna flying back to her home country, Kenya.

Diamond is the cousin of Tanzanian socialite-cum-DJ, Romeo Abdul Jones, professionally known as Romy Jones.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.pulselive.co.ke/entertainment/diamonds-brother-and-official-dj-romy-jons-and-wife-kay-jord-expecting-their-first/xm2z3s8|title=Diamond's brother Romy Jons and wife expecting their first child (Video)|date=31 July 2019|website=Facebook}}</ref> He also has two sisters, musician Queen Darleen, and entrepreneur-socialite Esma Platinumz.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.capitalfm.co.ke/thesauce/diamond-platnumzs-elder-sister-queen-darleen-married-as-a-second-wife/|title=Diamond Platnumz's elder sister Queen Darleen married as a second wife|first=The|last=Sauce|date=3 December 2019}}</ref>

In 2010, he endorsed Tanzania's dominant ruling party, the [[Chama Cha Mapinduzi]] (CCM) and its presidential candidate, [[Jakaya Kikwete|Jakaya Mrisho Kikwete]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2DlUeI8T6FY |title= Mimi na mchagua Jakaya Mrisho Kikwete |date=28 August 2010 |language=sw|trans-title=I have selected Jakaya Mrisho Kikwete |access-date=17 September 2014}}</ref> He has released further songs with lyrics supporting CCM, such as "''CCM Tusonge Mbele''" ("CCM Let's Move Forward").<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QkZdDn2U6iI |title=Diamond Mbagala Remix CCM Tusonge Mbele |language=sw|trans-title=Mbagala Remix CCM Let's Move Forward |date= 30 August 2014 |access-date=17 September 2014}}</ref>

===Commercial activities===
On 23 January 2019, Diamond Platnumz was officially introduced as Pepsi Brand Ambassador in East Africa.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://dailyactive.info/2019/01/24/diamond-officially-appointed-as-pepsi-brand-ambassador/|title=Diamond officially appointed as Pepsi brand ambassador|first=Wendy|last=Kahaso|date=24 January 2019}}</ref>

On 13 September 2019, Diamond Platnumz became Brand Ambassador of [[Parimatch (company)|Parimatch]] Africa.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.igamingbusiness.com/diamond-platnumz-brand-ambassador-parimatch-africa|title=Diamond Platnumz brand ambassador for Parimatch Africa|date=13 September 2019|website=iGaming Business}}</ref>

On 25 September 2019, Diamond was named the Nice One Brand Ambassador.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.pulselive.co.ke/entertainment/diamond-platnumz-appointed-nice-one-brand-ambassador/ezxj7bz|title=Diamond lands another ambassadorial deal (Video)|date=25 September 2019|website=Facebook}}</ref>

On 4 March 2020, Diamond Platnumz was unveiled as the new Brand Ambassador of Coral Paints (Tanzania).<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.pulselive.co.ke/entertainment/diamond-platnumz-appointed-as-the-new-brand-ambassador-of-cotral-paints-in-tanzania/rhl8bkn|title=Details of Diamond's press conference that went down in Dar es Salaam|date=4 March 2020|website=Facebook}}</ref>
