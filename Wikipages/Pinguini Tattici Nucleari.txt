'''Pinguini Tattici Nucleari''' ([[English language|English]]: "Tactical Nuclear Penguins") is an Italian [[indie rock]]/[[Indie pop|pop]] band formed in 2010.<ref name=lettera43>{{Cite news|url=https://www.lettera43.it/pinguini-tattici-nucleari-sony/ |title=La storia di successo dei Pinguini Tattici Nucleari  |website=lettera43.it |author=Francesco Ruffinoni |date=19 January 2019 |access-date=5 February 2020}}</ref><ref name=larionews>{{Cite news|url=http://fascinointellettuali.larionews.com/pinguini-tattici-nucleari-la-band-bergamasca-dalle-molteplici-forme/|title=Pinguini Tattici Nucleari, la band bergamasca dalle molteplici forme|website=larionews.com|author=Camilla Volpe|date=2 March 2016}}</ref>

They debuted in 2012 with the EP ''Cartoni animali'' and their first studio album ''Il re è nudo'' was released in 2014.<ref name=lettera43/><ref name="treccani">{{Cite web|url=http://www.treccani.it/enciclopedia/pinguini-tattici-nucleari|title=Pinguini Tattici Nucleari|website=[[Treccani|Treccani Encyclopedia]]|access-date=17 February 2020}}</ref>

The band participated at the [[Sanremo Music Festival 2020]] with the song "Ringo Starr", getting the 3rd place.<ref name="treccani"/><ref>{{Cite web|title=Sanremo 2020: Ecco chi sono i 22 Big in gara della 70esima edizione del Festival|url=https://www.comingsoon.it/tv/anticipazioni/sanremo-2020-ecco-chi-sono-i-22-big-in-gara-della-70esima-edizione-del/n98831/|website=Coming Soon|date=31 December 2019|access-date=5 February 2020}}</ref>

According to members of the band, their name is inspired by [[Tactical Nuclear Penguin]], a beer produced by Scottish [[brewery]] [[Brewdog]] in 2009.
