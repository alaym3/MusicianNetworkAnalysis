'''Adewale Mayowa Emmanuel''' (born 23 March 1994), known professionally as '''Mayorkun''', is a Nigerian singer, songwriter and pianist.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://mybiohub.com/2016/07/mayorkun-biography-age.html/|title=Mayorkun Biography {{!}} Age {{!}} MyBioHub|date=2016-07-04|work=MyBioHub|access-date=2018-11-01|language=en-US}}</ref> He released a cover of [[Davido]]'s "The Money" single and was discovered by the singer on Twitter.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://punchng.com/meeting-davido-was-a-blessing-mayokun/|title=Meeting Davido was a blessing – Mayorkun|work=Punch Newspapers|access-date=2018-11-01|language=en-US}}</ref> Mayorkun signed with Davido's record label [[Davido Music Worldwide]] (DMW) in 2016; his debut single "Eleko" was released under the label.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.360nobs.com/2016/04/hkndavido-music-worldwide-presents-mayorkun-eleko/|title=HKN/Davido Music Worldwide Presents: Mayorkun - Eleko {{!}} 360Nobs.com|website=www.360nobs.com|language=en-US|access-date=2018-11-01|date=2016-04-07}}</ref> Mayorkun released his debut studio album ''[[The Mayor of Lagos]]'' in November 2018.

== Early life ==
Mayorkun was born on 23 March 1994 in [[Osun State]], Nigeria.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://okhype.media/artist/21/mayorkun|title=Mayorkun Biography, Discographies & Lifestyle|website=Okhype.Media|language=en-US|access-date=2018-11-03}}</ref> He is the first child of three, born into a family of entertainers. His mother is a [[Nollywood]] actress and his father, an artist and movie producer. Mayorkun was part of the choir at a very young age where he, according to him, "learnt the rudiments of music".<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://guardian.ng/life/spotlight/mayorkun-the-mayor-of-lagos/|title=Mayorkun: The Mayor of Lagos|access-date=2018-11-03|language=en-US}}</ref> He has a degree in accounting from the [[University of Lagos]], and was in a banking job before he was discovered in 2016.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.takemetonaija.com/2016/05/singer-mayorkun-full-biography.html|title=Singer MAYORKUN Full Biography; Net Worth Car, Age, Father - TIN Magazine|date=2016-05-03|work=TIN Magazine|access-date=2018-11-03|language=en-GB}}</ref>

== Music career ==
Mayorkun decided to leave his bank job to search for another due to bad pay, when his cover video was spotted by [[Davido]] in February 2016.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.pulse.ng/buzz/davido-singer-signs-new-artist-mayor-kun-to-hkn-gang-id4759397.html|title=Davido: Singer signs new artist Mayor Kun to HKN Gang|last=Solanke|first=Abiola|access-date=2018-11-03|language=en-US}}</ref> [[Davido]] followed him on Twitter and Instagram, after which [[Davido]] asked him if he can do more than play the keyboard. He got a call from [[Davido]] the same day he wrote his resignation letter<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.naijaloaded.com.ng/others/got-call-davido-day-quit-bank-job-mayorkun-reveals|title=How I Got A Call From Davido The Day I Quit My Bank Job – Mayorkun Reveals|date=2016-08-31|work=Naijaloaded|access-date=2018-11-03|language=en-US}}</ref> and sent [[Davido]] some of the songs he already recorded. [[Davido]] decided to sign him to [[Davido Music Worldwide]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.nigerianeye.com/2016/05/hkn-artiste-mayorkun-opens-up-on-how.html|title=HKN artiste Mayorkun opens up on how Davido signed him|work=Nigeria News Today|access-date=2018-11-03}}</ref> Mayorkun released his first single under the label called "Eleko" in 2016. The video of the song gathered a million views in the first 10 days.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.theseunbadejo.com/mayorkuns-eleko-hits-one-million-views-on-youtube/|title=Mayorkun's 'Eleko' Hits One Million Views On YouTube {{!}} theseunbadejo.com|date=2016-04-17|work=theseunbadejo.com|access-date=2018-11-03|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.pulse.ng/entertainment/music/you-need-to-hear-this-mayorkun-seeks-to-recreate-new-magic-with-love-you-tire-id5662596.html|title=You Need To Hear This: Mayorkun seeks to recreate new magic with "Love you tire"|last=Akan|first=Joey|access-date=2018-11-04|language=en-US}}</ref> To his name, Mayorkun has several singles including Yawa, Sade, Che Che, and Mama. In October 2018, he released singles "Posh" and "Fantasy" featuring [[Olu Maintain]]<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://newsbreakers.ng/mayorkun-features-olu-maintain-in-new-album-titled-fantasy/|title=Mayorkun Features Olu Maintain in New Album Titled, Fantasy Newsbreakers|date=2018-10-31|work=Newsbreakers|access-date=2018-11-05|language=en-US}}</ref> in anticipation of his first studio album titled "[[The Mayor of Lagos]]".<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.pulse.ng/entertainment/music/mayorkun-fts-davido-dbanj-on-debut-album-the-mayor-of-lagos-id9071418.html|title=Mayorkun fts Davido, Dbanj on debut album, "The Mayor of Lagos"|last=Ohunyon|first=Ehis|access-date=2018-11-05|language=en-US}}</ref> The Mayor of Lagos was released on the 16th of November 2018.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.pmnewsnigeria.com/2018/11/16/mayorkun-debuts-album-mayor-of-lagos/|title=Mayorkun Debuts Album "Mayor Of Lagos"|date=2018-11-16|work=P.M. News|access-date=2018-11-21|language=en-US}}</ref> On December 1, 2018, it appeared on the [[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] World Albums chart, peaking at number 15.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/mayorkun|title=Billboard-Mayorkun|website=Billboard|access-date=December 3, 2018|language=en-US}}</ref>

In 2020 he was among the artistes scheduled for performance at the Convention center of the [[Eko Hotels and Suites|Eko Hotel and suites]], [[Victoria Island, Lagos|Victoria Island]], [[Lagos State|Lagos]] for the 2020 vanguard personality awards. Other scheduled artistes included [[Tekno (musician)|Tekno]], [[Timaya]] and many more.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-03-15|title=Timaya, Tekno, Mayorkun to thrill at Vanguard Awards|url=https://www.vanguardngr.com/2020/03/timaya-tekno-mayorkun-to-thrill-at-vanguard-awards/|access-date=2021-02-26|website=Vanguard News|language=en-US}}</ref>

=== ''The Mayor of Lagos'' concert ===
Mayorkun embarked on a nationwide tour in 2017 that saw him perform in over 35 schools.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.konbini.com/ng/entertainment/music/mayorkun-to-headline-lagos-concert-this-december/|title=Mayorkun To Headline Lagos Concert This December|last=Orubo|first=Daniel|date=2017-11-03|work=Konbini Nigeria|access-date=2018-11-04|language=en-US}}</ref> He ended the year 2017 with his first headline concert in [[Lagos]] called "The Mayor of [[Lagos]]".<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.pulse.ng/entertainment/music/mayorkun-singer-rounds-off-2017-with-mayor-of-lagos-concert-photos-id7730579.html|title=Mayorkun: Singer rounds off 2017 with "Mayor Of Lagos" concert [Photos]|last=Akan|first=Joey|access-date=2018-11-04|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.vanguardngr.com/2017/12/artiste-concerts-rocked-2017/|title=Artiste concerts that rocked 2017 - Vanguard News Nigeria|date=2017-12-30|work=Vanguard News Nigeria|access-date=2018-11-04|language=en-US}}</ref> The Mayor of [[Lagos]] concert continued in 2018 with a show in the city of Ibadan and the federal capital territory, [[Abuja]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.pulse.ng/entertainment/music/davidos-protege-mayorkun-invade-abuja-for-the-mayor-concert-id8724168.html|title=Davidos protege Mayorkun invade Abuja for The Mayor Concert|last=Ohunyon|first=Ehis|access-date=2018-11-04|language=en-US}}</ref> Also, a date for a [[Lagos]] edition was announced.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://thenativemag.com/communities/mayorkun-announce-mayor-lagos-concert-date/|title=Mayorkun announces 'The Mayor Of Lagos' concert date - The Native|date=2018-09-24|work=The Native|access-date=2018-11-04|language=en-US}}</ref> In 2018, Mayorkun did a tour of the  [[United Kingdom|UK]] alongside label mate Dremo, where he performed in about six cities. He also performed alongside label boss Davido at the [[Wireless Festival|wireless festival]].
