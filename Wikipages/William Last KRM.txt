'''Bofelo William Molebatsi''' (born 8 March 1996), also known by his stage names '''William Last KRM''', '''Motsetserepa''' and '''Mawilla''', is a [[Botswana|Motswana]] comedian, rapper and motswako artist.<ref>{{Cite web |date=2020-04-14 |title=William Last KRM |url=https://www.musicinafrica.net/directory/william-last-krm |access-date=2021-10-12 |website=Music In Africa |language=fr}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |title=William Last Krm Lyrics, Biography and Albums - AfrikaLyrics |url=https://afrikalyrics.com/artist/william-last-krm |access-date=2021-10-12 |website=afrikalyrics.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |date=2020-07-18 |title=5 precious Moments Of Motsetserepa And Daughter On Social Media |url=https://www.botswanayouth.com/5-precious-moments-of-motsetserepa-and-daughter-on-social-media/ |access-date=2021-10-12 |website=Botswana Youth Magazine |language=en-US}}</ref>

==Personal life==
William was born on 8 March 1996 in Gaborone, Botswana.<ref name="Last">{{Cite web |date=2021-10-03 |title=William Last KRM biography |url=https://www.safrolebs.com/blog/celebs/william-last-krm-biography/ |access-date=2021-10-12 |website=Southern African celebrities |language=en}}</ref> He is married and his daughter Amara William was born in 2019.<ref name="Story">{{Cite web |date=2020-12-18 |title=The Story of William Last KRM’s Ascent is One of Purpose Meeting Preparation |url=https://www.okayafrica.com/william-last-krm-tinto-viral-video-story/ |access-date=2021-10-12 |website=OkayAfrica |language=en}}</ref>

==Career==
After the continuous failures in his education life, he started make videos and upload them to social media.<ref name="KRM">{{Cite web |last=Yates |first=Bennett |date=2020-05-22 |title=All you need to know about the life of William Last KRM |url=https://briefly.co.za/62913-william-last-krm-bio-real-nationality-chris-brown-trending-videos-profile.html |access-date=2021-10-12 |website=Briefly |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |title=William Last KRM’s Dilemma |url=https://tswalebs.com/top-of-the/william-last-krm/william-last-krm-s-dilemma |access-date=2021-10-12 |website=TSWAlebs |language=en}}</ref> To gain attention, he used many mononyms in social network such as — "Skgakga", "Tshasa", "Motsetserepa", and "Rragwe Willian". His comedy videos went viral through the social media where he reached over a million followers on social media making him the most followed entertainer in Botswana.<ref name="MO">{{Cite web |last=Mathala |first=Sharon |date=2021-03-23 |title=William Last KRM, Director MO, a breath of fresh air » TheVoiceBW |url=https://news.thevoicebw.com/2021/03/william-last-krm-director-mo-a-breath-of-fresh-air/ |access-date=2021-10-12 |website=TheVoiceBW |language=en-GB}}</ref> After his first mini skit, William started to produce a stand-up comedy show.<ref name="Story" /> In the meantime, he produced the fashion clothing line 'Motsetserepa'.<ref name="Last" />

As a rapper, he made the singles: ''Gao romantic'', ''Romantic'' and ''Pimpin Bucket''.<ref name="KRM" /><ref name="Last" /> In 2020, William made the visual for his debut single ''Tinto'' collaborated with Director MO which received 1.8 million views on YouTube. In March 2020, he made the parody of Childish Gambino’s ''This is America aptly'' titled "This is Botswana" which received huge popularity on Facebook.<ref>{{Cite web |title=William Last KRM wants Mpho Sebina |url=https://tswalebs.com/relationships/william-last-krm/william-last-krm-wants-love-to-come-with-his-fame |access-date=2021-10-12 |website=TSWAlebs |language=en}}</ref> In March 2021, he released visuals for his single dubbed "How it feels" featuring Dato Seiko and Fella, from his debut album, ''William''.<ref name="Story" /><ref name="MO" /> In July 2021 at the Yarona FM Music Awards (YAMAS), William received nominations in categories for Best Hip hop award, Best Social Media award, People’s Choice Artist of the Year award, Best Male Single award and Song of The Year award for his record breaking album ''Tinto''.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Tlhankane |first=Mompati |date=2021-07-09 |title=William Last KRM, Han C top YAMAs nominees |url=https://www.mmegi.bw/lifestyle/william-last-krm-han-c-top-yamas-nominees/news |access-date=2021-10-12 |website=Mmegi Online |language=en}}</ref>

At the 2021 South African Hip Hop Awards, he received a nomination for Best International Act.<ref>{{cite web|title=Full list of 2021 SA Hip Hop Awards nominees {{!}} Fakaza News |url=https://fakazanews.com/2021/11/02/full-list-of-2021-sa-hip-hop-awards-nominees/|publisher=Fakaza News|access-date= November 2, 2021}}</ref>
