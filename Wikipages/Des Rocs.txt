'''Des Rocs''' is an American [[Rock music|rock]] artist from [[New York City|New York, New York]]. Des Rocs is a solo project produced and led by New York native Daniel Rocco. When touring Rocco is joined by his long time friends and bandmates, William Tully and Eric Mendelsohn.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Bio|url=https://desrocs.com/bio/|access-date=2021-07-30|website=Des Rocs|language=en-US|archive-date=July 30, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210730234358/https://desrocs.com/bio/|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Serra|first=Maria|date=2021-07-30|title=Des Rocs releases fierce single “MMC” and talks about his debut LP|url=https://www.altpress.com/features/des-rocs-mmc-new-album-lp/|access-date=2021-07-30|website=Alternative Press|language=en-US|archive-date=August 1, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210801221233/https://www.altpress.com/features/des-rocs-mmc-new-album-lp/|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-04-12|title=Des Rocs in Studio Z|url=https://wmmr.com/2021/04/12/des-rocs-in-studio-z/|access-date=2021-08-04|website=93.3 WMMR|language=en|archive-date=May 19, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210519203207/https://wmmr.com/2021/04/12/des-rocs-in-studio-z/|url-status=live}}</ref>

Des Rocs has toured with rock bands such as [[Muse (band)|Muse]], [[The Rolling Stones]], and [[The Struts]].<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|date=2020-08-24|title=Des Rocs is Hell-bent on Resuscitating Rock N’ Roll|url=https://americansongwriter.com/des-rocs-is-hell-bent-on-resuscitating-rock-n-roll/|access-date=2021-08-03|website=American Songwriter|language=en-US|archive-date=August 3, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210803031350/https://americansongwriter.com/des-rocs-is-hell-bent-on-resuscitating-rock-n-roll/|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=LIer to open for Rolling Stones on stadium tour|url=https://www.newsday.com/entertainment/music/des-roc-rolling-stones-tour-1.32015872|access-date=2021-08-03|website=Newsday|language=en|archive-date=May 21, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210521215419/https://www.newsday.com/entertainment/music/des-roc-rolling-stones-tour-1.32015872|url-status=live}}</ref><ref name=":2">{{Cite web|title=A Conversation About the Power of Music With Des Rocs |url=https://www.psychologytoday.com/us/blog/brick-brick/202012/conversation-about-the-power-music-des-rocs|access-date=2021-08-03|website=www.psychologytoday.com|language=en|archive-date=August 4, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210804082612/https://www.psychologytoday.com/us/blog/brick-brick/202012/conversation-about-the-power-music-des-rocs|url-status=live}}</ref> Influences for Rocco include [[Muse (band)|Muse]], [[Elvis Presley]], [[Jimi Hendrix]], [[Roy Orbison]], [[Kanye West]], and [[Queen (band)|Queen]], though Rocco's focus is individuality and sounding unlike other rock artists.<ref name=":1" /><ref>{{Cite web|title=Interview: Incendiary US artist Des Rocs - ==History==

=== Origins and EPs (2018–2020) ===
{{Expand section|more information on EPs and touring|date=August 2021}}
Rocco began performing in bars and basements from as early as 13 years old.<ref>{{Cite web|date=May 18, 2021|first=Chad|last=Childers|title=Des Rocs Announces First Headline Tour This Fall|url=https://loudwire.com/des-rocs-first-headline-tour-fall-2021/|access-date=2021-08-03|website=Loudwire|language=en|archive-date=August 3, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210803021113/https://loudwire.com/des-rocs-first-headline-tour-fall-2021/|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=August 21, 2019|first=Chad|last=Childers|title=Des Rocs Is Pushing Boundaries to Modernize Rock and Roll|url=https://loudwire.com/des-rocs-modernize-rock-interview/|access-date=2021-08-03|website=Loudwire|language=en|archive-date=May 19, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210519182856/https://loudwire.com/des-rocs-modernize-rock-interview/|url-status=live}}</ref> Before starting Des Rocs, he had performed in the band [[Secret_Weapons_(band)|Secret Weapons]], which had opened for bands such as [[Panic! at the Disco]], [[Fall Out Boy]], and [[Weezer]].<ref name=":2" /> Secret Weapons eventually went on permanent hiatus after bandmate Gerry Lang's health deteriorated with [[Lyme disease]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Gamboa|first=Glenn|date=2019-02-22|title=LI Sound: Secret Weapons' Rocco now Des Rocs|url=https://www.newsday.com/entertainment/columnists/glenn-gamboa/des-rocs-danny-rocco-secret-weapons-1.27592962|url-access=subscription|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210804082613/https://www.newsday.com/entertainment/columnists/des-rocs-danny-rocco-secret-weapons-1.27592962|archive-date=August 4, 2021|access-date=2021-08-03|website=Newsday|language=en}}</ref> He released his first EP, ''Let The Vultures In'', in November 2018.

=== ''Alive'' tour and ''A Real Good Person In A Real Bad Place'' (2021) ===
In the spring of 2021, Des Rocs announced the ''Alive'' tour, and a virtual livestream concert (also called the "Des Rocs Alive Virtual Concert".) Kicking off in the Fall of 2021, the tour will span 20+ cities across the United States, ending in the New York City's [[Bowery Ballroom]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Millar|first=Mark|date=2021-06-08|url-status=live|url=https://www.xsnoize.com/des-rocs-announces-worldwide-livestream-event/|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210922033854/https://www.xsnoize.com/des-rocs-announces-worldwide-livestream-event/|archive-date=2021-09-21|access-date=2021-09-21|language=en|title= DES ROCS announces worldwide livestream event
|website=XSNoize}}</ref> During the virtual concert, which was the first live playthrough of ''This is Our Life'', Des Rocs culminated with the track "Tick" from his yet-to-be-announced album. The track's lyrics also hinted at the title of the new album, ''A Real Good Person In A Real Bad Place''.

The album title and tracklist would be announced on July 30, 2021, coinciding with the release of the album's first single, "MMC" (Mickey Mouse Club).<ref>{{cite web|last=Kaufman|first=Spencer|url-status=live|url=https://consequence.net/2021/07/des-rocs-debut-album-new-single-mmc/|website=Consequence Heavy|language=en|access-date=2021-09-21|archive-date=2021-09-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210922034648/https://consequence.net/2021/07/des-rocs-debut-album-new-single-mmc/|title= Des Rocs Announces Debut Album, Unleashes Anthemic New Single “MMC”: Stream}}</ref> Des Rocs would release two more singles before the album's debut: "Imaginary Friends" and "Hanging by a Thread".
