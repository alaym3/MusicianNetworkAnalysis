'''Marcus & Martinus''' (both born in [[Elverum]], Norway 21 February 2002),<ref>{{cite web |title=Marcus & Martinus |url=https://marcusandmartinus.com/ |website=marcusandmartinus.com |access-date=7 July 2019}}</ref> occasionally known as '''M&M''', are a [[Norwegians|Norwegian]] pop-duo consisting of [[twin]] brothers Marcus and Martinus Gunnarsen. They have released three albums: ''[[Hei (album)|Hei]]'', ''[[Together (Marcus & Martinus album)|Together]]'' and ''[[Moments (Marcus & Martinus album)|Moments]]''. Since winning [[Melodi Grand Prix Junior]] in [[Melodi Grand Prix Junior 2012|2012]] they have won numerous [[prizes]] such as [[Spellemannprisen]] in 2017.

==Career==
===2012: ''Melodi Grand Prix junior''===
{{Main|Melodi Grand Prix Junior 2012}}
In 2012 Marcus & Martinus were contestants on the [[Melodi Grand Prix Junior 2012|eleventh season]] of ''[[Melodi Grand Prix Junior]]''. It was held at the [[Oslo Spektrum]] in [[Oslo]], Norway, and was broadcast live by [[Norwegian Broadcasting Corporation]] (NRK), hosted by Margrethe Røed and [[Tooji]]. They won the contest with the song "To dråper vann" (English: "Two Drops of Water").<ref>[http://nrksuper.no/super/mgpjr/ NRKSuper: Moro med tvillingbesøk] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160526030052/http://nrksuper.no/super/mgpjr/ |date=26 May 2016 }} (in Norway).</ref> The song peaked to number 8 on the [[VG-lista|Norwegian Singles Chart]].

===2015–2016: ''Hei & Together''===
On 23 February 2015, they released their debut studio album ''[[Hei (album)|Hei]]'' (English: ''Hi''). The album peaked at number 1 on the [[VG-lista|Norwegian Albums Chart]] in week 46 of 2015 (15 November 2015) after having charted for 35 weeks, the last 20 not leaving the top ten.<ref>Verdens Gang, 15 November 2015, p. 38</ref> The album includes the single "Plystre på deg" (English: "Whistle at You"). On 24 July 2015, they released the single "[[Elektrisk]]" (English: "Electric"), featuring vocals from [[Katastrofe]]. The song peaked at number 3 on the [[VG-lista|Norwegian Singles Chart]]. On 25 September 2015, they released the single "[[Ei som deg]]" (English: "One Like You") with Inner Tier. The song peaked at number 15 on the [[VG-lista|Norwegian Singles Chart]].  On 6 November they released the second version of their album Hei, "Hei (Fan Special)" with the same songs and some extras, one of the extras is Elektrisk. ===2017–present===
On 13 May.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nrk.no/mgp/marcus-_-martinus-leser-stemmene-fra-norge-1.13493637|title=Marcus & Martinus leser stemmene fra Norge|date=9 May 2017|website=Nrk.no}}</ref> On 21 May 2017, they released the single "Like It Like It" alongside American rapper [[Silentó]]. On 1 July, they released the single "First Kiss". The same month, on 14 July, they performed on Swedish Crown Princess Victoria's 40th birthday with their own song written especially for her ("On This Day"). In late July, they released the single "Dance with You". On 15 October 2017, they released their single "Make You Believe in Love". The single reached 34th place in Norway and 47th place in Sweden. It also featured in CelebMix's 21 songs by Under-21s in 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://celebmix.com/celebmixs-21-songs-by-under-21s/|title= CelebMix's 21 Songs by Under-21s|website=CelebMix.com|date=30 November 2017|access-date=7 February 2019}}</ref> On 4 November, they released their single "One Flight Away". On 15 November they released their single "Never" On 17 November, they released a new album named ''[[Moments (Marcus & Martinus album)|Moments]]''.{{citation needed|date=October 2018}} On 27 September, they released their single "Invited".
In 2019 they released an EP called ''SOON''. In 2020, they have released 2 singles, ''Love You Less'', and ''It's Christmas Time''. In 2021, they have been featured on the song ''Miserabel'' by Stig Brenner. Furthermore Marcus & Martinus released the single ''Belinda'' together with Latin star Alex Rose which reached 20th place in the Norwegian single charts.
