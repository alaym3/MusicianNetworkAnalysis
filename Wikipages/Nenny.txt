'''Marlene Fernanda Cardoso Tavares''' (born 19 November 2002), known by her stage name '''Nenny''', is a Portuguese singer-songwriter and [[rapper]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rimasebatidas.pt/atencao-a-nenny-a-filha-dos-filhos-do-rossi/|title=Atenção a NENNY, "a filha dos filhos do Rossi"|date=4 March 2019|website=Rimas e Batidas|language=pt-PT|access-date=19 December 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://sicnoticias.pt/cultura/2019-11-11-Nenny-a-rapper-de-Vialonga-que-sabe-onde-quer-chegar|title=SIC Notícias {{!}} Nenny: a rapper de Vialonga que sabe onde quer chegar|website=SIC Notícias|language=pt-PT|access-date=19 December 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://lifestyle.sapo.pt/fama/noticias-fama/artigos/cantora-nenny-deu-a-sua-primeira-entrevista-a-rita-ferro-rodrigues|title=Cantora Nenny deu a sua primeira entrevista a Rita Ferro Rodrigues|last=SAPO|website=SAPO Lifestyle|language=pt|access-date=19 December 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.elefantedepapel.pt/a-nenny/|title=A NENNY|date=19 November 2019|website=Elefante de Papel|language=pt-PT|access-date=19 December 2019}}</ref>

== Early life ==
She was born in [[Vialonga, Portugal]] to [[Cape Verdeans|Cape verdean]] parents.

At 9 years old she started writing her own songs.

With 11 years old she went to live in [[Creil]], [[France]] and later on [[Drancy]] where she lived alone with her mother, which later on she would write a song about. In September 2019 she moved to [[Luxembourg]] with her mother searching for better life conditions.<ref name=":0">{{Citation|title=Elefante de Papel entrevista NENNY|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LR3DjteQKok|language=en|access-date=2021-09-14}}</ref>

== Career ==

She started taking her music career more seriously in 2018, after receiving positive feedback for her [[Cover version|covers]] posted on her [[Instagram]] account. After a successful cover of "Devia Ir" from [[Wet Bed Gang]], the Portuguese hip-hop group decided to help Nenny grow on the music industry and got her a chance to record her work in a studio. The group are also from [[Vialonga, Portugal|Vialonga]] and saw this as an opportunity to share some local talent. With their help, Nenny releasead two times Platinum single "Sushi".<ref name=":0" />

In 2021 she performed at [[ColorsxStudios|COLORS]], presenting her new songs, "Tequila", in ''A COLORS SHOW'', and "Wave", in ''A COLORS ENCORE.''<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-01-25|title=Nenny mostra novo single no A COLORS SHOW|url=https://www.rimasebatidas.pt/nenny-mostra-novo-single-no-a-colors-show/|access-date=2021-03-01|website=Rimas e Batidas}}</ref>

In 3 July 2021, she performed in Sumol Summer Fest in [[Ericeira]], Portugal.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Cartaz|url=https://sumolsummerfest.com/cartaz/|access-date=2021-03-01|website=Sumol Summer Fes|language=pt-PT}}</ref>
