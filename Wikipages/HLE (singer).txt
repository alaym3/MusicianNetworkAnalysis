'''Hlengiwe Ntombela''' (born 12 April 1991), better known as '''HLE''', is a South African [[Gospel music|gospel]] singer and songwriter. She is a former member of the gospel choir Joyous Celebration, and currently performs as a solo artist.

== Biography ==

=== Life ===
Ntombela grew up in [[Newcastle, KwaZulu-Natal]], South Africa.<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|last=Sekudu|first=Bonolo|date=2019-06-19|title=Following God's lead|work=Move!|url=https://www.pressreader.com/south-africa/move/20190619/283274574253593|via=PressReader.com}}</ref> Her father was a bishop at Potters House Family Church.<ref name=":0" />

=== Career ===
Ntombela began her career as a backing vocalist for Ntokozo Mbambo.<ref name=":0" /> In 2012, she was asked to lead a song with the Joyous Celebration gospel choir, after [[Khaya Mthethwa]] was unable to do so because of a commitment to [[Idols South Africa]]. She joined the choir afterwards.<ref name=":0" />

Ntombela later left the choir and embarked on a solo career as HLE. She recorded her debut gospel album, titled ''Your Kingdom on Earth'', in 2019.<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web|date=2019-06-26|title=Hle Ntombela-Mthethwa launches her first ever gospel album|url=https://rosebankkillarneygazette.co.za/315233/hle-launches-new-gospel-hit/|access-date=2020-10-08|website=Rosebank Killarney Gazette|language=en-US}}</ref> The same year, she was featured on Benjamin Dube's album, ''Glory in His Presence.''<ref>{{Cite news|date=27 September 2019|title=Benjamin Dube releases his double disc album, Glory In His Presence|work=Just Nje|url=https://justnje.com/benjamin-dube-releases-his-double-disc-album-glory-in-his-presence/}}</ref>

In 2020, HLE released her debut solo single, "Dwala"; it debuted at number one on the [[iTunes]] charts.<ref>{{Cite news|date=25 February 2020|title=HLE's debut hits high note with #1 single on iTunes All-Genre Charts|work=GATEWAY NEWS|url=https://gatewaynews.co.za/hles-debut-hits-high-note-with-1-single-on-itunes-all-genre-charts/}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-02-01|title=Hle - Dwala »|url=https://www.ubetoo.com/music/hle-new-live-song-dwala-is-inspiring/|access-date=2020-07-31|website=uBeToo|language=en-ZA}}</ref> She launched a project, The HOW Project, which centers around the heart of a worshiper.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Hle Ntombela|url=https://afternoonexpress.co.za/guests/hle-ntombela/4935|website=Afternoon Express}}</ref> A month later, she released her second debut single, "You Are".<ref><nowiki>https://www.shazam.com/track/520047062/you-are-live</nowiki></ref> HLE released her debut album, ''Your Kingdom On Earth (Live)''.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Andile "FineSalt" Vilakazi|date=2020-08-15|title=HLE Announces Debut Album "Your Kingdom On Earth"|url=http://www.gospelcorneronline.com/hle-announces-debut-album-your-kingdom-on-earth/|access-date=2020-09-04|website=Gospel Corner Online|language=en}}</ref>

On July 1, 2020, HLE's weekly show titled "Adlib" premiered on One Gospel, Channel 331, DSTv. In this show, Hle hosts South African gospel musicians.<ref>{{Cite web|title=South African Gospel Singer HLE lands a hosting gig on One Gospel – Transafrica Radio|url=http://www.transafricaradio.net/south-african-gospel-singer-hle-lands-a-hosting-gig-on-one-gospel/|access-date=2020-07-31|language=en-US}}</ref>
