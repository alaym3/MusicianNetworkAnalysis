'''Paweł Ryszard Mikołajuw''' ({{IPA-pl|ˈpavɛw ˈrɨʂart ˌmikɔˈwajuf}}, born 2 December 1978), also known as '''Popek''', '''Popek Monster''' and '''Król Albanii''' (''Albanian King''),'''Abu Popeini al Ciapaq'''<ref>{{cite web|url=http://mma.pl/pierwszy-wywiad-popka-po-aresztowaniu-to-ja-chcialem-walczyc-z-pudzianem/|title=PIERWSZY WYWIAD POPKA PO ARESZTOWANIU: TO JA CHCIAŁEM WALCZYĆ Z PUDZIANEM!|publisher=mma.pl|language=pl|accessdate=29 November 2016}}</ref> is a [[Poles|Polish]] [[rapper]] and professional [[mixed martial arts]] fighter. In 2000, together with such rappers as Bosski Roman, Tadek, Pomidor and Kali founded the group Firma.<ref>{{cite web |title=Firma |publisher=hiphopedia.info |url=http://www.hiphopedia.info/index.php?site=show_artist&id=81&typ=Z}}</ref> Popek creates music from the border of [[dubstep]], [[Grime music|grime]] and [[gangsta rap]].<ref name="W lustrze widzę Monstera">{{cite web |title=W lustrze widzę Monstera |publisher=onet.pl |url=http://muzyka.onet.pl/publikacje/w-lustrze-widze-monstera,2,5458805,wiadomosc.html |accessdate=20 March 2013}}</ref>

Popek left his family home and dropped out of school at the age of 14. He speaks Polish, English and German.

Solo activities began in 2007, Popek has since released three albums, including one self-released. The album ''Monster'' was certified Gold in Poland and the [[Czech Republic]]. Firma has recorded 6 albums, Popek however was not involved in the production of the album ''NieLegalne Rytmy: Kontynuacja''.
