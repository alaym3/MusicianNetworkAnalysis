'''Victor & Leo''' are a Brazilian [[Música sertaneja|sertanejo]] duo made up of the brothers [[Vitor Chaves Zapalá Pimentel]] (b. 15 April 1975) and [[Leonardo Chaves Zapalá Pimentel]] (b. 4 October 1976).

Both brothers were born in [[Ponte Nova]], but grew up in [[Uberlândia]] in the region of [[Cerrado]], [[Minas Gerais]].<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|title = Victor & Leo :: Viva Por Mim|url = http://www.victoreleo.com/biografia/|website = www.victoreleo.com|access-date = 2015-06-08|first = Diálogo|last = webstudio}}</ref>

Their first live album, entitled ''[[Victor & Leo Ao Vivo]]'', was released in 2006, bringing them into the public eye with their first successful singles "Fada", "[[Vida Boa]]" and "Amigo Apaixonado". According to the [[Associação Brasileira dos Produtores de Discos|ABPD]], the album went double-platinum, meaning that more than 200,000 CDs have been sold in [[Brazil]].<ref>{{Cite web|title = ABPD {{!}} Associação Brasileira de Produtores de Disco|url = http://abpd.org.br/certificados_interna.asp?sArtista=Victor%2520&%2520Leo|website = abpd.org.br|access-date = 2015-06-12}}</ref>

Since then, the duo have enjoyed a successful career, with two of their albums and one of their DVDs winning Diamond certification. In 2013 they won the [[Latin Grammy Award]] for [[Latin Grammy Award for Best Sertaneja Music Album|Best Sertaneja Music Album]] with their album ''[[Ao Vivo em Floripa]]'', and have received five nominations for a Latin Grammy Award.<ref name=":0" />

Their most successful album is considered to be their third studio album, ''[[Borboletas]]'', which attained a Diamond certification, having sold more than 500,000 copies. It contains their number one hit of the same name, as well as "[[Deus e Eu No Sertão]]" and "[[Tem Que Ser Você]]", which also reached number one.<ref>{{Cite web|title = Victor & Leo - Deus E Eu No Sertao|url = http://top40-charts.com/song.php?sid=26964|website = Top40-Charts.com|access-date = 2015-06-12}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title = Victor & Leo - Tem Que Ser Voce|url = http://top40-charts.com/song.php?sid=24034|website = Top40-Charts.com|access-date = 2015-06-12}}</ref>

In 2015, their album ''Irmãos'' was nominated for the [[Latin Grammy Awards of 2015|16th Latin Grammy Awards]] in the [[Latin Grammy Award for Best Sertaneja Music Album|Best Sertaneja Music Album]] category.<ref>{{cite web|title=La lista completa de nominados a los Latin Grammy 2015|url=http://www.infobae.com/2015/09/23/1757537-la-lista-completa-nominados-los-latin-grammy-2015|website=[[Infobae]]|access-date=3 October 2015|language=es|date=23 September 2015}}</ref>
