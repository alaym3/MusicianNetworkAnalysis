'''Walls''' are an electronic band from the UK.They are a two piece composed of Alessio Natalizia and Sam Willis.

Italian Natalizia moved to [[London]] in 2008 and started his project Banjo or Freakout.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.banjoorfreakout.blogspot.com |title=banjo or freakout |publisher=Banjoorfreakout.blogspot.com |date=2013-05-22 |accessdate=2013-10-07}}</ref> Willis originated in Manchester and moved to London in 1996.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.kompakt.fm/releases/walls |title=// Walls // Walls |publisher=Kompakt.fm |date=2010-04-26 |accessdate=2013-10-07}}</ref> They have released two albums on [[Kompakt records|Kompakt Records]].<ref>{{cite web|author=Walls |url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/walls-mn0000231807/discography |title=Walls &#124; Discography |publisher=AllMusic |date= |accessdate=2013-10-07}}</ref>

Their first album (self titled) picked up ''[[Mojo (magazine)|Mojo]]'' 's 'Electronic Album Of The Year'. They were also featured in ''[[NME]]'' 's end-of-year list.

Walls won critical acclaim with their second album ''Coracle'', released in August 2011.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/coracle-mw0002199941 |title=Coracle - Walls &#124; Songs, Reviews, Credits, Awards |publisher=AllMusic |date=2011-08-16 |accessdate=2013-10-07}}</ref> Their first single from it, "Sunporch", was released on 29 August 2011.
