'''The Minds of 99''' is a Danish post-punk band, formed in 2012. Since then they have released one EP and two albums.

==Background==
The band, consisting of five childhood friends, won DR's Karrierrekanonen in 2013.<ref>[http://politiken.dk/kultur/musik/ECE2271240/sange-fra-en-rus-i-koege-det-var-naermest-som-i-twin-peaks/ Artikel bragt i Politiken, 25. april 2014]</ref> The first single, "Det er Knud som er død", from their debut album, is based on a poem by the Danish journalist and writer Tom Kristensen about the Danish scientist and national hero Knud Rasmussen. The song reached first place on Det Elektriske Barometer and became P3's Uundgåelige in February 2014.<ref>[http://www.sonymusic.dk/nyheder/?news_id=709 Pressemeddelelse fra Sonys hjemmeside]</ref>

==Awards==
In relation to their critically acclaimed debut album from May 2014, the band received numerous awards. Among others, the band won at the 2015 Årets Steppeulv for Song of the Year ("Det er Knud som er død"), as well as at the 2015 Carl Prisen for both Talent of the Year and Best Rock Songwriters of the Year. The band garnered a number of nominations as well at both Danish Music Awards (Danish Group of the Year, Best New Danish Act, Best Rock Publication), GAFFA-prisen (Best New Danish Act, Best Rock Publication) and Zulu Awards (Best New Danish Act).

==Performance at Orange Stage==
The Minds of 99 was the opening act at Roskilde Festival 2015. The concert was named as the best opener of the festival in years by numerous media outlets.<ref>[http://www.b.dk/koncerter/the-minds-of-99-aabnede-orange-med-et-brag The Minds Of 99 åbnede Orange med et brag - Koncerter | www.b.dk<!-- Bot genereret titel -->]</ref><ref>[http://soundvenue.com/musik/2015/07/the-minds-of-99-bedste-aabning-af-orange-scene-i-mange-aar-154305 The Minds of 99 – den bedste åbning af Orange Scene i mange år / Koncert<!-- Bot genereret titel -->]</ref>
