'''Kaya Wilkins''', better known as '''Okay Kaya''', is a Norwegian-American musician and actress from New Jersey.

==History==
Wilkins' first release as Okay Kaya was the song "Damn, Gravity", which was released in 2015.<ref name="vice">{{cite web|url=https://i-d.vice.com/en_us/article/zmxzby/premiere-okay-kaya-damn-gravity|title=premiere: okay kaya, 'damn, gravity'|last=|first=|date=|website=|publisher=[[Vice (magazine)|Vice]]|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|accessdate=24 January 2020}}</ref><ref name="complex">{{cite web |last1=Corrigan |first1=Graham |title=Who Is Okay Kaya? |url=https://www.complex.com/pigeons-and-planes/2018/05/who-is-okay-kaya |publisher=[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]] |accessdate=24 January 2020}}</ref> Wilkins released her first full-length album, ''Both'', in 2018.<ref name="pitchfork">{{cite web |last1=Slater |first1=Maya-Roisin |title=Okay Kaya: Both |url=https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/okay-kaya-both/ |publisher=[[Pitchfork (website)|Pitchfork]] |accessdate=24 January 2020}}</ref><ref name="vogue">{{cite web |last1=Valenti |first1=Lauren |title=This Rising Artist is Getting Startlingly Honest About Depression—One Album and Instagram at a Time |url=https://www.vogue.com/article/okay-kaya-wilkins-debut-album-both-mental-health-instagram-vampire-iud |publisher=[[Vogue (magazine)|Vogue]] |accessdate=24 January 2020}}</ref><ref name="vicemagazine">{{cite web |last1=Jackson |first1=Reed |title=Is Okay Kaya Living in Pop Music's Future? |url=https://www.vice.com/en_us/article/qvxxmd/okay-kaya-interview-future-of-pop-both-2018 |publisher=[[Vice (magazine)|Vice]] |accessdate=24 January 2020}}</ref> The album was recorded with her boyfriend Aaron Maine of the band [[Porches (band)|Porches]].<ref name="complex"/> In 2020, Wilkins released her second full-length album under the moniker Okay Kaya titled ''[[Watch This Liquid Pour Itself]]'' in 2020 via [[Jagjaguwar]].<ref name="theguardian"/><ref name="mtv">{{cite web |last1=Madden |first1=Emma |title=Okay Kaya Makes Sex Jams For People With Depression And Yeast Infections |url=http://www.mtv.com/news/3153199/okay-kaya-interview-watch-this-liquid-pour-itself/ |publisher=[[MTV]] |accessdate=24 January 2020}}</ref> The album received three out of five stars from ''The Guardian''.<ref name="theguardian">{{cite web |last1=Beaumont-Thomas |first1=Ben |title=Okay Kaya: Watch This Liquid Pour Itself review – strange dream-pop tales of thwarted lust |url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2020/jan/24/okay-kaya-watch-this-liquid-pour-itself-review |publisher=[[The Guardian]] |accessdate=24 January 2020}}</ref> She played her first acting role in the Norwegian drama ''[[Thelma (2017 film)|Thelma]]''. In August 2020, Wilkins released her mixtape, ''Surviving is the New Living.''<ref name=":0" />
