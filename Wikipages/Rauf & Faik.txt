'''Rauf & Faik''' ('''Рауф и Фаик''') are a [[Russia]]n [[pop music|pop]] duet, formed by twin brothers Rauf and Faik Mirzaev (Russian: ''Рауф и Фаик Мирзаев'') (born July 7, 1999; [[Izhevsk]], Russia).<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.muz1.tv/person/583|title=Rauf & Faik - Музыка Первого|website=www.muz1.tv|language=en|access-date=2019-11-16}}</ref> They are best known for their [[Russian language|Russian]]-language #1 single "Childhood" (Russian: Детство), released in 2018. The brothers are ethnically [[Azerbaijanis|Azerbaijani]]. They release music sung in both Russian and [[English language|English]], though they also speak [[Azerbaijani language|Azerbaijani]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.trend.az/life/showbiz/3044279.html|title="Rauf & Faik" признались в любви на азербайджанском языке (ВИДЕО)|date=2019-04-10|website=Trend.Az|language=ru|access-date=2019-12-24}}</ref>

== Biography ==
According to the brothers, they have always had a fascination with music, as they inherited musical talent from their maternal grandfather, who they knew as an opera singer and a director at an opera house in [[Baku]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://medium.com/@kerisavoca/rauf-faik-pain-and-memories-76f2e0eb2890|title=Rauf Faik — Pain and Memories|last=Savoca|first=Keri|date=2019-04-11|website=Medium|language=en|access-date=2019-11-16}}</ref> They began to study music at the age of 4; their neighbour saw that they were talented and offered to take them to a music-focused college, where they played the piano until 8 years old, where they transitioned to vocals.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=https://the-flow.ru/features/rauf-i-faik-interview-2019|title=Rauf & Faik — вы точно слышали их песни. А теперь почитайте большое интервью|website=The-Flow.ru|language=ru|access-date=2019-11-16}}</ref> They later sung with the House of Children's Creativity (Russian: Доме детского творчества) and music studio Above the Rainbow (Russian: Выше радуги).<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|url=https://popcake.tv/news/na-ih-kontsertah-hochetsya-vlyubitsya-vse-chto-nuzhno-znat-o-rauf-amp-faik|title=На их концертах хочется влюбиться: все, что нужно знать о Rauf & Faik|last=Дарья|website=POPCAKE|language=ru-RU|access-date=2019-11-16}}</ref> According to Music First (Russian: [https://www.muz1.tv/ Музыки первого]) their vocal range is 4 1/2 [[octave]]s.<ref name=":0" />

They grew up listening to 90s [[Soul music|Soul]], [[Rhythm and blues|R&B]], and Pop music, citing [[Brian McKnight]] and [[Michael Jackson]].<ref name=":1" /> More recently, they have commented on receiving inspiration from western artists such as [[XXXTentacion]], who they consider very inspiring, and to a lesser degree, [[Post Malone]];<ref name=":1" /> though their profile on Music First also lists [[Sam Smith]].<ref name=":0" />

Before focusing entirely on music (2014-2018), they worked as singers in a cafe.

== Music ==
Their first music video — released for the song "Love Remained Yesterday" — amassed several hundred thousand views, and thousands of likes,<ref>{{Citation|title=Rauf & Faik - Love remained yesterday|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PS1ZY3GY5TU|language=en|access-date=2019-11-16}}</ref> resulting in offers to sing at multiple venues.<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":2" /> A later music video for the song "I Love You" (Russian: Я люблю тебя), became the 28th most popular song of 2018 on the Russian social networking site [[VK (service)|VKontakte]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://sova.ponominalu.ru/novosti/vk-top-30-songs-2018/|title="ВКонтакте" назвала самые популярные треки 2018 года|last=Амиров|first=Фарит|date=2018-12-26|website=Интернет-издание «Сова»|language=ru-RU|access-date=2019-11-16}}</ref>

Their debut album, also titled "I Love You", was released later in 2018. According to the brothers, they invested only 1,500 [[Ruble]]s (approx. $25) into advertising the album. Despite little inherent publicity, the song "Childhood" (Russian: Детство) reached number one on VKontakte.<ref name=":1" />

"PAIN & MEMORIES", their second studio album, released on March 15, 2019, and quickly reached 8th place on [[Apple Music]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cosmo.ru/lifestyle/news/29-03-2019/trek-zacepila-artura-pirozhkova-neozhidanno-pokoril-apple-music/|title=Трек "Зацепила" Артура Пирожкова неожиданно покорил Apple Music|website=www.cosmo.ru|language=ru-RU|access-date=2019-11-16}}</ref>

Since beginning their professional music career, the brothers have toured in over 20 Russian cities, [[Estonia]], [[Finland]], [[Sweden]], and [[Germany]].<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":2" />
