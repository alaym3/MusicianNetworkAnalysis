'''10cm''' ({{Korean|hangul=십센치|hanja=|rr=Sipsenchi|mr=}}), is a South Korean musical act currently composed of singer-songwriter Kwon Jung-yeol.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Lee|first=Gyu-lee|date=2020-01-22|title=10CM gears up for comeback with new album|url=http://www.koreatimes.co.kr/www/art/2021/02/732_282329.html|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2021-02-04|website=The Korea Times|language=en}}</ref>

Originally a duo composed of Kwon and Yoon Cheol-jong, 10cm debuted in 2010.<ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=2012-06-23|title=김윤주 열애男 10cm 권정열 누구? ‘무도’ 센치한 하하 그 보컬|url=https://www.newsen.com/news_view.php?uid=201206231039252210|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2021-02-04|website=Newsen|language=ko}}</ref> With the release of the single "Americano," the group quickly became one of the most popular indie acts in South Korea,<ref>{{Cite web|last=Jackson|first=Julie|date=2014-01-08|title=Indie duo 10cm to follow K-pop’s lead with first U.S. concert|url=http://www.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20140108000770|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2021-02-05|website=The Korea Herald|language=en}}</ref> winning the This Year's Discovery Award at the [[2010 Mnet Asian Music Awards]],<ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=2010-11-29|title=투애니원 ‘2010MAMA’ 5관왕..미쓰에이 ‘올해의 노래’|url=https://www.donga.com/news/Entertainment/article/all/20101129/32931588/3|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2021-02-04|website=Dong-A Ilbo|language=ko}}</ref> followed by Best Pop Song at the 2011 [[Korean Music Awards]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=2011-02-23|title=인디계의 아이돌 10cm ‘최우수 팝’ 노래 부문 수상(한국대중음악상)|url=https://www.newsen.com/news_view.php?uid=201102231944211002|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2021-02-04|website=Newsen|language=ko}}</ref> Their first full-length album, ''1.0'' (2011) sold 30,000 copies, a record for an indie act at the time.<ref name="30,000_copies">{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=2012-10-10|title=십센치 "억지 세련미는 싫어..투박함 살렸죠"|url=https://www.yna.co.kr/view/AKR20121010206800005|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2021-02-05|website=Yonhap News|language=ko}}</ref> Following a slew of successful singles,<ref>{{Cite web|last=Kim|first=Yu-young|date=2016-10-07|title=10cm to release new album|url=http://www.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20161007000255|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2021-02-05|website=The Korea Herald|language=en}}</ref> and the release of the full-length albums ''2.0'' (2012)<ref name="30,000_copies" /> and ''3.0'' (2014),<ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=2014-11-04|title=십센치(10cm), 19일 정규 3집 ‘3.0’ 발매 확정…패션콘셉트 ‘눈길’|url=http://bntnews.hankyung.com/apps/news?popup=0&nid=04&c1=04&c2=04&c3=00&nkey=201411040911363&mode=sub_view|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2021-02-05|website=BNT News|language=ko}}</ref> the group achieved its first number-one hit on the [[Gaon Digital Chart]] in 2016 with the single "What the Spring??".<ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=2016-04-15|title=10cm '봄이 좋냐', 가온차트 3관왕 달성 '기염'|url=http://www.xportsnews.com/?ac=article_view&entry_id=713522|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2021-02-05|website=X Sports News|language=ko}}</ref>

Yoon left the group in July 2017 while facing charges for marijuana use.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Hong|first=Dam-young|date=2017-06-19|title=Former 10cm member admits to marijuana use|url=http://kpopherald.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=201707191337516426647_2|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2021-02-04|website=The Korea Herald}}</ref> Kwon has continued to promote as a solo artist under the name 10cm, releasing the album ''4.0'' in September 2017<ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=2017-09-01|title=십센치(10cm), 6시 발매된 정규앨범 성공적 차트 진입|url=https://www.hankyung.com/entertainment/article/201709015765I|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2021-02-04|website=The Korea Economic Daily|language=ko}}</ref> and several singles, including the [[Gaon Digital Chart]] top-ten hits "Mattress" (2018), "However" (2019), and "Borrow Your Night" (2021). His concert "Hotel room 1010"(2020) is the first paid online concert of Korean non-idol artist.<ref name=":0" />
==Career==
===Pre-debut===
Kwon and Yoon were both from [[Gumi, North Gyeongsang]].When yoon was in his first year of high school, he was part of a school band club called 'Mad Pulse',<ref name="maeil">{{cite web|url=http://news.imaeil.com/page/view/2011100807020112176|title=10대도시 전국 콘서트 구미출신 인디밴드 '십센치'|date=2011-10-08|publisher=maeilsinmun}}</ref> and Jeongyeol(who was in middle school at the time) brought a letter and a recorded tape of his work to Yoon to ask him if he can join the band.As jeongyeol gained some local fame with his singing skills, kwon was finally allowed to perform in Yoon's band.Because of this inclusion of Kwon to the team, Yoon(originally a vocalist) decided to take his position in the band as a guitarist to make room for Kwon.<ref name="haniexid">{{cite web|url=https://www.hani.co.kr/arti/culture/music/423006.html|publisher=[[Hankyoreh]]|title=‘오늘 밤’ 당신과 나, 사랑의 거리 ‘10cm'|date=2010-05-28}}</ref>Kwon and Yoon formed the band 'Haeryeong(해령)'in Daegu in 2004.<ref name="haniexid"/>After enlisting in the South korean military, the two formed the band 'Seven Hills' named after the army unit they served in, called chilbong.<ref name="haniexid"/>
===Birth of 10cm===
Around 2008 or 2009, the two arrived in Seoul to venture into the indie scene.<ref name="haniexid"/><ref name="maeil"/>The band debuted as a indie band at 'Club ta(打)', a famous live club located in Hongdae<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mk.co.kr/news/culture/view/2016/10/710827/|publisher=maeilkyongje|title=홍대 대표 라이브클럽 ‘클럽 타(打)’ 사라졌다|date=2016-10-11}}</ref>
