'''Ufuk Beydemir''' (born 14 April 1992) is a Turkish singer, songwriter and [[tennis]] coach.<ref name="hürriyet">{{cite news |title=Ufuk Beydemir kimdir ve kaç yaşında? 'Ay Tenli Kadın' ile ünlü oldu |url=http://www.hurriyet.com.tr/kelebek/hayat/ufuk-beydemir-kimdir-ve-kac-yasinda-ay-tenli-kadin-ile-unlu-oldu-41223004 |accessdate=6 July 2019 |work=[[Hürriyet Daily News]] |date=23 May 2019}}</ref> He became known in Turkey with the release of his song "Ay Tenli Kadın".<ref name="gq">{{cite news |last1=Bulutsuz |first1=Deniz |title=Müziğini Pozitif Frekansa Taşıyan Bir Müzisyen: Ufuk Beydemir |url=https://gq.com.tr/moty/muzigini-pozitif-frekansa-tasiyan-bir-muzisyen-ufuk-beydemir |accessdate=6 July 2019 |work=[[GQ]] Türkiye |publisher=gq.com.tr |date=24 January 2019}}</ref> In 2018, Beydemir ranked third on the list of nominees for "Musician of the Year Award" at the GQ Men of the Year awards by ''[[GQ]] Türkiye''.<ref name="gq"/><ref name="oylama">{{cite news |title=Yılın Kendini Gösteren Müzisyeni Oylama Sonucu |url=https://gq.com.tr/yilin-kendini-gosteren-muzisyeni |accessdate=6 July 2019 |work=[[GQ]] Türkiye |publisher=gq.com.tr}}</ref> Beydemir, who released his first album ''Sevda Gibi'' in 2018,<ref>{{cite news |last1=Demirkol |first1=Hikmet |title=‘Sevda Gibi’nin Hikayesi |url=http://www.hurriyet.com.tr/yazarlar/hikmet-demirkol/sevda-gibinin-hikayesi-40808496 |accessdate=6 July 2019 |work=[[Hürriyet Daily News]] |date=18 April 2018}}</ref> was given the "Most Powerful Newcomer-Singer" award at the 2019 PowerTürk Music Awards.<ref>{{cite news |title=Powertürk Müzik Ödülleri’ne Mabel Matiz damgası |url=https://www.sozcu.com.tr/hayatim/kultur-sanat-haberleri/powerturk-muzik-odullerine-mabel-matiz-damgasi/ |accessdate=6 July 2019 |work=[[Sözcü]] |publisher=sozcu.com.tr |date=14 April 2019}}</ref>
