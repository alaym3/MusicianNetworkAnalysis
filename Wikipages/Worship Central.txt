'''Worship Central''' is a British [[Christian music]] [[Contemporary worship music|praise and worship]] charity from [[Birmingham]], [[United Kingdom]], founded at [[Holy Trinity Brompton]] in 2006 by [[Tim Hughes]] and Al Gordon.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.crossrhythms.co.uk/articles/music/Worship_Central_Tim_Hughes_and_Al_Gordon_talk_about_worship_movement/44595/p1/|title=Worship Central: Tim Hughes and Al Gordon talk about worship movement - Worship Central|website=www.crossrhythms.co.uk|access-date=2018-05-17}}</ref> The charity exists to train [[worship pastor]]s for churches as well as writing and producing new music for churches.

== Background ==
The charity was founded in 2006 out of [[London]], [[United Kingdom]], where they were located at [[Holy Trinity Brompton]], while now they are based at [[Gas Street Church]]. Notable staff members and associate songwriters include [[Tim Hughes]], [[Ben Cantelon]], Al Gordon and [[Luke Hellebronth]].

== History ==
Worship Central released their first album, ''Lifting High'',<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.louderthanthemusic.com/document.php?id=693|title=Worship Central To Release 'Lifting High'|access-date=2018-05-17}}</ref> in 2009, then ''Spirit Break Out'',<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.louderthanthemusic.com/product.php?id=461|title=Worship Central - Spirit Break Out|access-date=2018-05-17}}</ref> in 2011, ''Counting On Your Name,'' also in 2011, ''Let It Be Known''<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.christiantoday.com/article/worship-central-hits-top-spot-with-let-it-be-known/32063.htm|title=Worship Central hits top spot with Let it be Known|access-date=2018-05-17|language=en}}</ref> in 2012, ''[[Set Apart]]'' in 2014, ''Mercy Road''<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.louderthanthemusic.com/product.php?id=2472|title=Worship Central - Mercy Road|access-date=2018-05-17}}</ref> in 2017 and ''Stir A Passion'' in 2018.

Their albums have been recorded by the labels [[Integrity Media|Integrity Music]] and [[Capitol Records]]. Let It Be Known reached number 45 in the UK Official Album Charts and lasted a record five weeks at number 1 at the Official Christian And Gospel Chart<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.officialcharts.com/chart-news/worship-central-return-to-top-of-official-christian-and-gospel-chart__3050/|title=Worship Central return to top of Official Christian And Gospel Chart|access-date=2018-05-17|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.officialcharts.com/chart-news/worship-central-still-at-number-1-on-official-christian-and-gospel-cha__2897/|title=Worship Central still at Number 1 on Official Christian And Gospel Chart|access-date=2018-05-17|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.officialcharts.com/chart-news/worship-central-top-the-official-christian-and-gospel-chart-__2882/|title=Worship Central top the Official Christian And Gospel Chart!|access-date=2018-05-17|language=en}}</ref>
